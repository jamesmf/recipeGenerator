SLOPPY JOES


2  lb. ground beef
1/2  c. chopped green pepper or celery
1  medium onion, chopped
1  Tbsp. brown sugar
1  tsp. dry mustard
1/4  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper
1  can tomato soup
1/2  c. catsup
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  Tbsp. vinegar

      In large skillet, brown ground beef, green pepper and onion; drain well. Stir in remaining ingredients. Cover; simmer 15 to 20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until thoroughly heated.
