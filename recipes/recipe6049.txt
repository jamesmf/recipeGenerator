AUTUMN APPLE SALAD


20  oz. can crushed pineapple
2/3  c. sugar
1 (3 oz.)  pkg. lemon jello
8  oz. cream cheese
1  c. diced Red Delicious apples
1  c. chopped celery
1  c. whipped topping
1  c. chopped pecans

      Put pineapple, it's juice and sugar in saucepan.  Bring to boil and cook 3 minutes.  Stir in gelatin and cream cheese; cool. Fold in nuts, celery, apples, and whipped topping.  Pour into 9 inch square dish.  Chill until set.
