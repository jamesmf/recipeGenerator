PISTACHIO SALAD


1 (3 oz.)  pkg. instant pistachio pudding
1 (20 oz.)  can crushed pineapple, undrained
1/2  c. miniature marshmallows
1/2  c. chopped nuts
1 (4 oz.)  carton whipped topping

      Pour dry pudding mix over undrained pineapple and mix.  Add marshmallows and nuts.  Slowly fold in whipped topping. Refrigerate 3-4 hours.
