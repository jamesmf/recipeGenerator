CHICKEN ITALIANO


3/4  c. Kretschmer original wheat germ
1/4  c. grated Parmesan cheese
1  tsp. oregano, crushed
1/8  tsp. garlic powder
2  (2 1/2 to 3 lb.) broiler-fryer chicken, cut up
1/4  c. milk

      Heat oven to 350 degrees;. Combine wheat germ, cheese and seasonings. Dip chicken pieces in milk. Coat in dry ingredients. Place in 13 x 9-inch glass baking dish. Bake 50 to 60 minutes or until juices run clear when chicken is pierced with a fork. Makes 4 servings.
