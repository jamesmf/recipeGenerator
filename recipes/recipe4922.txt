WINTER SALAD WITH WALNUTS AND APPLES


1  pkg. (10-oz.) any European-style salad mix, washed
1  thinly sliced tart apple
1  pkg. (4-oz.) Blue cheese, crumbled
1  c. walnut halves
1/4  c. vegetable oil
1/4  c. balsamic vinegar
  salt and pepper to taste

      Combine salad, apples, walnuts and Blue cheese.  Whisk together oil and vinegar; pour over salad.  Add salt and pepper to taste, then toss well.  Serve immediately.
