HOLIDAY WREATH


30  marshmallows
1/2  c. butter
1  tsp. vanilla
2  tsp. green food coloring
3 1/2  c. corn flakes
  red candied cherries

      Combine marshmallows, butter, vanilla and food coloring in top of double boiler; heat until smooth, stirring frequently.  Take off stove; gradually add cereal.  Drop by tablespoon onto waxed paper; cut in the shape of a 9-inch circle.  Add candied red cherries in groups of three.
