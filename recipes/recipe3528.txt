BREAD STICKS


1 1/2  c. warm water
1  Tbsp. yeast
2  Tbsp. sugar
1  tsp. salt
3 to 4  c. flour

      Mix and let rise 15 to 20 minutes.  Roll 1/2 inch thick or more and cut with a pizza cutter.  Melt 1/4 cup butter.  Dip bread stick on both sides and lay on cookie sheet.  Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese, garlic salt, sesame seeds, poppy seeds, parsley, red pepper, minced onion, or a combination of your favorites. Raise 10 minutes.  Bake at 400 degrees; for 15 minutes.
