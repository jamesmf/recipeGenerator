CHINESE CHICKEN SALAD


3  c. lettuce
1/4  c. sliced green onions
1 1/2  c. cooked chopped chicken
1/4  c. chopped red cabbage
1 (8 oz.)  can water chestnuts, chopped
1/4  c. frozen pea pods
1/2  c. Julienne carrots (optional)

 
Dressing:
3  Tbsp. soy sauce
1/2  tsp. garlic powder
2  Tbsp. rice vinegar
1/2  tsp. pepper
2  Tbsp. vegetable oil
1  Tbsp. sugar
1  can Chow Mein noodles

      Mix all salad dressing ingredients.  Toss with the chicken salad and serve on Chow Mein noodles.
