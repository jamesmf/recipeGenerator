SALMON CHEESE PIE


1  Pillsbury pie crust (top and bottom)
1  can salmon
1  Tbsp. grated onion
4  Tbsp. juice from salmon

      Open the salmon and flake. Save juice. Mix and spread in pie shell. Cover top with American cheese slices. Place pie crust on top. Cut slits in top and bake until brown.
Vegetable Sauce:
1/4  c. butter
1/4  c. plain flour
1  tsp. mustard
2  c. milk
1  can green peas, drained

      While baking, make sauce. Melt the butter in saucepan. Add the flour, mustard and milk. Let thicken, then add the peas. Serve over sliced pie.
