NO BAKE COOKIES


2  c. sugar
1/4  lb. oleo or butter
1/3  c. cocoa
1/2  c. milk
1  tsp. vanilla
1/3  c. peanut butter
3  c. dry oatmeal

      Mix sugar, oleo (room temperature mixes easier), cocoa and milk in small saucepan.  Bring to boil for about a minute and remove from heat.  Stir in vanilla, peanut butter and oatmeal.  Quickly drop by spoonfuls onto waxed paper and cool.
