PAULINE'S FIESTA CHICKEN


2 to 3 small  cans cream of chicken soup
2 1/4  c. sour cream
1/4  c. (or more) diced onion
1  can diced green chilies
2  c. diced, cooked chicken
1 large  pkg. Monterey Jack cheese, shredded
1  pkg. (12) flour tortillas (11-inch)

      Mix soup, sour cream, onions and chilies.  Mix chicken, cheese and 1/2 of soup mixture.  Spread some sauce on bottom of pan.  Place even amount of chicken mixture on each tortilla; spread across, roll up and place seam side down.  Cover evenly with remaining sauce.  Sprinkle some more cheese on top.  Cover with foil.  Bake 45 minutes at 325 degrees;.
