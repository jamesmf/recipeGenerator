FRANKFURTERS


1  lb. frankfurters (8 to 10)
8  to 10 frankfurter buns, warmed
  tomato salsa topping
  Italian two pepper topping
  New Chicago style topping

      Place frankfurters on grid over medium coals, turning occasionally.  Broil 8 to 10 minutes.  Serve grilled frankfurters on buns.  Prepare topping and use as desired.
