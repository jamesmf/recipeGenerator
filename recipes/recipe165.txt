HAWAIIAN MACADAMIA NUT COOKIES


1/2  c. sugar
1/2  c. brown sugar (packed)
1/2  c. butter (softened)
1  egg
1  tsp. vanilla
1 1/2  c. flour
1/2  tsp. baking soda
1/2  tsp. salt
4 to 6  oz. can Macadamia nuts, chopped
1  (12 oz.) bag white chocolate chips

      Preheat oven to 375 degrees;.  Mix sugars, butter or shortening, egg, and vanilla until creamy.  Mix in remaining ingredients.  Drop dough by teaspoon on ungreased cookie sheet.  Bake 8 to 10 minutes, until light brown.  Do not overcook.  Cool slightly before removing from cookie sheet.  Makes 3 dozen.
