INDIAN CORN STEW


2  medium onions, chopped fine
1/2  green pepper, chopped fine
2  Tbsp. butter
1  lb. ground beef
1 (No. 2)  can whole kernel corn
1 medium  can tomatoes
2  tsp. sugar
1  tsp. salt
2  Tbsp. flour, blended with 2 Tbsp. cold water

      Cook onions and green pepper with butter 5 minutes.  Add meat and brown well, stirring frequently.  Add corn, tomatoes, sugar and salt.  Simmer about 15 minutes.  Stir in blended flour and water; cook for a few minutes and serve.
