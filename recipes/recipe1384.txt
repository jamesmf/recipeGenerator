HICKORY PORK SHOULDER


1  (4 to 6 lb.) pork shoulder
2  tsp. garlic salt
2  tsp. garlic pepper
1/2  c. hickory chips

 
Basting Sauce:
1  c. vinegar
5  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
2/3  c. salad oil
3  Tbsp. butter
1  lemon, thinly sliced
2  cloves garlic, minced
3  Tbsp. grated ginger
2  Tbsp. dry mustard

      Rub meat with garlic salt and garlic pepper.  In a covered grill arrange preheated coals around a drip pan.  Place meat on grill over drip pan.  Pierce meat in several places.  Brush with Basting Sauce.  Lower hood on grill.  Grill for 2 hours, brushing meat every 30 minutes with sauce.  After 2 hours cover top of meat with aluminum foil.  Return to grill.  Add 1/2 cup hickory chips (presoaked).  Grill 1 1/2 hours.
      Basting Sauce:  In saucepan heat basting ingredients until flavors are blended.
