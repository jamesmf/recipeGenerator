NONNIE'S SPICY MEAT LOAF


2  lb. ground chuck or ground round
1 1/2  tsp. salt
1  tsp. black pepper
1/2  tsp. cumin
2  tsp. oregano
2  tsp. basil
1  tsp. sage
1  tsp. minced garlic
2  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  pkg. onion soup mix
2  eggs, beaten
1 small  can tomato sauce
1  can Hunt's special Italian-style tomatoes
1 1/2 to 2  c. crushed crackers
1  medium onion, chopped
1  medium bell pepper, chopped

      Mix all ingredients together. Add beaten eggs last and mix well. Bake in covered casserole dish at 350 degrees; for 1 hour. Remove cover and brown top for a few minutes. Spread 1 can of tomato sauce (small can) over top and cook for another 10 to 15 minutes, uncovered.
