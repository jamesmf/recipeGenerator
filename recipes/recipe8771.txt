BRISKET


3 to 5  lb. brisket
1  bottle Heinz chili sauce
1/4  c. brown sugar
1  Tbsp. mustard
1  clove garlic or garlic powder
  salt and pepper
2  onions
1  can beer

      Remove fat from brisket.  Place in pan.  Combine chili sauce, brown sugar, mustard, garlic, salt and pepper.  Pour mixture over meat.
      Slice 2 onions thinly on meat.  Pour 1 can beer AROUND meat, not on top.  Cover pan with foil.  Bake at 325 degrees; for 4 hours.
      Cool and slice thin with electric knife.  Put meat back in broth and heat on top of stove.
