PARTY CHICKEN


4  whole or 8 half chicken breasts (boneless, skinless)
8  strips bacon
1 (4.5 oz.)  jar dried beef
1 (10.75 oz.)  can cream of mushroom soup (undiluted)
1 (8 oz.)  carton sour cream

      Cover the bottom of a flat, greased 9 x 13 x 2-inch baking dish with dried beef. Wrap each chicken breast with a strip of bacon. Arrange chicken on dried beef. Mix undiluted soup and sour cream and pour over chicken. Bake uncovered at 275 degrees; for 3 hours or until done.
