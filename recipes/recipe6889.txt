SHRIMP CREOLE


1  c. flour
1  c. oil
2  c. onion, chopped
1/2  c. bell pepper, chopped
2  cloves garlic, minced
1  pack shrimp
1 large  can tomatoes
2 small  cans tomato paste or tomato sauce
1  can Ro-Tel
1/4  tsp. cayenne
  parsley flakes

	Make roux of flour and cooking oil. Add onions, bell peppers and garlic and cook until soft. Add tomatoes and tomato paste. Mix well and cook about 5 minutes. Add about 6 cups water and the rest of the seasonings. Let simmer for 1 hour. Add shrimp and cook for 15 minutes. Add parsley and continue to cook until sauce is thick. Serve over hot rice.
