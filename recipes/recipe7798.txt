QUAIL


2  quail (per person)
  fresh raw oysters
  Worcestershire sauce
  bacon

      Wash and pat dry quail (whole).  Stuff each one with raw oysters.  Season with Worcestershire sauce.  Place in a greased roaster.  Cover each quail with half strip of bacon, covering all exposed areas.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes or golden brown and thigh juice runs clear with knife.  Serve with milk gravy and grits.
