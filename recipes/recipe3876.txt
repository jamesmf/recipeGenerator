MEXICAN DIP


  soft cream cheese (small tub)
  chili (no beans, Armour, Hormel or Libby's 13 oz. can)
  green chilies, chopped (Ortega or Old El Paso)
  Mexican 4 cheese shredded pkg. (2 c.)
  Tostitos chips

      Layer in a shallow Pyrex dish in order. Put in microwave for 5 minutes (longer for some microwaves). Serve with chips.
