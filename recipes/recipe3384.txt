PEACH SALSA


2  lb. ripe peaches (about 6 large), peeled, pitted, and coarsely chopped
2  Tbsp. finely chopped red onion
1  Tbsp. chopped fresh mint
1  Tbsp. fresh lime juice
1  tsp. seeded, finely chopped jalapeno chile
1/8  tsp. salt

      In medium bowl, combine all ingredients; stir gently.  Cover and refrigerate at least 1 hour to blend flavors.  May be refrigerated up to 2 days.  Makes about 3 cups.  Good with grilled beef, chicken, pork, or fish.
