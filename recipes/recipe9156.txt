ADVENTUROUS BEANS


1  can wax beans, drained
1  can green beans, drained
1  can kidney beans, drained
1  can pork and beans
1  can tomato soup (zestier if you use barbecue sauce)
1 (6 oz.)  can tomato paste
1  c. brown sugar
1  Tbsp. dry mustard
1  lb. browned, drained and sliced Italian link sausage
1  lb. browned and drained hot or mild ground sausage

      Combine all ingredients into large casserole dish. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 1/2 hours.
