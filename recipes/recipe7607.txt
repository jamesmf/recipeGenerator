SAUSAGE CASSEROLE


1  lb. ground pork sausage
2  envelopes chicken noodle soup
1/2  c. uncooked rice
4 1/2  c. boiling water
1  green pepper, chopped
1  medium onion, chopped
1/2  c. celery, chopped
1/2  c. slivered almonds

      Brown sausage, drain and set aside.  In covered saucepan cook soup mix, rice and water for 7 minutes.  Then combine all ingredients and place in baking dish.  (Can prepare the day before needed.)  Bake 1 hour in 350 degrees; oven.  Good served with mushroom gravy.  Yield:  8-10 servings.
