CHICKEN-VEGETABLE GUMBO


2/3  lb. fresh okra pods
  vegetable cooking spray
1  c. coarsely chopped onion
1  c. sliced fresh mushrooms
1/2  c. sliced celery
1/2  c. chopped green bell pepper
2  cloves garlic, minced
2 (14 1/2 oz.)  cans whole tomatoes, undrained
1 (10 1/2 oz.)  can chicken broth
1/2  c. sherry
1  tsp. chicken flavored bouillon granules
1  tsp. dried Italian seasoning
1/4  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
2  c. cubed, cooked chicken breast (about 1 lb.)

      Remove tip and stem from okra.  Slice 1/4-inch slices and set aside.  Coat a large Dutch oven with cooking spray.  Place over medium heat until hot.  Add onions and next 4 ingredients.  Cook 5 minutes, stirring constantly.  Add okra, tomatoes and next 6 ingredients.  Stir well.  Bring to a boil; cover, reduce heat and simmer 1 hour or until vegetables are tender.  Add chicken; stir well.  Remove from heat; cover and let stand 5 minutes.  Yield: 2 quarts.   Good served over rice!
