GERMAN POTATO SOUP


3  lb. potatoes
  soup greens
1  bay leaf
1  small onion, quartered
2  bouillon cubes
  bacon
1  diced onion

      Peel, quarter and slice potatoes. Combine with water and all seasonings. Cook for 30 to 40 minutes. Dice bacon; fry. Add diced onion and fry until brown. Put in with potatoes. Add wieners or smoked sausage until heated. Eat with soup.
