ORIENTAL SALAD


1/2  c. Fat Free Italian Dressing
1/2  c. vinegar
4  packets of Sweet 'N Low
2  pkgs. of chicken flavored Ramen noodles
1  bag of cole slaw mix
1/2  c. of sunflower seeds
1  c. of finely chopped broccoli

      Mix vinegar, sugar, dressing and flavor packets from noodles together, microwave for 30 seconds, and refrigerate.  Crush noodles and mix with slaw mix, sunflower seeds and broccoli in a large bowl.  Pour liquid over salad mix and refrigerate.  Serve cold.
