QUESADILLA BREAD


2  c. Bisquick
1  c. sugar
1/2  c. Parmesan cheese
2  eggs
2  sticks melted butter or margarine
1  c. milk

      Combine dry ingredients first.  Combine the damp ingredients. Mix all thoroughly, but do not overbeat.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 1/2 hour in an ungreased rectangular baking pan.
