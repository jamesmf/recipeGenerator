EASY CHEESE BALL


2 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
1  envelope Lipton onion soup mix
  dash of Worcestershire sauce
  chopped nuts (walnuts or pecans)
1/2  jar Marzetti chunky Blue cheese salad dressing

      When cream cheese is softened, place in a bowl with the salad dressing, onion soup mix and dash of Worcestershire sauce.  Mix well (using hands).  Shape into a ball and place on a sheet of wax paper, covered with nuts.  Coat ball well and top with maraschino cherry.  Serve with crackers.  Recipe can be doubled to make 2 balls.
