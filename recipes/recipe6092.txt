GRAPE SALAD


3  oz. (1 pkg.) cream cheese, softened
1/4  c. powdered sugar
3  Tbsp. sour cream
1/4  tsp. vanilla
2  lb. seedless grapes
1/4 to 1/2  c. brown sugar

      Combine cream cheese, powdered sugar and sour cream in a bowl.  Blend well.  Stir in vanilla.  Fold in grapes.  Cover and chill.
      Sprinkle with brown sugar just before serving.
