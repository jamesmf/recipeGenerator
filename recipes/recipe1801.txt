BACON AND SWISS QUICHE


1/4  lb. Swiss, cut in 1/2-inch strips
12  slices bacon
1  pie crust (frozen)
4  eggs
2  c. heavy cream
1/4  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper

      Fry bacon.  Spread frozen pie crust with 1 tablespoon melted butter.  Crumble bacon into bottom of pie and add Swiss strips. Mix 2 cups heavy cream with 4 eggs and 1/4 teaspoon salt and pepper.  Pour over bacon and Swiss.  Bake at 425 degrees; for first 15 minutes.  Drop temperature to 350 degrees; and bake another 30 minutes.
