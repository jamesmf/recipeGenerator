SALSA PLUS


1  to 2 green or red peppers
2  tomatoes, seeded
1  cucumber, seeded
1  red or yellow onion
1  tsp. garlic, minced
2  Tbsp. cilantro, chopped (optional)
1/2  tsp. cumin (optional)
1 (20 oz.)  jar prepared salsa

      Chop all of the vegetables in bite-sized pieces in a 3-quart bowl. Add prepared salsa; mix and serve with corn chips of your choice.
