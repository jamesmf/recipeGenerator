MEXI-CASSEROLE


1 1/2  lb. ground beef
1  pkg. taco seasoning mix (reserve 1 tsp. for topping)
1  can tomato soup
1  can chili beans (omit this if you prefer)

 
Topping:
1  c. pancake mix
1  tsp. taco seasoning
1  c. shredded Cheddar cheese
1/2  c. milk
1  egg

      Brown ground beef.  Drain.  Stir in seasoning mix, soup and beans.  Simmer while preparing the topping.
       Combine all topping ingredients.  Blend well.  Pour meat mixture into 3-quart casserole.  Spoon topping over meat.  Bake, uncovered, in 400 degrees; oven for 20 to 25 minutes, until golden brown.
