MANDARIN ORANGE SALAD

Salad:
1/2  c. slivered almonds
3  tsp. sugar
4  c. lettuce
4  c. spinach
1  c. chopped celery
1/2  c. chopped green onions
1  can mandarin oranges, drained

 
Dressing:
1/3  c. oil
1/4  c. white vinegar
1/4  c. sugar
1  Tbsp. chopped parsley
1/2  tsp. salt
  dash of pepper
1/2  tsp. Tabasco

      Mix dressing ingredients thoroughly. Chill 1 hour.
      In a heavy skillet, cook almonds and sugar over medium-low heat until sugar melts and coats almonds, stirring frequently. Pour onto foil to cool.
      In a large bowl, combine lettuce, spinach, celery and onions.
       To serve, add almonds and oranges. Add dressing and toss.
