BEST EVER MEAT LOAF


1  pkg. Mrs. Grass soup
2  lb. ground beef
  garlic powder
2  c. shredded bread
1  c. brown/mushroom gravy, mixed
1  egg
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire
1  Tbsp. steak sauce
1  Tbsp. soy sauce

      Preheat oven to 375 degrees;.  Combine all ingredients except 1/2 cup gravy.  Bake for 1/2 hour.  Pour remaining gravy over loaf and bake for another 1/2 hour or until done in middle.
