BAKED CHOW MEIN CHICKEN


1 (5 oz.)  can chow mein noodles, crushed
1/3  c. chopped blanched almonds
1/3  c. butter or margarine
2  Tbsp. soy sauce
1  tsp. chopped fresh ginger
1/4  tsp. ground black pepper
6  boneless skinless chicken breast halves

      Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Place chow mein noodles in a plastic storage bag; crush noodles. Combine crushed noodles & nuts in shallow dish. Melt butter in microwave. Add soy sauce, ginger, & pepper to butter; stir to combine. Dip chicken breasts in butter mixture, then roll in noodles. Gently press noodle mixture onto chicken. Lay in bottom of 9 x 13 inch pan. Bake 25-30 minutes or until juices run clear when pierced with a fork.
