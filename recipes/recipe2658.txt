CORN DOGS


1  c. corn meal
1  c. flour
2  Tbsp. sugar
2  tsp. baking powder
1/2  tsp. salt
1  egg, slightly beaten
1  c. milk
2  Tbsp. melted shortening
1  lb. wieners

      Mix  corn  meal  with  flour,  sugar, baking powder and salt. Add  egg  and  milk;  blend  in melted shortening.  Mix well.  Dip wieners in batter.  Fry in deep fat.
