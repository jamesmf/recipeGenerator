AFRICAN ONE POT MEAL-AMERICAN STYLE


3  to 5 carrots
3  to 5 potatoes
3  to 5 celery stalks
1  yellow peppers
1  sweet onion
  crush: Basil, marjoram, garlic
1/4  c. chicken stock
  whole chicken cut into pieces

      Use a  casserole dish with lid. Spray Pam on inside of dish. Mix vegetables and place in dish first, then add stock. Place chicken pieces on top. Cover dish with lid.
      Bake in 350 degrees;F oven for 1-1/2 hours.
