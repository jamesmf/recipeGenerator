CHICKEN CUTLETS PARMESAN


1 to 2  lb. chicken breasts, sliced thin
2  eggs, beaten
8  oz. Mozzarella cheese
1/2  c. Parmesan cheese
  garlic to taste
  onion to taste
  bread crumbs
  basil (if available)

      Dip each cutlet in egg first, then in bread crumbs. Fry each cutlet in hot olive oil.
Italian Tomato Sauce:
1 (28 oz.)  can crushed tomatoes
  sauteed onions and garlic
  fresh basil (if available)
  little salt

      Add the saut√©ed onions and garlic, fresh basil and salt to the crushed tomatoes. Cook sauce for about an hour.
       Arrange cooked cutlets alternating with sauce, adding Mozzarella and Parmesan cheese between layers. Bake in oven at 350 degrees; for about an hour or cheese melts.
