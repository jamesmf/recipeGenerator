TACO SALAD I


1 (15 oz.)  can pinto beans
1  head lettuce, shredded
1  green pepper, chopped
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. Cheddar cheese, shredded
1 (16 oz.)  bottle Catalina French dressing
1  chopped onion
1  lb. ground beef
1  tomato, diced
1  (8 oz.) bag taco flavored Doritos

	Crumble ground beef into skillet; brown. Drain well and let cool. Place all ingredients in a large bowl.
	 Just before serving, crumble Doritos into salad. Add Catalina French dressing and mix well.
