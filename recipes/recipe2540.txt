WATERGATE SALAD


1  pkg. Jell-O pistachio pudding mix (large)
1 (20 oz.)  can crushed pineapple
1  large container Cool Whip
1/2  c. miniature marshmallows

      Mix dry pistachio pudding mix in medium-large bowl with crushed  pineapple.  Add Cool Whip and marshmallows.  Mix well and chill before serving.  Refrigerate.  Serves 8 to 10.
