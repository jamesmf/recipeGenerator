RASPBERRY CREAM DIP


6  oz. fat-free cream cheese, softened
1/2  tsp. ginger
1  c. raspberries, crushed
1  Tbsp. brown sugar
1  Tbsp. red wine vinegar

      In a small bowl, combine cream cheese, brown sugar, ginger and vinegar; blend until smooth. Stir in raspberries; refrigerate. Serve as a dip or dressing for fresh fruit. Makes 1 1/4 cups.
