HAM ROLLS


2  pkg. Frey's sliced ham
8  oz. cream cheese, softened
1/2  c. pecans, finely chopped
3  dashes Tabasco sauce
1/2  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/4  tsp. dry mustard
1/4  tsp. soy sauce
1/2  tsp. paprika
1/4  tsp. onion powder
  salt and pepper to taste

      Mix all ingredients together, except ham. Spread mixture on ham slices; roll and secure with toothpicks. Refrigerate several hours; slice and serve with toothpicks.
