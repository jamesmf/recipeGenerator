POTATO SOUP


4  potatoes
1  c. milk
1  can chicken broth
1  c. water
1  yellow onion
2  Tbsp. butter
1/2  tsp. celery salt

	Cut up potatoes and bring to boil in an uncovered pot of water for 5 minutes.  Drain water.	Add milk, chicken broth, water, onions, butter and celery salt.  Cook on medium heat for 15 minutes.
