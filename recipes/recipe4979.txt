CROCK POT BEEF STEW


1  1/2 lbs. stew meat
1  onion, chopped
2  c. chopped celery
2  c. chopped carrots
2  c. cubed potatoes (red potatoes)
1  can tomato soup
1  can cream of mushroom soup
  Salt and pepper to taste

      Put all in crock pot and cook on low 6-8 hours.  (Do not dilute soups.)
