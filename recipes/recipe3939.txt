SALSA


6  large vine ripened tomatoes
2  large onions
  1---32 oz tomato sauce
6  Jalapeno peppers
1  Tbsp. salt and pepper
1/2  Tbsp. garlic
1  tsp. lime juice
4  oz of tomato paste

      Chop onions, Jalapenos, and tomatoes. Mix the rest of the ingredients in a blender set on puree until semi chunky. Salsa should be a liquid chunky mixture. Eat with any type of chips.
