BROCCOLI, CAULIFLOWER SALAD

Dressing:
1  c. mayonnaise
1/2  c. sugar
2  Tbsp. vinegar

 
Salad:
1  bunch broccoli
1  head cauliflower
1/2  lb. bacon, fried crisp and crumbled
1  c. shredded Cheddar cheese

      Dressing:  Mix well and let stand in fridge for about 2 hours to blend flavors.
      Mix all greens, bacon and cheese.  Pour dressing over all and mix thoroughly.  Serve.  Good in place of coleslaw.
