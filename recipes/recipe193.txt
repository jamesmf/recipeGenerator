SANTA'S WHISKERS


1  c. butter or margarine
1  c. sugar
1  tsp. vanilla
1/2  tsp. rum extract
2 1/2  c. flour
1/2  tsp. nutmeg
3/4  c. finely chopped red and green cherries
1/2  c. chopped nuts
3/4  c. flaked coconut

      Cream butter and sugar together.  Stir in vanilla and rum extract.  Stir in flour, nutmeg, cherries and nuts.  Chill dough. Shape dough into rolls 1 inch in diameter.  Roll in coconut.  Wrap in waxed paper and chill.  (Dough may be frozen at this stage and held until needed.)  Slice cookies 1/4 inch thick.  Place on ungreased cookie sheets.  Bake in preheated oven at 375 degrees; for 5 to 7 minutes or until edges are golden.  Makes 14 dozen cookies.
