SOUR CREAM WAFFLES


1 3/4  c. all-purpose flour
1  Tbsp. sugar
1  Tbsp. baking powder
1/2  tsp. salt
8  Tbsp. (1 stick) unsalted butter, melted
1  c. milk
1/2  c. sour cream
3  large eggs

      Whisk flour, sugar, baking powder and salt in a large bowl to combine and make a well in the center.  Whisk butter, milk, sour cream and eggs in medium bowl and pour into well.  Whisk just until smooth;  do not over mix.  Spoon about 1/4 c. of batter into center of hot waffle iron and cook.
