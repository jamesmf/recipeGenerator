SAUSAGE AND KLUSKI NOODLES


1 1/2  lb. hot sausage, loose
1  med. onion, chopped
1  lg. green pepper, chopped
  Minced garlic to taste
1  c. Half & Half cream
1/2  c. Romano
1/4  c. fresh parsley, chopped
1  tsp. marjoram
  Kluski noodles, cooked, drained

      Brown sausage, pepper, garlic and onion.  Add cream, cheese, parsley and marjoram.  Blend together and mix into cooked kluski noodles.
