ROCK CORNISH HENS AU VIN BLANC


4  Rock Cornish hens
  salt and pepper to taste
4  Tbsp. tarragon leaves
  garlic salt to taste
1  stalk celery, diced
1  medium onion, diced
1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can chicken broth
2  Tbsp. flour

 
Basting Sauce:
1/2  c. butter
1/2  c. dry white wine
2  tsp. tarragon leaves

      Wash hens inside and out.  Dry.  Preheat oven to 450 degrees;. Sprinkle inside of hens with salt, pepper, tarragon and garlic salt.  Tie legs together.  Place close together in low pan without rack.  Mix basting sauce.  Roast, basting often, for 1 hour or until brown and tender.  Cook celery, onion and giblets in chicken broth for 45 minutes, strain.  Mix flour with 1/4 cup water until smooth.  Add to broth and cook until thick and smooth.  Serve with hens.  Serves 4.
