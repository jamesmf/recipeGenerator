CREAMY BAKED CHICKEN BREASTS


8  chicken breasts (skinless, boneless)
8  slices Swiss cheese
1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can cream of chicken soup (undiluted)
1/4  c. dry white wine
1  c. herb seasoned stuffing mix, crushed
1/4  c. butter or margarine, melted

	Preheat oven to 350 degrees;. Arrange chicken in a lightly greased 13 x 9 x 2 baking dish. Top each chicken breast with a slice of Swiss cheese.
	 In a separate bowl, combine soup and wine and stir well. Spoon evenly over the chicken.  Sprinkle with the crushed stuffing mix.  Drizzle butter over the crumbs. Bake for 45 to 55 minutes or until chicken is completely cooked. Serves 8.
