SPOOKY HAND TREAT


  candy corn
  clear plastic glove
  popcorn
  M&M's
  black yarn
  black plastic spider ring

      Place one candy corn in each finger of the plastic glove (they will be the fingernails).  Fill the glove with popcorn and a few M&M's.  Tie the bag with black yarn.  Place the spider ring on a finger.  Now it is a spooky hand treat to eat.
