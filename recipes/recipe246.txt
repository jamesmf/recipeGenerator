MUMBLES


1/3  c. butter
1/2  c. sugar
1  egg
  grated rind of 1/2 lemon
1  c. flour
1/3 level  tsp. salt
1 level  tsp. baking powder

      Beat the butter and sugar to a creamy mixture.  Add the beaten egg and lemon rind.  Sift dry ingredients together and add.  Drop by spoonfuls on a greased pan and bake about 10 minutes at 350 degrees;.
