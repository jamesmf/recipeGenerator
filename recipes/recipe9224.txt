OVEN BARBECUED SPARERIBS


10  lb. pork spareribs, cut into serving size pieces
2  large onions, chopped
1  c. finely chopped celery
2  c. ketchup
1  c. apple juice
1  c. water
1/2  c. lemon juice
1/4  c. packed brown sugar
1/4  c. ground mustard
1/4  c. cider vinegar
1/4  c. worcestershire sauce
2  Tbsp. paprika
2  Tbsp. prepared horseradish
1/4 to 1/2  tsp. cayenne pepper

      Place ribs in a single layer in large baking pans.  Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes.  Drain, reserving 1/4 cup drippings.  Turn ribs; bake 30 minutes longer.  Meanwhile, in a saucepan, saut√© onions and celery in reserved drippings until tender.
      Stir in the remaining ingredients; bring to a boil.  Drain ribs; pour sauce over ribs.  Cover and bake for 15 minutes; turn ribs and baste.  Bake 15 minutes longer or until tender.  Yield: 12 servings.
