MARDI GRAS SEAFOOD CASSEROLE


2  c. rice (uncooked)
4  c. sour cream
12  oz. mild green chiles, chopped
2  lb. grated Monterey Jack cheese
2  tsp. salt
1  lb. peeled boiled shrimp
1  pkg. frozen crab meat
  paprika
  butter

      Prepare rice, then stir in sour cream, salt, chiles and cheese. Mix well. Add seafood; mix again. Pour into 9 x 13-inch pan. Sprinkle with paprika. Dot with butter. Bake at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes.
