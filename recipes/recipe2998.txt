CHICKEN CHEESE BALL
(Appetizer)  

1 (3 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
1  can Underwood chicken spread
2  Tbsp. minced onion
3/4  tsp. soy sauce
1  Tbsp. mayonnaise

      Mix all ingredients together and make into a ball.  Can roll in chopped nuts (optional).  Chill.  Serve with crackers.
      Note:  May double recipe.
