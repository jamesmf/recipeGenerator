OVEN FRIED CHICKEN


  chicken
1/4  c. margarine
1/4  c. shortening
1/2  c. flour
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. paprika
1/4  tsp. pepper

	Melt in a 450 degrees; oven 1/4 cup margarine and 1/4 cup shortening. Mix flour, salt, paprika and pepper. Cover chicken with flour mixture and put in pan, skin side down, first for 30 minutes, then turn over and cook 30 minutes on other side.
