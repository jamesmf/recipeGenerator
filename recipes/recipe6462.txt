CHICKEN CASSEROLE


1  chicken
1  c. sour cream
1  can mushroom soup
1/4  c. milk
1  stick butter or margarine
40  Ritz crackers
2  Tbsp. poppy seeds

      Cook chicken and take meat from the bones.  Put on the bottom of casserole dish.  Mix sour cream, soup and milk.  Pour this over chicken.  Melt butter/margarine.  Put over the mixture in the dish.  Sprinkle poppy seeds over the crackers.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes or until bubbly.
