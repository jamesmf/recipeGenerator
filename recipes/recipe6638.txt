SLOPPY JOES
(50 Servings)  

8  lb. ground beef
3 (24 oz.)  bottles of catsup
  Dash of tabasco sauce
3  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
2  Tbsp. celery seed
5  Tbsp. prepared mustard
2  green peppers, chopped
8  medium onions, chopped
  Salt and pepper to taste

      Brown meat and onions.  Add other ingredients and cook until well blended for good flavor, at least one hour.
