HAM HOT DISH


1 1/2  c. diced leftover ham
1 1/2  c. uncooked noodles
2  c. seasoned white sauce
1  c. diced American cheese
1  Tbsp. grated onion
1  stalk celery, diced
1 3  oz. can mushrooms

      Cook noodles in boiling, salted water. Add cheese to hot white sauce. Add onions celery and mushrooms (browned in butter). Add noodles, ham, salt and pepper to taste. Turn into greased baking dish and cover with buttered crumbs. Bake at 375 degrees; for 30 minutes.
