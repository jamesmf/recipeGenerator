PEANUT-RAISIN-COCOA CHEWS


1  c. peanut butter
3/4  c. raisins
1/2  c. honey
1/4  c. cocoa
1/2  tsp. vanilla
1 1/3  c. chopped nuts, crushed
  cereal or shredded coconut

      Combine peanut butter, raisins, honey, cocoa and vanilla in a bowl; stir until well blended.  Spread chopped nuts, crushed cereal or shredded coconut in flat pan or dish; drop cocoa mixture by teaspoonfuls and roll into desired coating until well covered. Chill.  Makes about 30 candies.  You get more if you make them smaller.
