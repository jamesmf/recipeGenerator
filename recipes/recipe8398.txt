CHICKEN CASSEROLE


1  c. mayonnaise
1  c. cream of chicken soup
2  c. diced chicken
1 1/4  c. celery
1/2  c. slivered almonds
  crushed potato chips
  grated Cheddar cheese

      Blend well mayonnaise and cream of chicken soup.  Then add the chicken, celery and almonds.  Put in casserole; sprinkle potato chips, almonds and Cheddar cheese on top.  Bake in a 300 degrees; oven for 40 minutes.
