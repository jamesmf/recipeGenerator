CROCK POT STROGANOFF


1  lb. round steak or stew meat, cut in 1 inch pieces
1  pkg. dry onion soup mix
1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can cream of mushroom soup
1  c. mushrooms
  black pepper to taste
1  small container of sour cream

	Cook the first five ingredients on low all day.  I add the sour cream about 20 minutes prior to serving.  Serve over rice, noodles, or toast as desired.
