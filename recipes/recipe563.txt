MINI STRAWBERRY MILK SHAKE


1  c. skim milk
1/4  c. sliced unsweetened strawberries
1  tsp. honey
1  tsp. Sweet 'N Low
3  ice cubes, crushed
1/2  tsp. vanilla

      Combine all ingredients.  Blend at low speed until the ice is dissolved.  Makes 1 serving.  Contains 137 calories.  This is filling and satisfying.
