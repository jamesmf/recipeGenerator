TACO BURGERS


1  lb. ground beef
1 (16 oz.)  can tomatoes, cut up
1  tsp. chili powder
1  tsp. worcestershire sauce
3/4  tsp. garlic salt
1/2  tsp. sugar
1/4  tsp. dry mustard
8  hamburger buns, split and toasted
2  c. shredded lettuce
1  c. shredded American cheese (4 oz.)

      In skillet, brown meat; drain off excess fat.  Add drained tomatoes, chili powder, worcestershire sauce, garlic salt, sugar and dry mustard.  Stir well, breaking up large pieces of tomato. Bring to boiling point and reduce heat.  Simmer, uncovered for 15 to 20 minutes or until thickened.  Spoon onto toasted buns. Sprinkle each of the burgers with lettuce and cheese.  Makes 8 sandwiches.
