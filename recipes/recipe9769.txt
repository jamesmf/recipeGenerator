TUNA CASSEROLE


2  c. cooked macaroni
1  can condensed celery or mushroom soup
1  c. milk
1  c. grated Cheddar cheese
1/4  c. green pepper (optional)
1  Tbsp. minced onion
1  can tuna

      Cook and drain macaroni. Slowly heat soup, milk and cheese until the cheese melts. Mix macaroni, minced onion, tuna and soup mixture together and put into a greased 1 1/2 to 1 3/4-quart casserole. Bake at 325 degrees; for 30 minutes.
      Variations: Substitute a pound of wieners or a can of Spam or a pound of ham, cut up, for the tuna.
