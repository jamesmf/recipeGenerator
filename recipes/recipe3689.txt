QUICK PICKLES


7  c. cucumbers (sliced with skins on)
1  c. green peppers, sliced
1  c. sliced onion
2  tbsp. salt
1  tbsp. mustard seed
1  tbsp. celery seed
2  c. sugar
1  c. vinegar

      Place cucumbers in jar or large bowl.  Mix remaining ingredients and pour over cucumbers. Keep refrigerated. They will last several weeks.
