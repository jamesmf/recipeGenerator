CHOCOLATE MACADAMIA MUFFINS


1 1/2  c. semi-sweet morsels
1/3  c. butter
1 1/2  c. flour
1  tsp. baking soda
1/4  tsp. salt
1  c. chopped Macadamia nuts
2/3  c. sour cream
1/4  c. corn syrup
1  egg

      Preheat oven to 375 degrees;.  Combine over hot (not boiling) water, semi-sweet morsels and butter.  Stir until morsels are melted and mixture is smooth; set aside.  In a large bowl, combine flour, baking soda and salt.  Add nuts to coat well.  Make a well in the center of flour mixture.  In a small bowl, combine melted morsels, sour cream, corn syrup and egg until blended.  Add to dry ingredients; stir until just moistened.  Spoon into paper-lined muffin pans, filling each about 3/4 full.  Bake for 15 to 18 minutes.  Cool 5 minutes; remove from pans.  Cool completely or serve warm.  Makes about 15 muffins.
