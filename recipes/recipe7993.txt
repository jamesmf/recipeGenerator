HONEY-APPLE PORK CHOPS


4  boneless pork loin or butterfly chops
1 1/2  c. apple cider or apple juice
1/4  c. lemon juice
1/4  c. soy sauce
2  Tbsp. honey
1  clove garlic, minced
1/4  tsp. pepper

      Combine all ingredients, except pork chops; mix well.  Place pork chops in shallow dish; pour marinade over chops.  Cover and refrigerate overnight, turning occasionally.  Remove pork chops from marinade.  Place on grill approximately 6 inches above medium-hot coals.  Grill for 10 to 15 minutes, turning once and basting with marinade.
