FRUIT SALAD


1  large container Cool Whip
1  can Eagle Brand sweetened condensed milk
1 large  can fruit cocktail
1 large  can sliced peaches
1 (16 oz.)  can crushed pineapple
2 small  cans mandarin oranges
3  bananas, sliced
  miniature marshmallows
  chopped walnuts (optional)

      Whip together Cool Whip and condensed milk with a fork until mixed.  Add drained cans of fruit cocktail, sliced peaches (cut up), pineapple, and mandarin oranges.  Stir.  Then stir in bananas (cut up or sliced) and 3 or 4 handfuls of marshmallows.  Extra bananas and or marshmallows may be added until salad is thick. Chopped walnuts may be added to top for decoration or put into salad but are not necessary.  Refrigerate.
