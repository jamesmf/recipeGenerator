ROCKY ROAD CANDY


35  large marshmallows, divided
1 (12 oz.)  pkg. chocolate chips (semi-sweet)
1/2  lb. margarine
4  c. sugar
1 1/2  c. evaporated milk
1  tsp. vanilla
2  c. chopped walnuts

      Cut 15 marshmallows into fourths and put on a tray in the freezer.  In a large glass bowl, cut the margarine into the chocolate chips.  Set aside.
      Bring to a boil the sugar, milk and 20 whole marshmallows. Boil rapidly for 5 full minutes, stirring constantly.  Pour over butter and chip mixture.  Stir until it starts to thicken.  Add vanilla and nuts.  Add frozen marshmallow pieces and mix a little. Pour into a buttered pan and place in refrigerator until set.
