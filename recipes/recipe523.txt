CASPER'S CRACKER COOKIES


3  doz. round butter crackers
1  c. peanut butter (approximately)
2  lb. white dipping chocolate

      Spread the crackers liberally with the peanut butter.  Set aside.  Melt white dipping chocolate in a double boiler.  Holding each cracker with tongs, dip into the melted chocolate.  Spread wax paper under cooling rack and let the crackers dry on a rack.  Store in a cool place.  Makes 3 dozen.
