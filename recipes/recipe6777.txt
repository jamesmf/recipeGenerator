MEAT LOAF DELIGHT


2  lb. ground beef
1  tsp. salt
1  onion
2  dashes of pepper
1  egg, well beaten
1  Tbsp. margarine, melted
1/2  c. tomato soup
1/2  c. bread crumbs
2  Tbsp. flour

 
Topping:
1/3  c. ketchup
2  Tbsp. brown sugar
2  tsp. mustard

      Combine beef, salt, onion, pepper, egg, margarine, soup, bread crumbs and flour. Put in loaf pan and bake at 375 degrees; for 55 minutes.
      Combine topping mixture. Put on meat loaf and bake an additional 30 minutes or until done.
