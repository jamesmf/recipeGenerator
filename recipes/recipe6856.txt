RED BEANS AND RICE


2  c. Uncle Ben's long grain rice, cooked
1 (16 oz.)  can red chile beans in chile gravy
1  tsp. chili powder
1/4  tsp. cumin
  dash of garlic salt

      In saucepan, heat beans without letting them boil. Stir in chili powder, cumin and garlic salt. When piping hot, add warm rice and gently mix.
