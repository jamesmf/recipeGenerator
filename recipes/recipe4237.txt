REUBEN SPREAD


3 (2.5 oz.)  pkg. Buddig corned beef, chopped
1/4  c. onion, chopped
1 1/2  c. mayonnaise (not Miracle Whip)
1 (14 oz.)  can Bavarian-style kraut
1 1/2  c. shredded Swiss cheese

      Mix all ingredients together. Bake in a 350 degrees; oven for 30 minutes. Keep warm in a slow cooker. Serve on cocktail rye.
