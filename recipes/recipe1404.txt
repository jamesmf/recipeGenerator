SLAB RIBS ON THE GRILL


  slab of ribs
  barbecue sauce

 
Spray Bottle Containing:
1  qt. water
1  c. vinegar
2  Tbsp. garlic powder

      Put slab of ribs on hot grill.  After 1/2 hour, spray with mixture on each side as you are cooking it.  You can dab a piece of French bread on ribs while cooking with mixture on.  Spray as often as you like.  Cook until done, about 2 hours on high rack of grill; all depending on size of ribs.  Put barbecue sauce on last 1/2 hour of cooking.
