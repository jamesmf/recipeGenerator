BARBECUED CHICKEN WINGS


2 1/2  lb. chicken wings
1  small onion, chopped
3/4  c. chili sauce
1/4  c. vinegar
1/4  c. cooking oil
1/4  c. brown sugar
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  tsp. salt
  dash of pepper

      Place wings in baking pan. Peel and grate onion; mix with chili sauce, vinegar, oil, sugar, Worcestershire sauce, salt and pepper. Stir well. Pour over wings and bake about one hour or until done at 375 degrees;. Pour off fat and serve while hot.
