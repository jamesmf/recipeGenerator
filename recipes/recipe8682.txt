KING RANCH CASSEROLE


1 (8 oz.)  can Ro-Tel tomatoes and chilies
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  pkg. corn tortillas
1  chicken, cooked, deboned and cut into chunks
10  oz. mild shredded Cheddar cheese

	Preheat oven to 350 degrees;.  Mix Ro-Tel tomatoes and soups in a bowl.
	 In a 9 x 12 baking dish place a layer of tortillas on bottom of dish, then a layer of chicken.  Repeat this method about three times. Pour soup mixture over the last layer of chicken.  Sprinkle with cheese.  Cover with foil. Bake for 30 minutes.
