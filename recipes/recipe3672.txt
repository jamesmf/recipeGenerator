VEGETABLE BALL


2 8  oz. cream cheese
1/2  c. grated carrots
1/4  c. celery, chopped fine
1 2  oz. Jar Pimientos, drained
2  Tbsp. green pepper, chopped fine
1  tsp. seasoning salt
  dash of garlic powder

      Mix together and refrigerate for 2 hours.  Make ball and roll in sunflower seeds or chopped nuts.  Serve with crackers.
