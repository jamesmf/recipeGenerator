PERFECT CROSS RIBS ROAST


5  lb. beef crossrib roast
  salt and pepper

	Sprinkle with salt and pepper.	Insert meat thermometer into center of roast.  Let stand at room temperature.  Preheat oven to 375 degrees;.  Roast in oven one hour.  Turn off heat.  Do not open oven door for one hour.	Very important.  During resting period, beef continues to cook slowly and evenly distributes heat throughout beef.  Turn oven regulator to 300 degrees;.  Allow roast to reheat 30 to 40 minutes until beef has reached desired internal temperature. Beef will be pink and juicy all the way through when it registers 130 degrees;.
