CRUNCHY BAKED CHICKEN


4  boneless skinless chicken breasts
1  can cream of mushroom soup
4  slices American or Mozzarella
1/4  c. seasoned crushed croutons
  oregano
2  c. cooked rice

      Place chicken in a baking dish sprayed with nonstick spray. Place cheese slices on top of chicken.  Spread soup over chicken pieces.  Sprinkle croutons and oregano on top of soup.  Bake at 400 degrees; for 30 to 40 minutes until chicken is done.  Serve with rice.
