DIJON DRESSING


1/2  c. Dijon mustard
1/2  c. vegetable oil
1/2  c. white wine vinegar
2  Tbsp. grated Parmesan cheese
2  Tbsp. light corn syrup
1  Tbsp. chopped fresh or 1 tsp. dried oregano
1  tsp. garlic powder
1/2  tsp. celery seeds
1/4  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper

      Combine all ingredients; stir with a wire whisk until blended. Cover; chill at least 1 hour. Serve over salad greens. Yield: 1 2/3 cups.
