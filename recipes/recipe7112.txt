SWEDISH MEATBALLS


1  lb. ground beef
1/2  lb. ground pork
1/2  lb. ground veal
1/2  c. milk
1  egg
1  c. bread crumbs
1/4  c. finely chopped onion
2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
1/8  tsp. nutmeg
1/4  lb. butter
1/4  c. flour
1  pt. sour cream
1  tsp. parsley

      Mix first 9 ingredients together thoroughly; mixture may be soft.  Form into small balls and cook in 1/4 lb. butter.  When browned well, remove from pan.  In butter remaining in pan, add 1/4 cup flour, and 1 pint sour cream.  Stir well, scraping up all browned bits at bottom of pan.  Add 1 teaspoon parsley.  Return meatballs to pan and heat thoroughly.  Add 1/2 to 3/4 cup water to sauce if it's too thick.
