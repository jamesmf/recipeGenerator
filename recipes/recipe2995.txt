HOT BEEF SPREAD


1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
2  Tbsp. milk
1 (2 1/2 oz.)  jar dried beef, snipped into small pieces
2  Tbsp. instant minced onion
1/8  tsp. pepper
1/2  c. sour cream
  Chopped walnuts

      Mix cream cheese and milk until creamy.  Add dried beef, onion, pepper and sour cream.  Put in an 8 inch pie pan. Sprinkle with nuts.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 15 minutes.  Serve warm with crackers.
