MOCK CRAB SALAD


1  c. uncooked spaghetti
  salted boiling water
1/2  lb. cheese, grated
2  pimento peppers, chopped
4  slices pineapple, diced
2  c. diced celery
1  Tbsp. onion, chopped
1/3  c. mayonnaise
2  hard boiled eggs, optional

      Cook spaghetti in salted water until tender but firm; drain and add the remaining ingredients. Mix well. If desired, 2 hard boiled eggs may be added.
