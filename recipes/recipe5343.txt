MINCEMEAT-CRANBERRY SAUCE


1 (16 oz.)  can whole-berry cranberry sauce
1 (20 1/2 oz.)  jar mincemeat
1/4  c. orange juice
  vanilla ice cream

      In a saucepan over medium heat, stir cranberry sauce, mincemeat and orange juice until heated through.  Serve warm over ice cream. Store leftovers in refrigerator.  Yield:  about 3 1/2 cups.
