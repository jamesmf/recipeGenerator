BARBECUE SAUCE FOR CHICKEN


1 (12 oz.)  bottle catsup
1  c. brown sugar
1/4  c. vinegar
2  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
2  Tbsp. soy sauce
1  tsp. dry mustard
2 large  cloves garlic, minced
2  Tbsp. fresh lemon juice
1  Tbsp. chili powder
  hot pepper sauce to taste

      Combine all ingredients in 2-quart saucepan.  Heat to boiling; reduce heat and simmer for 5 minutes.  Yields about 2 cups.
