HOLIDAY BROCCOLI


2 (10 oz.)  pkg. frozen chopped broccoli
1  roll garlic cheese
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  stick oleo
1  onion, chopped
2  stalks celery, chopped
8  oz. sliced mushrooms
  bread crumbs

      Cook broccoli according to package directions and drain.  Saut√© celery and onion until tender.  Heat soup and cheese together until cheese melts.  Mix all ingredients together and pour into 2-quart casserole.  Sprinkle bread crumbs on top and place in a 350 degrees; oven.  Bake for 20 to 25 minutes or until bread crumbs are browned. Delicious!
