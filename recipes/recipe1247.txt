APPLE TEA COOLER


1/3  c. apple juice (unsweetened)
1/3  c. tea
  ice cubes
  lemon wedge (optional)

      Exchange List Approximation:  Fruit 1/2.
      Mix apple juice and tea.  Serve over ice cubes.  Garnish with lemon wedge if desired.
      Yield:  2/3 cup (1 serving), 39 calories per serving.
