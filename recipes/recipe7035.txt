MEXICAN CASSEROLE


  family size pkg. ground chuck
1  can cream of chicken soup
1 small  can enchilada sauce
1  bottle taco sauce
2  tsp. chili powder
1  pkg. corn tortillas
1  pkg. (12 oz.) sliced American cheese
1  small onion, chopped

      Brown ground meat, drain. Add soup, enchilada sauce, taco sauce, chili powder, and onion; simmer 10 to 15 minutes.
       Layer corn tortillas in bottom of 13 x 9 inch baking pan. Spoon meat mixture over tortillas; lay a slice of cheese over meat mixture. Place another layer of tortillas, meat mixture and then top with cheese. Bake at 350 degrees; for 40 minutes.
