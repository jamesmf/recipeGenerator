MEATBALL OVEN DINNER


1  lb. ground beef
2  Tbsp. chopped onion
1  Tbsp. chopped bell pepper
1/4  c. corn meal
1  tsp. salt
1 1/2  tsp. dry mustard
1  tsp. chili powder
1/2  c. milk
1  egg, beaten
1/4  c. flour
2  Tbsp. oil
3  c. tomato juice
3  potatoes, quartered
6  carrots, halved
12  small onions

      Combine meat, onion, bell pepper, meal, seasonings, milk and egg.  Form into 12 meat balls.  Sprinkle with flour; brown in hot oil.  Place in large casserole.  To oil in skillet, add remaining flour; blend and add tomato juice.  Cook until medium thick; pour over meat balls.  Arrange vegetables around meat balls.  Sprinkle with salt; cover.  Bake 2 hours in 350 degrees; oven.
