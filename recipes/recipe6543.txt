DAD'S DOOR COUNTY CHILI - VERSION #2


3  lb. ground beef
3  cans dark kidney beans
3  cans crushed tomatoes
3 (8 oz.)  cans tomato sauce
3  c. diced onions
4 1/2  Tbsp. horseradish
6  Tbsp. chili powder
4 1/2  Tbsp. oregano
1  Tbsp. basil
1  Tbsp. tarragon
1  Tbsp. thyme
1 1/2  tsp. crushed red pepper
1 1/2  tsp. baking soda*
1 1/2  tsp. salt

      Brown ground beef. Add remaining ingredients and simmer slowly for 2 hours or in crock-pot. Add brown stock as needed for moisture.
      *Takes the burp out of the Chili!
