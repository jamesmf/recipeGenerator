CHICKEN CASSEROLE


4  c. cooked chicken, diced
4  c. chicken broth (salt to taste)
2  eggs, beaten
1 1/2  c. chopped celery
1  large onion, chopped
1  c. diced American cheese
1  c. sliced water chestnuts
1  c. cream of mushroom soup
3 1/2  c. cracker crumbs (Ritz)
1/2  stick oleo

      Cook onion and celery in oleo until tender.  Combine with other ingredients, except 1 cup crumbs.  Put in large baking dish. Top with crumbs.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes.
