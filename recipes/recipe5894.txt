BLUEGRASS SALAD


1/2  c. vegetable oil
1/4  c. rice vinegar
1  Tbsp. balsamic vinegar
2  Tbsp. sugar
1  tsp. butter or margarine
3/4  c. walnuts
2  heads romaine lettuce, torn
2  pears, chopped
1  c. asparagus tips*
1/2  c. crumbled Blue cheese
1/2  c. dried cranberries

      Whisk together first 4 ingredients. Chill at least 1 hour. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. Add walnuts and saut 5 minutes or until lightly browned. Remove walnuts with a slotted spoon. Toss together lettuce, pears, asparagus and toasted almonds. Sprinkle with cheese and cranberries. Drizzle with dressing.
      *One cup broccoli or 1 cup snow peas may be substituted.
