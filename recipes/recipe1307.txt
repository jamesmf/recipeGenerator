SAVORY RICE BLEND


1  c. uncooked mixed wild and brown rice
1  small onion, chopped (about 1/4 c.)
2 1/2  c. chicken broth (1 Tbsp. chicken style season to 8 oz. water)
1  Tbsp. dried parsley flakes
1/2  tsp. tamari or soy sauce
1/4  tsp. dried basil leaves
1  medium stalk celery, chopped (about 1/2 c.)
3  Tbsp. soy margarine
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. ground sage

      Heat oven to 350 degrees;.  Cook and stir wild and brown rice, celery and onion in margarine until onion is tender.  Pour into ungreased 1 1/2-quart casserole.  Heat broth to boiling; pour over rice mixture.  Stir in parsley, salt, tamari, sage and basil.  Cook and bake 45 minutes.
