FRESH BROCCOLI SALAD


1 large  bunch broccoli, chopped
1/2  bottle bacon bits
1  bunch spring onions, chopped
1  bottle olives (with pimento)
1 large  bottle buttermilk Ranch dressing
  salt and pepper

      Wash and chop broccoli; add onions, olives, salt and pepper. Mix well. Add dressing and top with bacon bits.
