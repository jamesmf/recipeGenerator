CHERRY WHIPPED CREAM PARFAIT


1  c. whipping cream
3  Tbsp. sugar
1  tsp. vanilla
  dash of salt
1  c. dairy sour cream
1 (1 lb. 5 oz.)  can cherry pie filling

      Whip whipping cream with sugar, vanilla, and salt.  Fold in sour cream.  Alternate layers of cherry pie filling and whipped cream mixture in parfait glasses.  Chill.  Serves 8 to 10.
