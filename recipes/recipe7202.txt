EASY CHICKEN AND RICE


1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  can onion soup
1  c. rice
  chicken

      Mix all soups together well.  Add rice and put in a 9 x 13 pan.  Lay chicken on top of rice and soup mixture.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 60 minutes or until chicken is done.
