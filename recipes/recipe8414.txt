WILL'S TARRAGON CHICKEN


3  to 4 chicken breasts, boned and cubed
2  bay leaves
1  large onion, cut in slices
2  Tbsp. oil
3  Tbsp. flour
1  c. chicken broth
1 1/4  Tbsp. tarragon
1 1/4  Tbsp. black pepper
1  c. sour cream
  rice or egg noodles

      Brown salted chicken with 2 bay leaves and onion cut in slices.  The browner the better for flavor.  Stir in tarragon and pepper; add flour and chicken broth to thicken.  Add sour cream and pour over rice or egg noodles.
