CASSEROLE ROMA


3  c. pared potatoes, sliced
1 1/2  tsp. salt
1 (17 oz.)  can whole kernel corn, drained
1  onion, sliced
1  lb. lean ground beef
1  c. pared carrots, sliced
1 (15 1/2 oz.)  jar Ragu spaghetti sauce

	Grease a 3-quart casserole dish or 13 x 9 x 2-inch baking dish. Layer potatoes, corn, onion, beef and carrots. Season each layer with salt. Pour spaghetti sauce over all. Bake, covered, at 350 degrees; for 1 3/4 hours. Serves 6.
