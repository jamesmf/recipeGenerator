BROCCOLI CHEESE SOUP


3/4  c. onion, chopped
1  Tbsp. butter or margarine
6  c. water
6  chicken bouillon cubes
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. fine egg noodles
1  tsp. salt
2 (10 oz.)  pkg. frozen broccoli, chopped
1/4  tsp. garlic powder
6  c. milk
1  lb. pasteurized process cheese, cubed
  pepper to taste

	In a Dutch oven, saut√© onion in butter.  Add water and bouillon cubes. Bring to a boil and gradually add noodles and salt. Cook uncovered for 3 minutes.  Stir in broccoli and garlic powder. Cook for an additional 3 minutes and stir in milk.	Add cheese and pepper and stir until cheese is melted.
	Variation:  Use 2 tablespoons salad oil instead of butter, 1/8 teaspoon garlic powder and 1/8 teaspoon seasoned salt.
