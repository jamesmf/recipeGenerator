CHICKEN PUFFS


8  oz. onion and chives cream cheese
4  boneless skinless chicken breasts
1  can crescent rolls
1  stick butter
1  bag Pepperidge Farm bread crumbs
  lemon pepper

      Divide crescent rolls into 4 squares. Lay on wax paper in the refrigerator.
      Melt butter. Bake chicken sprinkled with lemon pepper at 350 degrees; for 20 to 25 minutes. Cut up into small chunks. Mix cheese with chicken. Add a small amount of lemon pepper. Spoon mixture evenly onto crescent rolls. Pinch edges of rolls together to make a ball and roll in butter and bread crumbs. Bake at 350 degrees; until brown, about 15 to 20 minutes.
