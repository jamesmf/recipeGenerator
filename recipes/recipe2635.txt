CHEESE CAKE

Crumb Crust:
2  c. graham cracker crumbs
1/2  stick butter
2  Tbsp. sugar

 
Cream Cheese Filling:
8  oz. cream cheese
1/3  c. lemon juice
1  can Borden condensed milk

      Crumb  Crust:  Blend  ingredients together;  spread out in a 9-inch pie pan.  If desired, bake at 375 degrees; for 8 minutes.
      Cream  Cheese  Filling:  Combine and beat until creamy. Pour into crust and freeze for 1 hour.
