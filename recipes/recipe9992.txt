BAKED TOMATO


2  fresh tomatoes
1  clove garlic, minced
1/4  tsp. tarragon
1/4  tsp. basil
1  Tbsp. Parmesan cheese
  fresh ground black pepper to taste

      Slice tomatoes in half. Place in shallow baking pan, flat side up. Combine seasonings and cheese; sprinkle over tomatoes. Bake at 350 degrees; for about 30 minutes or until tender. Makes 4 servings.
