NACHO CASSEROLE


1  lb. ground chuck
1  can Fiesta nacho cheese
1  can Cream of Mushroom soup
1  can Rotel, drained
1  bag tortillas
  cheddar cheese

      Cook ground beef, drain; crush chips and put in a 9 X 13 pan. Mix nacho cheese, cream of mushroom and Rotel into hamburger mixture.  Stir; bring to a boil.  Then, pour on top of nacho chips.  Sprinkle cheese on top.
