KRABBSALLAD
(Crab Salad)  

1 (6 1/2 oz.)  can crabmeat
1  tsp. grated onion
1/4  tsp. dill weed
  freshly ground black pepper
2  stalks celery, chopped
1 to 2  Tbsp. lemon juice
1/2  c. sour cream
  lettuce leaves

      Drain the crabmeat and remove any membranes.  Combine the crabmeat, celery, onion, lemon juice, dill, sour cream and pepper. Mix gently.  Arrange salad on a lettuce leaf.
