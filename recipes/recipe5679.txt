WATERGATE SALAD


1 (6 oz.)  can crushed pineapple with juice
1  pkg. instant pistachio pudding (sugar-free)
1  medium size container Cool Whip (fat-free)
1  c. nuts, chopped
1  c. miniature marshmallows

	Pour entire can of pineapple into bowl.  Sprinkle pudding mix over pineapple. Let set a few minutes. Mix other ingredients until well blended. Cover tightly. Keep in refrigerator overnight.
