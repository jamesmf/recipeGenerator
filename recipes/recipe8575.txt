MAMIE'S MEATLOAF


1 1/2  lb. Ground Beef
1  c. Tomato Sauce
3/4  c. Crackers
1  Egg, beaten
1/4  c. Onion, chopped
1/2  tsp. Salt
1/4  tsp. Pepper
1/3  c. Bell pepper

      Heat oven to 350 degrees.  Combine all ingredients.  Mix thoroughly and pour into 8x4 loaf pan.  Bake 1 hour.  Drain. Spread on topping of ketchup and brown sugar.  Bake about 15 minutes longer. Let cool about 30 minutes then slice and serve.
