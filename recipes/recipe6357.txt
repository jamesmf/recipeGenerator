PUMPKIN STEW


2  Tbsp. vegetable oil
2  onions, diced
2  cloves garlic, crushed
1/2  c. tomato sauce
1/2  c. water
1  c. frozen corn
1  tsp. salt
1  c. frozen peas
4  medium potatoes, sliced in wedges
16  oz. can pumpkin
1  c. skim milk
3/4  c. grated Gruyere cheese

      Saut√© onion and garlic in oil; add tomato sauce, water, corn, peas and salt. Simmer 10 minutes. Add potatoes, pumpkin and milk. Simmer at lowest possible temperature for 45 minutes to 1 hour. Serve with grated cheese. Also good served over white rice.
      Serving Size: 1 cup. Cal-238, Carbs-32, Protein-8, Fat-9.
