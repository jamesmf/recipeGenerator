HAM DIP


2  c. grated Cheddar cheese
1 1/2  c. sour cream
1  (8 oz.) cream cheese
1 large  can diced green chili peppers
1 small  can diced jalapenos
2  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
6 to 8  drops Tabasco sauce
16  oz. pkg. sliced ham, cubed

      Mix all together. Can either bake at 350 degrees; for 25 to 30 minutes (until cheese is melted) or have a function to go to put in crock-pot and plug in on low for about 4 hours or until cheese is melted and eat away with chips.
