HOMEMADE YOGURT


2  qt. milk
2  Tbsp. unflavored gelatin
1  c. (8 oz.) yogurt

      Scald  milk  (until  skins or 180 degrees;).  Let cool to 110 degrees;. Add gelatin  to  yogurt.  Stir  well.  After  milk cools, add yogurt to milk, stirring well.  Pour into 5 pint jars.  (Have jars warm.)  Let set on pilot light (complete bottom) of oven all night.  Take out in morning and put in refrigerator.  Let set until evening.  Yields 5 pints.
