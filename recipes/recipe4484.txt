STRAWBERRY SALAD


2  bags romaine lettuce
1  pt. strawberries, cut up
  Parmesan cheese or to taste
1/2 to 1  c. sunflower seeds or to taste

      Mix together and set aside.
Dressing:
3/4  c. mayo
1/4  c. milk
1  Tbsp. poppy seeds
1/3  c. sugar
1  Tbsp. white vinegar

      Mix together.  Toss together above ingredients with dressing when ready to serve.
