CHICKEN LICKIN' GOOD PORK CHOPS


6  to 8 lean pork chops (cut 1/2-inch to 3/4-inch thick)
1/2  c. flour
1/2  tsp. salt
1 1/2  tsp. dry mustard
1/2  tsp. garlic powder
4  Tbsp. vegetable oil
  Lemon pepper to taste
2  cans (10 1/2-oz.) condensed chicken and rice soup

      Dredge pork chops in mixture of flour, salt, dry mustard and garlic powder.  Heat vegetable oil in skillet; add chops, sprinkle with lemon pepper and brown on both sides.  Place pork chops in crockpot; pour undiluted soup over chops.  Cover and cook on LOW 8 hours or on HIGH 6 hours.  Serve with hot cooked rice.
