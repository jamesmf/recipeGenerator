FRESH FRUIT SALAD


  4-5  large delicious apples (peeled, cored and chopped)
8  medium firm bananas (sliced)
1 medium size  can pineapple chunks
1  qt. fresh strawberries, sliced
2  c. green grapes, whole

 
Dressing:
2 (5 1/2 oz.)  cans pure apricot nectar
1  c. sugar
1  Tbsp. cornstarch
2  tsp. vanilla

      Mix dressing until all the ingredients are dissolved.  Cook on stove top until sauce thickens.  Chill.  Mix with fruit.
