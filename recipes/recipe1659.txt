KARTOFFEL SALAT
(German Potato Salad)  

10  cooked potatoes
6  Tbsp. vinegar
1  Tbsp. water
1  Tbsp. flour
1  onion
6  Tbsp. oil
1  tsp. sugar
  salt and pepper to taste

      Dice cooked potatoes into small chunks, approximately 1/2-inch square.  Dice onion.  Place both in bowl.  Mix the vinegar, oil, water, sugar, flour, salt and pepper in small bowl.  Pour this mixture over the potatoes and onions.  Mix and serve.
