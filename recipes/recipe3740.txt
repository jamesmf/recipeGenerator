AGGIE MISTAKES


1  lb. hamburger
1  lb. Jimmy Dean sausage (regular)
1  tsp. oregano
1/2  tsp. garlic powder
1  lb. Velveeta cheese (hot)
1 to 2  pkg. party bread

	In a large pan, brown hamburger and sausage, cooking well. Add seasonings. Drain off any excess grease. Add cheese and mix until well blended.
	 On a large cookie sheet, lay out the party bread and spread cooled mixture on bread. Cove loosely with plastic wrap and put in freezer. When frozen, remove desired amount and bake at 350 degrees; for 10 minutes.
	Note: Keep remaining bread appetizers in Ziploc bags for future use.
