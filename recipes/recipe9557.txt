JAN'S CHICKEN


6  chicken breasts
2  c. sour cream
1/4  c. lemon juice
4  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
2  tsp. paprika
2  tsp. celery salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
1/4  c. butter, melted
1/4  c. shortening, melted
  Ritz cracker crumbs

      Combine first 7 ingredients in a large bowl and refrigerate overnight.  Roll in crumbs, place on cookie sheet and pour one-half of the butter-shortening mixture over the chicken.  Bake 45 minutes in 350 degrees; oven.  Pour over the remaining mix and bake 15 minutes more.
