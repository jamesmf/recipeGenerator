CHOCOLATE PINWHEELS


2  c. sifted flour
1  tsp. baking powder
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  c. shortening
2/3  c. sugar
1  egg, unbeaten
1  Tbsp. milk
1  sq. unsweetened chocolate, melted

      Sift flour once; measure.  Add baking powder and salt and sift again.  Cream shortening; add sugar gradually and cream together until light and fluffy.  Add egg and milk and beat well.  Add flour, small amount at a time, mixing well after each addition. Divide dough into 2 parts.  To one part, add chocolate and blend. Roll each on floured wax paper into rectangular shape 1/8-inch thick.  Lay plain sheet over chocolate sheet.  Remove wax paper. Roll like jelly roll.  Chill until firm.  Cut into 1/8-inch slices. Bake on ungreased cookie sheet at 375 degrees; for 10 minutes or until done.
