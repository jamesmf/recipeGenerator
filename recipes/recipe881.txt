ORIENTAL SHRIMP SALAD


1 (1 lb.)  can bean sprouts
4  c. cooked shrimp
2  c. chow mein noodles
1/4  c. green onions, minced
1/2  c. celery, minced
1 (5 oz.)  can water chestnuts, drained and minced
  Soy Mayonnaise (recipe follows)

      Rinse bean sprouts in cold water and drain.  Combine first 6 ingredients and mix with Soy Mayonnaise.  Serves 8.
Soy Mayonnaise:
1  c. mayonnaise
1 1/2  Tbsp. each:  lemon juice and soy sauce
3/8  tsp. ground ginger
1/2  tsp. Accent

      Mix mayonnaise with lemon juice, soy sauce, ginger and Accent.
