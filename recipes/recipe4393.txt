CHILI CON CARNE


1  lb. ground beef
1/3  c. onion flakes
2 to 3  Tbsp. chili powder
1  tsp. salt
  dash of instant garlic powder
16  oz. can diced tomatoes
1  can red kidney beans
2  Tbsp. vinegar

      Brown ground beef and drain.  Add remaining ingredients.  Mix well.  Cover and simmer 45 minutes.  Serves 6.
