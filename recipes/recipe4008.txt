SPINACH DIP


1  pkg. frozen chopped spinach (not cooked, but thawed and squeezed)
1  c. sour cream
3/4  c. mayonnaise
1  small onion, diced
1  can diced water chestnuts
1  pkg. Knorr vegetable soup mix

      Blend well with mixer or blender.  Refrigerate overnight. Serve with stone ground crackers.
