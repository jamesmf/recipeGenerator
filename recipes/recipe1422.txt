SIRLOIN KABOBS


1/2  c. soy sauce
2  Tbsp. brown sugar
2  Tbsp. salad oil
1  tsp. ground ginger
1  tsp. dry mustard
1/4  tsp. pepper
2  cloves garlic, minced
1 1/2  lb. top sirloin, cut into 1 inch cubes
  cherry tomatoes
  small whole onions
  mushrooms
  green pepper, cut into 1 inch squares

      Combine soy sauce, brown sugar, oil, ginger, mustard, pepper and garlic.  Mix well.  Add meat and let stand 2 hours.  Drain and reserve marinade.  Thread meat on skewers alternating with vegetables.  Broil over hot coals 5 to 6 minutes, turning frequently and basting with marinade; or broil in oven 3 to 5 minutes from heat until crispy brown.
