TUNA CASSEROLE


1 (8 oz.)  pkg. noodles (cook 6 minutes)
1/3  c. Sherry wine
9 1/2  oz. can tuna
1/2  tsp. chopped parsley
1/4  c. sour cream
1  clove garlic, crushed
6  oz. Cheddar or Swiss cheese, shredded
  salt, pepper and  a pinch of paprika

	Mix partially cooked noodles with all other ingredients, except cheese.  Sprinkle with cheese and bake in 325 degrees; oven for 30 to 35 minutes.
