CREAMY CHICKEN CACCIATORE


1  2 1/2 to 3  lb. Fryer, cut up
1  lg. Onion, ringed
2  tbsp. Margarine
1  c. Tomato, chopped
1  c. Green Pepper strips
1/2  c. Milk
1 1/2  tsp. diced Oregano Leaves
1/2  tsp. black Pepper
1/2  lb. Velveeta Cheese, cubed

      Brown chicken on all sides with onions in margarine.  Add tomatoes, green peppers, milk, seasoning and bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover, simmer 20 minutes or until chicken is tender. Uncover, continue cooking 10 minutes. Remove chicken to platter. Melt cheese with vegetables and serve over chicken.
