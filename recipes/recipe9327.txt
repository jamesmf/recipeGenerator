HASH BROWN SAUSAGE BAKE


16  oz. sour cream
10  oz. (1 can) soup, condensed cream of chicken
32  oz. (1 pkg.) cubed hash brown potatoes (frozen), thawed
2  c. smoked sausage
1  lb. processed cheese (Velveeta), cubed
1/4  c. onion, chopped
2  c. cornflakes, crushed
1/2  c. (1 stick) margarine, melted
4  oz. (1 c.) mozzarella cheese, shredded
3  green pepper rings

      In a bowl, combine sour cream and soup.  Stir in hash brown potatoes, sausage, cheese and onion.  Put mixture into a greased 3-quart baking dish.  Toss together cornflake crumbs and melted margarine.  Sprinkle mixture over top.
      Bake uncovered at 350 degrees; for 50 minutes.  Sprinkle with cheese and bake 10 minutes longer or until bubbly.
      Garnish with green pepper.
      Makes 8 servings.
