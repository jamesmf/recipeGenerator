ALMOND TOFFEE ICE CREAM


6  eggs
2 1/2  c. sugar
1  Tbsp. instant coffee
5  Tbsp. cocoa
3  pt. light cream or evaporated milk
2  tsp. vanilla
1/2  c. toasted almonds
  milk

      Beat eggs well.  Add sugar, coffee, cocoa, cream, vanilla and almonds.  Blend well.  Pour into 1 gallon freezer; add enough milk to fill freezer.  Freeze according to instructions.
