HOT CHICKEN SALAD


2  c. diced chicken, cooked
1  c. diced celery, raw
1/2  c. water chestnuts, sliced
1  Tbsp. lemon juice
1/2  c. mayonnaise
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  pkg. Chinese noodles

      Mix all ingredients except noodles; add salt and pepper to taste.  Top with noodles and bake at 400 degrees; until bubbly (about 20 minutes).  Double recipe for 9 x 13 inch pan.
