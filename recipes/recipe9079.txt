BEEF STROGANOFF


1  lb. ground beef
1  onion, chopped
1  c. sour cream
1  can cream of mushroom soup
2  Tbsp. flour
  salt and pepper
1/2  lb. fresh mushrooms, sliced or 1 small can sliced mushrooms

      Brown ground beef and onion; drain off excess fat. Stir in flour, salt and pepper. Stir in cream of mushroom soup and sour cream. Add mushrooms and let simmer for a few minutes.
      Serve over noodles or rice.
