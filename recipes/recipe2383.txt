EASY COUNTRY COBBLER


1  stick oleo
1  c. self-rising flour
1  c. sugar
1  c. milk
1  can fruit, drained or fresh

      Melt butter in 1 1/2 or 2-quart casserole dish; mix flour, sugar and milk in bowl.  Pour fruit into butter; pour flour mixture over fruit.  Place in 350 degrees; oven for 45 to 50 minutes.
