FRIED CAULIFLOWER


1  fresh cauliflower
  cracker meal or bread crumbs
1  egg, slightly beaten
  Parmesan cheese

      Cook cauliflower in salted water until tender, but not well done.  Separate buds and dip in egg.  Roll in meal or crumbs. Brown in hot oil, turning to brown all over.  Serve hot, sprinkled with Parmesan cheese.
