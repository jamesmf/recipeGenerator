SHOE PEG CORN SALAD


1  can shoe peg corn
1  can French-style green beans
1  can whole kernel corn
1 small  can English peas
1 small  jar of pimientos
5  stalks of celery
1/2  green pepper
1  small onion
1/2  c. salad oil
2  Tbsp. salad vinegar
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. dry mustard
  pepper to taste

      Mix together; combine with vegetable mixture.
