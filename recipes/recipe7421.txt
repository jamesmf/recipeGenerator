PECAN ORANGE ROUGHY


1  lb. orange roughy fillets
2  tsp. lemon juice
1  tsp. garlic powder
1/2  tsp. black pepper
1/4  tsp. white pepper
2  Tbsp. Parmesan cheese
2  Tbsp. finely chopped pecans
2  Tbsp. olive oil
1  tsp. paprika
1  tsp. onion powder
1/2  tsp. salt
1  Tbsp. dry bread crumbs
  Pam spray

      Preheat oven to 425 degrees;.  Spray Pam in shallow baking pan.  Place fish fillets in pan.  In small bowl, combine olive oil, lemon juice, paprika, garlic and onion powders, black and white pepper and salt; mix well.  Spoon or brush mixture over the fish.  To the remaining oil mix, add pecans, Parmesan cheese and bread crumbs; mix well.  Spoon mixture over fish evenly.  Bake at 425 degrees; for 10 to 15 minutes or until fish flakes easily with a fork.
