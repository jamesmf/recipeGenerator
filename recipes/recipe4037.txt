JALAPENO CHEESE SQUARES


1  jalapeno pepper
1  lb. Monterey Jack cheese, shredded
1  lb. sharp Cheddar cheese, shredded
6  eggs, beaten
5  oz. (1 small can) evaporated milk
4  oz. (1 large can) green chilies, chopped

      Remove seeds and chop jalapeno pepper.  Stir together all ingredients.  Place in a buttered 9x13-inch pan.
      Bake at 350 degrees; for 40 minutes.  Let cool and cut into squares.
