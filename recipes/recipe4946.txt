WILD RICE CHICKEN SALAD


2  pkg. (6.2-oz. each) long-grain and wild rice mix
2  jars (6-oz. each) marinated quartered artichoke hearts, undrained
4  c. chopped cooked chicken
1  medium-size red bell pepper, finely chopped
2  celery ribs, thinly sliced
1  can (2.25-oz.) sliced ripe olives, drained
1  c. mayonnaise
1 1/2  tsp. curry powder
  leaf lettuce

      Cook rice mix according to package directions.  Drain artichoke heart quarters, reserving 1/2 cup liquid.  Stir together rice, artichokes, chicken, and next 4 ingredients.  Stir together reserved 1/2 cup artichoke heart liquid, mayonnaise, and curry powder; toss with rice and chicken mixture. Cover and chill mixture 8 hours.  Serve on leaf lettuce.  Makes 8 servings.
