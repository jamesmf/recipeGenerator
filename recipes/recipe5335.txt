ITALIAN POTATO SALAD


3  lb. potatoes
1/3  c. Italian salad dressing
4  hard-cooked eggs, chopped
3/4  c. chopped celery
1/3  c. chopped onion
1/4  c. chopped cucumber
1/4  c. chopped green pepper
1/2  c. mayonnaise
1/4  c. sour cream
1  tsp. prepared horseradish
  chopped, fresh tomatoes

      Place potatoes in a saucepan; cover with water. Bring to a boil and cook until tender; drain and cool. Peel and cube potatoes; place in a large bowl. Add dressing and toss to coat. Cover and chill for 2 hours. Add eggs, celery, onion, cucumber and green pepper; mix well.
       In a small bowl, combine mayonnaise, sour cream and horseradish; mix well. Pour over potato mixture and toss to coat. Chill 1 hour. Top with tomatoes.
