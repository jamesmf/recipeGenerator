CRUNCHY NO-FRY CHICKEN


1/2  tsp. garlic powder
1/8  tsp. black pepper
1/8  tsp. ground red pepper
4  skinless, boneless chicken breasts
1/4  c. canned chicken broth
3/4  c. finely chopped corn flakes

      Mix dry ingredients together.  Dip chicken in broth then into corn flake mixture.  Bake at 400 degrees; for 25 minutes.
