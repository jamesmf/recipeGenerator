APRICOT-MUSTARD GRILLED PORK TENDERLOIN

Ingredients for 4 Servings:
1  pork tenderloin (about a lb.)
3  Tbsp. apricot preserves
5  Tbsp. honey mustard

      Season tenderloin with salt and pepper. In small bowl, stir together the preserves and mustard. Grill pork over a medium-hot fire, brushing with mustard mixture frequently, turning once or twice, until just done, about 15 minutes.
