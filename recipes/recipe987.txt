CHEESY ASPARAGUS OR BROCCOLI SOUP


3  Tbsp. butter or oleo
2  Tbsp. flour
1  tsp. salt
  dash of nutmeg
  dash of pepper
3  c. milk
1 (10 oz.)  pkg. frozen cut asparagus or broccoli, cooked and drained
6  oz. natural Cheddar cheese
  grated Parmesan cheese
  paprika to taste

      Melt butter and blend in flour, salt, nutmeg and pepper.  Add milk all at once.  Cook, stirring constantly until mixture thickens and bubbles.  Cook 2 minutes longer.  Add asparagus (cut into large pieces) or broccoli and cheese; stir until cheese melts.  Garnish with a sprinkling of Parmesan cheese and paprika.  Serves 6.
