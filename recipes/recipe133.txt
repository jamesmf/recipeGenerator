CHINCHIN NIGERIAN COOKIES


2  Tbsp. butter
1  c. flour
2  Tbsp. sugar
1  tsp. nutmeg
1  egg, well beaten
  vegetable oil (for frying)

      Rub the butter into the flour.  Add the sugar and nutmeg; mix well.  Mix in the beaten egg, forming a stiff dough.  Knead until smooth.  Roll out on a floured board about 1/4-inch thick.  Cut crisscross fashion, forming small diamond shapes.  Heat oil to 350 degrees; to 375 degrees;.  Fry the dough until it browns evenly.  Drain on paper towels.
