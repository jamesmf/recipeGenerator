SWISS STEAK SUPREME


2  lb. round steak, cut 1/2-inch thick
3  Tbsp. flour
1/4  c. margarine
1  (16 oz.) diced tomatoes
1  tsp. basil
1 1/2 to 2  c. Mozzarella cheese, shredded
1  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper
1  large onion
1  green pepper, chopped

      Cut meat into serving pieces and coat with flour.  Melt margarine in pan and brown meat on both sides.  Add tomatoes, salt, basil and pepper.  Cover and simmer for 1 hour until tender.  Add onion and green pepper.  Cook another 25 minutes.  Top with cheese until it melts.
