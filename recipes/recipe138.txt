M&M'S COOKIES


1  c. shortening
1  c. packed brown sugar
1/2  c. granulated sugar
2  tsp. vanilla
2  eggs
2 1/2  c. sifted all-purpose flour
1  tsp. baking soda
1  tsp. salt
1 1/2  c. M&M's (plain)

      Blend shortening, brown sugar and granulated sugar in a large bowl.  Beat in vanilla and eggs.  Sift next 3 dry ingredients together.  Add to sugar and egg mixture, blending well.  Stir in 1/2 cup M&M's candies.  Blend well.  Drop from teaspoon on greased cookie sheet.  Decorate with remaining M&M's.  Bake at 375 degrees; for 10 minutes until brown.  Makes 6 dozen.
