SWEET FRENCH DRESSING


1 (22 oz.)  bottle catsup
1 1/2  c. sugar
1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can tomato soup (undiluted)
1  c. cider vinegar
1  c. vegetable oil
1  clove garlic, crushed
1  tsp. paprika
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. dry mustard
1  tsp. Worcestershire sauce

      Combine all ingredients in container of electric blender. Process until smooth. Chill before serving. Serve dressing over salad greens. Yield: about 6 1/2 cups.
