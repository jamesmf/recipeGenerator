CHEESE-BEAN PIE


1/2  c. water
1  c. Bisquick
1 (17 oz.)  can refried beans
1 to 1 1/2  lb. hamburger
1/2  c. chopped onion
1 (10 oz.)  jar salsa
1  c. grated cheese
  sour cream
  avocado (optional)

      Mix Bisquick, water and beans together; pat into a pie plate to make shell (lightly greased pie plate).  Brown hamburger and onions together; drain grease and put hamburger into pie shell. Pour salsa over top of hamburger and then spread grated cheese on top.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes.  Serve with sliced avocado and sour cream.
      Use mild, medium or hot salsa.
