JACK POT CASSEROLE


1  lb. ground beef
1  onion, chopped
1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can condensed tomato soup
1 1/2  c. water
1 1/2 to 2  c. uncooked noodles
  pepper
1 (16 oz.)  can cream-style corn
1/4  c. chopped black olives
1  c. grated American cheese

	Brown meat.  Add onion; cook until golden.  Add tomato soup, and water noodles.	Cook until noodles are tender, stirring frequently.  Season to taste with pepper.  Add corn, olives and 1/2 cup grated cheese.	Pour into greased casserole.  Sprinkle with remaining cheese.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes.
