CHEESY CHILI HOT DISH


2  lb. ground beef
1/2  c. chopped onion
1/2  c. chopped green pepper (optional)
1 (12 oz.)  can whole kernel corn, drained
1 (15 oz.)  can tomato sauce
1  pkg. chili seasoning mix
1 to 1 1/2  lb. Velveeta cheese
4  c. tortilla chips
1/2  c. chopped tomato
  chopped green or black olives (optional)
1 1/2 to 2  c. Cheddar cheese

      Brown meat, green pepper and onion.  Drain.  Stir in corn, tomato sauce and chili seasoning.  Add Velveeta cheese, cut into cubes.  Stir till cheese melts.  Spread 1/2 of meat mixture into bottom of a 1 1/2 to 2 quart square casserole dish.  Top with 1/2 of chips.  Add rest of meat mixture.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 25 minutes. Top with rest of chips, tomatoes, olives and Cheddar cheese.  Bake until cheese melts.
