POTATO SOUP


6  slices bacon, fried and drained
3  Tbsp. bacon grease
3/4  c. onion
3  c. potatoes, cut small
1 1/2  c. water
2  cans cream of mushroom soup
  salt to taste
2 soup  cans of milk

      Pour off all but 3 tablespoons of bacon grease.  Saut onions in grease and add potatoes, salt and water.  Cook until potatoes are done.  Add soup and milk.  Serve with crumbled bacon on top. Makes 8 servings.
