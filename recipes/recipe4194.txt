PUMPKIN CHEESE BALL


16  oz. sharp cheddar cheese, shredded
1 8  oz. cream cheese
1 8  oz. chive and cream cheese dip
2  tsp. paprika
1/2  tsp. red pepper
1  stalk broccoli
  red and green apple wedges

      Combine cheese and next 4 ingredients. Chill 4 hours or until firm. Shape into ball to resemble a pumpkin.
