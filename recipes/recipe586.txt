QUICK GINGER CHICKEN


2  tsp. ground ginger
2  Tbsp. honey
2  Tbsp. low sodium soy sauce
4  boned, skinned chicken breast halves
  green onion strips (optional)

      Combine ginger, honey and soy sauce in a small bowl; stir well and set aside.  Place each chicken breast half between 2 sheets of heavy-duty plastic wrap; flatten to 1/4-inch thickness, using a meat mallet or rolling pin.  Coat grill rack with vegetable cooking spray; place on grill over medium hot coals.  Place chicken on rack and cook 4 minutes on each side, basting frequently with soy sauce mixture.  Place chicken on serving platter; garnish with green onion strips, if desired.  Yields 4 servings; contains about 201 calories per chicken breast half.
