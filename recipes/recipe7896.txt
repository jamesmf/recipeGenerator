TASTY DRY CHICKEN


1  chicken, cut and cleaned
3/4  cup orange juice
1/2  cup lemon juice
2  Tbsp. worcestershire sauce
2  Tbsp. tomato sauce
2  Tbsp. mayonnaise
1  tsp. mustard
  Salt to taste
2  tsp. coriander powder
2  tsp. cumin powder
6  Tbsp. butter
  Pinch turmeric

      Mix all ingredients well, except butter.  Slowly melt butter in pan and add marinated chicken.  Braise until chicken is thoroughly cooked.
