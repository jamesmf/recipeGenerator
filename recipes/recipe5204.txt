POTATO AND EGG SALAD


5  lbs. potatoes, peeled and cut into 1 inch chunks
6  large eggs
1/2  pound sliced bacon
1  cup mayonnaise
1/3  cup Dijon mustard
1/2   tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. black pepper
1/4  cup dill pickles, chopped

      In 5 quart sauce pan, place potatoes and enough water to cover; heat to boiling over high heat.  Reduce heat to medium-low; cover and simmer 10-12 minutes or until potatoes are fork-tender. Drain potatoes; cool slightly.
      While potatoes are cooking, hard-cook eggs;  In a 3-quart sauce pan, place eggs and enough cold water to cover by at least one inch. Heat to boiling over high heat.  Remove pan from heat; cover and let stand 15 minutes.  Pour off hot water and run cold water over eggs to cool.  Remove shells.  Cut eggs into 1/2 inch chunks.
      In a nonstick 12 inch skillet, cook bacon over medium-low heat until browned.  Drain on paper towels; crumble.
      In a large bowl, mix mayonnaise, mustard, salt and pepper. Add warm potatoes, bacon and pickles to bowl with mayonnaise mixture.  Stir with rubber spatula to combine.  Gently stir in eggs.  Serve warm or cover and refrigerate up to 2 days.
