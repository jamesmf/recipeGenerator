CARROT SOUFFLE


1  lb. carrots (or 2 C. sliced)
1/2  C. melted margarine
3  eggs
3  Tbsp. flour
1  C. sugar
3  tsp. baking powder
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. nutmeg
1  tsp. cinnamon
2  cans butter beans
1  can lima beans
1  can kidney beans
1  can pork & beans
1  onion, chopped
1/2  lb. bacon, browned and chopped
3/4  C. brown sugar
1/4  C. vinegar
1/3  C. ketchup
1/4  tsp. dry mustard

      Brown onion and bacon.
      Simmer brown sugar, vinegar, ketchup and mustard for 15 minutes.
      Mix all beans, bacon & onions with the brown sugar mixture in a crock pot.
      Cook for 1 - 2 hours.  Stir occasionally.  Add reserved liquid if needed!
