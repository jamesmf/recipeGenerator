FRESH STRAWBERRY SAUCE


2  Tbsp. sugar
1  Tbsp. cornstarch
1/2  c. water
1  Tbsp. lemon juice
2  c. sliced strawberries

      Combine  thoroughly  in  this  order.  Bring to a boil, then simmer  gently 1 to 2 minutes, stirring constantly until clear and  thickened.  Serve  warm  over pancakes or waffles. Also, good over ice cream.
