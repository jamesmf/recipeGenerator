MINESTRONE


1  lb. bacon, cut in 1/4-inch pieces
2  Tbsp. tomato puree or leftover sauce
2  cloves garlic
  pepper to taste
3 to 4  c. peeled, chopped potatoes
1  onion, chopped
1  c. chopped carrots
1  c. chopped celery
2 to 3  qt. chicken stock or water and bouillon
1  Tbsp. parsley
1  Tbsp. basil
1 (10 oz.)  pkg. frozen green beans
1 (10 oz.)  pkg. frozen peas
4  oz. broken spaghetti

      Brown bacon.  Add next 10 ingredients.  Cover and simmer for 30 to 40 minutes.  Add green beans, peas and spaghetti and simmer for 10 to 15 minutes.
