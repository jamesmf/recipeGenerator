3 LAYER CONGEALED SALAD


2  boxes (3 oz. each) jello, different flavors
1/2  box (3 oz.) lemon jello
1 1/2  c. miniature marshmallows
1 small  can crushed pineapple
1/2  c. mayonnaise
8  oz. cream cheese
1/2  container of Cool Whip (8 oz.)
1/2  c. boiling water

      Prepare 1 flavor jello as package directions; pour in bowl and let congeal.  Dissolve 1/2 pack of yellow jello in 1/4 cup boiling water.  Dissolve marshmallows in other 1/4 cup boiling water.  Mix cream cheese (softened) and mayonnaise; mix with yellow jello and dissolved marshmallows.  Pour this mixture over first flavor; let congeal.  When set, prepare other flavor of jello according to package directions.  Pour over second layer and let set until firm.  Before serving, spread Cool Whip on top.
      Note: Use different colors according to the season.
