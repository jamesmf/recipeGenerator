TUNA SALAD


1  can English peas
1  can tuna, drained
1  pkg. noodles (any kind desired)
1  diced tomato
  celery (if desired)
  salt and pepper to taste
1  can cream of chicken
1/2  c. mayonnaise

      Cook noodles until done and drain. Drain English peas. Mix all ingredients together. Serve with crackers or bread sticks.
