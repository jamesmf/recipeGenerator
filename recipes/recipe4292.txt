FRUIT SALAD
(Low-Fat)  

1  box sugar-free vanilla pudding (cooking type)
1  can pineapple tidbits, drained
2  bananas, sliced
24  seedless grapes, halved
2  oranges, peeled and cut in pieces

      Pour pineapple juice in measuring cup and add enough water to make 1 1/2 cups. Cook pudding and juice-water on stove until it boils and thickens. Cool. Mix with fruit and chill.
