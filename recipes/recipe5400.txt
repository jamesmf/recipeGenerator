SWEET AND SOUR COLESLAW


2  qt. cabbage, shredded
2  carrots, shredded
1  medium onion, chopped
1  green pepper, chopped (optional)

 
Dressing:
1  pt. vinegar
2 1/4  c. sugar
1  tsp. celery seed
1 1/2  tsp. mustard seed
1 1/2  tsp. salt

	Mix vegetables together. Boil all dressing ingredients together for 1 minute. Cool and mix well with vegetables. Keeps well in the refrigerator and freezer.
