UNSTUFFED PEPPER SOUP


1 1/2  lb. lean ground beef
3  large green peppers, chopped
1  large onion, chopped
2 (14 1/2 oz.)  cans beef broth
2 (10 3/4 oz.)  cans condensed tomato soup (undiluted)
1 (28 oz.)  can crushed tomatoes
1 (4 oz.)  can mushroom stems and pieces, drained
1 1/2  c. cooked rice

	In a large pot or Dutch oven, cook the beef, peppers and onions over a small medium heat until meat is no longer pink. Drain off excess grease. Stir in broth, soup, tomatoes and mushrooms.	Bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover and simmer for at least 30 minutes. Stir occasionally.  Add rice and heat thoroughly.
