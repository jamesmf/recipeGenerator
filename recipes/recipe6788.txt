HOT DISH


2  c. uncooked macaroni
3  c. diced ham
4  chopped hard-boiled eggs
1/2  lb. shredded cheese
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  can cream of celery soup
2  c. milk
  small jar pimentos

      Mix the above ingredients and pour into greased 9 x 13 pan. Cover and refrigerate overnight. Bake, uncovered, for 1 1/2 hours at 350 degrees;.
