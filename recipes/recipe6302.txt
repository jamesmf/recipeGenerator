SPINACH SALAD


1  pkg. fresh spinach
1  can sliced water chestnuts
1/2  c. bean sprouts
2  hard-boiled eggs
7  strips bacon, cooked and crumbled

 
Dressing:
1/2  c. salad oil
1/4  c. sugar
1/4  c. vinegar
2  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
3  Tbsp. catsup

      Wash spinach, dry and tear into pieces. Add water chestnuts, bean sprouts, eggs and bacon.
      To make dressing, combine salad oil, sugar, vinegar, Worcestershire sauce and catsup. Add to salad and toss.
