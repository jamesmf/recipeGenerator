EASY CHICKEN POT PIE


1  can cream of potato Soup
3/4  c. Milk
1/8  tsp. Pepper
1  Egg
1  c. all-purpose Baking Mix
1  c. frozen mixed Vegetables, thawed
2  cans (4.5 oz) chunk Chicken Breast, drained

      Preheat oven to 400 degrees.  Mix soup, 1/4 c. milk, pepper, vegetables and chicken in 9 inch pie plate.  Mix remaining milk, egg and baking mix.  Pour over chicken mixture.  Bake until golden brown.
