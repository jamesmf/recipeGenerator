CORNBREAD DINNER


1  lb. sausage
1/2  c. chopped onion
1  pkg. cornbread mix
3  Tbsp. sugar
1/2  c. chopped bell pepper
3/4  c. grated Cheddar cheese
1  can creamed corn
3/4  c. buttermilk or milk
1  egg

      Fry sausage with onion. Add remaining ingredients. Mix all together and bake at 375 degrees; for 45 minutes.
