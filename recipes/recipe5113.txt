FRENCH ONION SOUP


1  stick butter
1  medium onion
  bouillon cubes (2/bowl)
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
  salt and pepper to taste
  Mozzarella cheese
  French onion bread
  water (as much as needed)

      You will need cup/bowl.
      Melt butter and saut onions. Add Worcestershire sauce, bouillon cubes, water and salt and pepper. Bring to a boil and simmer for 15 minutes.
      Place bread in bowl (small amount) and pour in soup, then add sliced Mozzarella cheese on top. Put in oven on broil until cheese melts and slightly browns.
      Make sure bowls are oven-safe! Enjoy!
