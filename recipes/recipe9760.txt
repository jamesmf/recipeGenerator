HAMBURGER STROGANOFF


1  lb. hamburger
4  oz. can mushrooms, drained
1  c. chopped onion
1/8  tsp. garlic powder
1/4  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper
1/4  c. flour
1  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/3  c. ketchup
1/2  tsp. chili powder
3/4  c. sour cream (more or less) to taste
2 1/2 to 3  c. uncooked noodles

      In a skillet, brown hamburger and onion. Drain, then return to skillet. Add mushrooms, garlic, salt and pepper; stir in flour. Add Worcestershire sauce, ketchup and chili powder. Mixture will be thick. Just before serving, blend in sour cream to taste. Combine with cooked noodles to serve.
