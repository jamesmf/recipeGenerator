DEEP-DISH PIZZA CASSEROLE



1  lb. hamburger
1/2  c. chopped onion
3/4  c. water
1 1/2  tsp. oregano
1  c. Cheddar or Mozzarella cheese
1 (6 oz.)  can tomato paste
1  can (10) biscuits

      Cook hamburger and onion. Drain grease. Add water and tomato paste. Simmer 10 minutes. Spread biscuits in dish that is greased to make crust. Pour mixture over biscuits and add cheese. Cook at 350 degrees; for 20 minutes.
      You can add anything to mixture you'd normally put on pizza.
