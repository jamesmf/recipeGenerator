CHICKEN ROLL-UPS


4  chicken breasts
1  can crescent dinner rolls
1  c. Cheddar cheese, grated
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  (8 oz.) sour cream
1/2  stick butter

      Boil chicken breasts until cooked. Remove bone. Wrap chicken in crescent rolls. Layer in bottom of casserole dish. Mix together cheese, soup, sour cream and 1/2 stick butter, melted. Pour over chicken and rolls. Cook at 350 degrees; for 35 minutes.
