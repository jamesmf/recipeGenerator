FROZEN FRUIT SALAD


8  oz. cream cheese
1/4  c. lemon juice
1/4  c. mayonnaise
1  pt. heavy whipping cream
1  c. sugar
3  ripe bananas, mashed
12  oz. can crushed pineapple
1  c. pecans
1  (16 oz.) cranberry sauce, chilled

      Cream until smooth cream cheese, lemon juice, and mayonnaise. Whip the cream and add sugar; mix with cream cheese mixture. Add fruit and nuts. Dice chilled cranberry sauce; add to mixture. Freeze and serve.
