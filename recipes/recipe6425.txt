AUNT JANIE'S CRANBERRY SALAD


1  pound raw cranberries
1/2  cup diced celery
1  cup diced apples
1  cup crushed pineapple
1/2  cup chopped nuts
2  cups sugar
2  boxes orange jello
2  cups pineapple juice
2  cups hot water

      Preparation:  Grind cranberries.  Mix with sugar, and allow to sit overnight.
      To make salad:  Mix sugared cranberries with celery, apples, crushed pineapple, and nuts.
      Mix together jello, pineapple juice, and hot water.  Cool until thick but not set.  Mix with cranberries and other goodies. Refrigerate.
