SAUSAGE AND EGG CASSEROLE


1  lb. pork sausage
6  eggs
2  c. milk
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. dry mustard
2  slices bread, cubed
1  c. Cheddar cheese, grated

      Crumble and brown sausage.  Drain.  Beat eggs with milk, salt and mustard.  Layer bread cubes in bottom of buttered 9 x 13-inch casserole dish.  Sprinkle sausage over bread.  Top with cheese. Pour egg mixture over all this.  Refrigerate overnight.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 35 to 40 minutes.
