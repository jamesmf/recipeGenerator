LAMB DUMPLINGS IN YOGURT
(Sheesh Barak)  
Dough:
4  c. flour
1  c. water
1  tsp. salt

 
Filling:
1 1/2  lb. chopped lamb coarsely ground
1  med. onion, finely chopped
1  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. black pepper
1/4  tsp. cumin

 
Laban Sauce:
3  qt. Laban (yogurt)
2  tsp. salt
1  qt. cold water
1  egg
2  tbsp. flour

      Combine dough ingredients and knead well.  Mix filling ingredients.  Roll out dough approx. 1/8 inch thick.  Cut with round cookie cutter or whiskey glass. Put 1 heaping teaspoonful of meat filling in each circle.  Fold dough in half.  Bring right and left edges together to form a hat.  Set aside on cookie sheet sprayed with Pam.  Using a 8 qt. pot, blend laban sauce ingredients and stir occasionally until it comes to a boil.  Drop dumplings in and boil 15 mins.  Saut√© 1/2 stick  butter with 2-3 cloves of garlic until light brown.  Add to sauce.
