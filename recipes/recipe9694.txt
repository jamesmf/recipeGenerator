DILL FILLETS


1  lb. fish fillets
3  oz. fat-free cream cheese
2  fresh lemons
1  tsp. dill weed

      Spread softened cream cheese over 1 side of fillets. Place fillets cream side up in a sprayed nonstick 9-inch square pan. Thinly slice 1 lemon and place over top of fillets. Squeeze juice of other lemon over fish. Sprinkle top of fish with dill weed. Cover tightly with foil and bake at 400 degrees; for 15 to 20 minutes or until fish flakes. Yield: 4 servings.
      Cal-133, CHO-7g, Pro-21g, Fat-3g.
