EASY BROCCOLI BAKE


1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can cream of broccoli soup
1/2  c. milk
1  tsp. soy sauce
  Dash of pepper
1 (20 oz.)  pkg. frozen broccoli cuts (cooked & drained)
4  c. fresh broccoli (cooked & drained)
1 (28 oz.)  can French fried onions

      In 1 1/2 quart casserole, combine soup, milk, soy sauce and pepper.  Stir in broccoli and 1/2 can of onions.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 25 minutes.  Top with remaining onions.  Bake 5 minutes.  Makes 6 servings.
