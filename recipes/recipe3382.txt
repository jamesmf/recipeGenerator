VIDALIA ONION-CRANBERRY RELISH


1  large Vidalia or other mild sweet onion, cut into thin wedges
1  Tbsp. butter
1/4  c. dry red wine
  dash salt
3/4  c. packed brown sugar
4 inches  stick cinnamon
1 12-oz.  pkg. fresh cranberries (3 c.)

      Cook onion in butter in a medium skillet for 10 minutes or till tender, stirring frequently.  Add wine and salt.  Cook and stir 1 minute more.  Remove from heat and set aside.  Combine brown sugar, cinnamon, and 1 cup water in a medium saucepan; simmer, uncovered, for 15 minutes.  Add 2 cups cranberries and cook, uncovered, for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Add remaining cranberries; cook 10 minutes more or till berries pop, stirring frequently.  Remove cinnamon sticks.  Stir in onion mixture. Transfer to a medium bowl.  Cover and chill until serving time or up to 3 days.  Makes about 3 cups.
