SWEET POTATO PUDDING


2 1/2  c. milk
3  medium sweet potatoes
3  eggs
2  c. sugar
2  tsp. cinnamon
1  tsp. nutmeg
1/4  stick butter
1/2  c. slivered almonds

      Peel sweet potatoes and cut in chunks.  Combine all ingredients, except almonds, in blender or food processor. Process until sweet potato is grated.  Pour into a large buttered baking dish.  Sprinkle with slivered almonds.  Dust with cinnamon sugar, if you wish.  Bake at 300 degrees; for 1 1/2 to 2 hours.
