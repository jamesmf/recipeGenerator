TUNA BOATS


1/2  c. cheese, grated
2  hard-cooked eggs, chopped
2  Tbsp. celery, chopped
2  Tbsp. green pepper, chopped
2  Tbsp. pickle, chopped
1/3  c. mayonnaise
1 (6 oz.)  can tuna
4  hot dogs buns

      Combine cheese, eggs, celery, green pepper, pickle, mayonnaise, and tuna until well blended.  Fill hot dog buns.  Wrap each bun in foil.  Bake at 300 degrees; for 20 minutes.
