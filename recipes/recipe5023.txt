SWEET & SPICY MUSTARD


2  eggs, beaten
1/2  c. white vinegar
  Dash of red pepper
5  Tbsp. mustard
1/2  c. sugar
1  Tbsp. flour
1/2  tsp. salt
1  Tbsp. butter

      In blender, beat eggs and gradually add vinegar.  Add all other ingredients except butter.  Put in a double boiler or regular non-stick pan and cook and stir until thick.  Remove from heat.  Add butter to hot mixture and stir until melted.  Cool and store in refrigerator.
