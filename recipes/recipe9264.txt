HOT CHICKEN WINGS


2  lb. chicken wings
1/4  c. vinegar
1/2  c. oil (your choice)
1 large  bottle Franks Hot Sauce (12 oz.)

      Cut wings in half; marinate 1 hour in vinegar, oil and hot sauce.  Remove from marinade and drain.  Bake 1 hour at 350 degrees;.
      Note: The hot sauce contains cayenne pepper, vinegar, salt and garlic.  Serve with plenty of napkins as they are juicy!  Also, have plenty of cold drinks on hand, because they are peppery!
