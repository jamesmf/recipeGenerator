SAUSAGE SOUFFL


5  eggs
6  slices fresh white bread, cubed
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. dry mustard
1  lb. sausage
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. grated Cheddar cheese
2  c. milk
1/4  c. hot pepper sauce
1/4  c. honey
1/2  tsp. chili powder
1  tsp. salt
1  (5 lb.) bag frozen chicken wings or drummettes, thawed
  Blue cheese dressing (accompaniment)

      Mix the first 4 ingredients in a large, sturdy ziptop bag. Add chicken, seal bag and turn to coat chicken. Refrigerate, turning bag occasionally, at least 2 hours or up to 12.
      Heat oven to 375 degrees;. Line two 9 x 13-inch baking dishes with heavy-duty foil. Add chicken in a single layer. Bake, turning chicken once, 1 1/4 hours or until chicken begins to crisp.
      To serve:  Pour Blue cheese dressing into a bowl and place on middle of serving platter; surround with chicken.
