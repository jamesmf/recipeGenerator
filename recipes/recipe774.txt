CHICKEN AND HAM SALAD


1/3  c. mayonnaise
1/3  c. dairy sour cream
1  Tbsp. prepared mustard
1  Tbsp. lemon juice
2  c. diced, cooked chicken
2  c. diced, cooked ham
1  c. chopped celery
  salad greens
  Planters slivered almonds, toasted
  Ritz crackers

      In large bowl, combine mayonnaise, sour cream, mustard and lemon juice.  Mix in chicken, ham and celery.  Chill until ready to serve.
      To Serve:  Spoon salad onto individual salad plates lined with crisp salad greens.  Sprinkle with Planters slivered almonds. Serve with Ritz crackers.
