FLEISCHKACHLA

Blanket for Dough:
3  eggs
1  Tbsp. sugar
1 1/2  c. flour
1  tsp. salt
1 heaping  tsp. baking powder
  half and half (enough for soft dough

 
Filling:
3/4  lb. seasoned pork hamburger
1/2  lb. or more beef hamburger
1  egg or more
1  tsp. salt

      Roll out and put in meat filling. Close and deep-fry in oil. Fry slowly.
