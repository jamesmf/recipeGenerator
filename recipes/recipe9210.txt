SOUPERBURGER SANDWICHES


1  lb. ground beef
1  medium onion, chopped
1  can chicken gumbo soup
1  Tbsp. mustard
  dash of pepper
  hamburger buns

      Brown beef and onion together until beef is browned and onion is tender.  Spoon off fat.  Add soup, mustard and pepper and simmer 10 minutes, stirring often.  Serve on buns with slaw.
