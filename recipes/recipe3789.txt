COMPANY PICKLE CHUNKS


12  cucumbers
8  c. sugar
4  c. vinegar
2  Tbsp. salt
2  Tbsp. mixed whole spices

      Cover cucumbers (unpeeled) with boiling water.  Let stand 24 hours; pour off.  Repeat 3 times.  On the 5th day, pour off water and slice the cukes in 1/2-inch slices.  Bring to boil vinegar, sugar, salt and spices.  Pour over slices and let stand 2 days. Bring all to a boil and seal in jars.  An excellent sweet pickle.
