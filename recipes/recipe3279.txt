CURRY CANAPS


  rye or pumpernickel party sliced bread
1  tsp. curry powder
1/2  c. chopped green onions
1/2  c. Cheddar cheese
1  c. chopped ripe olives
1/2  c. mayonnaise

      Mix ingredients and spread on bread. Broil in oven until bubbly. Can be made earlier and broiled when time to serve. Spread can be refrigerated for 1 month. Makes 48 pieces.
