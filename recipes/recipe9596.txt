CALICO BEANS WITH TURKEY


1/2  lb. ground turkey
3/4  c. brown sugar
1/2  pkg. bacon, cut up
1  tsp. dry mustard
1  c. onion, chopped
2  tsp. vinegar
1  clove garlic, minced
1  lb. can lima beans, drained
1/2  c. catsup
1  lb. can kidney beans, drained
1  tsp. salt
1 (28 oz.)  can baked beans

      Heat oven to 350 degrees;. Brown turkey and bacon; pour off fat.  Add onions and garlic. Cook slightly. Mix all ingredients together. Pour into casserole and bake for 40 minutes.
