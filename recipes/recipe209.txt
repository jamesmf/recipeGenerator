BONBONS TEMPTATION PIE


15  chocolate creme-filled cookies
2  Tbsp. melted butter
1  pt. softened ice cream
  about 24 Bonbons brand ice cream nuggets
  whipped cream
  chocolate syrup

      Crush cookies.  Combine with butter.  Press into buttered 8-inch pie plate.  Bake in 350 degrees; oven 8 to 10 minutes.  Cool.
      Spoon ice cream into pie shell.  Cover surface with Bonbons nuggets, gently pressing into ice cream.  Freeze.  Before serving, cover pie with whipped cream and drizzle with chocolate syrup. Serve at once.  Premade pie shell may be used instead of cookies.
