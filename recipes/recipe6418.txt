THREE-CITRUS CHICKEN SALAD


9  c. torn mixed greens such as romaine, Boston or bibb lettuce, arugula, curly leaf lettuce or red leaf lettuce
2  c. cooked chicken, cut into strips
1 1/2  c. grapefruit sections (about 2 grapefruits)
1 1/2  c. orange sections (about 3 oranges)
1  c. thinly sliced red onion
2  Tbsp. olive oil or salad oil
2  Tbsp. lime juice
2  Tbsp. water
1  tsp. Dijon mustard
1/8  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper

      Divide greens among 6 plates. Arrange chicken, grapefruit sections, orange sections and red onion slices on top of greens.
       For the dressing, in a screw-top jar, combine oil, lime juice, water, mustard, salt and pepper. Cover and shake well. Drizzle dressing over salads. Makes 6 servings.
