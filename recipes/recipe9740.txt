VENISON IN BEER


3 to 4  lb. roast
3/4 to 1  tsp. salt
2  beef bouillon cubes
1  onion, sliced
12  oz. beer
6  slices bacon
1/2  tsp. pepper
1/4  c. boiling water
2  bay leaves or substitute with basil

      Salt and pepper the roast. Secure bacon around the meat with toothpicks and place in crock-pot. Dissolve bouillon cubes in boiling water. Add onion, bay leaves, bouillon and beer to the crock-pot. Cook for 4 to 5 hours.
       If gravy is desired, thicken pan juices with flour to desired taste and thickness.
