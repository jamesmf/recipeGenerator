NOODLE PUDDING


1  lb. broad noodles, cooked
6  eggs
1  c. sugar
1  pt. sour cream
1/2  lb. butter
12  oz. cream cheese, softened

 
Topping:
4  Tbsp. sugar
4  Tbsp. melted butter
1  c. cornflake crumbs

      Beat eggs and stir in sour cream and sugar. Melt butter and add to mixture. Add softened cream cheese and mix thoroughly. Add cooked noodles and pour into greased pan. Sprinkle topping mixture on top and refrigerate overnight. Bake at 325 degrees; for 1 to 1 1/2 hours until topping turns brown.
