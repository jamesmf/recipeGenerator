EASY RICE AND MUSHROOMS


1  c. uncooked rice
1  can beef consomm
1  can French onion soup
1 (4 oz.)  can mushrooms
1/2  c. butter or margarine, cut in pieces

	Combine rice, consomm,  French onion soup and mushrooms in 13 x 9 baking dish. Arrange butter over rice mixture. Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour.
