FRUIT SALAD


3  c. buttermilk
2 3 1/2  oz. boxes instant vanilla pudding and pie filling
1 8  oz. container frozen whipped topping, thawed
6  c. fresh fruit

      In large bowl, combine buttermilk and pudding.  Fold in whipped topping.  Place 1/2 of fruit mixture in a serving bowl.  Top with pudding mixture.  Place remaining fruit on top.  Makes 12 servings.
