BOURBON DOGS
(Appetizer)  

1 (16 oz.)  pkg. Hillshire Farm Lit'l Links
2  c. catsup
3/4  c. brown sugar
1  Tbsp. onion, chopped
1/2  c. water
3/4  c. bourbon

      Combine ingredients and heat for 25 minutes in saucepan, or in 300 degrees; oven.  The links may be heated separately, if desired. Makes approximately 50 appetizers.  May be kept in crock-pot on low and reheated, if necessary.
