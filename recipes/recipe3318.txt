CRANBERRY RELISH


1  lb. whole cranberries, washed and stems removed
1  large or 2 small oranges, peeled and seeded
2  medium apples, cored and seeded
4  c. sugar

      Place cranberries, oranges and apples in food processor with metal blade inserted.  Process until finely pureed.  Add sugar; pulse until sugar is thoroughly mixed.  Pour into medium bowl. Cover and refrigerate overnight or longer to enhance flavor. Makes 8 cups.
