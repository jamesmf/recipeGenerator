CHILLED VEGETABLE SALAD

Salad:
3/4  c. cauliflower florets
1/4  c. sliced cucumber, halved
1/4  c. chopped tomato
1/4  c. cubed American cheese
2  Tbsp. diced celery
2  Tbsp. sliced radishes
1  Tbsp. sliced green onions

      Toss together in medium bowl.
Dressing:
2  Tbsp. mayonnaise
1 to 2  Tbsp. sugar
1  Tbsp. prepared horseradish
1/4  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper

      Combine dressing ingredients in small bowl, mix well.  Pour over salad.  Cover and chill at least 1 hour.
