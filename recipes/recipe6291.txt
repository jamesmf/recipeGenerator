CURRIED CHICKEN SALAD


1  c. mayonnaise
1  Tbsp. fresh lemon juice
1  tsp. curry powder
3  c. cooked chicken, diced
1  c. celery, diced
1  c. seedless grapes
1 (11 oz.)  can mandarin oranges, drained
1  tsp. salt (may be omitted)
1  Tbsp. low sodium soy sauce

      Combine chicken, celery, salt and lemon juice.  Add 1/2 cup grapes (may be cut in half) and half of oranges to chicken mixture.
      Combine mayonnaise, curry powder and soy sauce to make salad dressing.  Toss dressing with chicken mixture.  Refrigerate at least 2 hours.  Serve on greens garnished with remaining oranges and grapes.
