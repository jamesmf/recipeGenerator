TACO SOUP


2  lb. ground beef
1  small onion
1 small  bottle mild Ortega taco sauce
3  cans (14.5 oz.) Sweet Sue beef broth
  salt to taste
1  tsp. chili powder
1/2  tsp. cumin
1 1/2  tsp. oregano
3  cans (16 oz.)  Bush's mixed beans
1 (14.5 oz.)  can Hunt's Choice diced tomatoes

      Brown meat and drain.  Put all ingredients in a large pot. Cook on low, covered, for 1 1/2 hours.  Serve with Mexican finely shredded cheese and Santitos tortillas chips.
