AUNT CILLE'S BAKED CHICKEN SALAD


4  c. finely chopped chicken
2  c. chicken stock
1  pkg. Pepperidge dressing
2 scant  c. chopped celery
1/2  tsp. salt
2  Tbsp. grated onion
1/2  c. chopped green pepper
1  c. mayonnaise
2  Tbsp. chopped pimiento
2  Tbsp. lemon juice
  dash of Worcestershire sauce
3  c. crushed potato chips
1/2  c. grated American cheese
1/2  c. chopped almonds

      Toss lightly all ingredients except crushed potato chips, grated American cheese, and chopped almonds.   Spoon into casserole.  Spread the potato chips, cheese, and almonds on top. Bake at 350 degrees; for 25 minutes.  Will serve 22 people.
