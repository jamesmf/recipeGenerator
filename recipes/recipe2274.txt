JACKIE'S FAVORITE MEAT LOAF


2  lb. ground beef
  fresh bread crumbs (about 4 slices)
2  eggs
1  small onion, finely chopped
1/2  c. milk
1  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper

      Thoroughly mix meat loaf ingredients.  Pack into loaf pan. Bake at 350 degrees; for one hour or until done.  Drain excess grease from pan.  Mix topping ingredients; spread on meat loaf.  Bake 10 to 15 minutes longer.  Remove from oven.  Let sit 10 to 15 minutes before slicing.
Topping:
3/4  c. ketchup
1/2  c. brown sugar
1  tsp. dry mustard
  pinch of nutmeg
