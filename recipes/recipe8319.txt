CHICKEN MUENSTER


2 or 3  lb. boneless, skinless chicken, cut in bite size pieces
1  lb. fresh mushrooms, sliced (more or less)
1/2  to 3/4 Muenster cheese, sliced
1  Knorr chicken bouillon cube
1  c. water
2  eggs, beaten
  bread crumbs
  cooking oil

	Marinate chicken in beaten eggs 6 to 8 hours or overnight. Drain chicken and coat with bread crumbs. Brown in oil in skillet. Put chicken in casserole pan. Top with mushrooms and cheese. Dissolve 1 chicken bouillon cube in cup of water. Pour over top. Bake at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes.
