SLOPPY JOES


2 1/2  lb. ground beef
2  small onions, chopped
1  tsp. garlic powder
2  Tbsp. brown sugar
2  Tbsp. mustard
2  Tbsp. liquid smoke
2  Tbsp. vinegar
2  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
2 small (24 oz.)  bottles catsup

      Brown meat. Drain. Saut onions. Add remaining ingredients and simmer for 2 hours or all afternoon in a large crock-pot or roaster. Salt to taste.
