PEANUT BUTTERED PORK LOIN


2  boneless pork loins (5 to 6 lb. total)
  Salt and pepper to taste
1/2  c. orange juice
1/4  c. creamy peanut butter

      Place pork loins in shallow roasting pan; season with salt and pepper.  Insert meat thermometer.  Bake at 350 degrees; for about 1 1/2 hours or until thermometer reads 170 degrees;.  Meanwhile, combine orange juice and peanut butter.  When thermometer reads 170 degrees; brush sauce on roast and continue baking and basting 15 to 20 minutes more.  Serves 15.
