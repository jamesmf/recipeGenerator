ONION SAGE DRESSING


2  Tbsp. plus 2 tsp. margarine
1  c. onions, chopped
1/4  c. diced celery
1 1/2  tsp. sage
1/2  c. chicken broth
2  tsp. fresh parsley
2  slices bread, cut into 1/2-inch cubes
  dash of salt

      Heat margarine until bubbly and hot.  Add onions, celery and sage.  Cook, stirring, until onions are golden, approximately 3 minutes.  Remove from heat.  Add rest of ingredients.  Toss, transfer to 1-quart casserole and bake at 325 degrees; for 30 minutes. Makes 4 servings; each serving contains 90 calories.
