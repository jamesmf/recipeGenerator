CANDIED CITRUS PEEL


  large grapefruit
2  large oranges
2  lemons
1 1/2  c. sugar
3/4  c. water

      Score the fruit with sharp knife into lengthwise sections. Remove from fruit; leave on white pith.  Place in saucepan; cover with water and bring to boil.  Remove from heat; let stand 10 minutes and drain.  Repeat process 3 times.  Cool and cut peel into 1/8 to 1/4-inch wide strips.  Put sugar and water in saucepan; bring to a boil.  Add peel; cook over medium heat 20 to 25 minutes, until peel is translucent.  Drain; cool and roll in sugar.  Dry on racks 48 hours.  Store in airtight containers.
