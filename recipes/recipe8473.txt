MEXICAN LASAGNA


1  lb. ground beef
1  c. salsa
1- 15  oz. can tomato sauce
1  envelope taco seasoning
16  oz. cottage cheese
2  eggs
1  tbsp. oregano
10  tortillas
1-1/2  c. shredded mozzarella cheese

      Brown meat and drain grease. Add tomato sauce, salsa and taco seasoning mix. Simmer, stirring frequently  for 5 minutes. In a separate bowl, combine cottage cheese, eggs and oregano. Break tortillas into 1/4 pieces and spread half of the broken pieces on the bottom of a 9 x 13 dish sprayed with a non-stick cooking spray. Top with half of the  meat mix, then the cottage cheese mixture Top with remaining tortillas. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese on top.   Bake in pre-heated 375 degrees; oven for 30 minutes. Remove and let stand for 10 minutes before serving.
