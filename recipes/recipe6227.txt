SUMMER FRUIT SALAD


1  cantaloupe, peeled and cut into 3/4-inch cubes
1 (20 oz.)  can pineapple chunks, undrained
1  unpeeled apple, cubed
2  peaches, peeled and cut into wedges
2  bananas, sliced int 1/4-inch slices
1  c. strawberries (halves)
1 (6 oz.)  can frozen orange juice concentrate, thawed and undiluted

      Layer fruit in a large bowl in order given.  Spoon orange juice concentrate on top.  Cover and chill for 6 to 8 hours. Yield: 8 to 10 servings.
