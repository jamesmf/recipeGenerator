STUFFED GREEN PEPPER SOUP


2  lbs ground beef, browned and drained
28  oz tomato sauce
14.5 oz  can of diced tomatoes with chili peppers, undrained
14.5 oz  can of diced tomatoes, undrained
1  c uncooked rice
2  c green peppers chopped (about 2 peppers)
2  beef bouillon cubes
1/4  c brown sugar, firmly packed
  salt and pepper to taste

      Place all ingredients in a slow cooker and cook overnight on the low setting.
       Makes 8-10 main course servings.
