TARRAGON CHICKEN


1/2  c. soy sauce
1/4  c. lemon juice
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
  salt and pepper
  garlic powder
  tarragon leaves

      Mix first 3 ingredients in a  2  cup container.  Finish filling with water to 2 cups.  Line  baking  dish  with  foil. Season skinned chicken with salt,  pepper  and  garlic powder. Place chicken in baking dish and  pour  liquid  over  chicken. Liberally  sprinkle  tarragon leaves over  chicken.  Place  in oven and bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees; for 25  to 30 minutes.  Turn chicken, put tarragon leaves on that  side  and finish baking, around 30 more minutes.
