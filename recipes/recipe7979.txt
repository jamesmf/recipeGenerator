MEATBALLS IN MUSHROOM SAUCE


1 to 1 1/2  lb. ground beef
1  c. milk
1  onion, chopped
1  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
4  slices bread, small pieces
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1/3  can milk

      Mix all ingredients but the soup and 1/3 can milk.  Shape into balls and brown in butter 8 to 10 minutes.  Place in 2 quart casserole dish.  Mix soup and milk.  Pour over meatballs.  Bake at 375 degrees; for 30 to 40 minutes.
