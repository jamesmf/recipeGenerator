APPLE JELLY


1 1/2  c. unsweetened apple juice
3/4  tsp. lemon juice
6  whole cloves
1  stick cinnamon
1 1/2  tsp. unflavored gelatin
1/3  c. cold water
  sugar substitute to equal 1/2 c. sugar

      Combine juices, cloves and cinnamon in heavy pan.  Bring to a boil and simmer 10 minutes.  Then, soak gelatin in cold water. Remove juice from heat; discard cloves and cinnamon.  Add gelatin and sugar substitute; mix well to dissolve.  Pour into 2 hot, clean 1/2 pint jars.  Cover lightly until cool, then cover tightly and store in the refrigerator.
      Note:  Use as a spread on bread, toast, crackers, etc.
      Yield:  22 servings.  Each serving:  1 tablespoon.  Free food (up to 2 servings per day).
