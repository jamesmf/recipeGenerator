CHICKEN YUMMY


3  whole chicken breasts
6 (1 oz.)  slices Swiss cheese
1  can cream of chicken soup
1/4  c. water
1  box Stove Top stuffing
1/3  c. melted butter

      Split, skin and bone chicken; arrange in shallow baking pan. Top each with a piece of cheese.  Stir soup and water together and spoon on top of chicken.  Sprinkle with stuffing and drizzle with butter.  Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees; for 50 minutes.
