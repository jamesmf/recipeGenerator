EVERY DAY BROCCOLI CHEESE CHICKEN


1  Tbsp. margarine
4  skinless, boneless chicken breasts halves, about 1 lb.
1  can broccoli cheese soup
1/2  can water
1/8  tsp. pepper
2  C. frozen broccoli, thawed

      In skillet brown chicken in hot margarine about 10 minutes or until browned on both sides.  Spoon off any fat.
      Stir in soup, water and pepper.  Heat to boiling; add broccoli.
      Reduce heat to low; cover and simmer about 30 minutes or until chicken is fork tender and broccoli is done.
