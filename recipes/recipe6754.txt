BAKED SPAGHETTI


1  c. chopped onion
1  c. chopped green peppers
1  Tbsp. butter
1 (28 oz.)  can tomatoes (with liquid)
1 (4 oz.)  can mushrooms, drained
1 (2 1/4 oz.)  can sliced ripe black olives, drained
2  tsp. dried oregano
12  oz. spaghetti, cooked and drained
1  lb. ground beef, cooked and drained
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1/4  c. water
1/4  c. grated Parmesan cheese
2  c. (8 oz.) shredded Cheddar cheese

      In a large skillet, saut√© onion and green pepper in butter until tender.  Add tomatoes, mushrooms, olives and oregano.  Add ground beef.  Simmer, uncovered, for 10 minutes.  Place 1/2 of the spaghetti in a greased pan or baking dish.  Top with 1/2 of the vegetable mixture.  Sprinkle with 1 cup of Cheddar cheese.  Repeat layers.  Mix soup and water until smooth.  Pour over the casserole.  Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.  Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees; for 30 to 35 minutes.  Sprinkle a few black olives on top. Makes 12 servings.
