CHOCOLATE FUDGE SYRUP


1  c. sugar
2  Tbsp. cocoa
1/2  stick butter
1 small  can Pet milk
1/2  tsp. vanilla

      Mix sugar and cocoa on the stove for 1 minute.  Add 1/2 stick butter.  When melted add 1 small can of Pet milk and boil for 2 minutes.  Add 1/2 teaspoon vanilla.  Good served with plain cake and vanilla ice cream.
