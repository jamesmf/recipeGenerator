CARROT BALLS


2  c. cooked and mashed carrots
1/2  c. grated Cheddar cheese
2  tsp. grated onion
1/2  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper
1/2  c. bread crumbs
2  eggs, slightly beaten

      Combine cheese, carrots, salt, pepper and onion.  Shape into 1-inch balls.  Roll in eggs and then in bread crumbs.  Fry in deep fat until brown.  Serves 4 or 5.
