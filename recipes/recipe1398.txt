CHERRY GLAZED HAM


  ham slices, cut 3/4 to 1-inch thick

 
Cherry Glaze:
1  c. cherry preserves
2  Tbsp. lemon juice

      Place smoked ham slices on grill as far as possible from coals. Brush with glaze.  Broil at low temperature, brushing with glaze and turning occasionally, for 20 to 25 minutes, or until well done.
      For Cherry Glaze:  Combine cherry preserves with lemon juice.
