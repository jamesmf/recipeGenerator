LIME JELLO SALAD


1 large  box lime Jell-O
1  c. boiling water
1  c. cold water
1  pt. (2 c.) cottage cheese
8  oz. cream cheese, softened
2  c. crushed pineapple (large), drained
1  c. chopped nuts (optional)

      Dissolve Jell-O in boiling water.  Stir in cold water.  Set aside.  In medium bowl, beat cream cheese until fluffy.  Stir in cottage cheese.  Add this mixture to the Jell-O and stir until well mixed.  Add pineapple and nuts.  Stir well.  Pour into dish and chill until set.
