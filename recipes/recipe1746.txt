SINGAPORE CHICKEN AND BLACK BEAN SALAD


3/4  c. picante sauce
1/2  c. rice vinegar
2  Tbsp. peanut butter
1  clove garlic, peeled
6  c. salad greens, torn in bite-size pieces
1/2  c. fresh cilantro leaves
1 (about 16 oz.)  can black beans, drained and rinsed
2  green onions, sliced
2  c. cooked chicken strips
1  large green or red pepper, cut into 1/4-inch rings

      In a blender place picante sauce, vinegar, peanut butter and garlic.  Cover and blend until smooth.  Toss salad greens, cilantro, beans and onions with 1/2 cup picante sauce mixture until evenly coated.
      Arrange on 4 plates.  Arrange chicken and pepper of salad greens.  Pour remaining picante sauce mixture over chicken.
