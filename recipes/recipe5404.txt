DILLY BEAN POTATO SALAD


1  lb. fresh green beans
4  lb. red potatoes
1  medium red onion, thinly sliced and separated into rings
1  medium Vidalia or sweet onion, thinly sliced and separated into rings
1  c. celery, chopped
8  dill pickles, sliced
2  Tbsp. snipped fresh dill or 2 tsp. dill weed
2  Tbsp. fresh parsley, minced
4  garlic cloves, minced

 
Vinaigrette:
3/4  c. olive or vegetable oil
1/3 to 1/2  c. tarragon vinegar or cider vinegar
1/3 to 1/2  c. white wine vinegar or cider vinegar
1  envelope Italian salad dressing mix
2  Tbsp. sugar
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. pepper
  celery salt and seasoned salt to taste

	Place 1-inch of water and beans in a skillet. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat. Cover and simmer for 8 to 10 minutes or until crisp and tender. Drain and set aside.
	Place potatoes in a large saucepan or Dutch oven and cover with water. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat. Cover and cook for 15 to 20 minutes or until tender; drain and cool. Cut into 1/4-inch slices. Place in a large bowl. Add the onions, celery, pickles, dill, parsley and garlic.
	 In a jar with a tight-fitting lid, combine the Vinaigrette ingredients. Shake well. Drizzle over potato mixture. Add beans; gently toss. Yields 14 to 16 servings.
