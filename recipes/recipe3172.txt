SQUASH RELISH


6  c. yellow squash, sliced thin
2  green bell pepper, diced
2  red bell peppers, diced
3  lb. onions, diced
3  Tbsp. salt
2  c. vinegar
3  c. sugar
3  tsp. mustard seed
2  tsp. celery seed

      Mix squash, bell peppers, onion, and salt; let stand one hour, then drain. Mix vinegar, sugar, mustard seed, and celery seed; bring to a boil and cook 20 minutes; add vegetables. Boil for 1 minute; pack and seal in sterile jars. Chill before serving.
