ITALIAN BEEF


4  lb. rump roast
2  cans beef broth (low sodium)
1  pkg. Good Seasons Italian dressing
2  tsp. oregano
1  tsp. basil
  sliced onions (optional)

      Cook all ingredients except onions on top of stove for 3 to 4 hours in covered pan.  During last 1/2 hour, remove meat; slice thinly with electric knife.  Return sliced meat to broth.  Add sliced onion and cook another 1/2 hour or until onions are soft. Serve as roast or make sandwiches when served on kaiser rolls.
