AZTEC TOMATO SAUTÉ


1/2  c. chopped onion
1/2  c. chopped green pepper
1  c. peeled, chopped tomatoes
1 (16 oz.)  can whole kernel corn, rinsed and drained
1  Tbsp. brown sugar (sub)
1  Tbsp. parsley flakes
1  tsp. chili seasoning

      Sauté green pepper and onions until tender.  Add tomatoes and corn.  Mix well.  Cook 5 minutes.  Add sugar, parsley flakes and chili seasoning.  Simmer 5 minutes more.  Makes 3/4 cup.
