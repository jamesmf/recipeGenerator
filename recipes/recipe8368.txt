PASGHETTI PIZZA


1 (16 oz.)  pkg. spaghetti
2  eggs
1/2  c. milk
1  c. Mozzarella cheese
3/4  tsp. garlic powder
1/2  tsp. salt

 
Topping:
1 (16 oz.)  jar spaghetti sauce
1  can pizza sauce
1 1/2  tsp. oregano
3  c. Mozzarella cheese
1  pkg. pepperoni

	Break spaghetti into 2-inch pieces. Cook, drain and cool.
	Preheat oven to 400 degrees;.  In a large bowl, beat eggs lightly. Stir in milk, 1 cup cheese, garlic powder and salt.  Add spaghetti and stir until combined.  Grease a 9 x 12-inch cake pan and spread the spaghetti in pan.  Bake 15 minutes; remove from oven.  Reduce temperature to 350 degrees;.  Spread sauces evenly over spaghetti. Sprinkle with oregano and put pepperoni on top.  Bake 15 minutes. Add remaining cheese and bake 15 minutes more.  Let stand 5 minutes at room temperature.
