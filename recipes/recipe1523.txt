AMBROSIA SALAD


1  pkg. strawberry jello
2  c. boiling water
1/2  c. cold water
1/2  c. chopped nuts (optional)
1  pkg. raspberry jello
1  pkg. frozen sliced strawberries (10 oz.)
1 (8 oz.)  can crushed pineapple
2/3  c. Carnation milk

      Use a 5-cup mold and chill 4 hours.
      Mix both packages jello with boiling water.  Add frozen strawberries.  Add cold water, canned milk, pineapple (juice and all) and nuts.  Chill, stirring every now and then.
