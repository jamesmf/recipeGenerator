CREAMY LETTUCE SALAD

Salad:
3  hard cooked eggs
1 (16 oz.)  pkg. ready to serve salad greens
1  medium tomato, diced
1/4  cup diced onion

      Cut one egg into wedges for garnish.  Dice remaining eggs. In a salad bowl combine the greens, tomatoes, onion and diced eggs. After mixing dressing pour over salad and toss to coat.  Garnish with egg wedges.  Serve immediately.
Topping:
3/4  cup mayonnaise or salad dressing
  3-4 Tbsp. milk
2  Tbsp. sugar
2  Tbsp. cider vinegar

      In a jar with a tight-fitting lid, combine the dressing ingredients; shake well.  Pour over the salad and toss to coat.
