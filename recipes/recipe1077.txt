VEGETABLE DIP


1  c. mayonnaise
1/2  c. sour cream
1  tsp. garlic salt
1  tsp. white vinegar
1/2  tsp. dry onions
1  tsp. horseradish
1  tsp. lemon juice
  curry powder to taste

      Mix well to blend; use as a dip for vegetables.
      Note:  By using imitation sour cream and diabetic mayonnaise, this can be very low calorie dip.
