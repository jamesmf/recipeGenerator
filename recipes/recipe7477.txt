MEAT LOAF


1 1/2  lb. lean ground beef
1/4  c. onion flakes
2  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
2  eggs
1/2  c. ketchup or tomato sauce (reserve extra for glaze)
3/4  c. bread crumbs or uncooked oatmeal
  Salt and pepper to taste

      Mix all ingredients together and put in a baking dish.  Poke holes in the top of the loaf to hold sauce.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes; glaze with extra ketchup or tomato sauce and bake for about 15 minutes more or until cooked through.
