MUSHROOM MEAT BALLS


1  can cream of mushroom soup
1/2  c. water
1  lb. ground beef
1/4  c. fine bread crumbs
2  Tbsp. minced onion
1  Tbsp. minced parsley
1  slightly beaten egg

      Mix soup and water. Use 1/4 cup of soup mixture with other ingredients. Shape into meat balls about 1 1/2-inches. Brown in 1 tablespoon shortening in skillet; pour off drippings and add remaining soup mixture. Cover and cook over low heat for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally. Serves 4.
