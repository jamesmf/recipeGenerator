6 WEEK PICKLES


  whole cucumbers
4  Tbsp. salt
3  Tbsp. pickling spice
3  Tbsp. alum
4  lb. sugar
  white vinegar

      Wash cucumbers; cut off ends. Put whole cucumbers in glass jars. Cover with vinegar; add salt, pickling spice and alum. Cover jar and set aside for 5 weeks.
      After 5 weeks, pour off vinegar. Slice cucumbers. Add sugar and set overnight. Return back to glass jars and wait 1 more week. After 1 week, refrigerate and enjoy.
