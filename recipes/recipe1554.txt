HOMEMADE BARBECUE SAUCE


1  c. onions, chopped
1/4  c. salad oil
1  (8 oz.) seasoned tomato sauce (1 c.)
1/2  c. water
1/4  c. brown sugar
1/4  c. lemon juice
3  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
2  Tbsp. prepared mustard
2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper

      Cook onions in oil until tender.  Add remaining ingredients. Simmer, uncovered, for 15 minutes.
