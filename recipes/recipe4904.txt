FROG-EYED SALAD


1/2 (1 lb.)  pkg. R & F Acini Di Pepe, uncooked
1  egg
1/2  c. sugar
1  Tbsp. flour
1/8  tsp. salt
1 16-oz.  can juice-packed crushed pineapple, drained, reserving juice
1 16-oz.  can fruit cocktail, drained
1  c. miniature marshmallows
1  c. whipping cream, whipped

      Cook R&F Acini Di Pepe as package directs; drain.  In a heavy saucepan, beat egg with wire whisk until foamy.  Stir in sugar, flour, salt and reserved pineapple liquid.  Over low heat, cook and stir until thickened and bubbly.  In a large bowl combine Acini Di Pepe with egg mixture.  Chill thoroughly, about 1 hour. Stir in pineapple, fruit cocktail, and marshmallows.  Fold in whipped cream; cover and chill thoroughly.  Stir before serving. Refrigerate leftovers.  Makes 6 to 8 servings.
