CHICKEN CASSEROLE


4  whole chicken breast, deboned
8  slices bacon
1  can dried beef, cut in pieces
1 4  oz. carton sour cream
2  cans cream of mushroom soup, undiluted

      Cut each chicken breast in half and wrap with bacon slice. Put the beef in an 8 x 10 inch baking dish. Place chicken on top of beef. Mix together the sour cream and the soup and pour over chicken. Cover pan and bake 1 hour in a 350 degrees; oven. Remove cover during last 20 minutes of baking.
