Fresh Peach Custard Pie


3  c. sliced fresh peaches
1  unbaked (9-inch) pastry shell
2/3  c. sugar
1/4  tsp. salt
2  Tbsp. all-purpose flour
2  eggs, beaten
1  c. light cream, scalded
1/2  tsp. ground nutmeg

      Place peaches in pastry shell.  Combine sugar, salt, and flour with eggs.  Stir in cream.  Pour over peaches.  Sprinkle with nutmeg.  Bake in moderate oven (375 degrees;) for 30 to 40 minutes or until knife inserted halfway between center and edge comes out clean.
