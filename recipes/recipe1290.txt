SOUR CREAM


1  c. water
1/2  c. raw peanuts
1/4  c. cornstarch or arrowroot
2  tsp. salt
1 1/2  tsp. onion powder
4  c. additional water

      Blend all ingredients, except additional water.  Pour blended ingredients into a saucepan.  Rinse blender with additional water and pour into pan.  Lightly boil, stirring, about 10 minutes, until thickened.
