CHICKEN CASSEROLE


4  c. cracker crumbs
4  c. diced chicken
1 1/2  c. diced celery
1 1/2  c. cubed cheese
2  eggs
1  large onion, diced
1  can cream of mushroom soup
4  c. chicken broth
1/2  tsp. pepper
1  tsp. salt

      Reserve 1 cup crumbs; mix remaining ingredients.  Place in  9 x 13-inch pan.  Top with reserved crumbs.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes.  Freezes well.
