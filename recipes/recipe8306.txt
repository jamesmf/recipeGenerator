SAUSAGE AND BEEF CHILI


1 1/2  lb. ground beef
1/2  lb. pork sausage
1  large onion, chopped
2 (15 1/2 oz.)  cans kidney beans, drained
1 (28 oz.)  can tomatoes
1  c. green pepper, chopped
1  c. celery, chopped
1 (6 oz.)  can tomato paste
2  garlic cloves, minced
2  tsp. salt
2  tsp. chili powder

	In a Dutch oven, cook beef, sausage and onions until meat is brown.  Drain off excess fat.  Stir in beans, tomatoes, green peppers, celery, tomato paste, garlic, salt and chili powder. Cover and simmer 1 to 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.
