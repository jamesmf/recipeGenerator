HOMEMADE PORK SAUSAGE


2  lb. ground lean pork
1/2  lb. ground pork fat
3/4  tsp. kosher salt
1/2  tsp. black pepper
1  Tbsp. sage
1  Tbsp. crushed red pepper

	Mix fat and lean pork.	Start out by adding the seasonings listed above.  Mix thoroughly.  Cook a small patty and taste. Adjust seasonings to taste.
	Note:  Brown patties on each side.  Pour off any fat.  Put 2 tablespoons of water in skillet.  Cover and simmer until liquid is gone.
