LEFTOVER MEAT LOAF PARMESAN


1  c. crushed saltines
1/4  c. grated Parmesan cheese
1  egg
3  Tbsp. water
4 leftover meat loaf  slices (each about 3/8-inch thick)
  shortening (for pan-frying)
  ketchup (optional)

      Combine the saltine crumbs and Parmesan cheese in a shallow dish; set aside. Beat egg with water; dip meat loaf slices in egg mixture, then in crumb mixture. Heat shortening in a skillet. Brown meat loaf slices on both sides. Serve with ketchup, if desired. Yield: 2 to 4 servings.
