PORTUGUESE SOUP


6  c. chicken broth
1  lb. smoked kielbasa sausage, diced
1  lb. potatoes, peeled and diced
15 1/4  oz. can kidney beans, drained
1  (14 1/2 oz.) diced tomatoes
1  medium onion, chopped
1  large carrot, diced
1/2  green pepper, diced
3  garlic cloves, minced

      Combine all ingredients in large pot.  Bring to boil, stirring occasionally.  Reduce heat; simmer until thick, 2 hours. Stir once or twice.  Season with pepper.
       Can be made 2 days ahead.  Refrigerate.
