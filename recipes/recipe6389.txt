BROCCOLI-BACON-CASHEW-RAISIN SALAD


1 large  bunch broccoli, washed, drained and cut
1/2  c. chopped red onion
1  lb. bacon, fried crisp, drained and crumbled
1/2  c. raisins
1/2  c. cashews

 
Dressing:
3/4  c. mayonnaise
1/4  c. sugar
2  Tbsp. vinegar

      Mix dressing ingredients; pour over broccoli, bacon, cashews and raisins. Let stand 2 hours.
