QUICHE
(Individual)  
Crust:
2 1/2  c. flour
2  sticks butter, softened
1/2  c. water

      You also need a 24 cup pan. With pastry cutter, cut in butter. Add water. Mix until dough forms. Use 2 1/2-inch cutter to form small cup crusts.
Filling:
2  eggs plus 1 yolk
1/2  c. cream
1/4  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. nutmeg

      Pour over in crusts (unbaked) chop:  ham, broccoli, tomato, etc.  Bake 30 minutes at 350 degrees;.
