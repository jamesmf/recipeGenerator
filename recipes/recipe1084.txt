CHILI CON CARNE


1  lb. 85% lean ground beef
1 1/4  tsp. salt
1  c. chopped onions
1  c. finely cut celery
1 (16 oz.)  can tomatoes, cut up, with liquid
1 small  can tomato paste
1 (8 oz.)  can tomato sauce
1/4  tsp. garlic powder
1/2  tsp. oregano
1  Tbsp. chili powder (more if desired)
1 (16 oz.)  can kidney beans or more
1/2  c. beef broth

      Use a 2 1/2-quart cooking pot.  Turn beef into pot, sprinkle with salt.  Stir over medium heat with a blending fork until all meat changes color.  Add onions and celery, mix well.  Cover and cook over medium heat 2 to 3 minutes.  Add tomatoes and liquid and all seasonings, mixing well.  Bring to a boil; cover.  Turn heat low and simmer gently for 25 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Add kidney beans and beef broth.  Simmer over low heat 15 to 20 minutes.
      One serving equals 3/4 cup.  Each serving contains:  3 lean meat exchanges, 1 bread exchange, 1 vegetable exchange.
