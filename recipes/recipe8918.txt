BREAKFAST CASSEROLE


1  lb. sausage
10  oz. pkg. mushrooms
1/2  stick butter
14  eggs
8  oz. Colby Jack cheese
  flour tortillas

      Brown sausage. Slice mushrooms and brown in butter. Beat eggs and pour over mushrooms. Cook until almost done and add sausage to mixture. Top with cheese. Place in oven until cheese is melted. Warm tortillas and serve with picante sauce.
