SOUP A L'OIGNON MAISON
(Onion Soup)  

3  Tbsp. butter
1  Tbsp. olive oil
1 1/2  lb. or 5 to 6 c. thin sliced yellow onions
1  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. sugar
3  Tbsp. flour
2  qt. hot beef broth
1  c. red wine
1  bay leaf
1/2  tsp. sage

      Melt butter and oil; add onion and stir.  Cover pan and cook on low heat 15 to 20 minutes until onions are tender.  Uncover, raise heat to medium-high; stir in salt and sugar.  Stir until sugar caramelizes.  Cook 30 minutes until onions are a dark brown.  Stir often.  Lower heat and stir in flour to make paste with onions. Cook slowly for 2 minutes.  Remove from heat.  Pour 1 cup hot broth, stirring with wire whip to blend flour.  Add rest of broth and ingredients.  Simmer 30 to 40 minutes.  Serve with French bread and Parmesan cheese.
