CHICKEN PUFFS


6  chicken breasts
  salt and pepper to taste
  oregano to taste
12  Pepperidge Farm patty shells (6 in box)
12  slices Mozzarella cheese

      Fry chicken breasts in pan.  Season with salt, oregano and a little pepper.  When done, remove from pan and cool.  Take meat off bones.  Roll out patty shells (tart side up) and fill each with chicken and 1 slice Mozzarella cheese.  Put on ungreased pan and bake at 350 degrees; for about 15 minutes or until golden.
