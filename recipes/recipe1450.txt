MINUTE STEAK ROLLS


4  Minute steaks (cubed beef)
1/4  tsp. marjoram leaves, crushed
  garlic salt to taste
  pepper to taste
4  Tbsp. cold, hard butter

      Sprinkle steaks with marjoram, garlic salt and pepper.  Cut butter into 4 sticks of equal size.  Place 1 stick of butter on each steak and roll ends over to completely cover butter; skewer to keep closed.  Broil over medium coals, turning often until steak is browned on outside, rare inside, about 10 to 12 minutes.
