QUICK & EASY BREAKFAST CASSEROLE


  4-6 slices of bread (any kind)
1  lb. bulk sausage meat
1  c. grated cheddar cheese
6  eggs
2  c. milk
1  tsp. dry mustard (optional)
1  tsp. salt
  Dash pepper

      Cube bread and place pieces in greased baking dish (13x9x2). Brown and drain sausage in skillet.  Spoon browned sausage over bread.  Sprinkle with cheese.  Beat together eggs, milk, mustard, salt, and pepper.  Pour over mixture in baking dish.  Bake in preheated 350 degrees; oven for 35-40 min.  (The cheese will rise to the top during baking.)
