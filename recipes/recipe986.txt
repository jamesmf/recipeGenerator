CHEDDAR-CHICKEN SOUP


2  Tbsp. margarine
1/2  c. chopped onion
1/2  c. chopped celery
1/2  c. chopped carrot
2  tsp. all purpose flour
1  c. water
1  pkg. instant chicken broth and seasoning mix
1/2  c. frozen asparagus cuts and tips
1/2  c. skim milk
2  oz. Cheddar cheese, shredded
4  oz. skinned and boned cooked chicken, diced
1  c. cooked small macaroni (shells, tubetti or elbows)
1  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
  dashes of salt and white pepper

      In 1 1/2 to 2-quart saucepan heat margarine over medium heat until bubbly and hot; add onion, celery and carrot and saut√© until vegetables are tender.  Sprinkle vegetables with flour and stir quickly to combine; gradually stir in water.  Add broth mix and, stirring constantly, bring to a boil.  Add asparagus and cook, stirring, for about 3 minutes; add milk and cheese and continue stirring and cooking until cheese melts.  Reduce heat to low, add remaining ingredients, and cook until thoroughly heated.
