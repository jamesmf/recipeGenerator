BEEF LOG
(Or Summer Sausage)  

5  lb. hamburger (not lean)
2 1/2  tsp. whole mustard seed
2 1/2  tsp. coarse pepper
2 1/2  tsp. Liquid Smoke
5  tsp. (rounded) Morton Tender Quick salt (do not substitute)
2 1/2  tsp. garlic salt

      Mix  all  ingredients  in  bowl.  Cover and refrigerate overnight.  Remove  once  a  day for 3 days.  Knead well for 5 minutes.  On 4th day divide into 6 or 7 rolls.  Lay on broiler pan with drip pan under it.  Bake 9 hours at 160 degrees;.  Turn over rolls in  4 1/2 hours.  At 9th hour, remove and wrap in paper towel to remove grease.  Roll in handy wrap, freeze or refrigerate.  Will keep in refrigerator 3 weeks.
      Note:  You  can use 1 pound of sausage for hamburger if lean.
