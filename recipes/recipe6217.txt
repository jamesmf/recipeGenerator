CRUNCHY CAULIFLOWER SALAD


1  medium head cauliflower
1  c. sliced radishes
1/2  c. sliced green onions
1 (8 oz.)  can sliced water chestnuts, drained
3/4  c. commercial sour cream
3/4  c. mayonnaise
2  Tbsp. caraway seed
1 (0.37 oz.)  pkg. buttermilk salad dressing mix

      Wash the cauliflower, break into flowerets.  Combine cauliflower and next 3 ingredients in a medium mixing bowl.   Toss gently.  Stir together remaining ingredients.  Pour over vegetables; stir well.  Spoon into serving bowl. Cover and chill before serving.  Yield: 6 to 8 servings.
