HOT CHICKEN SALAD


2  cups cooked chicken
2  cups chopped celery
3  Tbsp. lemon juice
3  Tbsp. minced onion
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. black pepper
1/2  cup chopped pecans
3/4  cup mayonnaise
1 small  can sliced mushrooms
1  can cream of chicken soup
  Potato Chips

      Mix all ingredients together, except soup and potato chips. Place in a 2 quart casserole.  Pour soup over top (do not mix in). Top with crushed potato chips.  Bake at 300 degrees for 30 minutes.
