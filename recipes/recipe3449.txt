VEGETABLE


2  cans crescent rolls
3/4  c. Miracle Whip
1  (8 oz.) cream cheese
1  pkg. Ranch dressing powder

      Lay rolls flat and crimp edges enough to hold the filling. Cook rolls by directions. Let cool, then mix together 1/2 cup each tomatoes, carrots, green onions, cucumbers, bell peppers, broccoli and celery. Sprinkle over rolls. Cut in 2-inch squares.
