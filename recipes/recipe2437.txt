POTATO CANDY


1  large Irish potato
1  bag powdered sugar
  smooth peanut butter

      Boil the Irish potato until it is soft.  Pour off excess water, then mash the potato.  Begin adding powdered sugar and beat. Continue adding powdered sugar until a stiff dough is formed.  Take a rolling pin and roll out in powdered sugar.  Roll into a thin rectangular shape.  Spread thin layer of peanut butter evenly over dough.  Roll dough up into a long round log shape.  Cut into slices like divinity.
