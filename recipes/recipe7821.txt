MARINATED FISH FILETS


2  Tbsp. soy sauce
2  Tbsp. corn oil
2  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  tsp. garlic powder
1  tsp. paprika
1  tsp. chili powder
  Fish filets

      Combine all ingredients (except for fish filets) and marinate fish filets in the mixture for 1/2 hour or more.  Broil 12 to 15 minutes.  Serve with tartar sauce.
