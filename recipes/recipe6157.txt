DUMP SOUP


1  can red kidney beans
1  can white northern beans
1  can pinto beans
1  can hominy, yellow or white
1  can corn
1  can diced tomatoes
1  can Rotel
1  pkg. taco seasoning
1  pkg. Hidden Valley Ranch Dressing mix
1  lb. ground beef, browned and seasoned with salt and pepper
  diced onion, optional

      Dump entire contents of all cans, ranch dressing mix and taco seasoning into a large pot and start to heat.  Brown ground beef, seasoning to taste with salt and pepper.  When ground beef is brown, add to the pot.  Serve over a bed of tortilla chips in bottom of bowl, with a spoonful of prepared Ranch Dip on top.
