ROTINI SALAD


12  oz. pkg. rotini
6  oz. pkg. Good Seasons Zesty Italian salad mix
16  oz. Kraft Zesty Italian dressing
2  cucumbers, diced
2  tomatoes, diced
1  bell pepper, diced
1  medium size purple onion, diced
  black olives, optional

      Cook rotini according to pkg. directions. Mix all ingredients together with rotini and refrigerate.
