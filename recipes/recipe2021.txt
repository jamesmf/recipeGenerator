CINNAMON BRITTLE


4  c. sugar
4  Tbsp. butter
1  c. corn syrup
2  Tbsp. vinegar
1  c. water
1/4  tsp. salt
  red food coloring
6 to 8  drops oil of cinnamon

      Put sugar, syrup, water, salt and food coloring in pan.  Stir only to dissolve.  Cook to 300 degrees;. Remove from heat.  Add butter, vinegar and oil of cinnamon.  Stir as little as possible. Pour into 2 (9 x 13-inch) greased pans.  Let cool and break into small pieces.
