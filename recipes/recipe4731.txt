SUPER BOWL GUMBO


5  Tbsp. butter
6  slices bacon, chopped
1  c. onions, finely diced
1/8  c. minced shallots
1/4  c. celery, medium diced
1  c. bell peppers (choice of colors), medium diced
2 (14.5 oz.)  cans stewed and chopped tomatoes
3  Tbsp. minced garlic
1  gal. fish, beef or chicken stock
10  bay leaves
2  tsp. dried thyme
2  tsp. dried marjoram
2  tsp. dried basil
2  tsp. dried oregano
4  lb. meat and seafood (see note)
1  lb. fresh or frozen okra, sliced 1/2-inch thick and sautéd in 2 tsp. oil until soft
  salt and black pepper to taste
1  tsp. hot sauce
1  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
8  c. cooked long-grain white rice

      In an 8-quart pot, melt butter and saut bacon until cooked but not crisp. Add onions, shallots, celery and peppers, continuing to saut for 1 minute. Add tomatoes and garlic; saut 3 minutes. Add stock slowly, continuing to stir; add bay leaves and seasonings. Bring to a boil, stirring frequently. Add meat and seafood, according to taste and budget. Bring mixture back to boil; reduce heat to medium. Cover and cook for 10 minutes. Skim mixture and reduce heat to simmer, covering and cooking 15 more minutes. Add sautéd okra; cover and cook for 15 minutes. Adjust seasoning to taste with salt, pepper, hot sauce and Worcestershire sauce.
       To serve, put 1/2 cup rice in bowl and add 1 1/2 cups gumbo. Makes 16 servings.
      Note: Meat and seafood suggestions include firm-fleshed fish, cut into 2-inch pieces, shelled shrimp, crab that has been shelled and picked clean, oysters with their liquor and chicken breasts, cut into 2-inch cubes.
