EASY LASAGNA


1 1/2 to 2  lb. super lean hamburger meat
1  box lasagna noodles (uncooked)
1 (12 oz.)  pkg. Cheddar cheese
1 (12 oz.)  pkg. Mozzarella cheese
1/2  c. Parmesan cheese
1/4  c. parsley flakes
1  jar Prego regular spaghetti sauce
1/2  tsp. Italian seasoning

      Brown hamburger meat. Add jar of spaghetti sauce and Italian seasoning. Let simmer 15 to 20 minutes. In large bowl, mix all cheeses and parsley flakes. Spray 11 x 14 baking dish with Pam. Put 1 cup of sauce in bottom of baking dish. Then add layer of noodles followed by layer of sauce on each of the noodles, then layer of cheese. Repeat layers: noodles, sauce and cheese until you have used all ingredients, finishing with layer of cheese. Cover with foil. Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour.
