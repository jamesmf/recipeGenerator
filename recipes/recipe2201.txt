COUNTRY HAM CHOWDER


2  medium potatoes, cut into 1/2-inch cubes
3  Tbsp. butter
3/4  c. chopped onions
1/4  c. chopped carrots
1/4  c. chopped celery
3  Tbsp. all-purpose flour
2 1/2 to 3  c. milk
1/4  tsp. pepper
  pinch of salt
2  tsp. Dijon mustard (optional)
1  c. finely chopped ham
1 1/2  c. grated sharp Cheddar cheese
  parsley or paprika for garnish

      Simmer potatoes and carrots in 3/4 cup water, covered, about 10 minutes or until tender.  Reserve any liquid for soup.  Melt butter in large saucepan.  Saut√© onions and celery until golden brown and soft.  Stir in flour and cook for 2 minutes, stirring well.  Add milk, potatoes with liquid, pepper, salt and mustard.  Bring to a slow simmer.  Add ham and cheese.  Cook over low heat for 5 minutes.  Serve hot.
