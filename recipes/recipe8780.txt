CRABMEAT CASSEROLE


2  c. crabmeat
2/3  c. chopped celery
1  can mushroom soup
2/3  c. mayonnaise
1  c. instant raw rice
1/2  bell pepper, chopped
1  can mushrooms, chopped
1  small onion, chopped fine
  grated cheese and bread crumbs for topping

      Mix together; place in casserole and cover with grated cheese and bread crumbs.  Bake at 325 degrees; or until crumbs are brown.
