CRUSHED DORITOS AND HAMBURGER CASSEROLE


2  lb. hamburger
1  onion, chopped (optional)
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  can enchilada sauce
1  can chopped green chilies (optional)
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1 (6 oz.)  can Milnot milk
7  oz. crushed Doritos
  shredded cheese

      Brown hamburger. Drain. Add all ingredients except Doritos and shredded cheese. Cook until mixture bubbles. Crush Doritos in bottom of 9 x 13 baking dish. Pour mixture over Doritos. Add cheese on top. Bake 10 to 15 minutes at 350 degrees;.
