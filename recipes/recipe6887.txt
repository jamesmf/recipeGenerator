CHICKEN AND RICE


4  to 6 pieces of chicken
1  onion, cut up
1/2  can Ro-Tel
1/2  bell pepper, chopped
1/2  tsp. garlic, minced
2  c. uncooked rice
2  chicken bouillon cubes
  parsley flakes (if wanted)
  onion tops, chopped fine
2  cans chicken broth
  salt to taste

	Put chicken, Ro-Tel, onions, bell peppers, garlic and bouillon cubes together in the pot. Cook until chicken is done; remove chicken from bone and return to pot. Add 2 cups of uncooked rice and 2 cans of chicken broth to pot. Cook on low heat until rice is tender.
