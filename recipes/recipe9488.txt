SAUSAGE AND RICE CASSEROLE


1 1/2  c. uncooked rice
1  lb. Jimmy Dean sausage with sage
1 (7 oz.)  can sliced mushrooms
1  large onion, chopped
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1/2  c. chopped green pepper
  shredded cheese

	Brown sausage in large skillet and drain. Add onion, mushrooms and green pepper, cooking over medium heat until onions are translucent. Cook rice according to package directions. Place cooked rice, sausage mixture and cream of mushroom soup in large bowl and mix thoroughly. Place in lightly greased baking dish and top with shredded cheese. Bake in 350 degrees; oven for approximately 25 minutes or until completely heated and cheese is melted.
