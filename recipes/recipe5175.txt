MACARONI SALAD


1  pkg. elbow macaroni
2  small, boiled red potatoes
2  boiled eggs
1/8  cup chopped parsley
1/8  cup chopped red bell peper
8  oz. ranch dressing
8  oz. sour cream
1  Tbsp. freshly ground black pepper
1  tsp. garlic powder
1  tsp. onion powder

      Mix all ingredients together and refrigerate until time to serve.
