EASY TACO HOT DISH


2  lb. hamburger
1  c. tomato juice
1  pkg. dry spaghetti sauce mix
8  oz. Mozzarella cheese, shredded
8  oz. American cheese, shredded
8  oz. nacho cheese Doritos, crumbled

      Brown hamburger; drain grease. Add tomato juice and spaghetti sauce mix; simmer.
       In a 9 x 13-inch pan, layer crumbled Doritos, cheeses, meat, cheeses and Doritos. Bake 15 minutes at 350 degrees;.
