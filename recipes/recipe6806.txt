CHEESE BUTTONS
(German Dish)  

2  eggs
1/4  tsp. salt
1  c. milk
1  tsp. baking powder
2  c. flour

      Mix well. Roll out thin. Cut in 3-inch squares.
Filling:
1 small  carton cottage cheese (dry)
1  egg
  salt and pepper to taste
  some sugar (if you want)

      Mix. Put 1 teaspoon of filling on squares. Fold over and seal. Cook in boiling water, slowly, until done. Cook at least 1/2 hour. Drain or can add cream and chicken base to make soup. Place buttons in buttered pan. Fry with browned bread crumbs. Can be served with potatoes.
