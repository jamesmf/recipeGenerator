TOMATO AND FETA SALAD


3  pt. cherry tomatoes, halved
12  oz. Feta cheese, cut into 1/4-inch dices
1/4  c. extra virgin olive oil
3  Tbsp. white wine or champagne vinegar
2  Tbsp. minced fresh basil
2  Tbsp. minced fresh parsley
1/4  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. freshly ground pepper
  pita bread, cut in wedges

      In serving bowl, gently toss together all ingredients. Warm pita bread, cut into wedges. Serve immediately or chill, covered, until ready to serve. Makes 8 servings.
      Presentation: Put tomatoes and Feta in bowl. Place in center of plate. Place pita wedges around bowl.
