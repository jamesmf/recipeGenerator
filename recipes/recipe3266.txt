HOT WING DIP


2 (10 oz.)  cans canned chicken, drained
2 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese, softened
1  c. Ranch dressing
1/2  c. hot sauce (you may add more if you like)

      Mix all ingredients together in large mixing bowl.  Place in baking dish (13 x 9 pan or two 9-inch pie plates) and bake in oven for 30 to 45 minutes at 350 degrees;.  Dip nacho chips or crackers into warm dip and enjoy.
