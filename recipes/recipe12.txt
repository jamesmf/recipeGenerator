CHOCOLATE COCA-COLA CAKE


2  c. flour
1  tsp. soda
2  c. sugar
2  sticks margarine
1  c. Coca-Cola
3  Tbsp. cocoa
1/2  c. buttermilk
2  eggs, beaten
1  tsp. vanilla
2  c. small marshmallows

      Mix flour, soda and sugar.  Heat margarine, Coca-Cola and cocoa over medium heat to boiling point.  Pour over flour mixture and stir.  Add buttermilk, eggs, vanilla and marshmallows.  Batter will be thin.  Bake in well-greased 3-quart Pyrex pan (13 x 9-inches) for 35 minutes at 350 degrees;.  Top with favorite frosting.
