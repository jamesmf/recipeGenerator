PIZZA CASSEROLE


2  lb. ground chuck
1  green pepper, diced
1  onion, diced
1  qt. Ragu Spaghetti sauce
1  can cream mushroom soup
1 12  oz. box wide noodles
3  c. mozzarella cheese
1  pkg. pepperoni

      Brown ground chuck, green pepper, and onion together.  Drain off grease.  Mix together spaghetti sauce and mushroom soup, then add meat.  Cook and drain noodles.  Mix with meat mixture and pour in baking dish.  Top with mozzarella cheese and pepperoni.  Bake until brown.  Approximately 30 minutes at 350 degrees;.
