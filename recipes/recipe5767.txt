HOT POTATO SALAD


1/2  lb. bacon
1/3  c. vinegar plus water to make 1/2 c.
1  slightly beaten egg
1  tsp. sugar
1  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
5  c. diced cooked potatoes
1/2  c. chopped onion

      Cook bacon; crumble. Combine 1/3 cup bacon drippings, 1 egg, salt, pepper, vinegar and water. Heat and stir until thickened. Add potatoes, onion and bacon. Toss. Heat through.
