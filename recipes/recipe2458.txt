BOILED CUSTARD


3  c. sugar
3/4  c. flour, sifted
4  egg yellows
3  qt. milk
  vanilla flavoring to taste
  yellow food coloring

      Beat egg yellows and add to milk.  Mix sugar and flour together and gradually add to milk.  Cook in a double boiler 45 minutes to 1 hour.  Will thicken.  Add vanilla flavoring to taste and yellow food coloring as desired.  Pour through strainer and refrigerate.
