GROOM'S CAKE


1  c. butter or margarine
1  c. water
2  c. all-purpose flour
2  c. sugar
2  eggs, beaten
1/2  c. sour cream
1  tsp. almond extract
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. baking soda

 
Frosting:
1/2  c. butter or margarine
1/4  c. milk
4 1/2  c. confectioners sugar
1/2  tsp. almond extract
1  c. chopped walnuts, pecans or almonds

      In a large saucepan, bring butter and water to a boil.  Remove from heat; stir in flour, sugar, eggs, sour cream, almond extract and baking soda until smooth.  Pour into greased sheet cake pan. Bake at 375 degrees; for 20 to 22 minutes or until cake is golden brown or tests done.  Cool for 20 minutes.
      Meanwhile, for frosting, combine butter and milk in a saucepan. Bring to a boil.  Remove from the heat; add sugar and almond extract.  Mix well.  Stir in walnuts.  Spread frosting over warm cake.
