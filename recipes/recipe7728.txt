CHICKEN POT PIE


4  chicken breasts, boiled and chopped
4  boiled eggs
1 1/2  c. chicken broth
1  can cream of celery soup
1  can English peas and carrots
1  stick butter
1  c. self-rising flour
1  c. milk

      Arrange chicken in bottom of baking dish. Mix broth, soup and English peas and carrots. Pour over chicken. Melt butter. Add flour and milk; mix well (batter will be thin). Pour over top; do not stir. Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour.
