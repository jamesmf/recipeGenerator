CANCUN CRAB


1  medium red pepper, diced
1  medium onion, diced
  butter
3 (6 oz.)  cans crab
2  eggs
3/4  c. seasoned bread crumbs
1 heaping  Tbsp. mayonnaise or to taste
3  Tbsp. Dijon mustard
1/4  tsp. pepper
1  tsp. cayenne pepper
8  drops Tabasco
4  English muffins, split and toasted
2  ripe tomatoes, sliced 1/4-inch thick
4  slices Swiss cheese, halved

      Saut√© red pepper and onion in butter.  Mix with crab, eggs, bread crumbs, mayonnaise, mustard, pepper, cayenne and Tabasco. Scoop onto English muffin halves in the following order:  English muffin, tomato slice, scoop of crab mixture and Swiss cheese slice.  Place on baking sheet.
      Bake at 325 degrees; for 15 to 20 minutes or until cheese has melted and crab is warm.
