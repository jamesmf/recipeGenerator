ONION CASSEROLE


  plain potato chips
  onion, thinly sliced
1  can cream of celery soup
1/2 soup  can milk
  margarine

      Amount varies based on size of dish.
      Preheat oven to 325 degrees;.  Butter dish.  Layer bottom of dish with crushed chips.  Next, top with layer of onions.  Next, top with mixture of milk, soup and small dabs of butter.  Continue to layer two or three times.  Finish off with butter.   Add milk if it looks a bit dry.  Bake 30 to 40 minutes, until onions are tender.
