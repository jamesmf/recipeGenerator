CHICKEN AND RICE BAKE


1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can cream of mushroom soup
1  c. water
3/4  c. regular white rice (uncooked)
1/4  tsp. paprika
1/4  tsp. pepper
4  chicken breast halves (boneless)

	In a 2-quart shallow baking dish, mix soup, water, rice, paprika and pepper. Place chicken on rice mixture. Sprinkle with additional paprika and pepper. Cover and bake at 375 degrees; for 45 minutes or until chicken is done.
	For creamier rice, increase water to 1 1/3 cups.
