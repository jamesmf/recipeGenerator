BUTTERMILK COOKIES


1  c. shortening
2  c. sugar
3  eggs
1  tsp. salt
1  c. buttermilk or sour milk
2  tsp. vanilla
1  tsp. soda
3  tsp. baking powder
4  c. flour

      Mix all ingredients together.  Drop by teaspoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheet.  Bake at 375 degrees; until golden.
Frosting:
8  oz. cream cheese
4  Tbsp. cream
1  tsp. vanilla
  powdered sugar to make spreading consistency

      Combine ingredients.  Frost cookies after they are baked and cooled.
