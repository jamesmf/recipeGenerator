BARBECUE SAUCE


1/3  c. salad oil
1/2  c. fresh or frozen chopped onions
1 (8 oz.)  can tomato sauce
1/2  c. packed brown sugar
1/4  c. white vinegar
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
4  tsp. chili powder
2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. dry mustard

      Heat onions in hot salad oil and cook until tender. Add remaining ingredients and cook until mixture is well blended. It will keep a long time in the refrigerator.
