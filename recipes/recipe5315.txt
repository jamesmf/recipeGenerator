


2 small  pkg. cherry jello
1 small  pkg. lime jello
1 large  can crushed pineapple
2 1/2  c. Coca-Cola
1/2  c. chopped nuts

      Mix together cherry jello, lime jello and crushed pineapple. Put on stove and cook until boiling. Take off stove and add Coke and nuts to mixture. Stir in pan well. Pour in bowl and refrigerate.
