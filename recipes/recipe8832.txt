O.U. BIG RED MEAT LOAF


2  lb. ground beef
1/2  c. chopped onion
2  eggs
1/2  c. milk
1/2  bell pepper, chopped
1/2  c. quick oatmeal
2  tsp. mustard
1  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper

      Mix ingredients together. Spread sauce on top. Bake at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes to 1 hour.
Sauce:
3  tsp. brown sugar
1/2  tsp. mustard
6  Tbsp. catsup
