CRABMEAT PIE


1/2  c. mayonnaise
2  Tbsp. flour
2  beaten eggs
1/2  c. milk
1 2/3  c. crabmeat
8  oz. cheese, diced
1/3  c. green onions or green pepper, finely chopped
  9-inch pie crust shell

      Combine and mix first 4 ingredients until well blended. Add the last 3 ingredients; mix well, then place in the pie crust. Bake at 450 degrees; for 15 minutes and then at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes.
