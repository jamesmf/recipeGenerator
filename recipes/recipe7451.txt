SHRIMP CASSEROLE


1/2  c. finely chopped green pepper
1/2  c. finely chopped onion
1/2  c. finely chopped celery
1 1/2  c. catsup
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
3  Tbsp. butter
1  Tbsp. sugar
1  c. boiled shrimp

      Saut pepper, onion and celery in butter.  Then add catsup, sugar and Worcestershire sauce; cook slowly on low heat for 10 minutes.  Add shrimp and simmer 5 minutes.  Serve over rice.
