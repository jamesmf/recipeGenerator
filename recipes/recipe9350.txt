TATER TOT CASSEROLE


1 1/2 to 2  lb. hamburger
1  lb. frozen tater tots
1  c. grated cheddar cheese
2  cans cream of celery soup, undiluted
3  Tbsp. onion flakes
  pinch of salt
  pinch of pepper
1  pkg. frozen peas, optional

      Put raw, crumbled hamburger in the bottom of a 13 1/2 X 8 inch dish.  Add salt and pepper.  Sprinkle grated cheese on top of the hamburger meat.  Pour cream of celery on top of the cheese. Next, add the onion flakes.   Add a layer of peas, optional.  Top with tater tots.  Cover and bake at 325 degrees for at least one hour or until the meat is cooked to your liking.
