TURKEY PASTA SALAD


2  c. cooked, cubed turkey
2  c. cooked rotini or macaroni
1  c. chopped celery
1/4  c. chopped onion
6  thinly sliced radishes

 
Dressing:
2  Tbsp. chopped parsley
1/2  c. mayonnaise
1 1/2  Tbsp. vinegar
1  tsp. prepared mustard
1/2  tsp. celery seed
1/2  tsp. salt

      Combine first 5 ingredients for salad.  Combine dressing ingredients and add to turkey mixture.  Stir gently.  Chill 4 hours or overnight.  Makes 6 cups.
