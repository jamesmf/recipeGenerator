LIGHT 'N FRUITY PIE


1 (3 oz.)  pkg. Jell-O brand gelatin (any flavor)
2/3  c. boiling water
2  c. ice cubes
1  (8 oz.) container Cool Whip, thawed
  fruit (optional)*
1  packaged graham cracker crust

      Dissolve  gelatin completely in boiling water, stirring about 3  minutes.  Add  ice  cubes  and stir until gelatin is thickened, about  2  to  3 minutes.  Remove any unmelted ice. Using wire whisk, blend in Cool Whip;  then whip until smooth. Fold in fruit, if desired.  Chill, if necessary, until mixture will  mound. Spoon  into  crust.  Chill 3 hours.  Garnish, if desired.
      *Suggested Fruit:  Can use one cup diced orange sections, fresh strawberries, or banana, 1 (8 3/4 ounce) can drained crushed pineapple or 1 (8 3/4 ounce) can drained fruit cocktail.
