PARTY PINK SALAD


  juice of No. 2 can crushed pineapple
24  large marshmallows
1  pkg. strawberry gelatin
2  c. cottage cheese
1  c. drained crushed pineapple
1  c. chopped pecans
1 (8 oz.)  box Cool Whip

      Combine pineapple juice and marshmallows.  Place over low heat. Add gelatin.  Melt marshmallows and dissolve gelatin.  Add all other ingredients.  Mix. Fold in Cool Whip last.  Place in refrigerator.  Will keep for several days.
