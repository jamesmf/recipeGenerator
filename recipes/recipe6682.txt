JOHN'S FAMOUS BROTHERHOOD HAM



1  boneless ham
1 regular  can Coca-Cola
1  c. dark brown sugar
1  c. honey

      Use heavy gauge aluminum foil and a large skillet or baking pan.  Line pan with the foil, crisscross and have pieces of foil long enough to cover ham.  Place ham in pan on foil and score the top of ham in crisscross pattern with sharp knife.  Fold foil up around the ham, then slowly pour can of Coca-Cola over entire ham. Pat brown sugar on top of ham, then drizzle the honey on the ham. Seal ham securely with foil and bake at 350 degrees; for 3 hours.
