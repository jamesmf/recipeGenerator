PINEAPPLE CHEESE BAKE


2 (15 1/4 oz.)  cans unsweetened pineapple tidbits, drained
1 to 2  c. grated sharp Cheddar cheese
3/4  c. sugar
4  Tbsp. flour
1  stack Ritz crackers, crushed
3/4  stick melted butter

      Mix pineapple, cheese, sugar and flour together.  Pour into a greased baking dish.  Cover top with crushed Ritz crackers.  Pour melted butter over top.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes.
