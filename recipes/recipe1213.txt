ALMOND MERINGUE COOKIES


4  egg whites
8  Tbsp. powdered skim milk
1  tsp. liquid artificial sweetener
1  tsp. vanilla extract
1  tsp. almond extract
  dash of cinnamon

      Beat egg whites until stiff.  Add skim milk powder; mix well. Add extracts and sweetener.  Drop by spoon onto cookie sheet.  Bake at 275 degrees; for 45 minutes.  Dust with cinnamon.  Makes 12 cookies.
