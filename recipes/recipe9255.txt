CHICKEN CORDON BLEU


6  boneless, skinless chicken breasts
6  slices fully cooked ham
6  slices Swiss cheese
2  eggs, beaten
2  Tbsp. chicken broth or dry white wine
1/2  c. flour
1/4  c. butter
1  c. fine bread crumbs
2  c. fresh sliced mushrooms
2  cans cream of mushroom soup, undiluted

      Rinse chicken, pat dry.  Place each breast between 2 pieces of wax paper.  Working from center to edges, lightly pound with the flat side of a meat mallet to 1/8 inch thickness.  Place 1 slice each of ham and cheese on chicken, folding to fit if necessary.  Starting from the short side, roll up jelly roll style and secure with a toothpick.  In a shallow dish, stir beaten eggs and 2 Tbsp. wine or broth.  Roll each breast in flour, dip in egg mixture and coat with breadcrumbs.  Preheat oven to 325 degrees;.  In skillet, melt butter; lightly brown chicken rolls on all sides. Remove from skillet and transfer to a 9 x 13 inch ovenproof dish. Reserve drippings.  Bake at 325 degrees; for about 40 minutes or until chicken is no longer pink.
