FREEZER SLAW


1  tsp. salt
1  medium head cabbage
1  grated carrot
1  c. vinegar
1/4  c. water
1  tsp. celery seed
1  c. sugar
1  tsp. mustard seed

      Shred cabbage and mix with salt.  Let stand 1 hour.  Squeeze out excess moisture.  Add carrot and let stand.
      Mix vinegar, water, celery seed, sugar and mustard seed in saucepan; bring to a boil for 1 minute.  Cool to lukewarm.  Pour over cabbage and carrot mixture.  Put in freezer container and freeze.
