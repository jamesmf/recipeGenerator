LOLLY'S SHRIMP SALAD


2  lb. boiled and peeled shrimp, chopped up
  mayonnaise (as you would making potato salad)
  celery seasoning (sprinkle)
  salt and pepper (sprinkle)
  Cajun seasoning (sprinkle)
1/2  small onion, chopped fine
3  to 4 boiled eggs, chopped up
1  tsp. mustard

      Mix all together and chill before serving.
