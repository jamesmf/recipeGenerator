HOMEMADE CANNED SPAGHETTI SAUCE


4  quarts tomatoes, peeled and quartered
1  c. onion, chopped
1  c. green pepper, chopped (optional)
3/4  c. sugar
3/4  c. Wesson oil
6  tsp. salt
2  tsp. black pepper
2  Tbsp. oregano
2 (12 oz.)  cans tomato paste

      Combine all ingredients, except tomato paste, and cook 1 hour.  Add the tomato paste and cook for 1 more hour.  Cook slowly and stir often.  Ladle into hot jars and seal.  Process in hot water bath for about 15 minutes.
      Note:  Sauce can also be frozen.
