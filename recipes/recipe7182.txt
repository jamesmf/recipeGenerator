COZY CABIN STEW


1  can cream of celery soup
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  packet Lipton onion soup
1  lb. beef cubes
  noodles or rice

      Mix soups together (no milk or water).  Mix in onion soup mix.  Add beef cubes.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 to 2 hours.  Stir occasionally.  Sour cream optional.  Boil noodles or rice according to directions.  Pour meat mixture over.
