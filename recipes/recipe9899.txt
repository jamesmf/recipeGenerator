CHEESY CORN BAKE


3  eggs, well beaten
1 (14 3/4 oz.)  can cream corn
3/4  c. unseasoned dry bread crumbs
1  Tbsp. onion, chopped
3/4  c. shredded cheddar cheese
1/2  medium green pepper, chopped
1/2  c. hot milk
1  tsp. seasoned salt
3/4  tsp. season. pepper
1/4  tsp. garlic powder with parsley

      In a large bowl, combine all ingredients.  Pour into ungreased 2-quart casserole.  Bake in 350 degrees; oven for 1 hour.  Let stand 10 minutes before serving.  Makes 6 servings.
