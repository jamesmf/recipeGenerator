CABBAGE AND KIELBASA


1  medium cabbage, shredded on thick side
1  lb. smoked kielbasa, sliced
1  medium onion, sliced
2  bay leaves
18  oz. catsup
  salt to taste

      In pot, put cabbage, onion, bay leaves, catsup and kielbasa. Fill with water about 1-inch over ingredients.  Cook until cabbage is done (not soft).  Serve as is or over boiled cut up potatoes.
