WATERGATE JELLO SALAD


1 large  can crushed pineapple
9  oz. Cool Whip
1  pkg. instant pistachio pudding
3  c. mini marshmallows
1  can fruit cocktail, drained
  coconut flakes
  chopped nuts
  cherries

      In a large bowl, add pineapple (with juice) and pudding; mix well.  Add fruit cocktail and marshmallows.  Fold in Cool Whip. Put in a pretty glass bowl and top with coconut, chopped nuts and cherries.
