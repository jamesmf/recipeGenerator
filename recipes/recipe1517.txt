RED, WHITE AND BLUE PIE


2  pie shells, baked
8  oz. cream cheese
1  c. sugar
1  tsp. vanilla
2  c. heavy cream
1  can blueberry pie filling
  strawberries (as needed)

      Soften cream cheese and beat with 3/4 cup sugar and vanilla. Beat cream and 1/4 cup sugar until thick; fold into cheese mixture. Pour into cooled pie shells.  Make a well in the middle and pour 1/2 can blueberries into each.  Decorate outer edge with strawberries.
