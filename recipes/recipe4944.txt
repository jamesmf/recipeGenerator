CHICKEN SALAD RIVIERA


1  can (20 oz.) pineapple tidbits, drained
1 1/2  lb. chicken meat, cooked and cut into chunks
1/2  c. frozen green peas, thawed
1/2  c. celery, sliced
1/2  c. seedless grapes, halved
1/2  c. toasted slivered almonds
3  Tbsp. green onions, chopped
  crisp salad greens
  Sunny Dressing

      Combine pineapple, chicken, peas, celery, grapes, almonds, and onion in a bowl.  Toss with Sunny Dressing.  Serve on salad plates lined with crisp greens.  Serves 4 to 6.
Sunny Dressing:
1/3  c. mayonnaise
1  clove garlic, pressed
1/3  c. sour cream
1  tsp. ground coriander
1/2  tsp. ground cumin
1/4  tsp. dry mustard
1/4  tsp. ground cloves
1/8  tsp. cayenne pepper

      Combine all ingredients thoroughly.
