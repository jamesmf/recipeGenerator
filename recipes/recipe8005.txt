3 SOUP CASSEROLE


1  can vegetable soup
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  can tomato soup
1  lb. ground beef
1  pkg. (8 oz.) egg noodles
1  small onion

      Brown ground beef and onion in skillet.  Cook noodles and drain.  In a large baking dish, put all ingredients and stir to mix well.  Bake at 350 degrees; until heated through.  Cheese cut in small chunks may be added before baking.
