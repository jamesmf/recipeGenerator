BRUSCHETTA


1  loaf French bread
1 3/4  c. (14.5 oz. can) Contadina Italian-style pear-shaped tomatoes
1/2  c. crumbled Feta cheese
1  Tbsp. olive oil
1 small  clove garlic, crushed
1/4  tsp. salt
1  Tbsp. finely chopped onion
1/4  tsp. dried oregano leaves, crushed
1/4  tsp. black pepper
2  Tbsp. chopped fresh basil

      Cut loaf in half lengthwise and then cut into 2-inch slices. Toast cut sides. Drain tomatoes thoroughly; chop tomatoes. Combine tomatoes with remaining ingredients. Spoon tomato mixture onto toasted slices. Broil 5-inches from heat source until tomato mixture is hot, about 2 minutes.
       Calories: 100, Fat: 3g.
