CHICKEN POT PIE


1  chicken, boiled and deboned
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  can Veg-All, drained
1/2  can chicken broth
1  stick butter
1  c. self-rising flour
1  c. buttermilk

      Mix first 4 ingredients and place in baking dish. Mix butter (melted) with flour and buttermilk. Pour over chicken in baking dish and bake until done and light brown on top, 30 minutes at 350 degrees;.
