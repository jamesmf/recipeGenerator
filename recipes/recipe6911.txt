ALL-AMERICAN CASSEROLE


1  c. rice
1  lb. ground beef
1  egg, slightly beaten
2  tsp. grated onion
2  tsp. salt
1  tsp. parsley flakes
1/8  tsp. marjoram
  dash of pepper
2 1/2  c. tomato juice
1/2  tsp. sugar
1  pkg. biscuit mix
1/4  c. American cheese

      Combine rice, beef, egg, onion, salt, parsley flakes, marjoram and pepper. Add 1/2 cup tomato juice; mix lightly. Shape mixture into 18 balls. Place in skillet. Add sugar to remaining tomato juice and pour over meatballs in skillet. Bring to boiling point.
      Turn oven to 450 degrees;. Follow directions on biscuit mix package to prepare. Add shredded American cheese to dry mixture before adding any liquid and continue directions. Spoon droplets of biscuit mix over top of casserole. Place in oven, without cover, for 15 to 20 minutes. Yield: 6 to 8 servings.
