CRUNCHY CHEESERONI


2  c. cooked Creamettes (macaroni)
1  lb. ground beef, browned and drained
1  can tomato soup
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  can French fried onion rings
2  c. shredded Cheddar cheese
1  medium green pepper, diced (optional)
1/4  c. chopped pimento (optional)

      Mix noodles, hamburger and the 2 soups together. Place 1/2 of the casserole mixture in a baking dish. The next layer is 1/2 of the cheese and 1/2 of the fried onions. Next layer is the second 1/2 of the casserole mixture. Bake at 350 degrees; for 25 minutes. Sprinkle remaining onions and cheese on top and bake for another 15 minutes.
