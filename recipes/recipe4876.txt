POTATO SALAD


  potatoes, cooked and diced
  onions to taste
  salt to taste
  celery seed to taste

 
Mayonnaise:
3 level  Tbsp. sugar
1  Tbsp. flour (rounded)
1  tsp. dry mustard
1  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper
2  eggs
1/2  c. vinegar plus 1 c. water
2 to 2 1/2  Tbsp. butter

      Mix first 5 ingredients together.  Add remaining ingredients. Boil, stirring constantly, until thickened.  Cool and pour over potato mixture.
