PERUVIAN LOMO SALTADO


2  Tbsp. vegetable oil
1/2  c. onion, sliced
2  cloves of garlic, minced
1/2  c. bell pepper (yellow or red) chopped
1 1/2  c. potato, cubed and cooked
1  Tbsp. jalapeno pepper, diced
1/2  lb. sirloin steak, sliced thin
1  tomato, chopped
  oregano
1  tsp. salt and pepper to taste

	Heat oil in a non-stick pan and sauté onion, garlic and bell pepper.  Cook potato, stirring occasionally, until potato just begins to brown.  Add chili and steak.  Sauté a few more minutes, stir in tomato, oregano, salt and pepper.  Serve with steamed rice.
