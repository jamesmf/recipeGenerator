ITALIAN BEEF IN THE CROCK-POT


4 to 5  lb. roast, cut up in chunks
2  to 3 beef bouillon cubes
1  tsp. garlic salt
2  Tbsp. Italian dressing

      Place meat, bouillon cubes, garlic salt and Italian dressing in crock-pot.  Add enough water to cover beef.  Cook on low for 8 hours.  Serve with garlic bread and salad.
