MUD PIE


1/2  pkg. Nabisco chocolate wafers
1/2  cube butter, melted
1  gal. coffee ice cream
1 1/2  c. fudge sauce

      Crush wafers and add butter; mix well.  Press into 9-inch pie plate.  Cover with soft coffee ice cream.  Put into freezer until ice cream is firm.  Top with cold fudge sauce.  Store in freezer approximately 10 hours.  Top with whipped cream and slivered almonds.  Serves 8.
