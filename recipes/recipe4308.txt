OVERNIGHT SALAD


1  head lettuce
1  head cauliflower
1  pt. mayo (not salad dressing)
  chopped onion to taste
  bacon to taste, crumbled
  Parmesan cheese to taste
1/2  c. sugar

      Layer lettuce, then cauliflower. Spread mayo on top of cauliflower. Sprinkle onion and bacon on top of mayo. Cover with sugar and Parmesan cheese. Let set in the refrigerator overnight. Stir before serving.
       You can add red cabbage, snow peas or any other vegetable for color and taste.
