HONEY LIME GRILLED CHICKEN


1/2  cup honey
1/3  cup soy sauce
1/4  cup line juice
4  boneless, skinless, chicken breast halves

      In a resealable plastic bag, combine the honey, soy, and lime juice, mix well.  Add chicken and turn to coat.  Seal and refrigerate for 30 - 45 minutes.  Drain and discard the marinade. Grill the chicken uncovered, over medium heat for 6 - 7 minutes on each side or until juices run clear.
