BREAD AND BUTTER PICKLES


8  qt. cucumbers, sliced
8  large onions, sliced thin
5  large sweet peppers, chopped or sliced
1  c. salt
2  qts. white vinegar
7  c. sugar
1  tsp. turmeric
3  tsp. white mustard
1/4  tsp. celery seed

      Combine cucumbers, onions and peppers.  Add salt and mix well. Let stand for 24 hours, then drain well.  In a large kettle, combine vinegar, sugar, turmeric, mustard and celery seed.  Bring to a boil, add cucumber mixture and bring to a boil again.  Spoon pickles into hot sterilized jars and seal.
