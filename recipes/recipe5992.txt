BAVARIAN SALAD


1  box lime jello
1 - 2  cans crushed pineapple, drained
1  c. pecans
1  c. grated american cheese
1/2  pt. whipped cream
20  lg. marshmallows
  Juice from pineapple plus enough water to equal 2 c. liquid

      Melt marshmallows in juice from pineapple and water over low heat.  Add lime jello and refrigerate until mixture is cool.  Then add other ingredients and chill until set.
