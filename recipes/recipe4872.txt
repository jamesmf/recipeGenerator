HEARTY MACARONI SALAD


6  hard-cooked eggs, cut in eighths
2  hard-cooked eggs, quartered for garnish
1 (7 oz.)  pkg. elbow macaroni
1 (7 oz.)  can crabmeat, drained and flaked
1/3  c. chopped celery
1/3  c. chopped onion
1/4  c. chopped parsley
1  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper
1  c. light mayonnaise or salad dressing

      Cook macaroni according to package directions; drain and cool thoroughly.  In a large mixing bowl, combine crabmeat, eggs, celery, onion, parsley, salt and pepper.  Combine with macaroni and chill before serving.  Garnish with additional hard-cooked eggs.
