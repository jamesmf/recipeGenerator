CAJUN PASTRY


1  c. Mahatma or Water Maid rice
1/2  c. chopped onion
2/3  c. chopped bell pepper
2  cloves garlic, minced
1  c. chopped celery
1/4  c. chopped parsley
1 (2 oz.)  jar pimentos, drained
1/4  c. margarine
1 1/2  tsp. seasoned salt
1/2  tsp. red pepper
1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can cream of mushroom soup
1  can Orleans crabmeat
1  can Orleans shrimp
8  small pie shells

	Prepare rice according to package directions. Preheat oven to 400 degrees;. In a medium skillet, saut next 7 ingredients. Add seasonings and mix until blended. Add crabmeat, shrimp and cooked rice. Stir to combine. Place about 1/2 cup mixture into each pie shell. Bake for 20 minutes.
