EASY CHICKEN GUMBO


3  Tbsp. margarine or butter
1 (16 oz.)  can cut okra, drained (I use fresh okra in season)
1  small onion, chopped
1/2  small green pepper, chopped
4  c. chicken or turkey broth
1 (16 oz.)  can whole tomatoes
1  small bay leaf
1  tsp. salt
  dash of pepper
2  c. cut up cooked chicken or turkey
1  Tbsp. snipped parsley (optional)
3  c. hot cooked rice

      Heat margarine in 3-quart saucepan until melted. Add okra, onion and green pepper; cook and stir until onion is tender. Stir in broth, tomatoes (with liquid), bay leaf, salt and pepper. Heat to boiling; reduce heat. Simmer, uncovered, 15 minutes. Stir in chicken and parsley; heat through. Serve chicken mixture over rice. Makes 6 servings.
