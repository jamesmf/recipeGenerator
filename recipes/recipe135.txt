CHOCOLATE KISS COOKIES


1  can Eagle Brand condensed milk
1/3  c. smooth peanut butter
2  c. Bisquick
1  tsp. vanilla
  granulated sugar for coating
1  pkg. chocolate kisses

      Beat Eagle Brand and peanut butter until smooth.  Add Bisquick and vanilla and mix.  Form 1-inch balls.  Roll in granulated sugar. Place 2 inches apart on ungreased cookie sheet.  Bake at 375 degrees; for 6 to 8 minutes.  Remove from oven.  Press chocolate kiss into center of each cookie immediately.  Remove from pan and allow to cool.
