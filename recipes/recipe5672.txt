FRUITY WALNUT SALAD


2  heads lettuce, cleaned and torn into pieces
1 to 1 1/2  lb. fresh spinach, cleaned
2  seedless oranges, peeled and sectioned
1/2  c. red onion, thinly sliced
1/2  c. walnuts, chopped
2  tsp. butter or margarine

	In a large bowl, combine the lettuce, spinach, oranges and onion. Using a saucepan, saut√© the walnuts in butter until lightly browned and add to the lettuce mixture.
Sweet and Sour Dressing:
1 1/2  c. canola oil
3/4  c. white vinegar
3/4  c. sugar or Splenda
1/2  tsp. salt (optional)
1 1/2  tsp. celery seed
1 1/2  tsp. dry mustard
1 1/2  tsp. paprika
1 1/2  tsp. grated onion (optional)

	Combine all ingredients in a jar and shake well. Chill well in refrigerator. Shake before serving. Pour on top of the salad and serve.
