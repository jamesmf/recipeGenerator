CUCUMBER PICKLES


7  C. thinly sliced cucumbers
1  C. thinly sliced onions
1/2  C. slivered green peppers
2  C. sugar
1  C. white vinegar
1  Tbsp. canning salt
1  tsp. celery seed
1  tsp. mustard seed

      Bring to a boil:  sugar, white vinegar, canning salt, celery seed and mustard seed.
      Marinate for 24 hours.
      May keep in refrigerator or freeze in plastic containers.
