LAYERED BEEF VEGETABLE CASSEROLE


1 1/2  lb. ground beef
1  c. chopped green pepper
1  large (1/2 lb.) potato, peeled and sliced
1 (28 oz.)  can tomatoes, mashed
1  c. sliced onion
2  tsp. salt
1  c. thinly sliced carrot
1/3  c. long grain rice (uncooked)

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees;. Saut√© ground beef, onions and green pepper until beef is browned. Drain fat. Add salt and mix well. Place sliced potato in bottom of 2 1/2-quart baking dish. Alternate layers of carrots, rice and meat. Pour mashed tomatoes over casserole and cover. Bake for 2 hours.
