GAIL'S ITALIAN BEEF


4 to 5  lb. pot roast
1  can beef broth
1  can beer
7  oz. Good Seasons Italian dressing mix
2  c. water
1  tsp. oregano
  pepper to taste
1 small  jar pepperoncini
1  can mushrooms

      Place roast in covered baking dish.  Add beef broth, seasoning mix, water, oregano and pepperoncini.  Cover and bake at 275 degrees; for 5 hours until tender.  Remove meat from pan and shred.  Place meat back in pan, add pepperoncini and mushrooms.  Cook 30 minutes more.
