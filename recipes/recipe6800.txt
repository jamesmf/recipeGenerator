CHICKEN HOT DISH

First Layer:
2  (6 oz.) herb seasoned croutons
1/2  c. melted oleo
1  c. broth

      Butter 9 x 13 pan. Put half of this mixture in pan.
Second Layer:
4  c. cut up chicken, cooked
1/2  c. celery
1/4  c. onion
1/2  c. salad dressing
1/2  tsp. salt

      Third Layer:  Layer rest of crouton mixture.
Fourth Layer:
2  eggs
1 1/2  c. milk

      Beat eggs and milk and pour over other 3 layers; cover. Place in fridge overnight. Take out 1  hour before baking. Put 1 can cream of chicken soup over top. Add a layer of grated cheese and bake at 325 degrees; for 1 hour.
