MAC AND CHEESE


1  bag elbow pasta
  oil
1  tsp. salt
1/2  block cheese (Velveeta cheese)
1/2  stick butter
1/2  c. sour cream
1  tsp. black pepper
1/2  c. milk
2  Tbsp. Miracle Whip

      Boil pasta.  After pasta is boiled (oil and salt in water) until soft, remove and drain water.  Mix all ingredients together. Once mixed, place in pan.  Then place in oven at 350 degrees; until cheese melts, approximately 15 minutes.
