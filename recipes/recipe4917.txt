SPINACH SALAD
(With Sweet and Sour Honey Dressing)  
Dressing:
1 1/2  tsp. dry mustard
1  c. white vinegar
4  Tbsp. soy sauce
1  Tbsp. fresh garlic, minced
4  tsp. fresh grated ginger
1/2  c. olive oil
3  Tbsp. sesame oil
1/8  c. lemon juice
3  Tbsp. sugar
3  Tbsp. honey
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. pepper

      Mix all ingredients and simmer on the stove for 5 minutes, or until sugar is completely dissolved.
Spinach Salad (Individual Serving):
3  oz. cleaned and stemmed fresh spinach or baby spinach
2  oz. mandarin orange segments
1  oz. thinly sliced red onions
1  oz. sliced fresh mushrooms
1  oz. coarsely chopped walnuts
2  oz. sweet and sour honey ginger dressing

      Toss spinach and dressing together, then top salad with remaining ingredients.
