ORANGE-GINGER PORK ROAST


1  tsp. dry mustard
1  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. white pepper
1/2  tsp. ground ginger
3 to 4  lb. boneless pork loin roast, rolled and tied

 
Glaze:
1  c. orange marmalade
1/4  c. light corn syrup
2  Tbsp. lemon juice
1/2  tsp. dry mustard
1/2  tsp. ground ginger

      Preheat oven to 325 degrees;.  Combine first 4 ingredients.  Mix well. Rub into pork roast.  Place roast on rack in shallow roasting pan.  Insert meat thermometer in thickest part of roast.  Cook, uncovered, 30 to 45 minutes per pound or until thermometer reads 170 degrees;.  Make glaze.
      Combine all glaze ingredients in a medium saucepan.  Cook over medium heat for 5 minutes, stirring often.  Baste with glaze every 10 minutes during last 30 minutes cooking time.  Let roast stand 10 minutes before carving.  Serves 10 to 12.
