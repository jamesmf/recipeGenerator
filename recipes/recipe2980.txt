CHUNKY SALSA


6  large tomatoes
4  fresh green chiles
4  jalapenos
1  bunch cilantro
1  medium onion
2  cloves garlic
1  lime
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. pepper
1  tsp. onion powder
1  tsp. garlic salt

      Chop all ingredients except for lime and spices. Leave your seeds in the green chiles while chopping up. Mix everything together. Add salt, pepper, onion powder and garlic powder. Mix thoroughly. Squeeze lime juice on top and mix well. Ready to serve.
