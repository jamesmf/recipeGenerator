SEVEN LAYER SALAD


1  head lettuce, torn into pieces
1  c. celery, thinly sliced
1/2  c. onions, chopped small
1 (10 oz.)  pkg. frozen English peas
1  c. mayonnaise
1  tsp. sugar*
2  tsp. seasoning salt*
1/2  c. shredded Parmesan cheese*
2  boiled eggs, chopped
4  strips cooked bacon or bacon bits

      *Can substitute lite Italian salad dressing mix for these ingredients.
      In a large bowl, layer lettuce, celery, onions and peas. Spread mayonnaise over peas and sprinkle sugar, salt and cheese on it. Cover and refrigerate for 24 hours. Before you serve salad, add chopped eggs and bacon bits or crumbled bacon pieces.
