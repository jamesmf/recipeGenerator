CRUNCHY PEA SALAD


1 (10 oz.)  pkg. frozen baby peas, thawed
1  c. celery, diced
1  c. cauliflower, chopped
1/4  c. green pepper, diced
1/4  c. green onion, diced
1  c. cashews, chopped
1/2  c. sour cream
1  c. prepared Hidden Valley Ranch (original Ranch salad dressing)
  bacon, crisp-cooked and crumbled

	Combine all ingredients except bacon. Chill. Garnish with bacon just before serving.
