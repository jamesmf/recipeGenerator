REUBEN SANDWICH


8  slices pumpernickel bread
1/2  lb. corned beef, sliced thin
8  oz. can sauerkraut, well drained
2 to 3  Tbsp. Miracle Whip
4  slices Swiss cheese
2  Tbsp. margarine

      Place corned beef, then sauerkraut, Miracle Whip and cheese on 4 slices of bread.  Top with other bread slices.  Spread butter on outside of bread.  Grill in skillet or on griddle until browned and cheese melts.
