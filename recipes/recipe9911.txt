CORN CASSEROLE


2  c. corn
2  tsp. sugar
2  eggs, well beaten
2  c. scalded milk
1/4  c. flour
1  tsp. salt
1  Tbsp. oleo, melted

      Mix corn, flour, sugar and salt together.  Combine eggs, melted oleo and milk and mix with corn mixture.  Pour into greased casserole and place in pan of water in 350 degrees; oven for 1 1/4 hours. Stir corn mixture twice in the first 30 minutes.  It is done when a silver knife inserted in center comes out clean.
