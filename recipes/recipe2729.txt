EASTER EGG FONDANT


1  can Eagle Brand sweetened condensed milk
1  (8 oz.) cream cheese
1/2  lb. confectioners sugar
  maple flavor to taste
2  c. peanut butter
  nuts, coconut or cocoa cherry flavor
  melted chocolate chips

      Mix milk and cream cheese until blended.  Add confectioners sugar and mix until blended.  Add maple flavor to taste and nuts, coconut or cocoa cherry flavor.  Refrigerate until solid and then dip in chocolate.
