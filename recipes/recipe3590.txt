PARMESAN-BACON STICKS


15  bacon slices, cut in half lengthwise (about 1 lb.)
1 (3 oz.)  pkg. thin breadsticks (30 breadsticks)
1/2 to 2/3  c. grated Parmesan cheese

      Wrap bacon strips around breadsticks; roll in cheese and place on baking sheets. Bake at 250 degrees; for 1 hour. Serve immediately. Yield: 2 1/2 dozen.
      Prep: 15 minutes. Bake: 1 hour.
