BARBECUED SPARE RIBS


3  lbs. spare ribs
1  lrg. onion
3/4  catsup
1  tsp chili powder
1/2  C. water
2  Tbsp. sugar
3/4  tsp. tabasco sauce
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce

      Put ribs in roasting pan.  Slice onions over top of ribs.
      Pour other ingredients over top of ribs.
      Bake in 350 degree oven for 2 hours.
      Country style ribs can be used also.
