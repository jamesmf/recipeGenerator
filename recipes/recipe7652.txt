EGG AND CHEESE BAKE


1  c. Bisquick
1 1/2  c. cottage cheese
6  eggs, lightly beaten
1/2  lb. grated Cheddar cheese
2  tsp. chopped onion
1  tsp. parsley flakes
1/4  tsp. salt
1  c. milk
1/4  c. butter

      Mix  ingredients in order given,  except  butter.  Melt butter in  9  x  13-inch pan.  Pour  in  cheese/egg  mixture, spreading evenly, then bake at 350 degrees; for 40 minutes.
