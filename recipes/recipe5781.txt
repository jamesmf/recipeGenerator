MUSHROOM SALAD
(Preparation Time: 20 Minutes Plus Chilling Time)  

1  lb. fresh mushrooms
1/2  lb. baby Swiss cheese, grated
2  bunches green onions, sliced

      Clean and slice mushrooms into a large salad bowl. Add cheese and onions. Chill.
Dressing:
1/2  c. oil
1/4  c. red wine vinegar
1  Tbsp. Cavender's Greek seasoning

      Mix dressing ingredients and chill thoroughly. Pour over mushroom mixture and serve. Yield: 10 to 12 servings.
