COCONUT CHEESE DROPS


1  can Eagle Brand milk
2  egg yolks
4  c. moist flaked coconut
1/3  c. Parmesan cheese

      Mix all ingredients.  Using teaspoon, shape into balls; place 1-inch apart on greased cookie sheet.  Bake at 325 degrees; until golden, about 8 or 9 minutes.  Cool on racks.  Delicious when dipped in chocolate!
