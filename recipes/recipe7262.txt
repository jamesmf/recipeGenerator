CHICKEN ENCHILADAS


2  c. chopped cooked chicken
1  c. chopped green pepper
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese, cubed
1 (8 oz.)  jar salsa, divided
6  flour tortillas (6-inch)
3/4  lb. (12 oz.) Velveeta cheese, cut up
1/4  c. milk

      Stir chicken, green peppers, cream cheese and 1/2 cup salsa in medium saucepan on low heat until cream cheese is melted. Spoon 1/2  cup of chicken mixture down center of each tortilla; roll up. Place in lightly greased 12 x 8-inch baking dish. Stir cheese and milk in small pan over low heat until smooth. Pour sauce over tortillas. Cover with foil. Bake at 350 degrees; for 20 minutes or until heated. Top with remaining salsa.
