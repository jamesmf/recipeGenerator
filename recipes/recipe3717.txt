CAN'T STOP EATING THEM PRETZELS


30 - 36  oz. bag pretzels
1  bottle of Orville Redenbacher Buttery Oil
1  envelope dry ranch dressing
3  Tbsp. dill weed
1  Tbsp garlic powder

      Combine in a microwave safe bowl, the buttery oil, dry ranch dressing, dill weed and garlic powder.
      Heat for 1 minute and stir.  DO NOT BOIL!
       Pour over pretzels and stir occasionally until cooled.  Cool and store in airtight container.
