MAINE LOBSTER STEW


4  Tbsp. butter
1  c. cooked lobster meat
2  c. milk
1/2  c. light cream or evaporated milk
  sprinkle of paprika
  freshly ground black pepper

      Melt 3 tablespoons butter in a heavy saucepan and add lobster meat.  Simmer until both lobster and butter turn very red, about 2 to 3 minutes.  Add milk and cream.  Heat almost to boiling, but do not boil.  Chill for at least 2 hours.  Reheat stew in a double boiler.  Sprinkle with paprika and add remaining 1 tablespoon butter and pepper.  Serve with oyster crackers and pickles.
