BARBECUE KRAUT


1 1/2  lb. ground beef
1  onion, chopped
1 (15 oz.)  can tomato sauce
1  c. brown sugar
1 (29 oz.)  can sauerkraut

      Brown ground beef with onion.  Drain.  Add the rest of the ingredients in a greased casserole dish and bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour.  This will make your mouth water & tingle.
