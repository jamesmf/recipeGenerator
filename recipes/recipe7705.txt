CHICKEN AND RICE CASSEROLE


1  can French onion soup
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  c. rice
1  whole chicken
4  c. chicken broth

      Boil chicken until done. Remove bones. Cook rice in chicken broth until tender. Mix all ingredients. Place in casserole dish. Fills a 3-quart dish full. Bake for 45 minutes at 325 degrees;.
