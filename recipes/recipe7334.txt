BERYL'S BABY BACK RIBS


4  lb. baby back pork ribs

 
Sauce:
1  c. catsup
1/2  c. dark corn syrup
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. Tabasco pepper sauce
1  c. vinegar
2  tsp. sugar
1/4  tsp. garlic powder
1/4  tsp. onion powder

      Combine sauce ingredients over high heat until boiling. Reduce heat; simmer 30 to 40 minutes until thick. Coat ribs, front and back, with sauce. Wrap ribs in aluminum foil. Bake for 2 1/2 hours. Remove ribs from foil and smother with more sauce.
