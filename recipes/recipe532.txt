STRAWBERRY PIE


1/3  c. lemon juice
1  can sweetened condensed milk
1 small  carton Cool Whip
1  pt. fresh strawberries
1  (9-inch) baked pie shell

      Mix lemon juice with condensed milk until it is thick.  Fold in Cool Whip, then strawberries.  Pour in 9-inch baked pie shell. Children love this pie.
