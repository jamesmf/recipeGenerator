OATMEAL PIE


2/3  c. sugar
1  stick butter
2  eggs or 1 jumbo egg
2/3  c. dark corn syrup
2/3  c. oatmeal, uncooked
1  tsp. vanilla

      Cream sugar and butter.  Add eggs and beat well.  Stir in rest of ingredients.  Pour mixture into an unbaked 9-inch pie shell. Bake at 325 degrees; for about 40 minutes.
