DAD'S DOOR COUNTY CHILI


3  lb. ground beef
3  cans dark kidney beans
3  cans stewed tomatoes
3 (8 oz.)  cans tomato sauce
3  tsp. salt
9  beef bouillon cubes
3  c. diced onions
4 1/2  Tbsp. oregano
1 1/2  tsp. thyme
1 1/2  tsp. black pepper
1  tsp. crushed red pepper
4 1/2  Tbsp. lemon juice
4 1/2  Tbsp. horseradish
4 1/2  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
6  Tbsp. chili powder
1 1/2  tsp. baking soda*

      Saut ground beef. Add remaining ingredients. Cook slowly 2 hours or longer.
      *Takes the burp out of the Chili!
