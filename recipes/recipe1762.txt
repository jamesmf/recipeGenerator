BAKED FISH, SPANISH STYLE


1 1/2  lb. fish (steaks or filets)
1 1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. paprika
1/4  tsp. pepper
1  green pepper, cut into rings
1  tomato, sliced
1  small onion, sliced
2  Tbsp. lemon juice
2  Tbsp. olive or vegetable oil
1  clove garlic, finely chopped
  lemon wedges

      From sunny Spain, a colorful, low calorie fish dish, garnished with a traditional Spanish medley of green peppers, tomatoes, onions and subtle seasonings.
      If fish pieces are large, cut into serving pieces.  Arrange fish in ungreased square baking dish, 8 x 8 x 2-inches; sprinkle with salt, paprika and pepper.  Top with green pepper rings and tomato and onion slices.  Mix lemon juice, oil and garlic; pour over fish.  Cover and cook in 375 degrees; oven for 15 minutes.  Uncover and cook until fish flakes easily with fork, 10 to 15 minutes. Garnish with lemon wedges.  Serves 6.
