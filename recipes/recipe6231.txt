FRESH TOMATO SAUCE


5  medium tomatoes, peeled
1/2  c. onion, chopped
2  Tbsp. margarine
2  cloves garlic, minced
1  Tbsp. parsley, chopped
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. basil
1/4  tsp. coarsely ground black pepper

      Chop tomatoes, removing seeds.  Saut√© onion in margarine. Stir in tomatoes, garlic, parsley, salt, basil and pepper.  Puree mixture in food processor or blender.  Serve over pasta or fresh vegetables.  Makes 2 1/2 cups.
