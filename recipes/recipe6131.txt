HONEY-FRUIT SYRUP


3/4  c. unsweetened pineapple juice or orange juice
2  Tbsp. honey
2  tsp. cornstarch
1/8  tsp. ground ginger
1  c. sliced strawberries, raspberries, and/or blueberries

      In a small saucepan, stir together pineapple juice, honey, cornstarch, and ground ginger.  Cook and stir over medium heat until thickened and bubbly, cook and stir for 2 minutes more.  Stir in fruit.  Serve over pancakes, waffles or French toast.
