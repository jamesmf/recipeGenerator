MACARONI SALAD


1  lb. macaroni
1  pt. salad dressing
  celery, chopped
  onion, chopped
1  Tbsp. sugar
  cucumber (optional)
  pickles (optional)
1/3  c. milk

      Cook macaroni.  Cool under cold water.  Mix everything together.  Put into refrigerator for at least 3 to 4 hours to settle.  Enjoy!
