CRANBERRY FRUIT DIP


1/2  c. nonfat or low-fat vanilla, lemon or peach yogurt
1/2  c. whole berry cranberry sauce
1/4  tsp. ground cinnamon
1/8  tsp. ground ginger

      In a medium bowl, stir together all ingredients. Serve with fruit. The dip can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Serves 8 (2 tablespoons per serving).
       Calories 37, Fat 0g.
