SOUTHWESTERN SALAD


2 1/2  c. corn chips
1/2  head iceberg lettuce, torn
1  c. (4 oz.) shredded Mexican or cheddar cheese
1  can (15 oz.) pinto beans, rinsed and drained
1  small tomato, seeded and diced
1/4 to 1/2  c. salad dressing of your choice
2  Tbsp. sliced green onions
1 to 2  Tbsp. chopped green chiles
1  small avocado, peeled and sliced

      In a serving bowl or platter, toss chips, lettuce, cheese, beans, tomato, salad dressing, onions and chiles. Top with avocado.  Serve immediately.  Yield: 6 to 8 servings.
