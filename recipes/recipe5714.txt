TABOULI


3  bunches parsley
1  c. fine burghul (#1 cracked wheat)
  2-3 bunches scallion
2  tomatoes, chopped fine
1/2  c. lemon juice
1/2  c. oil
  Salt, pepepr to taste
1  cucumber, peeled, cut in 4's

      Wash parsley & chop fine.  In bowl, cover burghul with warm water to soak for 10 mins. Water will be absorbed.  Combine parsley, soft, drained burghul and remainder of ingredients. Refrigerate.
