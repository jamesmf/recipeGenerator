HORSERADISH CREAM SAUCE


1/2  c. heavy whipping cream
3  Tbsp. mayonnaise
1  Tbsp. prepared mustard
1/2  tsp. salt
  Dash cayenne pepper
2  Tbsp. bottled prepared horseradish

      Beat heavy cream with an electric mixer until soft peaks form.  Set aside.  In a small bowl, stir together remaining ingredients.  Fold horseradish mixture into whipped cream.  Serve with standing rib beef roast or prime rib.
      Yield:  About 1 cup.
