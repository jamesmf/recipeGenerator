STEAK OR CHICKEN SHISH KABOBS


1 1/2  lb. steak or chicken, partially frozen
1/2  c. soy sauce
2  Tbsp. olive oil
2  Tbsp. honey
2  Tbsp. wine vinegar
1  clove of garlic
1/2  tsp. ground ginger
1/8  tsp. pepper

      Slice meat into 4 x 1/4-inch strips and set aside.  Combine remaining ingredients in a shallow dish.  Add steak, coating well. Cover and let sit in refrigerator overnight.  Remove steak from refrigerator and place on skewers.  Add fresh vegetables if you like.  Grill 2 to 3 minutes on each side or until desired level of doneness is reached.  Baste frequently with marinade.
