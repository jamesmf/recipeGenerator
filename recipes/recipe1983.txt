SPANISH PAVLOVA


3  egg whites (at room temperature)
3/4  c. sugar
1/4  tsp. salt
2  tsp. cornstarch
1  tsp. white vinegar
1  tsp. vanilla
  whipped cream
  strawberries or raspberries

      Beat egg whites until soft peaks form.  Gradually add sugar, salt and cornstarch.  Gradually add white vinegar and vanilla. Beat until shiny.  Pour into tube pan.  Bake at 250 degrees; for 30 minutes.  Turn off heat and leave in oven overnight.
      To Serve:  Fill center with whipped cream and pour fruit around Pavlova.
