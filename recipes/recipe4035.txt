CHEESE STICKS


1  c. all-purpose flour, sifted
1 1/2  tsp. baking powder
1/2  tsp. salt
2  tsp. butter
1  c. sharp cheese, shredded
1/2  tsp. cayenne pepper
1/2  c. water

      Sift flour, baking powder and salt into a medium size bowl. Blend in butter until mixture is crumbly.  Add cheese, cayenne pepper and water.  Mix well until mixture holds together.  Roll out and cut into strips.
      Bake at 425 degrees; for 10 minutes or lightly brown.
