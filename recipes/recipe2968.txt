SHRIMP SPREAD
(Dip)  

1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese, softened
1/2  c. sour cream
1/4  c. mayonnaise (Miracle Whip)
1  c. seafood cocktail sauce
2  c. (8 oz.) shredded Mozzarella cheese
2 (4 1/4 oz.)  cans shrimp, rinsed and drained
3  green onions, sliced
3/4  c. chopped tomato

      Beat cream cheese, sour cream and mayonnaise. Spread on a 12-inch serving platter (or whatever). Cover with seafood sauce. Sprinkle with cheese, shrimp, onions and tomato. Serve with crackers or chips.
