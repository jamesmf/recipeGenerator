JELLO SALAD


1  pkg. lemon Jell-O
1 small  can crushed pineapple
4  bananas, diced 4 ways
16  bite-size marshmallows
1  c. sugar
1  c. pineapple juice
2  Tbsp. flour
1  egg
1  pkg. Dream Whip

      Mix lemon Jell-O, crushed pineapple, bananas and marshmallows. Let stand until firm. Cook sugar, pineapple juice, flour and egg until thick and cool.  Spread over Jell-O mix. Mix Dream Whip and spread.
