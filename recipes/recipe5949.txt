COKE AND PIE CHERRY SALAD


1  can pie cherries
1/2  c. water
1  c. sugar
2  boxes cherry jello
  few drops red food coloring
1 small  can or bottle Coke
1 small  can crushed pineapple
3/4  c. chopped nuts

      Bring cherries, water and sugar to boil in saucepan. Add jello and food coloring. Let cool. Add Coke, pineapple and nuts. Chill as you would jello.
