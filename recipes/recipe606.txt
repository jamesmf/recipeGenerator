ORIENTAL STEAK SALAD


6  oz. sliced, cooked steak
2  c. broccoli florets, blanched
1  medium tomato, seeded and diced
1/4  c. sliced green onions
1  Tbsp. rice vinegar
2  tsp. each:  Chinese sesame oil, soy sauce and water
1/4  tsp. minced, pared ginger root

      In medium bowl combine steak, broccoli, tomato and onions.  In jar with tight fitting lid, combine rest of ingredients, cover and shake well.  Pour over salad; toss to combine.  Cover and refrigerate until chilled.  Toss again before serving.  Makes 2 servings; contains 277 calories per serving.
