CHICKEN AND RICE


1  fryer chicken
1  can mushroom soup
1  c. rice
2  c. milk
1  Tbsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper
1  Tbsp. oleo

      Grease baking dish with oleo. Pat rice in bottom of pan. Place cut up chicken on rice and pour milk, mushroom soup, salt and pepper over chicken. Bake in 300 degrees; oven until chicken is golden brown.
