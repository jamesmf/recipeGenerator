CAVATINI


1  lb. ground beef
10  oz. canned tomatoes
5  oz. tomato paste
1/3  tsp. oregano
1/4  tsp. garlic powder
  dash red pepper
1  tsp. salt
1 1/2  Tbsp. sugar
1  green pepper, chopped
12  oz. macaroni
12  oz. grated mozzarella cheese
3 1/2 to 4  oz. grated american cheese

      Cook beef and green pepper; drain. Cook macaroni; drain. Combine all except cheese, and put in baking dish. Sprinkle with cheese mixture and bake at 325 degrees until cheese melts.
