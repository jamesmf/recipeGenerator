CRISP FRIED CATFISH



1  qt. water
1/4  c. vinegar (white)
3  Tbsp. salt

 
Batter:
1 1/2  c. cornmeal
1/2  c. all-purpose flour
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. onion powder
1  tsp. seasoned salt

      Combine first 3 ingredients in large bowl.  Soak catfish in vinegar mixture 4 to 5 hours in refrigerator.
      Combine cornmeal, flour, salt, onion powder and salt in large plastic bag.  Drain catfish well.  Add catfish one at a time and shake until completely coated.  Pour oil to the depth of 2 to 3-inches into Dutch oven; heat to 375 degrees;.  Fry catfish 4 minutes on each side until golden brown.  Drain well on paper towels.
