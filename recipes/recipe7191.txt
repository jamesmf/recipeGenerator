CHEDDAR POTATO CASSEROLE


1  c. milk
1/4  c. oleo
1  tsp. onion flakes
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. ground pepper
1 (24 oz.)  pkg. frozen hash browns, thawed
3/4  c. shredded Cheddar cheese

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees;.  In bowl, combine milk, butter, onion flakes, salt and pepper.  Add potatoes and cheese; mix well. Spread in 9 x 13 greased pan.  Bake 1 hour.  Serves 8.
