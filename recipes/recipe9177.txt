THE MEXICAN DISH


1  lb. hamburger
1  onion
1 (16 oz.)  jar salsa
1  can black beans
8  oz. grated cheddar cheese
1  box Jiffy corn bread mix

      Grease a 9 x 13 inch baking dish.  Cook hamburger with onion; drain.  Mix Jiffy cornbread mix according to package directions. Layer baking dish in the following order: black beans, hamburger and onions, salsa, cheese, and cornbread mixture on top.  Bake at 400 degrees; for 30 minutes.
