MICROWAVE MEAT LOAF


1  lb. hamburger
1  egg
1/4  c. diced onion
3/4  c. oatmeal
6  soda cracker squares
1 small  can tomato sauce (save a bit)

      Mix well. Place in an 8 x 8 glass pan. Press in the meat mixture. Lightly cover with tomato sauce. Cover with clear wrap. Microwave 7 minutes. Turn dish and microwave 7 minutes.
