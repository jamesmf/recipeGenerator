LAYERED CASSEROLE


1 1/2  lb. lean ground beef
2  tsp. salt
2  tsp. sugar
1  can (16 oz.) tomatoes
1  can (8 oz.) tomato sauce
2  cloves garlic crushed or 1/2 tsp. garlic powder
  pepper to taste
1  pkg. (5 oz.) egg noodles, cooked and drained
1  C. dairy sour cream
1  pkg. (3 oz.) cream cheese, softened
6  green onions w/tops chopped
1  C. grated cheddar cheese

      Preheat oven to 325 degrees.  Cook meat in large skillet until browned.  Drain fat.  Add salt, sugar, tomatoes, tomato sauce, garlic, and pepper; simmer over low heat 5 to 10 min. Combine noodles with sour cream, cream cheese, and green onion. Arrange meat mixture and noodle mixture in alternate layers in a 2 qt. casserole dish. Top with grated cheese.  Bake 25 min.
