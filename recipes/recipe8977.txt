KING RANCH CASSEROLE


1  chicken (whole), cooked, boned and cubed
1  large onion, chopped
1  c. grated cheese
1  pkg. tortillas (flour)
1  c. mushroom soup
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  can Ro-Tel tomatoes with chili peppers
1  tsp. chili powder
1 1/2  c. chicken broth

      Cook chicken; bone and cut up. Layer in a baking dish the chicken, onion and cheese with tortillas. Mix soups, chicken broth, Ro-Tel tomatoes and chili powder together. Pour mixture over ingredients in baking dish. Top dish with grated cheese. Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour. (Cook onions before using.)
