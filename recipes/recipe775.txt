CHICKEN AND ORANGE SALAD


1/2  c. long grain rice
1  small onion, chopped
  salt and pepper to taste
1 1/3  c. cooked chicken, diced
2  stalks celery, chopped
4  stuffed olives, chopped
1/2  c. mayonnaise
2  oranges
  lettuce leaves

      Cook rice in boiling salted water until tender.  Drain and while still hot, mix with onion and seasonings; cool.  Add chicken, celery, olives and mayonnaise.  Toss lightly.  Peel oranges and split them into sections.  Cut 1/2 of the sections into small pieces.  Add to mixture.
      To Serve:  Place salad on bed of lettuce.  Garnish with remaining orange sections.
