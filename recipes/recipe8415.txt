CHICKEN PIE DELUXE


1 3/4  c. Pepperidge Farm herb stuffing
1 1/4  c. dry bread crumbs
1/2  c. margarine, melted
1 (10 oz.)  can cream of celery soup
1 1/2  c. boned chicken, packed
1  c. cooked peas (canned peas must be rinsed)
1  tsp. minced onion
  dash of pepper

      Mix stuffing, bread crumbs and margarine thoroughly; reserve 3/4 cup of topping.  Pack bread mixture in bottom of 2-quart casserole.  Mix remaining ingredients and pour on top of bread mixture.  Add reserved crumbs on top.  Bake in hot oven (425 degrees;) until hot and bubbly with crumbs browned.  Set 10 minutes before cutting into 6 squares.
