SPICED SWEET POTATOES


2  c. sliced sweet potatoes
  brown sugar substitute to equal 3 Tbsp. brown sugar
1/4  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. cinnamon
1/4  tsp. nutmeg
3  Tbsp. reduced calorie margarine

      Arrange sweet potatoes in nonstick casserole.  Combine sugar substitute, salt, cinnamon, and nutmeg; sprinkle over sweet potatoes.  Dot with margarine.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 10 to 15 minutes and serve.
