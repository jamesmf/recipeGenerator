CALICO SALAD


1/2  c. brown sugar
1/3  c. cider vinegar
2  tsp. celery seed
1/4  tsp. round turmeric
  Salt and pepper to taste
2  Tbsp. chopped onion
1 (16 oz.)  can cut green beans, drained
1 (16 oz.)  can sliced carrots, drained
1 (16 oz.)  can whole kernel corn, drained

      Combine brown sugar, vinegar, celery seed, turmeric and salt and pepper mix well.
      Add onion and vegies, stir gently to coat and chill overnight. Makes 6 servings.
