QUICK HAMBURGER CASSEROLE


1  lb. ground beef
1/4  c. chopped onion
1/4  tsp. salt
  pepper to taste
1 (16 oz.)  can mixed vegetables, drained
1 (10 1/2 oz.)  can tomato soup
1 (4 oz.)  can mushrooms (optional)

      Brown ground beef and onion; drain.  Add remaining ingredients and mix well.  Pour into 1 1/2-quart casserole; bake at 400 degrees; for 15 minutes.  Serve with hot biscuits.  Yield:  4 to 6 servings.
