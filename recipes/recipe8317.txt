CHICKEN CHILI


1  chicken, cut up
1  large onion
2  cans kidney or chili beans
2  cans tomatoes, diced
1  can chicken broth
1/2  jar salsa (chunky; medium, mild or hot) to taste

	Saute onions. Cook chicken until no longer pink. Add remaining ingredients. Simmer or place in crock-pot until hot.
