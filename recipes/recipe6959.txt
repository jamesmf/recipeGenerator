RICE DRESSING


1  lb. hamburger meat
1/2  pkg. Jimmy Dean sausage
1  large bell pepper, chopped
1  large onion, chopped
1  tsp. salt
2  tsp. black pepper
1  tsp. red pepper
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  can creamy onion soup
1 1/2  c. raw rice

      Mix together. Put in 9 x 13 inch baking pan. Cover with foil and bake at 350 degrees; for 1 1/2 hours.
