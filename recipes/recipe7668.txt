MACARONI AND CHEESE


3  Tbsp. oleo
2 1/2  c. uncooked macaroni
1  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
1/2  lb. Velveeta cheese, chunked
1  qt. milk

      Melt butter in baking dish.  Pour  macaroni in and stir until oleo coats it.  Add salt, pepper,  cheese and cold milk. Bake, uncovered, at 325 degrees; for 1 1/2 hours.  Do not stir.
