CARAMEL CREAM SANDWICH COOKIES


1  c. butter or margarine
3/4  c. firmly packed brown sugar
1  unbeaten egg yolk
2 1/4  c. sifted flour

      Cream butter and gradually add brown sugar; cream well.  Blend in egg yolk, then add flour.  Stir until mixture forms a dough. Chill if necessary.  Shape into balls size of a marble.  Place on ungreased baking sheets and flatten to 1/8-inch thickness with palm of hand.  Make a design by pressing with fork in one direction. Bake at 325 degrees; for 8 to 10 minutes until cookies begin to brown.  Remove from sheet while warm.  Place flat sides of 2 cookies together with frosting.
Brown Butter Frosting:
2  Tbsp. butter or margarine
1 1/4  c. sifted powdered sugar
1/2  tsp. vanilla
4 to 5  tsp. cream

      Slightly brown butter or margarine in saucepan.  Remove from heat and blend in powdered sugar.  Gradually add vanilla and cream until spreading consistency.
