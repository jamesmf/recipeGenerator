SOUTHWESTERN SAUSAGE AND CHEESE CASSEROLE


2 (7 oz.) or 4 (4 oz.)  cans chopped green chilies
6  corn tortillas, cut into 1/2-inch strips
1  lb. hot Italian sausage, cooked and drained
2  c. shredded Monterey Jack cheese
1/2  c. milk
8  large eggs
1/2  tsp. each: salt, garlic, onion, cumin and pepper
2  large ripe tomatoes, sliced
  light sour cream
  salsa

      Make the day before.
      Grease 9 x 13-inch pan. Layer half the chilies, half the tortilla strips, half the sausage and half the cheese. Repeat. In a bowl with a fork, beat eggs, milk and dry ingredients. Pour over layers. Arrange tomatoes on top. Sprinkle with paprika. Cover and refrigerate overnight. Bake at 350 degrees; for 45 to 55 minutes.
