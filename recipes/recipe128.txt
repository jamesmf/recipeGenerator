CASHEW COOKIES


2  c. flour
1  tsp. baking powder
1  tsp. baking soda
1/4  tsp. salt
1/2  c. margarine
1  c. brown sugar
1  egg
1/2  tsp. vanilla
1/2  c. sour cream
1/2  c. cashew nuts

 
Icing:
4  Tbsp. margarine
4  Tbsp. milk
2  c. powdered sugar
2  tsp. vanilla

      Sift flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt; set aside. Cream margarine and brown sugar.  Add egg and vanilla.  Then gradually add flour mixture alternately with sour cream.  Add nuts. Drop by teaspoons on cookie sheet.  Bake at 375 degrees; for 10 minutes.
      Icing:  Combine all ingredients.  Spread on cooled cookies.
