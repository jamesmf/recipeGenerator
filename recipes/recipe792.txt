TURKEY WALDORF SALAD


1  medium unpared red apple, cored and diced (1 1/4 c.)
1  Tbsp. lemon juice
2  c. coarsely diced, cooked turkey
1  c. halved and seeded red grapes
1  c. sliced celery
1/4  c. broken walnuts
1/4  c. finely chopped onion
2/3  c. mayonnaise or salad dressing
1/2  tsp. salt
  dash of pepper
  lettuce
1  hard-cooked egg

      Chill ingredients.  Sprinkle diced apple with lemon juice.  Add turkey, grapes, celery, walnuts, onion, salad dressing, salt and pepper and toss lightly.  Serve in lettuce-lined bowl.  Cut white of egg into wedges and arrange in shape of a flower.  Top with grated yolk.  Makes 5 to 6 servings.
