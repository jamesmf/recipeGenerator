HOT CHICKEN SALAD
(Makes 12 Servings)  

2  c. cooked chicken breast halves, cubed
2  c. celery, chopped
1/2  c. slivered almonds, toasted
1/4  c. onion, grated
2  Tbsp. lemon juice
1/2  tsp. salt
1  c. mayonnaise
1/2  c. Cheddar cheese, grated
1  c. potato chips, crushed

      Combine everything except cheese and potato chips. Put in greased baking dish. Sprinkle cheese on top, then chips. Bake 20 minutes at 450 degrees;.
       Double the recipe for a 9 x 13 pan.
