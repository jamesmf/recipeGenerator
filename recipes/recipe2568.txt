CHOW MEIN


3  lb. hamburger, salted and browned
4  c. celery
3  large onions, chopped
3/4  c. soy sauce
1 1/2  c. uncooked rice
3  cans cream of mushroom soup
3  cans cream of chicken soup
4  c. warm water
3  cans Chinese noodles

      Brown and salt hamburger.  Add remaining ingredients.  Mix and bake 1 hour at 350 degrees;.  Sprinkle Chinese noodles over top and bake an additional 15 minutes.
