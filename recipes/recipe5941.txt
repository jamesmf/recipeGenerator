FRUIT SALAD


1 large  can pineapple chunks
1  can mandarin oranges
1 medium  bottle cherries, cut in half
3  bananas, sliced
1  c. white grapes, cut in half
1 small  can sliced peaches
2  c. juice
2 small  pkg. vanilla instant pudding mix

      Drain all juice and use enough juice to make 2 cups. Mix juice with pudding mix and beat with mixer until thick. Fold in fruit and mix well. Chill before serving.
