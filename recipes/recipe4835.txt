MANDARIN CHICKEN SALAD AND DRESSING

The Greens:
  iceberg
  romaine
  baby spinach

 
The Fixin's:
  water chestnuts
  pea pods
  bacon
  diced red pepper
  mandarin orange segments

 
The Toppings:
  sliced almonds
1/4  c. toasted sesame seeds
  fried wontons

 
The Grilled Chicken Marinade:
1/2  c. soy sauce
4  sliced green onions
1  lb. boneless skinless chicken
1/4  c. orange juice
1 medium  clove garlic, minced

      Marinate 1 to 24 hours (the longer the time, the more flavor).
The Dressing:
1/3  c. sugar
2 1/2  tsp. onion juice (shred and strain through a fine mesh strainer)
1  tsp. soy sauce
  salt to taste
2 1/2  tsp. toasted sesame seeds
3  Tbsp. cider vinegar
1/2  tsp. dry mustard
1/2  c. olive oil

      Mix the first 6 ingredients with an electric mixer set on medium.  While mixing, slowly drizzle in the olive oil.  Beat until thickened. Add toasted sesame seeds and let stand for 5 to 10 minutes.
      Just before serving, toss together greens, chicken and fixin's with the dressing.  Top with almonds, toasted sesame seeds and fried wontons.  Enjoy!
