FIVE WAY CHILI


2  lb. ground beef
3  c. water
1 (15 oz.)  can tomato sauce
2  bay leaves
1/2  tsp. salt
2  tsp. ground cumin
1  tsp. ground cinnamon
1  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1 1/2  tsp. vinegar
1 1/2  tsp. ground allspice
2  Tbsp. chili powder

      Lightly brown beef. Add water and mix. Add all other ingredients. Simmer, uncovered, 3 hours. Stir occasionally or cook in crock-pot.
       Serve with all or none of the following: shredded Cheddar cheese, chopped onions, chopped jalapeno peppers. You may add chili beans. Also can be served over spaghetti.
