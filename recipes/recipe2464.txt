OLD FASHIONED BREAD PUDDING


6  slices white bread, crusts removed
2  Tbsp. reduced-calorie margarine, melted
3/4  c. sugar substitute, divided
1  tsp. ground cinnamon
1/2  c. raisins
  vegetable cooking spray
4  eggs, lightly beaten
2  c. skim milk
1  tsp. vanilla extract

      Brush bread lightly with melted margarine.  Sprinkle evenly with one-third of the sugar substitute and cinnamon.  Cut bread slices into quarters.  Layer bread and raisins in a 1 1/2-quart casserole coated with cooking spray.
      Combine eggs, milk, vanilla and remaining two-thirds of sugar substitute.  Pour over bread and raisin dish.  Place dish in a baking pan containing 1-inch of hot water.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 55 minutes to one hour or until knife inserted in center comes out clean.  Serve warm or chilled.  Yield:  8 servings.
