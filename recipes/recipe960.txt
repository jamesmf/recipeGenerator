ASPARAGUS SOUP


1  can (approximately 1 lb.) asparagus
  bouillon cubes or powder to make 4 c.
1  qt. boiling water
  dash of onion powder
  pinch of powdered mace

      Put asparagus pieces and liquid in blender and blend smooth. In saucepan, combine bouillon and boiling water; add asparagus. Add seasonings.  Bring to a boil.  Serve hot with a dot of unsalted butter on each cup or soup plate.
