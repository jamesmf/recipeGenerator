CHICKEN CASSEROLE


1  can potato soup
1  c. milk
2  c. cooked chicken, cubed
1  c. cheddar cheese
1  c. Durkee French Fried Onions
1  4  oz. pkg. crescent rolls
1  16  oz. pkg. vegetables (cauliflower, carrots, broccoli)

      Mix chicken, vegetables, 1/2 c. cheese, 1/2 c. onions, soup and milk.  Bake at 375 degrees; for 20 mins.  Place crescent rolls in strips.  Bake 15 mins. longer.  Sprinkle remaining cheese and onions on top.  Bake 3-5 mins. longer.
