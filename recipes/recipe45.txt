CHOCOLATE PARFAITS


1/4  c. cocoa
3  Tbsp. cornstarch
1/4  c. sugar
1/4  tsp. salt
1/2  c. cold milk
2 1/2  c. hot milk, scalded
1  tsp. vanilla
1  c. heavy cream, whipped or 1 pkg. prepared topping mix, whipped

      In 2-quart saucepan, combine cocoa, cornstarch, sugar, salt and cold milk.  Stir until smooth.  Gradually add hot milk, stirring constantly.  Heat over medium-low heat.  Stir constantly until mixture thickens, about 5 minutes.  Cool; stir in vanilla.  Place layers of pudding and whipped cream or topping mixture in parfait glasses to serve.  Makes 6 servings.
