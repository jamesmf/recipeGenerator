CHOCOLATE CARROT CAKE


2  c. sugar
4  eggs
1 1/2  c. oil
2  c. flour
2  tsp. soda
2  tsp. cocoa
1/2  tsp. salt
3  c. grated carrots
1  c. chopped nuts

      Beat sugar, eggs and oil.  Add dry ingredients, then carrots and nuts, mixing well.  Bake in greased 9 x 13-inch pan at 350 degrees; for about 40 minutes.  Frost when cool.
Cream Cheese Frosting:
1 (3 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
1  Tbsp. milk
1  tsp. vanilla
1/2  tsp. salt
2 1/2  c. confectioners sugar

      Blend cream cheese, milk, vanilla and salt.  Gradually add sugar; beat until smooth.  Add additional milk, 1 teaspoon at a time, if necessary.
