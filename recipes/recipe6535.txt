SHEPHERD'S PIE


3  Tbsp. oil
1 1/2  lb. hamburger meat
1  onion, finely chopped
1  large carrot, finely chopped
1  beef stock cube
1/2  tsp. mixed herbs
  seasoning to taste
2  sticks celery, finely chopped
2  green onions (shallots), chopped
1 1/2  lb. potatoes, cooked and drained
1  oz. butter or margarine
1/4  c. milk
1  egg

      Heat oil in a large pan and saut meat, onions and carrot over a high heat until golden brown.  Add crumbled stock cube, mixed herbs, seasoning, celery and green onions (shallots).  Simmer for 5 minutes.  Spoon into greased ovenproof casserole.  Mash potatoes with butter and milk and spoon onto meat.  Brush with lightly beaten egg and bake at 400 degrees; for 30 minutes.  Serves 4 to 6.
