MACARONI & CHEESE


12  oz. macaroni
12  oz. mild cheddar cheese, grated
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1 small  carton sour cream
1  c. mayonnaise
1 small  jar pimento
1  small medium chopped onion
1 small  box of Cheese-It crackers

      Cook macaroni.  Do not drain completely.  Butter 9 x 12 dish. Mix all ingredients except crackers.  Crush whole box of Cheese-It crackers and sprinkle on top.  Dot with butter.  Bake at 325 degrees; for 30-35 minutes or until crackers are brown.
