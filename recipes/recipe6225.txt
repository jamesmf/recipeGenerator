QUICK GREEK SALAD


1  head lettuce, torn
2  tomatoes, sliced
12  ripe or Greek olives
1  cucumber, sliced
1/2  lb. cubed Feta cheese

      Mix ingredients.  Sprinkle liberally with oregano and freshly ground black pepper.  Toss with a dressing of 1 part lemon juice and 2 parts good olive oil.
