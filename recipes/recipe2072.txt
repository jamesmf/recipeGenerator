CARAMEL APPLES


2  c. honey
3/4  c. evaporated milk
1  tsp. vanilla
3  Tbsp. butter
  pinch of salt
6  to 8 large apples

      Mix honey and evaporated milk together.  Cook, stirring constantly, to firm-ball stage or to 255 degrees; on a candy thermometer. Stir in vanilla, butter and salt.  Dip apples into caramel.  Spoon excess from bottom.  Place on greased cookie sheet.  Chill until caramel is firm.
