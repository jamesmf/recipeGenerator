GINA'S BREAKFAST CASSEROLE


1  can Crescent Rolls
1  lb. Sausage
1  c. Cheddar Cheese, shredded
6  Eggs

      Unroll rolls and lay flat in baking dish.  Brown sausage and sprinkle over rolls.  Sprinkle cheese over sausage.  Beat eggs and pour over top.  Bake at 350 degrees for 20-25 minutes or until lightly browned.
