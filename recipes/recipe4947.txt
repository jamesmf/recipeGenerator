THAI CHICKEN SALAD


4  boneless skinless chicken breasts
1/2  c. bean sprouts
2  green bell peppers, finely chopped
1  bag mixed salad greens
  Dressing

      Poach chicken in salted water until opaque.  Drain and shred into pieces.  Toss dressing with chicken.  Add sprouts and peppers; serve over mixed greens.  Serves 4 to 6.
Dressing:
1 1/2  tsp. olive oil
1  lime (juice only)
2  tsp. sugar
1  tsp. chili powder
2  Tbsp. Teriyaki sauce
1  Tbsp. water
3  Tbsp. rice wine vinegar
2  Tbsp. fresh ground ginger
4  scallions, chopped
1/2  c. fresh basil leaves, chopped
1/2  c. fresh mint leaves, chopped

      Combine all ingredients and mix thoroughly.
