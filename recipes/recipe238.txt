DANISH COOKIES #2


1/2  c. butter
1/2  c. lard
1  c. sugar
1  egg
1  tsp. vanilla flavoring
2  c. flour
1/2  tsp. baking soda
1/2  tsp. cream of tartar

      Cream butter and lard.  Add sugar and cream together thoroughly.  Add egg and vanilla.  Mix dry ingredients and sift in a little at a time.  Roll into small balls and flatten with fork. Bake in 350 degrees; oven until slightly brown.  Makes 48 cookies.
