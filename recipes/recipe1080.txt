VEGETABLE SOUP


1  c. meat stock or bouillon cube
1/2  c. mixed vegetables with carrots and peas
1/2  small onion, chopped
1/4  c. cabbage, shredded
1  stalk celery, diced
1/4  c. tomato juice
  salt and pepper

      Prepare vegetables and add to broth.  Boil together until vegetables are just tender for 20 minutes.  Makes 4 servings, each 1 cup.
