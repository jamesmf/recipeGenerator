TURKEY SOUP


1  Tbsp. butter
1  large sweet onion, diced (about 1 1/2 c.)
1  garlic clove, minced
1  celery rib, diced
2 1/2  qt. chicken broth
3  c. cubed cooked turkey
1  c. frozen green peas
3  carrots, peeled and sliced
2  medium potatoes, peeled and diced
1  tsp. pepper

      Melt butter in a large 8-quart stockpot.  Add onion, garlic, and celery; saut√© over medium-high heat 3 minutes.  Add chicken broth and next 5 ingredients.  Bring to boil; reduce heat, and simmer at least 1 hour, stirring occasionally.  Makes about 4 quarts.
