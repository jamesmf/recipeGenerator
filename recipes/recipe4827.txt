3 BEAN SALAD


1 (No. 2)  can green beans, drained
1 (No. 2)  can yellow beans, drained
1 (No. 2)  can kidney beans, rinsed
1  medium onion
1  medium green pepper
1/2  c. oil
3/4  c. sugar
1/2  c. vinegar
1/2  tsp. salt

      Mix oil, sugar, vinegar and salt.  Pour liquids over beans and let marinate overnight.
