LYNDA'S CHICKEN STEW


2  qt. water (or enough to cover chicken)
1  chicken
3  potatoes
42  oz. (1 large and 1 small can) tomatoes, whole
14  oz. (1 can) cream corn
1  small onion, chopped
15  oz. (1 can) English peas
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  tsp. lemon juice
1/2  c. (1 stick) margarine
1  c. ketchup

      Put water in a pan.  Add chicken and gently boil until tender.  Remove chicken from water and cool.
      While chicken is cooling, peel and dice potatoes.  Put potatoes in the chicken water (broth) and cook until tender.
      Debone and cut cooked chicken into pieces.
      Leave 3 cups of the chicken broth and the potatoes in the pan. Add the rest of the ingredients and mix together.
      Simmer 20 to 30 minutes.
