LASAGNA


1  lb. hamburger
1  medium onion, chopped
1/8  tsp. garlic powder
2 1/2  c. tomatoes
1 (8 oz.)  can tomato sauce
1  Tbsp. parsley
1  tsp. oregano
  salt and pepper to taste
1/3  c. water
9  lasagna noodles (or 8 oz.)
2/3  lb. Mozzarella cheese, thinly sliced
6  Tbsp. Parmesan cheese
1/2  lb. Velveeta cheese, sliced
8  oz. cottage cheese

      Brown  hamburger and onion in  large  saucepan;  drain. Add tomatoes, sauce, parsley, oregano,  salt,  pepper,  garlic powder and water.  Simmer for 20  minutes.  Cook  noodles  in water, salted, for 15 minutes.  Drain and lay in single layers until dry. Arrange half of noodles, 1/3  of  sauce,  half  of Mozzarella cheese,  2 tablespoons Parmesan  cheese  and  half Velveeta cheese and half of cottage cheese in  a  9  x 13-inch pan.  Repeat layers.  Cover with  remaining sauce and Parmesan cheese.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 to 35 minutes.
