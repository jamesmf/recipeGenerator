MACARONI SEAFOOD SALAD


2  c. macaroni
1 large  can shrimp or 1 1/2 lb. fresh shrimp*
1 1/2  c. chopped celery
1  Tbsp. chopped pimento
1/4  tsp. black pepper
1/2  c. diced pineapple
3  hard-boiled eggs
1  c. mayonnaise
  sliced stuffed olives
1 large  can green peas (optional)

      Cook macaroni; drain and chill.  Combine remaining ingredients. If fresh shrimp are used, boil 15 minutes, then blanch in cold water and remove shells and center black line.
      *Can substitute tuna or salmon.
