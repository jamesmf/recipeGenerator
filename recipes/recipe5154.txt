LAYERED SALAD


1  head lettuce, torn into small pieces
1  c. diced celery
4  hard boiled eggs, sliced
1 (10 oz.)  pkg. frozen green peas, thawed and uncooked
1/2  green pepper, diced
1  medium onion, diced
8  slices bacon, cooked and crumbled
2  c. mayonnaise
1 to 2  Tbsp. sugar
4  oz. cheddar cheese, shredded

      Layer first 7 ingredients in order given.  Combine mayonnaise and sugar.  Spread evenly over top of salad.  Sprinkle with cheese.  Cover and refrigerate 8 to 12 hours.  Garnish with parsley before serving.
