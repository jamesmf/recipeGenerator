GRAPE SALAD


  bunch of red grapes
  bunch of fresh broccoli, chopped
1  c. celery, chopped
1/2  c. young onions
2/3  c. slivered almonds
3/4  c. raisins
6  or 7 strips cooked crisp bacon, crumbled
1  c. mayonnaise
1/2  c. sugar
1  Tbsp. vinegar

      Combine mayonnaise, sugar and vinegar for dressing; refrigerate.  Combine rest of ingredients and refrigerate until serving.  Just before serving, add dressing to salad mixture and mix well.
