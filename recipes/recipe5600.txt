CORNBREAD SALAD


1  box Jiffy Cornbread Mix
4  med. Tomatoes
9  slices crisp Bacon
1  Bell Pepper
1  Onion, chopped
1/2  c. Pickle Relish
1/4  c. Pickle Juice
1  c. Mayonnaise

      Cook cornbread as directed on package and crumble.  Add chopped tomatoes, bacon, bell pepper, onion and pickle relish to cornbread.  Mix mayonnaise and pickle juice.  Put one-half of cornbread mixture in dish and cover with one half of mayonnaise mixture.  Put other half of cornbread mixture and top with remaining mayonnaise mixture.
       Refrigerate.
