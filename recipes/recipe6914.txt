BROCCOLI, HAM AND CHEESE CASSEROLE


2  pkg. frozen chopped broccoli
2  c. cut up fully cooked ham
1 (6 oz.)  pkg. Cheddar cheese
1  c. Bisquick
3  c. milk
4  eggs

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees;. Cook broccoli; drain and spread in ungreased 9 x 13 casserole. Layer ham, then cheese. Beat remaining ingredients together and pour over casserole. Bake 1 hour until knife comes out clean. Let stand 10 minutes before cutting.
