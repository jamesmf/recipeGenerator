TOMATO RELISH


24  large tomatoes, quartered
6  to 8 onions, diced
6  to 8 bell peppers, diced
6  hot peppers
5  c. vinegar
3  c. sugar
3  Tbsp. salt

 
For Rag Bag:
2  tsp. mustard seed
1  Tbsp. cloves
1  Tbsp. celery seed
4  bay leaves
2  tsp. pickling spice

      Make a rag bag.  Put the mustard seed, cloves, celery seed, bay leaves and pickling spice in the bag.  Put the bag and all other ingredients into large saucepan and cook on medium heat until thick.  May take up to 2 hours.
