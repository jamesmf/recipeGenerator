PECAN PIE


3/4  c. Karo syrup (light)
3  eggs, well beaten
1/2  c. sugar
2  Tbsp. flour
1/2  stick butter, melted
1  tsp. vanilla
1  c. nuts
  pinch of salt

      Mix flour and sugar together; add Karo syrup and eggs.  Add melted butter and vanilla.  Stir in nuts.  Pour into unbaked pie shell.  Bake at 425 degrees; for 15 minutes, then at 375 degrees; for 30 minutes.
