CHICKEN AND CHEESE CRESCENTS


3  c. cooked chicken
1/2  c. grated cheese
1  can crescent rolls
1  c. milk (evaporated)
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  c. grated cheese

	Flatten out each crescent roll. Fill wide end of each triangle with chicken and grated cheese. Roll up each crescent and pinch together. Place in 8-inch greased casserole dish. In a saucepan over medium heat, combine soup, milk and 1 cup grated cheese. Heat, stirring constantly, until cheese melts. Pour over crescents and bake at 375 degrees; for 20 minutes or until golden brown.
