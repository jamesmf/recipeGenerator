SURPRISE RASPBERRY JAM


5  C. chopped and peeled fresh tomatoes
4  C. sugar
1  Tbsp. lemon juice
2  pkgs. (3 oz. size) raspberry Jell-o

      In a large saucepan combine first 3 ingredients.  Cook and stir over high heat until mixture comes to a boil.   Reduce heat and simmer, uncovered, for 25 minutes.  Remove from heat.  Skim off foam if necessary.
      Stir in gelatin until completely dissolved.  Pour into jars.
      Makes about 7-eight oz. jars.  Cool to room temperature, about 1 hour.  Cover and let stand for 3 hours or until set, but not more than 24 hours.
      Place in refrigerator.  DO NOT FREEZE.  Lasts 3 weeks or longer in refrigerator.
