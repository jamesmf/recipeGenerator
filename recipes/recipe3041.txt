FIESTA BAKED CHEESE SPREAD


2 (8 oz.)  pkg. Philadelphia cream cheese, softened
1  (8 oz.) four cheese Mexican style shredded cheese
1 (4 oz.)  can chopped green chilies
1 1/4  c. sour cream, divided
1/4 to 1/2  tsp. ground red pepper

      Beat cream cheese in large mixing bowl with electric mixer on medium speed to soften.  Reserve 1/4 cup shredded cheese for garnish.  Mix remaining shredded cheese, chilies, 1/2 cup sour cream and ground pepper into cream cheese.  Spoon into 10-inch pie plate or quiche dish.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 20 minutes or until lightly browned around the edge.  Spread remaining 3/4 cup sour cream over baked cheese spread.  Sprinkle with reserved 1/4 cup shredded cheese.  Garnish with bell pepper cut-outs.  Serve with tortilla chips.
