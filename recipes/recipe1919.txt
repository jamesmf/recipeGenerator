CARNE ASADA
(Mexican Steak)  

1  lb. beef flank steak
1/2  c. lime juice or lemon juice
1  tsp. chili powder
1  tsp. dried garlic or 4 cloves, chopped (fresh)
1  Tbsp. dried oregano

      Combine ingredients in a plastic bag placed in a bowl to catch drips.  Refrigerate 24 to 48 hours.  Remove steak from marinade. Broil 2 to 9-inches from heat source about 5 minutes each side. Slice very thinly against the grain.  Makes 4 servings under 170 calories each.
