SWISH MEAT BALLS


1  lb. ground beef
1/2  c. onion
3/4  c. bread crumbs
1  Tbsp. parsley
2  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper
1  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  egg
1/2  c. milk

	Mix together above ingredients; cover and refrigerate 2 hours. Make balls and place on cookie sheet. Bake at 350 degrees; for 20 to 25 minutes. Drain on paper towels.
Sauce:
1/4  c. oil
1/4  c. flour
1  tsp. paprika
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper
2  c. water
3/4  c. sour cream

	In a skillet, put oil, flour, paprika, salt and pepper; bring to a boil. Remove from heat and add water. Put back on stove and bring to a boil. Lower heat and add sour cream. Add balls in sauce and cook about 5 minutes. Remove and put in crock-pot on low for 45 minutes to 1 hour.
