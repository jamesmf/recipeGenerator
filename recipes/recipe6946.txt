QUICK HOMEMADE CHILI


1  lb. lean ground beef
1/2  c. chopped onion
1/2  clove garlic, minced
1/2  medium green pepper, chopped
1 (15 oz.)  can chili beans (undrained)
2 (8 oz.)  cans Hunt's tomato sauce
1  tsp. salt
1 to 2  tsp. chili powder

      Lightly brown beef in skillet. Add onion and garlic; cook until tender. Add remaining ingredients. Simmer, stirring occasionally for 45 minutes. Makes 4 servings.
