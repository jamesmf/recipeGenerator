TEEN MIX


4  slices bacon, chopped up
1  small onion, chopped
1  lb. hamburger
2  lb. baked beans (undrained)
1 (12 oz.)  can tomatoes (undrained)
1  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  Tbsp. sugar
  salt and pepper to taste
  pkg. hamburger buns

	Cook bacon and onion. Add hamburger and cook until brown. Add remainder of ingredients. Cover and simmer over low heat for 20 minutes. Stir. Serve on a hamburger bun.
