FRUITY CHICKEN SALAD


1 large  can chicken
1  c. grapes
1 small  can mandarin oranges
1  large apple
1 small  can crushed pineapple
1/2  c. almonds or pecans
4 large  Tbsp. Miracle Whip salad dressing
  salt and pepper to taste

      Cut everything into bite size pieces. Mix in salad dressing. Chill and serve.
