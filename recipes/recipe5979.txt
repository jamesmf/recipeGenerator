BROCCOLI SALAD


1  bunch broccoli, stems removed
8  slices bacon, fried and crumbled
1  c. cheddar cheese, grated
1/2  purple onion, diced

      Cut broccoli florets into bite-sized pieces. Add all cheese, bacon and onion.  Mix dressing.
Dressing:
1/2  c. mayonnaise
1/4  c. sugar
1  Tbsp. vinegar

      Do not put dressing on salad until ready to use.
