MY MIRACLE SALAD


1  envelope Knox plain gelatine
1  pkg. lemon Jell-O
1 large  can fruit cocktail
1  container Cool Whip
2/3  c. chopped pecans
1/4  c. cold water
1  c. boiling water
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
1/2  tsp. salt

      Soften Knox gelatine in cold water.  Dissolve the Jell-O in boiling water.  Combine gelatine and Jell-O.  Allow to cool.  Add fruit cocktail.  Soften cheese with Cool Whip.  Add all other ingredients.  Mix well.  Refrigerate to congeal.  Serve on lettuce with mayonnaise.
