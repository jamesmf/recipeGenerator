SPICY SHRIMP CASSEROLE


2  lb. unpeeled, medium shrimp
3  c. long grain and wild rice mixture, cooked
1  c. (4 oz.) shredded Cheddar cheese
1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can cream of mushroom soup
1  Tbsp. butter
1/2  c. chopped green onions
2  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/2  tsp. dry mustard
1/4  c. milk
1  tsp. Cajun seasoning
1/2  tsp. ground pepper

      Peel shrimp; devein if desired.  Place cooked rice in a bowl. Add shrimp, cheese and soup to rice.  Melt butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat and add green onions.  Cook, stirring constantly, until tender.  Stir green onions, Worcestershire sauce and next 4 ingredients into rice mixture. Spoon into lightly greased 11 x 7 x 1 1/2-inch (or 2-quart) baking dish.  Bake casserole at 375 degrees; for 45 minutes.  Yield:  6 servings.
