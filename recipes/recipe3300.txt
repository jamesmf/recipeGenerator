CUCUMBER SANDWICHES


2  large cucumbers
1/2  onion, grated
2  Tbsp. vinegar
3  Tbsp. mayonnaise
8  oz. cream cheese
  salt to taste
  pepper to taste

      Peel cucumbers and cut lengthwise, taking out seeds. Grate cucumbers and onions. Add salt and pepper. Add vinegar and let set 30 minutes. Put mixture in colander and drain all liquid. Add cream cheese and mayonnaise; mix well.
