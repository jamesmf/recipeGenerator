WATERGATE SALAD


1  box pistachio pudding
2  c. mini marshmallows
1  c. coconut
1  can fruit cocktail
1  can pineapple chunks
2 (8 oz.)  cartons Cool Whip
1  c. pecans

	Mix pineapple, coconut, marshmallows and pecans together. Fold in Cool Whip. Sprinkle the dry pudding over folded mixture. Fold in.
