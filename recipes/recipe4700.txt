TAMMY'S HOT DOG SAUCE


10  lb. hamburg
4  Tbsp. cumin
4  Tbsp. oregano
8  Tbsp. cinnamon
8  Tbsp. paprika
8  Tbsp. chili powder
2  Tbsp. ground cloves
  salt and pepper to taste
4  medium onions, chopped

      Brown onions in pan with a little vegetable oil.  Add hamburg; brown.  When brown, add spices and 2 quarts water. Simmer for 3 hours.  Stir occasionally.  Can be frozen or canned. Yields a lot!
