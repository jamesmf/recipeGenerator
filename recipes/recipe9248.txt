CHICKEN DRESSING CASSEROLE


4  to 6 chicken breasts
1/2  pt. sour cream
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  can cream of celery or cream of mushroom soup
1  stick margarine
1  c. chicken broth
1  small bag Pepperidge Farm dressing mix

      Boil chicken in unsalted water; debone.  Line bottom of greased casserole dish with chicken.  Mix sour cream and soups together and spread over chicken.  Melt margarine in 1 cup of hot broth and mix with stuffing mix.  Spread over soup mixture and bake at 350 degrees; until hot and soup mixture is bubbly.
