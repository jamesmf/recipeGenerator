EASY POTATO SOUP


2  c. diced potatoes
1/2  c. chopped onion
3  Tbsp. butter
1/2  c. water
1 1/2  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper
2  c. milk
1  Tbsp. chopped parsley

      In saucepan, melt butter.  Add onion, potatoes, water, salt and pepper.  Cover and cook slowly until potatoes are very soft. Mash the potatoes with masher until fairly smooth.  Add milk; continue cooking until soup comes to a boil.  Add parsley.  Makes 2 servings.
