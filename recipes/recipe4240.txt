SPICY MEAT BALLS


2  lb. hamburger
1  c. corn flakes
2  eggs
2  Tbsp. soy sauce
1/4  tsp. pepper
1/2  tsp. garlic salt
1/3  c. ketchup
2  Tbsp. instant onion flakes

 
Sauce:
1  can jellied cranberry sauce
12  oz. chili sauce
2  Tbsp. brown sugar
1  Tbsp. lemon juice

      Mix hamburger and next 7 ingredients and form into balls. Place in a 9 x 13 baking dish. Mix together sauce ingredients and heat thoroughly. Pour over meat. Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour.
