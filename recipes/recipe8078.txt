GLAZED SPARERIBS


1/2  c. ketchup
1/4  c. vinegar
2  Tbsp brown sugar
2  Tbsp Worcestershire
1  tsp. celery seed
1/2  tsp. chili powder
  Dash of pepper
2 to 3  drops of hot sauce
2  lb. spareribs
  Lemon slices*

	Combine first 8 ingredients in a saucepan: bring to a boil. Remove from heat and set aside.  Cut ribs into serving size pieces; place bone side up in a shallow pan.  Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees; for 1 hour.  Baste ribs with sauce; turn over and bake 15 minutes.  Baste with sauce; turn again and bake 15 minutes.  Makes 3 to 4 servings.
	*Arrange lemon slice over ribs for last 30 minutes if desired.
