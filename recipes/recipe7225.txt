ONE AND ONE CASSEROLE


1  lb. lean ground meat
1 (1 3/4 oz.)  pkg. dry onion soup mix
1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can condensed mushroom soup
1  c. water
1  c. uncooked rice
1  c. sliced green onions

      Crumble ground meat in a buttered 2 1/2-quart casserole dish. Sprinkle with rice and soup mix. Blend mushroom soup and water. Pour over rice. Spread onion tops over mixture. Cover and bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour. Makes 6 servings.
