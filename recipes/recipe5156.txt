PFISTER SALAD


1 head or  bunch of Romaine lettuce
1/4  head cauliflower
1/4  lb. of bacon
1/2  red onion

      Clean and dry lettuce, cauliflower and onion.  Tear lettuce into pieces.  Slice cauliflower and onion thin. Fry bacon and crumble or slice thin and then fry.  Mix lettuce, cauliflower, bacon and onion and set aside.
Dressing:
1  c. Miracle Whip
1/4  c. parmesan cheese
2  Tbsp. sugar

      Mix dressing ingredients.  Pour over lettuce mixture and toss lightly.
