SPAGHETTI WITH CLAM SAUCE


1  lb. uncooked spaghetti or linguine
2  Tbsp. vegetable or olive oil
2 to 4  cloves garlic, minced
2 (6.5 oz.)  cans chopped clams, drained (liquid reserved)
1/2  c. chopped fresh parsley
1/2  c. dry white wine
1  tsp. dried basil
1/4  tsp. white pepper

      Cook pasta according to package directions; drain. In a medium skillet, heat oil; add garlic. Stir in reserved liquid from clams and parsley; cook and stir 3 minutes. Add clams, wine, basil and pepper. Simmer on low heat for 5 minutes. Pour sauce over pasta and serve immediately.
