STACK SALAD


1  head lettuce, shredded
1  green pepper, sliced
1/2  red onion, sliced
1  can sliced ripe olives
1  can LeSueur green peas, drained
1  can garbanzo beans or chickpeas, drained

      Put these ingredients in a tall clear bowl in order given. Cover with dressing (recipe to follow). Make sure dressing touches all sides of bowl. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese over top. Crumble bacon over top.
Dressing:
1 1/2  c. Hellmann's mayonnaise
1 1/2  c. sour cream
1  pkg. Ranch-style dressing mix

      Mix all together and put on top of salad.
