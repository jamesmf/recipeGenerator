SHOE PEG SALAD


1 small  can peas, drained
1 large  can Shoepeg corn, drained
1  bunch green onions, chopped
1  bell pepper, chopped
1 large  jar pimentos, drained

	In a large bowl, combine the ingredients.
Dressing:
1/2  c. oil
1/2  c. sugar
3/4  c. vinegar
  salt and pepper to taste

	In a saucepan, mix together the ingredients and bring to a boil.  Cool the mixture and pour over vegetables. Refrigerate overnight.
