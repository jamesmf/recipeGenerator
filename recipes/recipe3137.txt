CREAMY TACOS


1  can Rotel tomatoes
1  can (or 1 pt.) tomatoes
1  can Ranch Style Beans
1 small  can Pet evaporated milk
1  lb. Velveeta cheese
1  lb. ground meat
1  pkg. chili mix

      Blend both cans of tomatoes in blender or food processor. In a large pot, stir together tomatoes, beans, Pet milk, and cheese.
      In a skillet, brown ground meat; drain. Add chili mix. Add meat mixture to tomato mixture. Cook until cheese is melted. Serve over Doritos, Fritos, or your favorite chips.
