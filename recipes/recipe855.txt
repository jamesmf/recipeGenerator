CHICKEN TACOS


2  tsp. vegetable oil
1/2  c. sliced onion
2  garlic cloves, minced
1  medium tomato, chopped
1/2  c. tomato sauce
  dash of hot sauce
  dash of oregano leaves
  dash of salt
  dash of pepper
4  oz. skinned, boned and cooked chicken, diced
2  taco shells
1  oz. Cheddar cheese, shredded
  lettuce

      In a 9 or 10-inch skillet, heat oil.  Add onion and garlic and saut√© until onion is translucent, about 5 minutes.  Add tomato, tomato sauce and seasonings.  Cook, stirring occasionally, for about 5 minutes.  Add chicken and cook until heated through. Divide chicken mixture into taco shells, then top with cheese and lettuce.
