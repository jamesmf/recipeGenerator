CREAMED CHICKEN AND FETTUCCINE


2  whole chicken breasts
1  egg
  all-purpose flour
  salt and pepper
  butter or margarine
1/4  lb. sliced fresh mushrooms
1 1/2  c. milk
2  Tbsp. dry sherry
1/4  c. grated Parmesan cheese
6  oz. fettuccine, cooked
1  Tbsp. chopped fresh parsley

      Cut each chicken breast in half. Beat egg lightly in a small bowl. Combine flour, 1/2 teaspoon salt and 1/8 teaspoon pepper on wax paper. Dip chicken in egg. Dredge in flour mixture. Melt 2 tablespoons butter in a 10-inch skillet over medium-high heat. Add chicken and cook 10 minutes or until tender. Remove to a platter. Keep warm. Add 2 tablespoons butter to drippings in skillet. Add mushrooms and cook over medium heat until tender. Stir in 2 tablespoons flour, 1/2 teaspoon salt and 1/8 teaspoon pepper. Cook, stirring constantly, 1 minute. Stir in milk and sherry. Cook, stirring constantly, until mixture thickens. Stir in cheese until melted.  Remove 1 cup sauce. Add pasta to sauce in skillet, tossing to coat. Arrange on platter with chicken. Pour remaining 1 cup sauce over chicken. Sprinkle pasta with parsley.
