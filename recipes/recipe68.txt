VELVEETA FUDGE


1  (8 oz.)  Velveeta cheese
1  c. butter
1/2  c. cocoa
2  tsp. vanilla
2  lb. powdered sugar
1  c. nuts (optional)

      Heat cheese and butter on low heat until melted or microwave for 3 to 4 minutes.  Add cocoa, vanilla, powdered sugar and nuts. Mix well.  Mixture will be very thick.  Press into 9 x 13-inch pan. Chill.  No cooking and very delicious.
