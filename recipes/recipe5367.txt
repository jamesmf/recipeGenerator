BAKED POTATO SOUP


4  bacon strips, cooked and crumbled
2/3  c. butter or margarine
2/3  c. flour
7  c. milk
4  green onions, diced
4  large baking potatoes, baked, peeled and cubed
1 1/2  c. cheese, shredded
1  c. sour cream
3/4  tsp. salt (to taste)
1/4  tsp. white pepper (to taste)

	Make a roux of butter, flour and milk. Add potatoes and onions. Bring to a boil. Simmer 10 minutes. Add bacon, sour cream, cheese, salt and pepper.
