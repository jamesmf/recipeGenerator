SALAD DRESSING


1  c. sugar
1  c. ketchup
1 1/2  tsp. celery seed
1 1/2  tsp. paprika
1 1/2  tsp. salt
1 1/2  tsp. minced onion
1/2  c. vinegar
1 1/2  c. oil (canola)

      Beat slowly all but vinegar and oil.  Pour oil and vinegar very slowly.  Stir or shake.  Can be stored in refrigerator or not.
