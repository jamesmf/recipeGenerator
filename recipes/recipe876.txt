BEER FISH BATTER


1 1/2  c. flour
1  c. Italian bread crumbs
3  eggs
3  tsp. baking powder
1  tsp. lemon pepper seasoning
1  can stale or flat beer

      Combine all ingredients.  If batter is too thick, use water to thin.  Dip fish in batter and deep fry in hot oil.  Delicious with all vegetables:  broccoli, peppers, parsley, mushrooms, onions, etc.
