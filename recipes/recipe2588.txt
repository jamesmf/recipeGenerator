SOUTHERN PARTY POTATOES


2  lb. frozen hash browns
1/2  c. melted butter
1  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
1/2  c. chopped onion
1  can cream of chicken or cream of celery soup
1  carton sour cream
1 small  jar Cheez Whiz or 2 c. grated cheese
1/4  c. melted butter
2  c. crushed corn flakes

      Defrost potatoes.  Combine butter, salt, pepper, onion, soup, sour cream and cheese.  Mix thoroughly.  Pour over potatoes in 3-quart casserole.  Mix 1/4 cup butter and corn flakes; sprinkle over top.  Cover and bake at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes.  Serves 16.
