GLORIFIED RICE


1  c. Minute white rice (uncooked)
1 (16 oz.)  can fruit cocktail, drained
1 (8 1/4 oz.)  can crushed pineapple, drained
1  c. miniature marshmallows
1  c. thawed Cool Whip

      Prepare rice as directed on package.  Cool.  Add fruit and marshmallows.  Mix lightly.  Stir in Cool Whip.  Serve immediately.  Serves 8.
