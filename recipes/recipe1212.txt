CHOCOLATE CHIP COOKIES


1  c. flour
1/2  tsp. baking soda
1/4  tsp. salt
1/2  c. butter
4  tsp. liquid sugar substitute
1/2  tsp. vanilla
1  egg, beaten
1/2  c. semi-sweet chocolate pieces

      Sift together dry ingredients.  Cream butter.  Add sugar substitute, vanilla and egg, blending well.  Add flour mixture. Beat well.  Stir in chocolate pieces.  Drop by level teaspoonfuls onto lightly greased baking sheet.  Bake 8 to 10 minutes in a 375 degrees; oven.  Each cookie is 48 calories.
