SLOPPY JOES


2  lb. ground hamburger
12  sliced hamburger buns, buttered
2 (10 1/2 oz.)  cans tomato soup
2  Tbsp. prepared mustard
1/2  tsp. salt

      Wrap buns in aluminum foil and heat in a 325 degrees; oven for 15 minutes. Brown beef in skillet. Stir in soup, mustard and salt. Simmer over low heat 10 minutes, stirring occasionally. Spoon into warm buns.
