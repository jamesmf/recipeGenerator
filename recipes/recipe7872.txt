CHICKEN SHASLIK

Chicken:
4  chicken breasts, cleaned and cut into chunks
1  tsp. each, ginger and garlic paste
1-1/2  tsp. salt
1-1/2  tsp. cayenne pepper
2  tsp. paprika
1  Tbsp. lemon juice
1  Tbsp. olive oil
2  medium onions, cut into large chunks
1  large green pepper, cut into large chunks
1  pint whole cherry tomatoes
2  cans chunk pineapple, juice drained and reserved
  Wood or metal skewers

 
Sauce:
2 (8 oz.)  cans tomato sauce
1/4  cup vegetable oil
1-1/2  tsp. salt
1  tsp. black pepper
1/2  tsp. cayenne pepper
1/8  cup vinegar
4  Tbsp. soy sauce
1/2  cup fried onions, crushed
1/2  cup of reserved pineapple juice

      Marinate chicken with ginger, garlic, salt, cayenne pepper, paprika, lemon juice, and olive oil for 1/2 hour.  Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees;F.
      Thread the chicken cubes alternately with onion, green pepper, tomato, and pineapple chunks.  Place each skewer into a deep pyrex dish.
      Mix the sauce ingredients together and stir until well combined.  Pour sauce over the skewers and cover tightly with foil.  Bake in hot oven for 40-45 minutes, making sure that chicken is cooked thoroughly.
      Serve with hot rice.
