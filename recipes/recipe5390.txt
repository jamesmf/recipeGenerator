BROCCOLI SALAD II


4  c. broccoli, washed, drained and coarsely chopped
1/2  c. dry roasted peanuts, salted
1/2  c. raisins
1  Tbsp. minced onion
1  c. mayonnaise
1/2  c. sugar
1/4  c. red wine vinegar

	Mix the last 3 ingredients 1/2 hour before serving. Mix with broccoli and other ingredients. Refrigerate.
	Louise mixes broccoli, 1 pound cooked bacon, 8 ounces shredded sharp Cheddar cheese, 1 medium red onion, mayonnaise, sugar and 2 tablespoons white vinegar.
