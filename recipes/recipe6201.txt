TOMATO SOUP


12  qt. tomatoes
6  medium onions
1  celery
1  bay leaf
1 1/2  sticks margarine
1  c. flour
1/2  c. sugar
2  Tbsp. salt
1  tsp. black pepper

	Boil first 4 ingredients  together until tender. Strain our juice. Melt margarine. Add flour, sugar, salt and pepper. Pour juice into pot. Add thicken. Let come to good boil. Put in jar and seal.
