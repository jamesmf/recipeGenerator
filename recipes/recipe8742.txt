BAKE AND BREADED CHICKEN


  2-3 lb. chicken legs and thighs
1  8  oz. Zesty Italian Salad Dressing
1/2  c. grated Parmesan
2  c. Italian flavored bread crumbs

      Mix cheese and bread crumbs.  Dip chicken in salad dressing and coat with crumbs. Let stand for 10-15 mins. Place on foil-lined cookie sheet.  Bake 375 degrees; for 50-60 mins.
