REUBEN CHICKEN


4  chicken breast halves, skinned and boned
16  oz. can sauerkraut
  slices of Swiss cheese
  Thousand Island dressing

      Arrange chicken in greased baking dish.  Drain sauerkraut and spread over all.  You may not need all of it.
      Arrange slices of Swiss cheese on top and pour Thousand Island dressing over all.
      Bake 1 hour at 325 degrees.
