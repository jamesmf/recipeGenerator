SWISS CHEESE AND CRAB QUICHE


4  oz. natural Swiss cheese, grated
1  (9-inch) pie shell
2  green onions, sliced, with tops
1 (7 1/2 oz.)  can crab meat
3  beaten eggs
1  c. light cream
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. grated lemon peel
1/4  tsp. dry mustard
1/4  c. sliced almonds

      Preheat oven to 325 degrees;.  Arrange cheese evenly over bottom of pie shell.  Sprinkle in onions then crab meat.  Combine eggs, cream, salt, lemon peel and dry mustard; pour evenly over crab meat.  Top with sliced almonds and a sprinkle of cheese.  Cook in slow 325 degrees; oven for 45 minutes or until set.  Let stand 10 minutes.  Serves 6.
