MINNEAPOLIS COOKIES


2  c. brown sugar
1  c. soft shortening
2  eggs
2  c. flour
1  tsp. baking powder
1  tsp. soda
1  tsp. vanilla
1  c. coconut
6  oz. chocolate chips
2  c. quick cooking oatmeal
3/4  c. nuts

      Combine all ingredients.  Shape into balls and bake at 325 degrees; for 10 minutes.
