COTTAGE-STYLE PORK SPARERIBS


5  lb. cottage spareribs
3  Tbsp. salt
3  qt. water

 
Barbecue Sauce:
1/4  c. mustard
1/4  c. oil
1/4  c. vinegar
1/2  c. catsup
2  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1 1/2  tsp. sugar
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper

      Boil ribs, salt and water 30 minutes to 1 hour or until meat is tender; let stay in water at least 1 hour.
      Barbecue Sauce: Combine mustard, oil and vinegar in a bowl. Add catsup, Worcestershire sauce, sugar, salt and pepper. Blend all ingredients.
      Take ribs from water; pat dry and lay on grate to broil. Use a pastry brush and cover ribs with Barbecue Sauce. Broil until light brown. Turn ribs over and brush other side with sauce; broil until light brown.
