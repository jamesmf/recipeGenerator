CHICKEN SALAD


3  chicken breasts
3  hard boiled eggs
1/4 to 1/2  cup sweet pickles, chopped
1/4  cup celery, chopped
  Mayonnaise
  olives

      Cook chicken until tender.  Debone and chop.  Add chopped eggs, pickles and celery.  Add mayonnaise to desired spreading consistency.  Spread on favorite bread and top with olive.
