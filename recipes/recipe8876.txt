GREEN CHILI CASSEROLE


1  can green chili
1  doz. tortillas (corn)
1 1/2  lb. Longhorn cheese
1  medium onion
1  can mushroom or chicken soup
1 soup  can milk
1  small boned chicken

      Fry tortillas until hot and bubbling. Alternate in layers: tortillas, cheese, chili, chicken and onion. Pour soup and milk over top. Place in lightly greased casserole bowl. Cook at 350 degrees; for 30 to 40 minutes, until bubbling. Feeds 6 cowboys or 8 city slickers.
       Can use other meats or red chili sauce for variety. Great to cook up, seal and freeze.
