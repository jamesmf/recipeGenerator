DANISH PUFF

Lower Crust:
1/2  c. butter or margarine
1  c. all-purpose flour
2  Tbsp. water

 
Next Layer:
1/2  c. butter or oleo
1  c. water
1  tsp. almond flavor
1  c. flour
3  eggs

      Crust:  Cut butter or margarine into the flour.  Sprinkle with water and work dough into a ball.  Pat 1 1/2 teaspoons dough in 3-inch circles.  Put on a baking sheet.
      Next Layer:  Heat water and butter or margarine to a rolling boil in a medium saucepan.  Remove from heat and quickly stir in 1 teaspoon almond flavor and 1 cup flour.  Stir vigorously over low heat for 1 minute.  Remove from heat and beat in 3 eggs (one at a time) until smooth and glossy.  Spread 1/2 tablespoon on each circle.  Bake 45 minutes at 350 degrees;.  Cool and frost.
Frosting for Danish Puffs:
1 1/2  c. confectioners sugar
2  Tbsp. butter
1 to 2  Tbsp. warm water
1/2  tsp. vanilla

      Beat until smooth and of spreading consistency.  Makes enough for 2 dozen.
