TURKEY CHIP SALAD


2  c. diced, cooked turkey or chicken
1  c. chopped celery
2/3  c. mayonnaise or salad dressing
2  Tbsp. chopped canned pimento
2  tsp. prepared mustard
1/2  tsp. ground ginger
1/4  tsp. salt
  dash of pepper
1/2  c. coconut chips, toasted

      Combine first 8 ingredients.  Chill until serving time.  Stir in coconut chips.  Pile on salad greens and garnish with additional chips.  Makes 6 servings.
