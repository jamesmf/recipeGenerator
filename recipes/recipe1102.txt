LIME SALAD


2 (3 oz.)  boxes lime jello
1 (20 oz.)  can unsweetened crushed pineapple, drain (save juice)
2  c. boiling water
1  c. cold water
1  c. pineapple juice
1  c. pineapple
1  c. cottage cheese
1  c. Miracle Whip

      Put jello in large container.  Add boiling water and stir 2 minutes.  Add cold water and pineapple juice.  Stir and put in refrigerator.  Just before it is solid, add pineapple, cottage cheese and Miracle Whip.  Mix this with beater just until well blended, then put back in refrigerator until solid.
