OVEN FRIED FISH


2  lb. fresh or frozen cod or tilapia fish
2  Tbsp. canola oil
1/2  c. seasoned, fine, dry bread crumbs
1/3  c. yellow cornmeal
1  tsp. seasoning salt
2  Tbsp. Parmesan cheese
1  slightly beaten egg white

      Coat baking pan with canola oil.  Set aside.  Thaw fish if frozen.  Rinse and dry with paper towels.  Cut into 4-inch pieces.
      Preheat oven to 425 degrees;.  Heat baking sheet.  Stir together bread crumbs, cornmeal, salt and Parmesan cheese. Mix well.  Dip each fillet in egg white, then the crumb mixture.  Place fish on preheated baking sheet.  Bake, uncovered, 6 to 8 minutes (depending on thickness of fish) or until fish flakes easily.
