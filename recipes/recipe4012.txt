PICKLED BEETS AND EGGS


2  cans (14 oz.) small whole beets
1  c. cider vinegar
1/4  c. sugar
1  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. ground cloves
1  medium onion, sliced
12  hard boiled eggs

      Drain beets; reserve 1 cup juice.  In saucepan combine vinegar, sugar, salt, cloves, onion and beet juice.  Bring to boil, stirring occasionally.  Add beets and simmer covered 10 minutes.  Shell eggs and place in a gallon jar.  Pour in beets and juice; stir gently.  Cool and refrigerate covered at least 24 hours.  Makes 12 servings.
