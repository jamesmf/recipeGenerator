CHICKEN NUGGETS


3  Tbsp. butter, melted
2  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
2  chicken breasts, skinned, boned and cut into 1-inch pieces
50  wheat wafers, crushed
1/3  c. Parmesan cheese

      Combine butter and Worcestershire sauce. Add chicken and toss to coat. In plastic bag, put crackers and cheese. Add chicken. Shake to coat. Put chicken in pan. Bake at 450 degrees; for 7 minutes.
