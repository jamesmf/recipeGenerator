THE BEST VEGETABLE DIP
(You can cut this recipe in half.)  

1  qt. Hellmann's mayonnaise (only use Hellmann's)
2  pt. sour cream (4 c.)
5  Tbsp. dill weed
6  Tbsp. grated onions
2  Tbsp. dried parsley
3  Tbsp. Lawry's seasoned salt

      Mix thoroughly. Refrigerate 1 hour before serving. Serve with broccoli, carrots, cauliflower, bell pepper, radishes, celery and green onions.
