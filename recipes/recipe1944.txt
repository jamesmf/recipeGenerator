CHUPE DE MARISCOS
(Shellfish)  

  shrimp, mussels, clams or lobster (reserve liquid)
1/2  Tbsp. onion, finely chopped
2  Tbsp. butter
1 1/2  c. soft bread crumbs
1  c. milk
4  Tbsp. soft cheese
1  Tbsp. butter
2  egg yolks
  salt, pepper and cayenne
  water
  paprika

      Boil various shellfish for 30 minutes and reserve the water in which they were cooked.  Fry onion in 2 tablespoons of butter and paprika.  Add bread crumbs, milk and a little of the liquid from the shellfish.  Simmer until the consistency of sauce.  Add soft cheese, 1 tablespoon butter, egg yolks, salt, pepper and cayenne. Place the shellfish in the bottom of a clay casserole.  Pour the sauce over the top and bake, covered, in a moderate oven for 40 minutes.  Place uncovered under the broiler for the last 10 minutes.  If desired, top with hard-boiled eggs and mashed potatoes.
