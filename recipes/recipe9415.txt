PICANTE CHICKEN WITH BROWN RICE


3/4  lb. boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into strips
1 1/2  tsp. chili powder
1  tsp. ground cumin
1/4  c. cold water
1/2  c. sliced green onions
2  cloves garlic, chopped fine
1 14  oz. can whole tomatoes, undrained and cut up
1  c. frozen corn kernels
3/4  c. uncooked instant brown rice
2  Tbsp. chopped green chilies, undrained
1/2  c. water

      Heat in a nonstick skillet; add the chicken and sprinkle with chili powder and cumin.  Add 1/4 cup water, the green onions, and garlic.  Saut√© until the chicken is no longer pink  Stir in the tomatoes, corn, rice, chilies, and 1/2 cup water.  Bring to a boil.  Reduce the heat, cover, and simmer 5 to 10 minutes, or until the rice is done.
