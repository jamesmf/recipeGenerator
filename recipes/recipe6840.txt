ITALIAN PASTA BAKE


1  lb. ground beef
3  c. penne or rotini pasta, cooked and drained
1 (26 to 28 oz.)  jar spaghetti sauce
3/4  c. grated Parmesan cheese, divided
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. shredded Mozzarella cheese

      Brown meat in large skillet; drain. Stir in pasta, sauce and 1/2 cup of Parmesan. Spoon into 13 x 9 baking dish. Top with Mozzarella cheese and remaining 1/4 cup Parmesan. Bake at 375 degrees; for 20 minutes. Serves 6.
