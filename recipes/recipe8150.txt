


3  lb. ground beef
2  green peppers
2  cloves garlic
1/4  c. cooking oil
3 (15 oz.)  cans kidney beans
3  cans tomatoes
3 or 4  Tbsp. chili powder
2  Tbsp. cumin
1/4  tsp. Tabasco
1  c. water

      Cook ground beef, peppers and garlic in 1/4 cup cooking oil until beef is no longer pink. Add rest of ingredients and simmer.
