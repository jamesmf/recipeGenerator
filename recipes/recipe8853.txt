HASH, MONDAY


2  c. cooked meat, cubed (left over roast)
3  c. raw potatoes, diced
1  c. raw carrots, diced
1  small onion, sliced
1  c. gravy (can be made from bouillon cubes)
1/4  c. water
1  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
1/2  tsp. Worcestershire sauce

      Mix ingredients; press gently into a greased casserole and bake at 350 degrees; for about 1 hour, until vegetables are tender.
