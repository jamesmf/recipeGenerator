TUNA AND BROCCOLI BAKE


1 (16 oz.)  pkg. frozen broccoli cuts, thawed and well drained
2  slices bread, cut in 1/2-inch cubes
1 (12 oz.)  can Star-Kist solid white or chunk light tuna, drained and chunked
3  eggs
2  c. cottage cheese
1  c. shredded Cheddar cheese
1/4  tsp. ground black pepper

      Place broccoli on bottom of 2-quart baking dish.  Top with bread cubes and tuna.  In medium bowl, combine eggs, cottage cheese, Cheddar cheese and pepper.  Spread evenly over tuna mixture.  Bake in 400 degrees; oven 30 minutes or until golden brown and puffed.
