FRITTER FRIED OKRA


1  c. all-purpose flour
1  Tbsp. baking powder
1/2  tsp. salt
2  eggs, well beaten
1/3  c. milk
5  c. thinly sliced okra
  hot vegetable oil

      Combine flour, baking powder and salt in a medium mixing bowl.  Add eggs and milk, beating until smooth.  Add okra, stirring until coated.  Spoon okra into hot oil in a large skillet.  Cook, stirring occasionally, until golden brown.  Can also be deep fried.  Yield: about 6 servings.
