


2  c. coffee creamer
2  c. powdered milk
2  c. sugar
1  c. cocoa
1/4  c. instant coffee

      Blend all ingredients.  Store in airtight jar.  To use, place 2 rounded teaspoonfuls in a cup of boiling water.
