ORANGE SLICE COOKIES


1  c. white sugar
1  c. brown sugar
1  c. shortening
2  eggs, beaten
1  tsp. vanilla
1  tsp. milk
2  c. flour
1  c. quick oatmeal
1  tsp. baking soda
1/8  tsp. salt
1  lb. orange slice candy, cut and floured

      Cream white and brown sugars.  Cut shortening in.  Add beaten eggs, vanilla and milk.  Add flour, oatmeal, baking soda and salt. Stir in orange slice pieces.  Drop by teaspoonful onto cookie sheet.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 10 to 12 minutes.
