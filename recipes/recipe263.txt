POTATO CHIP OR PRETZEL COOKIES


1  c. butter
1/2  c. sugar
1  tsp. vanilla
1  c. crushed chips or pretzels
1/2  c. crushed pecans or walnuts
2  c. flour

      Cream together butter, sugar and vanilla.  Add remaining ingredients.  Stir well by hand.  Take 1 teaspoon of dough and roll into balls; press down with fork.  Bake at 350 degrees; until lightly browned.
