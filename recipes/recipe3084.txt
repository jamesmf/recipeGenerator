PARTY MIX


1/2  c. butter or margarine
1 1/2  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  Tbsp. garlic
1/2  Tbsp. seasoned salt
1  c. mixed nuts
2  c. Bran Chex cereal
2  c. Corn Chex cereal
2  c. Cheerios
2  c. pretzels

      Preheat oven to 250 degrees;. Heat butter or margarine in a 9 x 13-inch baking pan in oven until melted. Remove. Stir in garlic, seasoned salt and Worcestershire sauce. Add cereals, nuts and pretzels. Mix until all pieces are coated. Heat in oven 1 hour. Stir every 15 minutes. Spread on paper towels to cool. Makes 9 cups.
