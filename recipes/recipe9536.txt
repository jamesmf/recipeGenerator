ARTICHOKE AND SHRIMP CASSEROLE


1  can artichoke hearts (not marinated)
1  lb. cooked shrimp (do not overcook)
4  Tbsp. butter
1/4  lb. mushrooms
2  Tbsp. flour
1 1/2  c. milk
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/4  c. sherry
  salt and pepper
1/2  c. Parmesan cheese, grated

      Drain and slice artichokes and arrange in buttered baking dish.  Saut√© sliced mushrooms for about 6 minutes.  Add to baking dish.  Spread cooked shrimp over the mushroom and artichoke mixture.
      Make a cream sauce of flour, butter and milk. Add Worcestershire sauce, sherry, salt and pepper to sauce.  Pour over the mixture in baking dish. Sprinkle top with Parmesan cheese and paprika. Bake at 375 degrees; for 25 minutes. Sprinkle with fresh parsley before serving.
      This is easy and can be doubled easily.
