CHERRY COKE SALAD


1 large  pkg. cherry jello
2  c. hot boiling water
4  ice cubes
1  can crushed pineapple, drained
1/2  c. chopped pecans
1 small  jar maraschino cherries, chopped
1  king size Coke

      Mix jello and boiling water. Add the ice cubes and mix well. Add the pineapple, pecans, cherries and Coke. Stir and mix well. Congeal in icebox.
