CHICKEN DIVAN


2  c. cooked chicken breast
1  bunch cooked broccoli
1  can cream of chicken soup (can use Healthy Choice or Healthy Request)
1/2  c. fat-free mayonnaise
1/4  c. lemon juice
1/2  tsp. curry powder
  salt and pepper to taste
2/3  c. grated cheese
2/3  c. bread crumbs

      Precook chicken breast and broccoli. Place broccoli in casserole dish; layer chicken over it. Mix remaining ingredients and pour over chicken. Bake in 350 degrees; oven until cheese melts. Serves 6.
      Serving Size: 1/2 cup. Cal-241, CHO-19g, Pro-25g, Fat-7g.
