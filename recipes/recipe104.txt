APPLE COOKIES


1 1/3  c. brown sugar
1/2  c. butter
1  egg
1/4  c. milk
2  c. flour
1/2  tsp. salt
1  tsp. soda
1  tsp. cinnamon
1/2  tsp. nutmeg
1  c. finely chopped apples
1  c. chopped nuts

      Cream sugar and butter; add egg and milk.  Mix in dry ingredients.  Stir in apples and nuts.  Drop onto slightly greased cookie sheet.  Bake at 375 degrees; for 10 minutes or until done.
