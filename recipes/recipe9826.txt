EGGPLANT PARMESAN



1  eggplant
1  egg, beaten
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. grated Parmesan or Mozzarella cheese
  salt
  pepper
  vegetable oil
  flour

      Heat oil in deep skillet or fryer.  Cut eggplant into thin slices.  Dip in egg.  Dip in flour.  Place slices into hot oil and cook until lightly browned.  Remove from oil and drain.  Sprinkle with salt and pepper, then cheese.
