POPOVERS


1  c. flour
1/2  tsp. salt
2  eggs
1  c. skim milk

      Sift flour and salt together; set aside.  Beat eggs and skim milk; add to flour.  Beat until smooth and creamy.  Pour into heated greased muffin tins, filling half full or less.  Bake at 375 degrees; for 40 to 50 minutes.  Do NOT open oven for first 30 minutes.
