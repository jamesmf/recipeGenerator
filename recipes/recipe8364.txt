TUNA SALAD CRESCENT-WICHES


2 (6 1/2 oz.)  cans tuna, drained and flaked
1/2  c. dill pickles, finely chopped
1/2  c. salad dressing or mayonnaise
8 (4 x 4-inch)  slices American cheese
1/2  c. celery, finely chopped
1/2  c. chopped water chestnuts, finely chopped (optional)
2 (8 oz.)  cans Pillsbury crescent dinner rolls
  paprika (optional)

	Heat oven to 375 degrees;. Combine tuna, pickles, salad dressing, celery and water chestnuts. Separate crescent dough into 8 rectangles; firmly press perforations to seal. Spoon about 1/2 cup tuna mixture on one end of rectangle. Fold dough in half over filling. Press edges with a fork to seal. Bake at 375 degrees; on ungreased cookie sheet for 15 to 18 minutes or until light and golden brown. Remove from oven and top each with cheese slice. Sprinkle with paprika. Return to oven 3 to 5 minutes or until cheese softens. Serve immediately. Makes 8 sandwiches.
	To Make Ahead: Prepare, cover and refrigerate up to 2 hours. Bake as directed.
	To Reheat: Wrap loosely in foil and heat at 350 degrees; for 15 to 18 minutes.
