CROCK-POT POT ROAST


2 to 2 1/2  lb. pot roast
  garlic salt
  dried herbs
  cut up carrots
1  c. beef broth
  salt and pepper
8  to 10 small potatoes
1  yellow onion, chopped

      Coat pot roast with a little vegetable oil. Rub in salt, pepper and herbs. Put pot roast in bottom of crock-pot and place vegetables around it. Add the beef broth and cover the pot. Cook for 8 to 10 hours on  low.
