SALMON PATTIES


1  can pink salmon
2/3  c. crackers or enough to hold patties together
1  finely chopped medium onion
1/2  tsp. pepper
1/4  tsp. salt (optional)
  vegetable oil
1  egg

      Take bone out of salmon. In large mixing bowl, combine salmon, crackers, egg, onion, salt and pepper; mix well. Mixture will be moist. Shape into patties. In deep skillet, fry at 350 degrees in 1/2 inch of vegetable oil until golden brown on both sides.
