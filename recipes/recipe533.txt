ICE CREAM PIE


1  qt. ice cream
1  Keebler Ready-Crust chocolate flavored pie crust
1 (12 oz.)  jar Smucker's chocolate fudge topping
  whipped cream
  maraschino cherries
  nuts or walnuts

      Allow ice cream to soften.  Spoon over the Ready-Crust.  Cover and freeze until firm, about 3 hours.  Serve pie wedges with topping, whipped cream, cherries and nuts (decorate as desired). Oreo cookie "shells" crushed may be substituted for Ready-Crust.
