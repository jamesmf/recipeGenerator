APRICOT NOUGAT


16  oz. powdered sugar, sifted
6  Tbsp. butter, melted
2  Tbsp. orange juice
1/2  tsp. vanilla
11  oz. dried apricots, ground
1  c. nuts, finely chopped

      Combine powdered sugar, butter, orange juice and vanilla.  Add apricots and knead mixture.  Shape into balls and roll in nuts. Store in refrigerator or freezer.
