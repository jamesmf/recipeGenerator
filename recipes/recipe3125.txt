OVEN CARAMEL CORN


2  c. brown sugar
1  tsp. salt
2  sticks butter
6  qt. popped corn
1/2  c. corn syrup
1  tsp. butter flavoring
1  tsp. baking soda
1  c. nuts

      Boil brown sugar, butter, syrup, and salt 5 minutes. Remove from heat; add baking soda and butter flavoring. Pour and stir over popcorn and nuts. Place in 200 degrees; oven for 1 hour. Remove and cool completely. Store in covered container to keep crisp.
