SPONGE COOKIES


3  eggs
3/4  c. sugar
1  c. flour
1/8  tsp. salt
1  tsp. baking powder

      Beat the eggs until very light; add the sugar and beat again. Fold in gently the flour, salt and baking powder sifted together. Drop by teaspoonfuls on greased pans, some distance apart.  Bake in 350 degrees; oven until set.
