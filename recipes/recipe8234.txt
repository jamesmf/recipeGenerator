CHUCK ROAST AND GRAVY


5  lb. chuck roast
2  cans cream of mushroom
1  can tomatoes, diced
1 to 2  Tbsp. garlic powder
1 1/2  tsp. salt
1  tsp. black pepper
2 1/2  c. water
2  medium onions
1  pkg. Lipton onion soup mix

	Preheat oven to 350 degrees;. In large mixing bowl, blend mushroom soup, onion soup packet, tomatoes, spices and water together. Place chuck roast in a covered baking dish. Pour ingredients over the roast. Cut onion into quarters and add to the pan along the sides of the roast. Place lid on pan. Place in oven. Cook for 3 1/2 to 4 hours or until tender.
	Mashed potatoes and country green beans go very well with this dinner.
