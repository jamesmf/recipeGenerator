OATMEAL COOKIES


1/2  c. butter or margarine
1  tsp. vanilla
4  tsp. sweetener
1  egg
3/4  c. cold water
1  c. flour
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. baking soda
1  tsp. cinnamon
1  c. quick oatmeal
1/2  c. raisins
1/2  c. chopped nuts

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees;.  Combine butter, vanilla, sweetener, egg and cold water.  Beat, then add the rest of the ingredients.  Mix well and drop by spoonfuls on a greased cookie sheet.  Bake 8 to 10 minutes.
