GOULASCH


1  lb. beef tips
1  lb. pork stew meat
1  large onion, chopped
  salt and pepper to taste
  paprika
  dash of cayenne pepper

      Brown meat. Add chopped onion and seasonings. Let simmer until meat is tender. Thicken with a little flour mixed with water. Serve over boiled potatoes, noodles or dumplings.
