MAKE AHEAD LETTUCE SALAD


1  head iceberg lettuce, torn
1  large onion, chopped
1/2  c. celery, chopped
1/2  c. green pepper, chopped
8  oz. (1 can) sliced water chestnuts (drained)
10  oz. (1 pkg.) peas (frozen), thawed
1  c. mayonnaise
1  Tbsp. sugar
1/3  c. Parmesan cheese, shredded
4  strips bacon, cooked

      In a 6-quart bowl, layer half of the lettuce, onion, celery, green pepper, water chestnuts and peas.
      Combine mayonnaise and sugar.  Spread half over the salad.
      Repeat layers.  Top with cheese and bacon.
