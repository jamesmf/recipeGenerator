ORANGE GLAZED PORK CHOPS


  Pork chops
1  (6 oz.) Stove Top stuffing mix
1/4  c. margarine
1-1/2  c. hot water
1  can Mandarin oranges, drained & chopped
1/3  c. orange marmalade
1  tsp. Dijon mustard

      Stir contents of mix, 1-1/2 cups hot water, oranges and 1/4 cup margarine until mixture is moistened and set aside.  Spoon into a 8 x 12 baking dish - top with chops.  Mix marmalade and mustard, spread on chops and bake at 375 degrees for 45 minutes or until chops are cooked thru.  Serves 4.
