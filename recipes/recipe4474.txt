KNOEPHLA SOUP


6  peeled and diced potatoes
2  onions, chopped
1  carrot, chopped
1/4  c. chicken bouillon
1  c. celery, chopped
1/8  Tbsp. chives
1  Tbsp. parsley flakes
5  c. water
1/2  c. butter
1/8  tsp. pepper
1 (13 oz.)  can evaporated milk or 1/2 c. cream

      Put all ingredients except milk and chives in a kettle and cook until tender. Add milk and chives and bring to a boil. Add Knoepla, then reduce heat to simmer for 10 minutes.
Knoephla:
1  c. flour
1  egg
1/2  c. water

      Mix together. Cut into small pieces and drop into broth.
