FUDGE ROLL


3  c. graham cracker crumbs
1 (12 oz.)  can evaporated milk
1/8  tsp. salt
3  Tbsp. cocoa
1  Tbsp. liquid sweetener or equivalent amount of Sweet 'N Low
1  tsp. vanilla

      Combine milk and cocoa in top of double boiler; blend, then add sweetener and salt.  Heat to boiling point, then add vanilla.  Pour this mixture over the cracker crumbs and mix; shape into two rolls. Roll in small amount of the graham cracker crumbs, reserved from original 3 cups or roll in flaked coconut.  Chill two hours or overnight, then slice into 1/4-inch slices.  Nuts and coconut can be added to fudge for variety.
