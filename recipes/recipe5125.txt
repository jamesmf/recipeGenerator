POTATO SOUP


2  c. leftover mashed potatoes
2  c. milk
1  Tbsp. butter or margarine
1  small onion, minced
  salt and pepper to taste
1  Tbsp. dried parsley flakes

      Combine all ingredients in a large saucepan. Heat over low heat until the mixture reaches the boiling point. Sprinkle top with dried parsley flakes. Serves 4.
