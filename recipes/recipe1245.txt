EGGNOG


1  egg
1/3  c. nonfat dry milk
1/2  tsp. rum flavoring
1/2  tsp. vanilla
  artificial sweetener to equal 8 tsp. sugar
  dash of nutmeg
4  ice cubes

      Blend ingredients, except ice, on lowest speed of electric blender.  Add ice cubes, one at a time, while machine is running. Blend until smooth.
