BARBECUED BEEF SANDWICHES


1  1/2 lbs. ground beef
3/4  c. chopped onion
1 (8 oz.)  can tomato sauce
1/2  c. finely chopped green pepper
1/4  cup ketchup
1 1/2  tsp. salt
2  Tbsp. brown sugar
2  Tbsp. barbecue sauce
2  Tbsp. vinegar
1  Tbsp. prepared mustard
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/4  tsp. pepper

      Brown meat in large skillet; drain. Add onion and green pepper. Cook 5 minutes. Add remaining ingredients. Cover and simmer for 1 hour. Serve on buns.
