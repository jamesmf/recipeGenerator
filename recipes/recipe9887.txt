SPINACH SUPREME


1  pkg. frozen cut leaf spinach
1  can cream of mushroom soup (undiluted)
1/4  tsp. pepper
1/8  tsp. garlic salt
1  tsp. sesame seeds
1/2  c. sliced almonds

      Prepare spinach according to package directions; drain. In a large bowl, mix spinach, soup, pepper, salt and sesame seeds. Pour mixture into a buttered 2-quart casserole dish; top with almonds. Bake 20 minutes at 350 degrees;.
