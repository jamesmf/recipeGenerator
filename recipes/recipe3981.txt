PARMESAN CHEESE BITES


1  c. all-purpose flour
2/3  c. grated parmesan cheese
1/4  tsp. ground red pepper
1/2  c. butter or margarine, cut up

      Combine flour, cheese and red pepper; cut in butter with a pastry blender until crumbly, but still dry.  Gently press mixture together with hands, working until blended and smooth, 2 to 3 minutes.  Shape into 2 (4 inch long) logs.  (Or roll into a 10 x 7 inch rectangle, 1/4 inch thick, on a lightly floured surface. Cut lengthwise into 8 strips and crosswise into 4 strips, forming 32 squares.)  Wrap in plastic wrap, chill 8 hours or freeze.  Freeze up to 3 months, if desired.  Thaw overnight in refrigerator. Preheat oven to 350 degrees;.  Slice logs 1/4 inch thick.  Bake 12 to 15 minutes on ungreased baking sheets until lightly browned.  Yield: 32 appetizers.
