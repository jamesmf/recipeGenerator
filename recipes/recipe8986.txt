BARBECUED MEATBALLS


1  lb. hamburger
1  c. bread crumbs
1/2  c. sweet milk
1  tsp. salt
1/2  c. chopped onion
1  tsp. pepper
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  c. catsup
1  c. water

      Mix together hamburger, crumbs and milk. Shape into balls.
       Mix together other ingredients to form a sauce. Place meatballs and sauce in a casserole dish and bake for 45 minutes at 350 degrees;.
