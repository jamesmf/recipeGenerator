ZUCCHINI WITH CHICKEN SOUP


4  medium zucchini, sliced 1/2 inch thick
1/3  c. onion, chopped
6  Tbsp. margarine, melted
1  c. sour cream
1  can condensed cream of chicken soup
1  c. shredded carrots
2  c. seasoned bread crumbs

      Saut√© zucchini and onion in 4 tablespoons margarine until tender.  Combine zucchini and onion with sour cream, cream of chicken soup, shredded carrots and 1 1/2 cups bread crumbs in 1 1/2-quart greased casserole.  Sprinkle with remaining crumbs tossed with 2 tablespoons margarine.  Bake for 30 minutes at 350 degrees;.
      Note:  May substitute cream of shrimp or mushroom soup with chopped green peppers.
