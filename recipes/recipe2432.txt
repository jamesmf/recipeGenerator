COCONUT BONBONS


1  can Eagle Brand milk
1 1/2  boxes confectioners sugar
1  tsp. vanilla
1 large  pkg. flaked coconut
1/4  lb. butter
2  c. chopped nuts
1  lb. pkg. chocolate chips
1 (2-inch)  sq. paraffin

      Mix Eagle Brand milk, confectioners sugar, vanilla, coconut, butter and nuts.  Shape into balls.  Chill for 2 hours or overnight.  Melt chocolate and paraffin in a heavy saucepan over low heat.  Dip balls in mixture and place on foil.  Refrigerate until ready to serve.
