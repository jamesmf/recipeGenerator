BRAZILIAN CREME PIE


1  unbaked graham cracker crust

 
Filling:
4 (3 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
1/2  c. sugar
2  eggs, beaten well
2  Tbsp. creme de cacao

 
Topping:
1  c. sour cream (1/2 pt.)
2  Tbsp. Triple Sec

      Combine filling ingredients.  Beat until smooth and thick. Pour into crust.  Bake at 375 degrees; for 20 minutes.
      Mix topping.  Spread on pie.  Return to oven for additional 5 minutes.  Garnish.
