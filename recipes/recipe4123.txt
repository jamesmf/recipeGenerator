SAUSAGE PINWHEELS


2  c. flour
1/2  tsp. salt
2  tsp. baking powder
2  Tbsp. white cornmeal
1/4  c. vegetable shortening
3/4  c. plus 1 Tbsp. milk
1  lb. hot sausage, at room temperature

      Combine dry ingredients in mixing bowl.  Cut in shortening until coarse crumbs form.  Add milk.  Mix well.  Knead dough about 1 minute on floured surface.  Divide dough into 2 parts.  Roll each into 1/4-inch thick squares.  Spread each with 1/2 pound sausage.  Roll up from one end.  Refrigerate 1 hour.
      Preheat oven to 375 degrees;.  Slice 1/2-inch thick.  Place on ungreased cookie sheets.  Bake 15 to 20 minutes or until golden brown.
