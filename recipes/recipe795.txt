CHICKEN POTATO CORN CHOWDER


1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can cream of potato soup (undiluted)
1 (5 oz.)  can boned chicken or turkey, cut up, or leftover cooked chicken or turkey
2  c. milk
1 (16 oz.)  can cream-style corn
1  tsp. chicken bouillon or 1 cube, crushed

      In large saucepan, stir all ingredients until well blended. Bring to boil over medium heat.  Stir occasionally.  Makes 4 servings; contains 281 calories per serving.
