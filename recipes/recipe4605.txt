COLD AVOCADO SOUP


3  ripe avocadoes
1  c. low fat sour cream
1  can low sodium chicken broth, chilled
  juice from 2 limes
  salt and pepper to taste
1/2  tsp. chili powder, optional
  dash of tabasco, optional
  diced avocado and/or tomato for garnish

      Put all ingredients except garnish in a blender or food processor and blend until smooth.  Refrigerate until chilled. Pour the soup into serving bowls and top with garnish.
