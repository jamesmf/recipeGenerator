SNICKER APPLE SALAD


1  C. brown sugar
8  oz. cream cheese
16  oz. Cool Whip
3  - 5 Granny Smith apples
6  chopped Snicker candy bars
1/2  C. chopped peanuts (optional)

      Blend together brown sugar and softened cream cheese. Stir in Cool Whip.  Add cubed apples, chopped peanuts and candy bars.
      Chill 1/2 hour.  Serve.
