CHOW-CHOW


2  qt. cut up cabbage
2  qt. sliced green tomatoes
2  qt. sliced onions
1  pt. red sweet pepper
1  pt. green pepper
1  qt. vinegar
4  c. sugar
2  Tbsp. mixed spices
  salt

      Mix first 5 ingredients and soak with salt and water overnight. Drain and mix vinegar, sugar and spices. Boil 1 hour and put in hot sterilized jars.
