TUNA BALL


1  can light tuna
1 8  oz. cream cheese
2  hard boiled eggs yolks
1  onion, diced
1  Tbsp. mayonnaise
1  tsp. tabasco sauce
1/2  c. chopped nuts
1  Tbsp. parsley flakes
1  Tbsp. parmigiana cheese

      Mix all ingredients together.  Roll into round ball and serve on a round plate.  Eat on crackers.
