FREEZER SLAW


2  lb. cabbage, shredded
3  large carrots, shredded
1  small onion, chopped
1  tsp. salt
2  c. sugar
1  tsp. dry mustard
1/2  c. water
1  c. vinegar (5% acidity)
1  tsp. celery seed

      Combine cabbage, carrots and onion.  Sprinkle with salt.  Let stand 1 hour.  Drain.  In a saucepan, combine remaining ingredients.  Bring to a boil; boil 3 minutes.  Cool.  Pour slaw dressing over cabbage mixture; let stand 5 minutes.  Stir well. Ladle slaw into jars.  Leave 1/2-inch headspace.  Adjust plastic storage caps.  Label and freeze.
