GRANDMA DULA'S ROAST BEEF


5 or 6  lb. roast beef
1  medium onion, chopped
1/2  bell pepper
4  cloves garlic
1/2  c. celery
  salt and pepper
2  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
2  jalapeno peppers (without seeds)
  few drops of hot sauce

      Mix all ingredients well.  Cut about 1 or 1 1/2-inch holes all over in roast.  Stuff ingredients in holes and bake in oven for about 1 1/2 or 2 hours at 350 degrees;.
