POTATO SOUP


1/4  c. butter
1/4  c. flour
1/2  tsp. salt
  dash of pepper
2  c. milk
4  c. cooked, cubed potatoes
1  medium onion, chopped

      Melt butter in saucepan over low heat.  Blend in flour, salt and  pepper.  Add milk all at once.  Cook quickly, stirring constantly,  until mixture thickens and bubbles.  Remove sauce from  heat.  (Add  cheese  or  other flavorings at this point,  if so  desired.)  Add  onions  and potatoes to sauce. Return to heat and cook until hot.
