DRIED BEEF SPREAD


1 (2 1/2 oz.)  jar dried beef
2 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
2  Tbsp. milk
1/2  c. chopped green onions
1/2  tsp. dried dill weed
1/4  tsp. salt

      Chop dried beef into small pieces. Combine with softened cream cheese, milk, onions, dill weed and salt, mixing until smooth. Chill and serve with crackers.
