HAMBURGER DRESSING HOT DISH


2  lb. hamburger, browned and unseasoned
2  cans cream of chicken or cream of mushroom soup
1  can cream of celery soup
1  c. milk
1 (7 oz.)  pkg. seasoned croutons

      Spread hamburger into bottom of a cake pan. Mix remaining ingredients together. Spread over top of meat. Bake at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes. Cut and serve.
