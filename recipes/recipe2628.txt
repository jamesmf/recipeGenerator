


1 1/2  sticks oleo, softened
3  c. sugar
6  eggs
1 1/2  tsp. vanilla
2  Tbsp. cornmeal
2  (9-inch) pie shells

      Cream  oleo and sugar.  Add eggs one at a time, beating after each addition.  Add vanilla and cornmeal.  Pour 1/2 into each unbaked pie shell.  Bake 1 hour at 325 degrees;.
