FRESH SPINACH SALAD


1  bag spinach, washed and torn into pieces
1  can bean sprouts, drained
1 small  can water chestnuts, drained and sliced thin
2  hard-boiled eggs, cut up
8  slices bacon, fried crisp and crumbled up
1  small onion, thin sliced and separated into rings

      Toss all together in large bowl.
Dressing:
1  c. salad oil
1/2  c. wine vinegar
1/2  c. sugar
1/3  c. catsup
1  dash of salt

      Blend well and pour over chilled greens.
