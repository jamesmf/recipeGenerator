BROCCOLI CHEESE SOUP


1 small  pkg. frozen chopped broccoli
1  small onion, chopped
1  can chicken broth
3  Tbsp. butter
1/4  c. flour
2  c. milk
3/4  c. Velveeta cheese
  salt and pepper to taste

      In a sauce pan, cook (uncovered) broccoli and onions in chicken broth for 8 to 10 minutes. In another sauce pan, melt butter and add flour gradually. Add milk and stir with a wire whisk. Add broccoli and broth mixture to cheese sauce. Simmer on low for 5 to 10 minutes. Add salt and pepper to taste.
