CHICKEN ENCHILADAS


3  c. cooked-diced chicken
1  c. chopped onion
1  tsp. oil
2- 4  ounce cans green chilies, chopped
1/2  lb. cheddar or velveeta cheese, grated
1/2  lb. Monterey Jack cheese
12  - 10 inch flour tortillas
1  can cream of chicken soup
12  oz. sour cream

      Saut√© onions in oil. Add chicken, chilies and 2/3 of each cheese. Fry tortillas in small amount of oil for 20 -30 seconds, just to soften them.
      Spoon chicken mixture into each tortilla and roll up. Place folded side down in a greased 13 x 9 inch pan. Mix soup and sour cream and pour oven the top. Top with remaining cheese. Bake at 350 degrees;F. for 20 - 30 minutes.
