HOMEMADE ALFREDO SAUCE


1  pt. heavy cream
1  stick butter
2  Tbsp. cream cheese
1/2 to 3/4  c. Parmesan cheese
1  tsp. garlic powder

      In a saucepan, combine butter, heavy cream and cream cheese. Simmer until melted and mixed well. Add Parmesan cheese and garlic powder. Simmer on low for 15 to 20 minutes.
