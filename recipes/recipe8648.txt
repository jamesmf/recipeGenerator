CRAZY CRUST PIE

Crust:
1/2  c. flour
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  c. sour cream
1/2  tsp. baking powder
1/4  c. solid shortening
1  egg

	Mix ingredients.  Batter will be slightly lumpy. Spread in pie dish.
Filling:
1  lb. ground beef, lightly browned and drained
1  tsp. salt
2  tsp. chili powder (optional)
1/2  c. onion, chopped
1  (16 oz.) kidney or Ranch Style beans (undrained)
1 (6 oz.)  can tomato sauce
1/2  c. lettuce, chopped
1/2  c. tomato, chopped
1/2 to 1  c. shredded Monterey Jack, Cheddar or American cheese

	Preheat oven to 425 degrees;.  Combine all ingredients except lettuce, tomato and cheese.  Mix thoroughly.  Smooth into crust. Bake 20 to 30 minutes until crust is a deep golden brown. Sprinkle with lettuce, tomato and cheese. Slice and serve like pie.
