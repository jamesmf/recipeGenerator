CHEWY GINGER COOKIES


1  c. sugar
1/2  c. shortening
1  egg
1/3  c. molasses
1  tsp. cinnamon
1/4  tsp. cloves
1/4  tsp. nutmeg
1/2  tsp. salt
1 1/2  tsp. soda
2  c. flour

      Cream sugar and shortening.  Add egg and molasses.  Then sift remaining dry ingredients and add.  Roll into walnut-size balls. Roll in sugar and place on greased cookie sheet.  Bake at 325 degrees; for 15 minutes.  Makes 1 dozen.
