CARIBBEAN DUMPLINGS


2  c. flour
2  eggs
2  c. sour cream
1/2  tsp. salt
1  Tbsp. butter
1  tsp. baking soda
1  Tbsp. water
  shortening

      Sift flour; stir in salt and beaten eggs.  Gradually stir in the sour cream and let stand 2 hours.  Stir in melted butter and the baking soda, dissolved in the tablespoon of water.  Drop batter from a teaspoon into hot, deep fat at 380 degrees; and fry until golden. Drain on paper towel.
