BEEFY BISCUIT CUPS


1  lb. ground beef
1 14  oz. jar spaghetti sauce
2 8  oz. tubes large refrigerated biscuits
1  c. shredded cheddar cheese, more to taste

      In a skillet, brown beef and drain.  Stir in spaghetti sauce. Cook over medium heat for 5 to 10 minutes.  Press biscuits into the bottom of and up the sides of greased muffin cups.  Spoon 2 tablespoons of meat mixture into center of each cup. Bake at 375 degrees for 15 to 17 minutes or until golden brown.  Sprinkle with cheese.  Bake 3 minutes longer or until cheese is melted.
