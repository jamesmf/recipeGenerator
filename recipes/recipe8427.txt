QUICK AND EASY SKILLET SUPPER


1  pkg. link sausage (smoked, Bratwurst or even frankfurters)
1  head cabbage
1/2  pkg. egg noodles
1/2  stick margarine

      Slice smoked sausage and brown in large skillet.  Meanwhile, cut cabbage in chunks and steam.  Boil noodles and melt margarine. Combine everything in skillet and cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, for a few minutes.  Salt and pepper as desired.
