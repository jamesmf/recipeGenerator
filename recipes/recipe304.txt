BAGEL BITES



1  tsp. reduced-calorie margarine, softened
1/4  tsp. dried whole oregano
1/8  tsp. garlic powder
2  (1 oz.) frozen mini bagels, thawed and halved

      Combine first 3 ingredients and spread 1/4 teaspoon margarine mixture over cut sides of each bagel half.  Place bagel halves, cut side up, on a baking sheet; broil 6 inches from heat for 1 minute or until golden brown.  Yields 4 halves.
      Per Serving:  94 Calories, 2 g. Fat (19%), 0 mg.  Cholesterol and 119 mg.  Sodium.
