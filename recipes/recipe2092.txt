CAJUN CANDY


2  c. sugar
1 1/2  c. packed brown sugar
1/2  tsp. salt
1 1/3  c. milk
1  c. mashed yams
1/4  c. light corn syrup
1/4  c. butter or margarine
3/4  c. pecan halves
1 1/2  tsp. vanilla

      Combine sugars, salt, milk, yams and corn syrup in heavy 4-quart saucepan.  Stir until all is mixed thoroughly.  Cook over medium heat until sugar is dissolved and it comes to boil.  Reduce heat and cook to 224 degrees; on candy thermometer or soft-ball stage. Remove from heat; add butter.  Do not stir.  Cool to 110 degrees;.  Chop 1/2 cup pecans.  Add vanilla to cooled mixture.  Beat until candy stiffens and loses gloss.  Stir in chopped nuts.  Spread candy on greased 9-inch pan.  Top with last 1/4 cup pecan halves.  Cut into squares.
