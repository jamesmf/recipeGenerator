SIMPLY GOOD CHICKEN CASSEROLE


3  lb. chicken (broiler fryer or breasts)
1  qt. water
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. pepper
1  can cream of chicken soup, undiluted
1  can cream of celery soup, undiluted
1 (8 oz.)  carton sour cream
1/2  tsp. pepper
2  stacks Ritz crackers, crushed
1/4  c. butter, melted

      Put chicken in large pot with water, 1 tsp. salt and 1 tsp. pepper; bring to a boil.  Cover, reduce heat and simmer for 1 hour or until tender.  Cool chicken slightly; skin and debone and cut into bite-size pieces.  Combine chicken, soups, sour cream and 1/2 tsp. pepper; stir well.  Place 1/2 of crushed crackers in lightly greased 11 x 7 x 1 1/2 inch baking dish.  Spoon chicken mixture over crackers.  Top with remaining crackers and drizzle with butter.  Bake at 325 degrees; for 35 minutes or until lightly browned. Yield: 6 to 8 servings.
