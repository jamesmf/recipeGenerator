PECAN-PECAN PIE


1  c. maple syrup
3  tsp. soy margarine
1  tsp. pure vanilla flavor
1  tsp. lemon zest (lemon peel)
1  c. pecan halves (to place on top)
1  c. honey
2  tsp. egg replacer
1  tsp. lemon juice
1  c. pecan pieces for mixture

      Can add 1/2 teaspoon of liquid Braggs amino acids.
      Combine all ingredients in order as listed.  Can add a little whole-wheat pastry flour to thicken.  Pour into unbaked pie shell and place whole pecan halves on top.  Bake at 325 degrees; for 15 minutes. Reduce heat to 300 degrees; and bake 20 more minutes, until set.  Let cool and enjoy.
