STEAK AND POTATO SUPPER


1 1/2  lb. round steak, cut in serving size pieces
1/2  c. flour
4  Tbsp. fat
4  to 6 small potatoes
1  small onion
2  Tbsp. chopped parsley
1/2  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper
2 (8 oz.)  cans of tomato sauce

      Dredge steak in flour; brown on both sides in hot fat. Place meat in a 1 1/2 qt. casserole dish; pour off excess fat. Scrape up brown drippings left in pan and add to meat. Arrange potatoes with meat; sprinkle onions, parsley, salt, and pepper over all; add tomato sauce. Cover and bake at 350 degrees; for 1 1/2 hours.
