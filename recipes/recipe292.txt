PLUM PERFECT PARFAIT


1  lb. purple plums
1  Tbsp. cornstarch
3/4  c. sugar
  dash of salt
  dash of ground cinnamon
  dash of ground cloves
1  Tbsp. lemon juice
  vanilla ice cream

      Rinse plums.  Slice, removing pits.  Measure 3 cups.  Combine cornstarch, sugar, and salt in saucepan.  Stir in plums, spices, and lemon juice.  Cover and bring to boil.  Simmer 8 to 10 minutes. Cool.  Layer with vanilla ice cream in parfait glasses.  Makes 4 to 6 servings.
