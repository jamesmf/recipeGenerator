GARDEN PATCH BARBECUE SAUCE


1  small onion, quartered
1  clove garlic
  juice of 1/2 lemon
1  c. good thick ketchup
2  Tbsp. vinegar (apple)
1  tsp. dry mustard
1/2  tsp. basil
3  Tbsp. dark brown sugar
1  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. cayenne pepper
1/2  c. raisins
2/3  c. melted margarine

      Blend all ingredients in a blender until raisins are pulverized.  Remove and place in a saucepan.  Simmer on top of stove for about 40 to 45 minutes.  Makes 2 cups.  Great for pork!
