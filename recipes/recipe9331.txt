DEBRA'S CAMP STEW


10  oz. (1 can) BBQ chicken
10  oz. (1 can) BBQ beef
10  oz. (1 can) BBQ pork
14  oz. (1 can) corn, cream style
28  oz. (1 can) tomatoes, crushed
1/4  c. soy sauce
2  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
  black pepper (to taste)
4  medium potatoes, cooked and sliced

      In a crock pot, mix together:  chicken, beef, pork, corn and tomatoes.  Add soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce and black pepper. Mix well.  Add potatoes, mixing well.  Simmer 2 hours.
      Serve with crackers or corn bread.
