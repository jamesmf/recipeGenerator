CHEESEBURGER BAKE


1  lb. ground beef
3/4  c. chopped onion
1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can Cheddar cheese soup
1/4  c. milk
2  c. baking mix
3/4  c. water
1  c. shredded Cheddar cheese

      Heat oven to 400 degrees;. Generously grease 13 x 9 x 2 baking dish. Cook ground beef with onion; drain well. Stir mixture of soup, water and milk in a bowl, then add cooked beef, onions and baking mix. Stir until fully mixed. Pour into baking dish. Sprinkle with cheese. Bake 30 to 40 minutes.
