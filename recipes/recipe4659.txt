BETTY'S "LOVE" SOUP
(Minestrone)  

1  lb Round steak
1/2  head cabbage, chopped
6  quarts water
2  onions, chopped
6  stalks celery, sliced
4  carrots, sliced
1  can kidney beans, drained & rinsed
1  can tomatoes, crushed or whole
1  tsp parsley flakes
1  tsp thyme leaves
1  tsp marjoram leaves
1/2  tsp garlic powder
1/4  tsp black pepper
1/4  tsp crushed red pepper seeds
1  bag (16 oz) frozen mixed vegetables
1  cup uncooked ditalini or elbow macaroni

      Combine steak, cabbage, water, onions, celery, carrots, kidney beans, tomatoes and seasonings in a large soup pot.  Bring to a boil, cover and simmer for 2 hours.
      Add frozen mixed vegetables, cover and simmer for 1 hour. Remove steak and allow to cool.  Add 1 cup of macaroni, bring to a boil, then cover and remove from heat.  When steak is cool, cut into small pieces and add back into soup.
       Note: This soup has no salt added, so it is "Heart Friendly".
