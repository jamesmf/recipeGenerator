CRABMEAT AND CHEESE ON TOAST


1/2  stick butter
1/2  lb. grated mild Cheddar cheese
1  c. evaporated milk
1  can crabmeat
1  egg, beaten

      Melt butter in top of double boiler.  Add grated cheese and evaporated milk.  Stir until smooth.  Add crabmeat and mix well. Just before serving, add beaten egg and cook 1 minute.  Serve hot on toast.
      Add salad and you have an excellent brunch or Sunday night supper.
