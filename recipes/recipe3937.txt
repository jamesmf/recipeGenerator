CORN RELISH


1  small cabbage head
6  green tomatoes
6  ripe tomatoes
3  sweet green peppers
6  ears corn
6  onions
1 medium  jar of mustard
3  cups vinegar
3  cups of sugar

      Grind up cabbage, tomatoes, peppers, and onions. Cut corn from cob. Bring vinegar mustard and sugar to a boil. Add everything and cook until thick, Pour in jars and seal.
