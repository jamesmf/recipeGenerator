RICE POT JAMBALAYA


1  lb. sausage (sliced)
1  bunch green onions, chopped
1/2  bell pepper, chopped
1  can french onion soup
1/2  can water
1 1/2  c. rice
  Tony Chachere's Creole seasoning

      Mix all ingredients in an 8 c. rice pot and turn pot on. Very easy to cook. Good with green salad and french bread.
