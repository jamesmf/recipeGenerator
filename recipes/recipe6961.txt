SPANISH RICE DISH


1  lb. ground chuck
3  c. cooked rice
2  cans tomato sauce
1  can Rotel tomatoes
1  can corn
2  c. cheese (divided)

      Brown and drain ground chuck; mix all ingredients except 1/2 c. cheese; Pour into a 9 x 13 inch pan. Top with remaining cheese. Bake at 350 degrees; for 20 minutes.
