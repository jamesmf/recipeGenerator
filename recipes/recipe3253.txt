BACON CHESTNUTS


1  c. chili sauce
1  c. brown sugar
1/2  c. mayonnaise
2  cans whole water chestnuts
1  lb. bacon, cut in half

      Mix chili sauce, brown sugar and mayonnaise together.  Wrap chestnut with 1 strip bacon.  Spear with toothpick.  Wrap 2 cans chestnuts.  Place in single layer on baking dish.  Add sauce. Bake at 325 degrees; for 1 hour.  Serve warm.
