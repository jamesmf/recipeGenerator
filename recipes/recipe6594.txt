ST. PAUL'S RICE


2  lb. ground hot sausage, cooked and drained
9  c. boiling water
2  c. white rice
2  pkg. (4 envelopes) chicken noodle soup mix
2  large bell peppers, chopped
2  large Spanish onions, chopped
2  c. celery, chopped
2 (6 oz.)  pkg. sliced almonds
  paprika and parsley (to garnish)

      Brown sausage in skillet and drain. In a large pot, bring 9 cups of water to a boil. Add rice to water when boiling and cook for 7 minutes. Remove from heat. Add to rice and water the sausage, soup packages and vegetables. Stir all together. Then place mixture in casserole dish and bake 1 hour at 350 degrees;. Make for a large crowd. This recipe can be cut in half.
