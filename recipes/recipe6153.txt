ROMAINE AND STRAWBERRY SALAD


1  head romaine or spinach, bite size pieces
1  head boston or baby bib lettuce, bite size
1  pint. strawberries, sliced
1/2  red onion or 3 to 4 green onions, sliced
1  c. olive oil
1/3  c. wine vinegar
1  Tbsp. sugar
  juice of 2 lemons
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper

      Mix romaine, lettuce, strawberries, and onion.  Set aside. For dressing, mix olive oil, vinegar, sugar, lemon juice, salt and pepper.  This makes double the amount of dressing that you need. Pour dressing over greens before serving.  Toss to coat.
