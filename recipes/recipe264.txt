SAND TARTS


2  c. butter
2 1/2  c. sugar
4  c. flour
2  eggs, well beaten
1  egg white
  pecan halves

      Cream butter and sugar together.  Slowly add flour alternately with the eggs and mix thoroughly.  Chill overnight.  Roll out thin on a lightly floured board.  Brush cool cookies with egg white which has been slightly beaten.  Sprinkle with sugar and press 1/2 pecan in center.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 10 minutes.  Makes 17 dozen.
