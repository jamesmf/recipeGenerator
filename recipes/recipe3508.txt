SPINACH PAKORAS


2  1/2 cups gram flour
1/2  tsp. Pomegranate seed
1  tsp. Cumin seed
1  tsp. red chili
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. chat masala
1  tsp. baking soda
1  small onion
1  whole lemon
8  oz. frozen spinach
3/4  cup water

      Mix all ingredients together and scoop by teaspoonfuls; fry in hot oil.
