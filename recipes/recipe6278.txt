TUNA SALAD ON LETTUCE


1/4  c. diced parsley
2  Tbsp. chopped fresh dill
7  oz. can tuna, drained
3  c. shredded romaine lettuce
1  red onion, sliced thin
3  c. white kidney beans
2  scallions, sliced
3  stalks celery, sliced thin

 
Dressing:
2  Tbsp. red wine vinegar
1  tsp. Dijon mustard
1/3  c. vegetable oil

      To make dressing, mix vinegar, mustard and oil.  Beat well. Add kidney beans, scallions, celery, 2 tablespoons parsley, 2 teaspoons dill and tuna.  Toss lightly. Add salt and pepper to taste.  Chill, covered, for 1 hour. Place on bed of lettuce. Garnish with sliced onion and parsley.
