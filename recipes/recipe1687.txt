JEWISH HASH


5  lb. stew beef
2  large onions
  lemon pepper season
  red wine (1/2 c.)
1/2  c. garlic vinegar
3  Tbsp. brown sugar
  hickory smoked barbecue sauce
6  cloves

      Slice 1 onion in bottom of crock-pot.  Add meat, lemon pepper season and cloves.  Slice other onion on top of meat.  Add vinegar and water; cook on low for 12 hours.  After 12 hours, drain meat (most of liquid off) and add brown sugar and barbecue sauce.  Cook for 6 more hours on low.
