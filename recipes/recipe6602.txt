COCONUT-BEER SHRIMP


2  lb. medium shrimp
2 1/2  tsp. red pepper
1 1/8  tsp. salt
1  tsp. black pepper
3/4  tsp. paprika
1/2  tsp. each: garlic powder, onion powder, thyme and oregano
1  c. flour
1/2  c. beer
1  egg, beaten
1/2  tsp. baking powder
7  oz. bag flaked coconut

      Peel and devein shrimp, leaving tail on. Mix spices in covered bowl. Add shrimp and shake until coated. Combine flour, beer, egg and baking powder. Spread coconut on a baking sheet. Dip shrimp in batter, then lay in the coconut. Turn to coat well. Deep-fry until golden.
Spicy Orange Dip:
1  jar orange marmalade
3  Tbsp. horseradish
3  Tbsp. spicy brown mustard
1  tsp. grated lemon rind

      Mix together.
