TACO CASSEROLE


1  lb. ground beef
1  pkg. taco seasoning mix
1  medium onion, chopped
1  c. diced tomatoes
  Cheddar cheese, shredded
  Mozzarella cheese, shredded
1  pkg. tortilla chips, crushed
1/2  c. water

      Brown ground beef. Add taco seasoning mix, onion, water and diced tomatoes. Stir to mix well. Bring to a boil. Turn off heat. Using a 9 x 13 pan, place crushed tortilla chips in bottom of pan. Spoon half of meat mixture on top of chips. Sprinkle half of both cheeses on top. Spoon remaining meat mixture on top of cheese. Top with the rest of the cheese. Bake in 350 degrees; oven 15 minutes or until cheese melts.
