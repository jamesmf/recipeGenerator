SPAGHETTI SAUCE


1  lb. ground beef
1  medium to large onion
1 large  can whole tomatoes
1 (15 oz.)  can tomato sauce
1 (6 oz.)  can tomato paste
1 (6 oz.)  can water
1/2  stick butter
2  Tbsp. oregano
1  tsp. black pepper
2  Tbsp. Parmesan cheese
1/8  tsp. thyme
1/8  tsp. sage
1/4  tsp. rosemary
1 1/4  tsp. salt
1 1/2  tsp. basil
1  tsp. garlic salt
1  bay leaf
1  tsp. sugar

      Brown ground beef and onion.  Drain.  Make soup with remaining ingredients.  Add beef and onion.  Simmer one hour, stirring occasionally.
