BAWATSA
(Appetizer)  

1  frozen bread dough/pizza dough
1  large green pepper, diced
1  large onion, diced
2  lb. mild sausage (or 1 lb. mild and 1 lb. hot can be used)
2  c. pepperoni, sliced
1  large knob Mozzarella cheese, shredded
  Mushrooms (optional)

      Brown sausage in large skillet.  Drain well.  Spread dough on lightly greased cookie sheet.  Spread meat mixture on dough. Layer pepperoni, onions and green pepper on meat.  Sprinkle cheese on top.  Roll from short end, making sure ends of dough are secure. Bake at 350 degrees; for approximately 20 minutes.  Slice and serve hot.
