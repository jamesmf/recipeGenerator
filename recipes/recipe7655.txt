FRIED MUSH


1  qt. broth or water
3  c. cold water
3  c. cornmeal (white or yellow)
4  tsp. salt

      Heat broth.  Mix together cold  water  and cornmeal and salt. Add to hot broth and cook 1  hour.  Put  in Pyrex bread pan to cool and then refrigerate.  Slice 1/4-inch thick.  Roll in flour and fry in skillet.  Serve with apple butter, pancake syrup, etc.
