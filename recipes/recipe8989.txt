BEEF TIPS IN WINE SAUCE


3  lb. round steak, cut into bite-size pieces
1  pkg. dry onion soup mix
12  oz. pkg. egg noodles
2  cans golden mushroom soup
1  c. red wine

      Mix all ingredients. Pour in oven-proof baking dish and bake at 300 degrees; to 325 degrees; for 3 1/2 hours. Add pepper to taste and stir.
