MISSOURI COOKIES


2  c. sugar
3  Tbsp. cocoa
1/2  c. milk
1/4  lb. margarine
1  tsp. vanilla
1/2  c. crunchy peanut butter
  pinch of salt
3  c. quick-cooking oats

      Combine sugar, cocoa, milk and butter in saucepan; cook at full rolling boil for 1 minute.  Remove from heat.  Add vanilla, peanut butter and salt.  Mix well.  Add oats.  Drop by teaspoons on waxed paper and cool until firm.  Makes 4 dozen.
