CARIBBEAN PORK CHOPS WITH APPLE ISLAND SAUCE


2/3  c. water
1/2  c. onions, chopped
1/3  c. lemon juice
1  Tbsp. vegetable oil
2  tsp. light brown sugar
2  tsp. chicken flavored instant bouillon
3/4  tsp. each: ground allspice, black pepper, cinnamon and thyme leaves
1/4 to 1/2  tsp. cayenne pepper
4  boneless pork loin chops (about 1 lb.)

	Combine all ingredients except meat. Place meat in a shallow dish or plastic bag. Pour marinade over meat to cover. Marinate in the refrigerator 4 hours or overnight. Remove meat from marinade; heat marinade. Grill or broil meat as desired, basting frequently with marinade.
Apple Island Sauce:
1 1/2  c. chunky applesauce
1/2  c. water
2  Tbsp. plus 2 tsp. sugar
2  tsp. lemon juice

	In a small saucepan, combine all ingredients and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer 10 to 15 minutes.
