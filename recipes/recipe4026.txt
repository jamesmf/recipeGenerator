LAYERED NACHO DIP


16  oz. (1 can) refried beans
2 to 3  oz. (1/2 pkg.) taco seasoning mix
8  oz. (1 tub) sour cream
1  c. olives, chopped
2  large tomatoes, diced
1  onion, finely chopped
4  oz. (1 can) green chilies, chopped
1 1/2  c. Monterey Jack cheese, shredded
16  oz. (1 jar) salsa

      Combine beans and seasoning mix.  Spread mixture evenly in a 9x13-inch dish.  Layer the remaining ingredients as follows: sour cream, olives, tomatoes, onion, green chilies and cheese. Top with salsa.
      Serve with corn chips.
