PARMESAN CHICKEN


6  boneless skinless chicken breast halves
2  Tbsp. fat-free margarine, melted
1/2  c. grated fat-free Parmesan cheese*
1/4  c. dry bread crumbs
1  tsp. oregano
1  tsp. parsley
1/4  tsp. paprika
  salt and pepper to taste

      *Weight Watchers makes a fat-free Parmesan cheese.
      Heat oven to 400 degrees;. Spray a baking dish with vegetable oil cooking spray. Dip the chicken in the melted margarine (use liquid Butter Buds if you prefer). Combine the remaining ingredients and coat the chicken with the crumb mixture. Place in the baking dish and bake, uncovered, for 25 to 30 minutes or until tender and golden brown.
