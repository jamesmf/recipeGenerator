HUNGARIAN NOODLE BAKE


3  c. fine noodles
1  clove garlic, minced
1  Tbsp. butter
1/4  c. onion, minced
1 1/2  c. cottage cheese
1  c. sour cream
1  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
  dash of Tabasco
2  tsp. poppy seed
  salt and pepper to taste

	Cook noodles and drain. Preheat oven to 350 degrees;.  Saut√© garlic in butter and add onion.   Combine all ingredients. Bake in a greased 10 x 6 baking dish for 25 minutes.
