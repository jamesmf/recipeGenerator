BLACK BEAN SALSA


2 (15 oz.)  cans black beans, rinsed and drained
1 (17 oz.)  can whole kernel corn, drained
1  large tomato, seeded and chopped
1  large avocado, peeled and chopped
1  purple onion, chopped
1/8 to 1/4  c. chopped fresh cilantro
2 to 3  Tbsp. lime juice
2  Tbsp. olive oil
1  Tbsp. red wine vinegar
1  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper

      Combine all ingredients in a large bowl; cover and chill. Garnish if desired with avocado slice and fresh cilantro.  Serve with tortilla chips.  Makes 6 cups.
