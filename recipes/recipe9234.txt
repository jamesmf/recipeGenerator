COKE ROAST BEEF


2 to 3  lb. beef roast
1  can Coca-Cola
1  can cream of mushroom soup, or other flavor

      Preheat oven to 400 degrees;.  Place roast in bean pot (or covered roasting pan); cover with Coke and soup.  Bake, covered, for 2 1/2 to 3 hours until internal temperature reaches 170 degrees;.
      Note: Pork, turkey or chicken may be substituted for beef. Carrots, celery and potatoes may be added.
