CHICKEN CACCIATORE


1  Tbsp. olive oil
1  small green pepper, sliced or chopped
1  small onion,  sliced or chopped
1/2  tsp. garlic powder
1/4  tsp. black pepper
1  lb. boneless, skinless chicken breast, halved or sliced
3  Tbsp. sherry
26 or 28  oz. Ragu sauce

      In a 12-inch skillet, heat oil over medium heat and cook green pepper, onion, and garlic powder 3 minutes or till vegetables are tender.  Add chicken and brown 3 minutes.  Stir in sherry and pepper, cook 3 minutes.  Stir in Ragu sauce and cook 8 minutes or till chicken is no longer pink.  Serve over cook rice or pasta. Serves 8.
