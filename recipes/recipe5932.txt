HOT CHICKEN SALAD


1  c. grated cheese
1  c. cream of chicken soup
1  c. diced chicken
1/4  tsp. salt
1/2  c. sliced almonds
2  tsp. diced onion
1  c. cooked rice
3/4  c. mayonnaise
1  tsp. lemon juice
1  c. diced celery

      Combine undiluted soup with all ingredients, except cheese. Mix well. Put into casserole dish and spread cheese over top. Place in oven at 350 degrees; until warm through and cheese is melted.
