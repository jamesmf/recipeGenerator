SWISS CHICKEN


4  chicken breasts
  mayonnaise
  Swiss cheese slices
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1/4  c. chicken broth
  bread crumbs or croutons
  butter

      Place 4 chicken breasts (halved and boned, 8 fillets) in mayonnaise coated casserole.  Cover with slices of Swiss cheese. Mix 1 can cream of mushroom soup with 1/4 cup chicken broth; pour over chicken and cheese.  Cover top with bread crumbs or croutons and dot with butter.  Bake at 325 degrees; for 3/4 to 1 hour.
