TACO SALAD


1  head lettuce
1  lb. ground beef
1 8  oz. pkg. shredded cheddar cheese
1  large. onion, optional
4  medium. diced tomatoes
1  pkg. tortilla chips, crumbled
1  pkg. taco seasoning mix

      Brown ground beef, drain.  Add taco seasoning, reserving 1 Tablespoon.  Add lettuce, onion, tomatoes and cheese in large bowl.
Dressing:
1 8  oz. bottle Thousand Island dressing
1/3  c. sugar
1  Tbsp. taco seasoning

      Mix dressing ingredients and stir into salad.  Add crumbled tortilla chips before serving.
