SPLATTERED SPAGHETTI SAUCE


2  cans tomato paste or 3 cans tomato sauce
3  Tbsp. sugar
3 to 4  tsp. minced garlic
1  onion, diced
  basil to taste
1  jar Prego meat flavored spaghetti sauce (optional)

      Mix, tomato sauce, sugar, garlic, onion and basil; simmer. You can add browned ground meat if desired. A jar of Prego spaghetti sauce adds a good flavor and and makes a larger amount. Serve over cooked and drained spaghetti.
      FYI - No one knows for sure how this recipe got it's name, however, most spaghetti lovers could probably make a good guess.
