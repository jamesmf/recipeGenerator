BREAKFAST CASSEROLE


10  slices white bread, crust cut off
2  sticks margarine
1  lb. hot sausage, browned and drained
6  eggs, slightly beaten
2  c. milk
1  lb. sharp cheese, grated

      Butter bread after crust has been removed. Cut up into 2-inch pieces. Butter bottom of a 9 x 13-inch Pyrex dish. Place bread in dish. Mix eggs and drained sausage together. Add milk and cheese. Pour over bread. Refrigerate overnight. Cook at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes, covered with foil. Remove foil and cook 15 minutes, uncovered.
