POTATO SALAD


2  lb. small red potatoes, cooked and cubed
1  c. sliced celery
1/2  c. sliced green onions
1  c. mayonnaise
1/4  c. creamy mustard
2  Tbsp. vinegar
2  Tbsp. parsley flakes
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper

      In a large bowl, mix mayonnaise, mustard, vinegar, parsley flakes, salt and pepper. Stir in potatoes, celery and onions. Cover and chill until ready to serve.
