SUNFLOWER SALAD


1  large head cut up broccoli
1  c. raisins
1/4  c. dried onion flakes
10  strips bacon, fried crisp
1  c. sunflower meats

 
Dressing:
3 to 4  Tbsp. sugar
1/2  c. light Miracle Whip
1  Tbsp. vinegar

      Mix all salad ingredients together. In bowl, mix dressing ingredients. Pour dressing on salad; let stand awhile before serving.
