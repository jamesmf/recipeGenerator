HOT DISH


1  can vegetable soup
1  lb. ground beef
1/4  c. onion, chopped
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  qt. diced raw potatoes
1  c. celery, diced

      Brown ground beef and onion together. Mix in all other ingredients. Bake at 350 degrees; for approximately 1 hour or simmer in skillet on stove.
