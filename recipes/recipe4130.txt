HOT ARTICHOKE DIP


1  c. chopped artichoke hearts
1  c. grated Parmesan cheese
1  c. mayonnaise (Hellmann's)
1/2  c. chopped green onions
1/2  tsp. garlic salt
1/2  tsp. chopped parsley
1/2  tsp. white pepper
  paprika

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees;.  Combine all ingredients, except paprika and mix gently.  Spread into 1 1/2-quart baking dish.  Sprinkle with paprika.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 20 to 25 minutes.  Serve as a dip with crackers, chips or raw vegetables.
