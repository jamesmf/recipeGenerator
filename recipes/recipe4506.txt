TANGY BROCCOLI SALAD


1  c. Miracle Whip dressing
2  Tbsp. sugar
2  Tbsp. vinegar
1 medium  bunch broccoli, cut into flowerets (about 6 c.)
12  slices bacon, cooked crisp and crumbled
1/2  c. chopped red onion

      Mix dressing, sugar and vinegar in large bowl. Add remaining ingredients; mix lightly. Refrigerate 1 hour or until chilled.
