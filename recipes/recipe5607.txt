CHEDDAR HAM CHOWDER


2   C. water
2  C. cubed peeled potatoes
1/2  C. sliced carrots
1/2  C. sliced celery
1/4  C. chopped onion
1  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
1/4  C. butter
1/4  C. flour
2  C. milk
2  C. sharp cheddar cheese, shredded
1  can whole kernel corn, drained
1 1/2  C. cubed ham

      In a large saucepan, bring water, potatoes, carrots, celery, onion, salt and pepper to a boil.  Reduce heat; cover and simmer 8 to 10 minutes or until vegetables are tender.  Remove from heat; do not drain.
      In a medium saucepan, melt the butter.  Blend in flour.  Add the milk all at once; cook and stir until thickened and bubbly. Add cheese and stir until melted.  Stir into the undrained vegetables, return large pan to the heat.  Add corn and ham; heat through, stirring occasionally.
