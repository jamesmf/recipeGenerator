FRIED OYSTERS


1  qt. select oysters
1  c. water
1  egg
2  Tbsp. water
  buttered bread crumbs
  salt and pepper to taste

      Carefully look over oysters and remove any shell fragments. Pour 1 cup of water over oysters and drain.  Pat dry with paper towel.  Beat egg and add 2 tablespoons of water.  Combine bread crumbs with salt and pepper.  Dip oysters in egg mixture, then in seasoned crumbs.  Repeat.  Fry in deep fat at 375 degrees; to 385 degrees; for 30 to 40 minutes.  Serves 2 to 4.
