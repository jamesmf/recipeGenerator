PAN-FRIED ONION DIP


1  large onion
1  Tbsp. butter
1  Tbsp. oil
1  pinch cayenne pepper
3/4  c. sour cream
3/4  c. mayonnaise
  salt and pepper to taste

	Peel and thinly slice onions. Saut√© the onion in the butter and oil until the onions are caramelized, about 30 minutes. Cool. Add remaining ingredients and mix well. Chill for 1 hour. Serve dip with vegetables, chips or crackers. Makes 1 3/4 cups of dip.
