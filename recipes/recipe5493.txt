LAYERED SALAD


1  head lettuce, shredded
2  to 4 stalks celery, sliced thinly
  green onions, sliced thinly
  water chestnuts, sliced
1 (10 oz.)  pkg. frozen peas, rinsed in cold water
1  c. mayonnaise
1  Tbsp. sugar
  Romano cheese, grated

      Layer in bowl in order listed (amounts of first 5 items may vary depending on bowl size).  Spread mayonnaise over top and sprinkle with sugar and then cheese.  Garnish with chopped hard-boiled eggs and crumbled bacon.  Do not toss.
