CAULIFLOWER AND LETTUCE SALAD


1  lb. bacon, fried and crumbled
1  head cauliflower
1  medium red onion, chopped
1  large head lettuce
2  c. mayonnaise
1/2  c. sugar
1/2  c. grated parmesan cheese

      Cut lettuce, cauliflower and onion in large salad bowl.  Add bacon.  Mix mayonnaise, sugar and cheese in small bowl.  Add salad just before serving.  As an option, 1 can of ripe olives can be added.
