GAZPACHO


2  medium tomatoes, peeled and cut (1 1/4 lb.)
1/2  large cucumber, pared and cubed
1/2  large onion, chopped
1/2  green pepper, chopped
1  can pimento
24  oz. tomato juice
1/3  c. olive oil
1/3  c. red wine vinegar
1/4  tsp. Tabasco
1 1/2  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper

      Pure in blender for 1 minute the tomato juice, 1/2 cup cuke, pepper, onion, pimento and tomato. Add all other ingredients. Mix with whisk.  Refrigerate at least 3 hours, covered.  Serve in bowls.  May garnish with croutons, chopped chives on dollop of sour cream, chopped cucumber, pepper or tomatoes.  Makes 3 quarts.
