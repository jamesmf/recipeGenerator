MONTANA SPICE COOKIES


1 1/2  c. water
1 1/2  c. raisins
1  tsp. soda
1 1/2  c. sugar
1  c. shortening
3  eggs
1/4  tsp. salt
3  c. flour
1 1/2  tsp. baking powder
1  tsp. cinnamon

      Combine water and raisins; cook until water is absorbed.  Add the soda.  Cream together sugar and shortening; add the eggs.  Mix together salt, flour, baking powder and cinnamon.  Add creamed mixture; add raisins last.  Form into balls and flatten with bottom of a glass dipped in sugar.  Bake in 375 degrees; oven about 10 minutes.
