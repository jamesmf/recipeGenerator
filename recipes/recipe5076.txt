COTTAGE CHEESE POTATO SALAD


6  to 8 medium potatoes
1 1/2  c. chopped celery
1  Tbsp. vinegar
1 1/2  tsp. salt
1  c. mayo
1  small chopped apple
1  Tbsp. chopped stuffed olives
1  Tbsp. salad oil
1/8  tsp. pepper
1  lb. cottage cheese

      Boil potatoes in jacket. Cool, peel and slice. Add chopped celery, olives, vinegar, salad oil, salt, pepper, mayo, cottage cheese and apple. Mix gently.
