ANN'S CHICKEN CASSEROLE


5  c. rice
2  c. (or more) chicken (cooked)
1 1/2   c. celery, chopped
1  small onion, chopped
1  c. mayonnaise
21  oz. (2 cans) soup, cream of mushroom
8  oz. (1 can) water chestnuts
2 to 3  Tbsp. lemon juice
1  c. bread crumbs
1/3  c. (2/3 stick) butter or margarine, melted

      Cook rice according to package instructions and set aside.
      Cut chicken into pieces.
      Mix together all ingredients (except bread crumbs and butter). Refrigerate overnight.
      When ready to cook, pour mixture into a 9x13-inch casserole dish.  Sprinkle bread crumbs over top and pour melted butter over all.
      Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour (or more) until meat is done.
