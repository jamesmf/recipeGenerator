QUICK PRESERVES


2  c. fresh fruit in season
1  Tbsp. vinegar
2  c. sugar

      Use any fresh fruit in season or frozen fruit:  peaches, blackberries, strawberries, cherries, pears, apples.
      Combine fruit and vinegar in medium saucepan.  Bring to a boil and boil one minute with lid on saucepan.  Remove lid; add sugar and cook on medium heat for 20 minutes.  Let cool.
      This old recipe comes from western Kentucky and is delicious. Makes 1 pint.
