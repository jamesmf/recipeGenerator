EASY CROCKPOT CHINESE-STYLE PORK RIBS


4  lb. pork ribs
1/4  c. soy sauce
1  clove garlic, crushed
1/4  c. orange marmalade
2  Tbsp. catsup

      Combine soy sauce, marmalade, catsup and garlic. Brush on both sides of ribs. Place in crockpot. Pour remaining sauce over all. Cover and cook on low for 8 to 10 hours. Yield: 4-6 servings.
