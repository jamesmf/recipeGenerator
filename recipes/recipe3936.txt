BEER CHEESE


2  lbs velveeta cheese (soft)
  1---12 oz. stale beer (Budweiser)
  1---16 oz. Helmanns Mayonnaise
1  tsp. onion powder
1  tsp. garlic powder
2  tsp. red pepper (Cayenne)
2  tsp. tabasco sauce

      Open and let beer sit over night. Mix everything into a big mixing bowl. Beat until real smooth. Cover and let sit in refrigerator a day or so, then serve.
