PIZZA ROLL


1  loaf frozen bread dough, thawed
3  Tbsp. butter, melted
1  tsp. oregano, add to melted butter
8  oz. shredded Mozzarella cheese
  pepperoni, or other meat of your choice
  mushrooms, if desired
  pizza sauce

      Roll out bread dough, use flour underneath.  Brush with butter/oregano mixture.  Sprinkle with Mozzarella cheese and meat. Add mushrooms.  Roll up the long way and pinch dough together. Lay the dough on cookie sheet, pinched side down.  Brush with butter/oregano mixture again.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes, or until it bursts open and cheese oozes out.  Cut into slices and dip in pizza sauce.
