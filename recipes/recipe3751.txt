VEGETABLE RELISH DISH


1 large  can English peas, drained
1 (12 oz.)  can Shoepeg corn, drained
1 (14 to 16 oz.)  can green beans, drained (not French cut)
1  c. celery, chopped
1  c. onion, chopped
1  c. green pepper, chopped
1 medium  jar cooked pimento, drained and chopped

	Stir all ingredients together in a large bowl.
Sauce:
1/2  c. Wesson oil
1/4  c. sugar
3/4  c. apple cider vinegar
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. pepper

	Mix sauce ingredients.	Pour sauce over vegetables and let sit overnight in refrigerator.	Stir occasionally to mix.  (Will last for a week in the refrigerator.)
