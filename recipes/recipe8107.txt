BAKED WHOLE SALMON


1  large salmon 10 - 15 lbs.
  salt
  pepper
  lemon
  butter

	Rub fish with salt, pepper and lemon.  Wrap in foil.  Bake slowly at 250 - 300 degrees; for three hours or until done.  Remove foil. Remove skin.  Place salmon on large platter.  Serve with melted butter and lemon.  Perfect side dishes to accompany the baked salmon are parsleyed new potatoes and green peas with pearl onions.  Lemon meringue pie would make an excellent dessert.
