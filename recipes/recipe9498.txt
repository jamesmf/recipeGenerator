TACO BAKE


2  tubes crescent rolls
1  lb. ground beef
1  can refried beans
1  (12 oz.) bag sharp Cheddar cheese, shredded
1  pack taco seasoning
1  carton sour cream (if desired)

	Brown hamburger meat; drain excess oil. Add taco seasoning as directed on pack. Spray 13 x 9 baking dish with Pam cooking spray. Take one tube of crescent rolls and line bottom of dish. Then add one layer of refried beans and one layer of meat. Then add the next tube of crescent rolls. Layer the beans and meat again and add your shredded cheese to the top. Bake at 350 degrees; for about 20 minutes. Take out of oven and let it cool. Top with sour cream.
