PEPPER STEAK


1  lb. round steak, cut in thin strips
1  small chopped onion
1/2  c. chopped celery
1/2  tsp. black pepper
1/2  tsp. sugar
1  c. water with 2 cubes (beef bouillon)
1  green pepper, cut in strips
1  Tbsp. cornstarch
1  Tbsp. soy sauce

	Brown round steak with next 5 ingredients. Simmer 1 hour. Add green pepper strips over top. Cook 10 minutes, covered. Mix cornstarch and soy sauce well; add to steak. Stir well. Serve over rice.
