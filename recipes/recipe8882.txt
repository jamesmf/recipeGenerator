BACHELOR'S CHICKEN AND DUMPLINGS


1  can flaked chicken
2  cans cheap biscuits
2  cans chicken stock
  salt and pepper to taste

      Put chicken and stock in a pot and bring to a boil. Pinch each biscuit into 4 or 5 pieces and drop into the stock. Cook about 5 or 6 minutes. This receipt is easy to double.
