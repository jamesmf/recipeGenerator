BRODYINKLE'S KRAUT SALAD


2  cans sauerkraut (1 lb. size), rinsed and drained
2  jars (8 oz.) chopped pimentos, drained
1  large onion, chopped
1  large bell pepper, diced
1  bunch green onions (tops too), chopped
1 small  bunch celery hearts, chopped
1/2  tsp. fresh ground pepper
1  c. vinegar
1  c. sugar
1  Tbsp. celery seed

      Combine the vinegar, sugar and celery seed and bring to a boil. Cool and pour over kraut mixture. Refrigerate for 24 hours.
