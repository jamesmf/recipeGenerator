BARBECUE SAUCE


2  Tbsp. butter
1  onion, grated
2  cloves garlic
1/4  tsp. salt
1  Tbsp. chili powder
4  Tbsp. brown sugar
4  Tbsp. vinegar
4  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  c. catsup
1  tsp. Tabasco

      Melt butter in saucepan and cook onion and garlic until soft. Add 1 cup water and remaining ingredients.  Stir until well mixed. Place on low heat and simmer for 30 minutes.  This works well for pork chops, spareribs and chicken.  Great for the grill.
