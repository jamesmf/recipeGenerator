HOMEMADE BOURSIN CHEESE


1  c. whipped butter
2  c. softened cream cheese
1/2  tsp. garlic salt
1/2  tsp. garlic powder
1/4  tsp. dried basil leaves
1/4  tsp. oregano
1/4  tsp. seasoned salt
1/4  tsp. dill weed
1/4  tsp. celery seed

      In a large bowl, mix or knead all ingredients thoroughly. Transfer to an appropriate cheese dish/mold and refrigerate.
