CHILI


1  medium to large onion
1  medium green pepper
1  lb. hamburger
1 (18 oz.)  can tomato sauce
1 small  can tomato paste
1 (14 1/2 oz.)  can tomatoes
1/3  bottle catsup
  Worcestershire sauce
  hot sauce
  Italian seasoning
  oregano
  garlic powder or minced garlic
  onion salt
  salt and pepper to taste
  chili powder
2  cans chili beans (red kidney)

      Brown onion, green pepper and hamburger.  Add tomato sauce, tomato paste, tomatoes and catsup, then add Worcestershire sauce, hot sauce, Italian seasoning, oregano, garlic powder or minced garlic, onion salt, salt and pepper to taste, chili powder and 2 cans chili beans (red kidney).  Simmer for 2 hours.
