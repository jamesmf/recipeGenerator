BAKED PINEAPPLE


1 large  can crushed pineapple
1  c water
1  c sugar
2  tbsp cornstarch
2  egg yolks, save the whites
1  tbsp butter
  dash of salt

      Mix pineapple, water, sugar, cornstarch, egg yolks, butter and salt on top of flame until thickened.  Beat egg whites until peaks form.  Fold into mixture.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes.
