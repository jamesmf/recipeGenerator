KIBBEE WITH LABAN
(Kibbee Labaneea)  

  Basic Kibbee recipe using 1/2 c. more burghul

 
Laban Sauce:
1 1/2 - 2  qt. yogurt or laban
1  egg
2 -3  tbsp. flour
  Salt to taste
1 1/2- 2  qt. cold water
2  cloves garlic, chopped
  Rendered butter or oleo

      Make kibbee balls.  Pour 1 quart of yogurt, 1 egg white, salt and flour into a large saucepan.  Using a hand mixer, beat till smooth.  Add cold water and blend together.  Bring to a boil, stirring constantly with a wooden spoon so mixture will not curdle. When boiling, add kibbee balls.  In a saut√© pan, brown garlic in rendered butter or oleo.  Add to sauce and cook over low heat for 15-20 mins.
