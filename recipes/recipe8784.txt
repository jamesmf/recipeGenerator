FAMILY MEAT LOAF


1 1/2  lb. ground beef
1  c. soft bread crumbs
1  egg, slightly beaten
1/2  c. chopped onion
1/3  c. ketchup
1  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper

      Combine ingredients.  Form into a loaf (8 x 4 x 2) in shallow baking pan.  Bake in a 350 degrees; oven for 1 hour.  Let meat loaf stand 5 minutes before slicing.  Makes 6 to 8 servings.
