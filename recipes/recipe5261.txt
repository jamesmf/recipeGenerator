CRANBERRY SALAD


3  c. ground cranberries*
1  c. sugar
1  pkg. lemon Jell-O
2  c. boiling water
1  c. chopped celery
1  c. slivered almonds

	Add sugar to the ground cranberries and let stand 1 hour. Dissolve lemon Jell-O in boiling water. When it starts to congeal, add cranberries.  Add celery and almonds.  Place in pan and set in refrigerator to congeal.  Use dressing to taste.
	 (Note:  *If you freeze cranberries, they grind easier.)
