EASY ONION POPOVERS


3  eggs
1  c. milk
1  c. sifted flour
3  Tbsp. chopped onion
1/4  tsp. salt
1  Tbsp. melted shortening or oil

      Blend eggs, milk, onion, salt and oil. Add flour, beat until smooth. Beat one minute in electric mixer, or 3 minutes with rotary mixer. Fill well greased custard cups 1/2 full. Bake 400 degrees; for 40-45 minutes till golden, firm.
