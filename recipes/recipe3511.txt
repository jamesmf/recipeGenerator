CRAB RANGOON


8  oz. cream cheese
8  oz. crabmeat, flaked
1  clove garlic, finely minced
1/2  tsp. Worcestershire
1/4  cup green onion, thinly sliced
1  lb. wonton wrappers
1  egg yolk, beaten
  Oil for deep-frying

      Mix first 5 ingredients.  Refrigerate 30 minutes.  Place 1 tsp. of mixture on wrappers.  Moisten edges with egg yolk.  Fold to form a triangle.  Moisten the two furthermost points with egg yold and fold under.  Deep-fry in hot oil 2-3 minutes, until golden.
