QUICK AND TASTY GRILLED BEEF ROAST


3  lb. chuck roast
1  pkg. Lipton beefy onion soup mix

      Marinate roast in beefy onion soup mix all day and night (turn often).  Preheat gas grill; sear roast on both sides.  Turn grill to low setting and baste well.  Cook until inside temperature is about 140 degrees; to 150 degrees;.
