SPAGHETTI PIE


6  oz. spaghetti noodles
1/3  c. grated parmesan
1  c. cottage cheese
2  tbsp. butter
2  eggs

      Cook noodles; drain.  Melt butter in pan.  Toss in noodles. Beat eggs well, toss in cheese and then toss noodles well again. Line a (greased) pie pan or 8 x 8 inch pan with noodle mixture.
      LAYER:  Cottage cheese on top of noodle mixture then add your favorite sauce with ground beef.  Bake 20 minutes on 350 degrees; oven. Sprinkle top with 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese and bake for 5 minutes longer or until golden brown.
