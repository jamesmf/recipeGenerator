TACO SALAD


1 head lettuce or 1  pkg. salad
1  to 2 cucumbers
1 small  can chopped black olives
1  to 2 tomatoes
  Ranch dressing
1  bag Doritos taco chips

      Combine lettuce (wash and drain; same for bag of salad), peeled cucumbers (cut into small pieces), tomatoes (cut into small pieces) and black olives; refrigerate until ready to serve.
      Once ready to serve, add Ranch dressing (until mixture is covered) and crushed taco chips.
