LASAGNA MILANESE


1  pkg. (8 oz.) wide noodles
1  pkg. frozen chopped spinach
1  medium size onion, chopped
1  clove garlic, minced
2  Tbsp salad oil or olive oil
1  lb. ground beef
1  can (3 oz.) mushrooms
1  can tomato paste
1 (8 oz.)  can tomato sauce
1  bay leaf, crumbled
2  tsp. salt
2  tsp. oregano
2  eggs
1/4  c. chopped parsley
1  c. cottage cheese (small curd)
1/4   c. grated Parmesan cheese
1  tsp. basil
  sharp Cheddar cheese

	Cook and drain noodles.  Thaw and drain spinach.  In medium size frying pan brown onion and garlic slightly in 1 tablespoon oil.  Add ground beef. Cook until brown.  Stir in mushrooms including the liquid, tomato paste, bay leaf, 1 teaspoon of salt and oregano.  Simmer for 15 minutes.  Beat eggs in bowl; pour over noodles that are rinsed with cold water. Mix well.	Beat second egg inn bowl.  Add spinach, 1 tablespoon oil, parsley, cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, 1 teaspoon salt and basil.  Mix well. Pour 1/2 tomato mixture into oblong shallow dish.  Layer 1/2 noodles on sauce.  Spread the spinach mix.	Repeat noodle layer. Top with rest of tomato mix. Cover.  Bake in 350 degrees; oven for 45 minutes. Remove from oven. Arrange strips of cheese on top.  Bake until cheese is melted (5 minutes).  Serves 6 to 8.
