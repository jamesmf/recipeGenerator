CRANBERRY SALAD


1  can jellied cranberries
1 (8 oz.)  carton Cool Whip
  crushed soda crackers

      Refrigerate the cranberries until cold. Open can and push cranberries out onto a plate. Cut into slices. In a clear bowl, layer cranberries, Cool Whip, crushed soda crackers, cranberries, Cool Whip and crushed soda crackers. I make 3 layers.
