YOUNG FOLKS SLOPPY JOES


3  Tbsp. shortening
3  lb. hamburger
3  large onions, chopped
1  clove garlic, minced
1  Tbsp. salt
1 1/2  tsp. pepper
1/2  tsp. red pepper
2  tsp. chili powder
2  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/4  c. flour
1 1/4  c. canned tomatoes
3/4  c. ketchup

      Melt shortening in heavy  skillet.  Combine  hamburger, onions and garlic.  Cook in  skillet  until  lightly  browned. Add seasonings.   Stir in flour.  Add  tomatoes  and  ketchup mixing well.  Simmer 15 to 20 minutes until thickened.  Yields 20 servings.
