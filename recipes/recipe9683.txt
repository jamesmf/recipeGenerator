CHICKEN PARMESAN


4  skinless boneless chicken breasts
1/2  c. fat-free Parmesan cheese
2  egg whites
1/2  c. dry seasoned bread crumbs

      Pound chicken breasts until size doubles. Rub Parmesan cheese into each side. Dip chicken in egg whites and press bread crumbs on both sides. Saut 3 to 5 minutes per side in skillet sprayed with nonstick spray. Yield: 4 servings.
      Cal-248, Carbs-10g, Pro-36g, Fat-6g.
