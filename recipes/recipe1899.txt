TAQUITOS


1 (16 oz.)  can roast beef or meat of choice
1  doz. corn tortillas
1/2  c. oil
2  medium avocados, mashed
1/2  small onion
1/2  tsp. salt
1  Tbsp. lemon juice
1/2  c. salsa

      Mix avocados, onion, salt and lemon juice.  Set aside.  Warm tortillas over fire in skillet or on grill.  Place roast beef in center of tortillas and roll lengthwise; secure with toothpick. Fry in skillet with oil.  When done, remove and place on plate with paper towel.  Serve with avocado mixture or salsa as desired.
