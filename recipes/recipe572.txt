HOMEMADE CATSUP


3 (1 lb.)  cans whole tomatoes
1/4  c. malt or red wine vinegar
1/4  c. chopped onions
2  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/2  tsp. salt
1  clove garlic, crushed
2  pkg. Sweet 'N Low

      Place contents of one can of tomatoes in blender container; cover and process on medium speed for 30 to 40 seconds, or until smooth.  Repeat with each can.  In a large saucepan, combine tomatoes with all remaining ingredients except Sweet 'N Low. Simmer, uncovered, until sauce is reduced and thickened, 30 to 40 minutes.  Stir in sweetener.  One tablespoon has 6 calories.
