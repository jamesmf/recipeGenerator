SAUSAGE AND EGG CASSEROLE


3/4  lb. pork sausage
10  eggs
1  c. milk
3/4  tsp. salt
6  oz. (1 1/2 c.) grated cheese
1 1/2  c. croutons (garlic onion flavored)

      Brown sausage over medium heat. Drain fat. Place sausage in a greased 2 quart casserole dish.
      In a bowl beat eggs; stir in milk, salt and cheese. Pour this over sausage. Sprinkle with croutons.
      Bake uncovered in a 325 degrees; oven for about 45 minutes. Let stand for 5 minutes before serving.
