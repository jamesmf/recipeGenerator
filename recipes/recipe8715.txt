CHILI-CHEESE BAKE


1  c. uncooked rice
2  Tbsp. butter
1/2  c. onions, finely chopped
1/2  tsp. salt
1  c. canned milk
1  pt. sour cream (original)
1 (4 oz.)  can green chilies, chopped
  paprika
1/2  lb. Monterey Jack cheese, grated

	Cook rice per directions.  Set aside. Preheat oven to 325 degrees;.
	Make onion sauce by sauting onions in butter. Add salt, canned milk and sour cream after removing from heat.
	 In a 2-quart casserole dish, layer rice, sour cream, chilies, onion sauce and paprika.  Top with Monterey Jack cheese. Bake for 30 minutes or until bubbly hot.
	 This dish can be made a day ahead, refrigerated and then cooked for about 45 minutes.
