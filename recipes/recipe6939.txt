PORK CHOPS ON RICE PILAF


6  pork chops
1  Tbsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper
3  tsp. shortening
2  c. cooked rice
1/4  tsp. thyme
1  green pepper, diced
1  medium onion, diced
2 1/2  c. chicken stock or bouillon

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees;. Season chops with salt and pepper and brown in shortening. Remove chops and brown uncooked rice in drippings. Add remaining ingredients to rice. Place chops over rice. Cover and bake 1 hour.
