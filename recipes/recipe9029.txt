CHICKEN CASSEROLE


1  large fryer, boiled and boned (save broth)
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  can chicken broth
1  stick margarine
1  large Pepperidge Farm (herb) dressing mix or 2 small pkg.

      Mix 3 cans of soup thoroughly. Put chicken in bottom of casserole dish. Pour 3 combined soups over chicken. Melt margarine and stir into dressing mix and finish moistening with chicken broth. Spoon over the top of chicken and bake at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes or until top is golden brown.
       Add 1 small chopped onion and 2  chopped celery sticks to 3 cans soup. This makes it better and 1 can cream of celery soup (4 cans).
