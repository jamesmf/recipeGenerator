GRANDMOTHER SOUDER'S CHOW CHOW


1  gal. green tomatoes
1  large head cabbage
1  large onion (optional)
3  pt. vinegar
1  pt. sugar
1  Tbsp. salt
1  Tbsp. allspice
1  Tbsp. black pepper
1  Tbsp. mustard

      Grind tomatoes and cabbage; add the onion (if you preferred) and grind also.  Put in a large pan, cook until tender.  Put spices in a cloth when cool.  Add to tomatoes and bring to a boil. Put in hot jars and seal.
