KIBBEE BALLS


  Basic kibbee recipe using 1/2 c. more burghul

 
Filling:
  Rendered butter
  Pine nuts
1  onion, chopped
  Mint
  Gd. cumin
1  lb. gd. lamb or gd. chuck, opt

      Form meat into small cigar-shaped balls, moistening hands with cold water when necessary.  Perforate one end only and stuff middle of ball with filling mixture.  Close end.  Lay on tray. Can freeze at this point.  When frozen, remove from tray and put in freezer bags.  Can be deep fried, baked or grilled.
      FILLING: Brown pine nuts in butter till golden.  Remove nuts and saut√© meat and onions.  Add nuts and mix well.  Cool.
