SWEET POTATO PECAN BALLS


1 1/4  c. cooked, mashed sweet potatoes
1/2  c. sugar
2  Tbsp. butter, softened
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. ground cinnamon
1  tsp. vanilla extract
3  c. ground pecans
1/4  c. strawberry jelly

      Combine all ingredients except pecans and jelly in a large bowl, and blend well with a potato masher.  Shape into balls, using a measuring tablespoonful for each.  Roll balls in ground pecans until coated.  Place on a large cookie sheet at least 2 inches apart.  Indent top of each ball with a spoon, and fill with 1/2 tsp. jelly.  Bake in preheated 350 degrees; oven 15 to 20 minutes or until nuts are lightly toasted.  Yield: 24 balls.
