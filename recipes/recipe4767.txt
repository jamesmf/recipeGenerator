YOGURT CUCUMBER SALAD


1  medium cucumber, thinly sliced
1/2  c. thinly sliced red onion
1/2  c. plain low-fat yogurt
4  packets Equal
1/2  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper

      Combine cucumber and onion slices in a bowl. Combine remaining ingredients and pour over slices. Toss. Serve immediately. Makes 4 servings.
