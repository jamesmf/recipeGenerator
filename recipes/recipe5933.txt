POTATO SALAD


3  lb. potatoes
6  boiled eggs, diced
1  c. sweet relish pickles
1  medium onion, minced
1  bell pepper, diced
1 small  jar pimentos
1  c. mayonnaise or enough to moisten good

      Peel and dice potatoes. Cook in saucepan with water, enough to cover. Add salt to taste. When done, drain and cool. Mix all ingredients together; add a little black pepper. Sprinkle a little paprika on top.
