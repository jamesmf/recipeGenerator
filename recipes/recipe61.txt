CHOCOLATE CHIP SOUR CREAM BREAD


1/2  c. butter or margarine
2/3  c. sugar
2  eggs
1  c. sour cream
2  c. flour
1  tsp. baking soda
1  tsp. baking powder
1/2  c. semi-sweet chocolate chips

      Cream together margarine, sugar and eggs.  Mix in sour cream. Stir in flour, baking soda and baking powder.  Lightly stir in chocolate chips.  Grease and flour a 9 x 5 x 3-inch loaf pan.  Pour batter into prepared pan and bake at 350 degrees; for 45 to 50 minutes until done.  Cool.  Cover loosely with foil.
