JIFFY TURKEY PAELLA


1 (1 lb. 12 oz.)  can tomatoes (3 1/2 c.)
1 (1 pt. 2 oz.)  can tomato juice (2 1/4 c.)
1/4  c. cooking oil
1/4  c. chopped onion
1/4  c. chopped green pepper
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. garlic salt
  dash of cayenne pepper
1  c. long grain rice
2 (9 oz.)  pkg. frozen artichoke hearts, thawed
3  c. diced, cooked turkey
1/4  c. sliced, stuffed green olives

      In a large saucepan, combine tomatoes, tomato juice, oil, onion, green pepper and seasonings.  Stir in rice.  Bring to boiling.  Add artichoke hearts, turkey and olives.  Turn into a 3-quart casserole.  Bake, covered, in 350 degrees; oven 1 hour and 25 minutes or until rice is tender, stirring once or twice.  If desired, garnish with stuffed green olives.  Makes about 12 servings.
