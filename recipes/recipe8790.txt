LATTICE TOP CHICKEN


1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can condensed cream of potato or mushroom soup
1  c. milk
1/2  tsp. salt
2  c. cooked and cubed chicken
1  (16 oz.) bag frozen broccoli, cauliflower and carrots
1  c. shredded cheese
1 (2.8 oz.)  can Durkee onions
1 (14 oz.)  pkg. crescent rolls

      Combine soup, milk, salt, chicken, veggies, 1/2 cup cheese and 1/2 cup Durkee onions.  Place in 8 x 12 dish.  Bake, covered, at 375 degrees; for 20 minutes.
      Unwrap crescent rolls; separate into 2 rectangles.  Press together perforated cuts.  Cut each rectangle lengthwise into 3 strips.  Place strips on casserole to form lattice top.  Bake, uncovered, 15 minutes.
      Top lattice with remaining cheese and onions.  Bake, uncovered, 3 to 5 minutes or until golden brown.  Make 4 to 6 servings.
