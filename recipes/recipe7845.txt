CHICKEN ENCHILADA CASSEROLE


2 to 3  c. cubed cooked chicken
1/4  c. oleo
1  c. chopped green pepper
1  c. chopped onion
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  can Rotel tomatoes
12  torn up corn tortillas
2  c. shredded cheddar cheese

      In large pan cook pepper and onion in oleo  3 to 4 minutes. Remove from heat and add all other ingredients.  In 13 x 9-inch pan, alternate tortillas, soup mixture and cheese twice.  Heat in 325 degrees; oven till bubbling.
