CHICKEN CASSEROLE


1 (10 1/4 oz.)  can cream of chicken soup
1  can cream of celery soup
1  c. water
1  c. raw rice
1  pkg. dried onion soup
4  chicken breast halves

      Mix the soups together. Put rice in 2-quart casserole dish. Pour soups over rice. Arrange chicken breasts on top of rice. Sprinkle onion soup over chicken. Bake at 325 degrees; for 1 1/2 hours. Serves 6.
