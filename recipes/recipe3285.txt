POPCORN CRACKER JACKS


1  c. unpopped corn
2  c. brown sugar
1/2  c. white Karo syrup
2  sticks oleo
1/2  tsp. soda

      Pop corn and set aside.  Bring to a boil and boil 5 minutes the brown sugar, Karo and oleo.  Remove from heat and add soda.  Pour mixture over popcorn and stir well to coat.  Put on 2 foil lined cookie sheets.  Bake 1 hour and 10 minutes in a 225 degrees; oven.  Stir every 15 minutes to break apart.  Cool well and store in tightly covered container.  Can add cooked peanuts to popcorn before pouring syrup over mix.
