5 CUP SALAD


1 small  can mandarin oranges
1 regular  can pineapple chunks
1 -8  oz. sour cream
1  c. coconut
1  c. mini-marshmallows

      Drain juice off of pineapple and oranges.  Put all ingredients in medium to large bowl and fold together until mixed thoroughly.  Cover and refrigerate for 2 hours or until chilled.
