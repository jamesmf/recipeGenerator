BEST POTATO SALAD


1  c. mayonnaise
2  Tbsp. prepared spicy brown mustard
1/4  tsp. ground celery seed
1  tsp. salt
1  Tbsp. dehydrated onion flakes
1/2  tsp. dried dill weed
1/4  tsp. lemon pepper
4  c. cubed, cooked potatoes
8  hard-cooked eggs, peeled and chopped

      Combine mayonnaise, mustard, celery seed, dill, onion flakes, salt and pepper; mix well.  Add potatoes and eggs; mixing lightly. Chill.  Serves 6.
