POPPY SEED CHICKEN


1 large  can chicken
1 family size  can cream of chicken soup
16  oz. sour cream
2  boxes Stove Top cornbread stuffing
  poppy seeds

      Combine chicken, soup and sour cream and put in 9 x 13 baking dish. Prepare the stuffing per recipe on the box. Spread over the top of the chicken. Sprinkle poppy seeds over the top. Bake at 350 degrees; for 20 minutes or until bubbly.
