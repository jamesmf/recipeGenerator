TRADITIONAL FUDGE


2  Tbsp. butter
2/3  c. evaporated milk
1 1/2  c. sugar
1/4  tsp. salt
2  c. miniature marshmallows
1 1/2  c. semi-sweet chocolate chips
1/2  c. nuts (optional)
1  tsp. vanilla

      Combine butter, evaporated milk, sugar and salt in saucepan. Bring to a boil on medium heat, stirring constantly.  Boil 4 to 5 minutes.  Remove from stove and immediately add marshmallows, chips, nuts and vanilla.  Stir until blended and pour into pan to set.
