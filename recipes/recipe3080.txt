SWEET AND SOUR MEATBALLS


2  lb. ground beef
1  c. milk
1  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper
1  Tbsp. onion, chopped
1  c. soda crackers, crushed

 
Sauce:
1/3  c. vinegar
1  c. catsup
3/4  c. brown sugar
2  Tbsp. onion salt
1/2  tsp. salt
  dash of pepper

      Mix ground beef, milk, crackers, onion, salt and pepper. Form into meatballs. Don't brown. Place in 9-inch baking dish.
      Combine vinegar, catsup, brown sugar, onion salt and pepper. Pour over meatballs. Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour. (Cover with foil while baking.)
