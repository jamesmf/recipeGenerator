BROCCOLI SALAD


4  c. chopped broccoli
1 1/2  c. seedless red grapes, halved
1  c. mayonnaise
1/3  c. sugar
2  Tbsp. cider vinegar
12  slices bacon, cooked and crumbled
1/4  c. shelled sunflower seeds

      Optional:  Add 2 cups cauliflower, chopped.
       Combine broccoli and grapes. Mix mayonnaise, sugar and vinegar. Add to broccoli mixture. Toss. Chill. Before serving, add bacon and sunflower seeds.
