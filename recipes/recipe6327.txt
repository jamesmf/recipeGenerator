MCFARLAIN'S BARNYARD CHICKEN SALAD

Dressing:
1  cup Ranch Dressing
1 small  carton sour cream
1  cup mayonnaise
1/2  tsp mustard
1/2  tsp white pepper
1/2  tsp salt
1/4  cup chopped celery
1/4  cup onions
2  cups chopped cooked chicken (1/2 inch cubed)
1/2  cup dressing

      Toss and chill before serving.
