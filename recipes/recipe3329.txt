YELLOW CHEESE BALL


1  large cream cheese, softened
1  glass Roka Blue cheese
1  glass Old English cheese
1  glass Vera Sharp or 1 roll Nippy
1  Tbsp. wine vinegar
2  cloves garlic, mashed with 1/2 to 1 tsp. salt

      Blend soft cream cheese (with mixer) with rest of ingredients. Form into ball and chill.  Roll in chopped pecans.  (Can be frozen.)
