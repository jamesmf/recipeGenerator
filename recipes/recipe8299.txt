PORK CHOP CASSEROLE


6  pork chops
2  Tbsp. flour
1  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper
1  Tbsp. parsley, minced
2  c. raw potatoes, sliced
2  c. hot milk

	Brown chops on both sides in hot frying pan. Grease deep casserole dish and place 3 chops in dish. Combine flour, salt, pepper and parsley. Sprinkle half over chops. Cover with half of potatoes. Repeat layers. Heat milk in skillet in which chops were browned. Cover and bake at 350 degrees; for 1 1/2 hours. Uncover for the last 20 minutes to brown top.
