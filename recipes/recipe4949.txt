TURKEY-WALNUT SALAD


2  c. chopped cooked turkey
1/2  c. dried cranberries
1/2  c. mayonnaise
1/4  c. chopped walnuts, toasted
3  Tbsp. chopped fresh parsley
2  Tbsp. Dijon mustard
2  celery ribs, sliced
1  small red onion, chopped (about 1/2 c.)
1/4  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
  Mixed salad greens

      Stir together turkey and next 9 ingredients in a bowl.  Cover and chill at least 30 minutes.  Serve over salad greens.  Serves 6.
