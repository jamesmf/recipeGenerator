BROWNIES


2  Tbsp. margarine
1/3  c. sugar
1/4  c. cold water
1/2  tsp. vanilla
1/2  c. all-purpose flour
2  Tbsp. unsweetened cocoa powder
1/2  tsp. baking powder
2  Tbsp. finely chopped walnuts or pecans
1  tsp. powdered sugar

      Melt margarine; stir in sugar, water and vanilla.  Add flour, cocoa powder and baking powder and mix well.  Stir in nuts.  Pour batter into an 8 x 4 x 2-inch loaf pan sprayed with nonstick cooking spray.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.  Cool thoroughly.  Remove from pan and cut into 8 bars.  Sprinkle with powdered sugar.  Makes 8 servings.
      Per Serving:  104 Calories, 4 g. Fat, 0 mg.  Cholesterol and 68 mg.  Sodium.
