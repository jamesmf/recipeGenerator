DELIGHTFUL KIELBASA


1  lb kielbasa
16  oz sauerkraut, drained and rinsed
2  medium potatoes, peeled and cubed
2  apples, peeled, cooked and chopped
1/3-1/2  c water
  salt and pepper to taste

      Slice the kielbasa into 1/2 inch slices.  Brown in a skillet over medium heat for 3-4 minutes.  Stir occasionally.  Put potatoes in bowl and microwave on high for 5 minutes.  Add potatoes, sauerkraut, apples, water, salt and pepper to skillet. Mix well. Bring to a boil.  Lower to medium heat. Cover and simmer for 20 minutes or until apples and potatoes are tender.  Stir occasionally.  Add some Hungarian paprika before serving.
