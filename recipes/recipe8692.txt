PECAN ORCHARD CHICKEN


4  (6 oz.) chicken breasts
  salt and pepper to taste
1  lb. unsalted butter
1 (8 oz.)  jar orange marmalade
1/2  c. brown sugar
2  oz. lemon juice
2  c. pecans (medium pieces)

	Pound chicken breast to 1/2-inch thickness; salt and pepper and set aside.
	In a saucepan, combine all other ingredients, except pecans. Cook and stir slowly until bubbly and thick.  Add pecans and simmer 5 minutes more.
	Cook chicken breasts on barbecue (coals or gas grill) until juices run clear. Place chicken on large platter and top with sauce.
