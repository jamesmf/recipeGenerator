SCALLOPS ORIENTAL
(Low Cholesterol)  

2  lb. fresh scallops
1/4  c. honey
1/4  c. prepared mustard
1  tsp. curry powder
1  tsp. lemon juice
  lemon wedges

      Rinse scallops well and place in pan.  Combine next 4 ingredients and mix.  Brush scallops with the mixture.  Broil at 425 degrees; for 10 minutes or until slightly browned.  Turn scallops. Brush with remaining sauce.  Broil 10 minutes longer or until well browned.  Garnish with lemon wedges.  Serves 6.
