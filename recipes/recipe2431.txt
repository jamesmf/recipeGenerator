PEANUT BUTTER CUPCAKES
(And Frosting)  

2  c. flour
1/3  c. shortening
2  eggs
1  c. milk
1 1/2  c. brown sugar
1  tsp. vanilla
1/2  c. crunchy peanut butter

      Cream peanut butter and shortening; gradually add brown sugar, beating until light.  Add vanilla.  Add eggs (one at a time) beating until fluffy.  Add flour and milk alternately, beating after each addition.  Bake at 375 degrees; for 30 to 35 minutes.
Frosting:
1/2  c. peanut butter
1  lb. box confectioners sugar
7 to 8  Tbsp. milk
1  tsp. vanilla

      Blend 1/3 of sugar and peanut butter.  Alternately add remaining sugar and milk.  Add vanilla and ice the cupcakes.
