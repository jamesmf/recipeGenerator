BREAKFAST SAUSAGE & EGG CASSEROLE


1  pkg. sausage
6  eggs, slightly beaten
2  c. milk
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. dry mustard
2  slices day old bread, crushed
1  c. cheddar cheese, grated
3  Tbsp. onion, chopped

      Brown sausage in skillet.  Drain.  Mix all ingredients together and pour into an 11 x 7 x 2 casserole dish.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes.  Serves 6-8.
