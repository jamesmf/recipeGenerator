PARTY SALAD

1st Step:
1  pkg. family size lemon or orange Jell-O, dissolved in 4 c. hot water
1  c. marshmallows
3  diced bananas
1  can chunk pineapple, drained

      When Jell-O has set up and firmed, layer the top of Jell-O with marshmallows, bananas and pineapple.
2nd Step:
1  can pineapple juice (from drained pineapple)
1/2  c. sugar
1  egg, well beaten
1  Tbsp. flour
1 small  carton sour cream

      Boil pineapple juice, sugar and egg until thick.  When very cold, add sour cream.  Mix well and spread over salad.
