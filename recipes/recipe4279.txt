SLOW COOKER TACO SOUP


1  lb. ground beef
1  onion, chopped
2 (16 oz.)  cans chili beans, drained
1 (8 oz.)  can tomato sauce
2  c. water
2 (14.5 oz.)  cans peeled and diced tomatoes
1  pkg. taco seasoning mix
1  can chopped green chiles
1 (15 oz.)  can whole kernel corn, drained
1  Tbsp. chili powder
3/4  tsp. cayenne pepper (optional)

      Brown ground beef and drain. Combine all ingredients in a slow cooker and cook on low setting for 8 hours.
