MEXICAN DIP


2  can bean dip
8  oz sour cream
2/3  cup mayonnaise
1  packet taco seasoning
  Deans Guacamole dip

 
Topping:
  chopped green onions
  chopped tomato
  ripe sliced olives
2  cups shredded cheddar cheese

      Layer the above ingredients on large plate or shallow baking dish. Sour cream, mayonnaise, packet taco seasoning, should be mixed together.
