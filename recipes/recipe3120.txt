TEXAS TRASH


1  box Corn Chex
1  box Rice Chex
1  box Wheat Chex
1  box Cheerios
2  boxes slim pretzels
2  c. pecans
2  cans cashew nuts
2  cans peanuts
2  Tbsp. garlic powder
4  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
2  Tbsp. Louisiana Red Hot sauce
1  lb. butter

      Melt butter and mix spices. Mix all other ingredients and pour butter and spice mixture over it. Mix well. Bake at 250 degrees; for 2 hours; check often.
