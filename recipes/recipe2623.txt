SAUSAGE BREAD


1  loaf frozen bread dough, thawed
  garlic powder to taste
  pepper to taste
1/2 to 1  lb. sausage (Italian)
  red and green pepper, chopped
1  small onion, chopped
2  eggs, beaten
  Mozzarella cheese (8 oz.), grated
  melted butter
  sesame seed (for sprinkling)

      Cut bread loaf in half; roll each half into a square.  Sprinkle with garlic powder and pepper.  In a frying pan, crumble up sausage and add chopped pepper and onion; saut√© until done.  Drain.  Mix with 3/4 of the beaten eggs.  Put 1/2 of sausage mixture in middle of each square of bread.  Put grated Mozzarella cheese on top, and fold bread up letter style.  Brush sides of bread with egg mixture to seal edges.  Baste top with butter and sprinkle with sesame seed.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes or until brown.
