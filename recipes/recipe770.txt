CHICKEN SALAD MANDARIN

Dressing:
1/2  c. mayonnaise or salad dressing
1/3  c. creamy French salad dressing
1/2  tsp. curry powder
1/2  tsp. salt
  dash of pepper

 
Salad:
3  c. diced, cooked chicken or turkey
1 1/2  c. cooked long grain rice, chilled
1 (11 oz.)  can mandarin oranges, drained
1  c. diced celery

      Dressing:  Combine mayonnaise, French dressing, curry powder, salt and pepper.
      Salad:  In large bowl, combine chicken, rice, oranges and celery.  Pour salad dressing mixture over all.  Toss lightly. Chill at least 1 hour.  Serves 6 to 8.
