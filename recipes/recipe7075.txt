CHEESE BAKE


3  eggs, beaten
2  cans tomato sauce
1  c. milk
1  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
8  slices bread
1/2  lb. American cheese, grated

      Mix eggs, tomato sauce, milk and seasonings. Arrange layers of bread, cheese and sauce mixture in greased square pan. Bake at 325 degrees; for 50 minutes.
