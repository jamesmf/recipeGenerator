EASY CHICKEN BREAST


4  chicken breasts with bone
  Italian dressing
  Salt, pepper
4  slices tomatoes
  garlic powder
4  pats of butter
  Lemon juice
4  sheets of aluminum foil
  Shredded cheddar cheese

      Place 1 chicken breast on foil.  Top with dressing, a slice of tomato, pat of butter, garlic powder, lemon juice, salt, pepper and shredded cheese.  Wrap.  Repeat for additional pieces.  Bake 425 degrees; for 30-45 mins.
