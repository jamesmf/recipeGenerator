FRESH TOMATO SALSA


3  medium chopped tomatoes (about 3 c.)
1/2  c. sliced green onions with tops
1/2  c. chopped peppers (green)
2 to 3  Tbsp. lime juice
2  Tbsp. snipped fresh cilantro
1  Tbsp. finely chopped jalapeno chili pepper
1  tsp. finely chopped garlic (about 3 cloves)
1/2  tsp. salt

      Mix all ingredients.  If time permits, refrigerate the salsa for 2 to 3 hours before serving.  Makes about 3 1/2 cups.
