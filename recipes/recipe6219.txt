FROZEN APPLE-SNOW SALAD


1  c. crushed pineapple
2  eggs, well beaten
1/2  c. sugar
1/8  tsp. salt
3  Tbsp. fresh lemon juice
2  c. finely diced unpared apple
1/2  c. finely diced celery
1  c. heavy cream, whipped

      Drain pineapple, reserving syrup.   Add water to pineapple syrup to make 1/2 cup.  Combine syrup mixture, beaten eggs, sugar, salt and lemon juice.  Cook over low heat until thick, stirring constantly.  Chill.  Fold in apple, pineapple, celery and the whipping cream.
      Line muffin pan with paper liners.  Spoon mixture into cups. Freeze and cover with plastic wrap.
      To serve, turn out cup of salad onto lettuce and garnish with apple slices.  Can be frozen in 2-quart refrigerator tray and sliced.
