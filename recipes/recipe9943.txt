SCALLOP PINEAPPLE


4  c. bread crumbs (French or sourdough)
20  oz. can pineapple chunks, drained
3  eggs, beaten
1  c. butter, melted
1 1/2  c. sugar

      Mix bread crumbs and pineapple.  Put in baking dish.  Combine eggs, sugar and butter.  Pour over pineapple.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes.
