COOKIE SALAD


1  c. buttermilk
1 (3 oz.)  pkg. butter pecan or vanilla instant pudding
1 (8 oz.)  carton Cool Whip
1 (15 oz.)  can crushed pineapple, drained
1 (11 oz.)  can mandarin oranges, drained
1/2  pkg. fudge-strip cookies or chocolate frosted graham crackers, broken up

      Mix first 5 ingredients in order listed.  Close to serving time, add fudge-strip cookies or chocolate frosted graham crackers, broken up.
