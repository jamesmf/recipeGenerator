TERRIFIC TACOS


12  taco shells or tortillas (try soft tortillas made from corn or whole wheat)
1 (16 oz.)  can fat-free refried beans
2  tomatoes, chopped
2  c. shredded romaine or iceberg lettuce
4  oz. low-fat Cheddar cheese, shredded
  nonfat sour cream
  salsa

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees;. If using taco shells, place on baking sheet and bake for 6 minutes. If using tortillas, warm slightly.
       Heat beans in microwave or on stove top. In a medium bowl, combine tomatoes and lettuce. Fill taco shells or tortillas first with beans, then top with tomato-lettuce mixture, cheese, sour cream and salsa. Makes 12 low-fat tacos.
