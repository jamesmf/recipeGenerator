  vegetable cooking spray
2  Tbsp. lo-cal margarine
1  lb. fresh sugar snap peas
2 1/2  c. diagonally sliced celery
1  lb. fresh sea scallops
1/4  c. Chablis or dry white wine
3  Tbsp. lemon juice
1/2  tsp. dried whole dill weed
1/2  tsp. freshly ground pepper
2  Tbsp. chopped fresh parsley

      Coat a large nonstick skillet with cooking oil or spray. Place over medium heat until margarine melts.  Add peas and celery; saut 3 to 4 minutes or until crisp-tender.  Remove vegetables from skillet using a slotted spoon; set aside. Add scallops and next 4 ingredients to skillet.  Bring to a boil. Cover, reduce heat and simmer 5 to 6 minutes or until scallops are opaque.  Add vegetables and cook until heated.  Sprinkle with chopped parsley.
      "Served with a salad, very lo-cal."
