CHILI CON CARNE


1  lb. ground beef lean
1 1/4  tsp. salt
1  c. chopped onions
1  c. finely cut celery
1 (16 oz.)  can tomatoes, cut up with liquid
1/2  c. green pepper, cut fine
1/4  tsp. garlic powder
1/2  tsp. oregano
1  Tbsp. chili powder
1 (16 oz.)  can kidney beans
1/2  c. beef broth

      In 2 1/2-quart pot, Teflon or spray with Pam, place beef in pot.  Sprinkle with salt.  Cook until meat changes color.  Add onions, celery and green pepper; mix well.  Cover and cook over medium heat 2 to 3 minutes.  Add tomatoes and liquid and all seasonings, mixing well.  Bring to a boil; cover and turn heat low and simmer gently for 25 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Add kidney beans and beef broth.  Continue cooking over low heat for 15 to 20 minutes.
