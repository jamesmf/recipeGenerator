BREAKFAST PIZZA


1  can crescent rolls
  sausage
1  c. Mozzarella cheese
1  c. Cheddar cheese
6  eggs
1/2  c. milk

      Spread rolls on cookie sheet and bake for 5 minutes.  Brown sausage and sprinkle on top.  Sprinkle cheese on top of sausage. Mix 6 eggs and milk.  Pour slowly on top.  Bake at 325 degrees; for about 30 minutes or until done.
