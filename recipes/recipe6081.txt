POTATO SOUP


5  lb. potatoes
1/2  c. butter
1/2  c. flour
1  c. milk
2  c. Cheddar cheese, shredded
  salt (to taste)
  pepper (to taste)

      Boil potatoes in water.  In a separate pan, melt butter then add flour stiring until smooth.  Add milk, cheese, salt and pepper.
      Drain 1/4 of the water from the potatoes.  Add the cheese mixture to the potatoes.  If it is too watery, let it simmer longer.  If it is too thick, add more milk.
      Serve while hot.
