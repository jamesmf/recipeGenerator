SPINACH ROLL-UPS


1 (10 oz.)  pkg. frozen chopped spinach
1  c. mayo
1  (8 oz.) sour cream
1  bunch green onions, chopped
1 (1 oz.)  envelope Ranch style dressing mix
1 (3 oz.)  jar bacon bits
9  (10-inch) flour tortillas

      Thaw and drain spinach.  Stir together first 6 ingredients. Evenly spread onto flour tortillas.  Roll up, jellyroll fashion, pressing edges to seal.  Wrap in plastic wrap.  Chill 4 to 6 hours.  Cut into 1/2-inch thick slices.
