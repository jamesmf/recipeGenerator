PIZZA CASSEROLE


2  pounds ground chuck
1  onion
1  green pepper
1  quart ragu sauce
1  can cream of mushroom soup
12  ounce bag of wide egg noodles
3  cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
13  cups cheddar cheese, shredded
  sliced pepperoni

      Brown meat with onions and peppers.  Drain.  Add ragu and cream of mushroom soup.  Cook egg noodles for 4 minutes in boiling water, drain.  Add cooked noodles to beef mixture.  Place beef and noodle mix in a deep 9 by 13 inch pan.  Cover with mozzarella and cheddar. Top with pepperoni.  Cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes at 350 degrees.
