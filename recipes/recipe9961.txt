VEGETABLE MEDLEY


1  small head cabbage (about 1 lb.)
1  Tbsp. shortening
1  c. thin slices celery
1  medium green pepper, sliced
2/3  c. chopped onion
1  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper
1  Tbsp. soy sauce (if desired)

      Prepare 3 cups shredded cabbage.  Heat shortening in skillet. Add vegetables and stir.  Cover.  Steam 5 minutes or until crisp-tender, stirring.  Add salt and pepper.  If desired, stir in 1 tablespoon soy sauce.
