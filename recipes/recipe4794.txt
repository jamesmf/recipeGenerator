DILL PICKLE SOUP


4  large dill pickles, peeled and grated
3  Tbsp. butter or margarine
4  qt. chicken stock
1  c. sour cream
2  Tbsp. flour

      Saut√© grated pickles in butter and flour until completely wilted.  Add the stock and simmer slowly for one-half hour.  In small bowl, stir together sour cream and 1 cup of broth.  Stir in to soup and simmer.  Serve hot.  However, if soup is not sour enough, add pickle juice to taste.  Serves 6.
