


2 medium  cloves garlic, minced
2  Tbsp. olive oil
3 (10 3/4 oz.)  cans Campbell's tomato soup
3  c. cold water
1/4  c. wine vinegar
2  c. chopped cucumber
1  c. chopped green pepper
1/2  c. chopped onion croutons

	In small saucepan, cook garlic in olive oil; combine in a large bowl with soup, water and vinegar.  Chill for 4 hours. Serve in chilled bowls.  Pass chilled vegetables and croutons for garnish.  Makes about 6 1/2 cups.
