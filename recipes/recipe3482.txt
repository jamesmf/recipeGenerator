TEXAS BACON SNACKS


1  lb. bacon
3  eggs, beaten
1 1/2  tsp. dry mustard
3 1/2  tsp. vinegar
1 1/2  tsp. cayenne pepper
2  pkg. crackers, crushed

      Mix all ingredients except for bacon and crackers.  Cut bacon into 2-inch pieces.  Dip into the mixture and then roll in cracker crumbs.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 20 minutes or until brown.
