CHUNKY ITALIAN SOUP


1  lb. ground beef or beef tips
1  onion, chopped
2  cans (14.5 oz.) Italian tomatoes
1  can tomato soup with basil
4  c. water
2  garlic cloves, minced
2  tsp. dried basil
2  tsp. dried oregano
1  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper
1  tbsp. chili powder (optional)
1  tbsp. brown sugar
1  can kidney beans, drained
1  can Italian green beans, drained
1  carrot, chopped or grated
1 zucchini, chopped. 8  oz. rotini noodles, cooked

      Brown ground beef and onions; drain. Add other ingredients and simmer for 30 minutes or longer. Stir in: kidney beans, green beans, carrot, and zucchini. Simmer at least 15 minutes.  Add rotini noodles, stir.
       May sprinkle each serving with Parmesan cheese and serve with French bread.
