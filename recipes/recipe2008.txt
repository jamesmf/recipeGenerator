MEXICAN OVEN OMELET


1  c. milk
1/2  c. Bisquick baking mix
3  eggs
1/2  tsp. onion salt
2 (4 oz.)  cans whole green chilies
1/2 (8 oz.)  jar Del Monte salsa roja
1 (17 oz.)  can Del Monte burrito filling mix
1  c. shredded Cheddar cheese

      Lightly grease an 8 x 8 x 2-inch baking dish.  Combine milk and baking mix.  Beat in eggs and onion salt.  Drain and slice chilies lengthwise; place in dish.  Top with salsa roja, burrito filling mix and cheese.  Pour egg mixture evenly on top.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 to 40 minutes.  Garnish with radishes, olives or sour cream, if desired.  Serves 4 to 6.
