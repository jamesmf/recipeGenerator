SPINACH DIP


2 (10 oz.)  pkg. frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
1 (8 oz.)  can water chestnuts, drained and finely chopped
1  c. sour cream
1  c. plain yogurt
1  c. green onions, finely chopped
1  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. dried tarragon leaves, crushed
1/2  tsp. dry mustard
1/4  tsp. pepper
1  clove garlic, crushed

      Mix all ingredients. Cover and refrigerate to blend flavors about 1 hour. Serve with crackers.
