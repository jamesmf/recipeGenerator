NOODLES ALFREDO


8  oz wide egg noodles, uncooked
1/2  c margarine
1/2  c Half & Half
1  c parmesan cheese
1/4  tsp salt
  dash of pepper
1  tbsp dried parsley flakes

      Cook noodles. Heat margarine and Half & Half in 1 qt saucepan over low heat until margarine is melted.  Stir in remaining ingredients, keeping warm over low heat.  Pour sauce over hot noodles, stirring gently until noodles are well coated.
