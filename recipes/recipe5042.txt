CANNED COLE SLAW


1  large cabbage head
1  green pepper
1  c. chopped celery
1/2  c. chopped onion
2  c. sugar
1/2  c. vinegar
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. celery seed
1/2  tsp. mustard seed

      Mix  together  and  let  stand overnight.  Put in jars. (Make sure juice covers.) Let remain in boiling  water  bath 5 minutes.
