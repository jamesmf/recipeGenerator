EASY ITALIAN BEEF


2 to 3  lb. beef roast
1  pkg. Italian dressing mix
  jalapeno pepper slices
  celery

      Place roast in crock-pot.  Sprinkle with dry Italian dressing mix.  Cook on low for about 10 to 12 hours.  Halfway through cooking, add jalapeno pepper slices (to taste) and chopped celery. Remove from crock-pot and pull apart.
