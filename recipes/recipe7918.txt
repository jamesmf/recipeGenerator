COCONUT RICE


3  cups rice
1  can coconut milk
  Salt to taste
1/2  onion, sliced
1/2  Tbsp. sugar
2  cups water
2  tsp. cooking oil

      Rinse and soak rice for up to 15 minutes.  Add coconut milk, salt, onion, sugar, water and oil. Set to cook in a rice cooker. When cooked, let rice sit for 15-20 minutes.  Remove lid and mix well.  Serve with  Burmese Red Curry.
