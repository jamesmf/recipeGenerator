MEXICAN CASSEROLE


1  lb. ground chuck, browned (cooked chicken may be substituted)
1  onion, thinly sliced or grated
1  can (10 oz.) Rotel, diced
1  can Cream of Chicken soup
1  c. shredded cheddar cheese
1  pkg. tortilla chips (small)

      Brown the meat; drain and set aside.  Line the bottom of a baking dish with tortilla chips.  Sprinkle the meat on top of the chips.  Place sliced or grated onion on top of this.  Combine the Rotel and the cream of chicken soup; mix well.  Pour this mixture evenly over the meat.  Sprinkle grated cheese over the entire mixture.  Cook in 350 degrees; oven until hot (about 30 minutes, or until bubbly).
