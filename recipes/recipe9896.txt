ZUCCHINI AND CHEESE CASSEROLE


3  or 4 medium zucchini squash
1  c. American cheese
1  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. black pepper
2 or 3  Tbsp. margarine
1/2  c. or more crushed crackers (Club)
  chopped onion (if desired)

      Wash and cut the zucchini into 1/2-inch cubes. Put into a saucepan and cover with water. Cook 6 to 8 minutes or until tender. Drain. Put back into saucepan. Add the cheese, salt, pepper and margarine. Stir lightly until cheese melts. Pour into a greased baking dish and sprinkle the Club cracker crumbs over the top. Bake at 400 degrees; for 12 to 15 minutes or until lightly browned.
