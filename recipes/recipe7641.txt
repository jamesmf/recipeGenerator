CHICKEN STRATA


6 to 8  slices bread
3  c. cooked, chopped chicken
1/2  c. chopped onion
1/2  c. chopped celery
1/2  c. chopped green pepper
1/2  c. mayonnaise
  salt and pepper
2  eggs
1 1/2  c. milk
1  can cream of mushroom soup
  buttered bread crumbs
  shredded cheese

      Cut bread into 1-inch cubes.  Place half in bottom of 9 x 13-inch pan.  Mix chicken,  onion,  celery,  pepper,  mayonnaise, salt and pepper.  Spoon  over bread  crumbs.  Sprinkle remaining half of bread cubes on top.  Combine  eggs and milk. Pour over the top.  Cover and chill overnight.  Before baking, spoon soup over top and  sprinkle  with buttered bread crumbs. Bake at 350 degrees; for 40 minutes.  Sprinkle  cheese  over  top last few minutes of baking.
