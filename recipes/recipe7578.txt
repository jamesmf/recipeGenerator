CHAMPAGNE CHICKEN


5  chicken breasts, deboned
1/2  stick butter or margarine, softened
1  can chicken soup
1/4  can of water
1/4  c. sour cream
1 small  can of mushrooms
6  Tbsp. Champagne (or dry white wine)
  Salt and pepper

      Salt and pepper chicken breasts, place in a baking dish. Combine remaining ingredients well in a separate mixing bowl using a mixer on low speed to blend in the margarine.  Pour mixture over chicken and bake uncovered at 350 degrees; for 50 minutes to an hour. Serve with rice.
