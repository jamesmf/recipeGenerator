CHICKEN SALAD CASSEROLE


4  c. cooked chicken
2  c. chopped celery or frozen peas
2/3  c. cream of chicken soup
2/3  c. mayonnaise
2  Tbsp. lemon juice
1  tsp. chopped onion
1  tsp. salt
4  chopped, hard-boiled eggs
1  c. diced cheese

 
Suggested Toppings:
1  c. Parmesan cheese
2/3  c. almonds
1 1/2  c. potato chips

      Mix all 9 casserole ingredients together.  Put in 9 x 13-inch pan.  Put one of the toppings on.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 20 to 25 Serves 6 to 8. Also, leftovers are good cold.
