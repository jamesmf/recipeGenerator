STRAWBERRY GLAZED FRUIT SALAD


1  quart fresh strawberries, halved
1  can (20 oz.) pineapple chunks, drained
4  firm bananas, sliced
1  jar or pouch strawberry glaze  (can often be found in the produce section of grocery store)

      In a large bowl, gently toss strawberries, pineapple and bananas.  Fold in glaze.  Chill one hour.
