BEEF BURRITO CASSEROLE


8  to 10 large flour tortillas
1 to 1 1/2  lb. ground meat
1  pkg. taco seasoning mix
1  can refried beans
1 (8 oz.)  can tomato sauce
1  pkg. Colby-Jack cheese, grated
  picante sauce (optional)

 
Optional Toppings:
  lettuce
  tomato
  sour cream
  black olives

      Brown ground meat; drain excess fat.  Add seasoning and can of tomato sauce to meat; let simmer.  Spread 2 to 3 tablespoons beans on each flour tortilla.  Place about 3 to 4 tablespoons meat mixture.  Sprinkle some cheese and picante sauce on top of this; fold together.  Place in casserole dish.  If there is any meat mixture left, spread on top of burritos; sprinkle with cheese. Bake for approximately 20 minutes at 350 degrees;.  Serve.  Garnish with desired toppings.
