TAMALE CASSEROLE

Meat Filling:
2  tbsp. vegetable oil
1  medium onion,  chopped
1  lb. ground beef
4 large  cloves garlic, minced
  1-15 oz. can tomato sauce
1/4  c. dry red wine
4-1/2  tsp. chili powder
  salt and pepper

 
Cornbread:
3  eggs
1  c. (1/2 pint) sour cream
1  c. milk
1  tsp. sugar
1 7  oz. can green chili salsa
1-1/2  c. flour
1-1/2  c. yellow corn meal
1  tsp. baking powder
1  tsp. soda
1  tsp. salt
  dash hot pepper sauce
  1-12 oz. can corn with red and green peppers, drained
2  c. shredded jack cheese

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees;F.
       Heat oil in skillet. Saut√© onion until soft. Add meat and garlic and brown. Pour off grease and stir in tomato sauce, wine, chili powder and salt and pepper. Cook over moderate heat until liquid evaporates. Cool.
      Corn bread:  Whisk eggs until frothy.  Whisk in sour cream and milk.  Add remaining eight ingredients.  Stir in corn and cheese. Butter bottom and sides of 9 x 3 inch or 10 inch spring form pan. Wrap outside in foil.  Pour 1/2 the cornbread mixture in pan and spread evenly.  Top with filling.  Top with remaining cornbread. Bake at 350 degrees;F, 75-85 minutes.  Let sit at room temperature for 20 minutes before serving.
