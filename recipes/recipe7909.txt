FRIED CHICKEN


2  lbs. skinless chicken
  Vegetable oil
1  egg
1  cup milk
1  cup wheat flour
1  tsp. each salt and pepper
1  tsp. each cayenne pepper and paprika
1  tsp. garlic powder

      Heat oil in skillet or deep fryer.  Add pepper, salt, cayenne, paprika and garlic powder to chicken.  Cover chicken with flour.
      Fry chicken pieces 5-6 minutes, turning occasionally, until golden brown.  Serve with fries.
      Note:  You may also use an egg and milk batter if desired. Add 1 egg and 1 cup milk before seasoning chicken.
