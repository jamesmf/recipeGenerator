QUICHE


  unbaked pie shells
  cooked ham or bacon, crumbled (I use shaved ham from the deli)
  grated Swiss cheese
  grated cheddar cheese
3  eggs
1  c. evaporated milk
  salt and pepper, to taste
  sauteed onions
  sauteed mushrooms

      Put layer of meat, onions and mushrooms in bottom of pie shells.  Add Swiss and cheddar cheese.  Mix eggs, milk, salt and pepper together in a bowl.  Pour over meat and cheese.  Bake at 450 degrees; for 15 minutes; reduce heat to 350 degrees; and bake for 30 more minutes.
