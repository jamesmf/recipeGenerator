WILD RICE CASSEROLE


1  box Uncle Ben's wild rice (original)
1/2  c. butter
2  Tbsp. flour
1  c. milk
8  oz. pkg. cream cheese
1  tsp. salt
1  jar button mushrooms

      Cook rice. In a double boiler, melt butter. Add flour to make a paste. Add milk, cream cheese and salt until blended. Mix all ingredients together and put in 2-quart greased casserole. Bake at 325 degrees; for 25 to 30 minutes. Makes 6 to 8 servings.
