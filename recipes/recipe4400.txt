BUTTERMILK SALAD


1 large  can crushed pineapple
1  pkg. lemon Jell-O
1  pkg. orange Jell-O
2  c. buttermilk
1  c. grated cheese
8  oz. carton Cool Whip

      Heat pineapple to boiling.  Dissolve in this lemon and orange Jell-O.  Let cool thoroughly.  Add buttermilk, cheese and Cool Whip.  Mix all ingredients well.  Pour into a square or long dish. Place in refrigerator to chill well.  Will keep for several days. (Travels well.)
