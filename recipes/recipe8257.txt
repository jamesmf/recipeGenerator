PIZZA CASSEROLE II


1  lb. Italian sausage
12  oz. wide noodles, cooked according to pkg. instructions
2 (14 oz.)  jars pizza sauce
2  c. Cheddar cheese, shredded
6  oz. pepperoni, sliced

	Preheat oven to 350 degrees;. Crumble sausage into a medium skillet; cook over medium heat until browned, stirring occasionally. Remove sausage and drain on paper towels. In a large lightly greased casserole dish, layer 1/2 of the noodles, 1/2 of the sausage and 1/2 of the remaining ingredients. Repeat with second layer of noodles, sausage and remaining ingredients. Reserve several pepperoni slices to garnish top. Bake 35 to 40 minutes. Serve immediately. Serves 6 to 8.
