ISLAND PAN BARBECUE SHRIMP


1  lb. large or jumbo shrimp, cleaned and deveined
2  Tbsp. olive oil
1  Tbsp. finely minced garlic
1  Tbsp. chopped fresh rosemary
1  Tbsp. chopped fresh thyme
1/2  tsp. freshly ground black pepper
1/4  tsp. cayenne pepper
1/4  tsp. salt
2  fresh limes, halved crosswise (for garnish)

      Combine all ingredients, except limes.  Marinate at room temperature for 1 hour.  Heat a dry skillet over medium-high heat. When skillet is hot, lay shrimp in pan.  Cook shrimp 2 to 4 minutes per side, depending on size.  Brush shrimp with remaining marinade before turning.  Serve with lime.
