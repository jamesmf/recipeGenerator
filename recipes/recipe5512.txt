SPAGHETTI SALAD


1  lb. cooked spaghetti
1  green pepper, chopped
1  cucumber, chopped
1  onion, chopped
1  tomato, chopped
  parmesan cheese
1  bottle Zesty Italian dressing

      Bring a pot of water to boil and cook noodles for approximately 10 to 12 minutes or until tender.  Run cool water over noodles to prevent sticking.  Place the noodles in a bowl; chop the vegetables and place them in the bowl.  Sprinkle with parmesan cheese.  Pour Zesty italian dressing over the salad.  Mix the ingredients to coat.
