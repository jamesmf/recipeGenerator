PINEAPPLE SALAD


1  can sliced pineapple
  lettuce
  maraschino cherries
  sharp cheese
  mayonnaise

	Drain small can pineapple well. Wash lettuce. Drain. Using a large platter, start by placing leaves of lettuce in platter. Place pineapple rings around on top of lettuce. Place grated sharp cheese in middle of each pineapple. Place a little mayonnaise on top of cheese. Place a drained maraschino cherry on top. Cover and chill well. Makes a great lite dessert.
