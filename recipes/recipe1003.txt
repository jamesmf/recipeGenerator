RUSSIAN MUSHROOM AND POTATO SOUP


2  leeks or onions, chopped
2  large carrots, chopped
5  Tbsp. butter or oleo, divided
6  c. chicken broth
2  tsp. dill weed
2  tsp. salt
  dash of pepper
1  bay leaf
5  c. potatoes, diced
1  lb. fresh mushrooms, sliced
2  Tbsp. flour
1  c. half and half or cream

      Sauté onions and carrots in 3 tablespoons butter.  Stir in chicken broth, seasonings and potatoes; simmer 20 minutes or until potatoes are tender.  Remove the bay leaf.  In skillet sauté mushrooms in remaining butter 5 minutes.  Stir in flour and cream. Add to soup and bring to boil.
