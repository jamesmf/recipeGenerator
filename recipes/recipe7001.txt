SHRIMP CASSEROLE


1  c. onion, chopped
1  c. celery, chopped
1  c. bell pepper, chopped
4  cloves garlic, minced
1  stick butter
1  Tbsp. parsley flakes
1/2  c. onion tops, chopped
3  c. shrimp, peeled and deveined
3  c. cooked rice
1  can cream of mushroom soup
3  slices white bread (soaked in milk)
1/2  c. hot water
  salt and pepper to taste
  bread crumbs
  butter pats for garnish

      Saut√© onions, celery, bell peppers and garlic in one stick of butter. Add parsley flakes, onion tops, soup, shrimp, rice, bread pieces, and hot water. Add salt and pepper, if desired. Cook 8 to 10 minutes. Pour in a 2 qt. casserole dish. Top with bread crumbs and butter pats. Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 to 35 minutes. Delicious!
