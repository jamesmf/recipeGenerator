CREAMY CRANBERRY SALAD


1  can whole cranberry sauce
1  c. boiling water
1 (3 oz.)  pkg. strawberry gelatin
1  Tbsp. lemon juice
1/2  c. mayonnaise
1  apple, diced
1/4  c. chopped pecans

      Heat cranberry sauce. Strain out juice. Mix juice, water and gelatin. Stir until dissolved. Add lemon juice and chill until slightly thickened. Add mayonnaise and beat with rotary beater until fluffy. Fold in cranberries, apple and pecans. Chill until firm.
