ITALIAN SPAGHETTI


1  lb. ground beef
2  medium onions, chopped
1/2  large green pepper, chopped
1 (4 oz.)  can mushrooms
1  clove garlic, chopped
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  Tbsp. or more chili powder
1 (7 oz.)  pkg. spaghetti
  Parmesan cheese
1  Tbsp. butter
1 large  can tomatoes
1 (6 oz.)  can tomato sauce
  salt and pepper to taste

      Fry ground beef, chopped onion and pepper in butter until slightly browned. Add canned tomatoes, tomato paste, mushrooms, chopped garlic, Worcestershire sauce, chili powder and salt and pepper to taste. Simmer over low heat for 3 to 4 hours.
      Cook  spaghetti according to directions. Serve by pouring meat sauce over spaghetti and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese. Yields 6 to 8 servings.
