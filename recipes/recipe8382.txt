MEAT LOAF


1 1/2  lb. hamburger
1  slice bread, crumbled
1/2  c. oatmeal
1  Tbsp. sugar
1/2  tsp. salt
1  Tbsp. mustard
1  medium onion, chopped
  green pepper, chopped
3  eggs, beaten
1  c. tomato sauce
1/2  c. milk

      Bake at 350 degrees;.  During last half of baking, spread the following sauce over the top.
Sauce:
1  c. tomato sauce
1/2  c. catsup
2  Tbsp. brown sugar
1  Tbsp. vinegar
