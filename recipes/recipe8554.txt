FRIED VENISON STEAKS


  Venison steaks, tenderized
  Milk
1  c. Flour
  Salt
  Pepper
1/2  tsp. Cavender's All Purpose Greek Seasoning

      Soak steaks in milk overnight.  Drain.  Mix flour and seasonings. Dredge steaks in flour mixture.  Heat oil in large pan and fry steaks until done.  Drain well.
