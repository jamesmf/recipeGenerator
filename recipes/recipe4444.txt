CRUNCH BAKED HAM SALAD


3  c. cubed, cooked ham
1  c. chopped celery
2/3  c. mayonnaise
1/2  c. chopped pimento stuffed olives
1/4  c. chopped onion
1  Tbsp. lemon juice
1 1/2  tsp. prepared mustard
1  c. crushed potato chips

      Combine ingredients (not the potato chips).  Stir well. Spoon this into a lightly greased 8-inch baking dish.  Top with potato chips. Bake at 400 degrees; for 20 or 25 minutes.  Will serve six.
