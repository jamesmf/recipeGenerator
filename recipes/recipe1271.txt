CUCUMBER CREAM CHEESE SPREAD


2  c. cashew cheese
1/4  tsp. sea salt
1  tsp. lemon juice
1/4  tsp. prepared horseradish
2  tsp. minced onion
1/2  medium cucumber, peeled and finely chopped

      Cream the cheese until smooth.  Add next three ingredients and mix well.  Stir in onion and cucumber.  Put in covered container. Chill one hour before serving.
