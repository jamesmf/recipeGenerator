THREE BEAN SALAD


1  can each:  green beans, yellow beans and kidney beans
1  small green pepper
1  onion

 
Dressing:
1/3  c. salad oil
2/3  c. vinegar
3/4  c. sugar
1  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper

      Chop pepper.  Thinly slice onion and separate rings.  Drain beans and add onion and pepper.  Combine ingredients for dressing and pour over bean mixture.  Refrigerate overnight.
