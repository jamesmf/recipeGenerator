GRILLED BRATS IN BEER


4  to 6 bratwurst
1 1/2  c. onion, coarsely chopped
1 (12 oz.)  can beer
2  Tbsp. butter, softened
1/2  tsp. onion salt
1/2  c. shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
4  to 6 buns

      In skillet, put brats, onion and beer; simmer, uncovered, for about 30 minutes.  Mix butter and onion salt together; spread on buns.  Remove brats from beer; fill slit with cheese and place in buns.  Broil 4 inches from heat, until cheese melts.  Watch closely.
