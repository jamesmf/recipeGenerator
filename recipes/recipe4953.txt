BASIL GARLIC BUTTER


1  c. butter, softened
2  garlic cloves, pressed
1/2  c. firmly packed fresh basil leaves

      Process butter and garlic cloves in a blender or food processor until smooth.  Add the basil leaves and pulse 3 to 4 times or until basil is finely chopped.  Store in refrigerator up to 1 week.  If desired, shape butter into a log; wrap in wax paper and chill.  When firm slice into smaller rounds and freeze.
