BUUMER'S MEAT LOAF


2/3  c. bread crumbs
1  c. milk
1 1/2 to 2  lb. ground beef
2  eggs
1/4  c. chopped onion
1  tsp. salt
  pepper

      Soak bread crumbs in milk. Add eggs, meat, onions and seasonings. Mix well and form into a loaf; place in greased pan. Cover with Special Piquant Sauce.
Special Piquant Sauce:
3  Tbsp. brown sugar
1/4  c. catsup
1/4  tsp. nutmeg
1  tsp. dry mustard

      Mix all ingredients. Cover meat loaf with sauce. Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour. Serves 6 to 8.
