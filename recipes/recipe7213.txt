MEAL IN A PAN


1  lb. bacon, diced
8  potatoes, sliced
5  eggs, scrambled
1/4  c. milk
3  slices torn bread

      Cut and fry bacon; when halfway cooked, add potatoes.  Cover, stirring often, until potatoes are done.  Put bread in eggs to soak while potatoes cook.  Then add to pan until eggs are done. Bacon, potatoes, eggs and bread in a pan.
