CROCK-POT BAKED BEANS


1/4  lb. bacon
1  onion
1  c. brown sugar
1/4  c. white vinegar
2 large  cans pork and beans
1 large  can lima beans
1 large  can butter beans
1 large  can kidney beans

      Other beans may be substituted.
      Fry out bacon.  Remove from pan.  Saut√© chopped onion in 2 tablespoons bacon fat.  Stir in brown sugar and vinegar.  Simmer 5 minutes.  Drain all beans, except pork and beans.  Place in crock-pot.  Pour cooked mixture over beans.  Cover.  Cook on low for 6 to 8 hours.
