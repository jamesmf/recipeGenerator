APRICOT COOKIES


1  c. flour
1/2  tsp. salt
1  c. butter
1 (3 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
1  can apricot filling
1  Tbsp. powdered sugar (optional)

      Blend flour, salt and butter until crumbly (like pie dough). Blend in cream cheese.  Shape dough into 5-inch square.  Wrap in plastic and chill about 2 hours.  Roll 1/4 of the dough into 12-inch square, cut dough into 2-inch squares and put 1/2 teaspoon apricot filling on each square; fold over opposite corners.  Bake on ungreased cookie sheet 8 to 10 minutes at 350 degrees;.  Sprinkle with powdered sugar, if desired.
