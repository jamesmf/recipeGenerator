CHILLED AVOCADO AND CUCUMBER SOUP


2  large ripe avocados
2  large cucumbers
2  c. chicken broth
  juice of 2 limes
1/4  c. diced green chilies
1  c. (1/2 pt.) heavy cream
  salt to taste
  chopped chives (optional)

      Peel and seed avocados and cucumbers.  Blend avocados with 1 cup of broth until smooth in food processor or blender.  Stir in lime juice.  Set aside.  Blend cucumbers, remaining 1 cup of broth and chilies until smooth.  Combine with avocado mixture.  Stir in cream and salt to taste.  Serve very cold.  Garnish with chives, if desired.  Serves 4.
