ORIENTAL CHICKEN SALAD


3  chicken breasts, cubed and stir-fried
2  Tbsp. toasted sesame seed
2  oz. toasted slivered almonds
1  pkg. prepared coleslaw mix
2  green onions, chopped
1  pkg. per serving of oriental flavored ramen noodles, uncooked and crumbled
  seasoning packets from noodles
3  Tbsp. sugar (times the number of seasoning packets)
3  Tbsp. vinegar (times the number of seasoning packets)
1/2  cup oil (can use up to one cup for six packets)

      Mix and pour over salad. Let stand at least four hours. Enjoy, especially on a hot summer day.
