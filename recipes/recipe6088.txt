CONGEALED AMBROSIA SALAD


3  oranges
3  oz. (1 box) gelatin, orange
1 to 2  c. sugar
1  c. boiling water
8  oz. (1 can) pineapple, crushed
1  c. coconut
1  c. nuts, chopped
8  oz. (1 small tub) sour cream

      Peel oranges, cut into bit-size pieces and set aside.
      Combine gelatin, sugar and boiling water, stirring until dissolved.  Add oranges, crushed pineapple (with juice), coconut, nuts and sour cream.  Stir until well blended.
      Chill until firm, at least 2 hours.
