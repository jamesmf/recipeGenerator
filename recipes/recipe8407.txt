CHICKEN ENCHILADAS


4  chicken breasts
1  can diced tomatoes, drained (reserve juice)
1 (4 oz.)  can diced green chilies
2  green onions, chopped
1 (28 oz.)  can green enchilada sauce
1  pkg. shredded Cheddar or Colby cheese
1  pkg. burrito sized tortillas

      Heat oven to 350 degrees;.  Spray baking dish (13-inch) with oil cooking spray.  Boil chicken breasts until done.  Remove from water to cool; shred chicken.  Add can of diced tomatoes (drained), green chilies (drained) and green onions to chicken. Put 2 tablespoons of chicken mixture in tortilla and roll.  Place rolled tortilla, seam down, in baking dish.  Pour green enchilada sauce over rolled tortillas.  Sprinkle with cheese.  Bake until heated through and cheese has melted.  Serve with rice.
