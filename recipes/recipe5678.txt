ICY FRUIT SALAD


1  container Cool Whip
1/3  c. mayonnaise
1 large  pkg. cream cheese, softened
1/4  c. confectioners sugar
2 small  cans mandarin oranges, drained
1 (16 oz.)  can crushed pineapple, drained
1  c. shredded coconut
3  c. miniature marshmallows
1  c. pecans, chopped
1 small  jar maraschino cherries, drained and chopped

	In a large bowl, combine Cool Whip, mayonnaise, cream cheese and sugar. Blend thoroughly. Fold in the remaining ingredients. Chill at least 2 hours before serving. Serve on a bed of lettuce with a tea biscuit (or some other salty cookie).
