SAUSAGE WITH POTATOES


2  lbs. sausage
  3-4 potatoes, cut in cubes
1 or 2  tbsp. oil
1  or 2 onions
1  c. water
1  lg. pepper

      Brown sausage in large skillet with 1 tbsp. oil and 1 c. water.  When browned, approx. 20 mins., add potatoes.  Wait 15 mins, then add onions and pepper, a little water if needed.  Cover and cook on low heat another 20-25 mins.
