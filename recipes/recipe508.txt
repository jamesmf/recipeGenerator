BEAT 'EM UP COOKIES


3  c. oatmeal
1 1/2  c. brown sugar
1 1/2  c. flour
1 1/2  c. butter
1 1/2  tsp. baking powder

      Mix all ingredients in large bowl.  Knead, pound and mash.  The longer and harder you mix it, the better it tastes.  Roll dough into little balls.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 10 to 12 minutes.
