CHICKEN WITH GARLIC & VEGETABLES


2  tsp. olive oil
2  tsp. unsalted butter
1 1/2  lb. boneless, skinless chicken breast halves
2  carrots, peeled and cut into thin strips
1  medium leek - washed, trimmed and cut into thin strips
1  red bell pepper, seeded and cut into thin strips
2  cloves garlic, minced
1/2  c. canned crushed tomatoes

      Heat oil and butter in a large skillet over medium high heat. Sauté chicken breasts 4 to 5 minutes per side, turning occasionally, until chicken is opaque throughout.  Transfer to a platter and keep warm.  Add carrots to skillet and sauté over medium heat 1 minute.  Add leek, bell pepper and garlic and sauté another minute.  Stir in tomatoes and any juices from platter. Simmer 2 minutes or until vegetables are tender.  Season with salt and pepper to taste.  Serve vegetables over chicken.  Makes 4 servings.
