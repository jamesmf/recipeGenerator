SPLIT PEA SOUP


1  lb. split peas
3  qt. water
1  ham bone
2  carrots, sliced
1  large onion, chopped
1  stalk celery, chopped
  salt and pepper to taste

      Wash peas. Add cold water, vegetables and ham bone and simmer for 3 hours or until mixture is thick.  Remove ham bone.  Force peas through coarse sieve and season to taste.  If needed, dilute with milk.  Serve very hot with toasted croutons.
