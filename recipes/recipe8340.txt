LOIS' GOLDEN FRIED CHICKEN STRIPS


2  c. canola oil
4  lb. chicken breasts (boneless and skinless)
3  c. flour
1  Tbsp. Lawry's seasoned salt
1  Tbsp. garlic powder
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. black pepper

	Wash and cut chicken into 1-inch strips. Place flour, salt and pepper into plastic Ziploc bag. Add chicken and shake well. Place breaded chicken in frying pan in about 1-inch deep oil. Sprinkle chicken with seasoned salt and garlic powder. Fry for 4 minutes on both sides. Keep turning until golden brown.
