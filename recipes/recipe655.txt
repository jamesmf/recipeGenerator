DIABETIC CANDY


1  pkg. Sweet 'N Low white frosting mix
4  packets Equal sweetener
  dash of salt
1/4  c. milk
1/2  c. peanut butter
1 (3 oz.)  can chow mein noodles
1  c. chopped pecans

      Mix frosting, sweetener and salt with milk.  Add peanut butter, noodles and pecans.  Drop by spoonfuls onto waxed paper and refrigerate.  Makes about 20 cookies.
      Variation:  Add chocolate to frosting mix or use the chocolate mix and omit the peanut butter.  Add peppermint for added flavor.
