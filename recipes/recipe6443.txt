MARINATED SALAD


1  zucchini
1  cucumber
1/2  bunch of broccoli cut in small flowerettes
1  medium onion sliced thin
1 small  can mushroom pieces
1/2  bunch cauliflower cut in small pieces
1  tomato diced

 
Sauce:
1  cup vinegar
1  tsp. accent
1  tsp. garlic powder (not salt)
1  cup water
3  tsp. seasoning salt

      Place all vegetables in a container large enough to mix them easily.
      Mix together in a separate container the sauce ingredients. Pour over vegetables and marinate in refrigerator for several hours before serving.
