PORK AND MUSHROOM DISH


1  lb. pork tenderloin
2  cans green beans
2  c. mushrooms
  vegetable or canola oil

      Cut pork into 1/2-inch pieces (chunks).  Brown in oil.  You can roll pork in flour first if you want.  Add green beans and mushrooms and simmer on low for about 30 to 35 minutes.
