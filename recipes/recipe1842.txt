MEXICO-TEQUILA SALAD


1 (15 1/4 oz.)  can sliced pineapple, drained and 1/4 c. syrup reserved
1/4  c. lime juice
2  Tbsp. powdered sugar
2  Tbsp. tequila
2  Tbsp. vegetable oil
1/4  tsp. salt
3  medium avocados
  lime juice
2  large grapefruit
2  large oranges
  salad greens
  chopped walnuts
  salt

      Shake reserved pineapple syrup, 1/4 cup lime juice, the sugar, tequila, oil and 1/4 teaspoon salt in a tightly covered container. Refrigerate at least 1 hour.
      Cut avocados lengthwise in half; remove pits.  Peel avocados; cut into 1/2-inch pieces.  Sprinkle pieces with lime juice.  Cut pineapple slices in half.  Pare and section grapefruit and oranges; cut sections in half, if desired.
      Just before serving, toss avocado pieces with pineapple, grapefruit and orange sections; place on salad greens.  Sprinkle walnuts and salt over salad.  Serve with dressing.  Makes 8 servings.
      A salad created from the flavors of the popular Mexican drink, the Margarita.
