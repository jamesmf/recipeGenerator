FAMILY CASSEROLE


1  lb. hamburger, cooked, drained and crumbled
1  medium onion, halved and sliced
1/2  tsp. minced garlic
2  potatoes, peeled, sliced very thin
2  carrots, peeled, sliced very thin
1  c. grated cheddar cheese
1  can cream of chicken soup
1/2 soup  can of water
2  c. biscuit mix
  milk and water

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees;.  In a saucepan, combine beef, onion, garlic and 1/4 cup water; cook until onions are tender.  Spread half of potatoes in a 3 quart casserole.  Top with half of meat mixture, then carrots, then cheese, then remaining meat mixture. Mix soup with 1/2 soup can of water; pour over casserole and top with remaining potatoes.  Bake, uncovered at 350 degrees; for approximately 1 hour.  When potatoes are fork tender, combine biscuit mix with enough milk to make a thin consistency; pour over casserole.  Bake 5 to 10 minutes longer or until dough is lightly browned.  Yield: 6 servings.
      Note: You can increase oven temperature up to 450 degrees; to speed cooking time, if desired.
