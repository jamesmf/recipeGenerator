CHILI SAUCE


40  tomatoes
4  green peppers
16  small onions
1  bunch celery
2  lb. brown sugar
4  Tbsp. salt
2  Tbsp. celery
2  Tbsp. mustard seed
4  c. vinegar

      Chop tomatoes, pepper, onions and celery; put in large pot with other ingredients.  Boil at least 1 1/2 hours.  Put in jars and seal.
