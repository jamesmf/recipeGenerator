SPINACH SALAD WITH ORANGES


1  pkg. (10 oz.) fresh spinach, torn
1  can (11 oz.) mandarin oranges, drained
1  c. sliced fresh mushrooms
3  bacon strips, fried crisp and crumbled
3  Tbsp. ketchup
2  Tbsp. cider vinegar
1 1/2  Tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/4  c. sugar
2  Tbsp. chopped onion
1/8  tsp. salt
  Dash pepper
1/2  c. vegetable oil

      In a large salad bowl, toss the spinach, oranges, mushrooms, and bacon; set aside.  In blender or food processor, combine the ketchup, vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, sugar, onion, salt and pepper; cover and process until smooth.  While processing, gradually add oil in a steady stream.  Serve with the salad. Serves 4-6.
