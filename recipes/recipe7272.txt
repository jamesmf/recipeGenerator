DOUBLE-CHEESE CHICKEN CASSEROLE


1  small onion, chopped
1  stalk celery, chopped
1  green bell pepper, chopped
8  oz. spaghetti
1  c. cubed American cheese
1 (10 oz.)  can tomatoes with green chilies, chopped
1 (4 oz.)  jar chopped pimientos
1 (10 oz.)  can cream of mushroom soup
1 (4 oz.)  can sliced mushrooms
2  c. chopped cooked chicken
1/2  c. shredded Cheddar cheese

      Bring a large pot of water to a boil over high heat. Add onion, celery and bell pepper. Boil until tender, about 10 minutes. Add pasta to pot. Cook until tender, about 10 minutes; drain.
      Preheat oven to 350 degrees;. Grease a medium casserole bowl. Mix American cheese, tomatoes, pimientos, soup and mushrooms in prepared dish. Microwave on High until cheese is melted, about 5 minutes. Add chicken and pasta mixture to casserole. Stir well. Top casserole with Cheddar cheese. Bake until hot and bubbly, about 30 minutes.
