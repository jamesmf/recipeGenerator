CHICKEN STIR-FRY


1  lb. chicken strips
1  small onion
2  cans cream of mushroom soup
  salt and pepper
1  lb. bag frozen vegetables (cauliflower, broccoli and carrots)
  cooked rice (to serve 4)

	Cut up chicken strips and fry in a small amount of oil.  Add onions, mixed vegetables, salt and pepper to taste.  Cook for 15 to 20 minutes or until vegetables are barely tender, stirring occasionally so it won't stick.  Add soup and cook until steaming hot.  Serve over rice.
