PAELLA


1 1/2  lb. chicken pieces
3  Tbsp. olive oil
3  links chorizo
1/2  c. smoked ham, cubed
1  yellow onion, chopped
1  green pepper, chopped
1  tomato, chopped
2  cloves garlic, crushed
1 1/2  c. long grain rice
2 (10 oz.)  cans chicken broth
1  tsp. saffron
1  lb. raw shrimp, shelled and deveined
1/4  c. pimento strips
1/2  pkg. frozen green peas

	Brown chicken pieces in olive oil.  Add chorizo and break apart with a fork.	Add ham and brown slightly. Stir in onion, green pepper, tomato and garlic. Push vegetables and meat aside. Add rice to center of pan and saut 5 minutes.  Then add chicken broth and stir in saffron. Cover. Simmer 25 minutes. Stir in shrimp, pimento and peas. Simmer 5 to 10 minutes.
