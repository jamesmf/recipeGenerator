TALERINA


1  bell pepper
1  onion
3  ribs of celery
1  pod garlic
1  lb. ground chuck
1/4  lb. sausage
1 large  can of tomatoes, chopped
1  can tomato sauce
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  can or pkg. of shoe peg corn
1  Tbsp. salt
1  Tbsp. pepper
1  can chopped black olives
1  lb. grated sharp cheese
1  lb. pkg. wide noodles

	Chop then mix the following: brown bell pepper, onion, celery, garlic, ground chuck and sausage. Mix tomatoes, tomato sauce, Worcestershire sauce, shoe peg corn, salt, and pepper. Combine both mixtures together.  Cook as spaghetti.  Add chopped black olives, 3/4 pound grated sharp cheese. Mix one package cooked wide noodles.  Bake 45 minutes in buttered casserole at 350 degrees;.  Top with 1/4 lb. sharp cheese.  Makes 8 to 10 servings.
