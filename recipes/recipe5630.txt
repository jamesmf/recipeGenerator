LAYERED RASPBERRY PRETZEL JELL-O


6  oz. box raspberry Jell-O
2  C. hot water
10  oz. frozen raspberries, thawed for 10 - 15 minutes
8  oz. cream cheese
2  Tbsp. sugar
8  oz. Cool Whip
1  C. crushed pretzels
1/2  C. chopped pecans
1/2  C. sugar
1/2  C. melted butter

      First Layer:  Dissolve Jell-O in hot water; add raspberries. Put in greased bundt pan or Jell-O mold.  Refrigerate until firm.
      Second Layer:  Beat together cream cheese, and sugar; add Cool Whip until blended.  Put on top of Jell-O layer.
      Third Layer:  Combine crushed pretzels, pecans, sugar and melted butter in a cake pan.  Bake at 350 degrees for 10 minutes. Crumble and put on second layer when cool; refrigerate.
      Unmold when ready to serve.
