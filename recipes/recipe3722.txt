VEGETABLE PIZZA


2  tubes crescent rolls
2 - 8  oz. cream cheese
2/3  C. mayonnaise
1  tsp. dill weed
2  tsp. onion flakes

      Press crescent rolls into a cookie sheet and bake at 400 degrees for 10 minutes.  Cool.
      Mix together:  cream cheese, mayonnaise, dill weed and onion flakes.  Spread on cooled crust and top with your favorite vegetables:  tomatoes, peppers, onion, cucumber, olives, mushrooms, etc.
      Sprinkle grated cheese on top.
