BAKED CHICKEN BREASTS


6  boneless chicken breasts
1 (2 oz.)  jar dried beef, torn in small pieces
6  strips bacon
2  cans cream of chicken soup
1 (8 oz.)  carton sour cream

	Place dried beef in bottom of 9 x 13 pan that has been sprayed with Pam. Wrap chicken with bacon and place on top of dried beef. Mix together soup and sour cream. Pour over chicken. Bake at 350 degrees; for 2 hours, covered, and 1 hour, uncovered.
	 (You can use 1 can cream of chicken and 1 can cream of mushroom soup.)
