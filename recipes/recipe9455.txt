HAMBURGER AND MACARONI


1  lb. lean hamburger
1  can tomato soup (undiluted)
1  tsp. salt
1  c. macaroni
6 to 8  slices cheese

	Brown hamburger in skillet until done. Add salt. Cook macaroni according to directions on package. In casserole dish, place one layer hamburger, tomato soup and macaroni, then add 3 to 4 slices of cheese. Repeat the process. Bake at 375 degrees; until cheese has melted.
