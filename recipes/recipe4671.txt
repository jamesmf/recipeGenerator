MUSHROOM BARLEY SOUP


  Chicken  Bouillon cubes
  Mushrooms
  Carrots
  Onion
  Barley
  Salt
  Pepper
  Chili Powder
  Roux

      Boil water (once boiled), add bouillon cubes, carrots and onion (chopped small) add barley.  Cook until soft, add mushrooms. Make a roux, melt butter, add flour, stir in cold cream or milk and then stir until thick.  Once roux is finished combine vegetables and broth with the roux.  Add salt, pepper and chili powder to taste.
