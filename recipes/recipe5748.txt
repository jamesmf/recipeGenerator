HAMBURGER SOUP


1  lb. ground beef
1 (1 lb.)  can tomatoes, cut up
3  medium carrots, sliced
2  medium potatoes, cubed or macaroni
1  medium onion, chopped
1/2  c. celery, chopped
3 to 4  c. water
3  beef bouillon cubes
1 1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
1/4  tsp. oregano
1  pkg. frozen green beans

      Brown beef; drain off fat.  Add remaining ingredients except beans.  Bring to boil and reduce heat.  Cover and simmer for 1 hour.  Add green beans and continue to simmer 30 minutes more. Makes 6 servings.  I also add corn.
