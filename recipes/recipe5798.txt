CHERRY FRUIT SALAD


1  can Eagle Brand milk
2  c. Cool  Whip
1/4  c. lemon juice
1 small  can crushed pineapple, drained
1 (3 oz.)  box cherry jello
1/4  tsp. almond flavoring
2  large bananas, sliced
1  can cherry pie filling
1  c. pecans

      Blend Eagle Brand, Cool Whip, lemon juice, crushed pineapple, cherry jello and almond flavoring. Add bananas, cherry pie filling and pecans. Fold in. Chill.
