FRUIT PIZZA


  Pillsbury sugar cookie dough
8  oz. cream cheese
8  oz. Cool Whip
1/4  c. sugar
  assorted fruits--strawberries, kiwi, grapes, mandarin oranges, blueberries, etc.

      Spread cookie dough on pizza pan; bake at 350 degrees; for 10 to 15 minutes.  Cool.  Mix cream cheese with sugar and Cool Whip. Spread on cooled crust.  Layer fruits on top, as desired.
