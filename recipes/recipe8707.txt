TUNA MAC


8  oz. uncooked macaroni
8  oz. Velveeta cheese, chunked
1 (10 oz.)  pkg. frozen mixed vegetables, thawed and drained
1 (6 oz.)  can tuna (packed in water), drained and flaked
2  Tbsp. milk

	Prepare macaroni according to package directions.  Drain; add cheese and stir until cheese is melted. Add remaining ingredients and heat thoroughly.
