HOMEMADE FREEZER ICE CREAM


4  eggs
1/2  tsp. salt
3  Tbsp. pure vanilla
2  c. sugar
2  qt. half and half
  milk to fill container

      Beat eggs; add sugar and salt and beat again.  Stir in half and half and vanilla.  Pour into 5-quart ice cream freezer container and add milk until reaches the fill line.  Freeze according to machine directions.
