SHRIMP SCAMPI


1  tsp. reduced-calorie oleo, melted
1  tsp. vegetable oil
1  clove garlic, minced
1/4  tsp. pepper
1/2  lb. cleaned, uncooked fresh shrimp
1  Tbsp. chopped fresh parsley

      Combine first 4 ingredients in a shallow heat-proof casserole dish. Add shrimp and toss to coat. Spread shrimp in a single layer. Broil shrimp 4-inches from heat 3 to 4 minutes. Turn shrimp and broil an additional 3 to 4 minutes or until lightly browned. Sprinkle with parsley.
