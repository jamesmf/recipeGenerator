CHICKEN PARMESAN


4  boneless, skinless chicken breast
1  egg, beaten
3/4  c. Italian seasoned dry bread crumbs
1  jar Ragu sauce
1  c. shredded mozzarella cheese

      Preheat oven to 400 degrees;.  Dip chicken in egg, then bread crumbs. In 9 x 13 inch baking dish, arrange chicken.  Bake uncovered for 20 minutes.  Pour pasta sauce over chicken, top with cheese.  Bake an additional 10 minutes or until chicken is no longer pink.  Serve with hot cooked pasta.
