MEXICAN CORN DIP


2  cans Mexican corn, drained
1/2  cup miracle whip salad dressing
1/2  cup sour cream
2  cups sharp cheddar cheese
1  bunch green onions, chopped
  garlic salt
  salt
  pepper
  crushed red peppers

      Mix together and serve with corn chips.
      If you like it hot, add 3 chopped jalapenos.
