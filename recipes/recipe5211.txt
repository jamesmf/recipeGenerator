STRAWBERRY CONGEALED SALAD


3  pkgs. strawberry jello dissolved in 1 1/2 cups boiling water
1 (12 oz.)   pkg. frozen strawberries
1  can crushed pineapples with juice
2  mashed bananas

      Dissolve jello in boiling water.  Stir in frozen strawberries until thawed.  Add bananas and pineapples.  Pour into 9 x 13 inch glass dish.  Put in refrigerator until set.  Add topping after jello is set.
Topping:
1 (8 oz.)  carton sour cream
1 (12 oz)  package cream cheese, softened
2  Tbsp. sugar

      Mix ingredients together and spread on top of jello salad. Chill.
