CHICKEN POT PIE


4  boneless chicken breasts, boiled
3  c. chicken broth (from chicken)
1  (10 oz.) frozen mixed vegetables, cooked
1  c. cream of chicken soup

 
Topping:
1 1/2  c. flour
1 1/2  c. milk
3/4  c. butter
1  tsp. baking powder

      Put chicken in a buttered casserole dish. Add vegetables, cream of chicken soup and broth.
      Mix topping into dough and place on top. Bake at 375 degrees; to 400 degrees; for 30 to 45 minutes or until crust is brown.
       Mushrooms are good in this pie.
