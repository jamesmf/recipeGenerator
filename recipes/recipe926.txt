CAJUN BLACKENED FISH


4  Tbsp. margarine, melted
4  fish fillets
1/2  tsp. onion powder
1/2  tsp. garlic powder
1/2  tsp. white pepper
1/2  tsp. black pepper
1/2  tsp. cayenne pepper
1/8  tsp. thyme
1/8  tsp. sage
1/8  tsp. oregano

      Spread melted margarine over fillets.  Combine all spices and mix well.  Rub spices into fish.  Heat cast-iron skillet until very hot; watch carefully.  Cook fillets for about 3 minutes on each side, depending on thickness.  (Caution:  Cook outside or in very well ventilated area.)
