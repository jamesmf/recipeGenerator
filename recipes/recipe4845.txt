NO TUNA OR NOODLES SALAD


1  bunch broccoli, separated
1  head cauliflower, separated
1  bag frozen peas, thawed
2  c. chopped celery
1  large onion, chopped
3  c. Miracle Whip
1 1/2  c. sour cream
3/4  c. sugar
2  tsp. garlic salt
2  tsp. Beau Monde
2  tsp. pepper

      Mix and enjoy!  Serves a lot.
