OLD FASHIONED HAM LOAF


1  lb. ground ham
1/2  lb. fresh ground pork
1/2  lb. fresh ground veal
1 1/4  c. milk
1  c. bread crumbs
2  eggs
1/2  tsp. salt

	Preheat oven to 300 degrees;. Take all the above ingredients and mix together very well. Form into the shape of a small football. Put into a 10 x 6-inch baking dish or pan. Then spread on top the following mixture: 1 teaspoon mustard and 1/2 cup brown sugar. Bake 1 1/2 to 2 hours.
