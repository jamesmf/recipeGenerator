CORNBREAD SALAD

Salad Mix:
  cornbread
4  tomatoes
  sprinkle of salt
  sprinkle of pepper
1/2  lb. bacon, fried crisp and crumbled
1  bell pepper, chopped
1  medium onion, chopped
1/2  c. pickles, sweet

      Prepare and cook cornbread in a 9x13-inch pan.
      Peel and chop tomatoes, then sprinkle with salt and pepper and set aside.  Layer ingredients in bowl as follows: cornbread, bacon, bell pepper, onion, pickles and tomato mixture.
Dressing:
1  c. mayonnaise
1/4  c. pickle juice
1  Tbsp. sugar

      Mix ingredients together.  Drizzle dressing over top of salad mix.
