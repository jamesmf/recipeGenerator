TACO SNACK MIX


3  c. lightly salted peanuts
3  c. corn chips
3  c. Spoon-sized Shredded Wheat white cereal
2  c. lightly salted pretzels
1  pkg. taco seasoning mix
1/4  c. (1/2 stick) melted butter or margarine

      Combine all ingredients in large bowl.  Toss well to coat. Makes about 20 servings.
