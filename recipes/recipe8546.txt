MEATLOAF


1 1/2  lb. Ground Chuck
1  c. Tomato Juice
3/4  c. Oats (uncooked)
2  Eggs, beaten
1/4  c. chopped Onion
2  tsp. Salt
1/4  tsp. Pepper

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Combine all ingredients.  Mix well.  Press firmly into ungreased 8x4x2 meatloaf pan.  Bake at 350 degrees about one hour.  Let stand five minutes before slicing.
