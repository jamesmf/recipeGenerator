TENDERLOIN


4  lb. tenderloin
1/4  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. garlic powder
1/8  tsp. pepper
1  oven cooking bag
3/4  c. burgundy wine

      Sprinkle with seasoning.  Place in bag.  Pour in wine, tie and refrigerate overnight. Cut 6 1/2 inch slits in top of bag. Place on roasting pan and bake at 325 degrees for 50 to 60 minutes or 140 degrees on meat thermometer.
