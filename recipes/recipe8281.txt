BACON AND ONION STRATA


6  slices bacon
6  slices white bread, trimmed of crust
6  slices wheat bread, trimmed of crust
1 1/2  c. Cheddar cheese, shredded
2  Tbsp. green onions, minced
6  eggs
3  c. milk
1/4  tsp. ground black pepper
1  tsp. salt

	Fry bacon until crisp; drain and break into small pieces. Generously grease a 13 x 9-inch glass baking dish.	Arrange 6 bread slices in bottom of dish alternating bread types if desired. Sprinkle bacon pieces, cheese and onions over bread.  Top with remaining 6 bread slices, alternating or matching bread types. Beat eggs in a large bowl.	Add milk and pepper.  Pour mixture over sandwiches.  Cover and refrigerate at least 3 hours or overnight. Uncover dish.  Bake in preheated 325 degrees; oven for 50 to 55 minutes until puffed and golden brown and knife inserted near center comes out clean.
