FIVE BEAN SALAD


1 (8 oz.)  can red kidney beans
1 (8 oz.)  can wax beans
1 (8 oz.)  can green beans
1  can garbanzo beans
1  can bean sprouts
1  can water chestnuts, diced
1  clove garlic, minced
1/2  c. onion, diced
1/2  c. vinegar
1/4  c. olive oil
1  tsp. oregano
1  tsp. basil
  salt and pepper to taste

	Empty all cans into a colander. Drain well. Combine beans, water chestnuts, onion and garlic in a bowl. Combine the rest of the ingredients and pour over bean mixture; stir well. Cover and chill for 2 to 4 hours.
