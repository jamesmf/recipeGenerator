KING RANCH CHICKEN


1  whole chicken, boiled
1  can Ro-Tel
2  cans cream of mushroom (reduced fat)
  tortilla chips
  Cheddar cheese (low-fat)

      Boil chicken and cool.  Remove chicken from bones in small pieces (bite size).  Mix chicken with Ro-Tel and cream of mushroom. In 13 x 9 pan, layer crushed or broken tortilla chips along bottom of pan.  Place chicken mixture on top of chips.  Layer with shredded cheese (enough to cover top).  Place in oven on 350 degrees; for 25 to 30 minutes until casserole is bubbly.  Makes 12 servings.
      Cal-271, CHO-13g, Pro-27g, Fat-11g.
