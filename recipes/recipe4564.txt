BLUEBERRY SALAD


2 small  pkg. blackberry jello
2  c. boiling water
1 (15 oz.)  can blueberries (may use frozen)
1 (8 1/4 oz.)  can crushed pineapple, drained
1  (8 oz.) cream cheese
1/2  c. sugar
1/2  pt. sour cream
1/2  tsp. vanilla
1/2  c. pecans, chopped

      Dissolve jellos in boiling water. Drain blueberries and pine apples catching the juices; add enough water to make 1 c.; add to jello. Stir in blueberries and pineapple. When jello starts to set, pour into a 2 qt. flat pan; refrigerate until set. Combine sugar, sour cream, cream cheese, and vanilla; spread over jello layer; sprinkle pecans on top. Very sweet and rich. May be served as a dessert.
