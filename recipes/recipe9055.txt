HONEY MUSTARD TENDERLOIN


4  Tbsp. honey
2  Tbsp. brown sugar
2  Tbsp. cider vinegar
1  Tbsp. Dijon mustard
1  pork tenderloin

      Combine first 4 ingredients. Coat tenderloin with mixture. Place on preheated gas grill at medium heat for 15 to 20 minutes, basing occasionally, until meat thermometer registers 155 degrees;.
