BROCCOLI, HAM AND CHEESE CASSEROLE


1  head fresh or frozen broccoli
1 small  jar Cheez Whiz
1/4  c. milk
2 thick  slices ham
  bread crumbs

      Cook  broccoli 5 minutes in boiling unsalted water.  In a small  pan  melt cheese and milk.  Put broccoli in a baking dish. Put  chunks  of  ham  on  top of broccoli.  Pour cheese sauce  on top  of broccoli and ham.  Sprinkle bread crumbs on top of mixture.  Cook at 350 degrees; until bread crumbs are brown.
