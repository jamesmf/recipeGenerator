YOGURT DILL DIP


1  c. plain fat-free yogurt
1  Tbsp. chopped fresh dill or 1 tsp. dried dill
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. ground pepper
1/2  tsp. lemon juice

      Combine all ingredients in a small bowl. Cover and chill at least 2 hours before serving. Serve with fresh vegetables. Yield: 8 servings.
      Cal-18, CHO-2, Pro-2, Fat-0.
