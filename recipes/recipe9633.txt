AUSTRALIAN PEPPER STEAK


2  lb. round steak
2  cups chopped onion
2  crushed garlic cloves
2  Tbsp. melted butter
2  Tbsp. olive oil
1  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. celery salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
3  1/3 cups diced tomatoes
1  beef bouillon cube
1  large thinly sliced green pepper
2  Tbsp. cornstarch
2  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce

      Trim fat from steak. Cut into thin strips. Brown meat, onion and garlic in butter & oil in large skillet. Add salt, celery salt, pepper, tomatoes, bouillon cube and green pepper. Cover and simmer 30-45 minutes or until meat is tender. Combine cornstarch and Worcestershire sauce; stir into meat mixture. Cook until thickened. Serve with cooked rice or noodles. 6 servings.
