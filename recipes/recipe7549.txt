CHICKEN CONTINENTAL


1  can Cream of Mushroom soup
2 1/2  Tbsp. grated onion
  Salt and pepper
  Parsley flakes
1/8  tsp. thyme
1 1/3  c. water
1 1/3  c. Minute Rice

      Brown chicken pieces in 1/4 c. butter.  Stir soup, seasonings and water into pan drippings.  Cook and stir to boiling.  Spread rice in 1 1/2 quart casserole.  Pour all but 1/3 cup of the soup mixture over the rice.  Stir to moisten.  Top with chicken and remaining soup.  Bake covered at 375 degrees; for 30 minutes.
