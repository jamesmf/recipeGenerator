KHAJUR KI CHUTNEY


50  gm pitted dates
15  gm seedless raisins
1  tsp. zeera powder
1  tsp. chili powder
1 heaping  tsp. tamarind concentrate OR 3 Tbsp. lemon juice
125  ml water
3/4  tsp. salt
1  tsp. soft brown sugar

      Put all the ingredients in a blender and mix well. Sieve and throw out the residue. Serve the strained chutney with kababs, tikkas.
