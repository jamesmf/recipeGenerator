ROSE GARDEN CUCUMBER SALAD


  cucumbers
1  red onion

 
Sauce:
2  c. mayonnaise
2/3  c. sugar
1/2  c. cider vinegar
1  tsp. salt
1/2  c. half and half

      Peel cucumbers and slice lengthwise.  Seed and slice thinly. Slice onion very thin.  Mix together ingredients for sauce.  Put in cucumber and onion and marinate.
