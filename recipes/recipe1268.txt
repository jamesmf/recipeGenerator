VEGETARIAN PATE


1  lb. firm tofu, sliced thin or diced
1/2  c. yeast flakes
1/2  c. chives
1  tsp. parsley
  garlic to your taste
1  tsp. peanut oil
1/4  tsp. basil
1  stalk leeks
1/4  tsp. cayenne
1  pt. bean sprouts
1/4  tsp. vegie-sal
  a little lemon juice
1/4  tsp. thyme
1  tsp. sunflower oil

      Combine ingredients in blender until smooth.  Put in dish of your choice.  Refrigerate and serve on whole wheat crackers.
