VIDALIA ONION RELISH


2  large Vidalia or sweet onions, chopped
1  Tbsp. vegetable oil
1/4  c. green pepper jelly
1 1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. dried crushed red pepper

      Saut√© onions in hot oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat, stirring often, 10-15 minutes.  Add pepper jelly and remaining ingredients, stirring until jelly melts.  Makes 1 1/2 cups.  Wonderful alongside black-eyed peas and ham hocks.
