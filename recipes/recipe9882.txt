NEW POTATO CASSEROLE


2  lb. new potatoes
1  c. mayonnaise
1  lb. Velveeta, cubed
4 to 6  slices bacon, cooked crisp
  salt and pepper to taste

      Boil potatoes until tender; cool. Cut potatoes into quarters and put in a glass casserole dish. Cut Velveeta into cubes and mix with mayonnaise. Spread over potatoes; salt and pepper. Crumble bacon over top. Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes or until bubbly.
