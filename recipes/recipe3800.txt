STICKY CHICKEN WINGS


5  lb. chicken wings, tips cut off
1  c. soy sauce
1  c. white sugar
1  c. brown sugar

      Bake wings at 400 degrees; for 45 minutes.  Heat soy sauce and sugars over medium heat until the sugars are dissolved.  Pour over wings. Turn oven down to 300 degrees; and bake slowly for another 1 1/2 to 2 hours.  Turn them once in a while and keep them in a single layer. Use as either an appetizer or main course.
