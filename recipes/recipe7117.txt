BROCCOLI, RICE, CHEESE AND CHICKEN CASSEROLE


2  c water
2  c uncooked instant rice
2  cans condensed cream of chicken soup
4  stalks celery, chopped small
1/4  c butter
1  c milk
16  oz frozen broccoli, chopped
1  onion, chopped
1  lb processed cheese, cubed

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees;.  In a medium saucepan, bring water to a boil.  Mix in the instant rice.  Cover and remove from heat.  Let stand 5 minutes.  In a large skillet, melt butter.  Add onion and celery.  Cook until onion is beginning to be transparent.  Do not brown.  Add frozen broccoli.  Cook for a few minutes (until broccoli is not frozen).  Stir in soup and milk.  Heat until it begins to simmer.  Add cheese to hot mixture.  In a 9x13 inch baking dish, mix the prepared rice, cream of chicken soup, butter, milk, broccoli, onion and cheese.  Bake for 30-35 minutes or until cheese is bubbly and top is lightly brown.
