HEARTY BLACK-EYED PEA SOUP


1  lb. pork sausage, sliced
1  lb. ground beef
1  large onion, diced
4  c. water
3  cans black-eyed peas, drained
1 (28 oz.)  can diced tomatoes (undrained)
1 (10 oz.)  can diced tomatoes and green chiles
1  can chopped green chiles
4  beef bouillon cubes
4  tsp. molasses
1  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
3/4  tsp. garlic salt
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
1/4  tsp. cumin

      Cook sausage, beef and onion until no longer pink; drain. Add remaining ingredients. Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer for 45 minutes.
