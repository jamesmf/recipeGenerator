DEVILED CLAM DIP


2 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
1  small minced onion
  dash of Worcestershire sauce
  dash of garlic salt and pepper
2  cans minced clams
  juice from 1 can of clams or a little more
  parsley flakes

      Let cream cheese soften at room temperature.  Add juice from minced clams until mixture is the right consistency.  Add remaining ingredients.
