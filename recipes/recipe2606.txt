HONEY BREAD


1  pkg. yeast
1/4  c. water (for yeast)
1  c. hot water
1/2  c. oleo
1/4  c. honey
1  tsp. salt
3 1/4  c. sifted flour
2  eggs
1  c. oatmeal

      Soften  yeast in 1/4 cup water;  blend 1 cup hot water, oleo, honey  and salt in large bowl.  Cool to lukewarm.  Stir in  about 1 1/2 cups flour and beat until smooth and elastic. Blend  in yeast, eggs and oatmeal.  Add the rest of flour and beat  until smooth.  Cover and let rise in warm place about an hour.
      Stir  down and knead a little;  turn into 2-quart round casserole  dish.  Bake  55  to  60  minutes at 375 degrees;.  Allow to stand 10 minutes before removing from dish.
