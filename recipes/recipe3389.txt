AUNT VIOLET'S PICKLES


1  gallon hamburger dills
  4-5 pounds sugar
  Dash of garlic powder
1  bottle Tabasco

      Drain pickles.  Pour sugar over and add the remainder of the ingredients. Use a BIG bowl with a snap-on lid.  Stir well and shake often.  These are ready to eat in 24 hours.  DELICIOUS!
