PIMENTO CHEESE


1  lb. Velveeta Cheese, cubed
1/3  c. Milk
3  tbsp. Sugar
2  tbsp. Vinegar
1  (8 oz.) Pimentos (juice too)
1  Egg
1  c. Mayonnaise

      Heat cheese and milk until blended. Add sugar, pimento, vinegar and egg.  Cook about 15 minutes, stirring constantly. Remove from heat and add mayonnaise. Refrigerate.
