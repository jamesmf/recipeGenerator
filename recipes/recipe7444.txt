MIDDLETOWN OYSTER STEW


3  qt. oysters, washed and drained
1  Tbsp. oil
3  slices salt pork, diced
2 to 3  c. water or liquid from oysters
  salt and pepper to taste

      In frying pan, put oil and diced salt pork; fry slowly, turning constantly until crisp and brown.  Put into a 3-quart cooking pot.  Add oysters (there is some juice in the oysters), stirring constantly and gradually add water or juice. Salt and pepper to taste.  Bring to a slow boil and cook about 15 minutes. Serves 6 to 8.
