SQUASH DRESSING


2  c. squash, sliced
1  medium onion, diced
1  c. cold water
2  c. cornbread crumbs
1  can cream of chicken soup
1/4  c. butter, melted
1/4  tsp. pepper
  salt to taste

      Put squash and onion in water and simmer for 10 minutes.  Drain and mash.  Combine with remaining ingredients.  Pour into buttered casserole dish and bake at 350 degrees; for 25 minutes.
