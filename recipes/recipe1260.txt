PIMENTO "CHEESE" SPREAD


1  c. cashew pieces
1  c. water
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. onion powder
1/4  tsp. garlic powder
1/2  c. pimento and juice
2  tsp. fresh lemon juice

      Blend cashews, salt and water until very smooth.  Put in a pan and cook, stirring constantly, until it thickens.  Return to blender and blend garlic powder, pimento, onion powder, a pinch of thyme and lemon juice.  If used in another recipe, use raw.  If used as a sauce, return to stove and cook until bubbly, about 5 minutes, on low to medium heat.
      Cooked carrots can be substituted.
