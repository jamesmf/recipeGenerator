CHUNKY BARBECUE BEEF
(Serves 8)  

1 3/4  lb. lean beef, cubed
1 1/2  oz. pkg. dry onion soup mix
2 1/2  c. tomato juice
1/2  c. water
1/2  c. catsup
  salt and pepper to taste

      Brown meat in skillet sprayed with vegetable pan spray.  Add remaining ingredients; mix.  Place in 2-quart casserole dish; cover.  Bake at 325 degrees; for 2 hours, adding water if necessary.  Serve over rice, noodles or potatoes.
      Exchanges:  3 lean meat, 1/2 vegetable, 1/2 bread, 1/2 fat. (One-half cup serving of rice, noodles or potatoes equals 1 bread.)
