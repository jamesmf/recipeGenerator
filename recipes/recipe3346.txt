CHEESE BALL


2  c. shredded Cheddar cheese (8 oz.)
2  oz. Bleu cheese, crumbled
1  c. grated Parmesan cheese
1 (3 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese, softened
1/2  tsp. hot dry mustard
1 small  can Pet milk
  walnuts

      Beat all ingredients, except walnuts.  Shape into a ball and roll in walnuts.  Wrap with waxed paper.  Chill.
