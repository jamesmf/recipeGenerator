CABBAGE LASAGNA


1  head cabbage
1  container Ricotta cheese
1  bag Mozzarella cheese
6  slices Provolone cheese
1  egg
1 large  jar spaghetti sauce
3  lb. sausage, ground beef or ground turkey

      Boil cabbage until tender and cool.  Break leaves apart. Combine all cheeses and egg; mix well.  Brown meat and add sauce. In baking pan, alternate sauce, cabbage, cheeses and meat to fill pan.  Bake at 350 degrees; until bubbly, about 30 minutes.
