E. T. CANDIES


1 1/2  c. peanut butter
1/2  c. margarine, melted
1  tsp. vanilla
1 (6 oz.)  pkg. confectioners sugar
12  oz. pkg. chocolate chips
2  Tbsp. Crisco

      Mix peanut butter, margarine, vanilla and sugar; roll into balls, set on waxed paper.  Put toothpick in each ball and refrigerate.
      Melt chocolate chips and 2 tablespoons Crisco in double boiler. Dip each ball in chocolate, then chill, leave toothpicks in.
