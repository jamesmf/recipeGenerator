CHEESE BALL


8  oz. cream cheese
2  tsp. green pepper, finely chopped
2  tsp. pimento, chopped and drained
2  oz. Blue cheese
1  Tbsp. milk to soften the cheese
1/4  tsp. garlic powder
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  lb. pecans

      Soften cream cheese and Blue cheese with milk and mix thoroughly.  Add green pepper, pimento, garlic powder and salt. Mix well.  Form into a ball.  Roll cheese ball in finely ground nuts.
