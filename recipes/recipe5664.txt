CHICKEN, VEGETABLE AND MACARONI SALAD


3  c. diced cooked chicken
1 1/2  c. cooked, drained elbow macaroni
4  hard-cooked eggs, peeled and diced
1 1/2  c. finely diced celery
1  c. diced carrots, drained
1  c. frozen green peas, thawed

 
Dressing:
1 1/2  c. Miracle Whip
1/2  c. finely chopped onion
1/4  tsp. ground nutmeg
  salt and pepper to taste
3  Tbsp. milk or light cream
1/2  clove garlic, peeled and crushed
1  Tbsp. spicy brown mustard

	Place all salad ingredients in a large bowl. Toss lightly to mix.
	In a small bowl, whisk together all dressing ingredients until smooth. Pour over salad and toss well to mix. Cover and refrigerate until ready to serve.
