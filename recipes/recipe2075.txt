BUTTERFINGERS


1  lb. powdered sugar
2  c. Rice Krispies
2  c. peanut butter
3/4  c. margarine
6  oz. chocolate chips
1/2  lb. Hershey bar
1/2  bar paraffin

      Mix powdered sugar, Rice Krispies, peanut butter and margarine together and form into 1-inch balls.  Chill.  In double boiler, melt chocolate chips, Hershey bar and paraffin.  Dip peanut butter ball in chocolate mixture with toothpick.  Cool.
