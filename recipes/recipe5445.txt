HOT DOG CHILI I


1 1/2  lb. ground beef
1/4  c. onion, chopped
4  Tbsp. sugar
1  c. catsup
3  Tbsp. vinegar
1/4  c. water
  salt and pepper to taste
  chili powder to taste

	Brown ground beef. Add onion, salt, pepper and chili powder. Add sugar, catsup, vinegar and water. Stir and simmer 20 minutes.
