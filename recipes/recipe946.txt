BARBECUED LOBSTER


4  rock lobster tails

 
Marinade:
2  Tbsp. shoyu sauce
2  tsp. oyster sauce
1  clove garlic, minced
1  tsp. ginger, grated
1/2  tsp. oil
1 1/2  tsp. sugar
1/4  c. green onions, minced

      Clean and split lobster tails in half lengthwise along the back shells.  Combine marinade ingredients and marinate lobster 3 to 4 hours.  Broil meat side up in infrared broiler under high flame. Broil for 6 to 8 minutes on one side, then turn and broil another 4 to 5 minutes.
