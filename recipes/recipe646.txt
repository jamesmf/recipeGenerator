BANANA PUDDING


1  large bag vanilla wafers
5  bananas
3  eggs, separated
1/4  c. Sweet 'N Low
1 (14 oz.)  can Pet milk
1  tsp. vanilla
2  Tbsp. sugar
1/4  tsp. cream of tartar

      Line a pan with cookies and slice 1 1/2 bananas over cookies. Add more cookies and bananas.  Repeat until all bananas and cookies are gone, making a crust on top with cookies.  Set aside.  Separate eggs; put whites in a bowl and set in refrigerator.  Beat yolks well and put in a double boiler.  Add sweetener and milk and whip well.  Set over medium heat; keep stirring until thick.  Add flavoring and pour over bananas and cookies.  Beat egg whites until stiff.  Add 2 tablespoons sugar and 1/4 teaspoon cream of tartar. Spread over banana pudding.  Bake at 300 degrees; until brown.
