BREAD AND BUTTER PICKLES


4  qt. sliced cucumbers
6  sweet peppers, seeded and chopped
1  tsp. dry mustard
1 1/2  tsp. turmeric
2 1/2  c. sugar
4  large onions, chopped
2  tsp. mustard seeds
4  tsp. salt
3  c. vinegar

      Combine all ingredients in a large pot. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat. Simmer for 30 minutes. Pack in hot sterilized jars and seal.
