7 LAYER CASSEROLE


1  c. uncooked rice (washed)
2  c. can whole kernel corn (drained)
  salt and pepper 1 can tomato sauce
3/4  c. water
1/2  c. chopped onion
3/4  lb. to 1 lb. fresh ground beef
4  strips bacon

      Mix rice, corn, 1 can  tomato sauce, salt and pepper, onion, 1/2 c. water, and ground beef. Place in a baking dish and cover meat with bacon strips. Cover and bake in 350 degrees; oven for 1 hour. Mix 1 can of tomato sauce and 1/4 c. water and pour over meat and bake 30 minutes longer.
