SLOW COOKER HONEY GLAZED HAM


4  lb. boneless fully cooked ham
1 (12 oz.)  can 7-Up
1/4  c. honey
1/2  tsp. prepared mustard
1/4  tsp. ground cloves
1/4  tsp. ground cinnamon

      Place ham and soda into slow cooking pot.  Cover and cook at setting #3 for 6 to 8 hours or #5 setting for 3 to 4 hours.
      Thirty minutes before serving, combine remaining ingredients including 3 tablespoons drippings from bottom of slow cooking pot. Spread glaze over ham.  Cover and continue heating.  Let ham stand for 15 minutes before carving.  Makes 10 to 12 servings.
