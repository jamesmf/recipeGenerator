CARROT CASSEROLE


3  lb. carrots
1/2  c. butter
1 1/2  c. brown sugar
2  tsp. vanilla
3  eggs, beaten
2  Tbsp. flour
  powdered sugar

	Wash and scrape carrots and slice into 1-inch pieces. Parboil in enough water to cover carrots. When tender, drain and puree carrots in blender. Combine butter, brown sugar, eggs, vanilla, flour and carrots. Pour into a 9 x 13-inch greased pan and bake at 350 degrees; for 40 minutes. When done, sprinkle with powdered sugar.
