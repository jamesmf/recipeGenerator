BREADED RANCH CHICKEN


3/4  c. cornflakes, crushed
3/4  c. Parmesan cheese, grated
1  envelope Ranch salad dressing mix
8  (2 lb.) chicken breasts (boneless, skinless, halves)
1/2  c. butter or margarine

	In a shallow bowl, combine cornflakes, Parmesan cheese and salad dressing mix. Dip chicken in butter, then roll in cornflake mixture to coat. Place in a greased 13 x 9-inch baking dish. Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes or until chicken juices run clear.
