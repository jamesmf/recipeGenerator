CHICKEN FILLED DUMPLINGS

Broth:
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1 1/4  c. water
2  Tbsp. chopped onion

 
Dumplings:
1 (8 oz.)  can biscuits
1 1/2  c. cubed chicken
1/4  c. chopped celery
2  Tbsp. chopped onion
1/8 to 1/4  tsp. pepper

      Broth:  In  large  pan  combine  broth  ingredients and heat, stirring occasionally until bubbly.
      Dumplings:  Combine all  ingredients,  except biscuits, and mix well.  Separate biscuits into 10.  Pat  or roll out to about a 4-inch circle.  Place about 1/4 cup chicken mixture on each circle.  Wrap dough around mixture, firmly pressing edges to seal. Place sealed edges down,  of  dumplings,  into soup. Spoon soup over dumplings.  Cover tightly and cook over medium heat for 15 to 20 minutes or until dumplings aren't doughy.
      Note:  Dumplings can be made ahead.  Prepare, cover and refrigerate up to 2 hours before cooking.
