CHARLIE THE TUNA IN A BLANKET


1/4  lb. American cheese (1 c. cubed)
3  hard boiled eggs, chopped
1  (7 oz.) tuna, flaked
2  Tbsp. chopped onion
2  Tbsp. chopped olives
2  Tbsp. chopped sweet pickles
1/2  c. mayonnaise or salad dressing
6  wiener buns

      Combine first 7 ingredients.  Mix lightly.  Split buns and fill.  Heat wrapped in foil in slow 250 degrees; oven for about 30 minutes or until filling is bubbly and cheese is melted.  Great with chips and hot chocolate.
