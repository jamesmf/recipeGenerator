VENISON SALAMI


5  lb. ground venison
5  Tbsp. Morton Quick salt
1 -2  tsp. Hickory smoke flavoring
2 1/2  tsp. black pepper
2  1/2 tsp mustard seed

      Mix ingredients well.  Cover and refrigerate 4 days.  Mix well each day.  On 4th day form into long rolls and bake at 225 degrees for 4 hours.
