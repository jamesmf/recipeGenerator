HOT COCOA


4  c. powdered milk
3/4  c. cocoa
1 1/2  c. sugar or 15 pkg.  Equal
1  c. coffee creamer
1/8  tsp. salt

      Combine all dry ingredients, stirring well.  Store in tightly covered jar in a cool dry place.  To use, place 3 tablespoons of the mix in a cup.  Stir in small amount of boiling water to dissolve, then fill cup with water.
