CABBAGE ROLLS


1  large head cabbage
1  lb. ground beef
2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
1  c. oats
1  c. milk
2  tsp. Crisco oil
1  can tomato soup
2  Tbsp. brown sugar
1/2  c. hot water

	Dip cabbage leaves in hot water. Combine ground beef, salt, pepper, oats and milk. Mix well. Place a small amount of meat mixture into a cabbage leaf and roll up. Place oil in a large pan and place cabbage rolls in pan. Mix tomato soup, brown sugar and water. Pour over cabbage rolls and cook until meat is done and cabbage is tender.
