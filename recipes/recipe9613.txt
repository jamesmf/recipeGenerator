KOREAN MEAT LOAF


1  lb. low-fat chopped beef
2  stalks chopped green onion
1  clove minced garlic
1 1/2  Tbsp. soy sauce
1/4  tsp. pepper
1  egg
2  tsp. brown sugar
1/2  tsp. ground ginger
1  tsp. sesame seeds (optional)
1  tsp. sesame oil
1/2 to 3/4  c. plain bread crumbs (optional)

      Combine all ingredients and form into meat loaf.  Bake in 350 degrees; oven 50 to 60 minutes or until no juice is seen when pierced with fork.
      The aroma, while this is cooking, will make you ravenous!
