SEAFOOD CASSEROLE


1  lb. crab meat
1  c. mayonnaise
1/4  c. onion, chopped
1/2  tsp. salt
1  Tbsp. worcestershire sauce
1  lb. shrimp, cooked and deveined
1  c. celery, chopped
2  c. crushed potato chips
  paprika

      Combine all ingredients except the last two listed. Pour into a buttered casserole dish; top with crushed potato chips and sprinkle with paprika. Bake at 400 degrees; for 25 minutes or until thoroughly heated. Serves 6.
