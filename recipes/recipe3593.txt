PEAR RELISH


1  pk. pears
6  large onions
3  hot peppers (small or medium)
12  bell peppers (red)
1  Tbsp. pickling spices
2  lb. sugar
5  c. vinegar

      Grind pears, peppers and onions. Mix vinegar, sugar, spices and teaspoon of salt. Mix all together; bring to boil for 30 minutes. Put in clean jars while still hot.
