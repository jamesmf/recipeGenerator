MOM'S CHUTNEY CHEESE SPREAD


1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
1  jar Cheddar cheese
1/2  c. chutney, chopped
2  tsp. curry powder
1  tsp. dry mustard
1/2  c. chopped almonds
  additional chutney for garnish

      Mix all ingredients well, except almonds and additional chutney, in a food processor.  Garnish with remaining chutney and almonds.  Best if made the day before serving. Serve with plain crackers.
