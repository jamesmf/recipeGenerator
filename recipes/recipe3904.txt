SALMON LOG


1 large  can salmon
8  oz. cream cheese
1  tsp. lemon juice
2  tsp. grated onion
1  tsp. horseradish
1/4  tsp. salt
1/2  Tbsp. liquid smoke
1/2  c. chopped nuts
3  Tbsp. chopped parsley

      Drain salmon; combine with next 6 ingredients. Make into log and roll in nuts and parsley.
