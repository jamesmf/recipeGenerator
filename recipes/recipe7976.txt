BAR-B-Q BEEF


3 1/2 to 4  lb. chuck roast
1/4  tsp. cayenne pepper
1/2  tsp. minced garlic
2  small onions
2  tsp. horseradish
4  Tbsp. vinegar
4  Tbsp. Worcestershire
1  tsp. salt
2  Tbsp. flour
1  c. ketchup
3  c. water (2 c. first)

      Sear roast at 400 degrees; 1/2 hour to brown.  Mix all remaining ingredients and cook 45 minutes.  Pour over seared roast.  Bake slow and covered until meat shreds, about 3 1/2 hours. Take meat out of pan. Cool enough to handle.  Shred meat into large bowl. Pour sauce from pan over meat and mix.  Reheat as needed.  Serve in hamburger buns.
