TATER TOT CASSEROLE


1  lb. ground meat
1  pkg. Tater Tots
1  can tomato sauce
1  can tomato paste
1  can whole kernel corn
1  box Velveeta cheese

      Brown ground meat. Mix with tomato sauce and paste and whole kernel corn. Fry Tater Tots and place in the bottom of casserole dish. Cover with ground meat, tomato sauce and paste and whole kernel corn. Cover with cheese and place in oven or microwave until cheese melts.
