ACAPULCO'S GUACAMOLE


3  medium avocados, peeled and seeded
1  large fresh tomato, diced
1  fresh pepper, minced
1/4  c. onion, minced (optional)
2  tsp. salt
1/2  lemon, juiced

	Peel and seed avocados. Grind onions and pepper in mortar (molcajete). Mash avocados and combine with onion mixture. Squeeze juice of 1/2 lemon over avocados and blend well. Add diced tomato and salt; mix well. Serve with crispy tostado chips or hot corn tortillas.
