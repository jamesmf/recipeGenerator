VEGETABLE CASSEROLE


2 (12 oz.)  cans white corn
1 (16 oz.)  can French green beans, drained
1/2  c. chopped onion
1/2  c. chopped celery
1/2  c. green pepper
3/4  c. grated cheese
1  can celery soup
1  (8 oz.) sour cream

      Mix.  Put in 9 x 13 pan.  On top of mixture mix 1/2 cup crumbs and 1/2 cup oleo.  Cook 45 minutes at 350 degrees;.  Serves 12 to 15.
