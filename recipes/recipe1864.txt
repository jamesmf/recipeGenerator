SALSA ROJA


5  large ripe tomatoes, diced
1  c. chopped onion
3  large garlic cloves, crushed
1  tsp. salt
2 to 4  Tbsp. dry sherry (optional)
1/2  c. chopped green chilies, canned
1/4 to 1/2  tsp. cayenne
1/2 to 1  tsp. ground cumin
  minced parsley, basil and chives to taste

      Combine all ingredients except parsley, basil and chives in a saucepan.  Bring to a boil, lower the heat and cover.  Simmer for 40 to 50 minutes.  Add minced herbs to taste.
