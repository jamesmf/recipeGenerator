MEXICAN MEAT LOAF


2  lb. ground beef
1  large onion, diced
1  can tomato paste
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  can green chili peppers
1  jar El Paso taco sauce
1  c. Minute rice
1  pkg. small Doritos or corn chips
1  pkg. Cheddar cheese, grated

      Brown ground beef and onion and drain. Add tomato paste, mushroom and chicken soup, green peppers, taco sauce and cooked rice and simmer a few minutes. It's ready to serve over chips and cheese on top.
