APRICOT PINEAPPLE SALAD


1 large  pkg. apricot gelatin
2/3  c. sugar
2/3  c. water
1  c. crushed pineapple, drained
4  oz. jar apricot baby food
8  oz. cream cheese, softened
12  oz. frozen whipped topping

      Mix first 5 ingredients in saucepan and bring to a boil. Cool completely and fold in cream cheese and whipped topping. Pour into mold and refrigerate until set.  Yield:  8 servings.
      Note:  Orange gelatin can be substituted.
