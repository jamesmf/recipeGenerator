FABULOUS CHEESE BALL


2 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
1 large  bunch green onions (tops too), chopped fine
1  Tbsp. Hellmann's mayonnaise
3/4  Tbsp. mustard
1  tsp. cayenne pepper
1  tsp. paprika
1  tsp. Tabasco sauce
1  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/2  tsp. Accent
1  tsp. Lawry's seasoned salt
1  c. pecans, finely chopped

      Combine all ingredients, except pecans.  Shape into 1 large ball, or 2 small balls.  Roll in chopped pecans.  Chill overnight. Serve with assorted crackers.
