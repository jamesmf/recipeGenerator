BONNIE RAYBURN'S MEAT LOAF

Loaf:
1 1/2  lb. hamburger meat
1  beaten egg
2  Tbsp. onion, chopped
1/2 (8 oz.)  can tomato sauce (reserve 1/2 can for sauce)
1/4  c. bread crumbs
  salt and pepper to taste

	Preheat oven to 350 degrees;.  Combine all ingredients. Mix well. Shape mixture into a 12 x 7 1/2 x 2 loaf pan.
Sauce:
1  Tbsp. sugar
1/2  can tomato sauce
1/2  c. water
1  Tbsp. vinegar
2  Tbsp. mustard

	Mix sauce. Pour over meat loaf.  Baste every 15 minutes. Bake approximately 1 hour or until done.
