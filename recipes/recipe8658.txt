GIGI'S DELICIOUS SPAGHETTI DINNER


1  lb. ground meat
1  onion, chopped
1  large bell pepper, thinly sliced
4  cloves garlic, minced
2 large  cans diced tomatoes
1 small  can tomato paste
1  can beer
1 large  can (1 3/4 c. approximately) water
1 (8 oz.)  can sliced mushrooms
1  pkg. sliced pepperoni (optional)
1  tsp. oregano
1  tsp. rosemary
1  tsp. Italian seasoning
1  tsp. basil
  salt and pepper to taste
  cooked spaghetti
  Parmesan cheese, freshly grated

	In a large pot, saut√© meat, onions, bell peppers and garlic until meat browns. Drain. Add remaining ingredients and bring to a boil. Turn to low and simmer uncovered for 3 hours. Serve over spaghetti. Add freshly grated Parmesan cheese.
	 Note: The sauce can also be used for lasagna and manicotti dishes.
	 The Day After Lunch:  Toast sliced bread.  Add a slice of cheese, top with leftover sauce and broil. Add grated Parmesan cheese. Freezes well.
