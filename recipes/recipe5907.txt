ORIENTAL TOSSED SALAD

Dressing:
3/4  c. sugar
1/2  c. red wine vinegar
1  c. oil
3  Tbsp. soy sauce
2  pkg. Ramen noodles, crushed
1  c. chopped walnuts
1  bunch chopped green onion
1  bunch broccoli florets
2  pkg. romaine lettuce

      Mix all together right before serving. Serves 12 to 15.
