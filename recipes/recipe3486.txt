BREAD AND BUTTER PICKLES


6  qt. sliced cucumbers
6  medium sweet onions, sliced thin
1  c. salt
1 1/2  qt. vinegar
6  c. sugar
1/4  tsp. cayenne
1  Tbsp. celery seeds
1/2  c. mustard seeds

      Mix all ingredients in large saucepan and heat to simmer. Do not boil.  Pack cucumber mixture in hot jars.  Cover with vinegar mix and seal jars.  Let stand for a couple of weeks before eating.
