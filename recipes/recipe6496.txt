EGGPLANT CASSEROLE


1  medium eggplant, peeled
1  medium onion, chopped
2  cloves garlic, chopped
1/2  green pepper, chopped
  parsley
2  Tbsp. bacon drippings
4  slices dry bread
1  lb. shrimp
1  lb. crabmeat
  salt and pepper
  dash of red pepper
  buttered bread crumbs

      Boil eggplant in salted water until tender; drain and slice. Saut√© onion, garlic, green pepper and parsley in bacon drippings. Remove from heat; add eggplant.  Soak bread in water; squeeze out excess liquid.  Add to eggplant mixture.  Add seafood; season to taste.  Place in well-greased casserole or baking shells.  Top with buttered bread crumbs.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes.
