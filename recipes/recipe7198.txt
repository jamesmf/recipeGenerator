STUFFED MEAT LOAF


2  lb. hamburg
1/2  lb. cooked ham
1  box Stove Top stuffing
  cheese (optional)
  onions (optional)
  salt
  pepper
  garlic salt

      Cook Stove Top stuffing as directed on the box.  Take hamburg; roll out on a flat surface about 1-inch thick.  Take cooked ham; put slices to cover all hamburg.  Take stuffing; put into a line towards the center.  Even it all out.  Roll into a meat loaf; cook in oven at 350 degrees; for about 2 hours.  Enjoy!  Makes 6 to 8 servings.
