EASY CHICKEN POT PIE


1 2/3  c. frozen mixed vegetables, thawed
1  c. cut up cooked chicken
1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can cream of chicken soup
1  c. Bisquick baking mix
1/2  c. milk
1  egg

      Heat oven to 400 degrees;.  Mix vegetables, chicken and soup in 9-inch pie plate.  Mix remaining ingredients with fork until well mixed.  Pour into pie plate over chicken mixture.  Bake at 400 degrees; for 30 minutes or until golden brown.
