QUICK HAMBURGER CASSEROLE


1  lb. hamburger
16  oz. pork and beans
2  Tbsp. brown sugar
1  c. shredded cheese
1  tsp. salt
3/4  c. barbecue sauce
1  Tbsp. minced onion

      Brown hamburger, drain.  Stir in  salt, pork and beans, barbecue  sauce, brown sugar and  onion.  Heat  until  bubbly. Pour into 2 quart casserole.  Sprinkle  with  cheese.  Bake in preheated 375 degrees; oven for 25 to 30 minutes.
