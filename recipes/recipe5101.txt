BUFFET SALAD


1  pkg. lemon Jell-O (3 oz.)
1  pkg. lime Jell-O
2  c. boiling water
1  c. mayonnaise
1 (No. 2)  can pineapple
1  c. cottage cheese
2  Tbsp. chopped pimento
2  Tbsp. horseradish
1/2  c. nuts

      Dissolve Jell-O in hot water. Add pineapple with juice. Chill until slightly thick. Mix rest of ingredients and fold into congealed Jell-O. Mold in pan or muffin pans. Serves 12.
