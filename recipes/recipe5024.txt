TACO SALAD


2  cans Ranch style beans, drained
2  c. shredded cheddar cheese
1  head of lettuce, chopped or torn
1 small  bottle Creamy Italian dressing
  2-3 tomatoes, cut up
1  onion, chopped
1  small bag regular size Fritos corn chips

      Mix all together in large salad bowl.  You may reserve chips and add just before serving.
