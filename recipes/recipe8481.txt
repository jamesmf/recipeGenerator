BEEF STEW DELUXE


3  lb. beef stew meat (cut into cubes)
1  c. port wine
1  tsp. salt
1  can onion soup, undiluted
1  can mushroom soup, undiluted
1- 8  oz. can sliced mushrooms, drained
1/4  tsp. pepper

      IT IS NOT NECESSARY TO BROWN MEAT. Mix all ingredients in a covered casserole dish and bake at 300 degrees; for 3 hours. Serve over fluffy rice, noodles or whipped potatoes. Thicken gravy if necessary. Raise temperature of over to 400 degrees;.  Remove cover from pan and add 1 can of refrigerated canned biscuits to top of stew. Bake until biscuits are browned. Bake extra biscuits to serve stew over biscuit.
