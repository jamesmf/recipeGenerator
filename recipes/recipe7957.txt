MAC AND HAM CASSEROLE


1 (14 oz.)  pkg. Kraft Deluxe Macaroni & Cheese Sauce
1  cup frozen peas, thawed
1  cup cubed ham
1/2  cup milk
1/2  cup Kraft shredded mild cheddar cheese
1/2  cup crushed Ritz crackers
1  Tbsp. butter

      Prepare macaroni and cheese as directed on package.  Add peas, ham and milk.  Mix well.
      Spoon into 1 1/2 quart casserole; sprinkle with cheddar cheese.  Mix Ritz crackers crumbs with melted butter and top casserole. Bake uncovered 400 degrees for 15 minutes or until heated thoroughly.
