MINESTRONE SOUP


6  c. water
2 large  cans whole tomatoes
1/2  c. celery
2  c. carrots
4  medium potatoes, cubed
1  large onion, chopped
2  tsp. salt
1  tsp. sugar
1  tsp. black pepper
1  lb. ground beef
1  lb. ground pork
2  eggs
1/2  c. bread crumbs
2  tsp. salt
  dash of pepper
2  cans cut green beans, strained
1  can whole kernel corn, strained

      Put first 9 ingredients in large pot and put on stove to simmer.  While pot is simmering, prepare the following:  Mix together ground beef and pork, eggs, bread crumbs, salt and pepper. Roll into small 1-inch meatballs; add to boiling pot on stove. Simmer 30 minutes, then add green beans and corn.  Simmer another 10 minutes.
