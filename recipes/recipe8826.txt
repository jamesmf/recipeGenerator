MEAT LOAF


3  lb. ground beef
2  lb. ground pork or sausage (mild, hot, medium, to your liking)
4  eggs, beaten
1 1/2  c. carrots, ground or grated
2  c. milk
1 1/2  tsp. pepper
2  c. cracker crumbs
1  large onion
2  Tbsp. salt

      Mix well. Form into loaves; pour tomato over each loaf. Bake at 325 degrees; for 1 1/2 hours. Feeds 8 cowboys or 10 city slickers.
