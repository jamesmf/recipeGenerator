FRUIT SALAD


2  c. miniature marshmallows
1 large  can pineapple tidbits
1  c. coconut
1/2  pt. sour cream
1  c. miniature orange slices, drained
1/2  c. nuts

      Drain pineapple and save juice for thinning sour cream.  Mix juice with sour cream. Mix all fruit with the sour cream mixture. Put into covered dish and refrigerate overnight.
      Optional:  May add cherries, also.
