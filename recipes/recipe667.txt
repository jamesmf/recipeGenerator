CAPER SALAD DRESSING FOR PASTA


1/8  c. capers
1/4  c. red wine vinegar
1  garlic clove
2  tsp. salt
1  tsp. oregano
1/4  tsp. lemon pepper
1/4  tsp. seasoned salt
1/4  tsp. sesame seed
3/4  c. olive oil

      Puree the first 8 dressing ingredients.  Slowly pour in the oil, blending continuously until smooth.  Pour the dressing over the pasta.  Serves 4 deliciously.
