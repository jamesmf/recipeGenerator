BAKED BAR-B-Q MEATBALLS


2  lb. hamburger
1  c. milk
1  tsp. salt
1/2  c. cracker crumbs (14)
1  small onion, chopped
1 1/2  c. catsup
1/3  c. vinegar
3/4  c. brown sugar

      Mix hamburger, milk, salt, cracker crumbs and onion.  Make into meatballs.  Put in 9 x 13 pan (shallow pan).  Mix together catsup, vinegar and brown sugar.  Pour over meatballs.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour or until done.
