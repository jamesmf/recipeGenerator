NOODLE CASSEROLE


2  lb. ground beef
1  onion, chopped
1  can tomato puree
1  can tomato soup
1  jar olives, chopped
1  can mushrooms, sliced
1 large  jar spaghetti sauce
1 (12 oz.)  pkg. noodles
  salt and pepper to taste

      Brown beef and onion.  Add tomato puree, tomato soup, olives, mushrooms and spaghetti sauce.  Add salt and pepper to taste.  Mix with cooked noodles.  Pour in baking dish.  Bake 30 minutes at 350 degrees;.  Sprinkle with cheese.  Return to oven until cheese melts. This freezes well.
