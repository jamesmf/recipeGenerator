GOLOBKI (CABBAGE) CASSEROLE


1  small head cabbage, chopped
1  medium onion, chopped
1/4  tsp. pepper
1  lb. ground beef
1  can water (tomato soup can)
2  cans tomato soup
1  tsp. salt
1/2  c. instant rice (uncooked)

      Place chopped cabbage in bottom of 3-quart casserole.  Brown meat and onions.  Pour off fat.  Stir in rice, salt and pepper. Spread meat over cabbage with spoon.  Mix tomato soup with water and pour over mixture.  Cover and bake at 350 degrees; for approximately 1 1/2 hours or until cabbage is soft.  Serves 4 to 5.
