SLOW COOKER CHICKEN


1  c. sliced carrots
4  oz. diced onion
1/2  c. mushrooms (optional)
1/2  c. sliced celery
2 1/2  lb. skinned chicken pieces
3/4  c. bouillon
1 1/2  tsp. seasoned salt
1/4  tsp. marjoram
  dash of salt

      Place first 4 ingredients in bottom of slow cooker.  Add chicken pieces.  Mix next 4 ingredients together and pour over chicken and vegetables.  Cover.  Cook medium-high for 2 1/2 to 3 hours.  Serves 4.
