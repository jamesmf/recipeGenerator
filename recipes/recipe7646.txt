COUNTRY BREAKFAST PIE


1  lb. sausage
  9-inch unbaked pie shell
1 1/2  c. grated Swiss cheese
1/4  c. chopped green bell pepper
1/4  c. chopped red bell pepper
2  Tbsp. chopped onion
4  eggs, lightly beaten
1  c. light cream

      Fry,  drain  and  crumble  sausage.   Mix   cheese  and sausage.  Sprinkle in shell.  Combine other ingredients.  Pour in shell.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 35 to 40 minutes  or  until  set. Cool 10 minutes before serving.
