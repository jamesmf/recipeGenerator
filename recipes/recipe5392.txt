CAESAR SALAD


1/4  c. Parmesan or Romano cheese, grated
1/4  c. light mayonnaise
1  Tbsp. water
1  Tbsp. lemon juice
1/2  tsp. garlic, minced
1/2  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/4  tsp. black pepper
8  c. romaine lettuce, chopped
1  c. croutons

	Combine cheese, mayonnaise, water, lemon juice, garlic, Worcestershire sauce and pepper in a small bowl. Mix until creamy. Combine lettuce and dressing in a large bowl. Toss well to coat. Sprinkle with croutons.
