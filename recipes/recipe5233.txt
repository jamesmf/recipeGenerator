MACARONI RING SALAD


1  c. Mayo
1  c. sugar
1/3  c. vinegar
1 small  box of macaroni rings
2  c. shredded cabbage
  small onions, chopped
1/2  green pepper, chopped
1  sliced cucumber

      Cook macaroni rings, drain and cool.  Mix Mayo, sugar and vinegar.  Mix with macaroni rings and vegetables.
