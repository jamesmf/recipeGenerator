PINEAPPLE-ORANGE CAKE


1  pkg. yellow butter cake mix
4  eggs
3/4  c. Crisco oil
1 (10 oz.)  can mandarin oranges

 
Topping:
1 (15 1/2 oz.)  can crushed pineapple, drained
1 large  pkg. instant pudding (vanilla or coconut)
1  large container Cool Whip

      Add ingredients and mix well.  Pour in sheet pan.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 25 to 30 minutes.  Mix topping ingredients and put on top of cake when cool.  Refrigerate.
