CRANBERRY SALAD


2  pkg. cherry flavor gelatin
1/2  c. cold water
1  c. sugar
1  c. celery, chopped fine
1  c. raw cranberries, ground
1  orange, ground
1  can crushed pineapple, drained (save)
1/2  c. English walnuts
2  c. hot water
1  c. pineapple syrup
1  Tbsp. lemon juice

	Dissolve gelatin in hot water. Add cold water, sugar, syrup and lemon juice until partially jelled. Add other ingredients and pour into oiled mold.
