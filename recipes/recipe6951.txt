TACO IN A BOWL


3/4  lb. hamburger
2  c. water
1  lb. tomatoes, cut up
2  Tbsp. taco seasoning mix or more to taste
1  lb. can pinto beans
  corn chips
  shredded Cheddar cheese
1/3  c. chopped onion
1 (8 oz.)  can tomato sauce
  sour cream

      Fry hamburger and onion until meat is brown; drain off fat. Add water, undrained tomatoes, undrained beans, tomato sauce and taco seasoning. Simmer, covered, for 15 minutes. Ladle into 6 bowls and pass cheese, chips and sour cream to top each serving.
      Note: Kidney beans may be substituted for pinto beans.
