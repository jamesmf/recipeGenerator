MARINADES FOR MEATS

For Meatballs:
1  lb. can cranberry sauce
12  oz. chili sauce
2  Tbsp. brown sugar
1  Tbsp. lemon juice

      Heat and pour over meatballs.
For Wieners:
6  Tbsp. mustard
2  Tbsp. lemon juice
2  c. jellied cranberry sauce

      Heat and pour over wieners.
For Chicken and Pork Chops:
3/4  c. oil
1/4  c. red wine vinegar
3  Tbsp. lemon juice
6  Tbsp. soy sauce
2  Tbsp. worcestershire sauce
1  Tbsp. dry mustard
1  Tbsp. salt
1 1/2  tsp. pepper

      Mix together and pour over meat.  Let set in refrigerator until ready to cook.
