MARSHMALLOW GOODIES


1  bag large marshmallows
1  lb. caramel candies
1  can sweetened condensed milk
8  oz. oleo or butter

      Freeze marshmallows.  Melt caramel candies.  Add condensed milk and oleo or butter.  Coat frozen marshmallows in caramel and roll in Rice Krispies; use a toothpick for handling.  Cool on waxed paper.  Delicious!
