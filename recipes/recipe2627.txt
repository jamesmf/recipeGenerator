CHOCOLATE CREAM PIE


2  envelopes Dream Whip topping mix
2 3/4  c. milk
2 (4 oz.)  pkg. instant chocolate pudding mix
1  graham cracker pie crust
  Cool Whip

      Mix  1  cup of milk with topping mix until fluffy.  Add both packages  of chocolate pudding and remaining milk;  beat until fluffy.  Spoon  into pie shell.  Top with Cool Whip and chill about 3 hours.
