NOODLE MIX


2  c. egg noodles, dry
2  cans cream of celery soup
1  lb. hamburger meat
1  Tbsp. minced onion
1  Tbsp. minced garlic
1  Tbsp. parsley
  Water as needed, approximately 2 cans
  Salt and pepper to taste

      Cook egg noodles according to directions on package.  Drain and rinse in hot water.  Brown hamburger meat and drain.  Combine soup, onion, garlic and parsley.  Add water, stir.  Then combine with noodles. Serves 6.
