CHICKEN PIE


3  to 4 chicken breasts, boiled and cut in pieces
1  stick butter
2  cans Veg-All, drained
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  can mushroom soup
1  can chicken broth
1 1/2  c. self-rising flour
3/4  c. broth
3/4  c. milk

      Cook chicken. Remove from broth. Cool. Melt butter in baking dish (13 x 9). Add chicken to butter. Add 2 cans Veg-All, drained. Mix soups, flour, milk and broth together. Pour over top. Bake at 425 degrees; until brown.
