PEAR SALAD


2  C. pear juice, reserve pears
2 - 3  oz. pkgs. lemon Jell-O
2 - 8  oz. cream cheese
8  oz. Cool Whip

      Dissolve Jell-O in pear juice and bring to a boil.  Cool. Mix cream cheese and add Jell-O mixture to that.
      Transfer to blender and add pears.  Blend until smooth. Chill.  Once it is almost set, add Cool Whip.  Store in refrigerator.
