LAYERED TACO SALAD


1/2  lb. ground beef
1/2  c. onions, chopped
2  tsp. chili powder
1/2  tsp. salt
6  c. lettuce
2  c. tomatoes
4  c. Doritos chips
1  lb. Velveeta cheese

      Layer lettuce, tomatoes and chips, then browned beef with seasoning. Melt cheese and add on top of beef. Serve immediately.
