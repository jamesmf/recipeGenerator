GREEN TOMATO RELISH


2  qt. chopped green tomatoes
2  medium onions, chopped
2  qt. cold water
1/2  c. salt
1 1/2  c. white vinegar
1/2  c. boiling water
1 1/2  c. sugar
1 1/2  Tbsp. celery seed
1  Tbsp. mustard seed
1/2  tsp. each ground turmeric and cinnamon
1/4  tsp. powdered mustard

      Combine tomatoes and onions with water and salt in crock or bowl; soak for 3 hours.  Drain and rinse thoroughly with cold water.  Combine remaining ingredients and boil for 3 minutes.  Add tomatoes and onions; bring to a boil and simmer uncovered for 10 minutes.  Pack into hot sterilized jars and seal.  Makes 2 or 2 1/2 pints.
