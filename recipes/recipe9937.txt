HASH BROWN CASSEROLE


2  lb. pkg. hash brown potatoes
2 (8 oz.)  cartons sour cream
1  can cream of chicken soup
1/2  c. chopped onions
1/4  c. melted margarine
2  c. grated cheese
  salt
  corn flakes

      Defrost potatoes.  Mix all ingredients.  Put in oiled dish. Bake at 325 degrees; for 1 1/2 hours.  Crush 2 cups corn flakes.  Put on top.  Pour 1/4 cup margarine over top after it has baked 1 hour. Use 9 x 13-inch dish.
