SOUR CREAM ENCHILADAS


1 1/2  lb. hamburger, browned and drained
1 small  can green chilies
  onion
1  pkg. taco seasoning
1  can cream of chicken soup
12  oz. sour cream
6  flour tortilla shells
  Cheddar cheese

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees;. To hamburger, add onions, chilies and taco seasoning. Let simmer according to seasoning instructions. While hamburger simmers, mix cream of chicken soup and sour cream. Warm this. Pour half of sour cream mixture into bottom of lasagna or cake pan. Roll hamburger into flour shells and place on sour cream mixture. Pour the rest of the sour cream mixture over shells and cover evenly. Sprinkle cheese on top. Bake for 20 minutes at 350 degrees;.
