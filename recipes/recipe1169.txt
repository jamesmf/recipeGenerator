BLUEBERRY MUFFINS


2  c. flour
1/4  c. artificial sweetener
3  tsp. baking powder
1/8  tsp. salt
3/4  c. fresh or frozen unsweetened blueberries
1/4  c. soft margarine
1  beaten egg
1  c. milk

      Mix flour, sweetener, baking powder, salt, berries and margarine.  Add egg and milk.  Bake at 425 degrees; for 15 minutes.  Makes 15 medium size muffins.
