CORN CASSEROLE


4  eggs, beaten
1  pkg. jalapeno or Mexican cornbread mix
1/2  c. oil
2 (15 oz.)  cans cream-style corn
1/2  c. chopped onions
1/2  c. chopped bell pepper
1/2  c. shredded Cheddar cheese

      Blend eggs, cornbread mix and oil together. Add remaining ingredients and pour in greased baking dish. Bake at 400 degrees; for 30 minutes. Sprinkle paprika on top.
