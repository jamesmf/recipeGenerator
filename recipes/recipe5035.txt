GRANDMA'S MUSHROOM SOUP


3  Tbsp. butter
1  slice onion
1/4  lb. mushrooms (optional)
1  Tbsp. flour
4  c. chicken broth
  salt and pepper
  lemon juice
1/2  c. cream or rich milk

      Melt  butter  in  deep  pan.  Add  onion and mushrooms, chopped  fine.  Cook  slowly  10  minutes, stirring as needed. Add remaining  ingredients,  except  cream.  Bring to boiling point and  simmer  5  minutes.  Season to taste.  Just before serving, add cream.
