SIMPLE MEAT LOAF


2  lb. hamburger (lean)
1  lb. sausage (lean)
1  pkg. saltine crackers (crushed)
2  eggs, slightly beaten
1  medium onion, (diced)
1/2  bell pepper (diced)
2  tsp. ground sage
2  tsp. salt (more or less)
1/2  tsp. black pepper
  ketchup

      Mix all ingredients well except for ketchup,  then form into two loaves in baking pan. Cover with ketchup, top and sides.
       Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes or until done.
