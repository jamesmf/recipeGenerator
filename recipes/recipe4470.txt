THREE-BEAN CHILI


1  large onion, chopped
2  Tbsp. vegetable oil
2 (14 1/2 oz.)  cans tomatoes
1 (15 oz.)  can kidney beans
1 (15 oz.)  can pinto beans
1 (15 oz.)  can black beans
1  medium green pepper, chopped
1  c. chicken broth
1  c. picante sauce
1  tsp. ground cumin

      Rinse and drain beans; set aside. In a large pot, cook onion in oil until onion is tender, not brown. Add remaining ingredients; bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer 10 minutes, uncovered. Stir occasionally. Top with cheese, sour cream or any toppings of choice.
