BANANA LOAF CAKE


1  c. bananas, whipped
1  c. dates, chopped
1/2  c. nuts
1 3/4  c. flour
2  tsp. baking powder
1/4  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. soda
1/3  c. vegetable shortening
2  eggs

      Cream together shortening, eggs, bananas and dates.  Sift together the dry ingredients, then mix all together.  Bake one hour at 350 degrees;.
