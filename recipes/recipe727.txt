HOT AND SPICY PASTA



1/2  lb. lean pork, cut in 1/4-inch pieces
2  tsp. sea salt
1  tsp. hot red pepper flakes
1/4  c. red wine
1  pkg. red penne pasta
1  Tbsp. vegetable oil
1  c. thinly sliced shallots
2  c. crushed tomatoes
1  c. chicken stock or bouillon
1  c. heavy cream
1  c. grated Parmesan cheese
1/2  c. freshly chopped parsley

      Bring water to boil for pasta.  In a small bowl, mix pork, 1/2 the salt, pepper flakes and wine and marinate for 5 minutes.  Cook pasta al dente.  Heat large sauté pan over high heat.  Add oil and pork mixture and stir-fry for 1 minute.  Lower heat to medium high; add shallots and sauté for 1 minute.  Add tomatoes and remaining salt and sauté for 1 minute.  Stir in chicken stock and cream and simmer for 1 more minute.  Add drained pasta to sauce along with the cheese.  Toss and continue to cook until sauce is creamy and cheese is melted.  Add parsley and serve directly from the pan.
