EASY CHICKEN DISH


6  to 8 chicken breasts, cooked and cut into chunks
8  c. cracker crumbs
1 1/2  sticks margarine, melted
1  Tbsp. poppy seeds
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  can cream of celery soup
2  c. sour cream

	Preheat oven to 350 degrees;.  In a bowl, mix crackers with margarine and poppy seeds.
	 In a separate bowl, mix soups and sour cream. In a greased baking sheet, cover the bottom of the pan with 1/2 of the cracker mixture.  Place the chicken chunks over the cracker mixture and pour soup mixture over chicken. Top with the remaining cracker mixture. Bake for 1 hour.
