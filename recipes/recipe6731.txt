EASY CHICKEN AND RICE


1  chicken, rinsed and cut up or equivalent pieces
1  c. uncooked rice
2  cans mushroom soup
1 soup  can water
  salt and pepper

      Place chicken and rice in casserole or pan.  Spoon the soup over all.  Add water, salt and pepper.  Cover tightly with foil and bake at 350 degrees; for 1 1/2 hours.
      Herbs such as rosemary or parsley may be sprinkled on before baking.
