STUFFED CLAMS


2  boxes seasoned croutons
2 (8 to 10 oz.)  cans minced clams (do not drain)
  additional clam juice to moisten
1/2  c. oil
1  small onion, minced
  garlic salt
  parsley flakes
  paprika

      Combine first 6 ingredients.  Fill shells with mixture. Sprinkle with parsley flakes and paprika.  Bake 1/2 hour at 350 degrees;.
      Can be frozen on sheets for 1 to 2 weeks.  Makes a great first course, or, if using smaller shells, a great canape.
