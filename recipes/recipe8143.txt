CHICKEN PRIMAVERA


1  c. julienne sweet red pepper
1  c. sliced, fresh mushrooms
1/4  c. chopped green onions
3  garlic cloves, minced
1/4  c. butter
2  c. whipping cream
1  tsp. dried basil
2  grilled, boneless, skinless chicken breast halves, thinly sliced
1/2  tsp. salt
  hot, cooked angel hair pasta

      In a large skillet, saut the red pepper, mushrooms, onions and garlic in butter. Add the cream, basil, chicken and salt. Cook and stir until slightly thickened. Serve over pasta. Yields 4 servings.
