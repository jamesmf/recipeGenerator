CHEESEBURGER PIE


1  lb. ground beef
1/2  tsp. salt
2  eggs
1  c. milk
1  c. chopped onion
1  c. shredded Cheddar cheese
1/2  c. original Bisquick

      Preheat oven to 400 degrees;.  Grease 9-inch pie plate.  Cook beef and onion until beef is browned; drain.  Spread in pie plate. Sprinkle with salt and cheese.  Stir eggs, milk and Bisquick until well blended.  Pour on top of meat and cheese.  Bake 25 minutes. Makes 6 servings.
