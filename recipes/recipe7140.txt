POPPY SEED CHICKEN


8  boneless chicken breasts
1-1/2  tsp salt
1-3/4  sticks butter
2  cans cream of chicken soup
8  oz sour cream
3  packets Ritz crackers
1  tbsp poppy seed

      Boil chicken breasts with salt and a 1/4 stick of butter. Simmer for 1 hour and save the broth.  Next cut the chicken into small pieces and lay them in a rectangular dish.  Add cream of chicken soup, sour cream, and 1 cup of broth.  Mix together and spread in the dish.  Melt remaining butter in microwave. Add crumbled Ritz crackers and poppy seeds to melted butter.  Mix together and spread over the chicken mixture.  Bake at 375 degrees; for 30 minutes.
