PARTY POPS


1 (20 oz.)  pkg. refrigerated sugar cookie dough

      Mix brownie mix and put in a 12-inch pizza pan; bake for 30 to 35 minutes at temperature recommended on box.  Cool completely. Frost with white frosting mix, made bright orange with red and yellow food coloring.  Make a jack-o'-lantern face with candy corn and M&M's candy pieces.
