CHICKEN WITH SNOW PEAS
(Serves 4)  

6  Tbsp. peanut oil
1  lb. boneless chicken, cut in thin strips
1  bunch green spring onions, cut in 1-inch pieces
1 (6 oz.)  pkg. McKenzie's frozen pea pods
1 (8 oz.)  can bamboo shoots, drained
1 (8 oz.)  can sliced water chestnuts, drained
2  Tbsp. cornstarch, dissolved in 2 Tbsp. water
4  Tbsp. soy sauce
1  Tbsp. cooking sherry
1/2  tsp. sugar
1/8  tsp. garlic powder
1/8  tsp. ground ginger
1  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper

      CONVENTIONAL:  In small cup, combine cornstarch, water, soy sauce, sherry, sugar and seasonings.  In large frying pan, heat oil until very hot.  Add chicken and green onion.  Reduce heat.  Cook, stirring occasionally, until chicken is white and done.  Add vegetables and soy sauce mixture.  Cook on simmer setting, stirring occasionally, until mixture has thickened.  Pea pods should be hot, but still crisp.  Serve over fluffy white rice.
      MICROWAVE:  Use full power.  Evenly combine cornstarch, water, soy sauce, sherry, sugar and seasonings.  Preheat a 10-inch browning skillet for 4 1/2 to 5 minutes.  Quickly add oil, chicken and vegetables.  Cover with lid.  Cook for 4 to 5 minutes, stirring after 2 minutes.  Stir in soy sauce mixture and cook another 2 to 3 minutes, stirring after 1 minute.  Allow to stand 3 to 4 minutes.
