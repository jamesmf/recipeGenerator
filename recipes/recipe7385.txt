SAUERKRAUT AND PORK


2  lb. pork, cubed
1 1/2  lb. kraut
1/2  pt. sour cream
1  onion, sliced
  salt and pepper
1/2  tsp. paprika

      Brown onions in shortening.  Add paprika and meat.  Salt and pepper to taste.  Cook over low heat until meat is done.  Add kraut.  Cook until kraut is browned.  Add sour cream.
