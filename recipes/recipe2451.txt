APPLE DUMPLINGS


6  cooking apples
4  Tbsp. brown sugar
1 1/2  tsp. cinnamon
4  Tbsp. butter

 
Pastry:
3  c. flour
2  tsp. baking powder
1  tsp. salt
1  c. shortening
3/4  c. milk

 
Sauce:
1 1/2  c. sugar
1 1/2  c. water
1/4  tsp. cinnamon
1/4  tsp. nutmeg
1  Tbsp. butter

      Pastry:  Combine flour, salt and baking powder.  Cut in shortening.  Stir in water.  Roll out on floured board.  Cut pastry into 6 squares.  On each square, place 1 cooking apple (peeled and cored).  In center of apple, place 2 teaspoons of brown sugar, 1/4 teaspoon of cinnamon and 2 teaspoons of butter (dot on top). Moisten edges of pastry with water.  Bring edges to center and seal.  Place dumplings in a greased 12 x 8 x 2-inch baking dish. Bake at 375 degrees; for 35 minutes.
      Sauce:  Meanwhile, combine sugar, water, cinnamon, nutmeg and butter in saucepan.  Bring to a boil.  Simmer for 5 minutes.  Pour over dumplings when they are done and serve immediately.
