NO FUSS MEATBALL APPETIZER
(Or can use wieners.)  

1 (14 oz.)  pkg. frozen meatballs
1  Tbsp. soy sauce
1/2  c. chili sauce
1/2  c. grape jelly
1/4  c. Dijon mustard

      Combine soy sauce, chili sauce, jelly and mustard. Place meatballs in crock-pot and pour sauce over them. Let them cook 3 to 4 hours on low.
