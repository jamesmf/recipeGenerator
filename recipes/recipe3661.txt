CHEESE SAUCE


3  Tbsp. butter or margarine
1/2  tsp. salt
3  Tbsp. flour
1 1/2 to 2  c. grated Cheddar cheese
  few drops of Tabasco sauce
1  c. milk

      Melt butter in saucepan.  Add salt; stir to dissolve.  Add flour.  Heat until mixture is bubbly.  Add milk; cook until near boiling and mixture thickens.  Add cheese and Tabasco.
      This is a great sauce for vegetables like broccoli, cauliflower, etc.
