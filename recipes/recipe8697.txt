NO FUSS PORK CHOPS


3  or 4 boneless pork loin chops
2  Tbsp. oil
1  medium onion, chopped
1/2  c. pineapple juice
2  Tbsp. brown sugar
2  Tbsp. cider vinegar
  salt and pepper to taste

	In large skillet, heat oil. Add pork chops and brown on both sides. Add onions and cook until tender.
	Combine the pineapple juice, brown sugar, vinegar, salt and pepper and pour over pork chops. Cover and simmer until meat is tender (about 15 to 20 minutes). Serve with rice or noodles.
