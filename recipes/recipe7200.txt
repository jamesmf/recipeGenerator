SANDWICH STEAK ROLLS


  Stove Top stuffing
  sandwich steaks
1  can cream of celery soup or cream of mushroom

      Make stuffing as directed, one package for 4 rolls.  Wrap 2 steaks around 1 1/2 tablespoons stuffing.  Put as many as you want in baking dish.  Cover with soup.  Add a little water to rinse can.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 to 1 1/2 hours.  I cover for first 1/2 hour, then uncover for remainder.
