HEARTY SOUP


1  medium onion, chopped
1  or 2 garlic cloves
1  c. chopped celery
1  lb. hamburger meat
1  can fat-free chicken broth
1  can tomatoes with green chilies
1  can stewed tomatoes
1  can corn
1  can kidney beans
2  c. chopped cabbage
1  pkg. frozen vegetables or fresh vegetables, chopped
1/2  tsp. salt and pepper
1  Tbsp. chili powder

      Saut√© onion, garlic and celery in pan; spray with nonstick cooking spray. Add hamburger meat. Cook until meat is browned. Add remaining ingredients. Bring to a boil, then simmer until vegetables are tender. This makes a large amount.
