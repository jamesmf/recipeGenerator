CHICKEN CASSEROLE


2  c. shredded, cooked chicken
2  c. chicken broth
1 (7 oz.)  pkg. Creamettes (uncooked)
1/2  lb. Velveeta cheese, cubed
1  small onion, minced
2  cans cream of mushroom soup

      Mix ingredients in a large bowl and pour in 2 1/2 to 3-quart casserole dish. Refrigerate overnight. Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 1/2 to 2 hours.
