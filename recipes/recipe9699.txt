MARINATED PORK TENDERLOIN


1.5  lb. pork tenderloin roast
1  Tbsp. sherry
2  Tbsp. lite sodium soy sauce
2  Tbsp. brown sugar

      Combine sherry, soy sauce and brown sugar. Rub over meat and marinate overnight in refrigerator. Roast at 300 degrees; until tender (approximately 90 minutes). Makes 6 servings.
      Cal-181, CHO-3, Pro-23g, Fat-7g.
