GARLIC CHICKEN PIZZA


1  large Boboli bread pizza crust (thin or pan style)
  olive oil
2  or 3 chicken breasts
  fresh mushrooms
1  bag Sicilian shredded cheese
  fresh garlic or Polaner real chopped garlic
  onions
  garlic salt
  oregano

      Generously spread olive oil over top of Boboli bread and bake at 350 degrees; for 10 minutes. Spread fresh garlic from garlic press or Polaner chopped garlic on olive oil. Precook chicken breasts and cut into bite-sized pieces. Spread over crust. Top with mushrooms, onions, cheese, garlic salt and oregano. Bake at 350 degrees; for about 1/2 hour.
       Can substitute Louis Rich Carving Board chicken strips for the chicken breast.
