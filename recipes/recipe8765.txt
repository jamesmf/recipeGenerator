EASY  OVERNIGHT BREAKFAST


6  slices bread, cubed with crust
8  eggs, beaten
1  lb. sausage, cooked & crumbled
2  c. milk
1  c. cheddar cheese grated
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. dry mustard

      Grease 9x13 dish.  Layer bread and other ingredients.  Pour egg and milk mixture over.  Refrigerate overnight.  Remove 30 mins. before baking.  Bake 350 degrees; for 35 mins.  Great with coffee cake and fresh fruit.
