CHEESE WAFERS


2  c. (1/2 lb.) grated sharp Cheddar Cheese
2  sticks melted Butter
2  c. plain Flour
1/2 to 3/4  tsp. Red Pepper
1  tsp. Salt
2  c. Rice Krispies

      Mix all ingredients together and shape into small balls. Place on ungreased cookie sheet. Flatten ball with fork.  Bake at 350 degrees for approximately 20 minutes or until lightly browned.
