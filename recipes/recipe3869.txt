HAM 'N' CHEESE ROLLS


3  pkg. 20 rolls
1/2  lb. butter
3  Tbsp. mustard
3  Tbsp. poppy seed
1  medium onion, grated
2  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
  thin sliced ham, shredded

      Cream together butter, mustard, poppy seed, onion and Worcestershire sauce. Spread on cut open rolls. Top with ham and grated cheese. Place tops back on rolls. Bake at 400 degrees; for 15 minutes. If frozen, 30 minutes.
