MEAT LOAF ROLL


2  lb. hamburger
  salt and pepper
1  egg, beaten
1  can mushrooms
4  slices toasted bread
1 3/4  c. grated cheese
1/3  c. finely chopped onion

      Combine meat, salt, pepper and egg.  Mix well.  Pat out to  10 x  12-inch  on wax paper.  Cube  bread.  Combine  with cheese, mushrooms and onion.  Spread mixture on meat to within 1-inch of edge.  Roll meat around  stuffing.  Press  to  seal. Place seam side down and bake at 300 degrees; for 1 hour.
