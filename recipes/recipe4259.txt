AMBROSIA


1 (16 oz.)  can chunky pineapple
1 (8 oz.)  can crushed pineapple
1 small  pkg. instant pistachio pudding mix
1  c. chopped walnuts or pecans
1  c. shredded coconut
12 to 16  oz. nondairy whipped topping
1  c. miniature marshmallows

      Mix the chunky and crushed pineapple with all the liquids together and sprinkle the powdered pistachio pudding mix on top. Let this stand for 3 to 5 minutes.  Mix the nuts and coconut together in a separate bowl, then add to the pineapple/pudding mixture.  Blend in the whipped topping (thawed if you are using frozen) and marshmallows.  Refrigerate for at least 2 hours. Serve well chilled and enjoy.
