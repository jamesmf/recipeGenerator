SPINACH AND STRAWBERRY SALAD


1 1/2  c. sliced strawberries
1  small red onion, thinly sliced
1/2  c. enoki mushrooms (or whatever is available)
1  Tbsp. seasoned white balsamic vinegar
2  Tbsp. honey
2  tsp. Dijon mustard
6  c. fresh spinach, washed and dried
1  Tbsp. sunflower seeds
  salt and pepper to taste

      Place strawberries, onions, and mushrooms in bottom of dish. Make a dressing of vinegar, honey, and mustard; pour over strawberries, onion, and mushrooms. Place chopped spinach and nuts on top. Let set in refrigerator overnight; toss the next day and serve.
