BLACK BEAN AND VEGETABLE SOUP


1  large onion, coarsely chopped
1  Tbsp. Vegetable oil
1 (28 oz.)  can whole tomatoes (undrained), coarsely chopped   2/3 c. picante sauce
1 1/2  tsp. Ground cumin
1  tsp. Salt
1/2  tsp. Crushed basil leaves
1 (16 oz.)  can black beans, rinsed and drained
1  chopped green pepper
1  lg. Yellow squash or zucchini (or half & half) cut into 1/2-inch chunks (2 c.)

      In large saucepan or Dutch oven, cook onion in oil until tender.  Add tomatoes, picante sauce and seasonings.  Stir in beans, peppers, and squash; cover and simmer until vegetables are tender, about 15 minutes.  Makes about 8 (1 cup) servings.
      Suggested toppings: Steamed rice, sour cream, chopped cilantro or parsley, shredded cheese.
