MONTEREY JACK CHEESE SOUP


1/2  c. finely chopped onion
1  large tomato, chopped, peeled and diced
2  large green chilies, seeded, veins removed and chopped
1/2  garlic clove, minced
1  c. chicken stock
1 1/2  c. medium white sauce
1 1/2  c. milk
1/4  tsp. salt
  dash of pepper
1 1/2  c. grated cheese

      Put onion, tomato, chilies and garlic in pot with chicken stock; cook until tender. Remove from heat; slowly stir in white sauce, then milk, stirring constantly. Add salt, pepper and cheese. Simmer slowly over low heat until cheese melts. Serve immediately. Makes 6 servings.
