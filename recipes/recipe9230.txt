COCA-COLA PORK CHOPS


8  pork chops
  salt and pepper to taste
1  c. ketchup
1  c. Coca-Cola
  brown sugar

      Place the pork chops in a baking pan; season with salt and pepper.  Mix the ketchup and Coca-Cola; pour over the pork chops. Sprinkle with brown sugar.  Bake uncovered in a 350 degrees; oven for 1 hour or until pork chops are tender.  Serves 8.
      Note: Makes great chicken, also!
