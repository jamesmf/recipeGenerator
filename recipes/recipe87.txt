CHOCOLATE EGGNOG FROSTING


3  sq. unsweetened chocolate
1 1/2  c. 4x sugar
2  Tbsp. hot water
1  egg
4  Tbsp. softened butter
  rum or vanilla

      Melt the chocolate in a double boiler.  Remove from water; add the sugar and hot water.  Blend.  Add the egg and butter, one tablespoon at a time, beating thoroughly after each tablespoon. Add a little rum or vanilla.
