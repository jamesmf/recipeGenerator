ORANGE/PINEAPPLE CONGEALED SALAD


1 (6 oz.)  pkg. orange/pineapple jello
1  c. boiling water
2  c. miniature marshmallows
1  (8 oz.)  Cool Whip
3  oz. cream cheese, softened
1 small  can crushed pineapple
1  can mandarin oranges
1  c. nuts (optional)

      Add jello and marshmallows to boiling water.  Stir until jello is dissolved.  Drain juice from oranges and pineapple; add water to make 1 cup.  Mix cream cheese, fruit juice, fruit and nuts together.  Add to jello mixture.  Let chill until thick, then fold in Cool Whip.  (If this is to be molded, 1 envelope plain gelatin may be added with dry jello.)
