PICKLED BEETS


4  qt. cooked beets
2  c. sugar
1  tsp. salt
1 long  stick cinnamon (2 tsp.), ground
1  tsp. allspice or 1/2 tsp. cloves
3 1/2  c. vinegar
1 1/2  c. water

      Tie spices  in a bag and add to vinegar and sugar, etc. Simmer 15  minutes  before  adding beets.  Then heat through, about 5 minutes.
