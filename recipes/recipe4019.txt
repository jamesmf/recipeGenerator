MUSTARD PICKLES


1  qt. water
1  qt. white vinegar
2  c. sugar
4  Tbsp. table mustard
2  Tbsp. salt
2  Tbsp. salt
2  tsp. sugar
1/2  tsp. black pepper
3/4  c. vinegar
3/4  c. water

      Put all dry ingredients in jars where cucumbers have been packed.  Mix vinegar and water; bring to a boil.  Pour over cucumbers.  Place quart jars in a hot water bath canner; bring to a rolling boil; turn off immediately and let set in canner for a few minutes.  Remove jars from canner.  Be sure the jars seal.
