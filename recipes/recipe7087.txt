15 MINUTE CHICKEN AND RICE


4  skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1  can cream of chicken soup
1 1/2  c. water
1/4  tsp. paprika
1/4  tsp. pepper
1 1/2  c. Minute rice
2  c. fresh or thawed broccoli florets
1  Tbsp. oil

      In skillet over medium-high heat, cook chicken in hot oil covered for  8 minutes or until browned.  Set aside and pour off fat.  Add soup, water, paprika, and pepper.  Heat to a boil.  Stir in rice and broccoli.  Place chicken on mixture.  Cover and cook on low heat 5 minutes or until chicken is done.  Stir.  Serves 4. For creamier rice, increase water to 1 2/3 cups.
