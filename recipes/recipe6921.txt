EASY HOT DISH


1  lb. ground beef
2  medium onions, diced
1  c. diced celery
1/2  c. rice (uncooked)
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  can cream of mushroom soup
3  Tbsp. soy sauce
2  c. water

      Brown ground beef and onions in frypan. Add diced celery; mix with rest of ingredients. Pour in casserole. Bake for 1 hour and 30 minutes at 350 degrees;. Yield: 6 servings.
