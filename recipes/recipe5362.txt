TAFFY APPLE SALAD


1 (16 oz.)  can pineapple chunks (set juice aside)
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. Cool Whip
1 1/2  c. Spanish peanuts, chopped
2 to 3  c. diced apples (unpeeled)
4  c. mini marshmallows
1  egg, beaten well
1 1/2  Tbsp. white vinegar
1/2  c. sugar
1  Tbsp. flour

      Mix drained pineapple with marshmallows; set aside. Combine egg, pineapple juice, sugar, flour and vinegar in a saucepan; cook and stir on medium heat until slightly thick. Let cool. When cool, combine with Cool Whip, then fold in marshmallow mixture. Add chopped apples and 1 cup peanuts. Mix carefully. Sprinkle top with remaining peanuts.
