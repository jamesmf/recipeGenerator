SILKY FUDGE SAUCE


1/2  c. butter (no substitutes)
1  (4-oz.) sweet chocolate bar, chopped (Baker's German Sweet)
1  c. sugar
1  can (5-oz.) evaporated milk
1  Tbsp. light corn syrup
1/8  tsp. vanilla extract

      Melt butter and chocolate in a heavy saucepan over medium-low heat.  Stir in sugar, evaporated milk, corn syrup, and salt; cook over medium heat, stirring gently, 5 minutes or just until sugar dissolves and sauce is smooth.  Remove from heat; stir in vanilla. Serve warm.
