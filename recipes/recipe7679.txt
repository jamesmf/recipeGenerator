PIZZA CASSEROLE


2  c. cooked macaroni
1 to 1 1/2  lb. browned hamburger
4  oz. mushrooms, drained
2  c. pizza sauce
8  oz. tomato sauce
1  c. Parmesan cheese
1/2  pkg. pepperoni, sliced
1/2  c. onions
  salt and pepper
  oregano

      Mix all ingredients together and put  in 2 quart casserole dish.   Bake  at 350 degrees; for 30  minutes.  Sprinkle  1  cup Mozzarella cheese and heat until cheese melts.
