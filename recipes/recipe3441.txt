CUCUMBER CHEESE SPREAD


2  (8 oz.) cream cheese, softened
2  tsp. lemon juice
1/4  tsp. horseradish
1/8  tsp. hot sauce
2  tsp. minced onions
1/2  medium cucumber, finely chopped

      Combine cream cheese, lemon juice, horseradish, hot sauce, minced onions and cucumber; mix well. Makes 2 cups.
