CHICKEN WITH CURRY DILL SAUCE


2  Tbsp. butter or margarine
2  Tbsp. all-purpose flour
1/8  tsp. salt
  dash of pepper
1  c. milk
1/4  c. mayonnaise
1/2  tsp. dill weed
1/4  tsp. curry powder
6  bone-in chicken breast halves
1  Tbsp. vegetable oil

      In a saucepan over medium heat, melt butter. Add the flour, salt and pepper; stir until smooth. Gradually add milk and bring to a boil. Boil and stir for 2 minutes. Remove from the heat. Add the mayonnaise, dill and curry; stir until smooth. Set aside.
      In a skillet over medium heat, brown chicken in oil. Place in a greased, shallow, 3-quart baking dish. Pour sauce over chicken. Bake uncovered at 350 degrees; for 50 to 60 minutes or until meat juices run clear. Yield: 6 servings.
