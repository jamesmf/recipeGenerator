POTATO SALAD DRESSING


1/2  c. sugar
1/2  c. vinegar
2  whole eggs, well beaten
1  tsp. salt
  small amount black pepper

      Mix all ingredients together.  Cook in double boiler until thick, stirring constantly.  Remove from pan.  Add 2 teaspoons of prepared mustard.  Pour over potato salad while both potatoes and dressing are hot.  Mix well.  If more dressing is needed, add more Miracle Whip.
