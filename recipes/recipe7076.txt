SIX LAYER DINNER


1  lb. ground beef
1  c. onion, sliced
1  c. celery, sliced
2  c. carrots, sliced
2  c. potatoes, sliced
1  can cream of chicken soup or cream of celery soup
1/2  c. water

      Place meat in bottom of casserole. Add remaining ingredients. Top with can of soup and water. Cover and bake at 350 degrees; for 1 1/2 hours.
