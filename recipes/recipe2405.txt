PIG-LICKEN CAKE


1  box Duncan Hines yellow butter cake mix
1/4  c. Crisco oil
1  stick margarine
1  can mandarin oranges
2  eggs

      Beat cake mix, oil, margarine and eggs for 4 minutes; add juice from oranges.  Mix well.  Cut oranges into small pieces; fold into batter.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 35 minutes.  Use a 9 x 13-inch pan, well-greased and floured.
Topping:
1  large instant vanilla pudding
1 (16 oz.)  can crushed pineapple
1 (8 oz.)  carton Cool Whip

      Mix pineapple and pudding together until well blended.  Add Cool Whip and fold into the mix.  Ice cake when cool.  Refrigerate until ready to serve.
