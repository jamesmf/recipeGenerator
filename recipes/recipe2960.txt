PICKLE ROLL-UPS


  dill pickle spears
1  pkg. cream cheese
  garlic salt (for taste)
  deli style lunch ham

      Warm cream cheese in the microwave.  Add garlic salt to taste. Spread on medium to thin sliced deli ham.  Roll dill pickle up in it.  Cool in fridge for 10 minutes.  Slice in 1/2-inch sections.
