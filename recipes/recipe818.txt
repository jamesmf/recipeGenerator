DUCK WITH CHINESE FIVE SPICES


4  lb. duck
  green onion
  Chinese parsley

 
Sauce:
2 1/4  tsp. salt
1 1/2  Tbsp. sugar
2 1/4  tsp. Chinese 5 spices
4 1/2  Tbsp. shoyu
1  clove garlic
1/4  tsp. ajinomoto
3  tsp. oyster sauce

      Prepare fowl.  Combine ingredients to make sauce.  Rub fowl with sauce.  Pour remaining sauce into cavity of bird together with onion and Chinese parsley.  Roast in 300 degrees; oven 20 to 30 minutes per pound.
