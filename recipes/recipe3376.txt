CRAB MEAT APPETIZER


12  oz. cream cheese, softened
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
2  Tbsp. mayonnaise
2  tsp. lemon juice
1  small onion, grated
  dash of garlic salt
1  bottle Heinz chili sauce
1  can (6 1/2-oz.) crab meat
  fresh parsley

      Blend cream cheese, Worcestershire sauce, mayonnaise, lemon juice, onion and garlic salt by hand and spread on a shallow plate.  Pour a bottle of chili sauce on the spread.  Layer the crab meat on top of the sauce.  Sprinkle the top with fresh parsley.  Chill and serve with crackers.
