ORIENTAL SALAD


6  c. shredded green cabbage
2  c. shredded red cabbage
4  green onions
1  pkg. Ramen noodles
1/2  c. sliced almonds
2  tsp. sesame seed
8  oz. skinless chicken breasts

 
Dressing:
3  Tbsp. vinegar
1  dash of salt
1/2  tsp. pepper
2  Tbsp. soy sauce
1  Tbsp. fresh grated ginger
1  flavor packet from Ramen noodles
1  Tbsp. oil
2  Tbsp. sugar

 
Chicken Sauce:
2  Tbsp. soy sauce
2  Tbsp. molasses
1  clove crushed garlic
1  Tbsp. fresh grated ginger
1  Tbsp. rice wine

      Cut chicken into bite sized chunks and stir-fry in Chicken Sauce until done (3 to 5 minutes).
       Stir together dressing ingredients. Make salad in layers: green and red cabbage, green onions, Ramen noodles, chicken, almonds and sesame seeds. Drench dressing over all.
