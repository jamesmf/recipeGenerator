STRAWBERRY SHAKE


1  c. skim milk
3/4  c. strawberries
1/2  c. vanilla ice milk

      Put milk and berries in blender for 8 to 10 seconds.  Add ice milk and blend until smooth.  Serve immediately.
      Makes 3/4 cup per serving; 1/2 milk exchange, 1/2 fruit exchange and 1/2 fat exchange.  Contains 81 calories.
