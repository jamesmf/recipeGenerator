COZY VEGETABLE SOUP


2  tsp. olive oil
2  c. chopped green cabbage
1  c. chopped onion
1  c. sliced carrot
1  c. sliced celery
1  c. diced red potato
1  tsp. caraway seeds
1  c. water
2 (14 1/2 oz.)  cans vegetable broth
1  c. drained canned white beans (cannellini)
2  Tbsp. fresh minced parsley
2  tsp. chopped fresh or 1/2 tsp. dried dill
1/2  tsp. pepper

      Heat olive oil in a Dutch oven over medium-high heat. Add cabbage and next 5 ingredients; saut√© 2 minutes. Add water and vegetable broth; bring to a boil. Cover, reduce heat and simmer 30 minutes. Add remaining ingredients and cook until thoroughly heated. Makes 4 servings.
