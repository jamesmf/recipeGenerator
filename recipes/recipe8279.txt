VENISON MEAT LOAF


2  eggs
1  (8 oz.) tomato sauce
1  medium onion, finely chopped
1  c. dry bread crumbs
1 1/2  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper
2  lb. ground venison
2  Tbsp. brown sugar
2  Tbsp. spicy brown mustard
2  Tbsp. cider vinegar

	In a large bowl, lightly beat eggs. Add tomato sauce, onions, bread crumbs, salt and pepper. Add venison and mix well. Press into an ungreased 9 x 5 x 3-inch loaf pan. Combine brown sugar, mustard and vinegar. Pour over meat loaf. Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees; for 70 minutes.
	To make sandwiches, put chilled slices of meat loaf on Italian bread. Top with a slice of Mozzarella cheese and broil until cheese is melted.
