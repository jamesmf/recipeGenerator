CHEESEBURGER FRIED PIE


1 1/2  lb. ground meat
1  can Campbell's golden mushroom soup
1  can Campbell's fiesta nacho cheese soup
1  bag seasoned French fries
  Tony's to taste
  hot sauce (optional)

	Brown ground meat until no longer pink. Drain ground meat and return meat to skillet. Add both cans of soup and seasoning and mix well. Pour meat mixture into a greased 9 x 13-inch pan. Place frozen fries in a single layer on top of meat mixture. Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 to 35 minutes or until cheese bubbles over the top.
