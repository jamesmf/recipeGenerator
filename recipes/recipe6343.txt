TURNIP GREEN SOUP


1/2  lb. smoked pork sausage (chopped or sliced)
2  cans Great Northern Beans
2  cans Rotel tomatoes
1  pkg. Knorr dried vegetable flakes
1 16  oz. turnip greens with roots
1  pint tomatoes
1  onion, chopped
1 1/2  qt. water
  2-3 tsp. Tabasco

      Mix all ingredients and cook 2 1/2 to 3 hours. Good served with fried corn bread patties.
