PEPPER SPREAD


3  lb. cream cheese (12 oz.)
14 1/4  oz. red, roasted, peeled peppers
3  oz. crushed basil
1/3  c. spice red crushed wet pepper (1 Tbsp., 2 3/4 tsp.)
1/4  c. garlic powder (1 Tbsp.)

      Combine all ingredients in bowl, mixing thoroughly.  Mix until smooth.  Put in container.  Refrigerate until ready to use. Spread on crackers.
