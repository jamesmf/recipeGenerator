CRAB CASSEROLE


1/2  c. (1 stick) butter or margarine
1  Tbsp. flour
1  c. milk
1  lb. crabmeat (or imitation)
  salt (to taste)
  pepper (to taste)
1  c. breadcrumbs
1  c. sharp cheese, shredded

      Melt butter.  Add flour to butter.  Gradually add milk until smooth.  Add crabmeat, salt and pepper.  Pour into a 1 1/2-quart casserole dish.  Top with breadcrumbs and cheese.
      Bake at 350 degrees; for 20 minutes, until cheese melts and bread crumbs are brown.
      Serving suggestions:  Serve with green salad, baked potato and roll.
      Makes 4 large servings.
