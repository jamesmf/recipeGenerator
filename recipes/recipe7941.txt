CHICKEN AND DRESSING


1  large onion
1  cup chopped celery
1  cup margarine
8  cups cubed day-old bread
1  (10 inch) round pan baked corn bread, crumbled
2  tsp. sage
2  tsp. poultry seasoning
1 1/2  tsp. salt
2  cup chicken broth
1  cup milk

      In skillet sauté onions and celery in the margarine until tender (about 5 minutes). In large bowl combine bread cubes, corn bread, sage, poultry seasoning, salt, pepper, and sautéed vegetables.  Mix well.  Add eggs, broth and milk.  Toss to mix. Spoon into 9 x 13 inch baking dish.  Bake at 350 degrees for 30-40 minutes.
