CREAMY CHICKEN BAKE


4  to 6 skinless, boneless chicken breasts
8  slices Swiss cheese
1/4  c. dry white wine
1  can cream of chicken soup
1/4  c. butter, melted
1  c. stuffing mix

      Arrange chicken in a greased 9 x 13 baking dish. Top with Swiss cheese. Combine soup and wine. Spoon over chicken. Sprinkle stuffing over mix. Drizzle butter over stuffing. Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour.
