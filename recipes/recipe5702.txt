KITCHEN SINK SALAD DRESSING


1  c. vegetable oil
1 (10.5 oz.)  can tomato soup
1/2  c. brown sugar, packed
1/3  c. wine or white vinegar
1  tsp. dry mustard
1  tsp. garlic salt
1/2  tsp. onion salt
1/2  tsp. celery salt
1/4  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/4  tsp. paprika
  dash of Tabasco sauce
1  c. mayonnaise or salad dressing

	Combine all ingredients in a blender. Cover and blend at low speed then increase speed and blend until thoroughly mixed. Makes about 1 quart. Refrigerate covered.
