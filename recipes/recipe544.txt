ICEE


1/2  c. water
3/4  c. sugar
1  pkg. unsweetened Kool Aid (any flavor)
1  c. ice cubes

      Put water, sugar, Kool Aid and ice cubes in electric blender on high speed.  Gradually add more ice cubes and a little water until the amount reaches the top of the blender to make 5 cups. Alternate adding ice and water, pushing cubes to bottom to ensure they are getting crushed.
