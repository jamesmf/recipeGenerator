RUSSIAN SALAD

Top:
1 (3.2 oz.)  pkg. raspberry Jell-O
1 (15 oz.)  can blueberries (including juice)
1  c. boiling water

	Dissolve Jell-O in water. Add blueberries with juice. Let set until it starts to jell.
Bottom:
1  c. coffee cream
1  c. sugar
1  envelope Knox gelatine
1/4  c. cold water
2  c. sour cream
1  tsp. vanilla

	Bring cream and sugar to boiling point, but don't let it boil. Dissolve Knox gelatine in the cold water. To the gelatine, add sour cream and vanilla. Mix into the hot mixture. Pour into a 9 x 12-inch pan and let set until firm. Spoon blueberry topping onto bottom mixture.
