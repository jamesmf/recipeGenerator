STONE SOUP


1  qt. water
2  c. potatoes, cubed
1  c. onion, chopped
1/2  c. celery, chopped
4  bouillon cubes (chicken)
2  cans celery soup
1  can Veg-All, drained
1  lb. Velveeta cheese

      Cook the first 5 ingredients until tender. Then add celery soup, Veg-All and cheese. Simmer until cheese melts.
