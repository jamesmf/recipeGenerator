FESTIVE FRUIT SALAD


1/2  c. plain yogurt
1/2  c. orange sherbet
1/4  c. Kretschmer Honey Crunch wheat germ
2  medium bananas, sliced
2  c. green or red, seedless grapes
2  c. cubed apples (about 2 medium)
1 (8 oz.)  can pineapple chunks, drained

      Combine yogurt, sherbet and wheat germ; mix well. Add fruit; mix lightly. Chill 1 hour before serving. Makes 8 servings.
