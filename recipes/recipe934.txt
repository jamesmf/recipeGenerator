CAJUN QUICHE


  10-inch deep dish pie shell
1  c. Swiss cheese, grated
1/2  lb. cooked, peeled shrimp or crawfish, slightly chopped
1 small  can crabmeat, drained
2  green onions, finely chopped
4  eggs, beaten
1  c. whipping cream
1/2  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. cayenne pepper

      Sprinkle cheese evenly over unbaked pie shell.  Add chopped shrimp and crabmeat.  Then add green onions.  Mix eggs, whipping cream, salt and pepper.  Pour over shrimp.  Preheat oven to 400 degrees;. Place pie on cookie sheet and bake 40 minutes.  Let stand a few minutes if not completely "set" in center.
