MEAT BALLS FOR SPAGHETTI


2  lb. ground beef
3  eggs
1  Tbsp. parsley flakes
1/4  tsp. garlic
1/4  tsp. black pepper
1 1/4  tsp. salt
1  c. bread crumbs
3  Tbsp. Romano cheese, grated
1/4  c. milk

	Mix well with hands. Form into balls. In a frying pan with small amount of oil, brown on all sides. Drop in boiling spaghetti sauce.
