MACARONI AND CHEESE CASSEROLE


8  oz. (1 pkg.) macaroni, elbow
2  oz. (1 small jar) pimento, chopped
1/4  c. onion, chopped
10  oz. (1 can) soup, mushroom
1  c. mayonnaise
1/4  c. bell pepper, chopped
4  oz. (1 can) mushrooms
1 1/2  c. cheese, shredded
  cracker crumbs
  butter

      Cook macaroni according to package directions, drain and set aside.
      In a bowl, combine together:  pimento, onion, soup, mayonnaise, bell pepper, mushrooms and set aside.
      In a buttered 9x13-inch baking dish, place a layer of macaroni, a layer of the combined ingredients then sprinkle with cheese.  Repeat for a second layer.  Top with cracker crumbs and butter.
      Bake at 325 degrees; for 25 minutes.  Sprinkle with remaining cheese just before removing from oven.
