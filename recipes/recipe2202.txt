GOULASH


1  lb. ground chuck
1  small onion
1 (8 oz.)  box elbow macaroni
4 (15 oz.)  cans tomatoes
1 small  can tomato paste
1 small (1 lb.)  box Velveeta
1  tsp. Italian seasoning
  salt and pepper

      Cook macaroni.  Drain.  While macaroni is cooking, brown ground meat and onion with salt to taste.  Add all ingredients in large pot or Dutch oven.  Cook on low until cheese is melted and all ingredients are blended, stirring often to prevent sticking. Serves 8.
