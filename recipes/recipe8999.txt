EASY CHILI


1  lb. ground beef
1/3  c. chopped green bell pepper
1  onion, chopped
2  cans tomato sauce
1  can kidney beans (undrained)
1  c. water
1  envelope chili seasoning mix

      Brown beef in a skillet over medium heat. Drain. Add bell pepper and onion and cook until vegetables are crisp-tender. Stir in tomato sauce and remaining ingredients. Reduce heat. Cover and simmer 45 minutes.
