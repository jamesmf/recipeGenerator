RAISIN BROCCOLI TOSS


4  c. broccoli florets
3/4  c. chopped walnuts
1/2  c. raisins
1/2  c. golden raisins
1  c. salad dressing (mayo)
1/4  c. sugar
2  Tbsp. lemon juice
2  Tbsp. half and half cream
1  Tbsp. vinegar

      Toss first 4 ingredients together.  Combine rest of ingredients.  Pour over the first ingredients.  Toss to coat thoroughly.  Cover and refrigerate for 1 hour before serving.
