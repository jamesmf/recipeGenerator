HAM AND CHEESE POTATO BAKE


1  pkg. (2 lb.) O'Brien frozen hash browns
2  c. fully cooked ham
3/4  c. shredded Cheddar cheese, divided
1  small onion, chopped
2  c. sour cream
1  can (10 3/4 oz.) condensed cheese soup, undiluted
1  can (10 3/4 oz.) potato soup, undiluted
1/4  Tbsp. pepper

      Mix hash browns, ham, 1/2 cup cheese and onion.  In another bowl, combine sour cream, soups and pepper.  Add to potato mix. Mix well.  Put in a 3 quart greased baking dish.  Sprinkle with remaining cheese.  Bake at 350 degrees; for approximately an hour or until potatoes are tender.
