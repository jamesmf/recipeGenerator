DILL BREAD


1  pkg. yeast
1/4  c. warm water
1  c. creamed cottage cheese, heated to lukewarm
2  Tbsp. sugar
1  Tbsp. instant minced onion
1  Tbsp. butter
4  tsp. dill seed
1  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. soda
1  unbeaten egg
2 1/4  c. all-purpose flour

      Soften yeast in water.  Combine in bowl cottage cheese, sugar, onion, butter, dill seed, salt, soda, egg and yeast.  Add flour in 2 additions, beating well.  Let rise until light and doubled (50 to 60 minutes).  Stir down dough.  Turn into well-greased 8-inch round casserole.  Let rise until light (30 to 40 minutes).  Bake at 350 degrees; for 40 to 50 minutes.  Brush with butter and sprinkle with salt.
