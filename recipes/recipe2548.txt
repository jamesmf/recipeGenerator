ORANGE JELLO DELIGHT


2 small  pkg. orange Jell-O
3  c. boiling water
1 (6 oz.)  can frozen orange juice
20  oz. crushed pineapple, drained
2  cans mandarin oranges, drained
1 small  pkg. Jell-O instant lemon pudding and pie filling
1 small  carton Cool Whip
  grated Cheddar cheese

      Add 3 cups boiling water to Jell-O; mix well.  Add orange juice concentrate.  Add drained pineapple and oranges; mix.  Put in refrigerator and let set up.  Mix pudding (dry) with Cool Whip. Spread on top of set up Jell-O.  Sprinkle with Cheddar cheese.
