ORANGE CHEESE SPREAD


2  pkg. Jello orange flavored gelatin, 4 serving size
1 1/2  c. boiling water
8  oz. finely grated sharp cheddar cheese
1  pkg. cream cheese, softened
1  c. sour cream
1/2  c. chopped scallions
1/4  c. chopped parsley
3  Tbsp. horseradish
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce

      Dissolve gelatin in boiling water.  Combine remaining ingredients in a bowl with electric mixer until well blended. Gradually blend into gelatin.  Pour gelatin mixture into mold. Chill until firm, about 3 hours.  Unmold.  Serve as an appetizer with crackers.  Garnish with parsley and carrot curls, if desired. Makes 5 1/2 cups.
