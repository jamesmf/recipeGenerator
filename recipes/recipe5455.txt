PARISIAN SOUP


1  stick butter
1/2  c. chopped onion
1/2  c. chopped celery
4  chicken bouillon cubes
1/4  c. flour
1  c. diced ham
1/2  c. Velveeta
1  (15 to 17 oz.) frozen broccoli, cauliflower and carrots

      Cook frozen veggies in 3 cup of water until tender.  Keep all liquid.  Saut onions and celery in butter.  Add bouillon cubes; add flour to thicken.  Combine all ingredients and simmer until ready to eat.  Good next day also.
