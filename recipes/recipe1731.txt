CARROT CAKE


2  c. flour
2  tsp. baking powder
1 1/2  tsp. soda
2  tsp. cinnamon
1  tsp. salt
2  c. sugar
4  eggs
1 1/2  c. salad oil
2  c. carrots, grated
1  c. crushed pineapple
3/4  c. raisins
3/4  c. walnuts

 
Icing:
1  box 10x sugar
1/2  c. melted oleo
8  oz. cream cheese
1  tsp. vanilla

      Sift dry ingredients together, except sugar.  Beat eggs, oil and sugar.  Add dry ingredients.  Stir in carrots, crushed pineapple (not drained), raisins and walnuts.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 35 to 40 minutes.
