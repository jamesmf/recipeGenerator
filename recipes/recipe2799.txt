STUFFING


1 (15 oz.)  pkg. cornbread stuffing
1  lb. sausage, pan-fried and drained
1  c. margarine
2  c. celery, chopped
2  c. onion, chopped
2  Tbsp. parsley
2  eggs, slightly beaten
2  c. turkey stock or chicken bouillon
1  Tbsp. lemon juice

      Saut√© celery, onion, parsley and 3/4 cup margarine.  Add sausage.  Mix stuffing, stock and eggs; toss lightly until completely moistened.  Spoon into turkey cavity.  Rub turkey with lemon juice and 1/4 cup margarine.  Roast according to directions.
