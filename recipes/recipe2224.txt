PISTACHIO SALAD


1 (3 oz.)  pkg. instant pistachio pudding
1 (20 oz.)  can crushed pineapple (undrained)
1/2  c. miniature marshmallows
1/2  c. nuts, chopped
1  (4 oz.)  Cool Whip

      Pour dry pudding over undrained pineapple, then mix.  Add nuts and marshmallows.  Fold in Cool Whip.  Refrigerate 3 to 4 hours.
