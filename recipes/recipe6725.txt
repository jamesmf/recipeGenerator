CHICKEN AND RICE CASSEROLE


1  c. or more long grain rice
2  c. water for c. of rice
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  can mushroom soup
1  chicken, cut up or 6 chicken breasts
2  garlic, chopped

      Skin chicken.  Remove fat.  Season with small amount of salt, pepper and paprika.  Saut√© minced garlic 1 minute in small amount of oil.  Rapidly brown chicken.  Place rice in greased casserole, water and chicken.  Pour soups over all.  Cook, covered, at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes.  Uncover and cook 15 to 20 minutes longer.
      Use large casserole or two medium ones.
