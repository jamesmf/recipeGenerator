JEAN'S POTATO SOUP


8  - 10 Potatoes, peeled and chopped
1  can Cheddar Cheese Soup
1  can Cream of Celery Soup
1  Onion, chopped
2  tbsp. Cornstarch

      Cook potatoes and onion until tender.  Add soups and cornstarch to thicken.
      Note:  Mix cornstarch and 1/2 c. water; mix with  hot soup before adding to soup.
