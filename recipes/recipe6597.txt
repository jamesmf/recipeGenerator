BAKED PORK CHOPS AND RICE


4  center cut pork chops
1  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper
1  can cream of mushroom soup
3/4  c. water
1/2  c. uncooked white rice (not Minute rice)

      Place pork chops in a lightly greased baking dish. Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Combine remaining ingredients. Pour over pork chops. Cover and bake at 350 degrees; for 1 to 1 1/2 hours.
