SLOW COOKER CHILI


2  lbs. Ground Beef
4 - 16  oz. Kidney Beans, rinsed and drained
1  can (28 oz.) stewed Tomatoes, undrained
1  tsp. Dried Basil
1/2  tsp. Salt
1- 15  oz. can Pizza Sauce
1- 4  oz. can chopped green chilies
1/4  c. chopped Onion
4 - 5  tsp. chili powder
2  garlic cloves, minced
1/8  tsp. Pepper

      In a large skillet, cook beef over medium heat until brown. Drain.  Transfer to a 5 quart slow cooker. Stir in the remaining ingredients.  Cover and cook on low for 6 hours.
      Yield:  12 servings.
