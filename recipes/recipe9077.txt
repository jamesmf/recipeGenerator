CHICKEN AND DUMPLINGS


1  hen, washed and cut into pieces

      Place chicken in pot and cook until done.
Dumplings:
2  c. plain flour
  enough water (warm) to mix to rolling consistency

      Roll out thin on waxed paper. Let set at least 1 hour to toughen. Cut dumplings and add to chicken. Can boil 3 or 4 eggs and cut up in chicken. Simmer until dumplings are tender. Add 1 tablespoon butter and salt and pepper to taste.
