COLESLAW


1  head cabbage, slivered
2  carrots, peeled and grated
1  scallion, minced
1  red or green pepper, diced

 
Dressing:
1  c. rice vinegar
3/4  c. vegetable oil
1/2  c. sugar
2  tsp. dry  mustard
2  tsp. celery seed

      Dressing:  Mix ingredients in a saucepan and heat to simmer for 1 minute.
      Pour dressing over cabbage and toss.  Season with salt and pepper.  Cover and refrigerate.
