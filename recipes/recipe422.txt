TRASH


5  c. Kix or similar cereal
1  c. salted Spanish peanuts
1  c. raisins
1  c. banana chips
1  pkg. regular vanilla pudding mix
1/2  c. honey
1/2  c. peanut butter

      Lightly butter a jelly roll pan, 15 1/2 x 10 1/2 x 1-inch.  Mix cereal, peanuts, raisins and banana chips in a large bowl.  Mix pudding and honey in a small saucepan.  Heat to boiling over medium heat, stirring constantly.  Reduce heat.  Boil and stir one minute; remove from heat.  Stir in peanut butter.  Pour over cereal mixture; toss until evenly coated.  Spread in pan.  Refrigerate 30 minutes.  Break into bite size pieces.  Store in covered container.
