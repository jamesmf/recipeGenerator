FRUITY CHICKEN SALAD


1/2  (8 oz.) container soft-style cream cheese with pineapple
2  Tbsp. milk
2  tsp. lemon juice
1/2  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper
2  c. finely chopped, cooked chicken
1/2  c. chopped apple
1/2  c. chopped celery
1  thinly sliced green onion

      Stir together the cream cheese, milk, lemon juice, salt and pepper.  Stir in chicken, apple, celery and green onion. Makes about 2 1/2 cups or enough for 30 small cream puffs or about 18 heart-shaped biscuits.
