CHINESE CHICKEN SALAD


4  chicken breasts, cooked, diced and chilled
3  to 4 green onions, chopped
1  head lettuce, chopped
1  bottle sesame seeds, roasted
1  pkg. slivered almonds
1 (5 oz.)  can chow mein noodles

      Mix chicken, onions and lettuce. Add sesame seeds, almonds and noodles.
Dressing:
1/2  c. oil
6  Tbsp. red wine vinegar
2  tsp. salt
4  Tbsp. sugar
1  tsp. pepper
2  tsp. Accent

      Mix together. Pour over salad.
