PENNSYLVANIA DUTCH STYLE CHICKEN AND CORN SOUP


1  chicken (preferably 4 lb. hen)
4  qt. water
2 1/2  c. fresh or frozen corn
1  onion, chopped
1/2  c. chopped celery and leaves
  salt and pepper to taste
2  cooked eggs (optional)

      Cook chicken in water slowly until it is tender, adding salt 30 minutes before it is done.  Remove chicken and strain broth through a fine sieve.  Take meat from bones and cut in bite size pieces. Set aside.  Add corn to broth.  Add onion, chopped celery and seasoning; cook 5 minutes.  Five minutes before serving, add chicken, diced eggs and rivels.
Rivels:
1  c. flour
1  egg
1/4  c. milk

      Rub rivel mixture together with 2 forks until the size of peas and drop into boiling soup.  Cover and cook gently for 5 minutes. Delicious!!
