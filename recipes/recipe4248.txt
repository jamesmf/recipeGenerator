BREAD AND BUTTER PICKLES


2  gallons sliced cucumbers
8  onions
1/2  cup salt
4  cups vinegar
4  cups sugar
1/2  tsp. whole cloves
2  tsp mustard seed
1  Tbsp. celery seed
1  1/2 tsp turmeric

      Mix first 3 ingredients, let stand 3 hours.  Add the next 6 ingredients and bring to a boil.  Seal hot.
