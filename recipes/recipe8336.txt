FRIED CHICKEN I


2 to 3  lb. chicken
1  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper
2  c. flour
1  c. oil

	Wash chicken. Pat excess water from chicken with a paper towel. Salt and pepper chicken; roll in flour, coating both sides. Fry in hot oil in a large frying pan, cooking on medium heat, until both sides are golden brown. Remove and drain on paper towels. Continue cooking chicken until all chicken has been cooked.
