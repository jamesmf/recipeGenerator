SQUASH CASSEROLE


1  lb. squash
1  medium onion, diced
1  can mushroom soup
  salt and pepper to taste
2  Tbsp. butter
  cheese
1 1/2  c. cracker crumbs

      Stew squash and onions until tender enough to mash. Add salt and pepper. In 1 1/2-quart casserole, alternate layers of squash, cracker crumbs and soup. Bake at 400 degrees; until top browns. Sprinkle cheese on top.
