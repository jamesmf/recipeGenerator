ROTINI SALAD

Dressing:
1  c. oil
1/2  tsp. pepper
1/4  c. parsley flakes
1  + basil
1  c. vinegar
1  Tbsp. Accent
1  c. sugar
1  Tbsp. garlic powder
3+  dry mustard
1  tsp. oregano
1  Tbsp. salt

      Put in jar; let stand overnight.
Salad:
1  medium onion, diced
1  jar pimentos
1  green pepper, seeded and chopped
  mushrooms, sliced
  radishes, sliced
1  cucumber, peeled and diced
  black olives, sliced
  tomatoes, diced
2 (8 oz.)  boxes colored rotini, cooked

      Mix salad and dressing; let stand in refrigerator 24 hours.
