BAKED CORN


2 large  cans cream-style corn
1  large onion, chopped
1  medium bell pepper, chopped
1 (2 oz.)  jar pimento, chopped
1  c. grated cheese
2  Tbsp. sugar
1  c. fine crushed cracker crumbs
1  egg, well beaten
1/4  c. melted oleo or butter
  salt and pepper to tate
2/3  c. milk

      Mix all ingredients and bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour in one 9-inch casserole.
