O'MAMA'S "MEDICINAL" HOT TODDY


  juice of 1 lemon
2  Tbsp. honey
3  oz. bourbon or rye whiskey
  boiling water

      At the onset of cold or flu, put patient in bed under lots of covers.  Put lemon juice in tall mug.  Add honey and whiskey.  Fill mug with boiling water.  Stir well and have patient drink while very hot!
