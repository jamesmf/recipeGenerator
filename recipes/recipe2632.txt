COOKIE CAKE


1  can Eagle Brand milk
1  egg, slightly beaten
1  box yellow cake mix
1  c. chopped pecans
1  (12 oz.) bag chocolate chips
1/2  bag Heath Bits 'O Brickle

      Combine milk and egg.  Add cake mix and chopped pecans.  Pour into pan and top with chocolate chips and Bits 'O Brickle.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 to 35 minutes.  Tastes better if you let sit for 1 day.
