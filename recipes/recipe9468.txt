MEAT LOAF (LARGE)


3  lb. ground chuck
1  lb. sausage
  salt and pepper to taste
4  eggs
1 1/2  c. oatmeal
2  small or 1 large onion, grated
1  large carrot, grated
1  bell pepper, grated
1  sweet potato, grated
1  stalk celery, grated

	Mix all ingredients together; by hand is the best method. Press into a baking dish. Cover top with ketchup and bacon strips. Cook for 1 1/2 hours at 350 degrees;.
