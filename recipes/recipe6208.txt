CROCK-POT POTATO SOUP


6  potatoes, pared and cubed
2  onions, chopped
1  carrot, pared and sliced
4  chicken bouillon cubes
1  Tbsp. parsley
4  c. water
1 1/2  tsp. salt
  pepper
2  Tbsp. butter
1 (13 oz.)  can evaporated milk

      Put all ingredients, except evaporated milk in crock-pot. Cover and cook on low for 10 to 12 hours (high: 3 to 4 hours). Stir in milk during the last hour.  If desired, mash potatoes before serving.
      Garnish with chopped chives, bacon bits and shredded Cheddar cheese.
