BUSY DAY STEW


1 1/2 to 2  lb. stew meat
1  pkg. dry onion soup mix
1  can cream of mushroom soup (undiluted)
1  can mushroom stems and pieces (with juice)
1/2  c. diced onions or to taste

      Mix all ingredients well and place in a baking dish. Bake at 350 degrees; for 3 hours. Stir occasionally.
