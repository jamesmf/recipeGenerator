FESTIVE CRANBERRY SALAD


1 (14 oz.)  can sweetened condensed milk
1/4  c. lemon juice
1 (20 oz.)  can crushed pineapple, drained
1 (16 oz.)  can whole-berry cranberry sauce
2  c. miniature marshmallows
1/2  c. chopped pecans
  Red food coloring (optional)
1 (8 oz.)  carton frozen whipped topping, thawed

      In a bowl, combine milk and lemon juice, mix well.  Stir in the pineapple, cranberry sauce, marshmallows, pecans and food coloring if desired.  Fold in whipped topping.  Spoon into a 13 x 9 inch baking dish.  Freeze until firm, 4 hours or overnight.  Cut into squares.  Yield:  12 - 16 servings.
