HAMBURGER DIP


1  lb. hamburger
1  onion
  garlic powder
  salt and pepper
1  can refried beans
1  can nacho cheese soup
1  lb. velveeta cheese

      Brown hamburger and onions. Add garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Mix in refried beans and cheese soup. Add velveeta cheese and cook over low heat until melted. Serve with tortilla or Dorito chips.
