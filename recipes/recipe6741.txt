CAVATINA


1/2  c. shells
1/2  c. spirals
1/2  c. scroodles
3  c. Mozzarella cheese
32  oz. spaghetti sauce
1  lb. sausage
1 (3 1/2 oz.)  pkg. pepperoni
1 small  can mushrooms

      Brown sausage.  Drain.  Cook pasta and drain.  Mix all ingredients.  Bake at 350 degrees; until cheese melts, about 30 minutes. Use a 2 1/2-quart dish.  Serves 8.
