DUM DUM SALAD


1 (15 oz.)  can crushed pineapple, drained
1 (15 oz.)  can Thank You brand cherry pie filling
1  (8 oz.) container Cool Whip, thawed
1  can Eagle Brand sweetened condensed milk

      Put all ingredients in a bowl and mix with mixer at medium speed until mixed well.  Place in refrigerator for 1 hour.  Serve.
