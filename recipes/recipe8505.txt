GREEN BEAN STEW


1  onion, diced
1  clove garlic, chopped
1/4  c. olive oil
1  c. water
1 10  oz. can tomatoes
  salt and pepper to taste
1  lb. green beans

      Saut√© onion and garlic in oil. Cut beans in half. Add to onion and garlic. Cover and let steam for 30 minutes, stirring frequently. Add water and tomatoes until even with beans. Add seasonings to taste. Cook 15 minutes or until tender.
