OYSTER CRACKERS


1  pkg dry ranch dressing
1/2  tsp. dill weed
1/2  tsp. garlic powder
1/2  tsp. lemon pepper
3/4 - 1  C. cooking oil
16  oz. pkg. oyster crackers

      Combine oil and dry ingredients together.  Pour over crackers and stir until well coated.
      Place on cookie sheet and bake in low oven (225 degrees to 250 degrees) for 15 to 20 minutes, stirring every 5 minutes.
      Cool and store in airtight container.
