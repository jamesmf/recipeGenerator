ANGELS ON HORSEBACK


1  pt Large fresh oysters
1/2  tsp salt
1/8  tsp paprika
12  slices of Bacon, cut in half
1/8  tsp Black pepper
2  TBSP Fresh parsley, chopped

      Drain the oysters.  Lay an oyster across 1/2 slice of Bacon. Sprinkle each one with the seasonings and parsley.  Roll entire strip of Bacon around the Oyster and fasten with toothpicks. Place oysters on a rack in shallow baking dish.  Bake in a 450 degree oven for 10 mins, or until bacon is crisp.  Remove toothpicks and serve.
