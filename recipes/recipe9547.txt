HONEY MUSTARD SALMON FILLETS


1/4  c. Dijon mustard
1/2  c. honey
4  Tbsp. melted butter
1  tsp. curry powder
  salmon fillets

      Heat oven to 400 degrees;.  Combine mustard, honey, melted butter and curry powder to make sauce.  Put a small amount of sauce on bottom of greased baking pan.  Put in salmon skin side down.  Brush top with sauce.  Bake for 8 to 10 minutes.  Turn.  Baste with sauce generously. Continue to bake additional 8 to 10 minutes or until fish flakes.
      Sauce can be stored in refrigerator for future use on chicken.
