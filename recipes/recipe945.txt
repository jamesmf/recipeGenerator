ZUCCHINI SEAFOOD IMPOSSIBLE QUICHE


4  c. sliced zucchini, 1/4 inch thick
1 1/2  c. Bisquick
1 1/2  c. shredded sharp cheese (about 6 oz.)
1  c. chopped onion
1 (6 oz.)  can crabmeat
1  can tiny shrimp
1/2  c. vegetable oil
3  eggs
1  tsp. oregano
1  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper

      Grease baking dish.  Mix all ingredients and spread in dish. Bake, uncovered, at 400 degrees; for 25 to 30 minutes or until golden brown and knife inserted in center comes out clean.  Serves 6. The Bisquick mix makes a crust on top.
