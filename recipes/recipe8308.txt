BAKED CHICKEN WITH ORANGE NUT RICE


2  whole chicken breasts, cut in half or 4 split chicken breasts
1/3  c. all-purpose flour
2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. ground white pepper
1/3  c. vegetable oil
1  c. water
1/2  c. orange juice
1  Tbsp. orange rind, grated
1/2  c. onion, minced
1/4  c. butter, cut into pieces
2  Tbsp. pecans, chopped
2  Tbsp. pimento, chopped
1  Tbsp. lemon juice
1 1/2  c. instant rice (uncooked)

	Rinse chicken and pat dry with paper towels. Combine flour, 1 teaspoon salt and pepper. Coat chicken pieces on all sides with flour mixture. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add chicken and brown on all sides.
	In an 8 x 8 x 2-inch baking dish, combine water, orange juice, orange rind, onion, butter, pecans, pimento and lemon juice. Add rice and remaining 1 teaspoon salt. Stir to mix well. Place chicken, skin side up, in a single layer over rice mixture. Cover pan with foil. Bake in a preheated 350 degrees; oven for 35 minutes. Uncover and continue to bake for 15 minutes or until fork can be inserted into the chicken with ease.
