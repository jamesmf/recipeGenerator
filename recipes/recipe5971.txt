EGG DROP NOODLE SOUP


4  c. chicken stock
1/2  c. uncooked thin egg noodles
2  eggs, beaten
1  Tbsp. lemon juice
  salt and pepper to taste
1/2  c. finely chopped scallions

      Place chicken stock in large saucepan and bring to a boil. Add noodles, boil for 5 minutes, or until noodles are just tender. Reduce heat to simmer.  Stir eggs, lemon juice, salt and pepper together well.  Slowly add eggs to stock in a thin stream, stirring gently with a fork.  Simmer for 1 minute, or until egg has set.  Sprinkle soup with chopped scallions, and serve hot.
