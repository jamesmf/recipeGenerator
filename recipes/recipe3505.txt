HUMMUS-I


16  oz. garbanzo beans
2  Tbsp. tahini
1  Tbsp. lemon juice
1/2  tsp. salt
1  clove garlic
1/4  cup cold water
4  Tbsp. olive oil
  Snipped parsley

      Blend beans, tahini, lemon juice, salt, garlic and water. Mix in 2 Tbsp. olive oil by hand.  Arrange in dish.   Pour remaining olive oil and garnish with olives, snipped parsley, and paprika.
