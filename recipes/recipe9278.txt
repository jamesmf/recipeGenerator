MAW MAW WANNIE'S CASSEROLE


1  lb. ground beef
1/2  small onion, chopped
10  oz. (1 can) soup, vegetable beef
1  medium potato
1/2  c. Cheddar cheese, shredded
10  oz. (1 can) soup, cream of mushroom
1  sleeve Ritz crackers, crushed

      Mix ground beef and onion together.  Brown in a large skillet.  Drain off fat and add vegetable beef soup.  Simmer while peeling and cutting the potato into wafer-thin slices.
      Place a single layer of potato slices on the bottom of a lightly greased 2-quart casserole dish.  Spread a layer of the beef mixture, sprinkle a little cheese on top, then spread 1/2 can of cream of mushroom soup on top.  Repeat these layers.  Top with Ritz crackers.
      Cover dish and bake at 350 degrees; for 40 minutes.
