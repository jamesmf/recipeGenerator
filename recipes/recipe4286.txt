BROCCOLI AND CAULIFLOWER SALAD


1  head cauliflower
2  bunches broccoli
1/2  small purple onion
1 small  pkg. mild Cheddar cheese, shredded
1/3  c. bacon bits

 
Dressing:
1  c. Miracle Whip
1/4  c. sugar
2  Tbsp. red wine vinegar

      Chop broccoli, cauliflower and onion into tiny pieces. Put vegetables into a large bowl. Add shredded cheese and bacon bits.
       Mix dressing in separate bowl and pour over vegetables.
      If salad mix is too dry, you can double the dressing mix. Stir well and refrigerate until ready to serve.
