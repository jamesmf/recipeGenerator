SPINACH SALAD


1  bag spinach, washed and torn
3  hard-cooked eggs, chopped
6 to 8  slices bacon, fried and crumbled
1  can mandarin oranges
1 small  pkg. slivered almonds

      Toss.
Dressing:
1  c. oil
3/4  c. sugar
1/2  tsp. salt
1/3  c. catsup
1/4  c. vinegar
1 medium onion, grated or 1  tsp. onion powder
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  tsp. Accent

      Mix as needed. Mix salad and dressing together and serve. Serves  6 to 10.
