SHIPWRECK


1  qt. diced raw potatoes
3  Tbsp. butter
1  large onion, diced
1  small stalk celery, chopped (optional)
1  qt. beans, cooked (dry beans)
1/4  c. raw rice
2 small  cans tomato soup
1  lb. hamburger
2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
1/2  c. water

      Melt  butter  in baking dish.  Add  other  ingredients, putting  potatoes  on  bottom and  hamburger  on  top.  Season potatoes and hamburger.  Bake at 250 degrees; for 2 hours.
