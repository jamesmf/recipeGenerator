HAM AND BROCCOLI BAKE


1 (10 oz.)  pkg. of frozen broccoli or 2 cups of fresh
1/2  cup chopped onions
1/4  cup margarine
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1 soup  can of milk
1  cup shredded sharp cheese
3  cups cubed fully cooked ham
2  cups quick cooking rice
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce

      Cook broccoli until tender, drain well.  Cook onions in butter in sauce pan until done, but not brown. In a large mixing bowl stir together mushroom soup, milk and cheese.  Add broccoli, cooked onion, ham, rice and Worcestershire sauce, mix well.  Put in casserole dish.  Bake at 350 degrees until rice is done (about 45-50 minutes).
