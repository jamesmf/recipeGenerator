PIZZA LASAGNA


1 (16 oz.)  box lasagna noodles
1  lb. ground beef
1 (3 lb.)  jar Prego spaghetti sauce with meat
1  tsp. garlic powder
1/2  tsp. oregano
1  (16 oz.) container small curd cottage cheese
2 (8 oz.)  pkg. Mozzarella cheese
  Parmesan cheese
  pepperoni

	Fry ground beef and drain. Add spaghetti sauce, oregano and garlic powder to ground beef. Simmer. Cook noodles as package directs. Stir in cottage cheese to sauce. In greased baking dish, alternate layers of noodles, sauce and cheese. Place pepperoni on top. Bake at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes.
