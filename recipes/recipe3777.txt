CAJUN PRETZELS


2  16 oz bags beer pretzels  (sourdough)
1  tsp. broiled steak seasoning
1/2  tsp. chili powder
1/2  tsp. dill weed
1  pkg Hidden Valley Ranch  dressing mix (dry)
2  tsp. cayenne pepper
1  tbsp. Creole seasoning
1  c. salad oil

      Mix oil and all seasonings with a fork.  Pour over broken pretzels.  Toss to coat.  Bake at 200 degrees; for 2 hours until dry. Stir often to coat.
