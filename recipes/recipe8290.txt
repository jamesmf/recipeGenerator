EASY STROMBOLI


1  can Pillsbury pizza crust
1/4  c. pizza sauce
1/2  lb. ham, thinly sliced
1  c. Mozzarella cheese, shredded
1/4  c. green peppers, chopped
1/4  tsp. Italian seasoning

	Unroll dough. Place on greased cookie sheet. Press dough into a 12 x 8-inch rectangle. Spread sauce on dough to within 2-inches of long side. Top with ham, cheese, green peppers and Italian seasoning. Fold long side of dough over filling. Pinch to seal. Bake at 400 degrees; for 15 to 20 minutes or until golden brown. Makes 6 servings.
