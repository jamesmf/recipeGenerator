POTATO SOUP


6  medium potatoes
3  c. water
3  c. milk
2  tsp. salt
1  tsp. black pepper
  Chives (optional)
1/2  stick butter

      Cut potatoes in small cubes.  Pour in water and cook until tender on low heat.  Put milk, salt, pepper, chives and butter in and bring to a slow boil.  Cook for approximately 3 minutes.  Good plain or garnished with grated cheese.
