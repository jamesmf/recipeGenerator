FRIED CHEESE STICKS


8  oz. pkg. sliced Mozzarella cheese
1/4  c. flour
1  egg, beaten
1/4  c. bread crumbs
1/2  c. oil for frying

      Cut cheese slices into halves.  Roll until cheese forms sticks. Coat with flour.  Dip in egg; roll in bread crumbs.  Fry in hot oil in skillet for 1 minute or until golden brown.  Drain well.  Makes 16 servings.
