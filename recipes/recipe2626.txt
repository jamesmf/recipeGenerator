EASY PECAN PIE


  pastry for one-crust 8 or 9-inch pie

 
9-inch Pie:
3  eggs
2/3  c. sugar
1/2  tsp. salt
1/3  c. butter, melted
1  c. light corn syrup
1  c. pecans*

 
8-inch Pie:
2  eggs
1/2  c. sugar
1/4  tsp. salt
1/4  c. butter, melted
3/4  c. corn syrup
3/4  c. pecans*

      *I use more pecans.
      Beat  together  ingredients, except pecans.  Mix in the pecans.  Pour  into  pastry  lined  pan.  Bake  until  set and pastry is browned at 375 degrees; for 40 to 50 minutes.
