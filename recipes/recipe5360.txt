STRAWBERRY JELLO SALAD


2  pkg. strawberry Jell-O (small pkg.)
1  pt. frozen strawberries
1 1/2  c. boiling water
1  can crushed pineapple
1/2  bag mini marshmallows
8  oz. Cool Whip
8  oz. cream cheese
  nuts, finely chopped your own)*

      Dissolve gelatin in boiling water. Add pineapple, marshmallows and strawberries. Place in refrigerator and chill until set.
       Prepare dessert topping. Combine with cream cheese and spread over top of first layer. Sprinkle with nuts.
