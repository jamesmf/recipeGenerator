BREAKFAST PIZZA



1  tube crescent roll dough
2  c. frozen shredded hash browns, defrosted
1  c. shredded cheddar cheese
5  eggs
  salt and pepper to taste
2 1/2  oz. Canadian bacon, cut into cubes

      Heat the oven 400 degrees;.  Unroll crescent roll dough and fit into a 12 inch pizza pan, pressing seams together to form a crust. Sprinkle with the defrosted hash browns and cheese.  Beat the egg in a bowl and season with salt and pepper.  Pour over the pizza. Place Canadian bacon cubes on top.  Bake 20 minutes, until eggs are set and crust is golden.
