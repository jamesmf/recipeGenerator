J & S SLOPPY JOES


3  lb. hamburger
1  onion
2  Tbsp. butter
2  Tbsp. vinegar
2  Tbsp. brown sugar
4  Tbsp. lemon juice
3  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  Tbsp. dry mustard
1  bottle ketchup
1  c. water
1/2  c. chopped celery
  salt to taste

      Brown the hamburger and onion in the butter. Add the remaining ingredients. Simmer for 4 hours.
