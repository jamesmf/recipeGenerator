CHICKEN SPAGHETTI


1 small  pkg. of spaghetti, cooked and drained
1  boiled chicken, deboned
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  can or 2 c. chicken broth
1  onion, chopped
1/2  lb. grated cheese
1 small  jar ripe olives, chopped
1  can chopped pimientos
1  can green peas, drained

      Put layer of chicken and layer of mixed ingredients in casserole dish.  Alternate layers.  Cook at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.
