EASY REFRIGERATOR PICKLES


  cucumbers and onions (to fill gal. jar)

 
Brine:
3 1/2  c. sugar
1/3  c. salt
3  c. vinegar
1  tsp. turmeric
1  tsp. mustard seed
1  tsp. celery seed

      Slice cucumbers and onions.  Layer in a gallon jar.  Heat brine on stove to simmering.  Pour over cucumbers and onions in jar.  Refrigerate for 7 days, turning jar over once a day.
