ZUCCHINI FRITTERS


  vegetable oil
1/2  c. milk
1  egg, lightly beaten
1  c. all-purpose flour
1 1/2  tsp. baking powder
1/2 (1 oz.)  pkg. Ranch style dip mix
2  c. shredded zucchini

      Fill a deep-fat fryer or skillet with oil to a 2-inch depth. Heat to 375 degrees;.  Meanwhile, combine milk and egg in a mixing bowl. Stir together dry ingredients and add to egg mixture; blend well. Fold in zucchini.  Drop batter by rounded teaspoonfuls into hot oil.  Fry until deep golden brown, turning once.  Drain thoroughly on paper towels.  Yields 1 1/2 to 2 dozen.
