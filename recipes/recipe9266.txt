CHICKEN DELIGHT


6  chicken breasts
1  pkg. (4 oz.) dried beef
1  can mushroom soup
1  c. sour cream
6  slices bacon

      Cut up beef and place in casserole dish.  Pepper chicken and wrap 1 slice bacon around each serving.  Place on beef.  Mix soup and sour cream and pour over chicken.  Cover and cook at 275 degrees; for 3 hours.
