ZIPPY SWEET-SOUR SAUCE


2/3  c. brown sugar, firmly packed
2  Tbsp. flour
2/3  c. pineapple juice
1 1/2  tsp. Colman's mustard
1  tsp. water
1/3  c. vinegar
1  tsp. soy sauce

      Combine sugar and flour; gradually add pineapple juice. Heat. Moisten mustard with the water; add to mixture with vinegar and soy sauce. Bring to a boil. A delicious sauce to use with sausage balls or shrimp.
