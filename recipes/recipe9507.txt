EGG CASSEROLE


18  eggs, beaten
3  lb. sausage (mild or hot), cooked and crumbled
3  c. grated cheese
18  slices bread, crust removed and torn into pieces
3  tsp. salt
3  tsp. dried mustard
6  c. milk

      Mix ingredients, adding sausage and eggs last.  Pour into 2 (1 1/2-quart) casserole dishes.  Refrigerate overnight.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes.
