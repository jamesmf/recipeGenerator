HAWAIIAN COCONUT SPREAD


1  (8 oz.) container soft cream cheese
2  Tbsp. apricot, pineapple or peach preserves
1/3  c. flaked coconut

      Combine cream cheese and preserves, mixing until well blended.  Add coconut; mix well.  Chill.  Serve with nut bread slices.  Makes 1 1/3 cups.
      Variations:  Add 1/8 teaspoon anise seed.  Substitute 1/4 cup whole berry cranberry sauce for preserves.
