LITTLE BROWN JUG SUGAR COOKIES


1  c. soft shortening
2  c. brown sugar
2  eggs
1/2  c. buttermilk
3 1/2  c. flour
1  tsp. soda
1  tsp. salt
  coconut or nuts (optional)

      Mix together shortening, brown sugar and eggs.  Stir in buttermilk.  Sift together and stir in flour, soda and salt.  Add coconut or nuts.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 8 to 10 minutes.  Yields about 6 dozen 2 1/2-inch cookies.
