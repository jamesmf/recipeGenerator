CHICKEN DRUMMETTES


1  c. soy sauce
1  c. sugar
1/2  c. white wine or rice wine
1  Tbsp. ground ginger root
20  to 30 chicken drummettes

      Combine first 4 ingredients for sauce.  Marinate drummettes overnight, turning every few hours in sauce.  Bake at 400 degrees; about 30 minutes, then put under broiler for about 5 to 8 minutes, until crusty.  Serve as a finger food.
