CARROT SOUP


1/2  c. chopped onion
2  Tbsp. oleo
1/2  c. chopped celery
2  c. chicken broth
2  c. sliced carrots
1  c. milk
1/4  tsp. nutmeg

      Saut√© onion in oleo.  Add celery, chicken broth and sliced carrots.  Cook until carrots are tender.  Put all in blender. Blend until pureed.  Add milk and nutmeg.  Heat and serve.
      Note:  If thicker than you like, add more milk.
