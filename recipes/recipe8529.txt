SPAGHETTI PIE


6  oz. spaghetti
2  tbsp. margarine
2  eggs, beaten
1/3  c. grated parmesan cheese
1  pound ground beef
1/2  c. chopped onion
1  c. cream style cottage cheese
3/4  c. chopped green pepper
1- 7 1/2  oz. can tomatoes, cut up
1- 6  oz. can tomato paste
1  tsp. sugar
1  tsp. crushed dries oregano
1/4  tsp. garlic salt
1/2  c. mozzarella cheese

      Cook spaghetti until tender, drain.  Stir butter into hot spaghetti, stir in beaten eggs and parmesan cheese.  Form spaghetti mixture into a "crust" in a greased 16 inch pie plate.  Spread with cottage cheese.
       Cook ground beef, onion and green pepper until meat is brown and vegetables are done. Drain off fat. Stir in undrained tomatoes, tomato paste, sugar, oregano, garlic salt and heat through. Turn meat mixture into spaghetti crust. Cover edges with foil. Bake at 350 degrees;F. for 20 minutes. Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese; bake about 5 minutes more or until cheese is melted.
