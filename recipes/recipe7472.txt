VEAL MARSALA


3  to 4 veal scallops
3  oz. flour
1  oz. olive oil
2  oz. dry Marsala wine
1  oz. stock
2  oz. butter
2  oz. mushrooms, sliced
  Salt and pepper to taste

      Pound veal scallops and season with salt and pepper.  Flour and saut√© in olive oil until cooked.  Remove the scallops, set aside.  Degrease, then deglaze pan with marsala wine.  Add stock and sliced mushrooms; reduce until mixture begins to thicken. Return the scallops to sauce; add butter and cook until sauce becomes syrupy.  Plate veal and spoon sauce over veal.
