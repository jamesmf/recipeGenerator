JEWISH KUGEL


1  lb. flat noodles, cooked
6  eggs, lightly beaten
3  to 4 apples, peeled and sliced thin
32  oz. cottage cheese
1  pt. sour cream
1 1/2  c. golden raisins
1  tsp. vanilla
3/4  c. sugar

      Mix cheese, sour cream, eggs, raisins, sugar, vanilla and apples.  Add cooked noodles; mix well.  Pour batter into one large or two 13 x 9-inch pans.  Cover with foil; bake at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes.  Serve warm or cold.
