EXCELLENT HASH BROWN CASSEROLE


1 large  pkg. hash browns
1  lb. ground beef, browned with onion
1  box frozen peas
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  can cream of chicken soup
1/2  c. sour cream with chives
1 small  pkg. Cheddar cheese
1  can onion rings

      Put hash browns in a buttered large roaster or casserole. Top with beef and onions. Sprinkle with peas. Combine soups and sour cream and spread over peas; sprinkle with cheese. Top with onion rings. Bake at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes or more.
