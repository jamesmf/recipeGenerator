HAMBURGER AND VEGETABLE CASSEROLE


1 1/2  lb. lean ground beef
1  can condensed vegetable soup
1  can condensed vegetable beef soup
1  can condensed cream of mushroom soup
5  or 6 potatoes, peeled and cut into chunks
1  bag (16-oz.) frozen mixed vegetables (peas, green beans, corn and carrots)
  Salt and pepper to taste

      Brown ground beef until crumbly and no longer pink; drain fat.  Mix beef with the remaining ingredients.  Spoon into baking dish and bake at 350 degrees; for 2 hours.
