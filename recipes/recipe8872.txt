DANA'S CHICKEN AND RICE


1  c. uncooked rice
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  can cream of celery soup
1/2  c. milk
1  envelope Lipton onion soup mix
4  chicken breasts (may wish to cut into strips)

      Spray dish with Pam. Place rice/soup mixture into pan and then put chicken on top. Sprinkle dry soup mix on top. Cover with foil. Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 1/4 hours.
