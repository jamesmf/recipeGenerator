CREAMY CHICKEN SOUP


3  chicken breasts, cooked and cut in chunks (reserve broth)
1  can whole kernel corn, drained
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  can milk
1  can Ro-Tel (hot or mild, your choice)
1  Tbsp. butter or margarine
  salt and pepper to taste

	Boil chicken breasts in pot until cooked. Reserve broth. Cut into bite-size pieces.
	In large pot, add chicken, corn, cream of chicken soup, milk, Ro-Tel, salt and pepper and butter. If soup is too thick, you can add a little chicken broth to desired consistency.	Stir well and heat thoroughly.
