QUICK AND EASY PIZZA


2  c. all-purpose flour
1  Tbsp. baking powder
1  tsp. salt
2/3  c. milk
1/3  c. oil
2  c. pizza sauce
1/4  c. water
1  tsp. ground oregano
1/4  tsp. pepper
1  tsp. garlic powder
1/2  c. onion
1 (4 oz.)  can mushrooms
1  lb. browned hamburger
  shredded cheese

      Mix flour, baking powder and salt.  Add  milk  and oil. Pat dough into bottom  on greased pizza pan.  Mix pizza sauce, water and seasonings.  Spread  over  dough,  then  sprinkle on onion, mushrooms (well  drained),  hamburger and cheese.  Bake at 425 degrees; for 20 to 25 minutes.
