CRANBERRY SALAD


2  c. fresh cranberries
1  orange, peeled and diced
1  c. sugar
1  pkg. orange Jell-O
1/2  c. chopped celery
1/2  c. apples, diced
1/2  c. nuts, chopped
1  c. boiling water

      Puree cranberries and orange in a food processor.  Stir in sugar and let stand 2 to 3 hours.  Prepare Jell-O with boiling water and cool.  When Jell-O begins to set, combine all ingredients and pour into mold.
