HALLOWEEN BREAD
(A Spook Brings This To Us)  

1  c. sugar
1/2  c. brown sugar
1  c. cooked pumpkin
1/2  c. salad oil
2  eggs, unbeaten
2  c. flour
1  tsp. soda
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. nutmeg
1/2  tsp. cinnamon
1/4  tsp. ginger
1/4  c. water
1/2  c. pecans, chopped
1  c. raisins (optional)

      Combine and mix well the sugar, brown sugar, pumpkin, salad oil and eggs.  Sift together and add flour, soda, salt, nutmeg, cinnamon and ginger.  Stir in water.  Add pecans and raisins.  Mix well and spoon into greased and floured loaf pan.  Bake in preheated 350 degrees; oven for 1 hour or until toothpick inserted comes out clean.  When done, turn out on a rack to cool.  Yields 1 loaf.
