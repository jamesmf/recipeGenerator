DEER JERKY


3  lb. venison
1/2  c. soy sauce
1/2  c. Worcestershire sauce
2  tsp. monosodium glutamate
2  tsp. table salt
2/3  tsp. black pepper
2/3  tsp. onion powder

	Cut venison into 3/4-inch strips. Combine the remaining ingredients; pour over meat and refrigerate, covered, overnight. Drain; hang meat over the racks in oven and bake at 150 degrees; to 170 degrees; for 6 hours, until meat is dried.
