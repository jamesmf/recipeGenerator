EASY CHICKEN POT PIE


4  chicken breasts, cooked and cut up
1  stick margarine
1 large  can Veg-All, drained
1  cup chicken broth
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  c. Bisquick
1  c. whole milk or 2% milk

      Melt margarine in 9 x 13 x 2 inch baking dish.  Spread chicken over margarine.  Sprinkle Veg-All over chicken.  Mix broth with soup and pour over Veg-All.  Mix milk with Bisquick and pour over all ingredients.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour.
