VALENTINE DESSERT

First Part:
1/4  c. sugar
1  c. flour
1/2  c. nuts (pecans)
1/2  c. butter

 
Second Part:
2  stiffly beaten egg whites
2/3  c. sugar
1  qt. strawberries, thawed
1  Tbsp. lemon juice

      First Part:  Mix all ingredients and put in 8 x 8-inch pan. Bake at 350 degrees; for 20 minutes.  Stir occasionally.  Spread into dish, keeping a little for the top.
      Second Part:  Combine stiffly beaten egg whites and other ingredients.  Beat until mixed and fairly stiff.  Fold in 2 cups cream that's already beaten until stiff.  Pour in dish and put rest of crumbs on top.  Freeze at least 6 hours.  Unthaw about 1/2 hour before ready to serve.
