CHILI


1  lb. ground beef
1  medium onion, chopped
1  large green pepper, chopped
16  oz. can whole tomatoes
15  oz. can kidney beans (undrained)
2  tablespoons chili powder
3  oz. can tomato sauce
  salt to taste (optional)

	In large skillet, brown beef, onion, green pepper.  Drain excess fat.  Break up whole tomatoes.  Stir in remaining ingredients.  Cook in a Dutch oven at 350 degrees;until bubbly.  Stir to keep from burning.	If hotter chili desired, add hot peppers (bottled) and some of the pepper juice to suit your own taste. Serves 5 to 6 persons.
