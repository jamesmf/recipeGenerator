CATFISH COURTBOUILLON


2  lb. catfish, cleaned and cut up
  salt and red pepper
1  clove garlic, minced
1/4  c. oil
1/3  c. flour
1  medium onion, minced
1  rib celery, chopped
3/4  c. tomatoes, chopped
2  Tbsp. tomato sauce
1  bay leaf
1/2  tsp. sugar
3  pt. water
  dash of oregano
  dash of thyme
  juice from 1/2 lemon
1  Tbsp. parsley, chopped
1  Tbsp. green onion tops, chopped

      Season fish with salt, pepper, and garlic; put in covered dish and chill several hours.
       Make a roux by browning flour in oil, stirring constantly until brown. Remove from heat; add onions and celery. Mix well and return to heat; add tomato sauce, bay leaf, sugar, and water. Bring to a boil; reduce heat and simmer for 45 minutes. Add catfish, oregano, thyme and lemon; simmer for 20 minutes longer. Add parsley and green onions. Cook 1 minute longer. Serve with rice.
