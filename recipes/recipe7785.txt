SWISS STEAK


1 1/2  lb. round steak, cut in serving pieces
1  Tbsp. dry mustard
1/2  c. flour
  salt and pepper
1 1/2  c. tomatoes
2  Tbsp. brown sugar
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  c. sliced onions
1  diced carrot

      Pound dry mustard, salt, pepper and flour into steak. Brown in a little fat. Place tomatoes, brown sugar, Worcestershire sauce, onions and diced carrot in oven dish. Pour over steak. Cover and bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour.
