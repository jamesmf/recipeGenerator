CHOCOLATE CHIP CHEESE BALL
(Appetizer or Dessert)  

1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese, softened
1/4  c. powdered sugar
1/2 - 1  c. chopped pecans
1/4  c. butter, softened
1/2  c. mini chocolate chips

      Blend cream cheese, butter and powdered sugar until smooth. Stir in chocolate chips, 1/4 cup of pecans can also be stirred in. Chill 1 hour.  Form into balls and roll in remaining pecans to cover.  Serve with Nilla wafers, graham crackers or other crisp cookies.
      Variation:  Stir in 2 - 3 Tbsp. well drained and chopped Maraschino cherries.
