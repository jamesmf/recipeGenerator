HOT DOG CHILI III


10  lb. ground chuck
4  onions, chopped (medium to large)
  garlic powder (half 4 oz. bottle)
1 (6 oz.)  can tomato paste
1 (8 oz.)  can tomato sauce
4  oz. chili powder
  few sprinkles onion powder
  sprinkle of garlic salt
2  oz. paprika
1  tsp. black pepper
  pinch of salt
  water

	Mix ingredients. Cook on medium-high heat until it starts to boil; then cook on medium. After done, let simmer on low for 2 to 3 hours.
