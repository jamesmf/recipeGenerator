DIABETIC CHOCOLATE PIE


4  tsp. Sweet 'N Low
2  Tbsp. flour
3  egg yolks
1/2  c. milk
2  tsp. vanilla
1/4  c. cocoa
1/4  c. margarine

 
Meringue:
2  egg whites
1/4  tsp. Sweet 'N Low
1  tsp. corn syrup
1  tsp. cream of tartar or lemon juice

      Mix first 6 ingredients.  Cream in margarine.  Bake at 450 degrees; for 1/2 hour.  Make meringue with 2 egg whites, 1/4 teaspoon Sweet 'N Low, 1 teaspoon corn syrup and 1 teaspoon cream of tartar or lemon juice.  Return to oven about 10 minutes or until golden brown.
