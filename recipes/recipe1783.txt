SWEDISH HEIRLOOM COOKIES


1/2  c. Crisco
1/2  c. butter or margarine, softened
1  c. powdered sugar
1/2  tsp. salt
2  c. flour
1  Tbsp. water
1  Tbsp. vanilla
1 1/4  c. ground or finely chopped almonds or pecans
  powdered sugar

      Cream shortening and butter until light and fluffy.  Add 1 cup powdered sugar and salt and cream well.  Blend in flour.  Add water and vanilla; mix well.  Stir in pecans.  Shape into 1-inch balls and slightly flatten.  Bake on ungreased baking sheet at 325 degrees; for 12 to 15 minutes, until done.  Roll in powdered sugar while still warm.  Makes 8 dozen.
      In Sweden, almonds are used.
