OLD FASHIONED CORN PUDDING


2  c. corn (fresh is better)
2  c. milk
3  eggs, well beaten (6 eggs better)
1  Tbsp. sugar
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. nutmeg (optional)
1  stick butter

      Mix all ingredients together.  Beat egg whites separately. Bake until brown in 300 degrees; oven.  May be made in advance.
