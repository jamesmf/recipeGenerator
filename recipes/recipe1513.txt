POTATO ROLLS


1/2  c. warm mashed potatoes
1 1/2  c. warm water
1/2  c. oleo
6 1/2  c. flour
2  pkg. yeast
1/2  c. sugar
1  tsp. salt
2  eggs

      Sprinkle yeast in water.  Add sugar and salt.  Let bubble.  Add eggs and 3 cups flour.  Mix at high speed with mixer.  Add 2 cups more flour.  Use wooden spoon to mix rest of flour.  Let rise in icebox, covered, for 2 hours.  Remove and punch down.  This may be kept in icebox for 3 or 4 days.  Just take out as much as you need. Shape into rolls and let rise for 2 hours.  Bake at 350 degrees; in oven until done.
