FRESH BROCCOLI SALAD


1  bunch fresh broccoli
1  small purple onion, chopped
5  slices crumbled bacon
1/2  c. raisins
1/4 to 1/3  c. vinegar
1 1/2  Tbsp. sugar
  black pepper to taste
3/4  c. mayonnaise (not salad dressing)

      Wash and drain broccoli; peel stems and dice into small pieces. Separate flowerets; add onions, bacon, and raisins. Combine last four ingredients and whip with a wire whisk until smooth and creamy. Amounts of sugar and vinegar may be adjusted to taste. Pour dressing over broccoli and let set 2 or 3 hours before serving.
