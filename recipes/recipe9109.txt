ITALIAN STYLE MEAT LOAF


1  lb. lean ground beef
6  oz. hot Italian sausage
1 (14 1/2 oz.)  can Del Monte Italian style stewed tomatoes
1  c. fresh bread crumbs
1/2  c. chopped onion
1/2  c. chopped green peppers
1  egg, beaten

      In large bowl combine all ingredients; mix well. Place in 4 1/2 x 8-inch loaf pan. Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour; drain.
