PFEUSERLI
(Swiss Pancake Dabs)  

1  oz. butter
1  gill warm milk (1/2 c.)
3  to 4 spoonfuls flour, sifted
2  to 3 egg whites, stiffly beaten

      Melt butter in warm milk and stir in sifted flour to make batter.  Fold in the stiffly beaten egg whites.  Fry in walnut-size pieces in very hot oil.  Excellent with fall vegetables.
