HERBED CHICKEN CASSEROLE


3  large chicken breasts, cut in half and boned
  salt and pepper
1/4  c. butter
1  can cream of chicken
3/4  c. white cooking wine
1 (5 oz.)  can water chestnuts, drained and sliced
1 (3 oz.)  can sliced mushrooms, drained
2  Tbsp. chopped green pepper
1/4  tsp. thyme

      Brown chicken in butter, then arrange in 9 x 13 baking dish.
       Sauce:  Add soup to butter.  Slowly add wine.  Add rest of ingredients.  Heat to boil, then pour over chicken.  Cover and bake at 350 degrees; for 25 minutes.  Uncover and bake another 30 minutes. Add almonds and pimento for color.  Serve with wild rice.
