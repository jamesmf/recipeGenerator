POTATO SOUP WITH SOUR CREAM


2  lrg. or 4 sm. potatoes, diced
1  med. onion
1  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp white pepper
2  C. sour cream
  parsley, minced

      Boil peeled potatoes with salt and pepper for about 15 minutes.  Add sour cream and cook on low heat until the potatoes are tender.  Garnish with parsley.
