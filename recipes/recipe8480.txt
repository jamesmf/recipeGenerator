STUFFED GREEN PEPPERS


4  large green bell peppers
3/4  lb. ground beef
1/3  c. rice, cooked
1  tsp. salt
  dash pepper
1  egg
1/3  c. onion, chopped
1- 10 3/4  oz. can tomato soup
1 1/2  c. water

      Remove seeds and wash peppers. Mix beef, rice, salt, pepper, egg, onion and 1/2 c. soup. Stuff peppers lightly and place in shallow pan. Top each pepper with 1 tbsp. soup. Mix remaining soup and water, pour over peppers. Bake at 350 degrees; for about 35  to 45 minutes. Serves 4.
