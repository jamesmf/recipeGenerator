CHEESECAKE

Crust:
2  c. crushed graham crackers
1  Tbsp. sugar
1  stick butter, melted

 
Filling:
16  oz. cream cheese
1  c. sugar
3  eggs
1  Tbsp. vanilla

 
Topping:
1  large jello (strawberry)
20  oz. thawed strawberries
  whipped topping

      Line 9 x 13-inch pan with crust.  Cream filling ingredients until light.  Pour onto crust.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 25 to 30 minutes.  Cool well.  Mix jello with 2 cups hot water.  Add the strawberries.  Spread over filling.  Top with whipped cream.
