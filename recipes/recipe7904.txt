STUFFED BAKED TURKEY

Turkey:
1  turkey, 11-15 lbs.
1  tsp. ground cinnamon
1-1/2  tsp. cardamom
1/2  tsp. ground black pepper
1  tsp. ground ginger
1  Tbsp. mustard
2  tsp. salt
1  tsp. oregano
  Juice of 1 lemon
1  Tbsp. olive oil
1  Tbsp. vinegar
1  chopped onion

      Blend onion and all ingredients in a blender until a paste has formed.  Marinate the turkey in this paste overnight.
Stuffing:
1/4  lb. ground beef
1/2  onion
1  chopped tomato
1/2  tsp. cardamom
1/4  tsp. cinnamon
1/4  tsp. black pepper
1  tsp. dry ground mint
1  Tbsp. corn oil
1  Tbsp. salt
1  cup rice

      Cook the ground beef with oil and chopped onion, tomato and spices.  Add the rice and cook until it is half cooked.  Fill cavity of turkey with stuffing.  Cover with a foil and bake at 350 degrees;F for 5 hours or until done.
