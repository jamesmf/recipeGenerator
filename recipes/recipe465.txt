CRUNCHY POTATOES


1/3  c. oleo
3/4  c. crushed corn flakes
1 1/2  c. shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
2  tsp. salt
1 1/2  tsp. paprika
3  to 4 large potatoes, sliced 1/2-inch thick

      Melt oleo in jelly roll pan in 375 degrees; oven (any pan will do). Mix crushed corn flakes, cheese and seasonings.  Dip sliced potatoes in butter, then in corn flake mixture.  Lay slices in pan in a single layer.  Bake 1/2 hour or until potato tops are crisp and potatoes are tender.
