HAMBURGER CASSEROLE


1  medium onion
1  lb. ground meat
  pinch of onion salt
  pinch of pepper
1  c. cooked macaroni
  cheddar cheese, sliced
1 small  can tomato sauce

      Brown onion in butter.  Add ground beef.  Cook till done. Add onion salt, pepper, and macaroni to the meat.  Place in a greased casserole dish.  Top with slices of cheddar cheese.  Pour tomato sauce on top.  Bake in oven at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.
