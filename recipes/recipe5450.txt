CHICKEN TORTILLA SOUP


2  cans chicken and rice soup
2  cans cream of chicken soup
2  cans chicken broth
1  can mild Ro-Tel
1 small  can chicken
1  tsp. cumin

      Mix all ingredients together and simmer.  Serve with grated cheese and tortilla chips.  Can be done in crock-pot also.  Canned mixed vegetables can be added.
