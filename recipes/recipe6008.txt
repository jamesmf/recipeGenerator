TOSCANA SOUP


3  links Italian sausage
3/4  c. chopped onion
1  slice bacon, diced
1 1/4  tsp. minced garlic
1  can (13 3/4 to 14 1/2 oz.) chicken broth
2  c. water
2  medium potatoes, peeled and sliced
2  c. thinly sliced fresh kale
1/3  c. heavy whipping cream

      Heat oven to 300 degrees;.  Bake Italian sausage on jelly-roll pan 15 to 20 minutes; drain on paper towels.  Cool and cut into 1/4 inch thick slices.  Cook chopped onion and diced bacon in saucepan over medium heat, about 3 to 4 minutes.  Stir in minced garlic and cook 1 minute.  Add chicken broth, water and potatoes.  Bring to a boil; reduce heat to medium and simmer 10 minutes or until potatoes are tender.  Stir in fresh kale, whipping cream and sausage.  Cook 5 minutes.  Makes 6 servings.
