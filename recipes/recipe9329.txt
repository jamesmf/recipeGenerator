SHRIMP CREOLE


2  Tbsp. butter, melted
1  c. onion, chopped
1  c. green peppers
  garlic (to taste)
14  oz. (1 can) tomatoes, diced
1/3  tsp. paprika
  salt (to taste)
  pepper (to taste)
2  c. shrimp, boiled
  rice, cooked (to serve four)

      In butter, brown onion, green pepper and garlic.  Simmer until pepper is tender.  Add tomatoes, paprika, salt and pepper. Boil 5 minutes.  Add shrimp.  Boil for 10 minutes.
      Serve on rice.
