CHICKEN BISCUIT BAKE


1  can (10-3/4 oz.) cream of chicken soup, undiluted
2/3  c. mayonnaise (See note)
2 to 3  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
4  c. cooked chicken, cubed
3  c. cooked broccoli, chopped
1  medium onion, chopped
1  c. (4 oz.) shredded cheddar cheese
2  tubes (12 oz. each) refrigerated buttermilk biscuits
2  eggs
1/2  c. sour cream
2  tsp. celery seed
1  tsp. salt

      In a bowl, combine the soup, mayonnaise and Worcestershire sauce.  Stir in chicken, broccoli and onion.  Transfer to a greased 13 x 9-inch baking dish.  Sprinkle with cheese.  Cover and bake at 375 degrees; for 20 minutes.  Separate biscuits; cut each in half. Arrange, cut side down over hot chicken mixture.  In a bowl combine remaining ingredients and pour over biscuits.  Bake uncovered, 20 minutes longer or until golden brown.  Yield:  6 - 8 servings.
      Note:  Light or fat free mayonnaise may not be substituted for regular mayonnaise.  Ready in 1 hour or less.
