PORK CHOP-POTATO CASSEROLE


4  pork chops
1/2  c. sour cream
1  medium onion
1  tsp. salt
1  can mushroom soup
4  c. potatoes, thin sliced
2  Tbsp. chopped parsley
1/4  c. water

      Brown pork chops in skillet. Combine soup, sour cream and water in bowl. Mix well. Layer thin sliced potatoes, parsley, onions, soup mixture and pork chops in a 2-quart casserole. Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Bake, covered, at 375 degrees; for 1 hour and 15 minutes. Serves 4.
