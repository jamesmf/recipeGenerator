GREEN TOMATO RELISH


24  green tomatoes, chopped and drained
2  red bell peppers, chopped
2  green bell peppers, chopped
3  green hot peppers, chopped (more if desired)
8  large onions, chopped
5  c. vinegar
4  c. sugar
4  tsp. mustard seed
4  tsp. celery seed

      Let vinegar and spices come to a boil. Add tomatoes, onions and all peppers.  Cook 30 minutes.  Seal while hot.
