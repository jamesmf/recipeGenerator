ANYTIME CUCUMBER SLICES


3  to 4 large cucumbers, sliced
2  medium onions, thinly sliced
3  Tbsp. minced fresh dill or 1 Tbsp. dill weed
1  c. sugar
1/2  c. vinegar
1/2  c. water
1  tsp. salt

      In a bowl, combine cucumbers, onions and dill. In a saucepan, combine sugar, vinegar and salt. Bring to a boil. Pour over cucumber mixture. Cover and chill 3 hours or overnight.
