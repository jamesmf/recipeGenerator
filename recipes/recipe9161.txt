OVEN-FRIED FISH


2  lb. fish fillets
1/4  c. milk
2  tsp. salt
1  c. crushed corn flakes

      Heat oven to 500 degrees. Grease baking sheet. Combine milk and salt in a small pan or bowl and stir. Dip fish in milk and roll in crushed corn flakes. Place on baking sheet and bake for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown.
