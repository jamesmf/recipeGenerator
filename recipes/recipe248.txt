NO BAKE COOKIES


1  c. sugar
2  Tbsp. cocoa
1/2  stick margarine
1/4  c. milk
1/2  tsp. vanilla
1/4  c. peanut butter
1 1/2  c. oatmeal

      Mix together sugar, cocoa, margarine and milk in saucepan. Bring to boil and cook 1 minute.  Remove from heat and add vanilla and 1/4 cup peanut butter.  Mix well.  Add oatmeal.  Mix quickly. Drop by teaspoonful onto wax paper lined cookie sheet.  Chill. Makes 3 dozen.
