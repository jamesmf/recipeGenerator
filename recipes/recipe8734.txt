CHICKEN AND RICE


4  chicken breasts
1 1/2  c. Uncle Ben's rice
2  chicken boullion cubes
8  c. water
1/4 -1/2  c. pine nuts
1  onion, chopped
1  stick butter or oleo
  Salt, pepper, cumin to taste

      Boil chicken in water and skim off the  foam. Add boullion cubes.  When chicken is cooked, remove, cool and shred meat. Brown pine nuts in butter till golden brown.  Remove nuts and then brown onions.  Wash rice and put into broth.  Cover and let rice cook. When rice is nearly done, add chicken, onions, pine nuts and seasoning.
