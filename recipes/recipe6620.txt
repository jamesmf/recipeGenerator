PENNE PASTA CASSEROLE


1  box Penne pasta noodles
1  lb. ground beef
1  jar Ragu sauce
1  can Campbell's Cheddar cheese soup
2 (8 oz.)  pkg. Mozzarella cheese

      Cook Penne pasta noodles.  Brown ground beef.  In a greased lasagna pan mix noodles, ground beef, Ragu sauce, cheese soup, and 8 oz. Mozzarella cheese.  Cover pan with foil and bake 30 - 45 minutes at 350 degrees;.  Uncover and sprinkle the rest of the Mozzarella cheese.  Return to oven to melt cheese.
