PEACH-NUTTIEST TOPPING


1 (4 3/4 oz.)  jar strained peaches
1/3  c. light corn syrup
1  tsp. lemon juice
  dash of ground cinnamon
1  Tbsp. butter or margarine
1/4  tsp. vanilla
1/4  c. broken pecan halves

      In saucepan, combine first 4 ingredients.  Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until mixture boils.  Cook 1 to 2 minutes.  Stir in butter and vanilla.  Add pecans.  Serve warm over ice cream.  Makes 3/4 cup.
