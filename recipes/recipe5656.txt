OYSTERS ROCKEFELLER SOUP


5  Tbsp. butter
5  Tbsp. flour
1  bunch green onions, minced
2  cloves garlic, minced
1 1/2  c. chicken stock (broth)
3  c. cream, scalded
1 (20 oz.)  pkg. frozen chopped spinach, thawed
2 1/2  c. oysters, drained and minced
  salt, cayenne, Tabasco sauce, nutmeg, Anisette to taste (optional)

	In a saucepan, melt butter and add the flour, onions and garlic.  Cook until clear. Add the chicken stock and cream. Stir thoroughly. Remove from heat and cool a bit.
	 In a separate pot, cook the spinach per package directions; drain.  Put the oysters and spinach in a blender or food processor and blend well. Add to the soup mixture. Heat soup prior to serving. Season per your taste.
