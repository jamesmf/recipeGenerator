DUCK SOUP


1  duck, cut up and rinsed
1/2 to 1  lb. prunes and/or raisins
4  cloves
5  allspice
1  bay leaf
1/2  tsp. marjoram
  salt to taste
  duck blood
1/2  c. flour
1/4  c. sugar
1/2  c. vinegar

      Put duck in large soup pot; cover with cold water and bring to a boil.  Turn down heat and skim.  Simmer 1 1/2 hours with prunes, cloves, allspice, bay leaf, marjoram and salt.  When duck is tender, add blood.  Thicken soup with flour; flavor with sugar and vinegar.  Serve with potato noodles.
