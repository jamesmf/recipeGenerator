FOIL WRAPPED VEGGIES ON GRILL


3  medium zucchini, sliced
3  medium tomatoes, sliced
1  green pepper, sliced
1  envelope onion soup mix
1/4  c. margarine, softened
2  Tbsp. brown sugar
  salt and pepper

      Cut a large square of heavy-duty aluminum foil.  Mix all ingredients and place on square.  Fold up securely so no juices escape.  Place on charcoal grill, turning once during cooking, 45 minutes to 1 hour.  May also be placed in 350 degrees; oven.  Serves 6.
