DEVILED CRAB


1  lb. crabmeat (fresh)
1/4  lb. saltines, crushed
4  hard-boiled eggs
3/4  c. mayonnaise
1/4  c. butter, melted
2  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  tsp. Tabasco
1/2  tsp. dry mustard
  salt and pepper to taste
1  onion, chopped
1  bell pepper, chopped
2  ribs celery, chopped
1  Tbsp. lemon juice

      Combine crabmeat, cracker crumbs and eggs. Add onion, bell pepper, celery, mayonnaise, butter, Worcestershire sauce, Tabasco, dry mustard and salt and pepper. Mix all together well. May be cooked in quiche dish or in devil crab shells. Bake for 30 minutes at 350 degrees;.
