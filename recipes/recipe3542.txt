LINDA'S OLIVE CHEESE BALLS


15  oz. sharp Cheddar cheese, grated
3  Tbsp. butter
1/3  tsp. salt
1/2   c. plus 2 Tbsp. all-purpose flour
1/4  tsp. pepper
85  green olives

	Cream cheese and butter.  Add salt, flour and pepper.  Shape into 1-inch balls using olives for centers.  Freeze for at least 24 hours.  Remove from freezer and immediately bake in a preheated 400 degrees; oven.	Bake for 10 minutes.  Serve at room temperature.
