PUPPY CHOW MIX


9  c. Rice Chex
1  c. peanut butter
6  oz. semi-sweet chocolate bits
1  stick margarine
2  c. confectioners sugar

	Melt peanut butter, chocolate chips and margarine in microwave 3 minutes on half power. Pour over cereal and mix well. Put sugar in bag. Add cereal and shake until covered.
