OPEN-FACED MINI REUBENS


1/4  c. Thousand Island salad dressing
24  slices snack rye bread
1/2  lb. corned beef, thinly sliced
1 1/2  c. sauerkraut, well drained
4  oz. Swiss cheese, sliced

	Preheat oven to 400 degrees;. Spread about 1/2 teaspoon dressing on each bread slice. Top each with a slice of corned beef, trimmed to fit bread. Add 1 tablespoon of sauerkraut to each slice. Cut cheese to fit bread and place over kraut. Place on ungreased cookie sheet. Bake in oven for 10 minutes or until cheese is melted. Makes 24 appetizers.
	Note: Can be made up to 3 hours in advance. Cover with foil until ready to be served.
