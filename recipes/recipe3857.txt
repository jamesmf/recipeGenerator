AMANDA'S CHEX MIX


2  c. Corn Chex
2  c. Wheat Chex
2  c. Rice Chex
2  c. mixed Corn and Rice Chex
1  c. tidbits
1  c. pretzels
1/2  can mixed nuts
1  stick margarine
5  tsp. seasoned salt
8  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  tsp. garlic salt
5  tsp. Tabasco sauce (red)

      In saucepan, melt margarine. After melted, add rest of ingredients to saucepan. Add melted mix to large bowl. Stir with spoon until all pieces have sauce on them.
