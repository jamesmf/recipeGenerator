NACHO CHIP CHICKEN CASSEROLE


2  lb. skinless boneless chicken (can sub with ground beef)
2  cans cream of chicken
1  can tomatoes and green chilies
1/4  bag taco seasonings
3  c. mild Cheddar cheese
1  bag nacho chips

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees; to 375 degrees;. Bake 20 minutes. Boil chicken and cut in bite size pieces. Mix chicken, chicken soup, tomatoes and green chilies (drain can for a mild flavor), taco seasoning and 2 cups of cheese. Put chips in a greased baking dish. Spread mixture over chips. Sprinkle rest of cheese on top. Bake until cheese is melted and started to brown. Can garnish with shredded lettuce and sour cream.
