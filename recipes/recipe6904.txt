CHEESEBURGER CASSEROLE


1  lb. ground meat
1  pkg. American shredded cheese
1  pkg. Cheddar cheese
3  pkg. elbow noodles
  little to lot of Parmesan cheese
  season to taste

	Put ground meat to brown and strain. Put noodles in an oven pan. Mix ground meat and cheese together and put over noodles. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese on top to your taste. Put in 350 degrees; oven and cook until cheese is melted for about 15 minutes.
