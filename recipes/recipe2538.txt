CHILI SOUP


1  lb. ground beef
1/2  c. chopped green pepper
1/2  c. chopped onion
1  Tbsp. chili powder
2  cans Campbell's chili beef soup
1 soup  can water
1  c. chopped canned tomatoes

      In large saucepan, brown beef; cook with green pepper, onion and chili powder until tender.  Stir in remaining ingredients. Heat, stirring occasionally.  Makes about 6 1/2 cups.
