KAREN'S CHILI


1  lb. ground beef
1  pkg. Lil Smokies sausages, cut into sections
1  lb. sausage (Jimmy Dean or Bass Farm "hot")
1  can kidney beans
1  can diced tomatoes (Del Monte chili)
2 small  cans tomato sauce
1  pkg. chili seasoning
  water (if needed)

      Cook beef and sausage. Add all ingredients to crock-pot and cook 1 1/2 to 2 hours.
