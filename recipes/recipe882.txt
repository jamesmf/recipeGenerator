ZESTY HALIBUT SALAD


4  halibut steaks
1/2  onion
3  stalks celery
4  hard-boiled eggs, chopped
1  c. celery, chopped
1/4  c. sweet pickle, chopped
1  c. mayonnaise
  juice of 1/2 lemon
1/4  c. stuffed olives, sliced
  salt and pepper

      Cook halibut steaks with onion and celery in small amount of water.  Cool.  Flake and remove all bones.  Mix fish with the rest of the ingredients, seasoning to taste with salt and pepper. Serves 4 to 6. Shape in a fish mold if desired or serve on lettuce leaf.
