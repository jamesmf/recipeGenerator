LAYERED SOUTHWESTERN SALAD


1/3  c. chopped fresh cilantro
1/2  c. lime juice
1/2  c. olive oil
1/2  c. sour cream
1  tsp. sugar
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper
1 (16-oz.)  pkg. romaine lettuce, shredded
5  plum tomatoes, chopped
1 (15-oz.)  can black beans, rinsed and drained
1  small red onion, chopped
1 (8-oz.)  pkg. shredded Mexican 4-cheese blend
1 (15-oz.)  can whole kernel corn with red and green peppers, drained
1 (6-oz.)  can sliced ripe olives, drained
2  c. crushed tortilla chips

      Layer lettuce and next 7 ingredients in a 3-quart glass bowl. Serve immediately with vinaigrette dressing.  Makes 8-10 servings.
Vinaigrette Dressing:
1/3  c. chopped fresh cilantro
1/2  c. lime juice
1/2  c. olive oil
1/2  c. sour cream
1  tsp. sugar
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper

      Process all ingredients in a blender or food processor until smooth, stopping to scrape down sides.
