RAISIN SAUCE


1/3  c. raisins
1/2  c. water
1/3  c. jelly
1/2  tsp. grated orange peel
1/2  c. orange juice
2  Tbsp. brown sugar
1  Tbsp. cornstarch
  dash ground allspice
  dash of salt

	Combine raisins, water, jelly, orange peel and orange juice in saucepan.  Bring to boil.  Combine brown sugar, cornstarch, ground allspice and salt.  Stir into orange mixture.  Cook and stir until thick and bubbly.  Serve warm with cooked ham.  Makes about 1 1/2 cups.
