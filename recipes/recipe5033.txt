CHICKEN GUMBO SOUP


2  fryers or 1 (5 lb.) hen
6  qt. hot broth
12  peeled chopped tomatoes
3  c. sliced okra
2  bay leaves
1  lb. smoked, cooked, chopped ham
2  onions, saut√©ed
2  sprigs parsley
  salt and pepper to taste
1 or 2  c. celery, chopped (optional)

      Cook  or roast chicken until tender, roasting or baking gives richer flavor.  Remove meat from bones.  Chop and add to remaining ingredients.  Heat together, then  ladle  into jars. Process 60 minutes at 10 pounds pressure, or serve.
      Note:  Some add 1 or 2 cups cooked, brown rice.
