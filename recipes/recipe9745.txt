CHICKEN CASSEROLE


1  pkg. Stove Top stuffing (prepare moist recipe)
  chicken, cooked and diced
1 large  pkg. broccoli
  sliced Swiss cheese
  large can mushrooms, drained
1  can cream of chicken soup, diluted with water or milk

      Layer all ingredients in the order given in a 9 x 13-inch pan. Bake at 325 degrees; for 35 to 40 minutes.
