HOT MEXICAN CIDER


2  qt. apple cider
1/4  c. lemon juice
1 1/2  c. tequila
1/3  c. orange flavored liqueur
  lemon slices

      Combine apple cider and lemon juice in Dutch oven.  Cook over medium heat until mixture simmers.  Add tequila and orange liqueur. Cook until heated.  Pour over lemon slices.  Yield:  2 1/2 quarts.
