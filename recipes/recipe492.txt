JOHNNYCAKE


2  eggs, well beaten
2  c. buttermilk
2  Tbsp. molasses
2  c. cornmeal
1/2  c. flour
1  tsp. soda
1  tsp. salt
2  Tbsp. butter

      Mix eggs with buttermilk and molasses in mixing bowl.  Add all other ingredients; mix well.  Pour into 11 x 15-inch baking pan. Bake at 425 degrees; for 20 minutes or until cake tests done.  Cut into large squares.  Yields 24 squares.
