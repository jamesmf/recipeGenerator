DILLED TURKEY BREAST


1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can condensed cream of mushroom soup (undiluted)
3/4  c. chicken broth
3/4  c. sour cream
1  Tbsp. dried dill weed
  sliced, cooked turkey, warmed

      In a saucepan, combine soup, chicken broth, sour cream and dill weed. Cook until heated through, but do not boil. Arrange turkey slices on a platter; pour sauce over and serve immediately. Yield: 6 to 8 servings.
