CRANBERRY RELISH


1 (12 oz.)  pkg. fresh cranberries
1/2  c. water
1  tsp. brandy extract
1  tsp. grated orange peel
18  pkg. Equal

      Combine all ingredients, except Equal, in saucepan.  Bring mixture to a boil, reduce heat, cover and simmer for about 10 minutes or until cranberries are soft.  Remove from heat.  Stir in Equal.  You may strain to remove cranberry skins for a smoother sauce.  Cool to room temperature.  Delicious with turkey.  Makes 8 (1/4 cup) servings.
      Calories:  18 per serving.  Diabetic Exchange:  1/2 fruit.
