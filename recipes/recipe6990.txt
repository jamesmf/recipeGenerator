CHICKEN FILE GUMBO


3  lb. chicken
24  oysters (or shrimp)
3  sprigs parsley, minced
2  qt. hot water
  cayenne pepper
2  Tbsp. cooking oil
2  Tbsp. flour
1/2  onion, minced
2  Tbsp. file
  salt and black pepper, to taste

      Cut chicken in pieces; wash and wipe dry. Season well with salt and pepper; brown in hot cooking oil. Remove chicken; sift flour into hot oil and brown. Add onion and cook for 2 minutes. Add chicken, hot water, and a few grains of cayenne pepper; simmer until chicken is tender. Add oyster or shrimp and parsley; simmer 20 minutes. Remove from heat and serve with steamed rice.
