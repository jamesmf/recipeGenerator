CRANBERRY FRUIT SALAD


3  apples, peeled and diced (Gala or Mac's)
3  bananas, diced
1 (16 oz.)  can whole cranberry sauce
1 (20 oz.)  can crushed pineapple, drained
1/2  c. chopped walnuts
2  c. miniature marshmallows
1  (8 oz.) Cool Whip

      Drain pineapple real good.  Chop walnuts.  Put cranberries in bowl; add pineapple, apples, bananas, marshmallows and nuts.  Mix together with Cool Whip.
