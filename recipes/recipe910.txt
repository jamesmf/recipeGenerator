SEAFOOD APPETIZER CHEESECAKE


1  c. crushed buttery crackers
3  Tbsp. margarine, melted
2 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese, softened
3  eggs
3/4  c. dairy sour cream, divided
1 (7 3/4 oz.)  can salmon, drained and flaked
1  tsp. lemon juice
1/2  tsp. onion powder
1/8  tsp. pepper

      Combine crumbs and margarine; press onto bottom of 9-inch spring-form pan.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 10 minutes.
      Combine softened cream cheese, eggs and 1/4 cup sour cream, mixing on electric mixer until well blended.  Add remaining ingredients; mix well.  Pour mixture over crust.  Bake at 325 degrees; for 45 minutes.  Loosen cake from rim of pan.  Cool; remove rim. Spread with remaining sour cream.
