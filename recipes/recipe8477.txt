BUSY WOMAN'S DREAM CASSEROLE


1 1/2  lb. ground beef
1  medium onion, diced
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  pkg. -16 oz. Tater Tots

      Crumble raw ground beef in 9 x 9 inch baking dish. Cover with diced onion. Spread soup evenly over the mixture. place tater tots on top. Bake, uncovered at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes, until bubbly and tater tots are brown.
