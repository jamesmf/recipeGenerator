CRAB APPETIZER


1/2  lb. processed cheese spread
3/4  c. butter
1  lb. crab meat, shredded
1 (4 oz.)  can chopped mushrooms
2  pkg. English muffins, toasted lightly

      Melt cheese and butter. Add crab meat and mushrooms. Spoon on quartered muffins. Place under broiler until slightly browned.
