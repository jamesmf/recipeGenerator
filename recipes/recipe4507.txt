CORNED BEEF SALAD


2  c. curly macaroni
3  c. chicken or 1 can tuna or corned beef
4  or 5 medium shredded carrots
3  c. celery
4  boiled eggs
1 small  can peas, drained
1/2 small  can chopped pimento
1  medium onion
1  c. green grapes, cut in half
  Miracle Whip, with about 1/2 c. sugar

      Mix together.
