MEATLESS ONE POT MEAL
(Rice, Beans and Tomatoes)  

2 1/4  c. water
1  c. uncooked rice
  salt to taste
1 (14 1/2 oz.)  can Mexican stewed tomatoes
1 (15 1/2 oz.)  can pinto beans or red beans

      Heat the water to boiling. Add the rice. Salt to taste. Cook on low for 20 minutes. Add a little more water if needed. Then add the tomatoes and beans. Heat through and serve.
      Variation: Use black beans.
