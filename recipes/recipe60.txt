CHOCOLATE CHIP BREAD


1  c. Crisco
2  c. sugar
4  eggs
1  tsp. banana flavoring
1/2  tsp. cherry flavoring
2  c. mashed bananas
4  c. flour
2  tsp. soda
  pinch of salt
1/2  c. chopped maraschino cherries
1/2  c. chocolate chips
1/2  c. chopped walnuts

      Cream Crisco and sugar; add eggs and flavorings.  Beat well. Add mashed bananas.  Gradually add flour and dry ingredients. Remove beaters and stir in cherries, chocolate chips and nuts. Pour in 2 greased loaf pans.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 1/2 hours.
