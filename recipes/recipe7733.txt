EL WIFEO'S LASAGNA MEXICANO


1  lb. ground beef
2  large tomatoes, chopped
2  medium onions, chopped
12  corn tortillas
1 (1 3/4 oz.)  pkg. taco seasoning mix
1  lb. Mozzarella cheese
1 (8 oz.)  jar mild taco sauce

      Brown beef in frying pan and prepare per package instructions on taco seasoning mix. Line bottom of an oiled 9 x 13 casserole dish with 6 overlapping corn tortillas. Spread 1/2 taco mix over tortillas, 1/2 of onions and tomatoes and 1/2 of cheese. Cover with remaining tortillas and repeat process for second layer. Spread jar of taco sauce. Cover with foil. Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes. Remove foil. Bake 5 minutes.
