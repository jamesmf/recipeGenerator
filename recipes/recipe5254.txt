ERNIE'S TORTELLINI SOUP


8  c. water
1  bouillon cube
1  can chicken broth
1  can diced tomatoes
1 (7 oz.)  box tortellini filled with Parmesan
1  box frozen chopped spinach
1  pkg. dry ranch dressing mix

	Mix together in large pan 8 c. water, bouillon cube and chicken broth.  Add tomatoes with their juice.  Bring to a boil. Cook box of tortellini for about 15 minutes.  Meanwhile, micro-cook spinach until done.  Add spinach and ranch dressing mix to pot.  Simmer about 5 minutes.  Makes 12 cups.
