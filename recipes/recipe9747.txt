CHICKEN ENCHILADAS

Sauce:
1/4  c. butter
1/4  c. flour
1  c. onion
2 1/2  c. water
1  Tbsp. chicken bouillon
8  oz. sour cream

 
Filling:
4  oz. chopped green chilies
1  tsp. cumin
3  c. cooked chicken, shredded
2  c. Cheddar cheese
1/2  tsp. garlic salt

      Melt butter and mix with flour and onion. Add hot water and chicken bouillon; heat until thick and then add sour cream.
       Take 1 cup sauce, 1 cup cheese, all the chicken, chilies and spices and mix together. Take sauce and spread on both sides of a large flour tortilla. Add chicken mixture and roll. Place in a 9 x 13-inch pan. Should fill the pan with enchiladas. Pour remaining sauce over top of tortillas. Sprinkle cheese on top. Bake at 350 degrees; for 25 minutes or until bubbly.
