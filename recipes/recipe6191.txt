QUICK AND EASY SALAD


12  oz. Cool Whip
1/2  pt. cottage cheese
1  can mandarin oranges, drained
1  can (small) pineapple tidbits, drained
1 (3 oz.)  box orange Jell-O

	After frying a pan of country ham, add a little water to the drippings. Bring to a boil and serve with ham. You may add about 1 teaspoon of strong coffee for color.
