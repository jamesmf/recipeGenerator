DUMP SALAD


1 16-oz.  carton dry small-curd cottage cheese
1 16-oz.  carton Cool Whip
1 16-oz.  can fruit cocktail, drained
1  c. miniature marshmallows
1  box Jello (dry), orange, cherry, or strawberry flavor

      Mix in bowl in order given.  Let ripen in refrigerator for several hours or overnight.  Can be served on a lettuce leaf.
