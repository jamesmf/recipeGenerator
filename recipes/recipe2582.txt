PARMESAN POTATOES


1  stick butter
1/2  c. flour
1/2  c. Parmesan cheese
5  medium potatoes, cut in 1 1/2-inch cubes

      Melt butter in 9 x 13-inch pan.  Mix flour and Parmesan cheese in  plastic bag.  Shake a few potato cubes in bag at a time  and put  in  buttered  pan.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 1/2 hour. With  spatula turn  over and bake another 1/2 hour.  Leftover cheese may be put in freezer for next time.
