MEXICAN WEDDING RINGS


1/2  lb. butter
  dash of salt
1/2  c. powdered sugar
1/2  tsp. vanilla
2  c. flour
1  c. chopped pecans

      Cream butter, salt and sugar.  Add other ingredients and roll into 1-inch balls.  Bake at 325 degrees; to 350 degrees; for about 20 minutes. They will not be brown.  While hot, roll in powdered sugar.  If preferred, when cooled, roll in powdered sugar again.
