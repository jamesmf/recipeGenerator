LAYERED SALAD


1  head of lettuce, cut up
1  head of cauliflower, chopped
1  medium onion, diced
3  - 4 carrots, shredded
1  green pepper, optional
1 -2  c. shredded mild cheddar cheese
3/4 - 1  c. sugar
1  pt. Miracle Whip

       Then let stand for 45 minutes or until no grains of sugar are seen.  Put mixture on top of the cheddar cheese.  Then fry 1 lb. bacon and crumble on top of mixture.
      As long as you use the lettuce and cauliflower you can use any other vegetables that you want.
