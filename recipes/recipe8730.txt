MARZETTI


1  lb. gd. meat
2  tbsp. oleo
1/4th  green pepper, chopped
1  can tomato soup
1  can tomato paste
6  tbsp. water
1  tbsp. worcestershire sauce
1/4  c. sharp cheese
4  oz. noodles, cooked

      Brown meat & pepper in oleo.  Add remaining ingredients except cheese.  Cook for 1/2 hour.  Pour into 9x13 pan. Add noodles.   Mix with sauce.  Cover with cheese. Bake 375 degrees; for 45 mins.
