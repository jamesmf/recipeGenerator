WILD RICE SHRIMP BAKE


1 (6 oz.)  pkg. long grain wild rice
1  lb. uncooked, medium sized shrimp, peeled and deveined
1  medium green pepper, chopped
1  medium onion, chopped
1 (4 oz.)  can mushroom stems and pieces, drained
1  can cream of chicken soup (undiluted)
1/2  c. seasoned stuffing mix

      Prepare rice as directed on package. In a large skillet, saut√© mushrooms, green peppers, onions and shrimp. Shrimp will turn pink. Add soup to the rice and stir. Add shrimp mixture to rice and soup. Stir and transfer to a well-greased 2-quart baking dish. Sprinkle with stuffing mix. Bake at 350 degrees; uncovered for 20 to 25 minutes or until heated through. Serves 6.
