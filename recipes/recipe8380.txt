SWEET AND SOUR RIBS


4  lb. pork spareribs, cut in portions
1/3  c. brown sugar
3  Tbsp. cornstarch
1/2  tsp. dry mustard
1/8  tsp. ginger
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. chili powder
1  clove garlic
1/3  c. vinegar
1 1/2  c. tomato juice
1  c. water
2  tsp. soy sauce
1/2  tsp. Worcestershire sauce

      Wipe ribs with a damp paper towel.  Bake on rack in shallow pan at 450 degrees; for 35 minutes.  Combine remaining ingredients in pot and cook until slightly thickened.  Remove rack from roasting pan; drain grease.  Put ribs in bottom of pan; pour sauce over ribs. Cover tightly with foil.  Bake 1 1/2 hours at 350 degrees;.
