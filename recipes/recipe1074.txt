WHEAT AND CHEESE SNACKS


1  c. Kellogg's Most cereal
1/3  c. flour
1/4  c. margarine
1  c. Cheddar cheese, shredded

      Crush cereal to 1/2 cup.  In a small bowl, beat margarine and cheese until light and fluffy.  Stir in cereal and flour; drop by rounded teaspoon onto ungreased cookie sheet.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 10 to 12 minutes.
      Exchanges:  2 snacks equal 1/2 bread and 1 1/2 fat.
