BREATH OF SPRING SALAD


20  oz. (1 large can) pineapple, crushed
6  oz. (1 large pkg.) jello, peach
2  c. buttermilk
12  oz. (1 tub) whipped topping
1/2  c. pecans, chopped

      Pour pineapple, with juice, into a saucepan.  Bring to a boil.  Pour in jello mix, stirring until dissolved.  Let cool.
      Add buttermilk and stir to blend.  Fold in whipped topping and pecans.  Pour into a 9x13-inch pan.  Chill for several hours, until firm.  Sprinkle with additional pecans, if desired.
