GOURMET BAKED ACORN SQUASH


3  acorn squash, halved and seeded
2/3  c. diced celery
1 1/2  c. diced, unpeeled apples
1/4  c. oleo
1 1/2  c. soft bread crumbs
1  c. grated cheese
1/2  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper

      Place squash halves, cut side down, in baking pan in small amount of water.  Bake at 400 degrees; for 20 to 30 minutes or until almost tender.  Saut√© celery and apples in oleo in skillet for 5 minutes; stir in crumbs, cheese, salt and pepper.  Turn squash halves; fill with apple mixture.  Bake 10 to 15 minutes longer or until squash halves are tender.
