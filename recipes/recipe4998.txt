LIME GELATIN SALAD


2  small pkgs. lime Jello
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
1  c. chopped walnuts
1 small  can crushed pineapple

      Make Jello and let set to soft gel stage.  Mix in softened cream cheese.  Let get to soft gel stage again.  Mix in drained pineapple and walnuts.  Pour into a rectangular dish.  Let set up in refrigerator.  Cut into squares to serve.  (May serve on lettuce.)
