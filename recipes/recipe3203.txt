PAUL'S GUACAMOLE SALAD


  3-5 semi-soft avocados
1 small  can Herdez Salsa Casera
  Roasted, peeled, seeded, chopped green chiles (optional)
1  pkg. spicy guacamole mix (produce section at grocer)
  Shredded or chopped cilantro leaves to taste

      Peel, remove seed, and mash avocados.  Mash everything together.  Chill for at least one hour to re-hydate the guacamole mix ingredients.
