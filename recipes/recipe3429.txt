PEANUT BUTTER POPCORN BALLS


2  c. sugar
2  c. Blackburn syrup
2  c. peanut butter
1  tsp. vanilla
20  c. popped corn (3 c. unpopped)

      Cook  sugar  and  syrup  to  hard-ball  stage  on candy thermometer.  Add peanut butter and vanilla.  Pour over popped corn and shape into balls.  Yields approximately 40 balls.
