COTTAGE CHEESE SALAD


3  c. cottage cheese
12  oz. Cool Whip, thawed
2 (3 oz.)  pkg. orange jello
13 1/2  oz. (1 pt.) pineapple tidbits, drained
11  oz. mandarin orange sections, drained

      In  mixing  bowl,  blend  cottage cheese and Cool Whip. Beat until blended.  Stir in dry jello.  Fold in pineapple and oranges. Keep refrigerated.
