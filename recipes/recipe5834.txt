TARTAR SAUCE


1  pt. mayonnaise
1  c. sweet or dill pickle relish, drained
2  Tbsp. lemon juice
1  medium onion, chopped fine
1/4  tsp. garlic powder
  salt and pepper to taste

      Mix all ingredients together and serve. It will keep a couple of weeks in the refrigerator, if desired, in an airtight container.
