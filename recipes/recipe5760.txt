WALDORF SALAD


3  c. chopped apples
2  c. miniature marshmallows
1  c. thin celery slices
1/2  c. chopped walnuts

      Whip Miracle Whip salad dressing and Cool Whip to make dressing. Combine apples, marshmallows, celery and walnuts. Add enough salad dressing to moisten. Chill. Add apple slices before serving. Makes 6 to 8 servings.
