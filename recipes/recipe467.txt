PITA BREAD


1  pkg. yeast
1 1/2  c. lukewarm water (110~)
1  Tbsp. honey
1  tsp. salt
3 1/2  c. flour

      Dissolve yeast in water and stir in honey and salt.  Mix all ingredients together.  Knead dough for several minutes and then cut into 8 pieces.  Knead each piece several minutes.  Roll or shape into 5-inch round pieces about 1/2-inch thick.  Let rise 2 hours. Bake at 500 degrees; for 8 minutes.  Makes 8 pita breads.  Share with your friends!
