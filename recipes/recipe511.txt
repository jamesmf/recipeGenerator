CAKE MIX COOKIES


1  pkg. cake mix
1  c. packed brown sugar
2  Tbsp. soft butter
2  Tbsp. honey
2  eggs
1  c. chopped nuts

      Combine cake mix, brown sugar, butter, honey and eggs.  Stir in nuts.  Drop by spoonfuls onto cookie sheet.  Bake at 375 degrees; for 10 to 12 minutes.  Yields 5 dozen.
