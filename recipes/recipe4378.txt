CREAMY GARLIC DRESSING


1  c. mayonnaise
1  c. salad oil
1/2  c. half & half
2  cloves garlic, crushed or minced fine
1/2  tsp. oregano
1  dash worcestershire sauce
  salt and white pepper, to taste

      Put mayonnaise in mixing bowl.  Add salad oil, a few drops at a time, beating constantly.  Blend in well the half & half, garlic, oregano, worcestershire sauce.  Add salt and pepper to taste.
