WILD RICE AND CHICKEN CASSEROLE


2  boxes Uncle Ben's original wild rice (5 minute rice)
2  Tbsp. butter
2  Tbsp. flour
2  c. milk
1  c. grated Cheddar cheese
4  tsp. lemon juice
2 to 3  c. diced cooked chicken

      Prepare rice as directed on boxes. Spoon into lightly greased 1 1/2-quart casserole.
      Meanwhile, melt butter in saucepan.  Blend in flour.  Gradually add milk and stir over low heat until mixture is thickened and smooth.  Add cheese and lemon juice, stirring until cheese is melted.  Add chicken and sauce to rice in casserole.  Mix well. Bake at 350 degrees; about 20 minutes until hot and bubbling.  Enjoy!
