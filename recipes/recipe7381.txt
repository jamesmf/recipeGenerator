COUNTRY STYLE PORK CHOPS


6  pork chops
1  can cream of mushroom or celery soup
1  large onion, chopped
1  can water
  salt, pepper and garlic powder

      Sprinkle chops with seasonings and brown in skillet.  Place in casserole dish.  Mix soup, water and onions.  Pour over chops; cover and bake at 350 degrees; until very tender.  To brown chops a bit, uncover the last 15 to 20 minutes.
