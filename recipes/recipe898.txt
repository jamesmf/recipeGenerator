HOLIDAY DIP


3 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
3 (6 1/2 oz.)  cans crabmeat, drained
2  Tbsp. prepared mustard
1  Tbsp. powdered sugar
1/2  c. mayonnaise
1/2  c. white wine
1  Tbsp. onion juice
1  Tbsp. lemon juice

      Melt cream cheese in double boiler or soften in microwave. Add all other ingredients.  Heat until bubbly.  Serve from chafing dish with crackers or vegetables.
