CABBAGE PATCH STEW
(Makes 8 Servings)  

1  lb. ground beef
1  bell pepper, chopped
1  large onion, chopped
2  stalks celery, chopped
1  can tomatoes (15 oz.)
1  can Ranch Style beans (15 oz.)
1  can whole kernel corn
1  c. water
1  jalapeno, chopped
1  tsp. chili powder
1  small cabbage, cut up
  salt to taste
  pepper to taste
  garlic powder to taste

      Saut√© meat, bell pepper, onion and celery until brown. Add other ingredients. Season with salt, pepper and garlic powder. Simmer until cabbage is done.
