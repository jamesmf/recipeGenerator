BRUNSWICK STEW


1  lb. lean ground beef
2 (5 oz.)  cans Swanson white chicken chunks
1 (15 oz.)  can Castlebury barbecue pork
1 large  can cream-style corn
1 large  can green lima beans, drained
1 (14 oz.)  bottle hot ketchup
1 large  can potatoes, drained and diced
1  large onion, chopped
2  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1 large  can Shoepeg corn, drained

	Cook ground beef until brown, then drain. Put all ingredients in pot. Cook until all ingredients are done. Great for crock-pot.
