LAYERED SPINACH SALAD

Salad:
2  bags (6 oz. each) baby spinach leaves
1  small red bell pepper, diced (1 c.)
6  oz. fresh mushrooms, sliced (2 1/2 c.)
1  small yellow bell pepper, diced (1 c.)
1  pt. cherry tomatoes, cut in half (2 c.)
1  c. frozen petite peas, thawed
4  large hard-cooked eggs, coarsely chopped
4  strips bacon, cooked crisp, crumbled

      Have ready a 4 -5 qt. serving bowl, preferably clear glass or plastic (so the layers are visible); best to use a trifle bowl. Stir Dressing ingredients in a medium bowl until blended.  Layer Salad ingredients in bowl in order given, placing tomatoes cut sides up.  Spread dressing over the top. To serve: Sprinkle with bacon.  Spoon through the layers or toss salad to mix.
      Planning Tip:  Assemble the salad and refrigerate at least 2 hours or up to 1 day before serving.  Sprinkle with bacon and toss salad right before serving.
Dressing:
1  c. light mayonnaise
1/2  c. reduced-fat sour cream
2  Tbsp. each minced red onion and water
2  tsp. each cider vinegar, horseradish and sugar
1/4  tsp. each salt and pepper
