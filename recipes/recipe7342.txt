MOM'S MEAT LOAF


2  cans cream of mushroom soup
1  pouch dry onion soup mix
2  lb. ground turkey burger
1/2  c. dry bread crumbs
1  egg, beaten

      Mix 1/2 can mushroom soup with the other ingredients.  In a 2-quart oblong baking dish, firmly shape in a large loaf.  Bake in 350 degrees; oven 1 hour.  In medium bowl, combine remaining soup and 1/2 cup water.  Heat over low heat, stirring occasionally.  Pour over meat loaf and cook 30 minutes more.
