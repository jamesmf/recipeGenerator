TUNA AND POTATO CHIP CASSEROLE


1  can tuna, drained
1  can cream of mushroom or chicken soup
1/2  c. milk
1/2  c. celery, diced
1/4  c. onion, diced
1  Tbsp. margarine
6 1/2  oz. bag of potato chips

      Combine tuna, soup, and milk. Saut√© celery and onion in margarine and add to mixture. Crush enough potato chips to thicken mixture. Pour into a casserole dish and add a layer of crushed chips on top. Bake at 350 degrees; for 25 minutes.
