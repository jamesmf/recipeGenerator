APPLE CRUNCH


Topping:
2/3  c. brown Sugar Twin
1/4  lb. diet oleo
2/3  c. dry milk
4  tsp. cinnamon
1/2  tsp. nutmeg
3  c. oats

 
Filling:
8  apples, sliced
2  tsp. cinnamon
4  tsp. lemon juice
2  c. water
3  Tbsp. sweetener

      Mix topping well to make crumbs.  Cook filling until tender. Spread 1/2 crumb topping in bottom of 9 x 13-inch pan (ungreased). Add filling, then sprinkle remaining topping on top.  Bake 30 minutes at 350 degrees;.  Makes 8 servings.
      One-eighth of cake equals 1 bread exchange, 1 fruit exchange, 1 fat exchange and 1/4 milk exchange.
