VEGETABLE DIPPING SAUCE


1/2  c. reduced-fat peanut butter
1/2  c. low-fat plain yogurt
3  Tbsp. hot water
3  Tbsp. light soy sauce
1  Tbsp. dark sesame oil
  raw vegetables (your choice)

      Whisk all ingredients together. Use as dip for your favorite vegetables.
      Serving Size: 1/4 cup. Cal-197, CHO-13, Pro-9, Fat-13g.
