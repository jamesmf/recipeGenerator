CHOCOLATE HAYSTACKS


2  c. granulated sugar
1/3  c. cocoa
1/2  c. milk
1/2  c. butter or margarine
3 1/2  c. quick cooking rolled oats
1  c. flaked coconut
1/2  tsp. vanilla
  dash of salt

      In saucepan bring sugar, cocoa, milk and butter to full boil. Remove from heat; stir in remaining ingredients.  Drop quickly from teaspoon on waxed paper; cool.  Yields 48.
