SWEET PICKLE STICKS


3 3/4  c. white vinegar
3  tbsp. canning salt
3  c. sugar
4 1/2  tsp. celery seed
4 1/2  tsp. turmeric
3/4  tsp. mustard seed
  Pickling cucumbers
6   pint jars

      Wash cucumbers and cut into sticks.  Cover with boiling water and let stand for 4-5 hours.  Drain and pack into sterilized jars. Mix remaining ingredients in saucepan and boil for 5 minutes. Pour over cucumbers filling to 1/2 inch of the jar rim. Put caps on and screw band tightly.  Process for 5 mins.
      Note: Cucumbers can also be cut into chips instead of sticks. Delicious!
