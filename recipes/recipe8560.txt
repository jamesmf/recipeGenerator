GENTLEMEN PREFER CHICKEN


3  lb. Chicken, cut up
  Salt and Pepper to taste
1/4  c. Oil
1  can Lemonade Concentrate, thawed
3/4  c. crushed Pineapple, drained
3/4  c. chunk Pineapple, drained
2  Green Bell Pepper, cut in strips
1/4  c. Brown Sugar
1/4  c. A-1 Steak Sauce
1/2  c. Slivered Almonds

      Brown chicken in skillet with oil, salt and pepper with a little butter.  When brown place in baking dish.  Put remaining ingredients over chicken in the order given.  Bake uncovered at 400 degrees for 45 minutes.
