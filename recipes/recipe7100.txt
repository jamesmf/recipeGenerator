HOT SAUSAGES AND PEPPERS


1 -2  lb. hot Italian sausages
2  large green peppers, sliced
1  small onion, sliced
1 large  can tomato paste
1  pkg. egg noodles
2  Tbsp. butter

      Cut hot Italian sausage into bite-size pieces and brown in skillet.  Drain fat and add in onions, green peppers, and tomato paste and 3 cans of water.  Simmer 1 hour.  You may need to add more water.  Cook egg noodles, drain and add butter while noodles are hot so that the butter melts.  Serve sausages over noodles.
