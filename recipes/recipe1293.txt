SPLIT PEA SOUP


12  c. water
1  c. brown rice
2  tsp. basil
2  c. split peas
2  tsp. sea salt
2  c. chopped onions or 2 Tbsp. onion powder
2  c. diced carrots (optional)
2  c. dried celery or 2 tsp. celery seed

      Add rice and peas to boiling water.  Lightly boil 1 1/2 hours. Add remaining ingredients and continue to cook about 30 minutes.
