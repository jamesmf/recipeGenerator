MR. B'S CHILI


1 1/2 to 2  lb. round steak, chuck roast, etc., cut into 1/2-inch cubes
1  large onion, sliced and finely diced
1  or 2 fresh jalapenos, sliced
2  Tbsp. garlic, chopped
1  Tbsp. canola oil
2  Tbsp. tomato paste
1 large  can tomatoes
1  can beer
4  Tbsp. hot chili powder
2  Tbsp. cumin
1  Tbsp. garlic powder
2  tsp. Tabasco
2  tsp. cayenne pepper
1  tsp. red pepper flakes

      Brown the meat in the oil (in batches) along with the other first 3 ingredients. Mix the remaining (last 7 ingredients on list) together in bowl and allow to stand 2 to 3 minutes.
      After browning first ingredients, add can of tomatoes and beer/spice mixture in large pot. Let cook 5 to 8 minutes until bubbling over medium to medium-high temperature setting. Add 2 tablespoons tomato paste. Let cook 2 to 3 hours. Serve with cheese quesadillas or garlic bread.
