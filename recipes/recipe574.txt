BASIC CHEESE SAUCE


2  c. (1 lb.) low-fat cottage cheese
2  Tbsp. lemon juice
1/4  c. skim milk

      Place all ingredients in blender; blend until creamy.  (You can adjust consistency by adjusting milk used.)  Yields 2 cups. Approximate calories:  1 cup equals 190 calories; 1 tablespoon equals 10 calories.  Will keep up to 2 weeks.
      Add onion, parsley or garlic for other flavors.
