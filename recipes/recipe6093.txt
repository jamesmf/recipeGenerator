BROCCOLI-ORANGE SALAD


4  c. broccoli flowerets (fresh)
1  small purple onion
1  c. raisins
1 1/2  c. pecan pieces, toasted
3/4  c. mayonnaise
1/4  c. sugar
1 1/2   Tbsp. white vinegar
11  oz. (1 can) Mandarin oranges, drained

      Break broccoli flowerets into bite-size pieces (about 1 1/2-inches).  Thinly slice onion and separate into rings.
      Combine broccoli, onion, raisins and pecans together and set aside.  Combine mayonnaise, sugar and vinegar then add to broccoli mixture, stirring to coat.  Gently stir in oranges.
      Cover and refrigerate at least 3 hours.
