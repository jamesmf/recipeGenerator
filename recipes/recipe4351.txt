DUMP SALAD


1 (20 oz.)  can crushed pineapple, drained
1 (16 oz.)  carton cottage cheese
1 (3 oz.)  pkg. lemon Jello, dry
1  (8 oz.) container of Cool Whip

      Dump all ingredients together and mix well.  It is ready to serve.  The salad will keep well in the refrigerator for about 1 week.
      NOTE:  If you intend to use the salad right away you do not have to drain the pineapple.
