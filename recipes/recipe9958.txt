SCALLOPED TOMATOES


  stewed tomatoes (1 to 2 per person), cut in pieces or canned tomatoes may be used
  salt and pepper
1  onion or juice

      A bit of brown sugar helps the taste.  Oregano adds a pleasant taste.
      Cook tomatoes slowly 20 minutes.  For 4 cups of tomatoes, prepare 1 cup buttered bread crumbs or croutons.  Put a layer of crumbs in a buttered casserole and cover with tomatoes.  Repeat. Sprinkle the top with a thick layer of crumbs.  Bake at 400 degrees; until crumbs are brown.  Serves 6.
