EGG ROLLS FROM THAILAND


  ground beef
  raw chicken
  shrimp (optional)
  carrot
  bamboo shoots
  mushrooms
  salt
  pepper
  sugar
  pastry squares
1  egg, beaten

      Chop all (except pastry squares and egg) ingredients and stir fry. Drain off any grease. Fill center of pastry squares; fold up bottom and sides then roll up; seal with a beaten egg. Fry in hot corn oil.
