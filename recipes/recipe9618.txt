GRANDMAMA JONES MEAT LOAF


1  lb. ground beef
1/2  lb. ground pork
1  cup bread or cracker crumbs
1  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. celery salt
1  medium onion, chopped
1  medium bell pepper, chopped
1/2  tsp. pepper
1/4  tsp. paprika
1  pt. tomato juice
1  egg
1  can Franco American Beef Gravy

      Beat egg and add 1 cup tomato juice. Add to other ingredients except for  gravy. Mix well and bake 1 hr. at 350 degrees. Add can of gravy to remaining tomato juice and pour over loaf and cook for 30 minutes.
