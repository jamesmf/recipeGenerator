BARBECUE BUTTER BEANS


2 (15 oz.)  cans butter beans, rinsed and drained
3/4  c. brown sugar, packed
1/2  c. ketchup
1/2  c. onion, chopped
3  strips bacon, diced and uncooked

      In a bowl, combine beans, brown sugar, ketchup and onion. Transfer to a greased 1 1/2 quart baking dish.  Sprinkle with bacon.  Bake uncovered at 350 degrees; for 1 1/2 hours.
