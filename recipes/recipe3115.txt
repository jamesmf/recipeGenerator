FROSTED GRAPES


1  (8 oz.) cream cheese
2  Tbsp. margarine
1 1/2  c. powdered sugar
3/4  tsp. vanilla
1 1/2  lb. red or green grapes
1  c. walnuts, chopped

      Beat cream cheese, margarine, powdered sugar, and vanilla until creamy. Add grapes and walnuts; stir until well coated. Chill and enjoy.
