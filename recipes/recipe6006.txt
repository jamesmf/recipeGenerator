MEXICAN TACO SOUP


2  lb. hamburger, browned
2  onions, chopped or sliced
2  cans pinto beans, undrained
2  cans black beans, undrained
2  cans kidney beans, undrained
2  cans shoe peg corn, undrained
2  cans chicken broth
2  cans diced tomatoes
2  pkg. ranch dressing mix
2  pkg. taco seasoning mix

      Brown hamburger; drain off most of grease.  Add onion, and cook a few minutes more.  Transfer hamburger and onion to stock pot or large kettle; add all the other ingredients.  Cook slowly until the flavor strikes through and all ingredients are hot. Serve with crackers or tortilla chips.
