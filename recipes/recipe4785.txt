CHERRY DESSERT SALAD


1  can cherry pie filling
1 small  can crushed pineapple, drained
1  c. nuts, chopped
1  can sweetened condensed milk
1  c. small marshmallows
  whipped topping
1  c. coconut (optional)

      Mix all ingredients together with the exception of the whipped topping. Top the salad with the topping. Refrigerate overnight.
