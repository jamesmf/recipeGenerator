UNSTUFFED PEPPER SOUP
(Ready In One Hour or Less)  

1  1/2 pounds ground beef
3  large green peppers, chopped
1  large onion, chopped
2  cans (14  1/2 ounces each) beef broth
2  cans (10 3/4 ounces each) condensed tomato soup, undiluted
1  can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes, undrained
1  can (4 ounces) mushroom stems and pieces, drained
1  1/2 cups, cooked rice

      In a Dutch oven or large saucepan, cool the beef, green peppers and onion over medium heat until meat is no longer pink; drain.  Stir in the broth, soup, tomatoes and mushrooms.  Bring o a boil.  Reduce heat; cover and simmer for at least 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Add rice; heat through.  Yield: 10 servings.
