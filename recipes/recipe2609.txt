SOURDOUGH BREAD

Starter:
1 3/4  c. unsifted flour
1  Tbsp. each sugar and salt
1  pkg. yeast
2 1/2  c. warm water

 
Dough:
5 to 6  c. unsifted flour
3  Tbsp. sugar and 1 tsp. salt
1  pkg. yeast
1  c. milk
2  Tbsp. oleo

      To make Starter, combine flour, sugar, salt and dry yeast.  Add warm water to dry ingredients; beat 2 minutes.  Cover; let stand at room temperature 4 days.  Stir down daily.  To make dough, combine 1 cup flour, sugar, salt and dry yeast.  Combine milk and oleo in pan; heat over low heat until warm.  Add to dry ingredients.  Beat 2 minutes.  Add 1 1/2 cups Starter and 1 cup flour or enough to make thick batter.  Beat at high 2 minutes.  Stir in additional flour to make soft dough.  Turn out on floured board; knead until smooth.  Place in greased bowl, cover and let rise about 1 hour until double.  Punch down; let rest 15 minutes.  Shape; let rise about 1 hour.  Bake at 400 degrees; for 30 minutes.  To reuse Starter:  Add 1 1/2 cups lukewarm water, 3/4 cup flour, and 1 1/2 teaspoons of sugar to unused Starter.  Beat 1 minute.  Cover; let stand.  Stir down daily.
