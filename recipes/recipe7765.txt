DIJON CHICKEN


6  whole chicken breasts, halved
  pepper
1 1/2  c. sour cream
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. Italian breadcrumbs
  parsley
  salt
  garlic powder
1 1/2  c. Dijon mustard

      Lightly sprinkle chicken with salt, pepper and garlic powder. Combine sour cream and mustard in a shallow dish, mixing well.  Dip each chicken breast into mustard mixture and dredge in breadcrumbs. Arrange chicken in single layer in baking pans.  Bake at 375 degrees; for 50 minutes.
