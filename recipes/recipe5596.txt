JOANN'S POTATO SOUP


  8-12 med. Potatoes, diced small
3  stalks Celery, diced small
3  Carrots, diced small
1 sm.  pkg. Mushrooms, sliced thin
1  lg. Onion, diced
1/2  stick Butter
1  can Cream of Celery Soup
1  can Cream of Mushroom Soup
1  pkg. (1 lb.) Velveeta Cheese
  Salt & Pepper
  Parsley (optional) or fresh chives
  Milk

      Boil potatoes, celery, carrots, mushrooms and onion until tender.  Drain, reserving some of the water.  Return vegetables to stove and add butter, soups and velveeta cheese.  Add milk and reserved water until you get thickness you like.  Add salt and pepper to taste.  Add parsley and chives if desired.
