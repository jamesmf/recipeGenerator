PUMPKIN PIE


1  unbaked 9-inch pie shell
1  c. sugar
1  Tbsp. flour
1/2  tsp. salt
1  tsp. ginger
3/4  tsp. cinnamon
1/8  tsp. nutmeg
2  c. canned pumpkin
1 1/2  Tbsp. light molasses
1 1/2  Tbsp. melted butter
3  eggs
1 3/4  c. prepared eggnog
2  Tbsp. brandy

      Prepare your favorite pastry shell; refrigerate.  Mix sugar, flour, salt, ginger, cinnamon and nutmeg in a large mixing bowl. Add the pumpkin, molasses and melted butter and blend well. Slightly beat the eggs; add eggnog to the eggs.  Pour into pumpkin mixture and mix well.  Stir in brandy.  Pour into the pastry shell. Bake in 425 degrees; oven for 40 minutes or until silver knife inserted comes out clean.
