GUACAMOLE NO. 2


1  ripe avocado, peeled and mashed with fork
1  Tbsp. fresh lemon juice
1  tsp. onion powder
1/4  tsp. garlic powder
2  tsp. salt or Braggs Amino Acid
1/4  c. water
1  tomato, chopped
1/4  c. chopped green onions

      Place first six ingredients in blender and blend until smooth. Fold in chopped tomato and green onions.  Makes approximately 2 cups.
