PATTY'S ORIENTAL SALAD


  cooked chopped chicken breast
8  oz. pkg. chopped cabbage
2 1/2  oz. pkg. slivered almonds
1  Tbsp. sesame seeds
6  green onions
1  pkg. Ramen noodles (chicken)
  salt and pepper

 
Dressing:
1/2  c. oil
2 1/2  Tbsp. rice vinegar
2  Tbsp. sugar
  season pkg. from Ramen noodles

      Lightly brown almonds, sesame seeds and Ramen noodles. Mix with slaw, onions, chicken and seasonings. Pour dressing mixture over salad and toss. It is better refrigerated for a couple of hours.
