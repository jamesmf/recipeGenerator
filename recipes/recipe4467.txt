TACO SOUP


2  lb. ground beef
1  medium onion, chopped
1  can chopped green chilies (undrained)
1  can kidney beans
1  can pinto beans
1  can white hominy
3  cans stewed tomatoes
1  pkg. taco seasoning mix
1  pkg. Ranch-style dressing mix
1 1/2  c. water
  salt and pepper to taste

      Brown meat and onions. Add all other ingredients and bring to a boil for 4 to 5 minutes. Reduce heat and simmer 30 minutes. Serve with Cheddar cheese, chopped green onions and crushed tortilla chips on top. Freezes well.
