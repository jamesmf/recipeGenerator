ORANGE SALAD


1  container cottage cheese
1  box orange Jell-O
1  large fruit cocktail, drained
1  large pineapple tidbits, drained
1  large Cool Whip

      Mix cottage cheese and Jell-O. Add well-drained fruits.  Fold in Cool Whip.  Chill for 1 hour and salad is ready to eat.
       You may substitute raspberry Jell-O instead of orange.
