CABBAGE CASSEROLE


1 1/4  lb. hamburger, browned and drained
1  small head cabbage, cut up
1  medium onion, sliced thin
5  medium potatoes, sliced thin
2  cans cream of chicken soup
1 1/2  tsp. Lawry's seasoned salt
1/2  tsp. pepper

      Put first 4 ingredients in casserole in order listed.  Top with seasoned salt and pepper.  Pour soup over all and bake 1 1/2 hours at 350 degrees;.
