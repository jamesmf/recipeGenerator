CRAN-APPLE RELISH


1  lb. fresh cranberries
2  medium-sized, unpeeled apples
2  tsp. grated orange rind
3  Tbsp. lemon juice
1 1/2  c. sugar
1/2  tsp. ground cinnamon
1/2  tsp. ground ginger
1/4  tsp. ground cloves

      Wash cranberries and apples well.  Cut apples into quarters and remove cores.  Put cranberries and apples through a blender or food processor using medium blade.  Stir in remaining ingredients; cover and refrigerate several hours or overnight to blend flavors. Makes 3 1/2 cups.
