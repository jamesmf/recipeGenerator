CHICKEN AND DUMPLINGS


1  fryer (2 1/2 or 3 lb.)
  self rising flour
4  eggs
  salt to taste

      Brown cut up fryer in hot fat. Add enough cold water to cover chicken (about 2 qt.). Cook until chicken is tender; add just enough salt to taste. Beat eggs; add enough flour for a thick dough. Roll real thin on floured paper; cut small pieces and add to chicken broth.
