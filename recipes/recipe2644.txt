CREME DE MENTHE BARS


1  c. sugar
1/2  c. butter
4  beaten eggs
1  c. flour
1  tsp. vanilla
16  oz. can Hershey's chocolate syrup
2  c. confectioners sugar
1/2  c. butter
2  Tbsp. creme de menthe

 
Topping:
1  c. semi-sweet chocolate morsels
6  tsp. butter

      Combine first 6 ingredients.  Bake in 9 x 13-inch pan for 30 minutes at 350 degrees;.  Beat together confectioners sugar, butter and creme de menthe liqueur.  Spread on cool cake.
      Melt semi-sweet chocolate morsels and butter.  Allow to cool slightly.  Spread on top of creme de menthe mixture.
