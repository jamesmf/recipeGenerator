CHOCOLATE PUFFS


1/2  c. boiling water
1/4  c. shortening
1  c. sugar
1/4  c. cocoa
1 1/2  c. flour
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. baking powder
1/2  tsp. soda
1  well beaten egg
1/2  c. sour cream
1/2  tsp. vanilla

      Add water to shortening, sugar and cocoa; beat until sugar dissolves.  Add sifted dry ingredients alternately with combined egg, sour cream and vanilla.  Fill greased cupcake pans half full; bake in moderate oven at 350 degrees; for 25 minutes.  Top with marshmallow half before baking or frost with seven minute frosting.  Makes 1 1/2 dozen.
