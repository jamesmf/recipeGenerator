MOM'S LASAGNA II


1  lb. ground beef
2  garlic cloves, minced
1 1/2  c. water
1  can (15 oz.) tomato sauce
1  can (6 oz.) tomato paste
1/2 to 1  envelope onion soup mix
1  tsp. dried oregano
1/2  tsp. sugar
1/4  tsp. pepper
9  lasagna noodles, cooked and drained
2  c. (16 oz.) small curd cottage cheese
4  c. (16 oz.) mozzarella cheese, shredded
2  c. grated parmesan cheese

      In a large saucepan, cook beef and garlic over medium heat until meat is no longer pink; drain.  Stir in the water, tomato sauce and paste, soup mix, oregano, sugar and pepper.  Bring to a boil.  Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 30 minutes.  Spoon 1/2 cup meat sauce into a greased 13 x 9 x 2 inch baking dish.  Layer with 3 noodles and a third of the cottage cheese, mozzarella, meat sauce and parmesan cheese.  Repeat layers twice.  Cover and bake at 350 degrees; for 40 minutes or until bubbly and heated through. Uncover; bake 5 to 10 minutes longer.  Let stand 10 minutes before cutting.  Yield: 12 servings.
