CHRISTMAS WREATH COOKIES


1/2  c. melted butter
30  large marshmallows
1/2  tsp. green food coloring
1/2  tsp. vanilla
3  1/2 corn flakes

      Mix butter, marshmallows, coloring and vanilla in frypan.  Heat until a smooth consistency.  Add corn flakes.  Working quickly with buttered fingers, use a tablespoon to remove some mixture; shape into wreaths and put on waxed paper.  Sprinkle with colored sugar decorating sprinkles.  Be careful; mixture will be hot.
