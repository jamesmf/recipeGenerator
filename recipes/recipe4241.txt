SPICY ORANGE SALSA


2  large oranges, sectioned (about 2 c.)
1  Tbsp. lemon juice
1  Tbsp. minced red onion
1/2  bell pepper (any color), small dice
1/2  tsp. fresh or dried chopped cilantro
1  tsp. minced jalapeno peppers
1  tsp. minced garlic
1/4  tsp. ground cumin
  salt and pepper to taste

      Combine all ingredients in a large bowl and mix thoroughly. Serve over fish fillets.
