CURRIED CHICKEN SALAD


2  c. grilled, diced chicken
1  c. mayonnaise
4  c. cooked rice, chilled
1 1/2  Tbsp. curry powder
1/2  tsp. salt
1  tsp. sugar
1/2  c. diced onion
3  Tbsp. minced cilantro
1  c. diced pineapple, with juices
1/2  c. shredded carrots (3 carrots)

      Mix all ingredients in bowl.  Chill for at least 1 hour and serve.
