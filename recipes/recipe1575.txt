RUM BALLS


4  oz. butter
2  Tbsp. cocoa
1/2  c. sultanas or any other dried fruit
8  oz. confectioners sugar
2  Tbsp. shredded coconut
1/2  c. chopped nuts
2  Tbsp. rum, brandy or whiskey

      Mix softened butter.  Add sifted sugar, then all other ingredients and mix well.  Put into refrigerator to harden a little.  Roll into small balls and ice with chocolate icing or roll in chocolate sprinkles or coconut.
