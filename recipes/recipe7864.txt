SOUTHERN CHICKEN & DUMPLINGS


1  stewing chicken
1  onion
  Salt and pepper
1  c. sifted flour
2  tsp. baking powder
1/2  tsp. salt
  Dash of minced parsley
1/2  c. milk

      Clean chicken, cut into serving pieces, place in a pot. Cover with water, add onion, salt, pepper and cook until tender, about 2 1/2 or 3 hours.  Mix flour, baking powder, salt and parsley.  Add milk to make a thick batter.  Drop from a spoon into boiling chicken broth.  Cook about 20 minutes without raising lid. Place chicken on a platter and put dumplings around it.  Serves 6.
