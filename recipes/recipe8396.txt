CHICKEN AND DRESSING CASSEROLE


4  chicken breasts, cooked and shredded
1 regular  pkg. Pepperidge Farm stuffing
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  can cream of chicken soup
2  c. chicken broth
1  small onion
1/2  c. chopped celery
1  stick margarine

      Melt margarine; saut onion and celery.  Mix with stuffing. Place stuffing mixture in 13 x 9 pan, reserving 1/2 cup.  Top with shredded chicken.  Mix soups and broth and pour over chicken.  Top with reserved stuffing mix.  Bake 30 minutes at 350 degrees;.
