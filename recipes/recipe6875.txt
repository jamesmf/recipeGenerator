NOODLE STROGANOFF


1  lb. stew meat or ground beef
1/2  c. onion
1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can condensed cream of mushroom
1/2  c. sour cream
1/2  c. water
1/2  tsp. paprika (optional)
1/2  tsp. salt
2  c. cooked egg noodles

	Brown meat and cook onion; pour off fat. Add remaining ingredients. Pour into baking dish. Bake at 400 degrees; for 25 minutes.
