EASY MEXICAN PIZZAS


1  pkg. 10 super size flour tortillas
2  c. Mexican blend shredded cheese, divided
1  Tbsp. vegetable oil
1  lb. skinless boneless chicken breasts, cut into small pieces
1/4  c. chopped onions
1/4  c. chopped green peppers
1 (1.25 oz.)  pkg. Ortega taco seasoning mix, divided
1 (1 lb.)  can refried beans
  Ortega thick taco sauce

      Preheat oven to 375 degrees;.  Place 5 tortillas on greased baking sheets.  Sprinkle each with 1/4 cup cheese.  Place remaining tortillas on top of cheese.  Heat vegetable oil in large skillet over medium heat.  Add chicken, onions and green peppers.  Cook 4 to 5 minutes or until chicken is no longer pink.  Add 2 tablespoons taco seasoning mix and 1 tablespoon water; mix well. Remove from heat.  Combine refried beans, remaining seasoning mix and 1 tablespoon of water in small bowl.  Heat in microwave until warm.  Spread about 1/4 cup bean mixture on each tortilla.  Layer with a drizzle of taco sauce, chicken mixture, remaining cheese and toppings.  Bake for 5 to 8 minutes or until cheese is melted.
