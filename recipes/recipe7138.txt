PIQUANT FISH


2  - 3  pieces of orange Roughy

      Place fish in cold water and lemon juice for a few minutes. Place fish in a baking dish.  Pour sauce (recipe to follow) over fish coating very well.  Bake at 500 degrees; for 10  minutes or until flaky.
Sauce:
2  tsp margarine, melted
1  1/2 tsp apple cider vinegar
1  1/2 tsp lemon juice, fresh if possible
1  1/2 tsp worcestershire sauce
1/2  tsp yellow prepared mustard
  white pepper to taste

      Mix ingredients together well.  Pour over fish.
