CHOCOLATE CHIP CHEESECAKE

Crust:
1  c. Quaker oats (uncooked)
1/3  c. finely chopped almonds
1/3  c. firmly packed brown sugar
1/3  c. melted Crisco

      Heat oven to 350 degrees;.  Combine all ingredients; mix well.  Press onto bottom and 1-inch up sides of 9-inch spring-form pan or onto bottom only of 13 x 9-inch baking pan.  Bake 10 to 15 minutes or until golden brown.  Cool completely.
Filling:
3 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese, softened
1  c. sugar
1 1/2  tsp. vanilla
3  eggs
1/3  c. milk
1  c. mini semi-sweet chocolate chips
2  tsp. all-purpose flour

      Beat first 3 ingredients at medium speed of hand mixer until fluffy.  Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each. Gradually mix in milk.  Reserve 1 tablespoon chocolate chips; combine remainder with flour.  Stir floured chocolate chips into cream cheese mixture; pour onto crust.  Sprinkle with reserved chocolate chips.  Bake 50 to 60 minutes (45 minutes for 13 x 9-inch) or until almost set.  Cool completely; loosen side with knife and remove rim.  Chill.  Store, covered, in refrigerator.
