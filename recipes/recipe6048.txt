STRAWBERRY TOSSED SALAD


1/2  c. vegetable oil
1/3  c. sugar
1/4  c. red wine vinegar
1/4  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. paprika
1/8  tsp. pepper
6  c. mixed greens
2 1/2  c. sliced strawberries
1  c. shredded sharp cheese
1/2  c. chopped walnuts

      Mix oil, sugar, vinegar, salt, paprika and pepper in a jar with a lid; shake well to make dressing.  Toss remaining ingredients with dressing.
