HAM POTATO SCALLOP


1 (5 oz.)  pkg. scalloped potatoes
2  c. boiling water
2  Tbsp. butter or margarine
3/4  c. milk
2  c. cubed fully cooked ham
1 (10 oz.)  pkg. frozen cut green beans
1  c. (4 oz.) shredded Cheddar cheese

	Preheat oven to 400 degrees;.  In an ungreased 1 1/2-quart baking dish, combine potatoes with sauce mix, boiling water and butter.	Stir in milk, ham and beans.  Bake, uncovered, for 35 minutes or until the potatoes are tender, stirring occasionally.  Sprinkle with cheese. Bake 5 minutes longer or until cheese is melted.  Let stand 5 minutes before serving.  Yield:  4 servings.
