CHICKEN PARISIENNE


6  (5 oz.) chicken breasts, skinned and boned
1 (10 oz.)  can condensed cream of mushroom soup
1  c. sauted mushrooms
1  c. sour cream
1/2  c. sherry
  paprika

      Place chicken in shallow baking dish. Combine remaining ingredients, except paprika, and pour over chicken. Sprinkle generously with paprika. Bake in a preheated 350 degrees; oven for 45 minutes or until tender. Serve with hot, fluffy rice. Serves 4 to 6.
      Note: This can be assembled the day before it is to be served. Just refrigerate until ready to bake!
