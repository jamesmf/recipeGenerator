AUNTE'S PORK CHOP CASSEROLE


6  pork chops
  vegetable oil or Pam
  seasoned salt
1  can cream of celery soup
1/2  c. milk
1/2  c. sour cream
1/4  tsp. black pepper
1 (24 oz.)  pkg. hash browns, thawed
1  c. shredded Cheddar cheese
1  c. Durkee French fried onion rings

      Brown pork chops in skillet with oil.  Sprinkle with seasoned salt and set aside.  Combine soup, milk, sour cream, pepper and salt.  Stir in potatoes, 1/2 cup cheese and 1/2 can onion rings. Spoon mixture into 9 x 13-inch baking dish.  Arrange pork chops over potatoes.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 40 minutes.  Top with remaining cheese and onion rings.  Bake, uncovered, 5 more minutes.
