BAR-B-Q SAUCE SEASONING


26  oz. (1 box) salt
2  oz. (1 box) red pepper, ground
2  oz. (1 box) black pepper
2  oz. (1 box) Accent seasoning
2  oz. (1 box) chili powder
2 1/2  oz. (1 bottle) garlic powder
3  Tbsp. sugar

      Mix together all ingredients.  Use for all your barbecue: chicken, beef or pork.
