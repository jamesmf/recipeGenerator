MISSOURI MEAT LOAF


1  lb. hamburger
1/2  lb. sausage
3 medium  slices soft bread, cubed and 1 c. milk or 1 c. dry bread crumbs and 1 1/2 c. milk
1  egg, beaten
1/2  c. chopped onion
1 1/4  tsp. salt
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/4  tsp. each: pepper, dry mustard, celery salt and garlic salt

      Mix well.  Pat into a 9 x 9-inch pan.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 1/2 hours.
      Note:  If you use 1 1/2 pounds  of  hamburger  and omit sausage,  omit dry mustard and add 1 tablespoon of horseradish and catsup.
