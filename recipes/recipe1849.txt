GOULASH


1/2  pkg. elbow macaroni
1  tsp. chili powder
1/2  c. grated cheese
1  green pepper, chopped
1  lb. ground beef
  salt to taste
1  small chopped onion
1  can tomato soup

      Boil macaroni; drain well.  Brown ground beef; add chili powder and salt.  Mix with macaroni.  Add to cheese, onion and pepper. Place in greased baking dish and cover with 1 can tomato soup. Bake 1 hour at 350 degrees;.
