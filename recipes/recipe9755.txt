GREEN BEAN AND HAM CASSEROLE


3/4  c. cubed ham
3  c. potatoes, sliced or frozen Tater Tots
1/4  c. chopped onion
1  can green beans
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  c. milk
2  Tbsp. flour

      Place first 4 ingredients in a buttered casserole dish. Mix soup, milk and flour; pour over top. Bake at 350 degrees;, covered, for 1 hour, then 15 minutes, uncovered.
