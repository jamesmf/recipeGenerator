CRUNCHY COLE SLAW


1  lb. prepared coleslaw
8  chopped green onions
1/2  c. vegetable oil
2  pkg. Ramen noodles mix (Oriental), broken up
1/3  c. plus a splash rice wine vinegar
1 1/2  c. slivered almonds, toasted
1/4  c. sugar
1  c. salted sunflower seeds
2 seasoning  pkg. from noodle mix

      Put oil, vinegar, sugar and seasoning in container.  Remaining goes in serving bowl.  Shake oil mixture up well and pour over colelsaw mix just before serving.
