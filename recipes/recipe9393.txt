OVEN FRIED CHICKEN


1/4  c. margarine or butter
1/2  c. all-purpose flour
1  tsp. paprika
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
3  lb. fryer chicken, cut up

      Heat oven to 425 degrees.  Melt margarine in rectangular pan, 13 X 9 X 2, in oven.  Mix flour, paprika, salt and pepper.  Coat chichen with flour mixture.  Place chicken, skin side down, in pan.  Bake, uncovered, 30 minutes.  Turn chicken and bake about 30 minutes longer.
