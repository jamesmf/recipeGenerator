CHICKEN SAVANNAH


8  chicken breasts (skinless; 4 oz. raw weight)
40  oz. buttermilk
1  c. flour (self-rising)
1  Tbsp. salt
1/2  tsp. cayenne pepper
1/2  tsp. garlic powder
3/4  c. butter
1/2  c. honey
2  Tbsp. dark brown sugar
1/2  c. pecans (10% halves, 90% pieces)

      Soak chicken in buttermilk for 1 1/2 hours; remove and drain. Mix flour, salt, pepper and garlic.  Thoroughly coat chicken with mixture and allow to sit for 20 minutes.  Heat vegetable oil to 375 degrees; and fry chicken.
      Make glaze by melting butter and adding honey and brown sugar. Bring mixture to a low boil.  Add pecans and simmer for 20 minutes. Place chicken on a sheet pan and top with 1 1/2 ounces sauce per breast.  Place chicken in oven and bake at 400 degrees; for 8 to 10 minutes.
