STUFFED PEPPERS


1  c. cooked rice
1  lb. hamburger
4  large green peppers
1  can tomato sauce
  salt and pepper
  butter

      Wash and clean peppers.  Parboil peppers for 5 minutes.  Let cool and drain.  Combine rice, meat, salt, pepper and butter; mix well.  Stuff peppers; place peppers in a pan, uncovered, with a little water in the bottom.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour (check after 45 minutes).  Heat can of tomato sauce and serve with peppers.
