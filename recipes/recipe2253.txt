CHEESE GRITS


1 1/2  c. Quaker Quick grits
6  c. water
1  lb. Velveeta cheese
3  eggs
3/4  c. margarine
3  tsp. seasoned salt
2  tsp. salt

      Cook grits in water over direct heat for 5 minutes.  While hot, add cheese, beaten eggs, margarine and salts.  Put in 3-quart casserole dish.  Bake for 1 hour at 350 degrees;.
