SWEET AND SOUR MEATBALLS


3  lb. ground beef
1  can evaporated milk
2  c. quick oatmeal
1  medium onion, chopped
1/2  tsp. pepper
2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. garlic powder
2  Tbsp. chili powder
2  c. ketchup
2  c. brown sugar
2  Tbsp. liquid smoke
1/2  tsp. garlic salt
1/2  c. chopped onion

      Mix all ingredients and bring to a boil.  Pour over meatballs and bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour.
