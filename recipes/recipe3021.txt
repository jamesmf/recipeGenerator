HOT DIP


2 (4 oz.)  cans chopped green chillies, drained
2  eggs
2  Tbsp. milk
2  c. Cheddar cheese

      Spread drained chillies thinly in 10 x 6 baking dish. Sprinkle grated Cheddar cheese on top of chillies.  Beat eggs. Add milk and heat together with eggs.  Pour eggs and milk over chillies and cheese.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 20 minutes.
