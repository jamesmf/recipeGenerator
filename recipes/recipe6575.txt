POPPY SEED CHICKEN CASSEROLE


4  chicken breasts
1  can cream of chicken soup
1 to 1 1/2  tsp. poppy seed
  salt and pepper to taste
1/2  pt. sour cream
1  sleeve Ritz crackers
1/2  stick oleo

      Cook, debone and tear chicken into small pieces. Combine soup, chicken, sour cream and poppy seed. Place in 8 x 10 baking dish. Crush crackers on top of mixture. Add oleo (cut up) on top of crackers. Bake 30 to 35 minutes at 350 degrees;.
