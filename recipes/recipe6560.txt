CORNFLAKE BAKED CHICKEN


2  c. crushed cornflakes
1/2  tsp. black pepper
1/2  tsp. garlic salt
4  to 6 boneless skinless breasts
1/2  c. melted butter

      Dip chicken into melted butter. Roll in mixture of cornflakes, black pepper and garlic salt. Place pieces in 13 x 9 glass pan. Sprinkle with remaining butter and crumbs. Bake at 375 degrees; for about 1 hour or until done.
