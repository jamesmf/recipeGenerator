GREEK CASSEROLE DINNER
(Mousaka Kreas)  

1  lb. ground chuck
2  tsp. vegetable oil
1  tsp. sage
4  c. thinly sliced potatoes
1  c. water
1/4  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper
1 (3 1/2 oz.)  can French fried onions
1  pkg. spaghetti sauce mix
1 3/4  c. water

      In a frypan, brown meat slowly with the oil and sage.  Cook potatoes in 1 cup boiling water, in a covered saucepan for 5 minutes.  Drain.  Toss with salt and pepper and half can of French fried onions.  Prepare sauce mix using 1 3/4 cups water.  Arrange layers of potatoes, meat and sauce in a 2-quart buttered casserole dish, ending with sauce.  Cover dish and bake at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes.  Remove from oven.  Add remaining onions and bake 5 minutes longer.  Makes 6 to 8 servings.
