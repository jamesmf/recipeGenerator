JUMBO STUFFED SHELLS (BEEF)


1 1/2  lb. ground beef or 1/2  beef and 1/2	pork
1  egg
1/2   lb. Mozzarella cheese, diced
1/2   c. milk
2  slices bread
1  Tbsp chopped parsley
  salt and pepper
  jumbo shells
  pasta sauce
  grated cheese

	Break ground beef into small particles and saut√© in butter or olive oil.	Mix thoroughly meat, egg, Mozzarella, milk, bread, chopped parsley, salt and pepper.  Fill cooked shells.  Cover bottom of baking dish with pasta sauce.  Arrange filled shells side by side in single layer in baking pan.  Pour remaining sauce over filled shells and bake at 350 degrees; for about 30 minutes. Sprinkle with grated cheese and serve.
