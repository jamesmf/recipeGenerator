STEVE'S PORK ROAST


1/2  c. chicken broth
1/4 to 1/2  c. soy sauce
2  Tbsp. dry mustard
2  tsp. dried thyme
1/2  tsp. ground ginger
4 to 5  lb. rolled boneless pork loin

 
Sauce:
1 (10 to 12 oz.)  jar apricot preserves
2  Tbsp. chicken broth
1  Tbsp. soy sauce

      Combine first five ingredients.  Mix well to marinate roast in refrigerator 3 to 4 hours.  Remove roast and bake at 325 degrees; for 2 1/2 to 3 hours or until thermometer registers 160 degrees;.  Let stand 15 to 20 minutes before carving.
      Combine Apricot Sauce ingredients:  preserves, broth and soy sauce.  Heat.  Serve with roast.
