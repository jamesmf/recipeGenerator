MINCED CLAM AND CHEESE DELIGHT


2 (7 1/2 oz.)  cans minced clams (undrained)
1  Tbsp. margarine
1 to 2  Tbsp. flour
1/2 to 2/3  c. milk
4  oz. shredded Cheddar cheese
  cooked pasta

      Melt margarine. Add flour.  Cook and blend.  Slowly add clams and juice.  Stir until smooth.  Add milk.  Stir to thicken.  Add cheese; heat until melted.  Serve over cooked pasta.  Serves 4 to 6.
