ORANGE SHERBET SALAD


2 small  pkg. (or 1 large) orange jello
1 small  can mandarin oranges, drained
1 small  can crushed pineapple, drained
1 small  can pineapple tidbits
1  pt. orange sherbet

      Dissolve jello in 2 cups boiling water.  While hot, stir in 1 pint orange sherbet.  Add mandarin oranges, crushed pineapple and pineapple tidbits.  Stir and chill.
