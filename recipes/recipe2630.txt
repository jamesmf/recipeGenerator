MEXICAN CORNBREAD


1  c. yellow cornmeal
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. soda
1/3  c. melted shortening
1  c. sour cream
1 (8 oz.)  can cream-style corn
2  eggs, beaten
1 (4 oz.)  can chopped green chiles, drained
1  c. shredded Cheddar cheese

      Combine cornmeal, salt and soda; blend.  Stir in shortening; add sour cream, corn and eggs and mix well.  Add green chiles and cheese and stir.  Pour in greased 9 x 13-inch pan and bake at 375 degrees; for 35 to 40 minutes.
