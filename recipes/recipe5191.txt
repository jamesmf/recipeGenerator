CREAMY POTATO SOUP


5  cups potatoes (diced and boiled)
1/2  cup onion (diced)
4  chicken flavored  bouillon cubes
1/2  stick butter or margarine
2  1/2 cups milk
  pepper to taste
  parsley to taste
6  strips bacon (cooked crisp and crumbled)
1  cup grated cheddar cheese

      Combine potatoes, onion, bouillon and margarine in large saucepan.  Cover with water and cook until tender.  Add milk and reduce heat to low.  Cook until heated.  Add pepper. (Thicken with potato flakes if needed.)  Pour into serving bowls.  Top with bacon crumbs and cheese.  Serve with cornbread.  Yields 8 servings.
