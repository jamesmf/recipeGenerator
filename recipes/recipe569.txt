FREEZER SLAW


1  head cabbage
1  Tbsp. salt
4  tsp. Sweet 'N Low
1  green pepper
1  stick celery
1  c. vinegar
1/2  c. water
1  tsp. celery seed
1  tsp. mustard seed

      Grate cabbage and cover with salt.  Add Sweet 'N Low.  Let stand 1 hour and squeeze out the water; add green pepper and celery to cabbage.  Simmer the remaining ingredients for 15 minutes.  Add to the squeezed out cabbage; cover in tight bowl.  Will keep for weeks in refrigerator or put in bags and freeze as desired.
