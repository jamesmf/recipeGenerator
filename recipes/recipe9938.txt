HASH BROWN POTATO CASSEROLE


1  (16 oz.) bag frozen hash brown potatoes
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  can milk (to clean out soup can)
1  (8 oz.) container sour cream
1  c. shredded cheese
8 to 10  oz. thickly sliced ham, cubed (optional)

      Mix together soup, milk, sour cream and cheese.  Add the frozen potatoes.  Mix well.  Toss in the cubed ham.  Place in a greased shallow pan.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 50 minutes or until brown.
