ROAST PORK LOIN
("Slow Cooker Recipe")  

1  pork loin
1/2  c. brown sugar
1/4  c. onions, chopped
1 large  can sauerkraut
1 large  can tomato juice

      Place uncooked meat in a slow cooker.  Layer brown sugar, onions, and sauerkraut which has been washed and drained well over the meat.  Pour tomato juice on top of food.  Cook all day on low in slow cooker.
