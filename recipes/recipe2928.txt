ARTICHOKE DIP


2  cans artichoke hearts, drained
1  can green chili peppers, chopped
1  c. mayonnaise or "Lite" mayo
1  c. Parmesan cheese, shredded

      Cut or mash artichoke hearts. Add green chili peppers; mix well. Then add mayonnaise and Parmesan cheese; mix thoroughly. Place in baking dish. Put in the oven at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes (or until cheese is melted). Enjoy!
