MILD DILL PICKLES


1  qt. water
2  cloves garlic per quart jar
1/2  c. vinegar
  Dill
1/4  c. salt
  6-7 sterilized quarts

      Bring water, vinegar and salt to boil.  Meanwhile, fill sterilized jars with washed, medium sized pickles, garlic and couple sprigs of dill. Fill jars with hot liquid and immediately seal with sterilized lids.
