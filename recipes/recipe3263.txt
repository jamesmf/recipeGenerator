SHRIMP SCAMPI


3  Tbsp. olive oil
1  Tbsp. chopped garlic
1  lb. medium shrimp, deshelled
1/2  tsp. parsley
1/2  tsp. white vinegar
1 1/2  Tbsp. butter
1  Tbsp. white wine
1  lb. spaghetti
  salt and pepper to taste

      Heat oil and vinegar in skillet.  Add garlic until heated. Add vinegar, wine, salt and pepper to taste.  Then add shrimp. Cook shrimp 5 to 7 minutes (don't overcook).  Boil water.  Cook spaghetti until done.  Drain.  Serve shrimp and sauce over pasta.
