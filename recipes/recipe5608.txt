CHEESE VEGETABLE SOUP


10  oz. pkg. frozen veggies (broccoli mix is good)
1/2  C. chopped onion
1/4  C. margarine
1/2  C. flour
1/4  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper
1 3/4  C. milk
10 3/4  oz. can chicken broth
8  oz. jar processed cheese spread
1 1/2  tsp. prepared mustard

      Cook veggies to your liking.  Saut√© onion in margarine. Blend in flour, salt and pepper.  Stir in the milk.  Gradually heat to boiling;  stir constantly. Reduce heat and add remaining ingredients (broth, cheese, mustard).  Heat thoroughly.
      Makes 5 cups of soup.
