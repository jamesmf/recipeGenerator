PESHAWARI GOSHTH


1  lb. beef, small cubes
1  large onion
1  tsp. ginger
1  Tbsp. coriander
4  potatoes
  Salt to taste
1/2  cup oil
1  tsp. black pepper
  Green peppers
  Cilantro

      Warm the oil, then fry the beef.  Add to it ginger, salt, and coriander and saut.  Then add two cups of water to tenderize the beef.
      Separately, cut the onion and tomatoes into cubes and fry them.  When the beef is tender, saut once again until it is completely dry, then add the fried onions and tomatoes.  Garnish with cilantro and green peppers.  Serves about 4.
