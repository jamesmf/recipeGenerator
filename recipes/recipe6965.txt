CHICKEN STEW


1  chicken
  cooking oil
1/4  c. flour
  small onion, chopped
1  bell pepper, chopped
  salt and pepper to taste

      Cover bottom of skillet with cooking oil. Make a roux by adding flour and browning. Saut√© onion and bell pepper in roux.
       In another skillet, fry chicken pieces until brown. Add to roux and stir in enough water to cover chicken. Simmer until stew is thick and chicken is tender. Season with salt and pepper to taste or with your favorite seasoning.
