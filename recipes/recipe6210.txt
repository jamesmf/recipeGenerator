FAVORITE CROCK-POT CHILI


2 (1 lb.)  cans pinto beans, drained
2 (1 lb.)  cans tomatoes
2  lb. coarsely ground chuck, browned
2  medium onions, chopped
1  green pepper, chopped
2 to 3  Tbsp. chili powder
1 1/2  Tbsp. salt
1  tsp. pepper
1  tsp. cumin

      Put all ingredients in crock-pot in order listed.  Stir once. Cover and cook on low for 10 to 12 hours (high for 5 to 6 hours).
