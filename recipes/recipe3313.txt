TACO DIP


1  lb. ground beef
1  large chopped onion
1  pkg. French's taco mix
1 small  can chopped, mild, green chilies
1 to 2  cans refried beans
12  oz. pkg. shredded Cheddar cheese
1  bottle taco sauce
1  bag tortilla chips

      Brown and drain the ground beef and onion.  Add taco mix and green chilies.  Layer beans, meat, cheese and taco sauce.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 20 minutes.  Let stand 10 minutes.
      Optional toppings: Dollops of sour cream, chopped black olives or scoops of guacamole.
