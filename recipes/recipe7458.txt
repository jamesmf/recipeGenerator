TACO CHICKEN CASSEROLE


1  large whole chicken
2  cans cream of chicken soup
1 1/2  cans water
2 medium  jars Cheez Whiz
1  chopped medium onion
2  jalapeno peppers, chopped
2  bags Doritos chips (1 plain, 1 cheese)

      Boil chicken and cut up in large chunks.  Cook together and simmer 30 minutes the chicken soup, water, Cheez Whiz, onion and peppers.  Add chicken and cook 5 minutes longer.  Line casserole dish with Doritos chips. Pour in chicken mixture and top with chips. Cook at 300 degrees; for 20 to 30 minutes.
