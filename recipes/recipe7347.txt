TEXAS HASH CASSEROLE


2  large onions, diced
2  stalks celery, sliced
2  green peppers, diced
3  Tbsp. shortening
1 1/2  lb. ground round
2  c. diced tomatoes
1/2  c. rice (uncooked)
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. chili powder
1/4  tsp. pepper

      Saut√© onions, celery and green peppers slowly in shortening. Brown meat and add to onion mixture.  Add tomatoes, rice and seasoning.  Arrange in large casserole.  Cover and bake in oven at 375 degrees; for 45 minutes.
