FESTIVE CHEESE BALL


3 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese, softened
1 (4 oz.)  pkg. crumbled Blue cheese
1  c. (4 oz.) shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. chopped dates
1  c. golden raisins
1/2  c. chopped pecans, toasted

      Combine first 5 ingredients; divide in half. Shape each portion into a ball and roll in pecans. Cover and chill. Serve with shortbread cookies and gingersnaps or apple and pear slices. Yield: 2 (4-inch) cheese balls.
