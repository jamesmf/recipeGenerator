CHICKEN ON RICE


10  oz. (1 can) soup, mushroom
2/3  c. milk
3/4  c. long grain rice (uncooked)
1  oz. (1 pkg.) onion soup mix (dry)
2  (4 halves) chicken breasts

      Blend mushroom soup and milk.  Set aside 1/2 cup of mixture for later use.  Combine together:  soup mixture, rice and dry soup mix.  Pour mixture into a 2-quart baking dish.  Place chicken on top.  Pour the reserved soup mixture over chicken.
      Cover dish and bake at 325 degrees; for 1 hour.  Uncover and bake 15 minutes longer.
