PEPSI ROAST


1 3 to 4  lb. beef roast
1 (16 oz.)  bottle Pepsi
1  can mushroom soup
1  pkg. Lipton dry onion soup

      Place roast in pan which can be tightly covered.  Spread can of soup over roast.  Sprinkle dry onion soup mixture over the top. Pour Pepsi into pan and cover tightly.  Put into oven at 325 degrees; and roast for 3 1/2 to 4 hours.  Makes its own gravy.  Yield:  4 to 6 servings.
      Note:  This dish may be roasted at 250 degrees; for 6 to 8 hours with no fear of drying out.
