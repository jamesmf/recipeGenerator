MEAT FILLED JUMBO SHELLS


1 (12 oz.)  box San Giorgio jumbo shells, uncooked
1  lb. hamburger
1  lb. sausage
4  eggs, slightly beaten
1  c. bread crumbs
3 1/2  c. (32 oz.) jar spaghetti sauce
1  c. (4 oz.) shredded Mozzarella cheese
3/4  c. chopped onion
3/4  tsp. oregano
1/2  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper

      Cook  jumbo  shells according  to  package  directions, drain. (Cool in a single layer on wax  paper  or foil to keep shells from sticking together.) Brown hamburger and sausage in skillet; drain.  Combine meat, eggs,  bread  crumbs,  cheese, onions, oregano, salt and pepper.  Fill each  shell with about 2 tablespoons meat filling.  Spread a thin layer  of  sauce on bottom of 9 x 13-inch pan.  Place the shells,  open side down, in a single layer in the pan.  Cover  with  remaining  sauce. Cover with aluminum foil and bake at 350 degrees; for about 45 minutes or until hot and bubbly.  Fills about 36 shells.
