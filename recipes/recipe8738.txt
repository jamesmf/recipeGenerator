CHICKEN MARSALA


4  chicken breast
1 sm.  can mushrooms pieces
2  tbsp. butter
1/2  c. Marsala wine

      Dredge chicken in flour and brown in butter.  Place in baking dish.  Put  a little more butter in frying pan and brown mushrooms.  Add wine and bring to boil.  Pour over chicken.  Bake 325 degrees; for 1 1/2 hours.
