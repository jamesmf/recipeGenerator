CHEESE WAFERS


2  sticks margarine, softened
2  c. sharp Cheddar cheese, grated
2  c. sifted flour
1/4  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. red pepper
1  Tbsp. sugar (optional)
  pecans

      Cream butter and cheese. Sift dry ingredients together. Add to butter and cheese. Shape into small balls. Press 1/2 pecan into each ball. Bake on an ungreased cookie sheet at 300 degrees; for about 30 minutes. Do not overcook.
