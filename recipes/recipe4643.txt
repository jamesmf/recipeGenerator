ONION CUCUMBER SALAD


1  cucumber
1/2  medium red onion
1/2  c oil
1/4  c vinegar
1-1/2  tbsp sugar
1/4  tsp salt
  dash of pepper

      Pull tines of fork firmly down length of the cucumber. Repeat this all the way around the cucumber.  Thinly slice onion, separating rings.  Combine remaining ingredients.  Pour over vegetables.  Marinate 1 hour.  Drain to serve.
