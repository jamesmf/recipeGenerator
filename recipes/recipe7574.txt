EASY CHICKEN MARSALA


4  chicken breasts, with or without bones
1  pkg. fresh sliced mushrooms
4  Tbsp. butter
2  cans Cream of Golden Mushroom soup
  Lawry's seasoning salt

      Melt butter and saut mushrooms in butter on medium heat. Remove mushrooms from skillet and set aside.  Lightly sprinkle chicken w/Lawry's seasoning salt and sear in mushroom butter on medium-high heat.  Add two cans of soup and mushrooms.  Cover and cook on medium-low heat for one hour, checking occasionally to turn and stir sauce.
