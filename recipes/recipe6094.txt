BROCCOLI SALAD

Salad:
16  oz. English peas (frozen; optional)
1  head broccoli
1  head cauliflower
1  large sweet onion

      Thaw frozen peas.  Chop broccoli, cauliflower and onion into bite size pieces.  Add peas if desired.  Mix ingredients together.
Dressing:
1  c. mayonnaise
1/3  c. vinegar
1/3  c. oil
1/3  c. sugar

      Combine mayonnaise, vinegar, oil and sugar together.  Pour over salad and let set about 20 minutes before serving.
