GREEN TOMATO RELISH


8  c. firm green tomatoes, finely chopped
4  c. onion, chopped
1  c. hot pepper, chopped
2  c. sugar
4  c. vinegar
  Tony Chachere's Creole seasoning

      In a large stock pot, mix ingredients and bring to a boil. After mixture comes to a boil, remove from heat. Pour into hot jars immediately (to prevent tomatoes from softening) and seal. Makes 8 pints.
