CHEESE DELIGHT


1  lb. ground beef
1/2  tsp. thyme
1/2  tsp. basil
1 small  can tomato paste
1 large  can tomatoes
  garlic salt to taste
1 small  pkg. small shell macaroni
  sharp cheese

      Cook ground beef. Add remaining ingredients and simmer. Prepare macaroni; drain. Put half of macaroni in casserole. Add sharp cheese, grated or sliced, on top. Add remaining mixture and top with more cheese. Bake at 350 degrees; until cheese melts.
