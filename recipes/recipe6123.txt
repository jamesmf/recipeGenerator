HEAVENLY HASH


1  can pineapple chunks, drained
1  small container cottage cheese
1  orange Jello, dry
1 12  oz. Cool Whip
  chopped nuts, if desired

      Pour Jello over cottage cheese and toss.  Add pineapple, nuts, and Cool Whip.  Chill.  Garnish top with more nuts, if desired.  Any flavor of Jello may be used.
