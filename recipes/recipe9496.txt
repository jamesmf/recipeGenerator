SWEET AND SOUR MEAT LOAF


2  lb. ground beef
1  small onion
1/4  c. Ritz crackers
1  to 2 eggs
  dash of salt and pepper
1/2  c. sauce

 
Sauce:
1 (16 oz.)  can tomato sauce
1/2 to 2/3  c. brown sugar
1/3  c. vinegar
1  tsp. mustard

	Mix together and bake at 430 degrees; for 30 minutes. Pour off excess fat; cover with remaining sauce. Bake at 375 degrees; for 40 minutes.
