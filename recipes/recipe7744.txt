CHICKEN AND DUMPLINGS


2  to 3 chicken breasts
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  c. milk
  pepper
2  Tbsp. Crisco (solid)
1/2 to 3/4  c. milk
3  cubes chicken bouillon
1  stick butter
  salt
2  c. sifted flour
1  egg
4  Tbsp. cornstarch

      Cook chicken and cube. Add bouillon, soup, butter, salt and pepper. Blend flour and Crisco with a fork. Add egg; mix well. Add milk. Drop by teaspoonful into boiling stock when all the dough is in stockpot. Cover; turn stock off and let set 10 minutes. Thicken stock with cornstarch and small amount of water. Pour into stock before adding dumplings.
