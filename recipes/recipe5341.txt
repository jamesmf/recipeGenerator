MACARONI-CHICKEN SALAD


2  c. seashell macaroni
2  c. sliced celery
1 1/2  c. cooked chicken
1/2  c. onion
1/2  c. green pepper
1/2  c. sweet pickle cubes
1/2  c. stuffed olives
1  c. mayonnaise
2  tsp. mustard

      Cook macaroni; drain, rinse and drain.  Combine with the next 6 ingredients.  Combine mayonnaise and mustard; spoon over salad and toss gently.  Cover and chill.
