LEMON ICEBOX PIE


  juice of 3 lemons
1  can sweetened condensed milk
3  egg yolks
3  egg whites
6  Tbsp. sugar

 
Crust:
2  c. vanilla wafer crumbs
3  Tbsp. sugar
1/2  stick margarine

      Mix milk, juice and egg yolks together.  Pour into vanilla wafer crust.
      Beat egg whites until stiff.  Gradually add sugar; spread over lemon filling and bake pie 15 minutes in 350 degrees; oven.  When cool, place in refrigerator.
      Crust:  Mix vanilla wafer crumbs, sugar and melted margarine together.  Press into pie pan.  Stand vanilla wafers up around edge of pan.
