HOMEMADE PIZZA SAUCE


1/2  bushel tomatoes (26 lb.)
6 garlic cloves, chopped or 3/4  tsp. garlic powder
6  medium onions, chopped
1  c. chopped parsley
1/2  c. brown sugar
1/4  c. salt
3  Tbsp. oregano
2  Tbsp. basil
1  Tbsp. pepper

      Cook  tomatoes, onions, garlic and fresh parsley.  Then run through ricer.  Return to kettle and add remaining ingredients. Cook  to  desired thickness, about 4 hours.  Good for spaghetti too.
