SPICED CRANBERRIES


4  c. fresh cranberries
2  c. water
3  c. sugar
1  tsp. ground cinnamon
1/2  tsp. ground cloves
  pinch of ginger

      Wash cranberries; drain and set aside.  Combine remaining ingredients in a large saucepan; bring to a boil.  Add cranberries and cook 7 to 10 minutes or until skins pop.  Reduce heat and simmer 1 hour, stirring occasionally.  Remove from heat and let cool.  Chill until ready to serve.  Makes 2 cups.
