STEWED CHICKEN AND GRAVY


  large pkg. cut up fryer pieces
  oil
3  c. self-rising flour
  salt, to taste
  pepper, to toast
  garlic salt, to taste
1  onion, chopped

      In large Dutch oven, heat small amount of cooking oil. Mix flour, salt, pepper, and garlic salt; roll chicken in flour. Place chicken in hot oil; fry on both sides. Add water to cover chicken; add onions. Boil over medium heat until chicken is done; chicken will make it's own gravy.
