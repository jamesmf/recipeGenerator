PITA CHIPS WITH ZAATAR "THYME"


4  PITA BREADS
6  Tbsp. zaatar "thyme with whole sesame"
1/4-1/3  cup olive oil

      Cut pita into small squares and place on baking sheet.  Mix zaatar and olive oil with your hands and spread on each piece of bread.  Back in oven at 400 degrees; F for 15 minutes or until brown and crispy.
