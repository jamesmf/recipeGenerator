HAM CARIBBEAN


1 1/2  lb. ham, cooked
8  oz. can pineapple chunks
1/4  c. brown sugar
1  Tbsp. cornstarch
1/8  tsp. cloves
1/3  c. orange juice
1/2  c. raisins

      Slice ham at 1-inch intervals. Place on rack in pan. Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes.
      Meanwhile, drain pineapple, saving juice. In small pan, combine brown sugar, cornstarch and cloves. Add juice. Cook and stir until thickened. Add pineapple chunks and raisins. Spoon over cooked ham.
