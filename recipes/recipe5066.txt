SPAGHETTI SAUCE


1  qt. tomatoes or puree
1  pt. tomato paste
1  Tbsp. basil
1  Tbsp. oregano
1  tsp. salt
1 1/2  tsp. pepper
1  Tbsp. parsley flakes
2  cloves garlic or 1/4 tsp. powder
1  Tbsp. sugar
1  lb. hamburger
1  Tbsp. oil

      Put  oil  in  pot.  Cut  garlic  into  small pieces and brown. Leave garlic in pot.  Add puree and 2 cups water, then paste, basil, salt, oregano, pepper, parsley flakes and sugar. Bring sauce  to a boil and turn down to simmer.  Stir every 15 minutes. Brown  hamburger.  Add  to sauce and simmer 3 hours. Serve over spaghetti.
