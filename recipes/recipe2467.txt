STRAWBERRY-LEMONADE PUNCH


1 (6 oz.)  can Minute Maid lemonade concentrate
1 (6 oz.)  can Minute Maid limeade concentrate
1 (6 oz.)  can Minute Maid orange juice concentrate
2  pkg. frozen sliced strawberries, thawed
3  c. cold water
2  liters Fresca

      Combine the first 5 ingredients and stir well.  Add the Fresca and stir gently.
