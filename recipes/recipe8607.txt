CHICKEN BROCCOLI HOT DISH


1  pkg. Stove Top chicken stuffing mix
3  C. chicken, cooked chicken, cut up
16  oz. pkg frozen broccoli
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  can cream of chicken soup
1 sm.  can evaporated milk
1 1/2  C. crushed Ritz crackers
1  C. shredded cheese
2  Tbsp. melted butter

      Mix stuffing according to package directions; pat down in a 9 x 13-inch glass pan.  Pour cooked chicken over stuffing.
      Cook and drain broccoli; put over chicken.
      Mix soups and evaporated milk together and pour over broccoli. Mix crushed Ritz crackers, shredded cheese and melted butter together.  Sprinkle on top.
      Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.
