MACARONI-CORN CASSEROLE


1  can creamed corn
1  can whole corn (undrained)
1  c. uncooked macaroni
1/2  stick butter, melted
1 1/2  c. Velveeta cheese, cut in 1/2-inch cubes

      Grease a casserole dish and mix all ingredients right in dish. Bake at 350 degrees;, covered, for 30 minutes. Uncover and cook 15 minutes more. Check to see if done. If not, cook 15 minutes more.
