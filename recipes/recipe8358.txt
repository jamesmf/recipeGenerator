OYSTER STUFFING


4  qt. toasted bread cubes
1/2  c. butter
1  c. celery, chopped
1 1/2  c. onions, chopped
1/2  c. butter
1  qt. oysters (with liquid)
1/2  c. milk
1  tsp. sage
2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper

	Measure bread cubes into a large bowl. Melt 1/2 cup butter in skillet. Cook celery and onions until soft. Pour over bread cubes. Heat 1/2 cup butter in skillet; add oysters and liquid. Heat a few minutes; remove oysters. Pour liquid over bread cubes. Chop oysters; add to bread cubes mixture. Add milk, sage, salt and pepper. Mix well. Stuff lightly into turkey. Makes enough for a 16 to 18 pound turkey.
