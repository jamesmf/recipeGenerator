SPAGHETTI


1  lb. ground beef
2  Tbsp. minced onion
1  Tbsp. salt
1  Tbsp. garlic
1  (7 oz. or larger) uncooked spaghetti
1 (6 oz.)  can tomato paste
1  can tomatoes
1  Tbsp. sugar
1  Tbsp. parsley leaves

      In larger skillet, brown ground beef with onion and salt. Drain grease. Stir in tomato paste. Add remaining ingredients. Bring to bubbling, then simmer, covered, for 1 hour or until ready to eat.
