HOLIDAY SQUASH WITH CRANBERRIES


4  c. mashed cooked squash (butternut, hubbard)
2  eggs, beaten
1/3  c. melted butter
1/2  c. brown sugar
1 1/2  c. raw cranberries
1/2  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper
  dash of nutmeg

      Preheat oven to 400 degrees;.  Beat squash with 3 tablespoons melted butter and the eggs.  Slowly stir in cranberries, sugar, salt and pepper; spoon mixture into a 2-quart casserole.  Drizzle on remaining butter; sprinkle with nutmeg.  Bake, uncovered, for approximately 35 minutes.
