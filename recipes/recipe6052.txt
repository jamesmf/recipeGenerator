CRANBERRY RELISH


2  c. cranberries
1  unpeeled seedless orange, cut into pieces
1  c. sugar
2  Tbsp. orange liqueur

      In a food grinder or blender, coarsely grind cranberries and orange.  Add sugar and orange liqueur; stir until combined.  Cover and refrigerate at least one hour before serving.  Can be made up to one week ahead and refrigerated.  Makes about 2 cups.
      Note: Relish has a kick!
