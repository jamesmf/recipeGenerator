CROCKPOT CHILI


2  lb. ground chuck
1  or 2 medium onions, chopped
1  green pepper, chopped
3 to 4  sticks celery, chopped
1 (28 oz.)  can crushed tomatoes
1 (8 oz.)  can tomato sauce
2  cans kidney or pinto beans, drained
1  tsp. pepper
3  tsp. chili powder
  Hot pepper to taste
1  tsp. garlic salt.

      Cook ground chuck, drain fat.  Combine all ingredients in a slow cooker/Crock Pot and cook 4 to 6 hours on low, or until done.
