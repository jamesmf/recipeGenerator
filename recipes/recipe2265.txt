BROCCOLI-CHICKEN-CHEESE CASSEROLE


1 1/2 to 2  lb. fresh broccoli or 2 (10 oz.) pkg. frozen broccoli spears
1 1/2 to 2  c. cooked chicken, cut into strips
1/4  c. margarine
1/4  c. flour
1/4  tsp. salt
2 chicken bouillon cubes, dissolved in 2  c. hot water
1 1/2  c. shredded cheese
1  tsp. Worcestershire

      Cook broccoli about 10 minutes, until almost tender.  Drain and arrange in 12 x 8 x 2-inch baking dish.  Sprinkle with chicken; set aside.
      In saucepan melt margarine; blend in flour and salt until smooth and bubbly.  Gradually stir in bouillon until smooth.  Stir over low heat until thickened.  Remove from heat and stir in 1 cup of cheese and the Worcestershire until cheese melts.  Pour over chicken and broccoli.  Sprinkle with remaining 1/2 cup cheese. Bake in 400 degrees; oven 20 to 25 minutes, until hot.  Makes 4 to 6 servings.
