PUMPKIN DIP


1 (30 oz.)  can pumpkin
4  c. sifted powdered sugar
2  (8 oz.) cream cheese, softened
2  tsp. cinnamon
1  tsp. ground ginger

      Mix together sugar and cream cheese.  Beat until well blended and smooth.  Mix in remaining ingredients.  Store in airtight container in refrigerator.  Makes 7 cups.  Serve with gingersnaps. Can serve in hollowed out small pumpkins.
