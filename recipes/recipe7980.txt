PEPPER STEAK


1/4  c. Crisco oil
1  lb. round steak, cut into strips
1  medium onion, thinly sliced
2  green peppers, thinly sliced
1  Tbsp. cornstarch
1  tsp. sugar
1/2  c. water
2  Tbsp. soy sauce
1  tsp. garlic salt
1/4  tsp. pepper

      Preheat oil in electric skillet (uncovered) 325 degrees;.  Add meat and brown.  Remove to a serving dish.  Add onion and pepper slices to skillet and cook until tender.  Dissolve cornstarch in water and soy sauce.  Add sugar, garlic salt and pepper.  Pour into skillet with vegetables, stirring until sauce is thickened and clear.  Add meat and cook several minutes longer or heated through.  Serve over hot rice.
