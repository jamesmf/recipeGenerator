SAUERBRATEN MEATBALLS


1 1/2  lb. ground beef
3/4  c. crushed gingersnaps, divided
1  egg
1/2  tsp. caraway seeds
3/4  tsp. salt, divided
1/2  tsp. pepper, divided
2  Tbsp. oil (preferably olive oil)
1  onion, coarsely chopped
1  carrot, coarsely chopped
1  rib celery, chopped
1/2  tsp. ginger
2 (14 1/2 oz.)  cans beef broth and 1/2 c. more if freezing
1/4  c. red wine vinegar

      Combine beef, 1/4 cup gingersnap crumbs, egg, seeds, 1/2 teaspoon salt and 1/4 teaspoon pepper. Shape into 20 meatballs. In skillet, heat oil over medium-high heat. Add meatballs. Cook until browned (10 minutes). Remove from pan.
       In same pan over medium-high heat, add onion, carrot and celery; cook, stirring until softened (10 minutes). Stir in ginger, scraping up browned bits. Add broth and vinegar. Bring to a boil; cook, stirring (10 minutes). Strain over bowl, pressing vegetables with spoon. Return liquid to pan over medium to low heat. Stir in remaining cookie crumbs, salt and pepper. Add meatballs; heat 10 minutes. Serve or cool and transfer to freezer-proof container; freeze. Thaw in the refrigerator at least 6 hours. Reheat in pot, adding broth to thin. Heat 10 minutes. Makes 10 servings.
