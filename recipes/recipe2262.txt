CHICKEN LIVERS


1/2  c. margarine
1  medium onion, minced
1/2  lb. mushrooms, sliced
  salt and pepper to taste
1  c. instant rice
2  c. chicken broth, boiling
1/2  lb. chicken livers

      Heat 1/4 cup margarine in heavy saucepan.  Add onion and sauté 2 to 3 minutes.  Add mushrooms and cook 5 minutes.  Add rice and stir until rice just begins to brown.  Stir in boiling broth. Remove from heat and let stand, covered, for 10 minutes.  Add a little more broth if mixture is too dry.  Sauté livers in remaining margarine, after dusting them with flour, until done.  Add seasoning.  Stir gently into rice mixture.
