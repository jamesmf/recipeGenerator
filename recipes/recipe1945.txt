SOUTH OF THE BORDER SUPPER


1  lb. lean ground beef
1/2  c. water
1 (16 oz.)  can tomatoes
1 (15 oz.)  can kidney beans
1  egg
1/2  c. milk
1  Tbsp. salad oil
1/2  c. all-purpose flour
1/4  c. cornmeal
1/4  c. instant mashed potatoes
1  Tbsp. sugar
2  tsp. baking powder
1/4  tsp. salt
1  envelope chili seasoning mix

      Preheat oven to 425 degrees;.  Prepare chili as directed using beef, water, tomatoes and beans.  In medium bowl, beat together egg, milk and oil.  In a small bowl, stir together flour, cornmeal, potato flakes, sugar, baking powder and salt.  Add to egg mixture and mix well.  Spoon chili mixture into a 2-quart casserole.  Drop corn bread mixture by tablespoonfuls into hot chili.  Bake for 20 to 25 minutes.
