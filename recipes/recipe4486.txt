CURRY FRUIT SALAD


  canned peach halves
  canned pear halves
  canned apricot halves
  canned pineapple chunks
  maraschino cherries

 
Topping:
1/2  c. brown sugar
1/4  c. butter
1  Tbsp. cornstarch
1/2  tsp. curry powder

      Drain fruit well. Arrange on the bottom of a large greased pan so the fruit is in a single layer. Put fruit halves cut side down and sprinkle topping over all the fruit. Bake at 350 degrees; for an hour.
