POT ROAST


1  (3 to 5 lb.)  beef roast
5  to 6 med. potatoes, divided into quarters
1  med. onion, sliced
3  to 4 lg. carrots, sliced (or several baby carrots)
  salt and pepper to taste
1  Tbsp. Teriyaki sauce

      Place meat, vegetables, salt and pepper in crock pot with 1/4 to 1/2 cup of water, and cook on high for at least 4 to 6 hrs. or until tender. (Add 1 Tbsp. of Teriyaki sauce while cooking.)
