FUDGE POPS


1  box instant chocolate pudding and pie filling
2  c. skim milk
1/2  tsp. peppermint flavoring
1  pkg. Equal (sugar substitute)

      Set up 8 Tupperware ice pop tubs.  Empty 1 envelope pudding mix into bowl.  Add 2 cups skim milk.  Blend with beater on medium speed 2 to 3 minutes.  Add 1/2 teaspoon peppermint flavoring and 1 package Equal.  Beat into pudding mix.  Then put pudding into the 8 Tupperware ice pop tubs.  Put tops on tubs and set in freezer until frozen.  One of these pops equals out to 1/4 cup skim milk and 1/4 bread exchange.
