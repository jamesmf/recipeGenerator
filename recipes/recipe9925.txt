BEEFY BAKED BEANS


1  lb. ground beef
1  small green pepper, chopped
1/2  c. chopped onion
1 (16 oz.)  can pork and beans
1/2  c. catsup
2  Tbsp. brown sugar
2  Tbsp. vinegar
2 1/2  tsp. chili powder
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper

      Combine beef, green pepper and onion in skillet.  Cook until meat is browned.  Drain.  Stir in remaining ingredients.  Spoon mixture into lightly greased 10 x 6 baking dish.  Bake, uncovered, for 30 minutes at 350 degrees;.  Makes 4 to 6 servings.
