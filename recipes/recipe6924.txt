GOLD NUGGET MEAT PIE


1  c. soft bread crumbs
1/2  c. milk
1  lb. ground round steak
1  beaten egg
  onion
2  tsp. salt
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
3  Tbsp. catsup
2  Tbsp. oleo
1 (12 oz.)  can whole kernel corn, drained
1  c. tomatoes

      Soften bread crumbs in milk; add meat, egg, 2 tablespoons grated onion, 1 teaspoon salt, Worcestershire sauce and catsup and mix well. Line the bottom and sides of 10-inch pie plate with mixture. Bake at 350 degrees; for 40 minutes.
       Cook 1/4 cup chopped onion in oleo. Add corn, tomatoes and remaining salt. Simmer 10 minutes; pour into meat crust and continue baking for 5 minutes.
