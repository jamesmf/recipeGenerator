SLOW COOKER ENCHILADAS


1  lb. ground beef
1  c. chopped onion
1/2  c. chopped green pepper
1 16  oz. can kidney beans
1 15  oz. can black beans
1 10  oz. can tomatoes with green chilies
1/3  c. water
1  tsp. chili powder
1/2  tsp. ground cumin
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
1  c. cheddar cheese, cubed
4  oz. monterey jack  cheese
5  flour tortillas

      Brown beef, onion and green pepper. Drain, add the following 8 ingredients and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes. Combine cheeses. Alternate ground beef mixture, cheese, and tortilla, starting with 3/4 c. of beef in bottom of slow cooker. Cook 5 to 7 hours on low.
