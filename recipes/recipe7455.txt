STEW BEEF


  chunks of beef or venison
1  pkg. beef Polish sausage
  onions, diced
  carrots, cut in 1-inch cubes
  potatoes, cubed
  salt and pepper to taste

      Brown beef or venison; add Polish sausage.  Add potatoes, onions and carrots.  Cover with water and bring to a boil.  Reduce heat and simmer, stirring occasionally.  When meat is tender and vegetables are done, thicken stew with flour or cornstarch.  Add pastry, if desired.
