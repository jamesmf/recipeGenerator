ONION SOUP


3  Tbsp. butter
4  c. onion, thinly sliced
1/2  tsp. sugar
1  Tbsp. flour
  salt
  freshly ground pepper
4  slices French bread, dried or toasted
1/2  c. freshly grated parmesan cheese

      Melt butter in a large pot, add onions and cook very slowly, about 45 minutes, over low heat, stirring often.  Stir in sugar, flour and cook for 3 minutes.  Add water and simmer partially covered for 30 minutes.  Add salt and pepper to taste.
