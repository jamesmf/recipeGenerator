DIRTY RICE


6  c. cooked rice
1  c. chicken livers, boiled and ground
1  c. chicken gizzards, boiled and ground
  chicken broth from liver and gizzards
1  c. celery, chopped
1/2  c. bell pepper, chopped
1  c. green onions, chopped
1 small  pkg. ground meat
1 small  clove garlic, chopped

      Put last six ingredients in skillet and cover; cook slowly until tender. Add rice, livers, and gizzards; blend together. Add salt and pepper to taste.
