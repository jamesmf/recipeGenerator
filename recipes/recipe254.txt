PAINTBRUSH COOKIES


1  pkg. refrigerated sugar cookie dough
1  egg yolk
1/4  tsp. water
3  colors food coloring (or more)

      Slice roll in 1/4-inch slices, then place on baking sheet 2-inches apart.  Mix egg yolk and water, then divide mix into 4 small dishes.  Mix a drop of food coloring in each dish.  Paint designs on cookies with a small clean paintbrush.  Bake painted cookies in a 350 degrees; oven 8 to 10 minutes.
