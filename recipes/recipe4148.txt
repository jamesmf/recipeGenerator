GREAT DIP


1  small onion, diced
1/2  green pepper, diced
8  oz. cream cheese
2  c. shredded Monterey Jack cheese
16 to 20  oz. jar salsa

      Spread cream cheese in bottom of pie plate. Sprinkle on diced onion and pepper. Cover with salsa, then Monterey Jack cheese. Bake 25 minutes in 350 degrees; oven.  Serve with nacho chips.
