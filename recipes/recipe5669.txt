ROSE'S COLESLAW


1  head cabbage
1  carrot, grated

 
Dressing:
1/3  c. canola oil
1/2  c. apple cider vinegar
1/3 to 1/2  c. sugar
1  Tbsp. celery seed
  salt and pepper to taste
  onion powder to taste (optional)

	Finely slice cabbage with a sharp knife.  Do not use blender or chopper.  Put cabbage in a large bowl of water and refrigerate for several hours.  Drain water from cabbage.  Pat dry with paper towels.  Add carrots to cabbage.
	Prepare dressing. If needed, more sugar may be added to dressing. Pour over cabbage and mix well.
