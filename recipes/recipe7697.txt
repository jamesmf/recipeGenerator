SQUARE HAMBURGERS


2  lb. hamburger
2/3  c. dry bread crumbs
2/3  c. milk
1  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper
1/3  c. onion, chopped

 
Sauce:
1/4  c. onion, chopped
2  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  c. catsup
1/4  c. brown sugar

      Mix hamburger, bread crumbs, milk, salt, pepper and 1/3 cup onion.  Pat into 9 x 13-inch  pan.  Mix together remaining ingredients and pour over meat mixture.  Bake  at  350 degrees; for 30 minutes.  Cut into squares.
