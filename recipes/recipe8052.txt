MOM'S COMPANY BEEF CASSEROLE


1 (8 oz.)  pkg. noodles
1  lb. ground round
2 (8 oz.)  cans tomato sauce
1  c. cottage cheese
1 (8 oz.)  sq. Philadelphia Cream Cheese
1/4  c. sour cream
1/3  c. green onions, chopped
1  Tbsp bell pepper, minced

	Boil noodles; drain and rinse.	Brown meat lightly.  Add tomato sauce and set aside.  Mix cottage cheese, cream cheese, sour cream, onion and bell pepper with electric mixer until smooth.  Put 2/3 of noodles in 2 1/2 quart casserole.  Add all of cheese mixture (layer on top).  Add rest of noodles and meat (layer over top).  Cover and chill overnight.  Cook 1 hour at 375 degrees;, covered.
