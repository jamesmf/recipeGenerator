HONEY GLAZED HAM


1  boneless fully cooked ham 3-5 lbs.
1/2  cup water
1  cup honey
1/2  cup packed brown sugar
1  tsp. ground cloves
1/2  tsp. ground mustard

      Score the ham making diamond shapes 1/2 inch deep.  Place on a rack in a well greased foil lined roasting pan.  Add water to pan.  In a small bowl combine the honey, brown sugar, cloves and mustard.  Pour over ham.
      Bake uncovered at 350 degrees for 1 1/2 - 2 hours or until meat thermometer reads 140 degrees.  Baste with pan juices often. Add additional water if required.
