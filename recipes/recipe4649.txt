TUNA NOODLE SOUP


3  cans Campbell's Mushroom Soup
1  large bag egg noodles
3  cans of tuna (in water)
1  c milk
1  tbsp butter or margarine

      Boil bag of egg noodles.  Drain. Mix milk, butter, soup and tuna in a  pan.  Heat on medium heat for 3 minutes or until it is at a slight boil, mixing as it heats. Add mixture to noodles. Serve hot.
      You can also add peas and serve on toast.
