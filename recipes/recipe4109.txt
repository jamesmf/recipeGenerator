BLITZES


1  long loaf sandwich bread
2  egg yolks
1 1/2  c. melted margarine (cool)
3/4  c. sugar
1 1/2  tsp. cinnamon
1  (8 oz.) cream cheese (at room temperature)
1/2  c. sugar

      Cut crust off bread.  Roll thin with rolling pin.  Mix cream cheese, yolks and 1/2 cup sugar.  Beat with a spoon.  Spread mixture on bread.  Roll up.  Cut in thirds.  Dip in butter and roll in 3/4 cup sugar and cinnamon.  (Can be frozen; thaw first before baking.)  Bake at 350 degrees; for 10 minutes.
      Excellent with a cup of favorite tea.
