CHILI BEANS


1  lb. ground beef
1  pkg. McCormick chili mix
1  qt. home canned tomatoes
1/4  tsp. garlic powder
1  can kidney beans
1  c. hot water

      Brown ground beef and drain.  Add chili mix following the directions on package.  Sprinkle garlic powder over top.  Pour in kidney beans with a cup of hot water.  Bring to a slow simmer and serve.  Can use ground turkey.
