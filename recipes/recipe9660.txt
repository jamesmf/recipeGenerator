TURKEY CABBAGE CASSEROLE


1  medium head of cabbage cut into pieces
1  lb. ground turkey
1  cup onion & bell pepper mixture
1  can of Healthy Choice low sodium tomato soup

      Brown meat, onion & pepper together. Drain & cut cabbage. Layer cabbage, then meat, then cabbage again and pour soup on top. Bake at 350 degrees until cabbage is done (about 25-35 minutes).
