STUFFED SALMON


1/2  cup finely chopped onion
1  Tbsp. olive oil
1/4  cup fine, dry bread crumbs
1  Tbsp. water
2  tsp. finely shredded orange peel
4  (5-6 oz.) salmon fillets
1  tsp. lemon pepper

      Pre-heat oven to 450 degrees;F.
      Cook onion in oil 3 minutes.  Stir in bread crumbs, water and orange peel.  Make a pocket in each fillet by cutting a slit in the side about 2 inches long and almost through to the other side. Fill each pocket with 1/4 of the onion mixture.
      Place stuffed fillets in a baking pan.  Sprinkle with lemon pepper.  Bake for 4-6 minutes per 1/2-inch of thickness.
      Serves 4.
