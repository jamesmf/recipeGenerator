CABBAGE SPAGHETTI


1  lb. hamburg
2  large onions
3  Tbsp. oil
2  Tbsp. dried parsley
1/2  head cabbage
1/2  lb. spaghetti

      Brown hamburg, not fried, and 1 onion, chopped.  Cook spaghetti.  Cut cabbage longways as thin as possible to resemble spaghetti.  Put chopped second onion in pan; don't fry.  Lay sliced cabbage on top.  Lower heat; add parsley.  Stir to coat with oil. When cabbage is tender, add cooked drained spaghetti.  Stir to coat.  Let cook for 5 to 10 minutes.  Stir often over low heat. Serve with Parmesan cheese.
