TURKEY WITH CHESTNUT STUFFING


  20-22 lb. turkey

 
Stuffing:
3  loaves Italian bread, cubed  toasted
1  lb. butter or oleo
  or 3 lbs. unflavored croutons
6  c. chopped celery
1/2  c. chopped celery leaves
3  c. chopped onion
1/4  c. chopped parsley
1  tbsp. + 1 tsp. salt
1  tbsp. sage
1 1/2  tsp. bl. pepper
1  tsp. poultry seasoning
1  1/2  roasted, shelled chestnuts
2  lb. extra lean gd. beef
3/4  lb. pork sausage, not hot

      Sauté in butter all celery, onion, parsley and all seasoning. Mix well and set aside.  Brown & drain sausage.  Mix with gd. beef and croutons.  Pour sautéed mixture over meat & croutons.  Add chestnuts.  If too dry, add chicken broth.  Avoid making too soggy. Wash & drain turkey of all water.  Stuff all cavities.  Bake at 325 degrees; in low baking pan.  Cover with aluminum foil 6-7 hours.  Extra stuffing can be baked in casserole approx. 1 1/2 hrs.
      Note:  If fresh chestnuts are not available, you can use dry chestnuts soaked overnight than boiled 1 1/2 hr.  Use poultry pins to secure skin opening at cavities.
