MARINATED PORK STEAKS


4  pork steaks
1 small  bottle teriyaki sauce
1 small  bottle soy sauce
1 small  bottle Worcestershire sauce
  onion flakes
  garlic salt
  seasoned salt
  Tony Chachere's Cajun seasoning

      Salt lightly on both sides of meat with the garlic, seasoned salt and Cajun seasoning.  Sprinkle both sides with onion flakes. Coat thoroughly both sides with teriyaki sauce.  Coat thoroughly just one side with the soy sauce and Worcestershire sauce.  Put steaks side by side in airtight container and shake.  Turn over every hour for four hours.  Pour marinade over steaks on grill; cook slowly until tender and serve.
