QUICK JAMBALAYA


1  Tbsp. vegetable oil
1/2  cup green bell pepper, chopped
2  cups cooked rice
1 (14 oz.)  can tomatoes, undrained, chopped
1  cup medium salsa
1  tsp. salt
1/2  cup onion, chopped
2  cups chicken, cooked & chopped
1 (15 oz.)  can red kidney beans
1  tsp. dried thyme leaves

      In large skillet, combine oil, onion, & green pepper. Cook over low heat 5 minutes or until onion & peppers are tender, stirring occasionally. Stir in chicken, rice, beans, tomatoes, salsa, thyme and salt. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat; simmer 10-15 minutes or until flavors are blended.
