FLUFFY FRUIT SALAD


2 (20 oz.)  cans crushed pineapple
2/3  c. sugar
2  Tbsp. all-purpose flour
2  eggs, lightly beaten
1/4  c. orange juice
3  Tbsp. lemon juice
1  Tbsp. vegetable oil
2 (17 oz.)  cans fruit cocktail, drained
2 (11 oz.)  cans mandarin oranges, drained
2  bananas, sliced
1  c. heavy cream, whipped

	Drain pineapple, reserving 1 cup juice in a small saucepan. Set pineapple aside. To saucepan, add sugar, flour, eggs, orange juice, lemon juice and oil. Bring to boil, stirring constantly. Boil for 1 minute; remove from heat and let cool.
	 In a salad bowl, combine the pineapple, fruit cocktail, oranges and bananas. Fold in whipped cream and cooled sauce. Chill for several hours. Makes 12 to 16 servings.
