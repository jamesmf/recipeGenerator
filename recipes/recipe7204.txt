STRING PIE


8  oz. spaghetti, cooked and drained
1  small container cottage cheese
2  tsp. butter
1/3  c. Parmesan cheese
2  eggs
  parsley
  pepper
1/2  c. Mozzarella cheese, shredded
1  lb. hamburg, browned
1  jar spaghetti sauce

      Mix sauce and browned hamburg.  Put 1/2 of mixture in pie dish.  Put cooked spaghetti on top.  Spread cheese mixture on top. Cover with remainder of sauce.  Bake at 375 degrees; for 35 minutes.
