RANCH STYLE OYSTER CRACKERS


3/4  c vegetable oil
1 oz  package dry Ranch-style dressing mix
1/2  tsp dried dill weed
1/4  tsp lemon pepper
1/4  tsp garlic powder
12  oz pkg oyster crackers

      Preheat oven to 275 degrees;.  In a mixing bowl, whisk together vegetable oil, Ranch-style dressing mix, dill weed, lemon pepper and garlic powder.  Pour this mixture over the crackers in a medium bowl.  Stir until crackers are coated.  Arrange the crackers on a large baking sheet.  Bake in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes.
