EAST INDIAN DIP


1/2  c. orange marmalade
3  Tbsp. lemon juice
2  Tbsp. sugar
1  Tbsp. brown sugar
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. curry powder
1/4  tsp. ground ginger
3/4  c. sour cream

      Combine all ingredients except sour cream in saucepot; bring to a boil.  Simmer; stirring until mixture is blended. Remove from heat; cool.  Blend in sour cream.  Mixture thickens slightly on standing.  Cover and place in refrigerator.  Makes 1 cup.
