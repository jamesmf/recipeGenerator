SANTA FE STEW


1  lb. ground meat
1  pkg. Ranch dressing mix
1  pkg. taco seasoning mix
1  chopped medium onion
1  can kidney beans
2  cans pinto beans
1 large or 2 small  cans tomatoes
1  can whole kernel corn

      Brown the meat; drain off fat. Pour meat in a kettle or in crock-pot. Add the Ranch dressing mix, taco seasoning mix and onion. Add the kidney beans, pinto beans, tomatoes and corn. Heat it all together and enjoy.
