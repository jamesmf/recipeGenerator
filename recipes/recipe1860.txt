GUACAMOLE CHICKEN SALAD


1/2  c. mayonnaise or salad dressing
2  Tbsp. lemon juice
1/2  tsp. salt
3  c. chopped, cooked chicken
1  c. chopped celery
1  medium tomato, chopped
1  avocado, diced
  lettuce leaves

      Combine mayonnaise, lemon juice and salt; blend well.  Stir in chicken, celery, tomato and avocado; chill to blend flavors.  Serve on lettuce leaves.  Makes four servings.
