SHRIMP CASSEROLE


6 to 7  slices bread
1/2  lb. cheese (at least half Cheddar)
1 to 1 1/2  lb. shrimp
1/2  tsp. dry mustard
1/4  tsp. melted butter
3  eggs, beaten
1  pt. (16 oz.) milk
  dash of salt and pepper

      Cut crusts off bread and cut into small cubes.  Cut cheese into bite size pieces.  Place 1/3 of shrimp in casserole.  Top with 1/3 of bread cubes and 1/3 of cheese cubes.  Repeat 2 more times.  Pour butter over all.  Combine eggs, milk, mustard, salt and pepper.  Beat well.  Pour over all.  Cover and stand overnight or 3 hours.  Bake 1 hour at 350 degrees;.
