MEXICAN CHILI


2  Tbsp. vegetable oil
1  lb. ground beef
1  c. chopped onion
1/2  c. finely chopped green onion
1  c. tomatoes
1  tsp. salt
1  dash red pepper
1 to 2  Tbsp. chili powder
1  can kidney beans

      Brown ground beef; drain.  Saut√© onions in vegetable oil; add to meat.  Add tomatoes, salt, pepper and chili powder; bring to a boil.  Reduce heat, cover and simmer for 45 minutes.  Add kidney beans and cook for 20 to 30 minutes longer.
