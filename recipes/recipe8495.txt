SUMMER STEW


2  or 3 nice large green tomatoes
2  nice ripe tomatoes
2  green peppers
1  red pepper
4  or 5 medium potatoes, diced fine
2  medium onions, diced
1  stick oleo
  cooking oil
  salt and pepper to taste

      Chop vegetables into bite size pieces. Place all ingredients in heavy skillet and cook slow. You may want to boil potatoes a little before adding to the stew.
      Add a hot pepper  if you like it hot.
