OZARK HONEY OATMEAL COOKIES


1/2  c. shortening
1 1/4  c. sugar
2  eggs
1/3  c. honey
1  tsp. soda
1  tsp. salt
1 3/4  c. flour
1  c. rolled oats
  nuts, raisins or coconut (optional)

      Mix all ingredients.  Nuts, raisins or coconut may be added to mixture.  Drop by spoonfuls on cookie sheet.  Bake 10 minutes at 375 degrees;.
