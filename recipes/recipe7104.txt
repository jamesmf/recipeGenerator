POACHED SALMON WITH ZUCCHINI


4  salmon steaks
2  Tbsp. water
1/2  tsp. chicken stock or flavoring
3  medium zuchinnis (1 lb.)
1/4  tsp. salt, optional
3/4  tsp. minced fresh basil
1/2  c. white dry wine or skim milk

      Spray large saucepan with cooking spray.  Place salmon in saucepan.  Add milk or wine, water, and chicken flavoring or stock.  Cover and bring to a boil; reduce heat and simmer until salmon is done, about 6 minutes.  Cut zucchini into small strips and steam for 2-3 minutes.  Pour into a bowl and toss gently with basil.  Transfer to serving plates.  Top with salmon.  Garnish with sprigs of basil.
