SUGAR BACON


  brown sugar
  slab of bacon

      Lay out a sheet of wax paper.  Cover with brown sugar.  Batter both sides of bacon.  Lay out flat in an iron skillet.  Put oven at 350 degrees;.  Cook in skillet in the oven for 20 minutes.  When done, lay out flat on another piece of wax paper until cool.  Can cook eggs in drippings.
