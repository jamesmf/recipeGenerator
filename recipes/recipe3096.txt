BARBECUE SHRIMP


1  gal. shrimp, heads off
1  stick butter
  Tony's seasoning
  drizzle olive oil
  onion strips and bell pepper strips
3 to 4  Tbsp. chili powder
1  tsp. garlic, minced

	Dehead and wash shrimp well.  In a 9 x 13 pan, put in shrimp and the rest of ingredients.  Cover with foil and bake at 400 degrees; for about 1 hour, until backs lift off the shrimp.  Check every 15 minutes.  Shrimp will make water.  Eat with French bread.
