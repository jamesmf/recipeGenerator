HONEY-CINNAMON BREAD SPREAD


1/2 (8 oz.)  pkg. reduced-calorie cream cheese, softened
1/4  c. reduced-calorie soft margarine
1  Tbsp. honey
1/4  tsp. ground cinnamon

      Mix together.  Makes 11 (1-tablespoon) servings.
      Per Serving:  61 Calories, 6 g. Fat, 8 mg.  Cholesterol and 93 mg.  Sodium.
