SIRLOIN STEAKS WITH BLUE CHEESE BUTTER


4  top sirloin steaks
1/4  c butter, softened
1/4  c  blue cheese, crumbled
1  clove garlic, minced
1  tbsp green onions, sliced

      Trim excess fat.  Place on grill 4 inches from hot coals. Sear 3-5 minutes.  Turn steaks after cooking to your liking. Lightly score surface of steak.  Combine remaining ingredients. Spread steaks with blue cheese butter.
