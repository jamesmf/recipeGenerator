SPARERIBS, SAUERKRAUT AND POTATOES


2- 1/2  lb. spareribs
1  Tbsp. fat
1  tsp. salt
  dash pepper
1  1-lb.  can sauerkraut, drained
1  tbsp. brown sugar
4  potatoes, medium, whole
1 1/4  c. water

      Brown ribs in fat. Season with salt and pepper. Put sauerkraut in bottom of pot and sprinkle with brown sugar. Place ribs and potatoes over sauerkraut, add water. Cover and cook until ribs are tender. Serves 4.
