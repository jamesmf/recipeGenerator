TAFFY APPLE SALAD


2  eggs
1  c. sugar
2  Tbsp. flour
2  Tbsp. vinegar or apple cider
6  to 8 chopped apples
20  oz. can of crushed pineapple, drained
2 1/3  c. dry roasted peanuts, chopped
16  oz. Cool Whip

      Mix eggs, sugar, flour, and vinegar or apple cider in  medium saucepan.  Bring to a boil.  Remove from heat and combine with apples, pineapple, peanuts and cool whip. Store in refrigerator for 24 hours before serving.  This is a double batch.  Makes 10 to 12 servings.
