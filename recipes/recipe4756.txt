SHRIMP MACARONI SALAD


3/4  c. uncooked shell macaroni
3/4  lb. cooked, cleaned medium shrimp
1  c. chopped cauliflower
1  c. sliced celery
1/2  c. chopped onion
1/2  c. chopped green pepper
1/2  c. low calorie French dressing
1/4  c. chopped fresh parsley
1/4  c. chopped sweet pickle
1/4  c. low calorie mayonnaise
1  Tbsp. lemon juice
1/2  tsp. celery seeds
1/4  tsp. pepper

      Cook macaroni according to package directions. Drain. Combine all ingredients; toss to mix. Cover and refrigerate at least 8 hours. Serve cold. Makes about 12 (3/4 cup) servings.
