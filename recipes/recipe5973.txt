HOT CHICKEN SALAD


3/4  c. mayo
2  c. cooked diced chicken
2  c. diced celery (cook 5 minutes.)
3/4  c. water chestnuts
1/2  tsp. accent
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. onion juice
1  c. grated american cheese
1  c. crushed plain potato chips

      Lightly mix all ingredients except cheese and chips.  Pour into 8 x 8 baking dish.  Sprinkle with cheese then chips.  Bake at 300 degrees for 30 minutes.
