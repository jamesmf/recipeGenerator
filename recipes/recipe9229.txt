PORK ROAST WITH VEGETABLES


1  pork roast
  potatoes, sliced
  onion, sliced
1  can sauerkraut, undrained
  salt and pepper
  dijon mustard

      Grease baking dish; layer sliced potatoes, then sliced onions, then sauerkraut.  Sprinkle roast with salt and pepper. Coat with mustard.  Place roast on kraut.  Cover tightly with foil.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 2 1/2 to 3 hours or until done.
