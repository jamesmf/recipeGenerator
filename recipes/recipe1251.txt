THICK STRAWBERRY SHAKE


1/2  c. fresh strawberries (or frozen, unsweetened)
1/2  c. skim milk
3  packets Equal
1  tsp. lemon juice

      Combine all ingredients in blender.  Blend at high speed until smooth, about 1 minute.  Serve immediately.  If using fresh berries, add 3 ice cubes to make a thicker shake.  Makes approximately one (12 ounce) serving; 79 calories.  Diabetic exchange, 1 fruit and 1/2 nonfat milk.
