REUBEN CASSEROLE I


1  c. sour cream
1/2  c. Thousand Island dressing
1  large onion, chopped
1  (2 lb.) bag sauerkraut, drained
1  lb. corned beef, shredded
12  oz. Swiss cheese, shredded
10  slices rye bread, ground into crumbs
2  sticks margarine

	Mix sour cream, Thousand Island dressing, onion and sauerkraut well. Put mixture in a 9 x 13-inch greased pan. Layer shredded corned beef over kraut mixture. Add the shredded Swiss cheese. Top with rye bread crumbs. Drizzle the melted margarine over the top. Bake for 1 hour at 350 degrees;.
