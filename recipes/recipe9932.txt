BLENDER CORN PUDDING


1  can whole kernel corn, drained
3  Tbsp. flour
2  eggs
4  Tbsp. sugar
2  c. milk
1/2  stick margarine
1  tsp. salt

      In blender (or food processor) combine eggs, flour, sugar, salt and one cup milk.  Blend until smooth.  Add drained corn. Blend until almost no chunks remain.  In a 2-quart baking dish, melt butter.  Add blended corn mixture and other cup of milk. Stir with a spoon.  Bake at 375 degrees; about 45 minutes.  Stir twice during the first 20 minutes, scraping sides and bottom of dish.
