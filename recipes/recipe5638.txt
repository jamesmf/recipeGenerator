CRANBERRY SALAD


2  C. cranberries
2  oranges
1  C. sugar
1 sm.  pkg. orange gelatin
1  C. hot water
3/4  C. nut meats
1  apple, chopped fine

      Grind cranberries.  Extract the juice from the oranges and grind the rind of 1/2 orange.  Mix the rind, sugar and cranberries together and let stand.
      Mix the juice and water with the gelatin.  Add the cranberries when the gelatin starts to congeal.  Add the nuts and apple and chill.
