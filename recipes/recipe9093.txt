CHICKEN LOAF


2  c. chicken, cooked and chopped
2  Tbsp. Knox gelatine
2  sticks celery
1  pt. Miracle Whip salad dressing
1  can garden peas (with juice)
1  c. chicken stock
1  c. toasted almonds
2  Tbsp. pickle relish
  red pepper to taste
  salt to taste
  pepper to taste

      Soak gelatine in stock. Heat stock and melt gelatine. Mix remaining ingredients. Pour in loaf pan or mold.
