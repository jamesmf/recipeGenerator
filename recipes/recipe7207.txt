SUSIE'S CHICKEN DIVAN


2  lb. boneless chicken breast
2  boxes Stove Top stuffing (chicken)
16  oz. shredded Cheddar cheese
2  boxes frozen broccoli
2  can cream of chicken soup
1 small  jar mayonnaise

      Cook chicken until done.  Cut up into chunks.  Cook broccoli according to directions on box.  In a 9 x 13 pan, spread stuffing on bottom.  Add broccoli.  Put chicken on top of that.  Mix soup and mayonnaise together.  Pour over chicken.  Sprinkle cheese on top.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes.
