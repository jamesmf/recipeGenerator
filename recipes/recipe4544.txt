24 HOUR COLESLAW


1  head cabbage, shredded
1  green pepper, chopped
1  onion, chopped
1  c. vinegar
3/4  c. sugar
3/4  c. Wesson oil
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. celery seed
1  tsp. mustard seed

      Mix vinegar, sugar, oil, salt, celery seed and mustard seed; bring mixture to boil.  Pour over cabbage, onion and green pepper. Cover and refrigerate at once.  Serve the next day.
