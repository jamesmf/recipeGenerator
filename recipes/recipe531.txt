LEMON ICEBOX PIE


1  can sweetened condensed milk
3  eggs, separated
  juice of 3 lemons
  rind of 1 lemon, grated
1/3  c. sugar

 
Graham Cracker Crust:
1 1/4  c. cracker crumbs
1/4  c. sugar
6  Tbsp. melted butter

      Mix milk, egg yolks, lemon juice and rind together.  Beat egg whites until stiff and add 1/3 cup sugar.  Fold egg whites into milk mixture.  Spoon into graham cracker crust and sprinkle top with graham cracker crumbs.  Bake at 325 degrees; for 10 minutes.  Chill and serve.
      Graham Cracker Crust:  Mix ingredients.  Press into pie plate and bake at 375 degrees; for 6 to 9 minutes.
