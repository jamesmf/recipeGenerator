CHICKEN AND BROCCOLI


3  chicken breasts
2  (10 oz.) frozen chopped broccoli
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  can cream of chicken soup
2/3  c. mayonnaise
3/4  tsp. curry powder
1 1/2  tsp. lemon juice
1/2  c. grated Cheddar cheese

      Boil and debone chicken. Arrange half the uncooked broccoli in casserole dish. Put layer of chicken. Pour half mixture over broccoli and chicken. Repeat layers using remaining ingredients. Top with buttered Ritz crackers (3/4 package Ritz crackers and 3/4 to 1 stick melted margarine). Bake 30 to 45 minutes in 350 degrees; oven.
