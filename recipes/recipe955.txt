SCALLOPS ORIENTAL


2  lb. fresh or frozen scallops
1/4  c. honey
1/4  c. prepared mustard
1  tsp. curry powder
1  tsp. lemon juice
  lemon wedges

      Preheat broiler 5 minutes.  Rinse fresh scallops in cold water or thaw frozen scallops.  Place in baking dish.
      In saucepan, combine honey, mustard, curry powder and lemon juice.  Brush scallops with sauce.  Broil 4 inches from flame 5 to 8 minutes or until browned.  Garnish with lemon wedges.  Yields 8 servings.
