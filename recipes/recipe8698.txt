PORK CHOPS, ETC.


  thin sliced pork chops
  olive oil
  Italian bread crumbs
  Zatarain's Fish Fry mixture

	Trim as much fat as possible from the pork chops. Brush with olive oil.
	 In a shallow dish, combine half bread crumbs and half Zatarain's. Coat the pork chops thoroughly. Place on a greased cookie sheet and broil for approximately 4 minutes on each side. Broiling time should be adjusted to the thickness of the pork chops.
	Alternative Dish:  Follow recipe with chicken tenders or fish.
