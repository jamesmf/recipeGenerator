STRAWBERRY SHORTCAKE


1  box butter flavored cake mix (yellow)
2  (12 oz.) bowls Cool Whip
3  pt. boxes sweetened strawberries

      Bake cake as directed in 2 (9-inch) cake pans.  When done let cool on plate.  Take fork, poking holes in first layer.  Add 1 1/4 boxes of strawberries.  Carefully spoon on enough Cool Whip to cover cake.  Add second layer on top; repeat strawberries and Cool Whip to completely cover cake.  Take remaining 1/2 box strawberries and dribble over sides and top of cake.  Refrigerate until ready to serve.
