CRAB FETTUCCINI


6  oz. fettuccini
1/4  c. butter
2  cloves garlic, minced
1  c. half 'n half
8  oz. crab flakes
1/2  c. Parmesan cheese
1/8  tsp. black pepper

      Cook fettuccini and drain.  Lightly brown garlic in butter. Add half 'n half, crab and pepper.  Heat 3 minutes or just until bubbly, stirring occasionally.  Add Parmesan cheese and stir one minute.  Remove from heat and toss.
