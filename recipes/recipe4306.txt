ORIENTAL SLAW


1 large  pkg. slaw mix
6  green onions, chopped
1  pkg. sliced almonds, toasted
2  pkg. Ramen noodles, toasted
  Dressing

 
Dressing:
4  Tbsp. sugar
6  Tbsp. red wine vinegar
4  Tbsp. oil
1  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. black pepper

      Follow directions carefully.
      Ahead of time, toast almonds and crushed noodles (discard noodle seasoning) in 350 degrees; oven 10 to 15 minutes. Stir every 3 minutes to prevent burning. Let cool and may be stored in airtight container for later use.
      Mix dressing ingredients together and toss everything together just before serving.
