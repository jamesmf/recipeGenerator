CHOCOLATE RICE KRISPIES SQUARES


1 (12 oz.)  pkg. chocolate chips
1  stick butter
1/2  c. corn syrup
1  c. powdered sugar
2  tsp. vanilla
4  c. Rice Krispies

      Cook first 3 ingredients over low heat until smooth, then remove from heat.  Add 1 cup powdered sugar, vanilla and Rice Krispies.  Put in well greased pan and cool in refrigerator until firm.
