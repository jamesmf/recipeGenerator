BOLOGNESE DI BRAD


2  lb. ground beef (92% lean)
  Pasta (Your choice cooked as directed on package)
1/2  lb. sausage of your choice cut into small pieces
1  onion, diced
3/4  green pepper, diced
3/4  red pepper, diced
1 (28 oz.)  can tomato sauce
1 (14 oz.)  can tomato sauce
1 (14 oz.)  can diced tomatoes with garlic & onion flavor
1 (14 oz.)  can diced tomatoes with green chilies
1 (14 oz.)  can tomato paste

 
Seasonings (Divide Most of These in Half):
3  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
2  Tbsp. garlic powder
2  Tbsp. onion powder
2  Tbsp. crushed red pepper
2  tsp. Lea & Perrins sweet 'n spicy steak sauce
1  Tbsp. Italian seasoning
1  tsp. red hot (or comparable seasoning)
1/2  c. sugar

      In a large saucepan brown ground beef and sausage with the onion, peppers and all of the seasonings divided in half (except for the red hot, sugar and Italian seasoning).  Once thoroughly cooked, drain the fat (if any).  Now, add remaining ingredients and simmer for at least 40 minutes covered on low heat stirring occasionally.  Serve over choice of pasta.
      Footnote:  A tasty treat to go along with this is a nice French loaf of bread sliced in half with butter on both halves, garlic powder, paprika, and a dash of salt and cover with Mozzarella cheese.  Fold back together, roll in foil and bake at 350 degrees; for about 30 minutes.
