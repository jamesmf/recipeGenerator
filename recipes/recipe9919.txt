CHEESE AND SOUR CREAM POTATOES


2  lb. frozen hash brown potatoes
1  can cream of chicken soup
2  c. (1 pt.) sour cream
2  c. grated Cheddar cheese
1  medium onion, chopped
1  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
1/2  c. butter, melted
1  c. bread crumbs

      Combine potatoes, soup, sour cream, cheese, onion and salt and pepper and put in a 9 x 13 inch greased glass dish.  Mix melted butter and bread crumbs together and spread over top of potato mixture.  Bake for 45 minutes in a preheated 350 degrees; oven.
