FRUIT TEA SANDWICHES


1  c. butter or margarine
1 1/2  c. all-purpose flour
1  c. sugar
1/8  tsp. salt
4  large eggs
1  Tbsp. almond extract
3/4  c. peach preserves or spreadable fruit
  sifted powdered sugar
  mandarin orange sections

      Beat butter and sugar at high speed with electric mixture until creamy.  Add flour and salt, mixing well.  Add almond extract and eggs.  Beat at medium speed for 10 minutes.  (Necessary to get proper texture.)  Spoon batter into greased 15 x 10 x 1-inch jelly roll pan.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 18 to 20 minutes.  Cool in pan on wire rack for 10 minutes.  Remove from pan and cool completely.  Cut cake with a 2-inch heart shaped cutter.  Spread 2 teaspoons preserves on half the hearts.  Cover with remaining hearts. Sprinkle with powdered sugar and top with mandarin orange sections. Yield:  16 to 18 sandwiches.
