MOJADAS
(Colombian Style Cheese Biscuits)  

1  lb. sharp Cheddar, grated
3  c. Bisquick

      Mix together and add enough water (about 1 cup) to make a dough.  Roll dough in balls about the size of golf balls.  Bake on ungreased cookie sheet at 375 degrees; until golden brown, about 25 minutes.  Serve warm.  Can be made ahead and reheated.
