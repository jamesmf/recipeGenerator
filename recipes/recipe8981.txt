BEEF TACO BAKE


1  lb. ground beef
1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can tomato soup
1  c. salsa
1/2  c. milk
6  flour tortillas or 8 corn tortillas, cut into 1-inch pieces
1  c. shredded Cheddar cheese

      In skillet over medium heat, cook beef until browned, stirring to separate meat. Pour off fat. Add soup, salsa, milk, tortillas and 1/2 the cheese. Spoon into 2-quart shallow baking dish. COVER. Bake at 400 degrees; for 30 minutes or until hot. Sprinkle remaining cheese. Serves 4.
