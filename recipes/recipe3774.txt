STUFFED MUSHROOMS


1  lb. fresh mushrooms with stems
1  egg
  Italian dressing
  Grated cheese
1  chopped onion
  Minced garlic
  Parsley
  Black pepper
  Bread crumbs

      Remove stems from mushrooms.  In blender, add stems and remaining ingredients.  Stuff mushroom caps.  Place on a baking sheet.  Sprinkle oil lightly over mushrooms.  Bake 350 degrees; about 45 mins.  Top each with mozzarella and bake additional 10-15 mins.
