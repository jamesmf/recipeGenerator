LAYERED SALAD


1  head lettuce
1  medium onion
1  cucumber
1  can green sweet peas
4  large boiled eggs
1  c. mayonnaise
3  Tbsp. sugar
  bacon bits
  shredded cheese

	Break lettuce in a large flat bottom dish. Layer onions and cucumber over lettuce. Drain peas and layer over salad. Slice boiled eggs and layer on top. Mix mayonnaise and sugar. Layer over eggs. Sprinkle top with bacon bits and shredded cheese.
