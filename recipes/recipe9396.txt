FETTUCCINI WITH ASPARAGUS AND SHRIMP


1  lb. large shrimp, shelled and deveined
1  lemon, juice and grated rind
1/4 to 1/2  tsp. hot red pepper flakes
1/2  tsp. salt
1 16  oz. pkg. fettuccini or linguine
1  Tbsp. vegetable oil
1  medium onion, diced
2  red peppers, sliced in strips
1/2  tsp. salt
1  Tbsp. vegetable oil
1  lb. fresh asparagus, cut in 3 inch pieces
1/2  c. water
1  Tbsp. soy sauce

      In a bowl, mix shrimp with lemon juice, pepper flakes and salt.  Cook fettuccini according to package directions.  Keep warm in pot.  Heat oil and stir-fry onion, pepper strips and salt until peppers are tender-crisp; remove to bowl.  In same pan, heat oil and add asparagus and shrimp mixture.  Stir-fry until asparagus are tender-crisp and shrimp turn opaque, about 3 minutes.  Stir in pepper mixture and water; heat through.  To serve, toss fettuccini with shrimp mixture and soy sauce.  Sprinkle with grated lemon rind.  Serves 4.
