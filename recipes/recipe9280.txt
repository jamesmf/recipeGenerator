RICE AND BEEF CASSEROLE


2  c. rice
1 1/2  lb. ground beef
1/2  c. onion, chopped
1/2  c. celery, chopped
1/2  c. green pepper, chopped
10  oz. (1 can) soup, cream of chicken
1  lb. Velveeta cheese
1  tsp. garlic salt

      Cook rice according to package directions and set aside.
      In a skillet, brown together:  beef, onion, celery and green pepper.  Drain and set aside.
      In a sauce pan, mix together:  soup, cheese and garlic salt. Cook over medium heat until cheese melts.  Pour cheese mixture over rice and mix well.  Add ground beef mixture and mix well. Pour into a 9x13-inch casserole dish.
      Bake at 350 degrees; for 20 minutes.  Serve hot.
