BEAN SALAD


1 14 1/2  oz. can Seasoned French style green beans
1 14 1/2  oz. can French style green beans
1 8 1/2  oz. can baby peas
1 small  jar of pimento, chopped
2  stalks celery, chopped
1  medium onion, chopped

      Drain beans, peas and pimento.  Mix all ingredients together.
Dressing:
1/4  c. salad oil
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  c. vinegar
1  c. sugar
1  Tbsp. water

      Mix well and add to the above.  Let stand at least 3 hours or overnight.  Cover and refrigerate.  Drain well before serving.
