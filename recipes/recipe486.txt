GEMS


1/2  c. butter
1  c. sugar
2  eggs
1  tsp. vanilla
2  c. flour
2 1/2  tsp. baking powder
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  c. milk

 
Topping:
3  Tbsp. sugar
1 1/2  tsp. cinnamon
1/2  c. chopped nuts (optional)

      Cream butter and sugar together.  Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition.  Stir in vanilla.  Sift dry ingredients together and add alternately with milk.  Spoon into baking cups.
      Topping:  Blend sugar, cinnamon and nuts (optional) together and sprinkle on top of batter filled cups.  Bake at 375 degrees; for 120 minutes.
