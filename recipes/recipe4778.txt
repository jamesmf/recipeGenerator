CRUNCHY PEA SALAD


1 (10 oz.)  pkg. frozen peas, thawed
1 (8 oz.)  can sliced water chestnuts, drained
1  c. thinly sliced celery
1/2  c. sliced green onions
1/4  c. mayonnaise
1/4  c. sour cream
1/2  tsp. seasoned salt (optional)

      In a bowl, combine the first 4 ingredients. In a small bowl, combine mayonnaise, sour cream and seasoned salt if desired. Mix well. Add to pea mixture; toss to coat. Chill until serving.
