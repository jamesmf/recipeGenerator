BILL'S BARBECUE SAUCE


2 (15 oz.)  cans tomato sauce
1/2  c. minced onions
1/3  c. Worcestershire sauce
2  Tbsp. soy sauce
1/2  c. brown sugar
1/4  c. vinegar
1/4  c. water (to thin)
1  Tbsp. chopped garlic
1/3  c. molasses
1  tsp. Tabasco
1  tsp. chili powder
1/4  tsp. cayenne
1  Tbsp. liquid smoke

      Combine first 9 ingredients and simmer for 30 minutes. Then add remaining ingredients.
       This is seasoned for a "medium" hot sauce. Double Tabasco, cayenne and chili powder for "hot".
