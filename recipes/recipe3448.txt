GUACAMOLE


3  ripe avocados
1  ripe tomato
1 large  clove garlic, minced
1  medium onion, grated
  juice of 1 lemon
2  Tbsp. olive oil
2  dashes Tabasco sauce
  pinch of salt

      Peel avocados and tomato; mash together, leaving small lumps. Add all other ingredients. Mix well with hand. Add salt and stir with a spoon. Serve cold with taco Doritos.
