CROCK-POT BARBECUE SPARERIBS


5  lb. spareribs
1/2  tsp. hickory smoke salt
2  Tbsp. vinegar
  liquid smoke and salt
1/2  c. catsup
1/2  c. steak sauce
1/4  tsp. salt
1  tsp. minced onion
2  Tbsp. brown sugar

      Place spareribs on rack in baking pan. Brown in a 400 degrees; oven for 15 minutes. Turn and brown on the other side for 10 or 15 minutes. Drain fat. Put ribs in crock-pot. Combine remaining ingredients and pour over ribs. Cover and cook on low 6 to 8 hours. Makes 5 to 6 servings.
