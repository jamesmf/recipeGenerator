MOCHA CAPPUCCINO


1  c. hot strong coffee
2  tsp. unsweetened cocoa
1/2  c. hot skim milk, divided
2  Tbsp. Cool Whip, divided
1/4  oz. semi-sweet chocolate

      Combine coffee and cocoa, stirring until cocoa is dissolved; pour into 2 cappuccino or coffee cups.  Add 1/4 cup skim milk to each cup and stir.  Top each with 1 tablespoon Cool Whip and sprinkle with grated chocolate.  Makes 2 servings.
