POTATO SALAD


2 1/2  c. sugar
1  c. water
3/4  c. white vinegar
1  tsp. pepper
  celery salt
4  eggs, beaten
4  Tbsp. flour
2  tsp. salt
  dill seed
2  Tbsp. mustard
1  qt. Miracle Whip
10  lb. potatoes, cooked and peeled
12  to 18 boiled eggs

      Cook the first 9 ingredients together and stir until thick. Remove from heat. Stir in the mustard. When cool, add the Miracle Whip.
