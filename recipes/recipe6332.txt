TOMATO PARMESAN SALAD


1 1/3  cup vegetable or olive oil
1  cup red wine vinegar
2  cloves garlic, minced
  salt and pepper to taste
2  bunches romaine lettuce, torn
1  head iceberg lettuce, torn
2  small red onions, thinly sliced
2  large tomatoes, diced
4  oz. diced pimentos, drained
2/3  cup shredded Parmesan cheese

      In a small bowl or jar with a tight fitting lid, combine oil, vinegar, garlic, salt and pepper; set aside. In a large bowl, combine all remaining ingredients. Chill until ready to serve. Just before serving, whisk or shake dressing; pour over salad, toss.
