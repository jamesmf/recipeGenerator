SWEET ONION SALAD


1  c. water
1/2  c. sugar
1/4  c. light vinegar
3  to 4 sweet onions, thinly sliced*
1/2  c. mayonnaise
1/2  c. sour cream
  celery seed (to taste)

      *Vidalia, Texas Sweet or Florida Sweet.
      Combine water, sugar and vinegar in bowl.  Mix until sugar dissolves.  Add onions.  Mix well.  Chill for 24 hours.  Drain.
       Combine mayonnaise and sour cream in small bowl.  Add to onions.  Mix well.  Sprinkle with celery seed.  Chill until serving time.  May substitute additional mayonnaise for sour cream.
      Makes 6 servings.
