MEAT BALL STEW


2  c. Kellogg's Corn Flakes cereal
1  egg
1 (10 1/2 oz.)  can condensed beef broth, divided
1/2  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper
1  lb. ground beef
2  Tbsp. vegetable oil
1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can condensed tomato soup
1/2  c. water
1/4  tsp. thyme
2  c. frozen sliced carrots
1  medium onion, sliced into rings
1 (1 lb.)  can whole white potatoes, drained
  dried parsley flakes

      Measure Corn Flakes into large bowl.  Crush slightly.  Stir into egg, 1/4 cup beef broth, salt and pepper.  Beat well and add ground beef.  Mix well.  Make into balls and brown meat balls in oil in frying pan over medium heat.  Pour off excess fat.  Stir in the remaining beef broth, tomato soup, water, thyme, carrots, onion rings and potatoes.  Cover and cook over medium heat about 25 minutes or until meat balls are thoroughly cooked.  Pour into individual bowls.  Sprinkle with parsley flakes.
