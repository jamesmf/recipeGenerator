POTATO SOUP


4  large potatoes (grated)
1  small onion
1  pkg. thin sliced smoked ham
5  c. water
1  pt. half and half
2  qt. milk
1  lb. frozen hash brown potatoes
  salt and pepper to taste
1/2  stick oleo or butter

      Grate potatoes and onion.  Cook 20 minutes with water and ham. Add remaining ingredients.  Simmer 2 hour.  Add salt and pepper to taste.  Serve hot with crackers of your choice.
