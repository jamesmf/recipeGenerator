SPAGHETTI SUPERB


1  lb. ground beef
1/2  c. chopped onion
1/4  c. green pepper or pimento
1  c. cream of mushroom soup
1  c. tomato soup
1  c. water
1  clove garlic, minced
1/2  lb. spaghetti, cooked and drained
1  c. shredded sharp cheese

      Lightly brown meat.  Add onion and pepper.  Cook until tender. Add rest of ingredients except cheese.  Place in 3-quart casserole. Top with rest of cheese.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes or until cheese is melted and bubbly.  Serves 6 or 7. May be frozen.
