SPINACH MARIA


2  pkg. spinach
1  medium onion
1  Tbsp. butter
8  oz. cream cheese
1/2 to 3/4  tsp. cayenne pepper
3/4 to 1  c. Parmesan cheese

      Cook spinach according to package. Drain. Saut onion in butter and add cream cheese and Parmesan cheese. Cook until cream cheese is melted. Add cayenne pepper. Mix in spinach. Put in baking dish. Cook 30 minutes at 350 degrees;.
