LASAGNE


1  lb. ground beef
1  lb. pork sausage (ground)
1 big  jar Ragu (green pepper)
1  can tomato sauce (add oregano and parsley to sauce)
1  box lasagne noodles
2  pkg. Mozzarella cheese
1  large container Ricotta cheese
1 large  pkg. Provolone cheese

      Layer sauce, noodles, Ricotta, Mozzarella, sauce, etc. Bake until brown and bubbly.
