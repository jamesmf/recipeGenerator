POTATO SALAD


  new potatoes, cooked, peeled and cut in wedges
6  eggs, hard boiled
1/2  bunch celery
1  bunch green onion, chopped very fine
  salt, to taste
  pepper, to taste
  Wishbone salad dressing
  sour cream
  mayonnaise

      Mix potatoes, eggs, celery, and green onion.  Add salt, pepper, and dressing while still hot.  Put in the refrigerator overnight.  Before serving add equal amounts of sour cream and mayonnaise.  Chill and serve.
