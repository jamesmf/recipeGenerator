MICROWAVED HOT TURKEY SALAD


2 1/2  c. turkey, cooked and cubed
1 (8 oz.)  can water chestnuts, drained and sliced
2  pimientos, chopped
1/2  c. slivered almonds
1/2  c. sliced mushrooms
2  Tbsp. lemon juice
1 1/2  c. mayonnaise
  salt and pepper to taste
1/2  c. grated Cheddar cheese
1/2  c. canned French fried onions

      In a 2-quart casserole, combine turkey, water chestnuts, pimientos, almonds, mushrooms, lemon juice and mayonnaise.  Add salt and pepper to taste.  Top with cheese and onions.  Microwave, covered, on High for 8 to 10 minutes, giving dish a 1/4 turn after 4 minutes.  Serve hot.  Serves 6.
