QUICK PEPPER STEAK


1  medium onion
1  bell pepper
2  cans whole tomatoes
2  c. cubed meat
1 small  can mushrooms
1  Tbsp. oil
1  Tbsp. flour
  season to taste

	Saut onions and bell pepper in oil. Add meat and fry lightly. Sprinkle flour over mixture and stir to coat. Add tomatoes and mushrooms. Cover and simmer for about 30 minutes.
