BURRITO SUPREME


8  large flour tortillas
  Pam cooking spray
1  lb. ground beef or turkey
1  pkg. burrito seasoning mix
1  can crushed tomatoes
  onions (optional)
  sour cream
  shredded cheese
  picante sauce
1 large  can Wolf Brand chilli
  garlic powder

      Heat oven to 350 degrees;.  Prepare meat according to package, browning meat; drain.  After draining, add onions and crushed tomatoes.  Simmer for 10 minutes.  Drain excess liquids.  Fill each tortilla shell.  Roll and place in square baking pan sprayed with Pam.  Heat chilli in pan on top of stove.  Cover rolled tortillas with chilli; top with shredded cheese.  Heat in oven until cheese is melted.  Remove from oven.  Cool 5 to 10 minutes. Serve with sour cream and picante sauce.  Makes 4 to 8 servings. Enjoy.
