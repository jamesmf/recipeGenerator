CROCK-POT CHICKEN AND RICE CASSEROLE


1 small  can cream of chicken soup
1 small  can cream of celery soup
1 small  can cream of mushroom soup
4  large chicken breasts
1/2  c. diced celery
1  c. Minute rice

      Mix  in  crock-pot  the 3 cans of soup and rice.  Place the chicken on top of the mixture, then add the diced celery. Cook for 3 hours on high or 4 hours on low.  Makes 4 servings.
      More rice, about 1/2 cup, and 2 additional chicken breasts may be added to make 6 servings.
