SWEET POTATO CASSEROLE


3  c. mashed sweet potatoes
1  c. sugar
1/4  c. milk
1/2  c. margarine
2  eggs
1  tsp. vanilla
1  c. coconut

 
Topping:
1  c. chopped pecans
1/2  c. margarine
1  c. brown sugar
1/2  c. flour

      Mix first 7 ingredients in shallow dish.
      Add topping.  Mix topping ingredients and spread over casserole.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 20 minutes.
