PINEAPPLE AND CREAM CHEESE SALAD


6  pineapple slices
1  c. cream cheese
  purple grape juice
  French dressing
  lettuce leaves

      Work enough grape juice into the cream cheese to soften. Make into balls with the hands or with butter paddle. Place a slice of pineapple on a lettuce leaf. Put a cheese ball on top and pour grape juice and French dressing over all.
