ONION SOUP


6  large white sweet onions, thinly sliced
3  Tbsp. butter
6  bouillon cubes
6  c. hot water
1 1/2  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/2  tsp. pepper
6  slices French bread or toast
1/4  c. Parmesan cheese

	Brown onions in butter in a large deep skillet until tender. Add dissolved bouillon water and seasoning. Heat thoroughly. Place a slice of toasted bread in each soup bowl and pour soup over it. Sprinkle with cheese. Yield: 6 servings.
