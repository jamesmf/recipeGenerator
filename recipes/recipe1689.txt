YAMS AND APPLES


6  to 8 yams
5  to 6 Pippin or Golden Delicious apples
1/2  c. sugar
1  c. water
1/4  c. margarine
1 1/2  Tbsp. cornstarch
1/2  tsp. salt

      Boil yams until barely tender (whole or cut widthwise in big pieces).  Peel.  Cool.  Slice and alternate yams and peeled apple rings in margarined casserole dish.  Boil together:  sugar, water, margarine, cornstarch and salt until it begins to thicken.  Pour over yams and apples and bake, covered, at 350 degrees; for 1 hour.  Serves 8 to 10.
