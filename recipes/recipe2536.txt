SEAFOOD CHOWDER


3  cans Campbell's chunky clam chowder soup
1  lb. (15 to 20) large shrimp, shelled and deveined
1  medium bay leaf
1/4  tsp. hot pepper sauce
1  lb. whitefish, cut in 2-inch pieces

      In large saucepan, combine first 4 ingredients.  Cook over low heat 10 minutes; stir now and then.  Add whitefish; cook 5 minutes more.  Remove bay leaf.  Makes about 9 1/2 cups.
