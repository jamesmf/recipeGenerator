BAKED CHICKEN SALAD


6  C. chopped cooked chicken
1/2  C. toasted sliced almonds
3  tsp. lemon juice
1/2  C. minced onion
1/4  tsp. pepper
3  C. chopped celery
1 1/2  C. chopped mushrooms
2  C. cooked rice
1  can mushroom soup
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  can mushroom soup
1 soup  can salad dressing
2  oz. Amaretto (optional)
3  C. grated cheddar cheese
3  C. crushed potato chips

      Combine  the first 8 ingredients.  Mix together the soups, salad dressing and Amaretto.  Combine with chicken mixture.
      Top with:  3 C. grated cheddar cheese; top cheese with 3 C. crushed potato chips.
      Bake at 350 degrees for 1 1/2 hours or to heat thoroughly.
