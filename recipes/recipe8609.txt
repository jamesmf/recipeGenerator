BEATS ME! CHICKEN


5  or 6 pieces of chicken
1  can whole cranberries
1  envelope dry onion soup mix
1  bottle French dressing, fat free

      Place skinless chicken in a 9 x 13-inch pan.
      In a bowl mix cranberries, onion soup mix and French dressing. Pour over chicken.
      Cover and bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour and 15 minutes. Serve with cooked rice.
