SALMON LOAF


1 (16 oz.)  can salmon (reserve juice)
1  Tbsp. grated onion
1  Tbsp. lemon juice
1/2  tsp. celery salt
1/4  tsp. garlic salt
1  c. cracker crumbs
  Tabasco to taste
1  egg
  milk

      Put milk, onion, lemon juice, Tabasco, celery salt, garlic salt, salt, pepper, and egg in a bowl. Add salmon that has been boned and dark skin removed. Flake with a fork. Add oleo and crumbs. If dry, add salmon juice. Pour into a buttered casserole dish and bake uncovered at 350 degrees for 45 minutes. Serves 4-6.
