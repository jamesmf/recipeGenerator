THREE BEAN SALAD


1/2  c. vinegar
1/4  c. oil
2  Tbsp. sugar
1/4  c. sliced green onion
1  tsp. salt
  dash of pepper
  dash of garlic powder
1 (16 oz.)  can drained green beans
1 (16 oz.)  can drained chickpeas
1 (16 oz.)  can drained red kidney beans

      Mix vinegar, oil, sugar, salt, pepper and garlic powder.  Add beans and onions; toss well.  Cover and refrigerate 3 hours or overnight, tossing occasionally.  Serve on lettuce.  Makes 8 or 10 servings.
