CALIFORNIA SUNSHINE COOKIES


1/4  c. butter
1  c. sugar
2  eggs, beaten
1/2  c. milk
1  tsp. vanilla
1 1/3  c. flour

 
Filling:
1/4  c. butter
1  c. brown sugar
3  Tbsp. evaporated milk
1  c. coconut
1  c. nuts, chopped

      Cream butter and sugar.  Add eggs, milk, vanilla and flour. Mix well.  Bake in shallow pan at 350 degrees; until done.
      Filling:  Mix butter, brown sugar, milk and coconut.  Cook until thick.  Add 1 cup chopped nuts.  Spread on cake and return to oven.  Let brown; cool.  Cut in squares.
