QUICK PEPPERONI SPAGHETTI


1  large onion, chopped
1  green pepper, chopped
1  lb. ground beef
1 (3 oz.)  pkg. sliced pepperoni, chopped
1  can spaghetti sauce
1 (12 oz.)  pkg. spaghetti
1  c. shredded Mozzarella cheese
1  tsp. grated Parmesan cheese

      Combine onion, pepper, beef and pepperoni.  Cook until brown (drain).  Return to skillet.  Add sauce and bring to boil.  Cover and simmer 20 minutes.  Cook spaghetti (drain).  Arrange on an ovenproof platter.  Spoon meat sauce on top.  Sprinkle Mozzarella cheese over sauce.  Bake at 400 degrees; for 3 to 5 minutes.  Remove from oven.  Top with Parmesan cheese.  Serve immediately.
