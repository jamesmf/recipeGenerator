ORIENTAL HAMBURGER CASSEROLE


1 1/4 to 1 1/2  lb. hamburger
2  Tbsp. salad oil
2  small onions, chopped
1  c. celery, chopped
1  can mushroom soup
1  can cream of chicken soup
1 1/2  c. warm water
1/4  c. soy sauce
1/4  tsp. pepper
3/4  c. uncooked rice
1  can crisp Chinese noodles

      Brown meat.  Add onions, celery and soups.  Add water.  Stir in rice, soy sauce and pepper.  Put in greased casserole.  Cover and cook at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes.  Uncover; cook 30 minutes.  The last 15 minutes, add noodles.
