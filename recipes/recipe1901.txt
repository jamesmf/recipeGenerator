TACO TWIST


1  lb. ground beef
1 (15 oz.)  can tomato sauce
1  pkg. taco seasoning mix
1/4  c. chopped green pepper
1  c. shredded Cheddar cheese
3  c. (8 oz.) corkscrew macaroni, cooked and drained
1/2  c. sour cream

      Brown ground beef and drain.  Stir in taco seasoning mix, tomato sauce and green pepper.  Bring to a boil and remove from heat.  Combine cooked macaroni, sour cream and 1/2 cup Cheddar cheese and place in a casserole dish.  Top with meat mixture and remaining cheese.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes.
