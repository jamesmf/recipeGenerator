CAULIFLOWER SALAD


  medium sized head cauliflower
1  pkg. frozen peas, cooked and cooled
2  stalks celery, diced
  chopped onion

 
Dressing:
1  c. mayonnaise
1/2  tsp. pepper
6  Tbsp. sour cream
1/2  tsp. seasoned salt
3  Tbsp. sugar

      Cut up cauliflower and place in a bowl. Add frozen peas, celery and a little chopped onion.
      Dressing: Mix together and chill overnight.
