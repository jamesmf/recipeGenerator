CRAB BALL DIP


2  pkg. frozen crabmeat or shrimp
2 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese

 
Sauce:
1  c. ketchup
1  Tbsp. horseradish
  few sprinkles of Worcestershire sauce
  few pinches of garlic salt

      Drain crabmeat.  At room temperature, spread cream cheese into pie crust form on counter.  Spread 3 tablespoons sauce and all of crabmeat over cheese.  Sprinkle salt and garlic salt over it.  Squish into ball by folding edges.  Pour remaining sauce over ball and serve.  Refrigerate for 3 hours.
