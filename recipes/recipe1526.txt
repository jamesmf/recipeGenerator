STRAWBERRY CAKE


1  box white cake mix
3  Tbsp. flour
1  box strawberry jello
1/2  c. water
1/2  c. frozen strawberries
3  eggs
1  c. Wesson oil

      Mix cake mix, flour and jello in a bowl.  Add other ingredients and beat well.  Bake in layers at 350 degrees; for 25 minutes.
Filling:
1 1/2  boxes powdered sugar
1/2  c. frozen strawberries
1  stick butter

      Mix well with electric beater at low speed.  Pour mixture over cake.
