HOT POTATO SALAD


6  to 8 large potatoes, cooked, drained and sliced
1/4  lb. bacon, cooked
2  onions, chopped
2  Tbsp. flour
1  c. sugar
2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper
1  c. vinegar

      Put potatoes in casserole dish.  Brown onions in bacon grease.  Stir in flour.  Add sugar, salt, pepper and vinegar. Cook until thick.  Add crumbled bacon.  Simmer 15 to 20 minutes. Pour over potatoes and punch.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes.
