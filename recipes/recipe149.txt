CRISSCROSS PEANUT BUTTER COOKIES


1  c. white sugar
1  c. brown sugar
1  c. shortening
2  eggs
1  tsp. vanilla
3  c. flour
1/2  tsp. salt
1 1/2  tsp. soda
1  c. peanut butter

      Mix sugars and shortening.  Cream well.  Add eggs, well beaten, vanilla, the flour sifted with the salt and soda, then the peanut butter.  Mix well and knead.  Roll into balls about 1/4-inch across.  Place 1 inch apart on greased pan.  Press with fork lengthwise and then crosswise.  Bake at 400 degrees; for 5 to 10 minutes. Makes about 15 dozen cookies, 1 1/2 inches in diameter.
