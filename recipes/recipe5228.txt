FESTAL 6 VEGETABLE SALAD

Salad:
2 (17 oz.)  cans Festal midget peas (drained)
1  c. sliced green onions and tops
1  c. sliced red radishes
2  c. sliced celery
2  c. shredded carrots
2  c. fresh cauliflower florets

      Mix together.
Dressing:
1/3  c. salad oil
2/3  c. vinegar
1  c. sugar
2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper

      Mix well till sugar dissolves.  Mix with vegetable mixture. Marinate 24 hours or longer and drain before serving.
      Note:  1/2 of the recipe makes a lot for 2 to 4 persons.
