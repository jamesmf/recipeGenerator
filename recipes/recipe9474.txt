OVEN BAKED SPARERIBS


2  Tbsp. soy sauce
2  Tbsp. honey
3  Tbsp. hoisin sauce
1  Tbsp. vinegar
1   Tbsp. catsup
1  clove garlic
1  Tbsp. sugar
2  lb. spareribs

	Combine all ingredients. Pour over spareribs and marinate overnight, turning several times. Line shallow pan with foil.
	Preheat oven to 350 degrees;. Roast ribs, uncovered, for 45 minutes. Turn heat down to 325 degrees; and roast for 15 more minutes. (When shrinking occurs, ribs are done.)
