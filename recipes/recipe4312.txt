SAUERKRAUT SALAD


1  can sauerkraut, drained and rinsed
1  c. green bell pepper, diced
1  c. onion, diced
1  c. celery, diced
1 (2 oz.)  jar pimientos, drained
1  c. cider vinegar
2  c. sugar

      Combine first 5 ingredients in a serving bowl. Heat vinegar and sugar until dissolved. Cool to lukewarm and pour over vegetables. Mix well and refrigerate until serving time.
