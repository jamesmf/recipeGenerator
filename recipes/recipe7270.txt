STRIP DUMPLINGS


1  c. flour
1 to 1 1/2  tsp. baking powder
1/4  tsp. soda
1/2  c. buttermilk

      Stir buttermilk into dry ingredients; blend well. Turn out onto floured surface and knead until smooth and not sticky to the touch. Roll dough 1/4-inch thick; cut into 1/2-inch wide and 3-inch long strips. Drop into simmering soup or stewing chicken. Cover and simmer 12 minutes or until done. Makes 30 small dumplings.
