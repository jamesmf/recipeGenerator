KITCHREE
(Rice and Lentils)  

  2-3 cups Basmati rice
1  med. onion
3/4  cup masoor dal
1/2  tsp. ginger/garlic paste
  Pinch of turmeric
1/4  tsp. garam masala
1  green chili
2  Tbsp. chopped cilantro
2  sticks fresh mint, chopped
1/2  tsp. black cumin seeds
1  stick cinnamon
3  cardamom pods
3  cloves
1/2  tsp. salt to taste
  4-5 cups water

      Wash rice and dal.  Fry onion until brown.  Combine all the ingredients and add water.  Cook in rice cooker.  When done, mix and serve.
