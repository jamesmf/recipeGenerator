OATMEAL COOKIES


1 1/2  c. flour
1 1/2  tsp. baking soda
1 1/2  c. brown sugar
1 1/2  c. margarine, softened
3  c. rolled oats, quick
  powdered sugar

      Combine first 5 ingredients and mix by hand, the more the better!  Form into balls the size of a walnut.  Roll in powdered sugar.  Place on cookie sheets and flatten slightly.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 10 to 12 minutes.  Makes 6 dozen.
