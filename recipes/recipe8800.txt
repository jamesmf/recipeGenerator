SHRIMP SCAMPI


12  large shrimp (16 to 20 per lb. size)
1  bulb shallot, chopped
3  cloves garlic, chopped
1/2  c. white wine or juice of 1/2 lemon
2  Tbsp. virgin olive oil
1/2  stick butter
  fresh parsley, chopped
1  lb. linguini

      Peel and devein shrimp.  (Optional:  Cut along back of shrimp to butterfly for larger appearance and fancier looking shrimp.)
      Sauté chopped shallots and garlic in olive oil until soft.  Add white wine (or lemon juice) and reduce.  Add shrimp.  Sauté until pink.  Add butter and fresh chopped parsley.  If you need extra liquid, add some of the pasta water.
      Remove shrimp and place on bed of cooked linguini.  Pour butter and olive oil mixture over shrimp.  Molto bene!
