BUBBLE-UP PIZZA


3  pkg. buttermilk biscuits
1  jar spaghetti sauce, divided
3  c. shredded Mozzarella, divided
1  large garlic clove

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees;. In batter bowl, quarter biscuits with knife. Stir in 1 cup sauce and 2 cups of cheese. Spread mixture into 9 x 13-inch pan. Pour remaining sauce and cheese over the top. Bake 30 to 35 minutes.
