WALNUT CAKE
(Karithopeta)  

9  eggs
1  c. bread crumbs or zwieback
1  c. walnuts
1  tsp. baking powder
1  tsp. nutmeg
1  c. sugar
1  tsp. vanilla

 
Syrup:
1  c. sugar
1  c. water
1/2  c. lemon juice

      Boil syrup ingredients 12 minutes after it starts boiling.
      Beat egg yolks with sugar.  Add all dry ingredients.  Lastly fold in stiffly beaten egg whites.  Put in greased 9 x 13-inch pan and bake at 400 degrees; about 1/2 hour.  Cool.  Cut in diamond shapes and pour warm syrup over it.
