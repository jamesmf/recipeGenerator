HONEY CHICKEN & GRAPE SALAD


1/2  c. Sweet Honey Catalina Dressing (Kraft) or Light Done Right Catalina
1/4  tsp. grated orange peel
6  oz. grilled chicken breast strips
1 1/2  c. halved red grapes
3/4  c. sliced celery
4  c. torn romaine lettuce
  Hulled sunflower seeds

      Mix dressing and orange peel in large bowl.  Add chicken breast strips, grapes and celery.  Toss lightly.  Refrigerate several hours or overnight.  Toss with lettuce and sprinkle with sunflower seeds just before serving.
