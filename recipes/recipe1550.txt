RICH AND THICK MILK SHAKE


2 1/2  c. cold milk
1 (4 serving size)  pkg. instant pudding and pie filling (any flavor)
3  scoops ice cream (any flavor)
1 1/2  c. crushed ice

      Pour milk into electric blender container.  Add pudding mix, ice cream and ice.  Cover and blend at high speed for 1 minute. Serve at once.  Makes about 5 cups.
