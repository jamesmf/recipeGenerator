CHICKEN CRESCENTS


1  can crescent rolls
3  oz. pkg. cream cheese
2  Tbsp. soft margarine
1/4  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper
2  Tbsp. milk
1  Tbsp. chopped onion
1  Tbsp. chopped pimento
2  c. cooked chopped chicken

	Separate crescent rolls into 4 rectangles. Place on a cookie sheet. Blend all other ingredients. Spoon 1/2 cup of chicken mixture onto each rectangle. Roll the 4 corners of the crescent dough over chicken mixture to the center. Brush with melted butter or margarine. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes.
