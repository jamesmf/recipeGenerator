CZECHOSLOVAKIAN COOKIES


1  c. butter
1  c. sugar
2  egg yolks
2  c. flour
1  c. nuts, finely chopped
1/2 to 3/4  c. jam

      Cream butter and sugar until smooth.  Add egg yolks; mix until blended.  Slowly add flour and nuts; mix until blended.  Spoon 1/2 of the nut mixture into 8-inch square pan; spread evenly.  Cover mixture in pan with jam and pat remaining mixture over jam, covering jam completely.  Put in a cold oven; turn oven on to 325 degrees; and bake for 1 hour.
