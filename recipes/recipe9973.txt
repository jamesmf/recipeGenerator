HASH BROWN POTATO BAKE


2  lb. pkg. hash browns
1/2  c. butter, melted
1  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper
2  Tbsp. minced dry onion
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  pt. sour cream (16 oz.)
2  c. shredded cheese
1/4  c. butter, melted
2  c. corn flakes, crushed

      Thaw potatoes before mixing ingredients. Mix first 8 ingredients and put in a 9 x 13 pan. Mix the 1/4 cup melted butter with corn flakes and put on top. Bake for 45 to 60 minutes at 350 degrees;.
