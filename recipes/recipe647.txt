ICED BROWNIES


2  eggs
1  oz. bread
2/3  c. chocolate Alba powder
1  banana
2  packets Sweet 'N Low
  few drops chocolate extract
1/4  tsp. cream of tartar
1/2  tsp. baking soda

      Place all ingredients in blender and blend until smooth.  Pour into square Teflon pan.  Bake for about 12 minutes in 350 degrees; oven.
      Exchange total:  2 milks, 1 fruit, 1 bread and 4 ounces of protein.
Chocolate Frosting For Brownies:
1/3  c. chocolate Alba powder
1  packet Sweet 'N Low
  dash of liquid sweetener
  dash of chocolate extract
  skim milk

      Combine first 4 ingredients.  Add skim milk until icing is of spreading consistency.  Total is slightly over 1 cup of milk allowance.
