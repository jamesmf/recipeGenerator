JALAPENO CASSEROLE


1  bag nacho Doritos
2  cans mushroom soup
2  cans cream of chicken soup
2  cans boned chicken
2  c. grated cheese
  sliced jalapeno peppers

      Mix soups and boned chicken in saucepan; heat. Pour over Doritos chips in large 11 x 13-inch casserole dish. Top with grated cheese and sliced jalapenos. Heat in 350 degrees; oven until cheese melts. Serve and enjoy.
