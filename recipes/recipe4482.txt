BEEF STEW SUPREME


4  carrots, sliced
3  potatoes, cut up
2  lb. beef stew meat, cubed
1  c. consomme
1  tsp. garlic salt
1  tsp. each: pepper, fine herbs and onion powder
1  stalk celery, cut up

      The above ingredients should be place in the crock-pot in the order given. Stir several times; cover and cook.
       Cooking time: Low 10 to 12 hours; high 5 to 6 hours.
      Optional: Add small can of peas, drained, the last 1/2 hour.
