TATER TOT CASSEROLE


1 (16 oz.)  pkg. Tater Tots
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  lb. ground beef
1  tsp. onion flakes
1/2  tsp. garlic powder
1/4  tsp. black pepper
1  c. grated Cheddar cheese

      Fry ground beef; drain. In a casserole dish, mix frozen Tater Tots, soup, onion, garlic and black pepper. Bake in 350 degrees; oven for 30 to 45 minutes. Sprinkle grated cheese on top before baking.
