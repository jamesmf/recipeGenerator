


12  tortillas
1/3  c. salad oil
1 (10 oz.)  can enchilada sauce
  Cheese Sauce

 
Cheese Sauce:
1/4  c. butter
1/4  c. flour
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. paprika
2  c. milk
1 1/2  c. (6 oz.) cheese

      Fry tortillas in hot oil for 5 seconds on each side.  Dip tortillas in enchilada sauce; spoon some of the Cheese Sauce inside each and roll up.  Place seam side down in a baking dish. Combine remaining Cheese Sauce and enchilada sauce; pour over tortillas.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 25 minutes.
      To make sauce, melt butter; blend in flour, salt and paprika. Add milk and stir until sauce thickens.  Blend in cheese.
