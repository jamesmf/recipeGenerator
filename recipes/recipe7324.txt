OVEN-FRIED CATFISH
(Makes 8 Servings)  

6  oz. French bread (6 slices), cubed
1/4  c. mayonnaise (imitation)
4  tsp. water
48  oz. catfish, dressed
  cooking spray
  lemon wedges (optional)
  parsley sprigs (optional)

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees;. Place bread cubes in a food processor and process 30 seconds or until breadcrumbs are fine. Sprinkle breadcrumbs onto a baking sheet. Bake at 350 degrees; for 5 minutes or until lightly browned. Place breadcrumbs in a shallow dish; set aside. Increase oven temperature to 450 degrees;.
      Combine mayonnaise and water in a shallow dish; stir well. Dip fish, one at a time, in mayonnaise mixture. Dredge in breadcrumbs. Place fish on a baking sheet coated with cooking spray. Bake at 450 degrees; for 15 minutes or until fish flakes easily when tested with a fork. Serve with lemon wedges and garnish with parsley sprigs, if desired.
