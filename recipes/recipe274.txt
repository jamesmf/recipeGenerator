APPLE CRISP


5  apples, sliced
1  c. sifted flour
3/4  c. sugar
1  tsp. baking powder
3/4  tsp. salt
1  egg
1/3  c. melted butter
  cinnamon

      Layer apples in greased pie plate.  Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, salt and egg together.  Put on top of apples.  Sprinkle melted butter on top.  Sprinkle cinnamon on top.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 to 40 minutes.
