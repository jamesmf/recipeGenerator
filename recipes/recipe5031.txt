CHEDDAR CHOWDER


2  c. water
2  c. diced potatoes
1/2  c. diced celery
1/2  c. diced carrots
1/4  c. diced onion
1  c. diced ham
1 1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
1/4  c. flour
1/4  c. butter
2  c. milk
1 to 1 1/2  c. Velveeta cheese

      Cook  first  6  ingredients until tender.  Do not drain off water.  Make  a white sauce from next 6 ingredients.  Add to first ingredients.  Serve hot.  Yields 6 cups.
