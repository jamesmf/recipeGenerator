HASH BROWN CASSEROLE


1  large bag frozen hash browns, thawed
1  onion, chopped
1 (16 oz.)  carton sour cream
1  can cream of mushroom soup
2  c. Cheddar cheese
1  stick Ritz crackers
1  stick oleo

      Mix first 5 ingredients together in casserole dish. Bake 50 minutes. Remove from oven. Mix crackers and oleo. Put on top of casserole. Return to oven for 10 minutes longer.
