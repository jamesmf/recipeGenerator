SEVEN UP SALAD


1 8  oz. cream cheese
1 small  box lemon jello
1 12  oz. bottle 7-up
1  tsp. vanilla
1  tsp. sugar
  chopped nuts
  green food coloring

      Dissolve lemon jello in 1 cup boiling water.  Add cream cheese, 7-up, sugar and vanilla.  Add green food coloring and nuts.  Chill and serve.
