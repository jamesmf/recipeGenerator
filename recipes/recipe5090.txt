PEACH PIE FILLING SALAD


1  can peach pie filling
2  bananas
2  c. miniature marshmallows
1  can chunk pineapple
2  cans Mandarin oranges

      Drain canned fruit. Add to pie filling. Add marshmallows and sliced bananas. Chill until ready to serve.
      You may use apricot pie filling and less pineapple. More bananas and marshmallows may be increased to make a large salad.
