NONNIE'S CHICKEN AND RICE


4  skinless, boneless chicken breasts
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  can cream of mushroom soup
3 soup  cans of water
1 1/2  c. long grain rice
1/2  tsp. paprika
1/2  tsp. cumin
1/2  tsp. pepper
1  tsp. oregano
1  tsp. basil
1  tsp. garlic powder
1  tsp. sage
1  medium onion, chopped
1  medium bell pepper, chopped

      Brown chicken breasts on each side in small amount of oil. Combine chicken soup, mushroom soup, water, rice and spices. Mix well. Pour mixture in a 4-quart casserole dish. Arrange chicken breasts on top. Sprinkle with paprika. Cover and bake at 375 degrees; for 45 minutes or until done. May add more liquid halfway through cooking if needed.
