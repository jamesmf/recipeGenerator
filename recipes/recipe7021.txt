SWISS STEAK


1/4  c. flour
1/2  tsp. salt
  generous dash of pepper
1 1/2  lb. round steak
2  Tbsp. shortening
1  can (10 3/4 oz.) Vegetable soup
1/2  can water
1  c. onion, sliced

      Mix flour, salt and pepper; pound into steak. Place in skillet with shortening and brown; pour off grease. Add rest of ingredients and cover. Simmer 1 hour and 15 minutes or until tender, stirring often.
