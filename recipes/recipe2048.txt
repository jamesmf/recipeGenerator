MARZIPAN


1/4  lb. finely ground, blanched almonds
1/4  lb. confectioners sugar
1  egg white, beaten
1/4  tsp. salt

      Mix all ingredients; knead smooth.  Paste can be softened with a very little amount of lemon juice if it is too stiff.  Let stand covered 24 hours before using.  When ready to use, form into desired shapes.
