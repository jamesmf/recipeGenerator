POTATO SOUP


4  c. peeled, cubed potatoes
1  c. sliced celery
1  c. coarsely chopped onion
2  tsp. salt
2  c. water
1  c. milk
1  c. whipping cream
3  Tbsp. butter or margarine, melted
1  Tbsp. dried parsley flakes
1/8  tsp. pepper

      Cook  potatoes,  celery, onion and salt in 2 cups water about 20  minutes  or  until  potatoes  are  tender.  Stir in remaining ingredients.  Lightly mash with a potato masher and cook, stirring constantly, until soup is hot and thickened.
