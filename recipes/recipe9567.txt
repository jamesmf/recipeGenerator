CHICKEN SCALA


4  skinned and boned chicken breasts
  flour
  vegetable oil
1  can beef consomme
1/2 to 1  c. sour cream
  black pepper
  grated Parmesan cheese

      Flour chicken breasts and brown in oil. Drain fat from pan and add consomme to chicken; simmer 25 to 30 minutes.  Add 1/2 to 1 cup sour cream to make a creamy sauce. Add several shakes of black pepper.  Put chicken and sauce in baking dish. Sprinkle with cheese and brown under broiler.
