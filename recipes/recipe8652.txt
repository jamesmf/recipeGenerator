DELICIOUS FAUX LASAGNA


1  lb. hamburger meat
1  small onion, chopped
1  c. green pepper, chopped
2  cans diced tomatoes with spices
1 (6 oz.)  can tomato paste
3  c. cooked pasta (small noodles or mini-lasagna noodles)
2  c. shredded Mozzarella, divided

	Preheat oven to 325 degrees;.  In a nonstick skillet, cook hamburger meat, onion and green pepper until no longer pink; drain.  Blend in tomatoes and tomato paste.  Simmer for 5 minutes.  Remove from pan. Keep warm.
	In a casserole dish, place one-half of the cooked pasta. Pour one-half of the meat mixture over the pasta.  Sprinkle 1 cup cheese on pasta. Top with remaining paste, meat mixture and cheese in that order. Bake in oven about 20 minutes until hot and bubbly.
