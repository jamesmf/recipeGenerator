FIESTA CHICKEN AND RICE BAKE


1  can cream of chicken soup
1  c. chunky salsa or picante sauce
1  c. raw, long grain, white rice
1  c. whole kernel corn
1/2  c. water
4  boneless chicken breasts halves
  paprika
1/2  c. shredded Cheddar cheese

      Mix soup, salsa, water, corn, and rice in 2 qt. shallow baking dish. Top with chicken and sprinkle with paprika. Cover and bake at 375 degrees; for 45 minutes or until done. Sprinkle with cheese. Serves 4.
