ENCHILADA CASSEROLE


1 large (13 oz.)  pkg. Doritos (save 2 c. for topping)
2  Tbsp. grated onion
1 (10 oz.)  can chili with beans
10 to 15  oz. enchilada sauce
8  oz. can tomato sauce
1 1/2  c. shredded sharp Cheddar cheese

 
Topping:
2  c. Doritos
1 1/4  c. sour cream
1/2  c. shredded cheese

      Preheat oven to 375 degrees;.  Crumble 6 cups Doritos into large mixing bowl.  Add onion, chili, enchilada sauce and 1 1/2 cups cheese to crumbled Doritos.  Stir with rubber spatula.  Place mixture into 8 1/2 x 11 baking dish.  Bake for 20 minutes.  Remove from oven. Spread sour cream.  Top with rest of Doritos.  Sprinkle 1/2 cup cheese on top.
