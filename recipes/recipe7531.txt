CAJUN BAKED CATFISH


2  c. cornmeal
2  tsp. salt
1  tsp. pepper
8  (3- to 4-oz.) catfish fillets
1  Tbsp. Cajun seasoning (Tony Chachere or Zatarain)
1  Tbsp. lemon pepper
1/4  c. butter, melted

      Combine first 3 ingredients.  Dredge catfish in cornmeal mixture; place fillets, skin sides down, on a greased baking pan. Sprinkle Cajun seasoning and lemon pepper over fillets; drizzle with butter.  Bake at 400 degrees; for 30 minutes or until golden and fish flakes with a fork.
