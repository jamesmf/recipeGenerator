BEEF CASSEROLE WITH WATER CHESTNUTS


1  1/2 lbs. ground beef
1  med. onion, chopped
1  clove garlic, pressed
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. pepper
4.5  oz. can chopped ripe olives
5  oz. can water chestnuts, sliced
4 - 5  oz. thin noodles
4  oz. can mushrooms, drained
10.5  oz. can cream of chicken soup
1/4  C. dry red wine, optional
2  C. dairy sour cream

      Saut√© meat with onion and garlic.  Drain off fat.
      Add salt, pepper, olives and water chestnuts; heat together.
      Cook noodles and drain.
      Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  In a large bowl, combine noodles, meat mixture, mushrooms, soup, 1/2 can water, wine and sour cream.  Pour into 2 qt. casserole and bake 30 minutes.
      Makes 8 servings.
