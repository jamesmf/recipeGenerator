VEGETABLE SALAD


1  can whole kernel corn, drained
1  can green beans, drained
1  can peas, drained
1 small  can sliced mushrooms
1 small  jar pimientos
1  c. bell pepper, chopped
1  large onion, chopped
5  ribs celery, chopped

	Mix together. Pour dressing over vegetables and let stand in refrigerator for several hours.
Dressing:
1/4  c. sugar
1/4  c. white vinegar
1/4  c. canola oil
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. pepper
2  Tbsp. Italian salad dressing

	Mix together first 5 ingredients and bring to boil; let cool. Add 2 tablespoons Italian salad dressing.
