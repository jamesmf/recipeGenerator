TURKEY BURGERS


1  pkg. The Turkey Store Premium Fresh lean ground turkey
2  Tbsp. corn meal
2  tsp. ground cumin (if desired)
2  tsp. chili powder
1/2  tsp. salt (if desired)
1/4  tsp. pepper
1  Tbsp. lime juice
1  egg white
1  jalapeno pepper, seeded and finely chopped
1  Tbsp. oil
1  c. frozen whole kernel corn, cooked and drained
1/2 to 3/4  c. salsa or picante sauce

      In a medium bowl, combine ground turkey, corn meal, cumin, chili powder, salt, pepper, lime juice, egg white and jalapeno pepper; mix lightly. Shape into 5 (1/2-inch thick) patties. Heat oil in large skillet over medium-high heat until oil sizzles, about 1 minute. Add turkey patties. Cook 3 to 5 minutes on each side or until browned and no longer pink in center. Meanwhile, in small saucepan, combine and heat corn and salsa. Serve with turkey burgers. Serves 5.
