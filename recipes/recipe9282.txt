TATER TOT CASSEROLE


1 1/2  lb. ground beef
  salt (to taste)
  pepper (to taste)
1  medium onion, chopped
10 to 20  oz. (1 to 2 cans) soup, cream of chicken
8  oz. Cheddar cheese, shredded
30  oz. (1 bag) Tater Tots
  sprinkle of garlic powder

      In a skillet, brown ground beef until crumbly.  Drain off fat and place in bottom of a 9x13-inch casserole dish.  Sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste.  Put a layer of chopped onions on top then spread soup over onions.  Cover with cheese and place Tater Tots, side by side, over cheese. Sprinkle garlic powder over Tater Tots.
      Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 to 40 minutes or until Tater Tots are brown.
