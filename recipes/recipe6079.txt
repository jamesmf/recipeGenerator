BROCCOLI CHEESE SOUP


10  oz. (1 box) chopped broccoli (frozen)
10  oz. (1 can) soup, cream of chicken
10  oz. (1 can) soup, cream of mushroom
10  oz. (1 can) soup, cream of celery
3  c. chicken broth
1/2  c. milk
1/2  tsp. red pepper
15  oz. (1 medium jar) Cheez Whiz

      Thaw and drain broccoli.
      Combine soups and chicken broth.  Heat and stir until smooth. Stir in milk, red pepper and broccoli.  Simmer until broccoli is tender (8 to 10 minutes).  Add Cheez Whiz and heat until melted.
