CRUNCHY BEEF BAKE CASSEROLE


1 (7 oz.)  pkg. macaroni (2 c. uncooked)
1  lb. ground beef
1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can cream of mushroom soup
3/4  c. shredded Cheddar cheese
1 (14 1/2 oz.)  can whole tomatoes, cut up
1/4  c. chopped green pepper
3/4  tsp. seasoned salt
1 (3 oz.)  can French fried onions

      Prepare macaroni as directed on package; drain.  Brown ground beef; drain.  Combine all ingredients except onions.  Pour half the mixture into a 2-quart casserole.  Add 1/2 can onions.  Pour remaining mixture over onions.  Cover; bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes.  Top with remaining onions and bake, uncovered, for 5 minutes longer.
