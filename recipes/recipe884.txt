TUNA SALAD WITH SAUCE PACIFIC

Salad:
1  head lettuce, shredded
1  head romaine, torn in pieces
1  cucumber, sliced
1  small onion, sliced
1  stalk celery, sliced
2 (6 1/2 oz.)  cans tuna

 
Sauce Pacific:
1/2  c. sour cream
1/4  c. mayonnaise
2  Tbsp. chili sauce
1  Tbsp. lemon juice
1  Tbsp. parsley
1  Tbsp. horseradish
1  tsp. Worcestershire
1  tsp. curry powder
2  Tbsp. chopped onions

      Mix all ingredients together for salad, then mix the sauce. Toss salad with sauce before serving.
