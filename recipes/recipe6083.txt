ROBBIE'S TACO SOUP


1 1/2  lb. ground beef (or turkey)
1  onion, chopped
15  oz. (1 can) corn, whole kernel
15  oz. (1 can) tomato sauce
31  oz. (2 cans) chili beans
16  oz. (1 can) chili tomatoes, diced
4 1/3  oz. (1 can) chilies, chopped
1 1/4  oz. (1 pkg.) taco seasoning
1  oz. (1 pkg.) ranch dressing, dry mix (Hidden Valley)

      Brown beef and onion.  Add rest of ingredients (undrained) and bring to a boil.
      Simmer 20 to 30 minutes.
      Serve with crackers or chips.
