EASY HAMBURG CASSEROLE


1  lb. hamburg
1/2  c. sliced onions
1/4  c. green peppers
2  c. potatoes, sliced thin
1  c. carrots, sliced thin
2  c. canned tomatoes
1/2  c. shredded cheese
1/2  tsp. oregano
1/2  tsp. pepper
1  tsp. salt

      Put hamburg in bottom of 2-quart casserole; layer the potatoes on top of hamburg.  Add carrots, onions and chopped peppers.  Put the tomatoes and seasonings in next.  Top with shredded cheese.  Bake 2 hours at 300 degrees;.  Serves 6.
