CHEESE AND SAUSAGE ROLLS


16  sausage links
16  slices white bread
4  Tbsp. butter
1  c. shredded American cheese

      Cook sausage links and drain.  Cut crust from bread and roll flat.  Mix cheese and butter.  Spread on bread.  Roll sausage in each slice.  Bake on greased baking sheet at 400 degrees; for 10 to 12 minutes.  Slice and serve with toothpicks.  Makes about 40 servings.
