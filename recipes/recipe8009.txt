CHICKEN CHOW MEIN


1 1/2  lb. chicken, pork or beef, cut into bite size pieces
2  Tbsp. oil
6  Tbsp. soy sauce
1  c. water
  stalk celery, chopped
1  can (10 oz.) water chestnuts
2 1/2  c. bean sprouts
1  can (2 oz.) mushrooms
1  onion, chopped
3/4  c. water with 3 Tbsp. cornstarch mixed together

      Brown meat in oil.  Add soy sauce and 1 cup water.  Simmer 2 minutes.  Add celery and onion.  Blend in water and cornstarch mixture into pan.  Add water chestnuts, sprouts and mushrooms. Heat thoroughly and season with salt and pepper.  Put in oven at 350 degrees; for 1 hour.
