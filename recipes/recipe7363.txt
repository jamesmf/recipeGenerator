STUFFED GREEN PEPPERS


6  large green peppers
1  lb. ground beef
1/2  c. chopped onion
1 (16 oz.)  can diced tomatoes
1  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/2  c. long grain rice
1/2  c. water
1  tsp. salt
  shredded American cheese

      Cut tops and clean peppers (save tops).  Chop to make 1/4 cup; set aside.  Cook the whole green peppers for 5 minutes. Invert to drain.  Sprinkle insides of peppers with salt.  In a skillet, cook beef, onion and the 1/4 cup chopped green pepper until meat is browned.  Drain off excess fat.  Add undrained tomatoes, uncooked rice, water, salt and Worcestershire sauce. Bring to boiling; reduce heat.  Cover and simmer for 15 to 18 minutes until rice is tender.  Stir in cheese.  Stuff peppers with meat mixture.  Place in 10 x 6 x 2-inch baking dish.  Bake, covered, for 30 to 35 minutes at 350 degrees;.  Serves 6.
