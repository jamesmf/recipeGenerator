MOLDED FRUIT SALAD


2  eggs, beaten
1  c. sugar
1  Tbsp. flour
1 small  pkg. jello (apricot, peach or orange)
2 (11 oz.)  cans Mandarin orange sections, drained
6  medium sized bananas, sliced
1 (No. 2)  can crushed pineapple, drained (reserve juice)
1 (6 oz.)  pkg. mini marshmallows

      Drain pineapple, reserving juice.  Combine reserved pineapple juice, eggs, sugar and flour.  Cook over low heat until thick (about 5 minutes).  Add gelatin.  Stir until dissolved.  Cool before adding orange sections, bananas, pineapple and marshmallows. Chill in refrigerator for several hours before serving.
