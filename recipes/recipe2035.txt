CHOCOLATE NUGGET CANDY


1 (6 oz.)  pkg. semi-sweet chocolate chips
2/3  c. Pet evaporated milk
1/2  c. powdered sugar
2 1/2  c. crushed vanilla wafer crumbs (1/2 lb.)
1/2  c. finely cut pecans
1  tsp. vanilla

      Melt chocolate chips and Pet evaporated milk.  Remove from heat.  Add crushed vanilla wafer crumbs, powdered sugar, finely cut pecans and vanilla.  Mix well; let stand at room temperature 30 minutes.  Shape into balls or logs, 1 1/2-inches long, 1/2-inch across.  Roll in 3/4 cup pecans.  Roll in 1/2 cup sifted powdered sugar.  Chill 1 hour.  Makes 48 balls.
