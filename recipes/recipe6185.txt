ORIENTAL CHICKEN SALAD


1  head lettuce, cut into pieces, or 1 bag salad mix
3 to 4  c. cooked chicken breast, cut into chunks
4  oz. sliced almonds
1/4  c. poppy seed
1  can chow mein noodles

 
Dressing:
1/4  c. sugar
1/4  c. apple cider vinegar
2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper
1/2  tsp. vegetable oil

	Pour dressing over salad just before serving.
