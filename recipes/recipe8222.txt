OVERNIGHT BREAKFAST CASSEROLE


6  slices bread, trimmed and cubed
1/4  c. chopped green peppers
1  c. (4 oz.)  Cheddar cheese, grated
6  eggs
2  c. milk
1  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
  lb. cooked and drained sausage

      Layer ingredients in a 13 x 9 x 2-inch casserole in the following order:  bread cubes, cooked sausage, green peppers and cheese.  Beat together eggs, milk, salt and pepper.  Pour over layers.  Cover with foil and refrigerate overnight.  Bake uncovered at 350 degrees; for 35 minutes.
