PIG NUTS


1  lb. bacon
1 large  can water chestnuts (whole)
1/2  c. brown sugar
1/4  c. Miracle Whip salad dressing
1/2  c. chili sauce

      Cut bacon strips in half.  Wrap bacon around 1/2 or 1 whole chestnut.  Layer in baking dish and bake at 350 degrees; for 35 to 40 minutes.  Drain fat from dish.  Mix brown sugar, salad dressing and chili sauce.  Pour over chestnuts.  Return to oven for 15 minutes.  Very Good!
