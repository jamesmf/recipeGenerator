SPANISH RICE


3/4  lb. bacon
1  yellow sweet onion
2 (15 oz.)  cans tomato sauce
1 (15 oz.)  can diced tomatoes
1 (15 oz.)  can navy or Great Northern beans, drained and rinsed
1  tsp. garlic salt
1/2  tsp. black pepper
1  tsp. ground cumin
1/4  c. salsa
4 to 5  c. Minute rice (add as needed)

      Cut bacon into small pieces. Dice onion and brown together and drain. Add all ingredients EXCEPT rice. Simmer on medium-low for 30 minutes. Turn down to low. Add rice. Stir and shut off burner. Let stand 5 to 10 minutes for rice to soak up juices.
      Can serve as is or you may want to also get some green peppers, clean them out and stuff Spanish Rice into those. Bake until pepper is cooked.
