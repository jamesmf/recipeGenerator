MEXICAN LAYER DIP


1  lb. ground beef
1  can refried beans
1  c. chopped green chiles
1  can sliced mushrooms
  Cheddar cheese, grated
  taco sauce
  sour cream
  olives, avocados, tomatoes, etc.

      Brown and drain ground beef and season with oregano, salt, minced garlic and whole thyme leaves; put in bottom of 9 x 13-inch pan.  Spread refried beans over meat; then layers of green chiles and mushrooms and top with cheese and taco sauce.  Bake 30 minutes at 350 degrees;.  Cool and spread with sour cream.  Garnish with lots of green onions, olives, avocados, tomatoes, etc.  Serve with tortilla chips.
