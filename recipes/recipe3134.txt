MEAT BALLS


1 large  can of evaporated milk
3  lb. ground meat
2  Tbsp. chili powder
2  c. quick oats
1  c. onion flakes

      Mix all ingredients together and shape into 50 or more meatballs. Place in a shallow pan. Make sauce and pour over meat balls. Cover with foil and bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour.
Sauce:
2  c. catsup
1 1/2  c. brown sugar
1  Tbsp. liquid smoke
