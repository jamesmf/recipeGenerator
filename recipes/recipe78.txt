CHOCOLATE CARAMELS


1  c. light cream
1  c. butter
2  c. sugar
3/4  c. light corn syrup
1/2  tsp. salt
3  oz. (3 sq.) unsweetened chocolate
1/8  tsp. cream of tartar
2  tsp. vanilla
  almonds, pecans or walnuts (if desired)

      Combine first 7 ingredients in heavy saucepan.  Cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until sugar dissolves.  Cook until candy forms a firm ball in cold water (245 degrees; on candy thermometer). Remove from heat.  Stir in vanilla.  Pour in a 9 x 9 x 2-inch pan. Do not scrape pan.  Nuts can be pressed into top, if desired.  Let cool.  Cut into 3/4-inch squares.  Let dry.  Wrap.  Makes 144 caramels.
