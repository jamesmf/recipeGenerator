CABBAGE AND NOODLES


2  lb. head of cabbage, grated
2  Tbsp. butter, melted
2  Tbsp. vegetable oil
1 (14 oz.)  pkg. Hungarian homemade noodles (1/2 to 1 inch wide)

      Cook noodles in salted water until done.  Rinse and drain well.  Grate cabbage, salt, let stand for a few moments and squeeze out well.  Brown cabbage in oil.  Pour melted butter over noodles, and add cabbage and serve hot.
