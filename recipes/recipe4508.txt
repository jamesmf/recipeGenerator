BROCCOLI GRAPE SALAD


1  bunch broccoli, cut fine
1  c. celery, cut diagonal
1/4  c. green onion, chopped
1/2  c. bacon, fried crisp and drained
1  c. slivered almonds, toasted
1  c. green grapes
1  c. red grapes

 
Dressing:
1/4  c. sugar
1  c. mayonnaise
1  Tbsp. vinegar

      Mix together broccoli, celery, green onion, slivered almonds, green grapes and red grapes. Mix bacon in just before serving.
      One hour before serving, pour dressing over salad; mix.
