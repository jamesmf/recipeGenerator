STROMBOLI

Dough:
2 1/2  c. flour
1  Tbsp. yeast
1  Tbsp. sugar
1  tsp. salt
1  Tbsp. oil
1  c. hot water

 
Toppings:
  ham, Mozzarella cheese, pepperoni, Provolone cheese, turkey, mushrooms, hot dogs

      Mix dough ingredients together.  Divide  in half.  Roll each half  to  9 x 13-inches.  Layer  desired  toppings  down center. Fold sides, which have been cut in 1-inch strips over center (sorta braiding).  Brush top with  1 egg and 1 teaspoon of oregano.  Bake at 400 degrees; for 25  minutes.  Both will fit in a 9 x 13-inch pan.
