CREAMY CHICKEN SPREAD


1 1/2  cup chopped cooked chicken
1 - 8  ounce pkg. Cream cheese (softened)
1/2  cup chopped celery
1/2  cup mayonnaise
2  TBSP chopped onion
1  tsp onion powder
1/2  tsp salt

      Put all ingredients into the food processor and process until chopped.  Use as a sandwich spread or on crackers.  This is great!
