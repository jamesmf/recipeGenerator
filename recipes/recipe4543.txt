LAYER SALAD


1  medium head lettuce, broken into bite size
2  cucumbers, sliced
1/2  c. sweet onion, chopped
1/2  c. carrots, chopped
1/2  c. bell pepper, chopped
1  (10 oz.) sweet peas
2  boiled eggs, chopped
  bacon bits
1 1/2  c. mayonnaise
4  oz. finely shredded Cheddar cheese
2  Tbsp. sugar

      Layer all ingredients (you may add any ingredients you prefer). Top with mayonnaise. Sprinkle sugar on top. Add cheese and finish with bacon bits. Cover and chill before serving.
