HAWAIIAN BAKED PORK


3  C. canned crushed pineapple
4  med. sweet potatoes
2  Tbsp. brown sugar
5  shoulder pork steaks (can also use pork chops)
  salt and pepper
4  - 6 strips of bacon

      Put pineapple in large buttered baking dish.  Place pared and sliced sweet potatoes over pineapple and sprinkle with brown sugar.  Salt and pepper the pork and place on top of the sweet potatoes.  Place bacon strips on top of the pork.
       Cover and bake about 1 hour at 350 degrees, or until potatoes and pork are tender.
      Remove cover and increase temperature to 450 degrees for last 10+ minutes of baking to brown the pork and bacon.
