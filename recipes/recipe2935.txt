BROCCOLI BALLS


1  small bag stuffing
2 (10 oz.)  boxes chopped broccoli, thawed and squeezed
1/2  c. Parmesan
6  eggs, beaten
1  Tbsp. Accent
3/4  c. butter, melted
1  Tbsp. garlic salt
  dash of pepper

      Mix all ingredients and form into balls. Place on cookie sheet. Bake at 350 degrees; for 15 to 20 minutes.
       Great to make ahead, freeze balls for 10 minutes on cookie sheet and place in Ziploc Baggies. Take out and bake when needed.
