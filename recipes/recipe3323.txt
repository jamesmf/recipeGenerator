FREEZER SWEET PICKLES
(Very Good)  

7  c. pickles
1  c. sliced onions
2  Tbsp. salt
1 3/4  c. sugar
1  c. vinegar
1  tsp. celery seeds

      Wash and slice pickles.  Combine with sliced onions.  Add salt and mix well.  Let stand for 2 hours.  Drain water off pickles and onions.  Mix sugar, vinegar and celery seeds.  Pour over pickles and mix.  Put into freezer containers and put into freezer.  Will keep for 4 to 6 months.
