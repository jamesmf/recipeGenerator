PORK LOIN (STUFFED)


5  lb. pork loin
1 (6 oz.)  pkg. Stove Top mix (pork)

      Slice pork loin up to the bones, making pockets.  Mix Stove Top stuffing as directed.  Stuff mixture into pork loin.  Place into 13 x 9-inch baking dish.  Bake at 350 degrees; until roast is done, about 1 to 1 1/2 hours.
