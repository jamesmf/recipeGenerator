MEXICAN DIP


1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese, softened
1  can Hormel chili (without beans)
3 to 4  Tbsp. Old El Paso taco sauce
1  c. Cheddar cheese, grated

      Spread softened cream cheese on bottom of pie plate.  Cover with can of chili, taco sauce and grated cheese.  Bake at 300 degrees; for 30 minutes.  Serve with tortilla chips.
