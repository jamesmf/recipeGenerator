GRILLED LONDON BROIL


  London Broil steak
1  Kraft Zesty Italian dressing

      Place London Broil in large casserole or container with lid. Pierce meat with fork all over and pour bottle of dressing on and cover overnight.  Put more fork marks in to make sure it is completely marinated.  Place on hot grill approximately 20 minutes. Should be medium rare.  Slice thin to serve.
