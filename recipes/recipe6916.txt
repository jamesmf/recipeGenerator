CHICKEN FRIED STEAK


1  lb. hamburger
1/2  c. flour
1  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
  crushed cracker crumbs
1  to 2 beaten eggs

      Mix meat, flour, salt and pepper. Form into 1/4-inch patties. Dip into beaten eggs, then into cracker crumbs. Fry for 7 minutes.
      These can be fried immediately or frozen for later use.
