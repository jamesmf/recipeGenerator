GARFIELD'S LASAGNA


1  lb. ground beef
1 (15 1/2 oz.)  jar spaghetti sauce
2  eggs
1 1/2  c. cottage cheese
1/4  c. Parmesan cheese
6  oz. (1 1/2 c.) shredded Mozzarella cheese
1  tsp. oregano or Italian seasoning
9  lasagna noodles
3/4  c. hot water

      Wash hands!  Brown beef, drain grease.  This is done with adult help.  Mix spaghetti sauce into ground beef.  Beat eggs.  Add cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese and 1 cup shredded cheese to eggs. Add oregano or Italian seasoning.  Mix well.  Place 3 uncooked lasagna noodles in a 12 x 7 x 2-inch baking dish.  Measure 1 1/3 cups meat mixture and spread over noodles.  Spread 1/2 of egg-cheese mixture over meat mixture.  Repeat noodles, meat, then egg-cheese mixture.  Top with 3 more uncooked noodles and remaining meat mixture.  Pour the hot water around all 4 edges of dish. Cover with foil.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 1/4 hours.  Sprinkle remaining Mozzarella cheese on top and cool 10 minutes before serving.
