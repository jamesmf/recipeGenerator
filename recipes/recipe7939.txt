CHICKEN CASSEROLE


1  chicken, cut up
1/2  can cream of mushroom soup
1/2 soup  can of milk
1  cup uncooked rice
1/4  cup water
1  package onion soup mix

      In a buttered casserole dish place chicken.  Add rice, soup, milk, water and onion soup mix.  Cover dish with foil.  Bake 2 hours at 350 degrees.
