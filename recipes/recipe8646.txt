TATER TOT CASSEROLE


1  lb. extra lean ground beef
1  can 98% fat-free cream of mushroom soup
1  can 98% fat-free cream of chicken soup
1  pkg. onion flavored Tater Tots
3/4  c. finely shredded Cheddar cheese
3/4  c. Kraft nonfat shredded cheese
1  onion, chopped

	Preheat oven to 350 degrees;.  Brown beef and onion together.  Drain well. Mix in both cans of soup.  Spray Pam on a 9 x 13 pan and arrange Tater Tots in single layer.  Top with beef and soup mixture.
	In a bowl, mix the two cheeses together. Sprinkle on top of the meat mixture. Bake for 30 minutes or until hot and bubbling.
