FRESNO CHICKEN


3  whole chicken breasts (boneless and cut in half)
1 (4 oz.)  can diced green chiles
1 (2 1/2 oz.)  can chopped black olives
3/4  c. Monterey Jack cheese
3/4  c. cheddar cheese
3  Tbsp. finely chopped onions
1/3  c. melted butter
1/4  tsp. chili powder
1/4  tsp. ground cumin
1  c. crushed tortilla chips
  Sour cream and salsa for toppings

      Beat chicken breasts to 1/4 inch thickness.  Drain chiles and olives and mix with cheeses and onion.  Put equal amounts on each chicken breast and roll - secure with toothpicks.  Combine butter, chili powder and cumin.  Coat chicken in butter mixture and roll in crushed chips.  Place in greased casserole dish.  Bake at 375 degrees; for 30 minutes.  Garnish with sour cream and salsa.
