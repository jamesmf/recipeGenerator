PASTA AND CARBONARA SAUCE


4  eggs (at room temperature)
1/4  c. butter
1  lb. fettucini
1/4  c. cream
7  oz. salami
1  c. Parmesan cheese
1/4  c. fresh parsley

      Beat eggs and cream until blended.  Cook pasta.  Put cooked pasta in a hot baking dish (250 degrees;).  Mix in butter and salami; pour egg mixture over and toss.  Add cheese and parsley.  Serve warm.
