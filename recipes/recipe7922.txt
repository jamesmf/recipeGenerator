NOCHA KABAB


1  lb. boneless beef, cut into 1-1/2-inch cubes
1-1/2  tsp. ginger/garlic paste
1  tsp. green chili paste
1-1/2  tsp. salt
  Pinch turmeric
2  Potatoes, boiled and mashed
1  carrot, shredded
1  tsp. cumin powder
1  Tbsp. lemon juice
1  Tbsp. vinegar
1/2  bunch cilantro, chopped
1/4  tsp. monosodium glutamate

      Wash beef and combine with ginger/garlic paste, green chili paste, salt, and turmeric.  Add 1 cup of water and cover; place over medium heat until it comes to a boil.  Turn down the heat and cook until the beef is tender and water has dried up.
      Let beef cool a bit then finely shred with hands.  Combine shredded meat, mashed potatoes, shredded carrots, cumin powder, lemon juice, vinegar, chopped cilantro, and msg.  Mix well and shape into seekhs or round patties and pan-fry, turning once, until golden.
