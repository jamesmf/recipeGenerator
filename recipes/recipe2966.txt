SAUSAGE ROLLS


4  c. self-rising flour
1/4  c. meal
1/4  c. sugar
2/3  c. oil
2/3 to 1  c. milk
2  lb. sausage

      Sift dry ingredients together. Add oil and milk, enough to make stiff dough. Roll into 2 rectangles. Spread sausage on this. Roll in lengthwise. Wrap each in wax paper and chill. Slice about 1/4-inch thick. Bake at 350 degrees; about 20 minutes.
