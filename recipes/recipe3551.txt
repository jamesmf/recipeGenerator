SALMON SPREAD


8  oz. cream cheese, softened
1/4  c. green onion and tops
1/2   tsp. dried dill weed
1/2   tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/4  tsp. salt
  dash of pepper sauce (clear kind)
  dash of parsley leaves
1 large  can red sock-eye salmon

	Combine cream cheese, with other ingredients except the salmon.  Drain and flake salmon and gently fold into cream cheese mixture.  Chill overnight or at least 4 hours.  Serve with crackers and vegetable sticks.
	Note:  1/4 cup of chopped pecans can be used in the spread. 1/4 cup chopped celery can also be added.
