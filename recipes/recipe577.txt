BLENDER FRENCH DRESSING


1  c. tomato juice
4  Tbsp. wine vinegar
4  Tbsp. finely chopped green pepper
1  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. dry mustard
1  clove garlic or garlic powder to taste
2  tsp. dried onion flakes
1  envelope Sweet 'N Low

      Place all ingredients in blender and run 1 minute on low.  This recipe serves 6. Count tomato juice in daily allowance of calories.
