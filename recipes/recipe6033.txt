SEVEN LAYER SALAD


  shredded lettuce
  shredded carrots
  chopped cucumbers
  chopped red onions
  chopped radishes
  Lesueur green peas, drained
2  c. mayonnaise
1/2  c. sugar
1  bag shredded cheddar cheese
  Real bacon bits

      Layer vegetables in bowl in order listed.  Mix together mayonnaise and sugar; spread over top of peas.  Spread a bag of shredded cheddar cheese over the mayonnaise layer.  Sprinkle bacon bits on top.  Cover and refrigerate overnight.  Prior to serving, mix together.
