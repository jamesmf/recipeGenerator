BROCCOLI SALAD


1  large head fresh broccoli (chopped)
10 - 12  slices bacon
1/2  cup raisins

 
Dressing:
1  cup Miracle Whip Salad Dressing
1/2  cup sugar
2  tbsp apple cider vinegar

      Cook bacon hard and crumble over broccoli, add raisins.  Mix Miracle Whip, sugar and cider vinegar for dressing and stir into broccoli mixture.  Let marinate for 3 hours or more.  Can be made the day before.
