MEXICAN FIESTA


4  lb. lean ground beef
1  large onion, chopped
4  Tbsp. chili powder
4 small  cans tomato sauce
1  can water
1 small  can tomato paste
2  Tbsp. garlic salt
1 28-oz.  can pinto beans
  Crushed tortillas chips
  Hot cooked rice
  Condiments:  grated Cheddar cheese, chopped onion, chopped tomatoes, chopped lettuce, Picante sauce

      Brown ground beef and onion; drain well and transfer to a large pot.  Add next 6 ingredients and mix well.  Simmer 1 hour on low heat.  Layer following ingredients on individual plates as follows: crushed Doritos; hot cooked rice; the meat sauce; grated Cheddar cheese; chopped onion; chopped tomatoes; chopped lettuce, and Picante sauce.  Makes 8 servings.
