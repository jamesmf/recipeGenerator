BEEF STROGANOFF


1  onion, chopped
1  stalk celery, chopped
1/4  c. oleo
1  lb. ground beef
2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
1/4  c. catsup
2  tbsp. flour
1  can mushroom pieces
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  c. sour cream

      Sauté onion and celery in butter, stir in ground beef, flour, salt and pepper. Sauté for 5 minutes. Add soup, mushrooms and catsup. Simmer for 20-30 minutes. Stir in sour cream when ready to serve. Serve over noodles. I like to use rice too.
