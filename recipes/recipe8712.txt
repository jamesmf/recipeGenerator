TURKEY CHILI


2  Tbsp. olive oil
1  lb. ground turkey
2 or more  cloves garlic, minced
1  large onion, chopped
2  c. chicken broth
2 to 3  Tbsp. fresh cilantro, chopped
1 1/2  Tbsp. lime juice
1 1/2  Tbsp. ground cumin
1/2  tsp. dried oregano
1/4  tsp. salt (taste before adding)
1 (11 oz.)  can white Shoepeg corn (undrained)
1 (15 to 16 oz.)  can butter beans (undrained)
1 (15 to 16 oz.)  can cannellini beans (undrained, can use Great Northern beans instead)

	In a Dutch oven, heat 1 tablespoon of oil and brown turkey.
	 In a separate saucepan, heat 1 tablespoon of oil and brown the minced garlic and onion.  Combine all ingredients in the Dutch oven and heat to boiling. Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 20 minutes.
