SWISS CHEESE TARTS


1 (3 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese, softened
1/2  c. softened butter
1  c. all-purpose flour

      Combine cream cheese and butter; mix well.  Stir in flour.  Mix well.  Chill.  Shape into 36 balls.  Place each ball into individual well of 1 3/4-inch muffin tins.
Filling:
1  lb. crumbled Swiss cheese
1  egg, beaten
  dash of salt
7  pieces crumbled cooked bacon
1 (5 oz.)  can evaporated milk
2  dashes of hot sauce

      Spoon 2 teaspoons of Swiss cheese in tart shell.  Combine egg, milk, salt and hot sauce together.  Mix well.  Spoon 1/2 teaspoon into tart shells.  Sprinkle bacon on top.  Bake at 425 degrees; for 7 minutes.  Reduce temperature to 325 degrees; and bake 15 minutes or until set.  (This freezes well.)
