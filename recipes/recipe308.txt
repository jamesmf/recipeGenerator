SPINACH DIP



1 (12 oz.)  carton 1% low-fat cottage cheese
1 (10 oz.)  pkg. frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
1/2  c. low-fat sour cream
1/4  c. dry vegetable soup mix
1  tsp. lemon juice
1 (10 oz.)  can water chestnuts, drained and chopped

      Process in food processor the cottage cheese.  Place in bowl. Press spinach between paper towels until barely moist.  Add spinach and remaining ingredients to cheese.  Stir; cover and refrigerate 3 hours.  Serve with crackers or raw vegetables.  Yields 3 cups.
      Per Serving (1 Tablespoon):  14 Calories and .4 g. Fat.
