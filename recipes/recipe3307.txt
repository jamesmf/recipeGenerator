GUACAMOLE DIP


2  ripe avocados
  juice of 1 lemon
1  mild onion, minced
1  clove garlic, crushed
1/2  tsp. chili powder
1/2 to 1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  firm small tomato, chopped

      Mash avocados with lemon juice.  Stir in remaining ingredients; cover with plastic wrap and chill.
