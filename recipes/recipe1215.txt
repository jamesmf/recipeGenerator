CINNAMON COOKIES


5  Tbsp. margarine
1  c. sifted plain flour
1/2  tsp. baking powder
  pinch of salt
2  tsp. artificial liquid sweetener
1  tsp. vanilla
1  Tbsp. milk, fruit juice or coffee
1  tsp. cinnamon

      Cream margarine until light and fluffy.  Mix artificial sweetener with vanilla and milk or one of the other liquids.  Stir into flour mixture (flour, baking powder and salt) and mix well. Shape dough into 30 even-sized balls and place on cookie sheet. Flatten balls with fork dipped in cold water.  Bake at 375 degrees; for 10 to 15 minutes.
