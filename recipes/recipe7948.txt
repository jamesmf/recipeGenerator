EASY MEAT LOAF


1  egg lightly beaten
1 (10 1/2 oz.)  can condensed French onion soup, undiluted
1  1/3 cups crushed butter-flavored crackers (about 33)
1  pound lean ground beef
1 (10 3/4 ounces)  can condensed golden mushroom soup, undiluted

      In a bowl, combine the egg, onion soup and cracker crumbs. Crumble beef over mixture and mix well.  Shape into a loaf.  Place in a greased 11 x 7 x 2 inch baking dish.  Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.
      Pour mushroom soup over loaf.  Bake 1 hour longer or until meat is no longer pink. Meat thermometer will read 160 degrees. Drain.  Let stand for 10 minutes before slicing.
