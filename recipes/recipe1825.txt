LAYERED GUACAMOLE SUPREME

1st Layer:
1  can refried beans
1  tsp. chili powder
1 small  jar hot peppers (omit if too spicy)

 
2nd Layer:
3  small avocados
1  tsp. lemon juice
1/2  tsp. garlic powder
1  medium chopped onion
1/2  c. sour cream
1/2  c. Bac*Os

 
3rd Layer:
1  can pitted black olives, sliced

 
4th Layer:
1  tomato, diced

 
5th Layer:
1  c. shredded Cheddar or Monterey Jack cheese

      Use a 9 x 13-inch glass dish.  Assemble all ingredients in layers.  Mix 1st layer together and spread in bottom of dish.  Mash avocados.  Mix 2nd layer and spread over 1st layer.  Spread sliced olives next.  Add diced tomatoes next.  Top with shredded cheese. Serve with taco sauce and tortilla chips.  This can be served cold and made ahead.
