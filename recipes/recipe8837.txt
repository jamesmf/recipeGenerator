PEPPER STEAK


1  round steak or sirloin steak, cut in strips
1  green bell pepper, seeded and cut in chunks
1  medium onion, sliced
1  stick margarine
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1/2  can water
1 small  jar mushrooms
1 small  jar pimentos

      Saut√© steak, pepper and onion in the margarine. Add mushroom soup, 1/2 can water, mushrooms and pimentos. Bring to boil, then cover and simmer for 45 minutes to 1 hour. Serve over hot rice.
