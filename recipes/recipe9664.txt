HAM & BROCCOLI QUICHE


1 1/2  c. cubed, cooked ham
1 1/2  c. shredded Swiss cheese
1  c. broccoli florets
4  eggs
1  c. milk
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. dry mustard
1/2  tsp. black pepper
1  (9 inch) pie shell

      Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Layer ham, cheese & broccoli in crust. In medium bowl combine remaining ingredients and pour over ham/broccoli. Bake 35 to 45 minutes. Let stand 5 minutes before serving.
