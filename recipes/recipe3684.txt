SAVORY HERBS


1  Tbsp. Black Pepper
1  Tbsp. Dried Marjoram
1  Tbsp. Dried Parsley
1  Tbsp. Ground Basil
1  Tbsp. Sage
1  Tbsp. Ground Thyme
1  Tbsp. Garlic Powder
1  Tbsp. Onion Powder

      Combine and mix well; makes 1/2 cup. Omit or add ingredients to suit personal taste. Use in place of seasoning salt to flavor soups, meats and vegetables.
       Note: Do not use if placed on a bland diet restriction.
