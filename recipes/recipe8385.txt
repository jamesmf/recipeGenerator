BARBECUE MEATBALLS


1  lb. ground beef
1  c. bread crumbs
1/2  c. milk
  salt to taste
1/2  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/4  c. vinegar
1  Tbsp. sugar
1/2  c. water
1/2  c. ketchup
1/2  onion, chopped

      Mix together ground beef, bread crumbs, milk and salt.  Then roll into balls the size you wish and put in a glass pan.  Set aside.  Mix Worcestershire, vinegar, sugar, water, ketchup and chopped onion.  Pour this over the meatballs and bake uncovered for 45 to 50 minutes at 375 degrees;.
