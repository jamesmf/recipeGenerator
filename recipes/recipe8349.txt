POTLUCK CHICKEN CASSEROLE


8  c. chicken, cooked and cubed
2 (10 3/4 oz.)  cans cream of chicken soup
1  c. sour cream
1  c. cracker crumbs (butter flavored; 25 crackers)
2  Tbsp. butter or margarine, melted
1  tsp. celery seed
  fresh parsley and sweet pepper rings (optional)

	Combine chicken, soup and sour cream. Spread into a greased 13 x 9-inch baking dish. Combine crumbs, butter and celery seed. Sprinkle over chicken mixture. Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees; for 30 to 35 minutes or until bubbly. Garnish with parsley and red peppers, if desired. Yield: 10 to 12 servings.
