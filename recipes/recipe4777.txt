ARTICHOKE-FETA GREEN SALAD
(Makes 4 Servings)  

6  Tbsp. fat-free Italian salad dressing
1  Tbsp. red wine vinegar
1/4  tsp. dry mustard
1  c. artichokes (frozen), thawed
4  c. baby lettuce leaves, mixed
1  c. cherry tomatoes, halved
1/2  small red onion, sliced and separated
1/4  c. Feta cheese with basil and tomato

      Combine first 3 ingredients in a large bowl. Stir with a whisk. Stir in artichokes; marinate in the refrigerator 1 hour. Add salad greens, tomatoes and onion. Toss gently. Place 1 1/4 cups salad on each of 4 plates. Sprinkle each with 1 tablespoon cheese.
