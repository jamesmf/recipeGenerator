GOLDEN FRIED MUSHROOMS


  fresh mushrooms
1/2  c. milk
1  egg
1  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
2  c. flour
3/4  c. instant mashed potatoes
1/2  tsp. lemon pepper
  salt and pepper to taste

      Wash mushrooms thoroughly; drain.  Mix egg, milk and Worcestershire sauce; set to one side.  Mix flour, instant potatoes, lemon pepper, salt and pepper.  Marinate mushrooms, a few at a time, about 3 to 5 minutes in egg and milk mixture. Press into flour and potato mixture.  Make sure the mushrooms are coated.  Fry about 5 to 10 minutes.
