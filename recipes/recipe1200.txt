WHIPPED TOPPING


1/2  c. nonfat dry milk
1/2  c. ice water
2  tsp. sugar
1/2  tsp. vanilla

      Chill bowl and beaters.  Combine milk powder and ice water. Beat until stiff.  Add sugar and vanilla.  Serve immediately.
      Makes 2 tablespoons per serving; free, no exchanges.  Contains 12 calories.
