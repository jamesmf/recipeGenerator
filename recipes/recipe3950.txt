QUICK SNACK TIME PIZZA


1  can biscuits
  pizza sauce
  pepperoni
  sausage, cooked and drained
  shredded mozzarella cheese

      Heat oven to 350 degrees. Remove biscuits from can and flatten. Place onto, (vegetable sprayed), cookie sheet. Top each biscuit with 1 tablespoon pizza sauce. Place pepperoni and sausage on top of sauce. Top each with cheese. Bake 8--10 minutes or until biscuits are done and cheese melted.
