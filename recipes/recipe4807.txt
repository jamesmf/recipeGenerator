SWEDISH BEAN AND BACON SOUP


6  strips bacon, cut up
1  c. chopped onion
7  c. chicken broth (from cubes)
1 (15 oz.)  can each small white Great Northern beans and navy beans
2  c. sliced carrots
1  tsp. dried marjoram
1/2  tsp. ground allspice
1  small bay leaf
1  large potato, cubed
4  c. shredded cabbage

      Cook bacon in a 5-quart pot over medium heat 5 minutes or until crisp.  Drain on paper towels.  Pour off all but 1 tablespoon fat.  Saut√© onion in fat 4 minutes or until soft.  Add broth plus 1 extra cup water, the carrots, bacon, marjoram, allspice and bay leaf.  Bring to a boil; reduce heat.  Cover and simmer 10 minutes.  Add remaining ingredients and boil gently 10 minutes or until vegetables are tender.  Discard bay leaf.  Top servings with sour cream.
