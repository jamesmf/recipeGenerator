BLACK OLIVE GARLIC DIP


1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese, softened
1/2  c. butter
1 (4.5 oz.)  can chopped black olives, drained well
1  bunch green onions, chopped fine
  garlic powder
  salt

      Combine cream cheese and butter. Add olives and onion. Add about 2 tablespoons garlic powder and 1 teaspoon salt to taste. Chill overnight. Serve with garlic chips or crackers.
