POT PIE (TURKEY OR CHICKEN)


1  can cream of chicken soup
1  c. chopped turkey or chicken
1  bag frozen vegetables
1 1/4  c. bisquick
1/2  c. milk
1  egg

      Heat oven to 400 degrees;. Combine soup, meat and vegetables. Place in a 9 inch pie plate. Mix remaining ingredients, pour over meat mixture. Bake 30 minutes.
