OVEN FRIED CHICKEN


1  Tbsp. oleo
2/3  c. Bisquick baking mix
1 1/2  tsp. paprika
1 1/4  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
2 1/2 to 3 1/2  lb. broiler or fryer, cut up

      Heat oven to 425 degrees;.  Heat oleo in 9 x 13 pan until melted.  Mix baking mix, paprika, salt and pepper; coat chicken.  Place chicken, skin side down, in pan.  Bake, uncovered, 35 minutes.  Turn over; bake until done, about 15 minutes or longer.  Makes 6 servings.
