MEDITERRANEAN ARTICHOKE-CHICKEN-RICE SALAD


2 (6 oz.)  jars marinated artichoke hearts (undrained)
2  c. cooked Minute rice
2  c. cooked chicken, bite size
1/3  c. mayonnaise
1/2  c. chopped celery
1/2  c. thinly sliced scallions
  salt and pepper to taste

      Mix all ingredients and serve over lettuce.
