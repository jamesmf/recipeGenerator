HOT DOG SAUCE BY RALPH


10  lb. hamburg
1  c. chopped onion
1  c. chopped pepper
2  Tbsp. pepper
2  Tbsp. salt
7  Tbsp. paprika
2  Tbsp. garlic
7  Tbsp. oregano
4  oz. chili powder
2  Tbsp. cinnamon
2  Tbsp. cloves

      Cook hamburg, onion and pepper together until very fine.  Add pepper, salt, paprika, garlic, oregano, chili powder, cinnamon and cloves.  Combine with hamburg mixture.  Add 3 cups water.  Simmer 3 hours.
