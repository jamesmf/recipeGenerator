HUMUS


1  medium onion, finely diced
2  cloves garlic, minced
3  Tbsp. olive oil
2  cans chickpeas, rinsed and drained
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. coriander
1/2  c. chopped fresh parsley
6  Tbsp. lemon juice
1/2  tsp. cumin
1/4  tsp. cayenne

      Saut onion and garlic in oil in a skillet until browned. Combine onion/garlic mixture with remaining ingredients in a food processor. Blend until smooth. Let stand 30 minutes before serving. Serve with toasted pita bread slices and vegetables.
