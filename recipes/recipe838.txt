STIR-FRIED CHICKEN AND CASHEWS


2  whole medium chicken breasts, skinned, boned and cut in 1/2-inch cubes
2  Tbsp. soy sauce
1  Tbsp. plus 1/2 tsp. dry sherry
1  c. raw cashew nuts
2  Tbsp. cooking oil
1  Tbsp. hoisin sauce
  sliced green onion (optional)
  hot cooked rice

      In medium bowl, combine chicken, soy sauce and sherry; set aside.  In a 12-inch skillet or wok, stir-fry cashews in hot oil over high heat for 1 to 2 minutes or until browned.  Lift out with a slotted spoon and set aside.  Add the chicken mixture to the skillet.  Stir-fry over high heat for 2 to 3 minutes or until just done.  Stir in hoisin sauce and cashews.  If desired, sprinkle with sliced green onion.  Serve at once with hot cooked rice.  Makes 4 or 5 servings.
