GREEN TOMATO RELISH


2  gal. green tomatoes, quartered
1/2  gal. onions, chopped
1  pt. chopped hot peppers
1  qt. sweet peppers, chopped
4  Tbsp. canning salt
4  c. sugar
3  pt. vinegar
1  Tbsp. pickling spice or allspice

      Mix together salt, sugar, spice and vinegar; bring to a boil. Mix with the vegetables and cook for 5 minutes or until tomatoes turn light in color. Do not overcook. Pack lightly in jars.
