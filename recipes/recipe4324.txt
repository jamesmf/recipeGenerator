COTTAGE CHEESE SALAD


1 large  carton small curd cottage cheese
1 small  box jello (your choice of flavor)
1 large  can crushed pineapple, drained
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. mini marshmallows
1  small container Cool Whip
  fresh strawberries (to garnish)

      Mix container of cottage cheese with dry jello. Add miniature marshmallows, pineapple and Cool Whip. Mix. Garnish top with sliced or whole strawberries. Serves 12.
