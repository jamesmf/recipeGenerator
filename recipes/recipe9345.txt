CHICKEN DIVAN


3  chicken breasts, cooked and cut into quarters
2  pkg. broccoli, booked until tender
1  can cream of chicken or cream of mushroom soup
2/3  c. mayonnaise
1/3  c. evaporated milk
1/2  c. grated cheese
1  tsp. lemon juice
1/4  tsp. curry powder
1/2  c. fine bread crumbs or Pepperidge Farms dressing mix
1  Tbsp. butter

      Put cooked broccoli in baking dish, place chicken on top. Mix soup, milk, mayonnaise, cheese, lemon juice and curry together.  Pour over the broccoli and chicken.  Sprinkle with bread crumbs and butter.  Bake at 350 degrees for 25 to 30 minutes.  Serves 6 to 8.
