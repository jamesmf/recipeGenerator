FRUIT SALAD


1 large  can pears, sliced
1 large  can sliced peaches
1  can pineapple chunks
10  oz. frozen strawberries, thawed
1 small  package of instant vanilla pudding mix

      Drain the pears, peaches and pineapple. Put fruit mixture into a large bowl. Add strawberries and juice. Sprinkle with dry pudding mix. Let set several hours before serving. Refrigerate leftovers.
