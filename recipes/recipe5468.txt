EASY FRUIT SALAD


1 (20 oz.)  can crushed pineapple
1 (16 oz.)  can fruit cocktail, drained
2 (8 oz.)  cans mandarin oranges, drained
1 small  pkg. instant vanilla pudding
1 (9 oz.)  carton whipped topping
1/2  c. chopped pecans
1/2  c. flaked coconut (optional)

      Mix fruits in bowl.  Add pudding and mix well.  Fold in whipped topping.  Sprinkle pecans and coconut over the top.
