CORNED BEEF HASH


  leftover corned beef
  boiled potatoes
  cooked carrots
1  or 2 cubes butter
  yellow onions
  salt and pepper to taste

	(Note:	Amount of potatoes, carrots and onions will depend on amount of meat).
	Cut up corned beef, potatoes and carrots into pieces.  Put all through meat grinder.  Melt butter; add chopped onions.  Brown and add beef, potato and carrot mixture.  Cook and stir until hot enough to serve; season with salt and pepper to taste.  Maybe served with poached egg and ketchup.  Any leftover meats may be used instead of corned beef.
