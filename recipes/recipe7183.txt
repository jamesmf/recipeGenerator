BAKED SANDWICHES


16  slices bread
  boiled ham or Canadian bacon
  cheese
6  eggs
3  c. milk
1/2  tsp. dry mustard
1/2  tsp. salt
3  c. corn flakes
1/2  c. butter

      Lay 8 slices of sandwich bread in a well-greased glass baking dish.  Put ham or Canadian bacon on bread.  Add slices of cheese. Top with other slices of bread.  Beat eggs with milk; add dry mustard and salt.  Pour over sandwiches and let stand overnight in fridge.  Sprinkle with corn flakes or Special K, mixed with butter.  Bake 1 hour at 350 degrees;.
