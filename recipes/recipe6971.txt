QUICK CHILI CASSEROLE


1  can chili
2  cans whole kernel corn
1/2  pkg. taco seasoning
1  pkg. cornbread mix

      Mix and heat chili, corn, and taco seasoning. Pour into a 9 x 13 pan. Mix cornbread as directed on package directions. Pour over chili mixture. Bake at 375 degrees; until cornbread is done.
