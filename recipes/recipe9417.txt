ROASTED GARLIC LIME CHICKEN


6  boneless chicken breasts, about 1 to 2 lb.
2  Tbsp. lime or lemon juice
2  tsp. garlic salt
1  tsp. seasoned pepper

      Spray 13 X 9 X 2 inch baking dish with nonstick cooking spray; arrange chicken in dish.  Sprinkle chicken with lime juice, then garlic salt and seasoned pepper.  Roast in 400 degree oven until meat is no longer pink and juices run clear when cut, about 30 minutes.
