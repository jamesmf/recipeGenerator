"ZERO" HERB DRESSING


3/4  c. tomato or V-8 (6 oz. can)
1/4  c. red wine or sherry vinegar
1  Tbsp. chopped parsley
1  Tbsp. chopped chives or scallions
1  clove garlic, crushed
1/2  tsp. salt
  pinch of dried savory or oregano
  pinch of sugar
  pinch of cayenne
  fresh ground pepper

      In a bowl, whisk all ingredients until well blended. Alternately combine all ingredients in a jar; secure lid and shake until well blended. Remove garlic before serving. Makes 1 cup fat-free dressing. Great for all tossed salads/veggies!
      Cal-11, CHO-2.2, Pro-.2, Fat-0.
