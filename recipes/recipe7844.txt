TURKEY TETRAZZINI


2  Tbsp. oil
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  can cream of celery soup
2 1/2  c. cooked spaghetti
3/4  c. milk
2  c. cooked, chopped turkey
1  can mushrooms
1/4  c. ripe olives
1/4  c. chopped green peppers
1/4  c. chopped onion

      Cook peppers and onion in oil.  Mix with the remaining ingredients in a casserole dish.  Sprinkle 1/4 c. Parmesan cheese on top.  Bake at 350 degrees; for approximately 30 minutes until bubbly.
