HOT CHICKEN DIP


1  pkg. Cooked chicken
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  c. mayo
1/4  can tomato soup
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese (softened)
1  c. chopped celery
1/4  c. chopped green onion
1  tsp. onion powder
1  tsp. garlic powder

      Combine all ingredients in large saucepan and heat until cheese is melted.  Add chicken and pour in Pyrex dish.  Bake at 350 degrees; for approximately 25-30 minutes.  Serve hot with scoop Fritos.
