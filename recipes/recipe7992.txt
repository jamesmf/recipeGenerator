BREADED PORK CHOPS
(Cooking For Two)  

1  egg
1  Tbsp. barbecue sauce
1/3  c. dry bread crumbs
2  tsp. grated Parmesan cheese
1/4  tsp. dried oregano
1/8 to 1/4  tsp. lemon-pepper seasoning
1/8  tsp. onion salt
2  bone-in pork chops, 3/4 inch thick

      In a bowl, combine egg and barbecue sauce; mix well.  In another bowl, combine bread crumbs, cheese, oregano, lemon-pepper and salt.  Dip chops in egg mixture, then coat with bread crumb mixture.  Place in a greased 8 inch square baking dish. Refrigerate for 2 hours.  Bake, uncovered, at 325 degrees; for 1 hour or until juices run clear.
