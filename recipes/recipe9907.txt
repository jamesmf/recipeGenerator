VEGETABLE RICE MEDLEY


1  c. uncooked long grain rice
2 1/4  c. water
2  to 3  Tbsp. onion or vegetable soup mix
1/4  tsp. salt
2  c. frozen, corn, peas, or mixed vegetables

      Combine rice, water, soup mix and salt.  Bring to boil.  Add vegetables and return to boil.  Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 15 minutes.  Cook until rice and vegetables are tender.  Serves 4 - 6.
