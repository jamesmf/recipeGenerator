PEANUT BLOSSOMS


1/2  c. margarine
2/3  c. crunchy peanut butter
1/3  c. granulated sugar
1/2  c. brown sugar, packed
1  egg
1 1/2  c. flour
3/4  tsp. baking soda
1/2  tsp. baking powder
  chocolate kisses

      Beat margarine and peanut butter.  Gradually add sugars.  Cream thoroughly before adding egg.  Beat some more.  Combine flour, baking soda and baking powder; beat into creamed mixture.  Chill for at least 1 hour.  Make into balls.  Roll balls in sugar and press chocolate kiss in center of each.  Place 2 inches apart on greased pan.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 10 minutes.
