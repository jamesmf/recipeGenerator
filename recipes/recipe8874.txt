TACO RICE CASSEROLE


1 1/2  lb. ground beef, cooked and drained
1  pkg. taco seasoning
1  can Ro-Tel tomatoes, diced
1 small  box Mexican Velveeta, cubed
6  c. Minute rice, prepared
1  can corn
1  can kidney or Ranch Style beans

      Cook ground beef and drain. Add taco seasoning mix. Add other ingredients. Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 plus minutes. This recipe freezes well.
