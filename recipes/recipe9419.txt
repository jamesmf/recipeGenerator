BAKED CHICKEN BREAST


4  to 6 boneless breasts
  sliced Swiss cheese
1  can cream of chicken soup
1/2  stick melted butter
1  c. Pepperidge stuffing mix

	Salt chicken and place in casserole dish that has been sprayed with Pam. Place a slice of Swiss cheese over each piece of chicken. Pour undiluted soup over each one. Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour, covered. Melt butter and add to stuffing mix. Spread over chicken and return to oven, uncovered, for 15 more minutes.
