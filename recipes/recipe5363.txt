24 HOUR MARINATED SLAW


1  cabbage
3/4  c. oil
1  tsp. celery seed
1  c. vinegar
2  onions
1/2  c. sugar
1  Tbsp. dry mustard
2  Tbsp. sugar

      Spread cabbage and thinly sliced onions in flat pan; sprinkle 1/2 cup sugar over all. Combine all other ingredients for the dressing and bring to a boil. Pour over cabbage. Store in covered container (airtight) in refrigerator for 12 to 24 hours. Keeps a month.
