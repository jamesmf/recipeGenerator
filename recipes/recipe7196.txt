SWEET ONION CASSEROLE


1/2  c. uncooked white rice
3  onions, chopped coarsely
1/4  c. melted butter
1  c. grated Swiss cheese
2/3  c. half and half
1  tsp. salt

      Cook rice per box instructions.  Cook onions in butter in large skillet until limp, but not browned.  Combine all ingredients into greased 2-quart casserole.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour.  Servings: 8 to 10.
