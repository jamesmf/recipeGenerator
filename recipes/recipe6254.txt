LA MADELINE TOMATO SOUP


8 or 10 tomatoes, peeled and chopped or 4  c. canned tomatoes, crushed
  Parmesan cheese
4  c. tomato juice or 1/2 chicken stock and 1/2 tomato juice
12  to 14 basil leaves + more for garnish
1  c. heavy cream or light cream
1/2  c. butter (unsalted) or margarine
1/4  tsp. cracked pepper

      Combine tomatoes and juice in a large saucepan on medium heat.  Simmer 30 minutes.  Combine tomato and basil mixture in a blender or processor; do in small batches until smooth.  Return to saucepan on low heat.  Stir in cream and butter.  Heat thoroughly. Garnish with Parmesan cheese and basil.
