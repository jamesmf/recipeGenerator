CREAM OF BROCCOLI SOUP


1 (10 oz.)  pkg. frozen broccoli, chopped
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1 1/2  c. milk
  black pepper to taste
2  Tbsp. butter or margarine
  tarragon (if desired)

      Cook  broccoli  according to package directions; drain. Mix soup  with  milk;  stir  constantly while heating.  Don't boil. Add  broccoli,  pepper to taste, butter and herb.  Heat to bubbly and serve.
