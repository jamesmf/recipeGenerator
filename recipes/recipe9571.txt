GROUND TURKEY MEAT LOAF


1 1/2  lb. ground turkey
4  slices soft white bread, shredded
1  pkg. dry onion soup mix
1/2  c. milk
1  small onion, chopped fine
1 1/2  tsp. salt
1  tsp. thyme
1/2  tsp. pepper
1/2  tsp. garlic powder
2  Tbsp. dry parsley flakes

      Mix all ingredients with hands thoroughly.  Place in 9 x 5 x 3 loaf pan.  Bake in 350 degrees; oven, uncovered, 50 to 60 minutes.
