PASTA SALAD DRESSING


2  cloves garlic, crushed
1/2  c. salad oil
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. dry mustard
1  tsp. white pepper
2  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
6  anchovy fillets
3  Tbsp. grated Parmesan cheese
2  eggs
2  Tbsp. lemon juice

      Combine all ingredients and puree until smooth.  Add and toss with pasta and veggies.
