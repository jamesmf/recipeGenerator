CHILI RICE BAKE


1  lb. hamburger
1  c. chopped celery
2  c. cooked rice
2  eggs
1 1/2  c. milk
1  pack Chili-O seasoning mix
1  c. shredded cheese

      Preheat oven to 325 degrees;. Brown hamburger meat with chopped celery in skillet. Add cooked rice. Spoon into buttered, shallow casserole dish. Beat eggs together with milk and Chili-O seasoning mix. Pour over rice mixture. Sprinkle with cheese. Bake at 325 degrees; for 30 minutes.
