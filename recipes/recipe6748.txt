QUICK LASAGNA


1 (16 oz.)  jar Ragu spaghetti sauce with mushrooms
1  lb. ground beef
1/2  box lasagna noodles
1 (16 oz.)  carton cottage cheese
1  egg
2  Tbsp. Parmesan cheese
  salt to taste
2  pkg. sliced Mozzarella cheese

      Preheat oven to 400 degrees;.  Cook and drain ground beef.  Add Ragu. Cook noodles until tender.  Drain.  Mix egg, salt, pepper and Parmesan cheese with cottage cheese.  In an 8 x 11 1/2-inch baking dish, layer 1/2 noodles, Ragu mixture, cottage cheese mixture and Mozzarella.  Repeat layers.  Bake 15 to 20 minutes.
