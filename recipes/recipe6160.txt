BROCCOLI - CAULIFLOWER SALAD


1  head fresh broccoli
1  head fresh cauliflower
1  large sweet onion, thinly sliced
12  oz. pkg. bacon, cooked crispy
2  c. Cheddar cheese, grated
  Ranch dressing (small bottle)
2  Tbsp. sugar

	Cut broccoli and cauliflower into bite-size pieces. Add onion, bacon and cheese. Add dressing mix to cover vegetables. Chill; refrigerate leftovers.
