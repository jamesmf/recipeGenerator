CHICKEN TETRAZZINI


  chicken (save broth)
8  oz. spaghetti noodles
1  can cream of mushroom, chicken or celery soup
1 soup  can milk
  grated Cheddar cheese
  salt and pepper to taste
  mushrooms, green peppers, onion and celery (optional)

      *Could use turkey instead of chicken.
      Cook and debone chicken. Cook noodles in broth (include vegetables if you want). Mix chicken, soup and milk. Add noodles. Pour in casserole dish. Top with cheese. Cook at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes.
