PAMPERED PORK CHOPS


6  pork chops 1/2 - 3/4 inches thick
1/4  c. orange marmalade
1  pkg. onion soup mix
1/4   c. white wine
1  bottle Russian dressing
3  tablespoons brown sugar

	Rinse pork chops and place in a 13 x 9-inch Pyrex dish. Combine other ingredients and stir until well mixed.  Pour over pork chops.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 30-45 minutes or until done.
