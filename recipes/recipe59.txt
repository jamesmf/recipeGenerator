CHOCOLATE CHIP AND WALNUT MUFFINS


1/3  c. granulated sugar
1/3  c. packed brown sugar
2  c. all-purpose flour
2  tsp. baking powder
1/2  tsp. salt
2/3  c. milk
1/2  c. (1 stick) unsalted butter, melted and cooled
2  eggs, lightly beaten
1 1/2  tsp. vanilla
6  oz. (1 c.) milk chocolate chips
6  oz. (1 c.) semi-sweet chocolate chips
1/2  c. chopped nuts

      In a large bowl, stir together sugars, flour, baking powder and salt.  In another bowl mix together milk, butter, eggs and vanilla until blended.  Make a well in center of dry ingredients.  Add milk mixture and stir to combine.  Stir in chips and nuts.  Spoon batter into greased muffin cups; grease the edges around cups.  Bake in 400 degrees; oven for 15 to 20 minutes or until a cake tester comes out clean.  Remove muffins from oven and cool 5 minutes.  Remove from pans and cool on rack.  Serve warm or cold.  Makes 12 large muffins.
