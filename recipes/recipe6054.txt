CRANBERRY SALAD


2  c. fresh cranberries (1 bag)
1 small  can crushed pineapple (leave juice in)
1/4  c. orange juice
1/2  c. boiling water
1  c. sugar
1/2  c. chopped celery
1/2  c. chopped pecans
1 small  pkg. strawberry jello

      Grind cranberries; cover with sugar and set aside.  Dissolve jello in boiling water.  Add orange juice, pineapple, and rest of ingredients with cranberries to the jello.  Pour in mold and chill overnight.
