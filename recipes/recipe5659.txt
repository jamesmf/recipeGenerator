QUICK CORN CHOWDER


4  slices Oscar Meyer "ready to serve" bacon
1  can Campbell's Classic cream of potato soup
1  c. milk (low-fat or whole)
1 (11 oz.)  can Green Giant Corn Niblets (or equivalent amount frozen Niblets)

	Heat bacon in microwave for 20 to 30 seconds and set aside.
	 In a saucepan, mix soup, milk and corn. Break bacon into small pieces and add to mixture. Heat until simmers (about 5 minutes). Serve with oyster crackers, if desired.
