LUNCH MEAT HORS D'OEUVRES


1  pkg. lunch meat (ham or salami)

 
Filling:
1  (8 oz.) cream cheese
2  tsp. minced onion

      Let cream cheese get room temperature. Mix onion into cheese until smooth. Smooth filling over slices of lunch meat. Alternate lunch meat and filling until the stack is 5 slices high. Refrigerate overnight and cut into bite size stacks. Make filling approximately as thick as 1 slice lunch meat.
