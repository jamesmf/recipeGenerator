MARSHMALLOW BARS


2  c. flour
2/3  c. firmly packed brown sugar
1  c. butter or margarine, softened
1  tsp. vanilla
3  c. miniature marshmallows
6  oz. pkg. chocolate chips
3  Tbsp. butter or margarine
1  Tbsp. water

      Heat oven to 350 degrees;.  Grease 15 x 10-inch jellyroll pan.  In large bowl, combine flour, brown sugar, 1 cup butter and vanilla; blend well.  Spread in prepared pan.  Bake 15 minutes; remove from oven.  Sprinkle with marshmallows.  Return to oven; bake 5 to 6 minutes until marshmallows are slightly melted.  In top of double boiler, melt chocolate chips and 3 tablespoons butter.  Add water and stir until smooth.  Drizzle over marshmallows.  Cool and cut into bars.
