BARBECUE SAUCE


1  c. tomato juice
1  envelope powdered mix for chicken broth
2  Tbsp. lemon juice or vinegar
2  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  clove garlic
3/4  tsp. dry mustard
1  tsp. dehydrated onion flakes
1/2  tsp. paprika
  dash of coarse pepper
  dash of cayenne pepper
1/2  packet Sweet 'N Low (sugar substitute)

      Heat all ingredients in a saucepan until thickened.  Use as a baste.  May be used freely!
