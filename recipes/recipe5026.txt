TOMATO SPINACH SALAD


1/2  c. mayonnaise or dressing
1/2  c. grated parmesean
1/4  c. milk
1 1/2  tsp. dill weed
1 1/2  tsp. dried minced onion
1  1/2 tsp lemon pepper
2  c. cherry tomatoes
1  bag spinach torn

      In a small bowl or jar with tight fitting lid, combine first six ingredients; mix or shake well.  Chill at least 1 hr.  Just before serving, combine spinach and tomatoes in a large salad bowl. Whisk or shake dressing; pour over salad and toss.  (If dressing thickens, thin with additional milk if desired.)
