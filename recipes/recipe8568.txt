BAKED PORK CHOPS


6  Pork Chops
  Ketchup
  Coca-Cola
  Salt
  Pepper

      Wash and dry pork chops.  Place on waxed paper.  Salt and pepper on each side.  Place chops in foil lined baking pan.  Put a few dabs of ketchup about the size of a dime on each one.  Pour enough coca-cola to cover chops.  Cover with foil and seal.  Bake in 350 degrees oven for an hour or more depending on thickness of chops.
