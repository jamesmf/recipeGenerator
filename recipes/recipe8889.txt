ITALIAN DELIGHT


1  lb. ground beef
1  chopped onion
1 small  can tomatoes
1/2  box macaroni, cooked
1 small  can corn
1  can tomato soup
1  can water
1  tsp. salt to taste
1/4  tsp. black pepper
2  Tbsp. chilli powder
5  slices cheese

      Brown hamburger and onion in skillet. Place half the macaroni in bottom of greased casserole, then put a layer of meat and corn. Make second macaroni, corn and meat. Pour tomato soup over the whole thing and top with cheese. Bake at 350 degrees; for 40 to 60 minutes.
