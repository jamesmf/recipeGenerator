HOT CRANBERRY CASSEROLE


3  c. chopped apples
2  c. raw cranberries
1 1/2  tsp. lemon juice
1 1/2  c. sugar
1  stick melted margarine
1 1/3  c. quick cooking oatmeal
1  c. chopped nuts
1/3  c. brown sugar

      In 2-quart buttered casserole, put apples and cranberries. Cover with lemon juice and sugar.  Top with margarine, oats, nuts and brown sugar.  Bake, uncovered, in a 300 degrees; oven for approximately 1 hour.
