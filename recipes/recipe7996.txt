BAKED STUFFED MACARONI SHELLS


1/2  lb. hamburger
1/2  c. chopped onion
1/2  tsp. minced fresh garlic
1 (15 oz.)  can tomato sauce
1 (6 oz.)  can tomato paste
1/2  tsp. salt
  sprinkle of nutmeg
1 1/2  c. cottage cheese
1  egg
  oregano
1/4  tsp. pepper
1/4  c. grated Parmesan cheese
4  oz. jumbo macaroni (18 shells)

      In a large skillet, cook onion, garlic and meat.  Pour off any fat.  Stir in tomato sauce, tomato paste, salt, pepper and oregano.  Bring to a boil and turn to low and cook 15 minutes. Meanwhile in medium bowl, mix cottage cheese, egg, Parmesan cheese, parsley and nutmeg.  Heat oven to 350 degrees;.  Spread 2 1/2 cups tomato sauce mixture in baking dish.  Fill each shell with cheese mixture and arrange stuffed shells in dish.  Top with remaining sauce. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes.
