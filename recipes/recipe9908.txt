CHEESY POTATOES


1 (2 lb.)  pkg. frozen hash brown potatoes - thawed
1  c. onions, diced
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  pint sour cream
1  stick butter, melted
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. sharp cheddar cheese, shredded
1  cup corn flakes
1/4  c. melted butter

      Mix first 6 ingredients and place in large baking dish.  Top with 1 cup corn flakes and drizzle with 1/4 cup melted butter. Cover with foil and bake at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes.  Remove foil and bake another 15 minutes.
