BAKED CHICKEN


4  lb. boneless chicken breast
  salt to taste
  pepper to taste
1/4  c. water

      Wash chicken. Layer in pan with heavy-duty foil. Salt and pepper. Put light layer of Bertolli olive oil over it. Put 1/4 cup of water. Make tinfoil tent sealed on each end. Bake at 375 degrees; for 2 1/2 hours.
