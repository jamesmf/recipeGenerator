TACO SALAD


1  lb. ground beef
1  pkg. taco tortilla chips
1 (15 oz.)  can ranch style beans
1  medium head of lettuce
1  medium onion, chopped
4  medium tomatoes, chopped and well drained
1  avocado, cut-up
1  c. grated Cheddar cheese
1 small  bottle Catalina dressing

	Brown beef; slightly crush tortilla chips.  Mix together all ingredients except dressing.  Toss well.  Add dressing and mix thoroughly.  Serve immediately.
