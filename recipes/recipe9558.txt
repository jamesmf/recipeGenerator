CHICKEN AND RICE


4  cut up chicken breasts
1  box long grain and wild rice mix with seasonings
1  can golden mushroom soup
  Parmesan cheese

      Empty rice and seasoning packet into baking dish. Mix soup with a can of water.  Put chicken on top of rice and pour soup mixture over all.  Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes.
