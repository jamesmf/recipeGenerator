CASHEW CHICKEN


2  cans cream of chicken soup
3  or more cooked chicken breasts, deboned
1  can water chestnuts, chopped
3/4  c. cashews
  onion (optional)
1  Tbsp. mayo
  few crushed potato chips

      Mix all ingredients except for potato chips.  Add chips on top of mixture before cooking.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes.  Serve over rice.
