PORK CHOP CASSEROLE


4  bone-in pork loin chops (1/2 inch thick)
1  Tbsp. vegetable oil
1 1/3  c. uncooked long grain rice
1  c. orange juice
1  can condensed chicken with rice soup, undiluted

      In a large skillet, brown pork chops in oil; drain.  Place the rice in an ungreased shallow 3 quart baking dish; pour orange juice over rice.  Top with pork chops and soup.  Cover and bake at 350 degrees; for 40-45 minutes or until pork juices run clear and rice is tender.
