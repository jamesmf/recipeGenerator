PEANUT BUTTER PORCUPINES


1/2  c. peanut butter
1/2  c. honey or less to taste
1  c. toasted wheat germ
3/4  c. raisins
  coconut

 
Optional:
  sunflower seeds
  peanuts
1/2  c. cocoa powder

      Mix first 4 ingredients and roll into balls.  Add optional ingredients as desired, then roll in coconut.  Refrigerate until firm.  Recipe keeps well in the freezer.
