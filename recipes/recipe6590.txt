RED BEANS AND RICE


2  cans kidney beans
2  cans stewed tomatoes
  Polish sausage (as much as desired)
1/2  onion
2  bell peppers, diced
  rice
  salt and pepper

      Add beans with juice, tomatoes with juice and Polish sausage in chunks. Add bell peppers and onion all together in a pot. Bring to a boil. Add cornstarch and water to thicken. Salt and pepper to taste. Cook rice as instructed. Serve over rice.
