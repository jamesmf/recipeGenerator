HAMBURGER CASSEROLE


2  lb. hamburger
2  (10 1/2 oz.) mushroom soup
1  small bag Tater Tots

      Brown hamburger in skillet. Drain well. Add mushroom soup to hamburger. Pour mixture into a 9 x 12 baking dish. Layer Tater Tots on top. Cook in oven at 400 degrees; or until Tater Tots are brown.
