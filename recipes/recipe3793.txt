GOLDEN GLOW PICKLES


6  qt. ripe cucumbers, peeled and diced
1  doz. pickling onions, sliced
1/3  c. salt
2  Tbsp. turmeric powder
6  c. white sugar
1 1/2  c. cider vinegar
1/4  c. mustard seed
30  whole cloves

      Combine cucumbers, onions and salt and let stand 3 hours. Drain and add remaining ingredients.  Boil until transparent. Seal in jars.
