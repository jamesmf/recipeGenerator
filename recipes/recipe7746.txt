OVEN-BAKED CHICKEN


1  Tbsp. butter
2/3  c. Bisquick
1 1/2  tsp. paprika
1 1/4  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
3 1/2  lb. cut up chicken

      Heat oven to 425 degrees;. Melt butter in 13 x 9 x 2-inch baking dish. Mix baking mix, paprika, salt and pepper. Coat chicken. Place in dish. (Dish and butter should be hot.) Bake 35 minutes; turn chicken and bake 15 minutes more or until well done.
