MOCHA CAKE


2  c. flour
2  c. sugar
3/4  c. cocoa
2  tsp. baking soda
1  tsp. baking powder
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  c. oil
1  c. milk
1  c. coffee (liquid)
2  eggs

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees;.  Put ingredients in bowl one by one. Beat with electric mixer 2 to 4 minutes, batter will be thin. Grease 9 x 13-inch pan or two round pans with margarine and then dust with flour.  Bake 30 to 35 minutes.  Test for doneness with a toothpick or a cake tester.  Always test cake in the center of the cake.
