ICE CREAM WAFERS


1/2  c. Crisco
1/2  c. sugar
1  beaten egg
3/4  c. all-purpose flour
1/8  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. vanilla

      Mix in order given.  Beat vigorously.  Drop by teaspoons on greased cookie sheet.  Allow room to spread.  Bake at 325 degrees; to 350 degrees; approximately 10 minutes in a moderate oven.  This makes a delicate light cookie with a brown edge.  Serve with ice cream.
