GREEK BEAN SOUP


1 large  pkg. white navy beans (Great Northern), washed
3/4  large pot water
1/2  c. oil
1 small  can tomatoes or 3/4 can whole tomatoes if you want a more tomato taste
3  onions, chopped
1  whole garlic clove
4  stalks celery, chopped (include leaves)
1  lb. chopped carrots
  salt and pepper to taste
1  chopped green pepper
12  Greek olives (optional)

      Boil beans in water for 1/2 hour.  Add oil, tomatoes, onions, garlic, celery, carrots, salt and pepper.  Cook for 4 hours.  Add green pepper after 2 hours of cooking.  Add olives after 3 hours of cooking.  Serve hot.
