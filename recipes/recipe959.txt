SWEET AND SOUR CABBAGE SOUP


2  or 3 soup bones
1  head cabbage, shredded
1 1/2  qt. water
1 (1 lb.)  can tomatoes
2  onions, sliced thin
1  apple, peeled and diced
1/4  c. seedless raisins
1/4  c. lemon juice
1/4  c. brown sugar
1  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper

      Put the soup bones in a heavy saucepan.  Add the shredded cabbage and water.  Add the tomatoes, onions, apple and raisins. Add the lemon juice, brown sugar, salt and pepper.  Stir.  Bring to a boil; reduce heat and simmer for 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.  Makes 6 to 8 servings.
