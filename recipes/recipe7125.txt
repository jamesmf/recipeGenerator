CRESCENT CHEESEBURGER PIE


1  lb ground beef or turkey
1  sloppy joe mix
8  oz crescent rolls
4  oz mozzarella cheese, shredded
4  oz cheddar cheese, shredded

      Heat oven to 350 degrees;.  In large skillet, cook meat over medium-high heat until it is thoroughly cooked, stirring frequently.  Drain.  Make the sloppy joes according to the package directions.  Meanwhile, separate crescent roll dough into 8 triangles. Place triangles in ungreased 9 inch glass pie pan or 8-1/4 inch round glass baking dish; press over bottom and up sides to form the crust.  Sprinkle with the mozzarella cheese.  Top with cheddar cheese.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 25-30 minutes or until crust is deep golden brown. Let stand 10 minutes before serving.
      Cut into 8 wedges.
