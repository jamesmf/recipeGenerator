CATFISH MULLDOWN


2  lb. catfish, deboned
2  lb. potatoes, sliced
1  lb. bacon
2  lb. onions
1 (No. 303)  can tomatoes
  salt and pepper to taste

      Cook bacon until crisp in a large heavy pot. Remove bacon and set aside. Layer remaining ingredients, beginning with potatoes. Cook at low heat for about 2 hours.
