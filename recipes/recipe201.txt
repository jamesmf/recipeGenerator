PASSOVER BUTTER COOKIES


1 1/2  c. sugar
1/2  c. butter
4  eggs
2  c. cake meal
1  c. chopped nuts
  grated orange peel

      Cream sugar and butter.  Add eggs and beat well.  Add cake meal, nuts and orange peel and mix well.  Roll onto aluminum foil and shape into a roll about 1 1/2-inches in diameter.  Refrigerate overnight.
      Slice 1/4-inch thick and bake on ungreased cookie sheet at 350 degrees; for 15 minutes or until lightly browned.
