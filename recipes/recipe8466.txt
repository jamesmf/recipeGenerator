ZUCCHINI AND BEEF ITALIANO


4  c. sliced zucchini
1  lb. ground beef
1  c. chopped onion
1   clove garlic, crushed
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. basil
1/2  tsp. oregano
1/4  tsp. pepper
2  c. cooked rice
1  c. tomato sauce
1  c. cottage cheese
2  eggs, beaten
1  c. grated cheddar cheese

      Cook zucchini in boiling salted water 2 or 3 minutes. Drain well. Cook ground beef, onion, garlic and seasonings in skillet until onions are transparent. Stir in cooked rice and tomato sauce. Blend cottage cheese and eggs. Put half of zucchini slices in buttered, shallow two quart casserole; spoon on ground beef mixture. Spread cottage cheese over beef. Top with remaining zucchini. Sprinkle with cheddar cheese.
       Bake 350 degrees;F. 25-30 minutes (or until hot and bubbly).
