TATER TOT CASSEROLE


2  lb. extra lean ground beef
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  c. grated cheddar cheese
1 2  lb. pkg. frozen tater tots

      Pat ground beef in bottom of greased 13 X 9 X 2 pan.  Spread soup over meat and cover with grated cheese. Top with tater tots. Bake covered for 45 minutes in a 350 degree oven, and uncovered for 15 minutes.  Serves 8 to 10.
