CALICO BEANS


1  lb. ground beef
1/2  green pepper (diced)
1  medium onion (diced)
1  can lima beans
1  can great northern beans
1  can kidney beans
1/4  c. brown sugar
1  Tbsp. mustard
1 small  bottle ketchup

      Brown ground beef in skillet and drain.  Mix all ingredients together and put into baking dish.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes.
