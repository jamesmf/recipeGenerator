SWISS SALAD


1/2  c. mayonnaise or salad dressing
2  Tbsp. light cream
1  tsp. dry mustard
1/2  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper
4  c. bite sized salad greens (your choice)
1  c. shredded Swiss cheese (4 oz.)
1/4  c. sliced pimento-stuffed olives
1/2  c. Bac*Os

      Blend mayonnaise, cream, mustard, salt and pepper. Refrigerate.  Mix salad greens, cheese and olives in a large bowl. Just before serving, toss mixture with chilled dressing and Bac*Os.  Serves 6 to 8.
