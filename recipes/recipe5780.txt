OKRA SALAD


1 (16 oz.)  pkg. frozen, sliced and breaded okra
1  bunch green onions, chopped
2  medium tomatoes, chopped
5  slices bacon, cooked crisp and crumbled
1/4  c. vegetable oil
1/8  c. sugar
1/8  c. vinegar

      Fry okra according to package directions; drain and allow to cool completely. In a large bowl, combine okra with onions, tomatoes and crumbled bacon; mix gently. Add mixture of oil, sugar and vinegar; toss to coat. Chill until ready to serve.
