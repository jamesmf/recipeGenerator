CHEESE BALLS


2  c. (8 oz.) shredded cheddar cheese
1  c. butter
2  c. sifted flour
1/4  tsp. red pepper
1/2  c. chopped pecans

      Combine cheese and butter, mix until well blended.  Add flour, red pepper and pecans and mix well.  roll into 1-inch balls and bake on ungreased cookie sheet in oven of 425 degrees; for 10 to 15 minutes.
