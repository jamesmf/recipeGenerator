STUFFED GREEN PEPPERS


8  large green bell peppers
1  lb. ground beef
1/2  c. onion, chopped
3  medium tomatoes, peeled and chopped
2  c. cooked rice
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. basil, crushed
1/4  c. chopped green pepper
  dash of pepper
3/4  c. soft bread crumbs
1  Tbsp. melted butter or margarine

      Cut top out of the bell peppers and take seeds out; set aside.  Boil for 5 minutes in salty water.  Cook ground beef, onions and pepper until ground beef is brown and onions tender. Add chopped tomatoes; simmer for about 4 minutes or until tomatoes are cooked.  Drain off liquid.  Stir in rice, salt, basil and pepper.  Stuff peppers with the meat mixture.  Place in 13 x 9 x 2-inch pan.  Toss together bread crumbs and melted butter. Sprinkle on top of peppers.  Cook in 350 degrees; oven.  Bake 35 to 40 minutes or until done.
