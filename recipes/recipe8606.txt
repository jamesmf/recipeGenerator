CRANBERRY CHICKEN


1  chicken fryer, cut up or chicken breasts
1  can whole cranberry sauce
1  pkg. dry onion soup mix
1/4  C. French or Western dressing

      Heat together cranberry sauce, onion soup and dressing in a sauce pan to dissolve.  Pour over chicken in baking dish.
      Bake covered for 1 1/2 hours at 350 degrees.
      Uncover and continue to bake until meat comes off bone easily. Make gravy out of the liquid.
       Serve with rice.
