MEAT BALLS AND BAKED BEANS


2/3  c. canned milk
1 1/2  lb. ground beef
1/2  c. cracker meal
1  egg

 
Sauce:
1 1/2  c. catsup
3  Tbsp. brown sugar
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/2  c. chopped onions
1  tsp. garlic salt
1/2  tsp. salt
1  Tbsp. mustard
3  Tbsp. vinegar
3/4  tsp. dry mustard

      Combine all ingredients; mix well. Shape into balls. Bake in 350 degrees; oven until done.
      Make sauce. Simmer 10 minutes. Spoon 4 cups (two 1 pound cans) pork and beans into a 2-quart casserole. Mix some of the sauce into beans. Cover with meat balls and sauce. Bake in oven at 350 degrees; for 20 to 25 minutes.
