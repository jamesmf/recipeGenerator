NEW YEAR'S CRABMEAT CASSEROLE


3  Tbsp. butter
1/4  c. chopped green pepper
2  Tbsp. flour
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  c. milk
  dash of pepper
1 3/4  c. grated Cheddar cheese
1  lb. canned or frozen crabmeat
  buttered bread crumbs
1/4  c. grated Cheddar cheese
  paprika

      Cook green pepper in butter until tender.  Stir in flour. Combine soup, milk and pepper and gradually stir into flour mixture. Cook until smooth and thickened, stirring constantly. Add 1 3/4 cups of cheese and crabmeat and blend.  Pour into casserole. Top with bread crumbs and 1/4 cup Cheddar cheese. Sprinkle with paprika.  Bake in 375 degrees; oven for 30 minutes. Serves 6.
