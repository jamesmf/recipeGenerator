MEAL IN ONE FOR TWO


1/2  lb. lean ground beef
1/2  c. sliced celery
1  tsp. butter or margarine
1  tsp. lemon juice
1  c. cooked rice
1/3  c. mayonnaise
1/3  c. onion, sliced
1/3  c. pepper, sliced
1/2 small  clove garlic, minced
1/2  tsp. salt
  dash of Tabasco sauce
1  medium tomato, cut in wedges
1/2  c. corn chips, crushed

      Saut√© meat, onion, green pepper, celery and garlic in butter until tender-crisp. Add salt, pepper sauce, lemon juice, rice and tomatoes. Heat thoroughly. Stir in mayonnaise. Serve topped with corn chips. Makes 2 servings.
