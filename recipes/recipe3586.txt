MINIATURE PIZZA


1  lb. sharp Cheddar cheese
1  small onion, chopped
1  small bell pepper, chopped
8  strips bacon, fried and crumbled

      Mix all ingredients together with a small amount of mayonnaise to make a spread. Spread on party rye or pumpernickel. Cook in 300 degrees; oven for approximately 10 minutes or until cheese melts. Serve warm.
