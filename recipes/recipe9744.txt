CHEESY TURKEY AND SPAGHETTI


1  lb. ground turkey
1  Tbsp. vegetable oil
1 (16 oz.)  can Veg-All vegetables (undrained)
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  c. milk
12  oz. spaghetti or linguine, cooked
1  c. sliced mushrooms
3/4  c. Parmesan cheese

      In a large skillet, cook turkey in oil until no longer pink. Drain Veg-All, reserving liquid. Combine liquid with soups and milk. Add soup mixture to spaghetti and mix with turkey, vegetables, mushrooms and 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese. Spoon into 2-quart casserole. Sprinkle with remaining 1/4 cup cheese. Bake at 400 degrees; for 30 minutes.
