PEPPER STEAK


1  lb. beef chuck steak, cut in thin strips
1/4  c. oil
1  c. bell pepper, chopped
1  clove minced garlic
1  Tbsp. soy sauce
1  tsp. salt
1/4  c. water
1  c. onion, chopped
1/2  c. celery, chopped
1  Tbsp. cornstarch
1  c. water
2  tomatoes, chopped

      Brown beef in hot oil. Add garlic; simmer. Add soy sauce, salt, and water; cook 45 minutes. Add vegetables. Cook for 10 minutes. Blend 1 c. water and cornstarch; add and stir. Add tomatoes; cook 5 minutes. Serve over rice. 4 servings.
