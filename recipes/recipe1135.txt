CRUSTY MEXICAN BEAN BAKE

Crust:
1/2  c. all-purpose flour
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. baking powder
2  Tbsp. shortening
1/2  c. sour cream or yogurt (add 2 Tbsp. flour if using yogurt)
1  egg, beaten

 
Filling:
3/4  lb. ground beef
1/2  c. chopped onion
1  tsp. salt
2  tsp. chili powder
3/4  c. (6 oz.) tomato paste
1/2  tsp. Tabasco sauce
2  c. undrained, cooked kidney beans

      Stir together crust ingredients (may be slightly lumpy). Spread thinly with back of spoon on bottom and sides of shallow greased 2-quart baking pan.  Set aside.  Brown beef and onion in skillet; drain fat.  Add remaining filling ingredients.  Place in crust.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes.  Remove from oven.  Sprinkle with 1/2 cup grated cheese, 1/2 cup shredded lettuce and 1 cup chopped tomatoes.  These topping ingredients may be served as accompaniments.  Yield:  10 servings.  Crust can be made ahead and stored in refrigerator.
      Diabetic Exchanges:  1 serving equals 1 1/2 protein, 1 bread, 1 vegetable, 1 fat.  Also only 263 calories!
