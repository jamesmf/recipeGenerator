MEAT LOAF


1 1/2  lb. ground beef
2/3  c. bread crumbs
1  c. milk
2  eggs
1/4  c. chopped onion
  salt and pepper

 
Topping:
1/4  c. catsup
1  tsp. dried mustard
3  Tbsp. brown sugar
1/4  tsp. nutmeg

      (I double topping quantities.)
      Mix all meat loaf ingredients together (mix well).  Form loaf and put into pan.  Mix ingredients for sauce.  Spread over meat loaf.  Bake at 375 degrees; for 1 hour or until done.
