ORANGE CREAM SALAD


8  oz. pkg. cream cheese
1  c. undiluted evaporated milk
1 (3 oz.)  pkg. orange Jell-O
2  Tbsp. orange juice
1/4  c. sugar
1  c. hot water
2  tsp. grated orange rind

      Beat cream cheese with sugar until light and smooth. Gradually blend in milk. Dissolve jello in hot water. Cool slightly. Gradually add milk mixture. Stir in orange rind and juice. Pour into mold. Chill until firm.
