ITALIAN BEEF


3  lb. chuck roast
1  Tbsp. garlic salt
1  Tbsp. onion salt
1  Tbsp. basil leaves, chopped
1  Tbsp. oregano
1  Tbsp. black pepper
1  jar pepperoncini peppers (and juice)
1/4  c. soy sauce
  Mozzarella cheese
  hoagie buns

      Place meat, seasonings, peppers and juice in crock-pot. Cover with water.  Cook for at least 4 hours before you shred meat into small pieces.  Cook for 2 more hours, until tender.  Serve shredded meat on buns with cheese on top.
