BREAKFAST CASSEROLE


6  eggs
2  c. milk
1  c. Cheddar cheese
1  lb. Bob Evans sausage, crumbled
1  tsp. dried mustard
6  slices bread, diced
1  tsp. salt

      Mix together eggs and milk.  Add cheese, sausage, mustard, bread and salt.  Put in casserole dish.  Refrigerate overnight. Bake the next day at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes.
