MARZETTI


1  lb. ground beef
1 small  pkg. egg noodles
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  can tomato soup
1  pkg. shredded Cheddar cheese

      Brown hamburger meat. Boil noodles. Mix meat, noodles, cream of mushroom soup and tomato soup in casserole dish. Sprinkle Cheddar cheese on top. Bake at 350 degrees; until cheese has melted.
