APRICOT JELLO SALAD


1 large or 2 small  pkg. apricot jello
2/3  c. sugar
2  c. water
8  oz. cream cheese
1  large apricot baby food
1 large  can crushed pineapple, drained
1  large Cool Whip

      Bring the first 3 ingredients to a boil and cool before adding the cream cheese, baby food and pineapple. Add the two mixtures well, then add the Cool Whip. Chill.
