CUCUMBER SALAD


4  cukes, sliced
1  Tbsp. salt
1/2  c. chopped green peppers
1/2  c. chopped onions (optional)
1/2  tsp. celery seed
1/2  tsp. mustard seed
1/2  c. vinegar
1/2  c. sugar

      Let cukes and salt stand two hours.  Rinse and squeeze. Combine all the ingredients.
