PIZZA MUFFINS


  sourdough muffins
1  can tomato paste
1  Tbsp. sugar
  pinch of oregano
1  pkg. shredded Mozzarella cheese
  green olives, sliced
1  pkg. pepperoni

      Cut muffins in half. Mix tomato paste, sugar and oregano; spread onto muffins. Place pepperoni, olives and cheese on muffins and bake at 375 degrees; until golden brown.
      Can add or take away any toppings as desired.
