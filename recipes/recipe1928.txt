SPAGHETTI AJO E OJO


1/2  c. olive oil
2  tsp. chopped garlic (very fine)
1  tsp. salt
1  lb. thin spaghetti
2  Tbsp. chopped parsley
  pepper

      Both the spaghetti and the sauce can be made at the same time. Make the spaghetti according to the package directions; cook al dente.  In a small saucepan, put in the olive oil, garlic and salt over low heat; stir often.  When the garlic is a golden brown, the sauce is ready.  Pour over spaghetti and mix thoroughly.  Pepper and parsley the top and serve.  Serves 4.
