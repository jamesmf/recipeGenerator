SCALLOPED CHICKEN


1  chicken, cooked and cut up
1  qt. broth, thickened with flour
1 1/2  qt. bread, cut in small pieces
1/2  c. melted oleo
3/4  tsp. salt
2  tsp. chopped onion

      Mix bread, oleo, salt and onion. Add chicken. Put in 9 x 13-inch greased pan. Pour gravy over it. Bake for 35 minutes at 325 degrees;.
