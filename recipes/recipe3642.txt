NUTS AND BOLTS


3  sticks margarine
2  tsp. celery salt
2  tsp. onion salt
2  tsp. garlic salt
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  lb. peanuts and pecans, mixed
1/4 large  box Cheerios
3/4  box Wheat Chex
1/2  box Rice Chex
10  oz. bag pretzels

      Melt margarine and seasonings.  Mix together with nuts, cereal, etc. in a large roasting pan.  Bake at 250 degrees; for 2 1/2 to 3 hours, stirring about every 20 minutes.  Let cool on paper towels before storing.
