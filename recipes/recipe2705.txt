VELIKONOCNI SEKANA
(Easter Loaf)  

2  c. cooked, cubed veal
1 1/2  c. cooked, cubed ham
10  eggs, well beaten
1  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
1  Tbsp. chopped chives or onion
1  clove garlic, chopped

      Combine all the ingredients and stir thoroughly.  Pour into a greased 8 x 8-inch pan.  Bake for 45 minutes to 1 hour at 300 degrees;. Cut into squares.  Serve warm with celery sticks or cold with mustard.
