SAUSAGE BREAKFAST LOAF


1  lb. smoked sausage
1 1/2  c. (6 oz.) shredded Mozzarella cheese
2  eggs (1 scrambled and 1 used to seal the crust)
1 1/2  tsp. minced, fresh parsley
1/2  tsp. onion salt
1/2  tsp. garlic salt
1  (10 oz.) tube refrigerated pizza dough

      In a large skillet, saut the sausage; cool. In a bowl, combine the sausage, cheese, 1 egg that has been scrambled, parsley, onion salt and garlic salt; mix well.
       Unroll pizza dough. Roll into a 12 x 8-inch rectangle. Spread sausage mixture down the center of dough. Bring dough over filling. Pinch seams to seal. Place seam side down on a greased baking sheet. Tuck ends under. Beat remaining egg; brush over top. Bake at 350 degrees; for 25 to 30 minutes or until golden brown. Let stand for 5 minutes before serving. Yields 6 servings.
