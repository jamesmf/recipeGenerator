TOMATO SOUP SALAD


1  c. tomato soup (undiluted)
1  c. mayonnaise
1  c. celery, diced
1/4 to 1/2  c. onion, chopped
1 (3 oz.)  pkg. lemon jello
1  c. cottage cheese
1  c. bell pepper, chopped

      Heat undiluted soup until hot, but not boiling. Stir in lemon jello until dissolved. Add remaining ingredients and mix well. Pour into mold and refrigerate.
