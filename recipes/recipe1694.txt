OKSHEN KUGEL


8  oz. broad noodles
1  c. pot cheese
1/2  c. raisins
1  egg, slightly beaten
1/2  tsp. salt
1  tsp. cinnamon
2  tsp. sugar
3/4  c. sour cream
1/4  c. margarine or butter, melted

      Cook noodles as directed on the package; drain well.  Stir in remaining ingredients and half of the melted margarine.  Place in a greased casserole and pour over the remaining melted margarine. Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees; (180 degrees;C) for 1 hour.
