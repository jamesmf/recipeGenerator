STUFFED PEPPERS


4  green peppers
1  lb. ground beef
2  c. cooked rice
2 (8 oz.)  cans tomato sauce
1/4  c. onions, chopped
1  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper

      Cut peppers in half lengthwise. Remove seeds and wash. Combine beef, cooked rice, onions, salt, pepper and 1/2 can tomato sauce. Pile mixture into peppers in large baking dish. Pour sauce over each pepper. Cover tightly and bake at 350 degrees; for 1 1/4 hours, basting occasionally.
