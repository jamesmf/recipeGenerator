HAMBURGER TATER TOTS CASSEROLE


1 1/2 to 2  lb. hamburger
2  c. cream of mushroom, cream of chicken soup or 1 c. soup and 1 c. milk
1 bag mixed vegetables (frozen) or 2  cans your favorite vegetables
  pinch of celery salt
  pinch of salt and pepper
  garlic (if desired)
2  boxes or 1 large bag Tater Tots

      Brown hamburger and drain, then season. Lay in the bottom of an 8 1/2 x 11-inch dish. Next, layer vegetables or you can mix with hamburger. Pour 1/2 of soup in bottom if you mix vegetables and hamburger; mix soup also. Layer your Tater Tots on top of mixture and pop in the oven for 30 to 40 minutes at 350 degrees;.
       Can be put into 2 casserole dishes and freeze half of it.
