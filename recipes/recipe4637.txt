EASY CHILI


1  lb ground meat
1/2  c green pepper, chopped
1/2  c onion, chopped
1/2  c celery, chopped
1  can crushed tomatoes
1  can tomato soup
  chili powder to taste
  salt and pepper
1  can kidney beans (optional)

      Brown ground meat and drain. Add onion, green pepper, and celery. Heat thoroughly. Add tomatoes, soup, spices and beans. Cook on medium heat for 1 hour.
