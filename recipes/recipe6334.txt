ZUCCHINI ORANGE SALAD


2  medium zucchini, thinly sliced
1  medium onion, thinly sliced
1  cup chopped celery
1  can green beans, drained
1  can wax beans, drained
1  can mandarin oranges, drained
1  1/2 cups sugar
1  cup vinegar
1  Tbsp. water
1  tsp. salt

      In a large bowl, toss zucchini, onion and celery.  Cover with boiling water; let stand 1 hour.  Drain.  Add beans and oranges. Combine remaining ingredients in a saucepan.  Bring to a boil; boil for 1 minute.  Pour over salad; cover and refrigerate 24 hours before serving.
