UGLY DIP


2 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
1  lb. sausage (hot/mild)
16  oz. jar salsa

	Fry sausage, then drain well.  Put both packages of cream cheese and 1 jar of salsa in microwave for about 7 to 10 minutes or until cream cheese begins to melt.	Mix sausage with cream cheese mixture.  Can be served warm or cold with salsa, chips or toast.
