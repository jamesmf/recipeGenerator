BEEF-CHICKEN MAGNIFICENT


4  oz. dried beef, chopped
8  halved chicken breasts
8  slices bacon
3  oz. cream cheese
1  can Cream of Mushroom soup
1 1/4  c. water

      Spread beef in greased baking dish.  Wrap each chicken breast in slice of bacon.  Place on top of beef.  Combine cheese, soup, and water; pour over chicken.  Cover & bake 1 1/2 hours at 325 degrees;. Uncover and bake 1 hour.
