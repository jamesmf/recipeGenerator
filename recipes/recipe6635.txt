SUMMER SHEPHERD'S PIE


1  lb. hamburger, browned
1  small onion, minced or diced
1  can vegetable soup
1-1/4  tsp. salt
  Prepared instant mashed potatoes

      Mix above ingredients (except potatoes) and pour into an ungreased 1-1/2 quart casserole.  Top with prepared instant mashed potatoes.  Bake at 450 degrees; until potatoes are brown and pie is bubbly.  This can also be put in the microwave for 8 minutes until pie is bubbly.
