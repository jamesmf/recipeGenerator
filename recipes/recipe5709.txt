MACARONI AND LENTIL SOUP
(Makrooni Rahwa)  

1/2  c. lentils
1 1/2  c. elbow macaroni
2  qts. water
1/4  c. oil
1  garlic clove, minced
4  tsp black pepper
1  tsp. salt
1/2  med. onion, chopped fine
1/2  c. tomato paste
2  c. water

      Wash lentils.  Cook Cover lentils with water and bring to a boil.  Then cook for 15 mins.  Saut√© onion and garlic in oil until browned.  Add tomato paste, water and seasoning.  Blend well and bring to a boil.  When lentils are soft, add tomato sauce mixture and macaroni.  Bring to a boil.
