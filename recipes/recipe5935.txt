CRANBERRY CONGEALED SALAD


2 small  pkg. strawberry jello
1 1/2  c. cranberries
1  orange
2  apples
2  c. water

      Add sugar and water.  Heat and add jello.  Grind cranberries, orange and apples.  After jello is partly congealed, add ground fruit and nuts.  Don't peel fruit but take seeds out.  Use 8-inch square pan for salad.
