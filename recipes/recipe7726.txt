CHICKEN POT PIE


1 (10 oz.)  can cream of chicken soup
1 (9 oz.)  pkg. frozen mixed vegetables, thawed
1  c. cubed cooked chicken
1/2  c. milk
1  egg
1  c. Bisquick baking mix

      Preheat oven to 400 degrees;. In 9-inch pie plate, mix soup, vegetables and chicken. Mix milk, egg and baking mix. Pour over chicken mixture. Bake 30 minutes or until golden. Serves 4.
