SOUTHWEST SALAD


1 (15 oz.)  can black beans, rinsed and drained
1 1/2  c. frozen corn, thawed
1 1/2  c. chopped plum tomatoes
3/4  c. thinly sliced green onions
1/3  c. minced fresh cilantro
1/2  c. oil
1/2  c. lemon juice
2  tsp. salt

      Toss together first 5 ingredients in a bowl. Whisk together oil, lemon juice and salt; pour over black bean mixture, stirring gently to coat. Cover and chill at least 8 hours. Serve at room temperature or chilled.
