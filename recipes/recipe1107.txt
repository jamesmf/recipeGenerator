OVEN MEATBALLS


1/2  c. skim milk
1/4  c. chopped onion
2  eggs
1  tsp. salt
3  slices bread, crumbled
1 1/2  lb. ground beef

      Combine milk, onion, eggs and salt.  Add bread crumbs and let stand for 5 minutes.  Add meat and mix thoroughly.  Shape into 4 dozen small balls.  Bake at 375 degrees; for 25 minutes.
      Makes 6 meatballs per serving; 1/2 fat exchange, 1/2 bread exchange, 2 meat exchanges and 222 calories.
