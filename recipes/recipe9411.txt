BBQ CUPS


1  lb. lean ground beef
1/2  c. barbecue sauce
1  can refrigerator biscuits
3/4  c. grated cheddar cheese

      Brown meat; drain.  Add barbecue sauce and set aside.  Place biscuits in ungreased muffin cups. pressing dough up sides to edge of cup.  Spoon meat mixture into cups.  Sprinkle with cheese. Bake at 400 degrees for 12 minutes.  Serves 4 to 5.
