CHICKEN PARMESAN


1 1/2  sticks oleo
2  c. fine dry bread crumbs
3/4  c. parmesan cheese
  Parsley flakes
  Garlic salt
  Salt and pepper to taste
8  chicken breasts (boneless, skinless)

      Melt oleo and dip chicken pieces in melted oleo.  Roll in breaded crumb mixture.  Place in a 9 x 13-inch pan, lined with aluminum foil.  Bake at 350 degrees; for one hour, covered with foil. Remove foil and bake 15 minutes more.
