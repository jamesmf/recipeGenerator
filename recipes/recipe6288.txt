A DIETER'S DREAM SHRIMP SALAD


6  oz. shrimp, cooked
16  oz. green beans
1  garlic clove
1  Tbsp. salad oil
1/4  tsp. mustard powder
1/2  tsp. sugar
1/4  c. vinegar
2  chicken bouillon cubes
2/3  c. rice, cooked
1  c. celery, sliced
1/2  c. onions (sweet), sliced
2  c. lettuce, shredded
  soy sauce (optional)

      Rinse shrimp and cook.  Chill can of green beans.  Drain and save liquid.  Slice thinly the garlic clove and  crush in a large bowl.  Add oil, mustard, sugar, vinegar, shrimp and beans. Refrigerate.
      Heat bean liquid. Add bouillon cubes and stir until dissolved. Add enough water to make 2 cups. Add rice.  Bring to a boil and cook rapidly, uncovered, about 8 minutes.  Reduce heat to lowest point, cover and steam for 12 to 14 minutes.  Spread rice in a shallow pan to cool. Refrigerate for 10 minutes or until needed at serving time.  Add rice to shrimp and bean mixture.  Toss together with celery, onion and lettuce.  Serve with soy sauce.  Servings: 4.
