CHICKEN CASSEROLE


1  chicken (3 1/2 to 4 lb.)
1  can mushroom soup
2  beaten eggs
2  c. chicken broth
4  c. crushed Ritz crackers
1  medium diced onion
1 1/2  c. cubed cheese
1 small  jar pimento
1/2  can ripe olives
  slivered almonds (if desired)

      Cook chicken until tender; cube cooked chicken.  (Don't use the skin.) Add rest of ingredients to chicken; mix well.  Put in flat pan and bake at 350 degrees; for 40 minutes.  Cut into 4-inch squares.
      Will freeze well until baked.  Serves 10 to 12.
