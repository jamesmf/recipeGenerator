BEEF ROAST


1  pkg. Lipton dry onion soup
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  can cream of chicken soup
  potatoes
  carrots
1  (3 to 3 1/2 lb.) roast

      Butter bottom and 1/2 way up sides of pan.  Sprinkle onion soup in bottom of pan.  Lay roast on top of soup.  Put potatoes and carrots around roast.  Mix soups and 1 1/2 to 2 soup cans of water.  Pour on top of roast.  Cover and cook at 350 degrees; for 1 1/2 to 2 hours.
