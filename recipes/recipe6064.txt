JELLO FRUIT SALAD


2 small  boxes lemon jello
2  c. boiling water
3  c. small marshmallows
32  oz. can fruit cocktail, drained
1/2  pt whipped cream
1/2  c. mayonnaise
1/2  c. chopped pecans

      Mix jello with boiling water and marshmallows.  Let set until it starts to gel; then mix in other ingredients.  Chill.
