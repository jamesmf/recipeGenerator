ALMOND BARK CANDY


1  lb. white almond bark
1/2  c. chunky peanut butter
1  c. dry roasted peanuts
1  c. miniature marshmallows
1 1/2  c. Rice Krispies

      Melt almond bark in 200 degrees; oven.  Stir in peanut butter; add remaining ingredients.  Drop by teaspoons on waxed paper.  Cool until hardened.
