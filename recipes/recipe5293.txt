WHITE SAUCE


1  c. milk
1  (1/4 inch) onion slice
1  fresh sprig of parsley
2  Tbsp. butter
2  Tbsp. all-purpose flour
2  Tbsp. whipping cream
1/8  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper
  dash of nutmeg

	Bring first 3 ingredients to a boil.  Remove from heat.  Pour milk mixture through a wire-mesh strainer into a small bowl, reserving the hot milk.  Melt butter in large skillet over low heat.  Add flour gradually. Cook, whisking constantly, 1 minute. Gradually whisk in hot milk and cook over medium heat until thick and bubbly.  Whisk in whipping cream and remaining ingredients. Use immediately.
