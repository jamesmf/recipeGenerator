GREEN CHILI SQUARES


1/2  lb. Mozzarella cheese, shredded
1/2  lb. Cheddar cheese, shredded
8  eggs, beaten
2 (4 oz.)  cans chopped green chilies

      Grease 7 1/2 x 11 dish. Mix all ingredients together and pour into dish. Bake, covered, at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes, then lower heat to 250 degrees; for 30 minutes longer. Cut into squares and serve warm. Yield: 35 squares.
