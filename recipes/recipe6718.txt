BROCCOLI AND CHICKEN CASSEROLE


3 (10 oz.)  pkg. frozen chopped broccoli, thawed
2  cans cream of chicken soup
2  tsp. lemon juice
1  c. mayonnaise
6  chicken breasts, cooked and diced
1/2  c. shredded Cheddar cheese
1  c. crushed Ritz cracker crumbs
2  Tbsp. melted margarine

      Combine broccoli, soup, lemon juice, mayonnaise and diced chicken.  Pour into 9 x 13 casserole.  Sprinkle with cheese.  Top with cracker crumbs.  Drizzle melted margarine over crumbs.  Bake at 350 degrees; for approximately 45 minutes, until bubbly.
