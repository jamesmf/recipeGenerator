HOT DOG CHILI
(For a Group)  

2  lb. hamburger
2  onions, chopped
1 small  bottle tomato catsup
2  cans tomato soup
  water
  worcestershire sauce, to taste
3  tsp. chili powder
  salt and pepper, to taste

      Brown meat with onion; drain off grease.  Mix in catsup, soup, worcestershire sauce, chili powder, salt and pepper.  Add enough water to make a good consistency.  Cook slowly until done, stirring often.
      Note: A crock pot can be used for the cooking time, if desired.
