CONGEALED SALAD


1 small  box lime jello
1  c. water (boiling)
1 (8 oz.)  can pineapple tidbits or crushed
2  c. cottage cheese
1/2  c. salad dressing
1  c. juice from drained pineapple and/or water

      Drain pineapple (save juice to use later with water).  Heat water to boiling; add jello and when dissolved, add 1 cup juice and/or water.  Let set to a jelly like consistency.  Add cottage cheese, salad dressing and pineapple.  Stir well.  Place in mold and set in refrigerator until ready to eat.
