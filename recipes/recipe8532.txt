SAUERKRAUT


1  1/2 lbs. bulk sausage
1 large  can sauerkraut
1 1/2  c. celery, cut in small pieces
1  1/2 cups onion, cut in small pieces
  add water as needed

      Boil together until well done. Next; add one stick of margarine and 1 bag of croutons. Add salt, pepper and poultry seasoning to taste. You need enough croutons to make it hold together. Roll into balls and bake until done.
       These are excellent appetizers.
