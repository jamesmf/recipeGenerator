CHILI


3  lb. ground beef or chuck
1  large onion, chopped
2  chili sticks
1  can chopped tomatoes
4  c. tomato juice
  salt and pepper to taste
  chili powder to taste
  hot peppers to taste
  water
2  cans chili beans

      Brown beef and chopped onion.  Drain.  Add chili sticks, chili beans, tomatoes, tomato juice, salt, pepper, chili powder and hot peppers.  Stir until chili sticks are smooth with other ingredients.
