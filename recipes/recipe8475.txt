BASIC MEAT BALLS


1  lb. ground beef
1/2  c. fresh bread crumbs
1  egg, beaten
2  tbsp. onion, finely chopped
1/2  tsp. salt

      Place ground beef, bread crumbs, egg, onion and salt in bowl. Add specified ingredients to prepare either Italian, Oriental or Scandinavian meatballs. follow remaining procedures as directed in desired recipe.
