TERIYAKI CHICKEN


1/4  c. oil
1/4  c. soy sauce
2  Tbsp. catsup
1  Tbsp. vinegar
1/4  tsp. pepper
2  cloves garlic, crushed
1 small  can sliced pineapple
4  chicken breasts

      Combine all ingredients except pineapple slices and chicken in large frying pan. Add chicken, laying pineapple slices on top. Pour juice from pineapple into sauce and cover. Cook over low heat until thoroughly done.
