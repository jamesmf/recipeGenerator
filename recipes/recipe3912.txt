CHEESE RING


1  lb. sharp Cheddar cheese, grated
2  c. chopped pecans
3/4  c. mayonnaise
1  medium onion, grated
1  clove garlic, pressed
1/2  tsp. Tabasco
1  c. strawberry preserves

      Combine all ingredients, except preserves. Mix well. Chill. Mold into ring. Serve with strawberry preserves in center of ring and crackers around ring.
