MACARONI AND CHEESE


1 small  pkg. elbow Macaroni
1  lb. Velveeta cheese
1  stick Margarine
1 1/2  c. Milk
  Salt and Pepper to taste

      Boil macaroni in 2 qt. saucepan.  Drain when tender.  Cook cubed velveeta cheese, margarine and milk over low heat until melted and sauce is smooth.  Add macaroni, salt and pepper. Cook for 5 minutes on low heat.
