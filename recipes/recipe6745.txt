INSTANT HOT BROWNS


  mashed potatoes
  beefy mushroom soup (Campbell's)
  sliced bread

      Prepare mashed potatoes according to package directions. Open soup and heat.  Do not add water!  Arrange sliced bread on serving plates.  Spoon potatoes onto bread.  Ladle soup over all. Takes 10 minutes.  You will need 1 can soup, 6 slices bread and 2 cups of potatoes for 3 servings.
