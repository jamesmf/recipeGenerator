HOT CHEESEBURGER CHIP DIP


1  lb. hamburger
1  medium yellow onion, chopped
1  can sliced black olives
1 (6 oz.)  jar jalapeno peppers
  melted Velveeta cheese

      Fry hamburger and drain. Add chopped onion. Add sliced black olives and jalapeno peppers. Add melted Velveeta cheese to cover and mix. Serve warm with chips.
