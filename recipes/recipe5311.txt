CHILE CHILI


1 1/2  lb. lean ground beef
1  can Ro-Tel (hot) tomatoes
1 (4 oz.)  can whole chiles
2  cans chili beans (spicy)
2  cans kidney beans
1 or 2  pkg. chili seasoning (hot)
1  medium bell pepper
1  medium onion
1  can tomato soup
1 (6 oz.)  can tomato paste
1 (6 oz.)  can water

      Brown ground beef, pepper and onion together in a large pot. Add all other ingredients. Simmer for approximately 1 hour.
