RAISIN BREAD


1/2  c. quick oatmeal
1  tsp. baking powder
2  c. biscuit mix
1/4  tsp. salt
1/2  c. raisins
1  egg
1 1/4  c. skim milk
  sweetener equal to 1 1/4 c. sugar

      Combine dry ingredients, then mix rest of ingredients and add to dry ingredients.  Blend well.  Pour into 1-quart round casserole, sprayed with a pan coating.  Bake about 1 hour at 350 degrees;. Cool 10 minutes before turning out of pan.
