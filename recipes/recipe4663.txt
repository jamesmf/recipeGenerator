GREAT VEGETABLE SOUP


3  Tbsp Butter
1  quart Chicken Broth
1/4  head Broccoli (florets)
1/4  head Cauliflower
1/2  bunch scallions (sliced)
4  carrots (sliced)
3  to 4 turnips (chopped)
1/2  cup Savoy Cabbage (sliced)
1/2  cup Fresh Parsley (chopped)
