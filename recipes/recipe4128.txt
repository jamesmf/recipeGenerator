SPICED PECANS


1/2  tsp. ground ginger
1/2  tsp. ground cinnamon
1  tsp. ground cumin
1  tsp. chili powder
1  tsp. curry powder
1  tsp. garlic salt
1/2  tsp. cayenne pepper
4  Tbsp. olive oil
4  c. pecan halves

      Mix the herbs and spices together in a bowl.  Set aside.  Heat oil in nonstick skillet over low heat.  Add spice mixture.  Stir well.  Simmer 4 to 5 minutes to mellow the flavors. Put nuts in bowl and pour the spice mixture over them.  Toss well.  Spread nuts in single layer on baking sheet.  Bake at 325 degrees; for 12 to 15 minutes.  Stir with wooden spoon once or twice.
