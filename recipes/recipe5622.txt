TACO SALAD


1  lb. hamburger
1/2  pkg. taco seasoning
1  sm. head lettuce
1  sm. green pepper, cut into pieces
1  lrg. tomato, cut into pieces
1/2  C. black olives, drained
  shredded cheddar cheese, to taste
1/2  C. French, Western or Catalina dressing
  taco flavored chips, crushed

      Brown hamburger and taco seasoning, prepare according to package directions, using only 1/2 amount of water called for.
      Cool and mix with:  lettuce cut in bite sized pieces, green pepper, tomato, black olives.  Toss just prior to eating with shredded cheddar cheese, taco flavored chips and salad dressing.
