PEAR RELISH


2  qt. raw pears
4  large bell peppers
3  lb. onions
6  large dill pickles
2  Tbsp. salt
3/4  qt. vinegar
1  Tbsp. turmeric
2  Tbsp. red pepper
2  c. sugar
1  Tbsp. celery seed
5  Tbsp. flour
3  Tbsp. dry mustard

      Coarsely grind pears and mix with salt, sugar, and celery seed in a pot. Make paste of remaining dry ingredients and 1 c. vinegar in another pot and cook a few minutes. Add rest of vinegar and ground ingredients. Cook 15 minutes. Seal in pint jars while hot. Makes 10 pt.
