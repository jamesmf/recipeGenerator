EASY CHICKEN AND RICE SLOW COOKER


2  c. fresh mushrooms, sliced
1  c. celery, sliced
1/2  c. onion, chopped
1 1/2  tsp. dried dill weed
1/4  tsp. black pepper
2  pounds chicken thighs skinned and fat removed
1  can condensed cream of mushroom soup or cream of chicken
3/4  c. chicken broth
1 1/2  c. instant rice

	In a slow cooker combine mushrooms, celery, onion dill weed and pepper.  Top with chicken.  Pour soup and broth over chicken. Cover, cook on High 2 1/2 to 3 hours or Low 5 to 6 hours.  If using low turn slow cooker to high heat setting.  Stir uncooked rice into mushroom mixture.  Cover and cook for 10 minutes more.
