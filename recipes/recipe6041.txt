MACARONI SALAD


7  oz. macaroni
1  c. cooked peas
1/2  c. chopped celery
1/4  c. sweet pickle relish
3/4  c. mayonnaise
1  Tbsp. chopped green pepper
1  Tbsp. chopped onion
1  Tbsp. chopped pimento
1  tsp. salt

      Cook macaroni according to package directions; drain.   Mix macaroni with all the remaining ingredients.  Chill 2 hours before serving.  Makes 5 cups.
