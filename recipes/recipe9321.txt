PORK TENDERLOIN IN SOUR CREAM


2  pork tenderloins
  salt (to taste)
  pepper (to taste)
1  lemon, thinly sliced
3  Tbsp. butter
  flour (for dusting)
4  Tbsp. sour cream
2  tsp. capers

      Rub meat well with salt and place in a 9x13-inch baking dish. Add pepper and cover meat with lemon slices.
      Roast in 400 degrees; oven for 30 minutes, basting frequently with butter and pan drippings.
      Dust with flour.  Add sour cream mixed with capers and continue roasting approximately 45 to 60 minutes, basting frequently until thoroughly brown and tender.  Care should be taken to not overcook or meat will be dry.
      Makes 4 to 6 servings.
