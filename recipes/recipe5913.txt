SPAGHETTI SALAD


16  oz. box spaghetti
1  cucumber, chopped
1  green pepper, chopped
1  ripe tomato, chopped
1  small red onion, chopped
  small bottle Italian dressing
1  jar Salad Supreme dressing (this is in the spice section and is a dry mix)
  sesame seeds
  poppy seeds
  hot sauce to taste

      Cook spaghetti. Drain. Let cool. Then add vegetables and the Salad Supreme dressing, seeds and hot sauce. Pour the Italian dressing in last. Add at intervals until you get the moistness you want. Chill.
