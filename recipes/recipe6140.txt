CHILI


2  lb. lean ground beef
1/2  tsp. chili powder
2  tsp. salt
1  tsp. sugar
1  large onion, chopped
1/2  green pepper, chopped
2 8  oz. cans tomato sauce
1  can Rotel tomatoes
1  c. water
1  clove garlic, minced
1  tsp. oregano
1  tsp. cumin
2  cans kidney beans, drained
2  Tbsp. cooking oil
2  Tbsp. flour

      Brown beef, add other ingredients, except for beans, oil and flour.  Cover and simmer for 1 hour.  In small bowl, mix oil and flour until smooth.  Add beans.  Add this to kettle.  Cover and simmer, stirring occasionally for about 30 minutes.
