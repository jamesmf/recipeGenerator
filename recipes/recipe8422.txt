OVEN FRIED CATFISH


  vegetable cooking spray
1/4  c. cornmeal
1  tsp. thyme
1  tsp. basil
1/2  tsp. garlic powder
1/2  tsp. lemon pepper
2  tsp. Old Bay
4  catfish fillets (4 oz. each)
  paprika

      Spray baking sheet with spray.  Mix cornmeal and all seasonings, except paprika, on plate.  Coat fillets with cornmeal mixture and place on baking sheet.  Sprinkle with paprika.  Spray fillets with cooking spray.  Bake at 400 degrees; for 20 minutes.  Reduce heat to 350 degrees; and cook for 5 more minutes or until it flakes easily.
