PINEAPPLE-CHEESE SALAD


1 (20 oz.)  can chunk pineapple
1  c. Velveeta, cubed

 
Dressing:
1/3  c. sugar
2 rounded  Tbsp. cornstarch
2  Tbsp. margarine
1  egg, beaten
1  Tbsp. vinegar

      Drain pineapple and reserve syrup.
       Melt margarine; add cornstarch and sugar, then add egg and pineapple juice. Cook in double boiler. When cool, add 1 tablespoon vinegar. Pour over pineapple and cheese. Let set overnight.
