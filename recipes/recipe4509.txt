STRAWBERRY SPINACH SALAD


9  c. torn fresh spinach
1  pt. fresh strawberries, halved
1/2  c. sliced almonds, toasted

 
Dressing:
1/4  c. vegetable oil
2  Tbsp. sugar
2  Tbsp. cider vinegar
1  Tbsp. chopped onion
1  tsp. poppy seeds
1  tsp. sesame seeds
1/4  tsp. paprika
1/8  tsp. Worcestershire sauce

      In large bowl, combine the spinach, strawberries and almonds. Place dressing ingredients in a blender; cover and process until combined. Pour over salad and toss to coat. Serve immediately. Yields 6 to 8 servings.
      To sugar coat almonds, place in frypan and use 3 tablespoons of sugar per 1/2 cup almonds. Stir continuously until sugar melts and almonds are golden brown. Place on wax paper to cool.
