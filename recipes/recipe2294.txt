BARBECUED RIBS


4  lb. ribs
  salt and pepper
2  onions, diced
3  Tbsp. brown sugar
4  tsp. barbecue sauce
3/4  c. water
3/4  c. ketchup
2  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
2  Tbsp. vinegar
3  drops Tabasco sauce

      Boil ribs until tender.  Remove from water.  Place in dish or cooking bag.  Mix remaining ingredients and pour over ribs.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour.
