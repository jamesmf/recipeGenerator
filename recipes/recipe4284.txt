BLACK BEAN AND COUSCOUS SALAD


1  can black beans
1/3  c. olive oil
1 1/2  oz. sherry wine vinegar
1/2  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. fresh cracked pepper
1  glove garlic, chopped
1  tsp. cumin

      Drain beans and rinse. Mix above ingredients in a small bowl. Add black beans and put aside.
Step 2 Ingredients:
3  Tbsp. sherry wine vinegar
1/4  c. olive oil
1  glove garlic, chopped
1/2  oregano
  juice from 1 lemon
1/2  Tbsp. cumin
1/2  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper
2  Tbsp. olive oil
1  box instant couscous

      In a large bowl, whisk sherry wine vinegar, olive oil, garlic, oregano, cumin, salt, pepper and lemon juice. Prepare couscous using chicken stock and 2 tablespoons olive oil. Fold in dressing; remove any lumps.
Step 3:
1/2  red pepper, chopped
1/2  yellow pepper, chopped
1/2  orange pepper, chopped
3/4  c. fresh parsley
1/2  c. green onion, sliced diagonally

      Toss marinated beans with couscous. Add red pepper, yellow pepper, orange pepper, parsley and green onion. Serve at room temperature.
