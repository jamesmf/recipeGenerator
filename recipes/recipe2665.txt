SPICED TEA


1 1/2  c. Tang
1  pkg. orange flavor instant breakfast drink
1/2  c. sugar
1/2  c. instant tea
1  tsp. cinnamon
1/2  tsp. cloves

      Combine  all  ingredients  and store in tightly covered container.  For each serving use 1 heaping teaspoonful per cup of boiling water and stir until dissolved.
