LASAGNA


1  strip cooked lasagna
1/4  diabetic Italian spaghetti sauce
1/3  c. cottage cheese
1  oz. cheese (American Cheddar), sliced and diced
4  Tbsp. green pepper, sliced and diced

      Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes.  Yield:  1 serving.
      Exchanges:  1 1/2 bread, 1 vegetable, 1 1/2 fat.  Calories: 287.
