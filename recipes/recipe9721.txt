CHICKEN AND RICE


3  or 4 chicken breasts, thawed
1 (1 oz.)  pkg. dried onion soup mix
2 1/2  c. rice
2 1/2  c. water
1  can cream of mushroom soup

      Combine mushroom soup, onion soup, rice and water in a 9 x 13 baking dish. Put chicken breasts on top. Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 to 40 minutes or until chicken is done.
