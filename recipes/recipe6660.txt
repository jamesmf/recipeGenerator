SPICY ITALIAN ROAST


3 to 4  lb. rump roast
1 1/2  tsp. garlic salt
1 (4 oz.)  can sliced mushrooms
1  onion, diced
2 (1 1/2 oz.)  pkg. spaghetti sauce seasoning mix
2 (8 oz.)  can tomato sauce

      Season roast with garlic salt (I prefer powder).  Place in crock-pot.  Add mushrooms and onions.  Combine seasoning mix and tomato sauce.  Pour over all.  Cover and cook on low 10 - 12 hours (high:  4 - 6 hours).
      If desired thicken gravy and serve over hot pasta, rice, or mashed potatoes.
