CHILI


5  lb. ground meat
4  large onions
4  cans stewed tomatoes
6  cans pinto beans
2  packs McCormick hot chili seasoning
3  packs McCormick regular chili seasoning

	Brown ground meat and drain. Add onions, McCormick seasonings and tomatoes. Cook 45 minutes to 1 hour. Blend beans until creamy and add to chili. Cook 30 to 45 minutes more.
