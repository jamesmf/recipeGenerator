LAYERED CHICKEN CASSEROLE


2  cans green beans
2  boxes of stuffing mix
2  c. diced, cooked chicken
2  cans cream soup

      Pour green beans, undrained, into a greased 9 x 13 inch pan. Sprinkle stuffing mix on top. Layer with cooked chicken. Top with cream soup. Bake at 350 degrees;F for 35  - 40 minutes.
