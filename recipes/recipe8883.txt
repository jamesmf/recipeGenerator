FETTUCCINE WITH ALFREDO SAUCE


1 (12 oz.)  pkg. fettuccine (uncooked)
1/2  c. butter or margarine
1 1/2  c. grated Parmesan cheese
1  c. whipping cream
1/4  c. chopped fresh parsley

      Cook pasta according to package directions. Drain. Melt butter over medium heat. Add whipping cream. Stir in cheese. Add parsley. Toss sauce with hot pasta.
