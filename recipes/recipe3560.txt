BAKED CARAMEL CORN


1  c. butter or margarine
2  c. firmly packed brown sugar
1/2  c. light or dark corn syrup
1  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. baking soda
1  tsp. vanilla
6  qt. popped corn

	Melt butter or margarine; stir in brown sugar, corn syrup and salt.  Bring to a boil stirring constantly.  Boil without stirring for 5 minutes.  Remove from heat; stir in soda and vanilla. Gradually pour over popped corn.  Mix well.  Turn into a large shallow pan.  Bake in 250 degrees; oven 1 hour, stirring every 15 minutes. Remove from oven.  Cool completely.  Break apart and place in tightly covered container.	Makes about 5 quarts.
