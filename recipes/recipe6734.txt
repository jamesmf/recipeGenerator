MEXICAN CHICKEN


1  boiled chicken
1  pkg. tortillas
1  can cream chicken soup
1  can mushroom soup
1  can Ro-Tel (tomatoes and chilies)
1  bell pepper, chopped fine
1  onion, chopped fine
1/2  lb. Velveeta cheese, grated or sliced

      Remove bones from chicken and set aside.  Mix soups, Ro-Tel, pepper and onion.  Break tortillas into bite-size pieces.  In the bottom of an oblong  greased pan, pat a layer of tortillas, then a layer of chicken, then a layer of mixture.  Continue layers until all are used.  Cover with cheese.  Bake 45 minutes at 350 degrees;.
