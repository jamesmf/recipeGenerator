SPICY CHICKEN WITH OLIVES


3 large  cloves garlic
4  large boneless chicken breasts, halved
4  Tbsp. unsalted butter
1  tsp. grated lemon zest
1  tsp. ground ginger
1/2  tsp. cinnamon
1/2  tsp. black pepper
1/4  tsp. cayenne pepper
6  oz. pitted green olives
15-1/2  oz. can chick peas
  Small bunch scallions
  Small bunch coriander
1/4  cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
1  lemon, thinly sliced
1/4  cup mild olive oil

      Preheat oven to 400 degrees;F.  Crush garlic under flat blade of chef's knife; remove and discard peels.  Set aside.
      Wash and dry chicken breasts. Place breast halves in 14x7x2-inch non-aluminum baking dish.
      Melt butter over medium heat in small non-aluminum saucepan. Add lemon zest to butter.  Stir in garlic, ginger, cinnamon, black pepper, and cayenne pepper, and mix well.  Drizzle sauce over chicken.
      Bake chicken, uncovered, in lower half of oven 30 minutes.
      Meanwhile, drain olives.  Place chick peas in large strainer, rinse under cold running water, and set aside to drain.
       Wash and drain scallions and coriander.  Trim ends and discard. Thinly slice enough scallions to measure 1/2 cup.  Finely chop enough coriander to measure 1/4 cup.
      Combine lemon juice, lemon slices, scallions, coriander and olive oil in medium-size non-aluminum bowl.  Add drained olives and chickpeas and stir to combine.  Set aside, stirring occasionally.
      After chicken has baked 30 minutes, reduce oven temperature to 350 degrees;F.  Spoon olive mixture over chicken and bake another 15 minutes, or until juice run clear when chicken is pierced with a knife.
      Transfer chicken to serving platter and top with olive mixture and pan juices.
