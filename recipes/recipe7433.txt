CRABMEAT QUICHE


1  lb. crabmeat
8  oz. Swiss cheese, shredded
8  oz. Cheddar cheese, shredded
1  c. half and half
2  eggs, lightly beaten
4  green onions, chopped
2  Tbsp. flour
8  drops Tabasco sauce
2  frozen deep-dish pie shells

      Place all ingredients (except pie shells) together in a bowl and mix well.  Spoon the mixture into the 2 pie shells.  Place on a cookie sheet in a 350 degrees; oven and bake for 30 minutes.  Serves 6.
