BEAN CASSEROLE


1  lb. ground beef
1/2  lb. bacon
2 (28 oz.)  cans Bush's original beans (use juice)
2 (14 oz.)  cans dark kidney beans, drained
2 (14 oz.)  cans Great Northern beans, drained

      Fry and drain meat.  Fry and crumble bacon; add beans.  Add brown sugar to taste.  Heat in crock-pot.  Good leftover.
