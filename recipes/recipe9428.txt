BROILED STRIPED BASS


4  fish fillets
1  stick melted butter
  lemon juice
  Parmesan cheese
  paprika

	Spray broiling pan with Pam. Place fish skin side down. Drizzle each fillet with lemon juice. Drizzle each fillet with butter. Sprinkle with Parmesan and paprika. Salt and pepper to taste. Broil fillets on top rack for 10 to 15 minutes.
