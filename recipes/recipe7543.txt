CAJUN CHOW MEIN


1  lb ground beef
1  c. chopped onion
1  c. chopped celery
1  c. diced bell pepper
1 (4 oz.)  can mushrooms
1 (No. 2)  can tomatoes
4  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. Pepper
1  c. raw rice
  Few drops Tabasco sauce

      Brown ground beef.  Add remaining ingredients.  Cover and bring to boil.  Reduce heat to simmer.  Cook 30 to 40 minutes.  Yield: 6-8 servings.
