CREAM PUFFS


1  c. boiling water
  pinch of salt to boiling water
4  eggs
1 scant  c. butter
1  c. boiling water
1  c. flour

      Boil water, butter and salt.  Add flour and remove from stove. Stir until smooth.  Add 1 egg at a time.  Drop on ungreased baking sheet and bake in oven for 20 minutes.
      Filling:  Diet Jell-O instant pudding or regular instant pudding or your own custard filling.  Make hole or slice puffs and put in filling.
