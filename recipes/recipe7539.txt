BROCCOLI CHICKEN CASSEROLE


4  boneless/skinless chicken breasts
1  Tbsp. butter
1 large  pkg. frozen broccoli (any style)
2  cans Cream of Chicken soup
1  c. mayo
1  tsp. lemon Juice
1  tsp. curry powder
1/2 - 3/4  c. buttered bread crumbs

      Cook chicken until tender in salted water with butter. Cut chicken into bite size pieces if desired. Cook broccoli according to package directions.  Mix soup, mayo, lemon juice and curry powder together. Add broccoli and chicken to soup mixture and pour into a large casserole dish.  Top with buttered breadcrumbs.  Bake at 350 degrees;  for 30 minutes.
