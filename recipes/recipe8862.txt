SOUR CREAM CHICKEN ENCHILADAS


2  cans cream of chicken soup
16  oz. sour cream
1  can chopped green chilies
1  c. onion, chopped
3  to 4 chicken breasts
1  lb. grated cheese
10  tortillas (small)

      Boil chicken; chop. Heat soup, sour cream, chilies and onion together in saucepan. Fill tortilla with chicken, cheese and spoonful of sauce. Lay in casserole dish. Top with remaining sauce and cheese. Heat in oven at 350 degrees; for about 20 minutes or until cheese melts well.
