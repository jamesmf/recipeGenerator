OLIVE-CHEESE BALLS


3  Tbsp. butter, softened
1  c. grated sharp cheddar cheese
1/2  c. all-purpose flour
1/2  tsp. paprika
1/4  tsp. salt
2 1/2  dozen stuffed olives

      Mix first 5 ingredients to make dough.  Form mixture around each olive to make a small ball and place on ungreased cookie sheet.  Bake at 400 degrees; for 10-15 minutes, until slightly browned. Serve warm. Can be prepared ahead of time and put into freezer unbaked.  Bake as directed without thawing.
