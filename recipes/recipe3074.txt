BACON WRAPPED WATER CHESTNUTS


1  pkg. bacon
1  c. ketchup
1  c. brown sugar

      You will need toothpicks.
      Preheat oven to 350 degrees;.  Wrap 1/2 strip bacon around one water chestnut and secure with a toothpick.  Do this to entire can of water chestnuts.  Place in a baking dish and bake 1/2 hour or until bacon is done.  Drain grease.
       Mix ketchup and brown sugar; drizzle over the bacon wrapped chestnuts. Bake an additional 15 minutes and serve.
