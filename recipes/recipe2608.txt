ANGEL BISCUITS


5  c. self-rising flour
1/3  c. sugar
1  tsp. soda
1  c. shortening
2  pkg. yeast, dissolved in 1/4 c. warm water
2  c. buttermilk

      Combine  dry  ingredients.  Cut in shortening.  Stir in yeast and buttermilk.  Roll and cut.  Bake at 450 degrees;.  Will keep in refrigerator 1 week.
