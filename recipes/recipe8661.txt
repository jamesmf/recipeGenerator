CHILI


2  lb. ground meat
  salt and pepper to taste
2  Tbsp. flour
1  onion, finely chopped
3 (1 lb.)  cans chili beans
3  Tbsp. tomato sauce
1/2  Tbsp. garlic powder
1  Tbsp. chili powder
3  c. water (add more if needed to be proper consistency)

	In a large pot, brown meat.  Add salt, pepper, flour and onion.  Cook until onion in translucent. Add chili beans, tomato sauce, garlic powder and chili powder; mix well.  Add water and continue cooking for 1 hour.
	Note: A teaspoon of Kitchen Bouquet, gravy color, can be added to achieve a nice color.
