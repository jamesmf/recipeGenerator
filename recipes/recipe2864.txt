HOLIDAY BUNS

Dough:
2 1/2  c. flour
1  Tbsp. sugar
2 1/3  c. butter
1 cake or  envelope yeast
1  c. milk (warm)

 
Filling:
6  Tbsp. sugar
2  Tbsp. vanilla sugar
3  Tbsp. butter, softened

 
Topping:
1  egg
  sugar

      Dough:  Mix flour and sugar in a bowl.  Add butter and work into flour with fingers.  Dissolve yeast in milk and work into flour-butter mixture to form dough.  Roll out on floured board and cut into 3-inch squares.  Put a dab of filling in the center of each square and fold edges together.  Place in paper cupcake cups and let rise 1 1/2 hours.  Brush with beaten egg and sprinkle with sugar.  Bake at 400 degrees; for 6 to 7 minutes.
      Filling:  Cream all ingredients together.  Chill slightly if very soft.
