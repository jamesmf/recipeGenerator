BAKED LASAGNA


1  lb. ground beef
2  Tbsp. salad oil
1  medium onion, minced
4  cloves garlic, chopped
1  tsp. chopped parsley
2 (6 oz.)  cans tomato paste
2  c. water
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper
8  oz. lasagna
2  eggs
3/4  lb. Ricotta or creamed cottage cheese
3/4  lb. Mozzarella cheese, sliced
  Parmesan cheese

      In a large heavy pan, brown ground beef in oil with onion, garlic and parsley.  Add tomato paste, 2 cups water, salt and pepper; simmer 1 hour.  Meanwhile cook lasagna as directed on package.  Mix together eggs and Ricotta or cottage cheese.  In 13 x 9 x 2-inch baking pan, spread about 1/2 cup sauce, then alternate layers of lasagna, sauce, Ricotta and egg mixture and Mozzarella until baking pan is filled.  Top with sauce; sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.  Bake in moderate 375 degrees; oven about 30 minutes or until heated through.  Makes 8 servings.
