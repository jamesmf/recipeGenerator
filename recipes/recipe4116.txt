OLIVE-RYE APPETIZER


1 (3 oz.)  pkg. thinly sliced smoked beef, chopped
1  c. shredded Cheddar cheese
1  c. sliced ripe olives
1/2  c. mayonnaise
2 1/2  doz. whole grain rye crackers

      Combine beef, cheese and ripe olives with mayonnaise.  Spread 1 tablespoon of the mixture on each cracker.  Place on baking sheet.  Bake at 375 degrees; for 5 to 7 minutes or until cheese melts. Yield: 2 1/2 dozen.
