HAMBURGER STROGANOFF


1 1/2  lb. ground beef
1/2  c. chopped onion
  dash of garlic salt
2  Tbsp. flour
2  c. raw rice, cooked
  salt and pepper to taste
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  can mushrooms, drained
1  c. sour cream

      In skillet, brown meat, onion and garlic salt.  Stir in flour, add salt and pepper to taste.  Simmer  5  minutes.  Add soup and mushrooms and simmer 15 more  minutes.  Fold  in sour cream and heat only until warm.  Serve over rice.
