DANISH CHEESE CAKE

Crust:
1/4  lb. butter
6  Tbsp. sugar
2  eggs
2  c. flour
1  tsp. baking powder

 
Filling:
2  (8 oz.) pkg, cream cheese, softened
1 1/4  c. sugar
1/4  c. flour
4  eggs
  juice of 1 lemon
3  c. milk

      Cream butter and 6 tablespoons sugar; beat in eggs.  Add 2 cups flour and baking powder.  Mix well.  Pat into 9 x 13 x 2 1/2-inch pan.
      Mix together remaining ingredients and beat well until smooth. Pour into crust; sprinkle with cinnamon.  Bake at 300 degrees; for 1 1/2 hours.  Chill well before serving.
