MEAT SAUCE FOR SPAGHETTI


1  lb. ground beef
1  onion, chopped
1  rib celery, chopped fine
1 (6 oz.)  can tomatoes
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1 (6 oz.)  can tomato paste
1  tsp. chili powder
  salt and pepper to taste

      Brown ground beef, onion, celery, and tomatoes. Add remaining ingredients and simmer 2 1/2 hours. Stir frequently. If sauce becomes too thick, add small amount of water. Serve over hot cooked spaghetti. Serves 6.
