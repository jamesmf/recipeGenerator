MEXICAN ROLL-UPS


8  oz Cream Cheese (softened)
8 oz  carton sour cream
  small can green chiles
  small jar pimentos
  small jar black olives (optional)
1/2 small  jar jalapeno peppers (to taste)
8  oz fine shredded cheddar cheese
  flour tortilla shells

      Drain chiles, pimentos, olives, and jalapenos.  Combine all ingredients except flour tortillas and cheddar cheese in blender. Spread mixture on tortilla shells. Sprinkle with cheddar cheese. Roll each tortilla shell.  Refrigerate until firm.  When ready to serve, slice into 2 inch sections.  Store any unused portions in refrigerator. These keep well.
