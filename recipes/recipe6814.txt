CHICKEN SQUARES


3  oz. cream cheese
3  Tbsp. butter
1/4  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper
8  oz. tube crescent rolls
10  oz. chicken breasts
  cornflake crumbs
2  Tbsp. milk

      Blend 2 tablespoons butter and cream cheese until smooth. Add cooked, diced chicken, salt, pepper and milk.
      Separate crescent dough into 4 rectangles.  Spoon 1/2 cup chicken mixture in center of each.  Pull 4 corners to top and twist to seal.  Pour 1 tablespoon melted butter over top and sprinkle with cornflake crumbs.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 20 to 25 minutes until golden brown.
