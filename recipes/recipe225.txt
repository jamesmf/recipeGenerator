BANANA CHIP COOKIES


1  c. sugar
1  c. brown sugar
1  c. butter
2  eggs
1  c. mashed bananas
4  c. flour
1  tsp. baking soda
1/8  tsp. salt
2  c. butterscotch chips
1  c. pecans

      Cream together first 4 ingredients.  In a separate bowl, combine flour, baking soda and salt.  Add bananas and half of flour mixture.  Mix well.  Add chips and nuts; then rest of flour.  Drop on greased and floured cookie sheet.  Bake for about 12 minutes at 350 degrees;.  Makes a soft cookie and a large batch.
