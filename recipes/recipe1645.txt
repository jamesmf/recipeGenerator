SHRIMP SALAD A LA FINLAND


1  head lettuce
1  bunch leaf lettuce
2  to 3 chopped tomatoes
1  red bell pepper, chopped
1 small  can each:  corn, peas and green beans, drained
1/2  lb. Edam or Feta cheese, shredded

      Tear lettuce into bite size chunks.  Add all other ingredients and toss well.  Chill until serving time.  Serve with buttermilk dressing.
