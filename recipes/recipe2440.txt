PEANUT BUTTER FUDGE


1 1/2  sticks margarine
1 small  can evaporated milk
3  c. sugar
1 (7 oz.)  jar Marshmallow Creme
1 (12 oz.)  jar peanut butter

      Melt margarine over low heat.  Add milk and sugar.  Bring to rapid boil.  Boil for 5 minutes; stir constantly.  Remove from heat and add peanut butter, then Marshmallow Creme.  Pour into a buttered 13 x 9 pan.
