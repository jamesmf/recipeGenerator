IOWA CORN AND SAUSAGE CASSEROLE


1  lb. pork sausage
2  eggs, beaten
1 1/4  c. milk
1/4  c. chopped celery
1/4  c. chopped red or green peppers
1 1/4  c. milk
2  cans cream-style corn or 4 c. fresh garden corn
2  tsp. salt
1  small onion, chopped
1 1/2  c. cracker crumbs

      Partially cook sausage; drain.  Blend in eggs and milk.  Stir in remaining ingredients, except 1/4 cup crumbs.  Place in 1 1/2-quart casserole.  Top with reserved crumbs. Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour.  Yields 8 to 10 servings.
