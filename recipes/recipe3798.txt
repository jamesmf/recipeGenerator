RADISH AND CREAM CHEESE SPREAD


1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
1/4  c. butter
1/2  tsp. celery salt
  dash of paprika
1/2  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  c. finely chopped radishes
1/4  c. finely chopped green onions

      Mix cheese, butter, celery salt, paprika and Worcestershire sauce together until smooth.  Stir in radishes and onions.  Chill several hours to blend flavors.  Spread on crackers.  Makes 1 1/2 cups.
