ONION DRESSING


1 (14 oz.)  can evaporated skim milk
1/2  c. vinegar
1  envelope (about 1 1/2 oz.) onion soup mix
1/3  c. catsup
1/2  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/4  tsp. red pepper sauce

      In a covered jar, shake all ingredients together well.  Makes 2 1/2 cups dressing.
      Per Serving (1 Tablespoon):  20 Calories.
