50's Style Pizza

Crust:
2  c. flour
3  tsp. baking powder
2 to 4  Tbsp. shortening
1  tsp. salt
2  Tbsp. mustard
1/2  c. milk

      Measure dry ingredients into bowl.  Stir mustard into milk; blend milk and shortening into dry ingredients.  Roll or press into 9 x 13 cake pan.  Prick surface with fork.  Bake in hot oven, 425 degrees; for 5 to 7 minutes.  Remove.  Reduce heat to 375 degrees;.
Sauce:
2  cans (8 oz.) tomato sauce
1  Tbsp. sugar
1  Tbsp. minced onion
2  Tbsp. mustard
1/4  tsp. oregano

      Mix in saucepan and simmer 10 minutes.  Spread sauce over crust.
Toppings:
  hamburger, browned
1 1/2  c. or more Velveeta cheese, grated  (or Cheddar)
2  Tbsp. Parmesan

      Add toppings.  Bake 12 to 15 minutes more.  Can also add pepperoni and other toppings as desired.
