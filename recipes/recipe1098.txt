LOW CALORIE BEEF/CHICKEN GRAVY


1  c. beef or chicken bouillon
2  Tbsp. cold water
1  Tbsp. cornstarch
1  tsp. dehydrated onion flakes

      Add onion flakes to bouillon.  Bring to a boil.  Dissolve cornstarch in cold water.  Add cornstarch mixture to boiling bouillon.  Simmer until gravy thickens, stirring constantly, about 2 minutes.  Yields 1 cup.  Calories:  Negligible.  For diabetics: Exchanges:  Free.
