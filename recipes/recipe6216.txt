CHICKEN SALAD


1  (5 to 6 lb.) hen, cooked
4  medium carrots, grated
2  Tbsp. minced onion
2 1/2  c. salad dressing (Kraft is best)
2  Tbsp. prepared mustard
1 large  can shoestring potatoes

      Cut cooked chicken into large pieces.  Combine with carrots, onion, salad dressing and mustard.  This should be done one day in advance of serving.  Refrigerate.
      Just before serving, fold in the potato sticks.  Serve on bed of lettuce.  Yield: 10 servings.
