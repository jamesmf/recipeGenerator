CHICKEN & RICE


  6-8 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
2  boxes Uncle Ben's long grain rice with seasoning package
2  cans cream of mushroom soup
2  cans cream of celery soup
2  cans cream of chicken soup
1  envelope Lipton's Onion soup mix

      Mix rice and seasoning packet together with the 6 cans of soup.  Let stand for 15 minutes. Spread in 9 x 13 inch pan.  Lay chicken breasts on top of rice mixture.  Sprinkle onion soup mix on top of chicken breasts.  Cover with foil and bake 2 hours at 350 degrees. Makes 6-8 servings.
