SHRIMP AND SCALLOP CASSEROLE


2  lb. shrimp (large size)
1/2  lb. butter
1  Tbsp. ketchup
2  to 3 dashes Worcestershire sauce
1  tsp. A.1. sauce
  juice of 1/2 lemon
1  clove garlic, crushed
1  lb. scallops
1 small  can mushrooms or fresh
2  tsp. onion flakes
2  tsp. parsley
1/2  can seasoned bread crumbs

      Coat shrimp with oil and spread in a baking dish.  Mix butter, ketchup, Worcestershire sauce, A.1., lemon and garlic. Pour over shrimp.  Mix in scallops and top with crumbs.  Bake at 375 degrees; for 20 minutes or until bubbly.
