SPAGHETTI MARCO POLO


  Spaghetti, cooked
2  Tbsp. butter
  Olive oil
  Walnuts or pecans, chopped
  Black olives, chopped
  Green onions, chopped
  Pimiento
  Tuna (in oil), drained
  Parsley, chopped
  Swiss cheese
  Salt and pepper

      Mix all ingredients together.  Make as much spaghetti as you wish and add all other ingredients to taste.
