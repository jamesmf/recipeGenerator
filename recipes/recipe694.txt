PASTA PRIMAVERA WITH ALASKAN SALMON


1 1/2  c. sliced mushrooms
2  Tbsp. chopped onions
2  Tbsp. margarine
1  Tbsp. flour
1/8  tsp. basil
1/8  tsp. oregano
1/2  c. milk
1  lb. Alaskan salmon, cooked and flaked
1  small yellow squash, sliced
1/2  c. cooked peas
1/2  c. diced tomato
1  Tbsp. parsley
1/2  c. white wine
8  oz. spinach fettuccine, cooked and drained
  salt and pepper to taste
  lemon wedges

      Saut√© mushrooms and onions in margarine.  Add flour and herbs; cook and stir 1 minute.  Add milk; cook and stir until thick.  Add salmon, squash, peas, tomato, parsley and wine.  Toss hot noodles with vegetable mixture.  Season to taste with salt and pepper. Garnish with lemon wedges.  Makes about 6 servings.  Low calorie; contains approximately 267 calories per serving.
