PRESSURE COOKER SPARERIBS AND SAUERKRAUT


2  lb. or more country-style ribs or spareribs
1 large  can sauerkraut, drained
5  large onions, cut in quarters
1 1/2  c. water

      Brown spareribs in frying pan with oil or under broiler. Place trivet in pressure cooker. Pour in water and add sauerkraut, onions and spareribs. Cook at high pressure for 1 hour.  Serve with your choice of potato, etc.
      Onions sweeten up sauerkraut, giving dish a delightful flavor.
