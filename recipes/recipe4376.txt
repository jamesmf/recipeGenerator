MOM'S WALDORF SALAD


6  - 8 apples, cubed
1  c. walnuts, chopped
1  c. grapes
1  c. celery, diced
1/2  can sweetened condensed milk
2 - 3  c. Miracle Whip salad dressing
3  Tbsp. sugar

      Core and cube apples.  Mix apples, walnuts, grapes and celery together.  Stir in condensed milk.  Mix Miracle Whip with sugar and stir into apple mixture.  Refrigerate for 1/2 hour and serve chilled.
