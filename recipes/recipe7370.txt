HUNGARIAN GULYAS


1  lb. beef flank
2  Tbsp. lard
1  large onion
1  green pepper
2  large carrots
2  parsley roots and greens
2  stalks celery
1  fresh tomato
2  medium potatoes
1/4  tsp. paprika
1  Tbsp. salt
1 1/4  c. water
1  qt. water

      Cut meat into squares.  Wash.  Saut√© onion in lard; add paprika.  Stir well.  Add meat, salt, diced tomato and 1/4 cup water.  Cook slowly 1 hour.  Add washed and diced vegetables, except potatoes.  Add 1 cup water and cook slowly for 1/2 hour. Add potatoes and continue to cook for 15 minutes.  Add 1 quart cold water.  Let it come to a boil and cook for 10 minutes.  Serve as main dish.  Dumplings can be added.
