VEGETABLE DEER SOUP


3  lb. Venison, chopped
  Lima Beans
  Potatoes
  Carrots
  Celery
2  lg. Onions, chopped
1  tbsp. Garlic Salt
2  Jalapeno Peppers, chopped
2  qts. Tomatoes with juice
1  qt. water for each quart of tomatoes
2 (16 oz.)  cans whole kernel Corn
1  qt. bag Okra, cut up
  Salt to taste

      Cook venison until tender.  Put all ingredients into large pot and cook until vegetables are tender.
