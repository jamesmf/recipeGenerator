EVELYN'S PORK ROAST

Marinade for 3 to 6-Inch Pork Loin:
1/3  c. lemon juice
1/3  c. water
1  tsp. ginger
1  tsp. minced garlic
1  tsp. lemon pepper
3/4  c. brown sugar
2  Tbsp. catsup
1/4  c. soy sauce

      Marinate in refrigerator overnight.  Bake at 325 degrees; with marinade 3 hours or more!  Thicken with flour or cornstarch for gravy.  Slice; add little gravy on slices.
