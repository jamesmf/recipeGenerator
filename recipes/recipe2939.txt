COUNTRY HAM DIP


2 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
1  c. sour cream
1/4  c. finely chopped onion
1  c. finely chopped country ham
1/2  tsp. garlic powder
  Fritos Scoops

 
Topping:
1  c. chopped pecans
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire
1  Tbsp. melted margarine

      Bake country ham pieces on low heat for 1 hour or until very tender.  Mix all ingredients together and bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes.  Serve with Fritos Scoops.
