PIZZA PASTA


1 (16 oz.)  pkg. mostaccioli or penne pasta, cooked
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. pepperoni
1  c. sliced mushrooms
1 (8 oz.)  jar pizza sauce
1 (14 oz.)  can diced tomatoes
1  Tbsp. Italian seasoning
2  c. shredded Mozzarella cheese

      Mix pepperoni, mushrooms, sauce, tomatoes and seasoning. Layer pasta, sauce mixture and cheese in 13 x 9 pan.  Bake at 450 degrees; for 20 minutes.
