CHOCOLATE VELVET FROSTING


1 (6 oz.)  pkg. semi-sweet chocolate morsels
3  Tbsp. butter
1/4  c. milk
1  tsp. vanilla
1/4  tsp. salt
3  c. sifted confectioners sugar

      Melt chocolate morsels and butter over hot, not boiling, water. Remove from water.  Add milk, vanilla and salt.  Mix until well blended.  Beat in confectioners sugar gradually.
