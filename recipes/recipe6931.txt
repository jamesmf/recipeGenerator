HAWAIIAN PORK


1  egg
2  Tbsp. water
1/4  c. Gold Medal flour
2  c. cubed cooked pork
1  Tbsp. cornstarch
3  Tbsp. shortening
1 (13 oz.)  can pineapple tidbits (reserve syrup)
2  Tbsp. vinegar
2  Tbsp. soy sauce
1  medium green pepper, cut into strips
1  can water chestnuts, drained and thinly sliced
1 (3 oz.)  can mushrooms, drained

      Beat egg thoroughly in medium bowl. Add water, flour and salt; beat until smooth. Stir in meat, mixing until coated. Melt shortening in medium skillet. Add meat mixture; cook and stir until meat is brown. Remove from heat; keep warm.
