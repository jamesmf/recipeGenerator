EASY FRUIT SALAD


8  oz. (1 can) fruit, (your choice)*
3  oz. (1 small box) jello (your choice of flavor)
1  lb. (1 small tub) cottage cheese
8  oz. (1 small tub) Cool Whip

      *Use any fruit:  crushed pineapple, peaches, fruit cocktail, Mandarin oranges, etc.
      Sprinkle jello (dry) over cottage cheese, mixing well.  Drain fruit and add to mixture.  Fold in Cool Whip.  Chill until ready to serve.
      NOTE:  Recipe may be doubled by using larger sizes of all ingredients.
      NOTE:  This is also makes a good dessert.
