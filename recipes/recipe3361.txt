QUESO FUNDIDO


2  oz. chorizo sausage
2  c. (8 oz.) shredded sharp cheddar cheese
1  c. (4 oz.) shredded Monterey Jack cheese
1/2  c. dairy sour cream
1  can (4-oz.) chopped green chili peppers, drained
1 to 2  Tbsp. finely chopped canned chipotle chili peppers in adobo sauce
  chopped green bell pepper, chopped seeded tomato, and sliced ripe olives
  tortilla chips

      Cook sausage in a small skillet until no longer pink. Drain well on paper towels.  Combine cheeses, sour cream, and chili peppers in a large bowl; stir in cooked sausage.  Spread in a 1-quart baking dish.  Bake in a 375 degrees; oven for 20-25 minutes or till cheese is melted and bubbly, stirring once.  Top with chopped sweet pepper, tomato, and sliced ripe olives.  Serve immediately with tortilla chips.  Serves 8.
