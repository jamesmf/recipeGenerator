GIBLET GRAVY


5 to 6  Tbsp. oil
1/2  c. self-rising flour
  salt and pepper
2  boiled eggs
  chicken/turkey broth
  chicken/turkey giblets

	Heat oil in pan. Add flour, stirring until brown. Salt and pepper. Add 1 1/2 cups juice from chicken or turkey to thicken. Add chicken or turkey gizzards and pieces of shredded poultry. Add chopped eggs. Add more broth or water to thin gravy, if needed.
