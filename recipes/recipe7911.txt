CHICKEN `65'


4  lbs. boneless chicken, cut into 1-inch pieces
4  Tbsp. each, ginger and garlic paste
2  Tbsp. monosodium glutamate
2  Tbsp. soy sauce
1/2  Tbsp. black pepper
1/2  Tbsp. red food color
1  egg
2  Tbsp. cornstarch
  Salt as needed
1/2  cup yogurt
  Juice of 1 lemon
3  Tbsp. oil
  5-6 green chilies
10  curry leaves

      Mix chicken with the next 8 ingredients.  Marinate for 3 hours.  Then deep-fry the chicken pieces.  Reserve the marinade.
      In a bowl, add 1/2 cup yogurt, preferably sour.  Add the reserved marinade and the juice of 1 lemon to the yogurt.  Mix in the fried chicken also. Marinate for 15 minutes.
      Heat 3 Tbsp. oil in a pan.  Add green chilies and curry leaves.  Add the chicken mixture and stir-fry until all the paste gets cooked and looks oily.  Stir-fry for 5 minutes.
       Serve with lemon wedges and cilantro leaves.
