CATFISH STEW


2  lb. catfish fillets
2  lb. potatoes, cut in small pieces
2  lb. onions, cut in small pieces
1  lb. bacon
4 large  cans tomatoes
  salt and pepper
  a little hot sauce

      Cook fish until tender; add potatoes and onions. Fry bacon in frypan until crisp. Add bacon and drippings to other ingredients. Add tomatoes, salt, pepper and hot sauce to taste. Cook until all is tender.
