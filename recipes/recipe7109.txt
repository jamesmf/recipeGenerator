STRING PIE


1  lb. ground beef
1/2  c. chopped onion
1/4  c. chopped green pepper
1 -15 1/2  oz. jar spaghetti sauce
8  oz. whole wheat spaghetti noodles (hot, cooked, and drained)
1/3  c. grated parmesan cheese
2  eggs, beaten
2  tsp. butter
1  c. cottage cheese
1/2  c. (2 oz.) shredded mozzarella cheese

      Cook beef, onion, and green pepper in large skillet over medium-high heat until meat is brown, stirring to separate the meat. Drain fat.  Stir in spaghetti sauce and mix well.  In large bowl, combine spaghetti noodles, parmesan cheese, eggs, and butter; mix well.  Place in bottom of 13 x 9 inch pan.  Spread cottage cheese over top.  Bake in preheated oven 350 degrees; until cheese melts, about 20 minutes.  Makes 6-8 servings.
