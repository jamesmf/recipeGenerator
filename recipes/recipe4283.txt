AVOCADO POTATO SALAD
(Makes 8 Servings)  

6  medium red potatoes (unpeeled), cut in 1-inch cubes
2  avocados, peeled, pitted and cubed
2  Tbsp. fresh lime juice

 
Dressing:
1/4  c. fresh lime juice
2  cloves garlic, crushed
2  Tbsp. honey mustard
2  tsp. sugar
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. black pepper
1  Tbsp. olive oil
1/2  c. nonfat plain yogurt

 
Assembly:
1/2  c. sweet onion, chopped
1/4  c. fresh cilantro leaves, chopped

      Cook's Notes: Salad can be prepared up to 3 hours in advance.
      Prepare Potatoes and Avocados:  Boil diced potatoes in large pot until just tender.  While potatoes boil, cube avocados and toss with 2 tablespoons lime juice; set aside.  Drain potatoes and place in bowl of cold water.  When potatoes have cooled, drain well and place in large salad bowl.
      Make the Dressing: Whisk lime juice, garlic, mustard, sugar, salt, pepper, olive oil and yogurt in medium bowl. Taste dressing and adjust seasonings.
      Assemble The Salad: Pour dressing over potatoes and toss. Gently fold in avocado, onion and cilantro.
      Presentation: Serve at room temperature or cover tightly with plastic wrap and chill until serving.
