SLOPPY JOES


1  lb. lean ground beef
1  tsp. salt
1/4  c. onions, chopped fine
3/4  c. celery with greens, chopped fine
1/4  c. green pepper, chopped fine
1/2  tsp. grated lemon rind
1/4  c. tomato catsup
3/4  c. beef broth
1/2  tsp. dry mustard
1  tsp. Worcestershire sauce

      Fry meat in large skillet until meat changes color.  Pour off excess fat.  Add onions, celery and green pepper.  Stir and cook 1 minute or 2. Combine all other ingredients; mix well and simmer another 15 to 20 minutes.
