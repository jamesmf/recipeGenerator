CHICKEN TETRAZZINI


1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can cream of celery soup
1/2  c. milk
12  oz. cubed, cooked chicken
6  oz. pasta bow ties, cooked and drained
1/3  c. diced green pepper
4  oz. can sliced mushrooms
1  c. frozen English peas
1/4  c. grated Parmesan cheese

      Heat oven to 375 degrees;.  Generously grease a shallow 2 quart baking dish.  Mix undiluted soup and milk in a large bowl until well blended.  Add remaining ingredients except cheese and toss gently to mix.  Turn into prepared dish.  Sprinkle cheese evenly over top.  Bake 15 minutes or until hot and bubbly.  Makes 4 servings.
