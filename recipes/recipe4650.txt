WEDDING SOUP


  meatballs, dime sized
1  egg
1  small onion, diced
1/2-1  c Italian bread crumbs
1/4  c parmesan cheese, grated
1  lb ground beef
3  tbsp parmesan cheese, grated
48 oz  can chicken broth
1/2  c acini de pepe pasta
1/2  c chopped frozen spinach

      Cook meatballs as you normally would.  Keep rolled small.  Warm broth.  Add chopped spinach and boil.  Add precooked meatballs. Reduce to slow simmer.  Add acini de pepe until they are fully cooked.  Add 3 tbsp grated cheese and serve.
