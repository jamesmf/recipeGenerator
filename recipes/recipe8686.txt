TASTY CHICKEN TENDERS


3  c. rice crispy cereal, slightly crushed
1  Tbsp. garlic salt
1  tsp. dill weed
1/4  c. vegetable oil
1  lb. chicken tender or breasts (skinless and boneless)

	Preheat oven to 350 degrees;. Mix cereal, salt and dill. Dip chicken in oil.  Roll in cereal mixture to coat thoroughly.  Place on foil-lined pan. Bake uncovered for 30 to 40 minutes or until chicken is fully cooked.
