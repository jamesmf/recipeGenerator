HOMEMADE POT PIES


  deboned, cooked chicken, leftover turkey or favorite meat
1  can Veg-All
1  can cream of mushroom or cream of celery soup
1  can French-style green beans
1/2  c. chicken broth
1  stick melted oleo or butter
1  c. Bisquick
1  c. milk

      Mix the first 6 ingredients all together in baking dish.
      Mix together the Bisquick and milk. Pour over mixture in baking dish. Bake 1 hour at 350 degrees; or until done.
