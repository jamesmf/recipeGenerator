SMALANDSK OSTKAKA
(Cheese Cake)  

2  c. cottage cheese
3  eggs
2  c. light cream
1/4  c. flour
1/4  c. sugar
1/2  c. coarsely chopped almonds, lightly toasted

      Stir the cottage cheese by hand or with mixer until granular. Add the flour, eggs, sugar, cream and almonds.  Mix well.  Grease a 10-inch fluted pie pan or an 8-inch square baking pan.  Pour in the mixture and bake at 350 degrees; for 50 to 60 minutes or until a knife inserted into the center comes out clean.  Garnish with whipped cream and jelly.
