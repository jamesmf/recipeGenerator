SKILLET SPAGHETTI


1  lb. ground beef
2  tsp. chili powder
1 1/2  tsp. dried oregano, crushed
1  tsp. sugar
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. garlic salt
1 (6 oz.)  can tomato paste
2 1/4  c. tomato juice
2  Tbsp. instant minced onion
1 (7 oz.)  pkg. uncooked spaghetti
  grated parmesan cheese

      In 12 inch skillet, brown ground beef; drain.  Stir in chili powder, oregano, sugar, salt and garlic salt.  Blend in tomato paste.  Stir in tomato juice, onion and 3 1/2 cups water.  Bring to boiling.  Carefully add spaghetti; stir to separate strands. Cover and simmer, stirring frequently, for 30 minutes.  Serve with grated parmesan cheese.  Makes 4 to 6 servings.
