EASY DORM TUNA CASSEROLE


1  box Kraft Macaroni and Cheese
1  can Cream of Mushroom soup, undiluted
1  can of tuna
1 (4 oz.)  can sliced mushrooms (optional)
  Shredded cheese (optional)

      Prepare macaroni and cheese as directed on box.  Place in casserole and mix in cream of mushroom soup and tuna (and sliced mushrooms, if desired).  Can place shredded cheese on top and heat in 350 degrees; oven until bubbly.
