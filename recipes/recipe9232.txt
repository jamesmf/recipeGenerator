MAMA'S POTATOES AND CHOPS


3  Tbsp. butter
2 1/2  c. sweet milk
3  c. raw potatoes, sliced thin
3  Tbsp. flour
1 1/2  tsp. salt
6  thickly cut lean pork chops

      To make sauce, melt butter, then stir in flour until blended. Add milk and salt; cook over low heat until smooth.  Grease baking dish.  Add layers of potatoes and sauce.  Arrange pork chops on top.  (If potatoes are not covered, a little milk may be added.)  Do not cover chops with sauce.  Bake about 1 1/2 hours at 325 degrees;.
