MAMA'S CORNBREAD DRESSING


2  pans cornbread, crumbled
8  biscuits, crumbled
3  c. chopped celery and onion
4 to 5  c. turkey broth (at least)
2 1/2  tsp. poultry seasoning
1  raw egg

      Mix all ingredients together in large mixing bowl. Pour into 11 x 13 pan. Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes.
