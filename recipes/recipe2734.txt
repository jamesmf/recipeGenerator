YUGOSLAVIAN EASTER COOKIES


1  c. margarine
1/3  c. granulated sugar
2  egg yolks
1/2  tsp. brandy (flavoring if desired)
1  tsp. cloves
1  tsp. vanilla
2  c. all-purpose flour
1  tsp. baking powder
  confectioners sugar

      Heat oven to 350 degrees;.  Mix margarine, sugar, egg yolks and flavorings together thoroughly.  Blend flour, baking powder and cloves; stir into butter mixture.  Mix well.  Shape into 3/4-inch balls or shape into cylindrical 2-inch half moons.  Bake 10 minutes on lightly greased cookie sheet.  Do not let brown.  Let cool for 2 minutes, then remove from cookie sheet and dust with confectioners sugar.
