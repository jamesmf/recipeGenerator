CHICKEN AND BROCCOLI PIE


1  pkg. (10 oz.) frozen chopped broccoli, thawed and drained
1 1/2  c. shredded fat-free cheddar cheese
1  c. cooked chicken, cut up
1/3  c. chopped onion
1/2  c. Bisquick baking mix
1  c. skim milk
2  eggs
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper

      Heat oven to 400 degrees. Grease 9 inch pie plate. Sprinkle broccoli, 1 c. cheese, the chicken and onion in pie plate. Stir baking mix, milk, eggs, salt and pepper with fork until blended. Pour into pie plate. Bake 32-37 minutes or until knife inserted in center comes out clean. Sprinkle with remaining cheese. Bake 1 to 2 minutes longer or until cheese is melted.  Yield: 6 servings.
