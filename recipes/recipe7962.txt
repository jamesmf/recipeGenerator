RED MAGIC MEAT LOAF


1  1/2 lbs. lean ground beef
1  cup soft bread crumbs
1/3  cup and 1/4 cup ketchup
1/4  cup chopped onion
1  egg, slightly beaten
1/2  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper
1/4  cup crushed pineapple, drained

      Combine beef, bread crumbs, 1/3 cup ketchup, onion, egg, salt and pepper in a large bowl.  Mix well.  Form mixture into 8 x 4 x 1 1/2 inch loaf and place in shallow baking dish.  Bake at 350 degrees for 40 minutes.  Remove from oven.
      Combine remaining 1/4 cup ketchup with pineapple and spoon over top of loaf.  Bake an additional 30 minutes.  Let meat loaf stand for 5 minutes before slicing.  Serves 6.
