CHICKEN POT PIE

Pastry:
2 1/2  c. flour
1/2  tsp. salt
4  Tbsp. shortening
8  Tbsp. butter
  ice water (to moisten)

      Mix  up like pie crust.  Chill  in  refrigerator  while mixing up filling.
Filling:
1  can chicken soup or mushroom soup
2 soup  cans broth from boiled chicken
1/2  c. sour cream
3  c. deboned chicken
  salt and pepper

      Mix together and pour into pastry  lined  9  x  13-inch pan. Cover top with pastry, too.  Bake at 350 degrees; until crust is done.
