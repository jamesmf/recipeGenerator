CINNAMON COOKIES


2 1/2  c. flour
1 1/2  tsp. baking powder
1/2  tsp. salt
1  tsp. cinnamon
1  c. sugar
3/4  c. oil
2  eggs, beaten
1  tsp. vanilla

      Preheat oven to 375 degrees;.  Sift flour, baking powder, salt and cinnamon together.  Put sugar and oil in mixing bowl; mix well. Add eggs, then vanilla.  Add flour mixture all at once; beat well. Shape into 1/2-inch balls; roll in sugar.  Place on lightly greased baking sheets.  Flatten with fork; sprinkle with sugar.  Bake 10 to 12 minutes.  Remove to cooling trays.  Yields about 50.
