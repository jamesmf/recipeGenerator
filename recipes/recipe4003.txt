MEXICAN PARTY DIP


1 to 2  lb. hamburger
16  oz. sour cream
2 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
1  pkg. taco seasoning mix
1 large  jar salsa or picante sauce
1 to 2  c. shredded cheddar cheese

      Brown hamburger; add taco seasoning packet.  In another bowl, mix together sour cream and cheeses.  Reserve some of shredded cheese for topping.  Place cheese mixture in bottom of a large ovenproof casserole dish.  Follow with hamburger mixture.  Next, add salsa and top with remaining shredded cheese.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 to 45 minutes or until cheese is melted.  Serve with tortilla chips.
      Note:  You can also mix all the ingredients together and place in a fondue pot or crock pot to keep warm.
