BROCCOLI SALAD III


2 large  bunches broccoli flowerets, washed and drained
1  lb. bacon, fried, cut into small pieces and patted dry
3/4  c. chopped walnuts
3/4  c. white raisins

 
Dressing (Make Several Days In Advance):
2  c. Hellmann's mayonnaise (no fat/no cholesterol)
3/4  c. sugar
4  Tbsp. vinegar
1  c. chopped onion

	Blend when ready to serve.
	Loretta uses 1 bunch broccoli, 1/2 pound bacon, 1 small red onion, 1 can chopped water chestnuts (optional) and 1/4 cup raisins, blends with desired amount of salad dressing or mayonnaise and chills for 3 hours.
