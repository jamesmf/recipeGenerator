MOM'S MEATBALLS


1 1/2  lb. hamburger
1  c. evaporated milk
1/4  tsp. pepper
3/4  c. uncooked oatmeal
1 1/2  tsp. salt

      Combine above ingredients and form into meatballs.  Fry on both sides until brown.  Place meatballs into a baking dish.
Sauce:
1/2  c. chopped onion
2  Tbsp. vinegar
2  Tbsp. sugar
1/2  c. water
2  Tbsp. worcestershire sauce
1  c. ketchup

      Combine all ingredients.  Pour sauce meatballs.  Bake at 325 degrees; for 45 minutes.
