HANKY-PANKY


1  lb. ground beef
1  lb. hot pork sausage
3/4 to 1  lb. Velveeta cheese, grated
1/4  tsp. garlic salt
1/4  tsp. oregano
1  Tbsp. worcestershire sauce
2  loaves party rye bread
1  can Ro-Tel tomatoes, optional

      Brown ground beef and sausage in skillet.  Add cheese, garlic salt, oregano and worcestershire sauce.  Heat and stir until cheese melts.  Spread mixture on bread (about 1 tsp. per slice). Garnish with tomatoes for color and added flavor, if desired. Bake at 325 degrees; for 10 to 12 minutes.  Makes about 70.
      Can be made ahead, but do not bake and freeze.
