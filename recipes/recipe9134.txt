MEAT LOAF


2  lb. ground beef
2  eggs
1/4  c. ketchup
1  tsp. salt (to taste)
  pepper (to taste)
1  large onion
1  large green pepper
2/3  c. boiling water
2/3  c. dry oats

      Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Chop onion and green pepper. Combine ingredients and mix together. Put in baking dish and bake one hour. Glaze meat loaf with ketchup.
