FRESH RAISIN PIE

Filling:
1  stick melted margarine
1 1/2  c. sugar
3  eggs
1/2  tsp. cloves
1/2  tsp. cinnamon
1/2  tsp. allspice
2  Tbsp. vinegar
1  c. chopped pecans
1  c. raisins
1  tsp. vanilla

      Mix together all ingredients well.  Pour into an unbaked pie shell and bake at 325 degrees; for 30 minutes or until done.
Pie Crust:
1 1/2  c. flour (self-rising)
2  Tbsp. Crisco
2  Tbsp. cold water

      Mix together and roll out to make crust.  Place on top of pie filling.
