CRACKER JACK COOKIES


2  sticks oleo (1 c.), softened
1  c. white sugar
1  c. lightly packed brown sugar (when measuring, spoon it lightly in cup)
2  eggs
2  tsp. vanilla
1 1/2  c. flour
1  tsp. baking powder
1  tsp. soda
2  c. old-fashioned oatmeal
1  c. coconut
2  c. Rice Krispies
1  c. chopped nuts

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees;.  Thoroughly cream together oleo and sugars; add eggs and vanilla.  Mix well.  Sift together flour, baking powder and soda.  Combine flour and egg mixture; add oatmeal, coconut, cereal and nuts and mix well.  Drop by heaping teaspoonfuls about 2 inches apart on greased baking sheet.  Bake for about 10 minutes or until brown.  Remove from baking sheet immediately.  Makes 6 dozen.
