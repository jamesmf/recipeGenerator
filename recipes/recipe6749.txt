VEGETABLE LASAGNA
(Serves 9)  

3  c. grated zucchini
3  c. grated carrots
1  Tbsp. olive oil
3  large eggs
1  c. grated Parmesan
2 (10 oz.)  pkg. frozen spinach, chopped
2 1/2  c. spaghetti sauce
1/2  lb. lasagna noodles
8  oz. shredded Provolone cheese

      Saut zucchini and carrots separately in 1/2 tablespoon olive oil each until tender-crisp.  Combine eggs and Parmesan in 3 bowls.  Stir in 1/3 egg mixture separately into spinach, carrots and zucchini.  Spoon 2/3 cup sauce into 9-inch square baking dish. Place half of the noodles over the spaghetti sauce.  Spread spinach into dish, then top with 1/3 Provolone, then carrots, top with 1/3 Provolone, then zucchini and top with 1/3 Provolone.  Top with remaining noodles and spaghetti sauce.  Cover dish with aluminum foil.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes.  Remove foil and sprinkle some Provolone on top.  Bake 10 minutes longer.  Cool before cutting.
