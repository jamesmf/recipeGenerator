BARBECUED MEAT BALLS


1  c. bread crumbs
1/2  c. milk
1  lb. ground beef
1  tsp. salt
  pepper
1/2  c. chopped onion

 
Sauce:
1 1/2  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/4  c. vinegar
1  Tbsp. sugar
1/2  c. ketchup
1/2  c. water
1/4  c. green pepper
1/2  c. onion, chopped

	Mix bread crumbs, milk, ground beef, salt, pepper and onion. Roll into walnut-size balls. Place in baking dish.
	 Make sauce and pour over meat balls. Bake, uncovered, 45 minutes at 375 degrees;.
