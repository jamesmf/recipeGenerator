RED SNAPPER STEAKS


2  lb. red snapper steaks or other fish steaks (fresh or frozen)
1/2  c. melted fat or oil
1/4  c. lemon juice
2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/2  tsp. white pepper
  dash of liquid hot pepper sauce
  paprika

      Thaw frozen steaks.  Cut into serving size portions and place in well-greased, hinged wire grill.  Combine remaining ingredients except paprika.  Baste fish with sauce and sprinkle with paprika. Cook about 4 inches from moderately hot coals for 8 minutes.  Baste with sauce and sprinkle with paprika.  Turn and cook for 7 to 10 minutes longer or until fish flakes easily when tested with a fork. Serves 6.
