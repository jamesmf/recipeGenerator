HAWAIIAN YUM YUM


2  c. mashed sweet potatoes
1  c. sugar
1  egg, beaten
1  c. milk
1/2  tsp. salt
1  tsp. vanilla
1/2  c. coconut

      Mix ingredients.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes.
Topping:
1 small  can crushed pineapple (with juice)
1 small  jar red cherries (with juice)
1  c. sugar
3  Tbsp. cornstarch

      Mix.  Cook until thickened.  Cool.  Pour over baked sweet potato mixture.
