SALMON BAKE


1 large  can salmon, drained and flaked
1  can French onion rings
1  Tbsp. lemon juice
1/2  c. sour cream
2  eggs, beaten
  salt and pepper to taste

	Preheat oven to 350 degrees;. Mix all ingredients together and place in casserole that has been sprayed with vegetable oil.  Bake for 35 minutes or until bubbling.
