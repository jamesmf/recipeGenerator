EASY POTATO SOUP


1  (16 oz.) bag frozen hash brown potatoes
1  c. onion, chopped
1 (14 oz.)  can chicken broth
3  c. water (or less for thicker soup)
1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can cream of celery soup
1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can cream of chicken soup
2  c. milk
  salt and pepper to taste
1  c. chopped ham (optional)

	Combine hash brown potatoes, onion, chicken broth and water in a large pot and simmer for 30 minutes.  Stir in remaining ingredients. Heat thoroughly.
