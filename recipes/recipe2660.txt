WEDDING PUNCH


2 1/2  c. pineapple juice, chilled
1  pt. lime, lemon or raspberry sherbet
1  pt. vanilla ice cream
1 (12 oz.)  bottle ginger ale or 7-Up

      Combine  pineapple juice, sherbet and 1/2 of ice cream. Beat until  smooth.  Add 7-Up or ginger ale.  Spoon remaining ice cream into punch.  Serve immediately.  Yields 14 (1/2 cup) servings.
