BAKED CABBAGE


1  medium cabbage
2  eggs, lightly beaten
6  Tbsp. melted butter
3  Tbsp. heavy cream
1  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
1/2  c. bread crumbs

      Preheat  oven  to  375 degrees;.  Grease  a  2-quart casserole. Cook cabbage  in boiling, salted water for 10 minutes.  Drain and  let cool;  chop  finely.  In  mixing bowl, blend eggs, 3 tablespoons butter, cream, salt and pepper;  stir in cabbage. Turn into casserole.  Blend remaining butter with bread crumbs and sprinkle over cabbage.  Bake for 30 minutes.  Serves 6.
