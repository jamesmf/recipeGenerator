COPPER PENNY SALAD


4  cans carrots
1  c. green onions
1  c. chopped bell pepper

 
Dressing:
1  c. tomato soup
1/2  c. salad oil
3/4  c. vinegar
1  c. sugar
1  tsp. dry mustard
1/4  c. Worcestershire sauce

      Heat all dressing ingredients together.  Let cool.  Pour over vegetables and refrigerate overnight.
