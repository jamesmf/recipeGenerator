CHRISTMAS BONBONS


1  can sweetened condensed milk
2  lb. powdered sugar
1  c. oleo or butter
1  pkg. (7 oz.) flaked coconut
2  c. chopped nuts
1  c. glace cherries
4  pkg. (8 oz.) semi-sweet chocolate

      Combine milk, powdered sugar and oleo.  Mix until smooth and creamy.  Add coconut, walnuts and cherries; mix thoroughly.  Chill 1 hour.  Form into small balls.  Chill another 2 hours.  Melt chocolate.  Dip individually into chocolate.  Place on waxed paper to cool.  Chill in refrigerator.  Makes about 150 pieces.
      Can use any dipping chocolate.
