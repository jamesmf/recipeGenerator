SWISS STEAK


2  tbsp. flour
1  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
2  lb. round steak
1  tbsp. oil
1/2  c. minced onion
1/2  c. chopped celery
1/2  c. chopped green pepper
1 (14 -1/2 oz.)  can chopped tomatoes, drained
2  c. water
2  tsp. sugar

      Cut steak into serving sized pieces.  Dip in flour, salt and pepper and brown in oil.  Combine vegetables, water and sugar and pour over steak.  Cover; reduce heat to 200 degrees; and simmer 1 to 1 1/2 hours.  Thicken sauce for gravy (1 1/2 c. water mixed with 4 tbsp. flour).
       If preferred, place in casserole dish and cover with foil. Bake at 325 degrees; for 3 hours.
