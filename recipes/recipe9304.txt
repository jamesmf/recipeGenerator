CHICKEN TENDERS


1/4  c. (1/2  stick) butter
1  egg
1  c. milk
1/2  sleeve club crackers, crushed
2  tsp. garlic powder
1  tsp. paprika
1  tsp. parsley
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. pepper
16  oz. Kraft Three Cheese, shredded*
10  to 12 chicken tenders

      *This cheese mixture comes in a can (like Parmesan cheese).
      Line a cookie sheet with aluminum foil and spray with non-stick vegetable spray.
      Melt butter in a shallow bowl and set aside.  In a small bowl, beat egg and milk together and set aside.  Mix together:  crackers, garlic powder, paprika, parsley, salt and pepper.  Put mixture on wax paper.  Put cheese on another piece of wax paper.  Soak chicken tenders in egg mixture, then roll in crackers.  Dip one side only in melted butter.  Place chicken in the cheese, butter side down. Place chicken, cheese side up, on prepared cookie sheet.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 25 minutes or until golden brown.
