QUICHE LORRAINE


  9-inch pie shell
1  lb. bacon, fried to crisp
1  c. Cheddar cheese
1 medium onion, chopped or 1  bunch green onions
1  medium bell pepper, chopped
4  eggs
1  pt. half and half
1 small  can sliced mushrooms
1  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper

      Crumble bacon; add bacon, cheese, onion, pepper and mushrooms in pie shell. Beat eggs, half and half, mushrooms, salt and pepper. Pour into pie shell. Bake at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes to 1 hour, until done.
