COWBOY STEW


1 1/2  lb. ground beef
  salt and pepper to taste
1  medium onion, chopped
2  c. water
4  medium potatoes, cubed
1 (15 oz.)  can Ranch Style beans
1  can tomato soup
1 (12 oz.)  can whole kernel corn
1 (4 oz.)  can chopped green chilies
1 (16 oz.)  can whole tomatoes (undrained)

	Combine beef, onion, salt and pepper in a large heavy pot. Cook over medium heat until meat is cooked. Add water and potatoes to meat; cover and cook until potatoes are done. Add remaining ingredients and heat thoroughly.
