SPINACH SALAD WITH HONEY-DIJON DRESSING


6  cups torn spinach
6  cups torn red leaf lettuce
3/4  cup thinly sliced radishes
3  Tbsp. white wine vinegar
1  Tbsp. water
1  Tbsp. salad oil
1  Tbsp. Dijon mustard
1  Tbsp. honey

      Combine first three ingredients in a large bowl; toss well. Combine vinegar and remaining ingredients and stir well. Drizzle over salad and toss well.
