EASY BAKED CHICKEN


1  (3 lb.) chicken, cut up
10  oz. (1 can) soup, cream of mushroom
1  c. sour cream
1/4  c. water
  salt (to taste)
  pepper (to taste)
2  c. rice OR noodles

      Rinse chicken and pat dry.  Arrange in a 9x13-inch baking dish.  Mix together soup and sour cream.  Spoon mixture over chicken.  Drizzle water over chicken mixture in dish.  Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
      Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour or until chicken is tender.
      Serve over hot cooked rice or noodles.
