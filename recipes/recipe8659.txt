SLOPPY JOES


2  lb. lean hamburger
1/2  c. onions, chopped
1/2  c. green pepper, finely chopped (optional)
1/2  c. ketchup
2  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
2  Tbsp. mustard
1 small  can tomato sauce
  hamburger buns

	In a large skillet, fry hamburger meat until no longer pink. Add onions and pepper and cook until tender. Add remaining ingredients and simmer 15 to 20 minutes. Serve on hamburger buns (optional: with slices of onions).
