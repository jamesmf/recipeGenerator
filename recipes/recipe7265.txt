GREEN ENCHILADAS


2  lb. ground beef
  onion
8  oz. Velveeta cheese
1 small  can evaporated milk
1/2  pkg. green onion powdered chip mix
1 small  can chopped green chili peppers
1 small  jar chopped pimento
2  cans cream of chicken soup
1  pkg. sauted corn tortillas

      Brown the ground beef and onion in skillet. Mix separately the cheese, milk, chip mix, chili peppers, pimento and chicken soup. Layer the tortillas on the bottom of 9 x 13 Pyrex baking dish. Add mixture, then meat. Top with grated cheese. Bake 20 minutes at 350 degrees; until bubbly hot.
