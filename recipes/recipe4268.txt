BRITISH LIGHT CARROT SOUP


1/2  lb. carrots, chopped
1  large onion, chopped
1  stalk celery, chopped
1  Tbsp. butter
  dash of ginger/cayenne (carefully)
2 1/2  c. chicken stock
1 1/4  c. milk
  chopped parsley

      Sauté onion and celery in butter. Add carrots and spices. Sauté a bit longer. Add stock. Simmer 25 minutes. Cool. Put in blender. Return to pot and add milk when reheating. Sprinkle with chopped parsley.
