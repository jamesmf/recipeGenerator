TATER TOT TACO SALAD


4  c. frozen tater tots
1  lb. ground beef
1  pkg. taco seasoning
1  c. grated/shredded cheddar cheese
1 1/2  c. shredded lettuce
1  large tomato, chopped
4  Tbsp. taco sauce (or more)
1/2 to 3/4  c. sour cream

      Bake tater tots as directed on package. Cook meat; drain. Stir in taco seasoning. In serving dish, layer tater tots, meat, cheese, tomatoes, lettuce, taco sauce, and sour cream.
