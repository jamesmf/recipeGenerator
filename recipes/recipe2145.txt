FUDGE


5  c. sugar
1 1/2  sticks butter
1 large  can Pet milk
1 (7 oz.)  jar marshmallow cream
1  (12 oz.) bag chocolate chips
1 to 2  c. chopped pecans

      Mix sugar, butter and milk.  Boil for 8 minutes.  Remove from heat.  Add marshmallow cream and chocolate chips.  Stir until melted.  Add nuts and blend well.  Pour into a large, greased dish. Cool and slice.
