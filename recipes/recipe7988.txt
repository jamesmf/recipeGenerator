EASY BAKED HALIBUT


1  c. chopped onion
1  c. Miracle Whip
1  lb. halibut fillets or salmon
8  oz. shredded Cheddar cheese

      Grease 8 x 8 inch baking dish.  Cover with onions then Miracle Whip.  Place fillets on top.  Cover with cheese.  Cover with foil.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 1/2 hour.  Remove foil and bake another 10 minutes or till cheese is melted.
