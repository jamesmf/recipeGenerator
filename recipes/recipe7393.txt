ROAST CHICKEN AND POTATOES


3 to 4  cloves garlic, chopped
1/2  stick butter
  chicken bouillon
  chicken pieces
1 large  can tomato sauce
  water
  potatoes, peeled and cut in half

      Mix tomato sauce in small amount of water in a bowl.  Coat potatoes with sauce and place in roaster.  Sprinkle garlic, bouillon and dabs of butter on top of potatoes.  Do the same with chicken.  Cover chicken with remaining sauce.  Bake 1 1/2 hours, covered, at 350 degrees;.
