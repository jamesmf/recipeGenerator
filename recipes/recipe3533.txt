HOT DIP


8  oz. cream cheese
1  can chili, no beans
  onion, chopped
  green pepper, chopped
  sliced ripe olives
2  c. shredded cheese (Monterey Jack and Cheddar)

      In 9 x 13 inch glass pan, spread in order listed. Heat in microwave or 300 degrees; oven until cheese melts.  Serve with tortilla chips.
