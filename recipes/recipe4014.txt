FROZEN CUCUMBERS


2  quarts cucumbers, peeled and sliced
2  onions, peeled and sliced
1 1/2  c. sugar
1/2  c. vinegar
2  Tbsp. salt

      Soak cucumbers and onions in salt with water to cover for 2 hours.  Drain; do not rinse.  Add sugar and vinegar.  Stir well; put in small containers and freeze.
