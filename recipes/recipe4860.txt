BASIC WHITE SAUCE


2  Tbsp. butter
2  Tbsp. flour
1  c. soup stock
4  Tbsp. cream
  salt and pepper

      Heat butter.  Blend in flour until smooth.  Gradually add soup stock, stirring constantly.  Bring to boiling point.  Cook for 3 more minutes.  Add cream and seasoning.
      Mushroom Sauce:  Add 1/2 cup chopped cooked mushrooms to each cup of white sauce.
      Dill Sauce:  Add one tablespoon fresh chopped dill to each cup of white sauce.  Do not boil after adding dill as it will lose its color and aroma.
