CRANBERRY-ORANGE SAUCE


2  c. fresh cranberries
2/3  c. sugar
2/3  c. orange juice
2  Tbsp. grated orange rind
  Pinch of cinnamon
  Pinch of nutmeg

      Combine all ingredients together in a saucepan.  Place over medium heat, and bring to a boil, stirring often.  Reduce heat to low, and simmer, stirring often, until thick (about 10 minutes). Remove from heat and cool.  Serve warm or cold.
