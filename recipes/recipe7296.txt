TANDOORI CHICKEN BREASTS
(Makes 4 Servings)  

1  lb. chicken breast (no skin, no bone, ready to cook)
1/4  c. yogurt (skim milk)
1  clove garlic, crushed
1/2  tsp. paprika
1/2  tsp. turmeric
1/2  tsp. cumin
1/2  tsp. ginger (ground)
  dash of cayenne
  dash of ground pepper

      PREPARE CHICKEN AND REFRIGERATE WITH SEASONED PASTE AT LEAST 2 HOURS OR OVERNIGHT!
      Wash chicken and pat dry. Cut breast in half crosswise and put between pieces of wax paper or plastic wrap. Pound chicken gently with side of a meat mallet or bottom of a heavy glass to flatten each piece until 1/4 to 1/2-inch thick.
       Make tandoori paste by combining yogurt and all seasonings in a small bowl. Brush chicken pieces with seasoned paste. Wrap in plastic wrap and refrigerate at least 2 hours or overnight.
      Prepare a charcoal grill, letting coals burn until ashen. Oil rack or prepare with a nonstick cooking spray. Grill 2 to 4 minutes on each side, just until chicken is cooked through.
