SPICY KISSES


1  egg white
1  1/2 dashes salt
1/4  c. sugar
1/8  tsp. cloves
1/8  tsp. nutmeg
1  tsp. cinnamon
1  c. finely chopped walnuts
  walnut halves

      Beat egg white with salt until stiff.  Gradually beat in sugar mixed with spices.  Fold in nuts.  Drop from teaspoonful onto well-greased cookie sheet.  Top with walnut halves.  Bake at 250 degrees; for 35 to 40 minutes.
