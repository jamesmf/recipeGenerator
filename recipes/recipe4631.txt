BLUEBERRY SALAD


3  small pkg blueberry or blackberry gelatin
2  cans blueberries
3-1/2  c liquid, juice from berries and water
3  oz pkg cream cheese, softened
1-1/2  c hot water
2  pkgs Dream Whip

      Heat liquid  to a boil and dissolve 2 boxes of  gelatin. Fold in berries.  Pour into 9x13 pan.  Chill.  Prepare last box of gelatin in hot water.  Fold in cream cheese.  Cool.  Prepare Dream Whip.  Fold Dream Whip into gelatin mix.  Spread on chilled gelatin.
