POPEYE'S SPINACH


4 (10 oz.)  pkg. frozen, chopped spinach, thawed
1 (2 3/4 oz.)  box dry onion soup mix
1  pt. sour cream
1  c. seasoned bread crumbs
4  eggs, beaten
  Swiss cheese, shredded

      Drain the spinach in a sieve; do not press out water.  Combine the spinach, soup mix, sour cream and bread crumbs; mix well. Blend in the eggs.  Pour into a greased 2 1/2-quart casserole.  Top with cheese.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes.  Makes 10 to 12 servings.
