EGGNOG COOKIES


1/2  c. shortening
1  c. brown sugar
1/2  c. granulated sugar
1  tsp. vanilla
1  egg
1  c. eggnog
2 1/2  c. flour
1/2  tsp. soda
1/2  tsp. salt
1  tsp. nutmeg
1/2  c. finely ground nuts

      Cream shortening, sugars, vanilla, egg and eggnog.  Sift together flour, soda, salt and nutmeg; then add to sugar mixture. Add nuts.  Drop by spoonfuls on cookie sheet.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 10 to 12 minutes.
