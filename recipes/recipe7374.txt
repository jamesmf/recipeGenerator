QUICK CHINESE PEPPER STEAK


1  lb. steak
2  Tbsp. oil
1  medium onion
1  medium green pepper
3/4  c. water
4  Tbsp. soy sauce
1/2  tsp. ground ginger
3  c. cooked rice
1  packet au jus gravy mix

      Brown meat, onion and green pepper in hot oil.  Add remaining ingredients (except rice).  Cook for about 5 to 10 minutes.  Serve over rice.
