NORTHERN POTATOES SALAD


8  potatoes
6  eggs
2  stalks celery
4  radishes
1/2  onion
  pepper (optional)
4  Tbsp. Miracle Whip
1  Tbsp. mustard
  a little milk
1  tsp. sugar

	Cut up potatoes. Put in bowl. Cut celery and put in bowl. Cut radishes up. Put in bowl. Dice onion up. Put in bowl. Dice the eggs up. Put in bowl. Mix Miracle Whip, mustard, milk and sugar together. Pour over salad. Mix and slice eggs on top.
