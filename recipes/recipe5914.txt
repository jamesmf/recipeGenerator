SPINACH SALAD WITH ORANGES


1 (10 oz.)  pkg. fresh spinach, torn
1 (11 oz.)  can mandarin oranges, drained
1  c. sliced fresh mushrooms
3  bacon strips, cooked and crumbled

 
Dressing:
3  Tbsp. ketchup
2  Tbsp. cider vinegar
1 1/2  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/4  c. sugar
2  Tbsp. chopped onion
1/8  tsp. salt
  dash of pepper
1/2  c. vegetable oil

      In a large salad bowl, toss the spinach, oranges, mushrooms and bacon. Set aside.
      In a blender or food processor, combine the ketchup, vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, sugar, onion, salt and pepper. Cover and process until smooth. While processing, gradually add oil in a steady stream. Serve with salad. Yield: 4 to 6 servings (about 3/4 cup dressing).
