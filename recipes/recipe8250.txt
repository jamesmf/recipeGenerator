SWEET HOT AND SOUR MEATBALLS


1/2  c. refrigerated or frozen egg product, thawed
1/2  c. fine dry bread crumbs
1/2  c. onion, finely chopped
1/4  c. milk
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. black pepper
1  lb. pork sausage
1  lb. ground beef
3/4  c. apple jelly
1/3  c. spicy brown mustard
1/3  c. apple juice
1 1/2  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
  few dashes bottled hot pepper sauce

	In a large bowl, combine egg product, bread crumbs, onion, milk, salt and pepper. Add sausage and beef; mix well. Shape into 48 meatballs. Place meatballs in a shallow baking pan. Bake, uncovered, in a 375 degrees; oven 30 minutes or until done. Remove from oven; drain.
	Meanwhile, in a large saucepan, stir together jelly, mustard, juice, Worcestershire sauce and hot pepper sauce. Heat and stir until jelly melts and mixture bubbles. Add meatballs, stirring gently to coat. Cook 3 to 5 minutes or until sauce thickens slightly and meatballs are coated. Transfer meatballs and sauce to a serving dish. Cover tightly.
