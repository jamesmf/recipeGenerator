MEXICAN DELIGHT


1  lb. ground beef
4  oz. can green chiles, chopped
1 small  jar picante (mild or hot)
  Doritos
  American cheese slices

      Brown meat and drain. Stir in the chiles and simmer 10 minutes. Layer Doritos chips on top of meat mixture. Layer American cheese slices on top of Doritos and let cheese melt. If any meat mixture is left, remove cheese and Doritos. Add fresh chips and cheese. May be frozen or refrigerated.
