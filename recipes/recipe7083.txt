BRUNCH EGGS


12  pieces bread, cut in donut shapes
  Old English sharp Cheddar cheese pieces
10  oz. broccoli, chopped and cooked
2  c. ham, diced
3  c. milk
6  eggs
2  Tbsp. onion, minced
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. dry mustard

      Place scraps of bread (leftovers from donut shapes) in the bottom of a 13 x 9 pan. Then layer cheese, broccoli and ham. Mix together the last 5 ingredients and soak each piece of bread in mixture before laying them on top. Pour remaining mixture over the top. Cover and refrigerate overnight. Bake at 325 degrees; for 1 1/2 hours.
