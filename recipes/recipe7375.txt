EASY PEPPER STEAK


1  lb. beef stir-fry
2  Tbsp. olive oil
1  medium onion, slivered
1  large pepper, slivered
1  can water chestnuts
2  jars beef/mushroom gravy
3 to 4  c. Minute rice

      Brown beef and a little garlic powder in olive oil until done.  Pour in jars of gravy and mix well.  Put in peppers, onions and chestnuts and simmer for about 10 to 15 minutes until veggies are soft.  Serve over rice.
