CHOCOLATE GRAVY


2 heaping  tsp. Cocoa
2  c. Sugar
1/2  stick Butter
4 heaping  tsp. Self-rising flour
2  c. Milk
1  tsp. Vanilla

      Mix all ingredients, except vanilla.  Stir continuously with a wire whisk over medium heat until slightly thick. Remove from heat and stir in vanilla. Serve hot over biscuits.
