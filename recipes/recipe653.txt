CREAM CHEESE FROSTING


1  oz. imitation cream cheese
1  tsp. skim milk
1  Tbsp. confectioners sugar
  dash of salt
1/2  tsp. Sweet 'N Low
1  drop vanilla extract

      Cream the cream cheese and milk.  Add sugar, salt, Sweet 'N Low and vanilla.  Blend well.  Makes 3 tablespoons.  Contains approximately 8 calories per teaspoon.
