SCRAMBLED TOFU


2  Tbsp. oil
3  Tbsp. soy sauce
1/4  tsp. cayenne
3/4  c. onions, chopped
2  lb. tofu, crumbled
1/4  tsp. garlic powder
1  Tbsp. nutritional yeast

      Mix all ingredients except oil together well.  Warm skillet. Pour in oil.  Pour in all ingredients.  Scramble until brown. Serve hot with toast.
