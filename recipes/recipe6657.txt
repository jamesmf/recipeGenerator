PIEROGI ONE-DISH


12  potatoes, boiled and mashed
1  c. cheddar cheese, shredded
1 1/2  c. butter or margarine
1  onion, minced
2  c. cooked noodles (kluski)

      After mashing potatoes, mix in cheddar cheese.  Saut√© onion in butter.  In a well-greased casserole dish, place a layer of noodles, potatoes, then onions.   Repeat with second layer, cover and bake at 325 degrees; for 25 minutes.  Sour cream may be added when serving.
