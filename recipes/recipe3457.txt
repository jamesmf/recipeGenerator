SPECIAL CHEESE RING


1  lb. sharp Cheddar cheese, grated
1  c. pecans, chopped
  black pepper
  dash of cayenne pepper
1  c. mayonnaise
1  small onion, grated
  strawberry preserves

      Mix cheese, pecans, mayonnaise, black pepper, cayenne pepper and onion.  Mold into desired shape.  Place in the refrigerator until chilled.  When ready to serve, fill center with strawberry preserves.
