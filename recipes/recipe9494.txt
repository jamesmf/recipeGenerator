SWEDISH MEATBALLS


3  lb. hamburger meat
1  c. bread crumbs
2  eggs
1  medium chopped onion
1  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
  dash of chilli powder
  salt, garlic salt, pepper, oregano and cayenne pepper (if desired)

 
Sauce:
3 (10 oz.)  jars chilli sauce
3  lb. jar grape jelly

	Mix all ingredients and roll into small-size balls. Brown in skillet. Cook until slightly brown.
	To prepare sauce: Mix equal parts of chilli sauce and grape jelly. Heat slowly. Add meatballs. Keep hot until ready to serve. Makes a large crock-pot full.
