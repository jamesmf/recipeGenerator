COCKTAIL MEATBALLS


1  lb. lean ground beef
1  egg
2  Tbsp. water
1/2  c. bread crumbs
3  Tbsp. minced onions
1 (8 oz.)  can jellied cranberry sauce
3/4  c. chili sauce
1  Tbsp. brown sugar
1 1/2  tsp. lemon juice

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees;. In large bowl, mix together the ground beef, egg, water, bread crumbs and minced onion. Roll into small meatballs. Bake in preheated oven for 20 to 25 minutes, turning once. In a slow cooker or large saucepan over low heat, blend the cranberry sauce, chili sauce, brown sugar and lemon juice. Add meatballs and simmer for 1 hour before serving.
