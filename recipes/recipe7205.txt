CHICKEN CONTINENTAL


3 to 4  lb. chicken, cut up
1/3  c. flour
1/4  c. oleo
10 1/2  oz. cream of chicken soup
1 1/3  c. water
1 1/3  c. rice (Minute)
2 1/2  Tbsp. grated onion
1/2  salt
  dash of pepper
1  Tbsp. parsley
1/8  tsp. thyme
1  tsp. celery flakes

      Roll chicken in flour; brown in oleo.  Remove chicken; stir in butter, soup water and seasonings.  Bring to boil.  Spread uncooked rice into shallow baking dish.  Pour all but 1/3 cup of mixture over rice.  Add chicken and the rest of mixture.  Bake, uncovered, at 375 degrees; for 1 hour.
