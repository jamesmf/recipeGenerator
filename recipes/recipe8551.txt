MEATLOAF (BARBECUE STYLE)


1 1/2  lb. ground Beef
1  c. fresh Bread Crumbs
1  Onion, finely chopped
1  Egg, beaten
1 8  oz. can Tomato Sauce
3  tbsp. Brown Sugar
1 1/2  tsp. Salt
1/4  tsp. Pepper
1  tsp. Worcestershire Sauce
1/2  c. Water
3  tbsp. Vinegar
2  tbsp. prepared Mustard

      Mix together beef, bread crumbs, onion, beaten egg, salt, pepper, and 1/2 can tomato sauce.  Form into loaf and put in shallow pan about 7x10 inches.  Combine the rest of sauce with all other ingredients.  Pour over loaf.  Bake in moderate oven at 350 degrees for 1 hour and 15 minutes. Baste occasionally.  Makes 4 to 6 servings.
