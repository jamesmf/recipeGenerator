CHICKEN/STUFFING CASSEROLE


4  C. chicken
8  oz. Stove Top stuffing mix
1  C. chicken broth
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  C. sour cream
1/2  C. margarine
1  egg, beaten

      Cook and dice chicken; put in 9 x 13-inch pan.
      Mix together the soup and sour cream; pour over chicken.
      Then blend dressing mix, margarine, egg and broth; spread over sour cream/soup mixture.
      Bake at 350 degrees covered, for 45 minutes.  Uncover and bake 15 more minutes.
      Serves 8 - 10 people.
