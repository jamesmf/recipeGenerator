CANOPIES


1 (1/2 oz.)  jar sliced mushrooms, drained
1/4  c. chopped onion
1/2  c. shredded Swiss cheese
2  Tbsp. chopped black olives
1/2  c. Longhorn cheese
1/3  c. Miracle Whip salad dressing
2/3  c. chopped summer sausage
1  loaf cocktail rye bread

	Mix all together (can make ahead) and put on bread.  Broil until cheese melts.  Serve warm.
