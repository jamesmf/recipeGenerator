GUACAMOLE NO. 1


2  avocados
1/2  tsp. chili powder
1/4  tsp. cumin
1/4  tsp. coriander
1  tsp. lemon juice
1  Tbsp. onion, minced
1/4  tsp. sea salt
2  tsp. picante sauce
1/4  tsp. garlic powder

      Process avocados in food processor until smooth.  In bowl, stir remaining ingredients into avocados.  Refrigerate until ready to serve.  Makes 1 cup.
