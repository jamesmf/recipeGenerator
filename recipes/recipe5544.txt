PUDDING FRUIT SALAD


1  can 20 oz. pineapple tid bits (drained)
1  can 16 oz. sliced peaches (slice again smaller pieces) drained
1  can (small) mandarin oranges (drained)
2  Granny Smith or Jonathan apples cut up little pieces peeling on
1 large  box (sugar free) instant vanilla pudding
1 1/2  c. milk
3/4  c. sour cream
1/2  can frozen orange juice
1 1/2  c. miniature marshmallows

      Blend pudding with milk and orange juice until it thickens, add sour cream mix well. Stir in fruit, let set for 2 or 3 hours or over night.
