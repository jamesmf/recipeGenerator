BROCCOLI DIP


1 (10 oz.)  pkg. chopped broccoli
4  stalks celery, chopped
1  onion, chopped
1  roll Kraft garlic cheese
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  can cream of celery soup
1  stick butter

      Saut√© onions and celery in butter. Add broccoli and cook for a few minutes until broccoli is tender. Add garlic cheese and let it melt. Add cream of mushroom and celery soups. Salt and pepper to taste. Cook on low until hot. Serve with chips.
