ITALIAN CHILI


1  stick butter
1  lb. turkey burger
1/2  lb. hot sausage
1  onion
1  green pepper
1/2  tsp. cumin
1  tsp. Louisiana hot sauce
  dash of Tabasco
1  qt. hot salsa
46  oz. can V-8 juice
1  lb. penne pasta (uncooked)

      Fry the burger, sausage, onion and green pepper in the butter.  When cooked, add cumin, hot sauce, Tabasco and salsa. Simmer for about 30 minutes.  Add V-8 juice.  Bring to boil and add pasta.  Cook until pasta is just tender.  Salt and pepper to taste.
