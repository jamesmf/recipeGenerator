NO FUSS BEEF LASAGNA


1  lb. ground beef
1/4  tsp. salt
1 (26 oz. to 30 oz.)  jar spaghetti sauce
1 (14 1/2 oz.)  can Italian diced tomatoes
1/4  tsp. total red and/or black pepper
1  carton Ricotta cheese
1/4  c. Parmesan cheese
1 1/2  c. grated Mozzarella cheese
1  egg, beaten
10  Lasagna noodles

      Brown meat (8 to 10 minutes).  Mix spaghetti sauce, tomatoes, salt and pepper.  In medium bowl, beat egg.  Add Ricotta and Parmesan cheeses.  Spread 2 cups of meat and sauce on 13 x 9-inch pan.  Arrange 5 noodles one at a time, pressing into meat mixture. Spread cheese mixture on noodles.  Sprinkle with Mozzarella cheese and top with 1 1/2 cups meat sauce.  Arrange another layer of noodles, pressing into meat.  Sprinkle Mozzarella cheese on top. Tent foil lightly.  Bake 45 minutes at approximately 350 degrees;. Remove from oven and let sit 15 minutes.  Cut into 3-inch squares. Serve.
