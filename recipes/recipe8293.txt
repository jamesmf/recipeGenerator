ITALIAN ZUCCHINI AND SAUSAGE


1  lb. pork sausage, cooked and crumbled
1  medium onion, thinly sliced
5  small zucchini, cut into 1/4-inch slices
1  clove garlic, mashed
1 (8 oz.)  can tomato sauce
1  tsp. dried sweet basil leaves
1/2  tsp. dried oregano leaves
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  c. Cheddar cheese, shredded
  grated Parmesan cheese

	Pan-fry sausage until browned and crumbled. Add onion, zucchini and garlic; cook 5 minutes. Add tomato sauce and seasonings; cover and simmer 25 minutes or until zucchini is tender. Sprinkle with cheeses. Serves 6.
