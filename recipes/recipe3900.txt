CHEESE STRAWS


2  sticks margarine
2 (8 oz.)  pkg. sharp cheese
2  c. flour
1/2  tsp. red pepper
1/2  tsp. salt

      Let margarine get room temperature. Grate cheese. Mix all ingredients, may have to add a little more flour to work with it without it sticking to hands. Put through cookie press and bake at 300 degrees; for about 12 minutes. Don't bake until brown, they will be burnt.
