HOOTENANNY FRAPP


1 1/2  c. sugar
3 1/2  c. water
1 (6 oz.)  can frozen orange juice concentrate, thawed (undiluted)
2 (10 oz.)  pkg. frozen sliced strawberries (in syrup), thawed
1 (8 oz.)  can unsweetened crushed pineapple (undrained)
  ginger ale, chilled

	Combine sugar and water in a large saucepan; cook over medium heat until sugar dissolves, stirring constantly. Remove from heat; stir in orange juice concentrate, strawberries and pineapple. Pour mixture into 13 x 9 x 2-inch pan. Freeze until firm.
	To serve, thaw at room temperature. For each serving, spoon 1/2 cup of fruit mixture into a glass. Add 1 cup chilled ginger ale. Stir until slushy; refreeze remainder of fruit mixture. Use as needed. Yield: 18 servings.
