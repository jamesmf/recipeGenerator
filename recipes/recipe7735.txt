CHICKEN BROCCOLI DIVAN


1  (10 oz.) frozen broccoli spears, cooked and drained
1  can cream of broccoli soup
1/3  c. milk
1  Tbsp. butter, melted
2  Tbsp. dry bread crumbs
1  1/2 cubed cooked chicken
1/2  c. shredded Cheddar cheese

      In a 9-inch pie plate or shallow casserole, arrange broccoli. Top with chicken. Combine soup and milk. Pour over chicken. Sprinkle with cheese. Combine butter and bread crumbs. Sprinkle over cheese. Bake at 450 degrees; for 15 minutes or until hot in center.
