TOMATO RELISH


1 (16 oz.)  can tomatoes with liquid
1/4  c. finely chopped onions
1  tsp. salt
1/4  c. finely cut celery
1/4  c. finely cut sweet green peppers
1  tsp. celery seed
1  Tbsp. white vinegar
  dash of cayenne
  artificial sweetener to substitute for 1 tsp. sugar

      Place tomatoes and liquid into small bowl; cut tomatoes into very small pieces.  Add all remaining ingredients and mix well. Pour into a pint jar; cover tightly.  Store in refrigerator several hours before using.  Serve with hot or cold meats, beans or dried peas.
