CREOLE OKRA


1  onion
1  bell pepper
1  can Ro-Tel tomatoes
2  Tbsp. cooking oil
1  lb. whole okra or 1 box frozen whole okra
1  can stewed tomatoes
  salt and pepper to taste

      Wash and cut okra into 3/4-inch size.  Chop onion and bell pepper.  Saut√© onion and pepper until tender in oil.  Add stewed tomatoes and Ro-Tel tomatoes; add okra.  Stew on low setting in skillet or cook in baking dish in oven until okra is tender. Makes 4 to 6 servings.
