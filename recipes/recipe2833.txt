SPICED PECANS


1 egg white, beaten slightly with 1  Tbsp. water
1  tsp. salt
1  c. sugar, mixed with 1 tsp. cinnamon
1  lb. whole pecans

      Coat pecans with egg white mixture; sprinkle sugar mixture over nuts and toss, being sure to coat each nut.  Bake at 250 degrees; for 1 hour.
      Placed in a pretty container, this also makes a nice hostess gift at Christmastime.
