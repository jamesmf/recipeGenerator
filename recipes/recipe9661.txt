BAKED BREADED PORK CHOPS


3  Tbsp. margarine
1  c. coarse cracker crumbs
3  Tbsp. parmesan cheese
  Salt & Pepper
1/2  tsp. oregano
1  beaten egg
2  Tbsp. milk
6  pork chops

      Coat 9 x 13 inch pan with margarine. Mix dry ingredients. Mix egg and milk. Coat chops with crumbs, then egg mixture, then crumbs. Arrange in pan. Bake at 325 degrees for 30 minutes, turn chops bake 30 minutes longer.
