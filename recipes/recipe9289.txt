TACO BAKE


6  flour tortillas OR 8 (6 or 8 inch) corn tortillas
1  lb. ground beef
1/2  c. milk
10  oz. (1 can) soup, condensed tomato
1  c. salsa
1  c. Cheddar cheese, shredded

      Cut tortillas into 1-inch pieces and set aside.
      In a skillet, cook ground beef over medium heat until browned, stirring to separate meat.  Drain off fat.  Add tortillas pieces, milk, soup, salsa and 1/2 of the cheese.  Spoon into a 2-quart baking dish.
      Cover dish and bake at 400 degrees; for 30 minutes.
      Before serving, sprinkle with rest of the cheese.
