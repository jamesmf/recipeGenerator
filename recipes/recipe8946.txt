HUNGARIAN GOULASH


3 to 4  lb. chuck roast
  salt and pepper
  large chopped onion
4  whole cloves
2  whole bay leaves
2 large  Tbsp. paprika
4  cans Campbell's beef broth
  flour
  canola oil
  wide noodles

      Chop up chuck roast into bite size pieces. Salt and pepper and flour and brown in 2 tablespoons of oil. Add onion and cook until transparent. Add beef broth until it covers meat. Add 2 whole cloves, 2 bay leaves and paprika until soup is red looking, about 2 to 4 tablespoons. Cover and bring to boil, then turn down heat to simmer. Simmer for 2 hours or until roast is tender. Cook wide noodles and serve goulash over the noodles. Makes 6 servings. Preparation time: 2 hours.
       This recipe is adjusted for a large group by adding as much meat as you want and adding enough beef broth to cover the meat.
