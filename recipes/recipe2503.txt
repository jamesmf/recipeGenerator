SHRIMP-STUFFED MUSHROOM CAPS


  large mushroom caps
6  oz. softened cream cheese
1  can broken shrimp, drained (rinse in cold water for fresher taste)
1  small white onion, minced
1/2  tsp. sea salt
  dash of Worcestershire sauce
  dash of cocktail sauce

      Blend last 6 ingredients together and stuff into large mushroom caps.  Yields about 2 cups.
