CHICKEN CASSEROLE


2  c. boned chicken
2  c. cooked rice (1 c. uncooked)
1  can mushroom soup
1  small onion, chopped
  salt and pepper
1/2  c. chopped celery
1  c. mayonnaise
1  Tbsp. lemon juice
1  can sliced chestnuts or mushrooms or both
  small amount of chicken broth or mushroom liquid

      Mix well.  Sprinkle grated cheese or crumble croutons or potato chips on top.  Let set several hours.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 25 minutes.  If casserole is cold allow more time (50 minutes) until hot and bubbly.
