DILL PICKLES


13  c. water
3  c. vinegar
1  c. plain salt

      Bring to a boil the water, salt and vinegar. Pour over medium cucumbers that have been packed into 1/2-gallon jars along with the following: To each jar, place large bunch of fresh dill weed, 3 cloves of garlic, 2 pods hot pepper and 1/4 teaspoon alum. Pour boiling solution over this and seal immediately. Let stand 6 weeks before opening.
