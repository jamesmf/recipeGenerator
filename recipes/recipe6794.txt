PIZZA HOT DISH



2  lb. hamburger
1  onion, chopped
1  tsp. garlic salt
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. pepper
15 1/2  oz. jar Ragu spaghetti sauce
8  oz. can tomato sauce
1/4  tsp. oregano
8  oz. Cheddar cheese soup
  Mozzarella cheese
  mushrooms
8  oz. noodles

      Brown the hamburger with the onion. Add the garlic salt, salt and pepper. Drain. Add sauces and oregano. In kettle, cook noodles according to package directions; drain. Then mix in soup. Layer in casserole 1/2 the noodles, 1/2 the meat, remaining noodles and rest of the meat. Cover and bake at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes. Remove cover; sprinkle with Mozzarella cheese and bake until cheese melts.
