KRAUT SALAD


1 large  can or bag sauerkraut, drained and chopped
1/2  medium onion, chopped
1  carrot, coarsely grated
1  c. celery, chopped
1  c. green pepper, chopped
1  c. sugar
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  c. oil
1/4  c. white vinegar

      Mix sauerkraut, onion, carrot, celery and green pepper together. Mix sugar, salt, oil and vinegar together. Add to vegetables. Let stand overnight or longer. Refrigerate.
