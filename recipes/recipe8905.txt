HOT TAMALE PIE


  Fritos (small size)
  No. 303 can tamales
  onion to taste, chopped
  green pepper to taste, chopped
  cheese, shredded
  chili

      Spray 9 x 5 loaf pan. Put layer of Fritos. Unwrap tamales and place layer of tamales. Add onions, then peppers, then layer of cheese. Heat chili and pour over cheese. Add layer of Fritos. Bake in 350 degrees; oven for 30 minutes.
