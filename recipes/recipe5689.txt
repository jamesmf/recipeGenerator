MACARONI PEA SALAD


2 to 3  c. cooked macaroni or other small pasta
1  small green pepper, chopped (optional)
1  c. celery, diced
1/2  c. onion, chopped
1  can peas, drained
3  hard-boiled eggs, chopped
1  c. cubed cheese
  salt and pepper to taste
  Miracle Whip or mayonnaise (enough to coat all vegetables)

	In a large bowl, mix all ingredients except dressing. Gradually add enough dressing to properly coat vegetables.
