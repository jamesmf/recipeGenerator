PINEAPPLE CHEESE BALLS


2 - 8  ounce pkgs of Cream Cheese
1 - Large  can Crushed Pineapples (drained)
2  - TBSP chopped green pepper
1  - TBSP chopped onion
1 -  cup finely chopped pecans
2  to 3 cups of additional finely chopped pecans

      Mix the first 5 ingredients thoroughly and roll into balls. Roll each ball into the additional pecans and serve with crackers or celery.
      Can be frozen for a quick treat for unexpected guests.
