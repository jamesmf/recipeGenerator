CONGEALED SALAD


2/3  c. sugar
  medium size can crushed pineapple
1  box lemon jello
1  (8 oz.) cream cheese
1  c. chopped celery
1  c. chopped apples
1  c. pecans
1  c. Cool Whip

      Combine sugar and pineapple. Boil 3 minutes. Add lemon jello and set off eye of stove. Add cream cheese. Cool and add rest of ingredients. If you double this recipe, it will make a 9 x 13 pan; otherwise, 6 x 9 or 8 x 8.
