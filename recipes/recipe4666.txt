PAT'S BARBECUE SAUCE


1  diced onion
1  cup catsup
1/2  cup water
2  tsp worcestershire sauce
2  tbsp lemon juice
1/8  tsp celery powder
6  tbsp brown sugar
1/8  tsp minced garlic

      Fry onions first, then add all remaining ingredients.  Let simmer until ready.  Cool before using.  Good on Chicken or added to canned beans.
