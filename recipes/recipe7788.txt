SLOPPY JOES


1 1/2  lb. ground chuck
1/3  c. chopped onion
1/4  c. catsup
1/2  tsp. garlic salt
1/2  tsp. paprika
1/2  c. water
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  Tbsp. mustard
2  Tbsp. vinegar
2 small  cans tomato sauce
  salt and pepper

      Brown beef and onion in small amount of fat. Mix with remaining ingredients and simmer for 30 minutes. Serve on heated hamburger buns. Serves 4 to 5 generously.
