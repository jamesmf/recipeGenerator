BEEF LOG


2  lb. hamburger meat
1  c. water
2  Tbsp. Morton Tender Quick curing salt
1 1/2  tsp. liquid smoke
1/2  tsp. powdered garlic
1  Tbsp. coarse ground pepper
  dash of ground red pepper or cayenne (optional)

      Mix well. Roll in 1/2-inch rolls (this makes about 5 rolls). Wrap tightly in plastic wrap. Store in refrigerator for 24 hours; place on broiler pan. Bake at 300 degrees; for 1 1/2 hours. Cool. Keep refrigerated or frozen.
