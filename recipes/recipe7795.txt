SPEEDY SALMON PATTIES


1 (12 or 14 3/4 oz.)  can salmon, drained
1/3  c. chopped onion
1  egg
5  saltines, crushed
1/2  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/4  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper
2  tsp. butter or margarine

      In a bowl, combine salmon, onion, egg, saltines, Worcestershire sauce, salt and pepper. Shape into 6 patties. In a skillet, fry patties in butter over medium heat for  3 to 4 minutes on each side. Yield: 3 servings.
