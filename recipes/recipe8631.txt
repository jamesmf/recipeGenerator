HASH BROWN HAM CASSEROLE


16  oz. sour cream
10  3/4 oz cream of chicken soup
32  oz. pkg. hash brown southern style potatoes
2  C. cubed ham
8  oz. American cheese, cubed
1/4  C. chopped onion
1  C. milk
2  C. crushed corn flakes
1/2  C. melted butter

      Mix first 7 ingredients in large bowl; spread evenly in 9 x 13-inch pan.
      Mix crushed corn flakes and melted butter together and sprinkle over potato mixture.
      Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.  If not warmed through in 30 minutes, bake until brown on edges.
      Serves 8 - 10 people.
