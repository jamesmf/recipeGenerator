SHRIMP STUFFED PEPPERS


1  tsp. salt
3  cloves garlic
6  green peppers, tops cut off and cleaned
1/2  c. raw rice
1  can cream of mushroom soup
  juice of 1 lemon
  dash of pepper
2  Tbsp. grated onion
2  Tbsp. butter
1  tsp. parsley
1  lb. shrimp, or 2 (5 oz.) cans or 10 to 12 oz. frozen, thawed under running water
1  c. grated Swiss cheese
6  pats of butter
  paprika

      In cooking pot, size to hold 2 quarts of water, put salt, garlic and peppers.  Bring to a boil and boil 10 minutes.   In separate pot, boil 1/2 cup raw rice, drain.  Mix soup, lemon juice, pepper, onion and butter.  Heat, then add rice, parsley and shrimp.  Stuff peppers with shrimp mixture and top with 1 cup grated Swiss cheese, 6 pats of butter and paprika (for color). Bake at 350 degrees; for 40 minutes.
