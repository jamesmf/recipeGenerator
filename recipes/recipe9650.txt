CHICKEN CASSEROLE


2  boxes Uncle Ben's Long Grain & Wild Rice Mix
1  large fryer, cooked & boned
1  can cream of celery soup
1  onion, minced
1 (2 oz.)  jar pimentos
2  cups mayonnaise
1 (8 oz.)  can water chestnuts, diced
  Paprika
  Grated Parmesan cheese

      Cook rice as directed. Add all ingredients except paprika & cheese. Mix thoroughly. Pour into well-greased shallow 3 quart baking dish. Sprinkle with paprika & cheese. Bake at 350 degrees for 30-40 minutes until bubbly.
