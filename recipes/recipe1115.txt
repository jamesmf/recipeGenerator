DEE'S ROUND STEAK


2  lb. round steak
2  Tbsp. vegetable oil
1  tsp. ginger
1/2  tsp. garlic powder
2  c. beef bouillon (broth)
2  Tbsp. cornstarch
2  Tbsp. water
1 1/2  Tbsp. soy sauce
1  green pepper, cut in very thin strips (may be omitted)

      Cut off all fat from meat.  Cut into strips 1/2 x 3-inches or so.  Brown meat in oil; add ginger, garlic and broth.  Bring to a boil.  Lower heat, cover pan and cook 1 hour.  Blend cornstarch with water and soy sauce; stir into pan.  Add green pepper; cook until sauce is thickened and clear.  Serves 6.
