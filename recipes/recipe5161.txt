SPINACH AND ORANGE SALAD


10  c. torn raw spinach
2  c. fresh orange sections
1/2  c. sliced red onion
1 (4 oz.)  pkg. crumbled Feta cheese
1  c. Italian salad dressing mix for fat free dressing

      Toss spinach, oranges, onions and cheese in large bowl.  Add salad dressing and toss lightly.
      Substitute:  If you wish, you may substitute two (11 ounce) cans of mandarin orange segments, drained, for the fresh orange sections.
