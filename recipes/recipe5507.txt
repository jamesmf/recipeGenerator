MOMS MARTIN SALAD


2 3  oz. boxes of lime jello
2  c. boiling water
1 8  oz. softened cream cheese
1 8  oz. cool whip
1 large  can fruit cocktail
1 regular  can chunked pineapple
2  c. miniature marshmallows

      Drain juice from pineapple and fruit cocktail and set aside. Mix boiling water and jello until dissolved.  Add softened cream cheese to hot jello mixture.  Gradually beat in 1 cup of the fruit juices.  Beat until foamy.  Chill until soft set.  Then add fruit and cool whip.  Mix well with beater.  Then fold in marshmallows. Chill overnight or for several hours.
