THREE-BEAN SALAD


1 (15 1/2 oz.)  can cut green beans
1 (15 1/2 oz.)  can cut wax beans
1 (16-oz.)  can kidney beans
2/3  c. white vinegar
1/3  c. vegetable oil
3/4  c. sugar
1  small onion, finely chopped
1/2  c. small green bell pepper, chopped
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. pepper
1/2  tsp. celery salt

      Drain beans; combine in a large bowl with green pepper and onion.  Make dressing:  in a small saucepan combine the sugar, vinegar, oil, and seasonings.  Bring to a boil over medium hear to melt the sugar.  Pour dressing over the beans and toss to mix all together. Refrigerate for several hours or overnight.
