CREAMY CHICKEN AND BROCCOLI BAKE


1  pkg. frozen broccoli, chopped
2  c. cooked chicken or turkey ham, cut up
1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can cream of mushroom soup
1/4  c. milk
6  slices American cheese
1  c. Bisquick
1/4  c. margarine or butter

	Preheat oven to 400 degrees;. Cook broccoli as directed on package. Drain. Grease oblong baking dish, 10 x 6 x 1 1/2-inch. Layer chicken and broccoli in dish. Mix soup and milk. Pour over broccoli and chicken. Arrange chicken slices on top of soup mixture. Mix Bisquick and margarine or butter until crumbly. Sprinkle over cheese. Bake until golden brown, 25 to 30 minutes.
