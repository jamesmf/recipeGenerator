POTATO SALAD


8  medium potatoes, peeled and cubed
1  Tbsp. sugar
1/2  c. mayonnaise
1/4  c. cubed sweet pickles
1/2  c. diced celery
1  Tbsp. chopped pimento
1  Tbsp. vinegar
1  small onion, diced
1/2  tsp. salt

      Cook potatoes until tender; drain.  Mix together with all other ingredients.  Sprinkle paprika on top if desired.
