OLD FASHIONED SUGAR COOKIES


1  c. shortening
2  c. sugar
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. cinnamon
4  eggs
4  Tbsp. milk
4  c. flour
2  tsp. baking powder
1  tsp. baking soda

      Cream together shortening, sugar, salt, cinnamon, eggs and milk.  Sift together flour, baking powder and baking soda.  Mix the two groups of ingredients together.  Drop by teaspoon and flatten with glass bottom dipped in sugar.  Bake at 375 degrees; for 10 to 12 minutes.  Makes 4 dozen.
