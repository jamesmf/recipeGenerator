1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese, cubed
1  c. milk
1 1/2  c. cubed cooked potato
1 (6 oz.)  pkg. frozen, cooked, tiny shrimp, thawed and drained
2  Tbsp. dry white wine
1/2  tsp. salt
