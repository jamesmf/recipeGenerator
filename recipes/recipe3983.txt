VEGETABLE SANDWICH SPREAD


1/2  c. pickles
1/2  c. olives
1/2  c. onion
1  stalk celery
1  medium green pepper
2 (8 oz.)  pkg. lite cream cheese
2  tsp. worcestershire sauce
1  Tbsp. mayonnaise
2  pkg. artificial sweetener
1  tsp. Mrs. Dash seasoning
1  c. pecans, chopped

      Mix pickles, olives, onion, celery and green pepper in food processor; drain on paper towels and squeeze.  Blend cream cheese with rest of ingredients.  Add the processed vegetables; mix well.
