FROZEN FRUIT SALAD


1/2  c. mayonnaise
1  c. sour cream
1 (21 oz.)  can peach pie filling
1  or 2 fresh peaches, cut in chunks

      In a bowl blend together mayonnaise and sour cream. Add pie filling and peach chunks. Spoon into lightly greased 1 quart mold or 6 individual molds and freeze until firm. Remove from molds just before serving. Serves 6.
