CHICKEN CRESCENT WREATH


2  tubes (8 oz. each) refrigerated crescent rolls
1  c. (4 oz.) shredded Co-Jack cheese
2/3  c. condensed cream of chicken soup
1/2  c. chopped fresh broccoli
1/2  c. chopped sweet red pepper
1/4  c. water chestnuts, chopped
1  can (5 oz.) white chicken, drained (or 3/4 c. cubed cooked chicken)
2  Tbsp. chopped onion

      Arrange crescent rolls on a 12 inch pizza pan, forming a ring with pointed ends facing the outer edge of the pan and wide ends overlapping.  Combine the remaining ingredients; spoon over wide ends of rolls.  Fold points over filling and tuck under wide ends. (Filling will be visible.)  Bake at 375 degrees; for 20 to 25 minutes or until golden brown.  Yield: 6 to 8 servings.
