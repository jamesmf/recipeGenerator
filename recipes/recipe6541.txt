CHICKEN CHILI STEW


3  whole chicken breasts, boned and skinned
1  c. onion, chopped
2  cloves garlic
2  Tbsp. vegetable oil
2  cans (14 1/2 oz.) stewed tomatoes
1  can pinto beans
1  c. picante sauce
1  tsp. chili powder and cumin
1  tsp. salt
1  medium green pepper, chopped

      Cut chicken into 1-inch pieces.  Cook onion, green pepper, garlic and chicken until chicken is not pink.  Add rest of ingredients.  Simmer 20 minutes.
      Optional Toppings: Grated cheese, diced avocado or sour cream.
      Can also be served over rice or noodles.
