SAUSAGE


3  c. water
4 1/2  c. rolled oats
1  c. water
1  Tbsp. basil
1 1/2  tsp. dill seed
2  tsp. cumin
2  tsp. marjoram
1  tsp. oregano
2  tsp. garlic powder
2  Tbsp. sage
2  tsp. cayenne
1/4  c. sunflower seeds
2  tsp. thyme
2 1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  c. nutritional yeast

      Bring 3 cups water to a boil.  Blend remaining ingredients, except oats, until smooth.  Add to the 3 cups of boiling water, then add oats.  Cook for 1 minute, stirring.  Shape into patties or links.  Bake on a nonstick cookie sheet at 350 degrees;.  Cook patties 30 minutes on one side, 20 minutes on the other.  Links or meatballs, cook for 35 minutes.
