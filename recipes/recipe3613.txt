FRIED CHEESE MELT-A-WAYS


1  c. Bisquick baking mix
1/2  c. milk
1  egg
1  lb. Monterey Jack or Cheddar cheese, cut into 3/4-inch cubes

	Heat 2-inches of oil in deep fat fryer or saucepan to 375 degrees;. Beat 1 cup baking mix, the milk and egg with hand beater until smooth. Coat cheese cubes lightly with baking mix. Insert a wooden pick in each cheese cube. Dip into batter. Cover cheese completely. Fry several cubes at a time, turning carefully, until golden brown. Drain on paper towel.
