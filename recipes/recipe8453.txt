JUICY MEAT LOAF


1  lb. ground beef
1/2  c. uncooked Quaker oats (quick or old fashioned)
1  beaten egg
1/4  c. chopped onion
1 1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
2/3  c. tomato juice
1/2  tsp. sage

      Combine all ingredients and mix thoroughly; pack firmly into a loaf pan.  Bake in moderate oven at 350 degrees; for 1 hour.  Let stand 5 minutes before slicing.
