CHOW MEIN HOT DISH


1  lb. hamburger
1  onion
1  c. chopped celery
1  c. chicken rice soup
1  can mushroom soup
1/2  c. rice
1  c. water or more if needed
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  Tbsp. soy sauce

      Brown hamburger and onion.  Mix with all remaining ingredients.  Bake at 350 degrees; about 1 1/2 hours.  Serve with chow mein noodles.
