FETTUCCINE ALFREDO


1  pkg. (12-oz.) fettuccine pasta
1/2  c. butter
1  c. grated Parmesan cheese
2/3  c. Pet evaporated milk

      Prepare fettuccine according to package directions.  To cooked, drained pasta add butter, cheese, and evaporated milk. Toss to mix; serve immediately.  Makes 6 one-cup servings.
