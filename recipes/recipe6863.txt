NO FRYING FISH


  fish filets (as many as you need)
  milk
  hot sauce (5 good shakes; depends on how much fish)
  spray butter (for top of filets)
  lemon juice
  Tony's
  Italian seasoned breadcrumbs

	Preheat oven to 400 degrees;. Mix milk and hot sauce together and stir well. Soak fish filets about 20 minutes or so; drain well and roll in breadcrumbs. Line pan with foil and place fish in single layer in pan. Spray butter on top of breadcrumbs and then drizzle lemon juice (not much, just drop by drop). Bake until golden brown or bake for 30 minutes, then put in broiler to brown.
