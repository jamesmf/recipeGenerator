ETOUFFEE


1  qt. shrimp, crawfish, chicken
1  to 2 blocks butter
1  medium onion, chopped fine
1/2  c. chopped celery, fine (optional)
1  tsp. minced garlic
3  to 4 green onions, chopped fine
2  Tbsp. flour (all-purpose)
1/2  can Ro-Tel (mild)
1/2  can whole tomatoes (optional)
2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. cayenne pepper
  Tony's (if needed)
1  tsp. Worcestershire)

	Saut onions, celery and green onions in butter until clear. Add garlic and cook a few minutes more. Stir in flour until deep brown color is made. Add tomatoes and simmer for 20 minutes. Add seasoning and cook slowly for 25 minutes more (low heat). Add seafood or chicken and cook until done.
	 If you are using seafood, cook about 10 to 15 minutes more, stirring often.
