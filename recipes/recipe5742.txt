COVERED BRIDGE POTATO SALAD


10  potatoes
2  small onions
1  carrot
3  stalks celery
18  olives
8  hard-cooked eggs
4  slices bacon
2  Tbsp. (heaping) flour
1  c. sugar
4  eggs, beaten
1  Tbsp. (heaping) dry mustard
1  c. vinegar
1  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper

      Cook potatoes in jackets until tender; peel and dice.  Mince onions, dice carrot and celery.  Chop olives; slice hard-cooked eggs.  Combine potatoes, onions, carrots, celery, olives and sliced eggs; set aside.  Dice bacon; fry until crisp.  Stir in flour to make a smooth paste.  Combine sugar, eggs, mustard, 1 cup water, vinegar, salt and pepper; mix well.  Add to flour paste.  Place over low heat; cook until thick, stirring constantly.  Pour over potato mixture; mix lightly.  Let stand in cold place for several hours.  Yield:  12 servings.
