Banana Cream Topping


3  fully ripe bananas, peeled
2  Tbsp. brown sugar
2  Tbsp. honey
1  c. whipping cream, whipped or 1 (2 oz.) pkg. dessert topping mix

      Mash together bananas, sugar, and honey until smooth and strain through a sieve.  Fold banana mixture into whipped cream.  Or fold banana mixture into prepared topping using dessert topping mix. Makes 3 cups.
