CARAMEL PECAN CHEESECAKE


2  (8 oz.) cream cheese, softened
1/2  c. sugar
1/2  tsp. vanilla
2  eggs
20  caramels
2  Tbsp. milk
1/2  c. chopped pecans
1  graham cracker crust (8 or 9-inch)

      Mix cream cheese, sugar and vanilla with electric mixer.  Add eggs until blended.
      Melt caramels with milk on low heat, stirring, until smooth. Stir in pecans.  Pour caramel mixture into crust.  Top with cream cheese batter.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 40 minutes.  Cool.  Refrigerate at least 3 hours before serving.  Makes 8 servings.
