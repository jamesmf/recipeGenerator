JEANNE'S VEGETABLE SOUP


3 to 4  cans low-fat beef broth
2  cans water (more if needed)
  salt and pepper to taste
6  carrots, chopped
2  large onions, chopped
6  stalks celery, sliced in small pieces
3  medium potatoes (small cubes)
1  can stewed tomatoes
  head of cabbage, chopped or shredded

	In a large soup pot, combine the broth, water, seasoning, carrots, onions, celery and potatoes. Bring to a boil and simmer for 15 to 20 minutes or until potatoes are barely cooked.  Add the stewed tomatoes and cabbage and simmer until cabbage is cooked.
