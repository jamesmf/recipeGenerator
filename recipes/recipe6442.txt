LOW CARB CHILI


2  lb. ground beef
3  - 4 Portobello mushrooms (cut in small pieces)
1  green pepper
1  onion
4  cans tomato sauce
1  can chili bean, drained and rinsed
  chili powder
  ground cumin
  splenda

      Brown ground beef with pepper and onions.  Add in mushrooms. Add in remaining ingredients.  Simmer.
