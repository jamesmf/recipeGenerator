BEEF STEW
(Tomato Gravy)  

1 - 1 1/2  lb. stew beef
5  medium to large potatoes
  Carrots
1/3  medium onion
15  oz. tomato sauce

      Brown meat in oil, in pan you are going to cook stew in. Drain grease and add tomato sauce (plus 1 & 1/2 cans water), carrots, onion, & potatoes that have been cut into cubes. Salt & pepper. Cook with lid at medium temp. until tender.
