MANICOTTI


1  box manicotti, cooked as directed
1 (16 oz.)  carton Ricotta
1/2  c. bread crumbs
2  c. Mozzarella cheese, shredded
1/2  c. Parmesan cheese, grated
4  eggs, beaten
  dash of salt
1/2  stick butter
1 large  can tomato sauce
  garlic powder to taste
1  c. Mozzarella cheese

      Drain manicotti and place on foil in single layer.  Mix next six ingredients together.  Stuff pasta.  Melt butter; add tomato sauce and garlic powder.  Pour some sauce on bottom of baking dish.  Add manicotti in single layer.  Spoon remaining sauce over top.  Sprinkle 1 cup Mozzarella cheese over sauce.  Cover.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes.  Uncover during the last 15 minutes.
