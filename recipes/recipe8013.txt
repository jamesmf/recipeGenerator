EASY HOT DISH


1  lb. hamburger
1  pkg. (8 oz.) egg noodles
1  can mushroom soup
1  can chicken soup
1  can water
1  c. diced celery
1  onion
  salt and pepper

      Do not precook any ingredients.  Arrange hamburger and noodles in layers.  Pour over the soups.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 1/2 hours.
