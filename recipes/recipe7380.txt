HAMBURG PORCUPINES


1  lb. ground beef
1  c. cooked rice
1  egg
1/2  c. chopped onion
1  can tomato soup
1/2  can water
1/4  tsp. mustard

      Mix 1/4 cup soup with beef, rice, egg and onion.  Roll into meatballs and brown in shortening.  Blend remaining soup, water and mustard.  Add to meatballs; cover and simmer for 25 to 30 minutes on low.
