WASEHI


1  lb. ground beef
1/2  lb. pork sausage
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. medium noodles, cooked as directed
1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can cream of mushroom soup
1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can tomato soup
6  slices cheese

	Lightly brown meats; drain fat and set aside.  Cook noodles; drain.  Mix all other ingredients together with meat and noodles. Place in an 8 x 10-inch baking dish. Put cheese on top.  Bake at 350 degrees;, covered, for 30 minutes.
