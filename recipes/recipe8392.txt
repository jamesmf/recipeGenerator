MALAYSIAN BEEF


2  lb. beef sirloin
1  tsp. chili powder
3  Tbsp. lemon juice
1/2  lemon rind, grated
1  tsp. sugar
1 1/2  tsp. salt
1  tsp. powdered ginger
2  Tbsp. plum jam
  butter
1  tsp. garlic powder or 2 cloves garlic, crushed

      Cut meat into 1/2-inch strips.  Mix together ginger, chili powder, garlic and lemon rind.  Dip meat into mixture and brown in butter.  Combine sugar, jam, lemon juice, salt and 1 1/2 cups water.  Add meat and simmer 30 minutes.  Serve with rice.
