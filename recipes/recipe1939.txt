TEXAS THREE ALARM CHILI


1/3  c. chili pepper
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. red pepper
1  tsp. paprika
2  tsp. oregano
2  Tbsp. onion and garlic
2  Tbsp. mesa flour
8  oz. tomato sauce
16  oz. water
2  lb. cubed meat (your choice)

      Brown meat; add tomato sauce and 2 cans of water.  Add all dry ingredients except the mesa flour.  Simmer covered for 1 hour.  Mix mesa flour with warm water to form a thick but flowable paste.  Add to mixture and cook for 20 more minutes.
