CURRIED ASPARAGUS SOUP


1  lb. asparagus, tough ends trimmed, cut into 2-inch pieces
4  c. strong chicken broth
4  scallions, coarsely chopped (green and white parts included)
3  Tbsp. chopped fresh chives or scallion greens
2  tsp. lemon juice
1 to 2  tsp. curry powder or to taste
  salt and freshly ground pepper to taste

      In a medium saucepan, place the asparagus, broth and greens and bring to a boil.  Cover, reduce heat and simmer slowly for 12 to 15 minutes or until the vegetables are very tender.  Place the mixture in a food processor or blender and add the lemon juice and seasonings.  Puree until smooth.  Serve hot immediately or chill.
