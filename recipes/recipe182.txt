OKLAHOMA COOKIES


1/2  c. evaporated milk
1/2  c. margarine
2  c. sugar
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  c. cocoa
2  tsp. vanilla
1/2  c. peanut butter
3  c. oatmeal

      Mix evaporated milk, margarine, sugar, salt and cocoa in medium saucepan; bring to a boil and cook for 1 minute, stirring constantly.  Remove from heat; add vanilla.  Allow to cool for about 5 minutes.  Stir in 1/2 cup peanut butter and 3 cups oatmeal. Drop by teaspoonfuls on waxed paper.  Allow to harden.
