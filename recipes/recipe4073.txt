CHICKEN BALL


2 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
1  Tbsp. A.1. steak sauce
1 1/2  c. chopped cooked chicken
1/3  c. chopped bell pepper
1/3  c. chopped celery
2  Tbsp. dried parsley flakes
  salt to taste
1  c. chopped pecans

	Combine all ingredients except pecans. Form into a ball. Refrigerate to chill. Roll in chopped pecans. Serve with crackers.
