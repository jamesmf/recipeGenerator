TACO SOUP


1  1/2 lbs. ground beef
2  cans pinto beans (do not drain)
1  can whole corn
2  cans mexican style tomatoes
1  pkg. taco seasoning mix
1  pkg. Hidden Valley Dressing mix
3/4  cup water

      Brown ground beef and add all other ingredients.  Cook on top of stove at medium heat until heated.
