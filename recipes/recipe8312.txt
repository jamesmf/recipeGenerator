CHICKEN AND BROCCOLI ALFREDO


8  oz. fettuccine (uncooked)
2  c. fresh broccoli
2  Tbsp. margarine, melted
1  lb. skinless, boneless chicken breast, cubed
2  cans cream of mushroom soup
3/4  c. milk
1/2  c. Parmesan cheese, grated
1  c. carrots, sliced

	Prepare fettuccine according to directions. Add broccoli and carrots for the last 4 minutes of cooking time to make mixture. Drain. In a skillet, heat margarine. Add chicken and fry until browned, stirring often. Add soup, milk, cheese and fettuccine mixture and cook through, stirring often. Serves 4 to 6.
