BIT-O-HONEY CANDY


1 3/4  c. sugar
1/4  c. brown sugar
2  Tbsp. butter
2  big dollops peanut butter
1/2  c. milk
  pinch of salt
7  oz. marshmallow cream
1  tsp. vanilla

      Combine all ingredients, except cream and vanilla, in saucepan over medium heat and bring to a boil.  Let boil 10 minutes at a rolling boil, stirring occasionally.  Add cream and mix.  Add vanilla and mix.  Pour hot into buttered pie plate.  Chill until set.
