ROTTERDAM LIVER PATE


1  lb. calves or veal liver
1  lb. bacon
2  medium onions, chopped
1  tsp. pepper
1  Tbsp. salt
1/2  tsp. allspice
1/4  tsp. powdered cloves
1/4  tsp. dry mustard
3  eggs, separated
1  c. thick cream sauce*

      *Make with 3 tablespoons flour, 3 tablespoons butter and 1 cup of milk.
      Remove the membrane from liver and grind 3 or 4 times with the 1 pound of bacon.  Combine pepper, salt, spices and the cream sauce.  Cool.  In a small dish, beat the egg yolks well.  In another bowl, beat the egg whites until stiff.  Set aside until called for.  Add the liver to the cream sauce; blend in the well beaten egg yolks.  Fold in the 3 egg whites, beaten stiff.  Pour into buttered 2-quart Pyrex dish.  Bake over water in a preheated 350 degrees; oven for 1 1/2 hours.  Cool in pan.  Serve on crackers or toast.
