CHEESE AND SALSA DIP


2  lb. box cheese (any type)
1  large salsa (any type)
1  lb. roll of sausage (medium)

      Add onions and green pepper if desired.
      Melt cheese in crock pot add salsa and sausage, be sure to drain sausage and crumble. Large bag of tortilla chips. Great for cookouts, parties, and home shows.
