SKILLET CANDIES


1  c. oleo
1 1/2  c. light brown sugar
2  c. chopped dates
2  Tbsp. milk
2  eggs, beaten
1/2  tsp. salt
1  c. chopped nuts
1  tsp. vanilla
4  c. Rice Krispies
  coconut

      Melt oleo.  Add sugar and dates.  Cook over low heat.  Add milk and slowly stir in beaten eggs.  Bring to boil for 3 minutes. Remove from heat.  When cool, add nuts, vanilla and cereal.  Stir to mix well.  Form candy into ball and roll in coconut.  Makes about 6 dozen.
