OKRA-TOMATO CASSEROLE


6  c. sliced tender okra
3  medium fresh tomatoes, chopped
1/2  c. chopped onion
1/2  c. chopped green pepper
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
1  Tbsp. dry butter substitute

      Place layers of okra, tomatoes, onion, green pepper, salt and pepper in nonstick 2 1/2-quart casserole.  Repeat layers.  Top with dry butter substitute.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour.
