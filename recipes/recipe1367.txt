CUCUMBER RELISH


12  large cucumbers
12  sweet bell peppers
6  onions
1  large head cabbage
1/2  c. salt
2  lb. granulated sugar
3  pt. vinegar
1  pt. water
1  Tbsp. mustard seed
1  Tbsp. celery seed
1/2  tsp. turmeric

      Grind cucumbers, peppers, onions and cabbage.  Sprinkle with salt.  Let stand 2 hours.  Drain.  Mix sugar, vinegar, water, mustard seed, celery seed and turmeric.  Put on stove and bring to a boil.  Add chopped vegetables.  Bring to a rolling boil.  Seal while hot.
