HOMEMADE ICE CREAM


6  eggs
2 1/2  c. sugar
1  tsp. vanilla
1 (13 oz.)  can evaporated milk
  fresh fruit, mashed

      Beat eggs several minutes.  Beat in sugar one cup at a time. Add vanilla and evaporated milk and pour in 4-quart freezer container.  Either add fresh milk to full line in freezer container or add sweetened fresh fruit, reducing sugar in ice cream mixture by 1/2 to 1 cup depending on taste desired.  Yields 8 to 10 servings.
