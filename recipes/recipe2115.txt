HEATH CANDY BAR


35  saltine crackers
1  stick margarine
1  stick butter
3/4  c. brown sugar
12  oz. semi-sweet chocolate chips

      Line a cookie sheet with heavy foil.  Place crackers on sheets (7 rows x 5 rows).  Boil margarine, butter and sugar exactly 3 minutes.  Drizzle mixture over crackers.  Bake at 400 degrees; for 5 minutes.  Remove from oven and spread with chocolate chips.
