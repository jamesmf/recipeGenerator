MEATY MUSHROOM CHILI


1  lb. bulk Italian sausage
1  lb. ground beef
1  c. chopped onion
1  lb. fresh mushrooms, sliced
1 (46 oz.)  can V-8 juice
1 (6 oz.)  can tomato paste
1  tsp. sugar
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. garlic powder
1  tsp. dried oregano
1  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/2  tsp. dried basil
1/2  tsp. pepper
  sour cream (optional)

      In a large saucepan, cook the sausage, beef and onion over medium heat until the meat is no longer pink. Drain. Stir in the mushrooms, V-8 juice, tomato paste and seasonings. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat. Cover and simmer for 1 hour. Garnish with sour cream, if desired. Yield: 8 servings.
