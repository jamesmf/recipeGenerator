ONION MUSHROOM TURKEY GRAVY


  turkey giblets
1  bay leaf
2  c. water
1  can onion soup
8  Tbsp. turkey drippings
1/2  c. flour
1 (6 oz.)  can sliced mushrooms
3 1/2  c. water
  salt and pepper to taste

	Combine giblets (except liver) and neck with bay leaf and water in a saucepan.  Heat to boiling; stir in soup. Simmer 40 minutes. Add liver and simmer 20 minutes until tender. Remove giblets. Measure broth and add water (if necessary) to make 3 1/2 cups.
	In a separate saucepan, measure 8 tablespoons of drippings from turkey. Gradually blend in flour until smooth. Cook, stirring until bubbly. Gradually stir in 3 1/2 cups of broth, mushrooms and 3 1/2 cups water. Stir and boil 1 minute. Season with salt and pepper to taste.  Makes approximately 7 cups of gravy.
