JOHNNY MARZETTI


2  lb. ground beef
2  small onions, chopped
4  pieces celery, chopped
2  tsp. chili powder
  garlic salt to taste
2  tsp. salt
  green pepper (optional)
  mushrooms (optional)
2  cans tomato soup
2  cans mushroom soup
4  Tbsp. black olives, chopped
3  cans water
20  oz. egg noodles
1  lb. sharp Cheddar cheese

      Brown the first 8 ingredients together. Add the remaining ingredients. Simmer slowly for about 30 minutes. Add some tomato juice if mixture gets too thick. Grate and add the cheese. Cook slowly for about 30 minutes more.
