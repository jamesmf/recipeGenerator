ASIAN SLAW


2  pkg. beef Ramen noodles
1  (16 oz.) cole slaw mix
1  c. sliced almonds
1  c. sunflower seeds
1  bunch green onions, sliced
3/4  c. oil
2/3  c. vinegar
3/4  c. sugar
2  pkg. beef seasoning from Ramen noodles

      Crush noodles in bottom of 13 x 9 casserole dish.  Layer slaw mix, onions, almonds and sunflower seeds, but do not mix.  Then mix together oil, vinegar, sugar and 2 packages of the Ramen noodle seasoning mix.  Pour over other mixture, but do not mix.  Let chill 24 hours.  Then mix before serving.
