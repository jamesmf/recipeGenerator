VENISON ROAST


2 to 3  lb. venison roast
1  medium onion, chopped
1  c. mushrooms, chopped
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. garlic or 1 whole garlic clove, chopped
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
  hot sauce to taste
  dash of pepper
2  c. dry white wine
1  c. flour

      Place roast in a roaster pan with all ingredients, adding flour and wine last.  Mix thoroughly.  Cook at 350 degrees; until done.
