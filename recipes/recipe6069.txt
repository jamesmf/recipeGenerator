DORIS' JELLO SALAD


1 large  box strawberry jello
1  pint frozen strawberries
15  oz. can crushed pineapple
3  mashed bananas
1  c. chopped pecans
8  oz. carton sour cream

      Dissolve jello in 1 cup boiling water.  Add frozen strawberries, crushed pineapple, bananas and pecans.  Pour one half the mixture in a dish and let congeal.  Add sour cream to the other half of mixture; pour this over the first congealed layer.
      Chill and serve.
