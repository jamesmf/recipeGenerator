NEW YEAR'S PUNCH


750 ml  bottle Southern Comfort
6  oz. lemon juice
6  oz. can frozen lemonade
6  oz. can frozen orange juice
3  liters 7-Up
  red food coloring
  frozen bunches of grapes
  orange slices

      Chill ingredients.  Mix Southern Comfort, lemon juice, lemonade and orange juice in punch bowl; add 7-Up.  Add drops of red food coloring.  Float frozen bunches of grapes and orange slices. Yield:  32 servings.
