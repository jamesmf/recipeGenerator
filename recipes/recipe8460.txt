PEPPER STEAK AND RICE


  steak cut into strips
1  large onion, chopped
1  Tbsp. garlic salt
1  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. black pepper
3  Tbsp. soy sauce
2  large peppers cut into thin strips
2 15  oz. cans stewed or whole tomatoes
1  box of instant rice

      Brown beef strips slowly in 1 tsp. oil or shortening.  Add garlic salt, pepper and soy sauce.  Cook 3 to 4 minutes more.  Add green peppers and onion.  Cook 5 to 10 minutes until green peppers are tender.  Add a little water if needed.  Keep covered, then add tomatoes.  Stirring uncovered for 15 to 20 minutes more, or until thick and rich.  Serve over rice.
