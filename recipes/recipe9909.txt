BROCCOLI AND CHEESE


2  c. Minute rice
2  cans cream of mushroom soup
1 soup  can of water
1 large  jar Cheese Whiz
1  can mushrooms
1  onion, chopped
1  large bag frozen broccoli
  Dry bread crumbs

      Mix all the ingredients together.  Put into a 9 x 13 inch baking pan.  Bake at 350 degrees; for one hour.  Cover with dry bread crumbs before baking.
