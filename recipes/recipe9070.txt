HAM BALLS WITH RAISIN SAUCE


3  c. ground ham
1  c. uncooked quick cooking oats
1/4  tsp. ground cloves
1/4  tsp. ground cinnamon
2  large eggs
1/2  c. milk
2  tsp. prepared mustard

      Preheat oven to 400 degrees;. In large bowl with hands, lightly mix ham ball ingredients and 1/4 teaspoon pepper until combined. Shape into 12 balls, divided evenly. Arrange in shallow baking dish. Bake uncovered for 15 minutes.
Sauce:
3/4  c. raisins
1/3  c. brown sugar
1/4  tsp. cinnamon
3  tsp. vinegar
1  Tbsp. cornstarch

      Make sauce.  In small saucepan bring raisins, brown sugar, cinnamon, vinegar and 1 1/2 cups water to boiling.  Simmer 5 minutes.  In small bowl mix cornstarch with 1/4 cup water until blended.  Stir into raisin mixture.  Bring to boil, stirring constantly.  Simmer 1 minute, until thickened.  Pour over ham balls.  Bake 30 minutes longer, basting occasionally with sauce mixture.
