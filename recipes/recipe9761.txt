ROASTED VEGETABLE LASAGNA


  cooking spray
9  cooked lasagna noodles
2  c. spinach leaves, divided
1/2  c. shredded part-skim Mozzarella cheese
  fresh basil sprigs (optional)

 
Vegetables:
4  red bell peppers
1  tsp. olive oil
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. black pepper
6  yellow squash, halved lengthwise and cut into 1-inch pieces (about 1 1/2 lb.)
1  large onion, cut into 16 wedges
4  garlic cloves, minced

 
Cheese Mixture:
2  c. 2% low-fat cottage cheese
1 1/2  c. grated sharp Provolone cheese
1/4  c. chopped fresh basil
1  tsp. dried oregano

 
White Sauce:
3  Tbsp. all-purpose flour
1 1/2  c. 2% reduced-fat milk
2  Tbsp. chopped fresh basil
1/4  tsp. black pepper

      Preheat broiler.
      For Vegetables: Cut bell peppers in half lengthwise, discard seeds and membranes. Place pepper halves, skin sides up,  on a foil-lined baking sheet; flatten with hand. Broil 15 minutes or until blackened. Place in a zip-top plastic bag; seal and let stand 15 minutes. Peel and set aside.
      Preheat oven to 450 degrees;. Combine oil, salt, 1/2 teaspoon black pepper, squash and onion on a baking sheet; toss well. Bake at 450 degrees; for 20 minutes. Remove from oven; combine squash mixture and garlic in a bowl. Decrease oven temperature to 375 degrees;.
      For Cheese Mixture: Combine cottage cheese and next 3 ingredients in a bowl.
       For White Sauce: Place flour in a medium saucepan. Gradually add milk and stir with a whisk. Place over medium heat. Cook until thick, stirring constantly. Remove from heat and stir in chopped basil and 1/4 teaspoon black pepper.
      Spread 1/4 cup White Sauce in bottom of a 13 x 9-inch baking dish coated with cooking spray. Arrange 3 noodles over sauce; top with 1 1/4 cups cheese mixture, 1 cup spinach, 4 bell pepper halves, 2 cups vegetable mixture and 1/4 cup white sauce. Repeat layers, ending with noodles. Cover and bake at 375 degrees; for 15 minutes. Uncover and sprinkle with Mozzarella cheese. Bake an additional 20 minutes. Garnish with basil sprigs, if desired. Makes 9 servings.
