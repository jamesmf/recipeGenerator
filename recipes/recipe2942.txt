FROZEN HAM SANDWICHES


2  sticks melted margarine
1 1/2  Tbsp. poppy seed
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  small onion
3  Tbsp. mustard
  party rolls (in foil pkg.)
  Swiss cheese slices
  shaved ham

      Mix first 5 ingredients and refrigerate until mixture is spreadable. Split party rolls vertically. Cover tops and bottoms with butter spread. Add ham and cheese slices. Can freeze rolls in original package. Bake at 350 degrees; (covered in foil) for 35 to 45 minutes (frozen) or 30 minutes (thawed). Slice and serve warm.
