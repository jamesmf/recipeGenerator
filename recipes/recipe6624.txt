SALMON ON HALF SHELL


4  large baked potatoes
1/3  c. milk (add more if needed)
2  tsp. sauteed onions, chopped
3/4  c. canned or cooked salmon
1  clove garlic, minced
  salt & pepper to taste
  Dash of poultry & Italian seasoning
  Chopped parsley
  Fried onions, bacon bits, Cheddar cheese (if desired for garnish)

      Bake potatoes.  Cut in half and scoop out insides.  Mash with next 7 ingredients until smooth.  Refill potato shells without pressing down.  Sprinkle with fried onions, bacon bits or cheese if desired.  Brown in 450 degrees; oven about 10 minutes.  Serve with salad or vegetable.
