BROCCOLI, RICE, CHICKEN & CHEESE CASSEROLE


  6-8 chicken tenders or 2-3 cans boned chicken
1 (10 oz.)  pkg. frozen chopped broccoli
3  c. cooked rice
2  c. grated cheese, separated
1/2  c. milk
1 (10 oz.)  can Cream of Chicken soup
1  tsp. salt
  Dash of pepper

      If using chicken tenderloins, cook and cut into bite-size pieces.  Cook broccoli and drain.  Combine chicken and broccoli with 1 c. cheese and remaining ingredients.  Pour into 2 qt. baking dish.  Bake at 325 degrees; for about 30 minutes.  Top with remaining 1 c. cheddar cheese and bake long enough to melt cheese.
