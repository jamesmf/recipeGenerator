RAMEN COLE SLAW


1  bag cole slaw mix
2  Tbsp. sesame seeds
1/2  c. almonds, sliced and toasted
1 small  bunch green onions, chopped
1  pkg. chicken flavored Ramen noodles, broken into small pieces

	Preheat oven to 350 degrees;. Toast almonds 5 to 10 minutes. Remove from oven. Toss all ingredients in a large bowl. Keep chilled.
Dressing:
1/2  c. oil (sunflower)
2  Tbsp. sugar
1  Tbsp. cider vinegar
1/4  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
  seasoning pkg. from Ramen chicken-flavored soup

	Mix above ingredients and toss with salad just before serving. Do not store dressing in the refrigerator before tossing on salad.
