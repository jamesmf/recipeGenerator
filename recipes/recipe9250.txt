RICE, BROCCOLI AND CHICKEN CASSEROLE


1/2  c. onion, chopped
1/2  c. celery, chopped
1  c. cooked rice
1 (8 oz.)  jar Cheez Whiz
1  c. water chestnuts, sliced
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  box chopped broccoli, cooked and drained
2  chicken breasts, cooked and chopped

      Brown onions and celery.  Mix all ingredients and put in casserole dish.  Sprinkle Ritz cracker crumbs over top.  Bake at 375 degrees; until hot and bubbly.
