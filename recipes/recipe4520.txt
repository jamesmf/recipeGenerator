BEAN SALAD


1 (No. 2)  can petits pois peas, drained
1 (No. 2)  can French-style green beans, drained
1  bunch celery
1  green pepper
1  onion
1 large  jar pimiento pieces

 
Dressing for Salad:
1/2  c. oil
1  c. vinegar
3/4  c. sugar
1  Tbsp. water

	For dressing, mix all ingredients in a jar and shake well. Mix with vegetables which have been salted. To be real good, this salad should be made a day in advance.
