NEW YEAR'S WHEAT DISH
(Amen Masiouk)  

1  lb. whole wheat kernels
1/8  tsp. salt
1/2  c. powdered sugar
1/2  c. coarsely chopped walnuts
1/2  c. raisins, opt.
4  quarts water

      Place rinsed whole wheat in 4 quarts water.  Bring to full boil.  Reduce heat and cook until tender.  Cool.  Add sugar, walnuts and raisins.  Mix well.
