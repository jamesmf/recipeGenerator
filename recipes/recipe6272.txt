HOT MUSTARD


1/2  c. white vinegar
1/2  c. dry mustard
1/2  c. sugar
1  egg yolk

      In a small bowl, stir vinegar slowly into dry mustard until smooth. Cover and let stand 6 hours or overnight  at room temperature.  Pour into saucepan with sugar and egg yolk and cook over low heat, stirring constantly.  Cover and refrigerate up to 2 weeks.
