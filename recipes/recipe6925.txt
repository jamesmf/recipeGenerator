GROUND BEEF CASSEROLE


2  Tbsp. chopped onions
1  lb. ground beef
1 small  can tomato sauce
1 medium  can mixed vegetables
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper

      Brown onions and ground beef in hot fat in a skillet. Mix tomato sauce, mixed vegetables, salt and pepper in casserole dish. Add browned onions and beef. Cook in 350 degrees; oven for about 1 hour.
