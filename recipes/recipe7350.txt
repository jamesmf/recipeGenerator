HAMBURGER NOODLE CASSEROLE


1  lb. ground beef or ground chicken
1  (16 oz.) cooked egg noodles
1  c. cream of chicken soup
1  can drained corn
1  large onion
1  c. cream of mushroom soup
1  c. sour cream

      Brown ground beef and onion; drain.  Add noodles, soups, sour cream and corn.  Mix well.  Pour into 2-quart greased casserole dish.  Sprinkle top with cheese or bread crumbs.  Cover with foil. Bake at 350 degrees; for 35 minutes.
