MUNCH COOKIES


3/4  c. white Karo
3/4  c. brown sugar
12  oz. peanut butter
5  c. Special K cereal

      Heat Karo and brown sugar on stove.  Stir to dissolve sugar, but do not let it bubble.  Remove from stove and add peanut butter, stirring to mix well.  Pour over Special K cereal and mix well. Butter a 9 x 12-inch pan.  With hands buttered also, pat mixture into pan.
Topping:
6  oz. chocolate chips
6  oz. butterscotch chips

      Melt chips together.  Spread on top of cereal mixture.  Cool and cut in squares.
