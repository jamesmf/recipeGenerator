FORGOTTEN ROAST


3 to 4  lb. chuck roast
1  envelope dry onion soup mix
  potatoes
  carrots
  onions
1  can condensed cream of mushroom soup

      Place roast on a large piece of aluminum foil and arrange vegetables around roast.  Sprinkle meat and vegetables with dry onion soup mix.  Spoon undiluted mushroom soup on top.  Wrap loosely with foil, making sure it is sealed at top and sides. Place  in a roaster pan and bake at 275 degrees; for 4 hours or 325 degrees; for 2 1/2 hours.
