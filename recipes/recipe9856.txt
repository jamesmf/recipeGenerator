ITALIAN ZUCCHINI


8  medium zucchini squash
1 (No. 2)  can whole tomatoes
1  tsp. chopped green pepper
1  medium onion, sliced
  salt and pepper
  butter

      Cut zucchini in halves lengthwise.  Place in casserole dish. Pour tomatoes over squash.  Sprinkle on green pepper and place onion slices on top.  Season with salt and pepper; dot with butter.  Cover and bake.
