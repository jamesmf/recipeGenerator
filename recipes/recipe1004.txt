CREAM OF ONION SOUP


6  medium onions, sliced
1/4  lb. butter
1/2  c. flour
1 to 1 1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
8  c. chicken stock or 8 c. water
1 2/3  c. cream
  French bread
  Swiss cheese, sliced

      Saut√© onions in butter until tender.  Blend in flour, salt and pepper.  Cook over low heat for 10 minutes.  Gradually stir in chicken base or water.  Bring to a boil, stirring constantly.  Stir in cream just before serving.  Put in ovenproof bowls.  Add 1 slice toasted French bread.  Top with Swiss cheese.  Bake until cheese is melted.
