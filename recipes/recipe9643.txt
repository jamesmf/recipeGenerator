SPICEY BREAST OF CHICKEN


8  chicken breast halves, skinned
1/2  cup margarine, melted
1/4  cup lime or lemon juice
1  envelope Italian Salad Dressing Mix

      Place chicken in a 13x9x2 inch baking dish. Pour margarine and lime or lemon juice over chicken; sprinkle with salad dressing mix. Cover and bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour or until tender. Yield 8 servings.
