SEVEN LAYER SALAD


1  head lettuce, shredded
1  c. chopped Bell Pepper
1  c. chopped Onion
1  c. chopped Celery
1  c. English Peas, drained
1  c. Mayonnaise
3  tbsp. Sugar
2  c. Grated Cheese

      Layer lettuce, bell pepper, onion, celery and peas in a large bowl.  Mix mayonnaise, sugar and cheese. Spread over other ingredients.  Sprinkle with bacon bits if desired.
