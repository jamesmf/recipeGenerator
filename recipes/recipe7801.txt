BLENDER QUICHE


3  eggs
1/2  c. Bisquick
1/2  c. melted butter
1 1/2  c. milk
1/4  tsp. salt
1/2  c. ham or bacon, chopped
1  c. grated Swiss cheese

      Put all ingredients except meat and cheese in blender for a few seconds. Pour into 9-inch greased pie pan. Add meat and cheese and push down into egg mixture. Bake at 350 degrees; for 35 or 40 minutes until eggs slightly brown. Let set 10 minutes before cutting.
