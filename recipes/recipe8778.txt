CHILI


2  lb. hamburger
4 (15 oz.)  cans kidney beans
2 (15 oz.)  cans baked beans
1 (15 oz.)  can beef broth
1/2  c. maple syrup
1 (3 oz.)  can tomato paste
2  tsp. ground cumin
3 to 4  Tbsp. chili powder
2  garlic cloves, crushed
2  Tbsp. cilantro
  salt and pepper to taste

      Brown hamburger; add remaining ingredients.  Simmer for an hour or so.
