PARTY CHICKEN


8  chicken breasts (boneless and skinless)
8  slices bacon
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  (4 oz.) chipped beef
1/2  pt. sour cream

      Wrap each breast with piece of bacon.  Cover bottom of greased baking dish (about 8 x 12 x 12) with chipped beef. Arrange chicken breasts on chipped beef.  Mix soup and sour cream. Pour over all.  Cover and refrigerate.  When ready, bake at 275 degrees; for 3 hours, uncovered.
      Note:  This can be assembled a day ahead, if necessary.
