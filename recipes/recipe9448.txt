CROCK-POT CHILI


2  lb. ground beef
2  Tbsp. oil
2  c. chopped onions
1  clove garlic, minced
3  Tbsp. chili powder
1  bay leaf
1/2  c. beef stock
1 (28 oz.)  can tomatoes
3 (16 oz.)  cans red kidney beans
1  tsp. salt

	Brown beef in hot oil in skillet; discard excess fat. Put all ingredients into slow cooker. Stir once. Cover and cook on low for 8 to 10 hours. Discard bay leaf. Serve hot.
