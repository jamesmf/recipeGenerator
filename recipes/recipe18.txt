CHOCOLATE PUMPKIN CAKE


4  eggs
2  c. sugar
1  tsp. salt
2  tsp. soda
3  tsp. cinnamon
1  Tbsp. cocoa
1 1/2  c. pumpkin
1 1/2  c. vegetable oil
1  tsp. vanilla
2  c. flour

 
Frosting:
1  box powdered sugar
2  Tbsp. cocoa
1  stick margarine
1 (3 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
1  c. chopped nuts

      Beat eggs well.  Add sugar, salt, soda, cinnamon, cocoa, pumpkin, vegetable oil, vanilla and flour.  Pour in greased 13 x 9-inch pan.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes.
      Frosting:  Put ingredients in mixing bowl and beat well with electric mixer.  Frost when cool.
