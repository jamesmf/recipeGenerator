MEXICALI PLATTER


1  can refried beans
1  avocado, pureed
2  c. sour cream
1  jar taco salsa
2  c. grated Cheddar cheese
2  c. chopped tomatoes
1  c. chopped scallions

      On a large platter, mound in layers, starting with the refried beans and ending with the scallions.  Serve with Doritos chips or similar.
