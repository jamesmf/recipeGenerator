QUICK CARAMEL SAUCE


1  c. butter (no substitutes)
2  c. sugar
2  tsp. fresh lemon juice
1 1/2  c. whipping cream
1  Tbsp. vanilla extract

      Melt butter in a heavy saucepan over medium heat; add sugar and lemon juice, and cook, stirring constantly, 6 to 8 minutes or until mixture turns a deep caramel color.  Gradually add cream, and cook, stirring constantly, 1 to 2 minutes or until smooth. Remove from heat.
