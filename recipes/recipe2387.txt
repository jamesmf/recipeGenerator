WHITE CHOCOLATE BERRY CREAM PIE


1 1/2  c. cold milk
2  pkg. Jell-O white chocolate flavor instant pudding and pie filling
1  (8 oz.) tub Cool Whip
3/4  tsp. grated lemon peel
1  c. fresh or frozen strawberries
1  prepared graham cracker crust

      Pour milk into medium bowl.  Add pudding mixes.  Beat with wire whisk 1 minute.  Gently stir in whipped topping and lemon peel. Spoon strawberries into bottom of crust; top with pudding mixture. Refrigerate four hours or until set.
