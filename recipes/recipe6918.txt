CHICKEN-N-RICE


1  c. rice
1  can mushroom soup
2  c. chicken broth or milk
1  pkg. onion soup mix
1/4  c. butter
1 chicken, cut up or 2  lb. boneless chicken

      Put rice, soup, broth or milk and onion soup mix into greased casserole dish.  Dip chicken into melted butter and salt.  Place on top and bake for 2 hours for raw chicken, 1 hour for cooked chicken.
