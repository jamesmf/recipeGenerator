PIZZA CASSEROLE I


1  lb. ground beef
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. Mozzarella cheese
1 1/2  c. milk
1 (14 oz.)  jar Pizza Quick sauce (Ragu)
3/4  c. biscuit mix
2  eggs

	Brown meat; drain. Put in bottom of 9 x 13-inch casserole dish. Pour pizza sauce over meat. Top with cheese. Add biscuit mix, milk and eggs. Pour over meat and cheese; cover. Bake at 400 degrees; for 30 minutes.
