STRAWBERRY BREAD


1/2  c. butter
1 1/2  c. sugar
1  tsp. vanilla
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. lemon juice
4  eggs
1/2  tsp. soda
1/2  c. sour cream
3  c. flour
1/2  c. chopped nuts
1  c. strawberry preserves
1  Tbsp. red food coloring

      Blend  butter, sugar, vanilla, salt and juice;  beat in eggs one at a time.  Dissolve soda in sour cream.  Add to egg mixture. Fold  in  flour,  nuts, preserves and food coloring. Pour  batter into 2 large greased loaf pans.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 35 to 40 minutes or until bread pulls away from pan.
