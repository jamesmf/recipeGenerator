CHOCOLATE FILLED SNOWBALLS


1  c. soft butter
1/2  c. sugar
1  tsp. vanilla
2  c. sifted flour
1  c. chopped nuts
1  pkg. chocolate kisses
  powdered sugar

      Beat butter, sugar and vanilla until light.  Add flour and nuts; blend well and chill.  Shape dough around kisses, using 1 tablespoon of dough for each.  Roll to make ball.  Bake at 375 degrees; on ungreased cookie sheet for 12 minutes or until set but not brown. Cool slightly.  While warm, roll in powdered sugar.
