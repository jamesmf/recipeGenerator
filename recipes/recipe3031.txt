APPETIZER VEGGIE PIZZAS


2  pkg. crescent rolls
1  c. mayonnaise
2 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese, softened
1  pkg. Hidden Valley Ranch dressing mix
  finely chopped vegetables (carrots, broccoli, cauliflower, peppers) any kind
  Cheddar cheese

      Press dough onto cookie sheet.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 8 to 10 minutes.  Let cool.  Combine cream cheese, mayonnaise and salad dressing mix.  Mix thoroughly.  Spread over baked dough.  Top with vegetables and sprinkle with cheese.  Cut into squares.
