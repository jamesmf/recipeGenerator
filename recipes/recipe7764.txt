COMPANY BEEF


2  lb. ground beef
5  oz. macaroni
1  Tbsp. catsup
  dash of oregano
  salt
1  c. grated cheese
1  medium onion, chopped
1 (15 oz.)  can tomatoes, blended
1  Tbsp. steak sauce
  dash of chili powder
  pepper
1  can mushroom soup

      Brown meat and onions; drain. Boil 5 ounces of macaroni; drain. Mix all together with everything except cheese. Sprinkle cheese on top. Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes.
