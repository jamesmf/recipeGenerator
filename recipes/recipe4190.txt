LAZY LADY PICKLES


1  gallon cucumbers, sliced
4  tsp. non-iodized salt
4  tsp. powdered alum
4  tsp. pickling spice
  vinegar, enough to cover
6  c. sugar

      Cover cucumbers with vinegar and spices. Let set for 6 weeks in a cool, dark place. Stir once a week. At the end of 6 weeks, drain off vinegar, add sugar and let set for 24 hours.
