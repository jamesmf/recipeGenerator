MARGARITA FAJITAS


1 1/2  lb. round steak, fat trimmed
1/4  c. tequila
1/2  c. lime juice
1  c. cooking oil
4  Tbsp. liquid smoke
1  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/4  tsp. pepper
1/2  tsp. salt
3/4  tsp. paprika
1/2  c. chopped green pepper
1  c. chopped green onions (all)
1  c. chopped tomatoes
8  to 10 (6-inch) flour tortillas

      Slice partially frozen round steak into thin, long strips. Marinate 2 hours in flat dish with mixture of tequila and lime juice.  After draining tequila and lime marinade, mix cooking oil, liquid smoke, Worcestershire sauce, pepper, salt and paprika and marinate meat, covered, for four days.
      Heat wok over medium-high heat; necklace with oil mixture (marinade).  When hot, add steak strips, stirring occasionally. When 3 or 4 minutes from desired doneness, stir in onion and green pepper.  When done, ladle into hot plates, top with chopped tomato. Serve with warmed flour tortillas.  Serves 4.
