SPICY HOT MEATBALLS

Meatballs:
3/4  lb. ground beef
3/4  c. bread crumbs
1 1/2  Tbsp. minced onion
1/4  tsp. Texas Pete hot sauce
2  eggs, beaten
3/4  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper

      Mix together well; roll into balls.  Brown in skillet or in oven; drain.
Sauce:
3/4  c. ketchup
1/2  c. water
1/4  c. vinegar
1  Tbsp. minced onion (optional)
2  Tbsp. brown sugar
2  tsp. worcestershire sauce
1  tsp. mustard
1/4  tsp. pepper
1 1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. Texas Pete

      Mix all together; pour over meatballs and simmer.  Delicious!
