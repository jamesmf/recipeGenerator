GRILLED FRENCH BREAD


1  unsliced loaf French bread
1/2  c. soft butter
2  Tbsp. minced parsley
  onions
1/2  tsp. garlic powder
1  tsp. celery salt

      Mix butter, parsley, onions, garlic powder and celery salt. Cut bread into 1-inch slices, cutting to bottom crust but not through.  Spread butter mixture on each slice.  Wrap in heavy-duty aluminum foil.  Heat over hot coals for about 20 minutes.  Turn often during heating.
