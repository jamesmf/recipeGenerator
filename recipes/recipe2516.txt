SAUSAGE SNACKS ITALIANO


1 (8 oz.)  can Pillsbury quick crescent dinner rolls
1/4  c. grated Parmesan cheese
1  tsp. oregano
8  Brown 'N Serve sausage links

      Separate crescent dough into 4 rectangles; press perforations to seal.  Combine cheese and oregano; sprinkle over dough.  Cut each dough rectangle crosswise to form 2 squares.  Place a sausage link on each square; roll up.  Cut each roll into 3 to 4 pieces. Secure each with a toothpick.  Place cut side down on an ungreased cookie sheet.  Bake at 375 degrees; for 12 to 15 minutes until golden brown.  Refrigerate any leftovers.  Yield:  2 dozen appetizers.
