BARBECUE MEAT LOAF


1  1/2 lbs. ground beef
1  c. bread crumbs
1  large sweet onion, finely chopped
1  egg, beaten
1 1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
1 6  oz. can tomato sauce
1/2  c. water
3  tsp. vinegar
3  tsp. brown sugar
3  Tbsp. worcestershire sauce

      Combine first six ingredients in a large bowl.  Add 1/2 can tomato sauce.  Mix well.  Place mixture in a baking dish and shape into a loaf.  Combine remaining ingredients in a mixing bowl and pour over meat loaf.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour and 20 minutes or until done.
