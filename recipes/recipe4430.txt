CRUNCHY PEA SALAD


10  oz. pkg. frozen baby peas, thawed
1  c. diced celery
1  c. chopped cauliflower
1/4  c. diced green onion
1  c. chopped cashews
1/2  c. sour cream
1  c. prepared Hidden Valley Ranch
12  pieces bacon

      Combine all these ingredients, except the bacon.  Chill. Cook bacon until crisp and crumble over top of salad before serving.
