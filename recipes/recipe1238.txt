PEANUT BUTTER CANDY


2  Tbsp. peanut butter
2  Tbsp. raisins
2  Tbsp. milk
1  medium size graham cracker
1/2  tsp. sweetener (non-caloric)
1  tsp. vanilla

      Cream peanut butter until it is smooth.  Add 1 tablespoon of milk; mix well.  Add raisins; mix well.  Break up graham cracker and mix with other ingredients.  Add other tablespoon milk; add sweetener and vanilla; mix well.  Form into ball or make into bar shaped piece.
      Exchange:  1 meat exchange, 1 fruit exchange and 1/2 bread exchange.  Take milk from allowance for day.  Equal 147 calories.
