MONSTER JUMBLE


1/4  c. smooth peanut butter
1/4  c. (1/2 stick) Parkay margarine
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. garlic powder
2 1/4  tsp. French's Worcestershire sauce
4  c. Rice Chex cereal
4  c. Wheat Chex cereal
3/4  c. cocktail peanuts
1  c. candy corn

      Preheat oven to 250 degrees;.  Melt peanut butter with margarine in a large shallow roasting pan (15 x 10 x 2-inches) in oven until shiny and soft (about 5 minutes).  Remove.  Stir in salt, garlic powder and Worcestershire sauce until mixture is smooth.  Add Chex and nuts.  Mix until all pieces are coated.  Bake in oven 1 hour. Stir every 15 minutes.  Remove from oven.  Stir in candy.  Spread on absorbent paper to cool.  Makes about 9 cups.
