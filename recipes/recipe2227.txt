AMBROSIA PLATTER

Fruit:
2  star fruits, sliced
6  navel oranges, pared, sliced into rounds or sectioned and seeded
2  grapefruits, pared and sliced
1  pineapple, pared and cut into spears
1 (16 oz.)  jar stewed prunes, drained
  lettuce leaves

      Arrange the fruit in a visually appealing pattern on a lettuce lined platter.
Dressing:
3  Tbsp. honey
1/4  c. orange juice
1  c. unsweetened coconut (plain or toasted)

      Drizzle the honey over the fruit, then pour the orange juice over fruit and sprinkle with coconut.  Makes about 10 servings.
