GINGERBREAD BOYS


1/4  c. light or dark brown sugar
1/2  c. butter
1  jumbo egg or 2 medium
1/4  c. molasses
3 1/4  c. flour
3/4  Tbsp. ginger
1 1/2  tsp. baking soda
1/2  tsp. cinnamon
1/2  tsp. nutmeg
1/2  tsp. salt

      Beat sugar and butter until well blended.  Add eggs and molasses.  Stir in remaining ingredients.  Cover and chill 1 hour. Roll out to about 1/4-inch thick and cut out Gingerbread Boys. Bake at 350 degrees; for 10 minutes.  Decorate before baking with M&M's. When cool, make icing and decorate with bow ties, etc.  Makes 24 cookies.
