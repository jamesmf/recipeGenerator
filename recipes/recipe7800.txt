CRABMEAT POTATO BOATS


4  large baking potatoes
1/3  c. butter
1/2  c. milk
1  tsp. salt
  pepper to taste
1  Tbsp. grated onion
1  c. grated Cheddar cheese
1  can crabmeat

      Bake potatoes. Cut off tops. Scoop out insides and whip with all ingredients except crabmeat. Fold in crabmeat. Put back in shells. Bake in 450 degrees; oven for 10 to 15 minutes. May be made ahead and refrigerated or frozen. If frozen, allow 30 to 45 minutes baking time.
