OVEN GLAZED CHICKEN


4  chicken breasts (boneless)
1  can Campbell's new Italian tomato soup
1  Tbsp. water
1  Tbsp. vinegar
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  Tbsp. brown sugar, packed

	Bake chicken at 375 degrees; for 30 minutes. Combine above ingredients and spoon over chicken. Bake 30 more minutes.
