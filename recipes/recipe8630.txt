HAM AND ASPARAGUS BAKE


1/4  lb. margarine
6  slices bread
1  lb. sliced ham
2  cans asparagus, drained
4  oz. sliced Swiss cheese
1 1/4  C. milk
4  eggs
  onion, cut up
  salt and pepper

      Melt margarine; put in 9 x 13-inch baking dish.  Add bread. Put ham, then asparagus.  Add Swiss cheese.
      Mix together milk, eggs, onion, salt and pepper.  Pour over cheese.
      Bake uncovered at 350 degrees for about 1 hour.
