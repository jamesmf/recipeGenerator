CRABMEAT APPETIZER


1  can crabmeat, drained
1  jar Kraft Old English cheese
2  Tbsp. mayonnaise
1/2  Tbsp. seasoning salt
1/2  Tbsp. garlic powder
1/4  lb. butter
1  pkg. English muffins

      Melt butter.  Mix drained crabmeat with other ingredients. Spread on English muffin; split, top and bottom.  Place on cookie sheet for 6 to 8 minutes.  Broil.  Remove; cut into quarters.
