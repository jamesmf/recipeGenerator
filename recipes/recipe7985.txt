CRANBERRY CHICKEN


2  Tbsp. honey
2  Tbsp. orange juice
1  tsp. grated orange peel
1/2  tsp. ground allspice
1 (16 oz.)  can whole cranberry sauce
4  chicken breasts, boned and skinned

      Mix honey, orange juice, orange peel, allspice and cranberry sauce in small bowl.  Place chicken breasts in baking dish.  Pour 1/2 of mixture over chicken breasts.  Bake in preheated oven at 375 degrees; for 15 minutes, covered.  Turn chicken pieces, pour on remaining sauce, cover, continue to bake for 30 to 35 minutes more.
