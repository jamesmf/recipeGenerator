CHEESE OLIVES


1  jar pimento stuffed olives, drained
1  jar Old English Cheddar product
1  stick butter
1  c. plain flour

      Melt butter. Mix flour, Old English and melted butter together to make a paste. Flatten a small portion of paste in palm of hand and place 1 olive in the middle. Fold paste around olive. Repeat until paste is used up. Cook in a preheated oven at 350 degrees; until slightly browned.
