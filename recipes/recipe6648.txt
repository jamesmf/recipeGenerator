QUICK HOMEMADE BEEF STEW


1  lb. beef stew meat, cubed
1  Tbsp. oil
1 (14.5 oz.)  can stewed tomatoes
1  can sliced potatoes (optional)
1 (14.5 oz.)  can mixed vegetables
1 (12 oz.)  jar beef gravy

      Brown beef stew meat in oil until tender.  Add rest of vegetables and gravy and simmer for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Very good with warm rolls.
