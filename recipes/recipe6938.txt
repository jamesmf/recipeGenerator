PORK CHOPS WITH PINEAPPLE


6  pork chops
1 (20 oz.)  can pineapple rings
  flour
  salt (pinch)
  pepper (pinch)

      Mix flour, salt and pepper. Flour pork chops and brown in skillet with a small amount of oil. Place in oblong baking dish; arrange pineapple rings on top. Pour on 1/2 the pineapple juice. Cover and bake in 350 degrees; oven for 1 hour to 1 hour and 15 minutes.
