ZUCCHINI SOUP


1 1/2  lb. sliced zucchini
1/2  c. diced onion
3  Tbsp. butter
6  c. chicken broth
1/2  tsp. dill seed
4  tsp. Cream of Wheat
  salt and pepper to taste
1/2  c. sour cream (optional)
