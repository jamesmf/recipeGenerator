LEMON LIME CHICKEN


1  chicken, cut up
  salt and pepper
1/2  tsp. garlic powder
1  tsp. basil
1/4  c. lemon juice
1/4  c. lime juice
1/2  c. water
2  tsp. lemon peel
2  tsp. lime peel
1  lemon, sliced up
1  lime, sliced up

      Sprinkle chicken with salt and pepper, garlic and basil. Combine remaining ingredients except lemon and lime slices.  Pour over chicken.  Bake uncovered 30 minutes.  Turn and baste.  Bake 30 to 40 minutes more.  Garnish with fruit slices.  Bake at 400 degrees;. Calories:  235.
