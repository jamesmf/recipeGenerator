CRISP SWEET SLAW


4  c. shredded cabbage
1  c. chopped green pepper
1/2  c. chopped onion

 
Dressing:
1/4  c. cider vinegar
1/2  c. apple juice or cider
3  Tbsp. vegetable oil
8  packets Equal
1  tsp. celery seed
1  tsp. dry mustard
1  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper

      Combine slaw ingredients.  Whisk together dressing ingredients. Pour over slaw.  Toss to blend.  Refrigerate until served. Diabetic Exchange:  1 vegetable, 1 fat.
