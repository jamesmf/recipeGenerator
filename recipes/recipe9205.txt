SAUSAGE AND WILD RICE CASSEROLE
(Quick and Easy!)  

1  lb. Neese's sausage, hot or mild to taste
1  box Uncle Ben's Wild Rice
8  oz. carton sour cream

      Brown sausage and crumble into small pieces.  Prepare wild rice according to package directions.  Mix the sausage, rice and sour cream together and pour into a greased casserole dish.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 20 to 25 minutes.
