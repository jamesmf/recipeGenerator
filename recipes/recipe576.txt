CUCUMBER DRESSING


1/2  c. low-fat cottage cheese
1 1/2  Tbsp. low calorie mayonnaise
1  medium cucumber, peeled, seeded and diced (1 c.)
1/4  c. plain yogurt
1  Tbsp. onion, chopped
1  tsp. dried dill weed
  salt to taste
1  c. buttermilk

      In blender combine cottage cheese and mayonnaise; puree until smooth.  In medium bowl combine cottage cheese mixture, cucumber, yogurt, onion and dill weed until well blended.  Add salt to taste and buttermilk.  Contains 12 calories per tablespoon.
