CAJUN CATFISH


  McCormick Cajun seasoning
  catfish fillets
  bread crumbs
  butter
  lemon juice
  pecan pieces

      Amounts of ingredients increase with number of catfish fillets used.
      In a large sealable plastic bag, mix Cajun seasoning and bread crumbs to taste.  Rinse fish with water and pat dry.  One at a time place fish fillets in bag of seasonings and shake.  Place coated fillets into casserole dish.  Melt butter and pour over fish. Sprinkle fish with pecan pieces.  Spritz with lemon juice.  Bake at 450 degrees; for 15 to 20 minutes.
