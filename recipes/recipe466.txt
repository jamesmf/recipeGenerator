GINGERBREAD


1/2  c. sugar
1/4  c. butter
1  egg
1/2  c. milk
1/2  c. molasses
1 1/4  c. flour
1/4  tsp. soda
1 1/2  tsp. ginger

      Cream sugar and butter in a bowl.  Beat egg, then add egg, milk and molasses.  Sift flour, soda and ginger into mixture and beat well.  Bake in shallow greased pan (9-inch pie pan works well) at 350 degrees; for 30 to 45 minutes.
