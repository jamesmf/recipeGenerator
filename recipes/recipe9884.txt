ROASTED BROCCOLI FLORETS
(Like Popcorn)  

2  bunches broccoli
1  Tbsp. olive oil
  salt to taste

      Preheat oven to 450 degrees;. Cut broccoli into bite size pieces. Put in plastic bag. Pour 1 tablespoon olive oil over and coat. Pour pieces onto sheet pan in single layer and bake 25 minutes or until brown and crispy. Salt to taste after baking. Use in place of popcorn for a low carbohydrate diet. It is delicious fresh out of the oven. Not good left over.
