CHERRY SALAD


20  oz. (1 large can) pineapple, crushed
1/2  c. water
6  oz. (1 large box) jello, cherry
21  oz. (1 can) cherry pie filling
3/4  c. cola

      Drain juice from pineapple.  Bring juice and water to a boil. Add jello to hot liquid and mix.  Add cherry pie filling and cola. Let partially congeal before adding pineapple.  Chill until firm.
