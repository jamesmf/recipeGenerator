FROZEN CRANBERRY SALAD


1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
2  Tbsp. mayonnaise
2  Tbsp. sugar
1 (8 oz.)  can whole cranberry sauce
1 (8 oz.)  can crushed pineapple, undrained
1/2  c. chopped pecans
1 (8 oz.)  carton whipped topping

      Mix all ingredients together.  Spoon into foil cupcake liners in muffin pans or cake pan.  Freeze until firm.  Remove from freezer 20 to 30 minutes before serving.
