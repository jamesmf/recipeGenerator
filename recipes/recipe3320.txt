HOT FRUIT
(Relish)  

1/2  c. raw cranberries
1 large  can pineapple chunks
3  tsp. instant tapioca (Minute)
1 large  can peaches
1/3  c. brown sugar
1  tsp. cinnamon

      Drain fruit and mix all ingredients together.  Dot with butter and bake in shallow dish at 350 degrees;, uncovered, for 30 to 35 minutes.
      Note:  I sometimes add pears, also.
