BARBECUE BEEF ON THE GRILL


4 to 6  lb. loin-eye or rib-eye beef roast
  Worcestershire sauce
  garlic salt
  black pepper

      Salt and pepper roast, using a liberal amount of garlic salt. Marinate in Worcestershire sauce 24 hours or more.  Use enough Worcestershire sauce to cover meat.  You may use a plastic bag to help keep sauce in contact with meat.  Cook in covered charcoal grill for 2 1/2 to 3 hours or until done.  Slice in 1/2 to 3/4 inch thick slices and serve hot.
