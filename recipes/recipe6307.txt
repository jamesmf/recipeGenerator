BLUEBERRY SALAD


1  cup boiling water
2  pkgs. raspberry jello
1 9  oz. can crushed pineapple, undrained
1  can blueberry pie filling mix
1  cup sour cream
8  oz. cream cheese
1  cup chopped pecans

      Mix water and jello.  Add pineapple and pie filling mix.  Pour into 9 x 13 pan and chill until set.  Mix sour cream, cream cheese and pecans.  Spread this on top of filling.  Chill.
