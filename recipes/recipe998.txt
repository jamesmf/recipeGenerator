SHRIMP SOUP


1  can Boston or New England clam chowder
2  cans shrimp soup
1  can cream of celery soup
1  can shrimp or 1/4 lb. fresh shrimp
1  can crab or 1/4 lb. fresh picked crab
1  can minced clams
2 soup  cans milk
1  to 2 dashes of Tabasco sauce
2  to 3 dashes of Worcestershire sauce
  pepper to taste
  sherry to taste
  chives or parsley (for garnish)

      Mix all ingredients and heat thoroughly.  Garnish with chives or parsley.  Serves a large group.
