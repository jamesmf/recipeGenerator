POTATO-LEEK SOUP


2  medium potatoes
  bottoms of 3 large leeks
1/2  c. celery, chopped
1/2  c. carrots, grated
1/2  c. onion, chopped
1  pt. or more chicken broth
1 small  clove garlic
  salt and pepper to taste
  Accent

      Cook vegetables in enough water to keep from sticking until tender.  Add chicken broth and seasonings.  Add Accent while cooking.
      Serve hot with saltines or other crackers.
