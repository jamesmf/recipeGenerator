LOW FAT BEAN SOUP


2 (16-oz)  cans Navy beans, drained and rinsed
1  can reduced fat chicken broth
2  c. water
1 (6-oz)  package diced 97% fat-free ham
1  c. finely chopped onion
1  c. finely chopped celery
1  c. shredded carrots
2  c. diced, cooked potatoes
1/2  tsp. minced garlic
1/2  tsp. pepper
1  tsp. salt

      In large saucepan, cook potatoes until tender.  In another large saucepan, combine navy beans, chicken broth, water, ham, onion, celery, and carrots. Bring mixture to a boil. Stir in potatoes, garlic, pepper and salt.  Lower heat, cover and simmer for 30 minutes.
      Serves 6 (1-1/2 cup servings).
