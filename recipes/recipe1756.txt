INDONESIAN FRUIT SALAD


2  large bananas, sliced
2  medium oranges, sectioned
1/2  c. dates, slivered
1 (1 lb. 4 oz.)  can pineapple bits
2  Tbsp. white wine vinegar
1  tsp. curry powder
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  c. salad oil
  crisp salad greens
  cottage cheese
  chopped salted peanuts

      Combine bananas, oranges and dates in a large bowl.  Drain pineapple, reserving 1/2 cup juice.  Add pineapple to fruit. Combine juice with wine vinegar, curry powder, salt and salad oil. Shake well; pour over fruit and marinate about 1/2 hour.  Arrange on platter with crisp greens.  Spoon cottage cheese on top and fruit over all.  Top salad with peanuts and serve.
