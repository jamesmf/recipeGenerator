CRANBERRY SPINACH SALAD


1 (6 oz.)  package of salad spinach
1/2  cup Ocean Spray Craisins Sweetened Dried Cranberries
  Red onion slices

      Wash and clean spinach.  Divide evenly among 4 salad plates. Top each with 2 Tbsp. of sweetened dried cranberries and onions.
Dressing:
4  slices bacon or turkey bacon (crisp and crumbled)
1/2  cup plain or orange flavored honey
1/2  cup lime juice
2  Tbsp. Dijon mustard

      Combine dressing ingredients in a small glass mixing bowl using a wire whisk. Heat in microwave on high for one minute or until warm.  Makes about one cup.
