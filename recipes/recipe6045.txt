MEXICAN POTATO SALAD


5  medium size potatoes, cooked, cooled and cut into medium slices
1/2  c. finely chopped green onion
1/4  c. finely chopped sweet onion
1/2  c. finely chopped green pepper
  salt to taste
3/4  c. mayonnaise
1  oz. jar pimento
1  c. grated cheese or sliced cheese
1  tsp. pepper
3/4  c. half and half

      Mix onions, pepper, pimento, salt, pepper, mayonnaise and half and half.  Grease bottom and sides of a shallow baking dish. Alternate layers; potatoes, filling and cheese.  Bake at 375 degrees; for 15 minutes.  Top with sliced olives if desired.  Serve warm.
