OATMEAL FRUIT SQUARES


1  c. quick cooking oats
1  c. unsweetened applesauce
1/4  c. raisins
1 1/3  c. dry instant milk powder
1/2  stick (1/4 c.) diet or lite margarine
1  tsp. baking powder
1  tsp. baking soda
1  tsp. cinnamon
1  tsp. vanilla
4  envelopes Sweet 'N Low or equivalent

      Combine all ingredients, mixing well.  Pour into 8 x 8-inch pan.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes.  Cool and cut.
