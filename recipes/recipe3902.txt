BREAD AND BUTTER PICKLES


8 or 9  qt. cucumbers
6  medium onions
2  green peppers
3  cloves garlic
1/3  c. salt
5  c. sugar
3  c. vinegar
1 1/2  tsp. turmeric
1 1/2  tsp. celery seed
2  Tbsp. mustard seed

      Slice cucumbers; add cut up onions and bell peppers. Add whole garlic cloves and salt. Cover with cracked ice. Let stand 3 hours. Drain well.
      Combine remaining ingredients and pour over cucumber mixture. Heat just to boil. Put into jars and seal. Should make 8 pints.
