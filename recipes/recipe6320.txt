GARBANZO SALAD


  1-14 oz. can garbanzo beans (chick peas)
1  Tbsp. olive oil
2  Tbsp. wine vinegar
1  Tbsp. parsley, chopped
1 small  jar pimientos, chopped
  salt and pepper

      Rinse garbanzos in cold water. Combine with other ingredients and allow to stand in refrigerator for several hours. Serves 3-4.
