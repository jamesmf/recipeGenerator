BAKED BEAN CASSEROLE


1  lb. ground beef
1  lb. hot dogs, cut up
1  onion, chopped (optional)

      Brown together.  Pour into a deep-dish casserole or pan (at least 4 qt.)
Add:
4 - 6  cans of baked beans (depending on how many you want to serve)
1/4 to 1/2  c. ketchup
1/2 to 1  c. brown sugar

      The ketchup and brown sugar measurements depend on how sweet you want them.  Mix all together.  Taste for flavor.  Pour into pan with meat.  Bake at 400 degrees; for 20 minutes.  Turn back to 350 degrees; and bake another 40 minutes.  Turn off oven and let them set up.
