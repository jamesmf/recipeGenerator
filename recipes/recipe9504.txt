LEMON ROASTED CHICKEN


8  chicken quarters
1  c. Dijon-style mustard
1/2  c. apricot jam
1/4  c. lemon juice

      Preheat oven to 375 degrees;.  Pat chicken dry with paper towels. Place in roasting pan in oven.  Roast 40 minutes or until about 3/4 done.  Remove from oven.  Combine mustard, jam and lemon juice in small bowl.  Blend well.  Brush mustard mixture on both sides of chicken.  Bake, skin side up, about 10 to 15 minutes more or until golden brown.  Serve hot or cold.  Remaining sauce can be used for dipping.  Great for picnic!  Serves 6 to 8.
