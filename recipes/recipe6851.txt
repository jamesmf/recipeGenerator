CRUSTLESS QUICHE


12  eggs
1  can evaporated milk
2  c. Mozzarella cheese
3  Tbsp. dried onion bits
3  shakes Lawry's seasoning salt

      Grease 9 x 13 pan well. Sprinkle cheese on bottom of pan, then the onion bits. In bowl, beat eggs, milk and seasoning. Pour over the cheese and onion bits. Bake at 300 degrees; for 50 to 60 minutes. Be sure the center is done.
      Bacon bits, green peppers, mushrooms, etc., may be added if desired.
