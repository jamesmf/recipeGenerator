SAUERKRAUT HOT DISH #2


1 to 1 1/2  lb. hamburger
1  large onion, chopped
1 large  can sauerkraut
2  c. wide egg noodles, not cooked
2  cans cream of mushroom soup
1  can cream of celery soup
4  c. Mozzarella cheese, shredded

      Brown hamburger and onion, drain.  Layer in 9 x 13 pan: hamburger and sauerkraut.  Top with egg noodles, then soups.  Add 2 cups Mozzarella cheese.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 50 minutes.  Then top with remaining 2 cups cheese and bake until cheese melts.
