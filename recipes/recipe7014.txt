SHREDDED BEEF SANDWICHES


1  roast
1  pkg. Au Jus Gravy mix
1  jar pepperoni peppers
1  pkg. Italian dressing
  salt and pepper to taste

      Place roast in a baking dish and add remaining ingredients. Bake at 270 degrees; until tender. When tender, shred beef with a fork. Serve on rolls or buns. M-mmm!
