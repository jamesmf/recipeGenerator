RICE BALLS


1  lb. hamburger meat
2  c. Minute rice
1 (16 oz.)  can tomato sauce
1  egg, slightly beaten
1/2  tsp. chopped onion
  salt and pepper to taste
  pinch of sugar
  seasonings (optional)

      Combine hamburger meat, seasonings, rice and 1 cup of tomato sauce with the slightly beaten egg. Mix together well. Roll into 1 1/2 to 2-inch balls and place into large skillet.
       To remaining tomato sauce, add enough water to make 2 cups and stir in the pinch of sugar. Pour sauce over rice balls and bring to a boil. Lower heat to simmer and cook for 15 minutes covered.
