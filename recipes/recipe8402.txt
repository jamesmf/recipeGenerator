SESAME CHICKEN


2  tsp. sesame seeds
4  (4 oz.) skinned, boned chicken breast halves
1  tsp. vegetable oil
2  Tbsp. honey
2  Tbsp. reduced-sodium soy sauce
1/4  tsp. ground ginger

      In a nonstick skillet toast sesame seeds over medium to high heat.  Set aside.  Cut chicken breasts to 1/4-inch thickness (or pound to 1/4-inch thickness).  Heat oil in nonstick skillet over medium to high heat.  Add chicken; cook for 3 minutes each side or until lightly brown.  Combine sesame seeds, honey, soy sauce and ginger; pour over chicken and cook 4 minutes, turning chicken to coat with sauce.  Serve sauce over chicken.  Serves 4.
