QUICHE


4  slices bacon
3  eggs
1/2  c. biscuit mix
1/3  c. melted butter or oleo
1 1/2  c. milk
1/8  tsp. salt
  dash of pepper
2  c. shredded Swiss cheese

      Fry  bacon until crisp;  drain and crumble.  Beat eggs, biscuit  mix,  butter  or  oleo,  milk,  salt and pepper until smooth.  Pour into 9-inch or bigger pie plate or oblong baking dish.  Sprinkle  bacon  and  cheese  over  top.  Press gently. Bake at 350 degrees; for 35 to 40 minutes.
