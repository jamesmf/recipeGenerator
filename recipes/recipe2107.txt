TOOTSIE ROLLS


3  Tbsp. butter or margarine
2  sq. unsweetened baking chocolate
1/2  c. light Karo corn syrup
1  tsp. vanilla
3/4  c. powdered milk, sifted until not grainy
3  c. confectioners sugar

      Melt butter and chocolate.  Add syrup, vanilla, powdered milk and sugar.  Knead until well mixed.  Roll into roll, then cut when needed.
      For a sour burst of flavor, substitute 1 package Kool-Aid for the chocolate.
