POPPY SEED DRESSING


1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. dry mustard
1  tsp. paprika
1  tsp. poppy seed
1/2  c. white corn syrup
1/4 to 1/3  c. vinegar
1  c. corn oil

      Place all ingredients in a small bowl.  Beat until well blended and thickened.  Place in a covered container and chill several hours.  Shake before serving.  Yields 1 3/4 cups.
