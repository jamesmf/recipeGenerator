GRILLED CATFISH


6  cleaned catfish (1 to 1 1/2 lb.)
1  stick margarine, melted
  juice of 6 lemons
  dash of Tabasco sauce
1  tsp. prepared mustard
2  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. pepper
1  tsp. paprika

      After coals have burned, place fish on grill.  Combine all ingredients for basting sauce and baste frequently as fish cooks. Cook for 20 minutes on one side; turn and cook an additional 15 minutes, basting often.
