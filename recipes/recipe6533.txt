QUICKIE CASSEROLE


1 1/2  lb. ground beef
1  can diced tomatoes
1  can tomato sauce
2  tsp. salt
1  Tbsp. chili powder
3/4  pkg. long spaghetti
  cheese

      Brown meat in 12-inch skillet. Drain. Add tomatoes, tomato sauce, 1 teaspoon salt and chili powder. Bring to a boil. Cover and cook over low heat for 30 minutes. Cook spaghetti. Add 1 teaspoon salt until tender. Drain.  Place spaghetti in long casserole dish. Pour meat mixture over spaghetti. Cover with cheese.
