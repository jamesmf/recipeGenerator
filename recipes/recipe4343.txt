LINGUINE SALAD


1  lb. linguine
1 large  bottle zesty Italian dressing
1  bottle salad seasoning
1  tomato, finely chopped
1  cucumber, finely chopped
1  green pepper, finely chopped
1  small onion, finely chopped

      Cook and drain linguine.  While hot add 1 bottle Italian dressing and 1 bottle of salad seasoning.  When cooled add vegetables.  Mix well.  Refrigerate 24 to 48 hours before serving.
