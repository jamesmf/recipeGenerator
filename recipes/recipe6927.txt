HAM CASSEROLE


2  c. cooked chopped ham
2  c. cooked diced potatoes
1  c. chopped onions
1  c. grated Cheddar cheese
1  c. chopped celery
1  can French green beans
2  cans mushroom soup
1  tsp. salt (more if needed)

      Mix well and pour into large casserole bowl. Bake for about 40 minutes at 350 degrees;. Top with a can of French onion rings. (Cover to cook.)
