COUNTRY VEGETABLE SOUP


3  Tbsp. olive oil
2  stalks celery, chopped
1  medium carrot, peeled and sliced
2  cloves garlic, minced
1  small yellow onion, chopped
2 (15 oz.)  cans Great Northern or pinto beans
1  Tbsp. Kitchen Bouquet
1 (10 oz.)  pkg. frozen chopped spinach
1  c. potato, diced
1  small bay leaf
2 (14 oz.)  cans chicken broth
1  c. cabbage, shredded
1  c. zucchini, sliced
1/4  tsp. thyme
1 (8 oz.)  can tomato sauce

      In large heavy bottom pot, heat oil.  Saut√© celery, carrot, garlic and onion over medium heat until tender (about 15 minutes). Add beans.  Add remaining ingredients and simmer 1 hour.  May serve sprinkled with Parmesan cheese.  Serves 6.
