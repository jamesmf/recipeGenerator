SALAD


1  head Lettuce, cut up
3  pcs. Celery, chopped
1  Bell Pepper, chopped
1  red Onion, chopped
1  can English Peas, drained
1  c. Sour Cream
1  c. Mayonnaise
2  tbsp. Sugar

      Layer ingredients in order listed through peas.  Mix sour cream, mayonnaise and sugar mixture and pour on top of peas. Sprinkle bacon bits and cheese on top if desired.  Put in refrigerator.  Keeps well.
