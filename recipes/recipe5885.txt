GEORGIA CRACKER SALAD


1  sleeve saltine crackers
1  large tomato, finely chopped
3  green onions, finely chopped
1 1/2  c. mayonnaise
1  hard-boiled egg, finely chopped
  salt
  freshly ground black pepper

      In a medium size bowl, coarsely crush the crackers with your hands.  You should have big cracker pieces.  Add the remaining ingredients; mix well and serve immediately.  Season to taste with salt and pepper.  EXCELLENT!  Yield:  6 servings.  Prep time:  10 minutes.
