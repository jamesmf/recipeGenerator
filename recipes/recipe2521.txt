MUSHROOM SOUP


1  qt. mushrooms
1 1/2  qt. good rich stock (veal or chicken)
1  Tbsp. flour
1  Tbsp. butter
1  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper
  whipped cream
  croutons

      Wash and peel 1 quart mushrooms and dice.  Stir into them 1 1/2 quarts of good rich stock (veal or chicken).  Thicken with 1 tablespoon flour creamed with 1 tablespoon butter, 1 teaspoon salt and 1/2 teaspoon of pepper.  At the last moment, add a bit of whipped cream and croutons.
