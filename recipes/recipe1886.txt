MOLE POBLANO DE GUAJOLOTE
(Turkey or Chicken)  

1 to 2  lb. turkey pieces, cooked, sliced or cubed

 
Sauce:
2  to 6 garlic cloves
1/4  c. raisins
1/2  c. almonds or pine nuts
3  Tbsp. each sesame seed and chopped parsley
1/2  chopped green pepper
1/2  lb. tomatoes
2  torn tortillas
1  onion
2  to 3 green chilies (Anaheim)
2  Tbsp. olive oil
2  c. chicken broth
1/4  tsp. each:  cumin, cloves, cinnamon, coriander, sugar and anise
  fresh orange rind (optional)

      Process all but the chicken; simmer meat in sauce slowly for 30 minutes until meat is tender and takes on taste of sauce.  Serve on softened tortillas or cut slices of tortillas 1/2-inch wide in sauce.  Layer in sauce with tortillas.
   Serve with condiments: sour cream, slivered almonds, pine nuts, slices of avocados, salsa, slices of Mozzarella (or top with Mozzarella when warming up to serve). Gets better as sets. Serves 8 to 10.
Mint Sauce (Optional):
2  to 3 fresh mint leaves, chopped
3 to 4  tsp. balsamic vinegar
3  Tbsp. sugar
1  carrot, chopped
1  celery rib, chopped

      Add to sauce.
