MEDITERRANEAN BOUILLABAISSE


1/2  c. chopped onion
1  clove garlic
2  Tbsp. olive oil
1 (28 oz.)  can tomatoes (undrained)
1 (12 oz.)  can clam juice
1  c. dry white wine
2  Tbsp. chopped parsley
1/2  tsp. salt
1  bay leaf
  pepper to taste
1/4  tsp. thyme
1  lb. medium shrimp (raw), peeled
1  lb. firm, fresh fish
1/2  lb. scallops
1/2  lb. crab legs
2  (6 1/2 oz.) chopped clams (undrained)

      Saut√© onion and garlic in oil.  Add tomatoes, clam juice, wine, parsley, salt, thyme, bay leaf and pepper and bring to a boil.  Reduce heat and simmer 15 minutes.  Add shrimp, fish, scallops and crab legs. Cook 5 minutes.  Add clams and cook for another 10 to 15 minutes.  Serve with salad and garlic bread.
