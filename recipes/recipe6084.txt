LYNDA'S TACO SOUP


1  lb. ground round beef
1  large onion, chopped
1  oz. (1 pkg.) ranch dressing, dry mix (Hidden Valley)
1 1/4  oz. (1 pkg.) taco seasoning
27  oz. (1 large can) pinto beans
29  oz. (1 large can) corn, whole kernel
29  oz. (1 large can) tomatoes, stewed
10  oz. (1 can) tomatoes, rotel

      Brown meat with onions.  Drain off fat.  Mix together dry dressing and taco seasoning.  Mix into meat.  Then, without draining, mix in all other ingredients.
      Simmer 1 hour.
      NOTE:  Makes a great crock pot meal!
