


3  c. chopped apples
2  c. miniature marshmallows
1  c. thin celery slices
1/2  c. chopped walnuts
  Miracle Whip salad dressing

      Combine apples, marshmallows, celery and walnuts.  Add enough salad dressing to moisten.  Toss lightly.  Chill.  Add additional salad dressing and garnish with apple slices before serving, if desired.  Makes 6 to 8 servings.
