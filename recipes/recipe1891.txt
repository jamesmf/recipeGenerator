BEEF AND BEAN BURRITO


1  lb. ground beef
1  jar thick 'n chunky salsa
1  pkg. taco seasoning mix
1  can refried beans
1  can green chilies, chopped
10  flour tortillas
1  c. shredded cheese

      Brown ground beef; drain fat.  Drain salsa; reserve liquid. Add seasoning mix, beans, chilies and liquid from salsa.  Simmer, uncovered, 5 minutes.  Prepare flour tortillas.  Top with lettuce and cheese and 1 tablespoon of salsa.  Fold edge of tortillas over to cover filling.  Fold envelope style and roll.  Serves 10.
