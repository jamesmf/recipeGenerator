PISTACHIO AMBROSIA


2  cans (17 oz. each) fruit cocktail
2  cans (20 oz. each) pineapple chunks
2  cans (11 oz. each) mandarin oranges
4  pkg. (3.4 oz. each) instant pistachio pudding mix
2  c. (16 oz.) sour cream
1  carton (12 oz.) frozen whipped topping, thawed
  chopped pecans, optional

      Drain canned fruits; reserving 3 cups juice.  Set fruit aside; pour juice into a 4 quart bowl.  Add pudding mix and mix until smooth.  Stir in sour cream; add whipped topping and mix until smooth.  Fold in fruit; chill for several hours.  Top with pecans just before serving if desired.  Yield: 16 to 20 servings.
