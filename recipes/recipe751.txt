BAKED APPLE MACARONI


2  c. macaroni, cooked until partially tender
2 to 3  c. apples, sliced
1/2  c. cream
1/2  c. sugar
  half and half
  cinnamon to taste

      Alternate macaroni and apples in layers in baking dish. Combine cream and sugar and pour over macaroni and apples.  Pour half and half over macaroni and apples until they are covered. Sprinkle with cinnamon.  Bake at 325 degrees; until tender (about 30 minutes).  This dish can be used for breakfast, lunch or supper.
