SUGAR COOKIES


24  pkg. Sweet 'N Low
1  c. shortening
2  eggs
4 1/2  c. flour
1/4  tsp. salt
1 1/4  tsp. baking soda
4  tsp. baking powder
1  c. milk
1 1/2 to 2  tsp. vanilla
2  Tbsp. almond extract

      Cream together Sweet 'N Low and shortening.  Add eggs.  Sift together dry ingredients.  Add to creamed mixture.  Combine milk, vanilla and almond extract.  Alternate 1/2 of milk mixture to rest of ingredients and mix well.  Use 1/2 dough at a time and roll out on floured surface.  Use floured cutters to cut out.  Bake on ungreased baking sheet at 350 degrees; for 10 minutes.  Makes 5 dozen.
      Note:  Can be decorated with dry jello.  These are good frozen.
