CHERRY COKE SALAD


1  can (20 oz.) crushed pineapple
1/2  cup water
2  pkg (3 oz. each) cherry gelatin
1  can (21 oz.) cherry pie filling
3/4  cup cola

      Drain pineapple reserving juice, set fruit aside.  In a saucepan or microwave, bring pineapple juice and water to a boil, add gelatin, stir until dissolved, stir in the pie filling and the cola.  Pour into a serving bowl, refrigerate until slightly thickened, fold in reserved pineapple.  Refrigerate until firm.
