CHEESE BALL


2  (8 oz.) Philadelphia cream cheese
1  can crushed pineapple, drained
2  c. nuts (pecans)
1/4  c. green pepper
3  Tbsp. chopped onions
1  tsp. salt

      Put in blender green pepper and chopped onions.  Mix and put on wax paper.  Form into ball.  Put in refrigerator overnight. Shape more and roll in pecans.
