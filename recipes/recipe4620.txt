SPAGHETTI SALAD


1  lb. box spaghetti
1 small  bottle Zesty Italian dressing
1/2  bottle salad seasoning
1  bell pepper
1  red onion
1  cucumber
1  large tomato

      Cook spaghetti and cool.  Add dressing and 1/2 bottle salad seasoning.  Cut up vegetables small and add.  Let stand overnight. Tomato may be added just before serving.
