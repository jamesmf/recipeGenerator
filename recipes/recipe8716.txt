RAVIOLI CASSEROLE


1 (28 to 32 oz.)  jar spaghetti sauce, divided
1 (25 oz.)  pkg. frozen cheese ravioli, cooked and drained, divided
2  c. small-curd cottage cheese, divided
4  c. shredded Mozzarella cheese, divided
1/4  c. grated Parmesan cheese

	Preheat oven to 350 degrees;.  Spread 1/2 cup spaghetti sauce in bottom of 13 x 9 x 2-inch baking pan. Layer half of ravioli, 1 1/4 cups of spaghetti sauce, 1 cup of cottage cheese and 2 cups of Mozzarella.  Repeat layering.  Sprinkle Parmesan cheese on top. Bake, uncovered, for 30 to 40 minutes or until bubbly.  Let stand 5 to 10 minutes before cutting. Serves 6 to 8.
