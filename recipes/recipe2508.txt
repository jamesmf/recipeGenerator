DILLY SHRIMP DIP


1  can shrimp
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese (softened)
1/4  c. mayonnaise
1/4  c. milk
1  Tbsp. chopped onion
1/8  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. dill weed
1/2  tsp. Tabasco sauce

      Drain  and rinse shrimp.  Mix all ingredients together. Serve with crackers, fresh vegetables or potato chips.
