POTATO TOPPER


3/4  c. plain nonfat yogurt
2  Tbsp. prepared horseradish
1  Tbsp. fresh chives, chopped
1/4  c. red onion, chopped

      Mix yogurt, horseradish, onion and chives together. Serve heated with roast beef or chilled for baked potatoes.
       Serving Size: 1 tablespoon. Cal-8, CHO-1, Pro-1, Fat-0.
