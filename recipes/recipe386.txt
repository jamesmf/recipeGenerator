TROPICAL FRUIT SHERBET


2 1/4  c. nonfat buttermilk
1  c. peeled, cubed mango
3  medium kiwi fruit, peeled and quartered
1  medium banana, peeled and sliced
1 (6 oz.)  can frozen orange juice concentrate, thawed (undiluted)
1/4  c. lime juice
3  Tbsp. honey
1/4  tsp. coconut extract

      Mix all ingredients in electric blender or food processor until smooth (make sure the  top is on the blender or food processor).  Pour into 1  gallon  ice cream freezer and freeze according to  manufacturer's  instructions.  Serve immediately when ready.  Makes 7 (1/2-cup) servings.
      Per Serving:  73 Calories, 0.4 g. Fat, 0 mg.  Cholesterol and 42 mg.  Sodium.
