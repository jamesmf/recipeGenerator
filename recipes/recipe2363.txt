SOUTHERN CORNBREAD DRESSING WITH GIBLET GRAVY

Giblet Gravy:
1  qt. broth (chicken or turkey)
1  hard-boiled egg
  a little milk
3/4 to 1  c. cooked chopped giblets
1 to 2  Tbsp. cornstarch or flour
  salt, pepper and poultry seasoning

      Cook giblets in enough water to cover until tender.  Chop and set aside.  Heat broth and season.  Stir a little milk gradually into flour or cornstarch until smooth.  Add a little hot broth to this; stir until fairly thin and return to pot.  Simmer until a nice consistency (not too thick).  Add hard-boiled egg and giblets. Serve in gravy boat.
