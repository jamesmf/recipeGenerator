PONSETT


1  lb. boneless beef, pork or chicken, cut into 1-inch cubes
  black pepper
  garlic powder
1  c. shredded carrot
1  c. diced celery
1  bunch green onion, diced
1  can sliced mushrooms
  soy sauce
1  lb. pasta (any style)

      Season meat with pepper and garlic powder.  Saut√© in 2 tablespoons oil.  When browned, add approximately 1/4 to 1/3 cup soy sauce.  Cover and simmer while cooking pasta.  When pasta is half done, turn vegetables into meat.  Stir.  Recover and finish pasta.  Drain pasta and turn into meat and vegetable mixture. Toss to combine.  Serve at once.
      Takes about 20 minutes to cook.  Serves 3 to 6 (depends on whether your serve salad).  Colorful and very satisfying!
