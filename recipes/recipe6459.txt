BAKED CHICKEN AND RICE


1  chicken, cut up
1  c. rice (uncooked)
1/2  stick margarine
1  envelope onion soup mix
4  c. water

      Melt margarine in baking dish.  Sprinkle onion soup mix over margarine.  Salt and pepper chicken and place in pan.  Pour rice over chicken.  Pour water in pan.  Cover.  Bake at 350 degrees; for about 1 hour.
