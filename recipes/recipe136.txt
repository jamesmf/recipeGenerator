CHOCOLATE SUNDAE COOKIES


1 1/2  c. flour
1/2  tsp. each: soda and salt
2/3  c. brown sugar
1/2  c. shortening
1  unbeaten egg
1/4  c. maraschino cherry juice
2  Tbsp. milk
2  sq. (2 oz.) unsweetened chocolate, melted
1/2  c. chopped walnuts
1/4  c. maraschino cherries, chopped
18  marshmallows, cut in half
2  Tbsp. butter
2  blocks Hershey's chocolate
3  Tbsp. milk
1 1/2  c. confectioners sugar
1/2  tsp. vanilla
  pinch of salt

      Sift together first 3 ingredients; set aside.  Add brown sugar to shortening.  Blend in egg.  Stir in half the dry ingredients. Add cherry juice and 2 tablespoons milk; stir in remaining dry ingredients.  Mix well.  Blend in chocolate, walnuts and cherries. Drop by rounded teaspoonful onto ungreased cookie sheet.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 12 to 15 minutes.  Cut marshmallows in half and place on hot cookies.
      For Icing:  Melt butter and chocolate in top of double boiler; stir until blended.  Stir 3 tablespoons milk into sugar and beat until smooth.  Add vanilla, pinch of salt and chocolate mixture. Beat until smooth and thickened.  Frost cookies; top with nut halves.
