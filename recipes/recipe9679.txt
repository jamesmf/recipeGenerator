BREAKFAST DELIGHT


8  slices bread, cubed
2  pkg. cream cheese, softened
1  c. milk
1  c. maple syrup
12  eggs, beaten

      Arrange bread cubes in greased casserole dish. Beat eggs well; add milk, cream cheese and maple syrup and pour over bread. Let set overnight in refrigerator. Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.
      Options: Sprinkle with cinnamon or crumbled bacon.
