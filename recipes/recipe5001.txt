MARINATED VEGETABLE SALAD


  Small can of whole kernel corn
  Small can of English peas
  Small can of Lima Beans
16  oz. can of French-style green beans
  Small jar of pimento
  Green and/or red pepper slices
  Onion slices

 
Dressing:
1/2  c. oil
3/4  c. vinegar
1  c. sugar

      Boil and cool.  Pour over vegetables and let set overnight.
