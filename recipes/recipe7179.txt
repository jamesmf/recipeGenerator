DUTCH OVEN DINNER


1 1/2  lb. round steak
6  small carrots
2  stalks celery
3  slices onion
4  Tbsp. bacon fat
3  large potatoes, halved
3  c. water
1  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper
1  Tbsp. flour
2  Tbsp. water

      Cut steak into 6 individual servings.  Rub with salt and pepper.  Dredge with flour.  Pound slightly as for Swiss steak. On each piece of meat, place a small carrot, cut into quarters, slice onion and celery.  Wrap steak around vegetables.  Fasten with toothpick.  Brown on all sides in bacon fat.  Add the halved potatoes and 3/4 cup water.  Cover and when boiling, turn to simmer for 1 hour.  Remove to heated platter.  Thicken the drippings with flour blended with 2 tablespoons water.  Serve over each portion.  Serves 6.
