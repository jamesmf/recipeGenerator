BEEF AND DRESSING CASSEROLE


1/2  pkg. sage and onion dressing (Brownberry Farms)
1 1/2  lb. ground beef
3  stalks celery
1  medium onion
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  can cream of celery soup
1  can mushrooms (optional)

      Put dressing in a bowl with 1 cup hot water.  Cover and let stand (set aside).  Brown hamburger with chopped celery and onion - no salt.  Add cream of chicken soup and put in bottom of a casserole.  Spread dressing on top of meat.  Add 1/2 cup water to cream of celery soup and pour over all.  May add mushrooms to this if desired.  Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees; for 50 to 60 minutes.
