DEVILED EGGS


1  doz. eggs, boiled
1/2  lb. bacon, fried crisp, drained and crumbled
1/2  c. chopped green onions
1  tsp. mustard
4  Tbsp. mayonnaise
  salt and pepper
  paprika
  olives

      Halve eggs.  Mix together yolks, bacon, onions, mustard and mayonnaise and salt and pepper to taste.  Spoon into egg white halves.  Sprinkle with paprika and top with olive halves.
