SPICE CUPCAKES


2  eggs
1/2  c. oleo
1/2  Tbsp. sweetener
3/4  c. skim milk
1 1/2  c. flour
1 1/2  tsp. baking powder
1/4  tsp. soda
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. cloves
1/4  tsp. cinnamon
1/2  tsp. nutmeg
1/4  c. chopped raisins

      Cream eggs, oleo and sweetener together.  Add skim milk.  Then add remaining ingredients, except raisins.  Then fold in raisins. Bake 15 minutes at 350 degrees; for 30 to 35 minutes as a cake.
