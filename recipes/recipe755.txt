BISQUICK NOODLES


1  c. Bisquick
1  beaten egg

      Work Bisquick into egg.  Roll and stretch dough until paper thin.  Cut into 1 1/2-inch squares or regular noodle strips.  Cover lightly with a towel and allow to dry at least 6 hours.  Drop into simmering broth or thin gravy.  Cover and cook gently about 15 minutes or until tender.  Serves 4 to 6.
