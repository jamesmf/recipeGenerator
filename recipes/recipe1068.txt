GRAPE JELLY


1 1/2  c. pure grape juice (unsweetened)
1  tsp. lemon juice
2  tsp. Knox gelatine
1/2  c. cold water

      Combine grape and lemon juice.  Bring to a boil.  Reduce heat and simmer for 4 minutes.  Soak gelatine in cold water.  When dissolved, add to hot grape juice.  When cool, pour into small jars and refrigerate.  This is about 14 or 15 calories per tablespoon.
