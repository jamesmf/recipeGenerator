CHERRY CRISP

Fruit Layer:
1 (16 oz.)  can red sour pitted cherries
1 1/2  Tbsp. cornstarch
1/4  tsp. almond extract
4  pkg. Sweet 'N Low

 
Topping:
1/2  c. quick oats
2  Tbsp. chopped walnuts
1  Tbsp. oleo, melted

      Fruit layer:  Drain cherries; reserve 3/4 cup juice.  Combine small amount of juice, cornstarch and Sweet 'N Low in saucepan. Stir in remaining juice.  Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly until thickened and clear.  Remove from heat.  Add cherries and extract.  Put in 8 x 8-inch baking dish.
      Topping:  Mix oats and walnuts in small bowl.  Add oleo; mix well with fork.  Mixture will be crumbly.  Sprinkle topping over cherries.  Bake at 375 degrees; for 20 minutes or until top is brown.
      Exchanges:  1/2 cup serving equals 1/2 bread, 1 fat and 1 fruit.
