FRUIT DELIGHT


1 large  can Peach Pie Filling
2 small  cans Mandarin Oranges, drained
1  can chunky Fruit Cocktail, drained
1  can chunky Pineapple, drained into bowl
  2-3 Bananas, cut up in pineapple juice
1  box frozen strawberries, thawed

      Mix all together and refrigerate about 2 hours before serving.
