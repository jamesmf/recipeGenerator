COTTAGE CHEESE SALAD


1  lb. container small curd creamy cottage cheese
1/2  c. minced onions
1/2  c. minced red and green bell pepper
1/2  c. chopped fresh tomato
  salt and pepper
1/2  tsp. garlic powder (optional)

      Mix all ingredients. Cover and refrigerate. Serve on lettuce leaf with a slice of tomato or cut a whole tomato into 4 wedges and fill center with cottage cheese.
