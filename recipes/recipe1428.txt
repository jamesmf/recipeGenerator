APRICOT CHICKEN CUTLETS


3  lb. chicken

 
Marinade:
1/2  c. Dijon mustard
1/2  c. brown sugar
1/4  c. soy sauce
1/4  c. whiskey
2  tsp. Worcestershire sauce

      Combine ingredients and marinate chicken in refrigerator for 2 to 3 hours.
Basting Sauce:
1/2  c. apricot preserves
1/4  c. white vinegar
4 1/4  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  Tbsp. honey
1/2  tsp. crushed or regular black pepper

      In saucepan on stove, simmer sauce ingredients for 15 minutes. Grill chicken for a few minutes, then baste with sauce.  Turn and baste again.  Grill chicken to completion and use up sauce.
