CHICKEN-RICE CASSEROLE


  chicken breasts (1 to 1 1/2 lb. boneless, skinless), cut in bite size pieces
1  can cream chicken soup
1  can cream mushroom soup
1 soup  can water
1  c. Minute rice
1  Tbsp. butter
1  Tbsp. onion flakes
1/2  tsp. garlic powder
  salt and pepper to taste

      Put in crock-pot and cook slow all day.
