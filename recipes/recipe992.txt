CAULIFLOWER SOUP


2  c. chopped celery
3/4  c. onion slices
6  Tbsp. margarine
1/3  c. flour
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
4 1/2  c. chicken broth
3  c. milk
3 (10 oz.)  pkg. frozen cauliflower, cooked, drained and chopped
2  c. shredded Monterey Jack cheese

      Saut√© celery and onions in margarine.  Blend in flour and seasonings.  Gradually add broth and milk.  Cook, stirring constantly until thickened.  Add cauliflower and cheese.  Simmer until cheese is melted.
