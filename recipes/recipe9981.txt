CABBAGE CASSEROLE


1  medium cabbage, cut up
1  lb. ground meat
1  lb. smoke sausage
1 1/2  tsp. salt
1  Tbsp. chili powder
1  c. uncooked rice
1/4  c. cooking oil
1  medium onion, chopped
1  medium bell pepper
1  tsp. minced garlic
1  (16 oz.) whole tomatoes

	Mix all ingredients raw in a large bowl, then put into 9 x 13-inch pan and cook for 1 1/2 to 2 hours (maybe more depending on your oven). Stir 2 to 3 times during cooking. Cover to cook.
