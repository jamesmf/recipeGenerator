EASTER MORNING BREAKFAST


1  lb. bulk Bob Evans sausage
6  eggs, beaten
2  c. milk
6  slices bread, cubed
1  tsp. mustard
1  tsp. salt
1  c. Velveeta cheese, cut in small cubes

      Brown the sausage; drain.  Add the other ingredients.  Bake in an 8 x 12-inch pan at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes.  This may be mixed and put in the refrigerator overnight if using for breakfast. Serves 6.
