BROCCOLI-CAULIFLOWER SALAD


1  bunch broccoli, cut and separated into florets
1  head cauliflower, cut and separated into florets
8  bacon strips, fried and crumbled
1/3  c. chopped onions
1/3  c. sugar (can use artificial sweetener)
1  c. mayonnaise or salad dressing (fat-free is fine)
2  Tbsp. vinegar

      In a large bowl, combine broccoli, cauliflower, crumbled bacon and onions; set aside.
      In another bowl, combine mayonnaise or salad dressing, sugar or sweetener and vinegar; mix until smooth. Pour dressing over salad. Toss and chill.
