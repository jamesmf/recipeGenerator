MEXICAN TAMALE SOUP


1  lb. hamburger
1  onion, chopped
1  bell pepper, chopped
1  can beef broth
1/4  tsp. garlic powder
  salt to taste
1 large  can tamales, drained
1  can stewed tomatoes
1 (10 oz.)  can cream-style corn
2  cans beans with jalapeno (if you like it hot)
1/2  tsp. cumin seed
1  Tbsp. cornstarch

   Brown meat, onion and pepper. To this, add beef broth, tomatoes, corn, beans and seasonings. Simmer 1/2 hour; just before serving, put in cornstarch. Also, just before serving, cut tamales in 1-inch pieces. Heat and serve.
