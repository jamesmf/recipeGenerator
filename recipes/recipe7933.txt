BEEF TACO BAKE


1  lb. ground beef, turkey or venison
1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can condensed tomato soup
1  cup salsa
1/2  cup milk
1  cup shredded cheddar cheese
6  flour tortillas (cut into 1 inch pieces)

      In skillet, over medium heat, cook meat until brown. Drain. Add soup, salsa, milk, tortillas and half of the cheese.  Spoon into 2 quart shallow baking dish.  Cover.  Bake at 400 degrees for 30 minutes or until hot.  Sprinkle with remaining cheese. Serves 4.
