RIGATONI II


1  lb. ground beef
1/2  c. onion
1 (32 oz.)  jar meatless spaghetti sauce
1  tsp. pepper
1 (16 oz.)  carton cottage cheese
1  c. milk
1  tsp. salt
1 (16 oz.)  box rigatoni
  Parmesan cheese
2  c. Mozzarella cheese, shredded

	In a large skillet, lightly brown ground beef. Add onion. On medium heat, stir in sauce and pepper. Reduce heat and simmer for 10 to 15 minutes. In a bowl, stir cottage cheese, milk and salt. Add rigatoni after cooking it about 10 minutes. Stir good. Spoon 1/2 of rigatoni on bottom of pan; top with sauce and Mozzarella cheese. Repeat, then sprinkle Parmesan cheese on top. Bake at 350 degrees; for about 20 minutes.
