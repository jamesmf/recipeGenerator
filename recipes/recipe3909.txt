SHRIMP MOLD


1  can tomato soup
1  (8 oz.) cream cheese
1  lb. shrimp, chopped
3/4  c. chopped celery
1/2  c. chopped onion
1  c. mayonnaise
1 1/2  pkg. plain gelatin, dissolved in 1/4 c. water

      Bring soup to boil. Add cream cheese and stir until melted. Add gelatin to water and let stand 5 minutes. Add gelatin to hot soup and cool. Add celery, onion, shrimp and mayonnaise. Put in mold and refrigerate.
      To serve, unmold and serve with crackers.
