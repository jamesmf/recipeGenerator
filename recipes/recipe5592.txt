BACON-SWISS TOSSED SALAD


1/2  c. Mayonnaise
1  tbsp. Sugar
1/4  tsp. Salt
1/4  tsp. Pepper
6  c. Mixed Salad Greens
1  med. Red Onion, sliced
1  pkg. (10 oz.) frozen English Peas
8  oz. Swiss Cheese
1  lb. Bacon, cooked and crumbled

      In a small bowl, combine mayonnaise, sugar, salt and pepper. Layer salad.  Repeat layers twice. Cover and refrigerate.
