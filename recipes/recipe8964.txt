HERBED PORK WITH ORANGE SAUCE


1  pork loin (3 to 4 lb.)
2  tsp. thyme
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. sage
1/4  tsp. whole allspice
1/4  tsp. pepper

 
Sauce:
2 (12 oz.)  jars orange marmalade
1/4  c. Dijon mustard
1/4  c. Worcestershire sauce
1  tsp. ground ginger
4  garlic cloves, minced

      Rub pork loin with herb mixture. Roast in 425 degrees; oven for 60 to 90 minutes until done.
      Prepare sauce in small saucepan. Mix all ingredients and bring to a boil over medium heat.
      To Serve: Slice pork into 1-inch thick slices. Pour sauce over the top.
