CHICKEN WITH STUFFING


4  boneless skinless chicken breast halves
1  can condensed cream of chicken soup
1 1/4  c. water
1/4  c. butter or margarine, melted
1  pkg. -6 oz. corn bread stuffing

      Place chicken in a greased slow cooker.  Top with soup.  In a bowl combine the water, butter, and stuffing mix.  Spoon over chicken.  Cover and cook on low for 4 hours or until chicken juices run clear.
