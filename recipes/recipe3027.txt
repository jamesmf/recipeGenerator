SALSA


6  c. tomatoes, chopped
1  c. onions, chopped
1/2  c. green peppers or jalapen peppers
1  Tbsp. oregano
2 to 3  Tbsp. ground cumin seed
1  Tbsp. salt
1/4  c. vinegar
2  Tbsp. sugar

      Mix all ingredients.  Cook 30 minutes to 1 hour or until desired consistency.  Process in jars.  I use a water bath for 15 to 30 minutes.
