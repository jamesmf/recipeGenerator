CRANBERRY AND PINEAPPLE JELLO MOLD


2 small  pkg. raspberry Jell-O or any red Jell-O
1 1/4  c. boiling water
1 large  can crushed pineapple
1  can whole cranberry sauce
1  c. nuts, chopped
3/4  c. Port wine (optional)

 
Topping:
1  c. sour cream
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese, softened

      Dissolve Jell-O in boiling water. Stir in pineapple and cranberry sauce and wine if desired. Chill until mixture thickens slightly. Fold in nuts and turn into 2-quart serving dish. Chill until firm. When Jell-O is set, gradually beat sour cream into softened cream cheese until smooth. Spread over Jell-O. Chill.
