LEMON CHICKEN NUGGETS


1/2  c. flour
2  tsp. seasoned salt
2  tsp. paprika
1  tsp. onion powder
1  tsp. oregano
1/4  tsp. garlic powder
1  lb. chicken
1 1/2  tbsp. lemon juice

      Cube chicken into bite size pieces. Marinate 30 mins. in lemon juice.  Coat with above breading and fry until golden brown.
