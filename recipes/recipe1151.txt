BAKED ZUCCHINI CASSEROLE


4  medium zucchini
1/4  c. bread crumbs
2  Tbsp. Parmesan cheese
1/2  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. garlic salt
1/4  tsp. oregano
1/8  tsp. pepper
4  eggs

      Wash, trim and shred zucchini.  Mix zucchini in bowl with all the other ingredients, except Parmesan cheese and eggs.  Spoon the mixture into a greased baking dish.  Beat the 4 eggs and pour over zucchini.  Mix lightly with a fork.  Bake at 325 degrees; for 35 minutes or until the top is set.  Top with Parmesan cheese.  Makes 4 servings; 130 calories per serving.
      Exchanges per serving:  2 vegetables, 1 meat and 1/2 fat.
