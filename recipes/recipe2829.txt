APPETIZER QUICHE

Crust:
1 1/2  c. plain flour
1 1/2  c. crushed Ritz type crackers
2/3  c. butter-flavored Crisco

 
Filling:
2  c. shredded Swiss cheese
2/3  c. each chopped ham, crumbled, cooked sausage, diced pepperoni or crumbled cooked bacon
3/4  c. sliced green onions
1/4  c. snipped parsley
1 (4 oz.)  jar pimentos
5  eggs
1  c. whipping cream
1  c. half and half milk
1  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper

      Preheat oven to 400 degrees;.
      For Crust:  Combine flour and cracker crumbs.  Cut in Crisco until mixture resembles cornmeal.  Add 1/2 cup water.  Press mixture into bottom of ungreased 15 x 10-inch pan.  Prick with fork.
      For Filling:  Sprinkle cheese, ham, onions, parsley and pimentos evenly over crust.  Beat eggs, cream, half and half, salt and pepper.  Pour over filled crust.  Bake at 400 degrees; for 25 to 30 minutes.  Cool 5 to 10 minutes.  Cut into pieces and serve warm. Makes about fifty 1 1/2 x 2-inch appetizers.  Or can be served as larger portions with a salad for a meal.
