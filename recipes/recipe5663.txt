CHICKEN SALAD WITH FRUIT


4  to 6 chicken breasts, cooked and chopped
3  hard-boiled eggs, chopped
2  stalks celery, finely chopped
1/4  c. sweet pickle relish
1/4  c. green olives, chopped
  salt and pepper to taste
1  tsp. sugar
1/2  c. pecans or walnuts, chopped
3/4  c. seedless grapes, sliced
1  Golden Delicious apple, peeled, cored and diced
1  c. Miracle Whip or mayonnaise (more or less)

	In a large bowl, combine all ingredients, except dressing. Mix thoroughly. Add enough of the Miracle Whip or mayonnaise to properly moisten salad. May need more or less than 1 cup.
