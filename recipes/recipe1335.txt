HERBED CORN-ON-THE-COB


1  Tbsp. dried whole dill weed
1  Tbsp. water
1  clove garlic, minced
4 medium  ears frozen corn-on-the-cob
1  Tbsp. dried whole thyme
1  Tbsp. vegetable oil

      Combine dill weed, water, garlic, thyme and oil in a small bowl; stir well.  Spread herb mixture over corn and place each ear on a piece of heavy-duty foil; wrap tightly.  Bake at 450 degrees; for 25 minutes, turning occasionally.  Yields 4 servings.
