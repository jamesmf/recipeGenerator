RAFFERTY'S POTATO SOUP


6  to 7 medium potatoes, peeled and diced
1  medium onion, chopped fine
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
1/2  stick butter
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  can cream of celery soup
1 1/2  c. or more milk
1  tsp. garlic salt
  salt and pepper to taste

      In a large saucepan, cook potatoes and onion in water until tender.  Remove from heat.  Add cream cheese until melted.  Add rest of ingredients and serve hot.
