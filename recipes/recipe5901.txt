FANTASTIC SALAD


1 large  box orange Jell-O
1  large Cool Whip
1  can Mandarin oranges, drained
1 large  can crushed pineapple, drained
1  (16 oz.) cottage cheese
1/2  c. pecans, chopped

      Sprinkle dry Jell-O over Cool Whip in large bowl, being careful not to deflate Cool Whip. Add other ingredients and spoon into serving dish. Chill.
