MEAT LOAF


2  lb. ground beef
4  Tbsp. green pepper
4  slices bread
3/4  c. milk
2  eggs
3  Tbsp. catsup
1  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. black pepper
1/2  tsp. garlic salt
1/4  c. chopped onions
3/4  c. catsup
1  tsp. dry mustard
1/4  c. brown sugar

      Mix first 10 ingredients together and form into loaf.  Top with 3/4 cup catsup, dry mustard and brown sugar.
