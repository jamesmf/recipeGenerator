IT CASSEROLE


1  lb. shell macaroni (creamettes)
1  lb. hamburger
1  onion, chopped
22  oz. (2 cans) soup, tomato
10  oz. (1 can) soup, mushroom
8  oz. (1 pkg.) cream cheese
  salt (to taste)
  pepper (to taste)

      Cook macaroni according to package directions.  Drain and set aside.
      Brown hamburger and onions.  Add soups and cream cheese.  Stir on medium heat until cream cheese melts.  Add cooked macaroni, salt and pepper.  Mix well.  Place in a 9x13-inch casserole dish.
      Bake at 350 degrees; for 35 minutes.
