HUSH PUPPIES


1/2  c. sifted flour
1  Tbsp. sugar
1/2  tsp. salt
1 1/2  c. corn meal
1  small onion, finely minced
1  egg, beaten
3/4  c. milk
  oil

      Sift together flour, sugar, salt and corn meal.  Add onion. Combine egg and milk.  Stir into flour mixture.  Mix well.  Drop mixture from a teaspoon into hot oil and fry until lightly browned.
