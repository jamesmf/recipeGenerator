CRUNCHY CHICKEN SALAD


5 1/2  c. chopped, cooked chicken*
3  hard-cooked eggs, chopped (optional)
1  c. chopped celery
1  c. cubed sweet pickles
1  c. chopped pecans, toasted
1  c. seedless green grapes, halved
1  c. mayonnaise
1 (8 oz.)  can chopped water chestnuts, drained
1/2  c. olives, chopped (optional)
1  small onion, chopped

      Combine all ingredients; blend well.  Chill.  Serve on lettuce cups or use for sandwiches.  Yields 12 servings.
      *I buy the 2 cans of chicken at Sam's and it is the correct amount.
