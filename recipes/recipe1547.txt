SHERBET COOLER PUNCH


1 (48 oz.)  can pineapple juice
1 (48 oz.)  can orange juice
1 (48 oz.)  can Hawaiian Punch
1/2  c. lemon juice
3  bananas (riper the better)
3  c. sugar
1/2  gal. sherbet (any flavor but lime)
4  qt. 7-Up or ginger ale

      Mix the 3 juices.  Put lemon juice, sugar and bananas in blender.  Puree.  Pour into juices.  Add sherbet and let melt. Pour into containers and freeze.  When ready to serve, mix with 7-Up and stir until slushy.
