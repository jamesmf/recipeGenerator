QUICHE LORRAINE


1 1/2  c. grated cheese
8  slices cooked bacon, crumbled
3  eggs, slightly beaten
1  c. heavy cream
1/2  c. milk
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
1/2  tsp. dry mustard
1  Mrs. Smith's frozen pie shell

      Sprinkle cheese and bacon on bottom of frozen pie shell. Combine remaining ingredients, beat slightly.  Pour batter over top.  Bake for 45 minutes in 375 degree oven.
