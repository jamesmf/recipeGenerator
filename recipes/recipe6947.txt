QUICK TUNA CASSEROLE


1  can cream of mushroom soup
1/3  c. milk
1 (7 oz.)  can tuna, drained and flaked
1 1/4  c. crushed potato chips
1  c. cooked or canned peas

      Combine soup and milk in casserole dish. Add tuna, 1 cup potato chips and peas; blend. Sprinkle remaining chips on top. Bake at 350 degrees; for 25 minutes.
