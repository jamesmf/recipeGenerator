DIFFERENT MEAT LOAF


1 1/2  lb. lean ground beef
1/2  lb. ground pork
1/2  c. celery, chopped
1/2  c. onion, chopped
2  cloves minced garlic
1/2  c. green pepper, chopped
1  tsp. Emeril's Essence Bam
1  (8 oz.) container sour cream
1  egg, beaten
1/2  c. Pepperidge Farm stuffing

	Preheat oven to 350 degrees;.  Mix all together and shape into loaf. Pat more Pepperidge Farm stuffing on top. Bake for 1 1/2 hours.
