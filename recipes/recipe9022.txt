SPAGHETTI SAUCE


2  lb. hamburger or ground chuck
3  c. chopped onions
4  cans tomatoes or 2 large cans crushed tomatoes
1  can tomato paste
1  can tomato puree
1 garlic clove, chop whole one or add 1  Tbsp. crushed
  salt to taste
1  Tbsp. sugar
1  Tbsp. apple pie spice
1  Tbsp. cinnamon
1  tsp. Italian seasoning
4  bay leaves (if desired)
1/2  stick margarine

      Use only spices you might like in sauce.
      Brown onions and hamburger meat together. Skim grease out of hamburger and onions. Add other ingredients in crock-pot or regular pot and cook from 4 to 6 hours.
