ITALIAN EGGS


5  boiled eggs, sliced
4  Tbsp. butter
5  Tbsp. flour
1 small  can tomatoes
1/2  c. chopped celery
2  Tbsp. chopped onion
3/4  tsp. salt
1  c. grated cheese

      Melt butter.  Add flour.  When well blended, stir tomatoes, celery, onions and cheese into this.  Cook until slightly thickened.  Add salt and 1/3 teaspoon paprika.  Turn into buttered casserole, sprinkled with bread crumbs.  Dot with butter.  Bake in 350 degrees; oven for 30 minutes.
