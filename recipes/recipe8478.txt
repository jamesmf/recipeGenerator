LASAGNA


1  lb. ground beef
1  clove garlic
1  tbsp. basil
1 1/2   tsp. salt
  1-16 oz. can of tomatoes
12  ounces tomato paste
2  eggs
3  c. Ricotta cheese (cottage cheese may be rinsed)
1/2  c. Parmesan cheese
2  tbsp. parsley flakes
1  tsp. pepper
1  lb. Mozzarella cheese
1 10  oz. pkg.  lasagna noodles

      Brown meat and remove fat. Add next 5 ingredients. Simmer 30 minutes. Meanwhile, in separate dish beat eggs. Add remaining ingredients (except Mozzarella cheese). Set aside, then cook lasagna noodles until tender. In a 13x9x2 inch pan, arrange a layer of noodles, then 1/2 of cheese mixture, then a layer of Mozzarella cheese, then meat sauce. Repeat, making sure to end with meat sauce on top. Bake at 375 degrees; for 30 minutes.  Let stand 10 minutes before serving.
