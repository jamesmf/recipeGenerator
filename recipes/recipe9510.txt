CITRUS GLAZED LEG OF LAMB


1/2  c. honey
1/2  c. water
2  Tbsp. cornstarch
1/4  tsp. cinnamon
1/4  tsp. nutmeg
1  small orange, sliced and seeded
1  lemon, sliced and seeded
1/4  c. raisins
1/4  c. walnuts or pecans
1  (5 to 6 lb.) bone-in leg of lamb

      For glaze:  Combine cornstarch, cinnamon and nutmeg in saucepan.  Stir in honey and water.  Add orange and lemon slices. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly.  Reduce heat and simmer 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Remove and discard orange and lemon slices.  Add walnuts and raisins.
      Place leg of lamb on rack in roasting pan, fat side up.  Bake, uncovered, at 325 degrees;, 30 minutes per pound or 170 degrees; on meat thermometer for medium.  Brush glaze on lamb during the final 30 minutes of baking, reserving some glaze to serve with the leg of lamb at the table.
