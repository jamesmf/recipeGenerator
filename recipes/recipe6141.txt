TACO SALAD


1/2  lb. hamburger
1  small onion, diced
1  can kidney beans
2  tomatoes, diced
1  pkg. taco seasoning
1  head of lettuce, shredded
1 1/2  c. shredded cheese
1  small bag nacho chips

      Fry hamburger and diced onion until done.  Add kidney beans and taco seasoning, cool.  Mix in diced tomatoes, shredded lettuce and cheese.  Add dressing, recipe follows.  Break up chips and mix into bowl.
