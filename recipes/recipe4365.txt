PISTACHIO SALAD


1 (3 oz.)  box pistachio pudding
1  c. miniature marshmallows
  Nuts and Maraschino cherries
1 (20 oz.)  can crushed pineapple with juice
1  c. Cool Whip

      Combine pudding powder and all other ingredients, in a serving bowl, mix well.  Decorate with nuts and cherries and let set.
