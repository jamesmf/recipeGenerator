SINFUL SALAD


8  oz. sliced water chestnuts, soaked in almond extract overnight
3  bananas
4  c. frozen strawberries, sliced
3  c. crushed pineapple
1  c. boiling water
1 (6 oz.)  pkg. strawberry jello (sugar-free)
2  c. plain yogurt

      Mix fruit and drained chestnuts. Dissolve jello in water. Mix with fruit. Pour 1/2 of mixture in 9 x 13-inch pan and refrigerate 1 hour, then spread yogurt over all. Put other 1/2 of jello on top. Refrigerate. Makes 16 servings.
