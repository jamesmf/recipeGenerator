SIMPLY DELICIOUS PORK CHOPS


6  pork chops (3/4-inch thick)
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  Tbsp. lemon juice
1  Tbsp. catsup
2  Tbsp. cooking oil
1  Tbsp. soy sauce

      Arrange chops in single layer in a baking dish. In small bowl, combine oil, catsup, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce and soy sauce. Spread half of this over chops. Bake, uncovered, 30 minutes at 350 degrees;. Turn chops; spread remaining mix. Bake 30 minutes more uncovered. Chops can be grilled using mix for basting, but double the sauce amount.
