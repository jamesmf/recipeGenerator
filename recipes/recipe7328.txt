CRISP BAKED FISH FILETS


1  lb. fish filets
2  Tbsp. oil
1/3  c. cornflake crumbs
  salt and pepper to taste

      Preheat oven to 500 degrees;. Wash and dry filets and cut into serving pieces. Dip in oil and coat with cornflake crumbs. Arrange in single layer in a lightly oiled shallow baking dish so that the pieces do not touch each other. Season. Bake 10 minutes without turning or basting. Serves 4.
