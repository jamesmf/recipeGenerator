RICE PILAF


1/2  c. onion, chopped
1/2  c. mushrooms, chopped
1/4  c. celery, chopped
1  garlic clove, minced
1  Tbsp. butter or margarine
3/4  c. long grain rice
1 1/2  tsp. instant chicken bouillon granules
1/8  tsp. pepper
2  slices bacon, cooked and crumbled
1 1/2  c. water

      Cook onion, mushrooms, celery and garlic in butter. Stir in rice, bouillon, pepper and water. Bring to boil and reduce heat. Cover and simmer 15 minutes. Add bacon.
