PICKLED EGGS


2   Tbsp. sugar
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. mixed spices
2  cups cider vinegar
  12-16 hard boiled eggs

      Add sugar, salt and spices to vinegar. Dilute vinegar 1/3 with water if too strong. Simmer about 8 minutes. Strain and pour over the peeled eggs arranged in a quart jar. Seal and let stand two days before using. Add sprig of dill, a few caraway seeds, a slice of garlic, a clove or onions to brine as desired. Chill.
