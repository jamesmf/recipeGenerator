CRANBERRY CAKE


1 (19 oz.)  pkg. lemon cake mix
1 (3 oz.)  pkg. softened cream cheese
3/4  c. milk
4  eggs
1 1/4  c. ground, fresh cranberries, drained
1/2  c. ground walnuts
1/4  c. granulated sugar
1  tsp. mace

      Beat 2 minutes the cake mix, cream cheese and milk.  Mix in the eggs.  Combine.  Fold into batter the cranberries, walnuts, sugar and mace.  Pour into greased 10 x 4-inch tube or 12-cup Bundt pan. Bake in 350 degrees; oven for 60 to 65 minutes.  Cool 20 minutes; remove from pan.  Dust with confectioners sugar.  Yield:  one 10-inch cake.
