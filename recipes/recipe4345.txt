SHOEPEG MARINATED SALAD


1 - 2  cans white shoepeg corn
1  can early young peas
1  can French cut green beans
1 small  can lima beans (optional)
1/2  c. diced onion (purple or white)
1/2  c. diced pepper (green, red or yellow)
1/2 - 3/4  c. diced celery

 
Dressing:
1  c. vinegar
1  c. sugar
1/2  c. vegetable oil

      Mix all ingredients, add dressing.  Keeps for one week  in refrigerator.
