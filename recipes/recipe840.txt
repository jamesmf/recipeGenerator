CHICKEN FRICASSEE CUBAN STYLE


4 to 5  lb. skinned fryer chicken parts
1  tsp. m.s.g.
5  cloves minced garlic
1  onion, chopped
1/2  chopped green bell pepper
2 (15 oz.)  cans tomato sauce
4  bay leaves
1  tsp. cumin seed
1 1/2  tsp. dried leaf oregano
1  c. raisins
1  c. stuffed green olives
1  tsp. cider vinegar

      Place chicken parts in a large deep skillet.  Add m.s.g., garlic, onion, green pepper, tomato sauce, bay leaves, cumin, oregano, raisins, olives and vinegar.  Cover and bring to a boil. Turn heat down and simmer 1 hour, turning chicken occasionally. Remove chicken to a platter and serve the sauce over rice or noodles.
