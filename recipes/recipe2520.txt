MINESTRONE


1  (2 lb.) beef soup bone
2  lb. shin beef, cut up
4  qt. water
1 1/2  lb. tomatoes, peeled
1 (15 oz.)  can tomato sauce
2  medium onions, cut up
1/4  c. chopped parsley
2  cloves crushed garlic
2  Tbsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
2  tsp. basil leaves, crushed
2  c. sliced carrots
2  c. sliced celery
4  c. uncooked shells or macaroni
2  pkg. Italian green beans
10  oz. peas
1  lb. can red kidney beans

      Place soup bone and beef in large pot (8 quart).  Add water; bring to boil; skim.  Add tomatoes, sauce, onions, parsley, garlic, salt, pepper and basil; bring to boil.  Reduce heat and simmer 1 1/2 hours.  Add carrots and celery; cover and simmer 1/2 hour. Remove meat and bones; trim fat off meat and discard.  Cut meat into pieces and return to soup.  Heat to boiling; add uncooked pasta, green beans and peas.  Simmer 20 minutes.  Stir in kidney beans; heat to boiling.  Makes about 6 quarts.
