TAMALE CASSEROLE WITH CORN CHIPS


1  lb. hamburger
1  c. chopped onion
1  clove minced garlic
2 (8 oz.)  cans tomato sauce
1  can condensed beef bouillon
1  pkg. frozen whole kernel corn
1 tall  can pitted ripe olives (black)
1/2  c. chopped parsley
2  Tbsp. chili powder
1  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. Accent
2 (3 1/2 oz.)  pkg. corn chips

      In 2 tablespoons olive oil, brown meat, onion and garlic.  Add the rest of the ingredients, except corn chips.  Heat to boiling. Stir in chips; remove from heat and cool.  Put in a casserole and top with 1 1/2 cups grated American cheese.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour.  Serves 8.
