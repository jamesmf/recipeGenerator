CABBAGE CASSEROLE


1  medium onion, chopped
1  Tbsp. chili powder
1 (16 oz.)  can whole tomatoes
1  medium cabbage, chopped
1  bell pepper, chopped
1  lb. smoked sausage
1  lb. ground meat
1/2  c. cooking oil
1  c. rice
1 1/2  Tbsp. salt
3  cloves garlic

      Grind up sausage and cook down with ground meat; drain.  Mix all ingredients together and bake at 350 degrees; for 90 minutes.
      Note:  If you like spicy, use spicy sausage in place of smoked and add an extra tablespoon of chili powder.
