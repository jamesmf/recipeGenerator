CALICO BEANS


1/2  lb. hamburger
1/2  lb. bacon
1/2  c. chopped onion
1/2  c. catsup
1  tsp. salt
1/4  c. brown sugar
1  tsp. dry mustard
2  tsp. vinegar
1/2  c. white sugar
1  can pork and beans
1  can kidney beans
1  can green lima beans

      Brown the hamburger and cut up bacon. Add the onion and cook slightly. Drain off fat. Add remaining ingredients. Mix well. Bake 1 hour at 350 degrees;.
