CHICKEN AVOCADO SALAD FOR 3


4  breasts of chicken
  salt
1  bay leaf
2  hard boiled eggs
2  stalks celery
2  green onions
  mayonnaise
  avocados

	Cook chicken breasts covered with water.  Add salt and bay leaf.  Bone, skin and cube chicken breasts.  Mash eggs; chop celery and green onions.  Combine; add mayonnaise to consistency you want.  Serve on half avocados.	Garnish any way you want.
