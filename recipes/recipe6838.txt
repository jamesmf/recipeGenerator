SPAGHETTI PIE


1 (7 oz.)  pkg. spaghetti noodles
2  Tbsp. butter or margarine
1/3  c. Parmesan cheese, grated
2  eggs, well beaten

      Cook and drain the spaghetti. While hot, stir in eggs, butter and Parmesan cheese. Shape into a buttered 10-inch pie plate. Top spaghetti with 15 ounces Prego spaghetti sauce. Spread pepperoni over pie. Sprinkle with 1 to 2 cups Mozzarella cheese. Bake at 350 degrees; for 20 minutes. Let stand 5 minutes and cut into wedges.
      Can be refrigerated, if so, bake 30 to 35 minutes.
