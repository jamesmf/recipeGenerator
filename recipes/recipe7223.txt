CHICKEN GUMBO SLOPPY JOES


1  lb. hamburger meat
1  c. chicken gumbo soup
3  Tbsp. ketchup
1  Tbsp. mustard
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper

      Cook hamburger meat and drain off excess fat. In skillet with hamburger, add soup, ketchup, mustard, salt and pepper. Heat this mixture until it bubbles, then serve on a hamburger bun.
