STUFFED GREEN PEPPERS


6  large green peppers
5  c. boiling, salted water
1  lb. ground beef
2  Tbsp. chopped onion
1  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. garlic salt
1  c. cooked rice
1 (15 oz.)  can tomato sauce, divided

      Cut thin slice from stem end of each pepper.  Remove all seeds and membranes.  Wash inside and outside of peppers.  Drop in boiling salted water.  Cook for 5 minutes.  Carefully remove peppers and drain well.
      Cook ground beef and onion in skillet until onion is tender. Drain off excess fat.  Stir in salt and garlic salt.  Add one cup tomato sauce and rice.  Heat well.  Lightly stuff peppers with 1/2 cup mixture each.  Stand peppers upright in ungreased 8 x 8 x 2-inch baking dish.  Pour remaining tomato sauce over peppers. Cover and bake at 350 degrees; for 35 minutes.  Uncover and bake 15 minutes longer.  Yield:  6 servings.
