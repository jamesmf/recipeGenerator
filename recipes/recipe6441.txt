LO-CALORIE SAUERKRAUT SALAD


1  lb. sauerkraut
1/2  cup vinegar
3  tsp. sweetener
3  peppers
1  cup celery
1  medium onion
1  large carrot
  pimento
1  Tbsp. oil

      Rinse sauerkraut.  Drain.  Combine vinegar, sweetener, and boil.  Cool.  Chop rest of ingredients and add sauerkraut.  Add vinegar mixture.  Mix all.  Refrigerate overnight.
