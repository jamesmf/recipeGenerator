BAKED RABBIT


1  wild rabbit, cut up
1  stick butter
  salt and pepper to taste

	Heat oven to 300 degrees;. Place rabbit in 13 x 9-inch baking dish. Melt butter and pour over rabbit. Salt and pepper to taste. Cover with foil and bake until tender 2 1/2 to 3 hours. Pour off liquid and use to make gravy.
