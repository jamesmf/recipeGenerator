CHICKEN DIVAN


10  oz. frozen broccoli, par-boiled
2  chicken breasts, cooked and cubed
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1/2  C. mayonnaise
1/2  tsp. curry powder
1/2  C. shredded cheese of your choice
1/2  C. seasoned bread crumbs
1/4  C. melted butter

      Layer broccoli and chicken cubes in a 7 x 11-inch casserole dish.
      Combine soup, mayonnaise, curry powder; mix well and pour over layered ingredients.
      Sprinkle shredded cheese on top.
      Mix melted butter and bread crumbs; add over cheese.
      Bake in 350 degree oven for 30 minutes or until done.
