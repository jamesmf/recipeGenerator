BROCCOLI GRAPE SALAD


1 1/2  c. broccoli florets
3/4  c. red seedless grapes, halved
1/2  c. chopped celery
1/2  c. chopped green onions
1/2  c. chopped water chestnuts
1/4  c. raisins
1/4  c. almonds, thinly sliced
1/2  c. plain nonfat yogurt
2  Tbsp. low-fat mayonnaise
1  tsp. honey

      In a large bowl, combine broccoli, grapes, celery, onion, water chestnuts, raisins and almonds. Mix yogurt, mayonnaise and honey. Pour dressing over broccoli mixture and gently stir. Cover and refrigerate before serving. Yields 4 servings.
      Serving Size: 1 cup. Cal-110, Carbohydrate-19g, Fat-3, Pro-3.
