HERBED FETA SPREAD


1  (8 oz.) Feta cheese (traditional)
1  (8 oz.) cream cheese
1/2  c. mayonnaise
1/2  c. sour cream
1/3  c. chopped sun-dried tomatoes
2  garlic cloves, minced
2  green onions
2  Tbsp. herbs de Provence
2  tsp. hot sauce
  raw vegetables

      Combine Feta cheese, cream cheese, mayonnaise and sour cream in mixing bowl. Beat with an electric mixer on medium speed until mixture is blended. Add sun-dried tomatoes, garlic, herbs and hot sauce. Mix until spreading consistency. Gently add chopped green onions. Cover and chill until ready to serve. Serve with raw vegetables or crackers.
