BREADED RANCH CHICKEN


3/4  C. crushed corn flakes
3/4  C. parmesan cheese
1  envelope dry ranch salad dressing mix
8  boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1/2  C. butter or margarine, melted

      In a shallow pan, combine corn flakes, dressing mix and parmesan cheese.  Dip chicken in butter, then roll in corn flake mixture to coat.
      Place in a greased 9 x 13-inch pan.  Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.
