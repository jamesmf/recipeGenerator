GOULASH


2  lb. lean, boneless beef chuck (no fat)
2  Tbsp. corn oil
2  medium onions, chopped
2  tsp. salt
2  tsp. paprika
  dash of pepper
3  c. water
2  cloves garlic, minced
1  c. plain low-fat yogurt
1  tsp. caraway seed
2  Tbsp. tomato paste
1  Tbsp. cornstarch or flour

      Cut beef into 1-inch square pieces.  Heat oil and add the meat. When meat is browned on all sides, add the chopped onion and garlic; saut√© 2 minutes.  Add salt, paprika, pepper, tomato paste, caraway seed and flour or cornstarch.  Stir thoroughly, add water and simmer very slowly for 1 1/2 hours or until meat is tender. Stir in yogurt.  Remove from heat immediately.  Serves 6.
      Exchange per serving:  3 meats, 1/2 milk, 1 fat.
