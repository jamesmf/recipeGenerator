EASY CHILI


1  1/2 lbs. ground beef
1  lg. onion, chopped
1  small green pepper, chopped
2  garlic cloves, minced
2 (16 oz.)  cans hot chili beans, drained
2 (16 oz.)  cans diced tomatoes
2 to 3  Tbsp. chili powder
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. pepper
1  tsp. ground cumin
  16-32 oz. tomato juice (depending on consistency you prefer)
  1-2 Tbsp. sugar
  jalapenos - optional

      Cook ground beef, onion, green pepper and garlic cloves over medium heat, stirring until beef crumbles and is no longer pink. Drain grease. Place mixture in 5 qt. slow cooker. Stir in beans and remaining ingredients. Cook on high 3 to 4 hours or on low 5 to 6 hours. If cooking on top of stove, simmer about 1 1/2 hours.
