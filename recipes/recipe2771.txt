BLACK CAT COOKIES


1/2  c. shortening
1  c. sugar
1  tsp. vanilla
1  egg
2  Tbsp. milk
2  c. flour
1  tsp. baking powder
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. baking soda

      Mix thoroughly the shortening, sugar and vanilla.  Blend in egg and milk.  In separate bowl, sift remaining ingredients.  Add to first mixture; blend well.  Drop by teaspoon 2-inches apart on lightly greased cookie sheet.  Grease bottom of glass; dip in sugar and use to flatten dough.  Bake at 400 degrees; for 8 to 10 minutes.  While still hot, place Hershey's Kiss in center of cookie, 2 chocolate chips for head and 1 for tail.  With toothpick, spread chocolate to make cat shape.
