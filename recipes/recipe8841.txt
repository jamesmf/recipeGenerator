AFTER CHURCH ROAST


4 to 5  lb. boneless beef roast
1  pkg. Lipton onion soup mix
1  c. golden mushroom soup
5  lb. potatoes
1  lb. carrots

      Brown roast. Add onion soup mix and mushroom soup on top of roast. Add water to roast; bake, covered, in 300 degrees; to 325 degrees; oven for 2 hours before adding potatoes and carrots. Finish cooking while at church.
