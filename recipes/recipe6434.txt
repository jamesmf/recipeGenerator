GARDEN VEGETABLE SOUP


2/3  cup sliced carrots
1/2  cup diced onion
2  garlic cloves, minced
3  cups fat free chicken broth
2  cups diced green cabbage
2  Tbsp. tomato paste
1/2  tsp. dried basil
1/2  tsp. dried oregano
1/4  tsp. salt
1/2  cup diced zucchini

      In a large saucepan sprayed with nonstick cooking spray, saut√© the carrot, onion and garlic over low heat until softened, about 5 minutes.  Add broth, cabbage, tomato paste, basil, oregano, and salt.  Bring to a boil.  Lower heat and simmer, covered, about 15 minutes.  Stir in zucchini and heat 3 -4 minutes.
      Per serving: 42 calories, 0 gram fat, 2 gram fiber.
