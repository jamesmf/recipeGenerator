MARTY'S MEATBALLS


2  lb. Bob Evans sausage (or any other sausage with low fat content)
2  lb. ground turkey
1 1/2  c. oatmeal
1 (16 oz.)  jar grape jelly (apple will work also)
1  bottle barbecue sauce (Kraft)

	Combine sausage, turkey and oatmeal.  Roll meat into marble-size balls and broil 5 to 8 minutes.  Melt jelly in a large skillet at low to medium high temperature. Add barbecue sauce. Mix well.  Add meat balls.  Cover and simmer 30 minutes.  Stir occasionally.  Serve hot in chafing dish.
