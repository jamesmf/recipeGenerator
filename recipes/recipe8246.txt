PORKIEPINE MEATBALLS


1  lb. hamburger
1  c. rice
1  onion, chopped
1  green pepper, chopped
1 (16 oz.)  bottle ketchup
2  c. water

	Mix hamburger, rice, onions and green pepper. Make into meatballs. Brown in a deep skillet. Once they are brown, pour in ketchup and water. Simmer about 45 minutes. More water may be added while simmering.
