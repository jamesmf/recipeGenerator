SHRIMP CREOLE


1/2  c. chopped onions
1/2  c. chopped celery
1  clove garlic, minced
3  Tbsp. salad oil
1 (1 lb.)  can (2 c.) tomatoes
1 (8 oz.)  can seasoned tomato sauce
1 1/2  tsp. salt
1  tsp. sugar
1  tsp. chili powder
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
  dash of Tabasco sauce
1  tsp. cornstarch
2  tsp. water
12  oz. shrimp or sea legs
1/2  c. chopped green peppers

      Cook onions, celery and garlic in hot oil until tender, but not brown.  Add tomatoes, tomato sauce and seasonings.  Simmer uncovered 45 minutes.  Mix cornstarch with 2 teaspoons of water. Stir into sauce.  Cook and add shrimp and peppers.  Cook 5 minutes more.  Serve over rice.
