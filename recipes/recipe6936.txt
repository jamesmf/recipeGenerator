OLD FASHION POT ROAST


3 to 3 1/2  lb. roast
1  envelope Lipton onion soup mix
2 1/4  c. water
1 large  can mixed vegetables
2  tsp. oil

      Brown roast in skillet in 2 teaspoons oil. Take out of pan and put in crock-pot. Add the rest of the ingredients. Cook until done or tender, approximately 4 hours on high.
       You may not want to add the vegetables until halfway through the cooking time.
