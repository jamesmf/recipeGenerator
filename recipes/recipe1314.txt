EGGPLANT TOFU BURGERS


1/2  lb. tofu
1  c. minced onions
1/2  c. nuts
1/4  c. tahini
1/4  c. fresh parsley
1  tsp. dill
1/4  tsp. salt
1 1/2  c. eggplant, diced and steamed
1  c. corn flakes
1  Tbsp. soy sauce
1  tsp. basil

      Mix all ingredients and form into patties.  Bake on a lecithin-prepared cookie sheet for 15 minutes each side.
