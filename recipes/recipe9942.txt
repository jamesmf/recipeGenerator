PINEAPPLE-CHEESE CHRISTMAS CASSEROLE


2 large  cans pineapple chunks
2  c. shredded Cheddar cheese
1  c. sugar
6  Tbsp. flour
1  tube Ritz crackers, crushed
4  oz. butter, melted

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees;.  Mix together pineapple and cheese. Set aside.  Mix together sugar and flour.  Add to the fruit and cheese mixture.  Spread mixture in an 8 x 11-inch or a 9 x 12-inch casserole dish.  Spread crushed crackers over top.  Pour melted butter over all.  Bake for 35 to 40 minutes.  Serves 12.
