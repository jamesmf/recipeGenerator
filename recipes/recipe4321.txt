BLUEBERRY SALAD


2  pkg. grape Jell-O
2  c. boiling water
1 (16 oz.)  can crushed pineapple (undrained)
1  can blueberry pie filling

 
Topping:
2 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
16  oz. sour cream
2  tsp. vanilla
3/4  c. sugar or 1 c. powdered sugar
1  c. nuts, chopped

      Blend Jell-O, water, pie filling and pineapple until Jell-O is dissolved. Pour into oiled 13 x 9 x 2-inch glass dish. Chill until set. Cream together cream cheese, sour cream, sugar and vanilla. Spread over chilled Jell-O mixture. Sprinkle with nuts. Chill several hours.
