WATERGATE SALAD


1  c. chopped pecans
1  c. miniature marshmallows
1 (20 oz.)  can shredded pineapple
1 (3 1/2 oz.)  pkg. pistachio pudding
1  large Cool Whip
1/2  c. coconut

	Drain pineapple. Mix pudding with juice of pineapple. Mix with Cool Whip. Stir in pineapple; mix well. Stir in marshmallows, then stir in pecans. Refrigerate at least 1 hour.
