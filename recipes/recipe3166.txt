CHOW CHOW


8  onions, chopped
4  green peppers, chopped
1  qt. vinegar
4  heads of cabbage, chopped
12  lb. green tomatoes, chopped
3  Tbsp. salt
1 3/4  c. sugar
8  large cucumbers, chopped
1  Tbsp. cinnamon
1  Tbsp. allspice
3  Tbsp. dry mustard
1/4  tsp. ground cloves

      Mix all chopped vegetables. Mix with salt and sugar; let sit overnight. Add the final four ingredients. Bring to a boil and cook for 5 minutes. Place in sterilized jars and seal. Place in boiling water for 5 minutes.
