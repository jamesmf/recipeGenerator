MARINATED BROCCOLI SALAD


2  bunches broccoli, cut in pcs.
1  6  oz. can olives
1  2  oz. jar sliced pimentos

 
Marinade:
1 1/2  c. cider vinegar
1/4  c. cold water
1/2  c. oil
2  tbsp. sugar
1  tsp. salt
1  tbsp. dill
1  clove garlic, minced

      Combine marinade and pour over broccoli.  Cover and chill at least 12 hrs. Before serving, too broccoli with olives and pimentos.
