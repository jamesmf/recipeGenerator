BROCCOLI CHICKEN HOT DISH


2  small packages frozen broccoli
2 1/2  c. cooked chicken
1  can mushroom soup and 1/2 c. mayonnaise, mixed
1  can water chestnuts, sliced
8  oz. Velveeta cheese, diced

      Grease pan and place ingredients in pan in the above order. Bake at 350 degrees; for 40 minutes.  Add 1 can onion rings and bake another 10 minutes.
