VEGETABLE VINAIGRETTE


1  tsp. salt and pepper
1/2  c. oil
1  Tbsp. water
3/4  c. vinegar
1  c. sugar
1  can French-cut green beans, drained
1  can green peas (petite), drained
1  can white Shoe Peg corn (Green Giant), drained
1  jar pimentos, chopped
1  green pepper, chopped
1  onion, chopped
1  c. celery, chopped

      Mix in saucepan the first 5 ingredients and bring to a boil. Set aside and let cool.
      Variation on the Dressing:  It can be made without cooking on stove.  Put all ingredients in jar and refrigerate 24 hours.
      Put all veggies in a bowl. Pour liquid over veggies. Stir and let set in the refrigerator for several hours. Will keep up to 2 weeks in the refrigerator.
