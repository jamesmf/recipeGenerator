BREAKFAST CASSEROLE


2  lb. sausage
6  eggs
2  c. milk
14  slices bread
3  c. Cheddar cheese
1  tsp. dry mustard

      Cook sausage; chop into small pieces.  Mix eggs, milk and dry mustard.  Take sides off bread and cube.  Line bottom of casserole dish with bread.  Then add sausage and cheese; pour egg mixture on top.  Put in refrigerator overnight.  Cook at 325 degrees; for 1 hour.
