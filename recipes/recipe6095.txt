CAULIFLOWER DELIGHT

Salad:
1  head lettuce
1  head cauliflower
1  medium onion
1/2  lb. bacon

      Cut up lettuce.  Chop cauliflower and onion.  Fry bacon until crisp.  Crumble into bits.  Sprinkle over lettuce mixture.
Dressing:
1  c. mayonnaise
1/4  c. sugar
1/4  c. Parmesan cheese
2  Tbsp. vinegar

      Mix together:  mayonnaise, sugar, cheese and vinegar.  Pour over lettuce mixture.
      Let set 4 to 5 hours before serving.  Toss, then serve.
