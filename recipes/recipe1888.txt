FAJITA POT ROAST


3  lb. fajita meat
1  (3 oz.) bag fajita seasoning
1 (8 oz.)  bottle lime juice
1  1/2 onions, chopped
2  green peppers, chopped
1  stalk celery, chopped
1 (24 oz.)  jar picante sauce

      Marinate fajita meat in fajita seasoning and lime juice for at least 1 hour.  Put roast in roasting pan with the onions, green peppers and celery.  Pour over top the picante sauce.  Bake at 250 degrees; for 2 1/2 to 3 hours.  Cut in strips and serve with flour tortillas.
