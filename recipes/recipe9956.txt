SUPREME SQUASH CASSEROLE


2  c. cooked and drained squash
1  small onion, chopped
1  carrot, grated
1  c. sour cream
1  can cream of chicken soup
  seasoned croutons
  Ritz crackers and butter
  salt and pepper to taste

      Place croutons in bottom of casserole dish.  Spread mixture on top.  Cover with cracker crumbs and melted butter.  Bake 30 to 40 minutes at 350 degrees;.
