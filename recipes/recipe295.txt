HAVE-A-CRAN-MELON BALL PARFAIT


1 (1 lb.)  can jellied cranberry sauce
1 (7 oz.)  bottle (about 1 c.) lemon-lime carbonated beverage
1  medium honeydew melon, halved, seeded, and scooped into balls

      Turn cranberry sauce into mixing bowl.  Beat until sauce is smooth.  Resting bottle on rim of bowl, slowly pour in lemon-lime beverage.  Mix gently with up and down motion.  Pour into 1-quart refrigerator tray and freeze.  Break into chunks.  Turn into chilled mixing bowl.  Beat until fluffy.  Return to tray and freeze.  Layer cranberry ice and honeydew balls in 4 chilled parfait glasses.  Serves 4.
