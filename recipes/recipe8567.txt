TERESA'S CHICKEN ROLLS


4  medium boneless Chicken Breasts
3  oz. (1 pkg.) Cream Cheese
4  pkg. of 8 count Crescent Rolls
  Lemon Pepper to taste
3  tbsp. Margarine

      Chop cooked chicken.  Add other ingredients.  Tear apart rolls.  Put chicken mixture in widest part of rolls.  Bake at 350 degrees for 15-20 minutes.
