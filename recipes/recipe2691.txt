RED VALENTINE CAKE


2  oz. red food coloring
3  Tbsp. instant chocolate drink powder (Quik)
1/2  c. shortening
1 1/2  c. sugar
2  eggs
1  c. buttermilk
2 1/4  c. flour
1/4  tsp. salt
1  tsp. vanilla
1  tsp. baking soda
1  Tbsp. vinegar

      Combine food coloring and chocolate drink; let stand.  Mix shortening, sugar, eggs and cocoa paste.  Beat buttermilk; add to sugar mixture.  Add flour, salt and vanilla; beat well.  Mix in by hand the soda and vinegar.  Pour into two 8-inch greased baking pans.  Bake 30 to 35 minutes at 375 degrees;.
Icing:
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
1  Tbsp. milk
1  Tbsp. vanilla
1  box powdered sugar

      Let cheese soften.  Add all ingredients.  Beat until smooth.
