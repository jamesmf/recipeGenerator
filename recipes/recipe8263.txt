POT ROAST OF BEEF


3  medium potatoes, thinly sliced
2  large carrots, thinly sliced
1  onion, sliced
1  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper
1  (3 to 4 lb.) boneless beef rump roast or brisket of shoulder
1/2  c. water or beef broth

	Put vegetables in bottom of crock-pot. Salt and pepper meat, then put in pot. Add liquid. Cover and cook on low for 10 to 12 hours (on high for 5 to 6 hours). Makes 12 to 14 servings.
	For 5 1/2-quart unit, you may cook a 4 to 5 pound roast. Add listed amounts of vegetables and cook as directed.
