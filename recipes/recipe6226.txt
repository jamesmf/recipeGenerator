ROASTED POTATO SALAD


4  c. unpeeled small red potatoes, quartered
1  c. Miracle Whip salad dressing
4  slices crisply cooked bacon, crumbled
2  hard-boiled eggs, chopped
1/4  c. sliced green onions
1/4  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. black pepper
1/4  tsp. mustard seed

      Heat oven to 400 degrees;.  Place potatoes in a 15 x 10 x 1-inch baking pan, sprayed with no-stick cooking spray.  Bake 30 to 35 minutes or until brown and tender.  Stir occasionally.  Mix all other ingredients in a large bowl.  Add cooked potatoes.  Mix lightly.  Serve warm or cold.  Yield: 6 servings.
