MEAT LOAF


1 1/2  lb. ground beef
1/2  c. tomato ketchup
1/3  c. tomato juice
2  eggs
3/4  c. bread crumbs
1/4  c. finely chopped onions
2  tsp. mustard
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper

      Mix all ingredients and put it in a loaf pan. Bake at 400 degrees; for 35 to 45 minutes or until center is done. Drain off the fat. Let set for 5 minutes before serving.
