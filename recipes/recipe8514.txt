TUNA CASSEROLE


1 6  oz. can tuna
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1 small  pkg. egg noodles
  potato chips

      Cook noodles as directed on pkg. Mix with tuna and soup (may need to thin with a little milk). Drain noodles. Grease casserole dish, layer noodles and tuna mixture ending with tuna mixture. Top with potato chips. Bake at  325 degrees; for 20 minutes.
