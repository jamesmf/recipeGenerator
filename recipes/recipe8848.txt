HAM LOAF


2  lb. ground ham
2  lb. lean ground beef
3  eggs
1 1/4  c. cracker crumbs (unsalted)
1/2  tsp. pepper
2  c. milk

 
Sauce:
3/4  c. brown sugar
1  tsp. dry mustard
1/4  c. vinegar

      Cook sauce ingredients until a sauce forms.
      Mix together meat, slightly beaten eggs, cracker crumbs, milk and pepper. Shape into 2 loaves in 1 large pan or 2 smaller ones. Bake until almost done. Remove from oven and baste with sauce. Continue baking until done. Garnish with sliced pineapple and maraschino cherries.
