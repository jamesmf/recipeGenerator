MEXICAN CASSEROLE A LA BATTLE


1  lb. lean ground beef
1  can refried beans
1 8  oz. can tomato sauce
1 medium  pkg. tortilla chips
1/2  lb. grated cheddar cheese, or more to taste
1  pkg. taco seasoning mix

      Brown beef; add taco mix and about 1/3 cup water, plus 4 ounces tomato sauce. Simmer.  Combine 4 ounces of tomato sauce with refried beans, stir and heat.  Crush 1/3 to 1/2 package chips and layer in bottom of casserole dish; top with beef.  Spread beans evenly on top of beef mixture.  Crush remaining chips; combine with cheese and sprinkle on top of beans.  Bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes or until heated through.
