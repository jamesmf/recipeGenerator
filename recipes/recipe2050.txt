BUTTERSCOTCH BALLS


2  boxes confectioners sugar
3  sticks butter
12  oz. jar peanut butter (crunchy)
2 (6 oz.)  pkg. butterscotch tidbits
1/2  block paraffin

      Melt butter and mix with sugar and peanut butter, like you would dough.  Form small balls and refrigerate 5 to 10 minutes. Melt paraffin and tidbits in top of double boiler.  After this has melted, dip ball with toothpick and put on wax paper.
