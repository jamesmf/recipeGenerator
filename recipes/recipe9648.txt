CHICKEN PIE


1  whole chicken, boiled
  2-3 cups chicken broth
1  can cream of chicken soup
3  boiled eggs, sliced
1  cup milk
1  cup self-rising flour
1  stick melted butter

      Cook chicken or hen until tender. Debone chicken and place in casserole dish or pan and layer sliced eggs on top. Mix 2 or 3 cups of chicken broth with can of soup; pour over chicken. Mix milk, flour and melted butter. Pour over chicken mixture. Bake at 350 degrees for about 1 hour.
