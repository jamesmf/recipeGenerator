EGG CUSTARD PIE


2  c. milk
3  eggs
1/2  c. sugar
1/2  tsp. vanilla

      Beat eggs; add sugar and milk that has been heated.  Add vanilla.  Spread a little of egg white over crust and pour in egg and sugar mixture.  Bake at 300 degrees; for about 30 minutes, until brown. Will make 1 deep-dish pie or 2 small pies.
