MEXICAN ROLL-UPS


2 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
1 small  can ripe olives, chopped
1  can green chilies
1/2  c. finely chopped onion
1  pkg. (10) flour tortillas

      Mix first 4 ingredients together. Spread on tortillas. Roll up jelly roll style. Refrigerate. Slice crosswise and serve with salsa.
