GRIT SOUFFL


1  c. grits, cooked according to pkg. directions
4  eggs
1  stick butter
3/4  c. milk
1  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1 1/2  c. sharp Cheddar cheese, grated
1/8  tsp. garlic powder
  pepper to taste

      Add all ingredients to cooked grits, except the eggs, milk and 3/4 cup of the cheese. Cool the grit mixture for a few minutes. Beat in the eggs and milk. Pour into a buttered casserole dish. Top with remaining cheese and bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour.
       You can add peppers to this casserole and/or any herbs/spices you desire. The flavor is enhanced by creative ingredients.
