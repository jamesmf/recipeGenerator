CHICKEN POT PIE


3  to 4 cooked chicken breasts, cut in pieces
1  can peas and carrots
1  can cream of chicken soup
  biscuit dough for top

      Mix cooked chicken, peas, carrots and soup in baking dish. Cover and bake for 20 minutes in 375 degrees; oven.  Remove from oven and place biscuit dough on top.  Leave lid off and bake until biscuits are brown.
