HAMBURGER AND POTATO CASSEROLE


1  lb. ground chuck
4  to 5 potatoes, sliced thin
1  small onion, chopped
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  c. shredded cheese
1/2 soup  can of water
  salt and pepper to taste

      Brown ground chuck in skillet; drain. Layer ground chuck, potatoes, onions and cheese in 9 x 9 baking dish. Mix soup and water. Pour mixture over layers. Cover and bake at 400 degrees; for 40 minutes. Uncover and bake about 20 minutes longer.
