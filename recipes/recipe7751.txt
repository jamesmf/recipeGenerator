MACARONI AND BEEF


1 1/2  lb. ground beef
1  medium chopped onion
1 (16 oz.)  can whole tomatoes, chopped
1 (8 oz.)  can tomato paste
1  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper
2  c. macaroni noodles

      In skillet, brown beef and onions. Drain off excess fat. Add tomatoes, salt and pepper. Simmer over low heat for 30 minutes. Cook macaroni according to directions; drain. Combine cooked macaroni and beef into large serving bowl.
