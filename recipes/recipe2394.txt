COCONUT PIE


1 1/2  c. sugar
4  c. milk
4  large egg yolks
  pinch of salt
8  Tbsp. flour
1  stick butter
1  can coconut
2  tsp. vanilla

      Mix sugar, flour and salt in heavy saucepan until smooth.  Add milk.  Mix well.  Beat egg yolks; strain and add to mixture.  Add butter; cook until thick (stirring constantly with wooden spoon). Remove from heat; add vanilla and 1/2 of coconut.  Mix. Pour into baked pie shells.  Sprinkle with rest of coconut on top of meringue and slowly brown.
