HAM AND SCALLOPED POTATOES


  potatoes, thinly sliced to fill 9 x 13-inch pan
  ham (desired amount)
1  can mushroom soup
1  can celery soup
1 1/3  c. milk
  salt and pepper
  cheese

      Peel potatoes before slicing.  Cube  ham.  Layer  potatoes, ham, salt, pepper and cheese in a  greased  9  x 13-inch pan.  Mix soups and milk together and pour  over top.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour or until tender.
