TURKEY SALAD


1/2  c. sugar-free mayonnaise
1  Tbsp. lemon juice
1/4  tsp. sea salt
1/8  tsp. marjoram
  dash of cayenne
  ripe olives
  tomato slices
3  c. cooked turkey, chopped

      Combine mayonnaise with lemon juice, sea salt, marjoram and cayenne.  Mix turkey with dressing and arrange on a large platter with ripe olives and tomato slices.
