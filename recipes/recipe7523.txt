SAUSAGE AND APPLE TORTA


1  folded refrigerated unbaked pie crust
2  large tart apples, peeled and thinly sliced
1/2  c. onion, finely chopped
1/4  c. butter
12  oz. bulk sweet Italian sausage
1  clove garlic, minced
2  c. (8-oz.) shredded Italian-style cheese blend
1  c. ricotta cheese
1/4  c. crumbled garlic-and herb feta cheese
2  eggs, slightly beaten

      Preheat oven to 450 degrees;.  Unfold piecrust and use to line a 9-inch pie plate.  Bake according to package directions.  Set aside.  Reduce oven temperature to 350 degrees;.
       In a large skillet melt butter and cook apples and onion over medium heat for 5 minutes or until tender.  Transfer mixture to a medium bowl.  In the same skillet cook the sausage and garlic until meat is no longer pink; drain on paper towels.  Spoon sausage into prebaked crust.  Top with the cooked apple mixture. Combine cheeses and eggs; spoon evenly over apple mixture.  Bake for 35 to 40 minutes or until a knife inserted in center comes out clean.  Let stand 10 minutes on a wire rack before serving.  Makes 8 servings.
