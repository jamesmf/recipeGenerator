SOUTH AMERICAN CHOCOLATE


1/2  lb. sweet cooking chocolate
1  c. strong coffee (hot)
6  c. milk

      Melt chocolate over hot water; add coffee.  Stir well.  Cook over low heat 1 minute; remove.  Scald milk; add.  Simmer until piping hot.  Beat until frothy on top.  Set over hot water for 10 minutes to blend flavors.  Makes 1 1/2 quarts.
