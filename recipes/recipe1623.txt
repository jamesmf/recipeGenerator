RAW SWEET POTATO CAKE


1 1/4  c. cooking oil
1 3/4  c. sugar
4  eggs
2  c. flour
2  tsp. baking soda
1  c. chopped pecans
2  tsp. baking powder
2  tsp. cinnamon
1  tsp. salt
3  c. grated sweet potatoes

      Mix sugar and eggs together.  Add oil.  Blend in dry ingredients.  Add sweet potatoes and nuts.  Bake in a sheet pan at 350 degrees; for 35 to 40 minutes or cook in a tube pan at 300 degrees for approximately 1 hour.
      Note:  Carrots may be used in place of the sweet potatoes.
