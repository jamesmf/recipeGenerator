LEBANON NUT COOKIES


1 1/2  c. butter
3  c. sifted flour
1 1/4  c. finely chopped walnuts
1/3  c. granulated sugar
1/2  tsp. cinnamon
1  c. powdered sugar

      With pastry blender or 2 knives scissor-fashion, cut butter into flour.  Add about 1/4 cup water and toss quickly together, forming a smooth ball of dough.  Form dough; break off walnut-size pieces.  Roll into balls and place on cookie sheet.  Make large indentation in center of each, forming little nests.
      Combine nuts, sugar and cinnamon; fill center of cookies.  Bake 25 to 30 minutes or until light brown at 325 degrees;.  Stir together powdered sugar and 5 teaspoons water.  Use to dribble over cookies. Store, covered.  Makes 40 to 50 cookies.
