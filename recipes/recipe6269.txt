LEMON SAUCE


2  Tbsp. butter
2  Tbsp. flour
  dash of salt and pepper
1  c. less 1 Tbsp. light cream
1  Tbsp. lemon juice
1/4  tsp. grated lemon peel
1  Tbsp. fresh minced parsley

      In a small saucepan, melt butter and blend in flour, salt and pepper.  Slowly blend in cream and lemon juice, stirring constantly until the mixture is thickened.  Add lemon peel and simmer for 2 minutes. Add parsley and serve over fish steaks.
