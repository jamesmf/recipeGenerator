CANDY APPLES


2  c. sugar
1  c. water
1  c. white corn syrup
1  doz. apples
1  tsp. vinegar
1  tsp. red food coloring

      Combine water, sugar, vinegar, syrup and food coloring in large saucepan.  Cook slowly, stirring constantly, until candy reaches hard-ball stage.  Spoon over apples until coated.  Let stand on wax paper.
