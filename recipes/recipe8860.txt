SAUSAGE CORN CHOWDER


8  oz. sausage
1/3  c. sliced green onions
1  can cream of chicken soup (do not add water)
1 soup  can milk
1  can whole kernel sweet corn (undrained)
1/2  c. grated or shredded Swiss cheese

      Brown sausage. Drain. Add onions. Cook 1 minute. Place in crock-pot, adding other ingredients. Place cheese on each serving.
