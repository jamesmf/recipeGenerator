MEATBALLS


25  lb. ground chuck
1 3/4  c. parsley
20  c. bread crumbs
4  doz. eggs
1 12  qt. water (more if needed)
2  c. Parmesan cheese
1/4  c. garlic
  Less than 1/4 c pepper
1/2  c. salt
1/2  c. oil

      Mix all ingredients together.  Form meat balls and bake at 400 degrees; for 12 mins. or until brown.
