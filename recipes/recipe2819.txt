CRESCENT ROLLS


3/4  c. warm water
2  pkg. dry yeast
4  c. flour
1/2  c. sugar
1  tsp. salt
2  eggs
1/2  c. shortening or butter

      Dissolve yeast in water.  Stir remaining ingredients, except half of flour, into yeast.  Add rest of flour and mix until smooth. Scrape dough from side of bowl; cover with cloth.  Let rise in warm place until double, about 1 1/2 hours.  Divide in half.  Roll about 1/4-inch thick into a circle.  Spread with soft butter.  Cut into 16 pie shaped pieces.  Beginning at rounded edge, roll up.  Place on pan, point down.  Repeat with other half.  Cover; let rise until double, 1 hour.  Brush with butter.  Bake in 400 degrees; oven for 12 to 15 minutes.
