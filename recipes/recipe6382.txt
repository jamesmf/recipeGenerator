CABBAGE SOUP


1  lb. ground chuck
1 large  can tomatoes, chopped
1  can tomato paste
2  c. diced potatoes
2  c. diced carrots
2  c. chopped celery
1  to 2 medium onions, sliced or chopped
4  c. water

      Brown ground chuck with sliced onions. Add can tomatoes, tomato paste, potatoes, carrots and celery. Add water and bring to boil, then simmer 1 to 2 hours (until vegetables are tender).
