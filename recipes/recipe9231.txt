STUFFED PORK CHOPS


4  to 5 pork chops, browned on both sides
4 large  slices sour dough bread, cubed
1/4  tsp. poultry seasoning
  salt and pepper
2  Tbsp. melted butter
1/4  c. water
1  (10 3/4 oz.) cream of mushroom soup
1/3  c. water

      Put pork chops in baking dish.  Mix together bread cubes, poultry seasoning, salt and pepper, melted butter and 1/4 cup water.  Press bread mixture on top of chops.  Mix together mushroom soup and 1/3 cup water; heat.  Pour soup over bread mixture; cover and bake 1 hour at 325 degrees;.
