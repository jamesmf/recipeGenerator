HOT DOG CHILI II


3 1/2  lb. hamburger
2  Tbsp. paprika
2 1/2  Tbsp. chili powder
1 1/2  tsp. red pepper or hot sauce
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. celery salt
1  tsp. black pepper
1  garlic bulb, minced

	Mix all ingredients with 2 cups of water and bring to a boil. Add 2 more cups of water and simmer for 3 1/2 hours. Mash with potato masher as it cooks.
