RANCH DRESSING AND DIP MIX


2  Tbsp. plus 2 tsp. dried minced onion
1  Tbsp. dried parsley flakes
2 1/2  tsp. paprika
2  tsp. sugar
2  tsp. pepper
1 1/2  tsp. garlic powder

 
Dressing:
1  c. mayonnaise
1  c. buttermilk

 
Dip:
1  c. (8 oz.) sour cream

      In a small bowl, combine the first seven ingredients.  Store in an airtight container in a cool dry place for up to 1 year.  Yield: About 6 tablespoons mix (enough to make 6 batches).
      To prepare dressing:  In a bowl, combine 1 tablespoons mix with mayonnaise and buttermilk.  Refrigerate.  Yield: 2 cups.
      To prepare dip:  In a bowl, combine 1 tablespoons mix and sour cream.  Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving.  Yield: 1 cup.
