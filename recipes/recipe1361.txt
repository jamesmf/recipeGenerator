DEVILED EGGS


6  hard-boiled eggs
1/4  c. mayonnaise
1  tsp. prepared mustard
1/2  tsp. salt
  dash of pepper
1  Tbsp. pickle relish
1  tsp. minced onion
  paprika for color

      Peel eggs and cut in half lengthwise.  Remove the yolks and mash them, then add remaining ingredients.  Refill the egg whites and sprinkle with a little paprika on top.  Chill until ready to serve.
