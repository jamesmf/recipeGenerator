CHICKEN STYLE SEASONING


1 1/3  c. yeast flakes
3  Tbsp. onion powder
2 1/2  tsp. garlic powder
2 1/2  tsp. vegie-sal
1/4  tsp. marjoram
1  tsp. celery seed
2 1/2  Tbsp. Italian seasoning
2  Tbsp. parsley, dried
2  Tbsp. turmeric
1/4  c. savory

      Blend ingredients, except parsley.  Stir or shake parsley in mixture in airtight container.  May be used as any other chicken style seasoning.
      Chicken Style Seasoning may be used to prepare all types of chicken soups, gravies, stock and any other dishes requiring a chicken flavor.
