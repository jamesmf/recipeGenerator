PEACH ASPIC


1  envelope plain gelatin
2 (3 oz.)  pkg. peach flavored gelatin
1 1/2  c. water
1  c. orange juice
  grated rind of 1 lemon
3  Tbsp. lemon juice
1 1/2  c. mashed fresh Georgia peaches
1/4  c. sugar

      Soften plain gelatin in 1/4 cup cold water.  Dissolve plain and peach flavored gelatin in 1 1/4 cups boiling water.  Add orange juice, rind and lemon juice.  Add mashed peaches, sweetened to taste.  Pour into a 6-cup ring mold and chill until set.  Serve with Cream Cheese Dressing.
Cream Cheese Dressing:
1 (3 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
1  Tbsp. mayonnaise
1  peach, mashed

      Cream the cream cheese until smooth.  Add mayonnaise and peach, mashed.  Unmold aspic and fill center with dressing.  Garnish with mint leaves.
