EASY GOULASH


4  c. elbow macaroni
1  lb. hamburger
2  cans tomato soup
2  cans Ro-Tel tomatoes with green chilies (undrained)
3  garlic cloves, crushed
  Tones crushed red pepper to taste
1/2  medium onion, diced or dried onion

      Brown hamburger and drain off fat.  Add tomato soup, Ro-Tel tomatoes, crushed garlic and red pepper.  Let simmer while you cook the noodles.  Drain noodles and mix together.
