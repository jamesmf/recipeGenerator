SLOPPY JOE BISCUIT BAKE


1 1/2  lb. ground beef
1  small onion, chopped
1  envelope Sloppy Joe mix
2  c. spaghetti sauce
1 (8 oz.)  can tomato sauce
2  c. shredded Mozzarella cheese
2  c. shredded Cheddar cheese
1  can flaky buttermilk biscuits

	In a large skillet, cook ground beef and onion over medium heat until meat is no longer pink; drain. Add Sloppy Joe mix, spaghetti and tomato sauce and heat. Transfer to a greased 13 x 9 x 2-inch baking dish. Sprinkle with cheeses. Pull biscuit layers apart and place a thin layer over top of cheese. Bake, uncovered, at 375 degrees; for 15 to 20 minutes or until biscuits are golden brown.
