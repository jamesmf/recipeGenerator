SPANISH RICE


1/4  c. margarine
1  medium onion, thinly sliced
1/2  medium green pepper, diced
1  c. rice
2  c. undrained stewed tomatoes
1  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper

      Heat margarine in saucepan; add onion, green pepper and rice. Cook and stir over high heat until lightly browned.  Cover and simmer for 30 minutes.  Add 1/2 pound ground beef if desired. Serves 4 to 6.
