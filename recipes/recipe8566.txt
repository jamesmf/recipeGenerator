BROCCOLI CHEESE CHICKEN RICE BAKE


1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can Chicken Broccoli Cheese Soup
4  skinless Chicken Breasts
1/2  c. Milk
1 6  oz. pkg. Rice Chicken and Vegetable Rice Mix

      Prepare rice mix according to package directions.  In a 12x8 inch baking dish arrange rice mix, lengthwise across center of dish.  Arrange chicken over rice, overlapping if necessary. Combine soup and milk.  Pour over chicken.  Cover with foil.  Heat oven to 400 degrees.  Bake for 15 minutes.  Uncover and bake 10 more minutes until chicken is fork tender.
