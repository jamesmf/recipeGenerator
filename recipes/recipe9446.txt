CREAMY BAKED CHICKEN BREAST


4  chicken breasts, boned and skinned
8  slices Swiss cheese
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  c. herb seasoned stuffing mix
1/4  c. melted butter

	Arrange chicken in a lightly greased 13 x 9-inch baking dish. Top with cheese slices. Combine soup and pour over chicken. Sprinkle with stuffing mix. Drizzle melted butter over crumbs. Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour.
