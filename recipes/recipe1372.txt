CALIFORNIA BLEND VEGETABLE SALAD


1  pkg. California Blend vegetables
1  can mushrooms or fresh mushrooms
3  or 4 stalks celery, cut up
4  green onions
1  can pitted black olives
1/2  c. green olives
2  fresh tomatoes, cut in small pieces or whole cherry tomatoes

      Cook California Blend vegetables for 2 minutes; drain.  Add mushrooms, celery, green onions, black olives, green olives and tomatoes.
Dressing:
1  pkg. regular Hidden Valley
1  pkg. Creamy Italian
2/3  c. salad oil
1/4  c. vinegar

      Let dressing set about 20 minutes.  Pour over mixed vegetables.
