TACO DIP OR SALAD


1  lb. extra lean ground beef
1/2  lb. shredded cheese (extra sharp)
1 16  oz. salsa
1  can refried beans
1  pkg. taco seasoning

      Microwave beef on high for 8 minutes.  Mix beans, salsa and taco seasoning.  Pour over beef and microwave another 8 minutes. Add cheese and return to oven.  Let stand 2 minutes.  Serve with tacos and sour cream.
