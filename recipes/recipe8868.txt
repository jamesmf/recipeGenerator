CORNBREAD DRESSING


6  c. broken cornbread
4  c. broken biscuits or white bread
4  c. chicken or turkey broth
4  eggs, beaten
2  tsp. salt
1  Tbsp. poultry seasoning
4  c. broth
1 1/2  c. chopped onion
1  c. chopped celery
1/3  c. margarine
1  tsp. pepper
2  tsp. sage

      Soak cornbread and biscuit pieces in 4 cups broth. Melt margarine. Add celery and onion; cook until tender. Add with eggs to cornbread mixture. Add 4 cups broth and seasonings; mix well. Bake in a shallow uncovered pan at 325 degrees; for 1 hour. Makes 12 cups.
