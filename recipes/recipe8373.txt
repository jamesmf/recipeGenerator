MARINATED FLANK STEAK


  flank steak
1/4  c. soy sauce
1/4  c. olive oil
1  lemon, squeezed
2  garlic cloves, sliced
  fresh ground pepper

      Mix marinade ingredients and pour over flank steak.  Marinate overnight or at least 4 hours, in refrigerator, turning several times.  Grill.  Best if served rare to medium-rare.  Slice thinly across the grain.
