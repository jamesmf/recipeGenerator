CHICKEN CHEESE BALL


1  can of chicken spread
1/2  cup mayonnaise
3/4  cup chopped pecans
8  oz cream cheese
1/2  tsp. garlic powder

      Mix all ingredients together, except pecans. Put into rounded bowl and chill. Turn upside down into serving plate and coat outside with pecans. (Could be shaped into a ball or in a dish with the pecans on top.)
