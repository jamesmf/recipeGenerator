VEGGIE BARS


1 (8 oz.)  pkg. crescent rolls
8  oz. softened cream cheese
1/2  c. mayonnaise
1/2  envelope Ranch dressing
1  c. broccoli, finely chopped
1/2  c. tomatoes, finely chopped
1/2  c. carrots, shredded
1  c. green onions, chopped
2  oz. shredded sharp cheese
1  c. cauliflower, finely chopped

	Spread crescent rolls on a cookie sheet. Flatten and join rolls together. Bake at 350 degrees; for 10 minutes, then cool.
	Mix together cream cheese, mayonnaise and dressing. Mix the finely chopped veggies together in a large bowl and just before serving, spread the cream cheese mixture on the cooled crust. Spread the veggies on top and press them into the cream cheese. Top with shredded cheese. Cut into bars and serve.
