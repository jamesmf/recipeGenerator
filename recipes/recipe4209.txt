WELSH RAREBIT


1  oz. margarine
1  oz. flour
2  tsp. yellow mustard
  pinch of salt
1  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
15  oz. 1% milk
1 1/2  lb. grated cheese (reduced fat)
2  eggs

      Melt butter; add flour, mustard, salt and Worcestershire sauce. Add milk and cook until thickened. Add cheese and cook until cheese is melted. Add egg and cook 3 minutes. Serve on toast. Sprinkle with paprika. Yield: 10 servings.
      Calories: 184, Pro: 22gm, CHO: 7gm, Fat: 6gm.
