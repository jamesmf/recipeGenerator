MARINATED BAKED CHICKEN


1/2  c. Italian salad dressing
1/2  c. soy sauce
6  bone-in chicken breast halves
1/8  tsp. onion salt
1/8  tsp. garlic salt

	In measuring cup, combine salad dressing and soy sauce.  Pour 3/4 cup of dressing in a large Ziploc plastic bag.	Add chicken. Refrigerate 4 hours or overnight, turning several times. Refrigerate remaining marinade for basting.  Drain chicken, discarding marinade.  Place chicken, skin side up, on a rack in a roasting pan.  Sprinkle with onion salt and garlic salt.  Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees; for 45 to 60 minutes until juices run clear or 170 degrees; on meat thermometer.  Brush occasionally with reserved marinade.
