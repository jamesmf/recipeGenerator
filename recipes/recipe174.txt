JEWISH NUT COOKIES


1  c. oleo
1 1/4  c. sugar
3  eggs
3  tsp. cocoa
1  c. flour
  pinch of salt
1  c. nuts
1  can Eagle Brand sweetened condensed milk
2  c. coconut
1  can Pillsbury chocolate fudge icing

      Cream oleo, sugar and eggs.  Add flour, cocoa, salt and nuts. Spread on greased 11 x 15-inch pan.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 20 minutes. While still hot, add Eagle Brand condensed milk over top and smooth out.  Put coconut on top and bake additional 15 minutes at 350 degrees;. Cool completely and ice with Pillsbury chocolate fudge icing.
