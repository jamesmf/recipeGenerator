CHRISTMAS SALAD


2  c. ground cranberries
3/4  c. sugar
2  c. miniature marshmallows
2  c. diced apples
1/2  c. green grapes, halved and seeded
1/2  c. crushed pineapple, drained
1/2  c. chopped pecans
1  c. Cool Whip

      Mix cranberries, sugar and marshmallows together; refrigerate overnight. The next day, add the remaining ingredients. Mix all together. Chill in refrigerator before serving. Makes 12 servings.
