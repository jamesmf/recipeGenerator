DENVER SANDWICH RING


1 1/2  c. pancake mix
1/3  c. oil
1/4  c. milk
1  egg
1/2  c. ham, diced
1/3  c. diced onion
1/3  c. chopped green pepper

      Mix well the pancake mix, oil, milk and egg. Add the ham, onion and green pepper. Drop by tablespoon in a circle on a cookie sheet. Bake at 425 degrees; for 15 to 20 minutes until brown.
Sauce:
1  can cheese soup
1/3  c. milk

      Heat through. Spoon over individual servings.
