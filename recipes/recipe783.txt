ORIENTAL SALAD

Salad:
1  lb. green or nappa cabbage, coarsely shredded
4  green onions, chopped
1/2  c. sunflower seeds
1  c. turkey, chopped
1 (3 oz.)  pkg. Top Ramen noodles, dry, broken into small pieces

 
Dressing:
3/4  c. rice vinegar
1/4  c. rice wine
5  Tbsp. sugar
6  Tbsp. salad oil
6  Tbsp. sesame oil
  seasoning packet from noodles (chicken flavor)

      Mix dressing ingredients and blend thoroughly.  Combine salad ingredients.  Pour dressing over salad ingredients and toss until coated.  Makes twelve 1/2 cup servings.
