SPICY TEXAS TRASH


1  stick margarine
1  c. bacon fat (drippings)
1  tsp. red pepper
1  tsp. salt
1  Tbsp. garlic powder
1  Tbsp. chili powder
1  c. sesame sticks
1  c. whole pecans
1  c. mixed nuts
2  c. Cheerios
2  c. Cheese Nips, Cheddar Goldfish, or Tid Bits
2  c. Rice Chex
2  c. Corn Chex
2  c. Wheat Chex
2  c. thin pretzels

      Add to margarine mixture, stirring well after each addition. Bake at 200 degrees; for 1 to 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.
