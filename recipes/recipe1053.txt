TURKEY VEGETABLE SOUP


1/2  stick margarine
2  medium onions, diced
2  Tbsp. flour
3  c. chicken broth
1  c. potatoes, diced
1/2  c. carrots, sliced
1/2  c. celery, diced
1/2  tsp. poultry seasoning
2  c. cooked turkey, cubed
1 1/2  c. half and half
1  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. ground pepper

      Melt margarine in large saucepan over medium-high heat.  Add onions; saut√© until translucent.  Stir in flour.  Add broth, potatoes, carrots, celery and poultry seasoning.  Simmer until vegetables are tender.  Add turkey and half and half.  Add seasonings.  Cover and continue simmering until heated through. Serve hot.
