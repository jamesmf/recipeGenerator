CHICKEN ENCHILADAS


  butter
1/2  c. chopped onions
3  or 4 chicken breasts, boiled, boned and shredded
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  can cream of mushroom soup
2 small  cans chopped green chilies
1  carton sour cream
  flour tortillas
  grated Cheddar cheese
  sliced black olives
  salt and pepper to taste

      Saut onions in butter. Add to shredded chicken with 1 can of chopped green chilies. Roll in flour tortillas.
       Top With: Mix sour cream, 1 can of cream of chicken soup, 1 can cream of mushroom soup, 1 can of chopped green chilies and salt and pepper. Sprinkle with black olives and grated Cheddar cheese. Bake at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes until hot and bubbly.
      Note: You can soften flour tortillas in hot oil for just a few seconds.
