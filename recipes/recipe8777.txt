CHICKEN-ZUCCHINI PARMIGIANA


3  cloves garlic
2  Tbsp. oil
4  boneless chicken breasts
  dash of salt and pepper
3  medium zucchini, sliced thin
1/2  tsp. thyme
4  oz. Mozzarella cheese
1/2  c. spaghetti sauce

      In large skillet, sauté garlic in oil until brown.  Discard garlic.
      Sauté chicken in oil 8 minutes or until lightly browned. Sprinkle salt and pepper on chicken.  Remove from skillet; set aside.
      Add zucchini to skillet.  Sprinkle with thyme.  Cook 5 minutes; push to sides.  Arrange chicken in the middle of skillet.  Top with cheese, then the sauce.  Cook, covered, 3 minutes until hot.  Serve with side dish of spaghetti.
