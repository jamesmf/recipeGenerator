OCTOBERFEST RIBS


2 (16 oz.)  jars sauerkraut, drained
1  Tbsp. caraway seed
2  medium onions, halved
2  tart, green apples, peeled and cut into wedges
1/4  c. packed brown sugar
2  lb. pork spareribs

      In a Dutch oven, layer all ingredients in the order listed. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer, covered, 2 to 3 hours or until the ribs are tender. Yield: 6 servings.
