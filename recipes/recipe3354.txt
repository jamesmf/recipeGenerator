PIMENTO AND CHEESE


1  lb. sharp cheese, grated
14  oz. pimentos, drained and mashed
3  Tbsp. sugar
1  c. Hellmann's mayonnaise (be sure it is Hellmann's)

      Makes approximately 1 quart.
      Mix ingredients well.  After it sits a while, add more mayonnaise if needed.  Makes great sandwiches!
