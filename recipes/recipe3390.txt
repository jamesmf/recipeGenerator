BEEF DIP


1 large  pkg. dried pressed beef (finely chopped)
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese, softened
2  Tbsp. milk
1/2  c. sour cream
1/4  c. chopped green pepper
2  Tbsp. onion flakes
1/4  tsp. pepper
1/4  tsp. garlic salt

      Mix all ingredients.  Turn into 1 qt. casserole dish.
      Bake at 350 degrees; for about 20 minutes.  Serve warm with bread, crackers, chips.
Topping:
1/2  c. chopped pecans
2  Tbsp. butter or margarine

      Mix together and either microwave for 1 minute or saut. Spoon over dip.
