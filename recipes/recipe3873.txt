HOT VIDALIA DIP


1 1/2  c. chopped Vidalia onions
1 1/2  c. shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
1 1/2  c. mayo

      Preheat oven to 375 degrees;.  Stir together the chopped onions, shredded sharp cheese and the mayo.  Pour into an ungreased quiche pan or comparable Pyrex dish.  Sprinkle generously with paprika. Bake in a 375 degrees; oven for 45 minutes or until bubbly.  Place a triple layer of paper towels across the top of the dish to absorb any oil that surfaces.  Serve hot with crostini, Italian toast or a favorite savory cracker.  Provides 18 light servings or 10 generous servings.
