SWEET POTATO CASSEROLE


3  c. sweet potatoes, mashed
1/2  c. butter, melted
2  eggs, beaten
1  c. sugar
1  tsp. vanilla

      Mix together all ingredients.  Put in casserole bowl.
Top:
1/3  c. flour
1/2  c. butter, melted
1  c. brown sugar

      Mix ingredients; pour over potato mixture.  Bake at 400 degrees; for 30 minutes.
