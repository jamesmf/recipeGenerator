SQUASH PICKLES


8  c. sliced squash
2  c. sliced onions
1  Tbsp. salt
1  c. chopped green peppers
2  c. cider vinegar
3 1/2  c. sugar
1  tsp. celery seed
1  tsp. mustard seed

      Combine all ingredients, except squash.  Bring to boil.  Add squash and bring to full boil.  Put in hot sterilized jars and seal.
