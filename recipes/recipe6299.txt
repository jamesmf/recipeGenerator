CHICKEN RICE SALAD


1/3  c. French dressing
2/3  c. mayonnaise
1  tsp. salt (optional)
1/8  tsp. pepper
1 1/2  c. cooked, diced chicken
1  c. diced celery
1  c. drained mandarin oranges
1  c. chunk pineapple
1/2  c. chopped walnuts
3  c. cold fluffy rice

      Combine French dressing, mayonnaise, salt and pepper to make dressing.
      Combine chicken, celery, mandarin oranges, pineapple and walnuts. Toss with dressing. Toss entire mixture lightly with rice using fork to mix.  Chill.  Best if made the night before and chilled overnight. Serves 6.
