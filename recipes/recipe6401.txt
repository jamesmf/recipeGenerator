FRESH SPINACH AND STRAWBERRY SALAD


2  bunches fresh spinach or 2 pkg. pre-washed spinach
2  pt. fresh strawberries
1  Tbsp. poppy seeds
2  Tbsp. sesame seeds

 
Dressing:
1/2  c. sugar
1 1/2  tsp. minced onion
1/4  tsp. paprika
1/4  c. salad oil
1/4  c. vinegar

      Clean spinach and hang up in a plastic bag with a hole in it so it can dry out. Clean and slice strawberries and set aside.
      For Dressing: Place the dressing ingredients in a blender and blend for 2 minutes. Add the poppy seeds and sesame seeds to the dressing. Mix the spinach and strawberries and toss lightly with the dressing. Serves 8.
