SEAFOOD CHOWDER


1/2  c. chopped onion
1/2  c. chopped celery
1/2  c. chopped carrots
4  Tbsp. butter or margarine
1/2  c. flour
1  c. chicken broth
4  c. milk
5  slices American cheese
1 small  can salad shrimp
1 small  can minced clams
1  lb. crabmeat
  salt and pepper to taste
1  tsp. Old Bay or crab seasoning

      Saut√© finely chopped vegetables in butter until tender.  Blend in flour.  Add chicken broth and stir until smooth.  Add milk; simmer but do not boil.  Add cheese and stir until melted.  Add seafood and seasonings.  This soup may be made and kept on low setting in crock-pot for several hours; stir occasionally.  Serves 8.
