DIVINITY


2 1/2  c. sugar
1  c. cold water
1/2  c. white Karo
1/2  tsp. vanilla
2  egg whites
1/2  c. chopped nuts (optional)

      Boil sugar, water and Karo until it spins a thread.  Beat egg whites.  Pour half of syrup mixture over whites and beat at medium speed.  Cook other half of syrup until hard-ball stage (250 degrees; to 266 degrees; on candy thermometer).  Add to the egg white mixture and beat by hand until stiff.  Add vanilla (and nuts if desired). Put by teaspoon onto waxed paper.
