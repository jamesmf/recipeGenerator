FRUIT SALAD


2  pkgs. strawberry jello
1  can pineapple chunks (drained)
1  can mandarin oranges (drained)
1  can fruit cocktail (drained)
1  can peach pie filling
  2-3 bananas (sliced)
1  pkg. frozen whole strawberries
  miniature marshmallows

      Dissolve jello in 1 1/2 cups boiling water.  Stir in frozen strawberries until thawed. Add pineapples, oranges, fruit cocktail, pie filling and bananas.  Pour into large bowl. Refrigerate until set.  Add miniature marshmallows.
