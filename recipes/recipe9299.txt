PEPPER JACK STUFFED CHICKEN


2  boneless chicken breasts
2  oz. Pepper Jack cheese
1  tsp. taco seasoning
1  Tbsp. vegetable oil

      Flatten chicken to 1/4-inch thickness.  Cut cheese into strips.  Place cheese down center of chicken.  Fold chicken over cheese and secure with toothpicks.  Rub with taco seasoning.  Heat oil in a skillet.  Add chicken and brown on all sides.  Put in a greased 8x8-inch dish.
      Bake uncovered at 350 degrees; for 25 to 30 minutes, or until juice runs clear.
