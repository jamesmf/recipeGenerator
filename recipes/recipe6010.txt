CANNED SOUP


12  ears corn, cut off
2  gal. tomatoes, peeled and quartered
4  pods green pepper, chopped (optional)
1  c. butter beans
1  c. green peas
1 1/2  c. diced carrots
1  c. okra, chopped
6  medium onions, diced
1  c. sugar
1/2  c. salt
1/2  c. vinegar
2  qt. water

      Mix all ingredients in a large stock pot and bring to a boil. Continue to cook for 45 minutes.  Ladle into hot, sterile quart jars.  Seal and process in a hot water bath for about 15 minutes.
