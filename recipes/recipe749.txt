MACARONI PUDDING


1  qt. milk
4  oz. elbow macaroni
3  eggs
1/2  c. sugar
1 1/2  tsp. lemon rind
1  tsp. vanilla
1/2  tsp. nutmeg
1/2  c. chopped dates

      Scald milk in heavy saucepan; do not boil.  Add macaroni; simmer, uncovered, stirring frequently until tender.  Beat eggs; add sugar and beat until thick and lemon colored.  Remove mixture from heat; stir in egg mixture and remaining ingredients.  Pour in lightly greased baking dish and bake in a 300 degrees; oven for 55 minutes or until knife inserted in center comes out clean.  Serve warm or cold.  Makes 6 to 8 servings.
