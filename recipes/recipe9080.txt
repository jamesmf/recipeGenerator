SLOPPY JOES


1  lb. ground beef
1/2  c. chopped onion
1/4  c. chopped green pepper
1  clove garlic, minced
2  c. canned tomatoes
2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
  hamburger buns

      Brown meat in heavy skillet.  Add onion, green pepper and garlic; cook 5 minutes.  Add tomatoes and seasonings.  Cover and simmer 30 minutes.  Serve over split toasted hamburger buns.
