CHOCOLATE COOKIES


1  c. brown sugar
1  c. shortening (Crisco)
4  sq. dark chocolate, melted
2  eggs
1  c. milk
3  c. flour
1/2  tsp. soda
1  tsp. baking powder
1  tsp. salt
1/2  c. chopped nuts
1  tsp. vanilla

      Mix and drop on greased cookie sheet.  Bake 12 to 15 minutes in 350 degrees; oven.
Icing:
1/2  c. Crisco
4  c. powdered sugar
4  Tbsp. hot water
1  tsp. vanilla
  pinch of salt
2  sq. melted chocolate
1  Tbsp. hot water

      Mix first 5 ingredients.  Put 1/3 cup icing in a separate bowl and add 2 squares melted chocolate and 1 tablespoon hot water. Spread cookie with white icing.  Add a dab of chocolate icing and top with a pecan half.
