GRECIAN CHICKEN BREASTS


6  boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  salt & pepper to taste
10  oz. pkg. frozen chopped spinach, thawed
8  oz. feta cheese, crumbled
1/2  C. mayonnaise
1  clove garlic, minced
1/4  C. flour
1/2  tsp. paprika
12  strips bacon

      Preheat oven to 325 degrees.  Cut a pocket into each chicken breast; salt & pepper to taste; set aside.
      Thoroughly drain and squeeze liquid from thawed spinach.  Do Not cook.  Combine with feta cheese, mayonnaise, and garlic. Stuff into chicken breast pockets; dividing evenly.
      Combine flour and paprika; lightly coat stuffed chicken breasts.  Wrap strips of bacon around each and place on baking rack in baking dish.
      Bake uncovered at 325 degrees for 1 hour or until chicken tests done.
