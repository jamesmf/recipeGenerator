SWEET PICKLES


1  gal. cucumbers, sliced
2  c. lime
8  c. sugar
7  c. vinegar
2  c. water
1  Tbsp. salt
2  Tbsp. pickling spices
1  Tbsp. celery seed

      Mix lime in 1 gal. of water and soak cucumbers overnight using crockery or enamel ware. (Do not use aluminum containers.) Drain and wash cucumbers; soak in ice water 2 or 3 hours. Mix together sugar, vinegar, water, salt and heat. Place pickling spices and celery seed in a bag made of cheese cloth. Add cucumbers and bagged spices. Cook 40-45 minutes. Remove spice bag and put in clean sterile jars. Seal in jars while still hot.
