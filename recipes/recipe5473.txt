STRAWBERRY HEAVEN


1 large  pkg. strawberry Jell-O
1  c. boiling water
3  medium bananas, mashed
1 large  can crushed pineapple, drained
1 1/2  pt. frozen strawberries, thawed
1  c. chopped pecans
8  oz. pkg. sour cream

      Dissolve Jell-O in boiling water.  Let cool while combining banana, pineapple, strawberries and pecans.  Add fruit mixture to Jell-O; pour 1/2 mixture into 13 x 9 pan and let set in refrigerator several minutes.  Spread sour cream on partially set Jell-O and top with remaining mixture.  Return to refrigerator until ready to serve.
