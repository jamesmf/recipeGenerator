CHEESY CROSTINI


1  c. (4 oz.) shredded Swiss cheese
1/2  c. mayonnaise
1/2  c. sour cream
1  envelope (0.7-oz.) Italian salad dressing mix
1  French bread loaf, cut into 40 slices
1  small green bell pepper, diced
1  small red bell pepper, diced
1  can (4.5-oz.) sliced ripe olives, drained

      Stir together Swiss cheese and next 3 ingredients.  Spread 1 side of each bread slice evenly with cheese mixture; sprinkle with diced bell peppers.  Place bread slices on a baking sheet and bake at 350 degrees; for 10-12 minutes.  Top with olive slices.  Serve immediately.
