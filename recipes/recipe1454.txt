PORK TENDERLOIN WITH MUSTARD SAUCE


1 to 1 1/2  lb. pork tenderloin, trimmed of fat
1 to 2  cloves garlic, minced
1  tsp. olive oil
1/2  c. plain low-fat yogurt
1/4  c. Dijon mustard
  sugar substitute to taste
2  Tbsp. snipped, fresh chives

      Combine garlic and olive oil.  Rub on pork tenderloin and refrigerate 2 to 4 hours.  Grill about 20 minutes, turning often until done through.  Mix yogurt, mustard, sugar substitute and chives.  Serve sauce with grilled tenderloin.
