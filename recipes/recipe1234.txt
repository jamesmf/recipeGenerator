STRAWBERRY ICE CREAM


2  c. skim milk
1  c. evaporated skim milk
20  pkg. Equal
2  c. strawberries
2  beaten eggs
1  pkg. strawberry NutraSweet jello, dissolved in 1/2 c. hot water

      Combine this and freeze in electric ice cream freezer.  One serving equals two cups.
      Two cups equal 1 milk, 1/2 fruit, 1/2 egg exchange and 8 calories.
