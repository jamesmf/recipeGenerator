FLAPJACK


3  oz. butter
3  oz. brown sugar
1  Tbsp. dark Karo syrup
5  oz. Quaker oats

      Heat oven to 350 degrees;.  Grease 8-inch cake tin.  Cut butter into 4 parts and melt in saucepan with sugar and syrup; stir well with wooden spoon.  Remove from heat and add oats; stir well.  Spoon mixture into cake tin and put into oven for 15 to 20 minutes. Remove from oven and leave to cool for 25 minutes.  Cut into 8 or 12 pieces; lift out and leave until cold.  Store in airtight container.
