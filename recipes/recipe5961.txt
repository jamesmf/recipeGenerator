TEXAS STYLE CHILI


3  lbs. ground beef
1  lb. hot bulk sausage
3  medium onions, chopped
5  cloves garlic, minced
1/4  c. chili powder
1 1/2  Tbsp. all purpose flour
1  Tbsp. sugar
1  Tbsp. oregano
1  tsp. salt
1  can (28 oz.) chopped tomatoes (undrained)
3 (16 oz.)  cans kidney beans, (drained)

      Combine ground beef, sausage, onion, and garlic in a Dutch oven.  Cook until meat is brown, stirring to crumble meat.  Drain. Stir in next 6 ingredients and cover.  Simmer 1 hour, stirring occasionally.  Add kidney beans and simmer an additional 20 minutes.  Serves 8 - 10.
