CREAMY MOCHA CANDIES


1 (11 1/2 oz.)  pkg. (2 c.)  Nestle milk chocolate morsels
1/4  c. heavy cream
2  Tbsp. coffee flavored liqueur

      You will also need about 3 dozen (1-inch) foil candy cups.
      Combine over hot (not boiling) water, Nestle milk chocolate morsels and heavy cream; stir until morsels melt and mixture is smooth.  Remove from heat; add coffee flavored liqueur.  Transfer to small bowl and cover.  Chill in refrigerator for 1/2 hour. Using pastry tube with rosette or star tip, pipe chocolate mixture into foil candy cups.  Chill in refrigerator until firm, about 1 hour or store in refrigerator until ready to serve.  Makes 3 dozen.
