SESAME PORK WITH GARLIC CREAM SAUCE


1 1/2  lb. pork tenderloin
2  Tbsp. vegetable oil
1/4  c. sesame seeds

 
Sauce:
1  Tbsp. butter
2  cloves garlic, chopped
3  oz. cream cheese
1/3  c. milk
1  tsp. chives

      Brush grill rack with oil. Slice tenderloin into 12 slices. Flatten to 1/2-inch. Brush with oil and coat with sesame seed. Grill on medium heat 12 to 15 minutes. Serve with sauce.
       Note: Pork may be cooked in skillet on stove top if desired.
      Sauce: Melt butter in skillet; saut garlic for 2 minutes. Reduce heat to low. Add cream cheese and milk. Cook, stirring, until smooth. Garnish with chives.
