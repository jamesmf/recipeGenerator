HAM BALLS


3  c. Bisquick
1 1/2  c. finely chopped fully cooked ham
4  c. Cheddar cheese
1/2  c. Parmesan cheese
2  Tbsp. parsley flakes
2  tsp. mustard
2/3  c. milk

      Heat oven to 350 degrees;. Lightly grease jelly roll pan, 15 1/2 x 10 1/2 x 1-inch. Mix all ingredients thoroughly. Shape mixture into 1-inch balls. Place about 2-inches apart in pan. Bake 20 to 25 minutes or until brown. Remove from pan. Serve warm. Makes about 7 dozen.
