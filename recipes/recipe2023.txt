CRACKER BRITTLE


12  (2 1/2 x 5-inch) cinnamon flavored graham crackers
1  c. butter or margarine
1/2  c. sugar

      Heat oven to 350 degrees;.  Line 15 x 10-inch jelly roll pan with foil. Arrange crackers over bottom of prepared pan.  In small saucepan, combine butter and sugar.  Bring to boil over medium heat; boil 2 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Remove from heat; pour and spread evenly over crackers.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 15 to 18 minutes or until golden brown.  Cool completely.  Break into pieces.  Makes 36 servings.
