SCALLOPED OYSTERS


1  pt. oysters
6  Tbsp. milk
1/2  c. melted butter
1 1/2  c. cracker crumbs
  salt
  pepper

      Wash oysters in cold water.  Add cracker crumbs to melted butter; mix well.  Cover the bottom of a baking dish with a thin layer of crumbs.  Add a layer of oysters.  Season with salt and pepper; add 1/2 the milk.  Repeat and cover top with remaining crumbs.  Bake in moderate oven for 30 to 45 minutes.
