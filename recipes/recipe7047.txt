HAM AND BROCCOLI CASSEROLE


1 (10 oz.)  pkg. broccoli
1/2  c. onion, chopped
2  Tbsp. margarine
1  can cream of chicken soup
2  c. diced ham
1  c. uncooked rice
1/2  c. shredded cheese
1/4  c. milk
1/2  tsp. Worcestershire sauce

      Cook rice according to usual directions; set aside. Cook broccoli according to pkg. directions and drain. Saut√© onion in margarine. Combine all ingredients and spoon into a casserole dish. Garnish the top with extra cheese if desired. Bake uncovered for 25 to 30 minutes at 350 degrees;.
      Tip: After the holidays, take any leftover ham and chop; put in a pint freezer bag in quantities of 2 c. each. A wonderful solution for leftovers and so handy!
