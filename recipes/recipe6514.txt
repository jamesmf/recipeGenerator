COOL CASSEROLE


1 1/2  lb. ground beef
1  medium green pepper, chopped
1  medium onion, chopped
1 small  can cream of mushroom soup
1 small  can tomatoes with juice, chopped
3 small  cans spaghetti with tomato or cheese sauce
  American cheese slices

      Brown meat with onions and peppers. Drain and add all other ingredients. Top with cheese slices. Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes, uncovered.
