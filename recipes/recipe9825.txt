EGGPLANT FRITTERS


1  eggplant
1/2  c. flour
2  tsp. baking powder
1  large egg
2  Tbsp. sugar
  salt

      Peel and cube eggplant.  Cook in boiling, salted water until tender; drain and mash.  Beat eggs; blend in flour, sugar, baking powder, eggplant and salt.  Drop by teaspoons in hot fat, turning to brown.
