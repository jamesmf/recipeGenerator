TURKETTI


1 1/2  c. broken raw spaghetti
2  c. or more cut up turkey or chicken
1/4  green pepper, chopped (optional)
1/2  small onion, chopped
1/2  jar pimento
1/2  tsp. salt
  pepper
1  c. broth
1/2  lb. grated cheese
1  c. mushroom soup

      Save  1/2  cup cheese for top.  Bake  at  350 degrees;  for  45 minutes or longer.  Uncover for last 20 minutes.
