TASTY POT ROAST


4  lb. beef roast
1  tsp. salt
2  tsp. dry mustard
1/2  tsp. cinnamon
1/2  tsp. nutmeg
3  medium onions, sliced
1  clove garlic
2  to 3 bay leaves
2  Tbsp. oil

      Rub salt into roast. Place in a large pan. Add dry mustard, cinnamon, nutmeg, onions, garlic, bay leaves and oil. Cover and cook slowly for 2 to 3 hours or until tender.
       For gravy, add 1 1/2 tablespoons flour and cold water. Mix together and add to drippings.
