CRISP CUCUMBER PICKLES


7  lb. cucumbers, cut in slices or sticks
2  c. pickling lime
2  gallons water
8  c. white distilled vinegar
8  c. white sugar
1  Tbsp. salt
2  Tbsp. mixed pickling spices

      Wash and cut cucumbers; soak overnight in lime and water. Next morning wash them in cold water and soak 3 more hours. Combine vinegar, sugar and spices, dissolving well. Pour over cucumbers and soak 5-6 hours or overnight. Then boil in liquid until transparent. Fill jars, seal and process.
