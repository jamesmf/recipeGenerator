CORN PUDDING


2  c. corn
1  tsp. sugar
1  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
2  eggs, well beaten
1  c. milk
1  Tbsp. butter, chopped
2  Tbsp. crushed crackers
2  Tbsp. green pepper, minced

      Mix ingredients thoroughly. Pour into a greased 1-quart baking dish. Bake 40 to 45 minutes. Serves 6 to 8.
