PREACHERS CASSEROLE


3/4  lb. hamburger*
1/4  lb. ground pork*
4  onions, chopped
2  c. celery, chopped
2  c. chow mein noodles
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  can tomato soup
1 1/4  c. water
1  tsp. chili powder
  salt and pepper

      *Or use 1 lb. hamburger.
      Brown meat.  Mix all ingredients.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes.  Put some chow mein noodles on top before baking.
