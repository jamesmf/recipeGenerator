BEEF-TOMATO-POTATO CASSEROLE


3  large potatoes, sliced
  salt and pepper to taste
1  lb. ground beef
1 medium  can tomatoes
1/4  tsp. oregano (optional)
  onion, sliced (optional)

      Arrange layer of potatoes in buttered 1 1/2-quart casserole; season with salt and pepper. Put ground beef on top of potatoes; pour tomatoes over beef. Sprinkle with oregano and spread with slices of onion. Cover and bake at 350 degrees; about 45 minutes. Remove lid and brown.
