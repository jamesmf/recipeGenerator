DUKE'S MIXTURE


8  c. corn chips
4  c. unsalted popcorn
1 1/2  c. walnuts
4  c. cheese crackers
3  c. salted mixed nuts
1  c. butter, melted
1/8  tsp. hot pepper sauce
2  tsp. curry powder
1/2  tsp. garlic salt
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  tsp. salt

      Combine first 5 ingredients, mixing well. Divide evenly into 2 (13 x 9 x 2-inch) pans.
      Blend together next 6 ingredients and pour over mixture in the 2 pans. Toss to coat evenly. Bake in 250 degrees; oven 55 to 60 minutes, stirring occasionally. Makes 5 quarts.
