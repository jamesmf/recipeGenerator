MEAT LOAF


2  beaten eggs
1 (8 oz.)  can tomato sauce
1/2  c. crushed cracker crumbs
1/4 to 1/2  tsp. poultry seasoning
1/4  tsp. pepper
1 1/2  lb. ground beef or chuck
1/4  c. finely chopped onions
2  Tbsp. chopped green bell pepper
1  tsp. salt
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce

      Combine all ingredients, except meat.  Add the meat; mix and shape into a loaf and place in 12 x 7 1/2 x 2-inch baking dish. Bake at 350 degrees; for about 1 1/4 hours.  Makes 6 to 8 servings.
