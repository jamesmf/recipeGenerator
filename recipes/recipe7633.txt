BREAKFAST PIZZA


1  pkg. crescent rolls
1  lb. sausage
1 small  pkg. hash browns, thawed
1  c. shredded cheese
5  eggs
1/4  c. milk
1/2  tsp. salt
1/3  tsp. pepper

      Roll  out  crescent  rolls  onto  pizza pan.  Brown and drain sausage.  Add on top of crust, then thawed hash browns. Beat eggs, milk, salt and pepper.  Pour over sausage.  Sprinkle with cheese.  Bake at 350 degrees; until browned, approximately 20 to 30 minutes.
