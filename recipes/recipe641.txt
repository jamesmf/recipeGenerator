DIABETIC PINEAPPLE ICE CREAM


  sugar substitute equal to 1 c. sugar
2  cans evaporated milk
1 1/2  Tbsp. vanilla flavoring
1  can crushed pineapple, packed in its own juice (no sugar added)
2  eggs

      Mix all ingredients together with whole milk, pour in mixer can and fill to the fill line with whole milk.  Freeze.
