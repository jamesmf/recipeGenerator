HARVEST FRUIT SALAD


20  oz. can pineapple chunks
1/2  C. sugar
2  Tbsp. all-purpose flour
1  egg, lightly beaten
2 - 11  oz. cans mandarin oranges, drained
20  oz. can pears, drained and chopped
3  Kiwi fruit, peeled and sliced
2  lrg. unpeeled apples, chopped
1  C. pecan halves

      Drain pineapple, reserving juice; set pineapple chunks aside. Pour juice into a small saucepan; add sugar and flour.  Bring to a boil.  Quickly stir in egg; cook until thickened.  Remove from heat and cool - then refrigerate.
      In a large bowl, combine pineapple chunks, oranges, pears, Kiwi, apples and pecans.  Pour dressing over and blend well. Cover and chill for 1 hour.
      Serves 12 - 16.
