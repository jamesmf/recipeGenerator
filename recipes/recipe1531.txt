BANANA PUDDING


1  can sweetened condensed milk
1  family size instant vanilla pudding mix
1 large  box vanilla wafers
1  (9 oz.) container frozen whipped topping
3  c. milk
1 large  bunch bananas (approximately 8)

      Beat milk and pudding mix; blend in whipped topping and sweetened condensed milk.
      Alternate wafers, bananas and pudding mixture in large dish, ending with pudding mixture on top.
