GREEN BEAN CASSEROLE


2 (1 lb.)  cans cut green beans, drained
3/4  c. milk
1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can condensed cream of mushroom soup
1/8  tsp. Durkee ground black pepper
1 (2.8 oz.)  can Durkee French fried onions

      Combine beans, milk, soup, pepper and 1/2 can of French fried onions; pour into a 1 1/2-quart casserole.  Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes.  Top with remaining onions and bake 5 minutes longer.  Makes 6 servings.
