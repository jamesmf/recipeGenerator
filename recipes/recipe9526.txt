GARDEN PATCH SPECIAL VEAL STEW


3  lb. veal, cut into 1-inch cubes
1 (28 oz.)  can tomatoes, undrained
1  c. coarsely chopped celery
4  medium carrots, sliced
3  medium potatoes, cubed
3  medium onions, chopped
1/2 to 3/4  c. red wine
2  c. peas or green beans
4  Tbsp. quick cooking tapioca
2  beef or vegetable bouillon cubes
1  tsp. each:  salt and sugar
1 /2  tsp. each: thyme, rosemary leaves and ground marjoram
1/2  c. fresh parsley

      Combine all ingredients in a 6-quart casserole.  Cover and cook 5 to 6 hours at 325 degrees;.  After 3 1/2 hours, stir well and continue cooking.  Remove from oven, let cool for a brief time. Serves 10.
      Great with warm French bread or corn bread.
