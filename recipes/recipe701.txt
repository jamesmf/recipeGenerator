STIR-FRIED VEGETABLES WITH PASTA


1  medium onion, chopped
1  stalk celery, chopped
1  green pepper, chopped
2  medium zucchini squash, sliced
4  pieces broccoli
4  stalks asparagus
  butter
6  mushrooms, sliced
3  tomatoes, quartered
1/2  c. freshly grated Parmesan cheese
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
10  oz. your favorite pasta (fusilli, angel hair or spaghetti), cooked and drained

      In a skillet or wok, saut√© onion, celery, pepper, zucchini, broccoli and asparagus in butter until tender-crisp.  Do not overcook.  Add mushrooms, tomatoes, cheese and spices.  Stir until thoroughly heated.  Do not overcook.  Serve immediately on hot buttered pasta and sprinkle with additional Parmesan cheese, if desired.
