HORSERADISH MUSTARD CREAM


1/2  c. chopped onion
1/4  tsp. thyme
1  tsp. fresh cracked white pepper
1  c. white wine
2 (8 oz.)  bottles clam juice
1  c. whipping cream
1  Tbsp. coarse mustard
1  Tbsp. horseradish

      In saucepan combine onion, thyme, pepper, wine and clam juice. Bring to a boil; reduce heat to medium-high and cook until reduced to 1 cup.  Add cream and cook until reduced to 1 1/2 cups.  Strain through a sieve.  Add mustard and horseradish.  Makes 1 1/4 cups. This goes great with pan-fried fish or crab legs.
