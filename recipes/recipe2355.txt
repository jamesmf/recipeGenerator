COUSIN ETHEL'S OATMEAL MOLASSES BREAD


2  pkg. yeast, dissolved in 1/2 c. water
  quick cooking oats
1  tsp. salt
1 1/2  c. boiling water
1/2  c. molasses
1/3  c. shortening

      Mix last five ingredients together and let cool until lukewarm. Add yeast mixture and add 2 cups sifted flour and 2 eggs.  Beat. Add enough flour to make a soft dough, about 3 1/2 or 4 cups. Grease top lightly.  Put in greased bowl.  Cover tightly and leave in refrigerator for 2 hours.  Take out.  Shape into 2 loaves. Cover and let rise until double in size.  Bake in moderate oven about 45 minutes.  This freezes well.
