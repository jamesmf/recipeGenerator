MISSISSIPPI FRIED CHICKEN WITH BROWN GRAVY

Gravy:
2  Tbsp. drippings
2  Tbsp. flour
1 1/2  c. chicken stock
  salt to taste
  freshly ground pepper

      Heat oil gently and stir in flour. Stir continually and cook for several minutes. It should not be allowed to get lumpy. Add hot liquid slowly, still stirring, until desired consistency is reached. Other seasoning may be added.
