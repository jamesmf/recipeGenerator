DUTCH BEAN SOUP


1  lb. navy beans
1  lb. pork or ham
2  medium onions (chopped)
1  tsp salt
1  can tomato soup (undiluted)
1  tsp. pepper
1  tsp. dry parsley
2  stalks celery (cut up)
1  clove garlic

      Soak beans overnight and drain.  Add water to cover and cook. Add seasoning and meat.  Cook until beans separate.  Add soup and cook another 10 minutes.
