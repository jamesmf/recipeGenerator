CRANBERRY RELISH
(Uncooked)  

1 (12 oz.)  pkg. (3 c.) cranberries, chopped fine
1/2  c. sugar
1/2  tsp. cinnamon
  chopped walnuts (if desired)
1 medium to large  can pineapple tidbits, drained
1 medium to large  can mandarin oranges, drained

      Mix chopped cranberries, sugar and cinnamon well.  Combine with pineapple, oranges and nuts.  Cover and place in refrigerator to blend.
