GARLIC SAUCE


1  med. onion, chopped
2  cloves garlic, chopped
1/4  c. oil
1/4  c. lemon juice
  Garlic powder
  Salt to taste

      In a blender or food processor, mince finely the onion and garlic and seasoning.  When minced, add oil and lemon juice. Blend well.  Sauce will turn white in color. Place in bowl and let sit to marinate.  Great with lamb or chicken.
