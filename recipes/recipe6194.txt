STELLA'S VEGETABLE SOUP


16  ears corn, cut off cob
2  gal. prepared tomatoes
2  c. lima beans
2  c. diced potatoes
2  c. diced okra
3  small onions, diced
2  pods hot pepper, diced
1  c. sugar
1/2  c. salt
1/2  c. vinegar

	Mix all vegetables together. Pour in stockpot. Bring to a boil, stirring often. Turn down to low heat and simmer until vegetables are done.
