POPOVER PIZZA


1  lb. ground beef
1  envelope spaghetti sauce mix
1/2  c. water
1  (8 oz.) Mozzarella cheese
1  c. chopped onion
1 (15 oz.)  can tomato sauce
1/2  tsp. oregano

 
Batter:
2  eggs
1  Tbsp. oil
1/2  tsp. salt
1  c. milk
1  c. flour
1/2  c. Parmesan cheese

	Brown beef in large skillet with onion; drain off excess fat. Add sauce mix, tomato sauce, water and oregano. Simmer for 10 minutes. Spoon into greased 9 x 13 baking dish. Top with Mozzarella cheese.
	 Prepare batter by beating together eggs, milk and oil. Add flour and salt and beat until smooth. Pour over pizza filling. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese. Bake at 400 degrees; for 30 minutes or until puffed and dark brown.
