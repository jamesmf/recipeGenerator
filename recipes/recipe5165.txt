MEXICAN FRITO SALAD


1  head lettuce (dark green leafy is good)
4  tomatoes, diced into small pieces
1  large onion, chopped
2 (15 oz.)  cans Ranch Style beans, drained
1  lb. cheddar cheese
1  bag regular Fritos
1  bottle Catalina dressing

      Mix lettuce, tomatoes, onions and beans.  Place in refrigerator to chill.  Grate cheese and crush Fritos.  Add cheese, Fritos and dressing right before serving.
