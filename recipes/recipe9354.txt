RASPBERRY CHIPOTLE WRAPS


1 5  oz. pkg. spring mix greens
2  Fully Cooked Seasoned Chicken Breasts
3/4  c. Good to Go Raspberry Chipotle Ranch Dip
2  Fresher Lasting Jumbo Avocados, sliced
2  c. shredded monterrey jack cheese
10  flour tortillas, room temperature

      In a large bowl, combine spring greens, chicken breasts, raspberry chipotle ranch dip, avocados and cheese.  Place 1/2 cup of chicken mixture on each tortilla and roll into a cone.  Secure each wrap with a sheet of foil or plastic wrap.  Refrigerate until ready to serve.
   Note:  This recipe can also be served as a salad without the flour tortillas.
