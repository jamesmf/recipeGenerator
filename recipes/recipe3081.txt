BAKED CINNAMON CHIPS


8  round flour tortilla shells
  cinnamon
  sugar

      Place round tortilla shells on baking sheet. Sprinkle or spray lightly with water to lightly wet top of tortilla shell completely. Sprinkle with cinnamon and sugar mixture. Bake in oven at 350 degrees; until light golden brown or crispiness occurs. Break or cut apart into chip dipping pieces. Place in bowl along side of apple berry salsa and enjoy!!!
