HUNGRY JACK BEEF CASSEROLE


1  lb. ground beef
1  tsp. salt
1 (16 oz.)  can pork and beans
3/4  c. barbecue sauce
2  Tbsp. brown sugar
1  Tbsp. onion, minced
1  can Hungry Jack biscuits (10 count)
1  c. Cheddar cheese

	Preheat oven to 375 degrees;. Brown beef. Stir in next 5 ingredients and heat until bubbly. Pour in 2-quart casserole. Cut biscuits in half to form 20 half circles. Place cut side down around edge of casserole. Sprinkle with cheese. Bake 25 to 30 minutes.
