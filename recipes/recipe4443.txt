HOT CHICKEN SALAD


4  c. chicken, cooked and diced
2  Tbsp. lemon juice
3/4  c. mayonnaise
3/4  c. cream of chicken soup
1  tsp. salt
2  c. celery, diced
4  hard-boiled eggs, diced
1  tsp. onion, dried
1 small  jar pimentos, chopped
1 1/2  c. potato chips, crushed
1  c. cheese, grated
2/3  c. almonds, sliced

      Thoroughly combine all of the ingredients, except for chips, cheese and almonds.  Place in a rectangular baking dish and top with the chips, cheese and almonds.  Let stand overnight in the refrigerator.  Bake at 400 degrees; for 20 to 25 minutes.  Serves 8.
