CARAMEL CORN


2  C. brown sugar
2  sticks margarine
1/2  C. white syrup
1/2  tsp. baking soda
1/4  tsp. cream of tartar
1/4  tsp. salt
16  C. popped corn

      Bring to a boil: brown sugar, margarine and white syrup and boil for 5 minutes.  Then add the baking soda, cream of tartar and salt.  Mix well and pour over the popped corn.
       Put in large cake pan or roaster and bake in a 200 degree oven for 1 hour - stirring every 10 - 15 minutes.
      Take out of oven and cool.  Store in an airtight container.
