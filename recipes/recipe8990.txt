CABBAGE BEEF ROLLS


10  to 12 cabbage leaves
1  lb. ground beef
3  Tbsp. onions, chopped
3  Tbsp. sweet pepper, chopped
3  Tbsp. celery, chopped
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/2  c. cooked rice
1  tsp. salt
  dash of pepper

      Wilt cabbage. Cover and set aside. Mix remaining ingredients. Drain leaves and spoon meat mixture in each leaf. Roll up and place in baking dish.
Sauce:
1  c. tomato juice
1/4  tsp. pepper
1/8  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. seasoning salt
1  Tbsp. butter
1 (8 oz.)  can tomato sauce (optional)

      Heat all ingredients. Pour over rolls. Cover. Bake for 1 hour at 350 degrees;.
      Variations: If you use tomato sauce, use less tomato juice. Cabbage may be cut in small chunks and placed in bottom of dish, then top with meat mixture and grated cheese.
