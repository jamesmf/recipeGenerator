FRESH SHRIMP SALAD


1  lb. fresh medium shrimp, cooked, peeled and deveined
1/2  c. mayonnaise
1/3  c. chopped celery
2  tsp. Old Bay seasoning
2  tsp. fresh lemon juice
1/4  tsp. worcestershire sauce

      Cut shrimp in half and combine with remaining ingredients. Cover and refrigerate 30 minutes or more.  Stir before serving. Serve on a bed of lettuce or on a croissant or sandwich roll. Makes 4 servings.
