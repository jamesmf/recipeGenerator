SMOTHERED PORK CHOPS


  pork chops
  pepper
  salt
  flour
  oil
1/3  medium onion, diced
  water

	Season chops with salt and pepper.  Roll in flour.  Fry slowly in oil until brown.	Remove from pan.   Pour off excess oil leaving a little to make gravy.  Fry onion in remaining oil until wilted.  Pour boiling water into onion.  Add chops to onion and water.  Cover and simmer until tender.
