CROCK-POT CHICKEN


1  fryer-broiler chicken, cut up and skin removed
2  cans (10 3/4-oz. each) low-sodium cream of mushroom soup
1  envelope dry mushroom-onion soup mix
10  Tbsp. teriyaki sauce
1/2  c. white or sherry wine
1/4  tsp. garlic powder
2  c. hot cooked rice

      Brown chicken pieces in a skillet; set aside.  Mix next 5 ingredients in a medium saucepan and heat to boiling point.  Put browned chicken pieces in crock-pot and pour heated mixture over chicken.  Cook all day on LOW.  Serve over hot cooked rice. Serves 4 to 6.
