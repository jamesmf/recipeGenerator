PINEAPPLE CHEESE SALAD


1  can (no. 2) chunk pineapple in heavy syrup
1/2  c. diced colby cheese
1  c. chopped nuts
1  c. miniature marshmallows
1  c. seedless grapes, cut  in half
4  Tbsp. sugar
1  egg, beaten
1  Tbsp. cornstarch

      Drain pineapple, save syrup. Mix cheese, nuts, marshmallows, and grapes. Heat 1 cup of syrup with sugar, beaten egg, and cornstarch. Stir and cook until it thickens. Cool. Pour over first pineapple mixture, stirring until coated. Refrigerate until serving time.
