SOUR CREAM POTATO SOUP


4  cups diced potatoes
2  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper
1/2  cup chopped onion
1/2  cup celery
3  cups water
2  cups milk
2  chicken bouillon cubes
3  Tbsp. margarine
1 small  carton sour cream
2  Tbsp. flour

      Cook potatoes, celery and onion in 3 cups water until tender. Add milk, bouillon cubes and margarine.  Mix flour with sour cream and add to the soup.  Stir constantly until thickened.  Stir in salt and pepper.  Serves 8-10.
