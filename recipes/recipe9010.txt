MEATBALLS


1  lb. ground beef
1  c. bread crumbs
1  c. catsup
1/2  c. water
1 1/2  tsp. sugar
1/2  c. green pepper
1/2  c. onion
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. pepper
3  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/2  c. milk

      Mix bread crumbs, milk and ground beef into balls. Mix remaining ingredients and pour over meatballs. Cook 45 to 60 minutes in oven at 350 degrees;.
