STRAWBERRY PIE

Crust:
6  graham crackers, crushed
2  pkg. Sweet 'N Low
6  tsp. melted oleo

 
Pie:
1 1/2  c. cold water
6  tsp. cornstarch
6  pkg. Sweet 'N Low
1 small  box strawberry jello
3  c. sliced strawberries

      Combine crushed crackers, Sweet 'N Low and oleo.  Press into greased pie pan.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 7 minutes.  Cool completely.
      Cook cold water and cornstarch until thick.  Remove from heat. Add Sweet 'N Low and jello.  Cool 1 hour.  Fold in strawberries. Pour into pie crust.  Chill.  Serve with whipped cream.
