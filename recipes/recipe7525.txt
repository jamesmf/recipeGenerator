SOUTHWESTERN CASSEROLE


1  lb. lean ground beef
1/4  c. onion, finely chopped
1  can (4-oz.) green chiles, diced
1  can (11-oz.) enchilada sauce
1  can (10 3/4-oz.) condensed cream of chicken soup
1  can (10 3/4-oz.) condensed cream of celery soup
1  dozen corn tortillas
2  c. Cheddar cheese, shredded

      Brown ground beef until crumbly and no longer pink; drain. Combine beef, onion, chiles, sauce and soups.  Cut tortillas into halves or fourths; layer with ground beef mixture and cheese in a 9-inch by 13-inch baking dish.  Top with cheese.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes.
