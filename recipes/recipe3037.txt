CHEESE STUFFED MUSHROOMS


1 1/2  lb. small fresh mushrooms
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese, softened
  dash of salt
  dash of Worcestershire sauce
  black pepper to taste
1  c. grated Parmesan cheese

      Rinse mushrooms and pat dry.  Remove stems (stems may be used in other recipes).  Place caps on a greased baking sheet.  Combine remaining ingredients, except 2 tablespoons Parmesan cheese.  Mix well.  Spoon mixture into mushroom caps.  Sprinkle each with reserved cheese.  Bake mushrooms at 350 degrees; for 20 minutes.  Serve warm.
