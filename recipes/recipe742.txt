CHICKEN PRIMAVERA


12  oz. garden spiral pasta
1/4  c. olive oil
1/4  c. margarine
1  onion, sliced
2  cloves garlic, minced
1/2  lb. mushrooms, sliced
3  c. broccoli flowerets
1  zucchini, sliced
1  carrot, julienned
1/3  c. white wine
1  tsp. basil flakes
1  tsp. parsley flakes
  salt and pepper to taste
2  c. cooked chicken, cubed
3/4  c. Parmesan cheese

      Cook pasta according to package directions.  In a large skillet, heat olive oil and margarine.  Add onion and garlic and cook 2 to 3 minutes.  Add mushrooms, broccoli, zucchini and carrot and cook 3 more minutes.  Add wine, basil, parsley, salt and pepper.  Simmer 5 minutes until vegetables are tender.  Toss pasta and chicken with vegetables.  Top with Parmesan cheese.  Serves 6 to 8.
