ITALIAN PASTA BAKE
(Makes 6 Servings)  

1  lb. ground beef
8  oz. broken spaghetti or 3 c. penne pasta, cooked and drained
1 (26 to 28 oz.)  jar spaghetti sauce
3/4  c. grated Parmesan cheese, divided
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. shredded Mozzarella cheese

      Brown meat in large skillet; drain. Stir in pasta, sauce and 1/2 cup of the Parmesan cheese. Spoon into 13 x 9 baking dish. Top with Mozzarella cheese and remaining 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese. Bake at 375 degrees; for 20 minutes. Preparation time: 10 minutes.
