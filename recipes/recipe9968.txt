SCALLOP CORN


1  (17 oz.) creamed corn
2  eggs, beaten
1/2  c. crushed crackers
1/4  c. oleo
1/4  c. evaporated milk
1/4  c. shredded carrots
1/4  c. chopped green peppers
1  tsp. chopped celery
1  tsp. chopped onion
6  drops Tabasco
1/2  tsp. sugar
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  c. shredded Cheddar cheese

      Mix well. Turn into 8 x 8 x 2 pan. Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes.
