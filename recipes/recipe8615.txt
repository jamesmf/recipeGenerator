OVERNIGHT CHICKEN CASSEROLE


1 3/4  C. uncooked small macaroni
2  C. cooked chicken
2  cans cream of mushroom or chicken soup
1/2  lb. Velveeta cheese, diced
3  hard boiled eggs, chopped
1 1/2  C. milk
  chopped pimento

      Mix all ingredients together and refrigerate overnight.
      Bake, uncovered at 350 degrees for 1 hour or so.
