CUCUMBERS AND SOUR CREAM


2  large cucumbers
1/2  c. salt
  water (enough to cover cucumbers)
8  oz. (1 tub) sour cream

      Peel and thinly slice cucumbers.  Add salt to water.  Add cucumbers to salty water and soak about 1 hour.
      Drain and wash cucumbers well.  Dry cucumbers with paper towels.  Put cucumbers in a bowl.  Add sour cream and stir well.
