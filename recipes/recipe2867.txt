CHRISTMAS MORNING ROLLS


1  bag (24) frozen dinner rolls
1 (3 3/4 oz.)  pkg. butterscotch pudding mix (not instant)
1/2  c. butter
3/4  c. brown sugar
3/4  tsp. cinnamon
1/2  c. chopped nuts

      Arrange rolls in greased, fluted tube pan.  Sprinkle dry pudding mix over rolls.  Cook butter and remaining ingredients over low heat until mixture bubbles; pour over rolls.
      Cover tightly with foil and let stand on countertop overnight. Next morning, bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes.  Let stand 5 minutes, then invert carefully onto serving dish.
