CHEESE BALL


8  oz. sharp cheddar cheese, grated
8  oz. mild cheddar cheese, grated
24  oz. cream cheese
1/2  tsp. Tabasco sauce
1/2  tsp. garlic powder
1  Tbsp. worcestershire sauce

      Set out cheeses to soften overnight.  Blend cheeses together with a mixer.  Add other ingredients, and mix well.  Make into 2 balls.  Coat with finely chopped pecans and parsley flakes.
