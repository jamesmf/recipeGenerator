ALMOND CRAB APPETIZER


1  pkg. Philadelphia cream cheese
1/2  onion, grated
1 (7 1/2 oz.)  can crabmeat (white)
1/2  c. Parmesan cheese
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
4  Tbsp. toasted, slivered almonds

      Mix first 5 ingredients together.  Put in microwave just to heat.  Top with toasted, slivered almonds.  Serve on small slices of bread or crackers.
