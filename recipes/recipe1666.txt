LEBKUCHEN COOKIES


2  tsp. baking powder
2  c. brown sugar
4  eggs, beaten
3  Tbsp. cocoa
2  Tbsp. marmalade
1/2  c. nuts
  pinch of salt
1/4  tsp. cloves
1/4  tsp. cinnamon
1/4  tsp. allspice
1 small  jar honey
2 1/2  c. flour

      Add marmalade and honey to the eggs.  Beat well.  Add all dry ingredients together and then stir into the egg mixture.  Bake in a 9 x 12-inch pan 20 minutes at 350 degrees;.  When cool, cut into bars and use a powdered sugar and milk frosting or sprinkle powdered sugar on top.
