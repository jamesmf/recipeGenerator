GREEN TOMATO RELISH


1  peck (or 12 1/2 pounds) green tomatoes
4  green sweet peppers
16  medium onions
6  cups sugar
1  quart vinegar
2 small  jars mustard
2  tsp turmeric
6  Tbsp. cornstarch

      Grind first 3 ingredients, sprinkle with salt and let stand 2 hours in a colander to drain.  Add next 5 ingredients.  Cook 30-40 minutes over medium heat.
      Stir to keep from sticking.  Put in hot jars and seal.
