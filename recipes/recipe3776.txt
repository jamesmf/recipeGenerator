PICKLED PEPPERS


1  peck firm peppers, sweet or hot
1  pt. & 1/2 water
1  pt. veg. oil
1/2  c. salt
1  qt. white vinegar
1/4  c. oregano
1  whole head garlic, chopped fine

      Sprinkle a bit of red pepper if peppers are sweet.  Mix brine, add chopped sliced peppers.  Stir every couple hours. After 8 hours, can peppers in jars.
