CHEX PARTY MIX


3/4  c. butter
1 1/2  tsp. seasoned salt
6  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
2  c. Corn Chex
2  c. Rice Chex
2  c. Wheat Chex
2  c. Bran Chex
1  c. mixed nuts
2  c. Cheerios
2  c. pretzels
2  c. Cheez-Its

      Preheat  oven  to 250 degrees;.  Heat butter in saucepan.  Stir in the  salt and Worcestershire sauce.  Pour over the rest of ingredients.  Heat  in oven 1 hour, stirring every 15 minutes. Spread out to cool.
