CHOCOLATE BONBONS


1/2  c. condensed milk
1/2  lb. coconut, shredded
1  box powdered sugar
1  stick margarine
2  tsp. vanilla
1  large bag chocolate chips
1/8  lb. paraffin

      Mix first 5 ingredients together.  Refrigerate 1/2 hour.  Form balls.  Freeze 1 hour.  Meanwhile melt the chocolate chips and 1/8 pound paraffin in double boiler.  Dip balls in chocolate mixture and set on wax paper.  Use toothpick to dip.  Cover holes by drizzling small amount of chocolate from spoon.
