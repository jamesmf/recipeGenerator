HAM ROLLS


2  pkg. chopped ham slices
2  pkg. cream cheese
1  bunch green onions (only use tips), chopped
1/2 to 1  c. chopped pecans

      Chop up onion tips very small. Mix together cream cheese, onion tips and pecans. Spread out mixture onto ham slice. Roll up and cut into bite size pieces. Use toothpicks to hold them in place.
