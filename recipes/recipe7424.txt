CHICKEN-BROCCOLI CASSEROLE


1  chicken
2 (10 oz.)  pkg. frozen broccoli spears
2  cans condensed cream of chicken soup
1  Tbsp. melted margarine
1/3  c. mayonnaise
1  c. shredded cheese
1  c. bread crumbs

      Boil chicken until tender; cut in small pieces.  Cook broccoli until tender; drain.  Arrange broccoli in 11 x 7 baking dish.  Place chicken on broccoli.  Combine soup, mayonnaise and cheese, then pour over chicken.  Combine bread crumbs and margarine. Sprinkle over top of casserole.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 25 to 30 minutes. Serves 8.
