FLAUTAS DE POLLO
(Chicken Flautas)  

1  large chicken
3  red chili pods
1/2  tsp. cumin
1/4  tsp. oregano
2  tomatoes
1  small onion
2  c. cooking oil
2  doz. corn tortillas
  grated Mozzarella cheese
1  clove garlic

      Wash chicken thoroughly; boil in water until tender.  Remove from broth; cool and shred.  Once you have shredded the chicken, sprinkle some salt and pepper to taste.  Dip tortillas in hot oil to soften, individually.  Place on a platter.  Place some shredded chicken on each tortilla.  Roll tightly and place on cookie sheet.
      Sauce:  In a skillet, add 2 cups of chicken broth, tomatoes, cumin, oregano, onion, garlic and chilies and cook.  Once cooked, mix in blender, then strain.  Add a tablespoon of oil to sauce and bring to a boil.  Spread sauce on top of flautas; add grated cheese and place in preheated oven at 350 degrees; for 15 minutes.  Serve hot.
