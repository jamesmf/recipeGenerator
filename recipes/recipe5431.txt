DOUBLE APPLE SALAD


6  medium apples (unpeeled), diced
1/2  c. chunked pineapple, drained
1/2  c. walnuts or pecans, chopped
1/2  c. celery, diced
1/2  c. raisins (dark or golden)
1/2  c. flaked coconut (optional)

 
Dressing:
1/2  c. mayonnaise
1/4  c. sugar
2  Tbsp. lemon juice

	Toss apples and all above ingredients well. Pour dressing over mixture and toss until apple mixture is well coated with dressing. Store in the refrigerator a few hours before serving. It will have a better flavor.
