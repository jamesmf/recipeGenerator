GREEN JACKET SALAD


1/2  c. olive oil
1/4  c. red wine vinegar
1  tsp. Accent
1  tsp. Lawry's seasoned salt
1/2  tsp. oregano
2  tsp. chives
2  tsp. parsley
  pita croutons (see preparation below)
1  medium tomato
1  medium head of lettuce

      Pita croutons:  Bake pita bread for 4 hours at 200 degrees; or until crisp.  Break into small pieces.  Do not brown pita bread.
      Mix dressing ingredients together well.  Set aside.  In salad bowl, mix chopped tomato, torn leaves from head of lettuce, and pita croutons.  Pour dressing mix over salad and toss well. Serve immediately.
