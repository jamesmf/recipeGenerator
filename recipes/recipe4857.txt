DAN'S HOMEMADE SALSA


1 (24 oz.)  can diced tomatoes
1 (8 oz.)  can tomato sauce
1  (4 oz.) medium green pepper, diced
1  small jalapeno, diced (use plastic gloves)
1  medium white onion, diced
1  tsp. granulated garlic
1  oz. lemon juice
1/2  bunch cilantro, chopped finely
1  tsp. salt
8  oz. water

      Combine all ingredients and mix well.  Refrigerate leftovers. Serve with taco chips.  Makes approximately 1/2 gallon.
