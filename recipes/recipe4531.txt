SOUP DELIGHT


6  potatoes
3  onions, cook tender
1  lb. ground beef
1  can Chef Boyardee spaghetti and meatballs
1  can tomatoes
1  can corn
  small bottle or 1 c. ketchup
1  stick butter

      Cook potatoes and onion until tender; drain water. Fry hamburger meat and drain. Add together and simmer 20 minutes; serve hot.
