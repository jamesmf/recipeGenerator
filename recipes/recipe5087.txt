QUICK AND EASY CHICKEN SALAD


4  boneless chicken breasts, cooked
1/2  c. slivered almonds
1/2  c. fat-free plain yogurt
1/2  c. red or green seedless grapes
  pepper to taste

      Toast almonds on a baking sheet for 6 to 8 minutes at 350 degrees;. Remove from oven and let cool. Cut grapes in half. Mix chicken, grapes, almonds and yogurt along with pepper. Chill.
