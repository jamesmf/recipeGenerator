GERMAN SLAW


1  large head lettuce, shredded
1  large green pepper, sliced or chopped
1  large onion, sliced or chopped
2  c. sugar
1  c. salad oil
1  Tbsp. celery seed
2  tsp. salt
1  c. vinegar

      Mix cabbage, pepper, onion and sugar together in large bowl. Bring the vinegar, salad oil, celery seed and salt to a boil. Pour over first ingredients and mix well. Cover and refrigerate overnight.
