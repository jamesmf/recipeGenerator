HONEY BAKED CHICKEN


4  skinned Chicken Breasts
8  tsp. Honey
4  tsp. Mustard (yellow prepared)
8  tsp. Oleo, melted
1/8  tsp. Curry
  Salt

      Put chicken in casserole dish.  Mix all other ingredients. Drizzle over chicken.  Bake for one hour, basting every 15 minutes at 350 degrees.  This is great served over Uncle Ben's long grain and wild rice.
