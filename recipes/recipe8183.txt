NO-FUSS SWISS STEAK


3  lb. boneless beef round steak, cut into serving-size pieces
2  Tbsp. vegetable oil
2  medium carrots, cut into 1/2-inch slices
2  celery ribs, cut into 1/2-inch slices
1 3/4  c. water
1 (11 oz.)  can condensed tomato rice soup (undiluted)
1 (10 1/2 oz.)  can condensed French onion soup (undiluted)
1/2  tsp. pepper
1  bay leaf

      In a large skillet, brown beef in oil over medium-high heat; drain. Transfer to a 5-quart slow cooker. Add carrots and celery. Combine the remaining ingredients; pour over meat and vegetables. Cover and cook on low for 6 to 8 hours or until meat is tender. Discard the bay leaf before serving. Thicken cooking juices, if desired. Yields 8 to 10 servings.
