POTATO SALAD


  approximately 18 potatoes
6  eggs
  chopped celery
  chopped radishes
  chopped green onion
  salt
1/2  jar Miracle Whip
4  Tbsp. vinegar

      Boil potatoes with skins on in salted water.  Remove skins when cooked.  Boil eggs; peel and chop.  Mix cubed potatoes, eggs, celery, radishes, green onion and salt.  Add Miracle Whip and vinegar.  Stir mixtures together.  Serve warm (then chill).
