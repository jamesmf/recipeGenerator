BEEF STEW


2  lb. beef stew meat
2  Tbsp. shortening
1  c. tomatoes
1  bay leaf
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. pepper
6  potatoes, peeled and halved
4  carrots
1  large onion
1  c. water (or tomatoes)
  flour, optional

      Cube stew meat in 1 1/2 inch cubes; flour and brown. Add tomatoes, bay leaf, salt and pepper. Simmer gently for 30 minutes; add potatoes, carrots, onions, and another c. of tomatoes or water; cover and cook slowly until vegetables are tender. If desired, gravy may be thickened with a little flour.
