BLUEBERRY SAUCE


1  qt. fresh blueberries or 1 bag frozen, thawed in cold water
1  c. sugar
3  Tbsp. cornstarch
1/4  tsp. cinnamon
  dash of salt
1  Tbsp. lemon juice
1/2  c. water

      Rinse berries under cold water.  Put sugar, cinnamon, cornstarch, water and salt in pot.  Stir over medium heat until it comes to a boil.  Keep stirring.  Boil 2 more minutes longer.  Add berries and lemon juice.  Bring back to boil; cook until thick, 3 or 4 minutes.  If too thick, add a little water.  Good over pancakes or ice cream or could be used for shortcake.
