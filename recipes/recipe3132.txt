HAM PINWHEELS


5  large flour tortillas
2  (8 oz.) cream cheese, softened
1 small  can chopped black olives
1 large  jar chopped pimentos
1  c. pecans, finely chopped
7  honey ham slices

      Combine softened cream cheese, olives, pimentos, and nuts. Spread mixture on tortillas; top with ham and roll up tight like a jelly roll. Chill and slice with an electric knife. Serve with a toothpick. Keep refrigerated and covered until ready to serve.
