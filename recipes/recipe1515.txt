PIG LICKIN' CAKE

Cake:
1 (18 1/2 oz.)  box butter yellow Duncan Hines cake mix
3/4  c. cooking oil
4  eggs
1 (11 oz.)  can Mandarin oranges and juice

 
Icing:
1 (3 3/4 oz.)  box instant vanilla pudding
1  (15 1/2 oz.) crushed pineapple, undrained
1  (9 oz.) container frozen whipped topping (Cool Whip, etc.)

      Beat all cake ingredients together for 4 minutes.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 20 to 25 minutes.  Bake in layers.
      Mix all icing ingredients by hand.  Ice cooled cake.  Best kept in refrigerator.
