BUTTERMILK BUNS


1/2  c. sugar
1 1/2  tsp. salt
2  pkg. yeast
1  egg
1/4  c. shortening
6 1/2 to 7  c. flour
2  c. buttermilk

      Heat buttermilk until it separates and is warm.  Combine sugar and salt with buttermilk in large mixing bowl.  Dissolve yeast in mixture; stir in egg.  Add shortening; add half the flour, stirring well.  Add remaining flour gradually, stirring until dough leaves side of bowl.  Place in greased bowl.  Cover with damp cloth and place in refrigerator for 1 hour.  Punch dough down; shape into rolls.  Place on sheet.  Let rise in warm place.  Bake at 400 degrees; for 12 to 15 minutes.
