ORANGE SALAD


1  can pineapple chunks
1  can crushed pineapple
2  cans mandarin oranges, drained
1 small  carton cottage cheese (small curd)
1 small  carton Cool Whip
1  pkg. orange-pineapple jello (dry)

      Sprinkle dry jello over cottage cheese and toss with all ingredients except Cool Whip. Fold in Cool Whip last.
