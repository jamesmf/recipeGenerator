MA'S MEATBALLS


2  lb. hamburg
8  slices bread
  parsley to taste
  oregano to taste
1  egg
  garlic salt to taste
1/2 to 1  c. milk
  shortening or oil

      Put all ingredients in bowl except bread.  Dip bread into milk; squeeze and crumble into meat mixture.  Roll into balls of any size.  Fry in frying pan with shortening or oil until brown all around.  Place in sauce; simmer for 4 or 5 hours.  Serve with noodles or spaghetti.  Can also be put into crock-pot.
