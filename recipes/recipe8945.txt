STUFFED STEAK ROLL


  round steak
  mustard
  chopped onion
  salt and pepper
  bread crumbs
  poultry seasoning
2 to 3  cans cream of celery soup, plus a little water

      You will need toothpicks and foil.
      Spread mustard on round steak. Add bread crumbs, chopped onion, salt, pepper and poultry seasoning. Roll the steak up and put toothpicks to keep from unrolling. Place in baking pan. Add soup over steak roll; cover with foil. Bake about 1 hour or until steak is done in 350 degrees; oven. Makes 4 servings. Preparation time: 1 hour.
