CHICKEN SAUCE PIQUANT


5  large boneless chicken breast
  Tony Chachere's Creole seasoning
1  lb. sausage
2  large onion
1  bunch green onions
1  or 2 bell peppers
1 or 2  cans Rotel tomatoes
1  can tomato paste
1  bunch celery
  minced garlic

      Chop up chicken into cubes. Brown sausage and chicken; add Tony's seasoning. Using a blender, blend celery, onion, bell pepper, and small amount of water. Saut√© for 10 minutes; add other ingredients and more water. Cook for 1 1/2 hours. Gravy will be thin. Serve over rice.
