PARMESAN SALAD


1  bunch broccoli (2 to 3 stalks)
1  head cauliflower, broken into tiny flowerets
1  medium onion, finely chopped
12  oz. jar Hormel Homestyle bacon pieces
1  c. mayonnaise
1/4  c. Parmesan cheese
1/4  c. sugar

      Break broccoli flowerets into bite size pieces. Thinly slice stem portion. Combine with cauliflower, onion and 3/4 jar bacon pieces. Toss with combined mayonnaise, Parmesan cheese and sugar. Sprinkle with remaining bacon pieces. Serves 6.
