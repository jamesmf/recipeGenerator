POTATO DISH


8  to 10 medium potatoes
4  Tbsp. butter
1/4  c. milk
1/2  pt. sour cream
2  Tbsp. grated onions
2  Tbsp. chopped chives
1/2  c. grated cheese
  salt
  pepper

      Leave skin on potatoes; boil potatoes and drain. Mash with butter and milk. Add salt and pepper to taste. Add sour cream, onions and chives. Spoon into buttered Pyrex dish (12 x 12). Cover with cheese and bake at 350 degrees; for 20 to 30 minutes.
