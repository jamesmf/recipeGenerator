CHEESE VEGETABLE DIP


1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
2  Tbsp. milk
1  c. sour cream
1/4  c. green pepper, chopped
4  green onions (and part of the tops), chopped fine
1/2  tsp. garlic salt
1/4  tsp. pepper

 
Optional Items:
4 1/2  oz. can black olives, chopped fine or
1 small  can shrimp or
1 (3 oz.)  pkg. chipped beef, cut fine or
1/2  c. pecans or walnuts

      Mix together and refrigerate until serving. Serve with Toasteds, celery or cauliflower or anything else you wish.
