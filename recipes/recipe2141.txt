HONEY CHEWS


1/2  c. butter
1  c. honey
1/2  c. chopped dates
1  c. chopped nuts

      Bring butter and honey to a boil and boil to 260 degrees; on candy thermometer or to the hard-ball stage.  Remove from heat and add chopped dates and nuts.  Stir for 2 minutes, then pour into buttered pan.  Cool.  Cut into squares.  May be wrapped in cellophane for gift giving or to store.
