BAKED STUFFED SHRIMP


1/2  c. toasted wheat bran
1  Tbsp. corn oil (Mazola)
1  Tbsp. lemon juice
1  clove garlic, crushed or garlic powder
4  jumbo shrimp (deveined)

 
No Butter Dipping Sauce:
1/2  c. lemon juice
1/2  c. orange juice
  small amount cornstarch

      Mix bran, oil, lemon juice and garlic together for stuffing. Put 1 1/2 teaspoons on each shrimp.  Bake for 25 minutes at 325 degrees; or until shrimp are white.
      Dipping Sauce:  Heat juices to boiling, then thicken with cornstarch dissolved in water.
      Makes 2 servings containing 170 calories each.
