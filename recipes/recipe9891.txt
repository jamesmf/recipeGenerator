SWEET POTATO CASSEROLE


2  cans sweet potatoes, cooked and mashed
1  c. sugar
1/2  c. milk
1/2  tsp. salt
1  tsp. vanilla
1/3  stick butter, melted
2  eggs

 
Topping:
1  c. brown sugar
1/3  c. flour
1  c. nuts, chopped
1/3  stick butter, melted

      Mash potatoes while hot. Mix above ingredients together (Not the Topping) and pour into a 2-quart casserole dish. Set aside.
      Take the topping ingredients and mix together. Pour over the mash potato mix. DO NOT STIR. Bake in 350 degrees; oven for about 30 to 35 minutes. Serve hot or cold. Best hot!
