BAKED CUSTARD


1  egg
1/2  c. milk
1/8  tsp. vanilla
  few grains salt
  sprinkle of nutmeg

      Beat the egg slightly; stir in milk, salt and vanilla.  If you wish, add artificial sweetener to flavor.  Pour into custard cup and sprinkle with nutmeg.  Set in pan of hot water and bake in 350 degrees; oven 45 minutes.  Other flavors such as almond, lemon, orange or maple may be used in place of vanilla.  One serving equals 1/2 cup milk and 1 meat exchange.
