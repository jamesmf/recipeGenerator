CHOCOLATE BUTTER FROSTING WITH PECANS


1  stick butter or oleo
1  tsp. salt
3  c. powdered sugar
1/4  c. chocolate syrup
1/4  c. milk
2  tsp. vanilla
1  c. pecans

      Mix softened butter or oleo.  Add salt, powdered sugar, chocolate syrup and milk; beat until fluffy with electric beater. Add vanilla and pecans; continue beating until nuts have been properly mixed into the frosting.
