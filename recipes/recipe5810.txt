MEXICAN STEW


1  lb. ground beef
1  can pinto beans (with or without jalapeno peppers)
1  can Ro-Tel tomatoes with green chilies
2  cans minestrone soup
1  can water

      Brown meat (drain). Add other ingredients. Simmer 20 to 30 minutes (good and fast). Serve with cornbread or crackers.
      Optional: Add 4 ounces of Velveeta cheese.
