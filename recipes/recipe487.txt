PEPPERMINT CUPCAKE CONES


1  pkg. white cake mix
18  flat bottom ice cream cones
1  pkg. fluffy white frosting
1/4  tsp. peppermint extract
  colored sugars (optional)
  red jelly beans (optional)
  gumdrops (optional)

      Prepare cake mix batter as directed on package.  Fill cones 2/3 full with batter.  Place on baking sheet and bake in moderate 375 degrees; oven for 20 to 25 minutes.  Cool on cake rack.  Prepare a fluffy white frosting.  Blend in peppermint extract.  Swirl frosting on cake cones.  Decorate as desired with colored sugars, red jelly beans and/or gumdrops.
