HOT FUDGE TOPPING


4  Tbsp. butter
4  squares semi-sweet chocolate
1 1/2  C. sugar
1 lrg.  can evaporated milk
  dash of salt
1  tsp. vanilla

      Melt chocolate and butter together in a saucepan.  Add sugar, milk and salt.  Cook slowly at low heat until thickened; about 15 minutes.  Add vanilla.
      Ready to serve over ice cream.
