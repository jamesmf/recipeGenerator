STRAWBERRY SALAD

Crust:
2  c. crushed pretzels
1/2  c. butter, melted
1/2  c. sugar

 
Filling:
1/2  c. sugar
8  oz. cream cheese
8  oz. Cool Whip
6  oz. strawberry Jell-O
1 1/2  c. hot water
10  oz. pkg. frozen strawberries

	Preheat oven to 350 degrees;. Mix first three ingredients and press into ungreased 9 x 13 pan. Bake for about 6 minutes. Cool.
	In a separate bowl, mix together the sugar, cream cheese and Cool Whip. Spread over pretzel mix.
	 Meanwhile, dissolve Jell-O in hot water. Add frozen strawberries. When it starts to set, pour Jell-O mixture over top of cream cheese.  Let set.
