ON-THE-GRILL FISH STEAKS


2  lb. fresh fish steaks, 3/4-inch thick
1/2  c. melted margarine or butter
1/4  c. lemon juice
1  dash hot pepper sauce
  paprika

      Place serving size portions of fish on well-greased grill. Spray grill well before putting on fish.  Baste fish with sauce and sprinkle with paprika.  Cook about 5 inches from hot coals for 8 minutes.  Baste with sauce, then turn and cook for 7 to 10 minutes longer or until fish flakes or turns shiny white.  Serves 4.
