VEGETABLE SOUP


1  qt. tomato juice
2  Tbsp. sugar
3  oz. tomato paste
  hamburger meat
  stew beef
  chicken
1  tsp. grease (either from your cooked meat or Wesson oil)
  vegetables (peas, carrots, corn, limas, potatoes, cabbage, onion)

	First, cook your meat(s). Add  tomato juice, paste, sugar and vegetables. Depending on how thick or thin you want your soup, you can add more tomato juice or water. Add oil. Simmer 45 minutes to 1 hour.
