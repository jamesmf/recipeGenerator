PAN FRIED ZUCCHINI


2  small garlic segments, crushed
2  Tbsp. oil
1  small sliced onion
3  c. sliced zucchini
1  tsp. salt
1 1/2  tsp. Accent
1  c. big chunks tomatoes

      Saut√© garlic in oil until golden brown.  Add onions and sliced zucchini; stir.  Add salt and Accent.  Cook on high heat for 5 minutes, while continuing to stir.  Add tomatoes; gently toss the vegetables for 1 minute.  Remove from heat immediately.
