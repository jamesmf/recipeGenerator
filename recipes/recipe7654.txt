FIVE-HOUR CASSEROLE STEW


2 1/2  lb. beef roast
6  carrots
6  stalks celery
3  onions
4  medium potatoes
1  pt. tomatoes
1  Tbsp. sugar
1  Tbsp. salt
3  Tbsp. tapioca
2  slices bread
  pepper to taste

      Cut  beef  in  1-inch  cubes;   dice  carrots,  celery, onions,  potatoes  and tomatoes.  Add  sugar,  salt,  tapioca, bread (cut fine) and pepper.  Put in roaster  and bake 5 hours at 250 degrees;.
