SNOW PEAKED MEAT LOAF


1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can golden mushroom soup
2  lb. ground beef
1/2  c. fine dry bread crumbs
1/3  c. onion, finely chopped
1  egg, slightly beaten
1  tsp. salt
4  c. mashed potatoes
  Cheddar cheese, shredded
1/4  c. water

	Mix thoroughly 1/2 cup soup, beef, bread crumbs, onion, egg and salt. Shape firmly into loaf (8 x 4-inch). Place on shallow baking pan. Bake at 375 degrees; for 1 hour. Frost with potatoes and sprinkle with cheese. Bake 15 minutes more. Blend remaining soup, water and 2 tablespoons drippings. Heat; stir occasionally. Serve with loaf. Makes 6 to 8 servings.
