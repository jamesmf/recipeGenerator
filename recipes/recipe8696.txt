APPLE PORK TENDERLOIN



1  pork tenderloin
1/2  c. apple juice
1/4  c. apple cider vinegar
3  Tbsp. brown sugar
1  clove garlic, minced
1  apple, cored and sliced into rings

	Preheat oven to 350 degrees;.  Place tenderloin in baking dish. Combine apple juice, vinegar, brown sugar and garlic and pour over tenderloin. Place meat and sauce in a baking dish and top with the sliced apples. Bake for 2 1/2 to 3 hours until tenderloin reaches internal temperature of 180 degrees; on meat thermometer or until the meat is no longer pink.	Remove to a platter and let cool for a few minutes before slicing.
