CRISP POTATO SKINS


6  medium potatoes (about 2 lb.)
1/4  c. butter or margarine, melted
1  tsp. soy sauce
  coarse salt, sour cream, chopped chives and/or bacon bits

      Bake potatoes at 400 degrees; for 45 minutes or until tender. Cool, then cut into quarters lengthwise. Scoop out pulp, leaving 1/8-inch shell. Reserve pulp for use in other recipes. Increase oven temperature to 500 degrees;. Combine butter and soy sauce. Brush on both sides of skins. Place on a baking sheet and bake for 10 to 12 minutes or until crisp. Top with coarse salt, sour cream, chives and/or bacon bits. Yield: 6 servings.
