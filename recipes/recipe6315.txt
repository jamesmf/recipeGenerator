TWENTY FOUR HOUR SLAW


1  medium cabbage, shredded
2  medium white onions, sliced & separated into rings
1/2  cup sugar

      Stir 1/2 cup sugar into cabbage and place half of this mixture in a large bowl. Cover with onion rings, then cover with remaining cabbage.
Dressing:
1  t. celery seed
1  t. sugar
1  1/2 t. salt
1  t. dry mustard
1  c. white vinegar
1  c. salad oil

      Combine dressing ingredients, except oil. Bring dressing to rolling boil. Stir in oil. Boil again. Pour over cabbage and onions. Do not stir. Cover and refrigerate for 24 hours.
