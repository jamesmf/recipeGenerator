CHOCOLATE CHEESECAKE


1 (8.5 oz.)  pkg. chocolate wafers
6  Tbsp. melted butter
12  oz. chocolate chips
3 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
1  c. sugar
1  Tbsp. brandy (optional)
3  eggs
8  oz. sour cream

      Crush wafers and mix with melted butter; pat into a 9 x 13-inch spring-form pan, leaving a 1-inch space at top.  Melt chocolate chips in double boiler.  Beat cream cheese, sugar and brandy until smooth.  Add chocolate chips and 3 eggs, 1 at a time.  Add sour cream; mix until smooth.  Pour into pan.  Bake at 375 degrees; for 1 hour. Cool on rack.
