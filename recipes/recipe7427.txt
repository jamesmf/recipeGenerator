CHICKEN TETRAZZINI


1/4  c. liquid Butter Buds
1/4  c. flour
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
1  c. chicken broth
1  c. evaporated milk
2  Tbsp. sherry
8  oz. pkg. spaghetti, cooked and drained
2  c. cooked cubed chicken
8  oz. can mushroom stems/pieces, drained
2  Tbsp. Parmesan cheese
1/2  c. grated Mozzarella cheese

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees;.  Blend Butter Buds, flour and seasonings over low heat in saucepan.  Stir and cook until bubbly. Remove from heat; stir in broth and milk.  Heat to boiling, stirring constantly.  Boil and stir 1 minute.  Add wine, spaghetti, chicken and mushrooms.  Pour in 9 1/2 x 11 baking dish, sprayed with Pam. Sprinkle cheese and bake, uncovered, for 30 minutes.
