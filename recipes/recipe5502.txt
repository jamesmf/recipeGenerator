HOT CHICKEN SALAD


2  c. cooked chicken, cut in small pieces
1  c. chopped celery
1  c. cooked rice
3/4  c. mayonnaise
1  c. cream of chicken soup
1  can sliced water chestnuts, drained and chopped
3  hard boiled eggs
1  tsp. lemon juice
1  small onion, chopped
1  tsp. salt

      Mix all ingredients and spread into a casserole dish.
Topping:
1/4  stick butter
1  c. crushed potato chips
  (may use buttered bread crumbs)

      Sprinkle crushed potato chips over top.  Melt butter and pour over chips.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 to 40 minutes.
