BAKED MACARONI AND CHEESE


1 (1 lb.)  box macaroni
1  (1 lb.) container curd cottage cheese
1  (1 lb.) container sour cream
1  (16 oz.) bag sharp Cheddar cheese, shredded
  salt

      Cook macaroni and set aside. Mix cottage cheese and sour cream with a little under half the bag of cheese. Then combine mixture with macaroni. Spray a 13 x 9 pan with cooking spray and pour macaroni mixture into pan. Top with remaining cheese.  Bake at 350 degrees; until cheese bubbles or turns golden brown, whichever is preferred. Enjoy!
