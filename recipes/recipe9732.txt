MEATBALLS IN SAUCE


2  lb. hamburger
1  c. sour cream
1  egg, slightly beaten
1  pkg. onion soup mix
1 1/2  c. dry bread crumbs
2  cans cream of chicken soup
1  can water

      Combine first 5 ingredients and shape into meatballs. Heat together the soup and water. Drop meatballs into gravy and simmer for 1 hour.
