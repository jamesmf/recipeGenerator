COCONUT SEAFOAM CANDY


2 1/2  c. brown sugar, packed
1/2  c. syrup
1/2  c. water
2  stiffly beaten egg whites
1  tsp. vanilla
1  box coconut

      Combine sugar, water and syrup.  Let boil until it will form a hard ball in cold water.  Remove from heat.  Add slowly to egg whites, continually stirring.  Add vanilla and coconut.  Stir well until stiff.  Spoon onto wax paper.
