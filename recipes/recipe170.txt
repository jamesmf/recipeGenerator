IOWA BUTTERSCOTCH COOKIES


1  c. Crisco
1  c. brown sugar
1  c. white sugar
2  eggs, unbeaten
1  c. nuts
1  tsp. vanilla
3  c. flour
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. soda
1  tsp. baking powder

      Cream Crisco and sugars; add eggs, nuts and vanilla.  Sift flour, salt, soda and baking powder together.  Stir into batter and mix well.  Divide batter in half and shape into 2 rolls. Refrigerate overnight.  Slice in 1/4-inch slices and place on greased and floured cookie sheet.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 10 to 12 minutes.
