PICKLED BEET SALAD


3  c. canned beets in juice
1/4  c. vinegar
2  Tbsp. sugar
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. ground cinnamon
1/8  tsp. ground cloves
  dash of pepper

      Drain beets.  Place in a medium bowl.  Reserve juice in a saucepan.  Add vinegar and remaining ingredients to beet juice. Bring juice mixture to a boil.  Immediately remove from heat. Pour juice mixture over beets.  Cover and chill.  Drain beets before serving.  Serve Vidalia sliced onion.  Yield: 6 servings.
