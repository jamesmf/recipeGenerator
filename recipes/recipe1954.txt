ZUCCHINI CASSEROLE


4  c. fresh zucchini
1  can Spanish rice
2  c. grated cheese
1  onion (medium size), chopped
1 small  jar pimiento, chopped
1  lb. ground beef

      Brown ground beef; set aside.  Precook zucchini 10 minutes (do not overcook).  Place zucchini into a greased deep casserole dish (one layer).  Alternate layers with ground beef.  Add Spanish rice, pimiento and onion.  Bake in 350 degrees; oven for 20 minutes.  Remove from oven.  Add grated cheese on top.  Place back in oven for 5 minutes. Garnish with paprika and parsley.  Will serve 8 to 10 people.
