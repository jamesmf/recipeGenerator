GRANDMA BETH'S SALSA


4  large tomatoes
1  jalapeno pepper (seeds out)
1  bunch green onions
1  medium purple onion
3 small  cans chopped green chilies
1  Tbsp. salt
1  Tbsp. pepper
1 1/2  Tbsp. garlic salt
1  tsp. chili powder
3  pinches of oregano or Italian seasoning
1/2  c. oil

      Chop everything and mix well. Chill for 1 hour.
