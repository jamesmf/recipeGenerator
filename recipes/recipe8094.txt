MAIN COURSE


1  lb. ground turkey
1  c. crackers, crumbled
1  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. black pepper
1/4  c. brown sugar
1/2  package mild chili mix
1  egg
1/2  c. ketchup
1  medium onion, chopped
1/4  bell pepper, chopped
1/2  c. evaporated milk

	Heat oven to 325 degrees;.  Mix turkey, crackers, salt, pepper and brown sugar in large bowl.	Add remainder of ingredients and mix well with hands.  Place all mixed ingredients in a 9 x 9-inch glass Pyrex dish and mold into loaf form.  Bake in oven for 1 hour. Servings:  16.
