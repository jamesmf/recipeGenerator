BEANIE WEANIES


1  can pork and beans
1/2  c. ketchup
1/2  c. sugar
4  hot dogs

      Preheat oven to 325 degrees; or use medium size pot on top of stove. Combine beans, ketchup and sugar; stir well.  Chop hot dogs into small pieces and add to beans.  Stir occasionally.
      Cook on top of stove for 15 minutes on medium heat or bake in oven for 30 to 40 minutes in casserole dish.
