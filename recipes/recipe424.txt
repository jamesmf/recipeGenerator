GRILLED SANDWICHES



1  c. chopped ham
1/2  c. chopped Velveeta cheese
4  hard-boiled eggs, chopped
1  small onion, chopped fine
4  tsp. relish
1/2  c. salad dressing

      Mix all ingredients together.  Spread thick on buns.  Wrap in foil.  Bake 12 to 15 minutes in 375 degrees; oven.  Can be frozen before baking.
