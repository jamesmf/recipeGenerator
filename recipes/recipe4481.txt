TORTILLA CHEESE SOUP


1  can Campbell's fiesta cheese soup
1  can Campbell's cream of chicken soup
1  can medium enchilada sauce
2  c. milk
1 (4 oz.)  can mild diced green chilies
1  lb. diced, cooked chicken
  shredded Cheddar cheese, sour cream and broken tortilla chips (garnish)

      Mix all ingredients together. Heat on the stove with a double boiler to avoid scorching or heat in a microwave, stirring every 1 to 2 minutes. Serve in bowls. Garnish with shredded cheese, broken chips and  sour cream, if desired.
