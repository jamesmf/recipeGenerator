HOMEMADE CHICKEN NOODLE SOUP


2  qt. water
1  (2 1/2 lb.) broiler-fryer chicken, cut up
1  onion, finely chopped
1  stalk celery, finely chopped
2  Tbsp. fresh parsley, minced or 1 tsp. dried parsley
2  tsp. salt
1  tsp. rosemary, crushed
1/8  tsp. pepper
2  c. uncooked noodles

	Place all ingredients except noodles in a kettle or Dutch oven. Bring to a boil. Simmer 1 hour or until chicken is tender. Remove chicken. Cool and discard skin. Remove meat from bones and chop. Return chicken to stock and bring to a boil. Stir in noodles. Simmer 20 to 30 minutes or until noodles are done.
