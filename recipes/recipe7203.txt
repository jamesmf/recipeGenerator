SLOPPY JOES


1  lb. ground beef
1/2  tsp. dry mustard
1  small chopped onion
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
4  tsp. lemon juice
1  (20 oz.) ketchup
1  c. chopped celery
2  Tbsp. vinegar
2  Tbsp. brown sugar
1/2  c. water

      Brown hamburg; drain.  Combine rest of ingredients.  Simmer for 20 minutes.  Add ground beef.
