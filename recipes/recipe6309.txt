CALICO POTATO SALAD


4  lg. red potatoes, peeled and cooked (about 2 pounds)
1  1/2 cups whole kernel corn
1  cup shredded carrot
1/2  cup chopped red onion
1/2  cup chopped green pepper
1/2  cup diced sweet red pepper
1/2  cup sliced pitted ripe olives

      Cube potatoes; combine with corn, carrot, onion, peppers and olives in a salad bowl. Pour dressing over; toss lightly. Cover and chill.
Dressing:
1/2  cup olive oil
1/4  cup vinegar
1  T. sugar
1  1/2 t. chili powder
1  t. salt
  dash hot pepper sauce

      In a small bowl, combine all dressing ingredients, cover and chill.
