BEAN DIP


1/2  c. sugar
1/2  c. cider vinegar
1/2  c. oil
1  can pinto beans, drained
1  can black-eyed peas, drained
1  can white corn, drained
1  green pepper, 1/4-inch diced
1  red onion, 1/4-inch diced

      Boil together first 3 ingredients. Mix in remaining ingredients. Serve with corn chips. If doubling, don't double the onion.
