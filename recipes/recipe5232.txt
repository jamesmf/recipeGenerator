GLAZED FRUIT SALAD


1 small  can apricot pie filling
1 large  can drained mandarin oranges
1  can drained chunk pineapple
1  bunch of seedless red grapes
2  bananas, peeled and sliced
1  c. miniature marshmallows
1/4  c. walnuts or pecans, chopped

      Mix the first four ingredients together.  Add the last three items right before serving.
