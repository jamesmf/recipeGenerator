CAULIFLOWER SALAD SUPREME


1  medium head cauliflower, broken into flowerets
1  medium head lettuce, torn into pieces
1  small onion, finely chopped
1  lb. bacon, fried, drained and crumbled

      Layer ingredients in the order listed in a large salad bowl.
Dressing:
2  c. kraft mayonnaise
1/3  c. sugar
1/3  c. grated parmesan cheese
1/2  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper

      Combine all ingredients in a bowl; stir well. Spread dressing over top of layered salad; cover and chill 6 hours or overnight. Toss salad well before serving. Garnish with bacon bits and chopped hard cooked eggs if desired.
