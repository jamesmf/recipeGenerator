ROSEMARY CREAM-CHEESE EGGS


4  eggs
1/4  c. cream
1  tsp. salt
3  sprigs rosemary, snipped into small pieces
3  Tbsp. cream cheese
  additional rosemary, optional

      Beat eggs, cream and salt together in a small bowl.  Pour mixture into a lightly greased, hot skillet, sprinkle rosemary on top, and scramble until slightly runny.  Add cream cheese in dollops, and fold in gently.  Garnish with rosemary sprig, if desired, and serve hot.  Makes 2 servings.
