CRABMEAT TREATS


1  c. crabmeat
1/4  c. Gruyere cheese, grated
1  Tbsp. dry white wine
1/2  tsp. salt
1  Tbsp. mayonnaise
  white bread, cut into rounds and toasted on one side

      Mix first 5 ingredients together.  Pile high on toasted rounds of white bread.  Bake in 450 degrees; oven until heated through.
