MACARONI AND CHEESE


2  c. macaroni
4  c. water
1/2  tsp. salt

      Bring water to a boil in a medium size pot.  Add salt and macaroni bring back to a rolling boil.  Reduce heat to a gentle boil.  Cook 8 to 10 minutes.  Drain and add sauce.
Cheese Sauce:
2  Tbsp. margarine
2  Tbsp. flour
1/8  c. sugar
1/2  tsp. salt
1  c. milk
4  oz. cheese (about 1 cup)

      Melt margarine in a sauce pan.  Stir in sugar, salt and flour until blended.  Next add milk and stir until thick.  Add cheese, stir until melted and pour over macaroni.  Mix well.  Pour into casserole dish and bake for 15 minutes at 350 degrees;.
