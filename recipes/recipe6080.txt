ONION-TOMATO SOUP


6  c. (2 large) sweet Spanish onions
1/2  c. (1 stick) butter
14  oz. (2 cans) tomatoes, stewed
31  oz. (3 cans) beef broth
1/2  tsp. basil
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper

      Peel and thinly slice onions.  In a large saucepan, saut√© onions in butter.  Add tomatoes, beef broth, basil, salt and pepper.
      Cook over low heat for 45 minutes.
      Serve with toasted French bread.
