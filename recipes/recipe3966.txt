ZESTY SNACK MIX


4  c. Chex cereal
4  c. corn chips
1  c. salted peanuts
1  c. quick cooking oats
1/3  c. butter, melted
3  Tbsp. honey
4  tsp. chile powder
1  tsp. dried oregano
1  tsp. onion salt

      In a large bowl, combine the cereal, corn chips, peanuts and oats.  Combine butter and honey; drizzle over cereal mixture. Sprinkle with chile powder, oregano and salt; toss to coat. Spread evenly on an ungreased baking pan.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 25 minutes, stirring once.  Cool.  Store in an airtight container.
