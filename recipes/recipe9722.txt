CHICKEN TETRAZZINI


1  can sliced mushrooms, drained
1/2  tsp. paprika
1/4  tsp. pepper
1/2  tsp. celery salt
1/2  c. chopped onion
2  c. cubed cooked chicken
3  c. cooked spaghetti (1-inch pieces)
2/3  c. grated Parmesan cheese
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  can cream of mushroom soup

      Mix well together and pour into greased 9 x 13-inch pan. Top with 1 cup crushed corn flakes mixed with 2 tablespoons melted butter. Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 to 45 minutes or until bubbly.
