CHICKEN PARMESAN


4  chicken breast halves, skinned and boned
2  Tbsp. cornstarch
1/2  tsp. oregano
2 (14 1/2 oz. each)  cans Italian-style stewed tomatoes
1/4  c. grated Parmesan cheese

      Preheat oven to 425 degrees;. Place chicken breasts into an 8 x 8-inch baking dish. Bake for 15 to 20 minutes. In a saucepan combine tomatoes, cornstarch and oregano. Cook on medium heat, stirring constantly until sauce thickens. Pour sauce over chicken and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese. Bake for 5 more minutes. Yields 4 servings.
      Optional: Can add a touch of hot sauce, basil or a sprinkle of garlic. Can top with Mozzarella cheese slices during the last 5 minutes to make even more authentic.
