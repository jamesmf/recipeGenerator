CHRISTMAS PICKLES


1 1/4  c. white sugar
1  medium onion, chopped fine
1  tsp. celery seed
3  Tbsp. vinegar
1  Tbsp. prepared horseradish
1  qt. dill pickles

      Drain 1 quart dill pickles and cut in strips.  Place back in jar and add ingredients.  Leave in refrigerator; shake often to dissolve sugar.
