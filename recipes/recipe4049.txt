HOT BROCCOLI DIP


1 10  oz. pkg. frozen broccoli
1  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  c. sour cream
1  clove of garlic, crushed
1  envelope onion soup mix

      Cook broccoli according to package directions.  Drain, reserving the liquid.  Add water to liquid to make 1/2 cup.  Place broccoli, liquid and sour cream in blender container.  Blend until smooth.  Pour into saucepan.  Add soup mix, Worcestershire and garlic.  Heat and serve.
