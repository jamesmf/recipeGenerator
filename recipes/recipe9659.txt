HOT TURKEY & CHEDDAR CASSEROLE


  butter
3  c. cubed cooked turkey
3/4  c. celery, chopped
1  can sliced water chestnuts
1/2  c. red & green bell peppers, chopped
1 1/2  c. mayonnaise
1  Tbsp. grated onion
2  Tbsp. lemon juice
2  c. shredded cheddar cheese, divided
1  c. shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
1  c. corn flake crumbs

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Coat a 9 X 13 inch baking dish with butter. In a medium bowl, combine turkey, celery, water chestnuts, bell pepper, mayonnaise, onion, lemon juice, 1 cup cheddar cheese and 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese; mix well.
      Place the mixture in the baking dish and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until heated through. In a medium bowl, combine the remaining 1 cup cheddar cheese, 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese and corn flakes. Sprinkle cheese mixture over baked turkey casserole and bake for 5-8 minutes longer, or until cheese melts.
