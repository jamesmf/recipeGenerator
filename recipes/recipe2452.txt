BUNNY PATCH DESSERT


1 (10.75 oz.)  pkg. frozen pound cake, thawed
1 (21 oz.)  can pie filling (any flavor)
1  box Cool Whip
1  c. coconut
  green food coloring
  jelly beans, assorted Easter candies and decorations

      Slice cake into 10 slices.  Line bottom of 12 x 8-inch baking dish with cake slices.  Top with pie filling and all of the whipped topping.  Refrigerate 1 hour or until ready to serve.  Tint coconut pale green with food coloring.  Sprinkle over center of whipped topping.  Decorate with jelly beans, Easter candies and decorations.
