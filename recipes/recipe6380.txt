ASIAN CABBAGE STEW


1  gal. cabbage, finely shredded
1 1/2  qt. carrots, grated
3  c. peppers (red and/or green), julienned

 
Dressing:
3/4  c. vegetable oil
3/4  c. Chinese rice vinegar
6  Tbsp. soy sauce
1/4  c. brown sugar
1  Tbsp. ginger root (fresh), grated
1  tsp. chili oil or Tabasco sauce

      Combine the vegetables and set aside. Whisk together the dressing ingredients. Pour the dressing over the vegetables and toss well. Marinate for 10 to 15 minutes. Garnish with chopped peanuts or sesame seeds if desired.
