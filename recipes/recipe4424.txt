CAULIFLOWER AND BROCCOLI SALAD


1  head cauliflower
1  bunch broccoli
1  large purple onion
1 (1 lb.)  pkg. bacon, fried crisp and crumbled

 
Topping:
1  c. mayonnaise
1/2  c. sugar
1/4  c. Parmesan cheese

      Separate cauliflower and broccoli into bite-size pieces. Layer in large container.  Separate onion into rings and place on top of vegetables.  Spread crumbled bacon onto this.
      Mix topping and spread over this.  Refrigerate overnight or up to 24 hours.  Mix well and serve.
