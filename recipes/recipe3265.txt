CRESCENT SQUARES


1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
1/2  c. sugar
8  pack crescent rolls
  strawberries
  mandarin oranges

      Beat together cream cheese and sugar until creamy.  Bake crescent rolls flat on cookie sheet at 325 degrees; until golden brown. Cool.  Spread cream cheese all over top of crescent roll.  Top with strawberries, oranges or other fruits.
