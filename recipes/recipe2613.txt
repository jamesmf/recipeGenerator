STICKY BUNS


2  loaves frozen bread, thawed
2  c. brown sugar
1 large  pkg. vanilla pudding
1  stick butter, softened
1  tsp. cinnamon
  nuts (optional)

      Tear 1 loaf thawed bread into irregular pieces and place in buttered 9 x 13-inch pan.  Sprinkle the mixture of brown sugar, pudding, butter and cinnamon over the bread pieces.  Tear up the second loaf of thawed bread and layer on top.  Cover with a wet towel and let rise about 4 hours.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes. Turn upside-down immediately.  Nuts and raisins may be added with the brown sugar layer.
