FRENCH FRY CASSEROLE


1  lb. ground chuck
  onion, chopped
  homemade sliced fries
  Velveeta cheese

      Brown ground meat (seasoned to your taste) and onion; drain. Season and fry french fries as usual; drain. Place ground meat back in skillet; place fries on top of meat. Place cheese over the top and let melt. Yum Yum!
