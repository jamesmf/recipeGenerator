ROCK CORNISH HENS WITH GRAPES


2  Tbsp. margarine
1  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper
2  Rock Cornish game hens
1  c. chicken broth
1/2  c. grape juice (unsweetened)
1/2 to 1  c. seedless grapes, halved

      Melt margarine in saucepan.  Add salt and pepper.  Split hens; place skin side up in shallow baking pan.  Baste with margarine. Roast hens for 1 1/2 hours uncovered.  When done, pour pan drippings into small saucepan.  Add chicken broth and grape juice. Simmer 15 minutes until reduced to 1 cup.  Add grapes; cook 2 minutes.  Calories:  206.
