BAKED BEANS AND HAMBURGER


1 large  can pork and beans
1  lb. hamburger
1  pkg. onion soup mix
1/2  c. catsup
1  Tbsp. vinegar
1  Tbsp. mustard

      Heat beans while frying hamburger. Add onion soup mix. After this is well blended, add catsup, vinegar and mustard. A pinch of brown sugar may be added if desired. Mix all together and put in baking dish. Bake for 30 to 45 minutes at 300 degrees;.
