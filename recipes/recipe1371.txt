VEGGIE SALAD


2 (No. 303)  cans green beans, drained
2 (No. 303)  cans peas, drained
1 (No. 303)  can white corn, drained
6  stalks celery
1 (4 oz.)  jar pimento
1  bunch green onion
2  medium green peppers
1  c. sugar
1  c. oil
1  c. vinegar
2  Tbsp. water
  salt and pepper to taste

      Mix all together and leave to marinate overnight.
