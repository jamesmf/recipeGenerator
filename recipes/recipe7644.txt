CRISPY CHICKEN CASSEROLE


2  c. diced cooked chicken
1  can cream of mushroom soup
3/4  c. mayonnaise
1  c. diced celery
1  c. cooked rice
1  Tbsp. lemon juice
1  small onion, grated
1  can mushrooms, drained
1  c. corn flakes, crushed
3  Tbsp. butter, diced

      Mix first 8 ingredients together and put  in  a 2 quart casserole dish.  Sprinkle corn flakes and butter on top.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes.
