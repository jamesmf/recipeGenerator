MARTA'S FRIED CATFISH


8  whole catfish
  a little garlic powder
  a little salt
4  c. cornmeal
2  c. oil

	First clean the fish.  Cut the fish into filets.  Then sprinkle filets with garlic powder and salt.  Next, pour oil into frying pan and turn stove on to medium-high heat.  While the oil gets hot, put cornmeal in bowl and dip filets in cornmeal.	When oil is hot, fry the filets until golden-brown.  Serve catfish hot, with a salad.  Feeds 4 people.
