BAKED CARAMEL CORN


5  qt. popped corn, kept warm
2  sticks oleo
2  c. brown sugar
1/2  tsp. soda
1/2  c. Karo
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. vanilla

      Mix melted oleo, sugar, Karo and salt. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly. Boil 5 minutes, without stirring. Remove from heat. Add vanilla and soda. Pour over corn. Bake at 250 degrees; for 1 hour and stir every 15 minutes.
