MONSTER COOKIES


2  eggs
1/2  c. butter
1/2  c. brown sugar
1/2  c. sugar
1/2  c. peanut butter
1  tsp. vanilla
1/2  c. quick cooking rolled oats
1  tsp. baking soda
1  (7 oz.) bag M&M's or 1 (6 oz.) bag chocolate chips
1 1/2  c. flour

      Blend eggs, butter, both sugars, peanut butter and vanilla until smooth; add oats, soda and M&M's or chips.  Mix well.  Add flour and mix well.  Drop by spoonfuls on cookie sheet.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 10 to 12 minutes.
