THREE BEAN CASSEROLE


1  onion, chopped
1/2  lb. bacon, diced
1  lb. ground beef
1  c. ketchup
1/2  c. brown sugar
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
2  Tbsp. vinegar
1/4  tsp. prepared mustard
1/8  tsp. ginger
1 (16 oz.)  can Campbell's pork and beans
1 (16 oz.)  can kidney beans, drained
1 (16 oz.)  can butter beans or lima beans, drained

      In skillet, fry onion and bacon until brown.  At the same time, fry the ground beef in another skillet until brown.  Drain off excess fat and add to onion and bacon mixture.  In a small bowl, mix ketchup, sugar, Worcestershire sauce, vinegar, mustard and ginger.  In another bowl, combine all the beans.  Grease a large casserole and spread a thin layer of meat mixture over the bottom. Cover with a layer of beans, then sauce.  Repeat layers.  Bake, uncovered, in a 350 degrees; oven for 1 hour.  Makes a large casserole .... and freezes well.
