PINEAPPLE CHEESE BALL


1/2  c. chopped nuts
1/2  tsp. seasoning salt
2  large cream cheese
1 small  can crushed pineapple

      Drain pineapple; add to cream cheese along with seasoning salt.  Mix thoroughly.  Place in container to serve.  May be made a day in advance.  Refrigerate until time to serve.  Top with pecans before serving.
