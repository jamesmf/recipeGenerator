TORTELLINI


2  pkg. tortellini (cheese-filled----cooked and drained)
2  or 3   tomatoes, chopped
1  green peppers, chopped
2  or 3  stalks of celery, chopped
1  can black olives, drained
4  or 5 green onions, chopped
1/2  bottle of McCormick salad dressing or 1/2 bottle of Spice Island (This is expensive, but it is what makes the salad)
  1-8 oz. bottle of Zesty Italian

      Mix all ingredients and leave in refrigerator overnight.
      Optional: Can add hearts of palm, eggplant, or zucchini.
      Makes a party size amount. Good for lent.
