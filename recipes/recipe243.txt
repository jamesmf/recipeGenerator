HICKORY NUT COOKIES


1  lb. butter, softened
1 1/4  c. confectioners sugar, sifted
4  c. cake flour
2  c. hickory nuts (the more the better)
3/4  Tbsp. vanilla

      Cream butter and sugar.  Stir in flour, nuts and vanilla.  Mix well.  Shape in 1-inch balls.  Bake 15 to 20 minutes at 325 degrees;.
