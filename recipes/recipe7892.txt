FRENCH ONION STEW


1  lb. cubed beef or lamb
12  oz. pearl onions, whole
4  large carrots, sliced
6  oz. small mushrooms, sliced
3  large celery stalks, chopped
1/2  cup tomato juice
1/2  cup water
1/4  cup fine tapioca
1  tbsp sugar
2  tsp. salt
2  bay leaves
1  tsp. basil
1/4  tsp. pepper

      Place all ingredients in clay oven stew pot.  Bake at 300 degrees;F for 3 hours, stirring only 3 times.  This can also be cooked in a slow cooker for the day.
