CHICKEN LIVERS


2  onions, sliced thin
2  stalks celery, sliced thin
1  Tbsp. diet margarine
1  lb. chicken livers
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. fresh ground pepper

      Saut√© onions and celery in margarine until celery is wilted. Remove and set aside.  Add livers and turn heat high.  Cook, turning once or twice, until done, approximately 3 to 5 minutes. Top with onions and celery.  Season with salt and pepper.
      Exchanges per serving - 1 serving = 1 fat, 3 meat, 1 "A" and 1 "B" vegetable.  Yields 4 servings, 279 calories per serving.
