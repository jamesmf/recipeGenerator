CHICKEN SALAD SURPRISE


3  pieces leftover fried chicken
2  boiled eggs
1  onion, chopped fine
  dill or sweet pickle relish
  celery, chopped fine
1/2  apple, peeled and chopped
  salt and pepper to taste
  real mayonnaise

      Place leftover chicken (deboned) in food chopper until finely chopped; add eggs and chop (pickle relish, celery and apple can be chopped in food chopper or by hand); mix together and season with salt and pepper to taste. Moisten with real mayonnaise to desired consistency.
      Hint: Tastes wonderful with spicy fried chicken leftovers!
