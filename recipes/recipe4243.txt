TOSTADA DIP


1  pkg. taco seasoning
1  can refried beans
16  oz. sour cream
1  pkg. dry party seasoning mix
  lettuce, cheese, tomatoes, peppers, olives, onions as desired (for topping)

      For the bottom layer, mix taco seasoning with refried beans. The next layer is sour cream mixed with dry party seasoning mix. Top with lettuce, cheese, tomatoes, peppers, olives, onions as desired. Serve with nacho chips.
