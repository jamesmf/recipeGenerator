FUDGE PIE


1/2  stick butter
1 1/2  c. sugar
3  Tbsp. cocoa
2  Tbsp. flour
1  tsp. vanilla
2  whole eggs
1/2  c. evaporated milk

      Mix sugar, cocoa and flour.  Melt butter; add to sugar mixture. Mix eggs and add.  Mix well; do not beat.  Add vanilla and milk. Pour into 9-inch unbaked pie shell and bake at 400 degrees; for 10 minutes. Reduce to 350 degrees; for 20 to 25 minutes.
