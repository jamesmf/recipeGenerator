CHICKEN-BROCCOLI CASSEROLE


1  whole chicken
1  large bag broccoli
1 small  box spaghetti
1/2  cup green onion, tops included
1 small  jar pimento
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  can celery soup
1/2  cup evaporated milk
1 (8 oz.)  jar Cheez Whiz

      Boil chicken and remove from broth when done. Debone chicken. Cook broccoli and drain. Cook spaghetti in chicken broth (do not drain). Add cooked broccoli, chicken, green onions, pimento, mushroom & celery soup, milk and cheese in spaghetti and chicken broth. Pour in large casserole and  bake at 325 degrees for 30 minutes.
