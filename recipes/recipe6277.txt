CRANBERRY RELISH MOLD


1  pkg. mixed fruit jello or cranberry jello
3/4  c. boiling water
1  chopped orange with skin
1  can whole berry cranberry sauce
1/2  c. chopped pecans

      Dissolve jello in boiling water.  Add orange, cranberry sauce and pecans.  Chill in mold until firm.  Yields 2 1/2 cups.
       Delicious with turkey and cornbread-sausage stuffing.
