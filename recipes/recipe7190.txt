7 LAYER DINNER


2  large potatoes, sliced
1  celery stick, chopped
2  carrots, sliced
1  green pepper, cut up
1  medium onion, cut up
1  lb. hamburg
1/2  c. rice or barley
2  c. tomato juice

      Layer ingredients; end with hamburg as top layer.  Pour tomato juice over top.  Salt and pepper to taste.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 to 1 1/2 hours.
