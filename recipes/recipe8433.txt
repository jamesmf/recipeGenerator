LAVON'S BAKED PANCAKE


1  stick margarine
3  eggs
3/4  c. flour
3/4  c. milk
  syrup (optional)
  fruit (optional)
  powdered sugar

      Preheat oven to 400 degrees;.  Melt margarine in 1-quart casserole dish.  Mix together eggs, flour and milk.  Pour into the casserole dish.  Bake for 30 to 45 minutes.  Top will be slightly brown, looks like fallen souffl.  Cut and serve warm with any topping: syrup, fruit (strawberries, peaches, berries).  Sprinkle with powdered sugar.
