OLD FASHIONED CANDIED YAMS


3  medium yams
1/2  c. granulated sugar
1  c. brown sugar
1  tsp. nutmeg
1  tsp. cinnamon
1/2  stick butter or margarine
1  tsp. vanilla extract
  pinch of salt (optional)
1 1/2  c. water

      Peel and cut yams in quarters.  Sprinkle granulated sugar and brown sugar over yams in a medium saucepan.  Add nutmeg, cinnamon, butter, vanilla, salt and water.  Cover and simmer over medium heat until yams are cooked and liquid forms a syrup.  Remove from heat and serve.
