FAVORITE HOLIDAY CAKE
(Peter Paul Mound Cake)  

1  c. Crisco shortening
2  c. sugar
5  eggs
1  c. all-purpose flour
1  c. self-rising flour
1  c. sweet milk
2  tsp. vanilla flavoring

      Cream shortening; gradually add sugar, beating until light and fluffy and sugar is dissolved.  Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition of egg.  Combine flours together; add to creamed mixture alternately with milk, beginning and ending with flour.  Add vanilla, mixing well.  Pour into 2 greased and floured 9-inch cake pans.  Preheat oven to 350 degrees;.  Bake for 30 to 35 minutes.  Remove from pans and cool completely.
      Split layers in half, rounding.  Place cut side up on cake plate.  Spread evenly with 1/3 cup of Coconut Filling.  Repeat with second, third and fourth layer.
Coconut Filling:
1  c. sugar
1  c. sweet milk
1  lb. frozen flaked coconut
12  marshmallows
1  tsp. vanilla flavoring

      Combine milk and sugar in saucepan; bring to a boil.  Add coconut and marshmallows.  Cook over medium heat for 5 minutes. Remove from heat and stir in vanilla.  Yields 2 1/2 cups filling.
Chocolate Filling:
2  c. sugar
1/2  c. butter or margarine
1  c. evaporated milk
2 to 3  Tbsp. cocoa

      Combine all ingredients; cook over medium heat, almost to soft-ball stage (230 degrees;), stirring constantly.  Remove from heat. Beat until thick enough to spread.  Yields enough frosting for Peter Paul Mound Cake.
