COOL SUMMER SALAD


1  box lemon Jell-O
1  lb. cup cottage cheese
1  can crushed pineapple, drained
1  large tub Cool Whip (regular size)

      Pour cottage cheese in bowl. Add Jell-O. Stir. Add pineapple. Stir. Fold in Cool Whip until smooth. Refrigerate if possible before serving.
      I have substituted different flavors of Jell-O:  strawberry, cherry, etc.
      Recipe shared by my sister, Sharon Newberry.
