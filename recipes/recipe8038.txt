CHICKEN BREASTS


6  chicken breasts
1 1/2  c. sour cream
1/4  c. lemon juice
3  tsp. horseradish
2  Tbsp. salt
  cracker crumbs

	Lay chicken breasts in oblong baking dish.  Mix sour cream, lemon juice, horseradish and salt together and put over chicken. Prepare day before; cover with foil and refrigerate.  When ready to use, roll each piece in cracker crumbs.	Bake at 350 degrees; approximately 1 hour.
