SCRABBLE
(Snack Food)  

12  oz. white chocolate
2  c. crunchy peanut butter
2 large (12 oz.)  boxes Golden Grahams cereal
1 (14 oz.)  box raisins
1 1/2  c. dry roasted peanuts
1  lb. box powdered sugar

      Melt together white chocolate and peanut butter in extra large pan. Mix together cereal, raisins and peanuts. Add to chocolate and peanut butter mixture and stir to coat. Sprinkle with powdered sugar. Store in airtight container.
