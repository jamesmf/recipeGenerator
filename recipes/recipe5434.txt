FRUIT SALAD OR DESSERT


1 large  can sliced peaches, drained
1 large  can mixed fruit, drained
1 large  can chunk pineapple, drained (save juice)
1  box instant vanilla pudding
1  tsp. Tang (if using sugar-free Tang, use 1/2 tsp.)
2  bananas
  nuts

	Mix pudding with pineapple juice and Tang. Mix with fruit and let chill. Just before serving, add bananas and nuts to garnish.
