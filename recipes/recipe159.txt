GINGERSNAPS


1  c. shortening
1  c. brown sugar
1  egg
1  c. molasses
2  Tbsp. vinegar
5  c. flour
1 1/2  tsp. soda
1/2  tsp. salt
1  Tbsp. ginger
1  tsp. cinnamon
1  tsp. cloves, ground

      Mix all ingredients.  Chill for 3 hours.  Pinch off about 2 teaspoons of dough and roll into a ball with hands.  Press with glass dipped in sugar and place on greased cookie sheet.  Bake at 350 degrees; for about 8 minutes.
