APPLE TURNOVERS


2  slices thin white bread (crusts removed)
1  apple, peeled, cored and sliced
1  tsp. lemon juice
1  Tbsp. water
1/4  tsp. cinnamon
2  Tbsp. white raisins

      Roll bread as thin as possible.  Cook remaining ingredients over low heat for 5 minutes or until tender.  Place 1/2 of apple mixture on one slice of bread.  Moisten edges with water and fold over in a triangle.  Repeat with 2nd slice of bread.  Bake at 425 degrees; for 7 minutes or until browned.
      Makes 2 servings with 98 calories each.
