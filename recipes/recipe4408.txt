FRUIT SALAD


2  c. sugar
2  c. water
12  oz. frozen orange juice
1  can crushed pineapple
1  can pineapple chunks
1  can mandarin oranges, drained
  maraschino cherries, drained
4  bananas, sliced

      Combine sugar, water and orange juice in saucepan.  Bring to boil over medium heat.  Cook for 3 minutes.  Cool.  Pour over combined fruits in a freezer-safe bowl.  Cover and freeze.  Thaw until slushy before serving.
