LOW CHOLESTEROL MARINADE FOR FISH


  any fresh fish fillets
1/2  c. white wine
1/4  c. corn oil
1/8  tsp. garlic powder
1/4  tsp. dried parsley
1/8  tsp. pepper
  dash of Tabasco sauce
  chopped black olives
  sliced fresh mushrooms
  chopped pimientos

      Combine the wine, corn oil, garlic powder, parsley, pepper and Tabasco sauce.  Pour over fish in oven pan.  Top with the olives, mushrooms and pimientos.  Let stand in refrigerator for 1 to 2 hours.  Bake fish at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes, less if thin pieces, or until fish flakes with fork.
