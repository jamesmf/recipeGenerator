FOUL SALAD (FAVA BEAN)


1 (15 oz.)  can foul mudammas "small fava bean"
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  cup chopped green onion
1/4  cup chopped parsley
1/2  cup chopped tomato
  juice of half lemon
1  cup water
1/2  Tbsp. olive oil

      Drain the foul, wash it and put in a small pan.  Add salt, 1 cup of water and let cook until it boils.  Pour mixture into bowl, add lemon juice, onion, parlsey and oil.  Mix well.
      Serve with pita bread.
