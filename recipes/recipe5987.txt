CREAMY WALDORF SALAD


2- 3  oz. pkgs. lemon jello
2  c. boiling water
1  c. cold water
1- 10  oz. can ready to serve vanilla pudding
1/2  c. celery, chopped
2  medium apples, unpeeled and diced
1/2  c. walnuts, chopped

      Dissolve jello in hot water, add cold water and chill until partially set.  Then add pudding and beat 2 minutes with mixer. Chill until mixture mounds when spooned.  Fold in celery, apples, and nuts.  Pour into 7 - 8 cup molds.  Chill until firm or overnight.  Serves 10 -12.
