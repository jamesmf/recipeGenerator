BROCCOLI ORANGE SALAD


4  c. fresh broccoli florets (about 1 1/2 lb. Fresh)
1/2  medium purple onion, chopped
1/2  c. raisins
1/2  c. pecan pieces
3/4  c. low-fat mayonnaise
1/4  c. sugar
1 1/2  Tbsp. white vinegar
1 (11 oz.)  can mandarin oranges, drained

      Combine first four ingredients in a bowl; set aside.  Combine low-fat mayonnaise, sugar and vinegar, add to broccoli mixture. Stir gently to coat.  Carefully stir in mandarin oranges.  Cover and refrigerate at least 3 hours or overnight before serving.  (It can be served immediately, but it's better after some refrigeration.)
      Yield:  6 servings.
