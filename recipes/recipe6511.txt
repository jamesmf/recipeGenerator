CHEESEBURGER CASSEROLE


1  lb. ground beef
1  medium onion
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  can tomato soup
1 large  pkg. shell noodles
10  oz. Velveeta cheese

      Cook beef. Add chopped onions and drain. Season with salt and pepper. Put beef and onion mixture in a saucepan. Add soups. NO water. Cut cheese into small squares and drop in saucepan. Cook on low. Cook noodles and drain. When cheese melts, pour over noodles and mix. Serve with rolls and a salad.
