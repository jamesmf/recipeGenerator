JUST GOOD STUFF


1 large  can peaches, sliced
1 large  can pears, sliced
1 large  can pineapple chunks
1 regular  can apricots, sliced
1 medium  jar maraschino cherries
1  c. walnuts
3/4  c. light brown sugar
1/2  stick butter
1  tsp. curry powder

      You can melt this in microwave.  Pour over fruit.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour in a large, oblong dish.  Refrigerate overnight.  Heat a little before serving.
