ITALIAN RICOTTA COOKIES


1  lb. margarine
3  eggs
1  lb. Ricotta
2  c. sugar
4 1/2  c. flour
4  tsp. baking powder

      Cream margarine, eggs and Ricotta.  Add sugar, flour and baking powder.  Roll dough with hands into very small balls.  Place on ungreased cookie sheet.  Bake in 350 degrees; oven for 10 to 15 minutes. Do not brown cookies.
      Icing:  Combine confectioners sugar mixed with very little milk.  Make as much as needed to cover cookies.
