HEALTHY NEW ENGLAND CLAM CHOWDER


2  Tbsp. margarine
1  large onion, finely chopped
1 (8 oz.)  bottle clam juice
1  c. water
2  to 3 medium potatoes, peeled and diced
2 (7 1/2 oz.)  cans minced clams
2  c. nonfat milk
1 small  can evaporated milk
  salt and pepper to taste

      Melt margarine in large saucepan.  Add onion and saut√© until limp.  Add clam juice, water and potatoes.  Simmer until potatoes are tender.  Add clams and milks.  Heat slowly over low heat about 45 minutes.  Season to taste with salt and pepper.  Makes about 6 servings.
