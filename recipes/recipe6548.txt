BARBECUED MARINATED CHICKEN


1/4  c. dry sherry
2  Tbsp. honey
1  Tbsp. soy sauce
1  tsp. dry mustard
1/2  tsp. ground ginger
1/4  tsp. paprika
1  chicken, cut up

      Marinate chicken in mixture of first 6 ingredients (easiest in a Ziploc bag; turn occasionally). Microwave chicken 18 minutes on High. Immediately transfer to barbecue and cook about 10 minutes longer, basting with marinade. (Discard excess marinade.)
