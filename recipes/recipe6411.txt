MOLDED ORANGE CREAM SALAD


1 (6 oz.)  can frozen orange juice concentrate
1/2  c. boiling water
8  oz. pkg. cream cheese
1 (3 oz.)  pkg. orange flavored gelatin
1/2  c. lemon juice
1  c. heavy cream

      Defrost frozen orange juice concentrate. Turn into a small bowl the contents of package of orange flavored gelatin. Add boiling water and stir until gelatin is dissolved. Add lemon juice, then the orange concentrate. Stir quickly to combine. Pour 1/2 cup of the gelatin mixture into a 5 or 6-cup mold. If necessary, chill remainder of gelatin. It should be of a heavy, syrupy consistency.
      Meanwhile in a large bowl, place cream cheese. Gradually add heavy cream. When cheese and cream are well combined, increase speed and beat until fluffy. Add gelatin gradually to cream and cheese; stir gently to combine. Turn into mold and refrigerate until firm.
