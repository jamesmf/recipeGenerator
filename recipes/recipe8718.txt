SUPER EASY POT PIE


2  c. frozen vegetables (mixed variety), thawed
1  can condensed cream of vegetable soup
1  c. Bisquick mix
1/2  c. milk
1  egg

	Preheat oven to 400 degrees;.  Mix vegetables and soup in 9-inch pie plate or casserole dish.  Stir remaining ingredients in separate bowl until well blended.  Pour over top of vegetable/soup mix. Bake about 30 minutes or until golden brown.  Yields 6 servings.
