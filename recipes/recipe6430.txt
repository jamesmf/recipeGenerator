CABBAGE STEW


1  pound ground beef
1  onion, chopped
1  can kidney beans
1 - 8  ounce can tomato sauce
1  can stewed tomatoes
1/2  head cabbage, chopped
1/2  cup water

      Brown ground beef.  Add other ingredients.  Cook until cabbage is tender.
      **Cooks Note - this dish is good served with mashed potatoes**
