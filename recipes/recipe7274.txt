CHICKEN POT PIE


2 pie crusts (1  pkg. Pillsbury refrigerated ready-made crusts in biscuit section)
  cooked, cubed chicken (1 lb. boned, skinned chicken breast or your choice)
14  oz. mixed vegetables (canned or frozen)
1  can cream of chicken soup (no added water or milk)
  salt, pepper and a little basil to taste
  fresh chopped onion and chopped celery (zap in microwave until soft)*

      *May substitute dried onion flakes, dried celery flakes and perhaps a little extra milk.
      Put chicken and vegetables in crust. Mix soup, milk, onion, celery flakes and seasonings. Pour over chicken and veggies. Cover with top crust and seal. Cut slits in top crust. Bake on middle rack at 425 degrees; for 15 minutes, then at 350 degrees; for 20 minutes. Let set 10 to 15 minutes before cutting.
