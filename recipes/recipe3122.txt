


5  Tbsp. margarine
1  tsp. paprika
1/2  tsp. crushed red pepper
1/2  tsp. ground cumin
11  c. popped popcorn
6  Tbsp. grated Parmesan cheese

      In a saucepan, melt the margarine. Stir the paprika, red pepper and cumin into the margarine. Let cool for about 5 minutes. Place popcorn in large bowl. Pour spice mixture on top; sprinkle with cheese. Toss until popcorn is coated.
