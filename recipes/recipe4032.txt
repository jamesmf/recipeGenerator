RANCH CHICKEN BALL


8  oz. cream cheese
10  oz. (1 can) chicken breast chunks
1  oz. (1 pkg.) ranch salad dressing, dry mix (Hidden Valley)
2  Tbsp. almonds, sliced (optional)

      Cream together cream cheese, chicken breast chunks and salad dressing mix.  Refrigerate for several hours in a Cool Whip tub which has been lined with Saran Wrap.  After several hours (or overnight), turn out onto a plate and top with almonds.
      Serve with Triscuits or favorite crackers.
