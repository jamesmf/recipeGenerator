GARLIC CRISPS


4  (8-inch) flour tortillas
  butter-flavored cooking spray
1  tsp. garlic salt

      Cut tortillas into quarters. Coat with cooking spray and sprinkle with garlic salt. Place on an ungreased baking sheet. Bake at 350 degrees; for 12 minutes or until golden. Cool. Store in an airtight container. Yield: 16 crisps.
       Prep: 4 minutes. Bake: 12 minutes.
