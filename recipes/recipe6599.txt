MIDWEST CHOPS AND POTATOES


6  (1-inch) pork loin chops
1  Tbsp. vegetable oil
1 1/2  tsp. seasoning salt
1  tsp. pepper
1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can cream of celery soup
1/2  c. milk
1/2  c. sour cream
1 (24 oz.)  pkg. frozen hash browns
1  c. shredded Cheddar cheese, divided
1 (2.8 oz.)  can French fried onions, divided

      Sprinkle chips with seasoning salt and pepper. Brown both sides in hot vegetable oil in large skillet; set aside. Stir together soup, milk, sour cream, half of cheese and half of onions. Add hash browns and mix well. Pour mixture in 9 x 13 casserole dish. Lay pork chops on top of mixture. Cover with foil and cook at 325 degrees; for 1 1/2 hours or until chops are tender. Uncover and add rest of onions and cheese. Cook for 15 minutes until cheese melts and onions are crisp.
