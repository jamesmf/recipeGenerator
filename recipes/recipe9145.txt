GROUND BEEF AND CABBAGE CASSEROLE


1  lb. ground beef
1  large onion, chopped
  salt and pepper to taste
4  c. chopped cabbage
4  Tbsp. rice
1  can tomato soup
1  can water

      Cook ground beef and onion until browned. Drain and add rest of ingredients. Pour into baking dish and bake at 325 degrees for 35-40 minutes.
