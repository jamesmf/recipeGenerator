CREAMY FRENCH DRESSING


1  c. mayonnaise
1/4  c. red wine vinegar
1  Tbsp. paprika
1  tsp. salt
1/4  c. corn oil
1 1/2  Tbsp. sugar
1  Tbsp. dry mustard
1  clove garlic, minced

      Beat oil into mayonnaise. Add remaining ingredients and stir well. Chill before serving, Store in refrigerator.
