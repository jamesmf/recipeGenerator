SQUASH RELISH


2  qt. sliced, young, tender yellow squash
1  c. sliced onion
1  Tbsp. canning salt
1 1/3  c. sugar
1/2  c. diced green peppers
1/2  tsp. celery seed
1 1/2  tsp. mustard seed
1  c. vinegar

      Mix first 3 ingredients and let sit for 1 hour.  Bring next 5 ingredients to a boil; mix well.  Add squash and onion mixture; bring to a  boil and can.
