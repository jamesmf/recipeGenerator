CRUNCHY CHICKEN BALLS


1  c. finely chopped, cooked chicken
1/4  c. minced green onions
1/2  c. finely shredded, reduced-fat, sharp Cheddar cheese
1/8  tsp. pepper
2  Tbsp. reduced-fat mayonnaise
3/4  c. chopped, fresh parsley

      In a bowl, combine the first 5 ingredients; mix well. Shape into 3/4-inch balls. Roll in parsley. Cover and chill. Yield: 8 servings (2 dozen).
