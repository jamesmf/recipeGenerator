GREEN CHILES CASSEROLE


10  eggs
2  c. cottage cheese
2  c. monterrey jack or cheddar cheese
2 4.5  oz. cans chopped green chiles
1/2  c. flour
1  tsp. baking powder
1/2  tsp. salt

      Break eggs into a large bowl, and beat until light.  Add remaining ingredients, blending thoroughly.  Pour into a well-greased 2 quart baking dish, and bake in a 350 degree oven for 1 hour, or until top of casserole is browned and center is firm.  Cool for 5 minutes before slicing.  Serve with salsa and sour cream.  Makes 8 to 10 servings.
