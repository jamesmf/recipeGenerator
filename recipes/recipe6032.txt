DELICIOUS SALAD


1  head broccoli, chopped small
1  head cauliflower, chopped small
1  c. cheddar cheese, shredded
1  onion, chopped
1  lb. cooked bacon, crumbled
1  c. salad dressing (Miracle Whip or JFG)
1/2  c. sugar
2  Tbsp. vinegar

      Toss veggies with cheese and crumbled bacon.  Mix salad dressing, sugar and vinegar.  Add dressing to vegetable mixture. Store in refrigerator.
