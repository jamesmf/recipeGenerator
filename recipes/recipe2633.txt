APPLESAUCE CAKE


1/2  c. shortening
1  c. sugar
1  egg
1  c. applesauce
2  c. sifted flour
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. soda
1  tsp. baking powder
1  tsp. cinnamon
1/2  tsp. allspice
1/2  tsp. nutmeg
1/4  tsp. cloves
3/4  c. nuts

      Cream together the shortening and sugar; add egg and beat well. Add applesauce.  Sift dry ingredients together; add.  Add nuts. Place in greased 8-inch pan and bake at 350 degrees; for 50 to 60 minutes. Good with powdered sugar frosting.
