EASY NOODLE SUEY


1  lb. hamburger
1  lrg. onion cut fine & fried with meat
1  Pkg. wide noodles
1  can peas (juice & all)
1  can tomato soup

      Boil noodles in salt water.  Mix with other ingredients. Pour tomato juice over all.  Dot a little butter on top.  Mix and bake until top is nicely browned.
