BROCCOLI SUPREME


2 (20 oz.)  pkg. cut broccoli
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  c. mayonnaise
3  well beaten eggs
4  c. (two 8 oz. pkg.) chopped Cheddar cheese
1 1/2  c. Pepperidge Farm stuffing mix
6  Tbsp. butter

      Cook broccoli according to package directions; drain well. Set aside. Combine in large mixing bowl the soup, eggs and mayonnaise. Blend in 1 package of cheese. Fold in broccoli. Pour into buttered 9 x 13 x 2 pan. Sprinkle top with other package of cheese. Melt butter in saucepan. Mix in stuffing mix until well coated. Sprinkle over cheese. Bake at 350 degrees; for 50 minutes.
