MICROWAVE CHICKEN


2  lb. chicken pieces
  garlic or onion powder
  Lawry's seasoned pepper
1  lemon
  ground ginger
  soy sauce

      Wash chicken.  Skin and place in greased baking dish. Sprinkle with seasoning, lemon juice and soy sauce.  Cover and cook full power until chicken is tender, 10 to 15 minutes.
