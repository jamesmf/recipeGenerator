PESTO BREAD SPREAD


1/2  c. reduced-calorie soft margarine
1  Tbsp. grated Parmesan cheese
1  Tbsp. snipped fresh basil or 1 tsp. dried basil, crushed
1  clove garlic

      Mix ingredients.  Makes 8 (1-tablespoon) servings.
      Per Serving:  82 Calories, 9 g. Fat, 1 mg.  Cholesterol and 157 mg.  Sodium.
