MINESTRONE SOUP


1  lb. ground meat
1  onion, chopped
1  can Rotel tomatoes
2  cans Ranch Style beans
3  cans Minestrone soup
3 soup  cans water

      Brown beef and onions.  Add remaining ingredients and simmer at least 30 minutes.  Great served with cornbread, especially broccoli cornbread.  This soup is quick, easy and makes a lot.
