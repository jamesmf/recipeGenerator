CHICKEN DESIRIE


6  or 8 chicken breasts (boneless)
6 or 8  slices Swiss cheese
1  can cream of chicken soup
1/2  c. white wine
1/2  stick margarine

      Spray Pam on a 9 x 13 Pyrex dish. Place cheese on top of each chicken breast. Mix soup and white wine and pour over chicken. Sprinkle herb seasoned bread crumbs over the top and pour margarine over crumbs. Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees; about an hour.
