FRIED SHRIMP


1  egg, beaten
1  c. milk (maybe a little more)
1  c. corn meal
  plenty of garlic salt
  fresh shrimp

      Mix all ingredients until thin.  Place cleaned shrimp, split up back and deveined, in the mixture.  Remove 1 at a time; roll in corn meal.  Drop in hot cooking oil.  Cook to light brown.
