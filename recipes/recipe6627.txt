SALMON WITH ONIONS & CAPERS


2  tsp. olive oil
4  pieces skinless salmon filet (6 oz. each)
1/4  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. black pepper
1  medium onion, sliced
1/4  c. capers, drained
2  Tbsp. balsamic vinegar
1  tsp. sugar

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees; F. In a non-stick 12-inch skillet, heat 1 tsp. olive oil over medium high heat until hot.  Add salmon rounded side down and cook until lightly browned, about 2 minutes. Transfer salmon to 13 x 9-inch glass baking dish and sprinkle with salt and pepper.  In the same skillet, add remaining 1 tsp. oil, cook onion over medium heat 5 minutes or until tender.  Add capers, vinegar and sugar and cook 2 minutes stirring constantly.  Spoon caper mixture over salmon, cover with foil and place in the oven. Bake 12 to 15 minutes or until salmon flakes easily.  Serves 4.
