BUTTERED, TOASTED, SALTED PECANS


1  lb. pecan halves
1/2  stick butter
  salt to taste

      Put butter into a large pan.  Melt.  Add pecans into a single layer.  Stir to coat with butter.  Bake at 225 degrees; until toasted. Stir every 15 minutes.  Add salt halfway through.  Usually takes about 1 hour.
