BLACKENED SEASONING
(For Fish, Chicken And Steaks)  

1  c. paprika
1/3  c. salt
1/3  c. onion powder
1/3  c. garlic powder
1/4  c. black pepper
1/4  c. white pepper
3  Tbsp. cayenne pepper
3  Tbsp. dried thyme leaves
3  Tbsp. dried oregano leaves

      Mix ingredients together; store in a covered container.  Good for steaks, fish and chicken, cooked outdoors.  Fry in 1 tablespoon olive oil or butter and oil.
