DUMPY SALAD


1  can cherry pie filling
1  can drained crushed pineapple
1 large  box Cool Whip
1  can Eagle Brand milk
1/2  c. chopped pecans (optional)

      Beat cherry pie filling with mixer or blender.  Mix together pie filling, crushed pineapple, Cool Whip, Eagle Brand milk and pecans.  Refrigerate for 2 hours or more.  Serve.
