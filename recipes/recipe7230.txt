LAREDO SUPPER


1  lb. ground beef
1/2  c. green pepper, chopped
1  c. fresh tomato, chopped
2  tsp. chili powder
1/2  c. onion, chopped
1 (16 oz.)  can kidney beans, drained
1 (8 oz.)  jar Cheez Whiz (with jalapeno pepper, if desired)

      Brown meat; drain. Add onion and pepper. Cook until tender. Stir in remaining ingredients. Simmer 15 minutes. Serve warm with corn chips, if desired.
