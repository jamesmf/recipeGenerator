CHOCOLATE CREAM TRUFFLES


1 (11 1/2 oz.)  pkg. (2 c.)  Nestle milk chocolate morsels
1/4  c. sour cream
2 measuring  Tbsp. almond flavored liqueur
2/3  c. finely chopped toasted almonds

      Melt over hot, not boiling, water the Nestle milk chocolate morsels.  Stir until smooth.  Remove from heat.  Blend in sour cream.  Add almond flavored liqueur; mix well.  Transfer to small bowl.  Chill until firm.  Drop by rounded teaspoonfuls onto waxed paper-lined cookie sheets.  Shape into balls.  Roll in almonds until evenly coated.  Chill until firm.  Makes about 2 1/2 dozen truffles.
