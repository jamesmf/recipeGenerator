ELEGANT SEAFOOD CREPES


3  Tbsp. butter
3  Tbsp. flour
2  Tbsp. chopped chives
1 1/3  c. milk
3  Tbsp. sherry
1 (7 oz.)  pkg. frozen king crabmeat, drained, thawed and flaked*
1 (10 oz.)  pkg. frozen cooked shrimp, thawed and drained*
8  crepes

      *You may substitute other fresh seafoods.
      Melt butter in saucepan.  Blend flour and chives into butter. Gradually add milk and sherry.  Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until thickened.  Add crabmeat and shrimp.  Cook over low heat, stirring occasionally, until heated thoroughly.  Spread about 1/4 cup filling across center of each crepe.  Roll up.  Place in 9 x 13-inch baking dish.  Spoon remaining filling down center of crepes.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 8 to 10 minutes.  Makes 4 servings.
