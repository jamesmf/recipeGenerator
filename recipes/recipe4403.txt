COCA-COLA SALAD


1  pkg. strawberry Jell-O
1  pkg. cherry Jell-O
2  c. hot water
8  oz. pkg. Philadelphia cream cheese
12  oz. Coca-Cola
1/2  c. pineapple juice
1 large  can crushed pineapple
1  c. nuts

      Dissolve the Jell-O in your hot water.  Mix in cream cheese (cut in small pieces).  Add cola and juice.  When mixture begins to thicken, add pineapple and nuts.
