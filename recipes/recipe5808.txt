TORTILLA SOUP


1  lb. sausage
1  onion, chopped
  garlic powder
  Creole seasoning
2 (19 oz.)  cans minestrone soup
1  can stewed tomatoes
1  can Ro-Tel
1 large  can Ranch beans
1 large  can water

      Brown sausage and onion with garlic powder and Creole seasoning for 1 hour.  Drain well.  Add all other ingredients.  Simmer for 1 hour.  Serve with grated Cheddar cheese and chips.
