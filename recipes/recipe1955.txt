VEGETABLE OMELET

Sauce:
2  small tomatoes, peeled, cored and chopped
1  tsp. red wine vinegar
2  Tbsp. chopped onion
2  tsp. minced parsley
  Tabasco sauce
  salt

 
Omelet:
2  Tbsp. olive oil
1/3  c. each finely chopped onion and bell pepper
1/4  tsp. oregano powder
2  c. diced cooked red potatoes with skins
4  eggs, lightly beaten
  salt and pepper to taste

      Prepare sauce; set aside.  Heat oil in 9-inch nonstick skillet and saut√© onion and green pepper until softened.  Stir in potatoes; sprinkle with oregano and cook until potatoes are heated through. Distribute vegetables evenly over bottom of skillet; add salt and pepper to taste.  Pour eggs over evenly.  Cook over medium heat without stirring.  When edges begin to set, lift up carefully with spatula and allow uncooked egg to flow underneath.  When omelet is nearly set, place under a preheated broiler to firm and brown lightly.  Watch closely.  Cut in half; serve with sauce.  Serves 2.
