CHICKEN FAJITAS


1  whole chicken
3/4  c. butter or margarine
1  c. wine
1  pkg. large flour tortillas
  salt and pepper
  garlic powder
3  medium onions, sliced
3  green bell peppers, sliced
1  block Mozzarella cheese, shredded

      Boil chicken. Remove meat from bone and cube. Melt butter in a skillet. Add onion, bell pepper, wine and spices. Saut until tender. Add chicken. Simmer until liquid is absorbed. Place a small amount of mixture in a warm tortilla. Top with cheese and picante sauce. Roll up.
