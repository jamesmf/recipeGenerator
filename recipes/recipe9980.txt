SWEET POTATO CASSEROLE


3  c. mashed baked sweet potatoes (fresh)
1/2  c. sugar
1/2  stick butter, softened

 
Topping:
1  c. chopped pecans
1/2  c. brown sugar
1/2  stick butter, softened

	Wrap 5 to 6 medium sized sweet potatoes in aluminum foil and bake at 350 degrees; for about 1 hour (or until soft). Peel and mash sweet potatoes with butter and sugar, then put into a 9 x 13 pan.
	Mix pecans, brown sugar and butter together for topping. Crumble mixture over the mashed sweet potatoes in the pan.	Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes or until heated through.  Serve warm.  (Can be served as a dessert or a side dish.)
