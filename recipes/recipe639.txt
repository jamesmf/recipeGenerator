BLUEBERRY CRUMBLE


1/2  c. blueberries
2  packets Sweet 'N Low
3/4  oz. Grape-Nuts
2  Tbsp. plus 2 tsp. nonfat dry milk
  dash of nutmeg
2  tsp. diet margarine

      Put blueberries and sweetener in small baking dish.  Mix together next 3 ingredients.  Stir in margarine; pour dry ingredients over berries.  Microwave on High power for 1 minute and 20 seconds.  Makes one serving.
      Each serving provides 1 fruit, 1 cereal, 1 milk and 1 fat.
