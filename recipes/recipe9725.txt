CRAB MEAT LOAF


1  lb. imitation crab meat, chopped
1/2  c. mayonnaise
1/4  c. thinly sliced green onions
2  c. shredded Mozzarella cheese
1/8  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper
1  (1 lb.) loaf French bread, halved lengthwise
  garlic powder

      In a bowl, combine the first 4 ingredients. Stir in cheese, salt, pepper and garlic powder and spread over bottom of bread, then replace top. Wrap in a large piece of heavy-duty aluminum foil. Bake at 400 degrees; for 20 minutes or until heated through. Cut into slices. Yield: 8 servings.
