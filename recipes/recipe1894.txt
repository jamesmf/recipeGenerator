CHICKEN MEXICALI (HOT)


3  chicken breasts
1/2  chopped onion
2  Tbsp. butter
2  Tbsp. flour
1  c. chicken broth
2/3  c. evaporated milk
1  (7 oz.) taco sauce (hot)
1  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper
1/2  c. chopped green pepper
1/2  c. chopped celery
3  c. cooked rice
1 (4 oz.)  can sliced mushrooms
3/4  c. grated Cheddar cheese

      Saut√© onions in butter until tender.  Blend in flour.  Add broth, milk, sauce and seasonings.  Cook until smooth and creamy. Stir in other ingredients except cheese and spoon into 2-quart casserole.  Sprinkle with cheese.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes.
      For very hot Chicken Mexicali, add jalapenos.
