REUBEN SANDWICH


8  oz. Swiss cheese, julienned
8  oz. boiled ham, julienned
1  c. mayonnaise
8  oz. sauerkraut, well drained
  scant tsp. caraway seed, optional
  rye bread

      Butter one side of bread.  Add all ingredients and grill buttered side down until bread is toasted.  Recipe is really great when doubled.
