KING RANCH CHICKEN


3  or 4 boneless chicken breasts
  flour tortillas
1  chopped bell pepper
1  chopped onion
1  can Ro-Tel tomatoes
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  can cream of chicken soup

      Boil chicken about 15 to 20 minutes. Set aside. Line the bottom and sides of 9 x 13-inch baking dish with tortillas. Layer cooked chicken on bottom. Add in layers bell pepper and onion. Mix together tomatoes, mushroom soup and chicken soup. Pour mixture on the layers. Top with shredded cheese. Bake 20 minutes at 350 degrees;. Put cheese on and bake until cheese melts.
