COMPANY GREEN BEANS


4  cans green beans (I prefer Allen's brand)
1  onion, chopped
2  tomatoes, chopped
12  slices bacon, cooked and crumbled

      Put beans (uncooked) in 9 x 12 Pyrex dish. Layer onions, tomatoes and bacon on top of beans. Pour sauce over beans. Bake at 400 degrees; for 45 minutes.
Sauce:
6  Tbsp. butter
2  tsp. mustard
1  tsp. horseradish
6  Tbsp. brown sugar

      Melt above ingredients for sauce. Make this sauce first.
