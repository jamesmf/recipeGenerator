SOFT ENCHILADAS


1  lb. ground beef
1  lb. ground pork sausage
1  medium onion, chopped
  salt and pepper to taste
1 (10 oz.)  can diced tomatoes with green chilies
1  tsp. chili powder
1/2  tsp. cumin seed
1/2  tsp. oregano
12  medium size flour tortillas
1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can cream of chicken soup
1/2  lb. (8 oz.) Velveeta cheese

	Preheat oven to 350 degrees;.  Brown ground beef and sausage with chopped onion in a large skillet.  Salt and pepper to taste. Drain off excess grease.
	Add tomatoes with green chilies, chili powder, cumin seed and oregano. Cover and simmer 5 minutes. Place 3 to 4 tablespoons of meat mixture in center of each flour tortilla.  Fold edges of tortilla over meat mixture to make a roll. Place seam side down in an 13 x 9 x 2 ungreased baking dish. Sprinkle remaining meat mixture on top of rolled enchiladas.
	 Combine cream of chicken soup and Velveeta cheese in a medium saucepan. Heat until cheese is melted, stirring constantly. Pour over enchiladas. Bake for 10 to 15 minutes until bubbly.
	 This may be frozen.
