KING RANCH CHICKEN CASSEROLE


1/4  c. margarine
1  medium bell pepper, diced
1  medium white onion, diced
2  cans cream of chicken soup
2  cans diced Ro-Tel
2  c. canned chicken
1  (12 oz.) bag shredded Cheddar cheese
12  to 15 corn tortillas, cut into strips
  salt and pepper to taste

      Preheat oven to 325 degrees;. Dice up onion and bell pepper and cook with margarine until tender. Stir in chicken soup and Ro-Tel. In a 9 x 13 pan, spray with Pam and layer tortillas, sauce and cheese. Layer several times. Cook for 35 minutes. Top with cheese. Bake for 5 minutes longer. Let stand 5 to 10 minutes to thicken.
