CREME DOODLES


1  jar Marshmallow Creme
1  c. creamy peanut butter
1/2  c. honey
3  oz. milk chocolate candy bar
1 1/2  c. raisins
1  c. walnuts
2  c. coconut, shredded

      Stir Marshmallow Creme, peanut butter and honey together, then stir in chocolate, raisins and nuts.  Shape by spoonfuls into 1-inch balls.  Roll in coconut.  Chill.
