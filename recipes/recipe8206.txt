SHRIMP CREOLE


2  onions
1  c. celery
1  green pepper
1  hot pepper
1 1/2  lb. shrimp
1  can tomato sauce

      Brown onions, celery and peppers in butter. Add tomatoes and simmer at least 1/2 hour. Add peeled and deveined shrimp. Simmer 15 to 20 minutes. Salt and pepper. Serve over hot, cooked rice.
