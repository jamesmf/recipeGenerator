SPRING SALAD


4  c. dry shell macaroni
  carrots
  onion
  celery
  green pepper
  cucumber
  radishes
2  c. Miracle Whip salad dressing
1  c. sugar
1/2  c. vinegar
14  oz. can sweetened condensed milk

      Boil the macaroni. Cool completely. In a large bowl, combine salad dressing, sugar, vinegar and milk. Slice in the vegetables, using as many as you want. Add macaroni. Refrigerate overnight.
