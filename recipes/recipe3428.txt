KNOX BLOX


5  pkg. (Tbsp.) unflavored gelatin
2 1/2  c. cold water
2  c. cold water
2 (6 oz.)  pkg. (3/4 c.) jello
1/2  c. sugar

      Mix  and  dissolve  unflavored  gelatin  and 2 1/2 cups water and  let  set.  Bring  remaining  ingredients  to boil. Remove from  heat and add first mixture.  Add 1 cup cold water or milk. Put  in a 9 x 13-inch pan.  Chill 3 to 4 hours.  Cut in  1-inch squares  and eat with fingers.  Yields 9 x 13-inch pan 117 (1-inch) squares.
