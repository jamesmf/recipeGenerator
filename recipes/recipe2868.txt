HOLIDAY HEARTH LOAVES


1/3  c. softened butter
2/3  c. sugar
2  tsp. grated lemon peel
1/4  tsp. cinnamon
2  eggs
3  Tbsp. milk
1  tsp. lemon juice
2  c. flour
1  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. baking soda
1 1/2  c. peeled, shredded apple
1  c. chopped raisins
1/2  c. chopped nuts

      Cream together butter, sugar, lemon peel and cinnamon.  Beat in eggs until light and fluffy.  Beat in milk and lemon juice.  Stir together dry ingredients; add to creamed mixture, stirring until moistened.  Fold in apple, raisins and nuts; spoon batter into 3 greased miniature loaf pans, about 3 x 6-inches.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 40 to 45 minutes, or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.  Makes 3 loaves.
      Note:  Batter may be baked in 5 x 9-inch loaf pan.  Increase baking time to about 1 hour.
