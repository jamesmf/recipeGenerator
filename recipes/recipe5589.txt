LESLIE'S TACO SOUP


1  lb. Ground Chuck
1  Onion, chopped
1 14  oz. can chopped Tomatoes
1  can Rotel
1 14  oz. can whole kernel Corn
1 14  oz. can Kidney Beans
1 14  oz. can Pinto Beans
1  pkg. dry Taco Seasoning
1  pkg. dry Ranch Dressing mix

      Cook meat and onions until meat is no longer pink.  Mix all ingredients together. Cook until warm.  Serve over Fritos.  Serve with cheese and sour cream.
