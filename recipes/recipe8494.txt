CHICKEN AND DUMPLINGS


3  c. flour
1  c. milk
3  tsp. baking powder
2  eggs
1-1/2  tsp. salt
1  broiler-fryer chicken

      Beat eggs; add milk and mix well. Add  flour, salt and baking powder. Have dough very moist. Put a little flour in kneading bowl. Use a little at a time until you can roll out dough. Cut in strips.
       Cook chicken; remove from broth. Bring  broth to boil and add dumplings, slowly. Skin and bone chicken and add to broth with dumplings.
