BROCCOLI-GRAPE SALAD


1/2  C. mayonnaise
3  Tbsp. sugar
2  tsp. vinegar
3  C. fresh broccoli, chopped
1  C. red grapes, halved
2  slices crisp bacon, crumbled
1/3  C. cashews

      Stir together mayonnaise, sugar and vinegar.  Stir in broccoli, and grapes.
      Cover and chill up to 24 hours.
      Before serving, stir in bacon and cashews.
      Makes 4 servings.
