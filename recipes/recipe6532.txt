NACHO CASSEROLE


1/2  bag tortilla chips (round preferably)
1  lb. ground hamburger meat
1  pkg. taco seasoning
1  can refried beans
1 (8 oz.)  jar salsa (mild)
1  pkg. Mexican-style shredded cheese
1 (8 to 12 oz.)  jar Cheez Whiz

      Brown hamburger meat and add taco seasoning. Warm refried beans on medium heat while cooking hamburger meat. Put a layer of tortilla chips on bottom of deep casserole dish. Next, layer hamburger meat, shredded cheese, chips, more shredded cheese, refried beans, chips, salsa and Cheez Whiz. Place in oven at 350 degrees; for 5 minutes or until cheese is melted.
