BROCCOLI/CAULIFLOWER SALAD


1  head cauliflower
1  bunch broccoli
1  C. celery, chopped
1  sm. onion, chopped
1  pkg. frozen peas
1/4  C. bacon bits
1 1/2  C. sour cream
1 1/2  C. Miracle Whip
1/3  C. sugar
1  tsp. seasoned salt
1/2  tsp. pepper

      Combine the first six  ingredients.  Mix in a separate bowl the sour cream, Miracle Whip, sugar, seasoned salt and pepper. Pour dressing over dry ingredients and mix together.
      Refrigerate overnight before serving.
