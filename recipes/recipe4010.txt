CUCUMBER RELISH


12  to 14 large cucumbers
1  bunch celery
6  medium onions
2  Tbsp. salt
2  c. vinegar
3  c. sugar
1/2  tsp. turmeric
1  tsp. mustard seed

      Grind cucumbers, celery and onions; add salt.  Let stand 4 hours; drain.  Add vinegar, sugar, turmeric and mustard seed. Bring to boil for 1 minute.  Fill hot jars and seal.
