COWBOY SOUP


1  lb. ground beef
1  medium onion
1 (16 oz.)  can mixed vegetables
1 (10 oz.)  can Ro-Tel tomatoes and green chiles or less if you prefer the soup not so hot
1 (13 oz.)  can Spanish rice
1 (14 1/2 oz.)  can stewed tomatoes
1 (17 oz.)  can cream-style corn

      Brown ground beef and onion lightly in a 4-quart kettle. Add remaining ingredients; cover and simmer for 40 minutes.
