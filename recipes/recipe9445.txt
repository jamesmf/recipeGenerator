CORN BREAD DRESSING


3  c. corn bread crumbs
6  pieces toast (crumbs)
2  eggs, beaten
3  boiled eggs, cut up
1  medium onion, cut fine
1  medium bell pepper, cut fine
1  can celery soup
1  can cream chicken soup
1  can cream mushroom soup
1  Tbsp. poultry season
2  Tbsp. sage
  salt and pepper
  giblets and 2 cans broth from giblets

	Grease loaf pan. Mix well and put in loaf pan. Cook at 325 degrees; for 1 hour.
