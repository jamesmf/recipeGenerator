CHICKEN STEW


1  whole or 8 halves chicken breast
3  large diced potatoes
1/2  lb. sliced okra
1/2  c. ketchup
3  c. chicken stock (optional)
1 (28 oz.)  can tomatoes
1  large diced onion
1 (14 oz.)  can cream-style corn
1/4  c. barbecue sauce

      Cook, debone and chop chicken.  Place in 5-quart cooker or crock-pot the chicken, tomatoes, potatoes, onion and okra; cook for 8 hours.  Add corn, ketchup and barbecue sauce.  Cook 10 more minutes.  This makes the stew.  If you want soup, add the 3 cups chicken stock.
