CHOCOLATE SHEET CAKE


1  c. cold water
1  stick margarine
1/2  c. salad oil
4  Tbsp. cocoa
2  c. sugar
2  c. flour
1/2  c. buttermilk
1  tsp. soda
2  eggs

 
Icing:
1  stick margarine
1/4  c. cocoa
1/3  c. buttermilk
1  box powdered sugar (3 1/2 c.)
1  tsp. vanilla
1/2  c. chopped pecans

      Boil  water, margarine and oil.  Pour over dry ingredients and  beat  until creamy.  Add buttermilk, soda and eggs; beat again.  Pour  into  greased  and  floured jellyroll pan, approximately  17  1/2 x 11 1/2 inches.  Bake in 400 degrees; oven for 16 to 18 minutes.
      Icing:   Boil  margarine,  cocoa  and  buttermilk.  Add powdered   sugar,   vanilla  and  chopped  nuts;  mix.  Spread immediately on cooled cake.
