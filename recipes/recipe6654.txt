CHICKEN DIVAN


2  boxes chopped broccoli, thawed and drained
5  to 6 cooked chicken breasts, boned and shredded
2  cans cream of mushroom soup
1  Tbsp. lemon juice
1  c. mayonnaise
1  c. bread crumbs
2  c. shredded cheddar cheese

      Mix together cream of mushroom soup, lemon juice and mayonnaise.  In a 9 x 13 inch baking dish layer broccoli, chicken breast, soup mixture, bread crumbs and cheddar cheese.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes.  Can serve as is or over noodles or rice.
