CHEDDAR CHEESE BALL


1  c. finely shredded cheese
3  oz. pkg. cream cheese
2  Tbsp. butter
2  Tbsp. milk or dry white wine
1  Tbsp. finely chopped green onion
1  Tbsp. diced pimento
1  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
  dash of Tabasco sauce
  nuts

      Soften first 3 ingredients to room temperature. Add remaining ingredients. Beat until smooth. Chill for 4 to 24 hours. Shape into ball and roll in nuts.
      NOTE: Grate cheese before softening.
