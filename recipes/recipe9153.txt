CHILI


1  lb. ground beef
1 (14.5 oz.)  can tomato sauce
1 (14.5 oz.)  can kidney beans, with liquid
1 (14.5 oz.)  can pinto beans, with liquid
1/2  c. diced onion
1/4  c. diced green chiles
1/4  c. diced celery
2  medium tomatoes, chopped
1  tsp. cumin
2  Tbsp. chili powder
1  tsp. black pepper
2  tsp. salt
1  c. water

      Brown ground beef in skillet over medium heat; drain off fat. Using a fork, crumble cooked beef into pea-size pieces. In large pot, combine beef plus remaining ingredients. Bring to simmer over low heat and cook, stirring every 15 minutes, for 2 to 3 hours.
