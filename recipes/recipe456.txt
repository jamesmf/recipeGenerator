STEW


1  lb. hamburger
2  carrots
1  medium onion
2  potatoes
2  stalks celery
1 (10 oz.)  pkg. frozen peas, thawed
  salt and pepper
1  can tomato soup
1/2  can water

      Brown hamburger until it is no longer pink.  Add cut up vegetables, peas, seasonings, soup and water.  Bake in 350 degrees; oven or on top of stove until vegetables are tender.  Stir as needed.
