SPINACH SALAD


1  bag fresh clean spinach
1  can Chinese chow mein noodles
1  can sliced water chestnuts, drained
1  can bean sprouts, drained
4  hard-cooked eggs, chopped
1/2  lb. bacon, fried crisp and crumbled

	Tear spinach into large salad bowl. Crisp the bean sprouts by placing in refrigerator for a couple of hours.  Add noodles, sprouts, water chestnuts and chopped eggs. Crumble bacon over all. Add desired amounts of dressing just before serving.
Dressing:
1/3  c. white vinegar
1  tsp. salt
3/4  c. sugar
1  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  red onion, chopped
1  c. canola oil

	Place all ingredients in a blender. Blend until creamy.  Keep dressing refrigerated.
