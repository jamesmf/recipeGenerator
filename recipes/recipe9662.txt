POTATOES/HAM/CHEESE BAKE


  Mashed Potatoes
  Ham Slices
  Cheddar Cheese, shredded

      Prepare mashed potatoes and place in a casserole dish. Place small slices of ham on top of potatoes. Sprinkle cheese on top of ham. Place in a 375 degree oven until cheese melts. Leave in oven a little longer and let cheese brown a bit.
