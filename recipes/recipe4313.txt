SPINACH SALAD
(Korean)  
Salad:
1  pkg. fresh spinach, washed
1  can bean sprouts, drained
8  slices crumbled bacon
3  hard-boiled eggs, diced

 
Dressing:
1  c. salad oil
3/4  c. sugar
1/3  c. catsup
1/4  c. vinegar
1/2  tsp. salt
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce

      Break up (multi-washed) spinach. Toss with bean sprouts, bacon and eggs. Blend all dressing ingredients well and pour over greens.
      Note: Heated dressing is real tasty over the greens.
