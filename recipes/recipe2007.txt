MEXICAN TAMALE PANCAKES


1  c. yellow cornmeal
1  c. boiling water
1  c. milk
1/3  c. oil
1  egg, well beaten
1  c. biscuit mix
1  tsp. each ground cumin and salt
1/2  tsp. chili powder
2  tsp. baking powder
1 (8 oz.)  can whole kernel corn, well drained
1 small  can chopped ripe olives, well drained

      Condiments:  1 to 1 1/2 cups each shredded Cheddar, chopped green onions including tops, chopped tomatoes, sour cream and prepared taco sauce or salsa.
      Put cornmeal in medium size bowl; pour boiling water over and stir well.  Let cool about 10 minutes.  Add oil, milk and egg; mix until well blended.  Add baking mix and other dry ingredients; beat until smooth.  Stir in corn and olives.
      Preheat griddle and grease well.  Spoon batter (about 3 tablespoons for each cake) and spread to make 4-inch circles.  Cook until tops are bubbly and appear dry; turn and cook until browned. Pass bowls of condiments.  Serves 4 generously or 6 with other items.
