CRAB CASSEROLE I


2  pkg. frozen artichoke hearts
3/4  lb. fresh mushrooms, sliced
12  oz. noodles, cooked and drained
4 (10 3/4 oz.)  cans cream of mushroom soup
2  Tbsp Worcestershire sauce
2  lb. crabmeat
1/2  c. Sherry
  Parmesan cheese, grated

	Saute artichoke hearts and mushrooms separately.  Mix well with remaining ingredients, except cheese.	Put in 3 quart casserole.	Sprinkle cheese over mixture.  Bake 30 to 35 minutes at 350 degrees; until heated through.  Serve 12.
