SQUASH CASSEROLE


5  or 6 squash
1  Tbsp. bacon grease
1  Tbsp. butter
  salt and pepper to taste
1  Tbsp. minced onion
1  c. sour cream
1  can cream of chicken
1  pkg. Pepperidge Farm herb stuffing
3 or 4  slices cheese

      Cook squash with bacon grease, butter, salt, pepper and minced onion until tender.  Drain well.  Mix 1 cup of sour cream and can of cream of chicken with squash.  Put a layer of herb stuffing, a layer of squash and a layer of cheese (9 x 9 pan).  On top, put a layer of herb stuffing.  Bake 30 minutes at 350 degrees;. Serves 8 to 10.
