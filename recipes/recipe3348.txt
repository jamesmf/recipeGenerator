CHIPPED BEEF BALL


8  oz. pkg. cream cheese, softened
1/4  c. dairy sour cream
1  tsp. horseradish
1/4  c. Parmesan cheese
3  oz. pkg. chipped dried beef, chop finely

      Mix cream cheese, sour cream, horseradish and cheese with 1/2 cup dried beef.  Refrigerate at least 15 minutes for easier handling.  Shape into a ball and roll in remaining dried beef. Refrigerate at least 20 minutes before serving.  Makes a 4-inch ball.
