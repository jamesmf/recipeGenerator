SALMON LOAF


1  can (15 1/2 oz.) salmon
1/2  c. stuffing mix
1  tsp. chopped onion
1  egg
1/2  c. mayonnaise
1/2  tsp. hot sauce

      Empty salmon with liquid into a bowl; pick out and discard apparent bones.  Add remaining ingredients.  Mix well with a fork until mixture is firm enough to form into a loaf.  Place in a small loaf pan or pie pan.  Bake in a 400 degrees; oven for 30 minutes. Serve from the baking dish.  Makes 6 servings.
