SOFT PRETZEL SHAPES


1  envelope dry yeast
1 1/2  c. warm water
1  tsp. salt
1  Tbsp. sugar
4  c. flour
  coarse salt

      Soften yeast in water.  Mix 1 teaspoon salt, sugar and flour into it and knead.  Give each child a ball of dough to roll and twist into any shape.  Lay on greased cookie sheets and brush with beaten egg and sprinkle with coarse salt.  Bake at 425 degrees; for 12 to 15 minutes.
