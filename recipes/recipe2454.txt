GERMAN CHOCOLATE TURTLE BARS


1  box German chocolate cake mix
3/4  c. melted margarine
1 small  can evaporated milk
1 (14 oz.)  pkg. vanilla caramels
1  c. chopped nuts
1 (6 oz.)  pkg. chocolate chips

      Melt caramels with 1/3 cup evaporated milk in double boiler. Mix cake mix, margarine, 1/3 cup evaporated milk and nuts.  Press 1/2 of mixture in a 9 x 13-inch pan.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 6 minutes. Sprinkle chips over this crust; pour the caramel mix on and dab the remaining cake mixture on top.  Bake an additional 15 to 20 minutes at 350 degrees;.  Let cool.  Cut in squares.
