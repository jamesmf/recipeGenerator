PEANUT BUTTER COOKIES


1/3  c. flour
1/4  tsp. soda
1/4  tsp. baking powder
1/8  tsp. salt
2  Tbsp. shortening
2  Tbsp. peanut butter
1  tsp. Sweet 'N Low
1  egg, beaten

      Mix and stir in order given:  flour, soda, baking powder, salt, shortening, peanut butter and Sweet 'N Low.  Add beaten egg.  Drop by teaspoonfuls on greased cookie sheet.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 10 minutes.
