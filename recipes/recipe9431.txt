CABBAGE PATCH STEW


1 1/2  lb. ground beef
4  celery ribs, chopped
1  medium onion, chopped
7  c. chopped cabbage
2  cans (16 oz.) kidney beans, rinsed and drained
1 (28 oz.)  can diced tomatoes
3  c. beef broth
1 (15 oz.)  can tomato sauce
2  medium carrots, chopped
1/2  tsp. sugar
  pepper to taste

	In large pan, cook beef, celery and onion over medium heat. Cook until meat is no longer pink and vegetables are tender. Add remaining ingredients; bring to a boil. Simmer for 1 hour or until carrots and cabbage are tender.
