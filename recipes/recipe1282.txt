ARTICHOKE SALAD


1  artichoke
1  green bell pepper
2  carrots
3  Tbsp. flax seed oil
  a dash of parsley
1  purple onion
1  tomato
2  Tbsp. apple cider vinegar
1/4  c. celery

      Wash the artichokes well and boil in covered pot 45 minutes to 1 hour.  When ready, peel them until you can see the center. Remove the "chokes" with a spoon and discard.  Cut the artichoke heart and other vegetables into bite size pieces.  Combine and toss with vinegar and oil.
