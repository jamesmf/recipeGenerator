SAUSAGE AND STUFFING APPETIZERS


1/4  c. butter
1/2  c. water
1  box Stove Top stuffing for pork
1  egg
1/4  lb. sausage
1  lb. bacon

      Melt butter with water.  Stir in stuffing mix (or use prepared); add egg and sausage.  Blend with hands.  Chill one hour or so.  Shape into balls.  Cut bacon in thirds.  Wrap around balls and insert toothpicks.  Cook at 375 degrees; for 35 minutes.
