MEXICAN PINWHEELS


3 - 8  ounce pkgs Cream Cheese (softened)
2 - small  cans diced black olives
1 - small  can green chiles
1  1/2 to 2 cups - shredded cheddar cheese
1/2  cup - chopped onion or green onion
15  - 20 small tortilla shells (fewer if large)

      Mix all together.  Spread 1/4 to 1/3 cup of mixture all over tortilla.  Roll up in plastic wrap and chill a few hours.  Slice about 1/2 inch thick, eat the ends.  Serve with Picante Sauce.
