GRILLED FISH


2  lb. fish
1/2  c. salad oil
1/3  c. freshly squeezed lemon juice
1/4  c. chopped onion
2  Tbsp. capers and juice (optional)
2  Tbsp. catsup
1  tsp. salt
2  Tbsp. sugar
2  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
4  bay leaves, crushed
1/4  tsp. pepper
2  cloves garlic, finely chopped
  paprika

      Marinate fish for 30 minutes in mixed ingredients.  Cook on grill about 8 minutes on each side or until fish flakes easily with fork.  Baste fish while grilling and sprinkle with paprika.  Baste shrimp or scallops only, don't marinate.
