HAM LOAF

Loaf:
2  lb. ground ham
1 1/2  lb. ground chuck
  Salt & pepper
2  eggs, beaten
1  c. milk
3/4  c. bread crumbs

      Mix ingredients for ham loaf.  Make into a loaf or meat balls.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 2 hours.
Sauce:
1  c. brown sugar
1  tsp. dry mustard
1/2  c. vinegar
1/2  c. water

      Bring sauce ingredients to a boil and boil for 5 minutes. Pour sauce over ham loaf for the last 15 minutes of baking.
