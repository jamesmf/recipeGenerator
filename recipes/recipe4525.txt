TACO SOUP


2  lb. ground meat
2  packs taco seasoning
1  pkg. Hidden Valley Ranch dressing
2  cans pinto beans
1  can hominy beans
1  can corn
1  can green chilies, chopped
2  cans stewed tomatoes
1  can Ro-Tel
2 to 4  cans water

	Brown ground meat and drain. Add everything and cook for 30 to 45 minutes. Serve over tortilla chips. Top with cheese.
