BREAKFAST BRUNCH


1  lb. sausage
1  (8 oz.) crescent rolls
2  c. grated Mozzarella cheese
4  eggs, beaten
3/4  c. milk
1  c. grated Cheddar cheese
1/4  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper

      Brown sausage.  Drain well.  Line a buttered 13 x 9 x 2-inch baking dish with crescent rolls.  Press edges together.  Sprinkle with sausage and Mozzarella cheese.  Combine remaining ingredients and beat well.  Pour over sausage and cheese.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 25 minutes, then sprinkle with 1 cup grated Cheddar cheese.  Put back in oven for 5 minutes.
