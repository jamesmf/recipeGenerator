PEA SALAD


1 large  can English peas, drained
1/4  c. sour pickle
1  c. pimientos, chopped
  pinch of sugar and pepper
2  Tbsp. mayonnaise
1/2  c. onion, chopped fine
2  hard-boiled eggs, chopped
  pinch of salt
2  Tbsp. Thousand Island dressing

      Mix all the ingredients. Chill. Serve and enjoy.
