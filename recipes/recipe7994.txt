TURKEY BURGERS


1  lb. ground turkey
1  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
1/2  c. diced celery
2  Tbsp. minced onion
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce

      Combine ingredients and shape into 4 to 6 patties.  Melt 1 Tbsp. butter in skillet.  Cook over medium heat 3 to 4 minutes on each side until well browned.  Do not overcook.  Serve on hamburger buns.  Good with horseradish sauce.
