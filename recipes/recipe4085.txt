NACHO CHEESE DIP


1  (8 oz.) cream cheese, softened
1  pkg. chilli mix
1  lb. ground beef
15  oz. tomato sauce
15  oz. can pinto beans
1 (16 oz.)  pkg. mixed Mexican Cheddar cheese

	Brown ground beef in saucepan. Drain. Add chilli mix, pintos and tomato sauce to ground beef. Bring to a boil. Let simmer about 20 minutes or until thick. Take softened cream cheese. Spread in bottom of baking dish. Then spread beef chilli mixture on top of cream cheese. Next, sprinkle Cheddar cheese on top. Bake at 350 degrees; until cheese on top is melted.
