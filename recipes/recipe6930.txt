HAMBURGER AND RICE DELUXE


1  lb. hamburger
3  c. water
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. coarse pepper
2  tsp. onion salt
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1 1/4  c. rice

      Brown hamburger in cast-iron skillet until tender. Add 1 cup water, salt, pepper, onion salt and sauce; simmer over low heat until boiling. Add rice and cover. Cook approximately 3 to 5 minutes; add remaining water. Cover and let rice steam, stirring occasionally. Rice may need more water. After rice is done, stir well and serve with hot vegetables. Yield: 5 servings.
