NEW YEAR'S COOKIES


2  pkg. dry yeast
1/2  c. sugar
1/4  c. cream
1/4  c. margarine
1/4  c. water
3/4  c. milk
1 1/2  tsp. salt
3  eggs
1  c. raisins
3 1/2  c. flour

      Dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water; add warmed milk, margarine and cream.  Add salt and beaten eggs, then flour and floured raisins.  Let rise 30 minutes to 1 hour and fry in hot fat. Drop by tablespoons in hot fat.  Drain on paper towels and glaze after they have cooled.  Makes 5 dozen.
