TACO RING


1  lb. ground beef
1  pkg. taco seasoning
  lettuce, shredded
  tomatoes, diced
  cheese, shredded
  sour cream
2  pkg. crescent rolls

	Brown ground beef; drain. Add seasoning.
	 Preheat oven to 350 degrees;. Open crescent rolls. You are going to make a ring with big side slightly touching little point of triangle. Place beef on ring. Then pull outer corner up and over beef and tuck to inside of ring. Bake for 20 minutes or until golden brown. Cut into sections and top with lettuce, tomatoes, cheese and sour cream.
	 You can add cheese to top of beef before closing ring.
