ENCHILADA SAUCE


3  Tbsp. canola oil
1  Tbsp. chili powder
2 (10 1/2 oz.)  cans tomato puree
1/4  tsp. ground cumin
1/4  tsp. sea salt
2  Tbsp. whole-wheat flour
1 1/2  c. vegetable broth
1/2  tsp. oregano
1/4  tsp. garlic powder

      Heat oil in skillet.  Stir in flour and cook a few minutes, stirring constantly.  Stir in chili powder.  Then add other ingredients.  Simmer 15 minutes.
