HOMEMADE KETCHUP


1  gal. tomato puree
1  medium onion, chopped
10  drops clove oil
1  c. vinegar, undiluted
4  Tbsp. salt
3 1/2  c. sugar

      Mix  and  boil  30  minutes.  Add 6 rounded tablespoons cornstarch.  (Mix  cornstarch  with small amount of cold water or tomato juice first.) Boil and can.
