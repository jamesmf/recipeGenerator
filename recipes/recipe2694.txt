"I LOVE YOU" COOKIES


2  sticks butter
1/3  c. granulated sugar
4  c. all-purpose flour
1/2  tsp. ground cloves
1/2  tsp. ground mace
2/3  c. dark molasses

      In bowl, cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Gradually add dry ingredients and molasses; mix well.  Chill 2 hours.  On lightly floured surface, roll out dough to 1/8-inch thickness.  Using 3-inch cookie cutter (heart shaped), cut out cookies.  Place on greased baking sheet.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 8 to 10 minutes or until golden brown.
Icing:
1  c. sifted 10x sugar
1  tsp. vanilla
2  Tbsp. water

      Combine icing ingredients; mix well.  Decorate cookies with icing.
      Note:  Store in airtight container.
