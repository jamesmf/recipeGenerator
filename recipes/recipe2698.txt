STRAWBERRY SALAD


1 (3 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
1/2  c. mayonnaise
1 (12 oz.)  pkg. sliced strawberries
1  c. pecan pieces
1  c. whipping cream
1/4  lb. finely chopped marshmallows (miniature may be used or cut up large one with kitchen shears)
1 (20 oz.)  can crushed pineapple, drained

      Soften cream cheese.  Blend in a small amount of stiffly beaten cream.  Fold cream cheese into remainder of whipped cream.  Mix other ingredients.  Put into heart shaped mold.  Freeze until firm. Let stand 3 minutes to thaw.  Serve on lettuce.
