CRAB 'N SPAGHETTI


1  large onion, chopped
1  green pepper, finely chopped
1/2  c. sliced fresh mushrooms
1/4  c. margarine
2  c. tomatoes
1 1/2  c. crabmeat
1  c. sour cream
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. spaghetti, cooked
1/2  c. grated sharp cheese

      Preheat oven to 325 degrees;.  Saut onion, green pepper and mushrooms in margarine in skillet.  Add tomatoes, crabmeat, sour cream and spaghetti. Mix gently.  Pour mixture into greased 9 x 13-inch casserole.  Top with cheese.  Bake at 325 degrees; for 30 minutes. Serves 6 to 8.
