LAMB CASSEROLE


2  lb. ground lamb
2  cloves garlic, minced
1 (16 oz.) and 1 (8 oz.)  can tomato sauce
1  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
6  green onions, sliced
6  oz. shredded Cheddar cheese
  paprika
1  pkg. linguini noodles, cooked and drained
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese, softened
1 (16 oz.)  pkg. sour cream

      In skillet, brown lamb and garlic. Stir in tomato sauce, salt and pepper. Simmer 10 minutes, uncovered. Place noodles in greased 13 x 9 dish. Top with tomato sauce mixture. Beat cream cheese and sour cream until smooth. Stir in onion. Spread over meat. Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes. Take out and sprinkle Cheddar cheese and paprika. Cover and let stand 5 minutes. Serve.
