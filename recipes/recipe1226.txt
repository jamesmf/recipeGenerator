RICE PUDDING


2  egg whites
1 1/2  c. skim milk
2  drops yellow food coloring
1/2  c. sugar substitute
1/4  tsp. salt
1  tsp. vanilla
1/2  c. raisins
1/4  tsp. cinnamon
1 1/2  c. cooked rice

      Combine egg whites, milk, coloring, sugar, salt, vanilla, raisins and cinnamon.  Mix and blend well.  Add rice and pour into shallow quart casserole.  Bake at 325 degrees; for 45 minutes or until a knife comes out clean when tested in the middle.  Serve warm.
