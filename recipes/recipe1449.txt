TURKEY BREAST


2  turkey breasts, skinned and boned

 
Marinade:
1  c. apple juice
1/2  c. vegetable oil
1/2  c. soy sauce
1  tsp. minced garlic
1 1/2  tsp. ground ginger

      Butterfly and flatten turkey breasts.  Mix marinade.  Add turkey.  Let stand 2 hours at room temperature, or all day in refrigerator.  Grill.
