CRUNCHY ROMAINE SALAD


1  head romaine or green curly lettuce, washed and torn
1  bunch broccoli, florets chopped
1  bunch green onions, chopped (tops and bottom)
1  can Mandarin oranges, drained
6  Tbsp. butter
1  c. nuts, chopped
2  pkg. Ramen noodles, crushed (discard flavor packet)

      Mix lettuce, broccoli, onions and oranges. Brown nuts and noodles (crushed) in butter. Put nut and noodle mixture over lettuce mixture just before serving.
Dressing:
1  c. oil
1  c. sugar
1/2  c. red vinegar
3  Tbsp. soy sauce
  pepper to taste

      Mix oil, sugar, vinegar, soy sauce and pepper together. Shake well. Add to salad just before serving.
