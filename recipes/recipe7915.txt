CHICKEN FRY


3  chicken breasts
1  tsp. garlic paste
1  grated onion
1/2  tsp. crushed cumin
1  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. black pepper
1  tsp. freshly ground red chilies
  Juice of 1 lemon
3  Tbsp. ghee (shortening), divided
  Flour
  Breadcrumbs
1/2  Tbsp. dried mint
1  egg, beaten

      Wash breasts and divide in halves like chops.  Mix garlic, grated onion, cumin, salt, pepper, chili, lemon and 1 Tbsp. ghee. Spread over halved breasts and place in well-buttered dish.  Cover with foil and bake in oven for a half hour at 350 degrees;F. Cool chicken breasts on wire rack.
      When all liquid has drained off, dredge each breast in flour, then coat well with breadcrumbs seasoned with dried mint. Heat the remaining shortening in a frying pan.  Dip breasts in beaten egg and pan-fry.
