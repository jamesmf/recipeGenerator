POTATO SALAD DRESSING
(Large Batch)  

1  cup vinegar
2  cups sugar
4  or 5 eggs

      Mix sugar and vinegar together.  Add slightly beaten eggs. Cook until thick.  If it doesn't thicken; add a little cornstarch to it.  Stir to a boil for a little while; set off to cool to see if it will thicken or set in cold water.
