GREEN PEA SALAD


1  head lettuce, torn into bite size pieces
1/2  c. celery, chopped
1/2  c. sweet onions
15  oz. (1 can) English peas
2  c. mayonnaise or salad dressing
2  Tbsp. sugar
  bacon or bacon bits

      Layer lettuce, celery, onion and peas in glass casserole or large salad bowl.  Mix sugar and mayonnaise together and spread over top.
      Refrigerate for 24 hours.
      At serving time, top with crumbled bacon or bacon bits.
