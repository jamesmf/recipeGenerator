CHILI TORTILLA CHIPS


6  flour tortillas (7-inches in diameter)
  nonstick cooking spray
  chili powder

      Heat oven to 350 degrees;. Lightly spray tortillas with nonstick cooking spray. Sprinkle with chili powder. Turn tortillas over and repeat process. Stack 2 to 3 tortillas and cut into 8 wedges; repeat process. Place on cookie sheet. Bake 8 to 10 minutes until crisp and lightly browned. Makes 12 servings.
       Calories 60, Fat 1g.
