ONE CUP SALAD


1  c. cottage cheese
1  c. shredded coconut
1  c. sour cream
1  c. maraschino cherries, drained
1  c. crushed pineapple, drained
1  c. miniature marshmallows

      Mix all ingredients together and refrigerate before serving. Better if made the day before and stirred while kept in refrigerator.  Do not freeze!
