TURKEY GRAVY


1  Tbsp. butter or margarine
3  Tbsp. flour
1/4  tsp. salt
1 1/2  c. turkey broth

	Melt butter or margarine over low heat; mix in flour and salt. Heat and stir until bubbly. Add broth slowly, stirring constantly. Cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until thickened. Makes 6 servings.
Variation:

	Giblet Gravy: Add 1/2 cup of chopped cooked giblets to turkey gravy. Heat for a few minutes to blend flavors.
