SHRIMP DIP


1  pkg. frozen tiny shrimp
1 lg.  pkg. cream cheese
1  Tbsp. lemon juice
  dash of salt and pepper
1/2  Tbsp. worcestershire sauce
1  T. catsup
1  sm. onion, finely grated
1  sm. sour cream
  1-2 drops hot sauce
1  Tbsp. mayonnaise

      Allow cream cheese to soften. Mix all ingredients together with cream cheese. When well mixed, add shrimp and stir well. Chill until ready to serve.
