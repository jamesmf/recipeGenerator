FULL OF BEANS DIP


2 (9 oz. each)  cans jalapeno bean dip
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
1  c. sour cream
1/2  c. chopped onion
  Tabasco sauce to taste
2  c. grated Cheddar cheese (reserve 1/2 c.)

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees;.  In large bowl, mix all ingredients, except cheese.  Add 1 1/2 cups cheese; mix again gently.  Put mixture into an 8 x 8-inch glass dish coated with nonstick vegetable spray.  Sprinkle dip with reserved cheese.  Bake for 15 to 20 minutes.  Serve with nachos.
