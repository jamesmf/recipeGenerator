EASY BEEF TIPS


2  lbs. beef chuck roast, cubed
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  envelope dry onion soup mix
1  C. lemon-lime soda
3  Tbsp. quick cooking Tapioca

      Place beef in 2 qt. baking dish.  Pour mushroom soup, dry onion soup mix, lemon-lime soda and tapioca over beef; stir until blended.
      Cover and bake at 275 degrees for 4 hours.
      Serve over noodles, rice or mashed potatoes.
