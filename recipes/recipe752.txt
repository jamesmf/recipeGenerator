SWEET NOODLES


1/2  lb. wide noodles
1/2  lb. sour cream
8  oz. cottage cheese
2  eggs, slightly beaten
1/4  lb. margarine
1/2 to 3/4  c. sugar
1  tsp. salt
1  can peaches, drained
  cinnamon

      Cook noodles according to package directions.  Mix sour cream, cottage cheese, eggs and margarine with sugar and salt.  Stir into drained noodles.  Put in glass baking dish and place peaches on top.  Sprinkle with cinnamon.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 1/2 to 3/4 hour.
