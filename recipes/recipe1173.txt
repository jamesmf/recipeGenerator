PANCAKES
(Makes About 10)  

1 1/4  c. flour
2 1/2  tsp. baking powder
1 to 1 1/2  Tbsp. Sugar Twin
1/2  tsp. salt
2  medium eggs
1 1/4  c. nonfat milk
2  Tbsp. diet margarine, melted

      Mix dry ingredients in one bowl.  Beat eggs in another bowl; stir in nonfat milk and melted margarine.  Combine wet and dry ingredients.  Stir just until dry ingredients are wet.  Use about 3 tablespoons of mixture for each pancake.  Cook over low heat until bubbles appear on top.  Turn and brown on other side.
