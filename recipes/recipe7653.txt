EGG-N-CHEESE STRATA


3  tsp. butter, softened
6  slices day old bread
1  c. (4 oz.) cheese, shredded
6  eggs, slightly beaten
1 1/2  c. milk
1/2  tsp. salt
  dash of pepper

      Butter bread and cut in  small cubes.  Alternate layers of bread cubes and cheese in buttered  2 quart casserole dish. Blend together eggs, milk and  seasonings.  Pour  egg  mixture over bread-cheese mixture.  Cover  and  refrigerate overnight. Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour or until golden brown.
