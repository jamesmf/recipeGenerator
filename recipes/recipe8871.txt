TUNA CASSEROLE


  Kraft macaroni and cheese dinner
1  onion
1  green pepper
1  can tuna
1  can mushroom soup
  grated cheese
1  pimento

      Prepare macaroni and cheese dinner according to directions on package. Put in skillet a teaspoon oleo and cook until tender the onion, pimento and pepper. Add tuna, mushroom soup and grated cheese. Mix together with dinner and bake in 350 degrees; oven for 35 minutes.
