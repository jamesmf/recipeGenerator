POPPY SEED DRESSING


1 1/2  c. sugar
2  Tbsp. dry mustard
2  tsp. salt
2/3  c. vinegar
3  Tbsp. onion juice
2  c. Wesson oil
3  Tbsp. poppy seed

      Mix sugar, mustard, salt and vinegar. Add onion juice and mix thoroughly. Add oil slowly, beating constantly on slow speed until thick. Add poppy seed and refrigerate.
