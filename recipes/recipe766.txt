GLAZED CHICKEN WINGS



2  c. duck sauce
4  oz. honey
3  Tbsp. lemon juice
3  Tbsp. soy sauce
3  Tbsp. brown sugar
2 to 3  lb. chicken wings or wingettes (little wings without tips)

      Mix first 5 ingredients in a bowl, set aside.  Cut chicken at joints; place in a row in roasting pan.  Place the sauce over the wings and bake until golden brown in a 350 degrees; oven.
