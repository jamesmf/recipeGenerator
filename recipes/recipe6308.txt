BROCCOLI SALAD


2  bunches broccoli
1/2  c. raisins
1/2  c. chopped pecans
1/2  c. purple onion, chopped
12  slices bacon, fried and crumbled
1 1/2  c. mayonnaise
3/4  c. sugar
3  Tbsp. vinegar

      Combine mayonnaise, sugar & vinegar; mix well. Refrigerate dressing about 3 hours. Fry bacon and crumble. Trim broccoli, using only florets. Add pecans, raisins and onions to broccoli. Mix with dressing when ready to serve. Serves 12.
