COLLEEN'S LIME JELLO


1 (6 oz.)  box lime Jello
1 1/2  c. hot water
1 1/2  c. cold water
1  (8 oz.) container Cool Whip
1 large  can crushed pineapple, well drained, reserve juice
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese, softened with 1 - 3 Tbsp. pineapple juice
1/4 to 1/2  c. chopped walnuts

      Dissolve Jello in hot water mix well, add cold water and mix. Place in refrigerator for 1 hour until just starting to set. Once Jello is ready, mix remaining ingredients into Jello with electric mixer on medium speed.  Place in a 9 x 12 inch or 9 x 13 inch dish and refrigerate until firm.  Enjoy!
