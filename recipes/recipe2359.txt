STRAWBERRY BREAD


3  c. flour
1  tsp. soda
1  tsp. cinnamon
2  c. sugar
2 (10 oz.)  pkg. frozen strawberries (reserve 1/4 to 1/2 of juice)
1  tsp. salt
1 1/4  c. cooking oil
4  eggs, well beaten

      Mix all the dry ingredients together.  Make a hole in the center of the dry ingredients.  Pour all the liquids in the hole and mix by hand.  Bake in 2 greased and floured loaf pans at 350 degrees; for about 1 hour.
Filling for Petite Sandwiches:
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
  strawberry juice
1/2  tsp. sugar

      Combine the cream cheese, juice and sugar to make filling.  Cut bread thin and spread filling between slices to make sandwiches.
