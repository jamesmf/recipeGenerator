ZUCCHINI SAUSAGE SKILLET


2  medium zucchini cut in half lengthwise
1  lb. bulk sausage
1  can mushroom soup
1/2  c. onion, thinly sliced
1 medium  clove garlic, minced
1  medium tomato, cut in wedges

      Cut squash into 1-inch pieces.  Shape sausage into 16 meatballs and brown in skillet.  Pour off fat.  Add remaining ingredients except tomato.  Cover and simmer 15 minutes or until done.  Stir often.  Add tomato and serve.
