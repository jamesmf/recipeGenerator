GROUND BEEF CASSEROLE


1  lb. ground beef
1  can cream of mushroom soup
3/4  c. milk
1 to 2  Tbsp. instant minced onion
1  c. sharp Cheddar shredded cheese
1/4  c. chopped green pepper
1  tsp. salt
4  oz. elbow macaroni
2/3  c. ketchup
  dash of black pepper

      Heat oven to 350 degrees;. Cook and stir meat until done. Drain meat. Cook macaroni as directed on package. Drain. Mix remaining ingredients. Pour in ungreased casserole dish. Cover. Bake 40 to 45 minutes.
