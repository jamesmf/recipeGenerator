MANDARIN CHICKEN


1 1/2  c. uncooked long grain rice
4  boneless skinless chicken breast halves (1 lb.)
1/2  c. sliced fresh mushrooms
2  c. orange juice
1  c. chicken broth
1/2  tsp. salt
  pepper to taste
1 (15 oz.)  can mandarin oranges, drained
  paprika and minced fresh parsley (optional)

      Spread rice in a greased shallow 3-quart baking dish. Top with chicken and mushrooms. Pour orange juice and broth over all. Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Cover and bake at 350 degrees; for 45 to 50 minutes or until chicken juices run clear and rice is tender. Garnish with oranges, paprika and parsley, if desired. Yield: 4 servings.
