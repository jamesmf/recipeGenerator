PARMESAN CHICKEN


1/2  c. grated Parmesan cheese
1/4  c. flour or Bisquick
1  tsp. paprika
1/2  tsp. salt
  dash of pepper
1  (3 lb.) frying chicken, cut up
1  egg, slightly beaten
1  Tbsp. milk
1/4  c. oleo, melted

      Combine cheese, flour and seasonings. Dip chicken in combined egg and milk; coat with cheese mixture. Place in greased baking dish. Pour melted margarine over chicken. Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour.
