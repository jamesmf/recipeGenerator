WALDORF SALAD


2  medium apples, red, unpeeled, cut into pieces
1/2  cup white raisins
1/2  cup broken nut meats
1/2  cup sour cream
1  tsp. vinegar
3/  Tbsp. sugar
  pinch salt

      Mix the first three ingredients in a bowl.  Mix the dressing of sour cream and other ingredients until smooth.  Pour over fruit and nuts and place in refrigerator until  ready to serve.
