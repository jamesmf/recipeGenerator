HOT CHICKEN SALAD


2  c. cooked diced chicken (use canned chicken as substitute)
2  c. diced celery
1  can sliced water chestnuts
1  c. slivered almonds
2  Tbsp. minced onions
1  c. Miracle Whip
2  Tbsp. lemon juice
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  can cream of chicken soup
1/4  tsp. pepper
1  c. shredded cheese

      Mix first 10 ingredients well. Top with cheese. Just before heating, top with a generous layer of crushed potato chips. Bake at 400 degrees; for 20 to 30 minutes.
