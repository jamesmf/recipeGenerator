CHICKEN WRAPPED WITH BACON


4  boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can cream of chicken soup or cream of mushroom soup
1  (8 oz.) container sour cream
1  Tbsp. flour
4  slices raw bacon

	Spiral a slice of bacon around each chicken breast and place in a glass baking dish. In a medium mixing bowl, mix soup, sour cream and flour. Spoon mixture over top of chicken breasts. Can be topped with cut up green onions. Bake 1 hour at 350 degrees;.
