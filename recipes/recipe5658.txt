BROCCOLI CHEESE SOUP


2  c. broccoli, finely chopped (can use frozen)
2  cans chicken broth
1  garlic clove, minced
1  can diced tomatoes with juice
1/2 small  can green chilies
6  oz. Velveeta light, cubed

	Cook broccoli until tender; drain. Add broth, garlic, tomatoes and chilies. Bring to a simmer. Lower temperature and add Velveeta. This makes approximately 6 cups of soup at 1 pint per cup.
	Optional: Can freeze servings of soup in individual portions for quick easy meal.
