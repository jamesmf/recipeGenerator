BRIDES PINK PUNCH


3  oz. pkg. strawberry gelatin
1  pkg. strawberry powdered drink mix
2 1/2  c. sugar
2  qt. water
1 (46 oz.)  can pineapple juice
10  oz. 7-Up
2  pt. pineapple sherbet

      Mix 1 cup boiling water with one package gelatin; let cool (do not let gel).  Mix with the cooled gelatin the following ingredients:  drink mix, sugar, water and pineapple juice.  Chill in refrigerator.  When ready to serve, add chilled 7-Up and pineapple sherbet.
