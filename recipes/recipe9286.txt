SWEET AND SOUR MEATBALLS

Meatball Mixture:
1 1/2  lb. hamburger, lean
1 1/4  oz. soup mix, onion (dry)
2/3  c. evaporated milk
2  tsp. Worcestershire sauce

      Mix all ingredients together and form into small balls. Broil until brown.
Sauce Mixture:
1  c. ketchup
1/2  c. brown sugar
2  tsp. Worcestershire sauce

      Cover browned meat balls with sauce.
      Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes.
