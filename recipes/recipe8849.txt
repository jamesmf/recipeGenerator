MOCK CHICKEN-FRIED STEAK


1  beaten egg
1/4  c. milk
1  tsp. salt
1  lb. ground beef
1  c. crackers (20 saltines)
2  Tbsp. chopped onion
1/4  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
2  Tbsp. cooking oil

      Mix egg, 1/2 cup cracker crumbs, milk, onion, chili powder, salt and Worcestershire; add ground beef and mix well. Shape into patties 1/2-inch thick; coat with remaining cracker crumbs. Cook in hot oil over medium-high heat about 3 minutes on each side.
