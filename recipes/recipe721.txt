MACARONI WITH ASPARAGUS HAM ROLLS


4  Tbsp. butter
2  Tbsp. flour
2 1/2  c. milk
1  c. Swiss cheese
6  slices ham
  Dijon mustard
18  to 24 asparagus spears, cooked
8  oz. (2 c.) elbow macaroni

      Melt butter; blend in flour.  Add milk, stirring constantly. When thick, add cheese.  Spread each ham slice with mustard.  Wrap around 3 to 4 asparagus spears.
      Cook macaroni; drain.  Turn macaroni into a 2 quart baking dish.  Cover with 2 cups cheese sauce.  Place ham rolls on top. Pour on remaining sauce.  Bake at 325 degrees; for 25 to 30 minutes.
