TACO DIP I


1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
1  can chili beans (Hormel)
1  (12 oz.) Cheddar cheese

	In a 9 x 13 glass pan, spread cream cheese on bottom of pan. Pour chili beans over cream cheese. Sprinkle Cheddar cheese on top. Bake in 375 degrees; oven until cheese is melted. Serve with nacho chips.
