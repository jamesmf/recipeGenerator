PUMPKIN PIE


1  unbaked pie shell (9-inch)
1 (16 oz.)  can pumpkin
1 (14 oz.)  can Eagle Brand milk (sweetened condensed)
2  eggs
1  tsp. cinnamon
1/2  tsp. ginger
1/2  tsp. nutmeg
1/2  tsp. salt

      Preheat oven to 425 degrees;.  Combine ingredients (except pie shell) in large bowl.  Pour in shell and bake in oven for 15 minutes. Then reduce to 350 degrees; and bake for 35 to 40 minutes.  (Knife inserted near edge comes out clean.)  Cool.  Refrigerate leftovers.
