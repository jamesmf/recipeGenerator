POTATO SOUP


2  Tbsp. margarine
1/3  c. chopped celery
1/3  c. chopped onion
4  c. diced potatoes
3  c. chicken broth
2  c. milk
1 1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
  dash of paprika
8  oz. shredded cheese
  croutons
  fresh chopped parsley

      In a large saucepan, melt margarine. Saut√© celery and onion until tender. Add potatoes and broth. Cover and simmer until potatoes are tender. Puree potatoes. Stir in milk and seasonings. Add cheese, croutons and parsley. Good on cold day, lunch or dinner.
