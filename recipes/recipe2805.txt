CRANBERRY BREAD


2  c. all-purpose flour
1  c. sugar
1 1/2  tsp. baking powder
1  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. soda
3/4  c. orange juice
1  Tbsp. grated orange peel
2  Tbsp. shortening
1  egg
1  c. fresh cranberries, halved
1  c. chopped pecans

      In a large mixing bowl, sift together dry ingredients.  Add orange peel, shortening, egg and orange juice.  Mix quickly, but well.  Add cranberries and nuts.  Blend.  Pour into a well-greased 9 x 5 x 3-inch pan.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 55 to 65 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.  Cool thoroughly before slicing.
