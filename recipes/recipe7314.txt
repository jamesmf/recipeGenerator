LEE'S FAVORITE MEATY MANICOTTI


1/2  lb. ground beef
1  clove garlic, minced
1/4  tsp. oregano
1  Tbsp. parsley flakes
1/2  c. grated Romano cheese
1/2  lb. Mozzarella cheese, diced
1  egg, slightly beaten
1/2  lb. (8) manicotti, parboiled or 1 box
1/2  c. chopped onion
1/2  tsp. salt
1  slice bread, diced
1  jar Ragu spaghetti sauce (15 1/2 oz.)

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees;. Brown meat, onion and garlic together in skillet. Spoon off excess fat. Stir in salt, oregano, bread, parsley and 1/4 cup each spaghetti sauce and grated cheese. Cool slightly. Mix in Mozzarella, cheese and egg. Fill hot manicotti with stuffing. Spread 1 cup spaghetti sauce in the bottom of a 12 x 7 ovenproof dish. Arrange manicotti in dish. Pour remaining sauce over the center third of the manicotti. Cover with foil and bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes. Remove foil. Sprinkle remaining 1/4 cup grated cheese over top and bake, uncovered, for 10 minutes more. Serves 4.
