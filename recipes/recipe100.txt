CHOCOLATE SODA


1/4  c. chocolate syrup
2  Tbsp. milk
  carbonated water
  vanilla ice cream

      For each serving, pour syrup and milk into a chilled 14-ounce or 16-ounce glass; mix well.  Add chilled carbonated water to fill glass 3/4 full.  Stir.  Add 1 to 2 scoops vanilla ice cream.  Fill to top with carbonated water.
