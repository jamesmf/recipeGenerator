STRAWBERRY FIG JAM


3  c. Figs, mashed
6  oz. strawberry Jello - with sugar
3  c. Sugar

      Put all ingredients in large pan.  Bring to boil. Stir constantly.  Boil at least 2-3 minutes to dissolve sugar.  Put into freezer boxes and freeze, or put into jelly jars and process 10 minutes in boiling water to seal jars.
