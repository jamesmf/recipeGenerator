AUNT BABE'S RICE SALAD


2  c. uncooked rice
  chicken bouillon
2 small  jars marinated artichoke hearts
7  spring onions
30  pimento stuffed olives
1/2  c. minced bell pepper
1  tsp. curry powder, or more to taste
1/3  c. mayonnaise

	Cook rice according to directions, using bouillon in place of water, set aside.  Drain artichokes slightly and chop.  Slice onions using green part also.  Slice olives and mix artichokes, onions, olive and bell pepper with rice.  Add curry powder to mayonnaise and mix with other ingredients.	Best served at room temperature.
