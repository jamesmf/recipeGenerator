CHICKEN MUSHROOM SOUP


1/2  lb. fresh mushrooms, sliced
1/4  c. butter
3  Tbsp. flour
1/2  tsp. salt
1  c. whipping cream
1  Tbsp. dry sherry
2  c. chicken broth
2  cooked boneless skinless chicken breasts, cubed

      Melt butter and cook mushrooms.  Sprinkle in flour and salt; cook until thick, stirring constantly.  Gradually whisk in cream, then broth.  Add chicken.  When hot add sherry and serve immediately.  Makes about 4 servings.
