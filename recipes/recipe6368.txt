MARINATED VEGETABLE SALAD


1 (15 oz.)  can French-style green beans
1 (15 oz.)  can whole corn
1 (15 oz.)  can medium size hominy
1 (15 oz.)  can carrots, cut in small pieces
1  c. chopped celery
1/2  c. chopped green pepper
3/4  c. cider vinegar
1  c. sugar
  salt and pepper to taste
  onion, thinly sliced
1  can tomato juice or 1 1/2 c. catsup

      Mix all ingredients. Refrigerate at least 8 hours. Makes 6 servings.
      Cal-227, Cho-55g, Protein-2g, Fat-1g.
