BREAKFAST CASSEROLE


6  slices white bread
1 1/2  c. shredded Cheddar cheese (6 oz.)
1  can asparagus tips or 1 (10 oz.) pkg. frozen asparagus
1  c. diced fully cooked ham
6  beaten eggs
3/4  tsp. dry mustard
1  Tbsp. chopped onion
1 3/4  c. milk
1/4  tsp. salt

      With 3-inch biscuit or doughnut cutter, cut one round from each slice of bread. Set rounds aside. Tear remaining bread scraps into 1-inch pieces; spread evenly in a 9 x 9 x 2-inch baking pan. Sprinkle the Cheddar cheese evenly over the bread. Then layer the asparagus over the cheese. Combine the cooked ham and chopped onions and layer this mixture over the asparagus. Arrange the bread rounds on the top.
       In medium mixing bowl, combine the egg, milk, dry mustard and salt. Stir until well blended. Pour over the mixture in the baking pan. Cover pan with foil. Chill in the refrigerator several hours or overnight. Uncover and bake in 375 degrees; oven for about 1 hour or until center is set. Let stand 10 minutes before serving. Makes 6 servings.
