BAKED MASHED POTATOES


2 1/2  lb. red potatoes, peeled and cut up
2  medium onions, chopped
1/2  c. water
1/4  c. olive oil
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. sage
1/2  tsp. pepper
1/3  c. buttermilk or sour cream

      Grease casserole dish. Add potatoes and onions. Mix together water, oil, salt, sage and pepper. Drizzle over potatoes. Stir together. Bake, uncovered, at 450 degrees; until tender. Mash or beat together with mixer, adding the buttermilk.
