HOT CRAB SANDWICH


1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese with chives
6  Tbsp. mayonnaise
1  lb. crabmeat
8  English muffins, pre-split
3  large tomatoes (16 slices)
  grated cheese

	Blend softened cream cheese with mayonnaise; fold in crabmeat.  Split muffins.  Place a tomato slice on each split muffin, cover with 1 large spoonful of crab mixture.  Top each with generous sprinkling of grated cheese.	Bake in 400 degrees; oven for 15 to 20 minutes until cheese melts.  Makes 8 servings.
