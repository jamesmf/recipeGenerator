TARTAR SAUCE


1  Tbsp. sweet relish
3/4  c. mayonnaise
1 1/2  c. fine chopped onion

      Combine all ingredients; blend well.  Cover and refrigerate until ready to serve.  Makes 1 cup of sauce.
       You can add 1 1/2 tablespoons parsley flakes and 1 tablespoon of chopped celery if you want it.
