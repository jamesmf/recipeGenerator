TORTILLA CHICKEN


4  to 6 chicken breasts, boneless and skinless
1  can Ortega green chilies, diced
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  can cream of chicken soup
6  corn tortillas
1  lb. grated sharp Cheddar cheese
1/2  c. milk
  salt and pepper to taste

	Preheat oven to 350 degrees;.  Wash chicken and pat dry.  Season chicken with salt and pepper.  Wrap in foil and bake for 1 hour or until tender, reserve juice.
	While chicken is baking, combine soups, green chili peppers and onion.  Let stand (the longer the better).	Cut chicken into 1-inch strips.
	In a buttered 9 x 13 baking dish alternate layers of tortillas, chicken and soup mixture.  Sprinkle cheese on top.	Combine the juices with the milk and drizzle over the cheese.  Bake for 1 hour.
