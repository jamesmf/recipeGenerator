MACARONI AND CHEESE


8  oz. (1 pkg.) macaroni
1 1/2  c. milk
1  tsp. salt
2  eggs, slightly beaten
2  c. Cheddar cheese, shredded (more if desired)

      Cook macaroni in boiling, salted water until tender.  Drain and set aside.  Add milk and salt to eggs and set aside.  In a 9x13-inch casserole dish, alternate layers of cooked macaroni and grated cheese.  Cover with milk and egg mixture.  Bake at 350 degrees; about 45 minutes or until firm.
