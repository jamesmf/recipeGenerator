ROAST AND GRAVY


3  lb. roast
1  can Golden Mushroom soup
1  can French Onion soup
  Tony Chachere's Creole seasoning
  water

      Season both sides of roast with Tony's seasoning; brown in iron skillet that has been sprayed with Pam. Put soups over roast; add enough water to cover roast. Cook uncovered in oven for 4 hours at 350 degrees;. Remove meat and slice. Place slices back into gravy.
