CAULIFLOWER BROCCOLI SALAD


1  head cauliflower
1  bunch broccoli
1  large red onion
1  lb. Cheddar cheese
1  lb. bacon
1  c. mayonnaise
1/2  c. sugar
1/4  c. vinegar

      Chop cauliflower, broccoli and onion.  Grate or cube cheese. Fry bacon crisp and crumble, then combine all together in large serving bowl.  Mix mayonnaise, sugar and vinegar until smooth. Stir in with other ingredients until all is well coated.  Chill for awhile before serving.  This keeps well in refrigerator in a tightly closed container.
