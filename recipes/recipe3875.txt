MARIE CHOPLIN'S CORNED BEEF MOLD
("The Best")  

1  pkg. lemon Jell-O
1  c. chopped celery
1  small onion, minced
1  c. mayonnaise
1  small green pepper, diced
1  can corned beef

      Stir lemon Jell-O into 1 cup of boiling water until dissolved. Remove from stove and let mixture cool. Mix with the remaining ingredients and pour into a mold. Refrigerate overnight.
