HUSHWI
(Stuffing)  

1  c. rice
1  lb. meat
1/2  can tomato paste
1  clove garlic, chopped
3  tbsp. pine nuts (snobar)
1/2  stick butter
  Salt, pepper, cumin to taste

      Melt butter and add snobar.  Add meat and toss until color turns.  Add to rice.  Add garlic, tomato paste and seasonings.
