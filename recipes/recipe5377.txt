BROCCOLI SOUP


1  (16 oz.) bag frozen broccoli
1 (14 1/2 oz.)  can chicken broth
1  c. milk
1/2  c. cream
2  Tbsp. butter or margarine
2  Tbsp. cornstarch or plain flour
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. parsley flakes
1/2  tsp. white pepper
1/2  c. onions, diced
1/4  tsp. garlic, minced

	In a pan, cook broccoli according to directions on bag. Drain and cut up in small pieces; set aside. Combine all other ingredients and mix well. Cook until onion is tender and add broccoli. Simmer until thick.
