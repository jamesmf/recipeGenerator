MEZETTI


1  lb. ground beef
1/4  c. chopped green pepper
1  c. chopped onion
1  Tbsp. chili powder
1  tsp. garlic salt
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. spaghetti
1  c. water
1  can tomato soup
1  can mushroom soup
1  c. grated Cheddar cheese
1  c. mushrooms (optional)

      Brown ground beef, green pepper, chopped onion, chili powder and garlic salt. Drain off grease. Sprinkle noodles on top and add 1 cup water. Mix soups and mushrooms, if desired, and pour over mixture. Simmer for 25 minutes. Add cheese and heat for 5 more minutes or until cheese is melted.
