GRANDMA'S PEAR SALAD


1 large  can pears, drained (reserve liquid)
1 small  box lime jello
1 - 8  oz. cream cheese
1/2  c. pecans
1 - 8  oz. cool whip

      Dissolve jello in 1 c. hot pear juice. Beat in cream cheese with mixture while juice is hot. Let jell 10 minutes. Stir in mashed pears and cool whip. Top with crushed pecans. Refrigerate.
