KOLACKY


1  lb. butter or oleo
4  c. flour
1/8  tsp. baking powder
1/2  pt. sour cream
1  tsp. milk

      Blend oleo, flour and baking powder as for pie dough; add sour cream and milk.  Work with hands until smooth.  Refrigerate overnight.  Roll and cut into squares, add filling and fold 2 opposite corners to meet each other.  Brush with egg white.  Bake at 375 degrees; for 20 minutes or until brown.
