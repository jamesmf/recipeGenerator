CHOCOLATE-COVERED STRAWBERRIES


1  pt. strawberries with hulls intact
2/3  c. semi-sweet chocolate chips
4  Tbsp. butter

      Cover cookie sheet with wax paper, set aside.  Wash and pat dry strawberries.  Melt chocolate chips and butter on top of a double boiler over simmering water, stirring until smooth.  Remove from heat; keep double boiler over water.  Dip each strawberry two-thirds of the way up into chocolate mixture; allow excess to drip off.  Place on prepared cookie sheet.  Allow chocolate to set. Chill until ready to serve.
