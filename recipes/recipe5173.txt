CAULIFLOWER SALAD BOWL


4  cups cauliflower, broken into pieces
1  cup pitted coarsely chopped black olives
2/3  cup chopped green pepper
1/2  cup chopped onion
1/2  cup vegetable oil
3  Tbsp. lemon juice
3  Tbsp. rice vinegar
2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
1/2  tsp. sugar

      Place all ingredients in bowl and mixture thoroughly.  Chill prior to serving.
