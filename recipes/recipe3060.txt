PICKLED OKRA


1/2  tsp. dill seed
  small okra to fill pt. jar

 
Per Jar:
1  hot green pepper
1  hot red pepper
1  clove garlic
1/2  tsp. dill weed
1  qt. vinegar
2  c. water
1/2  c. plain salt
1  tsp. sugar

      Put 1/2 teaspoon dill seed in bottom of pint jar.  Pack jar with small okra.  Add the peppers, garlic and dill weed.  Boil vinegar, water, salt and sugar.  Pour over vegetables.  Seal.
