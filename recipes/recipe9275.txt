ANN'S BEEF CASSEROLE


8  oz. (1 pkg.) macaroni
1  lb. ground beef
1  small onion, chopped
1  bell pepper, chopped
2 to 3  tsp. parsley flakes
1  Tbsp. Heinz 57 sauce
8  oz. (1 can) tomato sauce
1  tsp. salt (approx.)
1/2  tsp. pepper (approx.)
1  Tbsp. sugar (approx.)
10  oz. (1 can) soup, cream of mushroom
1  c. Cheddar cheese, shredded

      Cook macaroni according to package directions.  Drain and set aside.
      In a skillet, cook beef, onion and green pepper until beef is browned.  Drain off fat.  Stir in parsley flakes, Heinz 57 sauce, tomato sauce, salt, pepper and sugar.
      Place macaroni in a lightly greased 9x13-inch dish.  Add meat mixture and toss together. Spoon mushroom soup over meat and macaroni.  Sprinkle cheese over top.
      Bake at 350 degrees; for 20 to 25 minutes, until heated through and cheese is melted.
      Makes 6 to 8 servings.
