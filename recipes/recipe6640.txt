EGG STRATA


6  eggs
2  c. milk
2  c. Cheddar cheese
1/4  tsp. dry mustard
1/2  tsp. baking powder
1  large loaf of sliced bread
  Salt and pepper to taste

      Line bottom of a 9 x 12 pan with broken up pieces of bread. Scramble the eggs with the rest of the ingredients and pour over bread.  Refrigerate overnight.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes or until set.
      This makes a good breakfast served with bacon or sausage or a luncheon served with fresh asparagus. Serve for breakfast or lunch.
