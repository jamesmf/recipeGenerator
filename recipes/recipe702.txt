BAKED MACARONI AND CHEESE


1  c. milk
1  egg
2  c. elbow macaroni, cooked and drained
1/2 to 1  lb. grated extra sharp Cheddar cheese
  dots of butter (approximately 1/2 stick)
  bread crumbs
  sliced tomatoes
  salt and pepper to taste

      In large Pyrex casserole, well greased, put 1 cup milk and 1 egg.  Beat with fork until well blended.  Add in layers cooked macaroni, grated cheese, dots of butter, salt and pepper.  Sprinkle bread crumbs on top.  Arrange sliced tomatoes.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 25 to 35 minutes or until thoroughly heated through.
