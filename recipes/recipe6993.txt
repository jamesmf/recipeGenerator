ITALIAN CHICKEN


6  boneless and skinless chicken breast
1 (8 oz.)  bottle Italian salad dressing
6  Tbsp. butter
3  Tbsp. lemon juice

      Wash chicken breast and place in baking dish. Coat each piece with salad dressing and sprinkle with lemon juice. Place 1 Tbsp. butter on each chicken breast. Cover and bake at 300 degrees; for 2 hours.
