CABBAGE RELISH


4  c. cabbage, finely chopped
2  c. pimentos
1/2  c. vinegar
1/2  tsp. celery seed
  dash of cayenne pepper
1  medium onion, chopped
1/2  green pepper, chopped
1  Tbsp. salt
1/2  c. sugar
1/4  tsp. mustard seed
  carrot (optional)

	Mix all together and put in the refrigerator for 1 day. Then it is ready to eat.
