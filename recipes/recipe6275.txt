LINGUINE AND WHITE CLAM SAUCE


2 (7 1/2 oz.)  cans chopped clams or chopped clams from fish store
1 (8 oz.)  bottle clam juice
1/4  c. butter
4  garlic cloves, minced
2  Tbsp. flour
1/4  c. chopped parsley
1 1/2  tsp. thyme
  salt and pepper to taste

      Drain clams. Reserve liquid.  In saucepan, melt butter.  Add garlic. Cook until golden on low flame.  Blend flour into butter/garlic until smooth, slowly adding the clam juice and stirring constantly.  Add salt, pepper, thyme and parsley; cover and simmer 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Add chopped clams. Stir and pour over cooked linguine.  Makes 2 1/2 cups.
