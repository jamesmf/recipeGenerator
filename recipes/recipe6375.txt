PINEAPPLE-MANGO SALSA


2  mangos, peeled, pitted and cut into 1/2-inch cubes
1/4  whole pineapple, cut into 1/2-inch cubes
1/4  c. finely chopped red onion
  juice of 1 small lime
1  small jalapeno, seeded and minced
2  tsp. honey
2  Tbsp. minced fresh mint

      Toss together all ingredients. Cover with plastic wrap and let stand at room temperature 30 minutes. Serve with grilled seafood or tortilla chips. Makes 6 servings.
      Serving Size: 1/2 cup. Cal-64, CHO-16.8, Pro-.5, Fat-.29.
