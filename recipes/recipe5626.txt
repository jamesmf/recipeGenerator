BROCCOLI SLAW


1/2  C. vegetable oil
1/2  C. sugar
1/2  C. vinegar
1  pkg. chicken flavored Ramen Noodles
1  pkg. broccoli slaw
1/2  C. sunflower seeds
3/4  C. sliced almonds

      Mix oil, sugar and vinegar.  Add seasoning packet and noodles.  Let stand 1 to 2 hours.
      Add broccoli slaw.  Top with sunflower seeds and almonds; toss and refrigerate 1 hour before serving.
      Can make it the day before also.
