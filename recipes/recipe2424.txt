HEAVENLY WHITE CAKE


3  tsp. baking powder
2 1/4  c. flour
1  tsp. salt
1 1/2  c. sugar
1/2  c. shortening
1  c. strained light colored orange juice
1/2  c. egg whites (4)
1  tsp. grated orange rind

      Sift dry ingredients together into a big bowl.  Add shortening and 2/3 cup orange juice, unbeaten egg whites and orange rind. Beat again for 2 minutes.  Pour the thin batter into 2 layer pans, lined with waxed paper.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes.
