CHICKEN NUGGETS


1  c. dry bread crumbs
1/2  c. Parmesan cheese, grated
2  tsp. dried basil
2  tsp. paprika
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. pepper
3/4  c. butter or margarine, melted
2 1/2  lb. chicken breasts (boneless, skinless), cut in 1-inch cubes

	In a shallow bowl, combine all ingredients, except butter and chicken. Place butter in another shallow bowl. Dip chicken in butter, then roll in bread crumb mixture. Place in a greased 15 x 10-inch baking pan. Bake, uncovered, at 400 degrees; for 15 to 20 minutes or until juices run clear. Yield: 8 servings.
