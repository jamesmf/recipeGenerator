DAFFODIL EASTER CAKE


1  c. egg whites
1/2  tsp. salt
1  tsp. cream of tartar
1  c. plus 2 Tbsp. sugar
1/2  c. flour
1/2  tsp. vanilla
6  egg yolks
2/3  c. flour
1/2  tsp. orange extract

      Beat until foamy the egg whites.  Add salt and cream of tartar. Beat until stiff but not dry.  Fold in carefully 1 cup plus 2 tablespoons sugar.  Divide mixture.  Fold into one part 1/2 cup flour (sifted 5 times) and vanilla.  Fold into other part egg yolks (beaten), 2/3 cup flour (sifted 5 times) and orange extract.  Place by spoonfuls into ungreased 9-inch tube cake pan, alternating yellow and white batters as in marble cake.  Bake in 325 degrees; oven for 60 minutes.
