CHICKEN AND DUMPLINGS


4 to 5  lb. chicken (whole or cut into parts)
5  Tbsp. margarine
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. black pepper
2  c. all-purpose flour
1/2  c. milk
1/2  c. water
2  Tbsp. shortening
1 1/2  c. milk

      In large pot, cover chicken with water and boil slowly until tender.  Remove chicken to platter.  Add salt, pepper and margarine to broth.  Simmer to blend seasonings.
      In mixing bowl, combine flour, milk, water and shortening. Pour out on floured board and knead until firm (adding flour if necessary).  Press the dough flat on floured surface.  Cover with clean cloth and let stand for 1 hour.  Roll dough out knife blade thin.  Cut into 2-inch squares.  Drop into boiling broth.  Cover and cook for 10 minutes on high heat.  Reduce to low heat.   Add chicken and 1 1/2 cups milk.  Stir gently.  Remove from heat, add salt and pepper to taste.
