SHELL BAKE


2  c. shell macaroni (uncooked)
1  c. milk
1 (12 oz.)  can luncheon meat (Spam), cubed
1 (10 1/2 oz.)  can cream of mushroom soup
1/4  c. shredded cheese
1/4  c. green pepper, diced

      Prepare shell macaroni according to directions. Drain and set aside. In medium saucepan, combine soup, milk and cheese. Cook and stir until cheese melts. Stir in remaining ingredients. Turn into a 2-quart baking dish. Bake at 325 degrees; for 30 minutes. Makes 6 servings.
