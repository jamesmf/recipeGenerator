SCALLOPED CORN AND TOMATOES


2  pkg. frozen white corn
1/4  c. butter or margarine
1  c. thinly sliced onion
4  tomatoes (large), peeled and chopped
2  tsp. sugar
2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
2  Tbsp. butter
2  c. fresh bread crumbs

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees;.  Place thawed corn into a 2-quart casserole.  In a large skillet, saut√© onions in 1/4 cup butter until tender and golden. Stir in tomatoes, salt, sugar and pepper until well blended.  Combine with corn.  Top with bread crumbs. Drizzle with 2 tablespoons melted butter.  Bake, uncovered, 30 to 35 minutes.
