TUNA AND MACARONI SALAD


2  c. cooked macaroni
1/2  c. celery, chopped
1/2  c. onion, chopped
2  boiled eggs
1  can tuna fish, drained
  mayonnaise
  salt and pepper
  paprika

      Mix all together with enough mayonnaise to desired consistency. Salt and pepper to taste; stir. Sprinkle top with paprika.
