SUN BURST STIR-FRY


2  bags Success White or Brown rice
1  can (20 oz.) pineapple chunks
1/3  c. plus 1 Tbsp. Kikkoman Soy sauce, divided
2  Tbsp. cornstarch, divided
3/4 to 1  tsp. crushed red pepper
3/4  tsp. garlic powder
1/2  c. water
1  lb. boneless pork or chicken, cut in thin strips
2  Tbsp. vegetable oil
1 (16 oz.)  pkg. frozen oriental vegetable medley

      Prepare rice according to package directions; set aside. Drain pineapple, reserving 1/4 c. juice  Blend  pineapple juice with 1/3 c. soy sauce, 1 Tbsp. cornstarch, pepper, garlic powder, and 1/2 c. water; set aside.
       Combine pork or chicken with remaining 1 Tbsp. cornstarch and 1 Tbsp. soy sauce.  Heat oil in hot wok or large skillet over high heat. Add pork or chicken; stir-fry 2 minutes.
       Add frozen vegetables; stir-fry 4 minutes. Stir in soy sauce mixture; cook and stir 1 minute or until sauce boils and thickens. Stir in pineapple. Serve over rice.
