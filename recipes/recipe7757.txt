CHICKEN POT PIE


1/2  c. butter
3/4  c. sliced mushrooms
1  c. milk
1/2  tsp. pepper
2  c. sifted flour
1  Tbsp. baking powder
3/4 to 1  c. milk
1  c. finely chopped celery
1/3  c. enriched flour
1 1/2  c. chicken broth
2  c. coarsely chopped, cooked chicken
1  tsp. salt
1/4  c. shortening

      Melt butter in large saucepan. Add celery and mushrooms. Cook slowly for about 5 minutes. Stir in flour, salt and pepper. Add milk and chicken broth gradually, stirring constantly. Cook until sauce is thickened. Stir in chopped chicken. Pour mixture into a greased 2-quart casserole.
      DROP BISCUITS: Sift together flour, baking powder and salt. Cut in shortening  until mixture is crumbly. Add milk to make a drop batter. Drop by spoonfuls onto chicken mixture. Bake in a hot 425 degrees; oven for about 20 minutes.
