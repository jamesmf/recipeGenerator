HOT CRAB DIP


8  oz. pkg. cream cheese
1  Tbsp. chopped onion
1  Tbsp. horseradish
2  Tbsp. milk
7  oz. can crab meat, drained and flaked
  salt and pepper to taste

      Stir ingredients well and put in casserole dish.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 25 to 30 minutes.  Serve with crackers or chips.
