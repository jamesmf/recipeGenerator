MOM'S MEAT LOAF


2  Tbsp. butter
1  medium onion, chopped
2  lb. ground beef
1  egg, beaten
4  Tbsp. ketchup
4  Tbsp. Dijon mustard
4  Tbsp. steak sauce
4  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
5  drops hot sauce
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper
1/2  c. cornflake crumbs

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees;.  Grease loaf pan lightly.  Melt butter and sauté onion.  Combine meat and all other ingredients.  Add sautéed onions.  Dust top of meat loaf with cornflake crumbs. Bake 1 hour.  Broil last 5 minutes.  Let sit 10 minutes before slicing.
