CAJUN RUBBED FISH NUGGETS


1  tsp garlic powder
4  tsp paprika
1  tsp dry mustard
1  tsp crushed peppercorns
1/4  tsp cayenne pepper
1/4  tsp finely crushed fennel seeds

      Brush BBQ grill with oil.  Turn to medium high.  Place Cajun Rub and 1 pound of fish nuggets in a paper bag.  Shake until pieces are well coated.  Thread onto pre-soaked skewers.  Cook on hot grill for 3 - 5 minutes each side.  Serve with lemon wedges, as an appetizer for 4 or as a main dish for 2.
