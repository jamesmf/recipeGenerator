LAKE ERIE PERCH


  perch fish
1  egg, beaten
1/2  c. milk
1/2  c. self-rising flour
1/2  c. corn meal

	Mix eggs and milk in one bowl. Mix flour and corn meal in another bowl. Dip perch, one piece at a time, into egg mixture, then dip perch, one piece at a time, into flour mixture. In a deep-fryer, heat oil to 370 degrees;. Add perch and deep-fry until each side is golden brown.
