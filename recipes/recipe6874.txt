SHRIMP JAMBALAYA


3  c. water
3  c. rice
2  lb. shrimp, cleaned
1 1/2  c. chopped onions
1 1/4  c. cooking oil
  salt and pepper

	Add onions to 1/4 cup of cooking oil and cook until dark golden brown. Add shrimp to onions and saut. Add well washed rice with water; salt rice to taste. Bring to a boil until almost all the water has evaporated. Stir mixture well. Cover on real low fire until cooked, about 20 minutes.
