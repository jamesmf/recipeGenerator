CHOCOLATE CINNAMON RAISIN BREAD


1/2  stick butter
2/3  c. sugar
1  egg
2  c. sifted cake flour
1  tsp. baking soda
1/3  c. cocoa
1  tsp. cinnamon
1  c. buttermilk
1  c. raisins
3/4  c. chopped nuts

      Cream butter and sugar until light.  Beat in egg.  Sift dry ingredients together; add to creamed mixture alternately with buttermilk.  Stir in raisins and nuts.  Turn into a greased 9-inch loaf pan.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour.
