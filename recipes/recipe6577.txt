CHICKEN POT PIE


1  can Veg-All
2  cans white chicken
1  can cream of chicken soup
1/3  c. milk
  salt and pepper
2  pie crusts (Pillsbury in refrigerator section)

      Mix all ingredients and pour into pie crust. Place the other pie crust on top (cut a few slits in top) and bake at 375 degrees; for 30 minutes.
