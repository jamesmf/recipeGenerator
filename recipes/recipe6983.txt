SHRIMP SPAGHETTI


1  stick oleo
1  onion, chopped
2  bell peppers, chopped
2  cloves garlic, chopped
1  lb. cleaned raw shrimp
1 large  can stewed tomatoes
1  can tomato sauce
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. Velveeta cheese
1  c. oil
  seasoning to taste
16  oz. spaghetti

      Saut√© vegetables in oleo until tender.  Add stewed tomatoes and tomato sauce and stir; add 1/2 cheese.
      In a bowl, mix raw shrimp, oil, salt, pepper and any other desired seasonings; add to cooked vegetables. Add remaining cheese. Cook approximately 20 minutes.
      Cook spaghetti in salted water; drain. Add spaghetti to shrimp mixture; stir. Serve immediately or may be heated in microwave or oven. Delicious!
