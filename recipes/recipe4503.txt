MACARONI SALAD


2  c. dry macaroni
2  to 3 carrots, shredded
1  small onion, chopped
1  c. celery, diced

 
Dressing:
1  c. Miracle Whip
1/4  c. vinegar (white)
1/2  c. sugar
1/2 (14 oz.)  can Eagle Brand condensed milk
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper

      Boil macaroni until tender.  Drain and rinse in cold water; let drain dry.  Add carrots, celery and onion.
       Combine dressing ingredients. Pour over macaroni and mix well. Chill before serving.
