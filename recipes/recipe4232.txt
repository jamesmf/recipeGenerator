PICO DE GALLO


1  c. chopped tomato
3/4  c. tomato juice
1  bell pepper, chopped
1/4  c. chopped white pepper
1/4  c. chopped cilantro
4  to 5 chopped jalapenos
1  clove garlic, minced
1/4  tsp. pepper
1/4  tsp. salt

      Combine all ingredients in medium bowl and mix well. Refrigerate 30 minutes.
