BROCCOLI SWEET SALAD


2   Tbsp. red cider vinegar
1  c. mayonnaise
8  oz. shredded cheddar cheese
2  bunches broccoli
1/2  c. sugar
1  lb. bacon (fried, drained & crumbled)
1  medium red onion (diced)

      Chop broccoli into bite size pieces, using only flowerettes. Mix broccoli, onion, bacon & cheese, tossing to mix well. In separate container, mix mayonnaise, sugar & red cider vinegar together. Just before serving pour over broccoli & mix well.
