TRADITIONAL NO-BAKE PUMPKIN PIE


1 (14 oz.)  can sweetened condensed milk
1  egg, slightly beaten
1  tsp. ground cinnamon
1/2  tsp. ground ginger
1/2  tsp. ground nutmeg
1/2  tsp. salt
1  envelope Knox unflavored gelatine
2  tsp. water
1 (16 oz.)  can pumpkin
  9-inch graham cracker crust

      In a small bowl, blend milk, egg, cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg and salt; set aside.  In medium saucepan, sprinkle unflavored gelatine over water.  Let it stand 1 minute.  Stir over low heat until gelatine is completely dissolved, about 2 minutes.  Blend in milk mixture.  Continue stirring over low heat until mixture is slightly thickened, about 5 minutes.  Blend in pumpkin.  Turn into prepared crust; chill until firm.  Garnish, if desired, with whipped cream. Serves 8.
