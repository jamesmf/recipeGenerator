BROCCOLI AND CAULIFLOWER SALAD


1  medium head cauliflower
3 or 4  bunches broccoli
4  hard-boiled eggs
8  oz. box sour cream
1  c. mayonnaise
1  pkg. dry Hidden Valley Ranch dressing powder
  Cheddar cheese

      Mix  sour  cream  with mayonnaise.  Add dry powder mix. Mix well.  Add to washed and cut up cauliflower and broccoli. Toss. Sprinkle  shredded  Cheddar cheese on top.  Refrigerate until ready to serve.
