SHRIMP CRAB DIP


3  lb. boiled shrimp
2  lb. crab meat
3  (1/2 pt.) whipping cream
1 1/2  sticks butter
3  Tbsp. flour
6  lemons, juiced
3  cans cream of mushroom soup
  salt and pepper to taste
  sherry to taste

      Melt butter; make sauce by adding whipping cream, lemon juice, salt and pepper. Add mushroom soup and sherry. Mix well. Add shrimp and crab meat. Serve in timbles. Serves 75.
