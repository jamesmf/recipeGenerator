SQUASH RELISH


10  c. shredded yellow squash
2  green peppers, chopped
1 large  jar pimientos
4  large onions, chopped
1/4  c. canning salt
2 1/4  c. cider vinegar
4 1/2  c. sugar
1  Tbsp. nutmeg
1  Tbsp. turmeric
1  Tbsp. cornstarch
1  Tbsp. celery seed
1 1/2  tsp. black pepper

      Mix shredded squash, green peppers, onions and pimientos in a large container.  Pour 1/4 cup salt over the vegetables and let stand overnight.  Next day, rinse and drain vegetables.  Add vinegar, sugar and all spices.  Cook 30 minutes by bringing to a slow boil, then reducing heat until it just stops boiling.  Put in clean, hot jars and can.  Makes 5 to 6 pints.
