THREE BEAN SALAD


1  can wax beans, drained
1  can green beans, drained
1  can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
1  onion, cut into rings
1  green pepper, sliced
1/3  c. vinegar
1/2  c. oil
2/3  c. sugar

	Combine beans, onion and green pepper. In a separate bowl, combine vinegar, oil and sugar. Pour the vinegar mixture over the bean mixture. Add salt and pepper to taste. Place in the refrigerator. Stir every 4 hours for 1 day. Drain and serve.
