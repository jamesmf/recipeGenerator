BECCA'S CHICKEN CHOW MEIN


1/4  c. oil
1  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
2  c. sliced cabbage
3  c. thinly sliced celery
1  lb. can bean sprouts, drained
1 (4 oz.)  can water chestnuts, sliced
2  Tbsp. sugar
2  c. chicken broth
2 1/2  Tbsp. cornstarch
1/4  c. water
1/4  c. soy sauce
2  c. diced cooked chicken
  Chow Mein Noodles

      Heat oil, salt, & pepper in deep skillet or Dutch oven.  Add cabbage, celery, bean sprouts, water chestnuts, & sugar.  Stir in chicken broth; cook about 10 minutes.  Mix cornstarch, water, & soy sauce; add to vegetable mixture. stir until mixture thickens.  Add chicken and heat.
