FINGER PAINT


1 1/2  c. laundry starch (not instant)
1 1/2  c. soap flakes
1/2  c. talcum powder
1  qt. water
  food coloring

      Mix first 4 ingredients together and place over low heat.  Stir until slightly soupy but not gummy.  Divide paint into as many containers as colors desired.  Add food coloring.  When cool, cover and store.  Baby food jars are ideal.  Little artists will love it.
