TEXAS BARBECUE SAUCE


1 (14 oz.)  bottle catsup
1/2  c. cider vinegar
1  c. water
1  c. vegetable oil
1/3  c. Worcestershire sauce
  juice of 1 large lemon
1/4  c. sugar
1  medium onion, chopped fine
4  tsp. liquid smoke
3  or 4 garlic cloves, minced
1/2  tsp. ground red pepper
  enough Tabasco sauce to tickle the tongue

      Bring all ingredients to a rolling boil and cook for about 10 minutes.
