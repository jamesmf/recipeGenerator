PEANUT SCOTCH CRUNCHIES


1  lb. butterscotch Candi-Quik
1/2  c. peanut butter
2  c. miniature marshmallows
2  c. chow mein noodles
1  c. salted peanuts
1  c. Rice Krispies

      Melt Candi-Quik and peanut butter.  Combine marshmallows, Krispies, nuts and noodles in a large mixing bowl.  Pour Candi-Quik mixture over this; stir to combine.  Spread in 9-inch square on foil or waxed paper.  Cut into squares.  Yields 36 (1 1/2-inch) squares.
