MUSTARD SAUCE


1  Tbsp. finely chopped onion
2  Tbsp. margarine
1  Tbsp. flour
  dash of salt and pepper
1  c. milk
2  Tbsp. Dijon-style mustard

      In a small frying pan, melt margarine.  Cook onion over medium heat for 7 minutes and blend in flour, salt and pepper. Add milk slowly, stirring until thickened.  Add mustard and cook for 1 minute over low heat.  Serve with fish steaks.
