CABBAGE WITH SAUSAGE


1  head cabbage
2  apples
  Smoked Polish sausage, cooked and sliced
  Salt and pepper
  Garlic salt
  Black pepper
1  Tbsp. sugar

      Chop cabbage and cook about 20 minutes.  Chop apples and add. Add sausage and remaining ingredients and cook another 15 minutes.  Enjoy!
