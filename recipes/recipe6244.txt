EASY NEW ENGLAND CLAM CHOWDER


1/4  c. bacon, diced
1/2  c. diced onion
1  can cream of potato soup or 1 can cream of mushroom and 1 small can potatoes, diced
3/4  c. half and half
2  cans minced clams (undrained)
1  Tbsp. lemon juice
1/8  tsp. pepper

      Cook bacon and saut onion in bacon drippings.  Stir in half and half and remaining ingredients.  Heat to boiling and serve.
