GREEN BEANS
(Spanish Style)  

2  lb. green beans, washed and trimmed
1  medium onion, diced
1  c. chili sauce
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. allspice
  grated Parmesan cheese
4  slices bacon, cooked and crumbled

      Cook beans in small amount of boiling salted water until tender, about 15 to 20 minutes.  Fry bacon until crisp; remove from pan and saut√© onions until golden.  Add chili sauce and seasonings; simmer to combine flavor.  Pour hot sauce over hot beans and sprinkle with cheese and crumbled bacon.  Makes 6 to 8 servings.
