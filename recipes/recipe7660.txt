HAMBURGER HEAVEN


1  lb. ground beef
1  Tbsp. shortening
1  medium onion
1  tsp. salt
1/2  lb. sliced American cheese
1  c. celery, chopped
2  c. noodles (raw)
2 1/2  c. tomatoes

      Brown  meat in shortening.  Drain.  Place  in  2  quart casserole.  add onion, salt and  pepper.  Add remaining ingredients in layers.  Rinse tomato jar with  1/4  cup  water and pour over top.  Bake, covered, at 350 degrees; for 30 to 45 minutes or until done.
