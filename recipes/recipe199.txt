ORANGE SLICE COOKIES


1  c. shortening
1  c. brown sugar
1  c. white sugar
2  eggs
2  tsp. vanilla
1  c. coconut
2  c. plain flour
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. soda
1/2  tsp. baking powder
1  c. oatmeal
1  c. orange slice candy, chopped

      Cream shortening and sugars.  Add eggs and vanilla; add coconut.  Mix and add flour, salt, soda and baking powder.  Add oatmeal and candy pieces.  Drop by teaspoonfuls onto greased baking sheet.  Bake at 375 degrees; for 10 minutes.  Makes about 5 dozen.
