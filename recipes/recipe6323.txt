NUTTY PINK SALAD


1  can Eagle Brand Milk
1 small  can crushed pineapple, drained
1  can cherry pie filling
1  medium container whipped topping
1  cup chopped pecans

      Stir together first three ingredients, then fold in cool whip and pecans.  Chill until set.  Cut into squares and serve on lettuce leaf.  (Will keep for two weeks in freezer.)
