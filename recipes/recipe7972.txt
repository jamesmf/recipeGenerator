TACO PIE


1  lb. ground beef
1  large onion, chopped
1/2  cup salsa
2  Tbsp. taco seasoning
1/4  tsp. pepper
1  cup shredded cheddar cheese
2  eggs
1  cup milk
1/2  cup Bisquick baking mix

      In a large skillet, cook beef and onions over medium heat until meat is no longer pink; drain.  Stir in salsa, taco seasoning and pepper.  Transfer to a greased 9 inch pie plate. Sprinkle with cheese.
      In a bowl, combine eggs, milk and biscuit mix just until blended; pour over cheese.  bake at 400 degrees for 25-30 minutes or until knife inserted near center comes out clean.
