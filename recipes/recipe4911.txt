BROCCOLI, BACON AND MUSHROOM SALAD


3  c. broccoli florets
1/2  lb. bacon
1  c. sliced fresh mushrooms
1  c. sour cream
1  tsp. lemon pepper
1/2  tsp. salt
1  clove garlic, minced
1/2  tsp. Worcestershire sauce

      Steam broccoli until tender but still crunchy; cool.  Cook bacon until crisp.  Cool on paper towels and then crumble. Combine broccoli, bacon, and mushrooms in a large bowl.  In a small bowl combine remaining ingredients to make dressing.  Pour over the broccoli mixture.  Toss to combine. Chill for at least 30 minutes before serving.
