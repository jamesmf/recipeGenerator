TOMATO SALSA


1  large lime
2  lb. ripe tomatoes (about 4 large), diced
1/2  small red onion, finely diced
1  small jalapeno chile, seeded and finely chopped
2  Tbsp. chopped fresh cilantro
3/4  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. black pepper

      From lime, grate 1/2 tsp. peel and squeeze 2 tablespoons juice.  In medium bowl, combine lime peel and juice, tomatoes, onion, chile, cilantro, salt, and pepper; stir gently.  Cover and refrigerate at least 1 hour to blend flavors.  May be refrigerated up to 2 days. Makes about 3 cups.
