FROZEN FRUIT SALAD


1 small  can orange juice
2  cans water
1/2  c. sugar, dissolved in the water
1 medium  can crushed pineapple (undrained)
6  bananas, cut up
2  pkg. frozen strawberries
1 small  can apricot nectar

      Freeze for 24 hours. Take out of freezer 2 hours before serving. Serve mushy. Makes 3 quarts.
