PAY DAY CANDY BARS


1  c. sugar
1  c. white syrup
1/2  stick butter
1  c. peanut butter
1  tsp. vanilla
4  c. corn flakes
2  c. Rice Krispies
1  c. salted nuts

      Boil sugar, syrup and butter for 1 minute.  Remove from heat. Add peanut butter and vanilla.  Stir until blended.  Add nuts, Rice Krispies and corn flakes.  Spread on cookie sheet and cool.  Cut into squares.
