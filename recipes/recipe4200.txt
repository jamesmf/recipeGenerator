CUCUMBER SALMON SPREAD


1  c. drained canned salmon
1/2  c. finely chopped cucumber
2  Tbsp. chopped green onion
2  Tbsp. lemon juice
1/8  tsp. cayenne pepper
1  Tbsp. low-fat mayonnaise
1  Tbsp. plain nonfat yogurt
  rye bread

      Mix salmon, cucumber, green onion, lemon juice and cayenne. Stir in mayonnaise and yogurt, mixing until well blended. Serve sandwich or cocktail-style on rye bread. Cover and refrigerate remaining spread.
       Serving Size: 1/4 cup. Cal-255, CHO-32, Pro-13g, Fat-8g.
