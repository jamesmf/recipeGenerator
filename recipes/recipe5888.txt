MOM'S POTATO SALAD


6  or 7 good size potatoes
1  lb. bacon
1  bunch sliced green onions
  mayonnaise
  celery seed to taste
  salt to taste
  pepper to taste

      Boil potatoes in their skins. When done, peel skin off and cut potatoes into cubes. Fry bacon until crisp. Crumble onto potatoes. Add green onions, celery seed, salt and pepper. Add mayonnaise. Stir until mixed together. Potatoes will still be chunky.
