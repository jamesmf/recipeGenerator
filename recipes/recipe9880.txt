MIXED VEGETABLE CASSEROLE


1  can mixed vegetables
1/2  c. American cheese, grated
1/2  c. onion, chopped
4  Tbsp. mayonnaise
1/4  stick margarine, melted
  crackers, crushed (for topping)

      Drain mixed vegetables. Mix all ingredients except for crackers. Pour into a baking dish and top with crushed crackers. Bake at 350 degrees; until brown on top.
