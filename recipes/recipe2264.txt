GRILLED CHICKEN BREASTS


8  to 10 chicken breasts (boneless)

 
Marinade:
1/2  c. vegetable oil
  juice of 2 lemons
2  Tbsp. soy sauce
1  Tbsp. Dijon mustard
1  tsp. garlic salt
1  tsp. oregano
1/2  tsp. black pepper

      Mix marinade ingredients well by shaking in jar or blending with whisk.  Pour over chicken breasts and refrigerate 1 to 2 hours.  Grill breasts over hot coals for approximately 20 minutes or until done.  Turn once or twice during grilling time and baste with remaining marinade.  May also be broiled in oven.
