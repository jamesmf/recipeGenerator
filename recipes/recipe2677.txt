BLACK-EYED PEA SALAD


2 1/2  c. cooked black-eyed peas, drained
3/4  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
1/4  tsp. hot sauce
1  Tbsp. lemon juice
1/4  c. salad oil
2  Tbsp. mayonnaise
2  Tbsp. pimento
4  slices bacon, fried and crumbled
1 1/2  tsp. liquid smoke
2  tsp. prepared mustard
1  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/2  c. onions, chopped
2  hard-boiled eggs, chopped

      Mix all ingredients.  May use cooked green beans, red beans, limas.
