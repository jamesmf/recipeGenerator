CHICKEN BREAST CASSEROLE


4  whole chicken breasts, split and skinned
3  Tbsp. minced parsley
1/4  tsp. black pepper
1/4  tsp. red pepper
2  eggs, beaten
2/3  c. Italian bread crumbs
1/2  c. margarine
2  cans golden mushroom soup, undiluted
8  oz. sour cream
1/3  c. dry sherry
1 (4 oz.)  can mushrooms, drained

      Sprinkle chicken with parsley and pepper.  Dip in eggs; coat well with bread crumbs.  Melt margarine in skillet; brown chicken on all sides.  Remove chicken to a large baking dish.  Reserve drippings in skillet.  Add remaining ingredients to drippings and stir well.  Pour sauce over chicken.  Bake uncovered at 350 degrees; for 40 minutes.
