TACO SALAD


1 1/2  lb. hamburger
  chopped onion
16  oz. can refried beans
24  oz. chunky salsa
8  oz. sour cream
  cheddar cheese, grated
  garnishes (onion, tomato, banana pepper, lettuce)
  tortilla chips

      Brown hamburger and onion; drain grease.  Spread refried beans in bottom of casserole dish.  Mix salsa with hamburger; pour over refried beans.  Cover with Saran wrap and microwave 4 minutes.  Add cheese; cover and microwave 2 minutes or until cheese is melted.  Spread sour cream on top.  Add garnishes of your choice and serve with tortilla chips and lettuce.
