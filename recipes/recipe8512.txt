CHICKEN CONFETTI


4 or 5  lb. broiler fryer chicken, cut up
1  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper
1/4  c. salad oil
1/2  c. chopped onion
1  clove garlic, minced
2  cans tomatoes (16 oz.) each
1  can tomato sauce (8 oz.)
1  can tomato paste (6 oz.)
2  tbsp. parsley
2  tsp. salt
1  tsp. basil
1/4  tsp. pepper
7 or 8  oz. spaghetti, cooked and drained
  grated parmesan cheese

      Wash chicken, pat dry. Season with 1 tsp. salt, 1/8 tsp. pepper.
      In a large skillet or Dutch oven, brown chicken.  Pour off all but 3 tbsp. fat.  Add onion and garlic, cook and stir until onion is tender.  Stir in chicken and remaining ingredients except spaghetti and cheese.  Stir in spaghetti and cheese.
       Cover tightly; cook chicken slowly 1 or 1 1/2 hours, or until tender, stirring occasionally and adding water if necessary. Skim off excess fat. Serve on spaghetti; sprinkle with Parmesan cheese. 4 to 6 servings.
