BARBECUES


2  lb. hamburger
1/2  onion, chopped
1/2  tsp. vinegar
1/2  c. ketchup
1  tsp. chili powder
1  tsp. prepared mustard
1  can tomato soup
1  Tbsp. sugar
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
  hamburger buns

      Brown hamburger and onion together. Add salt and pepper to taste. Add remaining ingredients and simmer for 15 minutes.
