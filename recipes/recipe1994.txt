SOPAIPILLA


4  c. flour
1 1/2  tsp. salt
1  tsp. baking powder
1  Tbsp. sugar
1  Tbsp. shortening
1  pkg. cake yeast
1/4  c. warm water
1 1/4  c. scalded milk

      Mix dry ingredients.  Mix yeast in warm water.  Melt shortening in scalded milk.  Combine all ingredients; knead until smooth and glossy.  Let rise 45 minutes.  Knead and roll dough 1/4-inch thick. Cut into 3-inch squares.  Drop into very hot 450 degrees; oil.  Remove after each square has puffed up and lightly browned.  Cut small hole in each and fill with honey.  Serve immediately.
