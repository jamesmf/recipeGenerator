HOLIDAY AMBROSIA


6  medium oranges, peeled and sliced
1  c. pitted dates, cut in halves lengthwise
1/2  c. flaked coconut
1  c. sour cream
2  Tbsp. brown sugar
2  tsp. fresh grated orange peel

      In glass compote or serving dish, arrange alternate layers of orange slices, dates and coconut; chill.  Combine sour cream, sugar and 1 teaspoon orange peel; chill.  Serve over fruit, sprinkled with remaining orange peel.  Makes 6 to 8 servings.
      Note:  It is easiest to grate peel from whole orange before peeling and slicing.
