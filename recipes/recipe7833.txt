TEXAS HASH


1  lb. ground beef
3  large onions, sliced
1  large green pepper, chopped
1 (16 oz.)  can tomatoes
1/2  c. uncooked rice
2  tsp. salt
1 to 2  tsp. chili powder
1/8  tsp. pepper

      Set oven at 350 degrees;.  In large skillet, cook and stir meat, onions and green pepper until meat is brown and vegetables are tender.  Drain off fat.  Stir in tomatoes, rice, salt, chili powder and pepper.  Heat thoroughly.  Pour into ungreased 2-quart casserole.  Cover, bake 1 hour.  Yield:  4 to 6 servings.
