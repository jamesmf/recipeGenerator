INDEPENDENCE DAY SALAD


2  c. cooked, chopped ham
4  c. cooked, cubed potatoes
2  c. diced celery
1  c. torn lettuce
1/4  c. finely diced pickles
1/4  c. minced green pepper
4  hard-cooked eggs, sliced
1  tsp. salt
  dash of pepper
1  tsp. mustard
1/4  c. sour cream
1  c. mayonnaise
  watercress
  ripe olives

      Combine ham, potatoes, celery, lettuce, pickles, green pepper and 2 eggs.  Mix seasonings, sour cream and mayonnaise.  Blend into ham mixture.  Pile onto bed of lettuce.  Garnish with sprigs of watercress, remaining eggs and ripe olives, if desired.  Yields 6 to 10 servings.
