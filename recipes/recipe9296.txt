CHICKETTI


2 1/2  c. spaghetti
3  c. chicken, chopped (cooked)
28  oz. (2 cans) chicken broth
1/2  c. bell pepper, chopped
1  small onion, chopped
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper
3  c. Cheddar cheese, shredded

      Cook spaghetti according to package directions.  Drain and set aside.
      Debone and chop chicken.  Mix together all ingredients, reserving 1/2 cup cheese for top.  Pour into a 9x13-inch casserole dish.  Top with remaining cheese.
      Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes or until bubbly.
