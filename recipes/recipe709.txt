LASAGNA FLORENTINE


3/4  c. chopped onion
2  cloves garlic, finely chopped
2  Tbsp. olive oil
2 (26 oz.)  jars Classico pasta sauce (any flavor)
1  (15 or 16 oz.) container Ricotta cheese
1 (10 oz.)  pkg. frozen chopped spinach, thawed and well drained
1  lb. Mozzarella cheese, shredded
1/2  c. grated Parmesan cheese
2  eggs
1 (1 lb.)  pkg. Creamettes lasagna, cooked and drained
  parsley (optional)

      In large pan, cook onion and garlic in oil.  Add pasta sauce; simmer 15 minutes.  In bowl, mix Ricotta, spinach, 1 cup Mozzarella, Parmesan cheese and eggs.  In 15 x 19-inch baking dish, layer 2 cups sauce, half the lasagna, half the remaining sauce, all of the spinach mixture, half the Mozzarella, remaining lasagna and sauce.  Cover; bake at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes or until hot.  Uncover and top with remaining Mozzarella.  Bake for 15 minutes.  Let stand 15 minutes; top with parsley, if desired.  Refrigerate leftovers.
