HOT DISH SUPREME


1  pkg. macaroni (uncooked)
2  cans cream of mushroom soup (small)
2  c. milk
1/2  c. grated cheese
4  hard-boiled eggs, diced
2  cans tuna or chicken
  salt and pepper
1  can mushrooms
1  medium onion, diced
1/2  c. green peppers, cut fine
1  c. celery, diced

      Mix together and let stand in refrigerator overnight. Bake 2 hours at 350 degrees;.
