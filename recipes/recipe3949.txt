SPANKIES


  1---16 oz roll of sausage
  1---8 oz package velveeta cheese
1/2  tsp. red pepper
1  tsp. oregano
1  package small round or square rye bread

      Brown sausage in skillet, do not drain. Add spices. Lay slice cheese on top of mixture. After cheese melts mix thoroughly. Spread meat mixture on each slice of bread and put on an ungreased cookie sheet. Bake in a 400 degree oven until mixture starts to bubble. Remove from oven and enjoy!!!
