OVEN FRIED CHICKEN


1/4  c. wheat germ
1/4  c. mashed potato flakes
1/4  c. buttermilk
1/2  tsp. paprika
1/2  tsp. garlic salt
  dash of pepper
1  broiler, cut up and skinned

      Sprinkle chicken with pepper and salt.  Let stand for 30 minutes.  Mix other dry ingredients and put in plastic bag.  Dip chicken in buttermilk.  Place a few pieces at a time in dry mixture and shake.  Place chicken in a baking dish which has been sprayed with a nonstick preparation.  Bake at 350 degrees; for about 35 to 40 minutes or until chicken is brown and tender.
