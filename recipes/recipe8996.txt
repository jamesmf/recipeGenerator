COMPANY MEAT BALLS


1  lb. ground beef
2 to 3  slices bread
2/3  c. milk
1  egg
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
1/4  c. catsup
1  onion, chopped
2  grated carrots

      Soak bread in milk. Mix all the ingredients. Shape into balls and fry or bake until brown. Place in baking dish and cover with 1 can cream of mushroom soup and 3/4 can water. Bake at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes. Serve with rice.
