CHILI


6  cans pinto beans (1 with jalapenos)
2  cans diced tomatoes
2  cans tomato sauce
1 to 2  lb. hamburger meat
1  diced onion
  salt, pepper, chili powder, cayenne pepper and cumin

      Brown meat with onion. Add everything else in a large stockpot. Season to taste. Let simmer over low heat for 2 to 3 hours. Best if cooked the day before.
