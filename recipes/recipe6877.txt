CHICKEN ENCHILADAS


2  c. chopped cooked chicken
1  c. chopped green bell pepper
1  c. chopped onion
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese, cubed
1 (8 oz.)  jar salsa
8  (6-inch) flour tortillas
3/4  lb. (12 oz.) Velveeta pasteurized process cheese spread, cubed
1/4  c. milk

	Stir chicken, bell pepper, onion, cream cheese and 1/2 cup salsa in saucepan on low heat until cream cheese is melted. Stir process cheese spread and milk in saucepan on low heat until smooth. Spoon 1/3 cup chicken mixture down center of each tortilla and roll up. Place seam side down in a lightly greased 12 x 8-inch baking dish. Pour cheese sauce over tortillas; cover with foil. Bake at 350 degrees; for 20 minutes. Pour remaining salsa over tortillas.
