SPAGHETTI WITH SHRIMP SAUCE


1/2  c. oil
2  c. shrimp cooked or canned
1  clove garlic, minced
1/2  c. minced parsley
2 (8  oz. cans tomato sauce
1  can water
1/2  tsp. salt
1  pkg. (8 oz.) spaghetti, cooked
  pinch of sweet basil
1  onion, chopped
1/2  tsp. sugar

      Cook onion, garlic and parsley lightly in oil. Stir in tomato sauce, water and seasonings. Simmer 30 minutes. Add shrimp. Taste and add a little salt if needed. Simmer gently 10 minutes longer. Serve over hot spaghetti with parmesan cheese.
