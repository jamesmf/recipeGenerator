BARBECUE MEATBALLS


1  lb. beef
  c. bread crumbs
1/2  c. milk
1  Tbsp. salt
1  Tbsp. pepper
1/2  c. chopped onion
1/2  c. chopped green pepper
1  c. ketchup
1/2  c. water
1/2  Tbsp. sugar
3  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce

      Mix together. Pour over meatballs. Bake at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes.
