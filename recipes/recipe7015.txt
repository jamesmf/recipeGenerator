NO PEEK STEW


2  lb. beef stew meat
1  c. celery
1  c. carrots
2  Tbsp. tapioca
1  c. potatoes
1  c. onions
2  cans Snap-E-Tom (tomato juice cocktail)
1  can V-8 juice
  salt and pepper to taste

      Mix meat, chopped vegetables, and seasonings in a pot. Combine tomato juice cocktail and V-8 juice; dissolve tapioca in juices. Pour over meat and vegetable mixture. Cover and bake 5 hours in 250 degrees;oven. Do not peek! Serve with cornbread and green salad.
