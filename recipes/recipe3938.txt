OLD GREEN TOMATO KETCHUP

Step 1:
1  gallon green tomatoes, ground
1/2  cup salt

      Mix and let stand for 2 hours.  Place in bag and drain overnight.
Step 2:
1  small head cabbage
3  onions
2  quarts chopped apples
2  small red hot peppers

      Grind all together, and add to the drained tomatoes.
Step 3:
3  pints vinegar
1  Tbsp. allspice
1  tsp. celery seed
2  lbs. brown sugar
1  tsp. ground cloves
1  tsp. turmeric

      Bring to boil. Add ground mixture, mix and bring to a boil again. Seal in pint jars while hot.
