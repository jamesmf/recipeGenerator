ANGEL HASH SALAD

Sauce:
2  tsp. flour
1  c. pineapple juice
1/2  c. sugar
2  egg yolks
1  c. whipped cream

      Cook together until thick, stirring constantly. Cool. Add whipped cream. Pour over fruit mixture.
Salad:
2  c. apples, sliced
5  bananas, sliced
1 large  can pineapple chunks, drained
1/4  c. nuts
  cherries (to garnish)

      Marshmallows and grapes may be added if desired.
