EASY CHICKEN POT PIE


10  oz. (1 large can) chicken, deboned
15  oz. (1 can) Veg-All (drained)
10  oz. (1 can) soup, cream of chicken
10  oz. (1 can) soup, cream of mushroom
1/2  c. (1 stick) butter
8  (1 can) crescent rolls

      Mix together:  chicken, Veg-all and soups.  Pour into a buttered 9x13-inch casserole dish.  Dot top with butter.  Put separated crescent rolls on top like a crust.
      Bake uncovered at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes or until rolls are brown.
