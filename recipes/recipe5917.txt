SWEET 'N SOUR SLAW


1  c. white vinegar
2  tsp. celery seed
1  green pepper, finely chopped
1  red onion, chopped
1  head cabbage, chopped
1  tsp. salt
1/2  c. vegetable oil
1  c. plus 2 Tbsp. sugar

      In small saucepan, mix together vinegar, oil, celery seed and 2 tablespoons sugar. Bring to a boil and stir until sugar dissolves. Cool. Mix green pepper, onion and cabbage. Pour 1 cup sugar and salt over and mix. Pour cooled dressing over vegetables. Chill for several hours.
