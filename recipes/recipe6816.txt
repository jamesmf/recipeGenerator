ITALIAN SKILLET CHICKEN


4  boneless, skinless chicken breasts
6  Tbsp. Parmesan cheese
3  Tbsp. flour
2  Tbsp. olive oil
1/2  onion, finely chopped
1 (14 1/2 oz.)  can Del Monte Italian-style stewed tomatoes

      Mix flour and cheese together in plastic bag. Take chicken and put in bag; coat chicken with mixer. Put olive oil in frypan and brown the chicken and then add onions and can of tomatoes; simmer for about 20 minutes. Can serve with pasta. While simmering, make pasta.
