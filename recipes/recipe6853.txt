HASH BROWN BREAKFAST CASSEROLE


8  eggs
3/4  c. milk
1/2  stick margarine
8  squares frozen hash browns
1 1/2  c. ham
1 1/2  c. cheese

      Beat eggs and add milk. Melt butter in pan, 9 x 13. Arrange hash browns in pan. Put ham and cheese over hash browns. Pour egg mixture over all and bake at 325 degrees; for 45 to 50 minutes or until eggs are set.
