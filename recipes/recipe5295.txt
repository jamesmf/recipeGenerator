TARRAGON SHALLOT VINAIGRETTE


4  tsp. Sherry vinegar
1  Tbsp. minced shallot
1/2  tsp. Dijon mustard
1/2  tsp. salt, or less
1/4  tsp. black pepper
1/4  c. extra-virgin olive oil
2  tsp. finely chopped fresh tarragon

	Whisk together vinegar, shallot, mustard, salt and pepper. Add oil in slow stream, whisking until emulsified.	Whisk in tarragon.  Delicious on a salad with thinly sliced fennel, red onion, and shaved parmesan. Enjoy!
