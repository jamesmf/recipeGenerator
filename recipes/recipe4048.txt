OLVITA CROSTINI


1 4.2  oz. can black olives, finely chopped
1/2  c. pimento stuffed green olives, chopped
1/2  c. grated parmesan cheese
4  Tbsp. butter, softened
1  Tbsp. olive oil
2  garlic cloves, minced
3/4  c. monterrey jack cheese
1/4  c. parsley, fresh, minced
1  crusty French baguette cut into 24 thin slices

      Mix all ingredients.  Arrange bread slices on baking sheet. Spread olive mixture over each.  Broil 3 to 4 minutes until toasted and bubbly.
