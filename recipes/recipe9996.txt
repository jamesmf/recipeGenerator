ITALIAN MARINATED TOMATOES


20  large tomatoes
1  c. vegetable oil
1/2  c. red wine vinegar
2  tsp. salt
1  tsp. basil
2  tsp. oregano
1/2  tsp. pepper
1  clove garlic

      Place oil and all ingredients, except tomatoes, in shaker and shake until well mixed. Pour dressing over tomatoes; toss gently to mix well. Cover and refrigerate 2 hours.
