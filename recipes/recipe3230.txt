MEXICAN HATS


1  lb ground beef, cooked and drained
1  red pepper, chopped
1  green pepper, chopped
1  medium vidalia onion, chopped
2  c cheese, shredded
1  c ranch dressing
1  pkg won-ton wrappers

      Place wontons in a muffin pan.  Bake 350 degrees; for 5 minutes. They will form crispy star-like cups.  Mix together all ingredients in a bowl.  Mixture should be moist but not soupy. Scoop mixture  into each cup using a heaping teaspoon.  Sprinkle top of mixture with a little shredded cheese.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 10 minutes and serve warm.
