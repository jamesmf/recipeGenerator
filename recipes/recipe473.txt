MICROWAVE CINNAMON TWISTS


1 (7.5 oz.)  pkg. buttermilk biscuits
1/4  c. melted butter
1/3  c. sugar
1 1/2  tsp. cinnamon

      Roll each biscuit into a 9-inch rope.  Melt butter for 45 seconds.  Combine sugar and cinnamon.  Dip each rope into cinnamon-sugar mixture and twist.  Place in a circle on a glass plate.  Microwave on High 2 to 2 1/2 minutes.
      Kids love to make these!
