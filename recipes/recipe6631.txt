STUFFED PEPPERS


4  large green peppers
1  Tbsp. butter or margarine
1  small onion
1  clove garlic, pressed
1  lb. ground beef (or chuck, round or sirloin)
1  c. cooked rice
1  tsp. salt
1 (8 oz.)  can tomato sauce
1  tsp. basil
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
3/4  c. grated Cheddar cheese

      Butter baking dish.  Cut green peppers in half lengthwise. Remove the stems, membranes, and seeds.  In a saucepan, boil the peppers in water to cover for 5 minutes.  Drain.  Place in the prepared baking dish.
      In a skillet, melt the butter and saut√© the onion and garlic until tender.  Add the ground beef and brown lightly.  Add the rice, salt, tomato sauce, basil, and Worcestershire sauce and 1/2 c. cheese.  Mix well.  Fill the peppers with the mixture.  Bake in a 350 degrees; oven for 30 minutes.  Top with the remaining cheese and bake for 10 more minutes or until the cheese is browned.  Serves 4.
