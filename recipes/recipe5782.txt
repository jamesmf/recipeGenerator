MOM'S SALAD


10  oz. romaine lettuce
10  slices bacon, cooked
1  bunch green onions, chopped
4  oz. Blue cheese, crumbled
1 small  pkg. pine nuts
1  c. oil
1/2  c. balsamic vinegar
1  envelope/pkg. dry pesto
  sugar to taste

      Mix oil, balsamic vinegar, pesto and sugar together to make the dressing.
       Place pine nuts in shallow pan and bake at 350 degrees; for 10 to 15 minutes or until lightly roasted.
       When ready to serve, combine lettuce, onions, bacon  and cheese. Pour dressing over the salad and mix.
