CHICKEN, CHEESE ENCHILADAS


1  medium onion, chopped
1 1/2  c. cooked shredded chicken
1 small  jar salsa
1  tsp. cumin
6  flour tortilla shells
2  Tbsp. butter
3  oz. cream cheese
2  c. grated cheese
1 small  can enchilada sauce

      Heat oven to 350 degrees;. Cook onion in butter until tender. Stir in chicken, salsa, cream cheese and cumin. Heat thoroughly. Add 1 cup grated cheese and stir until melted. Spoon mixture in shells and wrap. Lay in 9 x 13 pan. Pour enchilada sauce over enchiladas, then sprinkle grated cheese over this. Bake in oven 15 minutes.
