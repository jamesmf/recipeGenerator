BAKED MACARONI AND CHEESE


1 1/2  c. elbow macaroni
2  Tbsp. butter
2  Tbsp. all-purpose flour
1/4  c. milk
1  c. light cream
1/4  tsp. paprika
1/2  tsp. white pepper
2 1/2  c. grated sharp cheese
  salt

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees;.  Boil macaroni according to package directions; drain and set aside.  Meanwhile, melt butter in a medium saucepan over low heat.  Blend in flour, stirring constantly.  Gradually stir in milk and cream.  Cook, stirring constantly, until mixture boils and thickens, about 2 to 3 minutes.  Remove from heat and add paprika, pepper and 2 cups of the cheese; stir until cheese is melted.  Gently stir in macaroni and salt to taste.  Place in a buttered 1-quart casserole dish. Garnish top with remaining 1/2 cup of cheese and dot with additional butter.  Bake 25 to 30 minutes.
