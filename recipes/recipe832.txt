GOLDEN TURKEY LASAGNA


1/2  c. chopped onion
1/2  c. chopped green pepper
3  Tbsp. oleo
4  oz. can mushrooms
1/4  c. pimento (optional)
10  oz. can cream of mushroom soup
1/3  c. milk
1/2  tsp. basil
8  oz. lasagna noodles, cooked
1 1/2  c. small curd cottage cheese
3  c. cooked turkey
2  c. shredded Cheddar cheese

      Saut√© onion and green pepper in melted oleo.  Stir in mushrooms, pimento, soup, milk and basil.  Heat.  Arrange 1/2 of noodles in a greased 13 x 9 x 2-inch dish.  Layer 1/2 sauce, cottage cheese, turkey and Cheddar cheese.  Repeat layers.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes.
