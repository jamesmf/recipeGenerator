PICANTE SALSA SAUCE


1 (28 oz.)  can whole tomatoes
1  can Ro-Tel tomatoes
1/2  small onion
1/2  tsp. garlic powder
1  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper
1  to 1 1/2 pickled, canned jalapeno peppers

      Drain both cans of tomatoes.  Put Ro-Tel, onion, jalapeno, salt, pepper and garlic powder into blender.  Blend until onion is chopped.  Add whole tomatoes and blend with an on-off, on-off (2 times) for chunky salsa.  Pour into covered jar.  Chill well before using.  Makes about 3 cups.
