CHICKEN CURRY


1  Tbsp. butter
1  c. finely chopped pared apple
1  c. sliced celery
1/2  c. finely chopped onion
1  clove garlic, minced
2  Tbsp. cornstarch
2 to 3  tsp. curry powder
3/4  tsp. salt
3/4  c. cold chicken broth
2  c. milk
2  c. diced cooked chicken
1  can (3-oz.) sliced mushrooms, drained
  Hot cooked rice
  Curry condiments

      In saucepan, melt butter; add apple, celery, onion, and garlic.  Cook till onion is tender.  Combine cornstarch, curry, salt, and broth.  Stir into onion mixture; add milk.   Cook and stir until mixture thickens and bubbles.  Stir in chicken and mushrooms; heat through.  Serve over hot cooked rice and pass curry condiments (raisins, shredded coconut, chopped peanuts, and chutney).  Serves 6.
