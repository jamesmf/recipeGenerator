BAKED HAM


1  large cooked ham, have sliced
1  box brown sugar
1  bottle Coke or Dr. Pepper

      You also need 1 large aluminum cake pan.
      Put sugar in bottom of pan. Place ham in pan. Partially cover with foil or leave open. Pour over this 1 bottle of Coke. Baste occasionally while baking in moderate 250 degrees; oven 1 to 2 hours.
