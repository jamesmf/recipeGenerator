LEMON CUTOUT COOKIES


1/2  c. butter or margarine
1/2  c. brown sugar
1  egg
1  tsp. vanilla
1/2  tsp. lemon extract
2  c. flour
1/2  tsp. baking powder

      Beat together butter or margarine, brown sugar, egg, vanilla and lemon extract; set aside.  Mix flour and baking powder, then blend all ingredients together.  Refrigerate dough for 1 hour. Roll dough to 1/4-inch thick.  Use cookie cutters for shapes.  Bake 6 minutes at 350 degrees; on ungreased cookie sheet.
