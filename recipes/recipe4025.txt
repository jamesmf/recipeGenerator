HOT LAYERED MEXICAN DIP


30  oz. (1 can) refried beans
2  c. sour cream
1 1/4  oz. (1 pkg.) taco seasoning mix
16  oz. (1 jar) picante sauce
1  c. Cheddar cheese, shredded

      In a 9x13-inch baking dish, layer ingredients as follows: refried beans, sour cream, taco seasoning mix, picante sauce and Cheddar cheese.
      Bake at 350 degrees; until heated through, about 30 minutes.
      Serve with tortilla chips.
