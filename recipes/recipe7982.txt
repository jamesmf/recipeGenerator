TASTY BARBECUES
(Double For More Than 4 People)  

3  lb. hamburger
1/3  c. vinegar
1/3  c. Worcestershire sauce
  salt and pepper to taste
1 1/2  c. ketchup
1/2  c. brown sugar
1 1/2  c. chopped onions
1/2  c. Pace thick and chunky salsa
1/3  c. prepared yellow mustard

      Fry onions and meat until meat crumbles.  Stir remaining ingredients in a bowl and add to meat mixture.  Simmer until thick.
