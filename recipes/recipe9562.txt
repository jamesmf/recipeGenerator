CHICKEN LUSH


1 1/2 to 2  c. cooked chicken, cut up
1  c. mushroom soup (undiluted)
1/4 to 1/2  tsp. thyme
1/2  c. lo-cal mayonnaise
1  can sliced water chestnuts
2  hard-boiled eggs, chopped
  buttered bread crumbs
  slivered toasted almonds (optional)

      Mix first 6 ingredients together. Cover with buttered bread crumbs and slivered toasted almonds, if desired.  Bake in uncovered casserole 45 minutes in 350 degrees; oven.
