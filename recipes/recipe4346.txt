CHRISTMAS TREE SALAD


1  box lime Jello
1  can crushed pineapple
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. Philadelphia cream cheese (softened)
1  c. Cool Whip
1/2  c. chopped nuts

      Dissolve Jello in 1 cup boiling water.  Let cool.  Add Philadelphia cream cheese, pineapple and Cool Whip.  Mix with beater.  Add nuts.  For tree effect pour salad mixture in coffee cup liners.  Gel.  Dip in hot water to loosen.  Turn upside down on leaf of lettuce.  Top with Cool Whip.  Place a Maraschino cherry on top of tree for effect, or pour into small square pan.
