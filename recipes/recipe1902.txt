TACOS SEALED WITH REFRIED BEANS


  lettuce wedge (1-inch thick)
1  large tomato, diced
1  c. shredded cheese
1  lb. ground beef
3  Tbsp. chopped onion
1 (15 oz.)  can tomato sauce
1  tsp. garlic salt
1  tsp. chili powder
  dash of pepper
10  taco shells
  bottled taco sauce
1  can refried beans

      Brown ground beef; add onion, tomato sauce, garlic salt, chili powder and dash of pepper.  Simmer, uncovered, for 15 minutes. Heat beans and taco shells.  Fill shells with beef, cheese, lettuce, tomatoes and seal with beans.
