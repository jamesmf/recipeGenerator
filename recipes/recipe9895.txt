TEXAS CHILE HOMINY


2  cans golden hominy, drained
3  Tbsp. onion, chopped
1 1/2  c. sour cream
1 1/2  c. Monterey Jack cheese, shredded
4  oz. can chopped green chiles, drained*
1/2  c. seasoned bread crumbs

      Mix first 5 ingredients and pour in baking dish. Sprinkle bread crumbs on top. Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 to 45 minutes.
