BLACK-EYED PEA SALAD


1  can black-eyed peas
1  can Shoepeg corn
1 small  jar pimento
1/2  chopped onion
1/2  chopped green pepper

 
Marinade:
3/4  c. sugar
1/2  c. vinegar
1/2  c. olive oil
  salt and pepper to taste

      Drain black-eyed peas, corn and pimento. Combine with onion and green pepper. Marinate 2 to 4 hours, then drain and serve. Serves 6.
      Cal-316, Carbs-39, Pro-1, Fat-18.
