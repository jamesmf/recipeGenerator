CLASSIC GREEN BEAN CASSEROLE


1 (10.75 oz.)  can cream of mushroom soup
3/4  c. milk
1/8  tsp. pepper
2 (14.5 oz.)  cans French-style or cut green beans, drained
1 (2.8 oz.)  can French fried onions

      Preheat oven to 400 degrees;.  Combine soup, milk and pepper in an 8 x 8-inch baking dish.  Mix well.  Stir in beans and half of the onions.  Bake 10 minutes; stir.  Sprinkle casserole with remaining onions.  Bake 5 minutes or until hot and onions are golden.
