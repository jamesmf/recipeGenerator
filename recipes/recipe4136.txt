OVERNIGHT PICKLES


6  c. sliced cucumbers with skin
2  c. sugar
1  c. cider vinegar
1  onion, sliced thin (optional 1 to 2)
2  Tbsp. salt
1  Tbsp. celery seed

      Heat sugar, vinegar, salt and seed until sugar is melted. Pour over cucumbers and onion in a large bowl.  Cover and put in refrigerator overnight.  Put in jars next day.  These will store well for months if kept in the refrigerator.
