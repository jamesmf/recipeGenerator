PEAR RELISH


1  gal. grated pears
8  sweet bell peppers, chopped
2 1/2  tsp. salt
4  tsp. allspice
2  qt. apple cider vinegar
8  c. grated onions
2  hot peppers, chopped
2  Tbsp. dry mustard
5  c. sugar

      Place pears, onions, bell pepper, hot pepper, salt, mustard, allspice, sugar and vinegar in a pot and boil for 1 hour on low temperature.
