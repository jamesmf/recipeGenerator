MANGO BREAD


2  c. flour
1/2  tsp. salt
1 1/2  c. sugar
3  eggs
2  tsp. baking soda
3/4  c. oil
2  tsp. cinnamon
2  c. chopped mango
1/2  c. chopped nuts

      Mix all ingredients.  Pour into 4 x 9-inch bread pans.  Let stand 20 minutes before baking.  Bake for 1 hour at 250 degrees;.  Makes 2 loaves.
      This recipe is an interesting variation on another traditional Bahamian recipe, banana bread.
