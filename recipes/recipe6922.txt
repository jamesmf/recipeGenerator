FARMER'S PORK CHOPS


4  pork chops
1  clove garlic, chopped
4  potatoes, sliced
2  large onions, sliced
1/4  tsp. pepper
1 1/2  c. sour cream
2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. dry mustard
1/2  c. flour

      Trim fat from chops and roll in flour. Brown chops in garlic in hot fat over medium heat. Season with salt and pepper. Place sliced potatoes in baking pan; top with pork chops. Separate onion slices into rings and lay over chops. Blend in sour cream and mustard. Pour over top. Bake 1 1/2 hours at 350 degrees;. Makes 4 servings.
