HOT DISH


1  lb. ground beef
1/2  lb. ground pork
  pepper
1/2  pkg. rotelle pasta
1  can Cheddar cheese soup
1  c. Mozzarella cheese
1  jar Ragu Robusto (sausage and cheese)
1 small  can tomato sauce
1  tsp. sugar

      Brown beef and pork seasoned with pepper.  Add remaining ingredients.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 to 45 minutes.  15 minutes before done, sprinkle top with sharp Cheddar cheese.
