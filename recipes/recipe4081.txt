LEXINGTON STYLE DIP


3  c. apple cider vinegar
2/3  c. brown sugar
1/2  c. catsup
2  Tbsp. Texas Pete
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. pepper
1  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  tsp. onion powder
2  tsp. Kitchen Bouquet browning sauce

	Combine all ingredients in a large pot. Bring to a boil, then turn down to low heat and simmer until sugar melts. Let sit for several hours before serving over barbecue.
