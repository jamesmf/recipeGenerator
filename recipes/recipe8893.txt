TACO CASSEROLE


8  oz. rotini (about 3 c. dry)
1/2  lb. lean ground beef
1/2  c. chopped onion
  chopped jalapeno (if desired)
2  cans pinto beans, drained and rinsed
1 large  jar chunky garden style spaghetti sauce
1 small  can green chiles
1  pkg. taco seasoning
1/2  c. water
2/3  c. shredded Cheddar cheese

      Heat oven to 400 degrees; and spray a 9 x 13 pan with Pam. Cook rotini as directed on package and drain.
      Meanwhile, in large nonstick skillet, brown ground beef and onion; drain, if necessary. Stir in beans, spaghetti sauce, chiles, seasoning mix and water. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer until heated through. Combine beef mixture with the rotini and spoon into casserole dish. Top with cheese and bake about 20 minutes or until hot and bubbly and cheese is melted. Serves 8.
