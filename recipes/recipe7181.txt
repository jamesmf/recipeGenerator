STANDING RIB ROAST



6  lb. rib roast
  garlic clove
  sage
  rosemary
  thyme
  oil

      Clean garlic clove; cut in half.  Spear roast with pockets, different depths.  Place 1 piece garlic in each.  Rub roast with oil.  Combine seasonings and rub to coat.  Place on rotisserie; cook 18 minutes per pound.  For medium, 160 degrees;; rare, 140 degrees; and for well, 170 degrees;.
