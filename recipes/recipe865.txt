SEAFOOD NEWBURG


6  Tbsp. butter or margarine
2  Tbsp. flour
1/8  tsp. nutmeg
  dash of paprika
1  tsp. salt
3  Tbsp. sherry
3  c. cooked shrimp, lobster, crab or scallops
2  c. light cream
3  egg yolks, slightly beaten
  bread crumbs (if desired)

      Melt butter; stir in flour, nutmeg, paprika and salt until smooth.  Stir in sherry, then seafood; cook until heated through. Combine cream and egg yolks and slowly pour into mixture; stir constantly, continuing until thick and smooth.  Serve with toast points.  Top with bread crumbs and bake until brown, if desired.
