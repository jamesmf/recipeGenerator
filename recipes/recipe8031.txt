BEEF STROGANOFF III


1 1/2  lb. fresh mushrooms
3  lb. sirloin, good cut steak, cut in strips
2  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
  salt and pepper to taste
3/4  tsp. nutmeg
1/2  c. Sherry (optional)
1  stick butter or margarine
2  Tbsp. flour
1  pt. sour cream

	Wash and cut mushrooms.  Season meat and brown in butter. Transfer to casserole.  Brown mushrooms in same pan and put them in casserole.  Put flour into remaining juices in pan and add sour cream.  Cook over medium heat for 2 to 3 minutes, stirring constantly.  Pour sauce over meat.	This can be prepared the day before.  Casserole should be heated in a very low oven (250 degrees; to 300 degrees;) for 1 hour.  Serves 8.  Serve with rice or English muffins.
