CAJUN SHRIMP SOUP


1/2  c. chopped green pepper
1/4  c. sliced green onion
1  clove garlic, minced
1  Tbsp. butter or margarine
3  c. vegetable juice cocktail
8  oz. bottle clam juice
1/2  c. water
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. thyme, crushed
1/4  tsp. basil, crushed
1/8 to 1/4  tsp. red pepper flakes, crushed
1  bay leaf
1/2  c. uncooked long grain rice
12  oz. frozen cooked shrimp
  hot pepper sauce, if desired

      In a large saucepan, saut√© green pepper, onion and garlic in margarine until tender.  Stir in vegetable juice, clam juice, water, salt, thyme, basil, pepper flakes and bay leaf.  Bring to boil.  Reduce heat; add rice.  Cover.  Simmer 15 to 20 minutes or until rice is tender.  Stir in shrimp; simmer uncovered 3 to 5 minutes or until shrimp heat.  Remove bay leaf.  Add hot pepper sauce to individual servings as desired.  Yields 4 servings.
