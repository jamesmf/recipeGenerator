STEAK ROLL-UPS


1 1/2  lb. boneless round steak
1/4  c. chopped onion
1/4  c. butter or margarine, melted
2  c. fresh bread crumbs
1/2  c. chopped celery
1  Tbsp. dried parsley flakes
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. poultry seasoning
1/4  tsp. pepper
1  c. all-purpose flour
2  Tbsp. cooking oil
1  can (10 3/4 oz.) cream of mushroom soup, undiluted
1 1/3  c. water
3/4  tsp. browning sauce, optional

      Pound steak to 1/3 inch thickness.  Cut into six pieces. Combine the onion, butter, bread crumbs, celery, parsley flakes, salt, poultry seasoning and pepper.  Place 1/3 cup on each piece of steak; roll up and fasten with a toothpick; roll in flour.  In a large skillet, brown roll-ups in oil.  Combine soup, water and browning sauce if desired; pour over roll-ups.  Cover and simmer for 2 hours or until meat is tender, turning occasionally.  Yield: 6 servings.
