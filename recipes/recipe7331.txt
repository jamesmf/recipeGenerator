LEMON PEPPER BAKED SALMON


4  (1-inch) salmon steaks
3  Tbsp. bread crumbs
1  tsp. Adams lemon pepper
1  tsp. Adams dill

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees;. Combine all ingredients. Sprinkle over top of salmon steaks. Bake at 350 degrees; for 10 to 20 minutes. Turn once and sprinkle with mixture.
