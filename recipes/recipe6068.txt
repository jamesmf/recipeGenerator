ORANGE PECAN SALAD


1 large  can mandarin oranges, drained
1 large  can chunk pineapple, drained
1  c. coconut
8  oz. cream cheese
1/4  c. sugar
  pecans, halved or chopped

      Mix sugar and cream cheese together.  Stir in oranges, pineapple and coconut.  Spread in glass dish.  Sprinkle pecans on top.  For best results, chill for 1 to 2 hours.
