EASTER EGGS WITH ONION SKINS


  dried onion skins
  hard-boiled eggs

      You will also need cheesecloth and thread.
      While water is boiling, wrap eggs with dried onion skins and cover with cheesecloth and tie with thread.  Put in boiling water. Turn heat down and cook eggs to desired way.  When done, put in cold water and remove cheesecloth and onion skins; rinse.
