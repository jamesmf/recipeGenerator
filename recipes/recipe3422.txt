ZUCCHINI APPETIZERS


3  c. thinly sliced zucchini (about 4 small)
1  c. Bisquick baking mix
1/2  c. finely chopped onions
1/2  c. parmesan cheese
2  Tbsp. parsley
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  dried marjoram or oregano leaves
  Dash of pepper
1  clove garlic, finely chopped
1/2  c. vegetable oil
4  eggs, slightly beaten

      Mix all ingredients.  Spread in 9 x 13 greased pan.  Bake until golden brown, about 40 minutes, at 350 degrees;.  Cut into pieces about 1 x 2-inch.  Makes 4 dozen.
