"CROCK-POT" BARBECUE CHICKEN


1  frying chicken, cut up
3/4  c. onion, chopped
3  Tbsp. brown sugar
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/4  tsp. thyme
1  can condensed tomato soup
1/4  c. vinegar
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. sweet basil

      Wash chicken and place in crock-pot. Combine all other ingredients and pour over chicken. Cover and cook on low for 6 to 8 hours.
