CLASSIC MACARONI SALAD


1  c. mayonnaise
2  Tbsp. vinegar
1  Tbsp. mustard
1  tsp. sugar
1  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
8  oz. elbow macaroni, cooked and drained
1  c. sliced celery
1  c. chopped green or red sweet pepper
1/4  c. chopped onion

	In a large bowl, stir together the mayonnaise, vinegar, mustard, sugar, salt and pepper until smooth. Add remaining ingredients; toss to coat well. Cover. Chill. Makes 5 cups. Great with grilled hamburgers.
