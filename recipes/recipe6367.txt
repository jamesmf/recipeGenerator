HARVEST APPLE SALAD


2  red apples
2  golden apples
2  Granny Smith apples
1  banana
1  c. low-fat peach yogurt

      Core and cube apples. Peel banana; cut lengthwise and then cut into 1/2-inch pieces. Blend apples, bananas and yogurt. Keep refrigerated until serving.
      Serving Size: 1/2 cup. Cal-58, Cho-13.7g, Protein-1.0g, Fat .50g.
