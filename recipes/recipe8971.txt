SALAMI


2  lb. hamburger
1  c. water
1/4  tsp. garlic powder
1/2  tsp. onion powder
1/4  tsp. mustard seed
1  Tbsp. liquid smoke
3 level  Tbsp. Morton Tender Quick salt
1  tsp. peppercorn (optional)

      Mix together and make a roll 12-inches long. Wrap in aluminum foil with shiny side in. Refrigerate 21 hours. Poke holes in bottom of foil (underneath side). Place on rack of broiler pan with 1/2 to 1-inch of water in bottom of pan. Water should not touch foil. Bake 1 1/2 hours at 325 degrees;.
