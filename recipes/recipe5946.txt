TUNA SALAD


2  cans tuna, drained
1/3  c. sweet relish pickles
4  hard-boiled eggs, chopped
1/2  green pepper, chopped
1/2  medium onion, chopped
3/4  c. mayonnaise
1 small  jar pimento
  salt and pepper to taste

      Mix all ingredients together and chill. Serve with crackers or on lettuce leaf.
