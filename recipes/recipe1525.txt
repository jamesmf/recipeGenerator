STRAWBERRY AND PRETZEL SALAD


2  c. pretzels, crushed (not fine)
3/4  c. butter, melted
3  Tbsp. sugar
1 large  pkg. strawberry jello
2  c. boiling water
2 (10 oz.)  pkg. strawberries or 2 c. fresh strawberries
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
1  c. sugar
2  c. Cool Whip

      Mix together pretzels, butter and 3 tablespoons sugar.  Press into 13 x 10-inch pan.  Bake at 400 degrees; for 8 to 10 minutes.  Do not overbake.  Mix jello, boiling water and strawberries.  Stir until dissolved.  Place in refrigerator to cool for 30 minutes.  Mix cream cheese and sugar.  Fold in Cool Whip.  Spread over pretzel mixture.  Pour cooled jello on top and refrigerate.  Let set 4 to 5 hours before serving.  Top with Cool Whip.
