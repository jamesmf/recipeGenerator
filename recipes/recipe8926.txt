CHICKEN SPAGHETTI


1  chicken or 4 large chicken breasts
1  pkg. spaghetti, cooked
2  cans cream of chicken soup
1  can Ro-Tel tomatoes
1  can diced tomatoes
1  can green chilies
2  cans black olives
1  can sliced mushrooms
1  lb. Velveeta cheese, cubed
  chicken broth
  salt, pepper and garlic powder

      Boil chicken; debone and cube. In large roaster, combine all ingredients except spaghetti and chicken broth. Add spaghetti and enough broth to thin if too thick. Bake at 350 degrees; until cheese melts, approximately 45 minutes.
