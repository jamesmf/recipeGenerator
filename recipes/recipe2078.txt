BABE RUTH BALLS


1  lb. powdered sugar
2  sticks butter (soft)
1  c. peanut butter
3/4  c. graham cracker crumbs
1 1/2  c. nuts
1  c. coconut
12  oz. pkg. chocolate chips
1/2  block paraffin

      Mix sugar, butter and peanut butter.  Add graham cracker crumbs, nuts and coconut and chill.  Roll into bite-size balls, then dip into the chocolate and paraffin which were melted together.
      Candy Melts or Candiquick can be used instead of chips and paraffin.
