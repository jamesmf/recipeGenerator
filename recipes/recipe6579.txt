SOUR CREAM CHICKEN


6  chicken breasts (with skin on)
1  pt. sour cream
1/2  tsp. celery salt
1/2  tsp. Tabasco
1 1/2  c. fresh French bread crumbs
1/4  lb. butter

      Marinate chicken breasts in sour cream, celery salt and Tabasco at least 2 hours before cooking.
       Preheat oven to 350 degrees;. Place chicken, skin side up. Sprinkle bread crumbs on each breast. Melt butter and pour over each breast to moisten crumbs. Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour and 15 minutes.
