SWEET & SOUR MEATBALLS


1 (8 oz.)  jar of grape jelly
1 (12 oz.)  jar chili sauce
2  lbs. ground chuck

      Mix jelly and chili sauce in a large pan.  Roll meat into small bite size balls.  Brown in skillet.  Add meat to sauce and cook about an hour.  (Crock pot works well.)  Use toothpicks to serve for a party or serve over rice as a meal.
