SAUSAGE AND CHEESE BAGELS


1  pkg. pre-sliced plain bagels (6)
1  lb. sausage (mild or hot)
8  oz. Velveeta cheese, chunked

      In skillet, brown sausage; lower the skillet temperature and add cheese. When cheese has melted, remove from stove. Cut bagels in half and lay open in a greased baking dish. Spoon sausage and cheese mixture onto the top of the bagel. Cook in a 350 degrees; oven for 10 to 15 minutes.
      Optional: Serve with salsa.
