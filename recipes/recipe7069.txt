BAKED CHICKEN LOAF


4  lb. chicken, cooked
1  Tbsp. minced parsley
1/4  tsp. pepper
2  eggs
2  c. bread crumbs
1/2  tsp. salt
1  c. milk
2  Tbsp. butter, melted

      Remove chicken from bones. Chop finely. Mix chicken with 1 3/4 cups crumbs, parsley, salt, pepper, milk and eggs. Stir in butter. Pour in loaf pan. Sprinkle remaining crumbs on top. Bake at 325 degrees; for 45 minutes.
