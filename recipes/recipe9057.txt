PORK CHOP AND RICE CASSEROLE


6  large pork chops
1  c. raw rice
2  c. boiling water
1  can mushroom soup
  salt and pepper

      Cook rice in boiling water (1 teaspoon salt) 14 minutes. Spread in bottom of large casserole dish. Place raw pork chops on top. Salt and pepper chops. Spoon half the mushroom soup over. Add water to remaining soup to fill can. Stir well and pour over dish. Bake at 325 degrees; for 1 hour, uncovered. Serves 6.
