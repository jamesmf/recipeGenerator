OYSTER CASSEROLE


1  c. butter, melted
1/2 (16 oz.)  pkg. saltine crackers, crushed
2 (8 oz.)  cans oysters
1 1/2  Tbsp. heavy whipping cream
1  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
2 (14.75 oz.)  cans cream-style corn

      Preheat oven to 425 degrees. Grease a 9x13 inch casserole dish. Drain oysters, reserving juice. In a small mixing bowl combine butter and crushed saltine crackers. Cut the oysters into small pieces, set aside. In a small mixing bowl combine cream, oyster juice and Worcestershire sauce. Spread one can of corn onto bottom of casserole dish. Layer 1/2 of the oysters over the corn, 1/2 of the cracker mixture. Repeat layering with remaining ingredients. Pour the oyster juice mixture over the top of the entire casserole. Cover the casserole dish with aluminum foil. Bake casserole for 20 minutes. Remove foil and bake an additional 30 minutes.
