APPLE FRITTERS


1 3/4  c. flour
  pinch of salt
  tart apples, sliced
  oil for frying
1/2  can (6 oz.) beer
  cinnamon and sugar
  powdered sugar

      Make a batter using the flour, beer and salt.  Coat slices of apples in the cinnamon and sugar mixture and allow to stand 30 minutes.  Dip the apple slices in batter and fry in the hot oil until light brown in color.  Sprinkle with powdered sugar and serve while hot.
