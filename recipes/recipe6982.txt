CHICKEN SPAGHETTI


2  to 3 chicken breasts, cooked
16  oz. spaghetti
1/4  c. bell pepper, chopped fine
1  c. celery, chopped fine
1  c. onion, chopped fine
1  stick margarine
1  can tomato soup
1  can cream of mushroom soup
2  c. chicken broth
  salt and pepper to taste
1/4  tsp. chili powder
1/2  tsp. garlic powder

      Boil chicken, remove from broth. Reserve 2 cups chicken broth; chop chicken into small pieces and set aside. Cook and drain spaghetti, set aside. In a skillet, melt margarine and saut√© bell pepper, celery, and onion; add broth; simmer about 15 minutes. Add soups and simmer until well mixed; add chicken and spices; simmer for 15 minutes. Remove from heat; add spaghetti and mix. Place in a casserole dish and cover the top with cheese. Warm in oven or microwave until the cheese melts and serve hot. Delicious!
