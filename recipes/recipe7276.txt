CHICKEN BROCCOLI


2 (10 oz.)  pkg. cooked, drained broccoli
1/2  c. grated sharp cheese (coon cheese)
4  chicken breasts, cooked and boned
2 (10 3/4 oz.)  cans chicken creamed soup (undiluted)
1/2  c. mayonnaise
  juice from 1 lemon

      Place broccoli in buttered 12 x 8 x 2 1/2-inch casserole. Lay cooked chicken pieces on top of broccoli. Mix soup, mayonnaise and lemon juice. Pour over mixture. Sprinkle grated cheese over casserole. Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes or until hot and cheese is melted.
       If you are unable to find Kraft coon cheese (black label), substitute Romano.
