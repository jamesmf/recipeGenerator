HOT CHICKEN SALAD CASSEROLE

Casserole:
1  c. chicken, cooked
1  c. rice
  chicken broth (enough to cook rice)
2/3  Tbsp. onion, chopped
1  c. soup, cream of chicken
3/4  c. mayonnaise
3  Tbsp. lemon juice

      Dice chicken and set aside.  Cook rice in chicken broth.  Mix all ingredients together and place in a buttered 8x8-inch casserole dish.
Topping:
2  Tbsp. butter, melted
1  c. Ritz crackers, crushed

      Mix ingredients together.  Put on top of ingredients in dish. Bake at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes.
