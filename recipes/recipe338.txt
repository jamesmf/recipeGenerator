APPLE BREAKFAST SAUSAGE


1/2  lb. ground turkey
2  Tbsp. soft bread crumbs
1/2  c. shredded apple
1/4  tsp. leaf sage, crushed
1/4  tsp. pepper
1/8  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. paprika
  dash of ground nutmeg

      Mix the ingredients in a large bowl and shape into 1/2-inch thick patties.  Spray rack of broiler pan with nonstick spray coating.  Arrange patties on unheated rack.  Broil 4 to 5 inches from the heat about 10 minutes or until no pink remains; turn once. (Or spray a large skillet with nonstick spray coating and cook sausage over medium heat for 8 to 10 minutes or until no pink remains.)  Makes 4 servings.
      Per Serving:  101 Calories, 5 g. Fat, 35 mg.  Cholesterol and 116 mg.  Sodium.
