BAKED ROUGHY WITH CARIBBEAN SAUCE


4  orange roughy fillets
  salt
  pepper
2  carambola, star fruit, sliced very thinly, optional
1/2  c. low-fat sour cream
2  Tbsp. fresh lime juice
2  Tbsp. fresh cilantro, chopped
1  jalapeno, seeded and chopped
1/8  tsp. salt
  Non-stick cooking spray

      Heat oven to 450 degrees.  Spray a shallow baking pan with non-stick spray.  Lay fish fillets in bottom of baking dish. Season fish lightly with salt and pepper.  Bake fish in upper 1/3 of oven for 10 minutes.  Meanwhile, combine sour cream, lime juice, cilantro, jalapeno and salt in a food processor or blender to make sauce.  Process 1 minute or until cilantro and jalapeno are finely chopped.  Slice carambola and lay over fillets.  Serve with sauce.
