ARTICHOKE CHEESE DIP


1 (15 oz.)  can artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
12  oz. Mozzarella cheese
1  c. mayonnaise
1  c. Parmesan cheese
1  c. chopped shrimp (small)
1/2  tsp. minced garlic
1  tsp. chives
1/2  tsp. parsley
1  tsp. lemon juice

      Mix all ingredients in a 5 x 9-inch baking dish. Cook at 325 degrees; for 45 minutes. Serve warm with crackers or fresh bread.
