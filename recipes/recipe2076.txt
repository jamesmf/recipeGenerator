NEEDHAMS


8  oz. pkg. shredded coconut
1  stick oleo
3/4  c. mashed potatoes (hot)
2  lb. confectioners sugar
1/2  tsp. salt
2  tsp. vanilla
4  sq. unsweetened chocolate
1 (12 oz.)  pkg. chocolate chips
1/2  cake paraffin wax

      Melt ingredients.  Use toothpicks to dip squares in hot chocolate.  Put on waxed paper to cool.
