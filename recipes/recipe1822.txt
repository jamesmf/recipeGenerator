TACO PINWHEELS


1  pkg. taco mix
2  pkg. cream cheese (large)
1/2  lb. Cheddar cheese
1 medium  can chopped olives
12  flour tortillas

      Mix first 4 ingredients to a smooth mixture and spread on tortillas.  Roll up and wrap in Saran Wrap.  Refrigerate.  When ready to use, slice like pinwheels and bake at 350 degrees; for 10 minutes.
