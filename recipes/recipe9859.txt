BAKED BEANS


4  slices bacon
1/2  c. chopped onion
2  Tbsp. brown sugar
2 (16 oz.)  cans pork and beans
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  tsp. mustard

      Cook bacon until crisp. Drain, reserving 2 tablespoons drippings in skillet. Crumble bacon. Cook onion in drippings until tender. Add with bacon to remaining ingredients. Mix well. Bake, uncovered, in 1 1/2-quart casserole in 325 degrees; oven for 1 1/2 hours.
