BLACK CHERRY JELLO SALAD


1  can fruit cocktail (No. 303 can)
2  Tbsp. sugar
1 (3 oz.)  box black cherry jello
1  c. sour cream
1  small Cool Whip

      Drain the fruit cocktail about 30 minutes. Add the sugar and jello; mix, then let chill until firm.
      Mix together the sour cream and Cool Whip and put on top of jello mixture.
       Any kind of jello can be used.
