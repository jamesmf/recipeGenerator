SQUASH PUFFS


3  lb. yellow squash (1 c. cooked)
1  egg, beaten
1/3  c. cornmeal
1/3  c. all purpose flour
1  tsp. baking powder
1/2  tsp. salt
1  medium onion, grated

      Cook squash till tender and drain. Mash and measure 1 cup. Combine dry ingredients and mix well. Add squash, egg, and onion; stir. Drop by level teaspoonfuls into hot oil. Cook until golden brown, turning once. Yields 2 dozen.
