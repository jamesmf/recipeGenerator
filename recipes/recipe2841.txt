HAMWICHES


1 1/2  c. butter, softened
4  tsp. dry mustard
4  tsp. instant minced onion
24  slices cooked ham
4  tsp. poppy seed
24  slices Swiss cheese
24  hamburger buns

      Combine butter, mustard, onion and poppy seed.  Spread this mixture on buns and add ham and cheese slices.  Wrap sandwiches in aluminum foil and freeze.  To serve, heat (still wrapped) in 400 degrees; oven for 35 minutes.
