HOLIDAY SWEET POTATOES


1/2  c. margarine
6  sweet potatoes, cooked, peeled and sliced
1  tsp. baking powder
1  tsp. vanilla
1  tsp. ground cinnamon
2  eggs
3/4  c. sugar
3/4  c. buttermilk
3/4  c. pecans (optional)
  marshmallows

      Add margarine to hot potatoes.  Combine with next 6 ingredients with mixer.  Stir in pecans, if desired.  Place mixture in greased 2-quart casserole and bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes.  Top with marshmallows during the last 10 minutes of cooking.  Serves 10 to 12.
