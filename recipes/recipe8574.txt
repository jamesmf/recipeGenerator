MARY SUE'S CHICKEN


1  jar of Dried Beef
6  Chicken Breasts
  Bacon
8  oz. Sour Cream
1  can Cream of Chicken Soup

      Put oil in bottom of baking dish.  Put dried beef in bottom of pan.  Place chicken on top of dried beef.  Put 1/2 slice of raw bacon on each piece of chicken.
      In a bowl, mix sour cream and soup.  Pour over chicken. Cover and bake at 300 degrees for 3 hours.  Uncover for last 30 minutes. Serve over rice.
