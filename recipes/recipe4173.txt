CHUTNEY ROLL


1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese, softened
1/2  c. chopped almonds
1/2  Tbsp. dry mustard
1/2  c. chutney
3/4 to 1  Tbsp. curry
  assorted crackers
  peanuts (optional)

      Mix all of the ingredients together and shape into a roll. Chill.  Serve on platter with assorted crackers.
      This is also nice rolled in chopped, unsalted peanuts.
