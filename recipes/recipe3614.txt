EASY CARAMEL POPCORN


3  qt. popped corn
1  c. packed brown sugar
1/2  c. Karo syrup (dark or light)
1/2  c. margarine
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. baking soda
1/2  tsp. vanilla

	Preheat oven to 250 degrees;. Place popped corn in a large roasting pan. Let it stay in oven while preparing glaze.
	 In medium saucepan, combine brown sugar, corn syrup and margarine. Bring to a boil over medium heat, stirring constantly. Without stirring, boil 4 minutes. Remove from heat. Stir in baking soda and vanilla. Pour over popped corn and toss to cover well. Bake for 1 hour, stirring frequently. Store in tightly covered container.
