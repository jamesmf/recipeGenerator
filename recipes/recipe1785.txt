SALSA DI VINGOLE-WHITE CLAM SAUCE


1/3  c. olive oil
3  cloves garlic
12  oz. drained minced clams
1  c. clam juice
1/4  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
2  Tbsp. butter
  pinch of basil
  pinch of oregano
  dash of Tabasco or cayenne pepper
  celery and green onion (optional)

      Heat oil in heavy saucepan.  Add garlic, celery and green onion and cook at medium heat for 1 minute while stirring.  Add clam juice, clams, salt and pepper.  Simmer, covered, for 2 minutes. Remove from heat.  Add butter.  Stir.  Serve at once.  Makes 4 to 6 servings or 1 pound linguine.
