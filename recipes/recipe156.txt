FRENCH LACE COOKIES


2/3  c. butter
2  c. quick oats
1  c. sugar
2/3  c. flour
1/4  c. light corn syrup
1/4  c. milk
1  tsp. vanilla
1  (11 1/2 oz.) chocolate morsels

      Melt butter in saucepan.  In bowl, mix butter, oats, sugar, flour, corn syrup, milk and vanilla until well blended.  Drop by level teaspoon, 3 inches apart onto foil-covered cookie sheets. Spread thin with a spatula.  Bake at 375 degrees; for 5 to 7 minutes.  Melt chocolate morsels over hot water.  Spread chocolate onto the flat side of one cookie.  Add another cookie to top to make a sandwich.
