NOODLE SALAD


1  head romaine lettuce, torn in small pieces
1  bunch green onions, chopped
2  stalks celery, chopped
1/2 to 1  stick butter or olive oil
2  pkg. Ramen noodles (any flavor; do not use flavor packet), broken up
1  c. slivered almonds
1/2  c. sesame seeds

      Toss first 3 ingredients together. Saut√© noodles in butter or olive oil until slightly brown. Cool and toss with greens. Add almonds and sesame seeds.
Sauce:
3 to 4  Tbsp. soy sauce
1/2  c. sugar
1/2  c. olive or canola oil
1/2  c. red wine vinegar

      Shake sauce and add to greens and noodle mixture just before serving.
