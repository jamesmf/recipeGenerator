BARBECUE BEEF BRISKET


1  tsp. hickory smoke
  garlic salt
  seasoned salt
  celery salt
1/2  bottle Open Pit barbecue original sauce

      Brush brisket with hickory smoke on both sides.  Rub well with the 3 salts.  Marinate overnight.  Cover tightly in refrigerator. Bake, covered, at 250 degrees; for 3 hours.  Pour off half the juice and turn and bake 1 hour longer.  Pour barbecue sauce over and bake 1 hour longer.  Let cool and slice thinly.  Put in shallow baking dish covered with foil and freeze.  Reheat to serve.  This is so good to prepare and put into as many servings as you need for company later and freeze.
