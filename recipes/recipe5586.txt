POTATO SOUP


8  - 10 potatoes, peeled and diced
1  large onion, chopped
1  can Cream of Chicken Soup
1  can Cream of Mushroom Soup
1  can whole kernel Corn, undrained
1  can Cream of Broccoli Soup
1  can Rotel Tomatoes
1  lb. Velveeta, shredded

      Cook potatoes and onions in 5 c. water until tender.  Do not drain potatoes. Add remaining ingredients and cook until cheese is melted.  Stir occasionally.
      Make 10-12 servings.
