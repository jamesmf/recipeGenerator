HAM AND CHEESE CHOWDER


2  c. potatoes
1/2  c. water
  dash of salt
3  Tbsp. butter
1  c. onion, chopped
3  Tbsp. all-purpose flour
  dash of pepper
4  c. milk
1 1/2  c. ham, cooked and diced
6  oz. (1 1/2 c.) Cheddar cheese, shredded
  croutons (optional)

      Peel and cube potatoes.  Place water and salt in a small saucepan.  Bring to a boil.  Add potatoes and reduce heat.  Cook 15 minutes or until potatoes are tender.  Drain potatoes, reserving 1 cup of liquid.
      Melt butter in a 3-quart Dutch oven.  Add onion and saut√© until tender.  Blend in flour and pepper.  Sir in milk and the reserved potato liquid.  Cook over medium heat until mixture is bubbly and slightly thickened.
      Add cooked potatoes and ham.  Heat gently.  Remove from heat and stir in cheese.  Top with croutons (if desired).
       NOTE:  10 oz. (1 pkg.) frozen chopped broccoli (cooked) may be substituted for ham.
      Makes 6 to 8 servings.
