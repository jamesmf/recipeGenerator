HICKORY SMOKED CHEESE SAUCE


1  c. rolled oats
1  c. water
1  tsp. onion powder
1/2  tsp. garlic powder
5  shakes liquid smoke
1/4  c. sunflower seeds
1  tsp. salt
2  Tbsp. tomato paste (optional)
3  Tbsp. nutritional yeast
1  c. lemon juice

      Blend smooth all but lemon juice.  Add juice.  Lightly boil in a saucepan, stirring, 5 to 10 minutes.
