CLASSIC MEAT LOAF


1 1/2  lb. Ground Beef
1 (8 oz.)  can Tomato Sauce
1  Egg, slightly beaten
1  c. quick cooking rolled Oats
1  tbsp. instant, minced Onion
1  tbsp. instant, minced Garlic
1 1/2  tsp. Salt
1/4  tsp. Pepper

 
Sauce (Optional):
1/3  c. Catsup
1  tsp. Worcestershire Sauce
1  tbsp. Dark Brown Sugar
1  tbsp. prepared Mustard

      Mix ground beef, tomato sauce, egg, oats, onion, garlic, salt and pepper and put into a 9x5x2 inch loaf pan.  Bake for one hour 15 minutes at 350 degrees.
      Mix all ingredients of sauce together and spread over top of meat loaf before baking.
