SEVEN LAYER GREEN SALAD


1  head lettuce shredded
1  cup bell pepper (chopped)
1  cup celery (chopped)
1  red onion
1  pkg. frozen English peas (cooked and drained)
1  pint mayonnaise
4  Tbsp. sugar
1  cup hoop cheese
8  strips bacon (fried crisp)

      Place shredded lettuce in 9 x 13 inch dish. Top with pepper, celery, red onion and peas.  Mix mayonnaise sugar and cheese together.  Spread on top of salad. Top with broken bacon pieces. Refrigerate overnight.  Serves 12-15.
