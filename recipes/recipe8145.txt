CHICKEN SALSA SPAGHETTI


3/4  c. onions
3  Tbsp. butter
1  jar mild salsa
1  jar mild or medium nacho cheese dip (all depends on how spicy you want your casserole)
1  can cream of mushroom soup
  chicken that has been boiled and cut up into bite size pieces
  cooked spaghetti
2  c. grated cheese

      Saut onions in butter until clear, then place in the bottom of casserole dish. Mix the salsa, cheese dip and soup thoroughly. Add spaghetti and chicken and stir well.  Pour into casserole dish; top with cheese. Bake in 350 degrees; oven until bubbly. Allow to set for 10 minutes before serving.
