PEANUT SAUCE FOR PASTA


1/4  c. creamy peanut butter
2  Tbsp. hot water
1  Tbsp. cider vinegar
1  Tbsp. soy sauce
1  tsp. sugar
1  Tbsp. salad oil
1/2  tsp. hot pepper sauce
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. pasta

      In medium bowl with wire whisk or fork, beat peanut butter with water until smooth.  Stir in vinegar and next 4 ingredients until blended.  Add cooked and drained pasta; toss gently to coat well. Serve immediately.
