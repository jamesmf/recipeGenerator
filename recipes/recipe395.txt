BREAD PUDDING



1/4  c. margarine
7 to 8  slices bread
1  tsp. cinnamon
1  c. raisins
3  eggs
2/3  c. sugar
2  c. hot milk
1  tsp. vanilla

      Spread   margarine  on  bread.  Cut  each  slice  in  4 squares.  Cover  bottom of 12 x 7 1/2 x 3/4-inch pan with half of the bread squares.  Sprinkle with cinnamon and raisins. Top with remaining  bread,  margarine  side  up.  Beat eggs until light, adding  sugar gradually.  Add hot milk slowly to eggs, beating constantly.  Add  vanilla.  Pour  egg  mixture  over bread.  Set dish  in  hot water and bake at 350 degrees; for 50 to 60 minutes until set. Serves 16.
      Per Serving:  145 Calories, 5 g. Fat (30%), 54 mg.  Cholesterol and 107 mg.  Sodium.
