CHUNKY GUACAMOLE


2  medium avocados, cubed
1/2  c. diced tomato
1/4  c. minced onion
1  Tbsp. minced fresh parsley
1  tsp. minced garlic
2 to 3  Tbsp. lime juice
1/4  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. ground red pepper

      Coarsely mash avocado.  Add remaining ingredients, mixing well. Spoon into a serving bowl and serve immediately with unsalted tortilla chips.  Yield:  2 cups.  Serving size:  1 tablespoon (27 calories per serving).
