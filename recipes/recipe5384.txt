QUICK AND EASY CHICKEN NOODLE SOUP


1  pkg. Lipton Noodles-n-Sauce (chicken flavor)
7  c. water
1  large celery stalk, chopped
2  carrots, chopped
1  c. chicken broth
1 large  can Swanson white chicken
  salt and pepper to taste

	Mix all ingredients except Noodles-n-Sauce.  Simmer for 1 hour or until vegetables are tender.  Add Noodles-n-Sauce.  Continue to cook for 10 minutes.
