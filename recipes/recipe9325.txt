BREAKFAST CASSEROLE


1  lb. sausage
4  eggs
1  Tbsp. onion, chopped
2  c. milk
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper
1  c. Bisquick
1  c. cheese, shredded

      Brown sausage.  Drain and spread in a 9x13-inch casserole dish.  Combine eggs, onion, milk, salt, pepper and Bisquick, blending well.  Pour over sausage.  Sprinkle top with cheese.
      Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 to 35 minutes.
