MEATBALLS


1/2  lb. Italian sausage
1  lb. ground meat
1/2  c. seasoned bread crumbs
1/2  tsp. oregano
1/8  tsp. pepper
1/2  tsp. salt
1  egg
1/4  tsp. garlic salt or 1 clove fresh garlic
1/2  c. milk
2  Tbsp. Parmesan cheese, grated

	Combine all ingredients in a large bowl. Shape into whatever size meatballs you like. Brown in electric skillet with 3 tablespoons of olive oil. Brown on all sides. Serve with your own sauce.
