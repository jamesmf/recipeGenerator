BROCCOLI SALAD


1  bunch broccoli, washed and drained
1  small red onion, sliced thinly
1  c. grated Cheddar cheese
1/2  lb. bacon, fried, drained and crumbled
3  Tbsp. sugar
1/4  c. white vinegar
1  c. mayo

	Cut broccoli into florets. Add onions; set aside. Mix mayo, sugar and vinegar; pour over broccoli and onion. Refrigerate. (Can be put in plastic Ziploc bag or bowl with lid.)  When ready to serve, add bacon and cheese. Also good leftover.
