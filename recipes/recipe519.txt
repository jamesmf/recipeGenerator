RAISIN COOKIES


2  c. raisins
1  c. shortening
2  c. sugar
3  eggs
1  tsp. vanilla
4  c. flour
1  tsp. baking powder
1  tsp. soda
1  tsp. cinnamon
1/2  tsp. salt

      Put raisins into saucepan and cover with 2 or 3 cups of water. Cook 5 to 10 minutes.  Cream together shortening, sugar, eggs and vanilla.  Sift together 4 cups flour, baking powder, soda, cinnamon and salt.  In a separate bowl, mix shortening, sugar, egg and vanilla mixture with drained raisins.  Add 1 cup water raisins were cooked in to mixture alternately with flour mixture, stirring by hand.  Drop by teaspoonful on greased cookie sheet.  Bake at 375 degrees; until golden brown.
