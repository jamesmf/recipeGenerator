POTATO SALAD


1  c. mayonnaise
2  Tbsp. vinegar
1 1/2  tsp. salt
1  tsp. sugar
1/2  tsp. pepper
3  hard-cooked eggs, chopped
4  c. cooked peeled potatoes (5 to 6 medium)

	In a large bowl, mix first 5 ingredients until smooth. Add potatoes and toss to coat well. Sprinkle chopped eggs on top. Cover and chill. Makes 4 to 5 cups.
