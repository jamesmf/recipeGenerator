CHICKEN SALAD


4  to 6 chicken breasts
1/2  c. chopped green onions
1  c. chopped celery
1/2  c. sour cream
1/2  c. mayonnaise
  Lawry's salt to taste

      Boil chicken until cooked.  Remove chicken from bones.  Cut into small pieces.  Add green onions and celery.  In separate container, mix sour cream, mayonnaise and Lawry's salt.  When thoroughly mixed, add to chicken.  Refrigerate until served.
