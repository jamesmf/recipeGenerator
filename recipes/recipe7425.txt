CHICKEN CASSEROLE


6  to 8 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
3  cans cream of chicken soup
3 soup  cans of milk
8 to 10  slices cheese
3  Tbsp. butter
3  c. bread crumbs
2  bags Boil-in-Bag rice

      Place chicken breasts in a 9 1/2 x 13 1/2 baking dish.  Place 1 to 1 1/2 slices of cheese per chicken breast.  Combine soup with milk and pour over chicken.  Melt butter and combine with bread crumbs and pour on top of mixture.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes. Prepare rice as indicated on package.  Serve chicken over rice.
