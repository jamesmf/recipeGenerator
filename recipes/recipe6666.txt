DIPPITY DOGS


  Duncan Hines Buttermilk Pancake Mix
1/2  tsp. salt
1  lb. (10) wieners
  Crisco oil for deep frying
1/8  tsp. paprika
1/8  tsp. pepper
10  (4 1/2) inch wooden skewers

      Prepare pancake mix as directed on package for 7 - 8 pancakes.  Stir in salt, pepper, and paprika.  Dip wieners into batter then drop into deep oil heated to 375 degrees.  Fry until golden brown, 2 minutes.  Insert skewers lengthwise into wieners and serve.
