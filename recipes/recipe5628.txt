ORIENTAL SALAD


1  pkg. broccoli salad mix
5  green onions with tops, chopped
1 sm.  can water chestnuts, sliced
2  pkgs. Ramen Noodles
1 sm.  pkg. slivered almonds
2/3  C. sugar
1/2  C. vinegar
1  C. oil (olive or vegetable)
2  Tbsp. hot chili oil*
2  Tbsp. soy sauce

      Mix sugar, vinegar, oil, hot chili oil and soy sauce for dressing.
      Combine all other ingredients into your bowl.
      Pour dressing over this mixture 30 minutes before serving.
      *This oil is found in the Oriental section of your supermarket.
