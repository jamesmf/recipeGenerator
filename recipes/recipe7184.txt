HAM-BROCCOLI ROLL-UPS


2  c. broccoli spears, cooked and drained
6  slices ham
1/2  c. cheese sauce

      Place 1 broccoli spear on 1 slice ham and roll up.  Boil rest of ham slices around broccoli spears.  Place ham roll-ups in baking dish.  Pour cheese sauce over roll-ups.  Cover and bake in 350 degrees; oven for 10 minutes or until hot.
