CHEESE STRAWS


9  oz. shredded sharp cheese
1 1/2  sticks margarine
1 1/2  c. sifted self-rising flour
3  c. Rice Krispies
1/4  tsp. cayenne pepper

      Add grated cheese to softened margarine. Sift flour and pepper together. Then add to cheese and margarine mixture. Add Rice Krispies. Roll into small balls and mash with a fork. Bake on ungreased cookie sheet at 350 degrees; about 15 to 20 minutes.
