EMPANADAS RICAS DE CALABAZA


12  oz. regular beer
1  lb. lard
  flour as needed
1  lb. can whole pumpkin
  cinnamon and sugar to taste

      Pour beer into bowl.  Add lard.  Let stand at room temperature for about 3 hours.  Work flour into beer mixture.  After 3 hours, use enough flour until dough forms.  Form 3-inch balls and roll out into 5 to 6-inch diameter circles.  Fill with pumpkin or favorite fruit or meat filling.  Bake at 425 degrees; until golden brown and dust in sugar and cinnamon mixture (1 cup sugar to 1 teaspoon cinnamon). Can be frozen for 1 month.
