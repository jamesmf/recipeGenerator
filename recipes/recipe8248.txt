SWEDISH MEATBALLS I


1  lb. ground beef
1/2  lb. ground pork
1/2  c. onion, minced
3/4  c. fine dry bread crumbs
1  Tbsp. parsley, minced
1 1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper
1  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  egg
1/2  c. milk

	Mix all ingredients thoroughly. Shape into balls the size of a walnut. Brown in 1/4 cup hot fat or vegetable oil. Remove meatballs and make gravy. Return meatballs to gravy; cook 15 to 20 minutes. Yield: 6 to 8 servings.
Meatball Gravy:
1/4  c. flour
1  tsp. paprika
1/2  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper
2  c. boiling water
3/4  c. sour cream

	Stir the flour, paprika, salt and pepper into hot fat in the skillet. Stir in the boiling water and sour cream.
