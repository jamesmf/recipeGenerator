CABBAGE GUMBO


1  large head cabbage, shredded fine
1  large onion, chopped fine
1  beef round steak cubed (could substitute venison)
2  ham slices, cubed
2  lb. link sausage, sliced
1  pod hot pepper, seeds removed
1  Tbsp. shortening

      Fry meat in shortening, add onion.  Saut√©.  Add cabbage, cover with water, add pepper pod, cook until tender.  (Careful about adding too much water.)
Cream Sauce:
1  pint milk
  flour
  salt, pepper, cayenne pepper, to taste

      Whisk milk and flour until smooth, stir into meat and cabbage mixture while boiling.  Heat to boiling.  Serve over rice.
