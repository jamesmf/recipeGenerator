CHICKEN CHOW MEIN


1  cooked Chicken, cut up
2/3  c. rice, cooked in 2 c. broth
2/3  c. Mayonnaise
1 4  oz. can/jar Mushrooms
1  c. Celery, chopped
1  Onion, chopped
1  can Water Chestnuts, sliced
1  can Cream of Mushroom Soup

      Mix all ingredients well and pour in greased casserole dish. Bake at 300 degrees for 30 minutes.
