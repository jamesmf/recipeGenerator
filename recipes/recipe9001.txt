FLANK STEAK TERIYAKI


1/4  c. soy sauce
2  Tbsp. salad oil
2  Tbsp. honey
1  Tbsp. wine vinegar
1/2  tsp. garlic powder
1/2  tsp. ground ginger
1  large flank steak

      Combine all marinade ingredients. Marinate 1 large flank steak in refrigerator for about 4 to 6 hours. Refrigerate until about 1/2 hour before grilling. Broil meat about 4 minutes per side for rare or longer for desired degree of doneness. Slice thinly on the diagonal.
