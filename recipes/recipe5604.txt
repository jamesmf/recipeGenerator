SANTA FE WHITE CHILI


1 1/2  tsp. each- garlic powder & cumin
3/4  tsp. dried oregano
1  Tbsp. olive oil
1  - 1 1/4 lbs. boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1/2-inch cubes
1/2  C. chopped onion
14 1/2  oz. can fat-free chicken broth
4 1/2  oz. can chopped green chiles
1  bay leaf
  2-15 oz. cans rinsed and drained cannelloni beans
  shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  sliced green onion (optional for garnish)

      Combine garlic powder, cumin and oregano in small dish and blend; set aside.  Heat oil in large nonstick skillet over med-high heat.  Add chicken and onion; cook4 - 5 minutes or until chicken is lightly browned. Stir in chicken broth, chiles, mixed spices and bay leaf.
       Simmer on low heat 15 minutes.  Stir in beans and simmer 5 minutes longer.  Remove bay leaf.
       Serve topped with shredded cheese.
