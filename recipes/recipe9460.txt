KIM'S CHILLI


1  lb. hamburger meat
1  onion
1  tsp. chili powder
1  can tomatoes, cut
2  cans kidney beans
12  oz. tomato sauce
  salt and pepper to taste

	Brown hamburger meat with onion; drain off grease. Add other ingredients and simmer for 30 minutes.
	Can add more chili powder to taste.  Also use 6 ounces tomato sauce for 1 can kidney beans.
