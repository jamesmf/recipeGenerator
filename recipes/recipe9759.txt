HAMBURGER-RICE HOT DISH


1  lb. hamburger
2  onions, diced
1  c. chopped celery
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  can chicken noodle soup
1  can water for each can soup
1  c. raw rice

      Brown hamburger and onions together; drain. Spread into bottom of cake pan or casserole dish. Mix remaining ingredients. Add to the dish. Bake at 350 degrees; for about 1 hour or until rice is done.
