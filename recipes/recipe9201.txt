BUSH'S BEST ALL-AMERICAN CHILI


2  cans (16 oz.) Bush's Best chili beans
1  lb. ground beef
1  c. chopped onion
1  c. chopped green pepper
1  can (14 1/2 oz.) diced tomatoes
1  can (6 oz.) tomato paste
1/2  c. water
1/2  tsp. black pepper
1  tsp. salt
1  Tbsp. chili powder
2  tsp. oregano
1  tsp. sugar
  shredded cheddar cheese

      In large pan cook beef, onion and green pepper until meat is browned.  Drain excess grease.  Stir in remaining ingredients, except cheese.  Bring to a boil; cover, reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes.  Garnish with cheese.
