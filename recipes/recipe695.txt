BARBECUED CHICKEN ON SPAGHETTI


1  Tbsp. butter
3  lb. chicken
1  medium onion, chopped
1  can tomato soup
1  Tbsp. vinegar
1  Tbsp. lemon juice
2  Tbsp. brown sugar
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. mustard
1  tsp. Tabasco sauce
1  bay leaf
1/2  tsp. oregano
1/4 to 1/2  c. water
  spaghetti

      Melt butter in roasting pan and brown chicken lightly.  Mix all ingredients together except spaghetti and pour over chicken.  Roast at 350 degrees; for 1 1/2 hours.  Cook spaghetti and use the sauce on it. Recipe may be doubled or tripled.  Serves two people.
