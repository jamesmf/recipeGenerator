FROZEN STRAWBERRY SALAD


1 reg.  can Pineapple, crushed and drained
1  large container Cool Whip
1  can Strawberry Pie Filling
1  can Eagle Brand Milk
1  c. Pecans, crushed

      Mix all ingredients.  Pour in cupcake cups and freeze.  When ready to use, peel off paper and place on salad dish lined with lettuce or on each person's individual plate.
