BLACKBERRY DUMPLINGS


2  c. blackberries
2  c. sugar
2  c. water

      Boil blackberries, sugar and water; reduce heat to medium. After mixing biscuit dough, drop by spoonfuls into boiling mixture. Cook until dough is done, approximate time 10 minutes.
Biscuit Dough:
1  c. buttermilk
1/4  c. shortening
2  c. self-rising flour

      Cut shortening into flour until it feels like corn meal mixture.  Add buttermilk and stir.
