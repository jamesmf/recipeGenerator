PARTY SANDWICHES


3  pkg. dinner rolls
1  lb. Swiss cheese, sliced
1/2  lb. ham, thinly sliced

 
Sauce:
2  sticks margarine
1  medium onion, chopped
2  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1 1/2  Tbsp. mustard

      Saut√© onions and butter until clear.  Then simmer with other ingredients.  Slice rolls in half horizontally.  Spread sauce on both sides.  Top with ham and cheese.  Place rolls back in pan and cover with aluminum foil.  Bake for 20 to 25 minutes in a 350 degrees; oven.  The sandwiches are done when cheese has melted and the rolls are steamed.  Let cool, then separate rolls by cutting through cheese and ham with a sharp knife.  Rolls can be frozen and reheated.
