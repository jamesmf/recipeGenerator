SHRIMP BUTTER


8  oz. cream cheese
1/2  c. butter
1  c. mayonnaise
1/2  c. chopped onion
1  tsp. garlic
1  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
  salt and pepper to taste
1/2  lb. boiled shrimp
2  hard-boiled eggs

      Combine first 8 ingredients. Add shrimp and eggs. Mix, chill and serve with Town House crackers.
