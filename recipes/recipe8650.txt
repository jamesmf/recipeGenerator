TORTILLA CASSEROLE


1  lb. ground beef
1  pkg. taco seasoning mix
1  tsp. chili powder (optional)
1/2  tsp. cumin (optional)
1 small  can tomato sauce
3/4  c. water
8  corn tortillas
1  small onion, chopped
2  c. shredded Cheddar cheese

	Preheat oven to 350 degrees;.  Brown ground beef. Add taco seasoning, chili powder and cumin. Mix well.  Stir in tomato sauce and water. Simmer about 20 minutes.  Spray vegetable oil on a 2-quart casserole dish. Place 4 tortillas on bottom of dish.  Pour 1/2 meat mixture over tortillas. Sprinkle with 1/2 onions and 1/2 cheese. Repeat with remaining tortillas, meat mixture, onions and cheese. Bake approximately 20 minutes until cheese is bubbly. This dish can be frozen.
