POTATO SALAD


2 1/2  tsp. flour
3/4  c. sugar
1  tsp. salt
  dash of pepper
6  Tbsp. prepared mustard
2  Tbsp. butter, melted
2  eggs, beaten
1/4  c. water
1/4  c. vinegar
1  c. mayonnaise

      Mix  flour,  sugar, salt and pepper.  Add mustard, then add butter,  eggs, water and  vinegar.  Stir  and  cook  over medium heat until thick.  Add  mayonnaise  and mix with potato mixture. Best if added when potatoes  are  warm  and made the day ahead. May need to add more mayonnaise or pickle juice if dry.  Yields 1 gallon.
Potato Mixture:
3  qt. cooked, grated, potatoes
2  sweet pickles, diced
2  medium onions, chopped
4  hard-cooked eggs, chopped
