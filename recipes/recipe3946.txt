FRUIT DIP



  2-- 8 oz package cream cheese
  2--- 7 oz  jars marshmallow cream
1  small container cool whip
2  Tbsp. sugar
1/2  cup strawberry puree, (chilled)
2  mashed bananas

      Blend together in food processor, strawberry puree, sugar, and bananas. Place in bowl. Blend together cream cheese, marshmallow cream. Pour mixture in same bowl. Stir and fold in cool whip. When mixed well, chill 1-1/2 hours before serving.
