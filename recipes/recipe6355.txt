NO-PEEK STEW


2  lb. stew meat (pork, beef or lamb)
1  can French onion soup (undiluted)
1  can tomato soup (undiluted)
2 beef bouillon cubes or 2  tsp. granules
1  pkg. frozen stew vegetables
1/2  c. red wine

      Place all ingredients in a Dutch oven and cover. Bake at 250 degrees; for 5 hours or use a crock-pot. Do not peek! Makes 6 servings.
      Cal-200, Carbs-17, Protein-17, Fat-6.
