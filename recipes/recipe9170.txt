SAUSAGE CASSEROLE


1  pkg. crescent rolls (8 pack)
1  lb. Neese's hot sausage
6  eggs
1/2  c. milk
1  (8 oz.) shredded cheddar cheese
1  (8 oz.) shredded mozzarella cheese

      Cook and crumble sausage; drain well.  Press rolls into 9 x 13 inch casserole dish.  Sprinkle sausage over rolls.  Beat eggs slightly; add milk, salt and pepper to taste.  Mix well; pour over sausage.  Sprinkle cheese over all and bake at 400 degrees; for 20 minutes.
