ASPARAGUS AND HAM SPAGHETTI SAUCE


1/2  stick butter
  olive oil
1/2  c. thinly sliced onion
1  c. diced ham
1  lb. cut up asparagus
1/2  c. water
1  chicken bouillon cube
1/2  c. cream or half and half
1/4  c. Parmesan cheese
  spaghetti, cooked and drained

      Melt butter and olive oil in electric frypan.  Add onion, diced ham and asparagus.  Saut√©.  Add water and chicken bouillon cube and simmer for 15 minutes.  Add cream and Parmesan cheese.  Toss around.  Pour over cooked spaghetti.
