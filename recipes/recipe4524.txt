BLACK BEAN SOUP


1  lb. black beans
6  c. water
4  c. chicken stock
1/4  c. olive oil
1  large onion, chopped
1 1/2  tsp. cumin
1 1/2  tsp. oregano
2  tsp. salt
1 1/2  c. cooked rice
4  Tbsp. garlic, minced

	Cook beans in water. Cover and simmer (add salt) until most of the water is soaked up. Add stock and continue to cook 1 to 1 1/2 hours more.
	 In separate skillet, brown in olive oil garlic, onions, cumin and oregano. Add to beans after they have finished cooking. Cook 10 to 15 minutes longer.
	 Take 2 cups bean mixture and puree. Add back to soup along with rice. Top with sour cream and chives when served.
