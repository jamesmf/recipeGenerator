BLACK BEAN SALAD


1  can black beans, drained
1  can shoepeg corn, drained
1/2  purple onion, chopped
1/3  c. olive oil, extra light
1/2  c. vinegar
1/3  c. sugar

      Mix beans, corn and onion; set aside.  Mix olive oil, vinegar and sugar.  Pour over bean mixture; refrigerate.  Just before serving, add 1 package feta cheese.  Serve with large corn chips.
      Can also be used as an appetizer.
