SNAPPY CHICKEN FINGERS


1/2  c. shredded Mozzarella cheese
1/4  c. prepared pesto sauce
2  Tbsp. chopped pimento
1 (5 oz.)  can chunk white chicken
2  hard rolls (each 7-inches long), split and toasted

      Combine cheese, pesto and pimento.  Add chicken. Toss gently to mix.  Place roll halves on cookie sheet.  Top each roll half with chicken mixture.  Broil 5-inches from heat for 2 minutes or until cheese melts.  Cut each roll half into 4 slices.  Makes 16 appetizers.
