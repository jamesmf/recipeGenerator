SAVORY CHICKEN AND DUMPLINGS


1  c. cooked chicken
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  c. chicken broth
1  c. frozen peas and carrots
2  stems celery, cut
2  Tbsp. dried minced onion
1/4  tsp. summer savory
1/8  tsp. pepper

      Combine  ingredients  in large saucepan. Cover and cook for 5 minutes.
Dumplings:
1  c. biscuit mix
1/3  c. milk
1/8  tsp. savory
1/8  tsp. sage

      Combine biscuit mix, milk and herbs.  Stir lightly with fork and drop by spoonfuls onto chicken mixture.  Cook, uncovered, 10 minutes.  Cover and cook for 10 minutes more.  Serves 4 to 6.
      Per Serving:  204 Calories, 7 g. Fat (33%), 26 mg.  Cholesterol and 911 mg.  Sodium.
