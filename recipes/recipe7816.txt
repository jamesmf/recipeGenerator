SWEET & SOUR CHICKEN
(Microwave)  

2  broiler-fryer chickens, cut up (I use 4 chicken breasts)
1 (8 oz.)  bottle Russian salad dressing
1 (1 3/8 oz.)  envelope dry onion soup mix
1 (10 oz.)  jar apricot preserves

      In a large shallow baking dish, place chicken in single layer.  In bowl, mix together salad dressing, soup mix and preserves.  Pour over chicken.  Bake, uncovered, 9 minutes per pound.
For Dessert - Baked Apples:
  Apples
  Red Hots

      Core apples, fill with red hots.  Cover with wax paper.  Bake on high approximately 2 minutes per apple.
