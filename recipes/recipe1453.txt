GRILLED PORK CHOPS


4  (3/4 to 1-inch thick) pork chops
1/4  tsp. salt
3/4  tsp. lemon pepper seasoning
1/2  tsp. dried whole oregano

      Sprinkle pork chops with salt, lemon pepper and oregano.  Place chops 4 to 5 inches from coals.  Grill over low to medium hot coals 25 minutes or until the chops are no longer pink, turning once. Yields 4 servings.
