BISQUICK CHICKEN CASSEROLE


1  medium whole chicken or chicken pieces
2  cans cream of chicken soup
1  c. chicken broth
1 3/4  c. milk
1  stick butter
2  c. Bisquick
1/4  c. broth

      Bake or stew chicken, saving the broth.  Debone chicken and put in 13 x 9 inch pan.  Mix soup and 1 cup broth; pour over chicken.  Mix Bisquick with butter until crumbly; add 1/4 cup broth and milk.  Pour Bisquick mixture over top.  Bake at 400 degrees; for 30 to 40 minutes, until golden brown.
