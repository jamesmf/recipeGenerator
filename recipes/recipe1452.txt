PEPPER PORK CHOPS


4  pork loin chops, about 1-inch thick
2  cloves garlic, crushed
1  Tbsp. crushed coriander seeds
8  crushed peppercorns
1  tsp. brown sugar
3  Tbsp. soy sauce

      Combine all ingredients except pork chops.  Brush chops well with marinade.  Cover; set aside for 30 minutes, brushing occasionally with marinade.  Place chops on grill 5 to 6 inches above medium coals; reserve marinade.  Cook 8 to 10 minutes on each side.  Brush chops with marinade and grill an additional 10 to 12 minutes, brushing occasionally with marinade.
