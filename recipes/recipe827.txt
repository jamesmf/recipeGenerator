STUFFING AND TURKEY BAKE


1/2  c. (1 stick) butter or margarine
1 1/4  c. boiling water
3 1/2  c. seasoned stuffing crumbs
1 (2.8 oz.)  can French fried onions
1  can cream of celery soup
3/4  c. milk
1 1/2  c. cubed, cooked turkey
1 (10 oz.)  pkg. frozen peas, thawed, rinsed in cold water and drained

      Combine butter and boiling water.  Stir until butter melts, then pour over stuffing crumbs and toss lightly.  Stir in 1/2 can French fried onions.  Spoon mixture into 9-inch shallow buttered baking dish (1 1/2-quart).  Press stuffing in bottom and up sides of dish to form a shell.  Combine soup, milk, turkey and peas. Pour into stuffing shell.  Bake, covered, at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes. Uncover and top with remaining onions.  Bake 5 to 8 minutes longer. Makes 4 to 6 servings.
