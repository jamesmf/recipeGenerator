ARKANSAS COOKIES


1  c. melted butter
2  c. brown sugar
2  eggs
1  tsp. vanilla
1  tsp. salt
2  c. flour

      Cream melted butter and brown sugar.  Add eggs, vanilla, salt and flour.  Mix well.  Pat onto a greased and floured cookie sheet. Bake at 300 degrees; for 25 to 30 minutes.  Cool and cut into squares.
