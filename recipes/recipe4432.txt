OLD FASHIONED POTATO SALAD


1  c. mayonnaise
1  c. minced onion
3  Tbsp. white vinegar
2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
2  hard-cooked eggs, chopped
3  lb. potatoes, cooked, peeled and cubed (about 6 c.)
2  c. sliced celery

      In large bowl, stir together mayonnaise, onion, vinegar, salt, pepper and eggs.  Add potatoes and celery.  Toss to coat well.  Cover.  Refrigerate for at least 4 hours.  If desired, sprinkle with paprika.  Makes about 8 cups.
