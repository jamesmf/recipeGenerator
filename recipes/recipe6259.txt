CELESTIAL SOUP

Soup:
1 (6 oz.)  can frozen orange juice
3  c. tomato puree
3  tsp. salt
2  Tbsp. vegetable oil
1/2  onion, finely minced
  rind and juice of 1 lemon
1  c. water

 
Garnish:
3  Tbsp. sour cream or yogurt
2  large cucumbers, seeded and chopped
1/2  cantaloupe, seeded and chopped
2  Tbsp. parsley
8  ice cubes (1 in each soup mug)

      Combine soup ingredients and chill.  Serve in soup mugs. Garnish with sour cream, chopped cucumbers, chopped cantaloupe, parsley and an ice cube.  Yields 8 servings.
