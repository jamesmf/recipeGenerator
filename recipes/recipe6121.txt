STRAWBERRY AND CHICKEN SALAD


1 10  oz. pkg. romaine salad greens or 8 c. torn romaine
1 6  oz. pkg. Louis Rich Oven Roasted Chicken Breast Cuts
1 15  oz. can mandarin orange segments, drained
2  c. sliced strawberries
1/2  medium red onion, sliced
1/2  c. Planters sliced almonds
1/2  c. Kraft Special Collection Creamy Poppy seed Dressing

      Toss romaine in large bowl with chicken cuts, oranges, strawberries and onion.  Sprinkle with almonds. Serve with dressing.  Makes 4 servings.  For added flavor, caramelize almonds.
