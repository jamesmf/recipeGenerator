"BAKED EGGS"


8  slices buttered bread
2  c. diced ham
1  c. shredded mild Cheddar cheese
8  eggs, beaten
4  c. milk
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. dry mustard
1/4  tsp. pepper

      Cube bread small. Put in bottom of well-greased pans. Mix eggs, milk, salt, dry mustard and pepper. Pour over bread. Place ham and cheese on top. Foil and refrigerate overnight. Leave foil on and bake for 1 hour at 350 degrees;. Check 1/2 hour.
