BROCCOLI/CAULIFLOWER SALAD


1 1/2  c. light mayonnaise  (I use Miracle Whip light)
3/4  c. sugar
2  Tbsp. vinegar
7 or 8  c. broccoli and cauliflower florets, small pieces
1 1/2  c. raisins
1 1/2  c. sunflower seeds
1  small red onion (or other sweet onion)
1  lb. or less of crisp bacon, broken into small pieces (optional)

      Mix together mayonnaise, sugar and vinegar.  Add all of the remaining ingredients.  Mix and chill.
