CHICKEN DIVAN PATSY


2 (10 oz.)  pkg. frozen broccoli
2  c. cooked cubed chicken breasts
2  c. condensed cream of chicken soup
1  c. mayonnaise
1  tsp. lemon juice
1/2  tsp. curry powder
1/2  c. sharp cheese, shredded
1/2  c. buttered crumbs or almonds

      Cook broccoli until tender.  Drain and arrange on bottom of greased 11 1/2 x 7 1/2 x 2 1/2-inch baking dish.  Place chicken on top of broccoli.  Combine soup, mayo, lemon juice and curry powder.  Pour over chicken.  Sprinkle with cheese and top with crumbs or almonds.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes.  Serves 6 to 8.
