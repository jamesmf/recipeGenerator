SPINACH SALAD


1  pkg. spinach, washed
8 - 10  slices thick bacon, cooked crisp
1  red apple, chopped
1/2  c. pecans, toasted
1  c. vegetable oil
1/2  c. red wine vinegar
1/2  c. sugar
1/3  c. ketchup
  salt and pepper

      Mix the vegetable oil, vinegar, sugar, ketchup, salt and pepper.  Refrigerate until chilled.  Wash the spinach.  Toast the pecans.  Cook the bacon.  Chop the apple.  Mix with spinach and pour the dressing over it.
