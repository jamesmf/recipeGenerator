HUMMUS II


1  can (15 1/2 oz.) chick peas
1/2  lemon juiced
1 large  clove garlic
3  Tbsp. Tahineh
1/2  tsp. cumin powder
  Salt to taste
1/2  jalapeno pepper without seeds (optional)
1  Tbsp. olive oil

      Drain and wash the chick peas.  Boil in little water till the chick pease are very soft.
       Add salt and cumin to the boiling chick peas. Put chick peas mixture, including water, into blender.  Add garlic, tahineh, olive oil and blend until smooth.
      Pour in pretty bowl and decorate with olive oil, parsley, samak, and olives.
