HONEY-MUSTARD GLAZED SAUSAGE KABOBS


1  lb. smoked sausage
1  each:  green, red and yellow bell pepper
1  medium red onion
12  cherry tomatoes
3  Tbsp. honey
2  Tbsp. Dijon mustard
1  Tbsp. white wine vinegar

      Cut sausage into 24 pieces.  Cut peppers and onion into 1-inch pieces.  Arrange sausage and vegetables on 6 (12-inch) metal skewers.
      For the glaze, stir together honey, mustard and vinegar in small bowl.  Brush kabobs with glaze.  Grill 4 to 5 minutes over medium heat.  Turn; brush with additional glaze and continue grilling 4 to 5 minutes or until hot.  Makes 6 kabobs.
