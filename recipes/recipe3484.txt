CHILE CON QUESO


1  lb. sausage
2 large  cloves garlic, minced
1  chopped onion
1 large  can tomatoes
1  tsp. flour
1/4  c. evaporated milk
1  c. chopped green chiles
1  tsp. salt
1/2  lb. Jack cheese, grated

      Sauté sausage in frying pan, drain.  Add garlic and onion and sauté.  Add tomatoes, mash and cook down.  Mix flour and milk into a paste and add to sausage mixture.  When it is thickened a little, add the chiles, salt and cheese.  When the cheese has melted, pour the mixture into a chafing dish and serve with tortilla chips.
