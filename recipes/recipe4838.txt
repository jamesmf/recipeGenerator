LETTUCE WITH HOT BACON DRESSING


5  strips bacon
5  c. torn salad greens
2  hard-boiled eggs, chopped
2  green onions, sliced
1/2  c. sugar
1/2  c. vinegar
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. garlic powder
1/4  tsp. ground mustard

      In a skillet, cook bacon until crisp.  Remove the bacon and crumble.  Set aside.  Drain, reserving 1/4 cup drippings.  In a large bowl, combine greens, eggs, onion and bacon.  Add remaining ingredients to drippings and bring to a boil. Drizzle over the salad and toss to coat greens. Serve immediately.
