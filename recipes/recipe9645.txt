PLUMA'S COUNTRY CHICKEN AND RICE


1  c. uncooked rice
1  c. celery, sliced
3/4  c. chopped onion
2  tsp. parsley flakes
1/2  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper
1  can cream of mushroom soup
3/4  c. mayonnaise
1 1/2  c. water
  3-4 chicken breasts, skinned

      Place rice in 12x8 inch baking dish; cover with vegetables & seasonings. Combine soup & mayonnaise, gradually add water; mix until blended. Pour half of mixture over vegetables & rice; place chicken on top & add remaining mixture. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.
