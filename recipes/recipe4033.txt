ANDREA'S CHEESE BALL


8  oz. cream cheese
1/2  c. onion, chopped
12  oz. sharp Cheddar cheese, shredded
1/2  c. bell pepper, chopped
2  tsp. garlic salt
1/2  tsp. cayenne pepper
2  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  c. nuts, chopped

      Mix all ingredients (except nuts) and form a big ball.  Cover ball with nuts.
      Serve with crackers.
