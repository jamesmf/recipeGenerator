CRANBERRY RELISH


2  small bags fresh cranberries
4  whole oranges (without seeds and membranes; leave peeling on)
2  apples, cored (leave peeling on)
3  c. sugar or more to taste
1  c. pecans, chopped (can use walnuts or almonds)

      In a Cuisinart or food processor, combine cranberries, oranges, apples and pecans. After finely chopped in food processor, add sugar and mix well. Leave in the refrigerator for flavors to emulsify overnight.
