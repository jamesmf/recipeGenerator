HOMEMADE CHICKEN NOODLE SOUP


1  whole chicken (about 4 lb.)
5  large potatoes, cut up
1  large onion, chopped
1/2  c. celery, chopped
1/2  c. carrots, chopped
  salt to taste
  pepper to taste
1/2  bag egg noodles

      Boil chicken for an hour.  Drain water, but save.  Let chicken cool.  Add all other ingredients except noodles to chicken broth and boil again.  Remove chicken from bones and add meat to soup. Boil at least 1 hour; add noodles and serve.
