ITALIAN BEEF SANDWICHES


1 14 1/2  oz. can beef broth
2  garlic cloves, minced
1  tsp. dried oregano
1/2  tsp. pepper
1  medium green pepper, thinly sliced into rings
1  lb. deli roast beef, thinly sliced
6  hoagie or submarine sandwich buns, split

      In skillet, bring broth, garlic, oregano and pepper to a boil.  Add green pepper.  Reduce heat; simmer, uncovered until tender, about 5 minutes.  Remove green pepper with slotted spoon; keep warm.  Return broth to boil; add beef and cover and remove from heat.  Let stand for 2 minutes or until heated through. Place beef and green pepper on buns.  Serve with broth for dipping.
