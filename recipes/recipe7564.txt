CHOW MEIN (HAMBURGER)


1  lb. ground beef, browned, drained
1  can cream of mushroom soup, undiluted
1  can chicken rice soup, undiluted
1  can (4 oz.) sliced mushrooms, drained
  2-3 Tbsp. onion, chopped
  Dash of garlic powder
1  can (14 oz.) La Choy Meatless Chow Mein (Optional)

      Mix and simmer the above ingredients over medium heat.  Pour over hot steamed rice. Top with chow mein noodles.
