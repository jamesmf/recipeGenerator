ASPARAGUS LOAF WITH PECAN SAUCE


1 (No. 2)  can asparagus
2  Tbsp. butter
4 heaping  Tbsp. flour
1  c. milk
  salt and pepper to taste
4  eggs

 
Pecan Sauce:
1  Tbsp. butter
2  Tbsp. flour
1  c. cream
1  c. pecans

      Remove  tips  from  asparagus  and  place the remaining portions  around sides and bottom of an oiled loaf pan.  Put 2 tablespoons  butter  in double boiler;  add flour and milk and stir  until  thick.  Season  with salt and pepper.  Cool.  Add well-beaten  eggs and asparagus tips.  Pour into mold.  Set in pan of hot water.  Bake at 300 degrees; until firm, keeping water from boiling.  Serve with Pecan Sauce.
      Pecan  Sauce:  Melt  butter;  add  flour and stir until smooth.  Add  cream  and  cook until thick.  Add broken pecans and pour over loaf just before serving.
