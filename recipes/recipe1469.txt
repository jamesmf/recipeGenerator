MESQUITE GRILLED CAJUN TURKEY


3  lb. boneless breast of turkey, thawed
  oil
1  Tbsp. onion flakes
1  Tbsp. garlic powder
1/2  tsp. thyme leaves
1/4  tsp. cayenne
1/4  tsp. anise seed
1/4  tsp. ground cloves
1/4  tsp. ground allspice
1  bay leaf, crushed

      Prepare grill for indirect method.  Brush turkey with oil. Combine remaining ingredients and sprinkle over turkey.  Place turkey on grill.  Cover grill and cook according to package directions.  Wrap in foil and let stand 10 to 15 minutes.  Remove netting from turkey and slice turkey.
