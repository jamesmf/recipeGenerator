MANDARIN SALAD


1 small  box orange jello
1 15  oz. can crushed pineapple, drained
1  can mandarin oranges, drained
1 16  oz. box cottage cheese
1  small container cool whip
1  Tbsp. sugar

      Combine pineapple, orange segments, sugar and cottage cheese. Add dry jello.  Mix well.  Fold in cool whip.  Refrigerate a least 2 hours before serving.
