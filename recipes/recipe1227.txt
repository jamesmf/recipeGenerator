PERSIMMON PUDDING


1  qt. persimmon pulp
1  tsp. baking soda
3  c. plain flour
2  eggs, beaten
1  c. buttermilk
1  stick butter, melted
  flavor of your choice

      Mix flour and soda.  Add eggs, milk and flavoring.  Mix well. Stir in melted butter.  Bake in a 300 degrees; oven about 1 hour.
