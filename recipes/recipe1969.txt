TACO SHELLS (TORTILLA)


1 1/2  c. cold water
1  c. all-purpose flour
1/2  c. cornmeal
1/4  tsp. salt
1  egg
  vegetable oil

      Heat 8-inch skillet over medium-low heat just until hot.  Beat ingredients until smooth.  Pour scant 1/4 cup of the batter into skillet.  Rotate skillet until batter forms very thin tortilla, about 6-inches in diameter.  Cook tortilla until dry around the edges.  Fry in hot oil until crisp and golden brown.
