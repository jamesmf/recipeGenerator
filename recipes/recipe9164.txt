TAMALE ROUND ABOUT


2 1/2  c. browned ground beef
1/2  tsp. chili powder
1/2  tsp. ground cumin
1/4  tsp. salt
1  jar (16 oz.) Thick and Chunky Mild Salsa
1/4  c. red or green pepper, chopped (both if you want)

 
Ingredients for Cornbread Batter:
1 1/2  c. all-purpose flour
1  c. yellow corn meal
1/4  c. sugar
1  Tbsp. baking powder
1/2  tsp. salt
1 1/4  c. milk
2  eggs
1/4  c. oil
4  oz. sharp cheddar cheese (1 c.)
  sour cream (optional)

      Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Grease fluted bunt pan. Put these in layers: ground beef, mixed cornbread batter, seasonings, salsa, peppers and cheese. Bake for 35-40 minutes. When done, loose edges with knife before taking out of pan. Serve with sour cream, optional.
