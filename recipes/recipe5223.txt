CHICKEN SALAD


1  c. mayonnaise
1  c. grated carrots
1  c. celery, cut fine
1  can tuna, take balance in cooked chicken to make 3 cups
1/4  c. onion, minced
  slivered almonds

      Mix all of above ingredients.  Just before serving, add 2 cups of shoestring potatoes.  Serve on a lettuce leaf.
