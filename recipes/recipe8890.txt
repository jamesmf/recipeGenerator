ITALIAN CRESCENT CASSEROLE


1  lb. ground beef
1  Tbsp. instant onion
1  envelope spaghetti sauce mix
8  oz. tomato sauce
1 1/2  c. (6 oz.) Mozzarella or Jack cheese
1/2  c. sour cream
8  oz. can crescent rolls
1/2  c. Parmesan cheese
2  Tbsp. margarine or butter, melted

      Heat oven to 375 degrees;. In frypan, brown ground beef and onion. Drain. Stir in sauce mix and tomato sauce; heat until hot and bubbly. Combine cheese and sour cream. Pour hot meat mixture into ungreased 12 x 8 or 13 x 9-inch baking dish. Spoon cheese mixture over meat. Spread crescent rolls over top. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and butter. Bake at 375 degrees; for 18 to 25 minutes.
