POPPY SEED CHICKEN CASSEROLE


1  chicken, boiled and boned
2  c. cream of mushroom soup
1 (16 oz.)  carton sour cream
1  Tbsp. poppy seeds
10  slices bread, toasted
1  stick butter or margarine, melted

      Break chicken and pieces of bread up into small pieces.  Stir in other ingredients.  Bake at 350 degrees; for about 30 minutes.
