BROCCOLI AND CHEESE SOUP


3/4  cup chopped onion
1/2  cup shredded carrots
2  Tbsp. oil
6  cups water
6  chicken bouillon cubes
4  cups thin noodles
2   10  oz. packages broccoli
6  cups milk
1  pound velveeta cheese

      Saut the onion and carrots in the oil.  Add the water and chicken bouillon cubes until cubes are dissolved (about 15 minutes).  Add the noodles.  Cook until noodles are tender.
      In another sauce pan cook the broccoli until just tender.  Add this to the cooked noodle mixture.
      Add the milk and cheese and heat until cheese is melted.
