SUGAR HAM


2  fully cooked smoked hams, sliced
1  c. brown sugar
1 1/4  c. lemon juice

      Score each side of ham 1/4 inch deep in a diamond pattern. Combine brown sugar and lemon juice in a small pan and heat to boiling.  Place ham on grill; cook 15 minutes on each side, basting frequently with mixture.  Serve with grilled potatoes and deviled eggs.
