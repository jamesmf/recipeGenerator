EASY PLEASING MEAT LOAF


2  lb. lean ground beef
1 (6 1/4 oz.)  pkg. Stove Top stuffing mix
2  eggs, beaten
1/2  c. Kraft original barbecue sauce, divided

      Preheat oven to 375 degrees;.  Mix 1 cup water and all ingredients except 1/4 cup of barbecue sauce.  Shape meat mixture into an oval loaf in 13 x 9-inch baking dish.  Top with remaining barbecue sauce.  Bake 1 hour or until center is no longer pink.  Makes 8 servings.
