EASY GOULASH CASSEROLE


1  lb. hamburger
1  chopped onion
1  c. macaroni (raw)
1  can tomato soup
1/2  can water
1/2  c. catsup
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
  salt and pepper
3/4  c. Velveeta cheese, cubed

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees;.  Brown hamburger and onion.  Drain off fat and add to the other ingredients.  Put mixture in greased casserole and bake, covered, for 1 hour.
