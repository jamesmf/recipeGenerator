MEXICAN CORNBREAD CASSEROLE


1  lb. ground beef
1/4  c. onion, chopped
2  Tbsp. jalapeno pepper, chopped
2  eggs, beaten
2  c. cream style corn
2/3  c. milk
1 6.5  oz. pkg. cornbread mix
4  slices American cheese

      Preheat oven to 425 degrees.  Brown ground meat, drain.  Add onion and pepper, set aside.  Combine eggs, 1 cup corn and milk with cornbread mix.  Stir just till dry ingredients are moistened. Pour 1/2 of batter into greased 9 inch square pan.  Top with beef mixture, cheese slices and remaining cup of corn.  Pour remaining 1/2 of batter on top and bake 25 to 30 minutes.
