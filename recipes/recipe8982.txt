OVEN FRIED FISH FILLETS


1  lb. flounder or other fish fillets
2  Tbsp. low-fat mayonnaise
2  Tbsp. water
5  Tbsp. fine dry bread crumbs
1  tsp. parsley flakes
1  tsp. paprika

      Cut fillets into serving size pieces.  Coat fish with mixture of water and mayonnaise.  Combine bread crumbs, parsley flakes and paprika in a shallow dish.  Press fish fillets into crumb mixture to coat both sides.  Place on nonstick baking sheet.  Bake in a preheated 450 degrees; oven for 12 minutes or until fish flakes easily with a fork.  Serves 4.
