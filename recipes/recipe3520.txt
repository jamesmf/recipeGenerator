PEPPER RELISH


1  cup finely chopped green peppers
1  cup finely chopped sweet red peppers
1  small onion, chopped
1  cup vinegar
4  Tbsp. sugar
1/2  tsp. salt

      Combine the peppers and onion.  Bring the vinegar to boiling point, add the sugar and salt.  Pour over the vegetables. Let stand 2-3 hours before serving. Store in a tightly covered jar in refrigerator.  This relish will keep for about a week.
