CLASSIC MACARONI SALAD


1  recipe Old-Fashioned Egg Salad
1 (7 oz.)  box elbow macaroni, cooked and drained
1/2  c. chopped green pepper
1/2  c. mayonnaise
1 (2 oz.)  jar chopped pimientos, drained
  paprika (optional)

      In a bowl, combine egg salad, macaroni, green pepper, mayonnaise and pimientos. Cover and chill. Sprinkle with paprika, if desired. Yields 8 servings.
