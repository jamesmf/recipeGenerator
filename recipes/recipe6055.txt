FRESH CRANBERRY SALAD


1  pkg. raw cranberries
1 (3 oz.)  box cherry gelatin
1  envelope unflavored gelatin
1/4  c. cold water
1  c. sugar
2  stalks celery, chopped fine
1  ground orange, rind and all (remove pulp and end sections)
1 large  can crushed pineapple, drained
1/2 to 1  c. pecans

      Drain pineapple and reserve juice.  Dissolve cherry gelatin in 1 cup of boiling water; add drained pineapple juice.  Dissolve unflavored gelatin in 1/4 cup of cold water.  Pour into hot gelatin mix and stir.  Add sugar.  While cooling, grind cranberries, grind orange, chop celery and pecans; stir into gelatin mix, along with crushed pineapple.  Chill until mixture sets.  Can be molded or put into a serving dish.
