RUM PUDDING
(Romfromage)  

4  eggs, separated
2/3  c. sugar
1  envelope unflavored gelatin
1/4  c. water
1 1/2  oz. rum
1/2  pt. whipping cream, whipped

      Beat egg yolks and sugar until lemon colored.  Soak gelatin in 1/4 cup water and melt over hot water.
      Combine egg yolks and gelatin mixture and add rum.  Let stand until it begins to thicken.  Fold in stiffly beaten egg whites, then whipped cream.  Chill until firm.  Serves 12.  Serve with Raspberry Sauce.
