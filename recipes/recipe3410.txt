ONION SOUFFLE


1 (12 oz.)  pkg. frozen, chopped onions
3  (8 oz.) pkgs. of cream cheese, softened
2   c. grated parmesan cheese
1/2  c. mayonnaise

      Defrost onions and squeeze liquid out.  Mix all ingredients well.  Bake, uncovered, in 2 qt. casserole dish for 15 minutes at 425 degrees;.  Serve with corn chips (Scoops are best for dipping), Triscuits or Melba rounds.
