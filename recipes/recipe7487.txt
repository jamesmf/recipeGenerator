SPAGHETTI AND MEATBALLS FOR A CROWD

Meatballs:
3  lb. lean ground beef
1  lb. ground pork
4  c. onions, minced
6  cloves garlic, minced
1  c. fresh parsley, chopped
3  c. grated Parmesan cheese
3  c. finely ground bread crumbs
6  eggs
3  Tbsp. salt
3  tsp. pepper

      Mix all ingredients in a bowl and add enough water to moisten.  Shape into small balls and place in a single layer in a large baking pan.  Bake at 450 degrees; for 30 minutes.
Sauce and Spaghetti:
5  c. chopped onion
10  cloves garlic, minced
1/2  c. vegetable oil
5 (28-oz.)  cans tomatoes with juice
5  c. water
1  c. chopped parsley
1  tsp. dried basil
2 1/2  tsp. dried thyme
3  Tbsp. salt
1  tsp. pepper
1  tsp. crushed red pepper
5 (6-oz.)  cans tomato paste
4  lb. spaghetti
  Grated Parmesan cheese

      Saut√© the onion and garlic in oil in a large Dutch oven over medium-high heat for 5 minutes.  Add remaining ingredients except spaghetti and cheese; bring to boil, then lower heat and simmer for 1 1/2  hours.  Pour sauce over meatballs and cover.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 2 hours.  Cook the spaghetti according to package directions and drain well.  Serve the meatballs and sauce over the spaghetti and top with Parmesan cheese.
