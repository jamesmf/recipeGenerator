CHICKEN FETTUCINE ALFREDO


1  Tbsp. margarine
2 small  cloves garlic, minced
1  Tbsp. flour
1 1/3  c. milk
2  Tbsp. cream cheese
1 1/4  c. freshly grated parmesan cheese, divided
4  c. cooked fettucini (cooked without salt)
1  tsp. chopped fresh parsley, optional
2  c. chicken, cooked

      Saut√© garlic in margarine, then stir in flour.  Gradually add milk, stirring with a wire whisk until blended.  Cook mixture 8 minutes or until thick.  Stir in cream cheese and cook 2 more minutes.  Add 1 cup parmesan cheese, stirring until melted.  Cook fettucini according to package directions; add chicken.  Pour the hot cheese sauce over the hot fettucini and chicken.  Toss well to coat.  Top mixture with the remaining 1/4 cup of parmesan cheese and sprinkle on some black pepper.
