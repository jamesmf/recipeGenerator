MINI CHEESECAKES


2 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
2  eggs
2  tsp. vanilla
1/2  c. brown sugar
  vanilla wafers
  cherries or nutmeat

      Beat cream cheese, eggs, vanilla and brown sugar until well blended (will be slightly lumpy).  Put a vanilla wafer (round side down) in bottom of cupcake papers and fill 3/4 full with mixture. Top with cherry or nutmeat.  Bake 15 minutes at 350 degrees;.
