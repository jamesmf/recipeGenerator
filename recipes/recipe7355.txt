SATURDAY NIGHT SPECIAL


1  lb. hamburger
2  Tbsp. fat
1  Tbsp. brown sugar
3/4  c. catsup
1  tsp. mustard
1/2  tsp. salt
1  Tbsp. vinegar
2  Tbsp. diced celery
2  Tbsp. diced onion
1 (28 oz.)  can pork and beans

      Brown meat in fat; drain.  Add remaining ingredients, except beans.  Blend thoroughly.  Cook over low heat 20 to 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Add beans and heat.
