HUNGARIAN GOULASH


2  lb. beef, cubed
1/2  cup flour
1/2  cup onion, chopped
1  clove garlic, diced
3/4  cup catsup
  dry mustard, to taste
2  Tbsp. Worcestershire Sauce
1  Tbsp. brown sugar
  salt & pepper, to taste
  paprika, to taste
  water, as needed

      Saut√© onion & garlic. Dredge beef cubes in flour; brown in oil. Add other ingredients, add enough water to make gravy; simmer until all flavors blend. Serve over hot cooked noodles.
