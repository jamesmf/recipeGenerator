SPICY BEEF AND CABBAGE
(8 Carbs/Serving)  

1/4  lb. beef or turkey sausage
1  lb. ground beef
1/2  c. onion, chopped
2/3  lb. chopped tomatoes
1/4  c. tomato sauce
5  Brothers tomato alfredo
1 1/2  tsp. chili powder
1  tsp. garlic powder
  dash of red pepper flakes
2  c. shredded cabbage

      In a large Dutch oven, brown sausage, beef and onion; drain well. Add the remaining ingredients, except cabbage; mix well. Stir in the cabbage and simmer 20 minutes or until cabbage is tender. Serves 4.
