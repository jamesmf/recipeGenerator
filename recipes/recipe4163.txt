SPICED PECANS


1  egg white
1  Tbsp. water
1  c. granulated sugar
1  lb. pecan halves

      Beat egg white and water together to soft peaks. Put all ingredients into large bowl. MIX WELL. Spread into a single layer on cookie sheet (do not grease).  Bake in 250 degrees; oven for 1 hour, stirring every 15 minutes.
