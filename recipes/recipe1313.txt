BARBECUE TOFU


2  lb. previously frozen tofu
1/4  tsp. cayenne
3  Tbsp. peanut butter
1  c. oil
1  Tbsp. paprika
2  tsp. salt

      Thaw tofu.  Press out water and cut into 1 x 3-inch strips. Whip remaining ingredients until smooth.  Pour over tofu and squeeze in as evenly as possible.  Marinate 30 minutes.  While marinating, prepare sauce.
Barbecue Sauce:
2  medium onions
1/3  c. oil
2  cloves minced garlic
2 1/2  c. tomato sauce
1  c. honey
2  Tbsp. blackstrap molasses
1 1/2  tsp. salt
2  Tbsp. spike
3/4  tsp. cayenne

      Saut onions in oil and garlic.  Add remaining ingredients to onions and bring to a boil.  Reduce heat and simmer for about 30 minutes.  Lay tofu out on oiled cookie sheet.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 20 minutes or until golden brown.  Turn over and bake 20 minutes. Pour sauce over all pieces and bake 15 more minutes.
