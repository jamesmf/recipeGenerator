BARBECUE SAUCE


1  Tbsp. fat
1  Tbsp. water
1  medium onion, chopped fine
1  c. low calorie catsup
2  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  Tbsp. lemon juice
2  Tbsp. vinegar
2  cloves garlic
1  tsp. dry mustard
1  Tbsp. brown sugar
1  tsp. liquid sweetener
  pinch of oregano
  salt and pepper to taste

      Place fat in heavy pan.  Add 1 tablespoon water.  Add onion and brown.  Add remaining ingredients and simmer over low flame for 1 hour.  Remove garlic from sauce.  This sauce may be used for basting meat or chicken while cooking or as a hot dip for meats at the table.  Contains 240 calories.  Each tablespoon has 9 calories.
