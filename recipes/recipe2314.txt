ROASTED GARLIC POTATOES


2  Tbsp. vegetable oil
1  clove garlic
1  tsp. dried rosemary
4  medium potatoes
2  Tbsp. Parmesan cheese
1/4  tsp. pepper

      Lightly grease a large cookie sheet.  Wash potatoes and slice 1/8-inch thick.  Mix oil, garlic and rosemary.  Brush on potatoes. Sprinkle with cheese and pepper.  Bake at 400 degrees; for 30 minutes, until lightly browned and crisp.
