CASHEW CHICKEN BAKE


3  large chicken breasts, cooked and cut up
2  cans condensed cream of chicken soup
1 (15 oz.)  can fancy Oriental vegetables, drained
1  Tbsp. chopped onion
1/2  lb. sliced mushrooms
1  c. chopped celery
1/2  c. mayonnaise
1 (5 or 6 oz.)  can water chestnuts, sliced and drained
1  tsp. lemon juice
1 (3 oz.)  can chow mein noodles
2 to 3  c. cashews

      On the day before the casserole is to be served, mix all items together except the cashews.  Place in a 9 x 13-inch casserole and refrigerate.  Before baking, cover top with cashews.  Bake 45 minutes at 350 degrees;.
