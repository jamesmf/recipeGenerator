SOUTHERN CANNON BALLS


1  lb. hot bulk sausage
2  c. shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
2  c. biscuit mix
2  Tbsp. grated onions
1  Tbsp. poultry seasoning

	Combine all ingredients, mixing well. Roll into walnut-size balls. Place on ungreased baking sheet and bake at 400 degrees; for 15 minutes. Drain on absorbent paper. Serve hot!
