NETHERLANDS SALAD


3/4  head lettuce
1/2  c. cooked chicken, cut in strips
1/2  c. ham, cut in strips
1  Tbsp. chopped dill pickle
1/3  c. tomatoes, julienned

 
Dressing:
1  tsp. chopped chives
1  chopped hard-boiled egg
3  Tbsp. mayonnaise
2  Tbsp. tarragon vinegar
3  Tbsp. olive oil
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce

      Blend dressing and serve over salad.
