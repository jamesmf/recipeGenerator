OYSTER CASSEROLE


4  (12 oz.) containers oysters
1  c. chopped green onions with tops
1  c. minced fresh parsley
2  c. cracker crumbs
3/4  c. butter, melted
3  Tbsp. lemon juice
1  tsp. dry mustard
1  Tbsp. plus 2 tsp. Worcestershire sauce

      Drain oysters. Pat dry with paper towels. Arrange oysters in 12 x 8 x 2-inch glass baking dish. Top with layers of green onion, 1 cup parsley and cracker crumbs. Combine next 4 ingredients, mixing well. Pour evenly over cracker crumbs. Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 to 35 minutes. Yield: 12 servings.
