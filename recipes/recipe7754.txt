WILD RICE CASSEROLE
(Good Stick To Your Ribs Dish!)  

1  box Uncle Ben's wild rice
1 large  can B & B mushrooms
1  lb. pork sausage
1  small onion, chopped
1  small bell pepper, chopped
1  can Campbell's cream of mushroom soup
1  c. Kraft sharp grated cheese
1/2  stick margarine

      Brown sausage and drain. Add onions, peppers and mushrooms. Mix rice according to package. Add sausage and vegetables. Stir in soup. Stir in 3/4 cup of cheese. Top with remaining 1/4 cup. Use cooking instructions on rice box.
