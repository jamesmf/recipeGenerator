SPANISH CHICKEN CASSEROLE


1  chicken, cooked and deboned
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  can cream of celery soup
1  can Rotel tomatoes
1  lb. Velveeta cheese
1  large bag Nacho Cheese Doritos
3/4  c. water
  salt and pepper to taste

      Cook and debone chicken. Melt cheese, set aside. Combine soups, water, Rotel tomatoes, and chicken; bring to a boil; remove from heat. Layer chips, cheese and chicken mixture in a large baking dish. Top with cheese; bake at 350 degrees; until hot and bubbly.
      Tip: Sliced cheese may be used between layers and on the top.
