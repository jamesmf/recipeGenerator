LADYE'S WEDDING MOCHA PUNCH


1  gal. strong coffee
2  c. sugar
3  cans Hershey's syrup (3 or 4-inches tall)
1 to 2  tsp. vanilla
  Amaretto or kahlua (to spice up)
2  gal. milk
  French vanilla ice cream (10 to 15 scoops)
  cinnamon/nutmeg/chocolate shavings
  chocolate syrup
  maraschino cherries

      Mix together first 4 (or 5) ingredients.  Put in refrigerator. Gradually add 2 gallons of milk to mixture.  Garnish with whipped ice cream.  Add cinnamon, nutmeg, chocolate shavings, cherries and chocolate syrup on top.
