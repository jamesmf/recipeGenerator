HEAVENLY SALAD


1  pkg. lime or lemon Jello
1 small  pkg. Cream Cheese
1 small  can crushed Pineapple
1  c. boiling Water
3/4  c. chopped Nuts
1/2  pt. Cool Whip

      Dissolve jello in boiling water.  Allow to cool.  To cream cheese add pineapple and nuts.  Stir into jello. Fold in cool whip and pour into mold.  Chill and serve when firm.
