VANILLA ICE CREAM


1  tsp. gelatin
1/4  c. cold water
1 1/2  c. milk
1  Tbsp. flour
  dash of salt
1  beaten egg
4  tsp. liquid sweetener
2  tsp. vanilla
1 1/2  c. evaporated skim milk

      Soften gelatin in water.  Cook milk, flour, salt, egg and sweetener until mixture coats spoon.  Add softened gelatin and vanilla.  Whip evaporated milk until thick.  Fold into custard mixture.  Chill and freeze in ice cream freezer.  Yields:  1 quart; 16 calories per ounce.
