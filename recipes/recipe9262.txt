PARMESAN FRIED CHICKEN


1/4  c. all purpose flour
1/4  c. parmesan cheese, grated
1/4  tsp. black pepper
1  large egg white, lightly beaten
1  Tbsp. olive oil
3  boned chicken breasts, flattened

      Mix flour, parmesan cheese and pepper together.   Dip each chicken breast into egg white then dredge in flour mixture.  Heat oil in skillet and cook chicken on each side for 5 to 6 minutes, or until done.
