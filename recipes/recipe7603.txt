REVA'S MANICOTTI


1 large  jar of Ragu sauce
1  box manicotti noodles
1  container ricotta cheese
  Parsley
1/2  c. parmesan
  Salt and pepper
2  c. mozzarella

      Add 1 c. water to large jar of Ragu.  Place small amount in 9 x 13 pan.  Fill uncooked noodles and place in pan.  Cover with remaining sauce.  Cover with foil and bake at 350 degrees; for about 1 hr. If using glass pan, cook longer.
