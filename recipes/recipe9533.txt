EASY BAKED SHRIMP


3  to 4 large or jumbo shrimp
  Cheese Tid-Bits
1/2  c. melted butter
1 1/2  c. crushed crumbs
  pepper and garlic powder to taste

      Peel and devein shrimp.  Leave tail on and butterfly shrimp. Sprinkle Cheese Tid-Bits generously on shrimp. Dot with melted butter.  Add pepper and garlic powder.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 15 minutes.
