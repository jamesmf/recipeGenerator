SAUSAGE BALLS


1  lb. hot or mild sausage
3  c. Bisquick
8 to 10  oz. Cheddar cheese

      Grate cheese. Mix all ingredients well. Roll into balls the size of a quarter and bake on a large cookie sheet at 350 degrees; for 25 to 30 minutes (until brown). Place in a bowl lined with paper towels to absorb some of the grease.
