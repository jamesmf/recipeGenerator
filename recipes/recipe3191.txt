


2 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
2  tsp. lemon juice
1/4  tsp. prepared horseradish
1/4  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. hot sauce
2  tsp. minced onion
1/2  medium cucumber, peeled and finely chopped

      Beat cream cheese until smooth. Add next 4 ingredients and mix well. Stir in onion and cucumber. Put in covered container. Chill 1 hour before serving.
