ZUCCHINI RELISH


6  (4 to 6-inch) zucchini
2  c. onions
1  large green pepper
1/6  c. salt
1/2  tsp. turmeric
2 1/4  c. sugar
1/8  c. mustard seeds
1 1/4  c. cider vinegar

      You will need 6 small jars.
      Grind zucchini, onions and pepper; mix well. Add salt and mix again. Let stand overnight (room temperature).
      Next day, strain and rinse with cold water. Add remaining ingredients. Boil in a large pot for 20 minutes. Place into jars that have been sterilized.
