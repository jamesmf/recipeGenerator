CRYSTAL BOWL SALAD


  1-2 lb. bag cut up lettuce
1  can chunk pineapple (drained)
2  cans mandarin oranges (drained)
1  pint strawberries (washed and sliced)
1  pkg. sunflower seeds (not in shell)

 
Dressing:
2  c. splenda
1  tsp. dry mustard
2-3/4  c. oil
2  tbsp. onion
3  tbsp. poppy seed
1-1/3  c. white vinegar

      Mix all salad ingredients and toss. Blend splenda, mustard, oil, onion, poppy seed, and vinegar. Blend very well in blender.
      Toss and enjoy!!!
