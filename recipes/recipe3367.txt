SPICY BUFFALO WINGS


24  chicken wing/drumette pieces
1  envelope (1.0 oz.) Hidden Valley Ranch Milk Recipe Original Ranch Salad Dressing Mix
1/2  c. butter, melted
1/4  c. hot pepper sauce
3  Tbsp. vinegar
1/2  tsp. paprika

      Preheat oven to 400 degrees;. Dredge chicken in dry salad dressing mix.  Dip in mixture of butter, pepper sauce and vinegar.  Arrange in baking pan and sprinkle with paprika.  Bake 25-30 minutes. Turn, sprinkle with paprika and bake at 450 degrees; for 20-25 minutes, or until brown.  Serve with chunky blue cheese salad dressing/dip and celery sticks.
