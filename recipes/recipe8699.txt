SAUERKRAUT CASSEROLE


1 large  can sauerkraut, drained
1 small  can mushrooms, chopped
1  medium bell pepper, thinly sliced
2  whole onions, chopped
2  Tbsp. butter
1 (12 oz.)  can chopped tomatoes with juice
1  large link fully cooked Polish sausage, sliced 1/4-inch thick

	Drain the sauerkraut and set aside. Preheat oven to 350 degrees;.  In a large saucepan, saut mushrooms, peppers and onions in a little butter until soft. Add sauerkraut, tomatoes and sliced sausage mixing well after each addition.  Place in a covered dish and bake for 1 hour.  If casserole is too juicy, uncover and bake for additional 30 minutes.
