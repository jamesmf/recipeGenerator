BAKED TUNA PATTIES


1/4  c. finely chopped celery
6  Tbsp. butter or margarine
3  Tbsp. flour
1/4  tsp. paprika
3/4  c. milk
1/4  tsp. salt
1 (9 1/4 oz.)  can tuna
2  c. soft bread crumbs
2  Tbsp. chopped green onions
1  egg
2/3  c. finely crushed crackers

      In a saucepan, cook celery in 3 tablespoons of butter until tender. Stir in flour, paprika and salt. Stir in milk; cook and stir until thickened and bubbly. Cool slightly. Drain and flake tuna. Mix into sauce with bread crumbs and onions. Form into 8 (2 1/2-inch) patties. Beat egg with 1 tablespoon water. Dip patties into egg and then crackers. Place in lightly greased baking pan. Melt remaining butter and drizzle over patties. Bake at 350 degrees; for 20 to 25 minutes or until golden. Serves 4.
