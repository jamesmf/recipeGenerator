HOT DOG SAUCE


2  lbs. ground beef
2  lg. onions, chopped
1/4  c. vinegar
1/4  c. brown sugar
2 - 3  Tbsp. chili powder
2  tsp. salt
1- 8  oz. can tomato sauce
3  c. tomato juice

      Cook ground beef, onions, and salt until browned.  Drain. Mix with rest of ingredients and simmer for 1 or 1 1/2 hours.
