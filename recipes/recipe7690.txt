ROAST IN FOIL


1  roast
1  envelope dry onion soup mix
2  cans cream of mushroom soup
2  cans water

      Line 9 x 13-inch pan with large piece of aluminum foil. Place roast on foil; sprinkle with  onion  soup.  Mix mushroom soup with water.  Pour over roast.  Place  another  piece  of foil over top and seal edges well.  Bake  in preheated 325 degrees; to 350 degrees;  for  1 hour per pound or until  done.  Remove  roast  to platter and gravy into gravy dish.
