POPPY SEED CHICKEN


1  can creamy chicken mushroom soup
1  c. sour cream
6  chicken breasts, cooked and shredded
1/2  c. chicken broth
  salt and pepper to taste
1  Tbsp. poppy seeds
1 sleeve Ritz crackers, crushed and mixed with 1  stick melted margarine

      Mix soup, sour cream, shredded chicken, chicken broth and salt and pepper.  Pour into 13 x 9-inch baking pan.  Top with cracker mixture.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 20 to 30 minutes.
