MEATZA PIE


2  lb. ground beef
1 1/3  c. milk
1/2  c. fine dry bread crumbs
2  tsp. garlic salt
1 (4 oz.)  can mushrooms
2/3  c. ketchup
2  c. shredded Cheddar cheese
4  Tbsp Parmesan cheese
1/2  tsp. oregano

	Mix meat, milk, bread crumbs and garlic salt.  Lightly pat into the bottom of a jelly roll pan.  Spread ketchup over the meat. Arrange mushrooms on top.  Cover with shredded cheese. Sprinkle oregano and Parmesan cheese over all.  Bake in hot 450 degrees; oven for 20 minutes.
