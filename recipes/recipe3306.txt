DORITO DIP


8  oz. cream cheese
1  can chili with beans
4  oz. can chili peppers (mild)
1 1/2  c. grated Cheddar cheese
1 1/2  c. grated Mozzarella cheese
1  bag Doritos

      Spread cream cheese on the bottom of a 10-inch Pyrex pie plate.  Then, in order, layer the next 4 ingredients on top of the cheese.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 20 minutes (or microwave for 7 to 9 minutes).  Dip Doritos in the hot dip.
