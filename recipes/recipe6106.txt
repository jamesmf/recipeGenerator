BROCCOLI TURKEY SALAD


8  oz. (1 can) pineapple chunks, unsweetened
2  c. salad greens, torn
2  c. spinach (fresh), torn
2  c. broccoli florets
1  green pepper, cut in strips
1/3  c. red onion, thin sliced
2  c. turkey, cooked and cubed
1 1/2  c. olive oil
1  Tbsp. vinegar, red wine
1  Tbsp. poppy seeds
1  tsp. sugar
2  tsp. Dijon mustard

      Drain pineapple, reserving 2 tablespoons of the juice and set aside.
      In a large  bowl combine greens, spinach, broccoli, pepper, onion, turkey and pineapple.
      In a small bowl combine 2 tablespoons pineapple juice, olive oil, vinegar, poppy seed, sugar and mustard.  Mix well.  Pour over salad greens and serve immediately.
      Makes 10 servings (1 serving = 1 cup = 111 calories and 7 grams of fat).
