TANDOORI CHICKEN


  3-5 lb. chicken (cleaned and cut)
5  tsp. ginger/garlic paste
1-1/2  cayenne pepper
2  tsp. Tandoori paste or powder
1-1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. turmeric
  3-4 Tbsp. cilantro, chopped fine
2  Tbsp. oil

      Mix all ingredients and marinate for 6-24 hours.  Bake covered at 400 degrees;F for a half hour.  Bake uncovered another half hour.
