BURGERS


1  lb. hamburger meat
  salt and pepper to taste
2  large onions, chopped
4  Tbsp. sweet relish
2  Tbsp. prepared mustard
1/4  c. mayonnaise

      Brown meat and seasoning in skillet.  Drain grease from meat. Add onions, sweet relish, prepared mustard and mayonnaise.  Remove from heat immediately and serve on hamburger buns.
