CHICKEN BROCCOLI SPEARS


2  pkg. frozen broccoli spears or 1 large head fresh broccoli  - cut up
6  boneless chicken breasts, cooked
2 (10 3/4 oz.)  cans cream of chicken soup
1  c. mayonnaise
2  c. grated cheese
  bread crumbs or browned cornflake crumbs

	Cut up frozen broccoli or fresh broccoli into small bite size pieces.  Put in buttered casserole dish.  Cut up cooked chicken and place over broccoli in casserole.  Mix cream of chicken and mayonnaise - spread over chicken and broccoli. Sprinkle with cheese then breadcrumbs.  Bake at 375 degrees; for about 3/4  hour until done.  6 servings.
