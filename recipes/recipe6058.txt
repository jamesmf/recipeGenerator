BLUEBERRY SALAD

Gelatin Mixture:
1  box Royal blackberry jello
1 (16 oz.)  can crushed pineapple, drained
1  c. boiling water
1  pt. fresh blueberries

      Mix all ingredients.  Chill until set.
Topping:
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
1  (8 oz.) container sour cream
1/2  c. sugar
1  tsp. vanilla
1/2  c. chopped nuts

      Mix together all ingredients except nuts; pour on chilled salad.  Top with chopped nuts.
