TACO RING


1  lb. ground beef
2  pkg. crescent rolls
1  pkg. taco seasoning
2  c. grated cheese

      Brown meat, drain grease.  Add taco seasoning as directed on package.  Set aside.  Arrange crescent rolls flat in the shape of a ring.  Fill with meat, top with cheese.  Fold the edges of the rolls over the filling.  Bake at 350 degrees; until golden brown.  May be served with cream cheese, salsa, lettuce, tomatoes, and/or sour cream if desired.
