HAMBURGER SOUP


1  lb. ground beef, browned and drained
5  c. water
1 (16 oz.)  can tomatoes, cut up
1  c. onions, chopped
1  c. carrots, sliced
1  c. celery, chopped
1/3  c. pearl barley
1/4  c. catsup
1  Tbsp. instant beef bouillon
2  tsp. seasoned salt
1  bay leaf

      Bring to a boil. Reduce heat. Simmer for 1 hour. Add salt and pepper to taste.  Freezes well.
