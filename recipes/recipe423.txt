SNACK PIZZA



1  can biscuit mix
1  Ragu Pizza Quick sauce
  grated cheese
  anything else you like on pizza

      Let kids roll out their own biscuit dough and put Ragu Pizza Sauce on dough.  Then they may add grated cheese and any other toppings they like, such as mushrooms or sausage.  Bake at 400 degrees; for 8 to 10 minutes.
