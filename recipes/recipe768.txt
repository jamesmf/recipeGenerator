SPICY CHICKEN WINGS



1  c. flour
1  Tbsp. seasoned salt
2  tsp. red pepper flakes
1 3/4  lb. chicken wings
1/2  stick melted butter
1  Tbsp. lemon juice
1  tsp. Tabasco sauce
1  Tbsp. ground red pepper

 
Dip:
1  cucumber, peeled, seeded and minced
1/2  c. sour cream
1/2  c. plain yogurt
1  tsp. dill
  white pepper to taste

      Mix flour, seasoned salt and red pepper flakes.  Dredge wings in flour mixture.  Fry in hot oil.  Set aside.  Blend butter, lemon juice, Tabasco sauce and red pepper.  Dredge chicken in this mixture.  Warm to serve.
      Dip:  Drain minced cucumber for 1 hour.  Mix with sour cream, yogurt and dill; season to taste with white pepper.
