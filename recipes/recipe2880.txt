HOLIDAY CHEESECAKE


3 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
5  eggs
1  c. sugar
1/4  c. Amaretto almond liqueur
1/4  tsp. almond extract

 
Topping:
1 1/2  c. sour cream
2  Tbsp. sugar
1  tsp. vanilla extract

      Blend cream cheese, eggs, sugar, almond liqueur and almond extract; pour into graham cracker crumb shell (spring-form mold works well).  Bake at 325 degrees; for 50 minutes or until knife comes out without any cake on it.
      Topping:  Combine sour cream, sugar and vanilla extract; pour over the top of the cake.  Bake for 10 minutes.
