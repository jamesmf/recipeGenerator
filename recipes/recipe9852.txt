SPINACH ROCKEFELLER


1 1/2  lb. spinach (fresh or frozen)
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
1/2  tsp. garlic salt
1/3  c. bread crumbs
2  Tbsp. butter

      Cook spinach and drain.  Put back in pan; add cheese, stirring, over low heat.  Add garlic salt; pour into 1-quart casserole dish.  Top with bread crumbs; dot with butter.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 20 minutes.
