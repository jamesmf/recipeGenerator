THE BEST BEEF STEW


2  lb. boneless stew beef
2  onions, chopped
1  or 2 garlic cloves, to taste
1  Tbsp. salt
1  Tbsp. lemon juice
1  tsp. sugar
1  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/2  tsp. black pepper
1/2  tsp. paprika
2  bay leaves
  dash of cloves
4  cups water
4  carrots, sliced
6  medium potatoes, cubed
15  oz. can peas
15  oz. can whole kernel corn
  quart tomatoes

      Add to meat mixture and cook until vegetables are tender.
