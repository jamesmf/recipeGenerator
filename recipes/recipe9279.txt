MEAT AND POTATO CASSEROLE


2  slices bacon
1  lb. ground beef
1/3  c. onion, chopped
1  c. bread cubes (dry)
1/4  c. ketchup
1  tsp. chili powder
1/2  tsp. pepper
4  c. mashed potatoes (cooked and hot)
1/2  c. Cheddar cheese, shredded
1  egg, beaten
1  tsp. salt

      In a skillet, fry bacon until crisp, then put bacon on paper towel to drain.  Add ground beef and onion to drippings in skillet.  Cook until ground beef is brown and crumbly.  Add bread cubes, ketchup, chili powder and pepper.  Mix well and set aside.
      In a bowl, mix together:  mashed potatoes, cheese, egg and salt. Spread half of the potato mixture in a greased 1 1/2-quart baking dish.  Add ground beef mixture.  Top with remaining potato mixture.
      Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes.  Crumble bacon over top.
