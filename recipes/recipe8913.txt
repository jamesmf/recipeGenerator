BREAKFAST RICE


14  oz. box Minute rice
1  lb. bacon, chopped
8  eggs
  soy sauce
  chopped green onions (optional)

      Cook Minute rice according to directions. Fry bacon and drain. Scramble eggs. Chop green onions. In large bowl, combine all these ingredients after cooking. Season with soy sauce to taste.
      Suggestions: Serve with orange juice and fresh fruit.
