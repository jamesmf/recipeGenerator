HOT PEANUT BUTTER SAUCE
(For Dipping Meats And Vegetables)  

1  Tbsp. butter
1  small onion, chopped
2  Tbsp. chunky peanut butter
1  Tbsp. soy sauce
1  tsp. lemon juice
1  tsp. soy
1  tsp. peanut oil
1  tsp. dried red pepper flakes
1/2  c. milk

      This is a very hot dipping sauce used in Indonesian recipes or with noodles, beef or chicken.
      Heat butter in heavy pan; saut√© onion until it begins to brown. Add peanut butter, one tablespoon soy sauce, lemon juice and the mixture of soy, peanut oil and red pepper flakes.  Mix well. Continue stirring.  Slowly add milk to make a creamy sauce.  Makes one cup.
