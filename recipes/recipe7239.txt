TERIYAKI STEAK


4  servings steak (2 lb.), cut 1/2-inch thick
1  c. soy sauce
1  clove garlic, grated
2  tsp. ginger, grated
6  Tbsp. sugar
2  tsp. olive oil

      Mix preceding ingredients well. Marinate meat in mixture for a least an hour. Broil meat over charcoal or in oven until desired doneness, turning once. Slice across the grain.
