HAWAIIAN BREAD DIP


1 1/3  c. mayo
2  Tbsp. parsley
1  tsp. garlic powder
12  to 15 black olives, chopped
1  tsp. celery salt
1 1/3  c. sour cream
2  Tbsp. dehydrated onions
1/2  tsp. paprika
1  tsp. dill weed

      Combine ingredients.  Hollow out the center of a round Hawaiian bread and pour dip in the hollow.  Place pieces of bread around the loaf of bread and dip.
