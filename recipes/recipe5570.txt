ORIENTAL CHUNKY SALAD


1- 16  oz. bag shredded cole slaw
1- 2  oz. bag sliced almonds
1  Tbsp. sesame seeds, toasted
1  pkg. Ramen chicken soup noodles (dry uncooked)
3  green onions, sliced fine
1  Granny Smith apple, chopped (optional)

 
Dressing:
1  packet seasoning from the Ramen soup package
3  Tbsp. balsamic vinegar
2  Tbsp. sugar
1/8 to 1/4  c. canola oil

      Mix first six  ingredients (except noodles). Mix dressing and pour over salad and refrigerate at least 1 hour or until ready to serve. Add uncooked Ramen noodles just before serving.
