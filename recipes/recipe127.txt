CARROT WALNUT COOKIES


1  c. shortening
2  c. brown sugar
2  eggs
1/2  c. warm water
  pinch of salt
1  tsp. vanilla
4  c. flour
1 1/2  tsp. soda
2  c. shredded carrots
1/2 to 1  c. chopped walnuts

 
Frosting:
2  c. powdered sugar
1/2  tsp. pineapple flavoring
1 1/2  tsp. water

      Mix together the shortening, brown sugar and eggs.  Add water, salt, vanilla, flour and soda.  Mix well.  Add carrots and nuts. Drop onto ungreased cookie sheet.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 15 minutes.  Cool, then ice with icing.
      Frosting:  Mix together all frosting ingredients.  Blend well.
