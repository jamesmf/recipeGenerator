HARVEY HOUSE SLAW

Slaw Mixture:
1  large head cabbage, shredded or grated
1  onion, chopped
1  c. sugar
  chopped green pepper and shredded carrots, optional

      Mix together the vegetables and sprinkle the sugar on top. Set aside.
Dressing:
1  Tbsp. sugar
1  c. vinegar
3/4  c. Wesson oil (can cut down to 1/2 c. if desired)
1  tsp. dry mustard
1  tsp. celery seed
1  tsp. to 1 Tbsp. salt, as desired

      In a small saucepan, bring all dressing ingredients to a rolling boil.  Pour over cabbage immediately.  Cover to hold steam.  Do not stir; cover and put in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours.  Stir well when ready to serve.  Will keep for weeks in the refrigerator.  Yield: 12 to 16 servings.
