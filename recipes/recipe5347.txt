ORANGE CONGEALED SALAD


1  small apricot jello
1  small orange jello
1  envelope Knox gelatine
1 small  can frozen orange juice
1 large  can crushed pineapple (undrained)
1 small  can mandarin oranges, drained

 
Topping:
1 small  pkg. vanilla or lemon instant pudding
1  c. milk
8  oz. Cool Whip

      Dissolve jellos and Knox gelatine in 2 cups boiling water. Add orange juice, pineapple and oranges. Pour into large dish and congeal.
       Topping: Mix all ingredients together and top gelatine mixture.
