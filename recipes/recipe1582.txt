CRAWFISH CASSEROLE


5  to 6 medium crawfish (or lobster) tails
8  oz. pkg. medium noodles
2  c. evaporated milk
2  c. cream of mushroom soup
1  tsp. Accent
2  onions
  Season-All
  ketchup to color
  bread crumbs on top (optional)

      Boil crawfish and shell.  Cook noodles for five minutes.  Mix together all ingredients.  Bake 45 minutes to 1 hour at 350 degrees;. Serves 6 to 8. Serve with vegetables and salad.
