ENCHILADAS


1  lb. ground beef
1  onion
1  can enchilada sauce
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  pkg. flour tortillas, quartered
8  oz. Cheddar cheese, shredded
  refried beans and jalapeno peppers (optional)

      Brown ground beef and onion; drain.  Combine soup and enchilada sauce.  Grease a large casserole and layer tortillas and meat mixture.  Top with cheese.  Repeat layers until all mixture is used.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 35 to 40 minutes.
       For a little more spice, add a can of refried beans and chopped jalapeno peppers.
