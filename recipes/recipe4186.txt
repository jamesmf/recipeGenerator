CUCUMBER SANDWICH SPREAD


2  large cucumbers
1/2  c. vinegar
1/2  tsp. garlic salt
2 8  oz. pkg. cream cheese
3/4  c. mayonnaise

      Wash and grate unpeeled cucumbers using grater with 1/2 inch holes. add vinegar and salt, stir; cover and allow to stand overnight in refrigerator. Next day press out as much liquid as possible. Blend garlic salt, cream cheese and mayonnaise, combine with cucumbers.
