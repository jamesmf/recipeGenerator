APRICOT JELLO


6  oz. apricot Jell-O
2/3  C. sugar
2/3  C. water
8  oz. cream cheese, softened
1 junior size  jar apricot baby food with tapioca
1 large  can crushed pineapple, not drained
1 lrg.  can evaporated milk
1  C. nut meats, optional

      Bring Jell-O, sugar and water to boil, then add cream cheese, apricot baby food   and crushed pineapple; not drained.  Set aside and let cool.
      When cool, whip evaporated milk; then fold into Jell-O mixture.  Add nut meats, if desired.
      Serves 20.
