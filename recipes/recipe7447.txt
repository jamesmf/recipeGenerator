SAUSAGE OR HAM CASSEROLE


  cubed bread (enough to cover bottom of 9 x 13-inch baking dish)
1  lb. sausage or ham
  finely chopped onion (small to medium)
  chopped green pepper
  lots of grated cheese
3  c. milk
6  eggs, whipped with milk

      Cover bottom of baking dish with cubed bread.  Spread over cubed bread, 1 pound cooked and drained sausage or ham.  Sprinkle with chopped onion and green pepper.  Add lots of grated cheese and eggs, whipped with milk.  Cover well and refrigerate overnight. Cook at 350 degrees; for 35 minutes.
      You may add the cooked sausage and ham as the last ingredient. It is your preference.
