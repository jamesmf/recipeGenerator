EASY SPICY APPLE MOLD


2/3  c. red cinnamon candies (red hots)
1 1/2  c. hot water
3  to 4 medium apples
1 (3 oz.)  pkg. cherry gelatin

      Heat water in a medium saucepan.  Dissolve the cinnamon candies in the hot water.  Add apples. Cook until apples are tender.  Add gelatin; stir until dissolved.  Pour into your prettiest mold.  Excellent served with ham.
