THANKSGIVING APPLE CAKE


3  eggs
1 1/2  c. salad oil
2  c. sugar
3  c. flour
1/4  c. milk
1  Tbsp. cinnamon
1  tsp. soda
1  tsp. salt
2  tsp. vanilla
3  c. chopped McIntosh apples
1  c. chopped pecans

 
Topping:
1/4  lb. butter
1  c. brown sugar
1/4  c. milk

      Combine ingredients in order listed:  eggs, salad oil, sugar, flour, milk, cinnamon, soda, salt, vanilla, apples and pecans. (The amounts of cinnamon and pecans may be adjusted to suit your taste.)  Spread in a 9 x 13-inch cake pan; bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour.
      While cake is still hot, ice with topping.  Combine butter, brown sugar and milk; bring to a boil and cook 2 1/2 minutes. Spread over cake.
