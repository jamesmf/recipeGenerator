SCALLOPED POTATOES


8  medium size potatoes
1  stick margarine
1  c. grated mild Cheddar cheese
1  c. milk
  salt and pepper
2  Tbsp. flour

      Peel and slice potatoes.  Add water and boil until tender, about 15 to 20 minutes.  Remove from heat; drain water.  Pour potatoes in large greased casserole dish.  Slice margarine over potatoes.  Sprinkle cheese on top.  Mix milk with flour, salt and pepper.  Pour over potatoes.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes.  Serve hot.
