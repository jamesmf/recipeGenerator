POTATOES AU GRATIN DELUXE


24  oz. frozen shredded potatoes
2  c. rich milk
1  c. half and half
1  cube butter

      Put milk, half and half, butter, salt and pepper in pan.  Let come to a boil.  Put in the softened potatoes and let it come to a boil again.  It should be thick.  Add some Parmesan cheese to taste.  Bake in a 325 degrees; oven for 45 minutes or until brown.
