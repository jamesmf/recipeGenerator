POTATO-LEEK SOUP


2  medium potatoes
3  large leeks bottoms
1/2  c. celery, chopped
1/2  c. carrots, grated
1/2  c. onion, chopped
1  pt. (or more) chicken broth
1 small  clove garlic
1  tsp. Accent seasoning
  salt (to taste)
  pepper (to taste)

      Cook potatoes, leeks, celery, carrots and onion in enough water to keep from sticking until tender.  Add chicken broth, garlic, Accent, salt, and pepper.
      Serve hot with saltines or other crackers.
