BOSTON-STYLE FISH CHOWDER


3  slices bacon
1/2  c. chopped celery
1/2  c. chopped onion
1  clove garlic
1/4  c. flour
4  c. flour
2  tbsp. chicken flavor bouillon or 6 bouillon cubes
1 1/2  c. diced potatoes
1  lb. white fish fillets, fresh or f rozen, thawed and cut into bite-size pieces
2  c. light cream
2  tbsp. chopped pimiento

      In large Dutch oven, cook bacon till crisp; remove and crumble.  In drippings, cook celery, onion and garlic until tender.  Stir in flour.  Gradually add water and bouillon, stirring until smooth and well blended.  Bring to a boil.  Add potatoes; reduce heat and cook 10 mins.  Stir in fish; cook 15 mins. longer.  Add cream and pimiento;  heat through.  Garnish with bacon.  Refrigerate leftovers.
