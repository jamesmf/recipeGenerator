PEGGY'S BEEF SPREAD


8  oz. cream cheese
1/4  c. sour cream
1  tsp. horseradish
1/4  c. grated Parmesan cheese
1/2  c. chipped beef (buy 2 1/2 oz. jar), remove rind and chip into pieces

      Mix cream cheese, sour cream, horseradish, Parmesan cheese and chipped beef all together.  Spread on Wheat Thins or Ritz crackers.
