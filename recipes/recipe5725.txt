CRANBERRY JELLO SALAD


4  boxes small raspberry Jello
5  apples, peeled, diced
2  sticks celery, diced
2  cans whole cranberry sauce
1  can crushed pineapple, drained
3/4  c. nuts

      Make jello according to directions.  Add cranberry sauce to jello and mash.  Add remaining ingredients and chill 24 hours.
