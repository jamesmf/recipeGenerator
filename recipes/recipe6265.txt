SAUCE BEARNAISE


1/4  c. white vinegar (tarragon preferred)
1/4  c. dry white wine
1  Tbsp. finely chopped shallots
2  Tbsp. fresh, finely cut tarragon
1  Tbsp. fresh, finely cut parsley
1 1/2  c. Hollandaise made without lemon juice
  salt and pepper to taste

      Reduce by boiling vinegar, wine, shallots and tarragon to 2 tablespoons.  Strain and incorporate into Hollandaise with parsley.  Makes 2 cups.
