SAUERKRAUT SALAD


1  can (16 oz.) sauerkraut, drained and chopped
1  medium onion, chopped
1  medium green pepper, chopped
1  c. chopped celery
1  c. sugar
1/2  c. vinegar

      Place sauerkraut, onion, pepper and celery in large bowl. Combine sugar and vinegar; bring to a boil.  Pour over vegetables. Mix well.  Chill.
