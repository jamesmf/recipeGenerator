SUPER EASY POTATO SOUP


2 large  cans potato soup
1  can Cheddar cheese soup
2  Herdez Salsa Verde
1  can chopped green chilies
1  pt. half and half
3  to 5 potatoes, cut up and cooked
  bacon bits
  minced onion
  salt and pepper
  milk (if needed)

      This is a rich and satisfying chicken soup. To make it heartier, add carrots or other suitable vegetables.
