HUNGRY BOYS CASSEROLE


1 1/2  lb. ground beef
1/2  c. diced celery
1/2  c. chopped onion
3/4  c. water
1 (6 oz.)  can tomato sauce
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. paprika
1/2  tsp. monosodium glutamate
1 (1 lb.)  can pork and beans
1 (1 lb.)  can chickpeas or lima beans

      Saut√© ground beef, celery and onion in large skillet. Add water, tomato sauce, salt, paprika and monosodium glutamate. Add pork and beans. Simmer while preparing your favorite biscuit recipe; don't bake. Put meat mixture into 12 x 8-inch or 13 x 9-inch baking dish. Top with biscuits. Bake at 425 degrees; for 25 to 30 minutes until biscuits are golden brown. Yield: 6 to 8 servings.
