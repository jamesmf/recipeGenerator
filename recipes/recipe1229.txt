APPLE-OATMEAL BARS


1  c. quick cooking oatmeal
1  c. flour
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. cinnamon
1/4  tsp. nutmeg
3/4  tsp. soda
1/3  c. soft margarine
1  egg
1  tsp. vanilla
2/3  c. apple juice frozen concentrate, thawed
2  c. thinly sliced apples
1/2  c. raisins
1/2  c. nuts

      Combine dry ingredients.  Cream margarine and add egg, vanilla and apple juice concentrate.  Stir in dry ingredients.  Add thinly sliced apples, nuts, raisins and oatmeal.  Spray a 9 x 12-inch pan evenly in pan.  Bake in preheated 350 degrees; oven for 25 minutes.  Cut into 20 squares.
      Exchanges:  1 bar = 1 bread.  Calories:  1 bar = 100.
