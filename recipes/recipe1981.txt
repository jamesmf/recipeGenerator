BREAD PUDDING
(Spanish Style)  

1  (20 oz.) loaf white bread
2/3  c. sugar
1/4  c. margarine or butter
1/2  c. raisins
2  eggs
1  tsp. vanilla
1/2  tsp. ground cinnamon
1/4  c. pancake syrup
2  Tbsp. oil
  water
1/2  c. milk
1/2  c. cream of coconut (optional)

      Bread can be substituted with old pieces of hardened Italian bread.
      Soak bread in a large container of water for at least 4 hours or overnight.  After soaking, drain and squeeze out water; set aside.  Mix butter or margarine with sugar; add eggs, vanilla, pancake syrup and cinnamon.  Mix well.  Add oil and bread; fold in raisins.  Add cream of coconut, if desired.  Add milk and mix very well.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes, until firm or until toothpick inserted in middle comes out clean.  Remove from pan while hot. Enjoy!
