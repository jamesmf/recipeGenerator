WHITE BEAN AND OLIVE SALAD


1 (15 oz.)  can white beans, rinsed and drained
1  c. pitted ripe olives, halved and drained
1/2  c. chopped red onion
1  c. tomatoes (1/2 lb.), seeded and diced
1/3  c. minced parsley
2  Tbsp. lemon juice
1/2  tsp. basil, thyme and oregano
1/4  tsp. pepper
2  Tbsp. olive oil

      Combine all ingredients. Toss gently to mix.  Taste and add more lemon, if desired.  Makes 6 servings, 160 calories per serving.
