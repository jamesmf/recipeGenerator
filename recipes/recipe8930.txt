PORK CHOP SKILLET MEAL


4  pork chops
4  Tbsp. fat
4  slices onion
4  green pepper rings
4  Tbsp. rice
3  c. stewed tomatoes
1  c. diced celery
1  tsp. salt

      Brown chops on both sides in fat. Place slice onion and pepper ring on each pork chops. Place 1 tablespoon of rice in each ring. Pour tomatoes around meat. Add celery. Season with salt. Cover skillet and simmer for 1 hour. Yield: 4 servings.
