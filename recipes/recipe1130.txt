TANGY SWEET AND SOUR MEATBALLS


1  lb. ground round
1/2  c. minced onion
2  Tbsp. minced parsley
1  Tbsp. prepared mustard
1  clove garlic, minced
2  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  tsp. oregano
1  egg white
2  Tbsp. bread crumbs

 
Sauce:
1 (8 oz.)  can tomato sauce
3/4  c. water
2  Tbsp. soy sauce
2  Tbsp. lemon juice
2  packets Equal

      Combine meatball ingredients and shape into 1-inch balls. Brown in skillet sprayed with nonstick coating, turning to brown evenly.  Mix sauce ingredients, except Equal, and add to meatballs. Simmer 20 minutes.  Remove from heat.  Take meatballs out of sauce and place on serving dish.  Stir Equal into sauce and pour over meatballs.  Makes 8 servings, 3 meatballs each.
      Calories per serving:  124.  Traditional calories:  249. Diabetic exchange:  2 lean meat, 1 vegetable.
