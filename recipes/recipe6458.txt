APPLE CHICKEN SAUTÉ


2 to 3  Tbsp. cooking oil
2  Tbsp. sugar
1  Tbsp. cornstarch
  dash of pepper
3  Tbsp. vinegar
1/2  lb. deboned chicken
1/2  tsp. salt
1  apple, sliced lengthwise
1  bell pepper, sliced lengthwise
2  tsp. soy sauce

      Cut chicken in small bites; heat in 2 tablespoons oil.  Sauté until cooked.  Stir constantly, 3 to 5 minutes.  Remove from pan. Sauté bell pepper and apple until tender.  Add other tablespoon oil, if necessary.  Add chicken back to apples and pepper. Combine other ingredients and add to the mixture.  Cook until thickened.
