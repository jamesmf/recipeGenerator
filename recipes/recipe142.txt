COLORADO RANGER COOKIES


1  c. shortening
1  c. white sugar
1  c. brown sugar
2  eggs
2  c. flour
1/2  tsp. baking powder
1  tsp. soda
1  tsp. salt
2  c. quick oats
2  c. Rice Krispies
1  c. coconut
1  tsp. vanilla

      Cream shortening and sugars.  Add eggs.  Add flour, baking powder, soda and salt and work in oats, Rice Krispies, coconut and vanilla.  Mix thoroughly until mixture is a stiff dough.  Mold into small balls and press down on greased cookie sheets with fork. Bake in 350 degrees; oven until brown.
