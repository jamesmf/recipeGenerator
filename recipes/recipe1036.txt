TURTLE STEW


6  lb. cleaned turtle, cut into small chunks
10  medium potatoes, peeled and cut in half
10  carrots, peeled and cut into 2-inch pieces
1  large onion, sliced
1  medium head cabbage, cut into sixths
4 or 5  cans cream of celery soup
  salt and pepper to taste

      Shake turtle in flour and brown in large skillet.  Put browned meat on bottom of large baking roaster.  Put in potatoes, carrots and onion.  Put the cabbage on top and pour soup over top of everything.  Add salt and pepper to taste.  Bake at 325 degrees; for 2 1/2 to 3 hours or until done.  Serves 6 to 8 people.
