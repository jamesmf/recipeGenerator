HOT TAMALE BALLS


1  lb. ground meat
1  lb. Jimmy Dean hot sausage
1 1/2  c. corn meal
1/2  c. flour
1  tsp. garlic
2  tsp. chili powder
2  tsp. cumin powder
1  tsp. cayenne pepper
2  tsp. salt
3/4  c. V-8 juice

      Mix all together and roll into small balls. Mix and heat sauce; place balls in sauce and simmer for 1 to 1 1/2 hours. If sauce becomes too thick, add water.
Sauce:
1 large  can V-8 juice
2  tsp. chili powder
2  tsp. cumin powder
2  tsp. salt
