SHIRLEY'S CARAMEL PIE


3  egg yolks
1  c. milk
1  c. sugar
6  Tbsp. flour
2  Tbsp. butter
1/8  tsp. vanilla

      Mix flour and sugar and add 1/2 cup milk and mix well.  Beat egg yolks separately, then add to mixture.  Add remaining milk, butter, and vanilla.  Cook until thick (stir constantly).  Set aside.  Pour 1/2 cup sugar into small iron skillet and brown slowly.  Mix well into other mixture.  Pour into cooked pie shell; add meringue and brown.
