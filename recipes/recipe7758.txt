SLOPPY JOES


1 1/2  lb. ground lean beef
1/2  tsp. paprika
2 small  cans tomato sauce
1  Tbsp. prepared mustard
1/2  tsp. garlic salt
  salt
1/4  c. catsup
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/2  c. chopped onion
1/2  c. water
2  Tbsp. vinegar
  pepper

      Brown beef and onion in a little oil. Add catsup, Worcestershire sauce, water, vinegar, paprika, tomato sauce, garlic salt, mustard, salt and pepper. Simmer for 30 minutes. Serve on heated hamburger buns.
