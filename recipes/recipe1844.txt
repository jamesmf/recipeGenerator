SOUTH OF THE BORDER STEW


1 1/2  lb. beef chuck roast, cut in 1-inch pieces
1 1/2  lb. pork butt, cut in 1-inch pieces
3  Tbsp. lard or butter
2  cloves garlic, minced
2  onions, chopped
1  or more green chili peppers, seeded and chopped
2  red bell peppers, chopped
2  bay leaves
1  tsp. dried oregano
1/2  tsp. ground cumin
2  c. tomatoes, chopped
1/4  c. fresh lemon juice
1  c. raisins
1  c. whole blanched almonds
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. pepper
1/2  c. dry red wine
1/2  c. beef stock

      Brown meats in lard or butter.  Add garlic and onions and cook until onions are limp.  Combine browned meats and remaining ingredients in a slow cooker.  Cover and cook on low 8 to 10 hours. Serve with warm tortillas.
