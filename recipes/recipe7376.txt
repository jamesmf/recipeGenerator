BIGOS
(Polish Hunter's Stew)  

3 1/2  lb. chopped cooked ham
4  lb. smoked kielbasa, sliced and chopped
2  large onions (Spanish or Vidalia), chopped
2  c. carrots, chopped
4  beef bouillon cubes
1/4  c. caraway seeds
2  qt. sauerkraut, drained and rinsed
1  Tbsp. pepper
  Mrs. Dash seasoning
1  c. dry red wine
1  head cabbage, shredded

      Combine all ingredients in roasting pan.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 2 hours, then at 325 degrees; for 2 to 3 hours or until veggies are soft.
