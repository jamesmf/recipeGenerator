GRAPE SALAD


3  lb. seedless grapes (green or red)
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese, softened
1  c. sugar (more or less to taste)
1  (8 oz.) sour cream
  pecans, chopped (depends on you)
1  c. pecans
1/2  c. brown sugar
2  tsp. butter

      Cook until well coated (not runny); let stand until cool. Put on top of grape mixture or put chopped pecans.
