AIR GUARD BAKED BEAN RECIPE


1  gal. pork and beans
1/3  bottle dark molasses
1/2  lb. brown sugar
1 3/4  Tbsp. dry yellow mustard
2  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  Tbsp. liquid smoke
3/4  Tbsp. onion powder
1/3  bottle (32 oz.) ketchup
  ham or bacon

      Combine above ingredients and add cut up ham or bacon. Bake at 300 degrees; for 1 hour, stirring twice during baking.
