BAHIAN SHRIMP MUQUECA
(Muqueca de Camarao)  

2  Tbsp. dende oil
1  large yellow onion, finely chopped
3  cloves garlic, crushed
2  lb. fresh medium-sized prawns, shelled and deveined
1/2  tsp. salt
2  Tbsp. fresh lemon juice
4  large tomatoes, peeled, seeded and chopped
2  Tbsp. Italian parsley, chopped
1  tsp. black pepper
1  fresh red hot cayenne pepper
1  can coconut milk (14 fluid oz.)

      Heat the oil in a medium-sized pot and stir-fry the onion until golden brown.  Add the garlic and prawns.  Stir-fry for 3 minutes. Add the salt, lemon juice, tomatoes, parsley, pepper, cayenne and coconut milk.  Simmer for 5 minutes.  Serve with Brazilian Rice with coconut milk.  Serves 4 to 5.
