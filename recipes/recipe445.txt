KIDDIE FEAST


2  cans pork and beans
1  tsp. mustard
2  Tbsp. ketchup
2  Tbsp. Bull's Eye barbecue sauce
2  Tbsp. honey
1 (12 oz.)  pkg. skinless smoked sausage

      Mix pork and beans, mustard, ketchup, barbecue sauce and honey together in baking pan.  Cut sausage in small rings; add to bean mixture.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 35 minutes.  Yield:  8 servings.
