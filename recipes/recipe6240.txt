CURRIED ZUCCHINI SOUP


2  Tbsp. butter
1  medium onion, chopped
1  Delicious apple, peeled, cored and diced
2  tsp. curry powder
32  oz. chicken stock
1/4  c. rice
2  c. diced zucchini
1/2  tsp. salt
1  c. milk

      Cook onion and apple in butter until soft. Add curry and stir 4 to 5 seconds. Add chicken stock; bring to a boil. Add rice, diced zucchini and salt; cook until soft. Put through a food processor and then add milk.  Serve hot or cold with croutons or slivered almonds.  Serves 6.
      This soup freezes well, but add the milk after thawing soup.
