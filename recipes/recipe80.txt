MOCHA CREAM FROSTING


3  c. confectioners sugar
1/4  c. cocoa
1/3  c. butter or margarine
2 1/2 to 3  tsp. instant coffee granules
2  Tbsp. hot water
2 or 3  Tbsp. milk
1  tsp. vanilla

      Combine confectioners sugar and cocoa.  Cream butter with 1/2 cup cocoa mixture in small bowl.  Combine instant coffee and hot water until dissolved.  Add remaining cocoa mixture alternately with coffee and milk; beat until spreading consistency.  Blend in vanilla.  Makes about 2 1/4 cups frosting.
