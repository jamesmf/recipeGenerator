PIZZA CASSEROLE


1  lb. ground meat
2  c. shredded Mozzarella cheese
2  eggs
3/4  c. biscuit mix
1 (14 oz.)  jar pizza sauce
1 1/4  c. milk

	Cook meat until brown, stirring as you go; drain well and pour into a 9 x 13-inch pan. Top with pizza sauce and cheese. Combine biscuit mix, milk and eggs. Beat until smooth and pour over the top evenly. Bake at 400 degrees; for 30 to 35 minutes.
