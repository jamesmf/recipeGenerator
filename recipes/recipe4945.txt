SOUTHWESTERN CHICKEN SALAD WRAPS


1  jar (7-oz.) roasted red bell peppers
2  c. chopped cooked chicken
1  pkg. (8-oz.) cream cheese, softened
1  envelope (0.4-oz.) Ranch-style Buttermilk dressing mix
1/4  c. chopped ripe olives
1/2  small onion, finely chopped
1  can (4.5-oz.) chopped green chiles, drained
2  Tbsp. chopped fresh cilantro
1/2  tsp. pepper
1/4  c. pine nuts (optional)
8  (6-inch) flour tortillas

      Drain roasted peppers well, pressing between layers of paper towels; chop.  Stir together roasted peppers, chicken, and next 7 ingredients.  Cover and chill at least 2 hours.  Stir pine nuts, if desired, in chicken mixture.  Spoon evenly over tortillas, and roll up.  These can be eaten as sandwiches, or each roll can be cut into 5 slices and served as appetizers.
