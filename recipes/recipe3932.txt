HOLIDAY CHEESE BALL


  2---8 oz cream cheese (room temperature)
2  Tbsp. chopped onion
2  Tbsp. green pepper
1 small  can crushed pineapple (drained)
2  tsp. seasoned salt
2  cups chopped pecans

      Put one cup of pecans in mixture and shape into ball. Roll ball in one cup of pecans. Store in refrigerator.
