MINI-CHEESECAKES


12  vanilla wafers
2 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese, softened
1/2  c. sugar
1  tsp. vanilla
2  eggs

      Line muffin tin with foil liners. Place 1 vanilla wafer in each liner. Mix cream cheese, vanilla and sugar on medium speed until well blended. Add eggs. Mix well. Pour over wafers, filling 3/4 full. Bake 25 minutes at 325 degrees;. Remove from pan when cool. Chill. Top with fruit, preserves or pie filling.
