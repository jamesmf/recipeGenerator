FRUIT SALAD
(Macaroni Rings)  

1 small  box of macaroni rings
1  c. sugar
1/2  tsp. salt
2  Tbsp. flour
  juice from 1 large can chunk pineapple
2  Tbsp. lemon juice
2  beaten eggs
  grapes, red or green
  pineapple chunks
  bananas, sliced
1  pint of whipping cream

      Cook macaroni rings.  Drain.  Boil together sugar, salt, flour, pineapple juice, lemon juice and eggs.  Pour over macaroni rings. Mix and refrigerate overnight.  Add fruit.  Whip 1 pint of whipping cream.  Mix all together.  Nuts and marshmallows can be added if desired.
