SOUTH OF THE BORDER BUNDLES


1 (12 oz.)  jar salsa
2  c. cubed cheese
  about 3 c. chicken or ground beef, cooked
4  (8 inch) flour tortillas

      Brown ground meat and set aside. In medium saucepan, combine salsa and cheese; cook over medium heat until cheese melts. Set aside 1 1/4 c. of mixture. Add meat to remaining mixture in sauce pan. Spoon mixture into tortillas; roll tortillas and place (seam side down) in a greased baking dish. Cover with foil and bake at 375 degrees; for 15 to 20 minutes. Pour sauce, that was set aside, over bundles. Serve and enjoy!
