MINI MEATBALLS


1 1/2  lb. lean ground beef
3/4  c. crushed Corn Flakes or Special K
1  can French onion soup (all of it)
1  c. catsup
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/2  c. brown sugar

      Heat sauce in microwave until hot and blended. Spoon sauce over meatballs. Bake at 400 degrees; for 30 minutes.
