HOT TURKEY OR CHICKEN SALAD


2  c. cooked, cubed turkey
2  tsp. grated onions
2  c. cubed celery
2  Tbsp. lemon juice
1/2  c. toasted or smoked  almonds
1  c. mayonnaise
1/2  tsp. salt
1  c. crushed potato chips
1/2  c. grated Cheddar cheese

	Combine all ingredients except cheese and chips.  Toss lightly.  Pile lightly into large dish or casserole.  Sprinkle with cheese, then chips.  Bake at 450 degrees; for 10 minutes. Serves 4.
