ITALIAN BAGUETTE CHIPS

Italian Seasoning:
1/4  c. grated Parmesan cheese
1  tsp. dried basil
1/4  tsp. garlic powder
1  tsp. dried oregano
1/4  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. black pepper

 
Chips:
40  (1/4-inch) thick slices, diagonally cut French bread baguette
  cooking spray

      Preheat oven to 375 degrees;. To prepare Italian Seasoning, combine first 6 ingredients in a small bowl.
       To prepare chips, arrange the bread slices on 2 baking sheets coated with cooking spray. Coat bread with cooking spray. Sprinkle 4 teaspoons Italian Seasoning over bread. Bake at 375 degrees; for 8 minutes or until crisp. Yield: 40 chips. Serving Size: 4 chips.
