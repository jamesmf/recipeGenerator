BECKY'S BEAN SALAD


1 small  can whole green beans
1 small  can wax beans
1  can kidney beans
1  small green pepper sliced into rings
1  onion sliced into rings
2/3  c. white vinegar
1/3  c. oil
3/4  c. sugar
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. pepper

	Combine, drain, and rinse all beans.  Set aside.  Mix remaining ingredients together.  Add beans to mixture.  Marinate overnight.	Serve 8-10.
