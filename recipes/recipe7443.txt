MEAT LOAF


1 1/2  lb. ground beef
1/2  c. fine dry bread crumbs
1  egg, beaten
3/4  c. milk
1/4  c. finely chopped onion
1 1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
1/2  c. catsup

      Mix ground beef lightly with bread crumbs, egg, milk, onion, salt and pepper in a bowl.  Pack mixture lightly in a 2-quart loaf dish.  Cover with waxed paper.  Cook in microwave 5 minutes at Medium-high.  Top with catsup.  Rotate dish 1/2 turn.  Cook, covered, 15 minutes on High or until done.  Remove from microwave. Cover with aluminum foil and let stand 5 minutes.  Serves 6.
