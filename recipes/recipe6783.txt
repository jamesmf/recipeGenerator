"MARINADE FOR SMOKED GOOSE OR TURKEY"

Whole Goose or Turkey:
1/2  c. liquid smoke
1  c. Tender Quick salt
1/2  c. canning salt
1  gal. water

      Drain. Soak 12 hours. Bake at 325 degrees; for 3 to 4 hours.
3 Goose Breasts and Legs:
1 1/2  tsp. liquid smoke
1  Tbsp. Tender Quick salt
1 1/2  tsp. canning salt
1  pt. water

      Cut breasts into 1-inch strips. Drain. Bake at 350 degrees; for 2 to 3 hours.
