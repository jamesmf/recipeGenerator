CHICKEN AND WILD RICE CASSEROLE


1  chicken
1/2  c. celery
1  medium onion
1 (4 oz.)  can mushrooms, cut in small pieces
1  stick margarine
1  box wild and long grain rice
2  cans cream of chicken or mushroom soup

	Boil chicken; remove from bone and cut in small pieces. (Reserve broth.)  Cook rice according to package using chicken broth instead of water. Sauté celery, onion and mushrooms in margarine. Combine rice, chicken soup and sautéed vegetables and pour in a casserole. Bake at 400 degrees; for 30 minutes.
