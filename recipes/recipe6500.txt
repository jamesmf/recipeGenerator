BEEF BRISKET ANNA


4  lb. beef brisket
  flour (for dusting)
2  Tbsp. olive oil
2  large onions, chopped thin
1  lb. can tomatoes
1/2  c. raisins
1/4  c. sugar
1  lemon
1  clove garlic

      Dust beef with flour; season with salt and pepper. In large skillet, sear beef on all sides in olive oil. Add onions and saut√© until they begin to color.
       In blender, puree tomatoes. Combine with raisins, sugar and lemon, peeled, seeded and quartered, and 1 clove garlic, pressed. Pour sauce over meat and simmer, covered, 2 to 2 1/2 hours. Slice diagonally and serve with noodles. Serves 6 to 8.
