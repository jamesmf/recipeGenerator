KRUNCHY KRAZY KORN


8  c. puffed popcorn curls
1  c. firmly packed brown sugar
1/2  c. margarine or butter
1/4  c. light corn syrup
1/2  tsp. soda
1  tsp. vanilla

      Heat oven to 250 degrees;.  Place popcorn in foil lined pan.  In large saucepan, combine sugar, margarine and corn syrup.  Bring to a boil over medium heat, stirring constantly.  Simmer 2 minutes.  Remove from heat and stir in soda and vanilla.  Pour mixture evenly over popcorn; mix well.  Bake at 250 degrees; for 30 minutes, stirring twice during baking time.  Remove from pan immediately.  Cool completely.
