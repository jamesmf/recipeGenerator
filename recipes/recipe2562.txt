SPICY MEAT PIE


2  lb. ground beef
3/4  c. chopped onion
2  cloves garlic, minced
1 1/2  c. bouillon
1  tsp. ground sage
1/2  tsp. each ground mace and pepper
1/4  tsp. ground cloves
3/4  c. chopped celery leaves
3  Tbsp. cornstarch
1/4  c. water

      Saut√© meat, onion and garlic in large skillet.  Stir in next 6 ingredients.  Mix cornstarch and water; stir in gradually.  Bring to boil; reduce heat and simmer, covered, about 10 minutes.  Remove from heat and set aside.  Prepare pastry.
Mayonnaise Pastry:
2  c. sifted flour
1  tsp. salt
1/2  c. Hellmann's real mayonnaise
3  Tbsp. cold water

      Mix  flour  and  salt;  blend  in  real mayonnaise with fork. Sprinkle  on water;  mix well.  Press firmly into ball. Divide almost  in half.  Flatten larger portion;  roll out on lightly floured surface into 12-inch square.  Place in an 8 x 8  x  2-inch baking  pan.  Fill  with meat mixture.  Roll out remaining pastry.  Place over meat mixture;  seal edges.  Cut slits.  Brush with  egg  yolk and water, if desired.  Bake in 425 degrees; oven 30 minutes.  Serves 6.
