BAKED ROTISSERIE CHICKEN


4  split chicken breasts
  Tones rotisserie chicken blend
  butter flavored Pam spray

	Wash chicken and pat dry with paper towel. Arrange chicken in baking dish. Spray chicken with Pam, then sprinkle with rotisserie. Blend, coating well. Bake, uncovered, in 375 degrees; oven for 1 to 1 1/2 hours.
