LASAGNA


1 small  box lasagna noodles (no need to boil noodles)

 
Cheese Mix:
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
1/4  c. water
2  eggs
2  c. cottage cheese
1/2  c. Parmesan cheese
3  c. Mozzarella cheese

 
Meat Sauce:
1 to 2  lb. ground beef
2  (28 oz.) spaghetti sauce (your flavor choice; save some spaghetti sauce in jar for later)
1/4  c. water

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees;.
      Cheese Mix:  Mix ingredients in large bowl.
      Meat Sauce:  Brown ground beef.  Stir in spaghetti sauce.  Let simmer for 10 minutes.
      Spread a small layer of spaghetti sauce (which was saved in the jar).  Layer of raw noodles. Layer of cheese mix.  Layer of meat sauce.  Repeat the layers ending with the meat sauce.  Spread a little Mozzarella on top.  Cover with foil and bake at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes.  Remove the foil and bake for another 15 minutes. Cool for 15 to 30 minutes before serving.
