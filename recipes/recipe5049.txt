MACARONI SALAD


2 1/2  tsp. flour
3/4  c. sugar
1  tsp. salt
  dash of pepper
6  Tbsp. prepared mustard
2  Tbsp. butter, melted
2  eggs, beaten
1/4  c. water
1/4  c. vinegar
1  c. mayonnaise

      Mix  flour, sugar, salt and pepper.  Add mustard.  Then add butter,  eggs,  water  and  vinegar.  Stir  and cook over medium heat  until thick.  Add mayonnaise and mix with macaroni mixture. Best if  added  when macaroni is hot and made the day ahead. Yields 1 gallon.
Macaroni Mixture:
3  c. (12 oz.) uncooked macaroni, cooked
1  stick celery, diced
2  sweet pickles, diced
1  medium onion, chopped
4  hard-cooked eggs, chopped
