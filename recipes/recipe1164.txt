CRANBERRY - NUT BREAD


1  c. whole-wheat flour
1  c. white flour
1/4  c. sugar
1  Tbsp. baking powder
2  tsp. grated orange rind
1  c. orange juice
2  Tbsp. vegetable oil
1  egg
1  c. chopped fresh or frozen cranberries
1/3  c. raisins
1/3  c. chopped walnuts

      Combine the flours, sugar, baking powder and orange rind in a mixing bowl.  Add the remaining ingredients and stir until well blended.  Pour into an oiled 9 x 5-inch loaf pan.  Bake in a 350 degrees; oven for 45 to 50 minutes.  Cool for 5 to 7 minutes before removing from pan.  Turn onto a wire rack and cool thoroughly.  Store in a plastic bag or aluminum foil for at least 1 day before slicing. Serves 15.
