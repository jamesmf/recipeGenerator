DEVILED EGGS


6  hard boiled eggs, chopped
4  strips of bacon, cooked and crumbled
1/4  c mayonnaise
1  tbsp onion, minced
1/2  tsp salt
1/2  tsp pepper
1/4  tsp mustard
1  c cheddar cheese, shredded

      Combine all ingredients except for the cheese until creamy. Shape into 1 inch balls.  Roll in cheese.  Cover and refrigerate until serving.
