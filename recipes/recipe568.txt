GOURMET TOSSED GREEN SALAD


1  medium head lettuce
1  small head cauliflower
1  small sweet onion
1  medium green pepper, diced
1  pimiento, diced
6  large fresh mushrooms, sliced
1/2  c. Roquefort cheese, crumbled
1  clove garlic, crushed or grated
  low calorie French dressing

      Tear lettuce into bite-size pieces.  Wash cauliflower; remove green stalks and separate into tiny flowerets.  Cut onion in paper-thin rings.  Toss gently with green pepper, pimiento, mushrooms, cheese and garlic.  Chill about 1 hour.  Toss and serve immediately with low calorie French dressing.
