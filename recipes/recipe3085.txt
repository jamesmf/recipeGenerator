CRISPIX MIX


1  stick butter
1/4  c. white syrup
1  c. brown sugar
  dash of salt
1  box Crispix cereal
8  to 10 pretzels
2  c. peanuts
1  lb. M&M's
1  tsp. vanilla
1  tsp. baking soda

      Put oleo, syrup, brown sugar and salt in large microwave bowl. Cook on High 2 minutes in microwave. Stir. Repeat for 2 minutes. Add 1 teaspoon vanilla and 1 teaspoon baking soda. Stir. Pour over Crispix in brown paper bag and mix with spoon. Microwave in bag on High 1 1/2 minutes; shake. Repeat for 1 1/2 minutes. Shake 1 minute. Add pretzels and peanuts. Shake. Cool. Add M&M's.
