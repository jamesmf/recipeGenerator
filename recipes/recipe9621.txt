IRIS'S MEXICAN CORN BREAD


2  lb. ground beef
1  large chopped onion
4  hot chili peppers, chopped
8  oz. grated cheddar cheese
1  c. corn meal
1/2  tsp. salt
1  tsp. soda
2  beaten eggs
1  c. buttermilk
1/3  c. oil
1  can cream style corn

      Sift together meal, salt, & soda.  Combine with eggs, buttermilk, oil, & corn.  Pour 1/2 bread mixture in deep iron skillet or Dutch oven (greased), then add meat mixture.  Top with remaining bread mixture.  Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.
