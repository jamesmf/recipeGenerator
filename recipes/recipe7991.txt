BARBECUED PORK CHOPS


6  pork chops
  chopped onions
1/2  c. catsup
1/2  c. hot water
1  tsp. dry mustard
1/2  tsp. ground cloves
1  Tbsp. flour
2  Tbsp. brown sugar
  salt and pepper to taste

      Put pork chops in baking dish. Cover with chopped onions. Mix remaining ingredients and pour over the pork chops.  Bake at 350 degrees; about 1 hour.
