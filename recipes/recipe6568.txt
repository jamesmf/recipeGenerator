LEMON PEPPER CHICKEN


4  chicken breasts
4  tsp. butter or margarine
  salt (as desired)
  lemon pepper
1 1/2  c. water
1  can mushroom soup
1 1/2  c. rice

      Put 1 teaspoon of butter on chicken breast and sprinkle with lemon pepper and salt. Place in baking dish; cover and bake in oven at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes. Take out. Mix soup, water and more lemon pepper together and heat on stove. Pour chicken broth out of baking dish. Mix well with soup mixture. Add 1 1/2 cups rice to chicken and pour in baking dish. Bake for 30 minutes more.
