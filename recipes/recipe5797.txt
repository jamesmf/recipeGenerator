I CAN'T BELIEVE IT'S CAULIFLOWER SALAD


1  lb. bacon, cooked and crumbled
  lettuce
1  head cauliflower, chopped into tiny pieces in food processor
1  bunch green onions, chopped
2  c. mayo
1/2  c. sugar
  Parmesan cheese

      Combine bacon, lettuce, cauliflower and green onions. At serving time, mix together mayo and sugar and pour over lettuce; mix well and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
