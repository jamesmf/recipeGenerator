APRICOT MOLD


1 1/2  c. apricot nectar
2  small apricot jellos
2  jars junior apricots (baby food)
1  small frozen orange juice (6 oz.)
2  tsp. lemon juice
1/8  c. seltzer

      Heat nectar; dissolve jello.  Add junior apricots, frozen orange juice and lemon juice.  Add seltzer.  Pour into mold.  May be served with mayonnaise-lemon juice sauce.
