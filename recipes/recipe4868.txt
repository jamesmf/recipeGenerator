FISH SOUP


2  lb. fish meat, chopped
1  medium onion, sliced
2  large potatoes, sliced
2  large tomatoes, sliced
1/4  c. olive oil
2  garlic cloves, minced
2  bay leaves
1/4  c. finely chopped parsley

      Over medium heat, cook potatoes, onion, garlic and bay leaves in a saucepan for 5 minutes until onions are translucent.  Add remaining ingredients, except fish.  Boil until vegetables are tender.  Add fish and heat until cooked through.  Add salt and pepper to taste.
