MANDARIN ORANGE SALAD


2 small or 1 large  pkg. orange gelatin
1  pt. orange sherbet
1  can mandarin oranges
2 1/2  c. hot water
1  pt. whipping cream, whipped

      Dissolve Jell-O in hot water. Put in refrigerator until syrupy. Whip until fluffy. Beat sherbet into mixture; beat well. Fold in whipped cream and oranges. Pour into mold and refrigerate.
