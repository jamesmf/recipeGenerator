HOT CHICKEN ENCHILADA SOUP


1  dozen corn tortillas
1  onion, chopped
1  clove garlic, minced
2  Tbsp. oil
1 (4 oz.)  can green chilies, chopped
1 (14 1/2 oz.)  can beef broth
1 (10 oz.)  can chicken broth
1 (10 oz.)  can cream of chicken soup (undiluted)
1 (6 3/4 oz.)  can chunk style chicken (or use boiled chicken)
1 1/2  c. water
1  Tbsp. steak sauce
1/2  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  tsp. ground cumin
1  tsp. chili powder
1/8  tsp. pepper
3  c. cheddar cheese, grated
  paprika

      Cut 6 tortillas into 1/2 inch strips and set aside. Cut remaining 6 tortillas into triangles and fry in hot oil until crisp; (or you may use corn chips) set aside. Saut√© onion and garlic in oil in dutch oven. Add next 11 ingredients; bring to a boil. Cover and reduce heat; simmer for 1 hour. Add tortilla strips and cheese. Simmer, uncovered 10 minutes. Sprinkle with paprika. Serve with chips. Makes 8 cups.
