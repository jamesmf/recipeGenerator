ARIZONA SUGAR COOKIES


1  c. shortening
1/2  c. brown sugar
1/2  c. sugar
1  tsp. vanilla
1  egg
2 1/4  c. flour
1/2  tsp. soda
1/2  tsp. salt
1  tsp. cream of tartar

      Mix well shortening, brown sugar, sugar, vanilla and egg.  Sift and add flour, soda, salt and cream of tartar.  Roll in small balls.  Roll in sugar.  Do not flatten.  Bake on ungreased cookie sheet at 350 degrees; for 10 to 12 minutes.
