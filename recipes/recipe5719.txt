ORIENTAL SALAD


1  lb. bag broccoli slaw
1  bunch green onions
2  pkgs. beef ramen noodle soup mix
3/4  c. slivered almonds
1/2  c. sunflower seeds

 
Dressing:
1  c. oil
1/3  c. vinegar
2  pkg. flavor mix from soup
1/3  c. sugar

      Twenty-four hours before serving, make dressing.  Heat oil, vinegar, soup mix and sugar to dissolve.  Cool, refrigerate.  The day of serving, break noodles into pieces, add broccoli slaw, chopped onions, almonds and sunflower seeds.  Pour dressing over. Noodles will be crunchy at first.
