RAY'S STEAK BURGERS


1 1/2  lb. lean ground chuck
1  pkg. Knorr's dry beef/mushroom or onion soup mix
1  medium onion, chopped
1  green pepper, chopped
  Worcestershire sauce (about 1/4 c., more or less)
  garlic powder
  seasonings to your taste

      Mix well with hands and make patties.  Have the grill nice and hot.  Cook until done.
