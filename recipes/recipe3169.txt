MARIE'S GREEN TOMATO RELISH


2  qt. green tomatoes, quartered
2  c. onion, chopped
3/4  c. hot peppers, chopped
2 1/2  c. sugar
1 1/2  c. vinegar
3  Tbsp. salt
1  tsp. celery seed

      Put tomatoes, onion, and peppers in a large pot; mix remaining ingredients together and pour over tomatoes. Heat just until it begins to boil; remove from heat and pack in sterile jars; seal immediately.
