CHICKEN POT PIE


1  c. fat free cream of chicken soup
1  pkg. frozen mixed vegetables, thawed
1  c. cooked chicken, cubed
1  c. Bisquick
1  egg
1/2  c. milk

      In a 9 inch pie plate mix soup, vegetables and chicken.  In a bowl, mix milk, egg and Bisquick, pour over chicken mixture.  Bake at 400 degrees; for 30 minutes or until golden brown.
