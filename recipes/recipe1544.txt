BUBBLING JADE PUNCH


1 (3 oz.)  pkg. lime gelatin
1  c. boiling water
2  c. cold water
1 (6 oz.)  can frozen lemonade concentrate, thawed and undiluted
1  c. pineapple juice, chilled
1 (33.8 oz.)  bottle ginger ale, chilled
  whole fresh strawberries (optional)

      Dissolve gelatin in boiling water; stir in cold water, lemonade concentrate and pineapple juice.  Chill well.  Pour mixture into bowl.  Add ginger ale.  (Optional:  Garnish with strawberries.) Yield:  Approximately 10 cups.
