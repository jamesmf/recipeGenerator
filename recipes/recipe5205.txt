RICE SALAD


6  cups cooked rice
2  cups cooked black-eyed peas
1  1/2 cups diced cooked ham
1/2  cup each diced orange, yellow and red bell peppers
2/3  cup minced scallion
1/2  cup minced parsley leaves

      In bowl combine all ingredients. Add dressing and toss to combine.
Dressing:
1  cup mayonnaise
1/4  cup vegetable oil
1/4  cup white wine vinegar
2  Tbsp. Dijon-style mustard
  salt to taste
  pepper, freshly ground, to taste

      Whisk together all dressing ingredients.  Add dressing to the salad and toss to combine.
