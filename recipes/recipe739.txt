OYSTER PASTA


2  Tbsp. butter
2  Tbsp. shallots, minced, or green onions
12  shelled oysters
1  c. heavy whipping cream
2  Tbsp. lemon juice
1  Tbsp. Pernod
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  lb. thin spaghetti
4  oz. spinach leaves, julienned
1  egg yolk
  Parmesan cheese

      In a 10-inch skillet, put butter and shallots; saut√©.  Add oysters, cream, lemon juice, Pernod and salt; cook 5 minutes over low heat.
      Cook pasta; drain.  Add raw spinach and egg yolk; mix well. Toss with mixture from skillet.  Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
      Can use scallops, but only cook 2 or 3 minutes.  Makes 3 servings.
