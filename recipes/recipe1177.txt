SWEET POTATO PIE


2  whole eggs
1/2  tsp. nutmeg
1 small  can unsweetened pineapple, drained
6  pkg. Sweet 'N Low
1  tsp. cinnamon
1  c. skim milk
1 1/2  c. cooked sweet potato

      Put in unbaked pie shell.  Mix well and bake at 450 degrees; for 15 minutes, then at 350 degrees; until done.  Can put meringue on top or Cool Whip.
