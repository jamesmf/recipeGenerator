BURNT SUGAR COCONUT CANDY


4  c. white sugar
3  Tbsp. white corn syrup
1 3/4  c. milk, heated
1  tsp. baking soda
1/4  c. butter
1  tsp. vanilla
2  c. coconut

      In a heavy skillet, burn sugar to a medium brown.  Add Karo syrup to sugar.  Add milk a little at a time (do not stop boiling process).  Cook to soft-ball stage.  Remove from heat and add soda. Stir quickly until foam goes down.  Add butter, vanilla and coconut.  Beat very hard.  Pour into buttered dish to harden.
