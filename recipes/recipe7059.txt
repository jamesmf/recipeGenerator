CHILI


1  medium onion, diced
1  lb. ground beef
16  oz. canned tomatoes, diced
1  can tomato soup
8  oz. dark red kidney beans
1  tsp. salt
1 1/2  Tbsp. chili powder
1 1/2  Tbsp. flour
3  Tbsp. cold water

      Brown beef and onion. Add canned tomatoes, tomato soup and kidney beans to pan and simmer for 10 minutes. Mix salt, chili powder, flour and water in a measuring cup. Stir and make a paste, then add to pan and simmer for 45 minutes. Also, add 2 dashes of garlic powder. Cook uncovered.
