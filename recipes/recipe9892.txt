SWEET POTATO SOUFFL


3  c. cooked mashed sweet potatoes
1/2 to 3/4  c. sugar
1/4  c. milk
2  eggs
1  tsp. vanilla
1/2  c. melted butter
1/3  c. flour
1  tsp. grated orange peel
2  Tbsp. orange juice
1  tsp. cinnamon
1/4  tsp. each: nutmeg, cloves and ginger

      Combine all ingredients (except topping) with electric mixer. Add the eggs, one at a time. Beat into mixture with electric mixer. (The mixture can be beaten by hand, but the souffl will not be as light.)
Topping:
1  c. chopped pecans
1  c. brown sugar
1/2  c. melted butter
1/3  c. flour

      Combine topping. Sprinkle over souffl and bake at 350 degrees; for 40 to 45 minutes. Serves 8 people.
