JAM CAKE


1  c. margarine
2  c. sugar
6  eggs
1/2  c. raisins
1  c. coconut
1  c. nuts, chopped
1  c. blackberry jam
1  c. buttermilk
3  c. flour

      Cream margarine and sugar; add eggs.  Mix well.  Add remaining ingredients and mix.  Pour into 3 greased cake pans.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 to 40 minutes or until done.  Spread filling on first 2 layers and Caramel Frosting on top of third layer and sides.  Top with pecan halves.
Filling:
1/4  c. flour
1  c. milk
1/2  c. margarine
1  c. sugar
1  c. coconut
1  c. nuts, chopped

      Mix and cook until thick.
Frosting:
3  Tbsp. margarine
6  Tbsp. brown sugar
6  Tbsp. white sugar
1/2  c. Pet milk
10  marshmallows
1 1/2  tsp. vanilla
1 1/2  c. powdered sugar

      Mix margarine, brown sugar, white sugar and milk; boil for 2 minutes.  Remove from heat.  Add other ingredients.
