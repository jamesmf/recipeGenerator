HONEY WALNUT FRUIT SALAD


1/3  c. Sue Bee honey
1/2  c. light mayonnaise
1/2  c. chopped walnuts
3  cored, unpeeled, red or yellow apples, chopped
2  bananas, peeled and sliced
11  oz. can Mandarin oranges, drained
1  c. seedless grapes
1  Tbsp. lemon juice

      Blend honey and mayonnaise until smooth. Toss remaining ingredients with lemon juice. Stir in honey mixture and refrigerate until ready to serve. Makes about 7 cups.
