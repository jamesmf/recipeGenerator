PARMESAN CHICKEN


6  chicken breasts, boneless
1/2  lb. butter
1  clove garlic minced (or garlic powder)
1  c. Pepperidge Farm Corn Bread Stuffing Mix
1  c. Parmesan Cheese
1  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper

      Melt butter with garlic and simmer a few minutes. Mix bread crumbs and cheese.
      Dip chicken in melted butter mixture, then in bread crumbs and cheese mixture.  Place on rack in baking pan and place in oven at 350 degrees; for 1/2 hour; then reduce temperature to 300 degrees; for 1/2 hour. Do not cover.  (Can be prepared the day before and refrigerated until ready to bake.)
