MICROWAVE MAPLE NUT DIVINITY


1  c. sugar
1  c. brown sugar
1/2  c. light corn syrup
1/2  c. water
2  egg whites
  dash of salt
1/2  c. chopped nuts
1  tsp. imitation maple flavoring

      Combine sugars, corn syrup and water in buttered large glass mixing bowl.  Microwave for 15 to 17 minutes on roast or until candy forms a hard ball in cold water (250 degrees;).  Meanwhile, beat egg whites in large bowl until stiff peaks.  Pour candy syrup in thin stream into egg whites; beat constantly with mixer.  Add maple flavoring and salt; beat until mixture is stiff and dull.  Fold in nuts.  Drop mixture by teaspoonfuls onto wax paper.  Cool.
