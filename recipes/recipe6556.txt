CHICKEN ROTEL


1  stick butter
1  onion
1  green pepper
1  lb. Velveeta cheese
1  can English peas
1  can mushrooms
1  can Ro-Tel tomatoes
12  oz. spaghetti
4  to 6 chicken breasts

      Boil chicken, then cut into small pieces. Cook spaghetti. Dice up green pepper and onion. Cut cheese into small pieces. Mix all ingredients together. Bake at 350 degrees; until it bubbles.
