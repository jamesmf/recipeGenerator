GRILLED ASPARAGUS


1  clove garlic
1/3  c. olive oil
  salt and pepper to taste
1 to 1 1/2  lb. asparagus spears
8  to 10 halved cherry tomatoes
2  hard-cooked eggs, peeled and quartered
  minced parsley
  vinaigrette

      Crush garlic. Mix with olive oil in small bowl. Add salt and pepper; set aside.
      Wash and trim asparagus spears. Pat dry. Preheat grill.
       Brush spears lightly with garlic oil. Grill until tender and lightly browned, about 5 minutes; turn often. Transfer to serving platter. Garnish with cherry tomatoes and egg and sprinkle with parsley. Drizzle with vinaigrette.
