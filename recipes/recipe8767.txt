RICE CASSEROLE


1  6  oz. box Uncle Ben's Long Grain Wild Rice
2  cans College Inn (Beef or Chicken) Broth
2  cans Campbell's French Onion Soup
1  can Le Seur Sweet Peas
1  can mushrooms, drained (opt.)
2  sticks butter

      Mix all ingredients. (Do not cook the rice first)   Add sticks of butter. Put in 9x13 pan.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour.
