DRY BARBECUE RUB


1/4  c. salt
1/8  c. pepper
1 1/4  c. brown sugar
1/4  c. garlic powder
3  Tbsp. paprika
1  Tbsp. onion salt
3/4  c. chili powder
2  Tbsp. dry mustard
1  Tbsp. cumin seed

      Mix all ingredients together.  Let set at least 6 hours before using.  Rub on meat and bake in oven.
