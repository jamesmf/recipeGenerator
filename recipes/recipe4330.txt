SOUTHERN CHOCOLATE GRAVY


2 heaping  Tbsp. cocoa
1  c. sugar
3  Tbsp. flour
4  c. milk or water
1  tsp. vanilla
1  Tbsp. butter

      Mix cocoa, flour and sugar in a pot with a fork or whisk until well blended. Add all other ingredients and bring to a boil, stirring constantly, over medium heat. Continue cooking until the gravy is the desired thickness. Serve hot over homemade biscuits with lots of butter. Great for breakfast.
