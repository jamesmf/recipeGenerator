LONDON BROIL ON GRILL


  London Broil (3 inches thick, 3 to 5 lb.)

 
Marinade:
1/4  c. soy sauce
2  Tbsp. vinegar
  black pepper
  garlic clove

 
Gravy:
1  Tbsp. soy sauce
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  pkg. French onion soup mix
1  c. water

      Marinate meat in refrigerator 8 hours.  Heat grill to low heat. Remove meat from marinade.  Cook in pan, covered with gravy, and pan covered with foil in grill for 1 hour.  Slice thin.  Meat should be a little pink in center.  Serve with gravy from pan.
