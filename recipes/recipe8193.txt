QUICK AND EASY CHICKEN POT PIE


4  chicken breasts, cut up
2  cans Veg-All, drained and rinsed
1/2  stick margarine
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  can evaporated milk
3/4  c. Bisquick
3/4  c. milk

      Layer chicken and Veg-All; cut up margarine on top of this. Mix canned milk with soup and pour on top, then mix milk with Bisquick and pour on top of that mixture. Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour.
