MYSTERY PUNCH


1  chunk dry ice (size of hand)
2 (46 oz.)  cans pineapple juice
1 small  can frozen lemonade
2  qt. ginger ale
2  qt. sparkling water
1 small  can frozen limeade
1  pkg. fresh or frozen strawberries

      Place dry ice in punch bowl; pour on ingredients in order: pineapple juice, lemonade, ginger ale, sparkling water, limeade and strawberries.  Dry ice gives off smoke vapor.  When ice is exhausted, add more.  Serves 25.
