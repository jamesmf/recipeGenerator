CUCUMBER SALAD


1  C. mayonnaise
1/4  C. sugar
4  tsp. vinegar
4  med. cucumbers, peeled and sliced thin
3  green onions, chopped
1/2  tsp. dill weed
1/2  tsp. salt (optional)

      In a large bowl, combine mayonnaise, sugar, vinegar, dill and salt; mix well.
      Add cucumbers and onions; toss.
      Cover and chill for at least 1 hour.
