HERB STUFFED EGGS


12  hard boiled eggs, peeled
1/2  c. garlic and herb cheese spread
1/4  c. mayonnaise
1/4  c. Grey Poupon Dijon mustard
1/4  c. finely chopped green onions
2  Tbsp. finely chopped pimientos

      Halve eggs lengthwise; scoop out yolks into bowl. Set white halves aside. Mash yolks; blend in cheese spread, mayonnaise, mustard, green onions, and pimientos. Spoon into egg white halves. Chill until serving time. Makes 24 appetizers.
