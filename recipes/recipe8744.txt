EASY TURKEY AND STUFFING


1  lb. sliced turkey breast
1  pkg. Stove Top stuffing
1  can green beans, drained
1  c. chicken broth

      Make Stove top stuffing according to directions. On bottom of 9x13 pan, layer  stuffing and green beans.  Add cut up slices of turkey and mix together. Add chicken broth and bake in oven at 350 degrees; for 45 mins. to 1 hour.
