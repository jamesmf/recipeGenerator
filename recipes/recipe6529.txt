MEXICAN CASSEROLE


2  lb. lean ground meat
1  onion
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  can cream of chicken soup
1 large  can Ranch Style beans
1  can Ro-Tel tomatoes and green chilies
1  lb. Velveeta cheese
1  pkg. corn tortillas (12)

      Brown together ground meat and onion (chopped). Add soups, beans and tomatoes. Grease a large casserole dish. Shred half the tortillas and place in casserole. Spoon 1/2 the meat mixture over the tortillas. Slice half the cheese and layer over this. Repeat this process. Bake at 325 degrees; for 1 hour.
