SUNNY BROCCOLI SALAD


1  c. mayonnaise or reduced fat mayonnaise or salad dressing
1  small onion, finely chopped
1/2  c. raisins
1/4 to 1/3  c. sugar
2  tbsp. vinegar
7  c. chopped fresh broccoli
1/2  c. sunflower kernels
8  slices bacon, crisp-cooked, drained and crumbled

      In a large mixing bowl, stir together the mayonnaise, onion, raisins, sugar and vinegar. Add broccoli and stir to coat. Cover and chill for 2 to 24 hours.  Stir in sunflower kernels and bacon just before serving. Makes 12 to 16 servings.
