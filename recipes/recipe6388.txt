BASIC VINAIGRETTE DRESSING


1/2  small onion, peeled
1 large  clove garlic, peeled
2  Tbsp. olive or vegetable oil
3  Tbsp. wine vinegar
1/4  c. water
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
1  tsp. dried basil or oregano leaves
1/4  tsp. sugar

      Place all ingredients in an electric blender; cover and blend at high speed about 10 seconds or until smooth. Dressing can be stored in a screw-top jar in the refrigerator. For diet serving, allow 2 tablespoons. Makes about 2/3 cup dressing.
