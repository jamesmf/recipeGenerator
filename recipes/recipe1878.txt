STEAK WITH SOUR CREAM SAUCE
(Makes 6 Servings)  

3  lb. round steak, cut 3/4-inch thick
2  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper
1/2  c. regular all-purpose flour
2  Tbsp. butter
1 (1 lb.)  can tomatoes
1/2  c. chopped green pepper
1/3  c. chopped onion
1  Tbsp. regular all-purpose flour
1  c. dairy sour cream (at room temperature)

      Cut meat into serving pieces; sprinkle with salt and pepper. Coat meat with flour and score with a sharp knife.  Coat again with flour.  In skillet, melt butter; brown meat slowly on all sides. Combine tomatoes, green pepper and onion; pour over meat.  Cover and simmer over low heat 2 to 2 1/2 hours until meat is fork-tender.  Transfer meat to a warm platter.  Into liquid in skillet, blend 1 tablespoon flour; cook, stirring constantly, until thickened (about 5 minutes).  Remove from heat and stir in sour cream.  Serve immediately over Spanish steak.
