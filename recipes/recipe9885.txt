SPINACH-ARTICHOKE CASSEROLE


1 (8 oz.)  can artichoke hearts
2 (10 oz.)  pkg. frozen chopped spinach
8  oz. cream cheese
1/2  c. butter
8  oz. bacon, crisp fried and crumbled
  grated Parmesan cheese

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees;.  Drain artichokes; cut into squares. Cook spinach according to directions; drain.  Combine cream cheese and butter in a saucepan.  Cook over low heat until melted, stirring constantly.  Layer artichokes, spinach, cream cheese mixture and bacon in an 8 x 8 square casserole.  Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.  Bake for 30 minutes or until bubbly.
