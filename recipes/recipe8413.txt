CHICKEN AMBASSADOR


4  to 6 chicken breasts (bone in, with skin)
1  can beef consomm
1  stick butter
  poultry seasoning
1  can artichoke hearts (optional)

      Sprinkle poultry seasoning on chicken.  Melt butter in 9 x 13 baking dish.  Stir in beef consomm.  Add chicken; spoon liquid over chicken.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 1/2 hours, basting every 20 minutes.  Add artichokes during last 10 minutes; just to heat. Serve with rice or noodles.  This is easy to prepare and children like it.
