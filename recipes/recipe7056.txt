MARGARET'S MEAT LOAF


2  lb. hamburger
2  c. bread crumbs (4 or 5 slices)
1/2  c. milk
1/2  c. chopped onions
2  eggs
2  tsp. salt (use only 1 if using soy sauce)
1/4  tsp. pepper
1  tsp. curry powder
1/4  tsp. garlic powder
1/4  c. soy sauce
1  Tbsp. chili powder

      Mix all ingredients together. Bake 90 minutes at 350 degrees;. Let meat loaf set for a few minutes after cooking. Drain off fat.
