EASY CHEESE BALL


1  pkg. cream cheese, softened
1  pkg. corned beef, chopped
4  green onions, slivered
8  oz. finely shredded sharp Cheddar cheese

      Mix together cream cheese, chopped corned beef and green onion. Roll into a ball and then roll over shredded cheese, covering entire cheese ball.  Chill.  Serve with Sociables crackers.
