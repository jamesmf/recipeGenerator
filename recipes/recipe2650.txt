SOUR CREAM COFFEE CAKE

Mixture 1:
1/2  c. chopped walnuts
1/2  c. sugar
1  Tbsp. cinnamon

 
Mixture 2:
1/2  lb. butter (2 sticks), creamed
1  c. sugar
2  eggs
1  tsp. vanilla
1/2  tsp. salt
2  c. sifted regular flour
1  tsp. baking powder
1  tsp. baking soda
1/2  pt. (8 oz.) sour cream

      Mixture 1:  Mix together in small bowl; set aside.
      Mixture 2:  Mix ingredients together in large bowl.  In tube pan, slightly greased and floured, then alternate Mixture 1 and 2 in layers, starting with Mixture 2. Bake in 350 degrees; oven for 45 minutes.
