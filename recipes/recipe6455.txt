7 - UP SALAD


2  boxes Lemon Jello
2  cups 7 Up
1  # 2 1/2  Can crushed pineapple drained
1  cup marshmallows
2  cups hot water
2  large bananas diced

 
Topping:
1/2  cup sugar
1  cup pineapple juice
2  Tbsp. flour
1  egg
1  cup whipped cream

      Jello:  Mix jello, hot water, 7-Up.  Cool slightly.  Add pineapple, marshmallows, and diced bananas.  Let set.
      Topping:  Cook and cool the 1/2 cup sugar, 1 cup pineapple, 2 Tbsp. flour, and egg.  Add the 1 cup whipped cream.  Spread over the set jello.
