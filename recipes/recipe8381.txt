DUTCH MEAT LOAF


1  lb. ground beef
1/2  lb. sausage
1  c. fresh bread crumbs
1  medium onion, chopped
1/2  c. tomato sauce
1  egg, beaten
1 1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
  other 1/2 of tomato sauce
1  c. water
2  Tbsp. vinegar
2  Tbsp. brown sugar
2  Tbsp. prepared mustard

      Bake in a 350 degrees; oven for 1 1/4 hours, basting occasionally. Serves 6 or more.
