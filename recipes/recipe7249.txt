MEXICAN CORNBREAD CASSEROLE


1  c. yellow cornmeal
1/2  c. oil
6  to 8 jalapeno peppers, chopped
3/4  tsp. salt
1  c. sweet milk
2  eggs
1/2  tsp. soda
1  can cream-style corn
1  lb. browned hamburger, drained
1  medium onion, chopped
1/2  lb. rat cheese, grated

      Mix the first 8 ingredients together. Pour half of the cornbread mixture in a greased iron skillet. Layer in meat, cheese and onions. Pour remaining cornbread mixture on top and spread evenly. Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour or until golden brown.
