CROCKPOT PERFECT MEATLOAF


1  1/2 gd. beef
1  egg, beaten
1/2  c. bread crumbs
1/2  c. tomato juice
3  tbsp. chopped onion
3  tbsp. chopped bell pepper
3  tbsp. chopped celery
2  tsp. seasoned salt
1/4  tsp. thyme
  Catsup or chilli sauce

      Using a fork, mix all ingredients, except for catsup.  Shape into a loaf and put into greased crockpot.  Top with  catsup and pepper rings, if desired.  Cover and cook on high for 1 hour, then on low for 6-8 hours.  Quartered potatoes coated with butter may also be cooked with this recipe.  A dash of paprika will give the potatoes a nice browned look.
