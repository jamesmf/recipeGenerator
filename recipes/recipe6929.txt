HAMBURGER-RICE HOT DISH


1  lb. hamburger
3  Tbsp. butter
1/2  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper
1  onion, diced
1/2  c. raw rice
1  can mushroom soup
1  can chicken rice soup
1  c. water
1 1/2  c. diced celery
1 small  can mushrooms

      Brown hamburger in butter; add salt, pepper and onion. Add raw rice, mushroom soup, chicken rice soup, water, diced celery and mushrooms. Bake for 1 hour and 30 minutes at 350 degrees;. Yield: 8 servings.
