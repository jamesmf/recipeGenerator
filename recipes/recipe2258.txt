BARBECUED BRISKET


1  c. vinegar
3  Tbsp. prepared mustard
1 small  can tomato sauce
1  tsp. garlic salt
  pepper and paprika
4  lb. beef brisket
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire
  dash of Tabasco
1  Tbsp. sugar
1  bay leaf
  sliced onions

      Sprinkle brisket with pepper and paprika.  Slice onions on top to cover.  Mix remaining ingredients well and pour over meat. Marinate overnight.  Bake at 300 degrees; to 325 degrees; for about 3 to 4 hours, depending on oven and thickness of meat.  When ready to serve, slice very thinly against the grain and serve with sauce.
