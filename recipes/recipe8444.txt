SWEET AND SOUR MEATBALLS


1  lb. lean hamburger
1/4  lb. sausage
1  egg
1/4  c. bread crumbs
3/4  c. milk
2  Tbsp. onions
1/8  tsp. nutmeg
2  Tbsp. ketchup
1/2  tsp. salt

      Mix all ingredients well.  Shape into small balls and place on baking pan.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 20 to 25 minutes.  Do not over cook!
Sauce:
1  c. ketchup
1  c. jelly

      Mix well.  Heat to melt jelly and stir with whisk.  Pour over meat balls and simmer on low for 2 to 4 hours.
