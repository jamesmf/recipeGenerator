CHOCOLATE MINT FUDGE


1  lb. powdered sugar
1/2  c. cocoa
1/4  c. milk
1  tsp. peppermint extract
1  stick butter (1/2 c.)
1/2  c. chopped nuts

      Mix sugar and cocoa in 8 x 8 x 2-inch pan.  Combine milk and peppermint and pour over sugar mixture.  Lay a whole stick of butter in the middle of sugar mixture.  Microwave on full power for 2 minutes.  Remove pan and stir ingredients.  Stir in nuts and press fudge evenly into the pan.  Chill for 1 hour, then cut into pieces when fudge is hard.
