STRAWBERRY DELIGHT


1 large  pkg. diet strawberry gelatin
1/2  angel cake, torn into pieces
2 (10 oz.)  pkg. unsweetened frozen strawberries

      Mix jello with 2 cups boiling water.  Add strawberries.  Mix angel food cake into mixture.  Pour into 13 x 9-inch pan and chill. May add whipped topping on top if desired.
