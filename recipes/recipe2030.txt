FOREVER AMBER


3  c. water
2  c. coconut
2  c. chopped pecans
2  cans condensed milk (Eagle Brand)

      Mix all ingredients thoroughly.  Place in baking dish or pan. Bake at 350 degrees; for 25 to 30 minutes or until beginning to brown on top.  Let cool.  Then spoon out in bite sizes and roll in powdered sugar.
