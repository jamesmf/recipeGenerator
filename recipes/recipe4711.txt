CHICKEN NOODLE SOUP


1  can kernel corn
5  to 6 large potatoes
2  lb. chicken
1  box bow tie noodles
  salt and pepper
  rosemary, crushed

      Boil chicken until done.  Debone it and put in diced potatoes and corn.  Cook until potatoes are done.  Put in 2 tablespoons crushed rosemary, salt and pepper.  Put in bow tie noodles and cook until tender.
