CORNBREAD BROCCOLI CASSEROLE


2  boxes Jiffy cornbread mix
8  oz. cottage cheese
8  oz. broccoli (fresh or frozen)
1  small onion, chopped

 
Half Recipe:
1  box Jiffy cornbread mix
4  oz. cottage cheese
4  oz. broccoli (fresh or frozen)
1  small onion, chopped

      Mix cornbread as directed on box. Melt butter. Add onions, then broccoli, then cottage cheese. With the other 1/2 stick of butter, melted and grease your pan. For best results, use iron skillet. Use large skillet for full recipe. Bake at 375 degrees; until golden brown.
      For half recipe, use medium small skillet.
      P.S. Will be like cornbread only moist and good!
