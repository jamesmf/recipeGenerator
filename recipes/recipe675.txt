PASTA AND PEPPER SALAD


8 to 10  oz. multicolored rotini (corkscrew pasta)
8  oz. salami slices, cut into strips or frozen, cooked shrimp, thawed
1  medium green pepper, cut into short strips
1  medium sweet red pepper, cut into short strips
1/4  c. chopped onion

 
Dressing:
1/3  c. white or red wine vinegar
1  Tbsp. Dijon-style mustard
1  Tbsp. snipped fresh basil or 1 tsp. dried basil, crushed
1  clove garlic, minced
1/4  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. fresh ground pepper
1/4  c. olive oil

      Cook pasta in a large pot of boiling salted water until al dente.  Drain; rinse in cold water and drain again.  Transfer to mixing bowl.  Add salami or shrimp, green and red pepper strips and onion; toss.
      For Dressing:  In a mixing bowl combine wine vinegar, mustard, basil, garlic, salt and pepper.  Whisk in the olive oil in a thin stream until well combined.  Pour dressing over pasta-vegetable mixture and toss well.  Cover and chill salad for 2 to 4 hours. Makes 8 to 10 side dish servings.
