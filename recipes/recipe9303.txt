EASY CHICKEN AND RICE


14  oz. (1 box) Minute Rice (uncooked)
10  oz. (1 can) soup, cream of chicken
1 soup  can water
  salt (to taste)
  pepper (to taste)
4  (8 halves) chicken breasts

      Spray a 9x13-inch casserole dish with non-stick vegetable spray.  In a bowl, mix together:  rice, soup, water, salt and pepper in bowl.  Pour into the prepared casserole dish.  Place chicken pieces on top.
      Cover dish and bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour.
