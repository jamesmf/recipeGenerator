EGG AND SAUSAGE BREAKFAST CASSEROLE


1  lb. sausage, browned and drained
6  slices white bread, buttered
1 1/2  c. shredded sharp cheese
5  eggs
2  c. milk
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. dry mustard

      Lay buttered bread, butter side down, into oblong casserole dish and cut into pieces.  Put sausage (fried) on top.  Add shredded cheese.  Mix together on top.  Mix eggs, milk, salt and dry mustard together.  Pour over bread mixture.  Cover and refrigerate overnight.  Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees; for 40 minutes.
