CHICKEN CASSEROLE


1  whole chicken, cooked and deboned
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  can Ro-Tel chopped peppers and tomatoes
1  c. chicken broth
1  bag Tostitos, crushed
1  pkg. shredded Colby-Jack cheese

      Tear cooked chicken into small pieces. Mix soups, Ro-Tel and broth. Pour crushed chips into baking pan. Place chicken on top of chips. Pour liquid over chicken. Top with cheese. Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes.
