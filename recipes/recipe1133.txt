SWISS STEAK


1/4  c. flour
1  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
1  lb. beef round steak (about 3/4-inch thick)
1  Tbsp. oil
1  large onion, sliced
1 (16 oz.)  can tomatoes
1/2  c. beef broth
2  c. peeled sliced carrots

      Combine flour, salt and pepper.  Coat steak with seasoned flour.  With wooden mallet, pound meat between wax paper until it is 1/2-inch thick.  Cut into 6 serving pieces.  Heat oil; brown steak well, all sides.  Saut√© onion until tender.  Add tomatoes and broth.  Cover and simmer 1 1/2 hours.  Add carrots.  Cover; simmer 1/2 hour or until meat and carrots are tender.
