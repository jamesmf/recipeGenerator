ITALIAN CHICKEN


6  chicken breasts, boned and skinned
1/2  c. flour
1  Tbsp. Lawry's seasoning salt
1  tsp. poultry seasoning
1/2  c. evaporated milk

	Mix flour, salt and poultry seasoning. Dip first in milk, then in flour mixture. Dip again in milk, then again in flour mixture. Brown in canola oil.
