PORK IN WINE SAUCE


2  thick center cut pork tenderloins*
1  medium purple onion
1/2  c. white wine or dry vermouth
1  Tbsp. soy sauce

      Slice onion in thin rings and saut√© in olive oil until soft and beginning to brown slightly.  Remove and keep warm.  Season both sides or pork with black pepper and garlic powder.  Brown both sides of pork. Return onions to pan and add wine and soy sauce.  Cover and simmer for 35 to 40 minutes. Serves 2.
       Simple preparation results in delightful flavor!
      *Recipe also works well with thick, boneless chicken breasts, but check for doneness at about 25 minutes.
