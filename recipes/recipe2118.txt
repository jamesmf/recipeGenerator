MALLOW CUPS


1  large plain Hershey bar (1/2 lb.)
  Marshmallow Creme
1/8  stick paraffin

      You will also need mini paper cups (for candy).
      Melt candy bar and paraffin in double boiler.  Fill bottom of paper cup with chocolate.  Put 1/2 teaspoon Marshmallow Creme on top of chocolate.  Then cover with chocolate.  Place in refrigerator until it hardens.
