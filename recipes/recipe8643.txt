NO PEEK STEW


2  lbs. stew meat
2  lrg. onions, sliced
5  carrots, cut in chunks
4  potatoes, cut in chunks
3  stalks celery, cut in 1/2-inch pieces
1  Tbsp. salt
1  Tbsp. sugar
3  Tbsp. instant Tapioca
1/4  tsp. pepper
1 1/2  C. tomato juice

      Put raw meat in bottom of 9 x 13-inch baking pan.  Place raw vegetables on top of meat.  Combine salt, sugar, tapioca and pepper; sprinkle evenly over veggies.  Pour juice evenly over top. Cover with heavy duty foil and crimp edges to seal tightly.  Bake at 250 degrees for 5 hours and Don't Peek!
      Will serve at least 6 people.
      Do not make ahead!  When you do this be sure to do same day as the dry ingredients will not mix if made ahead.
