EASY CHEESY BURRITOS


1/2  lb. hamburger
2  Tbsp. chopped onion
2  Tbsp. taco season mix
1/2  c. water
1/2  c. pork and beans
2 to 2 1/2  c. flour
1/2  c. wheat flour
1  pkg. dry yeast
2  tsp. sugar
1  tsp. salt
1  c. hot water
2  Tbsp. oil

      Brown hamburger and onion.  Drain.  Stir  in season mix and water.  Cook over medium  heat  until  water  evaporates, stirring often.  Stir in beans.  Set aside.
      Combine flour, yeast, sugar, salt,  hot  water and oil. Mix well.   (Can  use  all  white flour.)  Roll  out  into  2 (10-inch) round or rectangle pieces,  on floured surface.  Cut each into 4 pieces.  Brush 2 tablespoons  taco  sauce  on each piece,  then 1/4 cup meat mixture in  center  and  wrap  dough around sealing the edges.  Put on cookie  sheet.  Let  rise 15 minutes.  Bake 15 to 20 minutes.  Put  cheese  slice  on  each burrito and bake 2 to 3 minutes longer.  (You can garnish with sour cream and olives.) Yields 8.
Substitute Taco Sauce:
1/2  c. ketchup
2  Tbsp. steak sauce
1  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  tsp. Texas Pete hot sauce

      Mix ingredients.
