NORWEGIAN COOKIES


2  c. sugar
2  eggs
2  c. ground oatmeal
2  c. ground raisins
2  c. plain flour
1  c. shortening
1  tsp. soda (dissolved in hot water)
1  tsp. salt

      Mix all ingredients.  Roll into balls and place 2-inches apart on baking sheet.  Bake 12 to 15 minutes in 350 degrees; oven.
