POTATO SALAD


5  lb. Idaho potatoes, boiled and sliced
8  hard-boiled eggs, sliced or chopped
1/2  onion, chopped
3/4  jar Miracle Whip
1  Tbsp. vinegar
1/2  c. sugar plus 1/2 of 1/3 c. sugar
1/4  tsp. each salt and pepper
2  Tbsp. milk
2  drops yellow food coloring

      Combine Miracle Whip, vinegar, sugar, salt/pepper, milk and food coloring. Add potatoes, eggs and onions; mix well. Refrigerate.
