SLAW SALAD


1  lb. bag of slaw
1/2  c. sunflower seed (not in shell)
1/2  c. almonds
1  pkg. Ramen dry soup mix (oriental)
1/2  c. oil
1/2  c. splenda sweetener
1/3  c. vinegar

      Mix slaw, sunflower seed, almonds, and noodles.
      Take seasoning packet from noodle package and mix with oil, sweetener, and vinegar. Add dressing  just before serving.
