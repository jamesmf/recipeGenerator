NUTTY HONEY DRUMSTICKS


8  chicken drumsticks
2  Tbsp. unsalted margarine
1/8  c. honey
1  Tbsp. Dijon mustard
1/4  c. bread crumbs
1/4  c. finely chopped pecans
  salt and pepper to taste

      Arrange chicken on foiled baking sheet.  Melt butter; stir in honey and mustard and simmer, stirring for 5 minutes.  Brush honey mixture generously on chicken.  Combine crumbs and pecans and sprinkle over chicken. Bake in preheated 350 degrees; oven for 50 to 60 minutes.
