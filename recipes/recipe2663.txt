SPICY PECANS


1  egg white, beaten
1  Tbsp. water
2  Tbsp. butter
1/2  c. sugar
  dash of salt, cinnamon, nutmeg and cloves
2  c. pecans

      Beat 1 egg white until frothy with 1 tablespoon water.  Melt butter in pan; stir a lot.  Add sugar, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg and cloves.  Put pecans in egg white and coat well, then put them into sugar and spices; cover well.  Place in pan with butter and bake at 250 degrees; until done.
