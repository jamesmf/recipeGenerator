CABBAGE SALAD DRESSING


3/4  C. mayonnaise or salad dressing
1/3  C. white vinegar
1/4 - 1/3  C. sugar
1/4  C. buttermilk
1/4  C. sour cream

      Pour ingredients into shaker; shake well.  Pour over cabbage, mix.
      Store leftover dressing in an airtight container in the refrigerator for several months.
