HOMEMADE SALAD DRESSING
(Especially For Potato Salad)  

1  c. egg yolks
1  c. sugar
1  c. vinegar
1  c. sour cream
1  tsp. dry mustard
1/4  tsp. salt

      Place all in top of double boiler and beat until smooth. Cool thoroughly and put in a glass jar. Refrigerate after cooling and it keeps for several weeks. Good also on macaroni salads.
