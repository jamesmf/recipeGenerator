ORIENTAL CABBAGE SALAD

Salad:
1  small head of cabbage, chopped
1  onion, chopped
1  package chicken flavored ramen noodles, crushed
2  tsp. sesame seeds, toasted
1/2  cup slivered almonds, toasted

 
Dressing:
2  Tbsp. sugar
1/2  cup oil
3  Tbsp. vinegar
1/2  tsp salt
1/2  tsp pepper
1  packet flavoring, from noodles

      To make salad:  Mix all ingredients together.  Just before serving, add dressing.
      To make dressing:  Mix all ingredients together.
