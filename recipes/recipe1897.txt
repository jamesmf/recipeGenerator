TACO BURGERS


1/2  c. long grain rice, cooked
3/4  c. water
1/4  c. onion, finely chopped
1/4  c. green pepper, finely chopped
1/2  c. celery, finely chopped
2  Tbsp. oil
2  eggs
1/4  tsp. ground red pepper
1/3  c. mild taco sauce
3  Tbsp. flour
1  c. refried beans
3  (8-inch) flour tortillas, cut in small pieces
1/4  c. grated Cheddar cheese

      Cook rice in water according to package directions.  Saut√© onion, pepper and celery in oil.  Beat eggs with pepper and taco sauce.  Stir in beans, tortillas, cheese, cooked rice and vegetables.  Shape into patties and place onto a hot oiled griddle. Cook until browned on each side, about 4 minutes per side.  Top each patty with 1 tablespoon sour cream, just before serving if desired.  Makes 7 patties.
      Variations:  Add 1 cup cooked beef, chicken or pork to rice-bean mixture before frying.
