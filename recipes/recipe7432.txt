CRABMEAT CASSEROLE


2  lb. backfin crabmeat
2  pkg. cream cheese
2  Tbsp. chopped spring onions
1  Tbsp. heaping horseradish
1  large sour cream
  garlic powder (optional)
  bread crumbs or stuffing mix (for top)
  Parmesan cheese

      Bake at 325 degrees; in 1 1/2-quart buttered casserole dish for 30 minutes.
