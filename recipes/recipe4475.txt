WILD RICE SOUP


2  cans potato soup
2  c. shredded American deluxe cheese
9  slices bacon
1  medium onion
1  c. cooked wild rice
2  pt. half and half

      Combine soup and cheese; heat over low heat until cheese is melted. Cook bacon and onions together. Drain on paper towels. Break bacon into small pieces. Add to soup mixture. Add half and half; simmer over low heat for 1/2 hour.
      Note: Can also be made in a crock-pot.
