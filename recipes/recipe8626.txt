PORK CHOP & POTATO BAKE


6  pork chops
  vegetable oil
  seasoned salt
1  can cream of celery soup
1/2  C. milk
1/2  C. sour cream
1/2  tsp. pepper
24  oz. frozen hash brown potatoes
1  C. shredded cheddar cheese
2 - 8  oz. cans fried onion rings

      Brown pork chops in lightly oiled skillet.  Sprinkle with seasoned salt to taste; set aside.
      Combine soup, milk, sour cream, pepper and 1/2 tsp seasoned salt.  Mix well; stir in hash brown potatoes, 1/2 C. cheddar cheese and 1/2 can of fried onion rings.  Mix well.  Spoon mixture into greased 9 x 13-inch baking pan.  Arrange pork chops over potatoes.
      Bake uncovered at 350 degrees for 40 minutes.  Top with remaining onion rings and cheddar cheese; bake for another 15 minutes.
