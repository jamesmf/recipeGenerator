BAKED FRENCH TOAST


6 to 8  slices thick sliced bread or Texas toast
12  oz. evaporated milk
4  eggs
1/4  c. brown sugar
1  tsp. vanilla
1  tsp. cinnamon

      Lightly coat 13 x 9 pan with cooking spray.  Arrange bread in bottom of pan.  Combine remaining ingredients; mix well.  Pour evenly over bread.  Cover tightly and refrigerate for several hours or overnight.  Preheat oven to 350 degrees;.  Spray bread with cooking spray.  Bake 30 to 35 minutes.  Serve with hot maple syrup, powdered sugar and cinnamon.
