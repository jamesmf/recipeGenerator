TACO BAKE


2  lb. ground beef
1 1/2  c. chopped onions
1 (15 oz.)  can tomato sauce
1 (15 oz.)  can kidney beans (undrained)
1 1/2  tsp. chili powder
1 1/4  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
12  taco shells
2  c. hard yellow cheese, grated
1/2  small head lettuce
2  medium tomatoes, sliced

      Brown beef and onions about 10 minutes.  Stir in tomato sauce, kidney beans, chili powder, salt and pepper.  Break taco shells in 1/2 at fold.  Arrange on bottom of 13 x 9-inch baking dish.  Spoon meat mix over tacos and sprinkle with cheese.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 15 to 20 minutes or until hot.  If desired, top with lettuce and tomatoes.
