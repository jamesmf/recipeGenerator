GOLDEN PARMESAN POTATOES


6  large potatoes
1/4  c. flour
1/4  c. Parmesan cheese
1/3  c. butter or oleo
3/4  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper

      Melt butter in 9 x 13 inch baking dish.  Peel potatoes.  Cut into quarters the long way.  Combine flour, cheese, salt, and pepper in a plastic bag.  Moisten potatoes with water and shake a few at a time in bag coating them well with the cheese mixture. Place potatoes in one layer in the baking dish.  Sprinkle any leftover cheese mixture over the potatoes.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour, turning once during baking.
