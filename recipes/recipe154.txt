EASTER BASKET COOKIES


1/2  c. shortening
1/2  c. butter
1  c. sugar
2  eggs
1  tsp. vanilla
3  c. all-purpose flour
1  tsp. baking powder
1/2  tsp. salt

 
Frosting:
2  c. 10x sugar
1  tsp. vanilla
2  Tbsp. butter
2  Tbsp. hot water
  food coloring
  candy decorations

      Beat shortening, butter and sugar until light and fluffy.  Beat in eggs, 1 at a time.  Combine dry ingredients; blend into mixture. Chill dough for easy handling; roll out on lightly floured surface. Cut out Easter shapes with cookie cutters.  Bake on ungreased cookie sheet at 400 degrees; for 6 to 8 minutes, until lightly browned.  Let stand a few minutes before removing to cool.
      Frosting:  Combine first 4 ingredients in bowl; beat until smooth.  Divide into 4 bowls; tint with food coloring.  Spread on cookies; decorate with candy as desired.
