LEMON ROASTED CHICKEN


1/4  c. Grey Poupon mustard
2  Tbsp. lemon juice
1  Tbsp. olive oil
3  lb. chicken (whole or cut up)

      Mix mustard and lemon juice in a small bowl. Place chicken on broiler pan. Brush with mustard mixture. Season with salt and pepper. Bake at 425 degrees; for 40 minutes or until done.
