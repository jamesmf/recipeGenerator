GIANT GINGERBREAD PEOPLE COOKIES


1  c. butter or margarine
1  c. firmly packed light brown sugar
1  c. sugar
1/2  c. molasses
2  eggs
6  c. flour
2  tsp. baking powder
2  tsp. ginger
2  tsp. cinnamon
1/2  tsp. salt
1  tsp. baking soda
1  tsp. ground cloves
2  egg whites
4 1/2  c. sifted confectioners sugar
1  tsp. vanilla
6 to 7  tsp. hot water

      Cookies:  Beat butter and sugars.  Add molasses and eggs.  Add combined flour, baking powder, soda, salt and spices.  Mix at low speed.  Divide dough into 3 portions.  Wrap each; chill on 17 x 14-inch cookie sheet.  Roll out each portion to 1/8-inch thickness. Use patterns to cut 2 people from one portion.  Remove excess dough (reserve).  Repeat with two remaining portions and reserved dough. Bake at 350 degrees; for 15 to 20 minutes.  Remove to wire rack; cool.
      Decorations:  Beat egg whites unitl foamy; add confectioners sugar.  Add vanilla and water, beating until glaze is thick and of spreading consistency.  Tint glaze with food coloring, as desired. Decorate with glaze and candies, as desired.
