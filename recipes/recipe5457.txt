BAKED POTATO SOUP


4  large baked potatoes, cubed
6  Tbsp. butter
1/3  c. flour
2  pt. half and half
20  oz. block Velveeta cheese
  white pepper to taste
  garlic salt or powder to taste
1  tsp. hot pepper
1/2  lb. bacon, crumbled
1  c. shredded Cheddar cheese
  fresh chives

      In a large soup pot, melt butter.  Add flour and stir until thick.  Add milk to mixture; stir and cook until thickened.  Add Velveeta and potatoes.  Season with other ingredients.  To serve, place soup in a bowl and top with bacon and chives.
