MARINATED VEGGIE SALAD


1/2  c. sugar
1/2  c. oil
1/2  c. vinegar
1  can shoepeg corn, drained
1/2  c. green onions, chopped
1  can Le Sueur peas, drained
1/2  c. bell pepper, chopped
1  can French style green beans, drained
1  can black olives, chopped
1/2  c. celery

      In sauce pan, heat sugar, oil, and vinegar until sugar is dissolved; remove and cool. Mix veggies together and pour cooled dressing over and toss. Can be refrigerated for 24 hours. Toss occasionally during this time. You may add spiral pasta to veggies if desired.
