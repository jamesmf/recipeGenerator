CAULIFLOWER BROCCOLI SALAD


1 large  bunch broccoli
1  large head cauliflower
1 (8 oz.)  jar pimentos
1  large onion
1  medium bell pepper

      Trim vegetables in bite size pieces and toss with pimentos, onion and bell pepper.
Dressing:
1  c. mayonnaise
1/2  c. vinegar
1/2  c. sugar or to taste
  salt and pepper to taste

      Mix well and pour over vegetables just before serving time. Dressing is better if made ahead and let set in refrigerator overnight.
