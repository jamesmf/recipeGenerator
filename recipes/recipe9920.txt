BREADED CAULIFLOWER


1  head cauliflower
2  eggs, beaten
  Bread crumbs
  Oil for frying
  Salt and pepper

      Break cauliflower into buds.  Simmer in salted water until fork tender but not too soft.  Cool.  Dip buds in beaten egg seasoned with salt and pepper and then roll in bread crumbs to coat.  Fry in deep oil at 350 degrees; until brown.  Drain on paper towels.
