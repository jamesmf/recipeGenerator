OVEN BARBECUE BEEF


5 to 6  lb. chuck roast
1  large onion, chopped
1  garlic clove, minced
3  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
  dash of Tabasco sauce
2  Tbsp. white vinegar
3  c. catsup
1/2  tsp. dry mustard
1/2  tsp. black pepper
4  c. ginger ale
1/4  tsp. lemon juice

      Place roast in large roasting pan.  Combine the remaining ingredients.  Mix well.  Pour over roast.  Cover with top to the roaster or heavy-duty aluminum foil.  Bake at 300 degrees; for 6 to 7 hours.  Cool.  Slice very thin or shred in food processor for sandwiches.
