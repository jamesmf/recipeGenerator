FAT-FREE CUCUMBER SALAD DRESSING


1  small cucumber, peeled
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  c. plain low-fat yogurt
1  Tbsp. reduced calorie mayonnaise
1/4  tsp. lemon juice
1  Tbsp. fresh parsley, chopped
1/4  tsp. dried dill weed

      Shred cucumber. Spread on paper towel and sprinkle with salt. Squeeze out excess moisture from cucumber.
       Combine yogurt, mayonnaise and lemon juice in a small bowl. Stir in rest of the ingredients.  Cover and chill. Stir well before serving.  Serve with salad greens.
