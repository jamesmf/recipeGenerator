PICKLED MUSHROOMS


2  lb. small mushrooms of equal size
1  c. cider vinegar
1  c. water
1/2  tsp. salt
1  Tbsp. sugar
1  crumbled bay leaf
3  grains allspice
10  peppercorns

      Wash mushrooms well and plunge into a large kettle of boiling salted water.  Cover and cook for 12 to 15 minutes.  In separate pot, combine cider vinegar, 1 cup water, salt, sugar and all the spices.  Bring to boil; reduce heat.  Cover and simmer 15 minutes. With slotted spoon, transfer mushrooms to jars.  Pour room temperature marinade over mushrooms to cover.  Seal and refrigerate overnight before serving.
