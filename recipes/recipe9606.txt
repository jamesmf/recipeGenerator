COUNTRY STYLE MEAT LOAF


1 1/2  lb. ground beef
1  c. soft bread crumbs
1  c. milk
1  egg
  dash of salt and pepper
1  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  small onion, chopped
1/2  c. ketchup
3  Tbsp. brown sugar
2  Tbsp. prepared mustard
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  c. water

      Soften bread in milk.  Add to meat together with egg, salt, pepper, onion and Worcestershire sauce.  Mix well and form into loaf.
      In small bowl or 2-cup pitcher, combine ketchup, brown sugar, mustard, salt and water.  Arrange meat loaf in cast-iron frying pan or Pyrex dish.  Pour mixture over meat.  Bake 1 hour and 15 minutes in 300 degrees; oven.  Baste occasionally.
