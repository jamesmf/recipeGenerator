CHICKEN-CHIP BAKE


2  c. cooked chicken, cut into bite-size cubes
2  c. sliced celery
3/4  c. mayonnaise
1/3  c. toasted slivered almonds
2  Tbsp. lemon juice
2  tsp. grated onion
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  c. shredded process American cheese
1  c. crushed potato chips

      Combine all ingredients, except cheese and potato chips. Pile lightly in a 9-inch by 9-inch ovenware dish.  Sprinkle with 1/2 cup grated cheese, then the crushed potato chips.  Bake in 425 degrees; oven for 20 minutes or until hot and bubbly.  Serves 6.
