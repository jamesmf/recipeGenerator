MEXICAN SALAD


1  package lemon jello
1  c. cold water
1  Tbsp. Sugar
4  tsp. Vinegar
1  tsp salt
1  c. cabbage, shredded
1  apple, diced with skin on
1/2  c. chopped celery
1/2  c. green pepper chopped
1/2  tsp. chili powder

      Dissolve lemon jello in 1 c. hot water.  Add rest of ingredients.  Mix;  place in oblong pan and place in refrigerator until set.
