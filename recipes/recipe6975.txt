NONIE'S LASAGNA


  large family pkg. ground meat
2  cans spaghetti sauce
16  oz. box lasagna noodles
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. shredded Mozzarella cheese
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. Cheddar cheese

      Brown ground meat; add spaghetti sauce and mix. Boil lasagna noodles until tender; drain. Layer noodles, meat mixture, and both cheeses. The final layer should be cheese. Bake at 350 degrees; until completely heated through.
