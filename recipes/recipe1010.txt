CORN CHOWDER SOUP


2  to 3 strips bacon
1  onion, diced
2  potatoes, cubed
1/2  c. water
1  can cream-style corn
2  c. milk
1/2  tsp. salt
1  c. finely sliced ham
1/2  c. Cheddar cheese, grated

      Fry bacon (2 or 3 strips) until crisp.  Remove bacon and fry onion until limp.  Add potatoes and water; boil until potatoes are done.  Add corn, milk, salt and ham.  Top with cheese and bacon. Simmer.
