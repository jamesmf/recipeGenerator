SHIPWRECK CASSEROLE


1  lb. ground beef
  salt and pepper to taste
  chopped onions
3  layers sliced potatoes
1/2  c. grated american or cheddar cheese
1 small  can pork and beans
1/2  to 3/4  can condensed tomato soup

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Place  ground beef in baking dish. Over the meat sprinkle layer of onions, 3 layers of potatoes, salt, pepper, cheese, pork and beans, and tomato soup. Cover tightly with foil. Bake 1 1/2 hours.
