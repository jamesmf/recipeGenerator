APPLE CAKE WITH CARAMEL SAUCE

Apple Cake:
2  c. white sugar
1/2  c. shortening
2  eggs
2  tsp. soda
1  tsp. salt
2  c. flour
2  tsp. cinnamon
4  c. apples, peeled and finely chopped
1  c. chopped walnuts

      Cream sugar and shortening together; add eggs and dry ingredients.  Then blend in apples and walnuts.  Pour batter into 9 x 13-inch pan.  Bake 1 hour at 350 degrees; in a 9 x 13-inch pan.
Caramel Sauce:
1/2  c. white sugar
1/2  c. brown sugar
1/2  c. cream
1/2  c. butter
1  tsp. vanilla
1/2  tsp. salt

      Mix sauce ingredients together and bring to a boil.  Serve warm over apple cake and top with whipped cream.
