SALMON ROLL


2  c. baked salmon, flaked, no bones
8  oz. cream cheese, regular or lite
1  Tbsp. lemon juice
1  Tbsp. minced onion
1  Tsp. horseradish
1/4  tsp. smoke flavor
1/4  tsp. salt

      Use king, silver or red salmon.  Stir all ingredients together.  Shape into roll, ball, log, or leave in bowl.  Chill for 3 hours.  Serve with crackers.
      Option:  Roll into chopped nuts.
