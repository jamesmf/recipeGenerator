SHRIMP SALAD


  Miracle Whip
  sugar
  half and half cream
  lettuce, torn to bite size pieces
  celery, cut diagonally in thin slices
  green, red or yellow pepper, slivered (optional)
  shrimp, either canned or frozen

      Mix Miracle Whip with sugar until dressing tastes sweet.  Thin with half and half cream until consistency of very heavy cream. Pour over remaining ingredients and it is ready to serve.  Can be served with hard-boiled eggs or deviled eggs and toast points. Quick and easy lunch.
      Lettuce:  Torn to bite size pieces and amount needed to serve number of persons.  Shrimp:  Amount for number of persons, either canned or frozen, thawed under running water, cleaned and tails removed.
