WINTER SLAW

Slaw:
1  each Granny Smith apple and tart red apple, quartered, cored and thinly sliced
2  Tbsp. fresh lemon juice
2  large celery ribs, thinly sliced
1  red bell pepper, cut in thin strips
4  c. thinly sliced green cabbage
2  large Belgian endives, halved lengthwise, cored and thinly sliced lengthwise
1  small red onion, thinly sliced
4  scallions, thinly sliced

 
Dressing:
1/4  c. unseasoned rice vinegar
3  Tbsp. olive oil
3/4  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. black pepper
1  c. crumbled Blue cheese

      SLAW: In a bowl, toss apples and lemon juice. Add remaining ingredients.
      DRESSING: In a bowl, whisk ingredients. Add to slaw with 3/4 cup cheese. (Can be refrigerated up to 2 hours ahead.) Top with remaining cheese. Makes 12 servings.
