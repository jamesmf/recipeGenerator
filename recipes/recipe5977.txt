SEVEN LAYER PEA SALAD


1  lg. bag frozen peas
1  head of lettuce
1  lg. green pepper
1  lg. onion
2  c. mayo or salad dressing
  bacon bits
  shredded cheese (any type that you desire)
2  1/2 florets broccoli
2  1/2 florets cauliflower
1/2  c. radishes
1/2  c. onions
1  c. Miracle Whip
1/3  c. vinegar
1/3  c. sugar
1/8  tsp. seasoned salt

      Better if refrigerated overnight.
