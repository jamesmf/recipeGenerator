SALSA CALIENTE


1 (16 oz.)  can crushed tomatoes
13  oz. can green chili salsa
2  Tbsp. vinegar
2  jalapeno peppers, to taste
6  green onions, chopped
1  Tbsp. hot pepper sauce (Tabasco)

      Combine tomatoes and chili salsa in medium bowl.  Add vinegar; stir until mixed well.  Let sit 15 minutes.  Add remaining ingredients; blend thoroughly.  Cover and refrigerate for at least 24 hours.
