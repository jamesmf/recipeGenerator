CHEESEBURGER PIE

Main Ingredients:
1  unbaked pie shell
1  lb. ground beef
1/2  tsp. oregano
1  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
1/4  c. chopped onion
1/4  c. chopped or sliced green pepper
1/2  c. bread crumbs
4  oz. tomato sauce

      Brown meat; add other ingredients and place in a pie shell.
Topping:
8  oz. grated American cheese
1  egg, beaten
1/4  c. milk
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. dry mustard
1/2  tsp. worcestershire sauce

      Mix topping and spread over meat mixture in pie shell.  Bake at 425 degrees; for 20 minutes.
