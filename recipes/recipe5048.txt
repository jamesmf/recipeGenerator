KOREAN SALAD


1  bunch spinach
5  strips crumbled bacon
3  or 4 hard-boiled eggs, sliced
1  can bean sprouts, drained
1  can water chestnuts, sliced and drained

 
Dressing:
1  c. oil
1/4  c. vinegar
1  c. sugar
  dash of salt
1  medium diced onion
  dash of catsup
  dash of Worcestershire sauce

      Chop spinach finely and  add  other  ingredients; toss. Just before serving, add dressing.
