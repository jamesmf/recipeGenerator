LASAGNA


2  lb. ground beef (or deer)
  Italian seasoning
  sugar, to taste
1 large  can tomato sauce
2  cans tomato paste
1  pkg. Monterey Jack and Cheddar cheese blend
8  oz. cottage cheese (small curd)
1  pkg. Swiss cheese
1  box lasagna noodles
  Parmesan cheese (to sprinkle on top)

      Brown ground meat and drain; add Italian seasoning. Add tomato sauce plus 3/4 can of water, tomato paste and sugar. Simmer on low. Boil noodles until done; drain and separate. Layer mixture of sauce, noodles, and cheese. Sprinkle more Italian seasoning and Parmesan cheese on top for extra flavor. Bake at 375 degrees; 30 to 40 minutes.
