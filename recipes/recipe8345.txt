ONE DISH CHICKEN AND RICE BAKE


1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can Campbell's cream of mushroom soup
1  c. water
3/4  c. regular long-grain rice (uncooked)
1/4  tsp. paprika
1/4  tsp. pepper
4  skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

	In a 2-quart shallow baking dish, mix soup, water, rice, paprika and pepper. Place chicken on rice mixture. Sprinkle with additional paprika and pepper. Cover and bake at 375 degrees; for 45 minutes or until done. Serves 4.
