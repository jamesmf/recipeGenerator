CHINESE COLE SLAW


3 -4  c. shredded cabbage
1/2  pkg. broccoli and carrot slaw
  6-7 green onions, sliced
1  pkg. slivered almonds
1  pkg. sunflower seeds
2  pkg. chicken flavored ramen noodles, mashed coarsely

      Mix the above together and set aside.
Dressing:
1  c. oil
1  c. sugar
3/4  c. vinegar
2  packs seasoning mix from ramen noodles

      Boil dressing ingredients until sugar dissolves. Then  cool completely.  Pour over slaw mixture.   Refrigerate overnight.
