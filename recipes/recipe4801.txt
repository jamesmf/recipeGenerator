POLISH POTATO-KIELBASA SOUP


1  lb. smoked kielbasa
1  c. onion, chopped
5  c. water
2  c. carrots
2  Tbsp. vinegar
2  c. celery, chopped
3  c. potatoes, cut in bite size
4  c. cabbage
3  oz. beef base
1  bay leaf
  salt and pepper to taste

      In a 4 to 5-quart pot, mix all ingredients, except potatoes and kielbasa.  Bring to a boil and simmer until veggies are half cooked (about 15 minutes).  Add potatoes; cook until they are tender (about 15 minutes).  Add sliced kielbasa and cook and additional 5 minutes.  Serve with fresh rye bread and real butter.
