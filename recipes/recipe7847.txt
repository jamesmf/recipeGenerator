CHILI


2  lb. ground beef
1  medium onion, diced
3  Tbsp. celery flakes
1  tsp. garlic powder
  Salt and pepper
2  tsp. (mild) chili powder
  Dash of sugar
1  family-size tomato soup
4  cans kidney beans
2  c. water

      Brown ground beef and drain.  Add remaining ingredients and cook until thick.
