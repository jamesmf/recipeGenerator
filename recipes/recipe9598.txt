MARINADE FOR PORK TENDERLOIN


1  pork tenderloin
3  Tbsp. soy sauce
1  Tbsp. ketchup
1  clove garlic, chopped fine
1  Tbsp. oil
1/2  tsp. oregano
1/2  tsp. black pepper

      Combine all ingredients. Place pork tenderloin in marinade and marinate 2 to 3 hours. Place pork and marinade in roasting pan and bake at 325 degrees; for 1 hour.  Add water as needed while baking. Serve with applesauce.
