FRENCH MEAT PIE


3/4  lb. beef
1/3  lb. fresh pork
1  small onion
1 1/2  c. water
  salt and pepper to taste
1/2  tsp. poultry seasoning
1/8  tsp. powdered cloves or nutmeg
  pastry for 2 crust pie

      Grind beef, pork and onion and mix well.  Add water and seasoning.  Cook and stir until all red color disappears from meat. Remove from heat and cool.  Add cracker crumbs to thicken.  Line 9-inch pie plate with pastry.  Fill with meat mixture.  Cover with top crust.  Cut slashes and flute edges.  Bake at 450 degrees; until brown.
