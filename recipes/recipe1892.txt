CHICKEN FAJITA


1  lb. boneless chicken breasts
2  Tbsp. lemon juice
2  Tbsp. oil
1  Tbsp. Mrs. Dash seasoning
1/2  tsp. oregano
1/2  tsp. pepper
1  large red and green pepper
1  large onion
1/2  lb. mushrooms
2  large tomatoes

      Cut chicken into bite size pieces.  Mix lemon juice, oil, seasoning, oregano and peppers and marinate chicken for several hours.  Stir-fry chicken until cooked.  Add cut up pepper, onion, mushrooms and tomatoes and stir-fry for 2 minutes.  Serve with hot tortillas and Spanish rice.  Grate cheese and lettuce for garnish.
