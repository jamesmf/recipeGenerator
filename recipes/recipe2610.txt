HOT CHEESE BISCUITS


1 small  jar cheese spread
1/2  c. butter flavor Crisco
1  c. flour
1/8  tsp. salt

      Mash cheese and Crisco; cream well.  Add flour and salt; mix well.  Roll into small balls; chill 1 hour.  Place on ungreased cookie sheet.  Bake at 450 degrees; for 10 minutes.  Balls will flatten as they cook.  Serve at your next brunch.
