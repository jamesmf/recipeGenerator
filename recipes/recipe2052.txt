LICORICE CARAMELS
(Ooh Good)  

1  can condensed milk
2  c. sugar
1  c. butter or margarine
1 1/2  c. light Karo syrup
1/2  tsp. salt
3/4  tsp. black food coloring
3/4  tsp. anise

      In heavy saucepan, combine condensed milk, sugar, butter, Karo and salt.  Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until 234 degrees; on candy thermometer; remove from heat.  Add food coloring and anise; pour into greased 9 x 13-inch pan.  Let cool 2 hours or more.  Cut and wrap in waxed paper.
