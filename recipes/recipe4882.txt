CREAMY PUMPKIN SOUP


3  strips bacon
1  c. chopped onion
2  cloves garlic, minced
2  c. cooked, mashed pumpkin
2  Tbsp. flour
1  can (12 oz.) evaporated milk, divided
3  c. chicken broth
1/2  tsp. curry powder
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
1/4  tsp. nutmeg
  sour cream (optional)

      Cook bacon until crisp; crumble and set aside.  In a saucepan, saut√© onion and garlic in 1 tablespoon bacon drippings until tender.  In blender or food processor, puree pumpkin, flour, 1/3 cup milk and onion mixture; add to pan.  Add broth, spices, and remaining milk. Bring to boil over medium heat.  Boil for 2 minutes.  Top servings with dollop of sour cream and sprinkle with bacon.  Serve with rolls, fruit, and cheese.  Serves 6-8.
