FRESH FRUIT SALAD

Fruit:
2  oranges, peeled and sectioned
2  apples, cubed
2  bananas, sliced
2  peaches, peeled and sliced

      Combine fruit and set aside.
Salad Dressing:
1  cup orange juice
1/2  cup sugar
2  tsp. cornstarch

      Combine all ingredients for salad dressing in a small sauce pan.  Cook, stirring constantly until thickened.  Cool.  Serve fruit with salad dressing.
