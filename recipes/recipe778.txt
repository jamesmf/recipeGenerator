CHINESE CHICKEN SALAD


1/2  pkg. maifun rice sticks
3  c. shredded, cooked chicken
1/2  c. chopped green onions
1  large head lettuce, shredded
2  Tbsp. toasted sesame seed

 
Dressing:
1  tsp. dry mustard
3  Tbsp. honey
2  Tbsp. sugar
2  Tbsp. soy sauce
2  Tbsp. sesame oil
1/2  c. salad oil
1/2  c. rice vinegar

      Break maifun sticks in 3-inch pieces.  Deep fry rice sticks in batches at 375 degrees; to 400 degrees; until they puff.  Drain.  Make dressing. Combine salad ingredients.  Add dressing.
