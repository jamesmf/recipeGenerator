FROZEN VIRGIN BANANA DAIQUIRI


2  tsp. rum extract
2  small fresh bananas, peeled and sliced
3  Tbsp. lime juice
6  packets Equal
1/2  c. pineapple juice
6  to 8 ice cubes

      Combine ingredients in blender.  Blend on high until thick and creamy.  Pour into tall glasses.  Makes 4 servings, 6 ounces each. Calories per serving:  78.  Diabetic exchange:  2 fruit.
