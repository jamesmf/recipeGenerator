CHILI SOUP


1  lb. hamburger
1  small onion
1  pt. tomato paste
1  pt. water
2  tsp. chili powder
1  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
1 1/2  Tbsp. brown sugar
1  pt. kidney beans, drained
  shredded cheese

      Brown  hamburger  and onion.  Drain.  Stir in remaining ingredients,  except cheese; cover and simmer 20 minutes.  Top each serving with cheese.
