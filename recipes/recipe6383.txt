CHEESEBURGER SOUP


1  lb. ground beef
3/4  c. chopped onion
3/4  c. shredded carrots
3/4  c. diced celery
1  tsp. dried basil
1  tsp. dried parsley flakes
4  Tbsp. butter
2  cans (14 1/2 oz.) chicken broth
4  c. diced peeled potatoes
1/4  c. flour
2  c. cubed cheese
1 1/2  c. milk
3/4  tsp. salt
1/4 to 1/2  tsp. pepper
1/4  c. sour cream

      In 3-quart saucepan, brown ground beef and onion; set aside. In same pan, saut carrots, celery, basil and parsley flakes in 1 tablespoon butter until veggies are tender (about 10 minutes). Add both cans of chicken broth and potatoes; add cooked hamburger. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat, cover and simmer 10 to 12 minutes.
       In small pan, melt 3 tablespoons butter and add flour. Cook and stir 3 to 5 minutes or until bubbly. Add to soup and bring to boil. Cook and stir 2 minutes. Reduce heat to low; add cheese, milk, salt and pepper. Cook and stir until cheese melts; take from heat and add sour cream.
