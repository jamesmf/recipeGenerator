CRANBERRY SALAD


1 large  box cranberry jello
1/2  c. sugar
  dash of salt
1  c. hot water
1  Tbsp. lemon juice
1  c. chopped fresh cranberries
1 (9 oz.)  can crushed pineapple
2  oranges, diced
1  c. chopped celery
1/2  c. chopped pecans

      Mix jello, sugar and juice in hot water.  Chill until thickened.  Add cranberries, oranges, celery and nuts.  Chill overnight.
