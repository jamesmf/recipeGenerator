DELUXE PEANUT BRITTLE


3  c. sugar
1  c. light corn syrup
1/2  c. water
1  lb. raw peanuts
3  Tbsp. butter or margarine
1 1/2  tsp. soda
1  tsp. salt
1 1/2  tsp. vanilla extract

      Combine sugar, corn syrup and water in a Dutch oven; cook over low heat to soft-crack stage (270 degrees;).  Stir in peanuts; cook to hard-crack stage (300 degrees;).  Remove from heat.  Stir in butter, soda, salt and vanilla.  Pour into 2 warm buttered 15 x 10 x 1-inch jelly roll pans and spread to edges of pan.  Cool and break into pieces. Yield:  3 pounds.
