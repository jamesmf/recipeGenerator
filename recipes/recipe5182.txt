WORLD'S BEST SPINACH DIP
(Chill Time: 2 hours. Prep: 10 min. Chill: 2 hrs)  

1  package (10 oz.) frozen chopped spinach, thawed and squeezed dry
1  container (16 oz.) sour cream
1  cup mayonnaise
1  package (1.4 oz.) vegetable soup and dip mix
3  green onions, chopped

      In medium bowl, combine spinach, sour cream, mayonnaise, recipe mix, and green onions.
      Cover and refrigerate until ready to serve, or up to 2 days to blend flavors.
      Stir before serving.
      Serve with water crackers or assorted raw vegetables to dip.
