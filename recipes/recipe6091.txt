STRAWBERRY DELIGHT SALAD


16  oz. (1 large tub) Cool Whip
12  oz. (1 can) pineapple, crushed
8  oz. (1 pkg.) sour cream
5  oz. (1 can) sweetened condensed milk (Eagle brand)
21  oz. (1 can) pie filling, strawberry*
1  c. pecans, chopped (optional)
16  oz. (1 bag) marshmallows, miniature (optional)

      Mix all ingredients together in a large bowl.
      Refrigerate (1 hour) or freeze depending on firmness preferred.
      *NOTE:  Cherry pie filling can be substituted very well.
