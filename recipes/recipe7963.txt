REUBEN SANDWICH


2  pieces Jewish Corn Rye, thinly sliced
1/2  pound corned beef
1/4  pound swiss cheese
4  oz. sauerkraut, rinsed and drained
4  oz. Russian dressing (recipe follows)

      Place half of corn beef and half of cheese on bread and repeat another layer.  Steam until corn beef is hot and cheese is melted.  Layer beef, cheese, sauerkraut, Russian dressing and repeat.  Top with bread.
Russian Dressing:
1/2  cup mayonnaise
1/2  cup chili sauce
1  Tbsp. horseradish, drained
1  Tbsp. pickles, sweet and sour, chopped
1  tsp. fresh lemon juice
1  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/4  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper

      In a medium bowl combine all ingredients until blended.
