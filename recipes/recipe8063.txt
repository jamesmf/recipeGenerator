QUICK CHICKEN POT PIE


1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can of cream of chicken soup
1  can of mixed vegetables
1  can of white boneless chicken
2  frozen pie shells
  salt & pepper to taste

	Mix first three ingredients together in bowl.  Add salt and pepper if you prefer.  Pour this mixture into one pie shell (slightly thawed).	Allow second pie shell to thaw completely. Cover pie with second pie shell.  Press pie shells together at edges and pierce with fork to allow heat to escape.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 25 minutes, or until desired brownness is reached.  YUM-YUM.
