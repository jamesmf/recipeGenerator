PECAN CRISPS


1 1/2  c. plain sifted flour
1  c. sugar
3/4  tsp. salt
1/2  c. soft shortening
1  egg, separated
3  Tbsp. milk
1  tsp. vanilla
1  c. finely chopped nuts

      Heat oven to 375 degrees;.  Sift flour, sugar and salt into bowl.  Mix thoroughly with fork the shortening, egg yolk, milk and vanilla. Form into balls size of walnut.  Place on ungreased baking sheet. Press 1/16-inch thick with bottom of greased glass dipped in sugar. Brush with slightly beaten egg white.  Sprinkle with pecans.  Bake 8 to 10 minutes.
