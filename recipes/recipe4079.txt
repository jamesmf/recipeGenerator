HERBED CHICKEN BITES


1  c. crumbled herb stuffing mix
1/4  c. Parmesan cheese
2  tsp. Accent
1  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. paprika
1/2  tsp. salt
4  chicken breasts (boneless)
1  stick margarine

	Mix together ingredients except chicken and margarine. Melt margarine. Cut chicken into bite-size pieces. Dip in melted margarine. Roll in crumb mixture. Place on baking pan. Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes or until nicely browned.
