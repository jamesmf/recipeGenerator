GREEN CHILI PIE


2  cans chopped green chillies
1  large (12 oz) mild shredded cheddar cheese
2  eggs
2  Tbsp. milk
  Tabasco sauce to taste

      Butter a casserole dish. Drain chillies and place in bottom of dish. Cover with cheese. Mix eggs, milk and tabasco sauce. Pour over all ingredients. Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.
