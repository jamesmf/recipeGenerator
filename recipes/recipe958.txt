RED SNAPPER MEXICANA


1 1/2  lb. snapper or sturgeon
1/2 to 1  c. flour
  salt and pepper to taste
3 to 4  Tbsp. butter
6  oz. (1 c.) salsa
12  oz. Monterey Jack cheese, grated
6  oz. Cheddar cheese, grated
  chopped green chilies
  chopped onion and parsley

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees;.  Dip fillets in flour, salt and pepper.  Melt butter in skillet and lightly brown fish on both sides, adding more butter as necessary.  Place fish in individual casserole dishes. Top each with salsa, cheeses, chilies, onion and parsley.  Bake about 10 minutes or until done.
