FROZEN HAM SANDWICHES


1  lb. thin sliced ham
1/2  lb. sliced Swiss cheese
1 1/2  tsp. poppy seed or celery seed
1/2  lb. butter
1  small onion
1  Tbsp. prepared mustard
1  tsp. Worcestershire sauce

      Mix  all  ingredients  together well,  except  ham  and cheese.   Spread onto  both  sides  of  hamburger  buns,  like mayonnaise.  Put on ham and cheese.  Wrap individually in foil and freeze.  Heat 1 hour at 375 degrees; in foil.  If eaten when made, heat at 350 degrees; for 1/2  hour  in foil.  Yields 2 1/4 dozen sandwiches.
