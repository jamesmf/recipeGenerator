CHICKEN NOODLE SOUP


6  chicken breasts, cooked
  small pkg. carrots, cut up, or baby carrots
  small bunch celery, cut in pieces
2  medium onions, cut up
3  chicken bouillon cubes
1  can tomato juice
1  can V-8 juice
  salt and pepper to taste
2  pkg. egg noodles, wide or extra wide

      Cook all vegetables and juice until vegetables are almost done.  Drop bouillon cubes in pot while boiling.  Add chicken which has been cooked and cut into chunks.  In a separate pot, cook egg noodles until done; drain and add to soup.  Season as desired with salt and pepper.  Good with crackers or by itself. Enjoy!
