TACO PIE


1  lb. hamburger meat
1  pkg. taco seasoning
1/2  c. water
1  can black olives (small)
2  c. crushed Fritos
1  c. sour cream
1  c. grated Cheddar cheese
1  can crescent rolls

      Brown meat and drain. Add seasoning, water and olives and simmer 5 minutes. Add sour cream. Press unrolled crescent roll dough into 10-inch pie plate. Pat 1 cup crushed Fritos in bottom of crust. Pour meat mixture on chips and sprinkle cheese on top. Sprinkle remaining chips on top. Bake at 375 degrees; for 20 to 25 minutes.
      May garnish with shredded lettuce, tomatoes or guacamole.
