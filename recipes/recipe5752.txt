MEATBALL MINESTRONE SOUP


1  lb. ground beef or ground turkey
1  egg, beaten
1/2  c. chopped onion
1/4  c. dry bread crumbs
1  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
1 (15 oz.)  can tomato sauce
1  can kidney beans with liquid
2 1/2  c. water
1/2  tsp. oregano
1/4  tsp. thyme
1  c. sliced celery
1/4  c. uncooked macaroni
1/4  c. chopped fresh parsley or 1 Tbsp. dry

      In mixing bowl, combine meat, egg, onions, crumbs, salt and pepper.  Shape into 30 one-inch balls.  In a large saucepan, brown meatballs on all sides.  Drain excess fat.  Add remaining ingredients, except macaroni and parsley.  Cover and simmer 20 minutes.  Add macaroni; simmer for 10 minutes or until tender. Stir in parsley.  Makes 2 quarts.
      Freeze in single size servings for later.
