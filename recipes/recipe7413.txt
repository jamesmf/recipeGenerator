DUMPLINGS


1 3/4  c. flour
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  c. water
1/2  tsp. baking powder (optional)

      Mix all ingredients together until well mixed.  Drop by teaspoon into boiling water.  Boil for 2 minutes.  First boil one. If too soft, add a little more flour to dough.  Dough will be a little sticky.
