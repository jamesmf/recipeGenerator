SESAME TURKEY WITH HONEY DIP


1/2  c. Hellmann's real mayonnaise
1  tsp. dry mustard
1  tsp. instant minced onion
1/2  c. fine dry bread crumbs
1/4  c. sesame seed
2  c. cubed, cooked turkey

 
Honey Dip:
1  c. Hellmann's real mayonnaise
2  Tbsp. honey

      Mix mayonnaise, mustard and onion; set aside.  Mix crumbs and sesame seed.  Coat turkey with real mayonnaise mixture, then crumb mixture.  Place on baking sheet.  Bake in 425 degrees; oven for 12 minutes or until lightly browned.  Serve hot with dip.  Serves 6.
      Honey Dip:  Mix 1 cup Hellmann's real mayonnaise with 2 tablespoons honey.
