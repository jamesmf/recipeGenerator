HALLOWEEN PUMPKINS


1  c. Eagle Brand milk
1/2  tsp. almond flavor
1 (3 oz.)  pkg. orange gelatin
2 2/3  c. finely grated coconut
1/2  c. ground blanched almonds
  orange food coloring

      In a medium bowl, combine milk, coconut, 3 tablespoons gelatin, almond flavoring and food coloring (to tint).  Form into small balls (about 1/2 tablespoon).  Sprinkle remaining gelatin into dish; roll each ball to coat.  Place on waxed paper.  Chill. Cloves can be stuck on balls.
      Other gelatins can be substituted for other colors.
