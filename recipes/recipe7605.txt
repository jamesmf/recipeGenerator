ROTEL CHICKEN CASSEROLE


1  large chicken, cooked tender and cut into small pieces
1  medium bell pepper, chopped
1  large onion, chopped
1 (12 oz.)  pkg. pasta, your preference
1  lb. Velveeta cheese
1  can Rotel and 1/2 of juice
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire
3/4  stick oleo

      Boil chicken, take out of broth.  Cook pasta in broth.  Saut√© bell pepper and onion and add to cooked pasta.  Add all other ingredients, place in casserole dish.  Bake at 350 for 30 minutes.
