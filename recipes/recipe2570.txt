CHICKEN CASSEROLE


1  c. rice
2  c. sliced, cooked chicken
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  tsp. lemon juice
2  Tbsp. melted butter
1/4  c. grated cheese
1/2  c. mayonnaise
1/4  c. chicken broth (optional)

      Boil  rice until done.  Add chicken, soup, lemon juice, butter,  grated  cheese,  mayonnaise  and  chicken broth.  Mix together and bake at 350 degrees; for 30 to 40 minutes.
