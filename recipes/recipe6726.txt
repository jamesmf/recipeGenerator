CHICKEN AND RICE SUPREME


1  c. mayonnaise
3  c. or more cooked diced chicken
1  pkg. Uncle Ben's long grain and wild rice, cooked according to directions on pkg.
1  can cream of celery soup
1 (4 oz.)  jar chopped pimentos
1  medium onion, chopped
1  can French style green beans, drained
1  can sliced water chestnuts, drained
  salt and pepper to taste

      Mix all ingredients. Pour into 3-quart casserole.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 25 to 30 minutes.  Serves 8 to 10.
