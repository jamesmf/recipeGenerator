ROTEL CHICKEN


4  Chicken Breasts, deboned
1  can Rotel Tomatoes
1  can Cream of Mushroom Soup
1  can Cream of Chicken Soup
1/4  lb. Velveeta Cheese
1  pkg. Doritos

      Cook chicken breast until done and take off the bone.  In a separate bowl mix tomatoes, soups and cheese together and place in microwave until cheese has melted.  Add chicken to the cheese mixture.  Crumble up Doritos and place in bottom of pan.  Layer chicken mixture and Doritos.  Cook at 350 degrees until bubbly.
