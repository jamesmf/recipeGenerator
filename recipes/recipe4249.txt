CHEESE BALL


2  pkg (8 ounces each) cream cheese
1  pkg (4 ounces) blue cheese
2  Tbsp. chopped chives
1/2  tsp. garlic salt
  parsley
  chopped nuts

      Have cheese at room temperature, mix all ingredients well. Chill then form into a ball.  Roll in parsley and chopped nuts. Refrigerate until ready to serve.
