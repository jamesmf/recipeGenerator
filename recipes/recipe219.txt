APPLE FRITTERS


1  c. flour
1 1/2  tsp. baking powder
3  Tbsp. powdered sugar
1/4  tsp. salt
1/3  c. milk
1  egg
2  medium-sized sour apples, pared and cut in slices

      Mix and sift dry ingredients.  Combine milk and egg (well beaten) and add to dry ingredients (beating constantly).  Dip apples in batter, spreading batter evenly over apples.  Fry in deep hot fat, 275 degrees; for 2 to 3 minutes or until fritters are golden brown.  Drain.
