CHICKEN WITH EGG DUMPLINGS


1  chicken
2  c. cooked diced potatoes
2  Tbsp. flour
1  c. milk

      Stew the chicken until tender. Add the cooked diced potatoes. Mix the flour with the milk. Add to stewed chicken. Top with Egg Dumplings. Cook about 20 minutes.
Egg Dumplings:
1 1/2  c. flour
1  tsp. baking powder
1  tsp. salt
1  egg
1/2  c. milk

      Beat egg well. Add salt and milk. Stir in sifted flour and baking powder. Drop by spoonful on chicken.
