CREAM OF TOMATO SOUP


2  c. tomato juice
1/2  stick margarine
5  Tbsp. flour
1/2  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper
2  c. milk
1/8  tsp. baking soda

      In small stew pan, place tomato juice and place on stove to warm.  In larger pan, melt margarine; remove from heat.  Mix in flour, salt and pepper; stir until smooth.  Add milk and place on stove; bring to a boil, stirring constantly.  Cook until thickened; remove from heat.  Stir baking soda into warm tomato juice and then stir into gravy mixture.  Serve with crackers or garlic toast.
