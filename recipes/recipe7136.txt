PEPPER SIRLOIN STEAK


2-1/2  lbs 1-1/2 inch thick sirloin steak
1/2  c butter
1/4  c fresh parsley, chopped
1/4  c onion, minced
2  tbsp Worcestershire sauce
1  tsp fresh ground pepper
1/2  tsp dry mustard

      Lightly score edge of steak at 1 inch intervals.  Preheat grill or broiler.  Combine all ingredients in saucepan.  Cook until butter melts.  Reserve 1/4 of mixture.  Place steak on grill or broiler.  Brush mixture on steak.  Cook about 6 minutes on each side for a medium broil.  Place on platter, drizzling remaining mixture on steak.
