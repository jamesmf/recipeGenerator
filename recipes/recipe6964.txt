COUNTRY CHICKEN


1/2  c. butter
  salt and pepper to taste
1  fryer, cut up
1  c. raw long grain rice
2 1/2  c. water
1 (1 1/4 oz.)  pkg. dry onion soup mix

      Melt butter in a deep, flat dish. Salt and pepper the chicken lightly; roll in the melted butter and arrange in a single layer in the dish. Sprinkle rice over chicken and add water slowly; sprinkle onion soup mix over rice. Cover tightly and bake a 350 degrees; for 1 1/2 hours.
