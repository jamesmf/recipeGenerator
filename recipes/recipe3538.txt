SPINACH BALLS #1


2  pkg. frozen spinach, chopped and drained
2  c. Pepperidge Farm crumb stuffing
6  eggs, beaten
1  tsp. pepper
1/2  tsp. thyme
1  Tbsp. garlic powder
1/2  c. Parmesan cheese
3/4  c. melted butter

      Mix all ingredients and shape into balls.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 20 minutes.  Can be frozen before baking.
