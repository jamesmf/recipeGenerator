CORN SALAD


1 (12 oz.)  can Shoepeg corn, drained
1  can Mexican corn, drained
2  oz. jar chopped pimentos
1  c. diced celery
1  small bell pepper, diced
1  small onion, diced
1/2  c. salad oil
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. pepper
1  Tbsp. mustard
1  tsp. sugar or more to taste
2 1/2  Tbsp. vinegar

      Combine the last 6 ingredients and spoon over mixture of the first 6 ingredients.
