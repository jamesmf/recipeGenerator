EQUAL YOGURT FRUIT DIP


1 (8 oz.)  pkg. low calorie cream cheese, softened
1  c. plain low-fat yogurt
1  tsp. vanilla
1  tsp. grated lemon rind
14  packets Equal

      Combine cream cheese and yogurt.  Blend until smooth.  Stir in remaining ingredients.  Chill.  Serve with fresh fruit.  Makes 2 cups or 8 servings; contains approximately 100 calories per serving.
