STRUFOLI


1  lb. flour
10  large eggs
4  Tbsp. margarine
2  Tbsp. vanilla
2  Tbsp. sugar
  grated peel of 2 oranges
  oil for frying
1  c. honey
  sprinkles

      Cut margarine into flour.  Add eggs, vanilla, sugar and orange peel.  Stir.  Knead dough until soft.  Let rest 10 minutes.  Roll small sections of dough into strips and cut in 1/2-inch pieces. Fry in oil until golden brown.  Drain.  Glaze with honey and sprinkles.
