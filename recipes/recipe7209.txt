FILLED MEAT LOAF


5  lb. hamburg
1  small onion
1  c. bread crumbs
1/4  c. Italian dressing
3  eggs
1  Tbsp. soy sauce
1  tsp. garlic powder
  salt and pepper
1 large  can cream of mushroom soup, mixed as directed
  bread (enough to put between hamburg layers)
  parsley flakes
  salt and pepper
1/2  c. butter, melted

      Mix first 7 ingredients together with hamburg.  Make layer on bottom of pan.  Put filling mixture next.  Then hamburg on top. Cover with mushroom soup.  Bake 45 minutes at 350 degrees; or until it looks done on bottom.
