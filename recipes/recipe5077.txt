FRITO-BEAN SALAD


1  bag chopped lettuce
1  onion, chopped
1  green pepper (if desired)
2  to 3 tomatoes, chopped
2  cans chili beans, drained
  shredded cheese
  Fritos
  Catalina French dressing

      Line bottom of a large bowl with lettuce. Add in layers the onion, green pepper, tomatoes and beans. Top with cheese, Fritos and Catalina dressing. Toss together just before serving.
