APPLESAUCE, CANNED


4  medium cooking apples
1/4  c. sugar or more to taste
  2-inch stick cinnamon

      Wash, pare if desired, quarter and core cooking apples. Simmer, covered, with cinnamon in a small amount of water until tender, about 10 minutes.  Remove cinnamon.  Press apples through sieve.  Stir in sugar.  Reheat to boiling.  Pour, boiling hot, into hot sterilized jars, leaving 1/2-inch headspace.  Stir with rubber bottle scraper or other non-metallic spoon to remove air bubbles. Adjust caps.  Process pints and quarts 20 minutes in boiling water bath.
