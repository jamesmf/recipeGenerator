CORN PUDDING


4 1/2  c. fresh corn or canned
1/4  c. sugar
1/4  c. sifted flour
1  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. black pepper
2  beaten eggs
2  Tbsp. melted butter
1 1/2  c. milk

      Add sugar, flour, salt and pepper to the corn.  Then add the beaten eggs and the melted butter.  Last stir in the milk.  Mix well.  Pour into a well-greased (use butter) casserole dish.  Bake in a 400 degrees; oven for approximately one hour or until light brown on top.
