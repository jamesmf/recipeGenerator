PEACH-GLAZED STUFFED PORK CHOPS


1 (8 1/2 oz.)  can peaches
1/3  c. hot water
2  Tbsp. margarine
2  c. Stove Top chicken flavored stuffing mix
4  pork chops (1-inch thick; with pocket cut for stuffing)
1/2  c. peach preserves
2  Tbsp. onion mustard

      Heat oven to 350 degrees;.  Drain and dice peaches, reserving syrup. Mix water and margarine in bowl.  Stir in stuffing mix, peaches and syrup.  Fill pork chop pockets with stuffing.  Arrange pork chops in 13 x 9-inch baking pan.  Mix preserves and mustard. Brush over pork chops.  Bake 60 minutes or until cooked through. Bake remaining stuffing in a casserole dish 30 minutes.  Makes 4 servings.
