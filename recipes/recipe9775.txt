BEEF PAPRIKA


2  pounds meat cubed
1  cup chopped onion
1  tsp. garlic powder
3/4  cup ketchup
2  Tbsp. worcestershire sauce
1  tsp. brown sugar
2  tsp. paprika
1/2  tsp. dry mustard
  dash red pepper
1  1/2 cups water
1/4  cup oil

 
Thickening:
2  Tbsp. flour
1/4  cup water

      Brown meat, garlic, and onion in oil in large pot.  Once meat has browned add remaining ingredients.  Simmer 2 1/2 hours and then add thickening.
