SPAGHETTI AND CABBAGE WITH CHEESE SAUCE


3/4  c. spaghetti
1 1/2  Tbsp. margarine
1 1/2  Tbsp. flour
1/2  tsp. salt
1  c. milk
1/4  lb. grated cheese (about 1 c.)
2  c. shredded cabbage
  bread crumbs

      Cook spaghetti until tender; drain.  Make sauce from margarine, flour, salt and milk.  Remove from heat; add cheese.  Stir until melted.  Alternate layers of cabbage and spaghetti in casserole dish.  Pour sauce over top.  Sprinkle with bread crumbs.  Cover and bake at 350 degrees; for 40 minutes.  Remove cover last 10 minutes.
