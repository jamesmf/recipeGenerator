KIBBET-ET-ROOHIB
(Monk's Soup)  

1 1/2  c. lentils, washed
1  tsp. salt
1  gal. water
2  c. fine wheat, washed
2  c. flour
1/2  c. seasoning used for kibbee (basil, mint, green pepper, onion)
1/2  tsp. pepper
1  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. cumin
  3-4 cloves garlic, crushed (to taste)
1  c. lemon juice
1/2  c. oil

      Boil lentils and salt in water.  Cool for 30 mins. over medium heat.  Mix together cracked wheat, flour seasoning, pepper, salt and cumin.  Form wheat mixture into small balls.  Add to lentils and water.  Add crushed garlic, lemon juice and oil.  If desired, add more water.
      Note: Lenten or Good Friday dish.
