FERN'S NO TROUBLE PICKLES


  pickling salt
  cucumbers
  alum
7 or 8  c. sugar
3  c. white vinegar
4  c. water

      Slice medium size cucumbers lengthwise; pack cold in quart jars.  Add 1 tsp. salt and pinch of alum in each jar.  Dissolve sugar in vinegar and water; boil rapidly.  Pour over cucumbers in jars and seal.  Process in boiling water bath for about 10 minutes, or until they begin to change color.
