MEXICAN CASSEROLE


1  lb. hamburger meat
1  chopped onion
1  pt. beans (pinto or Ranch Style)
1  can Ro-Tel tomatoes
1  chopped green pepper
1  can Spanish rice
1  bag Doritos chips
2  c. shredded Cheddar cheese

      Brown hamburger and onion.  Add beans.  Fry.  Blend tomatoes and pepper.  Add to meat mixture.  Mix. Add Spanish rice.  Mix. Using large baking dish, layer meat mixture, Doritos and cheese alternately.  Finish with cheese on top.  Heat in oven until melted.
