TOASTED AROMATIC PECANS


4  c. pecan halves (1 lb.)
1/4  c. butter
1  tsp. or more of Angostura Bitters
  seasoned salt

      Put pecans on a 13 x 9 baking sheet.  Toast in a preheated 300 degrees; oven for 20 minutes.  Meanwhile, melt butter, add Bitters and 1 teaspoon seasoning salt.  Pour mixture over pecans and stir; coating pecans evenly.  Cook for 15 minutes longer.   Add more seasoning salt if desired.  Remove; spread out on paper towels to dry.
