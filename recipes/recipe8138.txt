CHICKEN FEED


1  small onion, diced
2  c. fresh mushrooms, sliced
1  garlic clove, minced
1/2  tsp. dried thyme, divided
1  Tbsp. butter or margarine
4  c. cubed, leftover, cooked chicken
4  c. leftover gravy
1  chicken bouillon cube, crushed
  dash of pepper
3  c. mashed potatoes

      In a skillet, saut√© the onion, mushrooms, garlic and 1/4 teaspoon thyme in butter. Stir in the chicken, gravy, bouillon and pepper. Spoon into a greased 3-quart casserole.
      Combine potatoes and remaining thyme; spoon over mixture. Bake uncovered at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes. Yield: 6 to 8 servings.
