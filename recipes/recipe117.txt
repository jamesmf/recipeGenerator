BIRD'S NESTS


3  c. Rice Krispies
1  c. shredded coconut
1/3  c. light corn syrup
1/2  c. brown sugar
1  tsp. vanilla

      Combine cereal and coconut in bowl.  Combine other ingredients in saucepot and bring to a boil while stirring.  Remove from heat. Stir in cereal mixture.  Let cool, shape into bird's nests.  Fill with "jelly bean eggs".
