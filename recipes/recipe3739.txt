TEXAS CAVIAR


1 (15 1/2 oz.)  can black-eyed peas with jalapenos, rinsed and drained
1 (15 oz.)  can black beans, rinsed and drained
1 (11 oz.)  can Shoepeg corn, drained
2  medium tomatoes, seeded and chopped
1 1/3  c. mild salsa
1  c. medium salsa
1/2  c. chopped green pepper
3/4  c. chopped green onion
1 (2 1/4 oz.)  can sliced black olives, drained
1 small  envelope Italian salad dressing mix
1  tsp. ground cumin
1/2  tsp. garlic powder
1  jalapeno pepper, seeded and chopped (optional)

	Mix all ingredients in a large bowl. Chill. Serve with tortilla chips or crackers.
