GROUND BEEF CUPS WITH CREAMED POTATOES


3/4  lb. ground beef
1/4  c. bread crumbs
2  c. milk
  salt and pepper to taste
6  medium potatoes, diced

      Combine beef, bread crumbs, 1/2 cup milk, 1 teaspoon salt and dash of pepper; mix well. Line ungreased muffin cups with meat mixture about 1/4-inch thick. Place potatoes and remaining milk in saucepan; cover and cook slowly until thick. Season with salt and pepper. Fill meat cups with potatoes; bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes.
