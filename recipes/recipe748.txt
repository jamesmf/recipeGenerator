PASTA COCKTAIL


1 1/2  oz. pasta shapes
2  sticks celery
1  crisp eating apple
  lemon twists or paprika (for garnish)

 
Sauce:
4  Tbsp. mayonnaise
1  Tbsp. tomato ketchup
1  Tbsp. cream
2  tsp. lemon juice
  dash of Tabasco sauce

      Cook pasta until tender.  Drain and rinse in cold water to remove excess starch.  Mix together all sauce ingredients.  Slice celery.  Core and quarter apple, then chop.  Mix cold pasta, celery and chopped apple into sauce.  To serve, line sundae dishes with lettuce and fill with pasta cocktail.  Garnish with lemon twists or sprinkle with paprika.  Serves 6.
