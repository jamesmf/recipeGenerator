CHICKEN NOODLE SUPREME


1  lb. medium wide noodles, cooked and drained
1/2  lb. grated cheese
3  c. diced chicken
1/2  c. butter
1/2  c. flour
2 1/2 to 3  c. broth

      Make like a white sauce the  butter,  flour  and broth. Add the cheese to white sauce.  (You may  save  some cheese to sprinkle on top.) Mix together all  other ingredients and pour white  sauce  over it.  Bake at  350 degrees;  until  heated  through, approximately  30 to 45 minutes.  Can be made  a  day  or  two ahead and kept in refrigerator.  Freezes well!
