FETTUCCINE ALFREDO


1- 12  oz. pkg. uncooked fettuccine
1  c. grated parmesan cheese
1/2  c. soft butter
1/2  c. whipping cream
2  tbsp. parsley
1/4  tsp. white pepper

      Cook fettuccine in unsalted water.  Drain well.  Place in a large bowl, add remaining ingredients.  Toss and serve.  Serves 6.
