DEVILED EGGS


6  hard cooked eggs
1/4  c. mayonnaise
1  Tbsp. sweet pickle relish
1  Tbsp. vinegar
1/4  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
  paprika

      Slice eggs into halves lengthwise and carefully remove yolks. Mash yolks with mayonnaise.  Add remaining ingredients, except paprika.  Stuff eggs with the yolk mixture.  Garnish with paprika.
