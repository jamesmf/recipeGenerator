BIROUX


2  lb. lean hamburger
1  small head cabbage, chopped
1  onion, chopped
1  tsp. celery seed
1  tsp. red pepper
1  tsp. black pepper
1  pkg. Texas size frozen rolls
3  c. shredded cheese

      Brown hamburger meat and drain. Add spices and mix well. Add cabbage and onion. Cook 15 minutes. May need to add a little water. Roll thawed rolls on floured board to 6-inch square. Put spoon of cheese, then 1/2 cup of hamburger. Gather up 3 sides, then remaining side and pinch together. Lay pinched side down on greased baking sheet. Bake at 350 degrees; for 15 to 20 minutes, until brown.
