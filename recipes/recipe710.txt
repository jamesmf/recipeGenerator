SICILIAN CASSEROLE


1/2  lb. ground beef
1/2  lb. ground hot sausage
1  green pepper, diced
1  onion, diced
1 1/2  qt. spaghetti sauce
1  lb. wheels and corkscrews, cooked, or other pasta
1/2  lb. pepperoni
1 small  can mushroom pieces, drained
8  oz. Mozzarella cheese, shredded

      Brown hamburger, sausage, pepper and onion (drain grease).  Put 1/2 sauce in bottom of 9 x 13-inch pan.  Put in 1/2 pasta.  Add pepperoni.  Add mushrooms and 1/2 cheese.  Add rest of pasta, then rest of sauce.  Add remaining cheese.  Cover with foil and bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes.  Uncover and bake 15 minutes more.
