DATE-NUT PINWHEELS

Date Mixture:
2 1/4  c. chopped dates
1/2  c. sugar
1  c. water
1  c. chopped nuts

      Cook dates, sugar, water.  Add nuts when cool.
Dough:
1/2  c. Crisco
1/2  c. margarine
2  eggs
4  c. flour
1/2  tsp. soda
1/2  tsp. salt

      Combine all ingredients for dough.  Roll dough out and spread with date mixture.  Roll up in pinwheel.  Place in wax paper. Leave in refrigerator overnight.  Slice, then bake in 350 degrees; oven until lightly browned.
