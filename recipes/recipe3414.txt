SAUSAGE TARTLETS


  Pie crust mix
1  lb. hot sausage
1/4  lb. mushrooms, finely chopped
1/4  c. onion, minced
1  egg
1/2  c. milk
1/2  c. shredded cheddar cheese
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. marjoram, crushed
1/4  tsp. black pepper

      Prepare pie crust mix; press in mini muffin tins.  Bake at 400 degrees; for 5 minutes.
      Brown 1 pound hot sausage, crumble, drain well.
      Saut√© mushrooms and onion in sausage drippings.  Drain well.
      Beat egg slightly in medium sized bowl; add milk, cheese, salt, marjoram (crushed), and black pepper.  Mix together with sausage, mushrooms and onions.
      Fill tartlet shells.  Bake 15 minutes at 375 degrees;.  Remove to wire rack.  Let stand a few minutes before removing from pan.  These may be made ahead and frozen.  Heat frozen tartlets in hot oven 10 minutes.  Keep hot on warming tray.  This recipe makes about 4 dozen bite-size pieces.  Good served as an appetizer or with scrambled eggs and fruit for brunch.
