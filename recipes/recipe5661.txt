YUMMY SQUASH SOUP


2  lb. yellow squash, cut into small pieces (about 1-inch)
2 (8 oz.)  cans chicken broth
1  medium onion, chopped
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese (regular or light)
  salt and pepper to taste

	Combine squash and onion in chicken broth. Cook until tender. In blender, combine 1/2 of the squash mixture with 1/2 of the cream cheese and puree. Repeat for the rest of squash and cream cheese.  Return mixture to pot and simmer for 15 minutes. Season to taste.
	Note: This recipe is also good when made with potatoes.
