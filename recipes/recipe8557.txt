MIDWEST MEAT LOAF


1/2  lb. Velveeta cheese
1/4  c. Milk
2  tbsp. dry Mustard
1  lb. Ground Beef
1/3  c. Oats (uncooked)
1/4  c. Ketchup
1/4  c. Onion, finely chopped

      Stir Velveeta, milk and mustard in small saucepan over low heat until smooth.  Reserve 3/4 c. cheese for sauce.  Mix remaining ingredients and cheese in large bowl until blended. Shape into  loaf pan.  Bake for 40 minutes.  Pour cheese over and serve.
