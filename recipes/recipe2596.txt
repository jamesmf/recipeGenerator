ZUCCHINI CASSEROLE


4  c. sliced zucchini
1  lb. ground beef
1  onion, chopped
1  c. mayonnaise
1  c. Cheddar cheese
1  c. Parmesan cheese
2  eggs
  salt and pepper to taste
  butter
  bread crumbs

      Cook zucchini in 2 cups boiling water until tender.  Brown beef and onion; mix first 8 ingredients together.  Pour in casserole dish.  Dot with butter and cover with bread crumbs.  Bake 1 hour at 350 degrees;.
