TURKEY BREAST WITH ONION GRAVY


1  (5 lb.) turkey breast
1  can cream of onion soup
  seasoned salt

	Thaw turkey.  Rinse under water and dry with paper towels. Salt both sides with seasoned salt.  Place in crock-pot.  Add onion soup and 1/4 to 1/2 can of water.  Cover and cook on high setting 6 1/2 to 7 hours until fork-tender.  Remove turkey.  Add thickening made with water and flour and a little pepper.  Cook about 10 minutes or until gravy is thickened.
