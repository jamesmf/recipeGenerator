MEDIUM WHITE SAUCE


4  cups milk
1  Tbsp. chicken base
1/3  cup corn starch
  White pepper to taste

      Heat 3 cups milk and chicken base slowly over low heat until hot.  In a separate bowl, mix together 1 cup milk, corn starch and white pepper.  Slowly add corn starch mixture to saucepan and bring just to boiling.  Stirring constantly, simmer gently for an additional 2-3 minutes.  Makes 1 quart.
      For CHEESE SAUCE, use 2 cups shredded Cheddar or Swiss cheese and add to finished sauce, stirring until smooth.
      For THICK WHITE SAUCE, use an additional 1/4 cup corn starch.
