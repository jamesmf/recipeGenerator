MARIE'S 4-ALARM CHILI


1 1/2  lb. hot Italian sausage, casings removed and crumbled
1  large onion, diced
4  cloves garlic, minced
2  Tbsp. Wesson oil
2 (15 oz.)  cans Hunt's chili beans (undrained)
2 (15 oz.)  cans Hunt's tomato sauce
1 (28 oz.)  can Hunt's whole tomatoes, drained and crushed
1  c. beer or beef broth
2  Tbsp. Gebhardt chili powder
2  Tbsp. seeded and diced jalapeno peppers (optional)
1  tsp. cinnamon
  shredded Cheddar cheese
  sour cream
  green onions

      In Dutch oven, cook crumbled sausage, onion and garlic in hot oil until sausage is no longer pink; drain. Add remaining ingredients except Cheddar, sour cream and green onions. Simmer, uncovered, for 45 minutes.
       To serve, top each bowl of chili with a sprinkle of Cheddar, a dollop of sour cream and green onions. Makes 6 servings.
