PUMPKIN PIE



1 (16 oz.)  can solid pack pumpkin
1 (13 oz.)  can evaporated skim milk
1  egg
2  egg whites
1/2  c. Bisquick
2  Tbsp. sugar
8  packets Sweet 'N Low
2  tsp. pumpkin pie spice
2  tsp. vanilla

      Heat oven to 350 degrees;.  Lightly grease 9-inch pie pan.  Combine all ingredients and beat for 2 minutes with mixer.  Pour into pie pan and bake for 50 minutes or until center is puffed up.  Serves 8; contains 112 calories per slice.
