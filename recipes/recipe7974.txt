WONDERFUL PRIME RIB


1  prime rib
  favorite seasoning

      Preheat oven to 500 degrees.  Season prime rib with you favorite seasonings. Place in a roasting pan fat side up. Cook exactly 10 minutes per pound. After proper cooking time, turn off the oven and do not open the door for 8 hours. This is perfect for cooking overnight. This is great and you won't be sorry you tried it.
