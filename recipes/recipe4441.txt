MARINATED VEGETABLE SALAD


1  can early June peas
1  can white Shoepeg corn
1  can bean sprouts
1  can sliced water chestnuts
1 medium  jar pimentos
1 large  jar sliced mushrooms
2/3  c. sugar
2/3  c. white cider vinegar
2/3  c. vegetable oil

      Drain all liquids from your peas, corn, etc.  Combine those ingredients in a large plastic container.  Pour sugar, vinegar and oil into bowl.  Stir until well mixed.  Cover container and store overnight in refrigerator.
