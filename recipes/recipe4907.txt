EASY FRUIT SALAD


1 16-oz.  carton Cool Whip
  3-oz. pkg. orange Jell-O
1 16-oz.  can mandarin oranges, drained, divided
1 16-oz.  carton small curd cottage cheese
  maraschino cherries

      Mix all ingredients, except a few of the oranges and the cherries.  Put in pretty glass dish and decorate top with mandarin oranges and cherries.
