LAMMASMUHENNOS
(Lamb Stew)  

3  lb. boneless lamb, cubed
2  leeks, cut into 1/2-inch pieces
1  c. shredded cabbage
1/4  tsp. black pepper
1  Tbsp. chopped parsley
6  potatoes, sliced
4  carrots, cut into 1-inch pieces
1  medium onion, sliced
1  tsp. salt
3  c. water

      Place alternate layers of meat and vegetables in a large Dutch oven.  Sprinkle each layer with salt and pepper.  Add water.  Bring to a boil and reduce the heat.  Cover and simmer 1/2 hour or until the meat is tender.  Sprinkle with parsley before serving.
