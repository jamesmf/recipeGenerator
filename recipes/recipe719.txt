CHILI LASAGNA ROLLS


4  lasagna noodles, cooked and drained
1 (10 oz.)  can chili without beans
4 (1 oz.)  slices Cheddar cheese, cut in half
1/4  c. dairy sour cream
1  green onion, sliced

      Spread one side of each noodle with 3 tablespoons chili.  Lay 2 halves of cheese over the chili.  Roll up and place seam side down in a greased baking dish.  Spread remaining chili on top of rolls. Place grated cheese on top.  Bake, covered with foil, at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes or until hot.  To serve:  Top rolls with sour cream and green onion.
      To Microwave:  Microwave, covered with waxed paper, on High for 8 to 10 minutes or until heated through, giving dish a half-turn once.  Serve as directed.  Serves 2 to 4.
