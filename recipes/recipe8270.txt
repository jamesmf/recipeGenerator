CHEESE STUFFED RICE BALLS


3  eggs
2 1/2  c. cooked rice
1/3  c. butter, melted
2/3  c. grated Parmesan or Romano cheese
1/4  c. fresh parsley, minced
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
2  oz. Mozzarella cheese, cut into 3/8-inch cubes
1  c. dry bread crumbs
  oil (for deep-frying)

	In a bowl, lightly beat 2 eggs. Stir in the rice, butter, Parmesan cheese, parsley, salt and pepper. Cover and refrigerate for 20 minutes. Roll into 1 1/2-inch balls. Press Mozzarella cube into each; reshape balls.
	 In a small bowl, lightly beat remaining egg. Dip each ball into egg, then roll in bread crumbs. In a deep-fat fryer or electric skillet, heat oil to 375 degrees;. Fry rice balls until golden brown, about 4 minutes. Drain on paper towels. Yield: 6 to 8 servings.
