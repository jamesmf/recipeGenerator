SLICED COOKIES


1 1/2  c. shortening
3  c. brown sugar
2  eggs
6  c. sifted flour
4  tsp. baking powder
1/2  tsp. salt
2  tsp. vanilla
1/2  tsp. cinnamon
1/4  tsp. nutmeg
1  c. one of the following:  butterscotch chips, chopped nuts, dates, coconut, M&M's, etc.

      Cream shortening and sugar; add eggs, flour, baking powder and salt.  Add vanilla.  Mix well and divide dough into thirds.  Leave one section of dough plain; shape into a long roll the diameter of a half dollar.  To 1/3 dough add cinnamon and nutmeg.  Shape into roll.  To other 1/3 you may add butterscotch chips, chopped nuts, dates, coconut, M&M's or anything the kids like.  Shape, chill and store in fridge.  Slice and bake as needed.  Sprinkle with powdered sugar while warm.
