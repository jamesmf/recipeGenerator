MEXICAN CRESCENT PIE


1  lb. hamburger
1  pkg. taco seasoning
  2-4 Tbsp. water
1/4  C. sliced black olives
1/2  C. chopped onion
2/3  C. mayonnaise
1  can crescent rolls
2  med. tomatoes, diced
1 1/2  C. shredded cheese
8  oz. sour cream

      Brown hamburger.  Add seasoning and water.  Combine black olives, onion, sour cream and mayo in bowl.  Separate dough into triangles and place in ungreased 10-inch pie plate or 8 x 12-inch glass pan.  Spoon burger over crust.  Add tomatoes and 1 C. cheese. Spread sour cream mixture over the burger and then add remaining cheese.  Bake at 350 degrees for 25-35 minutes.  Serve with salsa.
