SPAGHETTI AND MEAT BALLS


3 to 4  lb. ground chuck
3  eggs
2  (6 oz.) tomato paste
1 large  can crushed tomatoes
1  c. Italian bread crumbs
1/4  c. Parmesan cheese
  salt and pepper to taste
  Tbsp.  Italian season
  Tbsp. oregano
1/2  c. milk
1  onion
1/4  c. oil
  garlic

      Mix together ground chuck, 3 eggs, Italian bread crumbs, Parmesan cheese, salt and pepper to taste.  Add milk and mix thoroughly.  In a large Dutch oven, put 1/4 cup oil.  Chop finely 1 onion and 2 buttons of garlic.  Saut√© onions until soft.  Add garlic, finely chopped.  Put in tomato paste, tomatoes (crushed), Italian spices plus oregano, salt and pepper.  Let simmer while you brown your meat balls.  When browned, add to tomato mixture.  Let simmer 3 hours at least.  Add a can of water to each paste can and about 1/3 tomato can.  Keep low heat.  Stir often.
