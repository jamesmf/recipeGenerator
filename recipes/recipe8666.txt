OVEN MEATBALLS


1  Tbsp. olive oil
1/2  onion, chopped
1  clove garlic, minced
2  lb. ground beef
1 1/2  c. Italian bread crumbs
1/2  c. milk
2  eggs
1 1/2  tsp. salt

	Preheat oven to 375 degrees;.  Saut√© onion and garlic in olive oil. Combine all ingredients; mix lightly but thoroughly. Shape into meatballs. Place in glass baking pan. Brown for 25 to 30 minutes.
	 Keep these basic meatballs on standby in the freezer in Ziploc bags or containers. It only takes a few minutes to heat them in sauce.
