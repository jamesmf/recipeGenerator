FRUIT BELL RINGER CANDIES


1  c. dates
1/3  c. apricots or peaches, dried
1/3  c. raisins
1  Tbsp. maple syrup
1/2  c. powdered sugar
1  c. uncooked prunes
1  c. nuts
2  Tbsp. undiluted frozen orange juice

      In processor, grind fruits and nuts.  Mix all ingredients together and shape mixture into balls.  The balls may then be dipped in melted chocolate and rolled in coconut or chopped nuts. Chill and keep in covered container.  A delicious candy that is not as sweet as other types.
