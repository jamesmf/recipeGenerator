CHICKEN EUGENE


  4-5 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1 thin  slice of ham for each chicken breast
1  can cream of mushroom soup (10.5 ounces)
1  contained of sour cream (12 ounces)

      Preheat oven to 400 degrees.  Lightly spray 9 by 13 baking dish with cooking spray.  Wrap each chicken breast with 1 slice of ham, place in baking dish.  Mix cream of mushroom soup and sour cream, pour over chicken.  Bake for 1 hour.
