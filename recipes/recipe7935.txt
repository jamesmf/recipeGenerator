BROCCOLI & CHICKEN CASSEROLE


1  whole chicken cooked and cut up
1 (10 oz.)  box of frozen broccoli or one bunch of fresh broccoli
1  can cream of chicken soup
1/3  cup mayonnaise
2  Tbsp. lemon juice
1/4  tsp. curry powder
1/4  cup sharp cheddar cheese

      Place cooked chicken in baking dish.  Put broccoli on top of chicken.  Mix soup, mayonnaise, curry powder and lemon juice together.  Pour over chicken and broccoli.  Put cheese on top. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.
