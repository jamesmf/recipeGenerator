CHOCOLATE POPCORN BALLS


1 1/4  c. sugar
1/2  c. cocoa
3/4  c. light corn syrup
2  tsp. cider vinegar
1/8  tsp. salt
2  Tbsp. butter
1/4  c. cream
2  qt. popped corn

      Combine sugar, cocoa, corn syrup, vinegar and salt in saucepan. Add butter; cook over low heat, stirring constantly until comes to boil.  Add cream; cook to hard ball stage.  Stir into popcorn. Butter hands and roll into balls.
