CANDY STRAWBERRIES


2 (3 oz.)  pkg. strawberry flavored gelatin
1  c. ground pecans
1  c. ground coconut
3/4  c. condensed milk
1/2  tsp. vanilla
  red sugar crystals
  slivered almonds

      Mix together gelatin, pecans, coconut, condensed milk and vanilla.  Shape mixture into strawberries; chill at least 1 hour. Roll chilled berries into sugar crystals.  Add food coloring to almonds to tint a delicate green.  Use for leaves and stems of berries.  Store in a cool place.  Makes about 48 strawberries, depending on size.  They are real pretty, so tasty and decorative for a party.
