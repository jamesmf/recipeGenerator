SPINACH BACON QUICHE


4  eggs
2  c. milk
2  c. shredded cheddar
1/4  c. finely chopped onion
4  bacon strips cooked & crumbled
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. dry mustard
1/4  tsp. paprika
1  pkg. (10 oz) spinach, chopped, cooked and drained
1  unbaked 10-inch pastry shell

      In a large bowl beat eggs; whisk in milk, 1 c. cheese, onion, bacon, salt, mustard, and paprika.  Add spinach.  Pour into pie shell.  Sprinkle with remaining cheese.  Bake at 400 degrees; for 40 min. or until a knife inserted halfway between the center and the edge comes out clean.
