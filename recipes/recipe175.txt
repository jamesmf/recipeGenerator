KENTUCKY SPICE COOKIES


3 1/2  c. sugar
1  c. shortening
1 1/2  tsp. cinnamon
3/4  tsp. cloves
3/4  tsp. ginger
3/4  tsp. allspice
2  tsp. salt
2  Tbsp. baking soda
1  c. dark molasses
5  c. flour
1/2  c. water
  sugar
  strawberry or raspberry jelly

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees;.  Cream together 3 1/2 cups sugar, shortening, spices, salt and baking soda.  Add the molasses and blend well.  Gradually mix in the flour.  Then add the water to make a dough of pie crust consistency.  For each cookie, take 1 teaspoon of dough and roll it into a ball, then roll the ball in sugar.  Place each on ungreased cookie sheet, make a small indentation in the top of each ball and fill with a dot of jelly. Bake for 8 minutes.  Remove from oven and immediately sprinkle with sugar.  Cool before storing.
