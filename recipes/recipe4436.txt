SHOEPEG CORN SALAD


2 (16 oz.)  cans Shoepeg corn, drained
1 (2 oz.)  jar pimentos, drained
1/2  c. green pepper, chopped
1/2  c. onion, chopped
2  stalks celery, chopped
1/2  c. sugar
1/2  c. vegetable oil
1/2  c. white vinegar
1  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper

      Mix all ingredients.  Cover.  Refrigerate and let marinate overnight.  Drain before serving.
