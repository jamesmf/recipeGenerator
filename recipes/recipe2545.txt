FRUIT SALAD


2  c. sliced strawberries
1 1/2  c. cubed cantaloupe
1 1/2  c. cubed watermelon
1 1/2  c. cubed honeydew melon
2  c. sliced peaches
2  c. seedless whole grapes
2  c. fresh whole blueberries
2  c. cubed apples
1/4  c. water
1 1/2  Tbsp. sugar or sweetener

      Mix all ingredients in a large bowl; chill for 1 1/2 to 2 hours and serve.  Use 1/2 watermelon as serving bowl.
