FROTHY FRUIT FRAPPE


1/2  c. fresh red raspberries
1/2  c. diced ripe banana
1/2  c. diced ripe peaches
1/2  c. unsweetened pineapple juice
1/4  c. sugar
  vanilla ice cream
  lemon-lime carbonated beverage

      Combine raspberries, banana, peaches, pineapple juice and sugar.  Divide among four 8 ounce glasses.  Chill.  For each glass, add a scoop of vanilla ice cream and muddle.  Tip glass and slowly pour lemon-lime carbonated beverage down side.  Add second scoop of ice cream.
