TEXAS HASH


2  large onions, sliced
3  Tbsp. fat or oil
2  c. canned tomatoes
1  tsp. chili powder
1/4  tsp. pepper
2  bell peppers, cut fine
1  lb. hamburger meat
3/4  c. uncooked rice
2  tsp. salt

      Cook onions and pepper slowly in hot oil until onions are transparent.  Add hamburger and fry, stirring constantly, until mixture falls apart.  Add tomatoes, rice and seasonings; mix well. Put in large greased casserole; cover and bake at 375 degrees; for 45 minutes.
