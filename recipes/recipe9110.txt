CARROT ONION BACON BAKE


2  lb. carrots, cut in slices
2  lb. onions, cut in pieces
1  lb. bacon
  salt and pepper to taste

      Cook carrots until tender.  Fry bacon in large frypan.  Remove bacon and cut in small pieces.  Saut√© onion in drippings.  Drain liquid from carrot.  Mix carrots, onions and bacon in baking dish. Add salt and pepper to taste.  Keep in oven on warm until serving time.
