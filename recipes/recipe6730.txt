CROCK-POT CREAMED CHICKEN


6  medium boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  salt
  pepper
  paprika
1/2  c. water
1  can cream of mushrooms soup
1  can sliced mushrooms, drained
1  c. sour cream
1/4  c. flour

      Sprinkle chicken breasts lightly with salt, pepper and paprika.  Place in a crock-pot.  Mix water, soup, sour cream and mushrooms.  Add flour.  Mix until well blended.  Pour mixture over chicken.  Cover and cook on low for 6 to 8 hours.  Serve chicken and sauce over rice or noodles.
