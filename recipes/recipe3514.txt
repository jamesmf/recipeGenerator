CHEESE BALLS


2  (8 oz.) pkgs. cream cheese
1 small  can of crushed pineapple
1/4  cup bell pepper
  3-4 green onions
2  cups broken pecans (divided in 1/2)
1  Tbsp. seasoning salt

      Blend ingredents well.  Put saran wrap in a large enough bowl to hold mixture.  Place in refrigerator a few hours or overnight. Use saran wrap to form a ball after it is thoroughly cooled.  Then roll in 1 cup of broken pecans.  Serve with Townhouse crackers or similar cracker.
