LOBSTER REMOULADE


1 1/2  lb. cooked lobster meat

 
Marinade:
1  hard-cooked egg yolk
2  cloves garlic
2  Tbsp. hot mustard (Creole)
1/4  c. salad oil
1  Tbsp. vinegar
1  c. mayonnaise
1/2  lemon, thinly sliced
1  Tbsp. paprika
1  Tbsp. horseradish
1/4  c. minced dill pickle
2  Tbsp. minced celery
1  Tbsp. minced parsley
1  Tbsp. minced green onion

      Marinade:  Mash egg yolk with garlic and mustard in a bowl. Beat in oil slowly, adding vinegar as needed.  Add remaining ingredients.  Cover and let stand several hours.
      Marinate lobster meat in sauce.  Serve on lettuce.  Makes 8 servings.
