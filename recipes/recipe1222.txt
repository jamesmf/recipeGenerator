PUMPKIN CUSTARD


2  eggs, slightly beaten
2  Tbsp. liquid sweetener
2  c. pumpkin
1/8  tsp. nutmeg
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. cinnamon
1/2  tsp. ginger
1  c. skim milk

      Combine eggs, sweetener and pumpkin.  Blend well.  Add salt, spices and milk; mix well.  Pour into 8-inch pie pan.  Bake in a moderate 350 degrees; oven 50 to 60 minutes or until done.  Cut and serve like pie pieces, although it has no pastry crust.
      Makes 6 servings.  One serving:  1 meat, 1-B vegetable exchange.
