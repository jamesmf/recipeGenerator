SOUTHWESTERN COUSCOUS SALAD


1  box couscous, cooked per box directions
1  pkg. Birds Eye vegetables (broccoli, corn, peppers or any kind of frozen mixed vegetables)
1  can beans, drained (Ranch Style beans)
1  can mild Ro-Tel
  cilantro
  cumin

      Cook the Birds Eye vegetables for approximately 5 minutes in the microwave. Mix together the first 4 ingredients. Add cilantro and cumin for taste. Serve either cold or warm.
