PINEAPPLE CRUNCH


1/4  lb. butter, melted
5  slices bread, cubed
3  eggs, beaten
1/2  c. sugar
1 (20 oz.)  can crushed pineapple

      Mix together butter and bread cubes until butter is absorbed. Set aside in mixing bowl.  Beat eggs well, then beat in sugar. Stir in undrained pineapple.  Place in baking dish.  Spread cubes on top.  Bake approximately 1 hour at 350 degrees;.  Good with ham.
