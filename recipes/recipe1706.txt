JAPANESE CABBAGE SALAD


1  chicken breast, steamed and cut in small pieces
2  Tbsp. sesame seed
1/2  c. slivered almonds
1/2  head cabbage, chopped
2  green onions
1  pkg. Ramen noodles, crumbled up

 
Dressing:
1 1/2  Tbsp. sugar
1/4  c. salad oil
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. Accent
1/2  tsp. black pepper
3  Tbsp. Japanese rice vinegar

      Mix together dressing ingredients.  Saut√© sesame seed, almonds and noodles in 2 tablespoons oil until brown.  Drain.  Mix together the rest of ingredients.  Add dressing and let stand overnight.
