MELT-IN-YOUR-MOUTH CHICKEN PIE


1  chicken (2 1/2 to 3 lb.)
1  can cream of chicken soup
1/2  tsp. pepper
1  tsp. salt
1  c. self-rising flour
1  stick melted margarine
1  c. buttermilk
2  c. chicken broth
1  can Veg-All, drained

	Cook chicken; take off bone and cut into pieces. Place in 9 x 13 bowl or pan. Spread Veg-All on top of chicken. In pan, mix and bring to boil 2 cups of chicken broth and soup. Pour over Veg-All and chicken.
	For Crust: Mix margarine, pepper, salt, flour and buttermilk together. Spoon batter over top. Bake at 425 degrees; for 25 to 30 minutes.
