CHICKEN PAPRIKA I


2 1/2 to 3  lb. chicken
3  Tbsp. butter
1  c. onions, chopped
2  tsp. paprika
2  chicken bouillon cubes
2  c. boiling water
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. flour
1  c. sour cream

	Wash chicken and cut into size pieces desired. Saut√© onions in melted butter until brown. Dissolve bouillon cubes in boiling water. Add paprika, salt and chicken. Cover and cook over low heat until tender. Cook approximately 1 hour. Stir flour into sour cream. Pour slowly into hot liquid. Stir; cover and cook about 5 minutes. Serves 4.
