JALAPENOS ENCURIDOS
(Pickled Jalapenos)  

  jalapeno peppers
  vinegar
  water
  olive oil
  salt
  pickling spices

      Wash jalapeno peppers.  Pack tightly in jar.  For every cup of vinegar, use 1/4 cup water, 1/4 cup olive oil, 1 teaspoon salt and 1 teaspoon pickling spices.  Heat mixture to boiling.  Pour over peppers, so that they are well covered.  Seal jars.  Process pint jars 10 minutes in hot water bath.
