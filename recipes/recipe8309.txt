BONELESS CHICKEN BREASTS


1  c. cornflake crumbs
2/3  c. Parmesan cheese
1/4  c. fresh parsley flakes, chopped or half the amount if dried
1  clove garlic, minced (approximately 1/8 tsp.)
1  tsp. salt
1/2  c. butter or oleo
  chicken

	Dip chicken in melted butter, then in remaining ingredients. Bake at 375 degrees; for 45 minutes.
