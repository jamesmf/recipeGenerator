FROZEN FRUIT CUP


1  pkg. (8 oz.) cream cheese
1/2  c. sugar
1  jar (10 oz.) maraschino cherries, drained
1  can (8 oz.) mandarin oranges, drained
1  can (8 oz.) crushed pineapple, drained
1/2  c. chopped pecans
1  (8 oz.) whipped topping

      Beat cream cheese and sugar; chop cherries.  Add pineapple, pecans and cherries to cream cheese.  Fold in whipped topping and oranges.  Line muffin cups with paper cups; spoon mixture into cups.  Freeze.  Remove from freezer 10 minutes before serving.
