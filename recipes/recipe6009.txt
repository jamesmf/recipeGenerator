GERALDINE'S VEGETABLE SOUP


1/2  lb. ground beef
1  large onion
2  qt. tomatoes or tomato juice
2  large potatoes, cut up fine
1  can mixed vegetables
  corn, peas, beans or leftovers
2  beef bouillon cubes
1  tsp. oregano
1  tsp. parsley flakes
1  tsp. poppy seed
1/2  c. tomato ketchup
1  tsp. onion or garlic salt
2  bay leaves
1/2  c. sugar
1  Tbsp. worcestershire sauce

      Brown ground beef and onion in kettle.  Add tomatoes and other ingredients.  Simmer for 1 hour.
