BROCCOLI AND TORTELLINI SALAD


6  slices bacon
20  oz fresh cheese-filled tortellini
3  heads fresh broccoli, cut into florets
1  c raisins
1  c sunflower seeds
1  red onion, finely chopped

      Place bacon in a large deep skillet.  Cook over medium-high heat until evenly brown.  Drain, crumble and set aside.  Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.  Cook tortellini in boiling water for 8-10 minutes or until al dente.  Drain and rinse under cold water. In a large bowl, combine broccoli, tortellini, bacon, raisins, sunflower seeds and red onion.  Pour on dressing (recipe to follow) and toss.
Dressing:
1/2  c mayonnaise
1/2  c white sugar
2  tsp cider vinegar

      Combine all ingredients together.  Pour over salad.
