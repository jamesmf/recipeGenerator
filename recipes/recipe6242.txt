SNOWY NIGHT SOUP


3  onions, chopped
2  Tbsp. butter
1  lb. ground beef
1  clove garlic, minced
3  c. beef stock
2 (28 oz.)  cans tomatoes
1  c. potatoes, diced
1  c. celery, diced
1  c. green beans, diced
1  c. carrots, diced
1  c. dry red wine
2  Tbsp. parsley, chopped
1/2  tsp. basil
1/4  tsp. thyme
  salt and pepper to taste

      In a large kettle, saut onions in butter until tender.  Stir in beef and garlic and saut until browned.  Add remaining ingredients.  Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer 1 hour.
