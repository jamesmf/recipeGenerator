PRIZE WINNING MEAT LOAF


1 1/2  lb. ground beef
1  c. or can tomato juice
3/4  c. oats (uncooked)
1  egg, beaten
1 1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees;. Combine all ingredients; mix well. Press firmly into a loaf pan or in an iron, greased frying pan. Bake for 1 hour. Let stand 5 minutes before slicing.
