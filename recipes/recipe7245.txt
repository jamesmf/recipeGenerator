NUTT HOUSE MEAT LOAF


2  lb. ground beef
4  eggs, lightly beaten
1  large onion, chopped
1/4  c. chopped celery
1  tsp. salt or to taste
2  tsp. seasoning salt or to taste
1  tsp. pepper
1  c. cracker crumbs
2  c. ketchup
  Tomato Sauce (recipe follows)

      Combine beef and eggs. Blend in onion, celery, salt, seasoning salt, pepper, crumbs and ketchup. Adjust seasoning. Mixture should be smooth and moist. Pack into a loaf pan. Bake in 300 degrees; oven for 1 1/2 hours. Serve with Tomato Sauce.
Tomato Sauce:
1 (14 1/2 oz.)  can stewed tomatoes
1/2  c. ketchup
2  Tbsp. minced onion
2  Tbsp. finely chopped celery
2  Tbsp. sugar or to taste

      Place contents of tomatoes in saucepan. Break up with a spoon. Add the ketchup, onion, celery and sugar. Bring to a boil. Cook until slightly thickened. Season to taste with salt and pepper. If desired, thicken with small amount (1 to 2 tablespoons) of instant dissolving flour or 1 to 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour dissolved.
