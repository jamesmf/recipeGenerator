CHOCOLATE LOGS


1 (12 oz.)  jar crunchy peanut butter
2  c. confectioners sugar
1  lb. dates, chopped fine
1/2  c. chopped walnuts
1 (12 oz.)  pkg. semi-sweet chocolate chips
1/2  bar paraffin wax (2 1/2-inch sq.)
1/4  c. butter, melted

      Knead peanut butter, butter, sugar, dates and nuts thoroughly. Press into logs 2-inches long, using 1 tablespoon mixture for each. Place on waxed paper and chill overnight.  Melt chocolate and wax in top of double boiler.  Insert a cake tester or hat pin through the end of each log; dip in chocolate and let any excess drip. Place on cookie sheet until firm.
