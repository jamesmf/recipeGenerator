CHICKEN POT PIE


15  oz. can peas and carrots
12  oz. can chicken
1  can cream of chicken soup
1/4  tsp. thyme or sage
2  - 9-inch frozen pie crusts

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
      Combine chicken, soup, veggies and thyme or sage.
      Put in 9-inch pie crust.  Cover with other crust.  Cut vents in top crust.
      Bake 30 minutes or more; until top crust is brown.
