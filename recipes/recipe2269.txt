CHICKEN CRESCENTS


4  skinless chicken breasts
1  onion
1  pkg. almonds, slivered
1  pkg. cream cheese
1  c. milk
1  Tbsp. butter
4  cans crescent rolls
  desired amounts of salt and pepper

      Boil chicken for about 30 to 45 minutes; drain and cool.  Tear chicken into thin pieces.  Add cream cheese.  Sprinkle salt over entire chicken.  Add about 1 tablespoon pepper.  Add almonds.  Cut up onion and add.  Stir in milk.  Melt butter in microwave.  Open crescent rolls and add mixture, everything except butter, onto crescent rolls, usually a teaspoon.  Close rolls and put on baking pan.  Pour melted butter over rolls and bake at 350 degrees; for about 10 minutes or until brown.
