SHRIMP & EVERYTHING
(4 Star)  

1  Tbsp. oil or butter
1  small onion, chopped
1/2  c. celery, thinly sliced
1  can stewed tomatoes
1  can tomato sauce
1/2  tsp. garlic powder
1  lb. shrimp, peeled & deveined
1  small green or red pepper, sliced thin
1/2  tsp. dried thyme
1 1/2  c. instant rice, uncooked
  Creole seasoning to taste

      Place shrimp, onions, & peppers in sauce pan and saut√© until shrimp are pink. Add rest of ingredients (except rice) and bring to a boil. Stir in rice. Cover and remove from heat. Let stand 5-6 minutes. Fluff. Add parsley, if desired, and serve.
