DELICIOUS CHICKEN BREAST


8  boneless chicken breast
1  pkg. of lean bacon
1 3  oz. jar of Armour Dried Beef
1  can Cream of Mushroom soup or Cream of Chicken
1 8  oz. sour cream

      Grease a 9 X 13 glass baking dish.  Preheat oven to 275 degrees.  Line baking dish with dried beef.  Wrap chicken breast with bacon and then place on top of dried beef.  Mix the soup and sour cream and pour over chicken.  Bake, uncovered for 2 1/2 hours.
