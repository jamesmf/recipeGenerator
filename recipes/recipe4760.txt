GREEN-AND-WHITE TUNA SALAD


1 (3.5 oz.)  can tuna, drained
1/4  c. finely chopped scallions (onions)
1/4  c. finely chopped celery
1/4  c. finely chopped green pepper
1/4  c. finely chopped fresh parsley
1  Tbsp. plain yogurt
1  tsp. Dijon-style mustard
1  tsp. lime juice

      In a medium bowl, combine first 5 ingredients. In a small bowl, whisk together yogurt, mustard and lime juice. Pour over the tuna mixture; toss lightly and serve. Makes 2 servings.
