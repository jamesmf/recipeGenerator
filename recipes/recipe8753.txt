EGGPLANT PARMESAN


4  eggplants
1  beaten egg
  Flour
  Spaghetti sauce
  Pepperoni, opt.
  Parmesan cheese

      Cut eggplant thin, dip in beaten egg then dip in flour.  In a frying pan with heated oil, fry eggplant slices till golden brown. Turn and cook on other side.  Remove and drain on paper towel. Season with salt and pepper.  Place spaghetti sauce on bottom of baking dish and add eggplant slices, layering with sauce.  Can add pepperoni, if desired.  Bake 350 degrees; for 45 mins. -1 hour. Add parmesan cheese when done.
