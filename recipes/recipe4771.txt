HEARTY SPINACH SALAD


4  slices bacon
2  Tbsp. sesame seeds
2  Tbsp. grated Parmesan cheese
10  oz. fresh spinach, washed
3  hard-cooked eggs, sliced
1/4  c. mayonnaise or salad dressing
1/2  c. sour cream
2  tsp. vinegar
2  tsp. sugar
1  minced green onion
1/4  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. garlic salt

      Fry bacon slices until crisp; remove from skillet. Brown sesame seeds in hot bacon fat. Remove from the skillet. Drain and cool them. Add Parmesan cheese to seeds and mix well.
       Tear spinach into bite-size pieces. Combine remaining ingredients (except eggs) for dressing, mixing well. Crumble the crisp bacon; toss lightly with spinach, hard-cooked egg slices, dressing and 3/4 cup of the sesame seed mixture. Sprinkle remaining sesame seed mixture over top. Makes 4 to 6 servings.
