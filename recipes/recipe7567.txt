CREAM OF WHEAT LOAF


1  1/2 lbs. lean ground beef
1  egg
1/4  c. Cream of Wheat
1/4  c. finely chopped onions
1/2  c. ketchup
3  Tbsp. worcestershire sauce
1  tsp. seasoning salt (Lawry's)

      Mix all ingredients well (best mixed with your hands).  Form for your choice of baking pan.  Bake uncovered on 400 degrees; for approximately 45 min.  Drain excess grease.  Top with ketchup and bake 5 more min.
