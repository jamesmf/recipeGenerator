CHOCOLATE HOT FUDGE SAUCE


3  sq. unsweetened chocolate, melted
1  c. sugar
2/3  c. evaporated milk
2  egg yolks
1/2  stick butter (1/4 c.)
1  tsp. vanilla

      Melt chocolate.  Add sugar.  Mix. Add 1/3 cup evaporated milk.  Mix. Beat egg yolks into other 1/3 cup evaporated milk.  Add to chocolate mixture and let cook for a few minutes over low heat. Add butter and vanilla.  Serve warm over ice cream, cream puffs, etc.
