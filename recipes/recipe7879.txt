PAALAK GOSHTH


1  bunch fresh spinach
1  pound chicken, lamb, or beef
1  tsp. each, garlic and ginger paste
1  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. green chili paste
1/2  tsp. cayenne pepper
1/8  tsp. turmeric
1-1/2  tsp. cumin powder
1-1/2  tsp. coriander powder
1  medium onion, thinly sliced
  Cooking oil
15  fenugreek (methi) seeds
4  cloves
  2-3 cardamoms
5  broken pieces of cinnamon
1 small  clove garlic, finely sliced
1/2-inch  piece ginger, finely sliced
1/2  tomato, sliced
  Few whole green chilies
2  Tbsp. sliced onion

      Marinate meat with garlic, ginger and green chili pastes, salt, cayenne, turmeric, cumin powder, and coriander powder.  Set aside.  Wash, drain, and finely chop spinach.  Set aside.
      Heat oil in a pot and add methi seeds, cloves, cardamom, and cinnamon.  Braise for about a minute then add sliced onions and sliced garlic/ginger.  Saut√© until onion turns golden brown.  Then add meat and braise over medium-low heat until the meat turns brown, adding a few drops of water as necessary.  Add tomato slices, green chilies, 2 Tbsp. sliced onion, and spinach and braise another minute or two.
      Add 1/2 to 1 cup of water and cook covered, over low heat until meat is tender, stirring occasionally.
