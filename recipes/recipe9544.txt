FISH STEW


1  large onion, chopped
1/2  c. celery, chopped with leaves
1  large garlic clove
2  Tbsp. butter
2 (1 lb.)  cans chopped tomatoes
1/2  c. dry white wine
1/2  c. minced parsley
1  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. rosemary
1/4  tsp. pepper and thyme
1  lb. fish (any whitefish, cod is very good)

      Saut√© onion, celery and garlic in butter. Add all other ingredients, except fish, and simmer for 30 minutes.  Add fish in chunks and cook gently for 7 to 10 minutes.
