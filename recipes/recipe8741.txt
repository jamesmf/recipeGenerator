COUNTRY STYLE CHICKEN KIEV


1/2  c. fine dry bread crumbs
2  chicken breasts, split
2  tbsp. grated Parmesan
1/4  c. white wine or apple juice
1  tsp. basil
1  tsp. oregano
1/4  c. chopped green onion
1/4  c. chopped parsley
1/2  tsp. garlic salt
1/2  tsp. salt
2/3  c. butter or oleo, melted

      Heat oven to 375 degrees;.  Combine bread crumbs, Parmesan, basil, oregano, garlic salt and salt.  Dip chicken breasts in melted butter, then coat with crumb mixture; reserve remaining butter. Place chicken skin side up in ungreased 9-inch square baking dish. Bake for 50-60 mins. or until chicken is fork tender.  Meanwhile, add wine, green onion and parsley to reserved butter (about 1/2 c).  When chicken is golden brown, pour butter sauce over chicken. Continue baking for 30-40 minutes or until sauce is heated through.  Serve with sauce spooned over.  Makes 4 servings.
