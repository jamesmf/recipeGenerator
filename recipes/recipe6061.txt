CONGEALED SALAD


2  pkg. (3 oz.) jello (any flavor, or 2 different kinds)
3  c. hot water
1  pkg. Dream Whip, prepared (or use 8 oz. Cool Whip)
2  c. miniature marshmallows
1 small  can crushed pineapple with juice
1/2  c. pecans, chopped

      Mix each pack of jello with 1 1/2 cup of hot water, dissolve. Pour into flat dish (not too deep).  Let congeal; cut into 1 inch cubes.  Add the jello cubes to the whipped Dream Whip or Cool Whip.  Add marshmallows, crushed pineapple and pecans; mix together.  Let set until firm.
