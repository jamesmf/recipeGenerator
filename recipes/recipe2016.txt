CHOCOLATE BRITTLE


2  c. butter
2  c. sugar
1/4  c. plus 2 Tbsp. water
12  (2.05 oz.) milk chocolate candy bars
3  c. chopped pecans

      Combine butter, sugar and water in a Dutch oven.  Cook over low heat until it reaches hard-crack stage (300 degrees;).  Remove from heat and immediately pour onto 2 buttered 12-inch pizza pans, spreading to edge of pans.
      Melt chocolate bars in top of double boiler over hot water. Spread chocolate over brittle; sprinkle pecans evenly on top. Press pecans into chocolate when firm.  Break candy into pieces. Makes about 4 pounds.
