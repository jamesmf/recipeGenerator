ENCHILADA CASSEROLE


4  lb. ground chuck
1 large  bottle of taco sauce
2  large (or 4 small) Old El Paso enchilada sauce
2  packages of shredded cheese
1  large (or 2 small) cream of mushroom or chicken soup
  powdered chili to taste
  corn tortillas (package of about 36)

      Brown ground meat, drain. Combine meat, onions, sauces, soups, and chili. Simmer till blended. Layer meat, tortillas, and cheese., ending with cheese in a 12 x 8 x 2 inch pan. Bake at 350 degrees; till cheese is melted and bubbly.
