RED CHILE
(Asado)  

3 to 4  lb. pork shoulder
1  lb. red chile pods
4  garlic cloves
3  tsp. oregano
2  Tbsp. salt
2  bay leaves
2  tsp. cuminos
3  Tbsp. white flour
1  tsp. cuminos

      Brown meat well.  Boil chile pods.  Blend well in blender.  Add garlic, bay leaves and 2 teaspoons cuminos to chile in blender. Fry meat with 1 teaspoon cuminos.  Add white flour; brown until tender.  Add chile, then oregano and salt.  Boil and simmer for 30 minutes, until well done.
