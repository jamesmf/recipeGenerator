HANKY PANKY


1  pkg. cocktail rye bread
2  lb. block mild Mexican Velveeta
1  lb. mild sausage

      Brown sausage and drain.  Melt cheese.  Combine sausage and cheese.  Place bread on cookie sheet, sides touching.  Spoon cheese and sausage mixture onto bread.  Bake at 350 degrees; until brown.
