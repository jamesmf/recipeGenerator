CREAMY CASHEW CHICKEN SALAD


4  c. cubed cooked chicken
1  c. chopped celery
1/2  c. chopped green pepper
1  jar (2 oz.) diced pimentos, drained
1/2  c. mayonnaise
1/3  c. whipping cream
1/4  c. sour cream
3  Tbsp. thinly sliced green onions
2  Tbsp. minced fresh parsley
1 1/2  tsp. lemon juice
1 1/2  tsp. tarragon vinegar or cider vinegar
1  garlic clove, minced
1/2  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper
3/4  c. salted cashews
  Leaf lettuce and additional cashews, optional

      In a large bowl, combine the chicken, celery, green pepper and pimentos; set aside.  In a blender, combine all the other ingredients, except the cashews and lettuce.  Pour the blended ingredients over the chicken mixture and toss to coat.  Cover and refrigerate until serving.
      Fold in cashews; serve in a lettuce lined bowl and garnish with additional cashews if desired.  Yield: 6 servings.
