HAMBURGER SURPRISE


1 1/2  lb. hamburger
2  c. Minute rice (uncooked)
16  oz. Velveeta cheese
10  oz. (1 can) soup, mushroom
1  c. milk
1/4  c. sharp cheese, shredded

      In a skillet, brown hamburger.  Drain off fat.  Add rice, cheese, soup, milk and mix together.  Pour into a 9x13-inch casserole dish.  Top with shredded cheese.
      Bake at 400 degrees; for 30 minutes or until bubbly.
