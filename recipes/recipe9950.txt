FRENCH RICE


1 (10 1/2 oz.)  can cream of onion soup (undiluted)
1/2  c. butter or margarine, melted
1 (4 1/2 oz.)  jar sliced mushrooms (undrained)
1 (8 oz.)  can sliced water chestnuts (undrained)
1  c. uncooked regular rice

      Combine soup and melted butter.  Stir well.  Drain mushrooms and water chestnuts.  Reserve liquid.  Add enough water to liquid to equal 1 1/3 cups.  Add mushrooms, water chestnuts, liquid and rice to soup mixture.  Stir well.  Pour into lightly greased 10 x 6 x 2 baking dish.  Cover and bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour.
