CHINESE EGG ROLLS


1  small cabbage, shredded
1  can bean sprouts
1  c. celery, chopped fine
1  (3 lb.) chicken, cooked and cut up (can use more if wanted)
1  can chopped water chestnuts
2 minced garlic  cloves or powder
3/4  c. soy sauce (more if needed)

      Bring cabbage, bean sprouts and celery to a boil, then drain. Stir-fry chicken for 3 minutes.  Add water chestnuts, garlic and soy sauce to chicken and stir-fry for 5 minutes more.  Mix fried and boiled vegetables (you can add more soy sauce if needed).  Let stand for a few minutes.  Roll into skins; seal with beaten egg. Deep-fry until crisp.
