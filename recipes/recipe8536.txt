BREAKFAST CASSEROLE


1 1/2  lb. sausage (cooked and drained)
1 1/2  c. hash brown potatoes (cooked slightly)
6  to 8 eggs (slightly beaten)
1  c. shredded cheddar cheese
3/4  c. evaporated milk
1  can cream of celery soup (or cream of mushroom)
1  jar mushrooms

      Mix eggs, milk, soup, cheese and mushrooms in a bowl. In cooking dish, layer sausage then hash browns. Pour liquids over the top Refrigerate 1 hour before cooking. Bake 45 minutes at 325 degrees;.
