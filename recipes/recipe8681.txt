TEXAS TWO-STEP CHICKEN


4  chicken breasts (boneless and skinless)
1 1/2  c. picante sauce
3  Tbsp. light brown sugar
1  Tbsp. Dijon-style mustard
3  c. cooked rice

	Preheat oven to 400 degrees;.  Place chicken in a 2-quart shallow baking dish.  Mix picante sauce, sugar and mustard.  Pour sauce mixture over chicken. Bake for 20 minutes or until chicken is done. Serve over rice.
