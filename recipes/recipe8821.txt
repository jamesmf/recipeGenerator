BAKED SWEET AND SOUR CHICKEN BREASTS


8  boneless skinless chicken breasts
1/2  c. orange marmalade
1/2  envelope onion soup mix
1/2  c. green pepper, chopped
3/4  c. orange juice
1/4  c. fat-free Italian dressing
1/2  tsp. lite soy sauce

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees;. In small bowl, combine marmalade, onion soup mix, green pepper, soy sauce, orange juice and Italian dressing. Spray large pan or dish with nonstick cooking spray. Place chicken breasts on pan and pour sauce over the top. Bake at 350 degrees; for 40 to 45 minutes. Entire recipe makes 8 servings.
