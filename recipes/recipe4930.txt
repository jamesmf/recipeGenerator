FROZEN FRUIT SALAD


1  pkg. (8 oz.) cream cheese, softened
1  c. mayonnaise
1  c. Cool Whip
1 28-oz.  can fruit cocktail, drained
1/2  c. maraschino cherries, halved
2 1/2  c. miniature marshmallows

      In large bowl blend cream cheese and mayonnaise.  Fold in remaining ingredients.  Spray a loaf pan lightly with vegetable cooling spray; add fruit mixture.  Freeze several hours until firm.  (For individual servings: spoon mixture into paper cupcake foils and freeze on a cookie sheet.)
