DEER, ONION AND POTATO CASSEROLE


6  large onions, sliced
6  large potatoes
2  cans mushroom soup
2  lb. ground deer meat or hamburger meat
4  Tbsp. grease
2  Tbsp. salt on meat

      Season meat and brown in grease. Peel and slice potatoes thinly. Make alternate layers of meat, potatoes and onions. Pour mushroom soup on top and bake at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes.
