SUMMERTIME SALSA


2 (15 oz.)  cans black beans, drained and rinsed
1 (15 oz.)  can Mexican-style corn, drained
2 (15 oz.)  cans diced tomatoes with green chile peppers, partially drained
2  fresh tomatoes, diced
2  bunches green onions, chopped

      In a bowl, mix together the black beans, corn and diced tomatoes with green chile peppers. Add freshly diced tomatoes and onion.  Mix, cover and refrigerate for at least 8 hours. Garnish with cilantro leaves.
      Serving Size: 1/2 cup. Cal-81, CHO-15.7, Pro-4.0, Fat-.33.
