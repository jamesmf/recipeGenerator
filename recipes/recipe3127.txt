WHITE CHOCOLATE CLUSTERS


1 (14 oz.)  pkg. white chocolate
2 1/2  c. Rice Krispies cereal
1/2  c. pecans, chopped (or more)
1/2  c. pretzel sticks, broken (or more)

      Melt white chocolate in microwave at 30 second intervals until melted. Stir in Rice Krispies, pecans, and pretzels. Stir until coated. Drop by teaspoonful onto wax paper or foil. Let set until firm.
