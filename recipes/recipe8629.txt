HAM QUICHE WEDGES


2  C. Bisquick baking mix
1/2  C. cold water
3  eggs, beaten
1  C. sour cream
1  C. shredded cheddar cheese
1/4  C. sliced green onions
1/2  tsp. onion salt
1  C. finely chopped fully cooked ham

      Heat oven to 425 degrees.  Grease a 12-inch pizza pan.
      Mix baking mix and water until soft dough forms; beat vigorously 20 strokes.  Gently smooth dough in ball on floured board,  Knead 5 times.  Press dough on bottom and up sides of pan with floured hands.  Bake 10 minutes.
      Mix eggs, sour cream, cheese, onions and onion salt; pour over crust.  Sprinkle ham over egg mixture.  Bake until set, about 25 minutes.
      Let stand 5 minutes before cutting.
