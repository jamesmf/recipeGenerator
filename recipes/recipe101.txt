CHOCOLATE STRAWBERRY COOLER


1/2  c. sliced strawberries
2  Tbsp. sugar
1  Tbsp. cocoa
1  c. milk
1/2  c. chilled carbonated beverage

      Combine strawberries, sugar, cocoa and half of the milk in blender container; blend well.  Add remaining milk and carbonated beverage; blend.  Garnish with dollop of whipped cream or ice cream and a strawberry, if desired.  Yields two 8-ounce servings.
