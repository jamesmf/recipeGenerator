FROZEN CRANBERRY SALAD


2  pkg. (8-oz. each) cream cheese, softened
2  cans (20-oz. each) crushed pineapple, drained
1  c. pecans, chopped
2  cans (15-oz. each) whole-berry cranberry sauce
1  carton (16-oz.) Cool Whip

      Mix cream cheese with next 3 ingredients.  Fold in Cool Whip. Spoon into molds or loaf pan.  Freeze until firm.
