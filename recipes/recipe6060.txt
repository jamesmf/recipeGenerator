CHRISTMAS SALAD
(3 Layer Salad)  
Layer 1:
1  c. boiling water
1/2  c. cold water
1 (3 oz.)  box lime jello
  small jar maraschino cherries, drained

      Mix lime jello with boiling water; dissolve.  Then add 1/2 c. cold water; let get syrupy.  Add maraschino cherries; pour in mold and jell.
Layer 2:
1 (3 oz.)  box lemon jello
1  c. boiling water
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese

      Mix lemon jello with boiling water; dissolve.  Add cream cheese; let get syrupy.  Add to the first layer and jell that layer.
Layer 3:
1 (3 oz.)  box strawberry jello
1  c. boiling water
1/2  c. cold water
1 (20 oz.)  can crushed pineapple, drained

      Mix strawberry jello with boiling water; dissolve.  Add cold water; let it get syrupy.  Add drained crushed pineapple; pour over lemon layer.  Congeal for several hours.  Turn out of mold on platter.
