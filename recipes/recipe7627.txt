BARBECUED PORK CHOPS


4  to 6 center cut pork chops
3/4  c. water
1/4  c. vinegar
1  Tbsp. sugar
2  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/2  c. catsup
1  tsp. salt
  dash of pepper

      Place   pork  chops  evenly  in  baking  dish.  Combine remaining  ingredients  and pour over chops.  Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees; for 1 hour.
