SUMMERTIME BURGERS


1  egg, slightly beaten
1 1/2  tsp. onion powder
1 1/2  tsp. prepared horseradish
1 1/2  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
3/4  tsp. salt
3/4  tsp. chili powder
2  Tbsp. barbecue sauce
1 1/2  c. Wheat Chex cereal, crushed to 1/2 c.
1 1/2  lb. lean ground beef

      Combine egg, onion powder, horseradish, Worcestershire sauce, salt, chili powder and barbecue sauce.  Blend well.  Mix in Wheat Chex crumbs and ground beef.  Shape into 6 patties.  Grill 8 to 12 minutes or to desired doneness.  Turn midway during cooking.  Brush with additional barbecue sauce.  Makes 6 hamburgers.
Surprise Burgers:
1  pkg. (3 oz.) cream cheese
2  tsp. barbecue sauce
1/4  c. chopped green onions with tops

      Form into 12 thin patties about 4 1/2 inches in diameter (scant 1/3 cup).  Combine cream cheese, green onions with tops and barbecue sauce.  Place rounded tablespoonful in center of 6 patties.  Cover each with second patty.  Carefully seal edges. Grill.
