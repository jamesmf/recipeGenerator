SPICY BEAN, CORN AND PEPPER SALAD


2  Tbsp. apple juice, undiluted
1/2  c. salsa
2  c. cooked black or red beans, rinsed and drained
1/3  c. red bell pepper, chopped
1  Tbsp. vinegar
1/4  tsp. black pepper
1  c. canned low sodium corn, rinsed and drained
2  scallions, diced

      In a small bowl, combine apple juice, vinegar, salsa and black pepper.  In another bowl, combine beans, corn, peppers and scallions.  Pour in salsa mixture.  Chill for at least 1 hour. Serves 8.
