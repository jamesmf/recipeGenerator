FABULOUS BEEF FAJITAS


1/2  c. prepared Italian salad dressing, divided
1/2  tsp. chili powder
1  lb. boneless sirloin steak, cut into 1/4 inch strips
1  medium sweet pepper, sliced
1  medium green pepper, sliced
1  medium onion, sliced and separated into rings
  flour tortilla

	In a large resealable plastic bag, combine 1/4 cup salad dressing and chili powder; add steak.  Seal and turn to coat; refrigerate for 30 minutes.  In a skillet, sauté peppers and onion in remaining salad dressing until crisp and tender.  Remove and keep warm.	Drain steak, discarding marinade in skillet, sauté steak for 6-10 minutes or until no longer pink.  Return vegetables to pan.  Heat through and spoon meat and vegetables down center of tortillas; fold in sides.  Yield:  4-6 servings.
