WADIE'S CHEESE BALL


3  (8 oz.) cream cheese
2  jars dried beef
2  tsp. Worcestershire
1  tsp. Accent
2  bunches green onions (tops also)

      Soften cream cheese; sprinkle Accent on the cheese and Worcestershire sauce. Stir in onion and dried beef (saving enough beef to roll cheese ball in). Serve with crackers (apple slices or stuff celery).
