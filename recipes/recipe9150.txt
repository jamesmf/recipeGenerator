HAM CROQUETTES


2  c. ground, cooked ham
1  c. mashed potatoes
1  Tbsp. chopped onions
2  c. fine dry bread crumbs
1  Tbsp. chopped parsley
1  Tbsp. water
1  egg, beaten
  oil, for frying
  salt and pepper to taste

      Combine ham, potatoes, egg, onions and parsley. Add salt and pepper. Shape into balls. Roll in bread crumbs. Brown in hot oil, turning often, until golden brown.
