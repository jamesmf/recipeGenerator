PIZZA BURGERS


1  lb. ground beef
1/4  c. chopped onions
1  c. pizza sauce
1/2  c. shredded Mozzarella cheese
8  buns

      Cook ground beef in large skillet until meat is brown.  Add onions and cook for 1 more minute.  Add pizza sauce and cheese to cooked hamburger.  Cook 2 minutes or until hot.
