FANCIES


1  c. butter, softened
1  c. powdered sugar
1  tsp. vanilla extract
1 1/4  c. flour
1  c. quick cooking oats
  chocolate raisins

      In small bowl, cream butter and sugar.  Add extract.  Stir in flour and oats.  Stir in chocolate raisins.  Shape into 2 rolls, each 1 1/2-inches in diameter.  Wrap in waxed paper and chill. (Can make drop cookies.)  Heat oven to 350 degrees;.  Cut rolls into 1/4-inch slices and place on ungreased cookie sheet.  Bake 10 to 12 minutes.  Makes 6 to 7 dozen.
