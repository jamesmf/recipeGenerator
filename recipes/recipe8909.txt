HAMBURGER SKILLET


1  lb. ground beef
2  medium size potatoes, sliced thin
3  c. shredded cabbage
1  c. carrots, sliced thin
2  cans mushroom soup
1  medium onion, sliced thin
4  slices American cheese

      Using electric skillet, press 3/4 ground beef in bottom of skillet. Then add cabbage, carrots, mushroom soup, onion and cheese. Cook at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes or until vegetables are done.
