SWEET POTATO CASSEROLE


3  c. mashed sweet potatoes
1  c. white sugar
2  eggs
1  tsp. vanilla
1/3  c. milk
1/2  c. butter
1  c. brown sugar
1/3  c. all-purpose flour
1/3  c. butter
1  c. chopped pecans

      Combine potatoes, white sugar, eggs, vanilla, milk and 1/2 cup butter.  Beat with electric mixer until smooth.  Pour into greased 2-quart casserole dish.  Combine brown sugar, flour, 1/3 cup butter and pecans.  Sprinkle over top of casserole.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes.
