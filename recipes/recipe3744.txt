CRAB ON CRISPY ENGLISH MUFFIN


1 (7 oz.)  can crab meat, drained
1  stick butter or margarine, softened
1 (5 oz.)  jar Kraft Old English cheese
1  pkg. English muffins

	Preheat oven to 325 degrees;. Mix crab meat, butter and cheese. Spread on split muffins. Cut into 1/4 wedges. Place quarters on a baking sheet. Bake for 15 to 20 minutes or until bubbly and browned. Serve hot.
