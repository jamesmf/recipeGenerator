MEXICORN SALAD


2 or 3 small  cans Mexicorn
8 to 12  oz. sharp Cheddar cheese
1/4  c. green onions
1 small  jar pimentos
1/2  c. sour cream
1/2  c. mayonnaise

      Mix above ingredients together. Taste to see if you need to add any salt. Add salt if needed.
      You can use Fritos to dip or use with vegetables.
