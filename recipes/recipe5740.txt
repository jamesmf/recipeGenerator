BROCCOLI AND CAULIFLOWER SALAD


1  head broccoli, cut in small heads
1  head cauliflower, cut in small heads
1  small onion, sliced
1  unpeeled red apple, chopped in about 1/2-inch pieces
2  stalks celery, sliced

 
Dressing:
1/3  c. white vinegar
1/3  c. salad oil
1/2  c. white sugar
1  c. Miracle Whip (not mayonnaise)

      Put all vegetables and apple into a bowl.  In another bowl, mix dressing ingredients.  Pour dressing over vegetables.  Mix well and marinate for 24 hours or at least overnight.
