STRAWBERRY SALAD

Crust:
3/4  c. Butter, melted
2  tbsp. Sugar
2  c. Pretzels, crushed

 
Filling:
8  oz. cream cheese
1  lg. container Cool Whip
1  c. Sugar

 
Topping:
2  pkgs. Strawberry Jello
2  pkgs. Strawberries, drained

      For crust, mix all ingredients together and bake for 10 minutes at 350 degrees.
      For filling, mix all ingredients and pour into crust.
      For topping, mix according to directions and pour on top of crust and filling.
