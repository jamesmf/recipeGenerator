SOUTHERN CORN SOUP


2  Tbsp. butter
2  Tbsp. flour
1  pt. milk
1  pt. bouillon or soup stock
1  can creamed corn (strain through a colander)
  salt and pepper to taste
  popcorn or croutons

      Melt butter and stir in flour.  Add milk, bouillon or soup stock and corn.  Season with salt and pepper.  Boil 5 minutes and serve in bouillon cups with popcorn floating on top.  Croutons can be used in place of popcorn.  Serves 4.
