HEARTY SIX-CAN CHILI


1  lb. hamburg
1/2  c. chopped onion
2  cans (15 1/2 oz.) chili beans in sauce
2  cans (14 1/2 oz.) diced tomatoes
1 (15 oz.)  can black beans
1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can cream of mushroom
1  tsp. chili powder

      Brown hamburg and onion.  Add the rest of the ingredients; mix well.  Bring to boil.  Reduce heat; simmer 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
