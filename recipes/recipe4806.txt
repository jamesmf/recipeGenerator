DUTCH COUNTRY BEAN SOUP


1  lb. Northern beans, soaked overnight
  ham bone
1/2  c. chopped onion
1  c. diced celery
  salt and pepper
1  can tomato sauce
1/2  c. diced potatoes
2  tsp. parsley

      Drain beans; add fresh water and cook slowly with ham bone for 2 hours.  Put in onion, celery, potatoes, tomato sauce, parsley, salt and pepper and simmer until vegetables are soft. Remove ham bone; trim off all meat and add to soup.
