IMLY AND KHAJUR CHUTNEY
(A Sweet and Sour Chutney)  

2  cups dates, pitted
1/4  cup tamarind (imly) deseeded
1  cup gud (packed brown sugar)
1  tsp. garam masala
1  tsp. cayenne pepper
1  tsp. cumin powder
  Pinch of asafetida
  Salt to taste
4  cups water

      Combine all ingredients.  Bring to a boil.  Reduce heat and simmer for another 15-20 minutes.  Cool and strain the mixture through a sieve.  You may also puree the cutney in a blender then strain it.
      Can be stored in refrigerator for up to a month and frozen for more than 6 months.
