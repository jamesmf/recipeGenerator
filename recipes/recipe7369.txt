EASY SWISS STEAK


3  Tbsp. vegetable oil
2  lb. round steak (1/2-inch thick)
1/4  c. flour
1  envelope dry onion soup mix
1 (8 oz.)  can tomato soup
1/2  c. water

      Pour oil in 7 1/2 x 12 x 1 1/2-inch baking dish.  Place in oven to heat at 400 degrees;.  Cut steak into serving size.  Pound 2 tablespoons flour into each side.  Place in a heated dish with oil and turn to coat both sides.  Sprinkle on soup mix.  Pour tomato soup, mixed with water, over all.  Cover tight.  Reduce temperature to 325 degrees;. Bake 2 1/2 to 3 hours until fork-tender.  Skim excess fat from gravy.
      Can add chopped onions to meat before baking.
