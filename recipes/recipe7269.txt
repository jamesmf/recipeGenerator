GREEN ENCHILADAS


1  can cream of chicken soup
1 small  can evaporated milk
1/2  lb. Velveeta cheese
1 small  can green chilies, chopped
2 small  cans pimentos, chopped
1  lb. ground meat, lightly browned
1  doz. tortillas
1/2  lb. Longhorn cheese
1  c. chopped onion

      Heat soup, milk and Velveeta cheese in double boiler until cheese is melted. Add chilies and pimentos. Brown meat. Combine grated Longhorn cheese and onion. Fill each tortilla with cheese/onion mixture. Roll lightly and place in baking dish. Pour cheese sauce over the enchiladas. Cover with foil and bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes.
