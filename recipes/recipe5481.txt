BROCCOLI AND CAULIFLOWER SALAD


1  cauliflower
1  lb. broccoli
1/2  lb. mushrooms
1  purple onion
2  celery stalks
  lettuce leaves (optional)

 
Dressing:
1  c. vegetable oil
1/2  c. white wine vinegar
1/2  c. sugar
1  tsp. dried Italian seasoning
2  tsp. dry mustard
1  tsp. salt (optional)

      Combine the cut up cauliflower, broccoli, sliced mushrooms, sliced onion and celery in a large bowl.  Set aside.  Combine the oil, vinegar, sugar, Italian seasoning, dry mustard and salt in a small bowl and stir well.  Pour over vegetables.  Toss gently to coat.  Cover and chill 3 hours.  May serve on lettuce leaves.
