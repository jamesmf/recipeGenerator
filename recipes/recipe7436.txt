DEVILED CRABS


1  lb. claw or regular crab meat
1/2  c. cracker crumbs
2  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  Tbsp. parsley flakes
1  Tbsp. celery seed
2  eggs, beaten
1/2  c. chopped onion
4  Tbsp. mayonnaise
2  Tbsp. prepared mustard
1/4  c. melted butter or cooking oil
  dash of hot sauce or 1 tsp. red pepper

      Mix well and pack into crab shells.  Sprinkle with paprika. Bake in moderate 350 degrees; oven about 1 hour.  Serve hot or cold.
