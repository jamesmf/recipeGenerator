CURRY AND RICE


1  large onion, chopped
3  large garlic buds
1  lb. meat, cut in pieces
2  medium potatoes
1 1/2  c. tomato puree
4  tsp. curry powder
2  tomatoes

      Put meat, onions and potatoes in skillet with oil. Fry. Add salt, tomato puree, tomatoes, curry powder and garlic. Simmer 1 hour. Serve over rice.
