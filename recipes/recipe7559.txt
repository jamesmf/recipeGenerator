CHICKEN POT PIE


1 1/2  sticks butter
2  cans white chicken
2  cans Veg-All
2  cans Cream of Chicken soup
1  c. chicken broth
2  c. Bisquick
2  c. milk

      Melt butter in 9 x 13 dish.  Put chicken on top.  Pour Veg-All on top of chicken.  Mix cream of chicken soup and chicken broth. Pour on top of Veg-All.  Mix Bisquick and milk and pour on top. Bake at 350 degrees; 45 minutes until brown.
