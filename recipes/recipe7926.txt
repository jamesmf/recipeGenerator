CHICKEN SHWERMA


3  chicken breasts
1  tsp. each salt and black pepper
1  tsp. cumin powder
1/2  Tbsp. each, chopped garlic and ginger
1  onion
1  tomato
3  Tbsp. olive oil
2  Tbsp. vinegar
  Pita bread for sandwich
  Tahina sauce

      Combine salt, pepper, cumin, garlic, ginger, olive oil, and vinegar in a big bowl with the chicken breasts and marinate in refrigerator for at least 4 hours.
      Transfer to a non-stick pan and cook over low heat.  Cut tomatoes and onions into small pieces and add it to the pan with the chicken.  Cook until chicken is tender.
      Serve inside pita bread with shredded lettuce and tahina sauce.
