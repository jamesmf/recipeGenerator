ENCHILADAS DE MOLE


1  doz. corn tortillas
  oil for frying (1/4-inch deep)
1 (8 oz.)  jar prepared mole poblano paste
3 1/2  c. chicken broth

      For the sauce, pour the oil that has separated from the contents of the jar into a skillet.  Heat the oil, add the mole paste and briefly fry (2 to 3 minutes) over medium heat.  Slowly add the chicken broth, stirring constantly.  Bring to a boil, then reduce to simmer.  Keep warm for tortilla preparation.
