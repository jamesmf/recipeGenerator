MOCHA TODDIES


3/4  c. instant coffee crystals
1/4  c. hot water
1 1/2  c. chocolate syrup
1  c. rum
1  tsp. vanilla
  hot milk
  vanilla ice cream

      In mixing bowl, combine coffee crystals and water.  Stir to dissolve crystals.  Add chocolate syrup, rum and vanilla.  Store, covered, in refrigerator.  To serve, stir tablespoons Mocha Base into 3/4 cup milk in a mug.  Top with ice cream.
