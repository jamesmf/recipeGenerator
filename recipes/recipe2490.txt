CUCUMBER CREAM CHEESE SPREAD


2 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
2  tsp. lemon juice
1/4  tsp. prepared horseradish
1/4  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. hot sauce
2  tsp. minced onion
1/2  medium cucumber, peeled and finely chopped

      Beat cream cheese until smooth.  Add next four ingredients and  mix  well.  Stir  in  onion  and  cucumber.  Put in covered container.  Chill one hour before serving.
