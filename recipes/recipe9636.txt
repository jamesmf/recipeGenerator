BARRACK SPECIAL


2  boxes Macaroni & Cheese
2  cans chicken, drained
2  jars salsa, mild, med., or hot
1  can black olives, drained & sliced
  Tortilla chips

      Cook macaroni & cheese as directed on box. Add chicken, salsa and black olives. Place in a large greased casserole dish. Bake at 350 degrees until bubbly in middle. Use tortilla chips and additional olives to garnish.
