HOT TOMATO SAUCE


1  pk. tomatoes
3  sweet green peppers
1  pod hot red pepper (more)
4  Tbsp. salt
2  c. sugar
3  c. vinegar
1  Tbsp. celery seed
2  Tbsp. mustard seed
1  Tbsp. whole allspice
2  sticks cinnamon

      Simmer tomatoes and peppers until mushy.  Run through food mill.  Add salt, sugar, vinegar and spices in bag and simmer until it thickens.  Bottle in sterilized jars.
