EASY SESAME CANDIES


1/2  c. light or dark Karo corn syrup
1/2  c. sugar
2  Tbsp. Mazola margarine
1  c. toasted sesame seeds

      Grease 8 x 8 x 2-inch baking pan.  In 1-quart saucepan stir together the corn syrup, sugar and margarine.  Stirring occasionally, bring to full rolling boil over medium heat and boil 5 minutes.  Stir in sesame seed.  Spread evenly in pan.  Cool. Remove from pan, break into pieces.  Makes about 3/4 pound.
