GREEN PEA SALAD


1/2  head shredded lettuce
1/2  c. chopped onion
1/2  c. chopped celery
1/2  c. chopped green pepper
2  Tbsp. brown sugar
1  can Lesueur green peas, drained
1 1/2  c. mayonnaise
1 1/2  c. grated cheddar cheese
1 (3 oz.)  bottle Hormel bacon bits.

      Prepare in 8 or 9 inch casserole dish.  Layer lettuce, pepper, celery, and onion.  Sprinkle on brown sugar; add peas. Spread on mayonnaise.  Top with cheese and bacon bits.  Cover and refrigerate 8 hours before serving.  This recipe can be doubled.
