SPICY POTATO SOUP


1 (5 1/4 oz.)  box au gratin potatoes
1 (11 oz.)  can whole kernel corn
1 (10 oz.)  can diced tomato and green chilies (Ro-Tel)
2  c. water
2  c. milk
2  c. cubed process American cheese
  salt and pepper to taste

	In a large pot, combine au gratin potatoes, corn, Ro-Tel and water. Mix well. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat. Cover and simmer 15 to 18 minutes or until potatoes are tender. Add milk, cheese and seasoning. Stir constantly until cheese is melted.
