ORIENTAL SALAD


1  head green cabbage, chopped
3  green onions, chopped
4  chicken breasts, cooked, cooled and diced
4  oz. slivered almonds
4  tsp. sesame seeds
4  tsp. sugar
6  tsp. vinegar
1/2 to 3/4  c. oil
2  chicken flavored Ramen noodle seasoning packets

	Toast almonds and sesame seeds. Cool. Toss cabbage, onions, chicken, almonds and sesame seeds. Combine sugar, vinegar, oil and flavor packets from Ramen noodle soup. Toss with cabbage mixture.
