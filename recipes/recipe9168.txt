SAUSAGE BREAKFAST CASSEROLE


1  lb. sausage
6  slices white bread, crust removed
1/2  c. shredded cheese
5  eggs
2  c. milk or half and half
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. dry mustard

      Cook sausage until crumbly; drain.  Spread each slice of bread with butter; cut into cubes.  Place bread cubes in a 13 x 9 x 2 inch dish.  Sprinkle with sausage and top with cheese. Combine remaining ingredients; beat well and pour over mixture. Chill 8 hours.  Remove and bake at 350 degrees; for 40 to 50 minutes.
