SPINACH SALAD WITH STRAWBERRIES AND PECANS


1 (10 oz.)  pkg. fresh spinach, torn
1  c. strawberries, halved
1  c. pecan halves, toasted
  poppy seed dressing

      Combine first 3 ingredients; drizzle with poppy seed dressing. Serve immediately. Serves 6.
      I use bottled dressing which can be purchased at grocery store.
