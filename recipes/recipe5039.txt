TOMATO SOUP

Part 1:
4  c. tomato juice
4  tsp. sugar
1/8  tsp. soda

 
Part 2:
4  c. milk
4  Tbsp. flour
1 1/2  tsp. salt
  a little pepper

      Part  2:  Mix  a  little  milk  with flour and add when mixture  is  almost  boiling.  Put each part in separate pans. (The  milk  mixture  pan  should  be large enough to hold both parts.) Heat both parts until just about boiling.  Remove from heat.  Add  part  1  to  part  2 very slowly, stirring all the time.  Serve immediately.
