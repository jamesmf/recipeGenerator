CAMPER STEW


1  lb. ground meat
1  can whole tomatoes
1/2  c. water
1  tsp. instant coffee powder
1 3/4  oz. chili mix
1  can pork and beans
  season to taste

	Put ground meat to cook in a large pot until brown. When the meat turns brown, drain the grease and return the meat to the pot. On a medium fire, mix in your tomatoes, water, coffee powder, chili mix, pork and beans and seasoning to taste. Stir real good and bring to a boil. Once the mixture is brought to a boil, lower the fire and let simmer for 20 to 30 minutes.
