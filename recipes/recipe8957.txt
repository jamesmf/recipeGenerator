HOMEMADE SALAMI


2  lb. ground round
1  tsp. coarse pepper
1  tsp. onion salt
1/2  tsp. garlic powder
2  Tbsp. Morton Quick curing salt
1  c. water
1  tsp. mustard seed
1  fresh jalapeno (optional)

      Mix ingredients and put in the refrigerator overnight. Shape into 2 rolls. Put on rack and bake at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes. Turn over and bake another 45 minutes.
