SQUASH RELISH


12  c. yellow squash, ground
4  c. onion, ground
2  c. bell peppers, ground
2  tsp. pickling salt
2 1/2  c. vinegar
6  c. sugar
1  tsp. celery seed
1 1/2  tsp. mustard seed
3/4  tsp. turmeric

      Sprinkle salt over ground vegetables.  Mix last 5 ingredients.  Let come to a boil.  Add vegetable mixture.  Let come to a boil.  Simmer for 20 minutes.  Put into hot sterile jars.  Seal.  Makes about 7 pints.
