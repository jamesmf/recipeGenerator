BROCCOLI SALAD


3  c. raw Broccoli, chopped
1/4  c. Green Onion, chopped (optional)
1  c. golden Raisins
8  slices crisp Bacon

 
Dressing:
1  c. Miracle Whip Salad Dressing
1/2  c. Sugar
2  tbsp. Vinegar

      Combine first 3 ingredients.  Mix dressing and pour over the broccoli mix.  Crumble bacon on top.  Serve chilled.
      Note:  Can substitute 1 c. of cauliflower instead of broccoli
