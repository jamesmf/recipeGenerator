CHEESY POTATOES


2  c. potato flakes
2  Tbsp. dried onion flakes
2  c. water
3/4  c. milk
3  Tbsp. margarine
1  egg
8  oz. cottage cheese
1  c. sharp Cheddar, shredded
  grated Parmesan cheese
  parsley (for garnish)

      Fix potato flakes according to package directions adding onion flakes to water.  Set aside to cool.  Add egg, cottage cheese and grated Cheddar, mixing well.  Put in well buttered casserole, top with Parmesan and garnish with chopped parsley.  Bake in 350 degrees; oven 30 to 40 minutes until set and lightly browned.  Serves 8.
