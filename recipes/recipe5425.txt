DEVILED EGGS


10  hard-boiled eggs
1 (3 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese, softened
1/4  c. mayonnaise
1  tsp. prepared mustard
1/8  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/8  tsp. salt
  dash of white pepper
  paprika

	Slice eggs lengthwise; remove yolks and set whites aside. In a small bowl, mash yolks with a fork. Add the cream cheese, mayonnaise, mustard, Worcestershire sauce, salt and pepper. Mix well. Stuff or pipe filling into egg whites. Garnish with paprika.
