SPICED PORK AND BEANS


2 medium  cans pork and beans
1/2  chopped bell pepper
2  stalks chopped celery
1/2  chopped onion
3  cloves garlic, chopped
1/4  tsp. Season-All
1/4  tsp. oregano
1  stick margarine
1/2  c. catsup
1/4  c. honey

      Cook under medium heat.  Place margarine, onion, bell pepper, celery, catsup, garlic, honey, oregano and Season-All all in saucepan.  Saut√© until green season is cooked.  Then pour in pork and beans, stirring constantly so that ingredients will not stick. Continue until dish is ready to eat.
