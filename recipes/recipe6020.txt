REFRIGERATOR SLAW


1  large head cabbage, chopped
2  medium onions, chopped
1  large carrot, shredded
1/2  pod green pepper, chopped
1  c. sugar
1  c. vinegar
3/4  c. Wesson oil
1  Tbsp. salt
1  tsp. dry mustard
1  tsp. celery seed

      Combine all vegetables in a large covered bowl.  Heat sugar, vinegar, oil, salt, dry mustard and celery seed to boiling; pour over vegetables.  Cool to room temperature; cover, and refrigerate.  This will keep 2 to 3 weeks in the refrigerator.
