BEEF WITH SOUR CREAM


1  lb. lean round steak
1/4  c. flour, seasoned with salt, pepper and garlic powder
  flour
2  Tbsp. vegetable oil
3/4  c. chopped onion
1  c. beef broth
1/2  tsp. thyme
1 (4 oz.)  can sliced mushrooms with liquid
1 (10 oz.)  pkg. frozen peas
1/2  c. sour cream
3 to 4  c. hot cooked rice

      Remove bone and fat from steak.  Cut meat into thin, narrow strips.  Dredge in flour; using large skillet, quickly brown meat on all sides in hot oil.  Add onions, broth, thyme, mushrooms and liquid from mushrooms.  Cover and cook 5 to 7 minutes.  Stir in sour cream.  Heat through; do not boil.  Serve over hot rice.
