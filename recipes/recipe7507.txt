PARMESAN CHICKEN


8  skinless boneless chicken breast pieces
1  c. mayonnaise
1  c. Parmesan cheese, grated
1/2  c. parsley flakes
2  c. bread crumbs

      Wash and take fat off chicken.  Pat dry.  Rub mayonnaise over each chicken breast.  Mix Parmesan cheese, parsley, and bread crumbs; dip chicken breast in dry ingredients.  Put on cookie sheet or shallow baking pan.  Bake in 350 degrees; oven for 30 minutes or until chicken is done.
