FESTIVE CARROT APPLE SALAD


2 - 3  c. grated Carrots
3  c. diced Apples (unpeeled)
1/2  c. Raisins or Grapes (red or green)
1/2  c. crushed Pineapple
1  c. Walnut or Pecan pieces (or sunflower seeds)
8  oz. Pineapple Yogurt

      Mix ingredients together. Refrigerate.  Can be made several hours before mealtime.  Makes 8 to 10 servings.
      Tips:  Lemon or Vanilla yogurt makes a nice substitute.
