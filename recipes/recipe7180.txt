TENDER CHICKEN NUGGETS


1/2  c. seasoned bread crumbs
1  Tbsp. Parmesan cheese
1  egg white
1  lb. boneless chicken breast, cut in 1-inch cubes

      In large resealable bag, combine bread crumbs and cheese.  In shallow bowl, beat the egg white.  Dip the chicken pieces in egg white, then place in bag and shake to coat.  Place in a 15 x 10-inch baking pan sprayed with nonstick cooking spray.  Bake, uncovered, at 400 degrees; for 12 to 15 minutes or until chicken is done. Yield: 4 servings.
