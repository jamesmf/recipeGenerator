TAHITI SALAD


4  c. cooked rice
1 (8 1/2 oz.)  can mixed vegetables, drained
1 (7 oz.)  can whole kernel corn/peppers
1  c. mayonnaise
1/4  c. vinegar
1/4  c. vegetable oil
1/2  c. diced celery
2  large tomatoes, chopped (use pimentos sometimes)
2  Tbsp. green peppers, chopped
2  Tbsp. onions, chopped
2  hard-boiled eggs, chopped
  dash of garlic powder
  salt to taste
  pepper to taste

      Combine all ingredients and mix well. Cover tightly and chill overnight.
