GWEN'S HAMBURGER CASSEROLE


2  lb. lean ground beef
1  c. water
2  Tbsp. butter
3  stalks celery, chopped
1  onion, chopped
1  box cubed bread stuffing mix
1/4  tsp. poultry seasoning
2  cans (10 1/2-oz. each) condensed cream of chicken soup
1  can mushrooms pieces and stems, drained

      Add butter, celery and onion to water and boil until vegetables are tender.  In a large bowl mix stuffing mix and poultry seasoning; mix in broth with celery and onion.  Pat beef lightly onto bottom of baking dish; sprinkle with salt and pepper. Layer stuffing mixture over meat, and cover all with the undiluted soup and the mushrooms.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 1/2 hours.  Let stand 15 minutes before serving.
