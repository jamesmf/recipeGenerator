CHOCOLATE CHIP COOKIES


1/2  c. Crisco (not oil)
1/2  c. sugar
1/2  c. brown sugar
1  egg
1/2  tsp. vanilla extract
1  c. plus 2 Tbsp. flour
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. baking soda
1  pkg. mini chocolate chips

      Mix Crisco, sugar and brown sugar.  Mix egg and vanilla extract.  Mix flour, salt and baking soda.  Combine all 3 mixtures and add chocolate chips.  Drop by teaspoonfuls on ungreased cookie sheet.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 10 minutes.
