TURKEY


2  c. turkey
1  c. celery
2  Tbsp. onion
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  can sliced water chestnuts
8  oz. sour cream
1  c. herb Pepperidge Farm stuffing with butter
1/4 to 1/2  c. broth

      Mix all ingredients. Top with stuffing. Bake at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes to 1 hour. Cool 1/2 hour, then add dressing to top.
