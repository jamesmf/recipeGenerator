BROCCOLI AND CHEESE SOUP


1 (10 oz.)  pkg. chopped broccoli
1/2  c. grated carrots
1/2  c. chopped celery
1/2  c. chopped onion
3/4  stick oleo
1/4  c. flour
2  c. milk
1  lb. Velveeta cheese, cubed
  pepper to taste

      Combine vegetables in large saucepan and cook until tender. Melt oleo in medium saucepan; stir in flour until smooth. Add milk; stir constantly. When mixture becomes thick, add cheese and stir until melted. Add vegetables and pepper. Mix well. Heat thoroughly. If soup is too thick, add more milk.
