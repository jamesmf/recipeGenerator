CRESCENT BACON BITES


1  pkg. crescent rolls
1  c. whipped cream cheese
1/4  c. bacon bits
1/4  c. chopped onion

      Roll crescent roll dough until thin.  Mix other ingredients, and spread on crescent rolls.  Roll up, starting with wide side. Crescent rolls will be in rectangle shape.  Refrigerate for 2 hours or until firm enough to slice.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 15 to 20 minutes until brown.  Serve warm.
