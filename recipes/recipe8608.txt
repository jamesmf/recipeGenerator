DEBORAH'S SWEET & SOUR CHICKEN


1/2  C. sugar
2  Tbsp. cornstarch
1/2  C. white vinegar
8  oz. can pineapple chunks, save juice
1/4  C. soy sauce
1/2  tsp. salt
1  tsp. minced garlic
1/2  tsp. paprika
1/2  tsp. ground ginger
1  C. chopped celery
  1C. chopped onion
3  C. cooked, diced chicken
1  C. chopped green pepper
  1-4oz. can mushrooms, drained
2  C. regular, uncooked rice
  1-8oz. can sliced water chestnuts, drained

      In medium saucepan, combine sugar and cornstarch; stir in vinegar, juice from pineapple, soy sauce, salt, garlic, paprika, ginger, celery and onion.  Bring to a boil; reduce heat.  Simmer, stirring constantly until thickened.
       Remove from heat. Stir in pineapple chunks, chicken, pepper, mushrooms and water chestnuts.
      Prepare rice according to directions.
      Serve over prepared rice.
