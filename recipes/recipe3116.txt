SPICY PECANS


1/2  c. sugar
  dash of salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, and cloves
2  c. pecans
1  egg white
1  Tbsp. water

      Combine sugar, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg and cloves; set aside. Melt butter in a pan. In a small bowl, beat egg white and water until frothy; add pecans and coat well. Place coated pecans in pan with melted butter. Bake at 250 degrees; until done.
