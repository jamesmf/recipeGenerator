APPLE SALAD


8  apples
2  bananas
1/2  c. chopped celery
1/2  c. raisins
1/4  c. coconut
1/2  c. peanuts
1/2  lemon, juiced (optional)
1/4  c. peanut butter
1/4  c. cream
1/2  c. sugar
1/2  c. mayonnaise

      Mix  fruit,  celery,  nuts and lemon juice.  Mix peanut butter,  cream,  sugar and mayonnaise well.  Refrigerate until ready to serve.
