CREAMY SWEET COLESLAW


1 medium green cabbage, shredded (or 8  c. coleslaw mix from the superrmarket produce section)
1 1/2  c. mayonnaise
3/4  c. sweet salad pickles, chopped
1/3  c. sugar
1/3  c. prepared mustard
1  Tbsp. celery seeds
2  Tbsp. cider vinegar
1 1/2  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper

      Stir together all ingredients in a large bowl.  Cover and chill for 1 hour.
