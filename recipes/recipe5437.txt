JELLO SALAD


1 1/2  c. water
1 (3 oz.)  pkg. lime Jell-O
1  Tbsp. mayonnaise
1  c. marshmallows
1 medium  can pineapple (crushed)
1  c. celery, chopped
2  bananas, sliced
1/2  c. nuts, chopped
1/2  c. maraschino cherries, chopped

	Heat water and add lime Jell-O. While hot, add mayonnaise and marshmallows. Let cool. Add pineapple, celery, bananas, nuts and cherries. Pour into a rectangular dish. Chill until firm.
