HOT BEAN AND CHEESE DIP


1 (14.5 oz.)  can diced tomatoes, drained and divided
1/2  tsp. hot pepper sauce
1/4  tsp. ground cumin
1 (16 oz.)  can pinto beans, rinsed and drained
1 (4.5 oz.)  can fat-free refried beans
1 (4.5 oz.)  can chopped green chilies, drained
1/4  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. dried oregano
  cooking spray
3/4  c. (3 oz.) shredded sharp Cheddar cheese

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees;. Combine 1 cup tomatoes with next 7 ingredients (1 cup tomatoes through chilies). Spoon tomato mixture into a 1 1/2-quart casserole dish coated with the cooking spray. Top with cheese. Bake at 350 degrees; for 20 minutes or until cheese is melted. Top with remaining tomatoes. Yield: 4 1/2 cups.
