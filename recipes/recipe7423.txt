CHICKEN BREAST SUPREME


4  large chicken breasts
1/4  c. butter
1  can condensed cream of chicken soup
1 (3 oz.)  can mushrooms, drained
3/4  c. cooking wine
1 (5 oz.)  can water chestnuts, drained and sliced
2  Tbsp. chopped green peppers

      In butter, lightly brown chicken breasts.  Arrange in baking dish.  Combine chicken soup, mushrooms, water chestnuts, green peppers, a pinch of salt, pepper and cooking wine (optional). Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour, covered, and uncovered for 1/2 hour. Serve over cooked rice or noodles.  Serves 8.
