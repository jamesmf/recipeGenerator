HOT DOG CASSEROLE


2  Tbsp. butter
2  Tbsp. flour
1/4  tsp. salt
  pepper
1  c. milk
6  hot dogs
4  to 5 medium potatoes, partially cooked
1/2  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/4  c. sharp cheese, grated
  Italian bread crumbs

      Melt butter in non-stick pan.  Add flour, salt and pepper. Stir until smooth and bubbly.  Stir in milk.  Add thinly sliced hot dogs.  Mix in partially-cooked potatoes, diced.  Add 1/2 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce and grated cheese.
      Place in greased casserole dish.  Top with bread crumbs.  Bake at 375 degrees; for 30 minutes.
