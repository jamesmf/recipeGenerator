BLUEBERRY SURPRISE JELLO SALAD


2 (3 oz.)  pkg. blackberry or raspberry Jell-O
2  c. boiling water
1 (15 oz.)  can blueberries, drained (I use Oregon brand; do not use pie filling)
1 (8 oz.)  can crushed pineapple, drained

      Dissolve Jell-O in boiling water. Drain blueberries and pineapple and measure liquid. Add enough water to make 2 cups. Add to Jell-O. Stir in blueberries and pineapple and pour in 2-quart dish; cover. Put in the refrigerator until firm.
Topping:
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
1/2  c. sugar
1/2  pt. sour cream
1/2  tsp. vanilla
1/2  c. nuts, chopped

      Combine cream cheese, sugar, sour cream and vanilla. Spread over firm Jell-O. Sprinkle with nuts. Serves 10 to 12. Enjoy!
