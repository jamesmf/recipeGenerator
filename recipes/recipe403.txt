WAFFLES AND MAPLE SYRUP


1  c. flour
1/2  c. cornmeal or 1 c. oatmeal
1  tsp. salt
2  tsp. baking powder
1  Tbsp. brown sugar
2  c. milk
2  eggs
2  Tbsp. oil
1  c. diced apples or other fruit
1/2  c. chopped nuts

      Combine flour, cornmeal or oatmeal, salt, baking powder and brown sugar in bowl.  Combine in another bowl the milk, eggs and oil.  Mix and beat with a whip.  Add liquid to dry ingredients and stir until just mixed.  Add apples and chopped nuts.  Bake on a moderately hot, lightly greased waffle iron.
Maple Syrup:
1  c. white corn syrup
1/2  c. water
1/2  c. brown sugar
  maple flavoring and butter flavorings

      Combine first 3 ingredients in a 4-cup glass bowl in microwave and cook on High, boiling, until sugar dissolves, about 3 minutes. Add a few drops of maple flavoring and butter flavoring.  Low-fat and delicious.
