BEEF STROGANOFF II


3  lb. stew meat
1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can golden mushroom soup
1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can cream of mushroom soup
1/2  c. water
1  pkg. of dried onion soup

	Brown beef in skillet.	Do not drain.  Place beef and all ingredients in 13 x 9-inch baking pan.  Mix together thoroughly. Cover and bake at 350 degrees; for 1 1/2 to 2 hours.
