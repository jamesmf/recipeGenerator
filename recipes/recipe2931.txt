BACON CHEESE BALL


8  oz. pkg. Cheddar cheese, shredded
1  lb. cooked bacon, crumbled
1  small onion, grated
1 to 1 1/2  c. mayonnaise
1/2  lemon, juiced

      Mix all ingredients and refrigerate at least overnight. Form into 2 balls. May roll in almonds or pecans if desired. Serve with crackers of choice.
