BAKED ORANGE CHICKEN


1  (6 to 7 lb.) roasting chicken
1  tangerine, quartered
1  onion, quartered
1  tsp. each rosemary and thyme
1/2  c. white wine
  salt and pepper

 
Glaze:
3  Tbsp. each: butter (melted), Dijon mustard and orange marmalade
3  Tbsp. Grand Marnier (orange liqueur)

      Wash and pat dry the chicken. Squeeze juice of the tangerine all over and in the cavity. Put the tangerine inside the cavity with the onion. Tie legs together with string and rub the bird with salt and pepper to taste. Place breast side down in a roasting pan. Sprinkle with rosemary and thyme. Add 1/2 cup of white wine to the bottom of the pan. Bake at 400 degrees; for 45 minutes.
       Quarter 4 peeled baking potatoes and microwave for 10 minutes.
      Glaze: Stir first 3 ingredients and then add the Grand Marnier.
       Take the bird out of the oven and turn breast side up; turn oven down to 350 degrees;. Add potatoes to the roasting pan. Finish baking while basting with Glaze for approximately 1 hour and 15 minutes. (Thermometer should read 190 degrees;.) Enjoy!
