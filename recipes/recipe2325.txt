CORN CASSEROLE


1 (16 oz.)  can whole kernel corn, drained
1 (17 oz.)  can cream-style corn
1 (7 1/2 oz.)  box corn muffin mix (such as Jiffy)
3/4  c. sour cream
1/2  c. milk
1  c. grated Cheddar cheese
1/2  c. margarine
1  egg, slightly beaten

      Mix all of the ingredients.  Pour into a 2-quart greased casserole dish.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 60 minutes.  Let stand 15 minutes before serving.
