OATMEAL CHOCOLATE CHIP COOKIES


1  c. butter
2  c. flour
1  tsp. soda
1  c. sugar
2 1/2  c. blended oatmeal
12  oz. chocolate chips
1  c. brown sugar
1/2  tsp. salt
1  (4 oz.)  Hershey bar
2  eggs
1  tsp. baking powder
1  tsp. vanilla
1 1/2  c. chopped nuts

      Measure oatmeal and blend in a blender to a fine powder.  Cream the butter and both sugars.  Add eggs and vanilla; mix together with flour, oatmeal, salt, baking powder and soda.  Add chocolate chips, Hershey bar (grated) and nuts.  Roll into balls and place 2-inches apart on a cookie sheet.  Bake for 10 minutes at 375 degrees;. Makes 50 cookies.
