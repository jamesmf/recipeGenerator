BARBECUE PORK LOIN


  juice from 2 lemons
1  c. ketchup
8  tsp. brown sugar
1/2  tsp. black pepper
1/2  lb. butter
6  drops Texas Pete
1  c. water (or more, if needed)
  Kraft Hot barbecue sauce
4  tsp. vinegar
  Pork Loin

      Boil pork loin 1 hour or until tender.  Mix other ingredients together in a pot over medium heat.  Heat, but do not boil.  Place pork in a baking dish; pour sauce over pork and cover with foil. Bake at 325 degrees; for 1 hour.
