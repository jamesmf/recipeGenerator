CHOCOLATE COVERED NOUGATS


1  can condensed milk
2  boxes 4x sugar
2  sticks butter
1  tsp. vanilla
1  qt. chopped nuts
2  boxes semi-sweet chocolate squares
1/4  to 1 block paraffin

      Mix condensed milk, 4x sugar, butter, vanilla and nuts; chill thoroughly.  Roll into small balls.  Melt paraffin in a double boiler first.  Add chocolate and melt.  Beat until smooth.  Dip balls into mixture until coated on all sides.  Drop on wax paper.
