CREAMY CHICKEN CASSEROLE


6  c. chopped chicken (cooked)
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  can Cheddar cheese soup
1  (16 oz.) sour cream
  crushed tortilla chips
1 1/2  c. crushed Ritz crackers
1/4  c. melted margarine

      Combine chicken, soups, and sour cream. Cover the bottom of a 9 x 13 inch baking pan with tortilla chips; add chicken mixture over the top. Combine crushed crackers and margarine; sprinkle over chicken mixture. Bake uncovered for 30 minutes at 350 degrees;.
