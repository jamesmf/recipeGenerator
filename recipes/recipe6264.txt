SAUCE MAYONNAISE


3  egg yolks (at room temperature)
1  tsp. lemon juice
1  tsp. dry mustard
1/2  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. white pepper
1 1/2  c. olive oil

      Place egg yolks into warm bowl and whisk vigorously until thick.  Add teaspoon lemon juice along with teaspoon of mustard. Add 1/2 teaspoon of the oil at a time, whisking constantly.  You can whisk in 2 tablespoons of boiling water gradually to make it creamier.  Makes 2 cups.
