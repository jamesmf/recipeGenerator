STRAWBERRY BUTTERMILK SALAD


1 large  pkg. strawberry jello
2  c. buttermilk
1  (9 oz.) container Light Cool Whip
1 (20 oz.)  can crushed pineapple, undrained
1  c. chopped nuts, optional

      Mix jello and pineapple in large saucepan; heat on stove until jello dissolves.  Do not boil.  Let cool; mix buttermilk and cool whip until well mixed.  Add to jello mixture. Pour into 8 by 12 inch oblong dish, and sprinkle nuts on top.  Chill in refrigerator.
