MACARONI-POTATO SALAD


7  potatoes
1 1/2  lb. macaroni
6  hard-boiled eggs
1  c. diced onion
1/4  c. sugar
3/4  qt. Miracle Whip

 
Dressing:
1/2  can milk (evaporated)
  dash of salt
1 1/2  c. celery

      Dice potatoes; cook.  Cook macaroni.  Boil eggs; dice.  Mix these 3 ingredients in large bowl.  Add celery and onion; stir. Beat milk, sugar and salad dressing.  Pour over potatoes and macaroni.
