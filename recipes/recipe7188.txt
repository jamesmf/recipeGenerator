ONE DISH CHICKEN BAKE


1 (16 oz.)  pkg. stuffing mix (for chicken)
4  boneless chicken breast halves
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1/3  c. sour cream or milk

      Mix stuffing mix as directed.  Place chicken in 13 x 9 pan. Mix soup and sour cream.  Spoon stuffing over chicken; add soup mixture.  Bake at 375 degrees; for 35 to 40 minutes.
