MEXICAN STUFFED PEPPERS


4  green bell peppers
1  lb. ground beef
1  large onion, chopped
1  envelope taco seasoning mix
2  c. cooked white rice
1  c. Cheddar cheese

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees;.  Cut tops off bell peppers.  Scoop out seeds and membranes.  Discard.  Heat a large skillet.  Add beef and onion.  Cook about 5 minutes until beef is browned; drain fat.  Add taco seasoning mix to skillet.  Cook about 5 minutes more.  Add rice, mixing well.  Cook for 3 minutes longer.  Scoop beef mixture into peppers.  Place in a baking dish.  Add about 1/4-inch water to dish; cover.  Bake for 20 minutes.  Uncover.  Sprinkle with Cheddar cheese.  Bake, uncovered, for 15 minutes longer.
