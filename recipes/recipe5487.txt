BAVARIAN COLD SLAW


1  medium head cabbage, chopped fine
1  tsp. non-iodized salt
1  c. white vinegar
1 1/2  c. sugar
2  c. celery, chopped finely
2  c. bell peppers, chopped
2  c. carrots, chopped

      Mix salt in cabbage and refrigerate for 1 hour.  Heat sugar and vinegar in saucepan; set aside and cool.  Mix celery, bell peppers and carrots with cabbage.  Pour vinegar and sugar over it and seal tightly.  Refrigerate.  Will keep up to 6 weeks.
