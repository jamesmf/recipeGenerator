BARBECUE SAUCE
(Makes 1 Quart)  

1/2  stick butter
1  c. mustard
1  c. honey
1  c. ketchup
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  c. vinegar
  salt and pepper (black and crushed red pepper) to taste

      Mix until blended well.  Good on pork, chicken and beef.  Also, if you like onions, chop 1/4 cup fine and add to sauce.  If you add onions, cook until onions are tender.
