MARINATED FLANK STEAK


2  lb. flank steak
2/3  c. soy sauce
1  tsp. garlic salt
1/4  c. liquid smoke (optional)
1/3  c. oil
1  tsp. ginger
2  tsp. dry mustard
2  Tbsp. molasses

      Mix marinade ingredients; stir well. Cover punctured steak. Refrigerate for 2 days (yes), turning often. Grill 20 to 30 minutes. Very tender and delicious.
