BREAD AND BUTTER PICKLES


4  lb. cucumbers, sliced
3  onions, sliced
1/2  c. coarse salt
5  c. sugar
5  c. cider vinegar
1 1/2  tsp. turmeric
1 1/2  tsp. celery seed

      Cover cucumbers, onions and salt with cold water and ice cubes.  Let sit about 3 hours.  Do not use metal container.  Mix sugar, vinegar, turmeric and celery seed and simmer 30 minutes. Pour cold water off cucumbers and pack in jars; cover with hot liquid.  Process 10 minutes in hot water bath.
