SAUSAGE RICE CASSEROLE


1  lb. sausage
2  cans cream of chicken soup
1  c. milk
1/2  c. diced celery
1  small onion, diced
3/4  c. raw rice

      Crumble sausage and fry until browned. Pour off all excess grease. Add other ingredients, mixing well. Put in baking dish that has been sprayed with Pam. Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour or until browned on top.
