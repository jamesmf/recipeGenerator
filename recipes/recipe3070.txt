TORTILLA PINWHEELS


1  pkg. flour tortillas
8  oz. sour cream
1  bunch green onions
2  c. shredded cheese
  green olives, chopped
1/2  tsp. garlic salt
1  jar salsa
8  oz. cream cheese
  small can peeled chopped green chilies
  small can black olives, chopped
1/2  tsp. onion salt

      Mix the cream cheese and sour cream together. Than add everything else to mixture. Spread mixture on flour tortillas and roll. Chill overnight in refrigerator. Cut in the morning. When serving, have salsa to dip pinwheels into.
