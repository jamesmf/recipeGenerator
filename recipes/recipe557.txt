SPINACH DIP


1  c. chopped fresh spinach
1  c. low-fat cottage cheese
1/2  c. chopped green onions
1/2  c. lite mayonnaise
1  Tbsp. reduced sodium soy sauce
1  clove garlic, minced
  sweet red pepper strips

      Combine first 6 ingredients in electric blender.  Process until smooth.  Cover and chill.  Garnish with red pepper strips.  Serve with raw vegetables.  Yields 1 2/3 cups.
