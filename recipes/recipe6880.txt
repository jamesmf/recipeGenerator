ROTEL SPICY MEAT LOAF


1 1/2  lb. ground meat
1  egg
1  small onion, chopped fine
3  slices bread, torn into pieces
1 (10 oz.)  can Ro-Tel tomatoes
2  tsp. chili powder
1/4  tsp. black pepper
2  cloves garlic, chopped fine
1/3  c. chili sauce

	Preheat oven to 375 degrees;. Combine first 8 ingredients and spread into ungreased 8 1/2 x 4 1/2 x 2 1/2 pan. Bake for 1 hour. Drain off excess drippings. Spoon chili sauce over the cooked meat loaf and bake for another 15 minutes.
