SOUTHERN POTATO SALAD


5  or 6 medium potatoes, diced
1  onion, diced
1/2  c. celery, diced
1/4  c. bell pepper, diced
  pickles or pickle relish (optional)
2  or 3 hard-boiled eggs

 
Dressing:
3  eggs, well beaten
1/2  c. sugar
1/4  c. flour
1/4  c. vinegar
2  c. milk
1  Tbsp. celery salt
  salt and pepper to taste

      Cook until mixture comes to a good boil, stirring continually. Pour over potatoes and stir.
