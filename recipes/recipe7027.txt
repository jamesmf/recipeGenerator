MEAT LOAF
(Microwave)  

2  lb. ground meat
2  eggs, beaten
1/2  c. catsup
1 1/2  c. (dry) oatmeal
1  pkg. Lipton onion soup mix
1/2  c. Carnation cream
  garlic powder, to taste
1/2  c. catsup
2  tsp. Worcestershire sauce

      Combine all ingredients (except last 2) and shape into a loaf in 2 qt. casserole dish. Cook on high for 20 minutes, rotating every 5 minutes until time is up. Mix the catsup and Worcestershire sauce and spread over top of meat loaf. Heat for 1 minute.
