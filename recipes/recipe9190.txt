TASTY MEAT LOAF

Meat Loaf:
1 1/2 to 2  lb. lean hamburger
1  envelope Lipton onion soup mix
1 1/2  c. bread crumbs
1  c. tomato ketchup
2  Tbsp. worcestershire sauce
  salt and pepper to taste

      Combine all ingredients and make a loaf.
Sauce:
1  c. cold water
1/2  c. ketchup
1/2  c. sugar
2  Tbsp. vinegar

      Mix together and pour over loaf.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour or until done.
