CURRIED CHICKEN BALLS


2  c. cooked chicken, ground
6  Tbsp. chutney, chopped
3  tsp. curry powder
  salt to taste
2  Tbsp. onion, finely chopped
4  Tbsp. mayonnaise
  chopped nuts

      Mix first 5 ingredients.  Add mayonnaise to moisten.  Form small balls and roll in chopped nuts.  Chill overnight.  Makes 50.
