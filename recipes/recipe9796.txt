PARMESAN BAKED CHICKEN
(Low Carb)  

  boneless skinless chicken breasts
1  cup Parmesan cheese
1  large bag Pork rinds
1/4  cup Soy flour
  Italian dressing - usually 1 large bottle

      Choose quantity of chicken you desire.  Soak in Italian dressing in baking pan.  Mix bag of pork rinds, cup of parmesan cheese and 1/4 cup of soy flour together in food processor.  Blend until texture is similar to bread crumbs.  Place mixture in baggie, add chicken and shake.  Place chicken pieces back in baking pan with the Italian dressing.  Bake for 45 minutes at 375 degrees.
