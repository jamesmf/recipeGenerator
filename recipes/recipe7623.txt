BAKED HAMBURGER PATTIES


1 1/2  lb. ground beef
1/2  c. tomato juice
3/4  c. quick oats
1  small onion, chopped
  salt and pepper
1  c. catsup
3  Tbsp. brown sugar
1/2  c. water
2  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
2  Tbsp. vinegar

      Mix  first  5  ingredients  together  well.  Make  into patties  at  least 3-inches in diameter.  Put into shallow pan and cover  with  rest  of  ingredients,  which have been well combined.  Bake  at 300 degrees; for 1 hour.  Turn at least once while baking.
