VEGETABLE DIP


2 8  oz. packages cream cheese
1/3  cup ketchup
3  Tbsp. French dressing
3  Tbsp. milk
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. garlic salt
3  Tbsp. chopped onion
1  tsp. parsley flakes

      Mix cream cheese and ketchup well.  Add rest of ingredients. This is especially good with raw cauliflower and broccoli.
