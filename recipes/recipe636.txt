SUGAR-FREE APPLE PIE


7  to 8 tart apples
2  Tbsp. flour
  dash of salt
8  or 9 packets Sweet 'N Low
1  tsp. cinnamon
1/4  tsp. nutmeg
2  Tbsp. butter
  pastry for 2-crust pie, unbaked

      Pare apples and slice.  Mix flour, salt, sweetener and spices. Add to apples.  Fill 9-inch pastry-lined pie pan.  Dot with butter. Adjust top crust.  Bake in hot 400 degrees; oven for about 50 minutes or until brown and apples are soft.
