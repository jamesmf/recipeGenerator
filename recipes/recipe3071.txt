CHRISTMAS TORTILLA ROLL-UP


1  pkg. Hidden Valley Ranch
2 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
1/3  c. chopped celery
1/4  c. green onion, minced fine
1/2  c. red pepper, chopped
1/4  c. green olives, chopped
  flour tortillas

      Mix ingredients together well. Refrigerate. Spread on tortilla; then roll tortilla up. Slice into 1-inch pieces.
