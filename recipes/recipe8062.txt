BAKED CHICKEN TORTILLA


5  whole chicken breasts
8  flour tortillas
1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can cream of celery soup
1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can cream of chicken soup
1  c. milk
1  c. green chili salsa sauce
1  grated onion
1 small  can chopped olives
1/2  lb. grated cheese (Cheddar)

	Wrap chicken in foil and bake 1 hour in 450 degrees; oven.  Cut into chunks.  Butter Pyrex dish and pour chicken juice into it.	Cut tortillas into 1-inch strips and layer on the bottom of the dish. Mix soups, milk, salsa, onion and olives together.	Layer the chicken pieces on top of tortilla strips and pour 1/2 soup mixture on top of them.  Repeat the layers and cover with cheese. Refrigerate 12 to 18 hours.  Bake in slow 300 degrees; oven for 90 minutes.  Serves 10.
