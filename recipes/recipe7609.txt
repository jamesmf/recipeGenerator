SOUPER MEAT AND POTATOES PIE


1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  lb. ground beef
1/4  c. chopped onion
1  egg
1/4  c. dry bread crumbs or oatmeal
2  Tbsp. parsley
1/4  tsp. salt
  Dash pepper
2  c. mashed potatoes
1/4  c. shredded mild cheese

      Mix thoroughly:  1/2 c. soup, beef, onion, egg, bread crumbs, parsley and seasonings.  Press firmly into a 9-inch pie plate. Bake at 350 degrees; for 25 minutes.  Spoon off fat.  ``Frost'' with potatoes.  Top with remaining soup and cheese.  Bake 10 minutes more until done.  Garnish with cooked sliced bacon if desired. Makes 1 - 9-inch pie.
