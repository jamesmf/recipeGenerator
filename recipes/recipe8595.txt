PIZZA HOTDISH


1  lb. ground beef
1/4  c. chopped green pepper, if desired
2  cans condensed tomato soup
1/2  tsp. garlic salt
1/2  tsp. oregano
1/2  tsp. onion salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
6  oz. cooked noodles
  sliced fresh or canned mushrooms
1/4  lb. grated cheddar cheese
1/4  C. grated parmesan cheese

      Brown meat, add peppers and saut√©, add soup and spices simmer.  Cook noodles per package instructions. Grease 9x13 pan. Pour in noodles, cover with meat sauce, then mushrooms and cheeses.  Bake covered 35 min. at 350 degrees.  Then bake uncovered for 10 min. more. Serves 6.
