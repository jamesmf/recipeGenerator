BEEFY RICE DINNER


1 (6 oz.)  pkg. beef flavored rice mix
1  lb. lean ground beef
1/3  c. chopped celery
1/3  c. chopped green peppers
1/8  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper
1/2  c. cheese

      Prepare rice according to package directions. In a large skillet, cook beef, celery and green pepper until meat is done. Drain. Add rice, salt and pepper. Transfer to a greased 2-quart dish. Sprinkle with cheese. Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees; for 10 to 15 minutes. Yields 4 to 6 servings.
