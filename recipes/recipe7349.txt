CORNED BEEF AND CABBAGE CASSEROLE


5  medium potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced
1  small onion, chopped
4  c. shredded cabbage
1  can cream of celery soup
1 1/2  c. milk
1 (12 oz.)  can corned beef
  salt and pepper to taste

      Lightly grease a 9 x 13 pan.  Layer potatoes.  Cover with chopped onion and shredded cabbage.  Crumble corned beef over this.  Mix cream of celery soup with milk.  Pour over the layers. Bake at 350 degrees; for approximately 1/2 hour.
