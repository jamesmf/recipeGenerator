BEEF SKILLET FIESTA


1  lb. ground beef
1/4  c. diced onions
1  tsp. chili powder
1 (2 oz.)  can whole kernel corn
1 1/4  c. bouillon (use 1 cube and water)
1/2  c. green pepper
1  Tbsp. salad oil
2  tsp. salt
1 (1 lb.)  can tomatoes
1 1/3  c. Minute rice

      Brown meat in oil over high heat in skillet, leaving meat in coarse chunks. Add onions; reduce heat to medium and cook until onion is tender, but not brown. Add seasonings, tomatoes, corn and bouillon; bring to a boil. Stir in green peppers. Bring to a boil again. Stir. Remove from heat. Cover and let stand 5 minutes. Fluff with fork. Makes 4 servings.
