HOT CHICKEN SALAD


2  c. cooked chicken, diced
4  hard boiled eggs, chopped
1  c. celery, thinly sliced
1 1/2  c. cooked rice
1/2  c. slivered almonds
1/2  c. mayonnaise
1/2  tsp. each salt and pepper
2  tsp. lemon juice
1  Tbsp. grated onion
1/2  c. chicken soup

 
Topping:
1/2  c. grated cheese (Tillamook)
2  c. crushed potato chips

	Mix all ingredients together except topping.  Pile lightly and evenly in an 8 x 8-inch casserole dish.  Top with the topping. Bake for 15 to 20 minutes in 450 degrees;  oven.
