CHEESE BALL


3  (8 oz.) cream cheese
1  lb. sharp Cheddar cheese
1 1/2  tsp. seasoned salt
1 1/2  tsp. chopped chives
1  tsp. celery seed
1/2 to 1  tsp. red pepper
2 (4 oz.)  cans chopped ham
1/4  c. chopped green olives
2  Tbsp. prepared mustard
1  tsp. garlic powder
1/2  tsp. salt

      Mix all ingredients and divide. Shape into 2 balls. Roll in chopped nuts, covering entire ball.
