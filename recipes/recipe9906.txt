POTATO PANCAKES


2  eggs
5  - 6 potatoes, grated
1/2  white onion, grated
3 - 4  Tbsp. flour
1/2  tsp. each salt and pepper
1  tsp. baking powder

      Grate potatoes and onions into a bowl.  Add flour, salt, pepper and baking powder.  Beat eggs and pour into potato mixture. Mix well.  Fry like pancakes until golden brown on both sides.
