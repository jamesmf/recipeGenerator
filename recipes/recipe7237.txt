ROUND STEAK ITALIANO
(Crock-Pot Recipe)  

1 1/2  lb. round steak
1/2  tsp. oregano
1 (15 1/2 oz.)  jar spaghetti sauce with mushrooms
1  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
1 (16 oz.)  can whole small onions, drained

      Cut steak into 5 or 6 serving size pieces. Coat with salt, oregano and pepper. In slow-cooking pot, put in meat and onions, then pour spaghetti sauce over the meat. Cover and cook on LOW for 7 to 9 hours or until meat is tender. Turn the control on HIGH for 10 to 15 minutes. Makes 5 or 6 servings.
      Serve with Italian green beans and toasted garlic cheese bread.
