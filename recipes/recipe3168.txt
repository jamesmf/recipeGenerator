GREEN TOMATO RELISH


5  lb. green tomatoes
1/2  lb. hot peppers
1/2  c. vinegar
2  c. sugar
1  lb. onions
1  clove garlic
1  tsp. salt

      Peel tomatoes and cut in pieces; place in a large pan. Chop peppers and garlic; cut onions in long slices. Combine all ingredients. Cover and heat over low heat until tender. Put in hot sterilized jars and seal immediately.
