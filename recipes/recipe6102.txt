ANDREA'S CORN SALAD


1  cucumber
1  yellow bell pepper
1  green bell pepper
1  tomato
1  purple onion
45  oz. (3 cans) white Shoepeg corn, drained
1/2  tsp. dry mustard
1/2  tsp. celery salt
1/2  tsp. black pepper
1  c. sour cream
1  c. mayonnaise

      Remove seeds and dice (about the same size as corn kernels); cucumber, bell peppers, tomato and onion.
       In a bowl, combine corn (well drained), diced vegetables, dry mustard, celery salt and black pepper.  Mix thoroughly.  Add sour cream and mayonnaise.  Stir and refrigerate overnight.
