DEVILED EGGS


12  Hard boiled eggs
4  Tbsp. mayonnaise
1  tsp. mustard
1  tsp. white vinegar
1  Tbsp. pickle relish
  Dash of salt and pepper

      Boil eggs 10 minutes. Soak boiled eggs in cold water for 15-20 minutes. Peel eggs and cut lengthwise. Take yolks and put into large bowl. Mix remaining ingredients well. Fill mixture into egg halves. Sprinkle paprika for a dash of color. Refrigerate until served.
