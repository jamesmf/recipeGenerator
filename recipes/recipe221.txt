CHEWS ME!


36  vanilla caramels (10 oz.)
3  Tbsp. light cream
1  c. corn flakes
2  c. crisp rice cereal
1  c. flaked coconut
1/2  c. chopped California walnuts
1/2  c. raisins

      Place 36 vanilla caramels (10 ounces) and 3 tablespoons light cream in top of a double boiler over simmering water.  Beat until caramels melt.  Stir occasionally.  Toss together 1 cup corn flakes, 2 cups crisp rice cereal, 1 cup flaked coconut, 1/2 cup chopped California walnuts, and 1/2 cup raisins.  Pour caramel mixture over dry mixture and mix thoroughly.  With buttered fingers, press rounded tablespoons of mixture lightly into balls. Place on waxed paper.  Makes about 4 dozen confection cookies.
