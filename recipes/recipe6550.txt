CHICKEN BREAST WITH WHITE WINE


6  to 8 chicken breasts
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1 (16 oz.)  carton sour cream
1  c. white cooking wine
  paprika
  yellow rice

      Salt and pepper chicken breasts. Place in large casserole. Mix cream of mushroom soup, sour cream and cooking wine. Pour the mixture over the chicken. Refrigerate overnight. When ready to cook, sprinkle generously with paprika. Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 1/2 hours. Do not cover or turn. Serve over yellow rice.
