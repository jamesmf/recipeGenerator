MARINATED BRISKET


1  well trimmed brisket

 
Marinade:
1  c. Worcestershire sauce
1  c. red wine
  juice of 2 limes
1  c. chopped parsley
1  tsp. onion salt
1  Tbsp. salt
1  Tbsp. pepper
1  can beef broth
2  cloves garlic, minced
2 to 3  Tbsp. unsalted butter or margarine

      Combine all ingredients for marinade.  Pour over the brisket, which has been placed in a glass container; cover.  Let set in refrigerator overnight.
      Place brisket, fat side down, on grill; sear on both sides. Wrap in foil; put back on grill or smoker and cook slowly for about 6 1/2 hours.
