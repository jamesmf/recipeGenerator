SMOTHERED PORK CHOPS


5  pork chops (1/2-inch thick)
1  packet Shake 'n Bake original coating mix
2  large onions, thickly sliced
1  Tbsp. oil
1 (12 oz.)  jar pork gravy

      Preheat oven to 375 degrees;.  Coat chops with mix as directed on package.  In 13 x 9 pan, place single layer.  Toss onions with oil; place over chops.  Bake 30 minutes or until cooked through. Pour gravy over chops; cover.  Bake another 15 minutes or until thoroughly cooked.
