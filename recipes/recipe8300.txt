PORK CHOP AND STUFFING CASSEROLE


6  pork chops, cut 3/4-inch thick
1/4  c. lard or drippings
1 1/2  tsp. salt
2  medium onions, chopped
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. herb seasoned stuffing mix
1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can cream of celery soup (condensed)
1/2  c. water
2  apples, chopped (about 2 c.)
3  c. zucchini squash, chopped

	Lightly brown chops in lard or drippings in large glass-ceramic skillet. Remove chops from skillet and sprinkle with 1/2 teaspoon salt. Cook onion in drippings in a skillet 3 to 5 minutes. Stir in stuffing mix and sprinkle with 1 teaspoon salt. Combine soup with water. Add chopped apple, zucchini and diluted soup to stuffing mixture, stirring lightly to combine. Place in a large glass-ceramic open roaster. Place pork chops on top of stuffing. Cover pan tightly with foil. Bake in a slow oven at 325 degrees; for 45 minutes or until chops are done. Yield: 6 servings.
	Smoked pork chops can be used. In this case do not brown chops.
