POTATO SOUP


6  to 8 potatoes, cut or diced
3  carrots, shredded
6  celery stalks, diced
2  qt. water
2  onions, chopped
6  Tbsp. butter or margarine
1  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper
  instant potato flakes

	Cook potatoes, carrots, celery and onions in water until tender. Add milk, butter and potato flakes until soup is of desired thickness.
