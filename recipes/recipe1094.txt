CHOCOLATE SYRUP


1/3  c. dry cocoa
1 1/4  c. cold water
1/4  tsp. salt
2  tsp. vanilla
3  tsp. liquid Sucaryl

      Combine cocoa, water and salt in saucepan.  Mix until smooth. Bring to a boil and simmer gently until smooth and thick.  Allow to cool 10 minutes.  Add vanilla and sweetener.  Store covered in refrigerator.  Stir well before using.  One tablespoon equals 9 calories.
