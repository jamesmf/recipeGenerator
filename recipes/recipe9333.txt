HAM AND CHICKEN SUPREME


2  c. ham, cooked
1 1/2  c. chicken, cooked
2  c. spaghetti
1 1/2  c. sour cream
1  c. cottage cheese
1  c. green onions, chopped
1/2  c. (1 stick) butter, melted
8  oz. mushrooms, sliced
  salt (to taste)
  pepper (to taste)
1 1/2  c. Cheddar cheese, shredded

      Cut ham and chicken into bite-size pieces.
      Break spaghetti into pieces, cook according to package directions and drain.  Add sour cream and cottage cheese.  Set mixture aside.
      Saut√© onion in butter.  Add mushrooms, chicken, ham, salt and pepper.  Heat thoroughly.  Stir in spaghetti mixture.  Put in a greased 2-quart casserole dish and sprinkle with cheese.
      Bake in 350 degrees; oven for 20 to 25 minutes.
