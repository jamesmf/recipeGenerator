ORIENTAL CHICKEN IN PUFF PASTRY


1  pkg. frozen broccoli spears
1/4  c. plus 2 Tbsp. Jiffy baking mix
1  tsp. ground ginger
2  cans chicken broth
1/4  c. margarine
1/2  tsp. garlic powder
3  Tbsp. lemon juice
2  c. cooked and cut up chicken
1  c. (8 oz.) sliced water chestnuts, drained
1  c. (4 oz.) mushroom pieces
1 (2 oz.)  jar diced pimentos, drained
1  box Pepperidge Farm puff pastry shells

      Prepare puffed pastry according to box.  Cook broccoli as directed.  Drain and cut up; set aside.  Melt margarine in 3-quart saucepan and stir in baking mix, ginger and garlic.  Cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until smooth and bubbly. Remove from heat; stir in broth and lemon juice.  Heat to boiling, stirring constantly.  Boil and stir 1 minute; reduce heat.  Stir in broccoli and remaining ingredients. Heat through.  Fill puff pastries with chicken mixture. Serve with a side dish of rice. Makes 6 servings.
      Colorful and easy to prepare.
