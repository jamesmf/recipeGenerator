SOURDOUGH ROLLS


1  pkg. yeast
1  c. warm water
3/4  c. oil
1  egg, beaten
1/4  c. sugar
1 3/4  c. buttermilk
6  c. (2 lb.) self-rising flour

      Dissolve yeast in warm water; add oil, egg, sugar, buttermilk and flour.  Mix well.  Cover and refrigerate at least 2 hours. Bake in muffin tins or roll out and cut and bake on cookie sheet at 450 degrees; for approximately 20 minutes.  Oil tops before baking. Mixture will keep two weeks in refrigerator.
