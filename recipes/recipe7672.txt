MEAT LOAF


3/4  c. dry bread crumbs
1/2  c. milk
1 1/2  lb. hamburger
1/2  lb. sausage
2  eggs, beaten
1/2  c. chopped onion
1/4  c. chopped green pepper
1 1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper

      Soak crumbs in milk; add  remaining ingredients and mix well. Pack into 8 1/2 x 4 1/2 x 2 1/2-inch loaf pan to shape. Invert on shallow baking pan.  Score diagonally with handle of wooden spoon. Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour.  Spread ketchup on top and bake 15 more minutes.
