TACO SOUP


2  lb. lean ground beef
1  onion, chopped
3 4  oz. cans chopped green chilies
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. pepper
1  can pinto beans
1  can Ranch style beans
1  can kidney beans
1 14 1/2  oz. can hominy or corn
3 14 1/2  oz. cans stewed tomatoes
1  pkg. taco seasoning mix
1  pkg. Ranch dressing mix
1 1/2  c. water
  shredded cheese, optional
  tortilla chips, optional

      In large Dutch oven, brown meat and onion.  Add all remaining ingredients, except for the last two.  Bring to a boil, reduce and simmer for 30 minutes.  Top with cheese and serve with chips. Freezes well.
