POTATO SALAD
(Patatas Alinadas)  

4  or 5 medium potatoes
1  green pepper
1  large tomato
5  spring onions
4  Tbsp. oil
4  Tbsp. vinegar
  salt to taste
2  boiled eggs

      Boil unpeeled potatoes (whole) until soft.  Boil eggs until hard-boiled.  Put in refrigerator until cool.  Peel potatoes and dice.  Add diced green pepper, tomato, chopped onions and chopped egg.  Add oil, vinegar and salt.  Serve immediately.
