APPLESAUCE COOKIES


1 (2-layer-size)  pkg. spice cake mix
1  c. raisins
1/2  c. cooking oil
1/2  c. applesauce
1  egg

      In large bowl, combine all ingredients; beat at medium speed of electric mixer for 1 minute.  Drop from teaspoon onto ungreased cookie sheet; bake at 350 degrees; for 12 to 15 minutes.
