BLT BITES


16  to 20 cherry tomatoes
1  lb. bacon, cooked and crumbled
1/2  c. mayonnaise or salad dressing
1/3  c. chopped green onion
3  Tbsp. parmesan cheese
2  Tbsp. snipped fresh parsley

      Cut a thin slice off of each tomato top.  Scoop out and discard pulp.  Invert the tomatoes on a paper towel to drain.  In a small bowl, combine the remaining ingredients; mix well.  Spoon into tomatoes.  Refrigerate for several hours.  Yield: 16 to 20 servings.
