DILL PICKLES


1  c. pickling salt (rock or ice cream)
3  qt. water
1  qt. vinegar
2  fresh heads dill
2  toes garlic
1/4  tsp. alum
1  large dried red chili

      Wash and clean cucumbers. Soak in ice water overnight. Pack in quart jars. Mix the first 3 ingredients. Bring to a boil. Next, add to cucumbers in jar the next 4 ingredients. Cover with vinegar mixture. Seal and put in a water bath for 5 minutes.
