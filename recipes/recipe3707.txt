CHEESE BALL


1 sm.  can Pineapple, crushed
2  lge. pkgs. Cream Cheese
  2-3 tbsp. Bell Pepper, chopped
1/2 sm.  can Black Olives, chopped
1/2 sm.  can Mushrooms, chopped
1/2 - 3/4  c. Ham, chopped
  Seasoning Salt
2  c. Pecans, chopped (reserve 1 c.)

      Mix all ingredients except 1 c. pecans.  Make into a ball. Roll in pecans.
      Makes two balls.
