DORITO CASSEROLE


1  lb. ground meat
1  onion, chopped
  small amount of oil or butter
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1 (6 oz.)  pkg. Doritos chips
1 small  can evaporated milk
1 (4 oz.)  can green chilies
8  slices American cheese

      Brown ground meat and onion in a small amount of oil; drain. Add cream of mushroom soup, milk, and green chilies; mix well. Add Doritos and mix together. Pour into a baking dish and lay slices of cheese over top. Bake at 350 degrees; for about 25 minutes.
