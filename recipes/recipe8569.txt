BAKED CHICKEN BREASTS


6  Chicken Breasts, skinned and deboned
  Salt
  Pepper
  Oleo, melted
  Potato Chips, melted

      Soak chicken in salt water for a while.  Remove from water and dry with paper towels.  Place breasts on waxed paper.  Salt and pepper on each side.  Dip in melted butter and then roll in potato chips.  Place in foil lined baking pan.  Cover with foil and seal.  Bake in 350 degree oven for one hour or more depending on thickness of breasts.
