CHRISTMAS GINGERBREAD COOKIES


1  c. shortening
1  c. light brown sugar
3  eggs, well beaten
1 1/2  c. molasses
6  c. bread flour
1 1/2  Tbsp. ginger
2 1/4  tsp. salt
1  tsp. cinnamon
1 1/2  tsp. soda

      Cream shortening and sugar; add eggs and molasses.  Stir dry ingredients together and add.  Chill.  Roll out not too thin.  Bake at 350 degrees; until dry, not crisp.  Makes enough dough for many shaped cookies.  Frost, if desired.
