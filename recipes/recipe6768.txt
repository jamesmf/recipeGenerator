CHICKEN CRESCENT ALMANDINE


3  c. cubed chicken, cooked
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  can water chestnuts
1 (4 oz.)  can mushrooms
1/2  c. chopped celery
1/2  c. chopped onion
2/3  c. mayonnaise
1/2  c. sour cream
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. crescent rolls
2/3  c. shredded Swiss cheese
1/2  c. slivered almonds
3  Tbsp. melted butter

      Mix the first 8 ingredients and cook until hot and bubbly. Put in ungreased 8 x 13-inch pan.
       Put the crescent rolls on top of chicken mixture. Mix together the Swiss cheese, almonds and melted butter. Put on top of rolls. Bake at 375 degrees; for 20 to 25 minutes. Says it will serve 12.
