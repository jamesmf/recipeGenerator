WARM CHOCOLATE RAISIN SAUCE


1 (1 oz.)  sq. unsweetened chocolate
12  regular marshmallows
1/4  c. water
1/3  c. raisins

      Combine all ingredients in heavy saucepan.  Cook over medium heat until marshmallows and chocolate are melted.  Blend well. Serve warm over ice cream.  Makes 3/4 cup.
