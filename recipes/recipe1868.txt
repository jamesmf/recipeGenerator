SALSA DE BARBACOA
(Barbecue Sauce)  

1  small onion, minced
1  clove garlic, minced
1/2  c. fat
1 1/2  tsp. dry mustard
1 1/2  tsp. salt
2  Tbsp. chili paste
2  c. strained tomatoes
6  Tbsp. vinegar
1/2  c. water
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce

      Cook onions and garlic in fat until tender; add remaining ingredients and boil for 5 minutes or until thick.  Pour over broiled or roasted meat, or use as sauce for reheating cold meat. Serve very hot.
