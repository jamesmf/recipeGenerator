SHRIMP AND CHEESE CASSEROLE


3  beaten eggs
1  can Cheddar cheese soup
1 1/2  c. milk
6  slices buttered bread
8  oz. sharp cheese, sliced
2 (5 oz.)  cans medium shrimp*

      Mix together the eggs, soup and milk.  Place 2 buttered slices of bread at the bottom of a greased 2-quart casserole.  Lay slice of cheese on each, then half of the shrimp.  Add a dash of salt and pepper.  Make another similar layer, then end with bread and cheese.  Pour soup mixture over all.  Bake at 350 degrees; about 40 minutes until brown and puffy.
      *Tuna or chicken can also be used.
