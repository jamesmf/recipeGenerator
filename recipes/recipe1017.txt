TEXAS STEW


2  lb. tenderloin tips
  salt, pepper and flour to coat meat
2  Tbsp. olive oil
1  c. beef stock
1  bay leaf
1  clove garlic, chopped
1  pinch of rosemary
1  bell pepper
1/2  c. butter
6  small onions
2  tomatoes
2  potatoes
1  tsp. parsley
2  c. red wine

      Cut meat into 1 1/2 inch cubes; season with flour.  Heat olive oil in deep iron skillet or Dutch oven.  Brown meat slowly; add other ingredients.  Cover tightly, turn meat often and simmer for 10 minutes.
