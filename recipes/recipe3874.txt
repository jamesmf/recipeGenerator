LAYERED TEX-MEX DIP



1 large  can refried beans
  salsa or picante sauce
1/2  c. mayonnaise
1  c. sour cream
1  pkg. taco seasoning
1  can sliced black olives
  shredded lettuce
  chopped green onions (optional)
  chopped tomatoes
  shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  shredded Cheddar cheese

      Mix beans with 1/2 cup sour cream. Put them as the bottom layer in your bowl. Mix mayonnaise, sour cream and taco seasoning, then layer over the bean dip. Add a layer of lettuce, then tomatoes, onions and black olives. Add layer of salsa or picante sauce. Top with shredded Monterey Jack cheese. Serve with chips.
