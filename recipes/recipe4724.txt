TACO SOUP


1  lb. Healthy Choice ground beef, cooked, drained and rinsed
1  small onion, chopped
1 (4 oz.)  can chopped green chiles
1  tsp. salt (optional)
1  pkg. lite taco seasoning
1  pkg. lite Ranch dressing mix
1 (14 1/2 oz.)  can hominy (undrained)
3 (14 1/2 oz.)  cans stewed tomatoes
1 (15 oz.)  can kidney beans (undrained)
1 (15 oz.)  can pinto beans (undrained)
1 1/2  c. water

      Mix cooked beef and remaining ingredients in a large pan. Simmer about 30 minutes. Serves 15.
