CHUCK WAGON COOKIES


1/2  c. butter
1/2  c. sugar
1  egg, beaten
1/2  tsp. vanilla
1/2  c. chunky peanut butter
1 1/2  c. flour
1  tsp. soda
1/2  tsp. salt
1  apple, pared and grated

      Cream butter and sugar; add lightly beaten egg and vanilla. Stir in peanut butter.  Sift dry ingredients and add to mixture. Add grated apple.  Form into balls and place on greased cookie sheet.  Press flat with fork.  Bake in 375 degrees; oven for 10 to 12 minutes.
