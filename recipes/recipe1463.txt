TURKEY BURGERS ON THE GRILL


1  lb. ground uncooked turkey
1/4  c. dry bread crumbs
2  Tbsp. finely minced onion
1/2  tsp. ground sage
1  tsp. prepared horseradish (more horseradish can be used)

      Mix ingredients and shape into patties.  This can be used with hamburger or can be made into a loaf.
