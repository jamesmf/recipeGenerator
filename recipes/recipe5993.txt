ORANGE JELLO DELIGHT


2 small  boxes orange jello
3  c. boiling water
1- 6  oz. can frozen orange juice
20  oz. can crushed pineapple, drained
2  cans mandarin oranges, drained
1 small  box instant lemon pudding or pie filling
1  small tub of cool whip
  cheddar cheese, grated

      Add 3 c. boiling water to jello; mix well.  Add frozen orange juice concentrate and drained pineapple and mandarins.  Mix and refrigerate until set.  Mix (dry) pudding with cool whip and spread on top of set jello.  Sprinkle with grated cheddar cheese.
