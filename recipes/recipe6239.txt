COLD CHICKEN SOUP


1  c. cream of chicken soup
2  c. homemade chicken broth
1  pt. light sour cream
1  tsp. curry powder

      Blend in blender.  Cool in refrigerator. Great summer soup!
      To make broth:  Poach 3 or 4 pounds of chicken in about 1 to 2 inches of water with onion, bay leaf and carrot.
