KRAUT SALAD


1 (No. 303)  can kraut, drained well
1 small  can pimento, chopped
2  c. celery, chopped
1  medium green pepper, chopped
1 1/4  c. vinegar
3/4  c. sugar or sugar substitute
  onion flakes (as desired)

      Heat vinegar and sugar to boil; pour over mixture of other ingredients. Store in refrigerator.
