HAMBURGER STROGANOFF WITH EGG NOODLES


1/2  c. minced onion
1  clove garlic, minced
1/4  c. butter
1  lb. ground beef
2  Tbsp. flour
1  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
8  oz. can sliced mushrooms with liquid
10 1/2  oz. can cream of mushroom soup, diluted with 1 can water
1  c. sour cream
2  Tbsp. minced parsley
1/4  c. sherry (optional)
12  oz. pkg. egg noodles

      saut√© onion and garlic in butter over medium heat.  Stir in meat and brown.  Add flour, salt, pepper and mushrooms.  Cook 5 minutes.  Combine diluted soup with sour cream.  Stir in meat mixture; add parsley and sherry (optional) and simmer 10 minutes. Serve over hot, drained egg noodles that have been cooked according to directions on the package.  Serves 4 to 6.
