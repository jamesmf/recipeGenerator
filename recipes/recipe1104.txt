SAUERKRAUT AND PORK CHOPS


4  lean chops
2  c. sauerkraut, drained
1  small apple, sliced

      Brown chops in skillet.  Mix sauerkraut with meat juices in skillet.  Layer 1/2 sauerkraut and apples in casserole.  Arrange the pork chops over the top.  Layer remaining sauerkraut and apples.  Cover and bake 1 1/2 hours at 350 degrees;.
      One chop and 1/2 cup kraut per serving; 2 meat exchanges, 1 vegetable exchange and 1/2 fruit exchange.  Contains 151 calories.
