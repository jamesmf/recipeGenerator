SWEDISH MEAT BALLS


2  lbs. Ground Beef
1  c. Bread Crumbs
3  tbsp. dehydrated Onions
  Garlic Powder
1  Egg
  Salt & Pepper

 
Sauce:
2  c. hot Water
4  tbsp. Lemon Juice
1/2  tsp. chili powder
1  c. Barbecue sauce
1  c. Ketchup
1/2  tsp. Paprika
1/2  c. Brown Sugar

      Mix ground beef, bread crumbs, onion, garlic powder, egg, salt & pepper and form into balls.
      Combine water, sugar and lemon juice in large saucepan.  Let come to a boil.  Add meat balls and boil for 10 minutes.  Add remaining ingredients and simmer for 30 to 40 minutes.  Serve meatballs in sauce.
