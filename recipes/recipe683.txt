ITALIAN WEDDING SOUP


4  chicken breasts, skinned
3  qt. water
  salt to taste
1/2  tsp. pepper
1  medium chopped onion
1/2  c. celery, diced
1  c. carrots, diced
1  pkg. frozen spinach
3/4  c. acini de pepe, cooked
1 small  can mashed tomatoes
  grated Parmesan cheese
1  Tbsp. parsley

      Wash chicken; place in large pot with water and boil.  Remove foam that forms.  Add salt, pepper, onion, celery and carrots. Cook 2 hours or until meat is tender.  Remove chicken and shred. Add spinach and already cooked pasta.  Add shredded chicken and tomatoes.  Cook 1/2 hour longer.  More water may be added if necessary.  Add cheese, garnish with parsley and serve while hot.
