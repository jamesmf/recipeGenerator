CHICKEN ROLL-UPS


1 (3 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
2  Tbsp. milk
1  Tbsp. onion, grated (optional)
2  c. cooked chicken
1  pkg. refrigerated crescent rolls
3  Tbsp. margarine, melted

	In a large bowl, mix cream cheese and milk until smooth. Stir in onion, if wanted, and chicken; mix well. Place 1/4 cup chicken mixture in each roll. Fold edges of roll around filling. Brush tops with melted margarine and place on an ungreased cookie sheet. Bake in a preheated oven at 350 degrees; for 20 to 25 minutes.
