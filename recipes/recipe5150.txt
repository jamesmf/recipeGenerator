EGG SALAD


1 (8 oz.)  pkg. Philadelphia Cream Cheese, room temperature
4  Tbsp. mayonnaise
1  small onion
5  hard boiled eggs
  Salt and pepper to taste
  Paprika

      Mash eggs.  Add all other ingredients and mix well.  Sprinkle paprika on top.  For a dip, use more mayonnaise to make thinner. This is real good on wheat thin crackers!
