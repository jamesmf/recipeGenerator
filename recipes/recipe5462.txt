VIRGINIA'S BAKED APPLES


2  c. sugar
1  c. water
1/4  stick oleo
3  lb. apples (Golden Delicious)

      Boil syrup of first 3 ingredients.  Peel, core and halve apples.  Drop in boiling syrup until tender (few at a time).  Place in baking dish.  Sprinkle with cinnamon and bake in moderate oven with remainder of syrup in pan, until done!
