MEAT LOAF


2  lb. ground beef
1/2  lb. ground pork
1/2  c. chopped onions
2  Tbsp. chopped bell pepper
1 1/2  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper
2  eggs, beaten
1 3/4  c. milk
3/4  c. rolled oats
1  c. water (around loaf while cooking)

      Beat eggs.  Add salt, pepper, chopped onion, chopped bell pepper, rolled oats and milk.  Mix well.  Add beef and pork.  The secret of a meat loaf is to have it very moist, hardly holds together in pan.  Shape meat into a loaf.  Bake in a heavy pan. Small amount of water (1 cup) around the meat loaf.  Baste several times during cooking period.  Bake in 375 degrees; oven for one hour or more, if needed.
