ZESTY DRESSING


1/2  c. tomato juice
1/4  c. chopped onion
3  Tbsp. red wine vinegar (garlic flavored)
1  tsp. sugar
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. oregano

      In covered blender container, blend at medium speed until smooth and slightly thickened.  Stir before serving.  Makes one cup.  Contains 5 calories per tablespoon.
