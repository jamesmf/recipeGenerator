PEPPER STEAK


1 1/2  lb. round steak
1/2  c. flour
1  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
1/3  c. salad oil
8  oz. can tomato sauce
1  c. water
1/2  c. chopped onion
1 1/2  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1 small  clove garlic, minced
1  beef bouillon cube
1  green pepper, sliced
  cooked rice (6 servings)

      Combine flour, salt and pepper on a plate. Cut steak in 1/2-inch strips. Coat thoroughly with mixture and brown in oil in a large skillet. Add tomato sauce, water, onion, garlic and bouillon cube. Cover and simmer for 1 1/2 hours. Uncover; stir in Worcestershire sauce and green pepper. Simmer 10 to 15 minutes. Serve over cooked rice.
