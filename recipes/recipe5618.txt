ANGEL FOOD SALAD


1  angel food cake, baked
6  oz. pkg. raspberry or strawberry Jell-O
1  C. boiling water
2  C. cold water
1  lrg. container Cool Whip
1  box frozen raspberries or strawberries

      Break up the cake into 1-inch pieces.  Set aside.  Mix Jell-O with boiling water, when dissolved, stir in cold water.  Add fruit and Cool Whip.  Pour over cake.
      Chill until firm.
