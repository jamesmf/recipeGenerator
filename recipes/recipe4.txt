CHOCOLATE ICE CREAM PIE

Crust:
1  c. flour
1  stick margarine
1/4  c. brown sugar
1/2  c. crushed pecans

 
Filling:
1  pkg. instant chocolate pudding
1  c. milk
1  pt. vanilla ice cream

      Crust:  Combine all ingredients.  Press into pie tin.  Bake 10 minutes at 400 degrees;.  (Browns quickly; watch closely.)
      Filling:  Add pudding mix to milk and beat.  Add ice cream. Beat about one more minute.  Pour into crust and refrigerate.
