POTATO SALAD


4  lb. Red Bliss potatoes
1 1/2  c. mayonnaise
3  Tbsp. vinegar
1  Tbsp. sugar
1  medium onion
  salt and pepper to taste

      Boil potatoes in their jackets until done.  Peel while still hot; let cool.  Slice in thin round slices.  Mix in mayonnaise, vinegar and sugar.  Grate onion and add with salt and pepper.  Mix again.  Chill thoroughly for 3 hours.
