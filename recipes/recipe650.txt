CHOCOLATE CHERRY ANGEL CAKE


8  large egg whites
3/4  c. sugar
1/4  tsp. almond extract
1/2  c. flour
1/4  c. cocoa
1/3  c. low sugar cherry spread (jam)
1/4  c. whipped topping
1 (8 oz.)  can pitted dark cherries, drained

      Heat oven to 375 degrees;.  At high speed, beat egg whites until soft peaks form.  Gradually add sugar (one tablespoon at a time), until stiff and glossy.  Add almond extract.  In a small bowl mix flour and cocoa.  Sprinkle over egg whites, half at a time.  Using rubber spatula, gently fold in.  Spoon into 8-inch tube pan.  Bake 25 minutes.  Invert cake.  Cool in pan.  Remove.  Cut into 3 layers. Spread with jam.  Frost with whipped topping and cherries.  Makes 16 servings.  Contains 84 calories per serving.
