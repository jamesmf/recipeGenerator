CINDY'S VERSION RED LOBSTER CHEESE BISCUITS


2  c. self-rising flour
1/4  tsp. cayenne pepper
1/4  tsp. dry mustard
6 to 8  Tbsp. butter flavor shortening
1  c. grated sharp Cheddar cheese
  about 1 c. milk
  melted butter
  garlic powder or garlic salt

      Mix flour, cayenne pepper and mustard in a large bowl.  Cut in shortening and fold in cheese.  Add enough milk to make a batter stiff enough to drop.  Drop biscuits on greased cookie sheet. Brush tops with melted butter and sprinkle with garlic powder/salt. Bake at 450 degrees; for 12 to 15 minutes.
