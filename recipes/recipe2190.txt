GRANDMOTHER'S CORN RELISH


1  qt. onion
1  qt. tomatoes
1  qt. sugar
1  qt. vinegar
1/4 to 1/2  c. salt
12  ears corn, cut from cob
  hot pepper

      Peel and chop the onions and tomatoes.  Cut corn from cob.  Mix all ingredients and cook slowly until mixture thickens, stirring constantly.  Do not let it stick.  Put in hot jars and seal.
