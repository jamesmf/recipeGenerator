PECAN FRITTERS


1 (14 oz.)  pkg. caramels
2  c. pecan halves
1  (24 oz.)  Hershey candy bar
2  Tbsp. cream
1/3  block paraffin wax

      Melt caramels in cream; stir in pecans.  Drop in small patties on wax paper.  Let set until firm.  Melt candy bar with paraffin wax.  Dip patties in melted chocolate.
