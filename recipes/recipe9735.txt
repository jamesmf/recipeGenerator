REALLY GOOD HOT BEEF


6  lb. chuck roast
2  cans cream of mushroom soup
1  envelope Lipton dry onion soup mix
1  can golden mushroom soup mix
  salt and pepper to taste
1/2 to 3/4 soup  can water

      Put roast in a 9 x 13-inch pan. Mix soups and water; pour over meat. Cover tight with foil and bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour per pound. Pull with fork when done and serve on buns.
       Can easily cut recipe in half or freeze half for a later meal.
