ORIENTAL BROCCOLI SLAW

Slaw Mixture:
1  pkg. broccoli cole slaw mix, shredded
3  green onions or 1 onion, chopped
1  can water chestnuts or almonds, cut up
2  pkg. beef Ramen noodles, crushed
1/2  c. sunflower seed

      Mix all ingredients in large bowl.
Dressing:
1/2  c. oil
1/4  c. vinegar
1/2  c. sugar
  Ramen seasoning pkgs.

      Mix and pour over slaw; let marinate.  Chill until ready to serve.
