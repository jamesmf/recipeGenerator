PIZZA HOT DISH


1  lb. hamburger or sausage or half and half
1  lb. cooked spiral noodles (half cooked)
4  oz. pepperoni
  small can drained mushrooms
1/2  green pepper, chopped
1  onion, chopped
1/2  c. sliced green olives
1/2  c. sliced black olives
  salt and pepper to taste
1  can pizza sauce
1/2 to 1  can Hunt's 4 cheese spaghetti sauce
16  oz. shredded Mozzarella cheese

      Brown meat with onion, green pepper and seasons. Drain off grease. Mix in pepperoni, olives, mushrooms and sauce. Heat until hot. Add to 9 x 13 greased pan with noodles; stir. Top with Mozzarella cheese. Sprinkle with parsley (optional). Bake until cheese is browned at 350 degrees; for 30 to 45 minutes.
