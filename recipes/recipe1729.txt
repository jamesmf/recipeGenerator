SEMSEMIA
(Sesame Cookies)  

1  lb. sesame seeds
8  oz. honey
3  Tbsp. peanut butter
3  Tbsp. sugar
1  Tbsp. rose water
1  tsp. cardamom
1 1/2  c. walnuts, chopped

      Brown sesame seeds slightly in the oven or on the stove top. Set aside.  Mix honey, peanut butter, sugar, rose water and cardamom in a saucepan.  Cook on low heat until it bubbles.  Stir in the sesame seeds and the walnuts.  Spread in a greased 9 x 14-inch (or similar size) pan to cool.  To serve, cut in diamond shapes.
