BEEF CREAM CHEESE SPREAD


1  jar chipped beef, wash and drain out salt
1 small  can of black olives, chopped
3 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese, room temperature
1  Tbsp. onion powder
1 small  can of mushrooms, chopped

	Chop the chipped beef and mix with all the ingredients. Chill and serve with assorted crackers.
