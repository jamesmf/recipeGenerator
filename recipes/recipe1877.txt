STUFFED GREEN PEPPERS AND SPANISH RICE


2  lb. ground sirloin
6  medium green peppers
1 1/2  c. rice, uncooked
1  egg
1/2  onion, chopped
1  large carrot, chopped
1 large  can tomatoes, chopped
1  tsp. paprika
  salt and pepper to taste

      Parboil green peppers; remove and keep water.  Mix together ground sirloin, 1/2 cup rice (uncooked), egg, salt and pepper. Stuff in peppers.  While stuffing peppers, have cooking in the water the following:  onion, carrot, tomatoes, salt, pepper and paprika.  After this has been cooking about 15 minutes, add peppers.  Cover with water if needed and cook 40 minutes.  Add the remaining 1 cup rice and cook 20 minutes.
