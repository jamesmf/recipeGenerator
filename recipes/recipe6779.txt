PARIS MEATBALLS


1 (13 oz.)  can evaporated milk
3  lb. hamburger
2  c. oatmeal
2  eggs
1  c. chopped onion
1/2  tsp. garlic powder
2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. chili powder

 
Sauce:
2  c. catsup
1/2  c. chopped onion
2  Tbsp. liquid smoke
1  c. brown sugar
1/2  tsp. garlic powder

      Mix and shape into balls (size of walnut). Place on cookie sheet (single layer). Bake at 350 degrees; for about 1/2 hour. Remove from cookie sheet and place in deep casserole dish.
       Mix sauce. Spoon over meatballs and bake for another 1/2 hour.
