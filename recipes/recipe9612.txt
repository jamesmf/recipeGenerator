


3 to 4  lb. roast
3  to 4 bay leaves
3/4  c. vinegar
3/4  c. brown sugar
14  oz. catsup
1  large onion, chopped
1  Tbsp. pickling spice
30  gingersnaps

      Put all ingredients in pot.  Put in enough water to cover meat.  Cook slowly 6 to 8 hours.  Remove meat; slice.  Put gravy through food mill.  Put meat back into gravy.
