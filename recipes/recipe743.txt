THREE CHEESE CHICKEN LASAGNA


8  oz. lasagna noodles
1/2  c. chopped onion
1/2  c. chopped green pepper
3  Tbsp. butter
1  can cream of chicken soup
6  oz. can mushrooms, drained
1/2  c. chopped pimento
1/3  c. milk
1/2  tsp. dried basil
1 1/2  c. cottage cheese
2  c. diced chicken
2  c. shredded American cheese
1/2  c. grated Parmesan cheese

      Cook noodles and drain.  Cook onion and green pepper in butter until tender.  Stir in soup, mushrooms, pimento, milk and basil. Lay 1/2 the noodles in a 13 x 9-inch pan.  Top with half each the sauce, cottage cheese, chicken and American and Parmesan cheeses. Repeat layers except for the last 2 cheeses.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes.  Top with remaining cheeses.  Bake a few minutes more.  Makes 8 to 10 servings.
