TACO SOUP
(Vegetarian)  

1  lb. ground meat substitute
1  chopped onion
2 (16 oz.)  cans pinto beans
1 (16 oz.)  can hominy
1  can diced tomatoes with green chilies
1  can tomato soup
1 (4 oz.)  can tomato sauce
1  pkg. taco seasoning
1  pkg. Ranch-style dressing mix

      In large saucepan, brown the ground meat substitute with the chopped onion in cooking spray. Add the rest of the ingredients and simmer for 30 minutes.
       Serving Size: 1 cup. Cal-338, Cho-31, Protein-20, Fat-14.
