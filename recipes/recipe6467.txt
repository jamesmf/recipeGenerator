CHICKEN POT PIE

Part 1:
1  cooked chicken, all bones removed
2  cans cream of chicken soup
1  can cream of celery soup
1 small  jar pimentos, drained
1  chopped onion
1  chopped bell pepper
1  (9-inch) pie shell or crust (deep-dish)
1  can sweet peas, drained
1  can string beans, drained
2  cans mixed vegetables, drained

 
Part 2:
1  c. milk
1  c. flour
3/4  c. mayo
1  tsp. salt

      Preheat oven to 300 degrees;.  You will need a jar with lid.
      Part 1:  Mix all ingredients, except pie shell, in large mixing bowl.  In a 9 x 13 dish, place thawed pie shell.  Pour all ingredients onto pie shell.  Cover ingredients with remaining pie shell/crust.
      Part 2:  Put all ingredients into a jar.  Shake ingredients until well blended.  Pour on top layer of pie crust.  Bake at 300 degrees; until golden brown, approximately 1 1/2 hours.
