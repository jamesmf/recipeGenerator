FRUIT SALAD & DRESSING


1 large  can fruit cocktail
1 small  can mandarin oranges
1  c. red grapes, sliced
1  apple, chopped
2  bananas, sliced
1/2  c. pecans

      Toss salad ingredients together in bowl.
Dressing:
1  egg
4  Tbsp. Sugar
4  Tbsp. Vinegar

      Beat egg well and mix with sugar and vinegar in small pan. Cook, stirring constantly, until thickens.  Cool and pour over fruit salad.
