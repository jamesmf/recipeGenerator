MUFFIN CONES


1  box Duncan Hines muffin mix
10  flat bottomed ice cream cones

      Mix muffin mix as directed.  Place the 10 cones in a muffin pan.  Cookie sheet can also be used.  Fill the cones with the batter approximately 1-inch from the cones' rims.  Bake at 325 degrees; for 15 to 20 minutes.  Test for doneness.  Decorate with yellow frosting and make "happy faces" with raisins, etc., or with a tube of chocolate icing.  Children can eat entire cone; contains no messy wrappers.
