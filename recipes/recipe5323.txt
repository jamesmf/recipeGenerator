FANCY FRUIT SALAD


1  can lite peaches, drained and cut up
1  can pineapple chunks
3  bananas, sliced
1  pt. strawberries or 10 oz. frozen, sliced
1  pkg. sugar-free vanilla pudding (instant; small box)
1/2 to 1  tsp. sugar-free Tang

      Mix fruit in order as listed. Mix Tang and dry pudding mix together. Sprinkle over fruit and toss together. Refrigerate overnight or at least for a few hours. Stir again before serving.
