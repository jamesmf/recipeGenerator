MEXICAN LASAGNA


2  lb. ground beef
1  large onion, diced
1  can Ro-Tel diced tomatoes with green chilies
2  pkg. taco seasoning mix
  flour tortillas
  sour cream
  Monterey Jack cheese
  Cheddar cheese

	Brown beef with onions. Add taco seasoning mix and Ro-Tel tomatoes. Layer meat mixture with shells, sour cream and cheese. Bake 30 minutes at 350 degrees;.
