FRUIT SALAD


1  pkg. (3 oz.) orange or apricot Jell-O
1  pkg. (3 oz.) vanilla pudding
1 1/2  c. boiling water
8  oz. Cool Whip
1  can mandarin oranges, drained
1  can fruit cocktail, drained  (or cut up fruit chunks)

      Mix Jell-O and pudding.  Add  boiling water.  Cool till it gets the thickness of cream.  Add Cool Whip.  Add oranges and fruit cocktail.
