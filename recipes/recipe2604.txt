BUTTERHORN ROLLS


1  pkg. yeast
1/3  c. water
1/2  c. milk, scalded
1  egg
1  tsp. salt
1/4  c. sugar
2 1/4  c. flour
  melted oleo

      Dissolve yeast in water.  Cool milk and add it with egg, salt and sugar, then add flour.  Mix and knead.  Let rise until double in size.  Roll out in circles and brush with melted oleo.  Cut in pie-shaped wedges; roll up.  Brush with oleo again.  Let rise. Bake 15 minutes in 400 degrees; oven or until golden brown.
      Note:  Recipe may be doubled.
