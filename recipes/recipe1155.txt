SWEET POTATOES


2  c. sliced sweet potatoes, cooked
  brown sugar substitute to equal 3 lb. brown sugar
1/4  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. cinnamon
1/4  tsp. nutmeg
3  Tbsp. reduced calorie margarine

      Arrange potatoes in nonstick casserole dish.  Combine sugar, salt and spices.  Sprinkle over potatoes and dot with margarine. Bake at 350 degrees; for 10 to 15 minutes.
