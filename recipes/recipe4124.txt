SAUSAGE ROLL-UPS


2  c. Bisquick mix
1/2  c. cold water
2  Tbsp. margarine, softened
1/3  c. grated Parmesan cheese
2  tsp. Italian seasoning
15  Brown 'N Serve sausage links

      Heat oven to 400 degrees;.  Mix Bisquick mix and water until soft dough forms.  Beat 25 strokes.  Smooth dough into ball on floured cloth-covered board.  Knead 5 to 8 times.  Roll dough in rectangle, 15 x 9-inches.  Brush with margarine.  Mix cheese and Italian seasoning.  Sprinkle over buttered pastry.  Cut dough into 15 (3-inch) squares.  Place a sausage on each square.  Roll up. Pinch dough to seal.  Cut each roll into 3 pieces.  Place seam side down on ungreased cookie sheet.  Bake approximately 15 minutes or until golden brown.  Can be served as is or arranged around a dish of your favorite mustard and served with toothpicks.
