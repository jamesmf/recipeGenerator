NO PEEK CHICKEN


1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  can cream of celery soup
1  can water
1 1/2  C. uncooked rice
1  pkg. dry onion soup mix
1 cut up whole chicken,  can us boneless skinless chicken breasts also
4  Tbsp. melted butter
  Additional mushrooms (optional)

      Combine first four ingredients in a 9 x 13-inch pan.  Lay chicken over top; sprinkle onion soup mix and melted butter over chicken.
      Cover with aluminum foil.  Bake at 350 degrees for 2 hours. Do not peek!
