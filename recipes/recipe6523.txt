LAZY LASAGNA


1 1/2  lb. ground chuck
3/4  elbow macaroni
2  c. cottage cheese
1 small  can tomato paste
1 small  can tomato sauce
1 medium  can tomatoes
  onion, chopped
2  tsp. sugar
  chili, oregano and garlic powder
1  c. each Cheddar and Mozzarella cheese
  salt and pepper

      Brown ground chuck and onion. Drain. Cook macaroni. Combine all ingredients. Bake for 25 minutes at 350 degrees;.
