TEXAS BARBECUE SAUCE


1  c. tomato juice
1/4  c. vinegar
1/4  c. catsup
1/4  c. Worcestershire sauce
2  Tbsp. brown sugar
2  Tbsp. paprika
1 1/2  tsp. onion salt
2  tsp. dry mustard
1/2  tsp. pepper
  several dashes of bottled hot sauce

      In a saucepan, combine tomato juice, vinegar, catsup, Worcestershire sauce, brown sugar, paprika, onion salt, mustard, pepper and hot sauce.  Simmer for 5 minutes.  Brush on chicken. Makes about 2 cups sauce.
