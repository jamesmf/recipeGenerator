SLOPPY JOES


1  lb ground beef
1  small onion, minced
1  small green pepper, minced
1  can tomato soup

      Place onion, pepper and ground beef in a large skillet and cook, crumbling the beef as it cooks.  After the meat is thoroughly cooked, add the tomato soup using a small amount of water to clean out the soup left in the can.  Stir to mix thoroughly and simmer on low heat 5-10 minutes. Salt and pepper to taste.
      Serves 4-6.
