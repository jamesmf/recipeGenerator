ORANGE TAPIOCA JELL-O


3  C. water
1 lrg.  pkg. orange Jell-O
2  sm. pkgs. Americana Tapioca
1  can mandarin oranges, be sure to drain well
1  sm. container Cool Whip

      Boil water, add Jell-O, stir; add tapioca mix, continue to cook until thickened (3 - 4 minutes).
      Remove pan from heat; pour into bowl, allow to cool slightly (about 10 - 15 minutes).
      Stir in Cool Whip.  Gently stir in mandarin oranges.
      Allow to chill in refrigerator.
