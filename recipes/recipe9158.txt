FISH FILLETS


2  lb. frozen cod or halibut fillets
1  c. chicken broth
1/4  c. olive oil
1/4  c. lemon juice
1  bay leaf
1  clove garlic, minced
1  Tbsp. grated onion
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. thyme
4  to 6 onion slices
4  to 6 tomato slices
1/4  c. corn flakes, crumbled
2  Tbsp. grated parmesan cheese
3  Tbsp. butter

      Cut fish into 4 to 6 portions, and put into shallow buttered casserole dish. Mix the next 6 ingredients and pour over fish. Let stand until fish is thawed. When ready to bake, top each piece of fish with 1 onion slice and 1 tomato slice. Sprinkle over top, a mixture of corn flake crumbs and parmesan cheese. Dot with butter. Bake at 350 degrees for 25 to 30 minutes, or until top is a golden brown. Serve with sauce from pan.
