HAMBURG POT PIE


1  lb. lean ground beef
1/2  c. chopped onion (I use 1/2 tsp. onion salt)
1 (15 or 16 oz.)  can green beans or 2 c. fresh or frozen
1  tsp. salt
1  Tbsp. sugar
1/4  tsp. pepper
1/8  tsp. oregano
1  can tomato soup or cream of mushroom
  unbaked pie crust

      Pour into an unbaked pie shell on 9-inch plate; cover with top crust.  Make slits for steam.  Bake at 400 degrees; for 25 minutes or until crust is golden brown.
