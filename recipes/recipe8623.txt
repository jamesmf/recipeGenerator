SPINACH MANICOTTI


1  lb. ground beef
1  C. onion
2 lrg.  cloves of garlic, crushed
28  oz. can whole tomatoes, undrained
8  oz. mushroom stem/pieces, drained
1/4  C. chopped fresh parsley or 2 tsp. dried parsley
1/2  tsp. salt
1  tsp. fennel seed
1  Tbsp. fresh or 1 tsp. dried basil
20  oz. frozen chopped spinach
2  C. sm. curd cottage cheese
1/3  C.grated parmesan cheese
1/4  tsp. ground nutmeg
1/4  tsp. pepper
14  uncooked manicotti shells
2  Tbsp. grated parmesan cheese

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Cook beef, onion, and garlic in skillet until brown; drain. Add tomatoes, mushrooms, parsley, salt, fennel seed, and basil in skillet; break up tomatoes, heat to boiling, then reduce heat, cover and simmer for 10 minutes.
      Spoon 1/3 of beef mixture in 13 x 9-inch pan; rinse spinach in cool water and drain; squeeze until dry.  Mix spinach, cottage cheese, 1/3 C. parmesan cheese, nutmeg and pepper.  Fill shells with spinach mixture.
      Place shells on beef mixture in baking dish.  Pour remaining beef mixture over shells, covering completely.  Sprinkle with 2 Tbsp. grated parmesan cheese.
      Cover and bake for 1 1/2 hours or until tender.
