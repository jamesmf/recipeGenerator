CRACKER SNACKS


4  oz. (2 boxes) "Crakin Good" soup and chili crackers
1  tsp. dill weed (dry)
1/4  tsp. garlic powder
1  oz. (1 pkg.) ranch dressing, dry mix
3/4 to 1  c. cooking oil

      Put crackers in large bowl.  Add seasonings then pour oil over all.  Stir every 15 minutes for three times.  Let sit for 2 hours. Store in Ziplock bags.  So-o-o Good!
