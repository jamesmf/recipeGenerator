COCA-COLA SALAD


2  small Cokes
1 small  can crushed pineapple
1  can Bing cherries, pitted
1  (3 oz.) cream cheese
1  c. chopped pecans
1  large black cherry Jell-O

      Drain pineapple and cherries.  Heat juice to just below boiling.  Pour over Jell-O and stir until Jell-O is dissolved.  Let cool.  Add Coke, cherries and pineapple.  Put in refrigerator. When Jell-O begins to thicken, chip up cream cheese into Jell-O and add pecans.  Pour into mold and return to refrigerator until it gels.
