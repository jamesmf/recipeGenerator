CRANBERRY MOLD


2  c. fresh or canned cranberries
1  c. sugar
  lemon juice to taste
1  pkg. raspberry or cranberry jello
15  large marshmallows
1  c. chopped nuts
1  c. chopped celery
1  c. grated apple
1  c. water

      Combine cranberries, sugar, water and lemon juice in a large saucepan; cook until cranberries pop.  Stir in jello and marshmallows until dissolved.  Remove from heat.  Cool, then stir in nuts, celery and apple.  Pour into large mold and chill until firm.
