WIMPY CHILI


2  lb. ground beef
2  cans tomato soup
2  cans chili beans
3/4  tsp. chili powder
1/4  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. garlic powder
1  tsp. vinegar
1/4  c. minced onion
1/2  c. water

      Cook ground beef until brown in a large pot.  Drain off grease. Add rest of ingredients. Bring to boil, then lower heat and simmer. Stir occasionally until ready to eat.
