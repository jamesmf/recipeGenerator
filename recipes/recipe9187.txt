BEST EVER MEAT LOAF

Loaf:
2  lb. ground beef
2  eggs
1  c. corn flakes, crushed
1  onion, chopped
2  tsp. pepper
2  Tbsp. parsley, minced

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees;.  Prepare sauce in a bowl and set aside. Combine meat loaf ingredients until evenly mixed.  Work in half of the sauce with the meat loaf.  Place the meat loaf in a dish or loaf pan and shape it.  Pour the remaining sauce on top and bake for 50 minutes or until center is no longer pink.
Sauce:
1/2  c. ketchup
1/3  c. brown sugar, packed
2  Tbsp. cider vinegar
1 1/2  tsp. brown mustard
1/2  tsp. ground nutmeg

      Mix all ingredients and set aside.
