PORK CHOP CASSEROLE


  2-4 pork chops
  3-4 potatoes
1  onion

      Spray casserole dish with Pam; place sliced potatoes and onion in layers.  Place pork chops on top; cover and bake at 350-375 degrees; for 30 minutes; turn pork chops over and continue baking for 30 minutes (til chops are done);  uncover the last 15 minutes.
      *A can of cheddar soup may be added for a different flavor (do not dilute soup).  Cover chops and veggies with soup.
