ORZO SALAD


1 (1 lb.)  pkg. orzo, cooked
2  c. chunk pineapple, drained
2  c. mandarin oranges, drained
1  c. sugar
1  egg
12  oz. Cool Whip

      Drain fruit and boil juice with sugar and egg.  Pour over cooked orzo noodles.  Let stand overnight.  Next day, add fruit and Cool Whip.  Very good.
