CROCK DILLS


6  to 10 cukes
2  Tbsp. pickling spice
4  cloves garlic
4  qt. water
1/2  c. kosher salt
1  c. vinegar
1  tsp. alum
  bunch of dill (fresh)

      Place dill on bottom of crock.  Place whole cukes next.  Add pickling spice and whole garlic.  Boil water; add salt, vinegar and alum.  Cool.  Pour over cukes.  Place lid on top.  Ready in 4 days.
