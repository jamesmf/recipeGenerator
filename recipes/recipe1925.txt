ENCHILADA DE POLLO CON CHILI VERDE
(Green Chili Chicken Casserole)  

1 (10 oz.)  can cream of chicken soup
1 (10 oz.)  can cream of mushroom soup
1  whole chicken, shredded
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. garlic salt
2  c. chopped green chilies
1/2 or 1  c. chopped onion
  shortening
  corn tortillas
  cheese

      Boil chicken until tender, then shred.  Combine chicken soup, mushroom soup, chicken, salt, garlic salt, green chilies and onion in a medium mixing bowl.  Heat 1/2-inch of shortening in heavy pan at medium-high heat.
      Quickly dip each tortilla into the shortening to soften.  Drain on absorbent towels.  Alternate ingredients in a greased 2-quart casserole dish, beginning with tortillas.  Cover casserole dish and bake in a 350 degrees; oven for 30 to 40 minutes.
