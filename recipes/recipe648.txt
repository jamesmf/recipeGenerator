CHOCOLATE CHIP DIABETIC COOKIES


1/4  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. baking soda
1  c. sifted flour
1/2  c. margarine
2 1/2  tsp. Sweet 'N Low
1/2  tsp. vanilla
1  egg, beaten
1/2  c. miniature chocolate chips

      Preheat oven to 375 degrees;.  Sift salt, baking soda and flour together.  Cream margarine and add sweetener, vanilla and egg, blending well.  Add flour mixture.  Beat well; stir in chocolate chips.  Drop by level spoonfuls onto a baking sheet which has been lightly greased.  Bake for about 9 to 10 minutes.
      Exchanges per serving:  One cookie equals 1/4 bread, 3/4 fat and 50 calories per serving.  Yields 3 dozen cookies.
