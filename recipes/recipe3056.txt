GREEN TOMATO RELISH


6  green peppers
4  red sweet peppers
12  green tomatoes
3  pt. vinegar
1  Tbsp. salt
12  medium onions
3  c. sugar
1  hot pepper

      Grind vegetables.  Pour boiling water over ground vegetables. Let stand 20 minutes.  Drain.  Heat vinegar, sugar and salt. Pour over vegetable mixture.  Simmer 20 minutes.  Place in jars and seal.  Makes about 6 pints.
