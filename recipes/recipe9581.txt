PASTA WITH TOMATO VODKA SAUCE


2  Tbsp. olive oil
1/2  c. chopped onion
1  clove garlic, finely chopped
1 (28 oz.)  can plum tomatoes with juice
1/2  c. vodka
1  tsp. crushed dried red pepper
1/2  c. heavy cream (at room temperature)
  salt
12  oz. fresh or dried pasta
2  Tbsp. parsley

      Heat oil in a large skillet. Add onion; saut√© until tender (not brown), 5 minutes.  Stir in garlic.  Add tomatoes, vodka and red pepper; break up tomatoes with side of spoon.  Cook until sauce is reduced by 1/3 and thickened, 15 minutes.  Stir in cream; simmer, stirring (do not allow to boil) 5 minutes. Season with salt.
      Meanwhile, cook pasta in plenty of boiling salted water until al dente, 2 minutes for fresh, 8 for dried.  Drain.  Toss with sauce and parsley. Serve immediately.  Serves 4.
      Prep:  10 minutes.  Cook: 25 minutes.
