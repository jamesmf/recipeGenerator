BARBECUED CHICKEN WINGS
(Best Ever)  

1/2  c. lemon juice
1/2  c. honey
1/2  c. cooking oil
1/2  c. soy sauce
1/2  c. sherry wine
2  c. chicken bouillon
1  clove garlic, crushed
1/4  tsp. curry powder
1/4  tsp. dry mustard
1  tsp. salt
  enough wings to fit into roasting pan

      Mix all ingredients except wings together and pour over chicken. Dot with butter. Bake at 400 degrees; for 1 1/2 hours.  Turn occasionally while baking.
