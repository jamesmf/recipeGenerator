RUTH'S JELLO SALAD


3 (3 oz.)  boxes Raspberry jello
1 (15 oz.)  jar applesauce
1  c. orange juice
1 (8 oz.)  can crushed pineapple, drained
1 (20 oz.)  bottle diet 7-up or Sprite

      Bring first four ingredients to a boil then let set til room temperature.  Add 7-Up or Sprite to jello mixture.  (Use large bowl as it will foam up.)  Mix well and pour into mold if desired and chill.
