JANE'S CUCUMBER BREAD AND BUTTER PICKLES


2  quarts thinly sliced cucumbers
1  green pepper sliced
1  medium sweet onion, sliced
2  tsp salt
1  1/2 cups granulated sugar
3/4  cup vinegar
3  tsp mustard or celery seed

      Mix vegetables and salt.  Soak for 3 hours, drain well.
      Heat sugar and vinegar to dissolve.  Put vegetables in containers or jars.  Cover with sugar and vinegar mixture. Freeze.  Good several days after defrosting.
