HOLIDAY YAM BAKE


1 (40 oz.)  can yams
1 (8 1/2 oz.)  can crushed pineapple and juice or raisins
2  Tbsp. light brown sugar
2  Tbsp. butter or margarine, melted
3  Tbsp. chopped pecans
3/4  c. miniature marshmallows

      Drain yams; mash well.  Drain pineapple; reserve juice. Add juice to yams.  Add sugar and butter; beat well.  Stir in pineapple  and  pecans.  Coat  inside of 1 1/2 quart casserole with  Pam.  Spoon  in  mixture.  Bake  for 20 minutes at 350 degrees;. Sprinkle  with  marshmallows.  Bake  for  10  minutes  longer. Serves 8.
