SEAFOOD FETTUCCINE


3/4  lb. cooked fresh/frozen shrimp
1 (4 oz.)  can sliced mushrooms
1/2  tsp. garlic powder
1/8  tsp. each salt and pepper
1/4  c. butter or margarine
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. fettuccine, cooked and drained
1/2  c. grated Parmesan cheese
1/2  c. milk
1/2  c. sour cream
  minced fresh parsley (optional)

      In large saucepan, saut in butter the shrimp, mushrooms, garlic powder, salt and pepper for 3 to 5 minutes. Stir in fettuccine, Parmesan cheese, milk and sour cream. Cook over medium heat for 3 to 5 minutes until heated through (do not boil). Serve.
