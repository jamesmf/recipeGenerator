PRIME RIB ROAST


1/4  c. worcestershire sauce
1  (5 to 6 lb.) prime rib roast (at room temperature)

      Pour worcestershire sauce over roast.  Place roast on a rack in a broiler pan.  Bake at 550 degrees; for 6 minutes.  Reduce heat to 350 degrees; and bake 18 to 20 minutes per pound.  This will be medium rare.
