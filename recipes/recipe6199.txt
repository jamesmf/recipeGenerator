TACO SOUP


2  lb. ground beef
1 (15 oz.)  can pinto beans
1 (15 oz.)  can kidney beans
1  chopped onion
3  cans Hunt's Mexican tomatoes
1  qt. tomatoes
1  can corn, drained
1  pkg. taco seasoning mix
1  pkg. Ranch dressing mix

	Brown the ground beef and onion. Drain. Simply dump all other ingredients in pot and cook for 1 hour. Serve with grated cheese, nacho chips and sour cream.
