CUCUMBER SALAD


2  cucumbers, pared and sliced
1/2  tsp. salt
1  c. sour cream
1  tsp. dill weed
1/4  tsp. dry mustard
1 1/2  Tbsp. vinegar
1  Tbsp. sugar
1  green onion, snipped
1/2  tsp. celery seed

      Combine all ingredients except for the cucumbers. Mix well and pour over cucumbers. Refrigerate several hours.
