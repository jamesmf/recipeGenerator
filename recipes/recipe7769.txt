TACO PASTA CASSEROLE
(Serves 6 to 8)  

1  lb. lean ground beef
1  packet taco seasoning mix
1  Tbsp. butter
1  large onion, chopped
1  large green pepper, chopped (optional)
1 large  box shell pasta
2  cans stewed tomatoes
1  jar chunky salsa
2  c. Cheddar cheese, grated

      In a large skillet over medium heat, brown ground beef and drain excess liquid. Add taco seasoning spices as directed on the package and stir to blend; reserve.
      In the same skillet, saut onions and green pepper (if desired) in 1 tablespoon butter until tender.
       Meanwhile, cook pasta as directed on the package and drain.
      In a 9 x 13-inch baking pan, spoon stewed tomatoes into the bottom of pan to cover.  Layer remaining ingredients in the following order:  pasta, ground beef and taco seasoning combination, salsa and cheese.  Repeat layering process until dish is full, making sure to finish the top layer with Cheddar cheese. Bake in a preheated 400 degrees; oven for 25 to 30 minutes.  Remove from oven and let stand for about 5 minutes.  Serve warm.
