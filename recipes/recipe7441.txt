HAMBURGER PIE


1  lb. hamburger
1 (15 oz.)  can sweet peas, drained
4 to 6  c. instant potatoes, prepared
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. shredded Cheddar cheese
2  pie crusts (9-inch)

      Brown and drain hamburger.  Combine hamburger, peas and 1 cup cheese.  Put into browned pie shells.  Put equal amount of potatoes on top of mixture.  Sprinkle remaining cheese on top of pies.  Bake at 350 degrees; until cheese has melted.
