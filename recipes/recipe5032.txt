CHICKEN CORN SOUP


4  c. cooked deboned chicken
2  c. potatoes, diced
2  pt. fresh or frozen corn
1  small onion, chopped
1/2  c. celery, chopped
  salt and pepper

      Add  potatoes,  corn,  onion and celery to three quarts broth. May  use  1/2 water and 1/2 broth.  Cook until vegetables are done.  Add  chicken.  Bring to a boil and serve.  If using canned corn, add it when you add chicken.
