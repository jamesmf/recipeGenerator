MUD PIES


2  sticks margarine
4  sq. unsweetened chocolate
1/2  c. milk
1  c. peanut butter
2  c. oatmeal

      Melt butter and chocolate.  Pour in milk.  Stir and bring to rolling boil for 3 minutes.  Take off heat; let cool for 2 minutes. Quickly add peanut butter and oatmeal.  Stir until thick.  Place spoonfuls on wax paper.  Let cool, then eat!
