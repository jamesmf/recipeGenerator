EASY BREAKFAST SQUARES


24  oz. O'Brien-style hash browns
2  c. diced ham
6  oz. shredded Cheddar cheese
6  oz. shredded Mozzarella cheese
9  to 12 eggs
1/2  tsp. salt
1  c. milk
1  medium onion, finely chopped

      Place thawed hash browns in a 13 x 9 pan. Sprinkle ham over the hash browns. Top the ham with the two cheeses. Beat eggs. Add milk, salt and onion. Pour over the top of the cheeses. Cover and refrigerate overnight or at least 3 hours. Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour or until set. Let stand 5 to 10 minutes before cutting.
      Tastes great with salsa.
