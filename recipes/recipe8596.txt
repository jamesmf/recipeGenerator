GROUND BEEF 'N TATER CASSEROLE


1  lb. ground beef
14  oz. can cut green beans
10  oz. can tomato soup
1/2  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. black pepper
2  cups prepared mashed potatoes
1  can (2.8 oz.) French Fried Onions
1/2  C. (2oz) shredded cheddar cheese

      Brown beef in skillet, drain. Combine beef, green beans, soup, salt & pepper. Pour into 1 1/2 qt. casserole.  Combine mashed potatoes and 1/2 can onions.  Spoon potato mixture in a mounded ring around top of outer edge of casserole. Bake, uncovered at 350 degrees for 25 min. Top potatoes with cheese and remaining onions and bake 5 minutes longer.
