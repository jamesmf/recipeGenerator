BONITA'S CHICKEN


6  boneless chicken breasts
6  (4 x 4-inch) Swiss cheese pieces
1/2  c. milk
2  cans cream chicken soup
2  c. herb seasoned stuffing mix (dry)
1/4  c. butter, melted

      Arrange chicken in baking dish sprayed with nonstick spray. Top with Swiss cheese.  Combine milk and soup.  Spoon over chicken.  Sprinkle with dry stuffing mix.  Drizzle with melted butter.  Cover.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 50 minutes.
