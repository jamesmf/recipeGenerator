BROCCOLI SALAD


1  bunch broccoli (use stem, cut up small)
1/2  c. raisins
1  c. sunflower seeds
12  slices bacon, cut up  small and browned

 
Dressing:
1  c. mayonnaise
1/2  c. sugar
2  Tbsp. vinegar

      Add raisins and bacon to broccoli; mix with dressing and refrigerate. Add sunflower seeds just before serving.
