STUFFED GREEN PEPPER BOATS


4  large green peppers, halved lengthwise
2 1/4  c. cooked rice
3/4  lb. American cheese, cubed
1  can cream of potato soup

      Remove seeds from peppers; parboil for 5 minutes.  Drain well.  Combine rice, cheese and soup; stuff peppers with mixture. Place each pepper half in aluminum foil cup.  Place in baking pan. Bake in preheated 350 degrees; oven for 30 minutes or until heated through.  Yield: 6 to 8 servings.
