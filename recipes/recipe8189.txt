PORK CHOPS CREOLE


6  thick pork chops
1/4  c. cooking oil
1  clove finely chopped garlic
1/2  c. chopped green pepper
1  small onion, chopped
4  medium, fresh, peeled tomatoes, chopped
1/2  tsp. thyme
1  tsp. salt
  pepper to taste

      Brown pork chops lightly in 2 tablespoons oil in large skillet. Saut√© garlic, green pepper and onion in 2 tablespoons oil in small skillet. Add tomatoes, thyme, salt and pepper. Add pork chops to sauce. Steam, covered, for 30 minutes or until tender.
