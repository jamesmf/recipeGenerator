MEAT LOAF ITALIANO


1/4  c. onion, finely chopped
2  Tbsp. butter or margarine
1/2  c. herb seasoned stuffing
1/2  c. beef bouillon
1  lb. ground chuck
1  Tbsp. parsley, chopped
3  Tbsp. Parmesan cheese, grated
1  egg, slightly beaten
1  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
1 (8 oz.)  can tomato sauce
1  tsp. oregano

	Saute onion in butter until tender. In a large bowl, combine stuffing and beef bouillon. Add all but the last 2 ingredients. Mix lightly until well blended. Shape into a loaf and place in a shallow baking pan. Bake at 375 degrees; for 30 minutes. Pour tomato sauce over top and sprinkle with oregano. Continue baking 30 minutes longer. Serves 4 to 6.
