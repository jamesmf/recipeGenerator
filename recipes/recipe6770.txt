STEAK SIZZLERS


2  lb. beef (1-inch chunks)
1/4  c. oil
1 1/2  tsp. lemon pepper
1  tsp. garlic powder
1/2  tsp. seasoning salt
1  c. salsa

      Put everything in a Ziploc bag and mix. Refrigerate at least 1 hour. Skewer and garnish with favorite vegetables and grill. Can be frozen. Thaw before grilling.
