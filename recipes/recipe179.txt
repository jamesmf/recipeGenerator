MICHIGAN ROCKS


3/4  c. butter or oleo
1 1/2  c. brown sugar
4  eggs
1  tsp. baking soda (in a little hot water)
1  tsp. vanilla
1 1/2  tsp. cinnamon
3  c. flour
1  lb. dates, chopped
1 1/2  lb. English walnuts, chopped
1  c. grapes, halved and seeded

      Mix in order the butter or oleo, brown sugar, eggs, baking soda in a little hot water, vanilla, cinnamon, flour, dates, walnuts and grapes.  Drop from teaspoon on baking sheet.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 8 to 10 minutes.
