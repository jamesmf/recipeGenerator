ARTICHOKE DIP


1  can artichoke hearts, chopped
1  c. mayonnaise
1  bunch green onions, chopped
1  c. + shredded Mozzarella cheese
1  c. Parmesan cheese
1  Tbsp. parsley
  dash of garlic salt

      Mix together.  Top with 1 package slivered almonds.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes.  Serve with corn chips.
