COCKTAIL WIENER WRAPS


1  pkg. cocktail wieners
1  pkg. bacon
  brown sugar

      Cut bacon slices into 2 pieces and wrap a cocktail wiener in each 1/2 slice.  Secure with a toothpick and place in casserole dish.  Sprinkle brown sugar over top of wieners.  Cook in 350 degrees; oven until bacon browns.
