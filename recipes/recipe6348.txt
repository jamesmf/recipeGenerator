CREAMY ITALIAN DRESSING


1  c. mayonnaise
1  Tbsp. lemon juice
1  Tbsp. water
1  Tbsp. sugar
2  Tbsp. vinegar wine
1  Tbsp. corn oil
1  Tbsp. worcestershire sauce
1  clove garlic, minced
  oregano, to taste

      Beat oil into mayonnaise, add all other ingredients. Chill before serving. Store in refrigerator.
