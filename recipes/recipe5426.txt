BLUE CHEESE DRESSING


1 (4 oz.)  pkg. Bleu cheese, crumbled
1 (3 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
1 1/2  c. mayonnaise or salad dressing
1/3  c. light cream (20%)
2  garlic, crushed

	Reserve 1/3 cup of the Bleu cheese. In a small mixer bowl, blend remaining Bleu cheese and the cream cheese on low speed. Add mayonnaise and cream. Beat on medium speed until creamy. Stir in reserved Bleu cheese. Cover and chill at least 3 hours to blend flavors. May add 2 garlic.
