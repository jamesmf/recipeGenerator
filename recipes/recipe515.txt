RICE KRISPIES TREATS


1/4  c. margarine
50  marshmallows
6  c. Rice Krispies cereal

      Melt margarine in a 4-quart saucepan; use low heat.  Add the marshmallows and stir until melted.  Remove from heat and stir in the cereal until well coated with the marshmallows.  Put in a well greased 13 x 9-inch pan and press down evenly with a greased spoon. Cool and cut into squares.  Serve in a piece of waxed paper as they are a little sticky.
