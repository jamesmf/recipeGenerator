CHICKEN ENCHILADA PIE


3  lb. chicken
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  tsp. chili powder
1  tsp. garlic powder
4  tsp. mixed onion
1/4  tsp. Tabasco sauce
1  c. chicken broth
4  c. corn chips
8  oz. sharp Cheddar cheese, grated

      Cook and bone chicken. Reserve 1 cup chicken broth. Combine soups, green chilies, spices, Tabasco sauce and chicken broth in double boiler. Blend well.
       Preheat oven to 350 degrees;. Cover the bottom of 2 1/2 or 3-quart casserole with 2 cups corn chips. Spread 1/2 chicken over this layer. Then spread half the sauce mixture and half the grated cheese; repeat. End with cheese. Bake at 350 degrees; for 25 to 30 minutes.
