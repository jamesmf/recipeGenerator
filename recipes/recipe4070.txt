CHEESE WAFERS


2  c. plain flour
1  stick margarine, softened
1/2  lb. sharp Cheddar cheese, softened
1  tsp. salt
1 1/2  tsp. cayenne pepper
  pecans

	Mix all ingredients (except pecans). Roll in 2 logs and chill. Cut in slices (1/4-inch). Place on greased cookie sheet. Add 1/2 pecan on top of each, if desired. Bake at 350 degrees; for 10 to 12 minutes.
