SOCIETY SUE SHRIMP


1  pt. half and half
2  bunches green onions (scallions)
1  lb. shrimp, peeled and deveined
1  stick butter
2  tsp. + Cajun Magic
8  oz. Mueller's twists

      Cook twists and set aside. Saut onions in butter. Add shrimp; saut about 3 minutes until pink. Add cream and Magic. Cook until thickens, stirring constantly (3 to 5 minutes). Toss in pre-cooked twists. Let sit for about 5 minutes. Toss and serve.
