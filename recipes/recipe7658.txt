GRANDMA'S BAKED CHICKEN POT PIE


6  medium potatoes
2  qt. canned chicken
  biscuit dough

      Peel, slice and cook potatoes.  Put in  bottom  of  9 x 13-inch pan.  Cover with chicken broth  or  water.  Lay cooked chicken pieces on that.  Make biscuit dough.  Roll thin.  Line sides of pan and cover top with squares.  Bake  at  350 degrees; until golden brown.
