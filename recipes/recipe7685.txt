POTATO-MEAT CASSEROLE


2  c. diced meat, preferably oven roast beef
4  c. diced raw potatoes
1/2  c. chopped celery
2 1/2  c. thin gravy
  salt and pepper to taste

      Mix first 4 ingredients and place in greased casserole. Pour gravy over top.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour.
      This is a great way to use leftover meat and gravy.
