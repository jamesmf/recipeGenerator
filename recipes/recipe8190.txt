PORK CHOPS AND POTATOES


8  lean pork chops
6  medium potatoes
2  medium onions
1  c. water
1/2  c. evaporated milk
1  c. flour
2  Tbsp. Italian seasoning
  small amount cooking oil
1/2  stick margarine
  salt and pepper to taste

      Remove bones from pork chops and cut into bite-size pieces. Toss with salt, pepper and Italian seasoning. Flour meat and brown in hot oil. Drain well and set aside. Peel potatoes and onions; slice into rounds. Layer potatoes, then onions into baking dish. Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Dot with margarine. Place meat on top. Mix water and milk together and pour over meat. Bake at 375 degrees; for 1 hour or until potatoes are tender.
