PINEAPPLE CHEESE BALL


2 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
8  oz. crushed pineapple, drained
2  Tbsp. finely minced onion
1/4  c. chopped green pepper
2  tsp. Lawry's seasoning salt
2  c. chopped pecans

	Bring cheese to room temperature. Stir in pineapple, onion, green pepper, seasoning salt and 1 cup of pecans. Chill well. Form into 2 balls. Roll in remaining cup of pecans.
