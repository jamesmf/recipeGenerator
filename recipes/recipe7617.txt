TORTILLA ROLL UP


2  c. shredded cheddar cheese
1 small  can sliced black olives
1 small  can chopped green chilies
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
1/2  c. sour cream
2  green onions, chopped
  Garlic salt
  Season salt
8  (8-inch) tortillas

      Mix the first 8 ingredients together.  Spread on tortilla. Roll up and wrap in Saran Wrap.  Refrigerate for 8 hours.  Slice and serve with salsa.  (Can be frozen.)
