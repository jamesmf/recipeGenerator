FIRECRACKER CASSEROLE


2  lb. ground beef
1  large onion, chopped
2  Tbsp. chili powder
1  tsp. Cumin
1  can chili beans
6  corn tortillas
1  cup mozzarella cheese, grated
1  cup cheddar cheese, grated
1  can Rotel Tomatoes (Hot or Mild)

      Simmer first 4 ingredients together for 10 minutes.  Spread in a 9x13 pan.  Layer remaining ingredients in the order listed above. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.
