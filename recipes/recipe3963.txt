SUGAR COATED PEANUTS



1  c. sugar
1/2  c. water
2  c. raw shelled peanuts, skins on
  several drops red food coloring

      Add  food coloring to water; dissolve sugar in water in saucepan over medium heat.  Add peanuts and continue to cook over medium heat, stirring frequently.  Cook until peanuts are completely sugared (coated and no syrup).  Pour on ungreased cookie sheet.  Separate peanuts with a fork.  Bake at 300 degrees; for 30 minutes, stirring at 10 minute intervals.
