SAUSAGE PILAU


1  lb. Jimmy Dean sausage (mild or hot)
2  links smoked sausage, cut into small pieces
1  large onion
1 large  can diced tomatoes
2  c. long grain rice (not instant)
4  c. water

      Brown sausage and onion; add tomatoes and water. Season with salt and pepper. Bring to a boil. Add rice. Cover and cook on low for 2 hours.
