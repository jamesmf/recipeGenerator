BROCCOLI SALAD


1  c. mayonnaise, or more, to taste
1/4  c. vinegar
1/4  c. sugar
6  c. broccoli florets, chopped
1  small onion, diced (purple onion is good)
1/2  c. Real bacon bits
1/3  c. raisins

      Whisk mayonnaise, vinegar and sugar.  Add rest of ingredients; mix well and refrigerate overnight.  Toss before serving.
