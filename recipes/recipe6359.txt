TORTILLA SOUP


1/4  lb. turkey bacon, diced
4  skinless boneless chicken breast halves, cut into cubes (about 1 1/2 lb.)
3 (13 3/4 oz.)  cans chicken broth
2  tsp. garlic powder
2  tsp. onion powder
2  tsp. ground comino
1  tsp. dried thyme
4  c. assorted vegetables (such as celery, carrots, zucchini, red pepper, cauliflower), cut bite sized
1  (10 oz.) frozen whole kernel corn
1/2  c. picante sauce
  shredded cheese
  tortilla chips

      In Dutch oven, brown chicken and bacon about 5 minutes or until fully cooked. Add broth and seasonings; bring to boil. Add vegetables and picante sauce; simmer 5 minutes, covered, or until vegetables are tender-crisp. Ladle into bowls and sprinkle with cheese and tortilla chips. Makes 6 servings.
