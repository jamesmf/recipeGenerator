DRIED BEEF DIP


1 small  jar dried beef
8  oz. cream cheese
1  c. sour cream
1/4  c. milk
1/4  tsp. black pepper
1/4  c. chopped onions
2  Tbsp. chopped green pepper

      Soften cream cheese. Add milk and mix. Add sour cream and mix. Add remaining ingredients and mix. Cook for appropriate time. Bake in oven at 350 degrees; for 15 minutes.
      Microwave:  Cook at High power for 3 minutes.  Stir.  Cook on High power again for 3 minutes.  Continue cooking until it becomes slightly thick.
