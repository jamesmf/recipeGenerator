CHICKEN SALAD


4  c. chicken, cooked and chopped
2  c. seedless grapes
1/2  c. sliced almonds, toasted
1/2  c. mayonnaise
1/4  c. sour cream
1  tbsp. lemon juice
1/4  c. chutney
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper

      Combine chicken, grapes and almonds. Combine mayonnaise and remaining ingredients. Add to chicken mixture and mix well.
