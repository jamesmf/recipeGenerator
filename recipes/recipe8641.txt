SWISS STEAK


2  lbs. beef round steak
3  Tbsp flour
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
2  Tbsp. vegetable oil
2  med. onions, sliced
2  celery ribs, chopped
14.5  oz. can diced tomatoes, undrained
2  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/4  tsp. dried oregano
3  carrots, sliced

      Cut meat into serving size pieces and roll in flour. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.  In a skillet, brown meat on both sides in oil.  Top with onions, celery and carrots.  In a medium bowl, combine tomatoes, Worcestershire sauce and oregano.  Spoon over veggies.  Cover and simmer for 1 1/2 - 2 hours.
      Alternative:  Cut meat into serving size pieces and roll in flour.  Place in bottom of slow cooker.  Top with onions, carrots and celery.  In a medium bowl; combine tomatoes, Worcestershire sauce and oregano.  Spoon over veggies. Add 1 C. water to slow cooker.  Cover and cook on high for approximately 4 hours.
