LINDA'S EASY PLEASING MEAT LOAVES


3  lb. ground beef
1 (6 oz.)  pkg. Stove Top stuffing mix (any variety)
1  c. water
2  eggs, beaten
1/2  c. Kraft original barbecue sauce, divided

      Mix all ingredients except 1/4 cup of the barbecue sauce. Shape meat mixture into 2 oval loaves, side by side in 13 x 9 baking dish. Top with remaining 1/4 cup barbecue sauce. Bake at 375 degrees; for 45 to 60 minutes or until center is no longer pink. Makes 6 to 8 servings.
