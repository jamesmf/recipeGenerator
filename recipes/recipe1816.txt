QUESADILLA SUPREME


1  lb. ground chuck beef
1  can refried beans and peppers
1  pkg. taco seasoning mix
4  (12-inch) flour tortillas
10  oz. grated Cheddar cheese
10  oz. grated Monterey Jack cheese
2  c. prepared guacamole
2 1/2  oz. can sliced drained black olives
2  chopped tomatoes
1  bunch green onions, chopped
1 (8 oz.)  carton sour cream

      Brown beef and season according to taco seasoning directions. Butter 1 side of tortilla and place on cookie sheet, butter side down.  Cover with the following layers:  beans, taco beef and two cheeses that have been mixed together.  Cover with another flour tortilla that has been buttered on the top side.  Bake 5 to 6 minutes at 500 degrees;.  Broil 1 to 2 minutes or until slightly brown and cheese is melted.  Remove from oven and top with guacamole, black olives, tomatoes, onions and a spoonful of sour cream.  Cut into 4 wedges to serve.
