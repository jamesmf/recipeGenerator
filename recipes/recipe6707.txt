BROCCOLI BEEF SQUARES


2  c. chopped, thawed and drained broccoli
1  lb. ground beef
4  g. mushroom pieces
2  c. shredded Cheddar cheese
1/3  c. chopped onion
2  c. Bisquick
1/2  c. cold water
1/4  c. grated Parmesan cheese
1  tsp. salt
  dash of pepper
1/2  c. milk
4  eggs

      Heat oven to 400 degrees;.  Grease 13 x 9 x 2 baking dish.  Heat 1-inch salted water to boiling.  Add broccoli.  Cover and cook until almost tender, about 5 minutes.  Drain thoroughly.  Cook and stir beef until brown.  Drain.  Stir in mushrooms, 1 1/2 cups Cheddar cheese and onion.  Mix Bisquick, water and remaining cheese until soft dough forms.  Beat 20 strokes.  Pat dough in dish with floured hands, pressing 1/2-inch up sides.  Spread beef mix over dough.  Sprinkle with broccoli.  Mix remaining ingredients.  Pour over broccoli.  Bake until knife comes out clean, 25 to 30 minutes.
