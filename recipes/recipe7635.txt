CHEESEBURGER CASSEROLE


3  boxes macaroni and cheese mix
3  lb. hamburger
2  c. pizza sauce or catsup
2  medium onions, finely chopped
1  c. shredded cheese
1  can mushrooms, drained

      Fix  macaroni as directed on box.  Brown hamburger with onions.  Salt  and pepper to taste.  Layer macaroni, hamburger and sauce in casserole dish.  Mix mushrooms in with hamburger after it  is  browned.  Sprinkle cheese on top.  Bake at 350 degrees; until  hot through.  Sometimes I mix all ingredients together and put in casserole dish.
