CHICKEN BREAST IN WINE


12  chicken breast halves
1 large  can sliced mushrooms, drained
1  stick butter or margarine
1  c. white wine
2  cans cream of chicken soup
1/2  can water
  salt and paprika

      Brown chicken in butter.  Put in large baking dish.  Sprinkle with paprika and salt.  Add mushrooms, water, soup and wine to butter left in skillet.  Cook about 5 minutes.  Pour over chicken in baking dish.  Bake at 325 degrees; for 1 1/2 hours.
