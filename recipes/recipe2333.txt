BROCCOLI OR SPINACH QUICHE


1/2  c. mayonnaise
1/2  c. half and half
1  tsp. salt
2  beaten eggs
1  Tbsp. flour
1/3  c. chopped onion
1  lb. Swiss or Mozzarella cheese
1  pkg. frozen broccoli or spinach
  deep-dish pie shell

      Thaw broccoli or spinach; let liquid drain off.  Mix first six ingredients together.  Add cheese; next add broccoli or spinach. Mix well together.  Put in unbaked pie shell.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 40 to 45 minutes.  Let set before serving.
