CROCKPOT STUFFED GREEN PEPPERS


6  green bell peppers, tops removed and seeded
1  lb. lean ground beef
1 1/2  c. cooked rice
1/2  c. finely chopped onion
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  c. ketchup
1  can (10 1/2-oz.) condensed tomato soup
1  can (16-oz.) tomato sauce
2/3  c. water
  Grated cheese for garnish (optional)

      Wash peppers; drain well.  Combine in medium bowl the ground beef, rice, onion, salt, and ketchup.  Mix well. Stuff peppers 2/3 full.  Arrange stuffed peppers in crock-pot. Combine tomato soup, tomato sauce, and water; pour over peppers.  Cover and cook on low for 6 to 8 hours.  Pour tomato sauce from crockpot into gravy bowl to pour over peppers when serving.  Sprinkle with cheese if desired.
