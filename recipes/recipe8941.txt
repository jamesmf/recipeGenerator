MEAT PIE


1  lb. hamburger or pork
2  c. cooked spaghetti
1  can whole kernel corn
1  can tomato soup
1  chopped onion
  salt and pepper

      Brown meat in 4 tablespoons fat. Add onion, salt and pepper. When brown, combine with corn, spaghetti and tomato soup. Cover with crust and bake until crust is brown.
