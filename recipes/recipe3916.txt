TRADITIONAL CHEX PARTY MIX


1/2  c. butter
1 1/4  tsp. Season-All seasoned salt
4 1/2  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
2 2/3  c. Corn Chex cereal
2 2/3  c. Rice Chex cereal
2 2/3  c. Wheat Chex cereal
1  c. salted nuts
2  c. pretzels

      Preheat oven to 250 degrees;. Melt butter in 10 x 15-inch pan. Stir in seasoned salt and Worcestershire. Gradually add cereal and nuts; mix until all pieces are coated. Bake 1 hour. Stir every 15 minutes. Spread on absorbent paper to cool.
