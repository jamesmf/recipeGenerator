CHEESY BROCCOLI-POTATO SOUP


2  c. chicken broth
1/3  c. chopped onion
1 (10 oz.)  pkg. frozen chopped broccoli
1 1/3  c. instant mashed potatoes (dry)
2  c. cut-up cooked chicken
2  c. shredded Swiss cheese (8 oz.)
2  c. milk
1/2  tsp. salt

      Heat broth, onion and frozen broccoli to boiling in 3-quart saucepan.  Reduce heat.  Cover and simmer 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Stir in potatoes until well blended.  Stir in remaining ingredients.  Heat over low heat about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until hot and cheese is melted.  Makes 6 servings, about 1 1/3 cup each.
