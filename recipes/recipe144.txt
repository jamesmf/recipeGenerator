TEXAS COOKIES


  whole graham crackers
2  sticks oleo
1  egg
2  Tbsp. milk
1  c. sugar
1/2  c. chopped nuts
1  c. coconut
1  c. graham cracker crumbs

      Line pan with butter (8 x 8-inch pan for regular recipe and 9 x 12-inch pan for 1 1/2 recipes).  Cover with whole graham crackers. Melt 2 sticks oleo.  Mix egg, milk and sugar together; add to melted oleo.  Bring to boil.  (Stir constantly to prevent burning.) Remove from heat; add nuts, coconut and crumbs.  Pour over the layer of graham crackers in the pan.  Top with whole graham crackers.  Pour icing over the mixture.  Chill 5 hours.  Freezes very well.  Make it the day before or make it ahead and freeze to use later.
Icing:
1/4  c. oleo
2  c. powdered sugar
2 to 4  Tbsp. milk
1  tsp. vanilla

      Combine all ingredients and pour atop graham crackers.
