FRESH BROCCOLI AND CAULIFLOWER SALAD


1  bunch broccoli
1  medium head cauliflower
1  medium onion

      Chop the broccoli, cauliflower and onion.
Dressing:
1  c. mayonnaise
2  Tbsp. vinegar
2  Tbsp. sugar
1 to 2  tsp. salt

      Mix all ingredients together and pour over salad.  Mix well before serving.
