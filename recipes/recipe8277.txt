STUFFED GREEN PEPPERS


4  green peppers
3/4  lb. ground beef
1/2  c. cooked rice
1  tsp. salt
  dash of pepper
1  egg
1/4  c. milk
1/3  c. onion, chopped
1 (10 1/2 oz.)  can tomato soup
3/4  c. water

	Remove seeds and wash peppers. Parboil 3 minutes. Combine rice, salt, pepper, ground beef, egg, milk and onion. Stuff peppers lightly and place in Mirro-Matic pan on rack. Add tomato soup and water. Cover pan. Set control at 10 and cook for 15 minutes after control jiggles. Reduce pressure normally for 5 minutes, then place pan under faucet. Serves 4.
