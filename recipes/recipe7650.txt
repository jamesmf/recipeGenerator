EASY FRIED CHICKEN


  salt, pepper, garlic powder
  buttermilk
  self-rising flour

      Skin chicken.  Sprinkle desired amount  of seasoning on each piece.  Dip in buttermilk; dredge in flour.  Put into hot oil (deep enough to cover  chicken  pieces.) Fry approximately 15 minutes on each side.  May also fry pork chops this way.
