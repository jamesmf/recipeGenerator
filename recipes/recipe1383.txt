FINGER LICKIN' PORK RIBS


1  large rack lean pork spareribs
1/4  lb. butter or margarine
1 (6 oz.)  bottle Louisiana hot sauce
1 (6 oz.)  bottle Worcestershire sauce
  garlic salt to taste

      Preheat gas grill on high for 15 minutes.  Lightly garlic salt both sides of ribs.  Put ribs on grill.  Close lid.  Rotate ribs frequently.
      Combine hot sauce, Worcestershire sauce, butter and garlic salt to taste in small pan.  Stir and simmer about 1/2 hour.
      When ribs are well browned, reduce heat on grill to low.  Brush ribs with sauce until meat pulls away from rib ends.
