CRANBERRY PECAN STUFFING


1/4  c. margarine
2  stalks celery, chopped
1  large onion, chopped
1  can (14 1/2 oz.) chicken broth
1  pkg. herb seasoned stuffing crumbs
1/2  c. cranberries
1/2  c. chopped pecans

      In a 4 quart saucepan, saut√© celery and onion in margarine until tender.  Add broth; heat to boiling.  Remove from heat; add stuffing, cranberries and pecans.  Mix well.  Use to stuff a 12 to 14 lb. turkey, or bake in a lightly greased baking dish at 400 degrees; until browned, about 20 to 25 minutes.
