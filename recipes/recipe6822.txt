MEAL IN ONE


1 1/2  lb. cubed stew meat
3  large carrots
2  stalks celery
3  medium potatoes, cut in half
3  Tbsp. oleo
1  envelope dry onion soup mix

      You will need foil.
      Use large sheet of foil.  Put into roaster.  Put all ingredients into foil and fold to seal so that juices cannot run out.  Place cover on top of roaster.  Bake in oven at 425 degrees; for 1 hour.  Reduce heat to 300 degrees; for 1 hour.
      Do not open foil during baking.
