PORK TENDERLOIN BAKE


2  pork tenderloins
2 to 4  slices bacon

 
Marinade:
1  Tbsp. onion, grated
1  clove garlic, crushed
1  Tbsp. wine vinegar
1/4  tsp. cayenne pepper
1/2  tsp. sugar
1/2  c. soy sauce

      Wrap tenderloins in bacon.  Marinate 2 hours or longer.  Bake, uncovered, at 300 degrees; for 2 hours.
