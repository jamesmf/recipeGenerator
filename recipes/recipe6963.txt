CORNBREAD DRESSING


1  c. yellow cornmeal
1  c. flour
1  tsp. salt
2/3  c. milk
2 1/2  tsp. baking powder
2  Tbsp. sugar (optional)
2  Tbsp. cooking oil
1  egg
1  large onion, chopped
1  can cream of celery soup
3  cans cream of chicken soup
2  cans of chicken broth
4  cans deboned chicken
6  hard boiled eggs, chopped
1  c. green onions, chopped

      Mix all ingredients with crumbled cornbread and bake about 45 minutes at 350 degrees;.
