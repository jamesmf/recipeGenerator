FAVORITE UNCOOKED TOMATO RELISH


1  lb. tomatoes, peeled and chopped
2  lb. celery, diced
1  large onion, chopped
1  c. salt
2  c. vinegar
6  c. sugar
2  red peppers, chopped
3  oz. mustard seed

      Combine tomatoes, celery, onion and salt; drain overnight in jelly bag.  The next day, add vinegar, sugar, peppers and mustard seed.  Stir.  Pour into sterilized glass jars with tight lids. Allow relish to age a minimum of 2 weeks before serving. Fills 12 to 16 small bell-style jars.
