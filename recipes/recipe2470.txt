YELLOW PARTY PUNCH


2 (6 oz.)  cans frozen orange juice
2 (16 oz.)  cans frozen lemonade
7  c. water
1 1/2  c. sugar
1  can pineapple juice
1  bottle ginger ale
1  bottle club soda

      Mix all ingredients except ginger ale and club soda.  Pour into containers and freeze.  Remove from freezer in time to get slushy at serving time.  Add equal amounts of ginger ale and club soda.
