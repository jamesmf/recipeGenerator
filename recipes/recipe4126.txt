SAUSAGE CHEESE APPETIZER


1  lb. hot sausage
1  lb. sharp cheese, grated
6  Tbsp. cracker meal
3  c. Bisquick

      Have all ingredients at room temperature.  Mix well.  Form into balls of desired size.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 12 to 15 minutes. Balls may be placed on cookie sheet and frozen.  Store in plastic bag in freezer.  May be baked without thawing.  Increase the baking time.
