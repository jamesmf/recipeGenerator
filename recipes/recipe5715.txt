LEBANESE POTATO SALAD


6  potatoes
1  clove garlic, crushed
1  bunch parsley, chopped
1/2  bunch fresh mint, chopped
1/3-1/2  c. salad oil
  Juice of 1 lemon
  Salt, pepper to taste

      Boil potatoes with skins on.  Do not overcook.  Cool until you can handle them.  Peel and salt while still warm.   Mix other ingredients thoroughly.  Add to diced potatoes.  Mix and chill until served.
