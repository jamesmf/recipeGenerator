SOUTHWESTERN CHICKEN SOUP


1-1/4  lbs. boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into thin strips
  1-2 Tbsp. vegetable oil
2 (14-1/2 oz.)  cans chicken broth
1 (16 oz)  pkg. frozen corn, thawed
1 (14-1/2 oz)  can diced tomatoes, undrained
1  medium onion, chopped
1  medium green pepper, chopped
1  medium sweet red pepper, chopped
1 (4 oz.)  can chopped green chilies
1-1/2  tsp. seasoned salt, optional
1  tsp. ground cumin
1/2  tsp. garlic powder

      In a large skillet, saut√© the chicken in oil until lightly browned. Transfer to a 5 quart slow cooker with a slotted spoon. Stir in the remaining ingredients.  Cover and cook on low for 7-8 hours.  Stir before serving.
