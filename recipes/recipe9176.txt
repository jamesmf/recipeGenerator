HAMBURGER CORN PONE CASSEROLE


1  medium onion, chopped
1/2  green pepper, chopped
1 1/2  lb. ground beef
1  can (8 oz.) tomato sauce
1  lb. can corn
1  pkg. Jiffy corn bread mix
1  egg
1/3  c. milk

      In skillet, brown onion, green pepper and ground beef; drain. Add tomato sauce, corn with juice, and salt and pepper to taste. Pour mixture in an 8 inch greased casserole dish.  Mix egg and milk together; add corn bread mix and mix well.  Spread on top of meat mixture in casserole dish.  Bake at 375 degrees; to 400 degrees; for 30 to 40 minutes.  Serves 4 to 6 people.
