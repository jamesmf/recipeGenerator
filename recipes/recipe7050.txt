SAUSAGE JAMBALAYA


3  lb. sausage, cut in small pieces
1  Tbsp. shortening
1/2  c. chopped bell pepper
1/2  c. chopped celery
4  c. water
  salt and pepper
3  c. raw rice
1  c. chopped onion
1  c. chopped green onions
2  Tbsp. tomato paste or 1/2 can tomato sauce

      Heat shortening; add onions, celery, peppers, and tomato sauce. Cook over medium heat in uncovered pot until onions are wilted. Cover and cook 15 minutes. Add sausage and green onions; cook 30 minutes. Add rice and water; cover and simmer until rice is done.
