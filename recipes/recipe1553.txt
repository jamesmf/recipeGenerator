BARBECUE RIB SAUCE


3 (28 oz.)  bottles Kraft barbecue sauce
1  lb. dark honey
1/2  lb. brown sugar
1/2  box chili powder
3/4  c. ketchup
1/2  bottle Worcestershire sauce
1/2 small  bottle Picka-Peppa sauce
  vinegar

      Mix all ingredients well.  Add enough vinegar to give sauce a bite.  Store in refrigerator.  Not necessary to cook.  Rub ribs with dry rubbing powder recipe first.
