MEXICALI DINNER BREAD


1  c. Quaker Masa Harina
1  c. all-purpose flour
1  pkg. taco seasoning mix
4  tsp. baking powder
1  Tbsp. sugar
1/2  tsp. salt
1  c. milk
1  egg
1/4  c. vegetable oil
1 (12 oz.)  can whole kernel corn, drained
1  c. shredded sharp Cheddar cheese

      Combine dry ingredients and add to combined milk, egg and oil. Mix just until dry mixture is moistened.  Stir in corn and cheese. Bake in a greased 9-inch square baking pan in a preheated 425 degrees; oven for about 30 minutes.
