AMERICAN CHOP SUEY


1  lb. hamburger
1  c. chopped onion
1  c. chopped celery (optional)
3  cloves chopped garlic
1  can Hunts spaghetti sauce
1  can chopped tomatoes
1  can tomatoes soup
1  lb. cooked macaroni
  salt and pepper to taste

      Brown hamburger with onion, celery and garlic.  Drain after cooking.  Mix spaghetti sauce with tomatoes and soup.  Add hamburger and cooked macaroni.  Can add salt or pepper if needed. Serves 6.
