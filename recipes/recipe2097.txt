ALMOND CLUSTERS


4  sq. vanilla flavored Candi-Quick
1 (12 oz.)  pkg. white chocolate chips
6  oz. pkg. milk chocolate chips
4  c. roasted almonds or peanuts

      Melt together in microwave chocolate chips and Candi-Quick according to directions on chocolate chip package.  Add roasted almonds and drop by teaspoonful on wax paper to set.  Very delicious.
