CHICKEN CORN CHOWDER


3/4  c. diced celery
3/4  c. chopped onion
1/4  c. butter
1 3/4  c. milk
2  cans (10 1/2 oz.) cream of chicken soup
1 (17 oz.)  can corn
1/2  tsp. basil
1/2  tsp. salt
  dash of pepper

      Saut√© celery and onions in butter until tender.  Add milk, soup, corn and seasonings.  Heat, stirring occasionally, until chowder is hot, but not boiling.  Makes 4 to 6 servings.
