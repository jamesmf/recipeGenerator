"CROCK-POT BAKED CHICKEN"


  whole 2 to 3 lb. chicken or "Pick of the Chick"
  salt and pepper
  herbs (any that you may like)
  lemon juice
  olive oil

      Wash chicken thoroughly; rub down with olive oil, salt and pepper. If you like herbs, sprinkle some on here. Spritz the chicken with lemon juice (as much or as little as you like). Dump it in the crock-pot. Cook on low all day or on high 5 to 6 hours.
