SPAGHETTI SLAW


2  cups broken spaghetti, cooked
3  cups cabbage, shredded
1  carrot, shredded
1  green pepper, chopped
1  cucumber, chopped
1  tomato, chopped

 
Sauce:
3/4  cup mayonnaise
2  Tbsp. vinegar
  sugar to taste
  salt to taste

      Mix vegetables with the cooked spaghetti.  Mix the sauce ingredients well.  Pour over vegetable mixture.  Refrigerate over night.
