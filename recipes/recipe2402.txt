RED VELVET CAKE


3  Tbsp. cocoa
2  bottles red food coloring
1 1/2  c. sugar
1/2  c. shortening
2  eggs
1  c. buttermilk
2 1/2  c. self-rising flour
1  tsp. vanilla
1  Tbsp. vinegar
1  tsp. soda

 
Icing:
1  (8 oz.) cream cheese
1  stick margarine
1  box powdered sugar
3  Tbsp. milk

      In mixing bowl, mix cocoa and food coloring together.  In another bowl, cream sugar and shortening together.  Then add food coloring mixture.  Blend in eggs.  Beat and add alternately buttermilk and flour.  Then add vanilla.  Mix with beater.  Then stir in by spoon vinegar and add soda.  Bake in greased and floured pans at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes.
      Icing:  Mix cream cheese and margarine.  Add powdered sugar and milk.
