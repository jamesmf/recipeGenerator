SALAD DRESSING FOR EGGS


1/2  c. white sugar
1 heaping  Tbsp. flour
1  c. milk
2  egg yolks
1  Tbsp. mustard
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  c. vinegar

      Put all ingredients except vinegar in a small saucepan; mix well and bring to a boil.  Add vinegar, stirring constantly.  Put in piece of butter the size of a walnut.  Boil until thick.  To serve, cut boiled eggs in half and pour sauce over them.
