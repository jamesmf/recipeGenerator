BUTTERMILK CANDY


1  c. buttermilk
1  tsp. soda
2  c. sugar
2  tsp. corn syrup
2  Tbsp. butter
1  tsp. vanilla
1  c. chopped nuts

      Add soda to buttermilk in a 4-quart saucepan.  Stir well and let stand one minute.  Add sugar, corn syrup and butter.  Cook to soft-ball stage, 236 degrees; to 238 degrees;.  Add vanilla and nuts.  Beat until begins to thicken.  Drop by teaspoon onto buttered cookie sheet.
      To vary:  Double the recipe and to half the finished product, add 1/2 cup coconut instead of nuts.  Use 9 x 9 x 2-inch pan.
