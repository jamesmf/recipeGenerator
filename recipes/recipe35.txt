CHOCOLATE LASSIES


1/2  c. shortening
3/4  c. sugar
1  egg, unbeaten
1/2  c. molasses
2 1/2  c. flour
1 1/2  tsp. baking soda
1/2  tsp. ginger
1/2  tsp. cinnamon
1  c. chocolate chips
1/2  c. chopped walnuts

      Cream shortening and sugar until fluffy.  Beat in egg and molasses.  Mix and sift flour, baking soda and spices; add.  Mix well.  Stir in chocolate chips and nuts.  Drop by teaspoonfuls on ungreased cookie sheets.  Bake 10 to 12 minutes at 375 degrees;.  Makes about 5 dozen cookies.
