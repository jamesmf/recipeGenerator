NOODLES AND APPLES


4  apples
4  Tbsp. butter, divided
2  c. cooked noodles
3/4  c. sugar
1  tsp. cinnamon

      Wash, core, peel and cut apples in 1/8's.  Melt 2 tablespoons butter in baking dish.  Place 1/2 of noodles in dish; then the apples.  Combine sugar and cinnamon.  Sprinkle with 1/2 of the sugar and cinnamon mixture.  Cover with remaining noodles.  Dot with remaining butter.  Sprinkle remaining sugar and cinnamon mixture on top.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 35 minutes.  Serves 4 to 6.
