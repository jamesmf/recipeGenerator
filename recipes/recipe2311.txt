POTATO PANCAKES


1  lb. white potatoes, peeled and shredded
1  medium size onion, shredded
1  medium size carrot, shredded
1/2  tsp. fresh lemon juice
1  egg, lightly beaten
1  Tbsp. all-purpose flour
1/2  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper
1/2  c. vegetable oil

      Combine potatoes, onion, carrot, lemon juice, egg, flour, salt and pepper in a bowl.  Heat oil in a large nonstick skillet.  Spoon 5 pancakes, 1 heaping tablespoonful each, into oil; cook 1 1/2 minutes per side.  Transfer to towel to drain.  Repeat with remaining mixture.  Serve with applesauce or sour cream if desired.
