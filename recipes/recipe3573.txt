CRAB-STUFFED MUSHROOMS


1  lb. fresh mushrooms, cleaned and stemmed
2  Tbsp. butter or margarine, melted
1 (7 oz.)  can crabmeat, drained and flaked
1/2  c. mayo
1/2  c. shredded Monterey Jack cheese
1  green onion top, chopped
1  Tbsp. snipped parsley (or dried)
3  drops bottled  hot pepper sauce
  salt and pepper to taste

      Brush mushroom caps with butter; set aside. Combine remaining ingredients. Fill caps. Place on broiler pan, filled side up. Broil 6-inches from heat about 5 minutes or until cheese is bubbly and golden and mushrooms are heated through.
