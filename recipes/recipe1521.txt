ICEBOX CHERRY PIE


1 (15 oz.)  can pie cherries
1  can Eagle Brand milk
3  medium lemons
1/2  pt. whipping cream, whipped well
1  c. chopped pecans

      Put Eagle Brand milk in a bowl.  Add lemon juice and cherries; stir well.  Fold in whipped cream and nuts.  Pour into a graham cracker crust.  Chill 2 hours.  Very rich, but delicious!
