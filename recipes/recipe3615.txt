GARLIC OYSTER CRACKERS


1  Tbsp. garlic salt
1  c. vegetable oil
1  pkg. Hidden Valley Ranch dressing mix
1  Tbsp. dill weed
2  pkg. small oyster crackers

	Mix first 4 ingredients. Toss into crackers and stir. (Use less garlic if you don't want a strong garlic taste.) To let flavors blend, leave to sit awhile before serving.
