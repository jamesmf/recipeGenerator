BAKED CHICKEN


4  split chicken breast
4  slices of bacon
1  can mushroom soup
1  c. sour cream
1  can chip beef

      Roll and wrap each chicken breast in one piece of bacon.  Place chip beef in bottom of a baking dish and lay chicken breast on top. Mix soup and sour cream together; pour over chicken breasts.  Bake for 3 hours at 300 degrees;.
