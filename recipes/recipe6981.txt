CHICKEN AND SPAGHETTI


1 1/2  sticks margarine
16  oz. spaghetti
1  onion, chopped
1/2  c. bell pepper, chopped
3/4  c. celery, chopped
1  fryer, cooked and deboned
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  can tomato soup
2  c. chicken broth
  garlic powder, to taste
  mild cheddar cheese

      Saut√© celery, bell pepper, and onions in margarine for about 15 minutes. Add broth and soups; add salt, pepper, and garlic powder. Add chicken to mixture and simmer while cooking spaghetti. Drain spaghetti; add to sauce mixture and mix well. Place in large baking dish and cover with shredded cheese. Cover with foil and let set till cheese is melted.
