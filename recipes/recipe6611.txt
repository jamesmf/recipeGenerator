TURKEY BREAST IN A BAG


1  thawed turkey breast (large)
1  c. apple cider vinegar
1/2  c. oil (olive or vegetable oil)
3  Tbsp. parsley flakes
1/4  c. salt
1/4  c. black pepper

      You will need 1 large cooking bag (Reynolds brand).
      Rinse turkey breast with cold water. Pat dry with paper towels. Follow directions for baking bag (as shown on package). Place turkey breast in prepared bag. Mix vinegar, oil, parsley flakes, salt and pepper together until blended well. Pour over turkey inside bag. Close bag as directed. Bake at temperature suggested in cooking bag directions and for time suggested per pound. Tender, juicy and delicious!
