GREEK EASTER COOKIES


1  c. butter or margarine
1/3  c. granulated sugar
2  egg yolks
1/2  tsp. brandy flavoring (if desired)
1  tsp. vanilla
2  c. flour
1  tsp. baking powder
  confectioners sugar

      Heat oven to 350 degrees;.  Mix the butter or margarine, sugar, egg yolks, brandy and vanilla together thoroughly.  Measure flour by sifting.  Blend flour and baking powder.  Stir into butter mixture; mix well.  Shape dough in small balls about 3/4-inch in diameter. Place cookies on a lightly greased baking sheet.  Bake 10 to 12 minutes or until cookies are set, but not browned.  Let stand 2 minutes before removing from cookie sheet.  When cool, dust with confectioners sugar and place a whole clove in center of each cookie.  Makes 8 dozen.
