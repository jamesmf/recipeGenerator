SMOTHERED FRESH CORN


1  Tbsp. cooking oil
2  medium onions, chopped fine
1 (8 oz.)  can tomato sauce
4  c. fresh corn or 3 cans whole kernel
3/4  tsp. sugar
1/4  tsp. cayenne pepper
1  tsp. salt to taste

	Saute onions in oil until brown, about 10 minutes. Add 1/4 cup water and cook for 5 minutes. Stir in tomato sauce and simmer 20 minutes. Add 1/2 cup water and cook for 10 minutes. Add corn, water, sugar and salt. Cook for about 40 minutes. Add pepper to taste.
	Remember, 3 cans whole kernel corn can be used in this recipe. Do not drain corn if this is what you are doing.
