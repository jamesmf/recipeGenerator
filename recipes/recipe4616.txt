ORANGE PINEAPPLE CONGEALED SALAD


2 large  pkg. orange jello
1 large  can crushed pineapple
1  tub Cool Whip
1  qt. of buttermilk
1  c. chopped pecans, optional

      In large sauce pan, combine jello and pineapple, with juice. Heat to a low boil.  Stir in Cool Whip, stirring constantly.  When Cool Whip is dissolved, remove from heat, add pecans.  Pour into 13 x 9 glass casserole or large jello mold.  Refrigerate for 3 hours.
