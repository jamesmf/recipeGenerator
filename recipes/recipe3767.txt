CHICKEN LIVERS


1  lb. bacon
  Container chicken livers
  French dressing
  Garlic powder
  Garlic salt
  Water chestnut, opt.

      Slice bacon strip into thirds.  Cut livers into 1 inch pieces. Marinate livers in French dressing and garlic salt for 15 mins.   Place liver in 1 piece of bacon with a slice of chestnut. Wrap up and secure with toothpick. Place on a wire rack sprayed with Pam.  Place another pan under grill to catch bacon drippings. Bake at 400 degrees; until bacon is crispy, usually 35-45 mins.
