CHRISTMAS DRESSING


1/2  lb. pure pork sausage
1  c. celery, finely chopped
1  c. onion, finely chopped
1  c. green pepper, finely chopped
1  stick margarine
6  chicken bouillon cubes
4  c. hot water
5  c. toasted white bread crumbs
6  c. crumbled cornbread
1  tsp. poultry seasoning
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. black pepper
4  eggs, slightly beaten
1  c. pecans, chopped finely

      In heavy skillet, sauté celery, onion and green pepper until tender.  Dissolve bouillon cubes in hot water; pour over bread crumbs and cornbread in large bowl.  Add sautéed vegetables.  Brown sausage in heavy skillet and add to bread mixture.  Combine remaining ingredients with mixture; toss lightly to mix well.  Form into 2-inch balls and bake for 30 minutes at 350 degrees;.  Garnish turkey platter with the balls.
