EGG NOODLES WITH GERMAN MEATBALLS


1  lb. ground beef
1  slice white bread, cubed
2  medium onions, grated
1  tsp. salt
  dash of pepper
1/2  c. firmly packed brown sugar
3/4  c. raisins
3 1/2  c. undrained sauerkraut
8  gingersnaps
2 (8 oz. each)  cans tomato sauce
8  oz. egg noodles (rigatoni), cooked and drained

      Combine beef, bread, half of onions, salt and pepper.  Shape into 1-inch balls.  In Dutch oven or large pot, combine remaining onions, sugar, raisins, sauerkraut, gingersnaps and tomato sauce. Add meatballs and simmer 1 1/2 hours.  Serve over cooked and drained noodles.  Serves 4.
