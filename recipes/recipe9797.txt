PINEAPPLE HAM LOAF


2  eggs
1/2  cup milk
1  cup finely crushed saltines
1/4  tsp. pepper
1  1/2 pounds ground fully cooked ham
1  pound ground fresh pork

 
Sauce:
1  cup packed brown sugar
1  tsp prepared mustard
1/3  cup vinegar
1/4  cup water
8  ounce can crushed pineapple undrained

      In a large bowl, beat eggs; add milk, crackers, and pepper. Add ham and pork, mix well.  Form into a 9 by 4 inch loaf.  Place in a shallow baking dish.
      In a small bowl, combine all ingredients for the sauce.  Pour over loaf.
      Bake at 350 degrees, until a meat thermometer registers 170 degrees, about 1 1/2 hours.  Baste frequently.  Enjoy!
      Yields 6-8 servings.
