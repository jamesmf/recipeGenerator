PARTY FINGERS


1  1/3 softened butter
5  Tbsp. powdered sugar
  pinch of salt
1  c. chopped pecans
2  c. plain flour
2  tsp. ice water
1  tsp. vanilla

	Cream butter and sugar. Add salt and pecans. Add flour, water and vanilla alternately. Shape into fingers or balls. Cook 30 to 40 minutes at 300 degrees; until lightly brown. Remove from cookie sheet and roll in powdered sugar.
