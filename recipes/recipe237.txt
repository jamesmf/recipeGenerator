DANISH COOKIES #1


1  stick butter
1/4  c. powdered sugar
1 1/2  c. sifted flour
1/8  tsp. salt
1  tsp. vanilla
1 small  jar maraschino cherries

      Mix all ingredients except cherries.  Take teaspoon of dough and wrap cherry in each.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 10 minutes.  Glaze.
Glaze:
1  c. powdered sugar
2  tsp. heavy cream
1  tsp. vanilla
1  sq. chocolate (add, if you want chocolate instead of vanilla)
