MACARONI AND CHEESE CASSEROLE


1  lb. Velveeta cheese
1 small  jar pimento pepper
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  can milk
1  c. mayonnaise
1/4  tsp. black pepper
1/2  tsp. salt
1  small onion
1/4  stick margarine
9  oz. shell  macaroni
  Ritz crackers
  margarine

      Sauté onion in butter. Cook macaroni as instructed on package. In large container, mix cheese, pimento, mushroom soup, milk, mayonnaise, pepper and salt. Mix with mixer. Add sautéed onions and cooked macaroni. Stir with spoon until all ingredients are mixed well. Pour into large casserole dish. Top with Ritz cracker topping and cook at 350 degrees; for 30 to 35 minutes.
      Topping: Crush Ritz crackers and stir in a little melted margarine.
