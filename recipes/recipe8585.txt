STROMBOLI


1  lb. Ground Beef, cooked and drained
1  lb. Sausage, cooked and drained
1  (16 oz. jar) Pizza Sauce
  Swiss Cheese, sliced
6  Hoagie Buns

      Combine ground beef, sausage and pizza sauce in crock pot. Let cook until heated through and through.  Spread butter on both sides of hoagies buns and place on cookie sheet.  Add cheese. Place in heated oven until cheese and butter is melted. Serve meat mixture on top of hoagie buns.
      Use lots of napkins. This is delicious, but messy.
