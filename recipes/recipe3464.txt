CUCUMBER RELISH


4  c. chopped cucumbers
2  large chopped onions
2  bell peppers, chopped
1/4  c. salt
3 1/2  c. sugar
2  c. cider vinegar
1  Tbsp. celery seeds
1  Tbsp. mustard seeds

      Combine the first 4 ingredients and soak in ice water for 2 hours. Drain and add all remaining ingredients. Cook for 10 minutes. Seal in jars.
