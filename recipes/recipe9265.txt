CHICKEN AND RICE


1/2  stick margarine
1  c. converted rice
6  pieces chicken
  salt and pepper
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  can water
1  pkg. dry onion soup mix

      Place in covered roasting pan in order listed (or cover pan with aluminum foil).  Bake 1/2 hour at 350 degrees;, then 2 hours or longer at 325 degrees;.
