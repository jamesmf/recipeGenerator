BAKED CHICKEN IN SHERRY


1/2  lb. (1 stick) butter
8  chicken breasts
10  oz. (1 can) soup, cream of mushroom
1  c. cooking sherry
  parsley flakes

      Put chicken and butter in a 9x13-inch dish.
      Bake uncovered at 400 degrees; for 15 minutes until brown.  Turn chicken to brown other side.  Continue baking for another 15 minutes.
      Mix together soup and sherry.  Pour over browned chicken. Sprinkle with parsley flakes.
      Cover with foil and bake at 400 degrees; for 1 hour, or until tender.
