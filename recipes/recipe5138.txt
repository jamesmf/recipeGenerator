FLUFFY FRUIT SALAD


2 (20 oz.)  cans crushed pineapple
1/4  c. orange juice
3  Tbsp. lemon juice
1  Tbsp. vegetable oil
2/3  c. sugar
2  cans (17 oz.) fruit cocktail, drained
2 (11 oz.)  cans mandarin oranges, drained
2  Tbsp. all-purpose flour
2  eggs, lightly beaten
2  bananas, sliced
1  c. heavy whipped cream

      Drain pineapple, reserving 1 cup juice in a small saucepan. Set pineapple aside. To saucepan, add sugar, flour, eggs, orange juice, lemon juice and oil. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly. Boil for 1 minute. Remove from heat. Cool.
      In a salad bowl, combine pineapple, fruit cocktail, oranges and bananas. Fold in whipped cream and the cooled sauce. Chill for several hours.
