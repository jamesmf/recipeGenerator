CREAMY HOT BEEF DIP


1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese, softened
1  c. (8 oz.) sour cream
3  oz. dried beef, rinsed and finely chopped
2  Tbsp. chopped green pepper
1 1/2  Tbsp. minced onion
1  tsp. dried green pepper flakes (optional)
1/2  tsp. garlic powder
  pepper to taste
  raw vegetables or crackers

      Combine first 8 ingredients in a 1-quart baking dish. Bake at 375 degrees;, uncovered, for 30 minutes or until hot and bubbly. Serve with raw vegetables or crackers. Yield: About 2 cups.
