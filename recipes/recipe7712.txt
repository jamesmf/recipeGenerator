CORNED BEEF CASSEROLE


1 (12 oz.)  can Libby's corned beef
1  can mushroom soup
1  can milk
1 1/2  c. grated cheese (Cheddar)
1 medium size  pkg. egg noodles
2  slices bread

      Boil noodles. Mix meat, soup, milk and 1 cup cheese. Pour over noodles. Dice and crumble bread on top and sprinkle with remaining cheese. Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 to 35 minutes.
