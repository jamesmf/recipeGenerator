MARINADE FOR BEEF


1/2  c. Wesson oil
1/2  c. red wine or wine vinegar
2  Tbsp. A.1. steak sauce
1/2  tsp. onion powder
1/4  tsp. garlic powder
1/2  tsp. thyme
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
1  tsp. sugar
  London Broil or sirloin tip roast

      For marinade combine first 9 ingredients.  Pour over beef; cover and refrigerate for several hours or overnight.  Cook beef on a grill, using the marinade as a basting sauce.
