HOLIDAY CHEESE BALL


2 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese, softened
1 small  can crushed pineapple, drained
1/2  green pepper, chopped
2  Tbsp. onion, chopped
2  Tbsp. seasoning salt
2  c. pecans, chopped

      Soften cheese at room temperature. Cream and gradually stir in drained crushed pineapple until smooth. Add 1 cup of pecans, green pepper, onion and salt. Chill mixture well. Form into a ball and roll in remaining cup of pecans. Chill until serving time. Garnish with maraschino cherries and parsley. Serve with assortment of crackers.
