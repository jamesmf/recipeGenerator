CREOLE GUMBO


6  crabs
2  doz. oysters
2  doz. shrimp
2  doz. okra
4  stems large celery
3/4  c. tomato pasta
2  small bell peppers
1 1/2  c. shortening
  salt and pepper to tate
1  Tbsp. hot sauce

	Chop seasoning and saut lightly. Make roux with flour and shortening. Put 3 quarts of water in pot and bring to boil. Add tomato paste and roux to boiling water. Add seasonings and crabs. Cook for about 1 hour. Add shrimp and oysters about 10 minutes before cooking is finished.
