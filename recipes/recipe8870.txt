HAY AND STRAW


  pasta (1 bag rotini; some fettucine if you have it)
1/2  lb. sausage
2 heaping  tsp. crushed garlic
  about 2 tsp. olive oil
1  c. Parmesan cheese
1/4  c. milk
2  Tbsp. butter

      Cook the pasta per package instructions. Put oil and garlic in pan and cook together for about 5 minutes. Add sausage and brown. Drain sausage grease. Return to hot pan which should have about 1/4 cup milk, 2 tablespoons butter and cooked pasta. Mix until butter is melted. Add 1 cup Parmesan. Serve.
