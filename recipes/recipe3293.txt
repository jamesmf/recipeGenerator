MEXICAN FUDGE


10  oz. grated Cheddar cheese
10  oz. grated Monterey Jack cheese
2  eggs
1  c. milk
1  c. flour
3/4  c. sliced jalapeno canned peppers

      Mix cheeses together. Spread in 9 x 13-inch pan. Beat eggs, milk and flour together. Pour over cheese. Sprinkle peppers on top. Bake for 30 minutes at 350 degrees;. Cut into squares. Good hot or cold.
