SQUASH CASSEROLE


2 1/2  lb. squash
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. black pepper
1  tsp. sugar
1  egg, beaten
1/2  c. green pepper
3/4  c. chopped pecans
1/2  c. diced onions
1/2  c. mayonnaise
1  c. Cheddar cheese
3/4  stick butter, cut in squares

      Cook squash until done, then mash with 1/2 cup or more of water.  Mix in all ingredients.  Sprinkle with cracker crumbs and top with paprika.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 35 to 40 minutes.
