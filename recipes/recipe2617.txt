SOUR CREAM CORN BREAD


1  c. self-rising corn meal
1 small  can cream-style corn
3  eggs
1/2  small sour cream
  little sugar and salt to taste

      Mix  corn  meal  and eggs;  add corn and mix.  Add sour cream, salt  and  sugar and mix.  Put in greased casserole or bread  pan and cook until done, approximately 20 to 25 minutes at 350 degrees;.
      Note:  More sour cream can be added according to taste.
