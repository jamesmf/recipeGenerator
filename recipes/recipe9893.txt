TATER TOT CASSEROLE


1  lb. ground beef
1  onion, chopped (optional)
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  can Cheddar cheese soup (optional)
1  c. milk
  salt and pepper to taste
1  pkg. grated cheese
1  pkg. frozen Tater Tots

      In skillet, brown meat and onion; drain. In large mixing bowl, combine all other ingredients (except grated cheese). Mix well and add meat mixture to soup/Tot mixture. Put in casserole dish and bake in oven at 375 degrees; for 45 minutes. In the last 5 to 10 minutes, pull out and add cheese to top; put back in oven.
