SPLIT PEA SOUP


1  (16 oz.) bag split peas
2 (14 1/2 oz.)  cans chicken broth
1  small onion, chopped
  water (enough to make 3 qt. liquid)
2  c. ham, cooked and chunked
  salt, pepper and garlic powder to taste
  carrots and celery (as desired)

	Bring all ingredients to simmer and cook until peas are soft, adding water as needed. Add ham chunks the last 1/2 hour. Add carrots and celery as desired.
