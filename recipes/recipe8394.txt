CHICKEN GREEN NOODLE CASSEROLE


1  (3 1/2 lb.) chicken or chicken breasts
1  c. bell pepper, chopped
1  c. onion, chopped
1  c. celery, chopped
1  stick margarine
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1/2  lb. American cheese, cubed
1 (6 oz.)  can sliced mushrooms
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. green noodles
1  c. cheese crackers, crushed

      Stew hen.  Reserve stock.  Cut chicken into bite-size pieces. Saut pepper, onion and celery in margarine.  Add mushrooms and chicken.  Boil noodles in reserved stock; drain well.  Combine with chicken mixture and pour into 2-quart casserole.  Top with crumbs.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 to 45 minutes or until well heated.
