CREOLE FRIED TURKEY INJECTION RECIPE


1/2  c. Tony Chachere's original Creole seasoning*
1/2  c. garlic powder
1/2  c. vegetable oil
2  c. water
2  Tbsp. Butter Buds (powder butter)
2  Tbsp. onion powder
2  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce

      Blend all ingredients together in a blender or food processor.  Use injector to inject throughout thawed turkey. Deep-fry turkey 3 minutes per pound.
      *Also a great rub on the outside of the turkey.  Rub with caution, it's hot!
