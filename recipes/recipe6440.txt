LAYERED SALAD


1  head of lettuce broken in bite size pieces
1  head of cauliflower
1  bunch of broccoli broken in bite sized pieces
1  medium onion sliced
1  pound bacon, fried until crisp, drained and crumbled

 
Dressing:
1/4  cup sugar
1/3  cup grated Parmesan cheese
1  cup mayonnaise

      Mix dressing well.  Put lettuce in a large bowl that has a lid, then add cauliflower, broccoli, and onion.  Cover with the dressing.  Sprinkle with the bacon.  Seal bowl with lid and refrigerate for 24 hours.  Toss and serve.
