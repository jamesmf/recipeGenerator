NELLE'S CHICKEN-BROCCOLI CASSEROLE


1 -10  oz. pkg. frozen broccoli
3  Tbsp. chopped onion
  4-5 cooked boneless chicken breasts
1 1/2  c. mayonnaise
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  can mushroom soup
3  eggs, beaten
1  Tbsp. lemon juice
1  Tbsp. melted butter
1/2  c. grated cheese
  croutons
  potato sticks
  salt and pepper

      Steam broccoli till tender; drain.  Add lightly beaten eggs, mayonnaise, lemon juice, onion, salt and pepper, soups, and 1 cup of cheese; mix well.  Cut chicken into bite size pieces; put into a casserole dish; then broccoli.  Spread soup mixture over broccoli and chicken.  Put 1/2 cup of cheese on top.  Top with croutons and potato sticks.
      Cook at 340 degrees; for 45 minutes.  Serves 8-12.
