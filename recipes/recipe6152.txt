PINK DESSERT


1 #2  can crushed pineapple
1 6  oz. pkg. strawberry Jello
1/2  c. sugar
1 1/2  c. water
1  lb. small curd cottage cheese
1 8  oz. Cool Whip

      Put pineapple with juice, sugar, water and dry Jello in pan. Bring to a good boil.  Cool.  When partially set, add cottage cheese and Cool Whip.  Mix well.  Refrigerate.
