BACON APPETIZERS


1  lb. bacon
1  c. mayonnaise
8  oz. Gouda cheese, grated
  cocktail rye bread

      Fry bacon until crisp; drain on paper towels, then crumble. Mix bacon, mayonnaise and cheese together.  Place mixture on cocktail rye bread.  Heat until cheese is bubbly at 350 degrees;.  Yields about 20 appetizers.
