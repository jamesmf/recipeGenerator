GRILLED HOT DOGS


  hot dogs
  onion
  sliced cheese
  bacon

      Slit hot dogs lengthwise.  Fill with finely chopped onion.  Cut cheese in 1/4-inch strips and place on top of onions.  Wrap with a slice of bacon and pin with toothpick.  Place on shallow pan and put under broiler or place on outside grill.  Broil until bacon is crisp.
