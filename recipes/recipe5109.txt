NEW YEAR'S DAY SOUP


1  c. diced smoked lean ham
2  celery ribs, chopped
1  medium onion, chopped
2  carrots, chopped
2  garlic cloves, minced
2 (15 oz.)  cans undrained black-eyed peas
2 (14 1/2 oz.)  cans low-sodium, fat-free chicken broth
2 (14 1/2 oz.)  cans no-salt added stewed tomatoes (undrained)
1 (14 1/2 oz.)  can no-salt added diced tomatoes (undrained)
1 (8 oz.)  can tomato sauce
1 1/2  c. fresh spinach
1/2  c. fresh parsley
1/2  Tbsp. pepper

      Saut√© ham, celery, onion, carrots and garlic in Dutch oven until tender. Stir in black-eyed peas, chicken broth, stewed tomatoes, diced tomatoes and tomato sauce. Bring to a boil. Cover, reduce heat and simmer 1 hour and 30 minutes. Stir in spinach, parsley and pepper.
