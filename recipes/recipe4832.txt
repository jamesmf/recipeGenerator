CABBAGE CRUNCH SALAD


4  Tbsp. sesame seeds, toasted lightly
1  c. slivered almonds, toasted lightly
1  pkg. rainbow slaw or broccoli slaw (sold at Meijers)
4  to 8 green onions, chopped
2  pkg. chicken flavored Ramen noodles, broken up in pieces

      Toss everything together.  Just before serving, add dressing.
Dressing:
4  Tbsp. sugar
1  c. oil (vegetable or canola)
6  Tbsp. vinegar
1  tsp. pepper
1  pkg. chicken flavoring (from Ramen noodles)

      Shake well and pour over salad.
