BAKED PARMESAN CHICKEN


10  boneless, skinless chicken breasts
4 1/2  c. bread crumbs
1 1/4  c. grated Parmesan cheese
1/3  c. parsley
1 1/2  c. butter
1  clove garlic
1  Tbsp. Dijon-style mustard
1 1/2  tsp. Worcestershire sauce

      Mix together crumbs, cheese and parsley.  Melt butter. Add garlic, mustard and Worcestershire sauce; stir well.  Let butter mixture cool, but not congeal.  Dip chicken in butter, then crumbs.  Bake 1 1/4 hours at 350 degrees;.
