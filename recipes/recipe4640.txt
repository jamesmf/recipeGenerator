KIELBASA SOUP


3  qts water
1-1/2  lbs beef kielbasa, cut in 1/4 inch slices
3  stalks celery, cut in small pieces
3  med onions, cut into 8 pieces each
4  med potatoes, peeled and cut into cubes
3  carrots, cut into small pieces
1/2  c barley
1/2  c lentils
1/2  c split peas
28 oz  can of whole or diced tomatoes
15 oz  can beef bouillon broth
2  beef bouillon cubes
1-1/2  tsp oregano
1-1/2  tsp salt
1/2  tsp fresh ground pepper

      Add all ingredients except potatoes to the boiling water. When water boils again, reduce to medium heat and simmer for 1-1/2 hours. When carrots are tender, add potatoes. Cook until potatoes are tender.
       Serve with a crusty bread and butter.
