ALBONDIGAS SOUP
(Mexican Meatball)  

1/2  tsp. dried mint leaves

 
Albondigas:
3/4  lb. ground beef
1  egg, well beaten
1/3  c. raw rice
3/4  tsp. crushed coriander
1/2  small onion, chopped
1/8  tsp. garlic powder
1  tsp. salt
2  tsp. red chili powder

 
Broth:
1 1/2  qt. beef stock
7  oz. tomato sauce

      Mix albondigas ingredients together.  Shape into little balls. Drop into boiling broth.  Cover tightly and cook about 30 minutes. Add mint about 10 minutes before soup is done.  Serve with tortilla chips.
