CHICKEN ENCHILADAS


4  lb. chicken, boiled and deboned
1  large onion, chopped
2  cans cream of chicken soup
  salt, to taste
  pepper, to taste
  oregano, to taste
16  oz. sour cream
1 large  can mushrooms, sliced
1  can green chillies, sliced
3  pkg. flour tortillas
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  pt. half and half
16  oz. sour cream
1  lb. grated Cheddar cheese
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  pt. half and half
8  oz. sour cream
1  lb. grated Cheddar cheese

      To make topping combine cream of chicken soup, half and half, sour cream, and cheese. Pour over top of tortillas. Bake at 350 degrees; for 35 minutes or until cheese topping is cooked into tortillas.
