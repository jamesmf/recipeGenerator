BLT MACARONI SALAD


2  c. elbow macaroni (uncooked)
5  green onions, finely chopped
1  large tomato, diced
1 1/4  c. celery, diced
1 1/4  c. mayonnaise
5  tsp. white vinegar
1/4  tsp. salt
1/8 to 1/4  tsp. pepper
1  lb. bacon, cooked and crumbled

	Cook macaroni; drain and rinse in cold water. In a large bowl, combine the macaroni, green onions, tomato and celery. In a small bowl, combine mayonnaise, vinegar, salt and pepper. Pour over macaroni mixture and toss to coat. Cover and chill for at least 2 hours. Just before serving, add bacon. Yield: 12 servings.
