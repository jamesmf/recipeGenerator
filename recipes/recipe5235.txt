PINK CLOUD SALAD


1  can cherry pie filling
1 medium  can crushed pineapple, well drained
1  container (8 oz.) Cool Whip
1  can Eagle Brand sweetened condensed milk
1  c. miniature marshmallows (optional)
1/2  c. chopped pecans

      Chill:  milk, pineapple, cherry pie filling and Cool Whip. Chill well.  Mix all ingredients and refrigerate overnight or at least 2 hours.
