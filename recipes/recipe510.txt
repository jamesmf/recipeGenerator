BROWNIES


1 1/3  c. flour (plain)
1  tsp. baking powder
2/3  c. cocoa
1  c. melted butter
2  c. sugar
3  eggs
1  tsp. vanilla

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees;.  Sift together first 3 dry ingredients. Mix melted butter, sugar, eggs and vanilla together until thick and yellow; add dry ingredients gradually, mixing well after each addition.  Pour into greased and floured pan and bake at 350 degrees; about 30 minutes or until brownies test done.
