CHICKEN NOODLE CASSEROLE


1  Chicken, boiled and deboned
1  can Cream of Mushroom Soup
1  can Chicken Noodle Soup
8  oz. Sour Cream
1/2  stick Margarine
2  tubes Ritz Crackers

      Melt margarine in 9x13 in. pan.  Crumble one tube of crackers in margarine.  Mix soups, sour cream and chicken, and pour over crackers.  Top with the other tube of crackers.  Lay a few parts of margarine on top of crackers.  Bake at 350 degrees for 40-45 minutes.
