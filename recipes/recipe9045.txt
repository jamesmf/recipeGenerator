SPECTACULAR CHICKEN


4  c. cooked chicken
1  can French-cut green beans
1  can cream of celery soup
2  c. cooked rice
1 small  jar pimiento
1  onion, diced
1  c. mayonnaise
1  tsp. curry (optional)

      Mix all ingredients and put in greased casserole. Bake at 300 degrees; for 1 hour. Serves 12.
