SALMON STEAKS


3  fresh or frozen salmon steaks (or other fish steaks 1 to 1 1/2-inches thick)
1/2  c. cooking oil
1/4  c. snipped parsley
1/4  c. lemon juice
1  tsp. dry mustard
1/4  tsp. salt

      Thaw fish if frozen.  Put in a shallow pan for marinade. Combine oil, parsley, lemon juice, onion, mustard, salt and dash of pepper.  Pour over fish.  Marinate covered in refrigerator 6 hours. Drain, reserving marinade.  Place fish in well-greased wire grill basket.  Grill over medium coals 8 to 10 minutes or until lightly brown.  Brush with marinade and grill 8 to 10 minutes more.
