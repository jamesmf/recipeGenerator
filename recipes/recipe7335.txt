ZAGREB PORK CHOPS


5  or 6 butterfly pork chops
1  pkg. onion soup mix
1/3  jar apricot preserves
1  bottle Russian dressing
1  clove garlic

      Mix dressing, onion soup mix, chopped up garlic and apricot preserves. Pour mixture over salted and peppered pork chops. Use a greased 9-inch Corning Ware dish. Bake, uncovered, for 1 hour at 375 degrees;. Serve over rice or egg noodles.
