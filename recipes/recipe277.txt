RHUBARB CRUNCH


1/4  c. flour
1  c. granulated sugar
5  c. diced rhubarb
1  c. rolled oats
1 1/2  c. flour
1  c. brown sugar, packed
1/2  c. butter or oleo

      Heat oven to 350 degrees;.  Combine 1/4 cup flour and granulated sugar; mix with rhubarb.  Put into buttered 13 x 9 x 2-inch baking dish. Combine oats, 1 1/2 cups flour and brown sugar.  Cut in butter. Crumble over top of rhubarb.  Bake 45 minutes.  Serve warm or cool with whipped cream.
