MEXICAN DIP OLE


2  lb. sausage, mild or hot
10  oz. (1 can) soup, chili-beef
10  oz. (1 can) soup, nacho cheese
16  oz. (1 can) refried beans
10  oz. (1 can) diced tomatoes and green chilies
1  c. Cheddar cheese, shredded

      Brown sausage in a skillet, stirring until crumbly.  Drain and set aside.
      Combine soups, refried beans and tomatoes and green chilies in bowl, mixing well.  Stir in sausage.  Spoon into a 9x13-inch baking dish.  Sprinkle with cheese.
      Bake at 350 degrees; for 25 minutes or until bubbly.
      Makes 36 servings.
