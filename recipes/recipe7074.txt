BREAKFAST CASSEROLE


3  potatoes, cooked and sliced
1/4  c. minced onion
1/2  c. cottage cheese
1/2  tsp. salt
6  slices bacon or 12 oz. sausage
1  c. Swiss cheese, shredded
5  eggs, beaten

      Fry bacon and onion together. Place in bowl and mix in the rest of the ingredients. Bake in 9 x 9 pan at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes.
