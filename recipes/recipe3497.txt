PEPPERY HOT WINGS


2  pounds chicken wings
  Vegetable oil
2  Tbsp. margarine or butter, melted
1  Tbsp. vinegar
  2-3 tsp. red pepper sauce
1/4  tsp. salt
2  tsp. cayenne pepper

      Heat oil (2-3 inches) in deep fryer.  Fry wings, 4-6 at a time, turning occasionally, until golden brown and done, 8-10 minutes.  Drain.  Keep warm in 275 degrees;F oven while frying remaining wings.
      Mix margarine, vinegar, pepper sauce, cayenne pepper and salt in bowl until well blended.  Add wings and toss until evenly coated with pepper sauce mixture.
      Serve with Blue cheese Dressing if desired.
      About 24 appetizers.
