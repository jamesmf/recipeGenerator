THUMBPRINT COOKIES


2/3  c. butter
1/2  c. sugar
2  egg yolks
1  tsp. vanilla
1/2  tsp. salt
1 1/2  c. flour
2  egg whites, lightly beaten
1/2  c. nuts (walnuts or pecans)

      Combine butter, sugar, egg yolks, vanilla, salt and flour. Roll into 1-inch balls.  Dip in egg whites.  Roll in nuts.  Place on greased cookie sheet.  Make thumbprint.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 15 minutes.  Fill with your favorite filling (jams, jellies, decorator frostings, etc.).
