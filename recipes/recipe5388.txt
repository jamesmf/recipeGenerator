BROCCOLI AND CAULIFLOWER SALAD


1  head cauliflower
1  medium onion
1  bunch broccoli
1  green pepper
  salt and pepper to taste

	Cut vegetables into small pieces.
Dressing:
1  c. mayonnaise
1/2  c. sugar
1/4  c. oil
1  c. sour cream
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce

	Mix. Add to vegetables. Let stand 2 hours or more.
