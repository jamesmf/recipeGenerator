SWEET PEPPER RELISH


12  large red peppers
12  large green peppers
12  large onions (6)
4  c. vinegar
4  c. sugar
2  Tbsp. mustard seeds (heaping)
2  Tbsp. salt

      Remove seeds from peppers and peel onions. Put peppers and onions through medium blade of food chopper or grinder. Place in saucepan and cover with boiling water. Let stand 3 minutes. Drain well. Cover again with boiling water. Let stand 10 minutes, then drain. Add vinegar, sugar, mustard seed and salt. Bring to boil. Simmer all for 10 minutes. Pour into hot sterilized jars and seal.
