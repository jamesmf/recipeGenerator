PEA CHOWDER


1  c. dried split peas
1/2  onion, chopped
4  c. water
1  baking potato, diced
1  tsp. salt
1  c. evaporated milk
1  Tbsp. margarine

      Bring peas, onion and water to a boil in a large pot over medium heat. Cook 20 minutes. Add potato and salt. Cook 20 minutes or until peas and potato are soft. Add evaporated milk and margarine. Heat and eat. Serves 6.
