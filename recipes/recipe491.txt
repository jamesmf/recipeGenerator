WACKY CAKE


2  c. flour
2  c. sugar
2  tsp. soda
1  tsp. salt
6  Tbsp. cocoa
2  c. water
2  tsp. vanilla
2  Tbsp. vinegar
10  Tbsp. oil

      Mix first 5 dry ingredients together in large bowl, then make a well in center.  Add liquids.  Mix well.  Bake in 9 x 13-inch pan at 350 degrees; for 35 to 40 minutes.  Use your favorite icing.
