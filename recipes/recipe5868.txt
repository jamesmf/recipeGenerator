TACO SOUP


2  lb. ground beef
2  cans hominy
1  can jalapeno Ranch Style beans
1  can pinto beans
2  cans kidney beans
1  can tomatoes and green chilies
2  c. water
1  pkg. dry Ranch mix
1  pkg. taco seasoning
  chopped onion

      Brown meat and drain. Add all ingredients. Simmer for 20 to 30 minutes. Serve with tostado chips or corn bread.
