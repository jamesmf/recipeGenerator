SOY STARTER OR BASE


4  c. water
2  c. soy flour
2  tsp. salt

      Blend or stir all ingredients until smooth.  Cook in an uncovered dish at 350 degrees; for 50 minutes (stir after 20 minutes).  Soy starter will keep in refrigerator for a few days or may be frozen. Freeze in 1/2 or 1-cup proportion in plastic freezer bags.  May be used for making soy milk, mayonnaise, yogurt, etc.
