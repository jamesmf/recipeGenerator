FRUIT JELLO MOLD


1 large  pkg. strawberry Jell-O
1  c. boiling water
2  to 3 mashed bananas
8  oz. container sliced strawberries (frozen)
16  oz. can crushed pineapple, drained
  small size sour cream
1  c. walnuts

      Melt Jell-O in water.  Add all ingredients except sour cream. Pour half of the mixture into mold and chill 1 hour.  Spread on sour cream and add remaining ingredients.  Chill.
