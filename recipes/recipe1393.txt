SHISH KABOB CHICKEN


2  whole chicken breasts, skinned, boned and cut in 1-inch cubes
1/2  c. vegetable oil
1/4  c. corn syrup
1/4  c. soy sauce
2  Tbsp. wine vinegar
1  clove garlic, minced
1/4  tsp. ground ginger
2  green peppers, cut in 1-inch cubes
  pineapple chunks, drained
  cherry tomatoes
1/2  lb. mushrooms

      Mix together vegetable oil, corn syrup, soy sauce, vinegar, garlic and ginger.  Pour over chicken and marinate 3 hours or overnight in refrigerator.  Drain well, saving marinade.  Arrange chicken, vegetables and fruit alternately on long skewers.  Grill over hot coals about 4 inches from heat 10 to 12 minutes.  Baste and turn occasionally.  Serves 4.
