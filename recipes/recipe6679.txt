MARINATED GRILLED CHICKEN BREAST


3/4  c. soy sauce
2  Tbsp. corn oil
2  Tbsp. brown sugar
1  tsp. paprika
1  tsp. garlic powder
1  tsp. black pepper
6  to 8 boneless chicken breasts

      Mix first six ingredients to make the marinade.  Wash the boneless skinless chicken breasts, then place in suitable covered container.  Pour in marinade, then let marinate 4 hours or better overnight.  Grill 4 to 6 minutes on each side or until done.
