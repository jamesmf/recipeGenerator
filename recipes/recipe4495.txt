CRAB SALAD


1/2 small  box ring macaroni
1  c. peas
1/2  c. carrots, diced
1/2  c. celery, diced
2  Tbsp. onions, diced
16  oz. imitation crab meat, diced (shrimp may be substituted)

      Cook the macaroni until tender. Drain and wash. Cut up vegetables.
Dressing:
3/4  c. Miracle Whip
1/2  c. sour cream
1  tsp. sugar
1 heaping  tsp. bold and spicy brown mustard

      Mix well and pour over salad; mix. Pepper to taste.
