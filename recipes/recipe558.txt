CREAMY ITALIAN DIP


1 (8 oz.)  carton plain low-fat yogurt
1  pkg. Italian dressing mix
1 (12 oz.)  carton low-fat cottage cheese

      Blend all ingredients together in blender on high speed until smooth and creamy.  Chill before serving.  Yields 2 1/2 cups. Contains 8 1/2 calories per serving.
