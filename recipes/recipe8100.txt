CHICKEN CURRY


1  chicken
2  Tbsp. vegetable oil
1/2  c. finely chopped onion
2  curry leaves (bay leaves will do)
1  inch piece of cinnamon
3  cloves garlic, crushed
1/2  tsp. ginger, crushed
1 1/2  tsp. paprika powder or (red chili powder for hot curry)
2  tsp. curry powder
1  pinch lemon grass
1  pinch ground saffron
  juice of 2 lemons
1  c. coconut milk
  salt to taste

	Skin and cut the chicken to required pieces then wash.	Add the oil to a pan and warm the oils, then add the onions, curry leaves, cinnamon, garlic, and ginger.  Fry till all turn golden brown or about 7 minutes. Add the chicken pieces and the rest of ingredients, except the coconut milk.  Stir well and let it fry on a low fire for about 10 minutes stirring often.  Then add the coconut milk and 1 cup of water and let it cook on medium fire for about 15 minutes.
