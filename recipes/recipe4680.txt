CAULIFLOWER SALAD


1/2  head cauliflower
1  pkg. Ranch dressing
1  c. milk
1/2  c. sugar
  ripe olives, sliced
1  chopped onion
3  stalks celery
  green pepper, chopped

      Wash and separate cauliflower.  Mix Ranch dressing, milk and sugar; set aside.  Slice olives; chop onion, celery and green pepper.  Combine all ingredients.  Chill and serve.
