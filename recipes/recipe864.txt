ITALIAN STYLE SEAFOOD STEW


1  clove garlic, crushed
1/2  medium onion, chopped
2 1/2  c. canned tomatoes, chopped (include liquid)
1  bay leaf
  pinch of cayenne pepper
1/8  tsp. fennel seed (optional)
1/2  tsp. thyme
1/2  tsp. basil
1/2  tsp. oregano
  salt and pepper to taste
1/4  lb. cod or other whitefish, cut in 1-inch cubes
1/4  lb. scallops, cut in 1-inch pieces
1/4  lb. shrimp, raw, shelled and deveined

      Combine garlic, onion, tomatoes and seasonings in saucepan. Simmer for 10 minutes.  Add cod or whitefish and simmer gently 2 minutes.  Add scallops and simmer 2 minutes.  Add shrimp and simmer 3 to 5 minutes just until shrimp turn pink.  Yields 3 servings. Each serving equals 3 1/2 ounces protein and 1 cup cooked vegetable portion.
