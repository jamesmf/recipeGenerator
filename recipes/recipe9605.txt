SIMPLE STEW


1  can family size Campbell's mushroom soup
1  pkg. dry onion soup mix
2 1/2  lb. stew meat
  carrots
  celery
  onions
  peeled potatoes

      Stir together mushroom soup and dried onion soup mix with 1/2 cup water. Place in casserole dish with meat and vegetables. Cover and bake at 325 degrees; for 3 hours.
       This recipe can be doubled, tripled, etc.
