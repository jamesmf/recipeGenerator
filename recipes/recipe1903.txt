PITAS


1  lb. ground beef
1  pkg. taco seasoning mix
3/4  c. water
1 (10 oz.)  can tomatoes and green chilies, drained
1 (8 3/4 oz.)  can corn, drained
5  (6-inch) pita rounds
1  c. shredded lettuce
3/4  c. shredded Cheddar cheese

      Cook ground beef and drain well.  Add seasoning mix, water, tomatoes and green chilies and corn.  Bring to a boil.  Reduce heat and simmer, uncovered, for 15 to 20 minutes.  Cut bread rounds in half.  Place on baking sheet and bake at 250 degrees; for 10 minutes, or until warm.  Fill each pocket half full with meat mixture and top with lettuce and cheese.  Yield:  10 sandwiches.
