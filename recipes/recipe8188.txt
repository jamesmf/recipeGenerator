PEACH-GLAZED PORK CHOPS


1 (8 1/2 oz.)  can peach slices (undrained)
  hot water
1/4  c. (1/2 stick) butter or margarine, cut into pieces
1 (6 oz.)  pkg. Stove Top savory herb stuffing mix
6  pork chops (1/2-inch thick)
1/3  c. peach or apricot preserves
1  Tbsp. Dijon mustard

      Drain peaches, reserving syrup; add hot water to syrup to measure 1 1/2 cups. Mix syrup mixture and butter in large bowl until butter is melted. Stir in contents of stuffing mix pouch and peaches; let stand 5 minutes. Spoon stuffing mixture into 13 x 9-inch baking pan. Arrange chops over stuffing mixture. Mix preserves and mustard. Spoon over chops. Bake at 375 degrees; for 40 minutes or until chops are cooked through. Makes 6 servings.
