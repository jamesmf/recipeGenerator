MORE PLEASE HOT DISH


1  lb. ground beef
1  onion, thinly sliced
1  c. celery, chopped
1/2  c. instant rice
3  Tbsp. soy sauce
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  can cream of chicken soup
1 1/2  cans of water
1 large  can Chinese noodles

      Brown ground beef. Add onions and celery. Cook until browned. Add remaining ingredients, except noodles. Bake at 350 degrees; for 50 minutes, stirring once or twice while baking. Sprinkle noodles over top. Bake 10 minutes longer. Serves 8 to 10.
