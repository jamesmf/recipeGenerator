LINGUINI WITH CLAM SAUCE


1/2  c. butter  or margarine
2  cloves garlic, chopped
2  Tbsp. flour
3  cans (6 1/2 oz.) minced clams
1/4  c. half and half
1/2  c. white wine
1/2  tsp. thyme
  some red pepper flakes (optional)
1/4  c. chopped parsley
1  Tbsp. lemon juice
2  oz. Parmesan cheese
1  lb. linguini

	In large frying pan melt butter or margarine.  Add chopped garlic and cook until soft.  Stir in flour and cook over low heat 1 minute.  Gradually add clam liquid, half and half, wine, thyme and pepper flakes.	Bring to a boil and simmer for 2 minutes. Stir in clams, parsley, lemon juice and Parmesan cheese.  Heat thoroughly.  Add salt and pepper to taste.	Cook linguini.	Drain well.  Pour sauce over pasta and mix gently.
