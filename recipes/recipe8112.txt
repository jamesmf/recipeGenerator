CEBICHE


2  lb. mixed seafood (shrimp, clams, firm fish)
1  large onion, thinly sliced
1/4  c. chopped cilantro or parsley
2  cans green chilies, finely sliced
3  tsp. olive oil
  salt to taste
  pepper to taste
  lime juice to cover (or mixture of lemon and lime juice)
2  ears of corn, cooked and chopped
2  sweet potatoes, cook

	Combine all ingredients, except corn and sweet potatoes and refrigerate two hours.  To serve place in a large dish.  Garnish with the corn and sweet potatoes.  Serving size:  8.
