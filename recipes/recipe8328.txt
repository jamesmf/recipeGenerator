CHICKEN POT PIE III


2 (10 3/4 oz.)  cans cream of chicken soup
2 (9 oz.)  pkg. frozen mixed vegetables
2  c. cooked chicken, cubed
1/2  c. milk
1  egg
1  c. Bisquick baking mix

	Preheat oven to 400 degrees;. In a 2 1/2-quart casserole dish, mix soup and chicken. Mix milk, baking mix and egg. Pour over chicken mixture. Bake 35 minutes or until golden brown.
