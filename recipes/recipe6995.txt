CRAWFISH DELIGHT


1  bell pepper, slivered
1  onion, chopped
1  can Rotel tomatoes with chilies
1  can cream of celery soup
2  c. raw rice
1 1/2 to 2  lb. crawfish tails with fat
3/4  c. oil (less if desired)
  salt and pepper to taste
4  c. water

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees;. Mix all ingredients together and pour into a large, greased baking dish. Bake for 30 minutes at 350 degrees; or until rice is tender.
