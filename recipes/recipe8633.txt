SALMON SOUFFLE


2  Tbsp. butter
1  C. milk
4  eggs
2  Tbsp. flour
1  C. flaked salmon
1  tsp. salt

      Make a white sauce with the butter, flour, milk and salt. Add salmon.
      Remove from heat.  Add well beaten egg yolks.  Cool.
      Fold in stiffly beaten egg whites.  Put in buttered baking dish.  Bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes.
