SPICED PEACHES


2 (29 oz.)  cans cling peach halves
1 1/2  c. sugar
4  cinnamon sticks
1/2  c. cider vinegar
2  tsp. whole cloves

      Drain peaches, reserving syrup. Combine peach syrup, sugar, vinegar, cinnamon sticks and cloves in a  saucepan. Bring to a boil. Lower heat and simmer 10 minutes. Pour hot syrup over peach halves and let cool. Chill overnight before serving. Store in the refrigerator. Makes 4 pints.
