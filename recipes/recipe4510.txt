"TACO SALAD"


1  lb. ground beef, browned and drained
1  can chili beans
1/2  onion, chopped
1  head lettuce, cut up
1 (8 oz.)  bottle Italian dressing
2  tomatoes, chopped
1/2  lb. Cheddar cheese, shredded
1  pkg. taco seasoning (on browned ground beef)

      Just before serving, add 1 bag crushed Doritos tortilla or nacho cheese tortilla chips and mix.
