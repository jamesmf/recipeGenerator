BROCCOLI SALAD


1  stick Cracker Barrel extra sharp Cheddar cheese, grated
1  large red onion, sliced thin
4  heads broccoli, cut in bite size pieces

	Place a layer of the cheese, onion and broccoli in a serving dish.  Repeat three times.
Dressing:
2  c. mayonnaise
2/3  c. sugar
8  Tbsp. vinegar
1  lb. bacon, fried until crisp

	Pour dressing on top of the layers. Put crumbled bacon on top. Cover tightly. Refrigerate overnight. Serves 8 to 10.
