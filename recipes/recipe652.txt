CHOCOLATE CAKE


10  Tbsp. self-rising flour
4  tsp. sugar
6  to 8 packets Sweet 'N Low
4  tsp. cocoa
2/3  c. powdered dry milk
2/3  c. water
4  tsp. margarine, melted
1  tsp. vanilla

      Mix dry ingredients together.  Add water, margarine and vanilla.  Blend well with mixer or spoon.  Pour into 1 quart loaf pan sprayed with Pam.  Bake at 400 degrees; for 10 to 15 minutes.
