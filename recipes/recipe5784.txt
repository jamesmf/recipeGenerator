LIME GELATIN SALAD


20  large marshmallows
2  c. boiling water
1 (3 oz.)  pkg. lime gelatin
1  c. crushed pineapple
1  c. chopped pecans
1  c. shredded American cheese
1  c. whipped topping

      Melt marshmallows in boiling water in bowl. Add gelatin; stir until dissolved. Chill until partially set. Add pineapple, pecans and cheese; mix well. Fold in whipped topping. Chill until firm.
