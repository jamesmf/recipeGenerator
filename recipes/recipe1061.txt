CHEESE STICKS


1/2  c. grated Parmesan cheese
1/4  lb. (1/2 c.) margarine, softened
16  slices sandwich bread
  paprika as desired (optional)

      Preheat oven to 375 degrees;.  With fork, combine cheese with margarine.  Spread mixture on bread, using about 1 1/2 teaspoons per slice.  Sprinkle with paprika if desired.  Cut each slice into 6 sticks.  Place in single layer on large baking sheet.  Bake for 8 to 10 minutes until brown and crisp.  Yields 96 sticks, 16 servings.
