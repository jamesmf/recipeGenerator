GREEN BEAN SALAD


4  pieces celery, cut fine
1/2  c. olives, sliced and drained
1  medium green pepper, cut up
1  medium red pepper, cut up
1  medium onion, cut up small
2  cans French sliced green beans, drained
1  can green peas, drained

      Drain green beans and peas in colander.
Dressing:
1/2  c. vegetable oil
2  c. vinegar
2  c. sugar

      Mix ingredients well.  Pour dressing over bean salad and put in refrigerator for several hours or overnight.  Drain off 1 hour before serving.  Save dressing and you can use once more.
