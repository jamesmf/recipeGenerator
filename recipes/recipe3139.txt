STROMBOLI


1  can pizza dough
5  slices American cheese
1  c. shredded Swiss cheese
6 or 7  slices ham, diced
12  slices pepperoni
1  small onion, chopped (optional)
  pizza sauce (optional)
2  eggs
1  tsp. garlic salt
1  tsp. Italian seasoning
  pizza sauce (optional)

      Roll out pizza dough thin; layer meats, onions, and cheeses. If desired, add pizza sauce to layers. Roll up dough and place in a pizza pan. In a small bowl, mix eggs, garlic salt, and Italian seasoning; brush top of dough with egg mixture. Bake at 450 degrees; until brown.
