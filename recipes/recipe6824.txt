TEX-MEX SKILLET


1  lb. lean ground beef
1/2  c. chopped onion
2 (10 oz.)  cans tomatoes
1 (16 oz.)  can kidney beans
1  tsp. chili powder
1/4  tsp. cumin
1/2  c. chopped Cheddar cheese

      Brown ground beef and onion. Add tomatoes, beans, rice, chili powder, cumin and 1 cup of water. Bring to boil, reduce heat and simmer, covered, 20 minutes or until rice is tender. Top with cheese.
