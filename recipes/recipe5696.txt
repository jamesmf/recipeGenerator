COCA-COLA BARBECUE SAUCE


1  can Coca-Cola
1  pkg. Lipton onion soup mix
1  bottle chili sauce
2/3  c. soy sauce
1/2  c. vinegar
1  Tbsp. ketchup
1  tsp. paprika
1  clove garlic, minced

	Preheat oven to 325 degrees;.  Lift meat from marinade and place on foil in roasting pan (enough foil to cover and envelope wrap). Roast for 3 1/2 to 4 hours.
	Gravy:	Pan drippings, when combined with browned flour, makes a wonderful gravy. Add beef bouillon mixed with water to desired consistency.
