CAULIFLOWER


2 (10 oz.)  pkg. frozen cauliflower
1 (10 1/2 oz.)  can cream of shrimp soup
2  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  c. grated cheese
  buttered bread crumbs

      Cook cauliflower according to instructions and drain well. In a casserole, combine cauliflower, soup, Worcestershire sauce and cheese.  Top with buttered bread crumbs.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes.
