BAKED SPAGHETTI
(Makes 12 Servings)  

1  c. onion, chopped
1  c. bell pepper, chopped
1  Tbsp. margarine
1  can diced tomatoes (with juice)
1  can mushrooms, drained
2 1/4  oz. ripe olives, sliced
2  tsp. dried oregano
1  lb. ground beef, cooked and drained
12  oz. spaghetti, cooked and drained
2  c. Cheddar cheese, grated
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1/4  c. water
1/4  c. Parmesan cheese, grated

      Saut√© onions and green pepper in margarine. When tender, add tomatoes, mushrooms, olives and oregano. Simmer 10 minutes. (Add beef if desired.) Place half of the spaghetti in greased 13 x 9 x 2-inch baking dish. Top with half of the tomato mixture. Sprinkle with 1 cup of cheese. Repeat layers. Mix the soup and water and pour over casserole. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese. Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees; for 30 to 35 minutes or until heated through.
