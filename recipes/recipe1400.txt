ROAST ON THE GRILL


  chuck roast
  meat tenderizer
  Worcestershire sauce
1/2  c. ketchup or A.1.
1/2  c. vegetable oil
1/2  c. wine vinegar with garlic

      Pierce chuck roast; cover with meat tenderizer and pepper well. Mix marinade of vegetable oil, ketchup, vinegar with garlic and a few shakes of Worcestershire sauce.  Cover roast and marinate at least 12 hours.  Grill and baste with marinade.  Roast has better texture when room temperature before grilling.
