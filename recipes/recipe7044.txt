SHIPWRECK


2  lb. ground beef
2  tsp. shortening
  salt and pepper
1/4  c. onion, chopped
2  c. potatoes, cubed
2  c. peas and carrots
1  can kidney beans
1  can tomato soup

      Fry (but do not brown) ground beef in shortening; drain and add salt and pepper to taste. In large Dutch oven, make layers starting with onions, potatoes, ground beef, peas, and carrots, beef, and kidney beans; add tomato soup last. Bake covered at 300 degrees; for 3 hours.
