BAKED CREAMED CABBAGE


1  medium head cabbage
1/2  c. boiling water
1 1/2  c. soy milk
3  Tbsp. soy margarine
3  Tbsp. whole-wheat flour
1/2  tsp. vegie-sal
1/4  c. whole wheat cracker crumbs

      Shred cabbage very fine and cook 9 minutes in boiling water. Drain cabbage well and place in buttered 1 1/2-quart casserole dish.  Melt margarine in saucepan and stir in flour and vegie-sal until smooth.  Add soy milk gradually; continue stirring until thickened.  Pour sauce over cabbage and sprinkle cracker crumbs over top.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 15 to 25 minutes.
