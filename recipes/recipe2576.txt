ZUCCHINI AND TOMATO


2  Tbsp. butter
1/2  c. chopped green pepper
1/4  c. chopped onion
2  c. sliced zucchini, 1/2-inch thick (about 3 small)
1/2  c. soft bread crumbs
1/4  c. grated Parmesan cheese
1  tsp. salt
  dash of pepper
2  c. tomato wedges
1/2  c. shredded Cheddar cheese

      Melt butter and saut√© green peppers and onions.  Add zucchini, bread crumbs, Parmesan cheese, salt and pepper.  Turn into 1 1/2 quart covered casserole.  Bake at 375 degrees; for 20 minutes.  Add tomatoes and bake 10 minutes more.  Melt Cheddar cheese on top and serve.
