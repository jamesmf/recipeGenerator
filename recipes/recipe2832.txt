RELISH


16  sweet red peppers
16  sweet green peppers
10  small onions
1  qt. vinegar
1 1/2  c. sugar
2 1/2  tsp. salt

      Cut peppers in half and remove seeds.  Chop peppers and onions fine.  Pour boiling water over them; let stand 5 minutes.  Drain off water, again cover with boiling water; let stand 10 minutes. Pour into muslin bag and drain overnight.  Add vinegar, sugar and salt; boil 20 minutes.  While hot, pour into sterile jars and seal. Makes about 3 pints.
