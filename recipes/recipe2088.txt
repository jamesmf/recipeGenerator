BOSTON CREME CANDY


3  c. sugar
1 1/2  c. cream
1/2  c. dark Karo syrup
1  tsp. vanilla
1  c. chopped walnuts

      Mix all ingredients except nuts; boil over medium heat, stirring occasionally, to soft-ball stage.  Let cool for 10 minutes.  Add nuts.  Beat until starting to thicken.  Pour into buttered dish.  When cool, cut in squares.
