BREAKFAST PIE


4  eggs
1  c. dairy sour cream
8  slices bacon, cooked and crumbled
1 1/2  c. shredded cheese

      In medium bowl, whisk together eggs and sour cream until blended.  Stir in bacon (or ham or sausage) and cheese.  Pour into greased pan (or a frozen deep-dish pie crust) and bake at 375 degrees; for 45 to 50 minutes.  Serves 8.
