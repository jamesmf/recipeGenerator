HAM DELIGHT


2  c. diced ham
1 (12 oz.)  can whole corn
1  small onion, grated
1  green pepper, chopped
2  eggs, slightly beaten
1  tsp. dry mustard
1/4  c. melted butter
1  c. seasoned croutons

      Mix all ingredients except croutons and butter.  Turn into a buttered casserole.  Top with croutons and dribble the melted butter over all.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes.  Makes 4 servings.
