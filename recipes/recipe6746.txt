LASAGNA


1  lb. sausage
1  lb. ground beef
1 (No. 2 1/2)  can tomatoes
14  oz. pkg. lasagna noodles
18  oz. can tomato sauce
2  cloves minced garlic
1  pkg. Lawry's spaghetti sauce mix
1  pt. dry cottage cheese
2  pkg. Mozzarella cheese

      Brown meat well.  Drain off fat.  Add other ingredients tomatoes, tomato sauce, garlic and spaghetti sauce mix.  Cook 8 ounce package lasagna noodles.  In pan put 1 layer noodles, 1/3 sauce, layer cottage cheese and Mozzarella cheese.  Repeat again twice.  End with layer sauce.  Sprinkle Parmesan on top.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes.  Let set 15 minutes.  Serve.
