ONE DISH CHICKEN AND RICE BAKE


1  can Campbell's cream of mushroom soup
1  c. water
3/4  c. uncooked white rice
1/4  tsp. paprika
1/4  tsp. pepper
4  boneless chicken breasts, halved

      In 2-quart shallow baking dish, mix soup, water, rice, paprika and pepper. Place chicken on rice mixture. Bake at 375 degrees; for 45 minutes. Serves 4 people.
