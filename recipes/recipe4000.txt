SALMON DIP


1  can (1 lb.) salmon
  juice of 1 lemon
2  tsp. chopped chives
1  tsp. minced onion
1/4  tsp. crumbled dried rosemary
1/8  tsp. coarse black pepper
1/4  tsp. salt
1  c. sour cream

      Drain salmon; remove and discard bones and skin.  Mash salmon; add remaining ingredients, except sour cream.  Mix well; fold in sour cream.  Chill for at least one hour.  Makes 2 cups. Serve with chips or crackers.
