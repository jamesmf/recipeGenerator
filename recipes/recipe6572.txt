PARMESAN CHICKEN


2  lb. chicken (fryer), cut up
1  stick margarine, melted
1 1/2  c. bread crumbs
  garlic powder
  parsley flakes
  Parmesan cheese

      Dip pieces of chicken in melted margarine. Roll in bread crumbs. Place in baking dish (I use a cookie sheet). Sprinkle with remaining seasonings. Pour remaining margarine over chicken. Bake at 450 degrees; for 25 minutes or until golden brown. Enjoy!
