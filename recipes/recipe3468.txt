GREEN TOMATO RELISH


4  qt. green tomatoes, peeled and chopped
1  c. onion, chopped
1 1/2  c. brown sugar
2  Tbsp. mustard seed
2  c. red sweet peppers, chopped
1  Tbsp. prepared horseradish
1/2  c. salt
2  qt. cabbage, chopped
1  Tbsp. celery seed
4 1/2  c. vinegar

      Boil beets until done. Peel and slice. Put in boiler. Put even amount of vinegar and sugar over beets. Cover good. Use no water. Boil long enough to dissolve sugar. Seal in jars.
