COLE SLAW


1  large head shredded cabbage
4  grated carrots
1  chopped onion
4  stalks chopped celery

 
Brine:
3/4  c. white vinegar
1  Tbsp. salt
1 1/2  c. sugar
3/4  c. oil
1  tsp. celery seed

      Bring brine to a boil.  Pour over vegetables; put in covered container.  Tastes better after 48 hours.  Will keep for two weeks in the refrigerator.
