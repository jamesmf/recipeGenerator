CHEESE BISCUITS


3  sticks butter
1  lb. plain flour
1  lb. sharp grated cheese
1  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. red pepper

      Cream butter; add flour, grated cheese, salt and red pepper. Mixture is very stiff.  Chill.  Roll on waxed paper and cut or squeeze through a cookie press.  May top with nuts.  Bake at 375 degrees; for 10 to 12 minutes.
