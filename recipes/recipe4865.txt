CONGEALED ORANGE SALAD


2  pkg. orange Jell-O
2  c. boiling water
1 small  can crushed pineapple and juice
1  c. grated cheese
1  c. mayonnaise
  chopped nuts (as desired)

      Dissolve Jell-O in boiling water.  Stir in pineapple and juice. Put in refrigerator and let congeal to soft state.  Then stir in cheese, mayonnaise and nuts. Return to refrigerator until time to serve.
