MACARONI AND CHEESE


8  oz. (about 2 c.) elbow macaroni, cooked and drained
1  c. milk
1  lb. Velveeta cheese
1/2  c. grated Parmesan
  pinch of salt

	Heat milk in medium saucepan. Cut Velveeta into small pieces and add to hot milk; stir until cheese melts. Add Parmesan and salt.  Then add macaroni.
