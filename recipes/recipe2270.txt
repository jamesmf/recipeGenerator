HOT WINGS


1 1/2  c. barbecue sauce
1/2  tsp. red pepper
1/2  c. orange juice
1/4  c. butter, melted
1/2  tsp. hot pepper sauce
1/2  c. flour
6  lb. chicken wings

      Heat oven to 400 degrees;.  In small plastic bag, combine flour and red pepper; coat chicken.  Bake 20 minutes.  Mix butter, hot pepper sauce and barbecue sauce with orange juice.  Pour half of mixture over chicken.  Broil 10 minutes.  Turn chicken; pour rest of sauce on.  Broil again until crisp.
