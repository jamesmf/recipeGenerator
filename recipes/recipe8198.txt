SAUSAGE GRITS CASSEROLE


1  lb. hot bulk sausage
1/2  c. chopped onion
1  c. raw grits (not quick)
8  oz. sharp Cheddar cheese, grated
2  eggs, beaten
1/2  tsp. salt
  pepper to taste
1/4  c. grated cheese

      Brown sausage and onion; drain. Cook grits according to directions. Combine sausage, grits, cheese, eggs, salt and pepper. Pour into a greased 9 x 13-inch pan. Top with remaining cheese. Bake 1 hour at 350 degrees;.
