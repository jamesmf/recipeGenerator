HERB DRESSING


1/2  c. fresh tarragon or 1/4 c. dried
1  clove garlic, crushed
1/8  tsp. crushed red pepper flakes
1/2  c. olive oil
2  Tbsp. red wine vinegar
1/2  c. sour cream

      Blend well and serve over mixed greens.  Yields 1 pint.
      Optional:  Serve topped with nasturtium flowers and leaves.
