SPAGHETTI


1  lb. ground round
1  medium onion, chopped
1  small green pepper, chopped
1 small  can tomato paste
2 small  cans tomato sauce
  salt, black pepper, red pepper, garlic, oregano and sugar (or Equal) to taste
1  lb. spaghetti noodles

      In medium pot (sprayed with Pam), brown meat and push to one side.  In same pot, add onion and green pepper; cook until tender. Mix meat, veggies, tomato paste and tomato sauce.  Add remaining ingredients (except spaghetti).  In another pot, boil spaghetti noodles according to package directions.  Serve spaghetti sauce on bed of spaghetti noodles.  Add Parmesan cheese, if desired.  Serve with garlic bread and tossed salad.  Enjoy!
