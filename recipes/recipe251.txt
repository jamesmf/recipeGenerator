OATMEAL MACAROONS


1  c. Crisco shortening
1  c. brown sugar
1  c. granulated sugar
1/2  tsp. vanilla
2  eggs, unbeaten
1 1/2  c. sifted flour
1  tsp. soda
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. cinnamon
3  c. regular oats
1  c. chopped nuts

      Blend all ingredients in order mentioned and bake in 350 degrees; oven for 12 to 15 minutes.
