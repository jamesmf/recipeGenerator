TACO SOUP


1 1/2  lb. hamburger meat
1  onion, chopped
3 (14.5 oz.)  cans stewed tomatoes
1  can pinto beans (undrained)
1  can black beans (undrained)
1  can whole kernel corn (undrained)
1  pkg. dry taco seasoning
1 (1 oz.)  pkg. dry Ranch dressing
1 (4 oz.)  can chopped green chiles
1 1/2  c. water

	Brown hamburger. Add onion and cook thoroughly.  Drain off excess grease. Add remaining ingredients and simmer for 30 minutes. This freezes well.
