VEGGIE BARS


2 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
3/4  c. mayonnaise
1  oz. pkg. dry Ranch dressing
1/2  c. shredded Cheddar cheese
2  pkg. crescent roll dough
  any of these vegetables, chopped:  broccoli, cauliflower, onions, radishes, carrots, green peppers, or whatever else you might like*

      *Need 1/2 cup each or whatever.
      Preheat oven to 325 degrees;.  Press into a greased 15 x 10 pan crescent roll dough.  Bake 10 minutes or until golden brown.  Mix cream cheese, mayonnaise, dry Ranch dressing and then add Cheddar cheese. Spread on cooled crust.  Sprinkle chopped veggies over top and press into cheese mixture gently.  Refrigerate.  Cut into squares.
