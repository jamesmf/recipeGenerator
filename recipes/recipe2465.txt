PECAN TOPPED BREAD PUDDING


1  loaf French bread, sliced 1-inch thick
1 1/2  c. milk
1 1/2  c. half and half
6  large eggs
1  tsp. vanilla
1/8  tsp. ground nutmeg and cinnamon

 
Topping:
1  c. light brown sugar
1  stick unsalted butter, softened
2  Tbsp. dark corn syrup
1  c. coarsely chopped pecans

      Butter a 13 x 9-inch pan.  Fill with bread slices so dish is completely covered with bread and filled to the top.  Mix milks, eggs and spices.  Pour over bread.  Cover tightly.  Refrigerate overnight.
      Heat oven to 350 degrees;.  Mix topping together.  Remove casserole from refrigerator and uncover.  Dollop the toppings over the casserole.  Bake, uncovered, until puffed and golden (40 minutes). Let stand 5 minutes.
