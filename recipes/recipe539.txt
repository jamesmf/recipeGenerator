RAINBOW PUNCH


8  c. water
1 (12 oz.)  can frozen orange juice
1 (12 oz.)  can frozen lemonade
1 (12 oz.)  can frozen limeade
2 liter  bottle chilled ginger ale
1  pt. rainbow sherbet
24  fruited ice cubes

      Combine water, orange juice, lemonade and limeade in punch bowl until well blended.  Before serving, add soda, sherbet scoops and ice cubes.  Fruited ice cubes are just ice cubes with various fruits (cherries, raspberries, blueberries, orange sections, etc.) frozen inside them.
