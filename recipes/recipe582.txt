MOCK SAUSAGE PATTIES


1  lb. ground turkey
1/4  c. salt free seasoned bread crumbs
2  Tbsp. low sodium chicken broth
2  Tbsp. minced onion
1  Tbsp. minced fresh parsley
1  tsp. vegetable oil
1/4  tsp. ground sage
1/4  tsp. ground thyme
1/8  tsp. ground black pepper
1  large egg white

      Combine first 9 ingredients in medium size bowl and blend well with hands.  Beat egg white with fork and add to mixture; mix with hands.  Shape into 12 patties.  Broil 3 to 4 inches from broiler for 4 to 5 minutes until light brown on nonstick cookie sheet. Turn and broil 2 to 3 minutes more.  Serves 6.
