HOT TURKEY SALAD


2  c. cooked turkey, diced
1  c. celery, chopped
1 1/2  c. cooked rice (equal to 1/2 c. raw rice)
1  tsp. green onions, chopped
1/2  c. slivered almonds (optional)
1  Tbsp. lemon juice
3/4  c. mayonnaise
1/4  c. chicken broth
1  can cream of chicken soup
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper
2  c. potato chips, crushed

      Mix first 11 ingredients and pour into casserole.  Cover with crushed potato chips.  Bake 30 to 40 minutes at 350 degrees;.  Serves 6.
