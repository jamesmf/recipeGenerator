GARLIC CHICKEN BREASTS AND RICE


1  c. minute rice
6  pieces split boneless chicken breasts
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1/2  stick butter, melted

      Wash chicken breasts and place into a baking dish.  Sprinkle well with garlic salt and add pepper to taste.  Mix together melted butter, 1 cup water, rice and soup.  Pour over chicken and bake at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes to an hour or until chicken is done.
