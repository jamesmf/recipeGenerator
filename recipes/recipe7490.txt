MANICOTTI

Crepes:
4  eggs
3/4  c. all-purpose flour
1  c. milk

      Whisk together eggs and milk.  Add flour and continue to whisk until all of the lumps are gone.  Heat a lightly oiled skillet to medium heat.  Spread 2 tablespoons of mixture on hot skillet like a very thin pancake.  Cook each side until lightly brown. Place crepe on a dish.  Repeat process until all of the batter is done.  Place a piece of wax paper between each crepe. Wrap crepes in foil and set aside.
      Preheat oven to 350 degrees;.  Coat a thin layer of marinara sauce in a baking dish.  Take a crepe and spoon filling into the center. Roll up the crepe and place it in the dish; continue until all the crepes are finished.  Cover with the remaining sauce and sprinkle the remaining 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese on top.  Bake for 30 to 40 minutes.
Filling:
1 1/2  lb. ricotta cheese
3  eggs, slightly beaten
8  oz. shredded mozzarella cheese
3/4  c. grated Parmesan cheese
2  tsp. parsley
  Salt and pepper to taste

      Mix ricotta, mozzarella, parsley, eggs and 1/2 c. Parmesan cheese together.  Add salt and pepper to taste.
Marinara Sauce:
1/4  c. olive oil
2  cloves garlic, minced
3 (8-oz.)  cans tomato sauce
2  cans water
  Pinch of oregano
  Salt and pepper to taste
