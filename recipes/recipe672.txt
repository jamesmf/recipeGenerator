MACARONI SLAW


1  c. Hellmann's mayonnaise
3  Tbsp. lemon juice
1 1/2  tsp. sugar
1 1/2  tsp. mustard
1  tsp. salt
  dash of garlic powder
4  c. cooked elbow macaroni
3  c. finely shredded green cabbage
1  c. shredded carrot
1  c. finely chopped green pepper
3  Tbsp. minced onion

      Gently toss together all ingredients.  Cover and chill several hours.
