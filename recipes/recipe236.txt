CRACKED SUGAR COOKIES


1  c. white sugar
1/2  c. brown sugar
1/2  c. shortening
1  egg
1/2  c. butter or margarine
1  tsp. vanilla
2  c. flour
2  tsp. soda
2  tsp. cream of tartar
1/2  tsp. salt

      Cream first 6 ingredients.  Sift next 4 ingredients and add to creamed mixture.  Roll into small 1-inch balls, then roll in granulated sugar (red and green sugar for Christmas).  Do not flatten.  Place on cookie sheet and bake 6 to 8 minutes in a 350 degrees; or 375 degrees; oven.
