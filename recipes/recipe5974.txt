CHICKEN SALAD


4  lg. chicken breast (boneless and skinless)
5  hard boiled eggs
1/2  c. chopped celery
1/2  c. sweet pickle relish
1  c. pecans
1/4  tsp. pepper
1/3  tsp. salt
1  c. mayo or salad dressing

      Boil chicken, drain and let cool.  Grind chicken, eggs, and pecans.  Mix together with celery, relish, salt, pepper and mayo. Chill 4 hours before serving.  Good with town house crackers.
