MARGARET'S CORN SALAD


30  oz. (2 cans) corn, yellow (drained)
1/2  c. green onion, chopped with blades*
2  tomatoes, chopped
1/2  c. Cheddar cheese, cubed
1/2  c. mayonnaise
  salt (to taste)
  pepper (to taste)

      *The whole green onion, including the top green part.
      Combine all ingredients.  Let set overnight before serving.
