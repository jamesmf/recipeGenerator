CHICKEN PICANTE
(Quick and Easy)  

2  chicken breasts (skinless and boneless)
1  c. your favorite picante sauce

	Place one cup picante sauce into a large saucepan.  Add chicken breasts to picante sauce and bring to a bubbling boil. Lower heat and continue gently boil for 20 to 25 minutes or until chicken is no longer pink inside.  Serves 2.
