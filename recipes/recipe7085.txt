TACO CASSEROLE


1  lb. ground beef
15  oz. can chili beans
8  oz. tomato sauce
2  Tbsp. taco sauce
2 to 3  tsp. chili powder
1  tsp. garlic salt
2  c. tortilla chips, coarsely broken
1  c. sour cream
1/2  c. green onions, sliced
1  tomato, chopped
1  c. shredded Cheddar or Monterey Jack

      Cook and stir ground beef in 10-inch skillet until brown; drain. Stir in beans, tomato sauce, taco sauce, chili powder and garlic salt.  Heat to boiling. Place chips in ungreased 2-quart casserole. Top with beef mixture. Spread with sour cream. Sprinkle with onions, tomato and cheese. Cook, uncovered, in 350 degrees; oven. Add tortilla chips around edge of casserole. Serve on shredded lettuce.
