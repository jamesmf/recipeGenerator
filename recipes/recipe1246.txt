BANANA SHAKE


1  c. skim milk
1/2  c. vanilla ice milk
1  small ripe banana

      Blend milk and ice milk in blender at high speed until smooth. Add banana and blend for 2 or 3 seconds.  Serve immediately.
      Makes 1 cup per serving; 1/2 milk exchange, 1/2 bread exchange, 1/2 fat exchange and 1 fruit exchange.  Contains 138 calories.
