ORANGE JELLO


1  (3 oz.) orange Jell-O
1  c. hot water
1  c. orange pop
1  (8 oz.) Cool Whip
1 large (15 oz.)  can mandarin oranges, drained
1/2 large  can crushed pineapple, drained

      Dissolve Jell-O in hot water.  Add pop; mix well.  Put into 9 x 13 pan.  Refrigerate until syrupy.  Beat in Cool Whip; add mandarin oranges.  Add crushed pineapple.  Put into bowl. Refrigerate until set.
