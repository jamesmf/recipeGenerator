PARMESAN CHICKEN BREAST


6  chicken breast halves (boneless and skinless)
2  Tbsp. margarine, melted
1/2  c. Parmesan cheese, grated
1/4  c. dry bread crumbs
1  tsp. dried oregano
1  tsp. parsley flakes
1/4  tsp. paprika
1/4  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper

	Preheat oven to 400 degrees;. Spray 15 x 10 x 1-inch baking pan with cooking spray. Dip chicken in melted margarine. Coat with combined remaining ingredients. Bake 20 to 25 minutes or until done.
