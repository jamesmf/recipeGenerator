MEAT LOAF


2/3  c. dry bread crumbs, soaked in milk
1 1/2  lb. ground beef
1  tsp. salt
2  eggs, beaten
1/4  c. chopped onion
1/2  tsp. sage

      Mix all ingredients together and top with sauce.
Sauce:
3  Tbsp. brown sugar
1/2  tsp. nutmeg
3/4  c. catsup
1  tsp. ground mustard

      Put meat in baking dish. Pour sauce over top and bake 1 hour at 300 degrees;.
