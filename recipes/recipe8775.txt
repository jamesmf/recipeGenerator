CHICKEN AND RICE CASSEROLE


1  stick oleo
2  cans cream of celery or cream of chicken soup*
1 1/2  cans water
1 1/2  c. rice (half cooked)
  chicken legs or thighs (about 5 each)**

      Melt butter; pour in bottom of casserole dish.  Add rice. Place chicken on rice.  Salt and pepper to taste.  Mix soup and water.  Pour this mixture over chicken.  Bake at 300 degrees; for 2 hours.
      *I use 1 can of each.
      **I use chicken breasts.
