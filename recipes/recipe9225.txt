PORK CHOPS


6  to 7 pork chops
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1/2  tsp. thyme
1  tsp. ginger
1  tsp. rosemary
1  can onion rings

      Brown pork chops on both sides at high temperature.  (Salt and flour before browning.)   Mix soup and spices together, and place with pork chops in a 9 x 13 inch pyrex dish.  Top with onion rings.  Cover with foil and bake at 350 degrees; for 45 to 50 minutes.
