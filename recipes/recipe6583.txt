THAI PEANUT CHICKEN WITH ROASTED GREEN BEANS


1  lb. fresh green beans
1  Tbsp. sesame seeds
4  chicken breasts, pounded thin
  olive oil spray

 
Sauce:
1/4  c. peanut butter
1/8  c. water
2  Tbsp. soy sauce
1  Tbsp. oriental chili paste
2  Tbsp. stir-fry sauce

      Sauce: Combine in bowl.
      Preheat oven to 450 degrees;. Clip green bean ends. Spray with olive oil. Toss 1 tablespoon of peanut sauce and sesame seeds and put on baking sheet to roast for 20 minutes. Spray frying pan with olive oil and saut chicken breast or use tenders and skewers. Serve sauce on the side or pour over.
