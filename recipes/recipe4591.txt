ITALIAN STEW


1  lb. Italian sausage
1 1/2  lb. stew beef
4  potatoes, sliced
4  carrots, sliced
1  can string beans
1  can stewed tomatoes
2  onions
1  can corn
3/4  c. ketchup
2  tsp. Italian seasoning

      Brown beef, sausage and onions. Place in pan. Add sliced potatoes, carrots, beans, corn and stewed tomatoes. Add ketchup and Italian seasoning. Mix. Bake 2 hours at 325 degrees;. Mix cornstarch and water and add after 1 hour.
