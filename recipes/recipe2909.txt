CHRISTMAS DECORATIONS
(Confectioners Glue Recipe)  

1  large egg white
1 3/4  c. confectioners sugar

      Blend in small mixer bowl.  Beat 2 minutes at high speed. Yield:  3/4 cup.
      Cut stockings out of cardboard.  Cover with red or silver foil; glue on Hershey's Kisses until covered.
      To make tree, cover Styrofoam cone with green or silver foil; glue on Hershey's Kisses.
      To make wreath, form circle with pipe cleaners 5 rows thick. Twist ends together; cover with foil.  Glue on Hershey's Kisses.
