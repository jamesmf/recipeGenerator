OVEN CHICKEN DINNER FOR ONE


1  skinless chicken breast
1  medium potato, peeled and quartered
1  small green pepper (in strips)
1  onion, quartered
2  carrots, sliced
  salt and pepper to tate
  pat of oleo

      Wash skinless chicken breast. Lay on square of foil. Layer other ingredients on top of chicken. Salt and pepper and lay on oleo. Sprinkle with water. Fold up foil and seal. Lay in pan. Bake in 350 degrees; oven for 1 hour.
      Recipe can be multiplied for any number of people.
      You can substitute chopped sirloin patty for chicken and sprinkle Worcestershire sauce over patty.
