TAMALE LOAF


1/2  c. shortening
1  medium onion, chopped
2 1/2  c. tomatoes
1  c. cornmeal
2  c. ground beef
2  eggs
1 1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper
1  tsp. paprika
3/4  c. canned corn

      Fry onion in shortening until brown; add tomatoes.  When hot, stir in cornmeal; cook 10 minutes.  Stir to prevent lumping. Remove from stove; add seasoning, meat, corn and eggs.  Blend well and pour in greased pan.  Bake for 45 minutes.
