GRANDMA'S SUGAR COOKIES


1/2  c. butter
1  c. sugar
2  eggs
1  tsp. vanilla
2 1/2  c. flour
1/2  tsp. salt
2 1/2  tsp. baking powder

      Cream butter; add sugar.  Add well beaten eggs and vanilla. Add dry ingredients.  Chill thoroughly.  Roll out on well floured board and cut with your favorite cookie cutter.  Bake at 375 degrees; for 10 minutes on well-greased cookie sheet.  Yields 4 or 5 dozen.
