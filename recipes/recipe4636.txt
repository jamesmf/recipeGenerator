DIXIE CHILI


2  lbs ground beef
4  onions, chopped
2  buds garlic
1 large  can tomato sauce
1-1/2  tsp vinegar
1-1/2  qts water
1  can chili beans
1  tsp cinnamon
1/2  tsp red pepper
4  tbsp chili powder
4  bay leaves
1  tsp All Spice
2  dashes worcestershire sauce
1  tbsp cumin
  salt to taste

      Brown beef and onions. Add rest of ingredients.  Cook over low flame for 3 hours.  Skim off excess fat.  Serve over spaghetti.  Top with grated cheese and raw chopped onions.  Serve with oyster crackers.
