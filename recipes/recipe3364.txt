CHEESE ROLL


2  lb. Velveeta cheese
1  pkg. (8-oz.) cream cheese, softened
  garlic powder to taste
1  c. pecans, chopped
1  Tbsp. Tabasco sauce
  chili powder

      Mix first 5 ingredients and divide into 3 parts.  Shape into rolls and roll in chili powder.  Refrigerate until serving.  Serve with assorted crackers.
