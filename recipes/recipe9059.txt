SAUSAGE CASSEROLE


1  lb. sausage, browned, drained, cooled and crumbled
6  eggs
2  c. milk
6  slices bread, crusts cut off and cubed
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. mustard
1  c. Cheddar cheese, cubed

      Mix all ingredients. Pour in 9 x 9-inch dish and bake at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes. May be made the night before and kept in refrigerator before baking and serving. Makes a complete breakfast.
