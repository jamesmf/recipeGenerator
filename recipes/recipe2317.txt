CHEDDAR BAKED POTATOES


1  can cream of mushroom soup
4  to 5 medium potatoes, cut into 1/4-inch slices
1/2  tsp. paprika
1/4  tsp. pepper
1  c. Cheddar cheese, shredded

      Combine soup, paprika and pepper.  In greased 2-quart baking dish, arrange potatoes in overlapping rows.  Sprinkle with cheese. Spoon soup mixture over cheese.  Cover; bake at 400 degrees; for 45 minutes.  Uncover; bake 10 minutes or until potatoes are fork-tender.
