SCHOKOLADESCHAUMDEN
(Chocolate Puffs)  

200  grams sugar
1  tsp. white vinegar
1  egg white

      Beat egg white until stiff.  Still beating, gradually add sugar and vinegar.  The mixture must remain stiff.  Add 1 tablespoon chocolate powder and 1 teaspoon cocoa to the egg white mixture. Place in little spoonfuls on an ungreased cookie sheet.  Bake it in a very slow 250 degrees; (120 degrees;C) oven for 15 to 16 minutes.  Cool.  Store in an airtight container.
