TACO SOUP


1  lb. ground round
1  large onion, chopped
1  can green chilies, chopped
1  pkg. taco seasoning
1  pkg. Hidden Valley Ranch
1  can pinto or chili beans
1  can corn
1  can tomatoes
2  cans Ro-Tel tomatoes

      Cook beef and onions; drain. Simmer other ingredients while beef cooks. Mix all together and simmer. Add a little water if needed. Cook for 30 minutes. Serve with sour cream, corn chips and cheese or Mexican cornbread. Serves 6 (1 1/2 cups).
