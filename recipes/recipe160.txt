GLAZED APPLE COOKIES


1/2  c. shortening
1 1/3  c. brown sugar, packed
1  egg
1/4  c. milk
2  c. flour
1/4  tsp. nutmeg
1/2  tsp. cloves
1  tsp. cinnamon
1  tsp. baking soda
1/2  tsp. salt
1  c. nutmeats
1  c. chopped, unpeeled apples
1  c. raisins or dates or half and half

      Cream shortening and sugar.  Add egg and milk.  Mix together flour and spices and add to creamed mixture; stir in nuts, apples and raisins.  Drop by teaspoon onto greased cookie sheet.  Bake at 375 degrees; for 10 to 12 minutes.
Glaze:
1 1/2  c. powdered sugar
2 1/2  Tbsp. hot milk
1  Tbsp. butter

      Mix sugar with milk and butter.  Ice cookies.
