SWEET POTATO CHOWDER


1 (16 oz.)  can sweet potatoes, drained
2 1/2  c. vegetable or chicken stock
1  c. milk
1  lb. frozen bag broccoli, corn and red peppers
1 (15 oz.)  can black beans, rinsed and drained
1  tsp. oregano
1/2  c. chopped scallions
  dash of hot sauce

      Pure yams, stock and milk in blender.  Heat mixture in large saucepan.  When hot, add remaining ingredients and stir well.
