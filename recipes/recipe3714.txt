REFRIGERATOR CUCUMBER SLICES


4  lbs. cucumbers (about 6 large), cut into 1/4-inch slices
3  med. onions, cut into 1/8-inch slices
3  C. sugar
3  C. vinegar
4  tsp. canning salt
1 1/2  tsp. mustard seed
1/2  tsp. alum

      In a large container combine cucumbers and onions.  In a large bowl combine remaining ingredients, stirring until sugar is dissolved.  Pour over cucumbers, mix well.
      Cover and chill overnight.  May be refrigerated for up to 2 weeks.
