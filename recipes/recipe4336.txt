CHEESEBURGER SOUP


1/2  lb. ground beef
3/4  c. onion, chopped
3/4  c. carrots, shredded
3/4  c. celery, diced
1  tsp. dried basil
1  tsp. dried parsley leaves
4  Tbsp. butter or margarine, divided
3  c. chicken broth
4  c. (1 3/4 lb.) potatoes, peeled and diced
1/4  c. all-purpose flour
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. process American cheese, cubed (2 cups)
1 1/2  c. milk
3/4  tsp. salt
1/4 to 1/2  tsp. pepper
1/4  c. sour cream

      In a 3-quart saucepan, brown beef; drain and set aside.  In the same saucepan, saut√© onions, carrots, celery, basil and parsley in 1-tablespoon butter until vegetables are tender, about 10 minutes. Add broth, potatoes and beef; bring to boil.  Reduce heat; cover and simmer 10 - 12 minutes or until potatoes are tender. Meanwhile, in a small skillet, melt remaining butter.  Add flour; cook and stir 3 - 5 minutes or until bubbly.  Add to soup and bring to boil.  Cook and stir 2 minutes.  Reduce heat to low.  Add cheese, milk, salt and pepper, cook and stir until cheese melts. Remove from heat; blend in sour cream.  Yields 8 servings (2 1/4 quarts).
