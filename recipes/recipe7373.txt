PEPPER STEAK


1  lb. steak, cut in strips
1  c. green pepper
1  c. mushrooms
2  Tbsp. soy sauce
1  Tbsp. cornstarch
1 1/2  tsp. all seasoning
1  c. onion
1/2  c. celery
2  Tbsp. oleo
1  c. water
1  tsp. salt

      Brown meat; add vegetables, cornstarch, water and soy sauce. Cook until veggies and meat are done.  Serve over rice or noodles.
