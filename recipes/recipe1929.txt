MOJARRAS O TRUCHAS AL MOJO DE AJO
(Fish In Garlic)  

6  perch or trout
3 or 4  cloves garlic
2  lemons
  salt and pepper
3  tomatoes, sliced
2  avocados, sliced
1  onion, sliced

      Wash fish.  Peel garlic and put in blender with juice from the lemons, salt and pepper.  Liquefy and pour over the fish.  Marinate one hour.  Meanwhile, cut tomato, avocado and onion.  Season with salt and pepper and toss to have ready to serve with fish.  Fry fish and serve.  Serves 6.
