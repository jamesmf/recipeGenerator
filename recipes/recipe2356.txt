PEARL'S BANANA BREAD
(Originally from Pearl Dunbar, who is baking this in Heaven!)  

1  c. sugar
1/2  c. shortening
2  c. flour, sifted at least once
2  eggs, well beaten
1  tsp. soda
3  bananas
1/2  c. nuts
1  tsp. vanilla

      Cream sugar and shortening with bananas.  Add eggs, flour and soda and stir well.  Add vanilla and nuts and stir well.  Pour into a loaf pan lined with wax paper.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour.
