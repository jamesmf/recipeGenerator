HOLIDAY SALAD


1  can Eagle Brand milk
1 (15 1/2 oz.)  can crushed pineapple, drained
1  can cherry pie filling
1  c. (8 oz.) toasted nuts
1  carton Cool Whip

      Mix all ingredients; pour into a mold or any type Pyrex dish. Chill or freeze as desired or it can be eaten immediately.  Makes 8 to 10 servings.
