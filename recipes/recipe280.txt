SUPER-EASY FRUIT COBBLER


1  stick margarine
1  c. flour
1  c. sugar
1  tsp. baking powder
1  c. milk
  pinch of salt
2 (No. 2)  cans fruit

      Use 13 x 9-inch pan.  Melt margarine in pan; set aside.  Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, milk and salt.  Pour this batter over melted margarine.  Do not stir.  Pour fruit, including juice, over batter.  Once again, do not stir.  Sprinkle with 1/2 cup sugar. Bake at 375 degrees; for 35 to 40 minutes.
