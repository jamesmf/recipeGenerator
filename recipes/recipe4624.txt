VEGETABLE SALAD


2  pkg. almond slivers
1  can Lesueur peas
1  can green beans
1  can Chinese vegetables
1  can water chestnuts
1  c. sugar
1  c. celery
1  medium onion
1/2  c. vinegar
1/4  c. oil

      Mix sugar, vinegar, and oil.  In separate bowl, drain and mix vegetables.  Add almond slivers.   Mix all ingredients together.
