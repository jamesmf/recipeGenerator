CHICKEN ENCHILADAS


1  c. rice, instant
13  oz. (1 can) chicken breast (drained)
1  c. salsa
4  oz. (1 can) green chilies, chopped
2  c. Monterey Jack cheese, shredded
8  (10 inch) flour tortillas
15  oz. (1 can) enchilada sauce

      Cook rice according to directions.
      Mix together:  rice, chicken, salsa, chilies and cheese. Spoon mixture into tortillas.  Tuck ends in and roll.  Place in a 9x13-inch baking dish.  Put cheese on top.  Pour enchilada sauce over all.
      Bake at 400 degrees; about 15 minutes, until cheese melts.
      NOTE:  Can substitute 1/2 lb. browned hamburger (drained) for chicken.
