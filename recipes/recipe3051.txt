TUNA PAT


8  oz. cream cheese (room temperature)
2 (7 oz.)  cans tuna, drained
2  Tbsp. chili sauce
2  Tbsp. snipped parsley
2  Tbsp. instant minced onions
1/2  tsp. hot pepper sauce

      Blend softened cream cheese with all ingredients.  Smooth oil or salad dressing on sides of a 2-cup mold.  Press ingredients into mold.  Chill 3 hours.  Best if chilled overnight.  Serve with crackers or chips.
