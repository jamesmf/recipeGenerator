SEAFOOD DIP


2  pkg. cream cheese
1 (8 oz.)  can crab
2  Tbsp. finely chopped onion
1  tsp. Worcestershire
1  Tbsp. garlic
  Mozzarella cheese, shredded

      Stir cream cheese (softened) and add all the rest of the ingredients, except cheese. Spread in a 9-inch pie pan. Top with cheese. Bake, uncovered, at 375 degrees; for 25 minutes until lightly browned. Serve with crackers, chips, etc.
