GREEN PEA SALAD
(12 Servings)  

3  regular-size LeSueur garden peas
3/4  c. celery, chopped
1  large green pepper, chopped
1  c. sharp grated cheese
1/2  c. onion, chopped
  mayonnaise
  salt and pepper

	Drain peas well. Add other vegetables. Stir in small amount of mayonnaise, salt and pepper. Refrigerate. Very good.
