CHICKEN RICE CASSEROLE


1  fryer, cut in pieces
2  cans cream of chicken soup
2  cans water
1  stick butter
1  medium onion, chopped
1 1/2  c. uncooked rice
  salt and pepper

      Wash chicken. Salt and pepper and place in a 9 x 13-inch baking pan or dish. Pour rice over chicken. Pour soup and water over rice. Saut√© onion in butter and mix with ingredients in dish. Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour or until chicken is done.
