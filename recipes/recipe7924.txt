INSTANT SEEKH KABABS


1  lb. ground beef (double-ground)
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. each, ginger and garlic paste
1  tsp. cayenne pepper
1  tsp. garam masala
1  green chili, finely chopped
1/2  bunch cilantro, finely chopped
4  Tbsp. cornstarch
2  Tbsp. oil

      Mix all the ingredients together, except oil.  Marinate in refrigerator for 1 hour.  With oiled hands, shape the beef mixture into seekhs and slide onto wooden or steel skewers.
      Heat oil and pan-fry the seekhs until golden-brown.  Serve with Tandoori naan and green chutney.
