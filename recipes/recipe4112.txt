CHEESE STRAWS WHOLE WHEAT


1  lb. sharp Cheddar cheese, shredded
1  c. whole wheat flour
1  c. unbleached white flour
1 heaping  tsp. cayenne pepper
3/4  c. unsalted butter, softened
  salt to taste

      Combine all ingredients, except salt, in food processor (2 batches) or by hand.  Process until mixture comes together in a ball.  Chill about 30 minutes.  Preheat oven to 350 degrees;.  Roll out dough, about 1/4-inch thick.  Cut into strips or rounds.  Bake on ungreased baking sheets for about 15 minutes or until golden brown.  Sprinkle with salt while still hot.  Serve hot with cocktails or cold with soups.
