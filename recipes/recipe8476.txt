MEAT BALLS


3  lb. ground beef
1  can (12 oz.) evaporated milk
1  c. oats (regular or quick cooking)
1  c. cracker crumbs
2  eggs
1/2  c. chopped onions
1/2  tsp. garlic salt
2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper
2  tsp. chili powder

 
Sauce:
2  c. catsup
1  c. brown sugar
1  tsp. garlic powder
1/4  c. chopped onion
  liquid smoke to taste

      Mix meat mixture and shape into 80 balls. Place in a single layer on cookie sheet and freeze. Store in sealed bag. Make sauce and stir until sugar is dissolved. Place meatballs in pan and pour sauce over. Bake 1 hour at 350 degrees;.
       Meat balls may be browned in a skillet and served with mushroom gravy.
