SAUCY FRANKS WITH RICE


4  bacon strips, uncooked
1/2  c. chopped onion
1/3  c. chopped green pepper
1/3  c. chopped celery
2  minced garlic cloves
1  c. pineapple juice
3/4  c. catsup
1/4  tsp. chili powder
1  lb. hot dogs or Lil  Smokies
  hot cooked rice to serve 8

      Cook bacon until crisp.  Drain on paper towel.  Reserve 1 tablespoon drippings and saut√© onion, green pepper, celery, and garlic until tender in saved drippings.  Add bacon.  Add pineapple juice, catsup, and chili powder.  Bring to a boil.  Add cut up hot dogs or smokies and simmer covered 5-8 minutes.  Serve over rice.
