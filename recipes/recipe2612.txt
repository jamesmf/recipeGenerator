MONKEY BREAD


3  cans biscuits
1  Tbsp. cinnamon
1/2  c. sugar
1  stick oleo
1  c. brown sugar
2  tsp. water
1/2  c. nuts (optional)

      Quarter biscuits; roll in cinnamon and sugar that have been mixed together.  Put in greased Bundt pan.  Melt oleo; add brown sugar and water; boil 2 minutes and pour over biscuits.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 25 minutes.
      Optional:  Place  nuts  in  bottom of pan before adding biscuits.
