HAMBURGER CASSEROLE


1  lb. ground beef
1  pkg. onion soup mix
1  c. raw rice
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  c. water

      Brown beef in skillet; drain fat. Mix all together. Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes until rice is done. Cover with grated cheese. Return to oven. Serve when cheese is melted.
