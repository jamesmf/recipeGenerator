TEN-CUP SALAD


2  c. drained crushed pineapple
2  c. drained mandarin oranges
2  c. coconut
2  c. mini marshmallows
1  c. sour cream
1  c. mayonnaise
3  Tbsp. white sugar

      Combine pineapple, oranges, coconut and marshmallows. Mix sour cream, mayonnaise and sugar; fold into the fruit mixture. Store in a bowl overnight. Serve chilled. This makes a BIG salad for a crowd.
