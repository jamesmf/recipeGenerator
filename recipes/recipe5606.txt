CHEDDAR CHOWDER


2  C. water
2  C. diced potatoes
1/2  C. diced carrots
1/2  C. celery
1/4  C. onion
1  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
1/4  C. butter
1/4  C. flour
2  C. milk
2  C. grated cheddar cheese
1  C. cubed ham

      Combine water, potatoes, carrots, celery, onion, salt and pepper in large kettle. Boil for 10 - 12 minutes.
      Meanwhile in small saucepan, make white sauce by melting the butter; add flour and stir until smooth (about 1 minute). Slowly add milk; cook until thickened.  Add the cheese to white sauce; stir until melted.  Add white sauce and cubed ham to veggies that HAVE NOT BEEN DRAINED.  Heat through.
