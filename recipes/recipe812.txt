BARBECUE SAUCE FOR CHICKEN


1  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper
1/3  c. vinegar
2  tsp. prepared mustard
1/4  tsp. ginger
1  Tbsp. paprika
1  tsp. sugar
2  Tbsp. Karo syrup
1  medium onion, chopped
1  c. tomato puree
1/4  c. butter
1/2  c. hot water
1/3  c. lemon juice
1/3  c. pineapple or grapefruit juice
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
  pineapple chunks

      Mix all ingredients in saucepan.  Heat to boiling point and pour over chicken.  Place chicken in oven with sauce.  Cook until done.
