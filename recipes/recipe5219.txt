WILD RICE SOUP


3  Tbsp. butter
1  Tbsp. minced onion
1/4  c. flour
4  c. chicken broth
2  c. cooked wild rice
1/2  tsp. salt
1 1/2  c. half & half
2  Tbsp. wine
2  stalks celery, chopped fine
1  c. cooked chicken, cubed
1/2  c. grated carrots
3  Tbsp. slivered almonds
  minced parsley
1  c. shredded cheese

      Melt butter in saucepan.  Saut√© onion and celery until tender. Blend in flour; gradually add broth.  Cook, stirring constantly, until mixture thickens and then stir in rice, carrots, almonds, chicken and salt.  Simmer 5 minutes.  Blend in half & half and wine. Heat to serving temperature and add cheese and parsley.
