MACARONI AND CHEESE


4  Tbsp. oleo, melted
2 1/2  c. macaroni (uncooked)
16  oz. shredded cheese (sharp Cheddar or whatever you prefer)
7  c. milk
  salt and pepper to taste

      Mix all ingredients in baking dish, 9 x 13 pan.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 to 1 1/2 hours.  Cook, uncovered; if gets too brown, cover.
