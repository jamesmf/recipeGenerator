PIMENTO CHEESE


1 (4 oz.)  jar pimentos
8  oz. sharp Cheddar cheese, shredded
1  tsp. mustard
2  Tbsp. mayonnaise

      Chop drained pimentos. Mix with cheese, mustard and mayonnaise. Allow to sit at room temperature for 30 minutes so flavors will blend. This keeps a week in the refrigerator. Yields about 1 1/2 cups.
