CRANBERRY/ORANGE CHICKEN


1/2  C. flour
1/8  tsp. salt
3  lbs. chicken, cut up
4  Tbsp. butter
2  C. whole fresh or frozen cranberries
1/2  C. chopped onion
2  Tbsp. grated orange peel
1 3/4  C. sugar
1 1/4  C. orange juice
1/4  tsp. ground ginger
1/4  tsp. ground cinnamon

      Combine the flour and salt; place in a plastic bag.  Shake chicken a few pieces at a time in the flour mixture.  Melt butter in a large skillet; brown chicken on all sides.
      In a sauce pan, combine remaining ingredients; bring to a boil and pour over chicken.  Cover and simmer 1 hour.
      Remove chicken to a warm platter.  Bring sauce to a boil and cook, stirring constantly until thickened.
      Serve sauce over chicken.
      Serves 4 - 6 people.
