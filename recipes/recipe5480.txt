BROCCOLI SALAD


1  bunch broccoli
1  c. Cheddar cheese, grated
8  slices bacon, cooked and crumbled
1  medium onion, diced

      Mix broccoli, cheese and onions.  Make dressing.
Dressing:
1/2  c. mayonnaise
1/4  c. sugar
1  tsp. vinegar

      Mix and pour over salad right before serving.  Add crumbled bacon.
