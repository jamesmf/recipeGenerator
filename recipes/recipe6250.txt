BUTTERNUT SQUASH AND LEEK SOUP


2  Tbsp. butter
  white of 2 leeks, sliced
4  minced garlic cloves
  salt
1/2  tsp. thyme
1/2  c. white wine
4  c. cubed squash
5  c. vegetable stock
  Gruyere cheese

      Cook leeks, garlic cloves, salt and thyme in the butter until tender.  Add white wine and cubed squash. Cover with vegetable stock.  Season with salt and pepper and sprinkle with Gruyere cheese and fresh thyme.
