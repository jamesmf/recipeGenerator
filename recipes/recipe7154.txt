BETTY'S CHILI BEAN BAKE


1/4  cup fat or salad oil
3/4  cup sliced onions
1  lb ground beef
1  cup thinly sliced celery
1  tbsp paprika
1/4  tsp allspice
1  can condensed tomato soup
1 (1 lb 4 oz.)  can kidney beans (drained)
2  tsp garlic salt
1  to 1 1/2 tbsp chili powder
1/2  cup grated american cheddar cheese

      Melt the fat in a large skillet.  Add onions and saut√© until tender and golden brown, add ground meat, brown lightly.  Add the rest of the ingredients except for the cheese.  With a 2 tined fork (to avoid breaking the beans) stir until blended.  Pour into a greased 1 1/2 qt casserole.  Sprinkle grated cheese over the top and bake in a 325 degree oven for 30 minutes.
      Serve over buttered thin noodles, rice or hot corn bread with a green salad.
