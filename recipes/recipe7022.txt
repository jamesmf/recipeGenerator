UPSIDE DOWN MEAT PIE


1  Tbsp. shortening
1  lb. ground beef
1  can (8 oz.) tomato sauce
1 1/2  tsp. chili powder
1  green pepper
1/2  c. onion, finely chopped
1  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
1  recipe cornbread

      Melt shortening in 10 inch skillet; add onion and ground beef and brown lightly. Add tomato sauce, salt, chili powder, and pepper; mix thoroughly; simmer while preparing cornbread batter. Slice green pepper into 7 rings. Turn meat out of skillet; place pepper rings around edge of skillet with one in the middle. Spoon meat back over rings; pour cornbread batter over meat. Bake at 425 degrees; for 20 to 25 minutes. Makes 6 servings.
