HOLIDAY BRUNCH CASSEROLE


1  lb. bulk sausage, cooked and drained
4  oz. mushrooms, sliced
1/2  c. green onions, sliced
2  medium tomatoes, chopped
2  c. Mozzarella cheese, shredded
1 1/2  c. buttermilk baking mix
12  eggs
1  c. milk
1 1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper
1/2  tsp. dried oregano leaves

	Layer sausage, mushrooms, green onions, tomatoes and cheese in a 13 x 9 x 2-inch baking dish which has been sprayed with nonstick cooking spray. Beat remaining ingredients; pour over sausage mixture. Bake, uncovered, in a 350 degrees; oven until golden brown. Cool about 30 minutes. Cut into 12 (3-inch) squares. Serves 12.
	May be mixed ahead and refrigerated to save time. Take from the refrigerator and bring to room temperature or use a refrigerator to ovenproof dish and bake.
	Health Hint: Use skim milk, egg substitute and reduced-fat cheese to eliminate 78 calories and 8 grams of fat.
