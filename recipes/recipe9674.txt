OYSTERS IN PATTY SHELLS


1  Tbsp. butter, melted
1/4  tsp. black pepper
1/8  tsp. red pepper
1  can cream of mushroom soup, undiluted
4  green onions with tops, chopped
1  clove garlic, minced
1/2  tsp. dried thyme
1  (12 oz.) container fresh oysters, drained
1 1/2  c. chopped celery
1/2  c. fresh parsley, minced
1  lb. fresh mushrooms, chopped
2 (10 oz.)  pkg. frozen patty shells, baked

      Combine butter and oysters in skillet; cook over low heat for 2 minutes. Add onion, garlic, parsley, celery and mushrooms; heat until celery is crisp-tender. Drain. Stir in seasonings and soup; cook just until thoroughly heated. Spoon into patty shells. Serve immediately. Yield: 12.
