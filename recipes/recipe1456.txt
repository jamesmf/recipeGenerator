SEA SCALLOPS WITH LIME BUTTER


18  sea scallops
3  limes
1/4  lb. unsalted margarine or butter, melted
2  Tbsp. lime juice

      Cut limes into quarters.  Alternately place 3 scallops and 2 lime wedges on each skewer.  Combine melted butter and lime juice. Grill over a medium-high flame for 3 to 4 minutes, basting frequently.
