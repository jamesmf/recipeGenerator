SCALLOPED CHICKEN


1  chicken, cooked and cut up
1  1/2 loaves of bread, cubed
1 1/2  c. chopped celery
1  medium onion, chopped
1  c. margarine
4  c. chicken broth
1/2  c. flour
4  c. milk
2  eggs, beaten
1/2  tsp. pepper
1  Tbsp. salt
2  tsp. poultry seasoning

      Saut√© celery and onion in  margarine.  Then  pour  over bread cubes.  Add chicken.  Mix remaining ingredients and pour into first mixture.  Bake at 275 degrees; for 1 1/2 to 2 hours in 2 (9 x 13-inch) pans which have been greased.
