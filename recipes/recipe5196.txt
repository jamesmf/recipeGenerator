GAIL'S QUICK POTATO SOUP


2  cups cubed raw potatoes
1 1/4  cup boiling water
1  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/4  cup finely chopped onions
1 1/2  cup milk
1  Tbsp. margarine
1/2  tsp. pepper

      In a 2 quart saucepan put potatoes, onions and water.  Cover and cook 15 minutes.  Slightly mash potatoes with a fork to thicken soup.  Add milk and rest of ingredients.  Heat; do not boil.
      Serve with cornbread.
