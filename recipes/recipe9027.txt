CHICKEN-BROCCOLI CASSEROLE


1  small onion, chopped
1/2  stick margarine
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1/2  can water
1 1/2  c. Minute rice, cooked
2  pkg. frozen chopped broccoli, cooked
3  c. chicken, cooked and in bite size pieces
1 large  jar Cheez Whiz

      In large pot, saut margarine and onion. Add soup, water and rice. Mix well. Add broccoli and chicken. Mix all together. Add Cheez Whiz and mix. Pour into casserole dish and bake at 350 degrees; for 40 minutes.
