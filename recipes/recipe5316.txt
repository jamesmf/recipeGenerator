CONGEALED SALAD


1 small  box lime jello
1 small  box lemon jello
2  c. boiling water
1 small  can crushed pineapple
1  c. mayonnaise
1  carton cottage cheese
1/3  c. sweet milk
1/2  c. chopped nuts

      Dissolve jello in boiling water. When cool, add pineapple, mayonnaise, milk, cottage cheese and chopped nuts. Place in refrigerator to mold.
