INMATE ROAST BEEF SPREAD


2  cans roast beef
2  cans chili with beans
1/4  bottle cheese (squeeze type)
4  jalapeno peppers
1/2  bag corn chips
2  pkg. Ramen noodles
1  pkg. flour tortillas

      Mix everything except the flour tortillas together and heat thoroughly. Place mixture in flour tortillas and enjoy. Serves 4.
