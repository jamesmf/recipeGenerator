MEATBALLS


2  lb. hamburger
3  slices bread crumbs
1/2  c. milk
1/4  c. onion, chopped fine
2  eggs
1 1/2  tsp. salt

 
Sauce:
1 1/2  c. catsup
6  Tbsp. butter
6  Tbsp. brown sugar
3  Tbsp. vinegar
6  Tbsp. water

      Mix first 6 ingredients well.  Shape into 1-inch balls. Bake on cookie sheet (with sides) at  375 degrees;  for  25  minutes. Drain off grease.
      Make sauce of sauce ingredients.  Simmer  sauce  for  5 minutes  and add to meatballs.  Put back in  oven  for  a  few minutes.
