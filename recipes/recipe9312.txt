CAROL'S EASY CHICKEN POT PIE


12  oz. (1 large can) chicken breast (drained)
10  oz. (1 can) soup, cream of chicken
15  oz. (1 can) Veg-All (drained)
  salt (to taste)
  pepper (to taste)
2  deep dish pie shells, slightly thawed

      Mix together:  chicken, soup, Veg-All, salt and pepper.  Pour into one pie shell.  Top with the other pie shell, pinching sides to seal.
      Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 to 45 minutes, until thoroughly heated and golden brown on top.
