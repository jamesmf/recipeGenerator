TOMATO CELERY SOUP


1  small onion, chopped
1/2  c. finely chopped celery
2  Tbsp. butter
1 (10 1/2 oz.)  can tomato soup
1  can soup
1  tsp. minced parsley
1  Tbsp. lemon juice
1  tsp. sugar
1/4  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper

      Saut√© onion and celery in butter.  Do not brown.  Add remaining ingredients.  Simmer for 5 minutes.  Celery will remain crisp.  Serve topped with unsweetened whipping cream and chopped parsley.  Serves 3 to 4.
