HOMEMADE CHEESE


3  gal. clabbered milk
2  Tbsp. butter
2  eggs, beaten
1  tsp. soda
1  tsp. sugar
3  tsp. salt
3/4  c. sweet cream

      Scald  clabbered  milk to 150 degrees; or until it's too hot to hold your  hand  in.  Pour  into  cheesecloth,  and let drain overnight.  Crumble  fine.  Melt  butter  in  skillet  and put curds  on  butter.  Mix  2  eggs, soda, sugar, salt and cream. Pour over curds in  skillet.  Heat slowly and stir until ropy. Pour into mold and cool.
