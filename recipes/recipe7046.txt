KIM'S CHILI


1  lb. ground beef
1  lb. sausage
1  large onion, chopped
1  can Rotel tomatoes
1 large  can Ranch Style beans
1 small  can tomato paste
  chili powder

      Brown ground beef, sausage, and onion; drain and put back in pot. Add other ingredients and enough water to cover ingredients about 2 inches. Add chili powder and salt to taste. Simmer for about 1 hour.
