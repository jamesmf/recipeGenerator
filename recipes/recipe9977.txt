CREAMY SPINACH BAKE


2 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese, softened
2  cans cream of mushroom soup
4 (10 oz.)  pkg. frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
2  cans French fried onions (2.8 oz. each)
2/3  c. saltines
1/4  c. butter, melted

      In a bowl, beat the cream cheese until smooth. Add the soup mix; mix well. Stir in spinach and onions. Transfer to a greased 2 1/2-quart baking dish. Combine cracker crumbs and butter. Sprinkle over spinach mixture. Bake, uncovered, at 325 degrees; for 30 to 35 minutes.
