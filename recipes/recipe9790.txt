MEATBALLS ESPANOL

Meatballs:
1  pound ground beef
1  cup soft bread crumbs
1/4  cup finely chopped onion
1/4  cup finely chopped celery
1 1/2  tsp. worcestershire sauce
1  egg
1 1/2  tsp. garlic salt
1/4  tsp. pepper

 
Sauce:
1  can (16 oz.) stewed tomatoes (canned or fresh)
12  oz. (2 1/2 cups) thinly sliced zucchini
1/2  tsp. crushed oregano
1/2  tsp. basil
1/2  tsp. sugar
1 1/2  tsp. garlic salt
1  cup beef broth (from beef bouillon)
1  Tbsp. cornstarch

      Combine and mix thoroughly beef, bread crumbs, onion, celery, worcestershire sauce, egg, salt, and pepper. Form into balls and place in a greased, shallow baking dish.  Bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes.
      While meatballs are baking combine the tomatoes, zucchini, oregano, basil, sugar, and garlic salt.  Simmer for 5 minutes. Combine the beef broth and cornstarch.  Stir this into the tomato mixture.  Pour over meatballs and continue to bake for 10 minutes or longer.  Serve over a bed of 3 cups of hot, cooked rice. Serves 6.
