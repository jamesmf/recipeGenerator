CONCH FRITTER


5  large conch
1  tsp. baking powder
1  egg, beaten
  fat for frying
1 1/4  c. flour
1/2  tsp. salt
1  c. milk
  small piece hot pepper

      Clean and pound conchs.  Slice very thin or dice very small. In bowl, sift together flour, baking powder and salt.  Make a well in the center.  Combine egg with milk and gradually add to center of flour mixture.  Blend gently to make a smooth batter.  Mix conch in batter; fry in deep fat.
      This is a famous dish from the Bahamas.
