EGGS IN A BASKET



6  slices soft whole wheat bread
  softened butter
6  eggs
1/4  c. grated cheese

      Preheat oven to 400 degrees;.  Trim crusts off bread; spread both sides of bread with butter.  Fit bread slices into cups of muffin tin. Place in oven until lightly toasted; remove from oven.  Break an egg into bread and top with cheese.  Return to oven and heat 12 to 15 minutes.  Slip baskets carefully from the tins.  Cool a bit.
