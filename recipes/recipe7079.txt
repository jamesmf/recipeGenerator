CHARCZYNSKI CHICKEN


  boneless chicken
1/4  lb. Swiss cheese
1  can cream of chicken soup
2 1/2  c. dry stuffing mix
1  stick margarine, melted

      Mix stuffing mix and margarine together. Grease 13 x 9 pan, then place chicken in it. Put cheese over chicken. Cover with soup, then layer stuffing mixture over top. Bake at 325 degrees; for 35 minutes.
