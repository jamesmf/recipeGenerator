COUNTRY APPLE CAKE WITH CARAMEL SAUCE

Cake:
2  Tbsp. oil
1  pkg. Pillsbury nut quick bread mix
3  c. apple pie filling (from two 21 oz. cans)
2  tsp. cinnamon
1/2  c. chopped walnuts
1  egg

 
Sauce:
1/2  c. sugar
1/2  c. butter
1  tsp. vanilla
1/2  c. packed brown sugar
1/2  c. whipping cream

      Heat oven to 325 degrees;.  Spray a 13 x 9-inch pan with nonstick spray.  In a large bowl, combine oil and egg; beat well.  Add bread mix, apple pie filling, cinnamon and nutmeg.  Stir until mix is moistened.  Stir in walnuts.  Spoon into pan; spread evenly.  Bake 45 to 55 minutes.  Cool 30 minutes.
      Meanwhile, in a medium saucepan, mix sugars, butter and cream. Bring to a boil, stirring occasionally.  Remove from heat; stir in vanilla.
      To Serve:  Cut warm cake into squares; top with warm sauce, ice cream or whipped cream, if desired.
