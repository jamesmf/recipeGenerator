ORANGE CHOCOLATE CHIP COOKIES


1 (3 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
1/2  c. butter
1/2  c. sugar
1  egg
1  tsp. orange rind, grated
1  tsp. vanilla
1  c. flour
  dash of salt
1 (6 oz.)  pkg. chocolate chips

      Cream the cream cheese, butter and sugar.  Add egg, orange rind and vanilla; mix well.  Add flour, salt and chips.  Drop by spoonfuls on cookie sheet.  Bake for 15 minutes at 350 degrees;.  Makes 3 dozen.
