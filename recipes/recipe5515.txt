TUNA MACARONI SALAD


1 8  oz. box macaroni
1  c. cheddar cheese
1  c. mayonnaise
1/4  c. pickles
1/4  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
1 7  oz. can of tuna
1/4  c. minced onions

      Cook macaroni and drain well.  Rinse in cold water.  Mix macaroni with rest of ingredients.  Chill and serve with crackers.
