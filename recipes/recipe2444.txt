"SUGAR COOKIES"


1  c. granulated sugar
1  c. confectioners sugar
1  c. butter or margarine
1  c. vegetable oil
2  eggs
2  tsp. vanilla
1  tsp. soda
1  tsp. cream of tartar
5 1/4  c. plain flour

      Cream sugars and butter or margarine.  Add eggs; stir in oil and vanilla.  Combine dry ingredients.  Add to mixture.  Blend well.  Refrigerate overnight.  Form into small balls; place on greased cookie sheet.  Use small glass dipped in white sugar to press balls flat.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 12 to 15 minutes.
