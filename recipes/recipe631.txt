APPLE BREAD PUDDING


4  slices thin bread
1 1/2  c. nonfat dry milk powder, liquified, or 1 can evaporated skim milk
3  eggs or equivalent Egg Beaters
5  packets Sweet 'N Low or Equal
1/2  tsp. cinnamon
1/4  tsp. nutmeg
3  apples, pared and diced

      Toast bread; cut into cubes.  Place in baking dish.  Heat milk to boiling; pour over bread.  Let stand 15 minutes.  Beat eggs with sweetener, cinnamon and nutmeg.  Add apples.  Combine with bread mixture and stir.  Bake at 325 degrees; for 35 minutes.
