CHICKEN SCALLOPINI


2  whole chicken breasts, skinned, boned and halved
1 egg, beaten with 1  Tbsp. water
1/2  c. Italian seasoned bread crumbs
1/4  c. olive oil
1  clove garlic
1/2  tsp. basil
1/4  c. sherry
2  Tbsp. lemon juice
2  tsp. capers

      Pound chicken breasts thin.  Dip chicken into beaten egg, then into seasoned bread crumbs.  Heat oil with garlic and remove garlic.  Brown chicken in oil for 3 minutes on each side over medium heat.  Add sherry, basil and lemon juice.  Top with capers. Simmer 5 to 6 minutes or until tender.  Makes 4 servings.
