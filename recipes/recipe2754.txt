MEMORIAL CHEESECAKE


1 (3 oz.)  pkg. lemon jello
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. Philadelphia cream cheese
1/2  c. sugar
1 large  carton Cool Whip

      Mix Jell-O with 1 cup hot water and set aside to cool.  Mix sugar and cream cheese and whip.  Add Jell-O and fold in Cool Whip. Pour in crust (graham or the 3 layer dessert crust).  Refrigerate.
Crust:
1 3/4  c. graham cracker crumbs, about 30 (2 1/2 inch square) crackers
1/4  c. pecans, chopped fine
1/2  tsp. cinnamon
2  Tbsp. sugar
1/4  c. melted butter or margarine

      Combine and press in pie plate.
