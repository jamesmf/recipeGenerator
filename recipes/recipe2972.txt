STUFFED BAKED MUSHROOMS


20  medium mushroom caps
8  oz. pkg. cream cheese, softened
3/4  c. freshly grated Parmesan cheese
1/4  c. real bacon bits
1  c. Cheddar cheese, shredded
2  gloves garlic, minced

      Preheat oven to 375 degrees;. In a mixing bowl, blend cream cheese, Parmesan, bacon bits and garlic. Spoon about 1 tablespoon of the mixture into each mushroom cap and sprinkle with the Cheddar cheese on top. Place mushroom caps in a baking dish and bake until cheese is melted. Serve hot!
