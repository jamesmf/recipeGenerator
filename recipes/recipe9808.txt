BAKED BEANS


1  lb. (plus or minus) ground beef
2  onions
2  Tbsp. mustard
2 large  cans pork and beans
1/2  c. barbecue sauce
1/4  c. catsup
2  Tbsp. syrup or honey
1/2  c. brown sugar or enough to cover beans
  salt, pepper, Tabasco and Worcestershire sauce to taste

      Brown ground beef and onions.  Mix all ingredients in casserole.  Cover with brown sugar and bake in a casserole at 350 degrees; for one hour.
