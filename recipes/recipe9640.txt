ITALIAN CHICKEN STEW


  Chicken Breast, boneless, cut into fingers
  Salt
  Lemon Pepper
15  oz. Del Monte Tomatoes w/basil, garlic & oregano
  Carrots, sliced
  Potatoes, sliced thin

      Sprinkle salt and lemon pepper on  chicken fingers and brown in a little oil in pan on stove top (just a few minutes). Add carrots, potatoes, and tomatoes; salt & pepper to taste. Put in oven at 350 degrees, without lid, for about 15-20 minutes. Then cover and cook until done, approximately 45 minutes to 1 hour.
