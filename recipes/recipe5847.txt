HODGE PODGE SOUP


1  lb. hamburger meat
  onion
  salt and pepper to taste
2  c. water
2  cans minestrone soup
2  cans Ro-Tel tomatoes
2  cans red kidney beans, drained

      Brown hamburger meat with onion, salt and pepper to taste and drain. Add remaining ingredients.
      OPTIONAL: Cook Garden Variety rotelle noodles as per package directions and add to soup.
      Cook on stovetop until hot.
