WILD RICE CASSEROLE


1  pkg. wild rice mix
1/2  c. chopped onion
1/2  c. margarine
3  c. chopped cooked chicken
1  c. cream of chicken soup
1 (6 oz.)  can sliced mushrooms
1/4  c. chopped pimento
1  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
1/2  c. chopped pecans
