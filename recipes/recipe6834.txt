CHICKEN ENCHILADAS


3  large chicken breasts, cooked and cut up bite size
1 to 1 1/2  lb. grated cheese (jalapeno, Cheddar, whatever you like)
2  jalapenos, diced up
12  tortilla shells
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  can milk (soup can)
3/4  c. salsa

      Place chicken breast in large bowl.  Mix in the cheese and jalapenos.  Put mixture on the tortilla shells.  Roll and place in 9 x 12 sprayed pan.  Mix together the soup, milk and salsa.  Dump over tortillas.  Sprinkle with some grated cheese.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 1/2 to 3/4 hour (cover with foil).
