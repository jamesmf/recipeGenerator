GERMAN COLE SLAW


2  1/2 to 3 heads cabbage, grated
1  large green pepper, finely cut
1  large onion, finely cut
4  stalks celery, finely cut

 
Dressing:
1/2  c. plus 2 Tbsp. vinegar
1  c. sugar
2  tsp. salt
1  tsp. mustard seed
1  tsp. celery seed

      Mix all dressing ingredients together.  Pour over cabbage and let set overnight.  Keep refrigerated.
