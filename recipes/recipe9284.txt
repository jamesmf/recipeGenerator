MEXICAN GOULASH


1  lb. ground beef
1  c. rice
1  c. kidney beans
2  onions, chopped
14  oz. (1 can) tomatoes
4 to 6  slices bacon

      In a skillet, brown ground beef.
      Cook rice according to package directions.
      Mix together:  ground beef, rice, kidney beans, onion and tomatoes.  Crumbled bacon and put on top.  Cook 5 minutes longer on low heat.
