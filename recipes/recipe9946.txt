REFRIGERATOR MASHED POTATOES


9  large potatoes, peeled
1  medium onion
2 (3 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
1/2  tsp. salt or to taste
1/4  tsp. pepper
2  Tbsp. butter

      Cook peeled potatoes and onion until tender in boiling water. Drain.  Mash until smooth.  Add remaining ingredients and beat until light and fluffy.  Cool and put in refrigerator until ready to use.
      To use, place desired amount in greased casserole.  Dot with butter.  Bake at 350 degrees; about 30 minutes.
