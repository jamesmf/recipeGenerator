CHILI-CHEESE DIP


2  oz. cream cheese, softened
1  can Hormel chili without beans
1 1/2  c. shredded Cheddar cheese

      Spread softened cream cheese into the bottom of glass dish. Then spread chili over cream cheese. Sprinkle Cheddar cheese over the chili. Bake at 350 degrees; for 20 minutes or until cheese starts to bubble. Serve with either Doritos or Tostitos.
