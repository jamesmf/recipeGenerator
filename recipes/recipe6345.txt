CAROLINA STYLE BARBECUE SAUCE


1/3  c. apple cider vinegar
1/2  c. yellow mustard
1/2  c. brown sugar
2  Tbsp. water
1  Tbsp. chili powder
1/2  tsp. black pepper
  dash Tabasco sauce
1/2  tsp. soy sauce
1  Tbsp. butter

      In a medium saucepan combine the vinegar, mustard, sugar, water, chili powder, pepper and Tabasco. Simmer for 25 minutes. Add the soy sauce and butter and simmer for 5 minutes longer. Use as  you would any barbecue sauce. Great for grilled chicken or pork.
