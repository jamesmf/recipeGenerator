TUNA NOODLE CASSEROLE


3  cans cream of chicken or celery soup
2  pkg. medium noodles
3 medium  cans tuna or 2 large cans of tuna
1  pkg. Velveeta cheese slices
1/4  lb. Velveeta cheese block, cubed
2  c. milk
  Butter
1  pkg. potato chips

      Cook noodles in salted water according to package directions. In a large buttered casserole dish mix soup, tuna and a little milk.  Add noodles to mixture along with cubed cheese.  Also place chunks of butter throughout.  Add 1 to 2 cups of milk throughout for moisture.  Crush chips over top of casserole. Cover with cheese slices and dot with butter pats.  Bake in a 325 degrees; oven for 45 - 50 minutes until good and hot and the cheese on top is nicely melted and browned.
