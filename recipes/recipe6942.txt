POT ROAST AND VEGETABLES
(In Cooking Bag)  

3 to 4  lb. beef roast
6  medium potatoes, peeled
1/3  c. water
  flour
6  carrots
1  envelope dry onion soup mix

      Trim excess fat from meat. Rub roast with flour. Place bag in pan and sprinkle 1/2 of onion soup mix on the bottom. Put roast in bag and arrange vegetables; add water. Tie bag and cook in preheated oven at 325 degrees; for 2 1/2 hours.
