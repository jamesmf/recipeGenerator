DANDY DOGS


1  c. self-rising flour
1/4  tsp. paprika
1/8  tsp. cayenne pepper
1/2  c. milk
2  Tbsp. yellow corn meal
1/2  tsp. dry mustard
1  egg
1  lb. wieners

      Mix all ingredients well; dip wieners in batter and deep fry until brown. Push a wooden stick into the end and enjoy good old dandy dogs. Best yet!
