ROAST LAMB
(Arni Psito)  

5  lb. leg of lamb
3  cloves garlic, minced
  juice of 2 lemons
  salt and pepper
  oregano
1  c. water
4  Tbsp. butter

      Cut incisions in meat; stuff with garlic mixed with salt and pepper.  Rub leg of lamb with lemon juice and sprinkle additional salt and pepper.  Place in roasting pan with water and top with butter.  Bake at 450 degrees; until browned; lower to 300 degrees;.  Cover and bake until done.  When meat is half cooked, peeled and quartered potatoes can be placed around meat.  Uncover 10 minutes before serving to allow potatoes to brown.  Makes 6 to 8 servings.
