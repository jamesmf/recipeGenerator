CHICKEN DRESSING CASSEROLE


1  whole chicken or 6 breasts
1  can cream of mushroom soup*
1  can cream of celery soup*
3/4  stick margarine
1/2  pt. sour cream
  Stove Top or homemade stuffing

      *Any creamy soup will work.
      Stew chicken; remove meat from bones if using whole chicken. Save 1 1/2 cups broth. Place chicken pieces in large casserole dish. Mix together soups, margarine and sour cream; pour over chicken and cover with prepared Stove Top or homemade stuffing. Pour broth over stuffing. Cover and bake at 350 degrees; for 40 minutes. Uncover and bake for an additional 20 minutes.
       Serve with cranberries and it's like a Thanksgiving feast.
