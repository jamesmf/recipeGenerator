SEAFOOD CREPES ACAPULCO


1/2  c. Jack cheese, shredded
1/2  c. Ricotta cheese
8  oz. crab legs (imitation or real)
2  green onions, sliced
1/4  c. canned diced green chiles
8  corn tortillas

 
Sauce:
3  Tbsp. butter
3  Tbsp. flour
1  c. milk
1/2  c. chicken stock
  salt and cayenne pepper to taste

      Mix cheeses, crab, onions and chiles.  Soften corn tortillas in hot oil for a few seconds.  Lay filling in middle of tortillas and roll up.  Lay, seam side down, in greased 9 x 11-inch baking pan. Spoon sauce over and bake 25 to 30 minutes at 350 degrees; until heated through.
      Sauce:  Melt butter.  Whisk in flour.  Stir in remaining ingredients.  Continue cooking while stirring until thickened. Yields 4 servings.
