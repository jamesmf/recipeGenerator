OKRA FRITTERS


1  c. thinly sliced okra
1/2  c. chopped onion
1/2  c. chopped tomato
1/4  c. all-purpose flour
1/4  c. cornmeal
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. curry powder
1/4  tsp. pepper
1  egg, beaten
  hot peanut oil

      Combine first 9 ingredients, stirring well.  Drop mixture by tablespoonfuls into hot oil.  Cook until golden brown, turning once.
