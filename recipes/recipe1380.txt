FRESH FRUIT SALAD


1  watermelon
1  cantaloupe
1  honeydew melon
2  bananas
1  c. seedless grapes
3  Tbsp. frozen orange juice concentrate
2  Tbsp. frozen lemonade concentrate

      Scoop out 3 cups watermelon balls and all the cantaloupe and honeydew balls you can get.  Combine melon balls and grapes in large mixing bowl.  Add the frozen juices and mix well.  Slice and add bananas just before serving.
