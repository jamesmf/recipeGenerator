SHRIMP AND FETA CHEESE WITH MOSTACCIOLI


1 1/2  lb. medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
2  Tbsp. lemon juice
6  green onions, chopped
1  clove garlic, peeled and chopped
2  Tbsp. olive oil
4  small ripe tomatoes, peeled and chopped
1/2  c. dry white wine
1  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. each:  pepper and basil
2  Tbsp. fresh parsley, chopped
3/4  c. cubed Feta cheese
1 (16 oz.)  pkg. mostaccioli or other short pasta

      Wash and pat shrimp dry.  Sprinkle with lemon juice.  Saut√© green onions and garlic in oil in a large skillet until tender, about 3 minutes.  Stir in chopped tomatoes, wine, salt, pepper, basil and 1 tablespoon parsley.  Lower heat; simmer, uncovered, for 15 minutes or until mixture thickens slightly; stir in shrimp and lemon juice; cook over medium heat just until shrimp turn pink and feel firm, about 5 minutes.  Remove from heat; add cheese and sprinkle with remaining parsley.
      Cook mostaccioli in boiling, salted water until tender but firm.  Drain and toss with shrimp-cheese mixture.
