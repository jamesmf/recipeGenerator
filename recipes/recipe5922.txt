BUTTERMILK DRESSING


1/2  c. buttermilk
1/2  c. mayonnaise
1  tsp. parsley flakes
1  tsp. instant minced onion
1/2  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/8  tsp. garlic powder
  salt and pepper to taste

      Measure ingredients into container, seal and shake well. Refrigerate several hours, then shake before serving. Makes 1 cup.
