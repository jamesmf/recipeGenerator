HOMEMADE CHICKEN AND NOODLES

Homemade Noodles:
1  egg, beaten
2  Tbsp. milk
1/2  tsp. salt
1  c. flour

	Combine egg, salt and milk. Add enough flour to make stiff dough. Roll very thin on floured surface and let stand 20 minutes. Roll up and slice 1/8-inch wide. Spread out and dry 2 hours.
Chicken:
1  whole chicken, cut in quarters and skinned
1  can chicken broth
  seasoning to taste

	Place chicken, broth and seasoning in large pot and fill with water. Place on medium heat and bring to a boil. Boil for 3 minutes then lower heat and simmer until chicken comes off the bone. Remove bones. Bring back to boil and add noodles. Cook about 10 minutes until noodles are cooked.
