MEAT LOAF


2  lb. hamburger
2  eggs, beaten
2  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  Tbsp. soy sauce
3  Tbsp. steak sauce (your choice)
  salt, pepper, Lawry's and garlic salt (to taste)
1  c. onion, chopped fine
1  c. crackers, crushed fine
1  Tbsp. deli mustard

      Mix all together. Bake in 9 x 13 pan at 350 degrees; for 1 hour.
      For Something Different: Add a box of prepared stuffing mix to the top before baking.
