CREAMY CAULIFLOWER SOUP


1  medium head cauliflower, broken into small pieces
1/4  c. chopped onions
1/4  c. butter
1/4  c. flour
3  c. chicken broth
2  c. milk
1  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
4  oz. shredded sharp cheese
  snipped chives

      Cook cauliflower in saltwater 10 to 15 minutes; drain.  In saucepan, cook onion in butter until tender.  Blend in flour.  Add chicken broth, milk and Worcestershire sauce.  Cook until slightly thickened.  Add cauliflower.  Bring to boil; add cheese.  Sprinkle chives over each serving.
