PINEAPPLE CONGEALED SALAD


1 (15 oz.)  can crushed pineapple
2  c. buttermilk
1  c. flaked coconut
1  (12 oz.) frozen whipped topping
1 (6 oz.)  pkg. orange flavored gelatin
1  c. chopped pecans

      Place undrained pineapple in saucepan; bring to a boil, stirring constantly. Remove from heat. Add gelatin, stirring until dissolved. Stir in buttermilk, coconut and pecans, then stir in whipped topping. Pour into a 13 x 9 x 2-inch dish. Chill until firm.
