MACARONI AMBROSIA


1/2  box acini de pepe (very small macaroni)
2 regular  cans chunk pineapple
2 small  cans Mandarin oranges
3/4  c. sugar
2  Tbsp. flour
1  Tbsp. cornstarch
1  tsp. salt
2  eggs
1  Tbsp. lemon juice
1  jar maraschino cherries
9  oz. Cool Whip

      Boil and drain acini de pepe.  Drain pineapple and oranges and chill.  Save juice.  Add sugar, flour, cornstarch, salt, eggs and lemon juice to fruit juice.  Cook until thick.  Cool.  Pour over macaroni.  Refrigerate.  Drain cherries.  Cut in halves.  Before serving, mix fruit, cherries, macaroni and Cool Whip.
