HAMBURGER, MACARONI AND CHEESE CASSEROLE


1  c. or 1/2 lb. hamburger
1  c. grated cheese
1  c. onion or less
1  c. cooked and drained macaroni
1  pt. tomatoes
  salt and pepper to taste

      Fry hamburger until light brown.  Saut onions in fat.  Pour in tomatoes and simmer 30 minutes.  Combine all ingredients.  Pour in casserole and sprinkle a little of the cheese on top and bake in moderate oven about 30 minutes.
