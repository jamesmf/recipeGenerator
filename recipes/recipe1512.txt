HARD ROLLS


1 cake fresh or 1  pkg. dry yeast
2  Tbsp. sugar
1  tsp. salt
2  egg whites, beaten stiff
1  c. lukewarm water
2  Tbsp. shortening
4  c. flour

      Soften yeast with 1/4 cup lukewarm water; add remaining water. Add sugar and shortening.  Add 1 cup flour; beat well.  Add yeast and egg whites; mix through.  Add remaining flour and salt to make soft dough.  Rise once; punch down and let rise again.  Brush with egg yolk diluted with water.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 15 to 20 minutes.
