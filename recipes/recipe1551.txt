STRAWBERRY MILK SHAKE


1  qt. strawberries
5  c. milk
1/2  c. cream
3/4  c. sugar
1/4  tsp. salt
2 1/2  tsp. lemon juice

      Crush strawberries and press through a coarse sieve; there should be at least 1 2/3 cups puree.  Combine with milk and cream; add remaining ingredients and mix thoroughly.  Chill well before serving.  Top each glass with a spoonful of whipped cream.  Serves 8 to 10.
