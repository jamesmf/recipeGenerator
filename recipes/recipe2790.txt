COLOR COOKIES


3  eggs
1  c. brown sugar
1  tsp. Karo syrup
1  stick oleo
4 1/2  c. oatmeal
1/2  c. M&M's candies
1  c. white sugar
1  tsp. vanilla
2  tsp. baking soda
1 1/2  c. peanut butter
6  oz. chocolate chips
1/2  c. raisins

      Mix ingredients.  Drop by teaspoon onto greased baking sheet. Bake at 350 degrees; for 8 to 10 minutes.
