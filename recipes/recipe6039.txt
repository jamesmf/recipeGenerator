JAPANESE SALAD


1  large head lettuce
3  small green onions
3  oz. Chow Mein noodles
2  Tbsp. poppy seed
4  oz. sliced almonds
4  Tbsp. sugar
4  Tbsp. vinegar
1/2  tsp. black pepper
1/2  c. oil
2  tsp. salt

      Mix lettuce, green onions, poppy seed and almonds; refrigerate overnight.  Mix sugar, vinegar, black pepper, oil and salt.  Just before serving, add noodles to salad; pour on dressing and toss.  Serve and enjoy!
