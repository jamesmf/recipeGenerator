CHICKEN ENCHILADAS


2  c. cooked boneless chicken, cut up fine
1  can cream of chicken soup
8  oz. sour cream
1  can green chilies chopped (4.5 oz.)
1  large onion, chopped fine
1  lb. grated medium cheddar cheese
12  flour tortillas

      Combine chicken, onion & 2/3 of cheese. Roll in tortillas. Put in pan. Mix soup & green chilies & heat; then add sour cream & heat thoroughly. Pour over tortillas, then add remaining cheese. Bake at 350 degrees for 25 minutes or until bubbly.
