MONSTER COOKIES


12  beaten eggs
2  lb. brown sugar
4  c. sugar
1  Tbsp. brown syrup (or white)
8  tsp. soda
1  lb. oleo
3  lb. crunchy peanut butter
18  c. oatmeal
1  Tbsp. vanilla
1  lb. chocolate chips
1  lb. M&M's candy

      Mix in order given:  eggs, brown sugar, sugar, syrup, soda, oleo, peanut butter, oatmeal, vanilla, chocolate chips and M&M's. Drop on a cookie sheet with a large spoon and then flatten batter with a fork.  Bake for 13 minutes at 350 degrees;.
