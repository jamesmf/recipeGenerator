CORNBREAD SALAD


1  c. self-rising cornmeal
2  Tbsp. plain flour
1  c. sour cream (8 oz.)
1  stick butter, melted
2  eggs
1  c. English peas
1  c. onions, chopped
1  c. green peppers, chopped
1  c. fresh tomatoes, chopped
1  c. sweet pickles, cubed
1  c. mayonnaise
1  Tbsp. sugar
1  c. shredded cheese (of choice)

      Mix cornmeal, flour, sour cream, butter and eggs. Bake until done at 350 degrees;. Let cool and crumble in a 9 x 13-inch dish. Add one layer at a time the peas, onions, pepper, tomatoes and pickles. Top with 1 cup mayonnaise and 1 tablespoon sugar, mixed well. Then sprinkle with the cheese.
