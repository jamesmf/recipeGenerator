CHICKEN SALAD


2 1/2  c. diced cooked chicken
1  c. nuts
1  c. celery
1  c. white seedless grapes, cut up
1  c. mayonnaise
1/2  c. Cool Whip
1  tsp. minced onion

      Combine all ingredients and chill.  Can be served with wheat crackers, or on your choice on bread, cut into your choice of shapes.
