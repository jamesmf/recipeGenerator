CHICKEN SALAD ON BUNS


2  c. diced, leftover, cooked chicken
1/4  lb. process American cheese, diced
1 to 2  Tbsp. pickle relish
1/4  c. salad dressing or mayonnaise
2  Tbsp. chopped onion
2  Tbsp. chopped green pepper
  kaiser rolls

      In a bowl, combine the first 6 ingredients; mix well. Spoon about 1/3 cup onto each roll. Wrap each tightly in foil. Bake at 300 degrees; for 20 to 30 minutes or until heated through. Yield: 6 to 8 servings.
