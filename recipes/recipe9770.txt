VEGETABLE CASSEROLE


2  cans cream of potato soup
1  c. milk
2  c. cut and cooked chicken
1/4  tsp. pepper
1/4  tsp. thyme
4  c. cut up frozen vegetables
1 (10 oz.)  pkg. refrigerator biscuits

      In a 3-quart shallow baking dish, mix soups, milk, pepper, thyme, vegetables and cooked cut up chicken. Bake at 400 degrees; for 15 minutes; stir. Arrange biscuits around edge of pan and bake for 15 minutes longer.
       This is a nice looking and great tasting dish.
