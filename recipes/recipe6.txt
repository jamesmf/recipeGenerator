CHOCOLATE PECAN PIE


1  c. sugar
1/2  c. all purpose flour
1/2  c. melted butter or margarine, cooled
1  egg, slightly beaten
1  tsp. vanilla extract
1 (6 oz.)  pkg. semi-sweet chocolate morsels
1  c. chopped pecans
1  unbaked 9-inch pastry shell
  ice cream, whipped cream or whipped topping

      Combine first 7 ingredients and pour into pastry shell.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 35 to 40 minutes.  If baked earlier in the day, reheat for 10 to 15 minutes.  Serve warm with ice cream, whipped cream or whipped topping.  Yields one 9-inch pie.
