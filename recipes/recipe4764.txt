


4  c. shredded cabbage
1  c. chopped green pepper
1/2  c. chopped onion
1/4  c. cider vinegar
1/2  c. apple juice or cider
3  Tbsp. vegetable oil
8  packets Equal
1  tsp. celery seed
1  tsp. dry mustard
1  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper

      Combine first 3 ingredients in bowl. Whisk together all remaining ingredients. Pour over slaw. Toss to blend. Cover and refrigerate until ready to serve. Makes 8 servings.
