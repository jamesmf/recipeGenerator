MANDARIN ORANGE SALAD

Salad:
1/4  to 1/2 head Iceberg Lettuce
1/4  to 1/2 head Romaine Lettuce
1  cup chopped Celery
  2-3 Green Onions with tips thinly sliced
1  can Mandarin oranges (drained)

 
Almonds:
1/4 to 1/3  cup sliced Almonds (with skin)
1  tbsp plus 1 tsp sugar

 
Dressing:
1/4  cup Salad oil
2  tbsp Vinegar
1/2  tsp Salt
  dash of pepper
  dash of tabasco sauce
1  tbsp snipped parsley

      1. Mix first 4 ingredients in a large bowl.
      2. Cook almonds and sugar in a small non-stick skillet over medium heat until sugar melts and almonds are lightly coated with caramelized sugar.  Watch carefully! Add almonds and 1 can of drained mandarin oranges to salad.
      3. Combine dressing ingredients into a covered jar and shake well, pour over the salad and toss when ready to serve.
