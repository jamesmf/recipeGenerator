CRABMEAT SPREAD


1  lb. pkg. imitation crabmeat, chopped fine
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese, softened
1  Tbsp. mayonnaise
1  Tbsp. sour cream
2  Tbsp. finely chopped onion
  dash of Tabasco sauce

	Mix cream cheese, mayonnaise, sour cream, onion and Tabasco until smooth. Stir in crabmeat. Serve on crackers with Cocktail Sauce.
Cocktail Sauce:
1  c. catsup
2  tsp. prepared horseradish
1  tsp. lemon juice

	Mix all 3 ingredients well. Add more horseradish if you like a stronger sauce.
