CHICKEN RICE CASSEROLE


1  lb. or so cooked, cubed chicken
1/4  c. chopped onion
1  c. chopped celery
2  Tbsp. shortening
1/2  c. uncooked rice
1  c. peas
1  tsp. salt
  pinch of pepper
1  c. chicken broth
1  c. water
1  can chicken rice or cream of chicken soup

      Brown chicken, onion and celery in the shortening.  Add rice, salt, pepper, chicken broth, water and soup.  Mix together.  Bake 1 to 1 1/2 hours at 325 degrees;.
