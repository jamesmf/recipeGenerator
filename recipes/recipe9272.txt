CHICKEN WITH NOODLES


5  chicken breast halves
1  tsp. poultry seasoning
2  cans cream of chicken soup
1  stick margarine
1  pkg. egg noodles

      Stew chicken with poultry seasoning until tender.  Remove chicken from broth; cool and debone.  Add noodles to broth and cook until tender.  Add chicken and cream of chicken soup; cover and simmer for 15 minutes.
