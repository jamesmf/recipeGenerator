MERINGUE

9-inch Pie:
3  egg whites
1/4  tsp. cream of tartar
6  Tbsp. sugar
1/2  tsp. flavoring

 
8-inch Pie:
2  egg whites
1/4  tsp. cream of tartar
1/4  c. sugar
1/4  tsp. flavoring

      Beat egg whites with cream of tartar until frothy.  Gradually beat in sugar, a little at a time.  Continue beating until stiff and glossy.  Do not underbeat.  Beat until sugar is dissolved.  Add flavoring.  Pile meringue onto hot pie filling, being careful to seal edges.  Bake at 400 degrees; for 8 to 10 minutes until delicately browned.
