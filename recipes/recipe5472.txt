STRAWBERRY SALAD


1 1/2  c. hot water
2  pkg. strawberry jello
1  pt. strawberries
1  can crushed pineapple
2  mashed bananas
1  c. pecans
1  carton sour cream

      Mix hot water and strawberry jello.  Add strawberries, pineapple, bananas and pecans.  Pour 1/2 of this in bowl and let congeal.  Spread 1 carton sour cream over it, then pour rest of jello mixture over sour cream.  Refrigerate until congealed.
