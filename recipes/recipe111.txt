BACHELOR BUTTONS


2  c. sifted flour
3/4  tsp. salt
1  tsp. soda
1  c. brown sugar, packed
3/4  c. shortening
1  egg
1  tsp. vanilla
1/2  c. of any of the following:  nuts, coconut, candied or maraschino cherries, chocolate chips or raisins

      Measure and sift together flour, salt and soda.  In a separate bowl, cream sugar and shortening; add egg, beating until light and fluffy.  Add vanilla and nuts or fruit and stir with rubber spatula to blend; add flour mixture.  Drop from a teaspoon onto greased cookie sheet or shape into crescents with floured fingertips.  Bake at 375 degrees; about 12 minutes.
