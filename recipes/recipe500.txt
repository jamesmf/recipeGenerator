PEANUT BUTTER CANDY


3  c. white sugar
3/4  c. plus 2 Tbsp. white syrup
1/4  c. plus 2 Tbsp. water
3  egg whites, beaten stiff
  peanut butter

      Mix syrup, sugar and water over high heat.  Bring to a boil and boil until hard ball forms.  Remove from heat.  Pour in egg whites and beat until thick and workable.  Roll into 1/8-inch thick sheet on a mixture of flour and confectioners sugar.  Spread with peanut butter.  Roll up into a log, chill and slice.
