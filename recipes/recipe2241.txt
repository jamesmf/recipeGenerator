SAUSAGE MILK GRAVY


2  Tbsp. sausage grease
1  patty sausage, cooked
1/2  c. all-purpose flour
  milk
  salt and pepper

      Heat grease in skillet.  Crumble the sausage into small pieces and add to hot grease.  Sprinkle flour into grease, stirring constantly, until all is combined.  Add milk until desired consistency; add salt and pepper to taste.
