BETTY'S ICICLE PICKLES


2  c. lime
1  gal. sliced cucumbers
9  c. sugar (5 lb.)
3  pt. apple cider vinegar
2  tsp. celery seed
2  tsp. pickling spice
  whole cloves
3  cinnamon sticks
  salt

      Mix lime and 2 gallons of water; soak cucumbers in solution for 24 hours. Drain and rinse well. Soak 2 to 3 hours more in plain water; drain. Make a syrup of sugar, apple cider vinegar, and celery seed; bring to a boil and pour over cucumbers. Let stand 12 hours. Add pickling spice, a few cloves, cinnamon sticks and salt. Boil 20 minutes. Pack in jars and seal. Wonderful!
