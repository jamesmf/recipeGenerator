COCKTAIL MEATBALLS


2  lb. hamburger
1/4  c. packaged bread crumbs
1  egg
1  envelope onion soup

      Stir together.  Make meatballs and brown in 1 Tbsp. butter. Mix 20 oz. bottle of tangy Brooks catsup and 1 (10 oz.) jar apple or currant jelly.  Stir and pour over meatballs.  Let simmer for 25 minutes.
