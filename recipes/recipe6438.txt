KANSAS CUCUMBER SALAD


1  cup low-fat mayonnaise
1/4  cup sugar
4  tsp. vinegar
1/2  tsp dill weed
4  medium cucumbers, peeled and thinly sliced
3  green onions

      In a large bowl, combine mayonnaise, sugar, vinegar and dill; mix well.  Add cucumbers and onions; toss.  Cover and chill for at least 1 hour.  Yield:  8 servings.
      Exchange:  2 vegetable, 1 1/2 fat.
      Serving size 2/3 cup.
      Calories 126.
