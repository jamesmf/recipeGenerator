PICADILLO


1  tsp. vegetable oil
1 1/2  c. chopped onion
1  jalapeo pepper, seeded and finely chopped
1 large  clove garlic, minced
1  lb. ground turkey or sirloin
1  large chopped and peeled Granny Smith apple
1/2  c. water
1/4  c. sliced pimento-stuffed olives
1/4  c. raisins
3/4  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
2  (3-inch) cinnamon sticks
1 (14.5 oz.)  can diced tomatoes (undrained)
  toasted slivered almonds

      Heat oil in large pan.  Saut onion 3 minutes.  Add jalapeo and garlic; saut 1 minute.  Add meat; saut 3 minutes or until brown, stirring to crumble.  Stir in apple and next 7 ingredients; bring to a boil.  Reduce heat and simmer, uncovered, for 30 minutes or until thick.  Remove from heat; remove cinnamon sticks and stir in almonds.  Makes five 1-cup servings.
