SPEEDY CHICKEN STEW


4 (10 1/2 oz.)  cans chicken broth
2  chicken bouillon cubes
1 (20 oz.)  pkg. frozen creamed corn
1 (10 oz.)  pkg. frozen baby lima beans
1  large potato, peeled and diced
1/2  large onion, diced
1/8  tsp. ground red pepper
1/4  tsp. dried thyme
3  c. chopped, cooked chicken
1 (14 1/2 oz.)  can seasoned diced tomatoes with garlic, basil and oregano
1 (6 oz.)  can tomato paste

      Combine first 8 ingredients in a Dutch oven.  Bring to a boil over medium-high heat, stirring often.  Reduce heat and simmer 15 to 20 minutes or until potatoes and lima beans are tender.  Stir in chicken, diced tomatoes and tomato paste; simmer 10 more minutes.
