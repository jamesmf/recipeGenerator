FRENCH ONION SOUP


3  lb. onions, sliced thin
1  stick butter
8  cans consomme
1  Tbsp. black pepper
1  Tbsp. thyme
  dash of salt
2  cans water
1  loaf French bread
  Swiss cheese slices
  Parmesan cheese

      Brown onions in butter. Add consomme, pepper, thyme, salt and water. Simmer for 1 hour.
      Pinch tops and sides of bread and line each individual bowl with 5 to 6 pieces of the bread. Fill bowls with soup, then place a slice of Swiss cheese on each. Sprinkle with Parmesan and paprika just before baking. Bake at 400 degrees; until brown, usually at least 15 to 20 minutes.
