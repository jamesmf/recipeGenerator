STUFFED SHELLS


1  pkg. jumbo shells
1  lb. ground beef
1  large onion
1  (8 oz.) bag shredded Mozzarella cheese
1/2  c. dried bread crumbs
1  egg
1  c. Parmesan cheese
  salt to taste
  pepper to taste
2 (15.5 oz.)  jars spaghetti sauce

      Cook and drain shells according to package directions. Brown ground beef and onion. Stir in Parmesan cheese and 3/4 of the Mozzarella cheese, bread crumbs, egg, salt and pepper. Stuff meat mixture into shells. Lightly grease pan and layer bottom with 1 jar spaghetti sauce. Place stuffed shells in pan and sprinkle remaining Mozzarella cheese on top of shells. Pour the second jar of spaghetti sauce on top. Bake at 350 degrees; for 40 to 50 minutes.
