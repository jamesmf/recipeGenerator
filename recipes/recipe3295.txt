TEXAS CAVIAR


1 (15 oz.)  can black-eyed peas with jalapeno, drained and mashed
1 (4 oz.)  can mild chopped green chilies
2  Tbsp. butter
2  Tbsp. finely chopped green onions
1/8  tsp. garlic powder
1 (5 oz.)  jar Old English sharp cheese spread

      Combine all ingredients in a microwave-safe dish. Microwave on FULL power 3 to 4 minutes, stirring often, until cheese is melted and dip is hot. Serve warm with chips or crackers. (Especially good with Fritos.)
