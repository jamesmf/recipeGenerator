CHERRY THUMBPRINT COOKIES


1  tsp. vanilla
2  sticks margarine
2  egg yolks
1/2  c. brown sugar
2  c. flour
1/2  tsp. salt
  maraschino cherries

      Mix first 6 ingredients and roll dough into small balls (about 3 dozen), then put thumbprint into center of ball.  Put cherry into thumbprint.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 8 to 10 minutes.
