BAKED BEANS


1 (53 oz.)  can pork and beans
6  slices thin sliced bacon
1  c. chopped onions
1/2  c. chopped bell pepper
1/2  c. catsup
1/2  c. barbecue sauce
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
2  tsp. prepared mustard
2  Tbsp. brown sugar or honey
2  tsp. liquid smoke (optional)

      Mix beans in large mixing bowl.
      To serve hot:  Mix all ingredients, except bacon, in large mixing bowl.  Strip with bacon.  Bake 1 hour at 350 degrees;.
      To serve cold:  Fry bacon crisp; crumble and set aside.  Saut√© onions and bell pepper in 2 tablespoons bacon drippings until onions are clear; do not brown.  Empty all ingredients in mixing bowl.  Mix well.  Pour in baking pan.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour.
