SWEET AND SOUR CHICKEN


2  Tbsp. oil
3  chicken breasts, cubed
1  can pineapple chunks (in syrup)
1/4  c. brown sugar
2  Tbsp. cornstarch
1/4  c. vinegar
1  Tbsp. soy sauce
1  chopped onion
3/4  c. green pepper

      Brown chicken in oil. Add all the other ingredients and cook until chicken and vegetables are done, about 40 minutes.
