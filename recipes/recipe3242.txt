HOAGIE SPREAD


1  head of Lettuce
2  Medium Tomatoes
2  Medium Onions
1/4  cup oil
1  or 2 tbsp of Italian Seasoning
1  or 2 tbsp of oregano
  Bologna
  Ham
  Pepperoni
  Hard Salami
  American Cheese
  Provolone Cheese

      Chop all the meats and cheese into small pieces.  Shred the Lettuce, dice the tomatoes and onions.  Mix all the ingredients together in a large mixing bowl.  Spread on an Amoroso or any long or round sandwich roll.
