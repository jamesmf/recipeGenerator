IN THE BAG TURKEY BREAST


3  Tbsp. lemon-pepper seasoning
3  Tbsp. garlic powder
1  tsp. dried thyme
1  tsp. crushed dried rosemary
1  (4 to 6 lb.) turkey breast
  vegetable cooking spray
1  Tbsp. all-purpose flour

      You will need 1 large (14 x 20-inch) oven cooking bag.
      Combine first 4 ingredients in a small bowl; set aside. Remove skin from turkey breast and pat dry.  Spray cooking spray generously on breast.  Sprinkle reserved seasoning mixture over turkey, coating thoroughly.  Set aside.  Place oven cooking bag in a 13 x 9 x 2-inch baking pan.  Add flour to bag; shake to coat inside.  Place turkey inside.  Close bag with nylon tie; cut six 1/2-inch slits in top of bag.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 15 to 19 minutes per pound.  Cook approximately 1 1/2 hours.  Remove from bag and slice.  Serves 6 to 8.
