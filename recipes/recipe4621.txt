SUNSHINE SALAD


1 -6  oz. box orange jello
1  c. sugar
2  c. boiling water
1 large  can crushed pineapple, drained
2 small  cans mandarin oranges, drained
1 -16  oz. sour cream

      Dissolve sugar and jello in boiling water.  Add sour cream, drained mandarin oranges, and drained pineapple.  Pour into 2 quart dish.  Chill before serving.
