STRAWBERRY PRETZEL SALAD


2  c. pretzels broken up
3/4  c. melted butter
3  Tbsp. sugar
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
1  c. sugar
1 8  oz. cool whip
1 large  pkg. strawberry jello
2  c. boiling water
2  (10 oz.) pkgs frozen strawberries, sliced

      Bottom layer combine pretzels, butter and 3 tbsp sugar.  Pat in 9 x 13 casserole dish and bake at 400 degrees; for 8 minutes. Cool.
      2nd layer:  Mix cream cheese, sugar and cool whip together. Spread over crust.
      3rd layer:  Dissolve jello in boiling water and add frozen strawberries.  Let it cool, do not let it set.  Pour over second layer and refrigerate.
