LIME CUCUMBER PICKLES


7  lb. cucumbers, washed and cut
2  c. lime
  water
2  qt. vinegar
4 1/2  lb. sugar
4  Tbsp. pickling spice, tied in cheesecloth
3  sticks cinnamon

      Cover cucumbers with lime and 2 gallons water. Soak 24 hours. Rinse; cover with clear water. Soak for 3 hours, changing water each hour. Heat vinegar and dissolve sugar. Add pickling spice and cinnamon. Add cucumbers when cool and let set overnight. Cook for 35 minutes on simmer. Pack in jars and seal.
