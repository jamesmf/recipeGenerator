STRAWBERRY MOUSSE


1  egg
2  envelopes unflavored gelatin
1  Tbsp. cornstarch
1  Tbsp. cold water
1  c. boiling water
2  c. sliced fresh strawberries or frozen, thawed and drained
1  c. plain, low-fat yogurt
8  packets Equal sweetener (NutraSweet)

      Combine egg, gelatin, cornstarch and cold water.  Stir to blend.  Add boiling water.  Blend thoroughly.  Puree strawberries. Add gelatin mixture.  Add yogurt and Equal; blend until mixture is smooth.  Chill overnight or until set, about 4 hours.  Blend again. Pour into dessert dishes and serve.  Makes 8 servings, 1/2 cup each.  Contains 57 calories per serving.  Diabetic exchange:  1/2 nonfat milk and 1/4 fruit.
