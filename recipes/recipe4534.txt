VEGETABLE STEW


  medium size cabbage head, cut in chunks
  carrots, diced
  onions, sliced
1  can corn
1  can green beans
  potatoes, cut in chunks
  tomatoes (1 can or fresh)
1  can butter beans
  salt and pepper to taste

      Cook cabbage and potatoes until nearly done. Add canned vegetables and salt and pepper to taste.
