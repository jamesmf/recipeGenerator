TACO BAKE


2  (8 oz.) tubes of crescent rolls
1  lb. ground beef
1  pkg. taco seasoning
1 (16 oz.)  pkg. cheddar cheese
1  (16 oz.) container sour cream
1  pkg. Doritos

      Brown ground beef, add taco seasoning (mix as directed on pkg.).  Set aside.  In a 9 x 13-inch greased deep-dish pan, layer crescent rolls and press into pan (make a second layer if need be). Prick holes in rolls with fork.  Bake at 375 degrees; for 6 minutes. Remove from oven.
      Spread taco meat mixture over crescent rolls.  Spread sour cream over meat mixture.  Sprinkle cheddar cheese over sour cream. Return to oven and bake 25 minutes.  Remove from oven and crumble Doritos on top of cheese.  Return to over 3 - 4 minutes to brown Doritos.  Cut in squares and serve.  Enjoy!
