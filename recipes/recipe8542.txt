CHEESY CHICKEN


2 1/2 to 3  lb. Chicken
1  can Golden Mushroom Soup
1  can Cheddar Cheese Soup
1  bunch green Onion (finely chopped)
1  tsp. sage

      Wash chicken, salt & pepper.  Place chicken in baking dish. Mix remaining ingredients and pour over chicken.  Bake 45 minutes to 1 hour at 350 degrees.  Makes good gravy to go over rice or potatoes.
