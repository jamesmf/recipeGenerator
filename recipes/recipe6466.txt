CHICKEN SPAGHETTI


1  medium onion, chopped
1  bell pepper, chopped
1  c. celery, chopped
1  can Ro-Tel tomatoes
  salt and pepper to taste
  uncooked spaghetti
2  cans creamed soup (chicken, celery or mushroom)
1  lb. Velveeta, cubed
4  large chicken breasts, cooked and chopped
1  can mushrooms, chopped

      Saut onion, bell pepper and celery in butter.  Add soup, tomatoes, mushrooms, cheese and chicken in a large pot.  Simmer for 25 to 30 minutes.  Meanwhile, cook spaghetti in the leftover chicken broth.  Combine spaghetti and sauce.  Pour into a 9 x 13-inch pan.  Bake for 20 minutes at 300 degrees;.
