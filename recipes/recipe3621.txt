SAUERKRAUT BALLS


4  Tbsp. margarine
1  medium onion, chopped
1 1/3  c. chopped ham or 1 lb. hamburger
1/2  garlic clove, minced
4  Tbsp. flour
1/2  c. beef broth or beef bouillon cube and 1/2 c. water
3  c. sauerkraut, drained and chopped
1  Tbsp. parsley, chopped
2  Tbsp. sugar

 
Breading:
1 1/2  c. flour
1  egg
1  c. milk
  breadcrumbs

	Melt margarine in pan and lightly brown onions. Add chopped ham or hamburger and garlic; lightly brown. Add broth, sauerkraut, parsley and sugar. Cook a few minutes more. Stir in flour and cook thoroughly. Should be as stiff as a croquette mixture. Let cool. Form into balls about 1-inch in diameter.
	Beat egg and milk together. Dip balls in flour, then in egg and milk mixture, then dip in breadcrumbs. Fry in deep fat until brown. Quantity: approximately 100 balls.
