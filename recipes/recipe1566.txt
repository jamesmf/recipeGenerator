NAPFKUCHEN
(Turban Cake)  

1/2  c. butter
1  Tbsp. lemon peel
1  tsp. vanilla extract
1  c. sugar, divided in half
4  eggs, separated
3/4  c. milk
2  c. sifted flour
2 1/4  tsp. baking powder

      Cream together butter, lemon peel, vanilla, 1/2 cup sugar and egg yolks.  Add milk slowly.  Beat egg whites until stiff.  Add 1/2 cup sugar.  Add flour and baking powder to creamed mixture.  Fold in stiffly beaten egg whites.  Turn batter into mold.  Bake about 55 minutes at 350 degrees;.  For variety, add 1/2 cup freshly ground poppy seeds or 1/2 cup rum soaked raisins.
