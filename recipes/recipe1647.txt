HERNEKEITTO
(Pea Soup)  

3/4  lb. green split peas
1  ham bone
1  medium onion, chopped
1/2  tsp. pepper
8  c. water
1  carrot, chopped
2  tsp. salt

      Soak the peas in water overnight.  In a large pot, bring the peas to a boil in the same water.  Skim.  Add the meat, carrots and onion and simmer about 2 hours or until the peas are tender. Remove the ham bone.  Take any meat off the bone and return to the soup.  Add the salt and pepper and serve.
