SPAGHETTI MEAT SAUCE


1/2  c. olive oil
2  onions, chopped fine
3  cloves, minced
2  lb. ground beef
3  c. tomato juice
1 1/2  c. tomato puree
1 (6 oz.)  can tomato paste
1/2  c. chopped celery
1/4  c. parsley, chopped
1 1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. red pepper
1  Tbsp. black pepper
1  tsp. dried basil
1  medium bay leaf
12  stuffed olives
1/2  c. green pepper, chopped

      Heat oil in deep frying pan.  Add onions, garlic and brown; add meat and brown slowly. Heat tomato juice, puree, and paste in deep kettle (3 qt. size). Add meat mixture, celery, green pepper, parsley, and other seasonings. Simmer slowly for 1 1/2 hours. Remove bay leaf. Add sliced olives; simmer 10 minutes longer. Serve over hot spaghetti. Top with Parmesan cheese. Makes 2 quarts spaghetti sauce. Freezes well.
