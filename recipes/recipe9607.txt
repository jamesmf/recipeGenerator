LEMON-DILL MARINATED FLANK STEAK


1 to 1 1/2  lb. beef flank steak (about 3/4-inch thick)

 
Marinade:
1/4  c. soy sauce
3  Tbsp. lemon juice
1  Tbsp. Dijon mustard
2  Tbsp. vegetable oil
1  tsp. dried dill
1  tsp. dried thyme
1/2  tsp. dried rosemary
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
2  cloves minced garlic (or pressed)
1/2  tsp. pepper

      Score meat on both sides in diamond pattern.  Place meat in a plastic bag. Pour marinade over steak.  Seal bag and refrigerate. Marinate 6 to 24 hours, turning bag occasionally.  Drain meat, then grill to desired doneness.  Thinly slice meat across the grain.
