TACO SOUP


2  lb. ground beef or ground turkey
1  small onion
  celery to taste
2  cans tomatoes
2  cans pinto beans
1  pkg. Ranch dressing mix
  garlic powder
1  can Ro-Tel
2  cans whole kernel corn
1  pkg. taco seasoning

      Brown meat and onion. Drain. Add other ingredients and simmer. Serve with cheese slices, tortilla chips, Doritos, etc.
