CRAWFISH STUFFED SQUASH


6  medium summer squash
2  eggs
1/2  c. green onions, chopped
1/3  c. celery, chopped
2  Tbsp. butter
1  c. peeled crawfish tails (may use shrimp)
1/2  c. milk
  Breadcrumbs (about a cup)
  Salt & Pepper to taste

      Boil squash in unsalted water until tender.  Drain and mash well.  Add eggs and mix well.  Saut√© onions & celery in butter until soft.  Add milk, crawfish and breadcrumbs and mix well. Season with salt & pepper and put in casserole dish.  Sprinkle top with breadcrumbs and bake at 350 degrees until brown.  Serves 6.
