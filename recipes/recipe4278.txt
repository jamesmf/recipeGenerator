LOW-FAT TACO SOUP


1  lb. lean ground round beef
1  small onion, chopped
1 (4 oz.)  can chopped green chiles
1  pkg. taco seasoning
1  pkg. Ranch dressing mix
1  can corn (with juice)
3  cans crushed tomatoes
1  c. pinto beans with juice
1 1/2  c. water

      Brown the ground round beef. Drain and rinse beef with hot water to remove grease. Add browned beef to all remaining ingredients. Bring to a boil. Cover and simmer for 1 hour, stirring occasionally. Serve with tortilla chips.
