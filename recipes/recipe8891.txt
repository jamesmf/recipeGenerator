CHICKEN TACO PIE


1  (2 or 3 lb.) chicken
2  cans enchilada sauce
1  can mushroom soup
1  large onion, chopped
1  c. chicken broth
1  c. grated cheese
1/2  tsp. garlic salt
  dash of pepper
11  oz. pkg. Fritos corn chips

      Boil chicken until tender. Retain broth; remove bones and cut chicken into bite size. Combine chicken, enchilada sauce, mushroom soup, onion, garlic salt and pepper. Grease baking dish and line with Fritos. Add chicken mixture. Sprinkle with grated cheese. Cover with Fritos. Pour 1 cup chicken broth over chicken mixture. Bake in 350 degrees; oven for about 30 minutes.
      This receipt may be prepared and frozen for future use. If frozen, bake in 350 degrees; oven for 1 1/2 hours.
