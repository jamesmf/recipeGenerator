GOLDEN FRENCH ONION SOUP AND CROUTONS


1/4  c. butter
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
2  large onions (about 3/4 lb. each)
2  cans (10 1/2 oz.) chicken or beef broth
2  cans water
1  Tbsp. packed brown sugar

 
Croutons:
1/4  c. butter
  grated Parmesan
3  slices bread, cut into 1-inch cubes

      Cut onions into fourths and slice. Bake at 325 degrees; for about 2 1/2 hours, stirring every hour until onions are a golden brown. Stir in broth and water to a boil. Serve with Parmesan croutons.
      Croutons: Heat oven to 400 degrees;. Heat butter in 13 x 9 x 2 pan. Toss bread cubes in butter. Sprinkle with cheese. Bake 10 to 15 minutes until brown.
