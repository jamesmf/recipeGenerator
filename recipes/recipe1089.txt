PINEAPPLE FLUFF


2  c. unsweetened pineapple (undrained)
1 1/2  c. dry milk
3  pkg. Equal
3  Tbsp. lemon juice
1  tsp. vanilla
1  tsp. butter flavoring
1  pkg. sugar-free lemon or lime jello

      Dissolve jello in 1/2 cup boiling water.  Pour this mixture in a blender.  Blend about 4 minutes.  Pour in a square dish and refrigerate for a few hours before serving.
