NUTTY CRESCENTS


1/2  c. butter
1/2  c. shortening
1/3  c. sugar
2  tsp. water
2  tsp. vanilla
2  c. all-purpose flour
1/2  c. chopped black walnuts

      Cream butter, shortening and sugar together.  Mix in water and vanilla, then flour and nuts.  Chill for 3 1/2 hours.  Form dough in long rolls, 1/2-inch across.  Cut in 3-inch lengths and shape in crescents.  Bake on ungreased cookie sheet in 325 degrees; oven for 15 minutes.  (Do not brown cookies.)  Remove from heat and cool slightly.  Roll in confectioners sugar.  Makes about 4 dozen.
