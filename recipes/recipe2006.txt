TACO SEASONING MIX


4  tsp. instant minced onion
2 to 4  tsp. chili powder (depending on how well you like chili powder)
2  tsp. salt
1  tsp. each:  cornstarch, crushed dried red pepper, instant minced garlic and ground cumin
1/2  tsp. oregano
1  lb. lean ground beef
1/2  c. water
2  Tbsp. tomato paste
2 heaping  Tbsp. taco seasoning mix

      To make taco filling, brown ground beef; drain excess fat.  Add water, tomato paste and taco seasoning mix.  Simmer for 10 minutes. Makes enough filling for 8 to 10 tacos.
