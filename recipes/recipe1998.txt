SANGRIA


2  parts red wine
1  part ginger ale or lemon-lime soda
  lemon and orange extract to taste
  sugar to taste
  champagne, brandy or cognac (optional)
  sliced apples, peaches, pineapple, orange, lemon, etc.  (any fruits other than banana, for garnish)

      Traditionally served in a glass pitcher with a wooden spoon.
