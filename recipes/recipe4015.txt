RECYCLED PICKLES


  Kosher dill pickles (46 oz. can)
2  c. sugar
4  tsp. Texas Pete
3/4  tsp. crushed red pepper
2  slices cloves of garlic

      Drain pickles; wash, slice thick.  Put back in jar and add all the other ingredients.  Shake until the sugar has dissolved. Set in refrigerator for 5 days before use.
