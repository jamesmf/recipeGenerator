ZIPPY BLUE CHEESE BALL


2  8  oz. cream cheese
4  oz. crumbled blue cheese
8   oz. finely shredded mild cheddar cheese
1  tsp. onion powder
1  tsp. garlic powder
1  tsp. worcestershire sauce
1  tsp. salt

      Mix all ingredients well.  Form into a ball shape and roll in ground walnuts.  Serve with crackers.
