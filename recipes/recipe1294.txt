GARDEN GREEN SALAD


2  c. torn romaine lettuce
1/2  c. sliced green onions (scallions)
1/2  c. thinly sliced celery
3  Tbsp. vegetable oil
1/2  tsp. dehydrated cane juice
1/8  tsp. pepper
2  c. torn fresh spinach
1  medium cucumber
8  cherry tomatoes, halved
1  tsp. dried parsley flakes
1/4  tsp. dried whole oregano

      Combine first 6 ingredients in a large bowl.  Toss well. Combine lemon juice and remaining ingredients in a jar.  Cover tightly and shake vigorously.  Pour over lettuce mixture.  Toss gently.  Yields  4 servings.
