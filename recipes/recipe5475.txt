BAKED CRANBERRY SAUCE


4  c. fresh cranberries
2  c. sugar
1/2  tsp. ground cinnamon
1 (13 oz.)  jar orange marmalade
3  Tbsp. lemon juice
1  c. coarsely chopped walnuts, toasted

      Wash and drain cranberries.  In large bowl, combine sugar and cinnamon.  Add cranberries and mix well.  Spoon into a 9-inch square pan and cover with foil.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes.  Add orange marmalade, lemon juice and walnuts to hot cranberry mixture, stirring well.  Store in refrigerator.
