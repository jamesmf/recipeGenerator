MILK GRAVY

Plain:
1/4  c. bacon, sausage or vegetable oil
1/4  c. all-purpose flour
1 3/4  c. milk (whole milk best)
1  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. ground black pepper

      Heat oil on medium heat in iron skillet.  Sprinkle flour over oil and *cook until well mixed.  Slowly stir milk into hot mixture; cook until blended.   Add salt and pepper.  Stir constantly until thickened, about 15 minutes.
      Yield:  About 2 cups.
      Serve with bacon,  sausage or fried chicken.  Great with biscuits.
With Sausage:
1  lb. pork sausage
1/4  cup flour

      Fry 1 pound pork sausage in iron skillet.  During cooking process, break up sausage in skillet with spatula.  Cook sausage until browned; add  flour and *follow above directions.
