PICKLED FISH


3  c. of fish, chunked
1 heaping  Tbsp. pickling spices
1  large onion, sliced
1 1/4  c. white vinegar
1/2  c. sugar
2  Tbsp. salt

      Mix fish, spices and onion together in a container with a lid.  Mix remaining ingredients and add.  Refrigerate for at least 7 days, stirring or shaking once or twice daily.
