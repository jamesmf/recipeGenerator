PASTA DEL SOL


2 (20 oz.)  cans Dole chunk pineapple
16  oz. medium-sized box seashell pasta
1 large  can Mandarin orange slices
1  red bell pepper, julienne cut
2  c. thinly sliced carrots
2  c. frozen peas

 
Dressing:
1  c. orange juice
1  c. vegetable oil
2  Tbsp. sugar
2  Tbsp. basil
1/2  tsp. pepper
3  Tbsp. orange peel, grated

      Drain pineapple; reserve 1 cup juice.  Cook pasta according to package directions.  Combine salad ingredients in large bowl.
      For dressing, combine reserved pineapple juice with other dressing ingredients.  Pour over salad.  Cover and refrigerate at least 2 hours.
