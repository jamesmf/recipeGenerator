CREAM CHEESE BALL


2 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
1/4  c. green peppers, chopped
2  Tbsp. onions, chopped
1 small  can crushed pineapple, drained
2  c. nuts, ground (pecans or walnuts)
1/4  tsp. salt

	Mix all ingredients, except 1 cup of nuts. Roll into 2 cheese balls. Roll cheese balls into remaining cup of nuts.
