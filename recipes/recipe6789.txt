SOUTH DAKOTA BAKED DINNER


1  lb. ground beef
1  medium onion
2  stalks celery
1  tsp. chili powder
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. pepper
1  can carrots (with liquid)
1  can tomatoes
3  new potatoes, cut up

      Brown hamburger, onion and celery.  Add the chili powder, salt and pepper.  Add the carrots and tomatoes.  Mix and add the potatoes.  Bake at 350 degrees; until potatoes are soft.
