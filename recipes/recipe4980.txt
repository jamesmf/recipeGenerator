DADDY'S SPECIAL RECIPE BBQ SAUCE


1/2  c. butter (or use 1/2 cup wesson oil)
1/2  c. vinegar
1/2  c. ketchup
1/4  c. Louisiana Hot Sauce
1  Tbsp. black pepper
1  tsp. cayenne
2  lemons

      Melt butter in saucepan, add vinegar, ketchup, hot sauce, peppers and juice of one lemon.  Drop 2 slices of remaining lemon in saucepan with above and boil.  Will keep for days.  It is the best sauce you'll ever taste and especially good on chicken!
