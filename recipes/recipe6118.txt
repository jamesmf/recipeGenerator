GERMAN POTATO SALAD


1/2  c. white wine vinegar
1  c. water
1  large onion, chopped
1  Tbsp. salt
1/4 to 1/2  tsp. pepper
5  Tbsp. sugar
  potatoes, cooked, peeled, sliced and drained
2  Tbsp. olive oil
  Miracle Whip
  eggs, hard boiled and chopped

      Mix vinegar, water, onion, salt pepper, and sugar in pot. Simmer for 5 to 8 minutes, cool.  Mix potatoes and vinegar mixture.  Add olive oil and Miracle Whip to taste.  Cover and place in freezer for 1 1/2 hours.  Before serving, add eggs on top.
