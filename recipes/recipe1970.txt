STUFFED ROLLS


1/2  lb. sharp Cheddar cheese, grated
1  medium onion, grated
1/2  clove garlic, chopped
1 small  can green Ortega chilies
1 small  can chopped olives, strained
1 (8 oz.)  can tomato sauce
1/4  c. oil
1  Tbsp. vinegar
1  pkg. French rolls

      Combine all ingredients, except French rolls.  Scoop out middle of French rolls.  Place 1 tablespoon of cheese mixture into roll. Bake at 350 degrees; for 15 to 20 minutes or until well heated.
