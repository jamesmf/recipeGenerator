HEARTY HAM CASSEROLE


1/2  lb. elbow macaroni
2  c. cooked ham, cubed
4  hard-cooked eggs, chopped
1/3  c. chopped onion
1 (11 oz.)  can Cheddar cheese soup
1/2  c. sour cream
1/2  tsp. dry mustard
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
1/2  c. crushed potato chips

      Cook macaroni according to  package  directions, drain. Place in 2 quart buttered casserole,  mixing well with remaining ingredients.  Top with potato chip  crumbs.  Bake  at 350 degrees; for 40 minutes or until hot through.
      Note:  May use potatoes in place of macaroni.
