CROCK POT DRESSING


  2-3 qts. Chicken Broth
1  lg. skillet Cornbread
4  slices White Bread
1  lg. Onion
3  Eggs
1/2  stick Margarine
3  tbsp. Sage
  Salt
  Black Pepper

      Crumble cornbread and loaf bread.  Mix together with chopped onions, sage, salt and pepper.  Melt margarine in 1 qt. broth, then mix with cornbread mixture.  Add beaten eggs after mixtures cools.  Continue to add extra broth as needed for a thin consistency.  Pour in crock pot.  Cook on low for 4 - 5 hours until onions are tender.
      Note:  Add cooked, chopped chicken before cooking.
