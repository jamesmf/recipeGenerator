COCONUT FLAKE COOKIES


1 1/2  c. plain flour
2  tsp. baking powder
1/2  tsp. salt
3  c. corn flakes
1  c. shortening
1  c. white sugar
1/2  c. brown sugar
2  eggs, unbeaten
1  tsp. vanilla
1 1/2  c. coconut

      Add corn flakes to sifted dry ingredients.  Cream shortening; add sugars and blend well.  Add eggs, one at a time, beating well. Add vanilla, then flour and corn flake mixture.  Mix well.  Add coconut.  Drop by teaspoon.  Bake at 375 degrees; for 12 to 15 minutes. Makes 5 dozen cookies.
