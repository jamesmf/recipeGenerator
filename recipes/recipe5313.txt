CHOCOLATE CHOW MEIN "HAYSTACKS"
(Microwave)  

1 (6 oz.)  pkg. semi-sweet chocolate pieces
1 (3 oz.)  can chow mein noodles

      In a 3-quart casserole or large bowl, place chocolate pieces. Cover with lid or plastic wrap.  Microwave on Medium or Low 4 to 5 1/2 minutes until melted.  Stir chocolate until smooth; add noodles and using 2 forks, toss to coat well.  On strips of foil or wax paper, form into 1 1/2-inch clusters.  Cool to set.  Can spray (butter) on dish.  Makes about 24 (1 1/2-inch) Haystacks.
