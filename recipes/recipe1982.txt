PUMPKIN PUDDING
(Budin de Calabaza)  

2  lb. pumpkin, peeled and cut in pieces
4  c. water
1  Tbsp. salt
3  eggs
2  Tbsp. butter
1/2  tsp. ground cinnamon
1/3  c. flour
1/3  c. sugar
1/3  c. milk

      Preheat oven to 400 degrees;.  Grease a 2-quart glass baking dish. Cook pumpkin rapidly in water and salt, covered, for 20 minutes. Drain and put pumpkin through a potato ricer.  Add remaining ingredients and mix.  Turn mixture into baking dish and bake about 40 minutes.
