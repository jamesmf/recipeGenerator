PRETZEL SALAD


2  c. pretzel sticks, broken
3/4  c. margarine, melted
3  Tbsp. sugar
2  c. boiling water
6  oz. strawberry jello (no sugar)
2 (10 oz.)  cartons frozen strawberries (juice and all)
1  c. granulated sugar
8  oz. cream cheese
2  c. Cool Whip

      Combine pretzels, margarine and 3 tablespoons sugar. Spread on bottom of 9 x 13-inch baking dish. Bake at 400 degrees; for 8 minutes. Don't overcook. Let cool. Whip cream cheese and sugar. Fold in Cool Whip. Spread over pretzel crust. Mix jello, water and strawberries. Let cool. Pour over cream cheese layer.
