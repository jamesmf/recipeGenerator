CHEESE POCKETS


1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
2  sticks butter
2  c. flour
  strawberry jelly or jam
  powdered sugar

      Mix together thoroughly cream cheese, butter and flour.  Form into ball and refrigerate overnight.  Leave at room temperature about 1 hour.  Roll out thin on lightly floured board.  Cut into about 3-inch rounds.  Fill with 1 teaspoon jam or jelly (your desired flavor).  Fold over and seal edges by crimping with fork. Bake on cookie sheet at 375 degrees; for 20 minutes.  Cool and dust with powdered sugar.
