CRANBERRY SALAD


1  lb. chopped cranberries
1  pkg. raspberry Jell-O with 2 c. water
1 3/4  c. sugar
1  c. apples, peeled and chopped
1  c. orange (no peeling)
1  c. celery, chopped
1  c. pecans, chopped

      Chop the cranberries and pour sugar over them. Let them set while the Jell-O thickens. Add all other ingredients to Jell-O mixture and pour into a mold or dish. Chill until firm.
