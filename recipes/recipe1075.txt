STRAWBERRY JAM


1  qt. cleaned strawberries
3 to 4  tsp. artificial sweetener
1  (1 3/4 oz.) pectin
1  Tbsp. lemon juice

      Crush strawberries in saucepan.  Stir in lemon juice, powdered pectin and sweetener.  Bring to a boil and boil for 1 minute, stirring constantly.  Remove from heat and continue stirring for 2 minutes.  Pour into sterilized jars half pint size, to within 1/4-inch of top.  Put on cap and screw tight.  When cool, store in refrigerator.  Yield:  2 1/2 pints.
