TACO DIP


1  large Philadelphia cream cheese
1  can Hormel chili without beans
  chopped onions and green peppers to taste
1  pkg. pepper cheese (or other if desired)

      Layer cream cheese, chili and cheese.  Sprinkle onions and green peppers on top.  Bake until the cheese is melted in a 350 degrees; oven.  Serve hot with Doritos or tortilla chips.
