CHEDDAR CHEESE ROLLS


1  lb. hamburger
2  Tbsp. barbecue sauce
1/4  c. bread crumbs
1  egg
1/2  tsp. salt
1  c. grated cheese
1/4  c. grated onion
2  Tbsp. water
2  Tbsp. flour

      Mix hamburger, sauce, bread crumbs, egg  and salt well. Pat on wax paper to 14 x 8-inches.  Mix  remaining ingredients and spread over meat to within 1-inch of edges.  Roll up jelly roll fashion. Chill several hours.  Slice  into  6  servings. Bake in shallow pan at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes.
