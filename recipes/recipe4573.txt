CHRISTMAS GELATIN SALAD


1 (3 oz.)  pkg. strawberry gelatin
1 (3 oz.)  pkg. raspberry gelatin
3  c. boiling water
1  c. pineapple juice
1  lb. cranberries, mashed (canned may be used)
1  small apple, chopped
1  (15.5 oz.) crushed pineapple, drained
1  c. sugar
1  c. chopped nuts

      Dissolve gelatin in boiling water; add pineapple juice; cool. Combine remaining ingredients and add to gelatin mixture. Pour into an oblong pan; Chill until firm. Cut into squares to serve.
