OMELET SANDWICH CASSEROLE


16  slices bread
8  slices ham
8  slices cheese
6  eggs
1/2  tsp. salt
3  c. milk
1/2  tsp. dried mustard

      Butter slices of bread.  Lay  buttered  side down, meat next, then cheese and bread with buttered  side up.  Mix eggs, salt, milk and dried mustard.  Pour  over  sandwiches.  Put in refrigerator  overnight.  Next morning, sprinkle  1  cup  corn flake crumbs over top.  Drizzle 1/2  cup  butter  over crumbs. Bake 1 hour at 350 degrees;.  Yields 1 (9 x 13-inch) pan.
