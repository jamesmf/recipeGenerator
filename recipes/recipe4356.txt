AUTUMN APPLE SALAD


1 (20 oz.)  can crushed pineapple, undrained
2/3  c. granulated sugar
1 (3 oz.)  pkg. lemon Jello, dry
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
1  c. red delicious apples, diced (unpeeled)
1/2  c. chopped nuts
1  c. celery, chopped
1  c. Cool Whip

      Place pineapple and sugar in saucepan and boil for 3 minutes.  Add lemon Jello.  Stir well and cool. Place cream cheese in medium mixing bowl.  Gradually blend in pineapple mixture. Fold in apples, nuts, celery, and Cool Whip.  Place in oblong 9 x 13 inch baking dish and refrigerate.  Keeps well.
