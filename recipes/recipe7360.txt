LAZY MARY STUFFED CABBAGE


1  small to medium cabbage head
1  lb. ground beef
1  onion
1/2  c. uncooked rice
1 (15 oz.)  can tomato sauce
1 (10 1/2 oz.)  can tomato soup

      Brown meat; set aside.  Chop cabbage leaves into less then 1 1/2-inch squares.  Place into a greased roasting pan.  Layer the cabbage.  Sprinkle salt and pepper on cabbage.  Place meat on top of layered cabbage.  Pour tomato sauce over layer.  Pour tomato soup and 10 1/2 ounces of water onto layers.  Dice onion and layer.  Pour rice over surface.  Bake, covered, at 375 degrees; for 1 1/2 hours.  Stir often while baking.  Easily serves eight.
