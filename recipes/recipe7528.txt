CAP'N BOB'S SALMON STEAKS


2  (10-oz. each) salmon steaks
1/2  c. olive oil
1/4  c. soy sauce
1/2  tsp. dried dill weed
1/8  c. lemon juice
  Pinch of ground cloves

      Whisk together olive oil, soy sauce, dill weed lemon juice and clove to make a sauce; brush over salmon steaks.  Place on broiler pan and broil steaks on both sides until lightly browned and fish flakes easily.  Do not overcook.
