HAM AND BEAN SOUP


1  c. dry navy beans
1 to 1 1/2  lb. meaty smoked pork hocks
1 1/2  c. sliced celery (3 stalks)
1  c. chopped onion (1 large)
3/4  tsp. dried thyme, crushed
1/4  tsp. pepper
1  bay leaf

      Rinse beans. Place beans in water. Cover and let soak in a cool place for 6 to 8 hours. Drain and rinse beans.
      In a 4-quart crock-pot, combine pork hocks, celery, onion, thyme, pepper and bay leaf. Stir in beans and 4 cups water. Cover and cook on low heat for 8 to 10 hours. Discard bay leaf. Remove meat. Coarsely chop. Slightly mash beans in crockery cooker. Return meat to cooker. Cover. Cook on high heat for 5 to 10 minutes or until heated through.
