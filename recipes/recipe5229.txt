FROG EYED SALAD


1/2  box Acini De Pepe (uncooked)
1/2  c. sugar
1/4  tsp. salt
1  can fruit cocktail
1  c. mini marshmallows
1  Tbsp. flour
1 (15.5 oz.)  can crushed pineapple, drained, (save juice)
1  c. whipping cream, whipped
1  egg

      Cook Acini De Pepe.  In heavy saucepan, beat egg with wire whisk until foamy.  Stir in sugar, flour, salt and reserved pineapple juice.  Over low heat, cook and stir until thickened and bubbly.  In a large bowl, combine Acini De Pepe with egg mixture. Chill thoroughly, about 1 hour.  Stir in pineapple, fruit cocktail and marshmallows.  Fold in whipped cream. Cover and chill.  Stir before serving.
