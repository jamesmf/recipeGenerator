EASY CHICKEN WALDORF SALAD


1/4  c. mayonnaise
2  Tbsp. lemon juice
6  oz. grilled chicken breast strips, cut up
1  c. chopped celery
1  c. chopped apple
1/4  c. sliced seedless green grapes
1/4  c. walnut pieces  (or pecans)
1/4  c. dried cranberries

      Mix mayonnaise and lemon juice in bowl.  Add other ingredients and mix lightly.  Cover and refrigerate several hours or chill until served.
