CORN PATCH SPAM BAKE


2 1/2  c. noodles
1  can Spam, diced
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  c. kernel corn
3/4  c. milk
1  tsp. butter
  salt and pepper to taste
  parsley flakes
  Mozzarella cheese

      Mix these ingredients together in a casserole dish. Add cheese on top and bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes.
