SHIPWRECK


1 large  can stewed tomatoes (undrained)
1 (16 oz.)  can kidney beans, partially drained
1  onion, finely chopped
1  green bell pepper, finely chopped
6  medium potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced
1 1/2  lb. ground beef, browned

      Combine tomatoes, beans, onion and bell pepper. Layer half of potato in a baking dish. Top with half of beef and half of tomato mixture. Repeat layers. Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 1/2 hours, removing lid to boil off liquid, if necessary.
