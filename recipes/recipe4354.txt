SEVEN LAYER SALAD


1  head lettuce, chopped
1/2  c. celery, diced
1/2  c. sweet onion, diced
1/2  c. green pepper, diced
4  eggs, hard boiled
8  strips bacon, fried and crumbled
4  oz. shredded cheddar cheese

      Layer first four salad ingredients in order.
Dressing:
2  c. Hellmann's mayonnaise
2  Tbsp. sugar
4  eggs, hard boiled and chopped
8  strips of bacon, fried and crumbled
4  oz. cheddar cheese

      When ready to serve top with chopped eggs, bacon and cheese.
