GOLDEN GLOW SALAD


1 large  pkg. lemon jello
1  can crushed pineapple, drained
2  c. boiling water
  juice from pineapple and cold water to equal 2 c.
  shredded carrots to your taste
  chopped nuts to your taste
2  Tbsp. vinegar

      Add boiling water to jello; stir until dissolved.   Add combined juice and cold water.  Mix in rest of ingredients. Chill until firm.
