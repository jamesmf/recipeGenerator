PINEAPPLE CREAM CHEESE BALL


2 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
2 small  cans crushed pineapple
1/4  c. chopped onion
1/2  c. chopped bell pepper
  salt to taste
2  c. chopped nuts

      Drain pineapple well. Mix with room temperature cream cheese, pepper and onion; add salt.
      Form into ball and roll in nuts. Chill.
      Can mix 1 cup nuts in cheese ball.
