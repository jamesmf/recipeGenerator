GREEN PEPPER COLESLAW


8  heads cabbage, shredded
10  green peppers, diced
6  c. sugar
6  c. vinegar
6  Tbsp. salt
3  tsp. pepper
3  tsp. paprika
6  c. Miracle Whip salad dressing

      Mix vinegar, sugar, salt, pepper, paprika and add to cabbage. Let stand 15 minutes, then drain.  Mix green peppers with cabbage.  Add salad dressing and toss.  Serves 100 people.
