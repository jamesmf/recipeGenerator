PINEAPPLE SALAD


1  can crushed pineapple
2/3  c. sugar
1  box lemon jello
8  oz. pkg. cream cheese
1  c. chopped apples
1/2  c. chopped pecans
1/2  c. chopped celery
8  oz. cool whip

      Mix pineapple and sugar in a sauce pan.  Boil for 3 minutes. Add jello and let cool.  Add cream cheese, apples, celery and pecans to mixture.  Add cool whip and refrigerate.
