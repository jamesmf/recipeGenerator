BAKED SPAGHETTI


1  cup chopped onion
1  cup chopped green pepper
1  Tbsp. butter or margarine
1  can (28 ounces) diced tomatoes with liquid
1  can (4 ounces) mushroom stems, drained
1  can (2 1/4 ounces) sliced ripe olive, drained
2  Tbsp. dried oregano
1  pound ground beef, browned and drained (optional)
2  cups (8 ounces) shredded cheddar cheese
1  can (10 3/4 ounces) condensed Cream of Mushroom soup diluted with 1/4 cups water
1/4  cup grated parmesan cheese

      In a large skillet, saut√© onion and green pepper in butter until tender.  Add tomatoes, mushrooms, olives and oregano.  Add ground beef if desired.  Simmer covered for 10 minutes.
      Place half of the spaghetti in a greased 13 X 9 inch baking dish.  Top with half of the vegetable mixture.  Sprinkle with 1 cup of cheddar cheese.  Repeat layers.  Mix the soup and water until smooth.  Pour over casserole.  Sprinkle with parmesan cheese.  Bake uncovered at 350 degrees for 30 - 35 minutes until heated through.  Yield 10 - 12 servings.
