MEATBALLS

Meatballs:
2  lbs. Ground Beef
1  envelope onion soup mix
1  C. bread crumbs
2  eggs
1/4  C. water

 
Sauce:
1 1/2  C. catsup
1  bottle chili sauce
1  jar grape jelly

      For meatballs mix ingredients and shape into small meatballs and bake on cookie sheet for 20 minutes at 275 degrees.   For sauce, heat ingredients together and put meatballs in the sauce. Simmer in crockpot on low.
