TOMATO BASIL TORTE


1  pie crust
1 to 2  Tbsp. mayonnaise
  dried basil and oregano to taste
2  cloves garlic, minced
  pepper
2  Roma tomatoes, thinly sliced
3/4  c. blend of Romano and Parmesan cheese

      Poke pie crust with a fork and bake for 5 minutes. Mix together mayonnaise, dried basil, oregano, garlic and pepper; spread on pie crust. Add tomato slices and cheese blend. Bake at 350 degrees; for 20 to 30 minutes until cheese is slightly browned.
