BUTTER COOKIES


1  c. flour
1/4  tsp. baking powder
1/2  c. oleo
1/8  tsp. vanilla
1 1/2  tsp. Sucaryl*
1/8  tsp. milk

      Cream oleo and vanilla.  Add the dry ingredients, Sucaryl and milk.  Chill dough for 20 minutes.  Form small balls.  Bake at 350 degrees; until brown, 15 to 20 minutes.  Makes about 24 cookies or less.
      *Can use low-calorie sugar to taste.
