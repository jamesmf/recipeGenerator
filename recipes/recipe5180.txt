SWEET YOGURT
(Bangladeshi Dish)  

1  1/2 cups plain yogurt
3  cups evaporated milk
1  cup (more or less) sugar

      First put yogurt and evaporated milk in a bowl. Then add sugar and mix these ingredients very well (a blender works well). Then put the mixed evaporated milk and yogurt in an oven proof glass tray.  Keep in oven at 100-110 degrees;F (lowest termperature in your oven) for 6-7 hours until it becomes thick, delicious yogurt.
