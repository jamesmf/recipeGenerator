HOT FUDGE SAUCE


2  c. sugar
2/3  c. cocoa
6  Tbsp. flour
2  c. milk
2  Tbsp. butter
2  tsp. vanilla
1/2  tsp. salt

      Thoroughly mix dry ingredients in medium saucepan. Add milk and cook until thick. Remove from heat. Add butter and vanilla. Refrigerate. May be served reheated or cold. Good on plain cake or for ice cream sundae.
