STRAWBERRY DELIGHT


2  c. crushed pretzels
3  Tbsp. sugar
3/4  c. melted butter
8  oz. cream cheese
1  c. sugar
  large Cool Whip
2  qt. sliced strawberries
2  pkg. strawberry pie gel

      Mix pretzels, sugar and butter in 9 x 13 pan.  Bake at 375 degrees; for 10 minutes.  Beat cream cheese, sugar and Cool Whip for 2 minutes.  Spread on cooled crust.  Mix strawberries and gel mix. Place on top of cream cheese mixture.  Refrigerate for 2 hours.
