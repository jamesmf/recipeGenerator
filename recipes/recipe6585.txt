CHICKEN AND RICE


4  to 6 boneless chicken breasts
1  c. rice (not Minute rice)
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  can cream of mushroom soup
  margarine
1  c. water

      In 9 x 13 casserole, pour both cans of soup and mix with 1 cup of water. Stir in rice. Lay chicken breasts on top of rice mixture. Melt desired amount of margarine and pour over top. Cover with foil and bake in a 350 degrees; oven for 30 minutes. Uncover and bake an additional 30 to 45 minutes or until chicken is cooked and tender.
