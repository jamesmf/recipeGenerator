CHICKEN CASSEROLE PIE


1  Chicken, cooked and boned
1  can cream of chicken Soup
  1-1 1/2 c. Chicken broth
  3-4 boiled eggs
1  c. Milk
1  stick oleo, melted
1  c. self-rising Flour

      Put chicken, soup, broth and eggs in casserole dish.  Mix milk, oleo and flour and pour over chicken mixture.  Bake 45 min. to 1 hour on 350 degrees until brown.
