BAKED SPAGHETTI


1  lb. ground beef
1  c. chopped onion
1/4  tsp. pepper
1/4  tsp. salt
1  (15 oz.) tomato sauce
1  tsp. chili powder
8  oz. spaghetti, cooked and drained
  Parmesan cheese
  Mozzarella cheese

	Brown ground beef. Add onions. Stir until tender. Add salt and pepper, then tomato sauce and chili powder. Simmer for about 25 minutes. Place half the cooked spaghetti in a casserole dish. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese, part of the Mozzarella cheese and 1/2 of the meat sauce, then repeat the remaining layer and sauce. Top with Mozzarella cheese. Bake for about 30 minutes at 350 degrees;.
