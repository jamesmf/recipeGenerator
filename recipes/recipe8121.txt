BACON WRAPPED PORK LOIN


1  pork loin
1/2  lb. bacon
1/2  c. orange juice
3  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
  salt and pepper as you like

      Wrap bacon around pork loin. Use toothpicks to hold bacon to loin. Salt and pepper loin. Put loin in bowl or marinade dish. Pour 1/2 cup orange juice over pork loin. Add Worcestershire sauce. Marinate for 8 to 24 hours. Wrap loin in tinfoil and grill at 350 degrees;.
