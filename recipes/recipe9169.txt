BREAKFAST CASSEROLE


1  lb. sausage
8  eggs, slightly beaten
1  c. cheddar cheese
1  (8 pack) crescent rolls

      Brown sausage and crumble.  Put sausage into bottom of dish and add eggs.  Top with cheese; then top with rolls.  (Can be put together the night before and refrigerated.)  Bake 25 to 30 minutes at 350 degrees;.
