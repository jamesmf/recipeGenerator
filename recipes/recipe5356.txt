SHOE PEG MARINATED SALAD


1  can French-cut string beans
1  can Shoe Peg corn (Green Giant)
1  can LeSueur English peas
1  onion, diced
1 medium  jar pimento peppers, chopped
1  green bell pepper, chopped
3  ribs celery, chopped

      Mix all and drain in a colander.
Marinade:
3/4  c. white vinegar
1  c. sugar
1/4  c. vegetable oil
1  tsp. salt

      Make a solution of ingredients; bring all to boil. Let cool completely.
      Mix all drained vegetables into vinegar mixture. Let set overnight. Will keep for 1 to 2 weeks.
