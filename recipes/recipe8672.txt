CHICKEN VERMICELLI


3  to 4 chicken breasts, cooked
1  pkg. vermicelli
1  can cream of chicken soup
1/2  can milk
1  can chicken broth
1  lb. Velveeta cheese
1  pkg. frozen broccoli, thawed
  salt and pepper to taste

	Preheat oven to 350 degrees;.  Boil chicken (reserve broth) and chop in bite size pieces. Boil vermicelli according to directions. Drain.
	In a saucepan, heat the soup, milk, broth and Velveeta cheese until the cheese is melted and the sauce is smooth. Add it to the chicken, broccoli, vermicelli and seasoning; mix well. Spray Pam in a baking dish and add to mixture.  Bake for 30 minutes or until thoroughly heated.
