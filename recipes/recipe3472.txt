A DELICIOUS RELISH


3  red bell peppers
4  green bell peppers
3  green tomatoes
12  ripe tomatoes
2  large onions, finely chopped
1  or 2 hot peppers
2  c. vinegar
2  Tbsp. salt
1  tsp. black pepper
1 1/2  c. sugar
1  Tbsp. whole cloves

      Chop all vegetables. Add seasonings. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer about 1 1/2 hours. Pack in sterilized jars and seal.
