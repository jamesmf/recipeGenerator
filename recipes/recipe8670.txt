MEAT LOAF WITH VEGGIES


1 1/4  lb. ground beef
  parsley
  salt and pepper to taste
2  slices fresh bread crumbs
  dash of Worcestershire sauce
1  beef bouillon
1  small potato, diced in small pieces (optional)
1  large or 2 small carrots, shredded
  paprika
1  egg
2  Tbsp. ketchup
1  small onion, sliced in rings
1/2  c. Cheddar cheese (optional)

	Preheat oven to 350 degrees;. Mix all ingredients until well blended. Bake about 1 to 1 1/2 hours.
	 Optional: Sprinkle cheese on top and return to oven for 5 minutes or until cheese is melted.
