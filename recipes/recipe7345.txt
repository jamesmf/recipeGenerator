BEEF TACO BAKE


1  lb. ground beef
1  (10 3/4 oz.) cream of tomato soup
1  c. Pace thick and chunky salsa
1/2  c. milk
6  flour tortillas or 8 corn tortillas (6 to 8-inch), cut into 1-inch pieces
1  c. shredded Cheddar cheese

      In skillet over medium-high heat, cook beef until browned, stirring to separate meat.  Pour off fat.  Add soup, salsa, milk, tortillas and half the cheese.  Spoon into 2-quart shallow baking dish.  Cover.  Bake at 400 degrees; for 30 minutes or until hot.  Sprinkle with remaining cheese.  Serves 4.
