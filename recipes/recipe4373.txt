KELLY'S BROCCOLI SALAD


3  or 4 hard-boiled eggs
2  bunches broccoli florets (cut into bite size pieces)
1/3  c. chopped Vidalia or purple onion
2  c. shredded cheddar cheese
1  lb. bacon, cooked and crumbled

      Mix first 4 ingredients together in a large bowl and set aside.
Dressing:
1  c. Miracle Whip
1/3  c. sugar
2  Tbsp. cider vinegar

      Mix all dressing ingredients together and add to broccoli mixture and chill.  When ready to serve, top with one pound of cooked crumbled bacon.
