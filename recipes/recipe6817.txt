BEEF BARBECUE SANDWICHES


3  lb. chuck roast
1  c. catsup
2  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  c. water
2  Tbsp. vinegar
1  tsp. sugar
1  tsp. onion salt
1/2  tsp. garlic salt
  salt and pepper to taste

      Cook the chuck roast until tender; shred into small pieces. Combine the remaining ingredients. Boil for 10 minutes, stirring constantly. Add beef and simmer for 1/2 hour in covered pan. Serve on buns.
