CHICKEN DIVAN


2 (10 oz.)  pkg. frozen broccoli
2  c. cooked chicken
6  Tbsp. butter
6  Tbsp. flour
3  c. chicken stock
1/2  c. milk
1/2  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  c. Velveeta Cheese, cubed
4  tsp. mustard
2  Tbsp. minced onion
2  Tbsp. dry sherry or wine
  Salt and pepper to taste

      Layer broccoli and chicken in bottom of 3-quart casserole dish.  In large pan, melt butter and slowly blend in flour.  Add the remaining ingredients to the butter/flour mixture.  Stir sauce over low heat until cheese is melted.  Pour sauce over broccoli and chicken.  Bake at 400 degrees; for 25 to 30 minutes.
