POPPY SEED CHICKEN


2  sticks butter, melted
4  rounds Ritz reduced fat crackers or soda crackers
2  Tbsp. poppy seeds
6  chicken breasts, cooked and cubed
2  cans cream of chicken soup
1 (8 oz.)  carton sour cream

      Mix together butter, crackers and poppy seeds. Pour half cracker mix into a 9 x 13 pan. Mix together chicken breasts, soup and sour cream. Pour meat mixture on top of crackers and top with remainder of crackers. Bake for 1 hour at 350 degrees;. (Make this into 2 casseroles and freeze one.)
