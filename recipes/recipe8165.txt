EASY SAUSAGE STRATA


1  lb. ground pork sausage
6  slices bread, cubed
2  c. shredded Cheddar cheese
6  eggs
2  c. milk
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. dry mustard

      Place sausage in a large, deep skillet; cook over medium-high heat until evenly brown. Drain and set aside.
      Layer bread cubes, sausage and cheese in a lightly greased 7 x 11-inch casserole dish. In a large bowl, beat together eggs, milk, salt and mustard. Pour into casserole on top of other ingredients. Cover and refrigerate at least 8 hours or overnight.
      Remove casserole from refrigerator 30 minutes before baking. Preheat oven to 350 degrees;. Bake in preheated oven for 50 to 60 minutes or until a knife inserted into center comes out clean. Let stand for 10 minutes before serving.
