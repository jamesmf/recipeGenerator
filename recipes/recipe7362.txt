STEAK ROLL-UPS


2  lb. round steak, thinly sliced
2  c. stuffing, mixed as directed on box
1  pkg. onion soup
1  can mushroom soup
1  can water
  butter
  salt and pepper

      Cut steak into pieces long enough to roll.  Sprinkle steak with salt and pepper.  Place 1/2 cup stuffing in center.  Roll pinwheel fashion.  Fasten with toothpicks.  Brown in butter; add soup mix.  Cover and simmer over low heat about 1 hour.  Baste occasionally.  Thicken with flour.
