CHICKEN PIE


4  large skinned chicken breasts
1  can cream of chicken soup or cream of celery soup
3  c. chicken broth
1 1/2  c. milk
1 1/2  c. self-rising flour
1 1/2  sticks melted margarine

      Cook chicken; save broth.  Cool chicken; cut up and put in baking dish.  Mix soup and broth; pour over chicken.  Mix flour with margarine until crumbly; add milk.  Pour over chicken and bake at 375 degrees; for about 30 minutes or until crust is golden brown.
      Note: If using Pampered Chef stoneware, bake at 400 degrees;.
