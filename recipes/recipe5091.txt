APPLE, PINEAPPLE, ORANGE SALAD


1  (20 oz.) unsweetened pineapple chunks
1/4  c. reduced fat margarine
2  Tbsp. lemon juice
1  Tbsp. cornstarch
2  Tbsp. water
  artificial sweetener equivalent to 2 Tbsp. sugar
3  c. chopped unpeeled red apples
2  c. green grapes
1  can mandarin orange slices

      Drain pineapple juice into a saucepan. Add margarine and lemon juice; cook over medium heat until margarine is melted. Mix cornstarch and water until smooth. Add to juice mixture. Bring to a boil and cook for 2 minutes. Reduce heat. Add sweetener and cool to room temperature. Pour over the fruit. Toss to coat. Chill at least 1 hour or overnight.
