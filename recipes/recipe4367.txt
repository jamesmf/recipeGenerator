MOLDED CORN BEEF SALAD


1  can corned beef
1 small  can peas, drained
1  medium onion, chopped
2  hard boiled eggs, chopped
1  pkg. lemon Jello (dissolved in 2 cups boiling water)
1  c. celery, diced
1  green pepper, chopped
1  c. mayonnaise
  Salt
  Pimento

      Make Jello and set aside to cool.  Break corn beef apart with a fork and add chopped vegetables, eggs, salt and mayonnaise.  Mix all with Jello and garnish with chopped pimento and chill until set.
