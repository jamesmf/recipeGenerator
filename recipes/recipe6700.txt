BREAKFAST CASSEROLE


1  lb. sausage
1  can crescent rolls
1  c. shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
1  c. Mozzarella cheese
6  beaten eggs
1/2  c. milk
1/8  tsp. pepper

      Cook sausage and drain.  Set aside.  Grease pizza pan.  Press crescent dough on greased pan.  Press to all sides.  Bake at 375 degrees; for 5 minutes on lower rack (until puffy).  Reduce heat to 350 degrees;. Spread sausage over dough and sprinkle cheese!  Combine remaining ingredients and pour over cheese.  Bake for 30 minutes at 350 degrees;.
