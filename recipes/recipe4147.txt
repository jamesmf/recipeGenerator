CHEESE/BACON DIP



8  oz. cream cheese, softened
1/2  c. mayonnaise
1  c. grated Swiss cheese
8  strips cooked, crumbled bacon (for topping)

      Mix together cream cheese, mayonnaise and Swiss cheese.  Put in pie plate. Top with bacon. Bake at 350 degrees; for approximately 20 minutes. Serve with crackers.
