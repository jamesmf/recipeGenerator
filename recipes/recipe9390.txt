PEPPER FILLETS


2  Tbsp. black pepper, coarsely ground
  steaks
4  tsp. butter
  tabasco sauce
  Worcestershire sauce
  lemon juice
2  Tbsp. cognac
  parsley, chopped
  salt

      Dry the steaks.  Press pepper into steaks with heel of hand. Let stand 30 minutes. Meanwhile, mix butter, tabasco sauce, worcestershire sauce, lemon juice, cognac and chopped parsley. Put a light layer of salt over heavy skillet on HIGH heat.  When the salt yellows, add the steak.  Lower the heat to moderate. Cook to desired doneness.  Heat the butter, worcestershire sauce, and tabasco on low; add cognac and flame.
