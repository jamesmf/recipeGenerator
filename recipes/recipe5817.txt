SHOEPEG CORN SALAD


1  can French-cut green beans, drained
1  can Shoepeg corn, drained
1  c. celery, chopped
1  c. sweet green pepper, chopped
1  c. green onions, chopped
1 small  jar diced pimentos

      Put in a bowl and mix.
Dressing:
1  c. sugar
1/2  c. oil
1/2  c. vinegar

      Bring to a boil. Pour over vegetables and refrigerate overnight or longer. Stir and serve.
