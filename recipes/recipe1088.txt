STRAWBERRY JELLO SALAD


1 (6 oz.)  box strawberry Jell-O
2  (10 oz.) small frozen strawberries
1 1/2  c. boiling water
1  c. crushed pineapple
2  large bananas
1 (8 oz.)  carton sour cream

      Dissolve Jell-O in water.  In a bowl, mash bananas and thawed strawberries, crushed pineapple and Jell-O mixture.  Pour half of this mixture into 9 x 13-inch cake pan.  Refrigerate until mixture has set.  Take out of refrigerator, spread sour cream and add remaining mixture over this and refrigerate again.  Cut in squares and serve.
