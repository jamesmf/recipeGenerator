EASY GOULASH


1  lb. ground beef
1  onion, chopped
1  clove garlic, minced
1  can tomatoes
1  can mushrooms
1  tsp. salt
1  Tbsp. Cavender's seasoning
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  Tbsp. red cooking wine
1  c. water
1  c. macaroni

      Brown beef in a large skillet.  Add onion and garlic; saut√© until tender.  Add tomatoes, mushrooms and seasonings.  Simmer 15 minutes.  Add remaining ingredients and stir.  Cover and simmer another 10 to 12 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Serves 4.
