SHRIMP PERI-PERI


1 garlic  clove (more if you like)
1  tsp. salt
4  Tbsp. olive oil
1 1/2  tsp. lemon juice
1/4  tsp. Peri-Peri or cayenne pepper
  boiled shrimp
6  Tbsp. melted butter

	Chop garlic and mix with salt. Combine with oil and lemon juice. Pour over shrimp and mix well. Add Peri-Peri or pepper. Marinate at least 30 minutes.  Arrange shrimp in shallow baking dish and pour melted butter over all.  Broil 5 to 8 minutes turning once.
