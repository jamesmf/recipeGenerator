CHICKEN AND BROCCOLI CASSEROLE


4  chicken breasts, cooked and cubed
1  pkg. cooked rice
2 (8 oz.)  pkg. frozen broccoli (defrost 8 minutes)
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. sliced fresh mushrooms
1 large onion, sauteed in 1/2  stick butter
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  can chicken soup
1  can Cheddar cheese soup
1  can water chestnuts
  salt to taste

      Mix all ingredients.  Place together in an oblong pan sprayed with cooking spray.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 45 to 60 minutes.
