COMPANY BEEF CASSEROLE


1  lb. hamburger
2  Tbsp. melted Crisco
1  medium onion, chopped
2  cans tomatoes
1  Tbsp. ketchup
1  Tbsp. steak sauce (optional)
1/4  c. green peppers
2  c. cooked macaroni noodles
2  c. grated cheese

	Brown hamburger in Crisco. Add onions, tomatoes, ketchup and steak sauce and simmer for 30 minutes. Add cooked macaroni. Put in a casserole dish. Sprinkle grated cheese over top and bake in a 350 degrees; oven for about 30 minutes or until cheese is melted.
