BERRY DELICIOUS CHICKEN


4  to 5 chicken breasts (boneless and skinless)
1 (16 oz.)  can whole cranberry sauce
1  can tomato soup (undiluted)
1  envelope onion soup mix
2  Tbsp. vinegar
1  Tbsp. sugar
1/4  tsp. garlic powder
  salt and pepper to taste

	Preheat oven to 400 degrees;.  Spray Pam on baking dish. Place chicken in a single layer in dish.
	 In a bowl, combine remaining ingredients. Pour over chicken. Bake uncovered 40 to 45 minutes or until chicken is done.
	Interesting combination of ingredients but very good.  Can be served with white rice or noodles.
