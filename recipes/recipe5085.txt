STRAWBERRY SALAD


2  c. crushed pineapple
3/4  c. margarine, melted
3  Tbsp. sugar
8  oz. cream cheese
1  c. sugar
8  oz. Cool Whip
6  oz. strawberry Jell-O
2 small  pkg. strawberries

      Dissolve Jell-O in 2 cups boiling water. Add strawberries with juice. Mix crumbs, margarine and 3 tablespoons sugar. Press into 9 x 13 pan. Bake at 400 degrees; for 8 minutes, no longer. Let cool.
       Beat cream cheese and sugar until combined. Stir in Cool Whip and spread on cooled crust. Pour strawberry mixture on top and chill.
