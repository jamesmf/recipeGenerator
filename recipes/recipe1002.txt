CREAM OF AVOCADO SOUP
(Cool, Simple and Elegant)  

2  avocados, peeled, seeded and cubed
1 (13 oz.)  can evaporated milk*
2  c. milk
1  c. chicken broth*
1/2  tsp. celery salt
1/2  tsp. garlic powder
1/4  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. white pepper
  watercress or parsley

      *Use more chicken broth, if desired, and less evaporated milk.
      Combine first 8 ingredients in a blender until smooth.  If blender is not available, combine the avocado and evaporated milk and mix until smooth.  Add remaining ingredients; mix until smooth. Chill thoroughly before serving with a garnish of watercress or parsley, if desired.  Makes 4 to 6 servings.  Looks beautiful served in glass bowls nestled in crushed ice.
