COUNTRY SPARERIBS AND SAUERKRAUT


  country spareribs
  potatoes
2  tsp. brown sugar
1  can sauerkraut
  carrots
  salt and pepper

      Brown the spareribs in a skillet with oil. Put the ribs in a crock-pot and then add sauerkraut, brown sugar, potatoes and carrots. Add about 1/4 cup of water. Cook on low for about 8 hours.
