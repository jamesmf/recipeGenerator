WHEAT AND TOMATO SAUCE


1  large onion, chopped
3/4  can of tomato paste
1  c. cracked wheat
3  c. water
  3-4 tbsp. oil
  Salt to taste

      Soak wheat in water to clean. (Wheat should absorb water.) Meanwhile, saut√© onion in oil on medium-high heat until onions start to brown.  Add tomato paste and 3 c. water.  Bring to a boil.  Add salt and softened wheat. Cover and let boil stirring occasionally.  Boil until it starts to dry about 20 mins.  Reduce heat and simmer.  Let stand about 10 mins.  Serve.
