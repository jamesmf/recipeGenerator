MARINATED VEGETABLE SALAD


2 (16 oz.)  cans garden peas, drained
2 (16 oz.)  cans Shoe Peg corn, drained
1  c. celery, chopped
1  green pepper, chopped
1  onion, chopped
1 small  jar pimento, chopped
3/4  c. vinegar
3/4  c. sugar
1/2  c. oil
1  tsp. salt

      Combine peas, corn, celery, green pepper, onion and pimento. Bring to boil the vinegar, sugar, oil and salt. Pour liquid over vegetables and marinate overnight. Serves 20.
