SCALLOP SOUP


1  lb. scallops
1/2  stick butter
1  clove garlic
2  stalks celery
3  to 4 medium carrots
2  to 3 medium onions
2  cans chicken broth
  salt to taste
  white pepper to taste
1  tsp. thyme
1/2 to 3/4  c. half and half
  cornstarch

      Sauté scallops in butter with garlic.  While sautéing scallops, dice celery, carrots and onions.  Heat chicken broth.  Add seasonings, vegetables and scallops.  Bring to a boil.  Lower heat to simmer and cook for about 45 minutes (until vegetables are tender enough to be pierced with a fork).  Add half and half.  Cook approximately another 15 minutes.  Thicken with cornstarch to desired thickness.
