QUICK QUICHE


1  c. Bisquick mix
2  c. milk
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
1/2  c. onion
4  eggs
1/2  c. fried sausage, crumbled
1  c. Mozzarella cheese
1/2  c. Cheddar cheese
1  pkg. chopped frozen spinach, cooked
1/2  tsp. basil

	Mix all of the ingredients in one bowl until the eggs are well blended. Pour into a buttered deep pie pan. Bake at 350 degrees; for 35 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean from the center.
