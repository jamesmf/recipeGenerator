MEATBALLS WITH ONION SAUCE


1/2  lb. ground beef
1/2  lb. ground pork
1  c. bread crumbs
1  tsp. salt
1  can onion soup
1  egg
2  Tbsp. bacon fat

      Mix meats, crumbs, egg and salt thoroughly.  Shape into small balls.  Brown in hot fat.  Add soup, cover and simmer gently. (Or bake in 350 degrees; oven until meat is well cooked.)
      Note: Tomato juice may be used instead of onion soup, if desired.
