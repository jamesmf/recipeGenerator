FRESH TOMATO SOUP


1/4  c. butter or margarine
1/2  c. chopped onion
1/4  c. all-purpose flour
1  c. water
6  medium tomatoes, peeled and coarsely chopped
1  Tbsp. minced fresh parsley
1  Tbsp. sugar
1  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. dried thyme
1/4  tsp. pepper
1  bay leaf

      Melt butter in large heavy boiler.  Saut√© onion in butter for 4 minutes.  Reduce to low heat.  Add flour and stir until smooth. Gradually add water.  Cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until thick and bubbly.  Add the next 7 ingredients.  Bring to a boil.  Cover and simmer on low heat for 30 to 40 minutes.  Remove and discard bay leaf.
      Spoon a portion of the tomato mixture into container of blender and process until smooth.  Repeat procedure with remaining tomato mixture.  Garnish with fresh parsley or bacon bits.  Yield: 6 cups (can be doubled).
