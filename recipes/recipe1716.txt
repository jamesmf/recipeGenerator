AGAR AGAR SRIKAYA
(A Cool, Refreshing, Typical Java Dessert)  

1  pkg. Agar-Agar powder (contains 2 pkg.)
1  c. brown sugar
1/4  c. granulated sugar
2  c. coconut milk
3  c. water
  pinch of salt

      Combine all ingredients together and bring to a boil.  Pour into a 7 x 7-inch pan or dish.  Let cool in refrigerator until firm.  Cut and serve.
