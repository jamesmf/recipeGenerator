ITALIAN WEDDING SOUP


1  lb. ground beef or sausage
1  tsp. garlic powder or 2 Tbsp. minced garlic
1  egg
1/2  cup Italian bread crumbs
2 (14 1/2 oz.)  cans chicken broth
1/2  cup Acine or Orzo noodles
1 (10 oz.)  pkg. chopped spinach

      Combine meat, garlic, egg, and bread crumbs.  Form into very small (1/2 inch) meatballs.  Cook through in skillet or bake. While meat is cooking bring broth to simmer in a large sauce pan. Add noodles and cook according to package directions.  Add meatballs and spinach.  Allow spinach to thaw.  Remove from heat and serve.
