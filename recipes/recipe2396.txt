BUTTERSCOTCH PIE


4  eggs
2  c. brown sugar
2  c. milk
1  stick margarine
3  Tbsp. cornstarch
1  Tbsp. vanilla

 
Meringue:
4  egg whites
8  Tbsp. sugar

      Mix sugar and cornstarch.  Add beaten egg yolks, milk, vanilla and margarine.  Cook until thick; turn heat on simmer.  Cover with lid and cook very slow for almost 10 minutes.  Pour into baked pie shell.  Top with meringue.
      Meringue:  Beat egg whites and sugar on high until stiff.  Put on top of pie and brown.
