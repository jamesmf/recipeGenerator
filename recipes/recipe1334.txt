HAYSTACKS

For every cup of dried beans, add:
1  tsp. vegie-sal
1  tsp. onion powder
1/4  tsp. garlic powder

      Simmer beans to blend flavors.  Purchase corn chips or brush uncooked tortillas with water and sprinkle with onion salt or garlic salt.  Lay on cookie sheet and dry out in 200 degrees; oven.  Break into little pieces.
      To Serve:  Place chips on plate.  Spoon beans on top.  Layer your choice of the following:  chopped tomatoes, finely diced onion, shredded lettuce, pimento cheese, shredded Garbonzo cheese.
