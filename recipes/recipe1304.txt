OAT LOAF


4  c. water
1  tsp. oregano
1  Tbsp. basil
1  Tbsp. onion powder
1  tsp. cumin
1  tsp. garlic powder
1 1/2  tsp. dill seed
2  tsp. salt
1/4  c. nutritional yeast
4 1/2  c. rolled oats

      Bring all ingredients to a boil.  Add 4 1/2 cups rolled oats. Continue to cook 1 minute.  Put mixture into a nonstick bread pan and bake at 350 degrees; for 1 1/2 hours.  Serve with peanut gravy.
