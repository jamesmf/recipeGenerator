SOUR CREAM CHICKEN ENCHILADAS


4  c. cooked shredded chicken
2  c. Cheddar/Jack shredded cheese
1  c. diced olives
12  flour tortillas

 
Sauce:
1  c. Ortega green chilies, chopped
3  cans cream of chicken soup
1  pt. sour cream
1  c. green onions
1/4  c. diced canned jalapenos (optional for a little kick!)

      In large mixing bowl, mix shredded chicken, Cheddar/Jack cheeses and olives. Place a flour tortilla down onto flat surface. Spread a small amount of sauce onto tortilla. Top with a spoonful of chicken mixture, then roll up and place into 13 x 9 pan. Repeat process with all tortillas. Top with the remaining sauce. Cover with aluminum foil and bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes.
