LENTIL SOUP


  ham or pork bone
1  lb lentils
3  qts. chicken stock (or ham)
2  lrg. onions, chopped
4  Tbsp. butter
1/2  C. carrots, grated
1  tsp. thyme

      Put bone, lentils and stock in deep pot.  Bring to a boil and simmer 1 1/2 hours.
       Remove bone.  In frying pan, saut√© onions and carrots in butter until limp, but not brown.  Add to lentils with thyme and simmer 1/2 hour longer.
      Season to taste.
      Serves 6.
