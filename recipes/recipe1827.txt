JALAPENO ROLLED TORTILLA BITS


24  flour tortillas, warmed until soft
3 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
1  pt. sour cream
1  small grated onion
5  jalapeno peppers, seeded and chopped
  dash of Tabasco sauce

      Mix together all of the ingredients except the tortillas. Spread on one side of a tortilla.  Roll tightly.  Close with a toothpick and place on cookie sheet.  Chill quickly in freezer 10 to 15 minutes.  Slice into bite sized pieces.  Insert toothpick into each bit and serve with picante sauce.
