RUSSIAN SALAD


3  large potatoes, cooked and diced
1 (6 1/2 oz.)  can crab
1 (6 1/2 oz.)  can white tuna
2 (6 1/2 oz.)  cans small shrimp
6  boiled eggs, chopped
1 (2 oz.)  jar pimiento, chopped
12  black olives, chopped
1 (16 oz.)  can peas and carrots, drained
1  Tbsp. chopped parsley
  salt and pepper
  oil
  vinegar
  lemon juice
  mayonnaise

      Mix the first 9 ingredients together. Add the remaining ingredients to taste. Serve on a bed of lettuce.
