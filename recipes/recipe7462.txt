ZIPPY MEAT BALLS


1  egg
3/4  c. soft bread crumbs (approximately 1 slice)
1/4  c. chili sauce
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. instant minced onion
1/8  tsp. garlic powder
3/4  lb. ground beef
1/4  c. minced celery
2  Tbsp. butter-flavor Crisco
  bottled barbecue sauce

      Beat egg in medium bowl.  Mix in bread crumbs, chili sauce, salt, onion, garlic powder, ground beef and celery.  Shape into 30 small balls, about 1-inch in diameter.  Melt Crisco in large skillet over medium heat. Add meat balls and brown on all sides. Continue cooking until done, about 12 minutes, including browning time.  Warm barbecue sauce while meat balls are cooking.  Serve on wooden picks.  Dip in warm sauce.
