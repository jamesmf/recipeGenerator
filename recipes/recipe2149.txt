CLOUD TOPPED PEANUT BUTTER FUDGE

Fudge:
1  c. evaporated milk
2  c. sugar
1  tsp. salt
1/4  c. butter
1 (12 oz.)  pkg. peanut butter morsels

 
Topping:
1/2  c. packed brown sugar
6  Tbsp. butter
1/2  c. corn syrup
2  c. sifted confectioners sugar
1  c. chopped walnuts

      Fudge:  In heavy pan, combine milk, sugar, salt and butter. Bring to boil over moderate heat.  Boil 8 minutes, stirring constantly.  Remove from heat; add morsels and stir until melted and mixture is smooth.  Spread into 9-inch foil-lined pan.  Chill 30 minutes.
      Topping:  In heavy pan, combine brown sugar, butter and corn syrup.  Bring to a boil.  Remove from heat and add sugar and walnuts.  Spread over peanut butter fudge layer.  Chill about 2 hours.  Makes 3 1/4 pounds.
