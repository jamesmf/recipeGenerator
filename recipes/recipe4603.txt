CUCUMBER SALAD


1  pt. cottage cheese
1  medium onion, grated
1  medium cucumber, grated
3/4  c. mayonnaise
3  oz. lime gelatin
1  c. boiling water

      Dissolve gelatin in 1 cup boiling water. Set aside until partially congealed. Mix together cottage cheese, onion, cucumber and mayonnaise. Fold mixture into gelatin. Refrigerate until firm.
