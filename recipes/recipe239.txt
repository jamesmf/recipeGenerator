LEMON LOVER'S DELIGHTS


1  pkg. lemon cake mix
1  (4 1/2 oz.) container Cool Whip
1  egg
  confectioners sugar

      Combine all ingredients except confectioners sugar.  Mix well. Shape into small balls.  Place on baking sheet.  Bake in preheated 350 degrees; oven for about 7 or 8 minutes or until lightly browned.  Roll in confectioners sugar.
