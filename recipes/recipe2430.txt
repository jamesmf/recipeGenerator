PUMPKIN ROLL

Cake:
3  eggs
1  c. sugar
2/3  c. pumpkin
1  tsp. lemon juice
3/4  c. flour
1  tsp. baking powder
2  tsp. cinnamon
1  tsp. ginger
1/2  tsp. salt
1  c. nuts, finely chopped
  powdered sugar

      Beat eggs on high for 5 minutes.  Stir in sugar and fold in pumpkin.  Add remaining ingredients.  Spread onto a well-greased cookie sheet.  Top with nuts.  Bake at 375 degrees; for 15 minutes.  Turn out on towel and sprinkle with powdered sugar.  Then roll up and let cool.  Unroll and add filling.
Filling:
1  c. powdered sugar
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
4  Tbsp. butter
1/2  tsp. vanilla

      Beat and spread on cake and roll again.  Keep refrigerated.
