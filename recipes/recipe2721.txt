PHILLY BREAD


2 1/4  c. flour, sifted
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
1/2  lb. margarine (stick)
1 1/2  c. sugar
1 1/2  tsp. vanilla
4  eggs
1 1/2  tsp. baking powder
1  c. candied fruit
1/2  c. pecans, chopped

      Thoroughly blend softened cream cheese, margarine, sugar and vanilla.  Add eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Gradually add 2 cups of flour mixed with baking powder.  Combine. Combine 1/4 cup flour with candied fruit and pecans.  Fold into batter.  Grease a 10-inch Bundt pan.  Sprinkle with chopped pecans. Pour batter into pan.  Cook at 325 degrees; for 1 hour and 20 minutes.  Cool 5 minutes.  Remove from pan.  When totally cool, sprinkle with powdered sugar.
