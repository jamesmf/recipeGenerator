ALL AMERICAN CHEESE SOUP


1/4  c. chopped onion
1/4  c. diced celery
1/4  c. shredded carrot
2  Tbsp. butter
1/3  c. flour
2  c. chicken broth
2  c. half & half
  salt, pepper and red pepper (as you like)
8  oz. shredded sharp process American cheese

      Cook but do not brown onion, celery, carrot and butter.  Mix flour with chicken broth, then add to vegetable mix.  Add half & half, seasonings and cheese.  Cook and stir till cheese is melted and soup is heated through.  Do not boil.
