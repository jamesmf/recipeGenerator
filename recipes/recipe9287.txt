HAMBURGER BEEF STROGANOFF


2  lb. ground beef
2  medium onions, chopped
20  oz. (2 cans) soup, cream of mushroom
1 soup  can of water
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. black pepper
2  Tbsp. flour
3  Tbsp. sour cream
  rice OR noodles (for serving)

      In a skillet, brown ground beef and add onions; cooking until tender.  Stir in soup and 1 soup can of water.  Add salt and pepper.
      Remove 1 cup of stroganoff mixture and blend with flour.  Stir flour mixture and sour cream into stroganoff, mixing well.
      Serve over rice or noodles.
      Makes 6 servings.
