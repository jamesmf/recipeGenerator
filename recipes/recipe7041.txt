TACO CASSEROLE


2  lb. ground meat
1  onion, chopped
1  can cream of chicken soup
2  or 3 jalapeno peppers
1  can enchilada sauce
1/2  lb. cheese
1  bag Doritos chips

      Brown ground meat and onions; drain. Add all other ingredients, except cheese and chips. Pour into a casserole dish; top with cheese and place crushed Doritos on top. Bake at 350 degrees; for 8 to 10 minutes.
