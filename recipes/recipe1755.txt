VEGETABLE JALFAREZI


1/2  c. green pepper, diced
1/2  c. carrots, chopped
1/2  c. green peas
2  tsp. cumin seed
  chili powder
  lemon juice
  butter
1/2  c. tomato, chopped
1/2  c. onions, chopped
1/2  c. beans, cut in 1-inch pieces
  salt to taste
  white pepper powder
  white vinegar
  tomato sauce

      Heat oil in pan.  Add cumin seed, onions, beans, green pepper and carrots.  Sauté for a minute or two.  Add the rest of the vegetables and sauté again.  Add salt to taste, chili powder and touch of tomato sauce.
      Just before serving, add a little lemon juice.
