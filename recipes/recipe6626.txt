REUBEN CASSEROLE


1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cooked egg noodles
5  oz. corned beef, chopped
1 (14-1/2 oz.)  can sauerkraut, drained
2  c. Swiss cheese, shredded
1/2  c. Thousand Island dressing
1/2  c. milk
1  Tbsp. prepared mustard
  Pumpernickel bread, crumbled

      Combine all ingredients in a bowl, except bread.  Pour into a 13 x 9-inch baking dish sprayed with cooking spray.  Crumble bread on top.  Bake at 350 degrees for 25 to 30 minutes.
