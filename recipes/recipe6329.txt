SHRIMP SALAD


1  lb. shrimp, boiled, peeled and deveined
3  hard boiled eggs, chopped
2  hearts celery with tops, minced
2  Tbsp. green pepper, minced
1/4  tsp. garlic salt
  salt to taste
  Tabasco to taste
1/2  c. mayonnaise
  juice of 1/2 lemon

      If shrimp are large, cut into bite size pieces. Add remaining ingredients and toss lightly.
