CHICKEN AND DUMPLINGS


1  small chicken (1 1/2 lb.)
1 1/2  c. water
1/4  tsp. salt
1/2  stalk celery (1/4 c.)
1/4  c. onion

 
Dumplings:
1  c. all-purpose flour
1  tsp. baking powder
1/2  tsp. salt
3  Tbsp. shortening
1/4  c. skim milk

      Disjoint chicken and remove skin; place in large pan.  Add water, salt, onion and celery; simmer 1 to 2 hours or until meat is tender.  Remove from bone; return chicken to chicken stock.  Bring chicken stock to a boil; add dumplings.  Cover and boil gently 8 to 10 minutes.
      Dumplings:  Combine flour, baking powder and salt.  Cut in shortening.  Add milk and make a stiff dough.  Roll dough out to about 1/8-inch thickness.  Cut into 1-inch strips or squares.
      Yields 6 servings; 3/4 cup serving may be exchanged for 2 medium fat meat and 1 starch.
