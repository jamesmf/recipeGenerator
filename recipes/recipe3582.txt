HOLIDAY CHEESE BALL


1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese, softened
1 small  can crushed pineapple, drained
1/4  c. green bell peppers, chopped
2  Tbsp. green onion (tops only), finely chopped
2  tsp. seasoned salt
1  c. pecans, finely chopped and divided

      Mix the first 5 ingredients plus 1/2 cup nuts. Form into a ball and coat with remaining nuts. Refrigerate several hours before serving.
