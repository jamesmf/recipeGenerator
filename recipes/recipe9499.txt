TUNA CASSEROLE


1  pkg. macaroni and cheese mix
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1 (6 oz.)  can tuna, drained
1  can June peas
1  c. grated cheese
1/2  bell pepper, chopped

	Prepare macaroni and cheese per directions. Add mushroom soup, tuna, June peas and bell pepper. Place into baking dish. Cook for 15 minutes at 400 degrees;. Add grated cheese and cook until cheese is melted.
