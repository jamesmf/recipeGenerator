FIVE-GRAIN CEREAL LOAF


1  c. 5-grain cereal
1 1/2  c. nonfat buttermilk
1  egg, beaten
2  Tbsp. brown sugar
2  Tbsp. plus 1 1/2 tsp. vegetable oil
2  c. unbleached flour
1  tsp. baking powder
1  tsp. baking soda
1/2  tsp. ground cinnamon
1/4  tsp. salt

      Stir together cereal and buttermilk and let stand 5 minutes. Add egg, sugar and vegetable oil, mixing well.  Combine the rest of the ingredients in a large bowl, stirring well.  Add to cereal mixture and stir until dry ingredients are moistened.  Spoon batter into an 8 1/2 x 4 1/2 x 3-inch loaf pan that has been sprayed with vegetable cooking spray.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 45 to 50 minutes; a wooden pick inserted in center should come out clean.  Cool 10 minutes in pan before removing.  Yields 15 servings.
      Per Serving (1/2-Inch Slice):  117 Calories, 3.3. g. Fat, 18 mg.  Cholesterol and 161 mg.  Sodium.
