SPAGHETTI AND BEEF CASSEROLE


1  lb. box spaghetti, cooked and drained
1  lb. ground beef, cooked and drained
1  lb. jar Ragu or Prego spaghetti sauce
1  c. cheddar cheese, grated
1  c. mozzarella cheese, grated

      Mix spaghetti, beef and sauce together.  Pour into a 9 x 13 inch pyrex dish. Top with cheddar and mozzarella cheese.  Bake at 350 degrees; until cheese melts.
