STRAWBERRY JELLO PRETZEL SALAD


2  c. pretzels, crushed
3/4  c. butter, melted
2  Tbsp. sugar
1  (8 oz.) cream cheese
1  (8 oz.) Cool Whip
1  c. powdered sugar
2  c. boiling water
6  oz. strawberry Jell-O
2 (10 oz.)  pkg. frozen strawberries

	In a 9 x 13-inch pan, combine pretzels, butter and sugar. Spread over bottom of pan and bake at 400 degrees; for 10 minutes. Cool.
	Blend cream cheese, Cool Whip and powdered sugar together in medium bowl. Spread over pretzel crust.
	Dissolve Jell-O in boiling water. Add strawberries and stir. Set for a few minutes until thick. Pour over cream cheese mixture. Set for 3 hours.
