BARBECUED SPARERIBS

Base Mixture:
3  lb. ribs, cut into serving pieces
  sprinkle of salt
1  lemon, thinly sliced
1  medium onion, chopped

      Place spareribs, meat side up in a 9x13-inch glass baking dish.  Sprinkle with salt.  Top with lemon slices and chopped onion.
      Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes.
Sauce Mixture:
1/4  c. molasses (unsulphured)
1  tsp. chili powder
1  Tbsp. celery seed
1/4  c. vinegar
1  c. ketchup
1/2  tsp. Tabasco sauce
1/4  c. Worcestershire sauce
2  c. water

      Combine molasses with chili powder and celery seed.  Stir in vinegar, ketchup, Tabasco sauce, Worcestershire sauce and water.
      Bake 1 hour longer, basting with sauce mixture 3 to 4 times.
