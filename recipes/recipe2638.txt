SWEDISH NUT CAKE


2  c. sifted flour
2  c. sugar
2  eggs, slightly beaten
2  tsp. baking soda
1/2  c. chopped nuts
1  tsp. vanilla
20  oz. can crushed pineapple, juice and all

      Put all into a large bowl; mix well with a spoon.  Pour into a greased and floured 10 x 13-inch pan.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 35 to 40 minutes.
Topping:
1/4  stick margarine
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
1/2  c. powdered sugar
1  tsp. vanilla

      Cream  margarine  and cream cheese.  Add powdered sugar and vanilla.  Beat well.  Spread on cooled cake.
