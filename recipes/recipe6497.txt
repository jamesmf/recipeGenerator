STUFFED EGGPLANT


2  eggplants
2  c. cubed ham
1  c. celery, chopped
1/2  c. Parmesan and Romano cheese
1/2  c. Progresso Italian bread crumbs
1  large onion, chopped
1  large garlic clove, chopped
3  Tbsp. shortening
  salt and pepper to taste

      Parboil until tender.  Cut in halves and scoop out meat.  Set aside and saut√© onion, garlic and celery and cook until tender. Mash pulp with ham, cheese, bread crumbs and mixture in skillet. Fill shells and cover with more bread crumbs.  Bake at 350 degrees; until crumbs are browned.
