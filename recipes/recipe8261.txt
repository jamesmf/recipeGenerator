POTATO TOPPED MEAT LOAF


1 (12 oz.)  jar Heinz homestyle brown or mushroom gravy
1 1/2  lb. lean ground beef
1  c. soft bread crumbs
1/4  c. onion, finely chopped
1  egg, slightly beaten
1/2  tsp. salt
  dash of pepper
2 1/2  c. hot mashed potatoes
1  Tbsp. melted butter or margarine
  paprika

	Measure 1/4 cup gravy from jar and combine with beef and next 5 ingredients. Form into a loaf (8 x 4 x 1 1/2-inch) in a shallow baking pan. Bake in 350 degrees; oven 1 hour. Remove from oven and carefully drain fat. Spread hot mashed potatoes over top and sides of meat loaf. Drizzle melted butter over top of potatoes. Garnish with gravy and paprika.
