WHITE CLAM SAUCE WITH LINGUINI


3  cloves garlic, minced
1/2  c. olive oil
1/2  c. butter
3  doz. fresh clams, chopped
1  c. clam juice
3  Tbsp. parsley, finely chopped
  salt to taste
1  tsp. lemon juice
1  tsp. oil or butter
1  lb. linguini
1/2  c. dry sherry or Chablis

      Saut√© garlic in 1/2 cup olive oil and 1/2 cup butter until golden.  Add clams, clam juice, parsley, salt and lemon juice. Simmer 5 minutes.  Put 1 teaspoon oil or butter in water.  Bring to boil.  Add linguini and cook 6 minutes.  Drain linguini; place on platter.  Pour wine into simmering sauce.  Cook 1 more minute and pour over linguini.  Serves 8.
