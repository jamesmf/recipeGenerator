SHE-CRAB SOUP


1/4  lb. butter
1/2  c. flour
1/2  c. finely chopped celery
1/2  c. finely chopped carrots
1/2  c. finely chopped onions
1  pt. milk
1  c. chicken broth
1  Tbsp. Old Bay seasoning
1  lb. crab meat
1/3  c. dry sherry
1  pt. half and half

      Combine butter and flour, cooking over low heat for 3 to 4 minutes.  Saut√© celery, carrots and onions.  Heat milk and chicken broth together.  Add butter, flour, seasoning, vegetables and crab meat.  Simmer over low heat for about 30 minutes.  Then add sherry and half and half.  Simmer for another five minutes.
