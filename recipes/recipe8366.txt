"BAKELESS" ZITI


12  oz. ziti
3  c. fat-free marinara sauce
1/4  tsp. crushed red pepper flakes
1  c. light Ricotta cheese
1  c. light Mozzarella cheese, shredded
1/4  c. Parmesan cheese, grated

	Cook ziti according to package directions; drain. Meanwhile, combine sauce and pepper flakes in a medium sized saucepan; heat through. Combine ziti, sauce mixture, Ricotta and Mozzarella in a large serving bowl. Sprinkle with Parmesan. Makes 6 servings. Preparation time: 3 minutes. Cook time: 10 to 12 minutes.
