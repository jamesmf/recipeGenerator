HOT CRAB DIP


1  lb. crab meat
8  oz. carton sour cream
2 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese, softened
4  Tbsp. mayonnaise
3  shakes garlic salt
  juice of 1/2 lemon
1/2  c. shredded cheese (Cheddar)

      Combine crab meat, sour cream, cream cheese, mayonnaise and seasonings. Place in a casserole. Top with Cheddar cheese and bake in a 325 degrees; oven for 25 to 30 minutes. Serve with party crackers or small rye bread.
