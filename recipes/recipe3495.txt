SALSA


  3-4 large tomatoes diced small
1  fresh jalepeno pepper, diced very fine
3 (4-1/2 oz.)  cans chopped green chilis
1  bunch green onions chopped end to end
1  Tbsp. salt (optional)
1 1/2  Tbsp. pepper
1/2  cup corn oil
1  tsp. chili powder
1 1/2  Tbsp. garlic salt
  Pinch oregano
  4-5 Tbsp. chopped fresh cilantro

      Mix all ingredients together in a large bowl and refrigerate at least 2 hours before serving.
