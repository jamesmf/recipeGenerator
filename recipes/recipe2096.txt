CHOW MEIN CANDY


1  small bag butterscotch morsels
1 small  can cocktail peanuts
1 small  can chow mein noodles
1  c. pecans, roasted in salt and butter

      Melt morsels.  Stir in all other ingredients, then spread on wax paper and cool.
      Note:  Get boiler hot, then remove from heat.  Add morsels and stir to avoid burning.
