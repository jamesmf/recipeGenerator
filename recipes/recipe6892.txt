SHRIMP FRIED RICE


6  eggs, beaten
1  onion, chopped
1  lb. shrimp, peeled
1/2  bell pepper, chopped
3  to 4 onion tops, chopped
2  c. cooked rice
1/2  stick butter
1  Tbsp. Tony's seasoning
1  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. garlic, minced
  parsley flakes
  black pepper to taste
1/4  tsp. cayenne pepper

	In butter, saut√© onion, bell peppers, parsley flakes and green onions until clear. Season shrimp with Tony's and add to vegetables. Cook on medium heat until shrimp are pink. Pour beaten eggs into shrimp and vegetables. Add rice and stir until rice is coated with egg mixture. Cover and cook on low heat until eggs are cooked.
