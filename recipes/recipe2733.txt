CHOCOLATE COVERED EGGS


8  oz. cream cheese
1  stick butter
2  lb. confectioners sugar
1  tsp. vanilla
  dash of salt
8 to 10  oz. peanut butter

      Mix all ingredients together and shape into eggs.  Coat with chocolate when eggs are cold and firm.  After dipping in chocolate, eggs may be decorated with cherries, frosting, nuts, coconut, etc.
Dipping Chocolate:
8  sq. (1 oz. each) semi-sweet chocolate
1/3  to 1/2 slab paraffin

      Melt chocolate in top of double boiler over simmering water with paraffin.
