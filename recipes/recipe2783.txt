PUMPKIN COOKIES


1/2  c. oleo
1 1/2  c. packed brown sugar
2  eggs
1  c. cooked pumpkin
1  tsp. vanilla
1  tsp. grated lemon peel
1  tsp. lemon juice
2 1/2  c. sifted flour
3  tsp. baking powder
1  tsp. salt
1 1/2  tsp. pumpkin pie spice
1/4  tsp. ground ginger
1  c. chopped walnuts
1/2  tsp. cinnamon

      Heat oven to 375 degrees;.  Grease cookie sheets.  Cream oleo and sugar until fluffy.  Beat in eggs, one at a time.  Stir in pumpkin, vanilla, peel and juice.  Sift flour, baking powder, salt and spices together.  Add to oleo mixture.  Stir in walnuts.  Drop by rounded teaspoonfuls on cookie sheet 1-inch apart.  Bake 12 to 14 minutes.  Cool on wire rack.  Makes 4 dozen cookies.
