EASY CHICKEN PIE


1  can Veg-All, drained
1  can cream of chicken soup
2  c. cooked boneless chicken
2  (9-inch) deep-dish pie crusts

      Mix Veg-All, soup and chicken; pour into 1 pie crust.  Put other pie crust over mixture.  Crimp edges with fork; trim sides. DO NOT VENT.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 40 to 45 minutes until brown.
