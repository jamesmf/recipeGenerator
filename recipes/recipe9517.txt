PORK TENDERLOIN WITH APRICOT SAUCE


1 (11 1/4 oz.)  can apricot nectar
3  Tbsp. vegetable oil
1/4  c. Kikkoman lite teriyaki marinade and sauce, divided
2  Tbsp. chopped fresh or dried rosemary
2  (3/4 lb.) pork tenderloins
1 (16 oz.)  can apricot halves, drained
1/4  c. balsamic vinegar
3/4  tsp. pepper
1/4  tsp. salt

      Combine 3/4 cup nectar, oil, 3 tablespoons teriyaki sauce and rosemary in a large shallow baking dish.  Add tenderloins, turning to coat.  Cover and refrigerate overnight.
      Combine apricot halves, vinegar, pepper, salt, remaining nectar and 1 tablespoon teriyaki sauce in container of an electric blender; blend until smooth.  Place in saucepan, bring to a boil over high heat and reduce sauce to 1 1/2 cups, about 10 to 15 minutes.  Place in dish; cover and refrigerate.  Remove tenderloins.  Grill 10 to 15 minutes on each side (160 degrees;).  Slice thinly and serve with apricot sauce.
