GRANNY'S ITALIAN DELIGHT


1 1/2  lb. ground beef
1  big onion
1  green pepper
1  can corn
2  cans tomato soup
1  can mushroom soup
1  pkg. or 1/2 pkg. thin spaghetti
1/2  c. grated cheese
  salt
  pepper

      Chop onion and green pepper. Saut√© onion and pepper. Add ground beef and brown lightly. Drain. Salt and pepper to taste. Add corn and soups. Cook slowly for 30 minutes to 1 hour. Cook the spaghetti in saltwater; drain. Pour sauce over noodles and mix. Place grated cheese over top. Put in oven at 375 degrees; until cheese melts. Serves 12.
