RAGU NO FRYING CHICKEN PARMESAN


4  boneless skinless chicken breast halves (about 1 1/2 lb.)
1  egg, slightly beaten
3/4  c. Italian seasoned dry bread crumbs
1 (26 oz.)  jar Ragu Old World Style pasta sauce
1  c. shredded Mozzarella cheese (about 4 oz.)

	Preheat oven to 400 degrees;. Dip chicken in egg and then in bread crumbs, coating well. In a 9 x 13-inch baking pan, arrange chicken and bake, uncovered, for 20 minutes. Pour sauce over chicken, then top with cheese. Bake for 10 minutes more or until chicken is no longer pink.
	 Serve, if wanted, with hot cooked pasta and garlic bread.
