CHOCOLATE RIPPLE DIVINITY


2  c. sugar
1/2  c. water
1/2  c. light corn syrup
1/8  tsp. salt
2  egg whites, unbeaten
1  tsp. vanilla
1 (6 oz.)  pkg. semi-sweet chocolate pieces

      Combine sugar, water, syrup and salt in a heavy saucepan.  Cook over low heat, stirring constantly until sugar is dissolved.  Cook to hard ball stage (252 degrees;) without stirring.  Beat egg whites until stiff.  Pour hot syrup slowly over egg whites, beating constantly. Add vanilla; continue beating until mixture loses gloss and will hold shape.  Fold in chocolate bits quickly; drop from teaspoon onto buttered waxed paper.
