LEMONADE COOKIES


1 1/2  c. oleo
1 1/2  c. sugar
3  eggs
3/4  tsp. lemon flavoring
3/4  tsp. vanilla
1/2  tsp. butter flavoring
4 1/2  c. flour
1 1/2  tsp. soda
1/2  tsp. salt
3/4  c. frozen lemonade, thawed

      Cream together oleo and sugar.  Add all remaining ingredients and chill 1 hour.  Drop by teaspoon on ungreased cookie sheet. Bake at 350 degrees; for 12 minutes.
Frosting:
2 3/4  c. powdered sugar
1/4  c. butter
  lemonade (as needed)

      Combine powdered sugar and butter; use lemonade for liquid.
