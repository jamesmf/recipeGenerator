COMPANY'S COMING CHICKEN BREASTS


8  boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  flour
  salt and pepper
  vegetable oil
1  can 99% fat free mushroom soup
2/3  c. mushrooms
1 16  oz. can artichoke hearts, quartered
1  c. reduced-fat sour cream
1/2  c. white wine

      Dredge chicken in flour, salt and pepper. Brown in oil in a heavy skillet. Place in a 13 x 9 x 2 inch baking dish. Combine soup, mushrooms, artichokes, sour cream and wine, pour over chicken. Bake uncovered at 350 degrees;F for one hour.  Serves 8.
