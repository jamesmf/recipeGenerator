JIM'S EASY BRUNSWICK STEW


1 large  can white chicken, undrained
1  1/2 cups pork, cooked & diced
1/2  lb. ground beef, browned & drained
1  onion, chopped
1 large  can tomatoes
1  can whole kernel corn, undrained
1  can chicken broth
1/4  cup vinegar
14  oz. ketchup
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
  salt & pepper, to taste
  cayenne pepper, to taste

      Brown ground beef and onion, drain. Add cooked pork and chicken. Add all other ingredients. Stir frequently. Simmer for approximately 1 - 1 1/2 hours. Serve over cooked rice. Great with crackers or cornbread muffins.
