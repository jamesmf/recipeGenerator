PIZZA CASSEROLE


1 1/2  lb. hamburger
1 (15 oz.)  can spaghetti sauce
1 (15 oz.)  can pizza sauce
  small pepperoni
16  oz. rotini noodles
  Mozzarella cheese

      Optional: Green peppers, onion, black olives, green olives, sausage, mushrooms, crushed garlic or whatever you like on pizza.
      Brown hamburger; drain. Add all other ingredients. Simmer while you cook the noodles. Mix all together. Put into a 9 x 13-inch pan. Sprinkle cheese over top. Cover with foil. Bake for 25 minutes at 350 degrees;.
