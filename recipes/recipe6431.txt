CHEESE SOUP


4  cups water
2  cups raw potatoes, dices
2  cups chopped onion
2  pkg. frozen Stir-fry vegetables with Oriental seasoning
2  chicken bouillon cubes
5  Tbsp. butter
2  cups celery soup, undiluted
1  lb. velveeta cheese, diced

      Boil all but cheese until tender.  Add dash of red pepper sauce and cheese.  Heat just until cheese melts.  Serve hot.
