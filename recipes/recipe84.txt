CHOCOLATE MARSHMALLOW FROSTING


1  sq. unsweetened chocolate
1/3  c. milk
2  c. powdered sugar
1  c. miniature marshmallows

      Melt chocolate in milk in top of double boiler over simmering water; stir until smooth.  Remove from heat and beat in sugar, 1/2 cup at a time, mixing thoroughly after each addition.  Cool slightly; fold in marshmallows.  Frost cookies.  Yields frosting for 4 dozen cookies.
