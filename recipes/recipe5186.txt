ALASKAN FRUIT SALAD


1  carton cool whip
1  carton cottage cheese
1  cup chopped pecans
1  pkg. orange jello
1  can mandarin oranges
1  can crushed pineapples
1  can fruit cocktail
  Coconut (flaked) if desired

      Drain all fruits well.  Mix all ingredients together well. Refrigerate overnight before serving.
