CANDY CANE COOKIES


1/2  c. Crisco
1/2  c. butter
1  c. powdered sugar
1  egg
1 1/2  tsp. vanilla flavoring or peppermint
2 1/2  c. flour
1/2  tsp. red food coloring

      Preheat oven to 375 degrees;.  Mix shortening, butter, sugar, egg and flavoring.  Add flour and mix.  Divide dough in half and add red food coloring to half of dough.  Roll a 4-inch strip using 1 teaspoon dough from each color.  Twist together and shape like a candy cane.  Sprinkle with granulated sugar.  Bake 10 minutes on ungreased baking sheet.
