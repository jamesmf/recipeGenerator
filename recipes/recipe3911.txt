SHRIMP MOLD


1  can tomato soup
1  c. mayonnaise
1  pkg. Knox gelatine
1/2  lb. cooked shrimp, chopped
1  c. chopped celery
1  c. green pepper
1  c. chopped onion
1  (8 oz.) cream cheese

      Heat  soup and cream cheese together. Stir until smooth. Mix Knox gelatine in 1/4 cup water. Let set 5 minutes. Add mayonnaise to soup mixture, then add remaining ingredients.  Mix well. Pour into lightly sprayed mold. Refrigerate overnight.
      Serve with Town House crackers.
