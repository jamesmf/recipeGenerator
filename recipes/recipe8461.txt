PINEAPPLE CASSEROLE


1 large  can pineapple, undrained
5  Tbsp. flour
1  c. sugar
2  c. mild cheddar cheese
12  oz. or 1 1/2 rolls Ritz crackers
1  stick margarine

      Mix flour and sugar with pineapple.  Add cheese and put in a baking dish.  Crush crackers and add melted margarine.  Place on top of pineapple and bake 15 minutes at 400 degrees;.
