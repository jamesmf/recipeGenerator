CHEESE SOUP


6  Tbsp. butter
3/4  c. minced onion
3  c. sharp Cheddar cheese
3/4  c. minced carrots
1/2  c. minced celery
4  Tbsp. flour
1/4  c. chopped parsley
1/8  tsp. white pepper
1/2  tsp. paprika
  pinch of red pepper
  dill to taste
4 1/2  c. chicken broth
1  c. heavy cream
2  Tbsp. sherry

      In a 3-quart saucepan, melt butter and saut√© carrots, onions and celery for 8 to 10 minutes. Sprinkle in flour and seasonings; blend well. Add chicken broth; stir until thick and smooth. Reduce heat to simmer. Add cream, sherry and cheese. Stir until cheese melts. Serve garnished with chopped parsley or chives.
