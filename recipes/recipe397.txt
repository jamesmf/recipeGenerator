CHOCOLATE ECLAIR CAKE


2  boxes graham crackers
2  pkg. French vanilla instant pudding
3 1/2  c. milk
9  oz. container Cool Whip

 
Chocolate Mix:
2  pkg. ready-blend chocolate or 2 (1 oz. sq.) chocolate, melted
1 1/2  c. powdered sugar
2  tsp. corn syrup
1  tsp. vanilla
1/4  c. milk
3  Tbsp. butter

      Butter  and line a 9 x 13-inch pan with whole crackers. Make pudding with 3 1/2 cups milk. Fold in Cool Whip. Pour 1/2 mixture over  crackers.  Add another layer of crackers.  Pour remaining mix  over  crackers.  Add another layer of crackers and top with Chocolate Mix.  Serves 20.
      Chocolate Mix:  Mix all ingredients and spread over top layer of  graham  crackers.  Refrigerate  for  2  days before serving.
      Per Serving:  226 Calories, 8 g. Fat (31%), 11 mg.  Cholesterol and 262 mg.  Sodium.
