DELICIOUS TACO DIP


1 1/2  lb. lean ground beef
1  pkg. taco mix
1 to 2  cans refried beans
1 small  bottle taco sauce
1  medium onion, chopped
1  can chopped green chilies
12  oz. shredded Cheddar cheese
  tortilla chips

      Brown meat and onions; drain grease.  Add taco mix and chilies. In large casserole, layer beans, meat, cheese and taco sauce.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 20 minutes.  Let set for 10 minutes.  Serve with dollops of sour cream, chopped black olives or scoops of guacamole after baking.
