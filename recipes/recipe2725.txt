CRISPY NESTS


1 (7 oz.)  jar Marshmallow Creme (about 2 c.)
1/4  c. creamy peanut butter
2  Tbsp. butter or margarine, melted
1 (5 oz.)  can chow mein noodles (about 3 c. )
1  c. chopped holidays plain M&M's
1  c. holidays peanut chocolate M&M's

      Combine Marshmallow Creme, peanut butter and butter; mix until well blended.  Add noodles and 1 cup holidays plain M&M's; mix well.  Drop by 1/3 cupfuls onto greased cookie sheet.  Shape with greased fingers to form nests.  Let stand until firm.  Dust bottom of nests lightly with confectioners sugar, if desired.  Fill with holidays peanut M&M's before serving.  Makes 10 to 12 nests.
