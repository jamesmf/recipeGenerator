COMPANY CASSEROLE


1  lb. ground beef
1  tsp. salt
1  medium onion, chopped
1/2  green pepper, chopped
1 (8 oz.)  box spaghetti
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  can tomato soup
1  can water
1 (4 oz.)  can sliced mushrooms
1  c. shredded cheddar cheese

      Brown ground beef, onion and green pepper.  Cook spaghetti and drain.  Mix all ingredients together.  Top with shredded cheese and bake at 350 degrees; until cheese is melted about 30 minutes.
