SAUSAGE CASSEROLE


1 (4 oz.)  pkg. (2 envelopes) chicken noodle soup mix
1/2  c. uncooked regular rice
2  c. boiling water
1  lb. mild pork sausage
2  c. chopped celery
1/2  c. chopped green peppers
1/2  c. chopped onions
1 (2 oz.)  jar diced pimento
1 (8 oz.)  can sliced water chestnuts, drained

      Combine soup mix and rice in a 1 3/4-quart shallow casserole dish; add boiling water, stirring until soup mix is dissolved. Set aside. Combine sausage, celery, green pepper and onion in large skillet; cook until sausage is browned, stirring to crumble. Drain well. Add meat mixture, pimento and water chestnuts to soup mixture; stir well. Cover and bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes. Yield: 6 to 8 servings.
