TUNA BUMSTEADS


1  can tuna
  Miracle Whip salad dressing
  bread
  cheese

      Fix your favorite tuna salad or simply mix 1 can of tuna with Miracle Whip salad dressing.  Pop one piece of bread into toaster. Spread the tuna on top.  Place a slice of cheese on the top and stick it on a cookie sheet.  Turn the oven on broil and put it under the broiler for a couple of minutes until the cheese melts.
