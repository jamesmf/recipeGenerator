BAKED BEEF STEW


1  can diced tomatoes, undrained
1  c. water
3  Tbsp. quick-cooking tapioca
2  tsp. sugar
1 1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper
2  lb. lean beef stew meat
4  medium carrots, cut into 1 inch cubes
3  medium potatoes, peeled and quartered
2  celery ribs, cut into 3/4 inch chunks
1  medium onion, quartered

      Combine the tomatoes, water, tapioca, sugar, salt and pepper in large bowl.  Add remaining ingredients; mix well.  Pour into greased 13 X 9 inch baking dish.  Cover and bake at 375 degrees for 1 3/4 to 2 hours.
