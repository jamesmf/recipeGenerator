BURNA BROED
(Brown Spice Cookies)  

1 3/4  c. flour
1/2  c. brown sugar
1  tsp. cinnamon
1  tsp. ground cardamon
1  egg yolk
  granulated sugar
1  tsp. baking powder
1/2  c. finely chopped almonds
1  c. plus 2 Tbsp. soft butter

      In a large bowl, mix all of the ingredients, except the granulated sugar.  Knead quickly.  Chill.  Shape the dough into a long roll 1/2-inch wide.  Cut off small pieces of the dough and roll into balls.  Dip the balls into the granulated sugar.  Bake at 375 degrees; on a buttered cookie sheet for about 12 minutes.
