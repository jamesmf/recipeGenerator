CABBAGE PATCH STEW


2  lb. ground beef or ground chuck
1  large onion, chopped
4  c. chopped cabbage
2  c. water
2 small  cans pinto beans
2 small  cans chopped tomatoes
2  Tbsp. chili powder
4  Tbsp. sugar
  salt and pepper to taste

      Brown beef and chopped onions.  Drain and add cabbage and water.  Cover and cook for 15 minutes slowly.  Add pinto beans, tomatoes and remaining ingredients.  Cook 20 minutes more.
