OLD FASHION CHICKEN NOODLE SOUP


3  chicken breasts
1/2  lb. fine noodles
  carrots, chopped fine
  onions (optional)
  salt
  pepper

      Boil chicken until done.  Take chicken apart; cut fine pieces. Take the rest of the ingredients into pan.  Cook until carrots are done.  Cover and simmer for at least 2 hours.
