FILLED PEPPER SOUP


2  lb. ground beef
1 (28 oz.)  can tomatoes
1 (28 oz.)  can tomato sauce
2  c. rice, cooked
2 to 3  c. green pepper chunks
2  beef bouillon cubes
1/4  c. brown sugar
1 1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper

      Combine all after browning hamburg in large pot.  Simmer at least 1 hour, stirring often.
