PAW PAW'S OLD FASHIONED POUND CAKE



1  c. butter (not margarine)
2  c. sugar
2  c. cake flour
6  eggs (medium)
1  tsp. pure vanilla

      Cream together butter and sugar until sugar is fairly well dissolved.  Add eggs, one at a time, while continuing to beat.  Add vanilla.  Fold in flour.  Grease and flour tube pan.  Put waxed paper in bottom of pan.  Pour mix into pan.  Bake in 325 degrees; oven for 1 hour.
