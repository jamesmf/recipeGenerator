STUFFED PEPPERS


  6-8 Bell Peppers, (blanche 5 minutes)
2  lb. ground beef
1  medium onion (diced)
1  c. minute rice
1  tsp. garlic powder
1  tsp. pepper
1  tsp. salt
4  eggs
2  tbsp. A 1 steak sauce
2  tbsp. Worcestershire sauce

 
Topping:
1 small  bottle catsup
1/2  c. sugar
1  c. tomato juice

      Blanche peppers for 5 minutes.  Mix first 9 ingredients except peppers. Place peppers in a 13 x 9 inch baking pan. Stuff with the beef mixture.
       In separate pan, heat to boiling, catsup, sugar, and tomato juice. Pour over peppers. Bake at 350 degrees;F oven 1 hour, or until meat is completely cooked.
