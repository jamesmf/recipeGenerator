TACO SALAD


1  head lettuce, chopped
1  can kidney beans, drained
1  medium onion, chopped
4  tomatoes, diced
1  lb. hamburger
8  oz. cheese, grated
1  pkg. taco chips
1  pkg. taco seasoning

      Brown   hamburger  and  taco  seasoning.  Drain.  Cool. Combine  all  ingredients except chips and dressing.  Add just before serving.
Dressing:
8  oz. Thousand Island dressing
1/3  c. sugar
1  tsp. taco seasoning
1  tsp. taco sauce
