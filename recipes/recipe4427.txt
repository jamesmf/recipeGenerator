LAYERED SALAD


1  head lettuce
1  head cauliflower
1  lb. bacon
2  c. mayonnaise
1/4  c. sugar
1/3  c. Parmesan cheese
2  green onions (optional)

      Place torn lettuce pieces on bottom.  Cauliflower pieces on next layer.  Combine mayonnaise, sugar and Parmesan cheese.  Mix thoroughly.  Spread mixture on top of lettuce, cauliflower and onions.  Seal.  Refrigerate overnight.  Add bacon just before tossing.
