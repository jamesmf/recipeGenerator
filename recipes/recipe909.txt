HOT SEAFOOD ENGLISH MUFFINS


1/2  c. grated Swiss cheese
1/2  c. sliced canned mushrooms
1  Tbsp. chopped onions
1/2  c. cooked shrimp, crab or tuna
1/4  c. mayonnaise
2  English muffins, split

      Combine first 5 ingredients and stir until blended.  Spread over English muffins.  Bake 10 minutes in a 400 degrees; oven, then broil until bubbly.
