AUNT MARY'S STRAWBERRY SALAD


2  pkgs. strawberry Jell-O
1  c. boiling water
2  pkgs. (10 oz. each) frozen strawberries (let them be almost frozen when you use them)
1 large  can crushed pineapple, drained
3  medium sliced bananas
1  c. chopped pecans
16  oz. sour cream

      Mix Jell-O per directions.  Add strawberries, bananas, nuts, and pineapple.  Pour half of this in dish and then add sour cream. Place the remainder of the mixture over the sour cream and refrigerate until time to serve.
      It is best if made the day before.
