RAVIOLI WITH HAM AND PEAS


2  Tbsp. butter or olive oil
1/2  c. chopped onion
1  tsp. minced garlic
1/4  c. chicken broth
1  c. frozen baby peas, thawed
1  c. heavy or whipping cream
1/4  tsp. pepper
2  pkg. (7- or 8-oz. each) fresh cheese ravioli or tortellini
2  oz. cooked ham, cut into strips
1  Tbsp. finely chopped fresh parsley

      Cook ravioli according to package directions.  Meanwhile, melt butter or heat oil in skillet over medium-high heat.  Add onion and garlic and cook until translucent, about 5 minutes.  Add chicken broth and cook 2 minutes.  Add peas, cream and pepper. Bring sauce to boil; boil 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Add ham.  Drain cooked ravioli; toss in a large heated serving bowl with cream sauce and sprinkle with parsley.  Makes 4 servings.
