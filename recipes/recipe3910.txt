SALMON LOG


1 large  can salmon
1  (8 oz.) cream cheese
1  Tbsp. lemon juice
2  Tbsp. grated onion
1  tsp. horseradish
1/4  tsp. salt
1/2  Tbsp. liquid smoke
1 1/2  c. chopped nuts
3  Tbsp. chopped parsley

      Drain salmon. Combine next 6 ingredients with salmon. Shape into log and roll in nuts and parsley.
      Serve with crackers.
