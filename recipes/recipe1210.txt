DATE COOKIES


2/3  c. water
1/4  c. oleo
1  c. chopped dates
1  egg, beaten
1/4  tsp. nutmeg
1/4  tsp. salt
1  tsp. cinnamon
1/2  tsp. soda
1/2  tsp. baking powder
1  Tbsp. noncalorie sweetener
1  Tbsp. water
1  c. flour

      Cook water, oleo and dates for 3 minutes and let cool.  Mix rest of ingredients.  Combine with cooled mixture.  Drop from spoon on cookie sheet.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 12 to 15 minutes.
