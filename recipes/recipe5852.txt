GERMAN COLESLAW


8  c. finely shredded cabbage
1/2  medium size green pepper, minced
1  c. finely chopped onion

 
Dressing:
3  Tbsp. sugar
3  Tbsp. hot water
3  Tbsp. vinegar
1/2  tsp. celery seed
1/2  tsp. salt
  pinch of pepper
1/2  c. vegetable oil

      Place cabbage, green pepper and onion in a large bowl and toss well to mix.
       For the Dressing: Combine sugar and water, stirring until sugar is dissolved. Stir in vinegar, celery seed, salt and pepper. Pour over slaw and toss well. Drizzle in oil and toss well again. Cover and let marinate in the refrigerator 2 to 3 hours. Toss well before serving.
