VIVA LA CHICKEN


1  pkg. lasagna noodles
5  chicken breasts, cooked
1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can cream of mushroom soup
1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can cream of chicken soup
1  can mushrooms
1 (7 oz.)  can diced Ortega chilies
1 1/2  lb. Jack cheese

	Cook noodles.  Drain and place a layer of noodles on bottom of dish.  Place layer of boned and skinned chicken.  Next place layer of soups and mushrooms, mixed together, then add a layer of grated cheese.  Continue until 3 layers are completed.  Top with noodles and cheese.  Bake in 350 degrees; oven for 45 minutes to 1 hour. Let sit for a few minutes before serving.
