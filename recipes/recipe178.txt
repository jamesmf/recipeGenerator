MEXICAN WEDDING COOKIES


1  c. butter or margarine, softened
1/2  c. sugar
1  tsp. vanilla
1/4  tsp. salt
2  c. flour
  confectioners sugar

      Preheat oven to 375 degrees;.  Cream butter, 1/2 cup sugar, vanilla and salt until fluffy.  Stir in flour until well blended.  Chill 30 minutes or until firm enough to handle.  Shape in 1-inch balls. Place 1 inch apart on ungreased cookie sheet and bake until light golden, 12 to 15 minutes.  Remove to rack while cookies are still warm; sift heavily with confectioners sugar.  Cool, then store airtight in cool, dry place.  Before serving, sift cookies with more confectioners sugar.  Makes about 48 cookies.
