REDUCED FAT CRANBERRY SALAD


12  oz. fresh cranberries, ground
1/2  c. walnuts, chopped
2  c. red grapes, cut in halves
1  can crushed pineapple
1  (8 oz.) container nondairy whipped topping ("lite" version)
1 1/2  c. sugar

      Grind cranberries.  Grind or chop walnuts.  Cut grapes in half, removing seeds if necessary.  Place the ingredients in a large mixing bowl.  Add sugar and pineapple.  Cover and marinate overnight in refrigerator.  Add whipped topping just before serving.  Yield:  Approximately 20 servings.
