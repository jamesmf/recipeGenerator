LAYERED SALAD


  lettuce and/or spinach, torn into small pieces
  chopped, small green onions
  chopped celery
1 or 2 small  cans LeSueur peas, drained
1  envelope Hidden Valley Ranch dressing
  shredded mild Cheddar cheese
  bacon bits or chips

      Layer the ingredients in a clear salad bowl.
       If you make the salad ahead of time, don't add the dressing, cheese and bacon until the last minute.
