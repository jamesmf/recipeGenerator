LUSCIOUS LAYER COOKIE MIX


1/2  c. chocolate chips
1  c. brown sugar
1 1/2  c. plain flour
1/2  tsp. baking soda
1/2  tsp. salt
1  c. quick cooking oats
1/2  c. pecans
1/2  c. chocolate chips

      (Layer ingredients starting at bottom of jar and working to top.)
      Mix 1 egg, 1/2 teaspoon vanilla and 1 stick softened butter with contents of jar.  Drop by spoonfuls onto a greased cookie sheet.  Bake 8 to 10 minutes at 350 degrees;.
