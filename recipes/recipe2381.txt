BLUEBERRY PIE


4  c. fresh blueberries
3/4 to 1  c. sugar
3  Tbsp. flour
1/2  tsp. grated lemon peel
  dash of salt
1 to 2  tsp. lemon juice
1  Tbsp. butter
1  (9-inch) double-crust unbaked pastry

      Combine blueberries, sugar, flour, lemon peel and salt.  Fill pie shell.  Sprinkle with lemon juice; dot with butter.  Adjust top crust.  Bake in hot oven (400 degrees;) for 35 to 40 minutes.  Serve warm.
      Like spice?  Add 1/2 teaspoon each cinnamon and nutmeg to filling.
