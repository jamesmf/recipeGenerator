DIFFERENT POTATO SALAD


8  to 10 potatoes, cubed and cooked
1/4  c. white wine
3  Tbsp. white wine vinegar
1  tsp. salt and pepper to taste
1/4  tsp. dry mustard
1/2  c. oil
1  tsp. tarragon leaves
6  green onions, sliced
2  Tbsp. parsley, chopped

	Layer cooked potatoes in a casserole dish. Sprinkle with wine.
	In a bowl, combine vinegar, salt, pepper and mustard. Beat until creamy.  Add tarragon, onions, parsley and dressing to potatoes and mix. Serve at room temperature.
