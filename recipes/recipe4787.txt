STRAWBERRY JELLO SALAD WITH SOUR CREAM


1 (6 oz.)  pkg. strawberry Jell-O
1 (10 oz.)  pkg. frozen strawberries
1/2  c. nuts
1/2  pt. sour cream
1  c. hot water
1 large  can crushed pineapple
2  to 3 bananas, mashed

      Dissolve Jell-O in 1 cup hot water. Add pineapple (juice and fruit), mashed bananas, strawberries and nuts. Pour half of mixture into a dish (9 x 13-inches) and firmly chill. Spread sour cream on firm Jell-O. Top with remaining Jell-O mixture and chill until firm. Serves 12.
