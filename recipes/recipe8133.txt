CHEESEBURGER PIE


1  lb. hamburger
1/2  c. evaporated milk
1/3  c. bread crumbs
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1 1/2  c. grated Cheddar cheese
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. pepper
1/2  tsp. oregano
1/2  c. onions
1/2  c. ketchup

      Brown meat with onions and drain. Stir in all but Worcestershire sauce and cheese. Cook 20 minutes at 400 degrees;. Then add cheese and Worcestershire sauce on top and cook for 10 more minutes. Let stand 10 minutes before eating. Serves 4 to 6.
