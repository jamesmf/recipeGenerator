DELICIOUS CHICKEN CORN SOUP
(Served At The Family Style Restaurant In Lancaster, PA)  

1  lb. chicken breast
1  qt. water
4  chicken bouillon cubes
1/2  c. diced celery
1/2  c. diced onions
1/2  tsp. white pepper
1  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. celery seed
1 (16 oz.)  can whole kernel corn
2 (16 oz.)  cans cream-style corn
1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can cream of celery soup

 
Dough Balls:
1  c. flour
1  egg
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. baking powder

      In large pot, place chicken, bouillon cubes and 1 quart water. Bring to a boil and simmer 1 hour or until chicken is done.  Remove chicken from pot; pick chicken from bone and discard bones and skin.  Dice the meat and return to pot.  Add remaining soup ingredients and simmer approximately 15 minutes, until celery and onions are tender.
      Mix Dough Balls by combining flour, egg, salt and baking powder in a bowl.  Mix thoroughly.  Sift the mixture through your hand into the soup and continue to stir.  Simmer for about 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Makes approximately 3 quarts.
