SPINACH SALAD


  Fresh spinach
  cooked crumbled bacon
  hard boiled eggs, for garnish

 
Dressing:
2/3  c. sugar
1  tsp. salt
3  tsp. prepared mustard
1  tsp. celery seed
1  c. oil
1  c. onion, chopped
1  tsp. pepper
1/3  c. vinegar

      Place spinach in large bowl.
      Mix dressing ingredients in blender.  Pour warm dressing over fresh spinach.  Add bacon and garnish with eggs.  Dressing keeps well in refrigerator and may be reheated.
