EASY STOVETOP CASSEROLE


  Ham chunks
  onion chunks
  carrot chunks (fresh or canned slices)
1  can chicken broth
1  can potatoes
  about 1/2 to 1 c. frozen broccoli
  Country Bob's seasoning

      Slightly precook ham and onions. Add remainder of ingredients and cook on medium heat for about 30 minutes.
