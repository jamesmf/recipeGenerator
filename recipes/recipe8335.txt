DRESSING OVER CHICKEN CASSEROLE


4  c. chicken breasts, diced
2  c. bread crumbs
4  eggs
2  c. milk
1  c. hot water
1  c. margarine
1  (10 oz.) bag Pepperidge Farm stuffing

	Mix chicken breasts, bread crumbs, eggs and milk. Put in a 9 x 12-inch greased pan. Combine water, margarine and stuffing. Spread over top of chicken mix. Bake at 350 degrees; for about 45 to 60 minutes or when brown on top.
	 To serve, cut into squares.
	 I make a gravy and pour over the casserole and serve.
