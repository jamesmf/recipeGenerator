TATER TOT CASSEROLE


1  lb. hamburger, browned
1  onion, diced
1  can cream of celery soup
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  can corn, peas or mixed vegetables, drained
1  pkg. Tater Tots

      Place hamburger and onion in bottom of casserole dish. Place cream of mushroom soup on next layer, then vegetables and cream of celery soup. Evenly space Tater Tots on top of casserole. Bake at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes.
