CHICKEN CASSEROLE


1 3/4  C. seasoned croutons
1/2  C. melted butter or margarine
1 1/2  C. cooked chicken (cut up)
1/2  C. milk
1  can cream of celery or cream of chicken soup
1  Tbsp. minced onion
  salt & pepper to taste

      Heat soup, milk, butter and onion.  Pour over 1 C. croutons and the chicken.
      Top with 3/4 C. crumbled or crushed croutons.
      Bake at 425 degrees for 10 - 15 minutes.
      May be prepared in morning, refrigerated and then baked later in the day.
