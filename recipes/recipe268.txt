Poppy seed and Lime Dressing


1/3  c. vinegar
1/4  c. lime juice
3/4  c. sugar
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. dry mustard
1  tsp. poppy seed
1  tsp. paprika
1  c. salad oil
1/2  tsp. onion juice

      Heat vinegar and lime juice to boiling.  Combine dry ingredients.  Add to hot mixture.  Stir to dissolve sugar.  Add oil and onion juice.  Beat with rotary beater until thoroughly mixed and slightly thickened.  Chill.  Serve on canned or fresh fruit. Makes 1 3/4 cups.
