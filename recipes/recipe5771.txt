JELLO SALAD


1 large  can pineapple, crushed
1 large  pkg. jello (any flavor)
2  c. buttermilk
1  (9 oz.) Cool Whip
1/2  c. nuts

      Dissolve jello in hot pineapple. Let cool until it begins to thicken. Add buttermilk; fold in Cool Whip and nuts. Pour into large 8 x 13-inch pan. Let set in refrigerator until firm. Cut into squares and serve.
