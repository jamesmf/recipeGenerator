MEXICAN GOULASH


1 (16 oz.)  can pinto beans
1  lb. ground beef
  salt to taste
1/2  c. chopped celery
1  large onion, chopped
1  small green pepper, chopped
1 small  can tomato puree
1 (14-15 oz.)  can diced tomatoes
1  tsp. pepper
1  Tbsp. chili powder
1  tsp. sugar

      Cook ground beef, salt, onion, celery and green pepper. Drain. Add rest of ingredients and simmer awhile to blend flavors.
