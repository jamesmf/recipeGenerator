MEXICAN DEVILED EGGS


8  hard-cooked eggs
1/2  c. shredded Cheddar cheese
1/4  c. mayonnaise
1/4  c. salsa
2  Tbsp. sliced green onions
1  Tbsp. sour cream
  salt to taste

      Slice the eggs in half lengthwise; remove yolks and set whites aside. In a small bowl, mash yolks with cheese, mayonnaise, salsa, onions, sour cream and salt. Evenly fill the egg whites. Serve immediately or chill until ready to serve.
