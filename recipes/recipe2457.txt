RUM SAUCED BANANAS


1/2  c. apple juice
4  Tbsp. brown sugar
1  tsp. margarine
3  large bananas, peeled and sliced (at least 2 c.)
1  Tbsp. rum

      Add brown sugar and margarine to the apple juice and simmer over medium heat.  Add bananas and cook until bananas become very soft.  Add rum and cook for 1 minute.  Remove from heat and cool slightly.  Serve over ice cream or cake.  Serves 3. Recipe doubles easily.
