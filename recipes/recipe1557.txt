APPLE STRUDEL
(Apfelstrudel)  
Dough:
10 1/2  oz. bread flour
1/6  oz. salt
1 1/2  oz. vegetable oil
5 1/3  oz. water (lukewarm)

 
Buttered Breadcrumbs:
10 1/2  oz. butter (unsalted)
10 1/2  oz. bread crumbs

      Knead flour, salt, oil and water into a medium-firm dough. Divide into 3 small round loaves.  Brush each loaf with melted butter and let sit for 1 hour.
Filling:
4 1/2  lb. apples (Golden Delicious), sliced
5 1/3  oz. granulated sugar
1 1/2  oz. dark rum (Myers)
5 1/3  oz. raisins
1/8  tsp. ground cinnamon
2  lemons (juice and peel)

      Peel, core and slice apples.  Mix in granulated sugar, raisins, grated lemon peel, lemon juice, rum and cinnamon and blend together well.  Roll the dough loaves with a rolling pin, then stretch rolled dough on a strudel sheet with the backs of your hands.  Coat 2/3 of dough sheet with buttered breadcrumbs.  Spread apple filling over remaining 1/3 of dough.  Tear off edges.  Shape strudel into roll by lifting strudel sheet.  Place strudel on a buttered baking sheet and brush with melted butter.  Bake strudel for 60 to 90 minutes in a 400 degrees; to 425 degrees; oven.
