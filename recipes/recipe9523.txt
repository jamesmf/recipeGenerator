POST THANKSGIVING TURKEY CASSEROLE


2  c. cooked turkey breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
2  c. sliced celery
1  c. mayonnaise
3  Tbsp. grated onion
1 to 1 1/2  Tbsp. lemon juice
1/4  c. slivered almonds
1  c. grated cheese
1/2  c. chow mein noodles

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees;.   In a 2-quart casserole, combine turkey, celery, mayonnaise, onion and lemon juice.  Top with almonds, cheese and noodles.  Bake 30 minutes.  Makes 4 to 6 servings.
