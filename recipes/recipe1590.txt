BAHAMAS DOVE


12  dove breasts, cut in bite-sized pieces
1  c. red wine
3  Tbsp. soy sauce
1  clove garlic
1/4  tsp. ginger
1/2  c. vinegar
1/3  c. packed brown sugar
1  medium bell pepper
1  can pineapple chunks and juice
3  Tbsp. oil
  flour

      Marinate dove at least 2 hours in wine.  Roll marinated dove in flour; brown in oil.  Mix soy sauce, ginger, vinegar and brown sugar; heat.  Add garlic, pineapple and pepper to dove.  Heat to boiling; add sugar mixture to dove.  Simmer about 45 minutes. Serve with steamed rice.
