PEPPY BURGERS


1  lb. ground beef
1/2  c. chopped onion
1  Tbsp. shortening
1  can (10 1/2 oz.) condensed chicken gumbo soup
2  Tbsp. ketchup
1  Tbsp. Prepared mustard
  dash pepper
6  buns, split and toasted

      Brown beef and onion in shortening.  Stir to separate meat particles.  Add soup and seasonings.  Simmer 5 to 10 minutes to blend flavors.  Stir often.  Serve on buns; 6 servings.
