SWISS STEAK


2  lb. round steak
3  Tbsp. fat
1/2  c. chopped bell pepper
1 1/2  c. sliced sweet onion
3/4  c. tomato catsup
3  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  Tbsp. vinegar
2  c. canned tomatoes
  salt and pepper to taste
  flour to brown steak

      Salt and pepper steak generously.  Beat in as much flour as steak will take.  Brown in 3 tablespoons fat. Pour sliced sweet onion and bell pepper over steak.  Mix together catsup, vinegar and Worcestershire sauce.  Pour over onions.  Cover.  Bake for 45 minutes in moderate oven (300 degrees; to 350 degrees;).  Then add tomatoes.  Cook until steak is very tender.  Total cooking time is approximately 1 1/2 hours.
