ALL AMERICAN BEAN BAKE


1  lb. frankfurters, sliced thin
3/4  c. chopped onion
1 1/2  tsp. butter
1 1/2  tsp. dry mustard
1  tsp. allspice
1/4  tsp. pepper
1 (16 oz.)  can B&M Brick Oven baked beans
1/3  c. catsup
2  Tbsp. brown sugar
6  oz. Cheddar cheese, shredded

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees;.  In skillet, lightly brown sliced frankfurters with onion, butter, mustard, allspice and pepper, mixing well.  Pour beans into a baking dish.  Add catsup and brown sugar; mix well.  Spread evenly over bottom of baking dish.  Layer frankfurters over the beans.  Top with cheese.  Bake for 30 minutes.  Serves 6.
