EYE OF ROUND ROAST


1  (4 1/2 lb.) eye of round roast
1 (4 oz.)  jar Chinese sweet-hot mustard
3  Tbsp. olive oil
2  garlic cloves, pressed
2  tsp. lite soy sauce
1  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
  Roasted Potatoes (uncooked)
  fresh rosemary and fresh sage sprigs (garnishes)

 
Roasted Potatoes:
4  medium potatoes, cut into 8 wedges
2  Tbsp. olive oil
2  garlic cloves, pressed
1  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper

      Place roast on an 18 x 11-inch piece of heavy-duty aluminum foil. Stir together mustard and next 4 ingredients; spread over roast. Fold foil over roast to seal. Place in a shallow roasting pan and chill at least 8 hours. Remove roast from foil; place in roasting pan.
       Bake, covered, at 450 degrees; for 20 minutes. Arrange uncooked Roasted Potatoes around roast and bake, uncovered, 25 more minutes or until potatoes are tender and roast is done. Remove from oven; cover and let stand 15 minutes before slicing. Garnish, if desired. Yield: 4 servings.
      Prep: 15 minutes. Chill: 8 hours. Bake: 45 minutes.
