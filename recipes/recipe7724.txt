BREAKFAST CASSEROLE


2  c. sausage, cooked and drained
10  slices bread, cut into cubes
2  c. grated Cheddar cheese
6  eggs, beaten
3  c. milk
2  Tbsp. parsley
3/4  tsp. dry mustard
1/2  tsp. salt

      Spray casserole dish with nonstick spray. Mix sausage, bread, cheese, eggs, milk, parsley, dry mustard and salt. Place in dish; cover and let set overnight in the refrigerator. Bake at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes.
