WADIE'S CHICKEN AND RICE CASSEROLE


1  chicken, cut up and skinned
1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can cream of chicken soup
1  can water
1  pkg. onion soup mix
3/4 to 1  c. regular rice

      Put rice, dry soup mix, canned soup and water in large casserole. Mix well. Lay chicken on top and bake at 350 degrees; for 1 1/2 to 2 hours.
