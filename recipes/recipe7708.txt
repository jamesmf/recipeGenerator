CHICKEN SPAGHETTI


3  or 4 chicken breasts, cooked and cut in pieces
1 (16 oz.)  box spaghetti, cooked by pkg. directions
1 1/2  c. milk
1/3  c. flour
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  lb. Velveeta cheese

      Mix milk and flour. Cook until thickened. Add soup and cheese. Stir until cheese melts. Add chicken. Pour over cooked spaghetti. Mix well. Bake at 350 degrees; until bubbly.
      Note: Bell pepper and onion may be added if desired.
