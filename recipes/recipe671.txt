PASTA SALAD WITH AVOCADO AND LEMON SAUCE


1  lb. medium pasta shells
2 to 3  Tbsp. olive oil
4  spring onions (scallions), chopped
2 to 3  Tbsp. chopped fresh parsley
  few strips thinly pared lemon rind (optional)

 
Sauce:
1  ripe avocado
  juice of 1 lemon
1  garlic clove, minced
1  tsp. sugar
  salt and pepper to taste
1/2  pt. lite cream

      Cook pasta shells in boiling salted water until al dente; rinse and drain well.  Mix with the oil and leave to cool.  For sauce, halve the avocado, remove the stone (pit) and scoop the flesh into a blender.  Add the lemon juice, garlic, sugar, salt, pepper and cream and blend together until smooth.  Mix the avocado and lemon sauce with the pasta and stir in the spring onions and parsley. Sprinkle with the strips of lemon rind, if desired.
