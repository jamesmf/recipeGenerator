ONION-TOMATO SOUP


2  large sweet Spanish onions (about 6 c.), sliced
1/2  c. butter
2 (14 1/2 oz.)  cans stewed tomatoes
3 (10 1/2 oz.)  cans beef broth
1/2  tsp. basil
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper

      Peel and slice onions thinly.  Saut√© in butter in large saucepan.  Add tomatoes, beef broth and spices.  Cook over low heat for 45 minutes.  Serve with toasted French bread.
