MACARONI AND CHEESE


1/2  lb. macaroni
1  egg, beaten
1  tsp. dry mustard
1  c. milk
1  tsp. butter
1  tsp. salt
3  c. grated sharp cheese

      Boil macaroni in water until tender; drain well. Stir in butter and egg. Mix mustard and salt with 1 tablespoon hot water and add to milk. Add cheese, leaving enough to sprinkle on top. Pour into a buttered casserole. Add milk. Sprinkle with remaining cheese. Bake at 350 degrees; for 45 minutes.
