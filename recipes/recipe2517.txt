LITTLE PIZZAS


6  English muffins, split
1 small  can tomato paste
2  Tbsp. sugar
1  tsp. salt
2  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
2  cloves garlic, diced
4  oz. Mozzarella cheese, grated
1/2  lb. sausage
  oregano

      Mix  together tomato paste, sugar, salt, Worcestershire sauce and garlic.  Spread thinly on muffins.  Sprinkle cheese over tomato   mixture.   Top  with  sausage.  Sprinkle  with oregano. Bake at 450 degrees; about 15 minutes.
