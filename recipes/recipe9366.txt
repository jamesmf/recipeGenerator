PORK LOIN WITH MUDDY WATER MARINADE


3/4  c. olive oil
1/3  c. red wine vinegar
1/4  c. lemon juice
1/4  c. water
1  Tbsp. oregano
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. pepper
1/2  tsp. paprika
  pork loin

      Mix marinade in a glass dish.  Add pork, turning to coat. Cover and refrigerate overnight, turning 3 to 4 times.  To grill: Use drip pan with 1 inch water.  Gas:  Medium/Low; place on grill over drip pan.  Cover, grill 30 minutes until it begins to brown. Turn and grill until 160 degrees.  Turn every 20 minutes.  Let stand 15 minutes before carving.
