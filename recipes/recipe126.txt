TOP O' THE STOVE COOKIES


2  c. sugar
1/2  c. milk
1  stick margarine
1/2  c. cocoa
1/2  tsp. salt
1  tsp. vanilla
3  c. uncooked quick oats
1/2  c. chopped walnuts
1/2  c. coconut

      Mix sugar, milk, margarine and cocoa and boil 3 minutes.  Add salt, vanilla and oats.  Cool slightly.  Add walnuts and/or coconut.  Drop by spoonfuls on waxed paper.  Makes about 2 dozen.
