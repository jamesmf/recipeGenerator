MEXICAN STEW


1 1/2  lb. stew meat
2  cans French-style green beans
2  cans stewed tomatoes
3  or 4 cut up potatoes
3  or 4 cut up jalapenos
  fresh garlic and onions
1  tsp. chili powder

      Brown meat in large pot with chopped garlic and onion. Cook a couple of hours until meat is tender. You can add salt and pepper at this time. Then add other ingredients and cook until potatoes are done. Serve over corn bread.
