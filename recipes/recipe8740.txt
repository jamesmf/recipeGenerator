FRIED CHICKEN


  4-5 cloves garlic
  Cooking oil
  Frying chicken parts

 
Dip:
4  cloves garlic
  5-6 tbsp. oil
1/4  tsp. salt

      Wash, drain and season the chicken parts with salt, pepper and garlic powder.  Fill a heavy cast-iron skillet half full with oil.  Heat to very hot and brown the garlic.  Fry the chicken till golden brown.  Remove chicken to platter.  Make the dip by smashing the garlic with the salt and gradually add the oil to it. Drizzle the dip over the chicken to serve separately.
