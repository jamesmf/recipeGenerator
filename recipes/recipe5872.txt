RED SALAD


1 small  pkg. strawberry jello
1/3  c. hot water
1  c. crushed pineapple
1  c. nuts
2  crushed bananas
1  pkg. frozen strawberries
1  c. sour cream
1  pkg. strawberry jello
1 1/2  c. hot water

      Dissolve 1 package jello in 1/3 cup hot water. Add crushed pineapple, nuts and crushed bananas. Add the frozen strawberries. Mix and let congeal. Spread the sour cream, beaten at room temperature, over first layer.
       Dissolve 1 package strawberry jello in 1 1/2 cups hot water. Let this congeal and pour over first mixture.
