SWISS VEGETABLE MEDLEY


16  oz. frozen mixed vegetables (such as broccoli, carrots and cauliflower)
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  c. shredded Swiss cheese
1/3  c. sour cream
1/4  tsp. black pepper
4  oz. pimento, drained and chopped
1  can French fried onion rings

      Combine vegetables (frozen), soup, 1/2 cup cheese, sour cream, pepper, pimento and 1/2 can French fried onions.  Pour into casserole.  Bake, covered, at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes.  Top with remaining cheese and onion rings.  Bake, uncovered, 5 minutes longer.
