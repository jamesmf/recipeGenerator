BROCCOLI CHEESE SOUP


4  c. water
2 (14 oz.)  pkg. frozen chopped broccoli
2  c. diced potatoes
1/2  c. chopped onion
3  chicken bouillon cubes
2  cans cream of chicken soup
1  lb. processed cheese, cubed

      Cook the first 5 ingredients for 20 minutes or until potatoes are done.  Reduce heat and add soup and cheese.  Simmer until well mixed and cheese is melted, stirring often to keep from sticking.
