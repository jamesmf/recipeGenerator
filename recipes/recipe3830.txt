CHEESE BALLS


1 (8 oz.)  pkg. sharp grated natural cheese
2 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
1  Tbsp. chopped onion
1  Tbsp. chopped pimento
1  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  tsp. lemon juice

      Mix well and press together into 2 small or 1 large ball. Roll in chopped pecans. Great with any cracker.
