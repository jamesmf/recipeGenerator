EASY CHICKEN 'N DUMPLINGS


1  qt. chicken broth (with some chicken)
2  c. flour
2  tsp. baking powder
1  tsp. salt
  milk

      Heat broth.  Thicken broth as  thin  gravy.  Mix flour, baking powder and salt.  Add milk as  for drop biscuits.  Drop onto boiling broth.  Put lid on.  Cook about 10 to 15 minutes.
