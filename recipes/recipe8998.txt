DUCHESS MEAT LOAF PIE


1  envelope onion soup mix
1 1/2  c. water
1 1/2 to 2  lb. ground beef
  hot mashed potatoes

      Stir soup mix into water. Measure out 1/2 cup and blend into ground beef. Shape beef into 8-inch round patty in large frying pan. Cut into six pie-shaped wedges. Pour remaining soup over. Cover and cook 20 minutes over medium heat. Remove cover. Add Kitchen Bouquet to gravy and cook down.
       To serve, spoon hot mashed potatoes on top of meat. Dip gravy from bottom of pan and drizzle over. Serves 6.
