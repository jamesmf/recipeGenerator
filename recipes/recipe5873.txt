LIME JELLO SALAD


1  pkg. lime Jell-O
1  c. boiling water
1/2  lb. miniature marshmallows
1  c. cottage cheese (small curd)
1  c. chopped nuts (optional)
1  c. crushed pineapple, well drained
1  c. Cool Whip

      Dissolve Jell-O in boiling water. Add miniature marshmallows. When mixture has cooled, add cottage cheese, nuts and crushed pineapple. When mixture has thickened, fold in Cool Whip. Keep refrigerated.
