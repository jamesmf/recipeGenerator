JELLO SALAD


1  pkg. lemon Jell-O
1  pkg. lime Jell-O
1  carrot, grated
1 flat  can crushed pineapple
8  oz. cream cheese, cut in small cubes
1/3  c. chopped pecans

      Mix Jell-O together as directed on box. Set in the refrigerator and let begin to jell. When Jell-O has begun to jell, add other ingredients and put in ring mold. Return to the refrigerator.
