HOT BROCCOLI DIP


2 (10 oz.)  pkg. frozen, chopped broccoli
2  rolls garlic cheese (Kraft)
2  (8 oz.) cream cheese
3  c. celery, chopped
2  medium onions
1  stick butter
2  cans cream of mushroom soup
2 small  pkg. slivered almonds
