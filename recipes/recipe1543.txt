ANGEL FROST PUNCH


2 (10 oz.)  pkg. frozen sliced strawberries
2 (6 oz.)  cans frozen pink lemonade concentrate
2  c. water
1  qt. vanilla ice cream
2 (7 oz.)  bottles (about 2 c.) lemon-lime carbonated beverage, chilled

      In blender container combine 1 package strawberries, 1 can lemonade concentrate, 1 cup water and 1/2 of the ice cream.  Blend until thick and smooth.  Pour into punch bowl.  Repeat with other half in blender.  Carefully pour lemon-lime beverage down side of punch bowl.  Gently stir to mix.  Makes 14 to 15 ounce servings of punch.
