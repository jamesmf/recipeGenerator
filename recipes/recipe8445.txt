SPEEDY MEATBALL SKILLET


2  c. sliced carrots
  meatballs (either make your own with chili powder and salt, or use bought ones)
2 10  oz. defrosted lima beans
1  can cream of celery soup
1/4  c. water
  onion, to your taste

      Partly cook carrots; add meatballs, (brown meatballs if desired), lima beans, soup and water to skillet.  Stir lightly to combine.  Cook slowly for 20 to 30 minutes, stirring occasionally. Mixed vegetables may be used instead of carrots and limas.
