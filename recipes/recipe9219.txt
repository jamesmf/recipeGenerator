RAISIN SAUCE FOR HAM


1/2  c. packed brown sugar
2  Tbsp. cornstarch
1  tsp. ground mustard
1 1/2  c. water
1/2  c. raisins
2  Tbsp. white vinegar
2  Tbsp. lemon juice
1/4  tsp. grated lemon peel
2  Tbsp. butter or margarine

      In a saucepan, combine brown sugar, cornstarch and mustard. Stir in water, raisins, vinegar, lemon juice and peel until blended.  Bring to a boil; cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thickened.  Stir in butter until melted.  Serve warm over sliced ham.  Yield: 2 cups.
