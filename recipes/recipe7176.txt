PORK BUTT PIE


2  lb. ground pork or sausage
1  medium size onion
1  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
1/2  tsp. cloves (ground)
3/4  tsp. cinnamon
1  c. water
1  large potato
2  pie shells

      Cook meat with onion, salt, pepper, 1/2 of cinnamon and cloves; add 1 cup water.  Keep stirring until it boils.  Simmer 1 1/2 hours.  Remove from heat; drain fat.  Then add remaining cloves and cinnamon.  Mash potato (do not add milk or butter).  Add meat with potatoes; mix well.  Place in pie shell and top on it.  Bake at 425 degrees; until golden brown.
