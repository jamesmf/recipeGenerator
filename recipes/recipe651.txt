SUGAR-FREE TINY APPLESAUCE CUPCAKES


1/2  c. margarine
1  egg
2  tsp. Sweet 'N Low
1 3/4  c. flour
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. cinnamon
1/2  tsp. nutmeg
1/4  tsp. cloves
1/4  tsp. ginger
1  c. applesauce (unsweetened)
2  tsp. vanilla
1/3  c. raisins
1/3  c. chopped nuts

      Cream margarine until fluffy.  Beat egg and sweetener until thick and lemon colored; add to margarine and mix well.  Sift all dry ingredients together; add to margarine mixture alternately with applesauce; blend well.  Add vanilla, raisins and nuts.  Pour into tiny tart tins, filling 2/3 full.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 15 to 20 minutes.  Makes 30.
