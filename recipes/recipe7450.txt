SEAFOOD CASSEROLE


1  (16 oz.) canned or fresh crabmeat
1  (16 oz.) canned or fresh shrimp
1  medium green pepper, chopped
1  medium onion, chopped
1  c. celery, chopped
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
1  c. or more mayonnaise
  saltine crackers, crushed and tossed with butter

      Combine all ingredients, except cracker crumbs.  Blend well. Place in baking dish; sprinkle buttered cracker crumbs evenly over casserole.  Bake 25 to 30 minutes.
