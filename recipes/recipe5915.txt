STRAWBERRY SALAD


1  pkg. fresh or frozen strawberries
2  boxes strawberry Jell-O (small)
1 small  can crushed pineapple
2  or 3 bananas
1  c. chopped nuts
1 1/2  c. boiling water

      Dissolve Jell-O in hot water.  Add other ingredients. Pour half of mixture in a dish and let congeal. After this has congealed, spread one cup sour cream over this. Then pour other half of mixture and let congeal.
