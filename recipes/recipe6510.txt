CAVATINI


2  (8 oz.) or 1 (16 oz.) rotini noodles, cooked and drained
1  lb. hamburger meat
1  lb. sausage
1  large onion, chopped
2  large Ragu sauce (garden style)
1  pkg. pepperoni
1 (12 oz.)  pkg. grated Mozzarella

      Brown meat and onions in large pan. Drain. Mix all ingredients together except pepperoni and cheese. Put in baking dish (large). Cover with pepperoni and cheese on top. Bake at 350 degrees; for 20 minutes. Serve with garlic bread.
