TATER TOT CASSEROLE


  Hamburger meat
  Mushroom soup
  Grated cheddar cheese
  Tater tots

      Cook meat and drain the grease.  Put in casserole dish.  Pour mushroom soup over meat.  Cover with grated cheddar cheese.  Top with tater tots.  Put in oven and bake at 375 degrees; until tater tots are golden brown.
      Note:  You can substitute any kind of meat you prefer for the hamburger meat.
