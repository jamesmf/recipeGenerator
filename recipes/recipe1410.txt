ASSORTED GRILLED HAMBURGERS


1 1/2  lb. ground meat
3/4  tsp. salt
  dash of pepper
1  tsp. monosodium glutamate

      Lightly mix all ingredients and shape into 6 patties.  Broil on grill over hot coals, turning once, about 10 minutes or until nicely browned.
Variations:
1/4  c. commercial sour cream
1  Tbsp. minced parsley
1/8  tsp. oregano
3/4  tsp. garlic salt
2  Tbsp. chopped fresh mushrooms
1/4  tsp. ground ginger
1  tsp. soy sauce
1  tsp. sugar

      Prepare as usual.
