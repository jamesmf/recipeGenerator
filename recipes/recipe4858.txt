BLACK BEAN AND CORN SALSA


1 (15 oz.)  can tomatoes
3  chopped tomatoes
1 (15 oz.)  can black beans
1 (15 oz.)  can corn, drained
2/3  c. red bell pepper
1  tsp. Goya adobo with pepper
  pepper to taste
3  Tbsp. jalapeno pepper, chopped
3  Tbsp. lime juice or wine vinegar
1/2  tsp. sugar
3  cloves garlic, chopped
1/2  tsp. cumin
1/2  tsp. chili powder
1/2  c. chopped onion
1/4  c. fresh cilantro, chopped
1 to 2  Tbsp. olive oil
1/2  tsp. salt

      Combine all ingredients.  Chill.  Serve with tortilla chips.
