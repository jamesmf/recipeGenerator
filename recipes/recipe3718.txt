CURRY CHEESE BALL


2  C. cheddar cheese, shredded
1  lrg. white onion, chopped
2  pkgs. softened cream cheese
  tabasco
2  Tbsp. curry
1  jar mango-chutney
1/2  C. chopped walnuts

      Mix together:  shredded cheese, onion, cream cheese, tabasco. Form into a ball.  Sprinkle curry on outside of ball.  Pour mango-chutney over ball and top with chopped walnuts.
      Serve at room temperature with crackers.
