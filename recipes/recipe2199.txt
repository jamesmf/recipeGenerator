DILLED OKRA


  okra
1  qt. vinegar
1  c. water
1/2  c. pickling salt
1  tsp. dill
1  clove garlic
  hot red pepper

      Boil together vinegar, water and pickling salt.  Put dill and garlic, sliced, in bottom of pint jar.  Add okra and 1 pod hot red pepper.  Pour vinegar mixture over and seal jars.
