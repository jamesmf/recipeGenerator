SAUERKRAUT SALAD


1- # 2-1/2  can kraut, drained
1/2  c. onions
1  jar pimento
1/2  c. green pepper (optional)
1  c. celery, chopped

 
Dressing:
1/2  c. sugar
1/2  c. vinegar
1/2  c. oil (not olive oil)

      Drain kraut and cover with boiling water. Let stand about 5 minutes then drain and rinse with cold water. Add remaining vegetables to kraut. Heat sugar, vinegar and oil to boiling and pour over vegetables.
