SWEET AND SOUR HOTDOGS


3  pkg. hotdogs
2  Tbsp. honey
1 medium  jar apple jelly
1 small  jar mustard
1  Tbsp. butter

      Cut hotdogs into bite size pieces.  Put hotdogs in skillet and brown in butter.  Stir in honey, jelly and mustard.  Stir until jelly melts and mixture becomes smooth.  Simmer for 45 minutes.
