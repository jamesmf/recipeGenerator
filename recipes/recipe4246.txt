VEGETABLE PIZZA

Crust:
1  pkg. crescent rolls

 
Middle:
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese, softened
1/2  c. mayonnaise
1/2  tsp. onion salt
1/2  tsp. garlic powder
1/2  tsp. dill weed

      Leave crescent rolls out 5 to 7 minutes before patting into pan. Bake at 350 degrees; for 10 minutes. Mix middle ingredients and spread over cooled crust. Top with chopped veggies of choice (shredded carrots, broccoli, etc.). Add a layer of shredded Cheddar cheese.
       Could substitute for the middle: garlic cream cheese or 8 ounces cream cheese and dry package of Ranch dressing mix.
