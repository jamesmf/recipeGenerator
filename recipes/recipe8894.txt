SHEPHERD'S PIE


2  lb. ground beef
1  medium onion, chopped
1  Tbsp. salt
1  Tbsp. pepper
4  Tbsp. ketchup
1 small  bottle Worcestershire sauce
4  Tbsp. flour
1  c. water
1  can mixed vegetables
  mashed potatoes

      Brown meat and onions. Add other ingredients. Mix well. Cook until thick. Add vegetables. Put in casserole pan and top with mashed potatoes. Spread with butter. Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes.
