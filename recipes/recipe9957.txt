GREEN TOMATO CASSEROLE


5  green tomatoes, sliced
1  c. chopped green onion
1  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
  lemon pepper to taste
3/4  c. grated Cheddar cheese
1  roll Ritz crackers, crushed
4 to 6  Tbsp. butter or margarine

      Place sliced tomatoes in a buttered 13 x 9 casserole.  Cover with onions.  Sprinkle with salt, pepper and lemon pepper.  Top with grated cheese and crushed crackers.  Dot with butter.  Bake, covered, for 45 minutes at 400 degrees;.  Bake, uncovered, for an additional 10 minutes.
