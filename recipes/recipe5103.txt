STRAWBERRY CONGEALED SALAD


2 2/3  c. coarsely chopped pretzels (put in plastic bag and roll fine)
1 1/2  sticks margarine
8  oz. cream cheese
1  c. sugar
8  oz. Cool Whip
2 small  pkg. strawberry Jell-O
2  c. boiling water
2 small  cartons frozen strawberries

      Mix the pretzels and margarine and spread. Bake at 350 degrees; for 10 minutes. Let cool.
      Mix the cream cheese, sugar and Cool Whip. Pour over pretzels.
      Then mix the Jell-O, boiling water and strawberries. Refrigerate.
