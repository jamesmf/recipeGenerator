ZESTY POTATO SALAD


2  lb. red-skinned potatoes, cooked and cut into cubes
1/4  c. dry milk
1/4  c. water
1/4  c. Dijon mustard
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. black pepper
2  c. frozen green beans, thawed
1  c. red bell pepper, chopped
1/4  c. green onions, sliced

	Combine dry milk, water, mustard, salt and pepper in a small bowl until well mixed. Combine potatoes, green beans, bell pepper and onions in a large bowl. Add milk dressing from small bowl and toss well to coat. Refrigerate until chilled.
