COCONUT COOKIES


2  eggs
3/4  c. sugar
1/3  c. flour
1/4  tsp. baking powder
1/8  tsp. salt
1  tsp. vanilla
1/2  tsp. grated lemon rind
1  c. dates or pecans
2 2/3  c. coconut
1  Tbsp. butter, melted

      Beat eggs; add sugar slowly while beating.  Add remaining ingredients, adding melted butter last.  Drop by teaspoon onto greased and floured baking sheet.  Bake about 15 minutes in a 325 degrees; oven.
