CHOCOLATE TURTLES


1  c. pecan halves
36  Kraft caramels
4  oz. semi-sweet chocolate chips

      Place 3 pecan halves on a buttered aluminum foil-lined cookie sheet.  Put a caramel on each group of pecans.  Put in a 350 degrees; oven for 10 minutes.  Remove from oven; flatten the caramels with a lightly buttered spoon.  Cover with melted chocolate (melt in double boiler) and place in refrigerator until hard.  These are delicious!!
