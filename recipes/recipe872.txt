HERB BUTTER FOR SALMON


1  c. butter
1  tsp. garlic, chopped
1/4  c. onion, chopped
2  tsp. parsley
2  tsp. basil
1  tsp. tarragon
2  tsp. celery salt
2  tsp. pepper
1/2  c. lemon juice
1  tsp. Dijon mustard

      Cream butter in food processor.  Add remaining ingredients and process until combined.  Spread on salmon before barbecuing.
