RED BELL PEPPERS PESTO AND SHRIMP


2  Tbsp. olive oil
4  large red bell peppers, cut small
1  c. diced carrots
1  c. diced celery
3  cloves garlic, diced
1  Tbsp. oregano
1  lb. fresh, cleaned shrimp
1 (14 oz.)  can diced tomatoes
2  c. pesto
1  c. water
1 (15 oz.)  can tomato sauce
  salt and pepper to taste
1  lb. pasta

      In large skillet, saut peppers, carrots, celery, garlic and oregano.  Add diced tomatoes, pesto, water, tomato sauce, salt and pepper.  Simmer 5 minutes. Add shrimp. Cook only until shrimp turn pink. Serve over cooked and drained pasta. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese
