APPLE-CRANBERRY CASSEROLE


3  medium apples (unpeeled), sliced
3/4  c. sugar
2  c. raw cranberries
  dash of salt

 
Topping:
1  c. quick oatmeal
1/2  c. brown sugar
1/3  c. flour
1/2  c. nuts
2/3  stick margarine*

      In a buttered casserole make 2 layers each apples and cranberries, sprinkling with sugar and salt.  Add 1/4 cup water, if desired.  Cover with topping.  Bake at 350 degrees; until cooked through and lightly browned, 30 minutes or more.
      *Mix the oatmeal, brown sugar, flour and nuts.  Place pats of margarine on top of casserole.
