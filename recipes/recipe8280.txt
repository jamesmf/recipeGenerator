VENISON SWISS STEAK


1  large steak
1  onion, sliced
1  can tomato soup
1  can of water
1  tsp. salt
  pepper to taste
  flour
2  Tbsp. vegetable oil
  Lipton onion soup mix

	Tenderize steak on both sides using a cleaved hammer and cut into serving size pieces.  Flour meat and season both sides with Lipton onion soup mix.  Cook in hot oil.  Place cooked pieces in baking dish or crock-pot.  Cover with tomato soup, water, onion, salt and pepper.  Cover and bake 1 1/2 to 2 hours or until tender or cook in crock-pot on low all day.
