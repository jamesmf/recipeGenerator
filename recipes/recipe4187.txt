MEXICAN BEAN DIP


1  lb. ground beef
1  pkg. taco seasoning mix
1  can refried beans
1 8  oz. sour cream
  salsa
  cheddar cheese, shredded

      Brown the ground beef and mix with taco mix. Put refried beans in the bottom of an 11 x 7 pan, top with ground beef, then sour cream, then salsa and sprinkle cheese over all. Put in 350 degree oven until cheese melts.
      You can add jalapeno peppers and black olives or anything else you like on top.
