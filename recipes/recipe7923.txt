ROAST BEEF


3  lb. beef roast
1/2  cup vinegar
1/2  cup lemon juice
1/4  cup soy sauce
2  tsp. each garlic and ginger paste
1  cup yogurt
  Salt to taste

      Wash meat and dry thoroughly using paper towels.  Pierce the meat and make a few gashes with a knife.  Marinate with all the ingredients and refrigerate for 3 hours.
      Place the meat in a pressure cooker or a pot with a tight-fitting lid and add 2 cups of water.  Cook over medium-low heat until meat is tender and the water has dried up.
      Cut into slices and serve with ketchup and fries.
