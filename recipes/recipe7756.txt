BREAKFAST QUICHE


8  eggs
2  c. milk
2  slices day-old bread
1  lb. sausage, cooked, scrambled and drained
1  c. grated sharp Cheddar cheese

      Mix all the ingredients and refrigerate overnight. When ready to cook, add salt to taste, Tabasco sauce to taste, oregano and 1/2 teaspoon mustard. Put in 9 x 11 glass dish and cook until firm (about 30 to 40 minutes) at 350 degrees;. Do not grease pan.
