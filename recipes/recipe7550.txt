CHICKEN & CREOLE RICE


4  large chicken breasts, boiled and chopped
6  c. cooked brown rice
1  can Cream of Chicken soup
1/2  stick of butter
  1-2 Tbsp. Creole spice

      Melt 1/2 stick of butter in a 2 qt. casserole dish.  Sprinkle about 1 Tbsp. of Creole spice on top of butter.  (I prefer Konriko brand.)  Mix together cooked rice, cooked chicken pieces, soup, and 1 - 2 T. of Creole spice.  Pour on top of spiced butter.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes or until heated thoroughly.  Serves 8.
