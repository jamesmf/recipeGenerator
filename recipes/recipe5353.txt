SALSA


3  medium tomatoes, peeled and chopped
4  oz. can diced green chillies, drained
1/4  c. sliced green onions
1/4  c. bell pepper, chopped
2  Tbsp. lemon juice
2  Tbsp. fresh cilantro, chopped
1  clove garlic, minced
1/2  c. tomato sauce

      Stir together all ingredients except tomato sauce. Place 1 cup of mixture and the tomato sauce in a blender. Puree. Stir into the remaining mixture. Cover and chill several hours before serving.
