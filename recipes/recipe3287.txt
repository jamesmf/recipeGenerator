SPICED PEACHES


1 (29 oz.)  can freestone peaches (halves or whole)
1/2  c. sugar
1/2  c. vinegar
1/4 to 1/2  tsp. cinnamon
  dash of nutmeg

      Drain peach syrup into saucepan. Add sugar, vinegar and spices. Bring to a boil. Simmer for 10 to 15 minutes. Pour over peaches and let stand 24 hours in the refrigerator. Could serve on a lettuce leaf and fill centers with a spoonful of currant jelly.
