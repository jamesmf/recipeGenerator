SIMPLE CHILI


4  cans tomato soup
2  cans kidney beans
2  lb. ground beef
1  c. chopped onions
2  Tbsp. chili powder
  salt and pepper to taste

      Brown and drain ground beef.  Mix together soup, beans, onions, chili powder, salt and pepper.  Add ground beef.  Cook over low heat until ready to serve.  Can simmer all day.
