BREAKFAST CASSEROLE


2  c. seasoned croutons
1/4  lb. shredded cheddar cheese
2  Tbsp. parsley
1  chopped onion
2  c  milk
6  to 8 eggs
  salt and pepper to taste
1  lb. chopped, fried bacon

      Spray pan. Layer croutons, bacon, cheese and onions. In a bowl mix eggs, milk, parsley, salt and pepper. Pour on top. Bake in a 325 degree oven for 50 minutes.
