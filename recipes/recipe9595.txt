EASY BACON ONION TART


1  pkg. crescent refrigerated rolls
4  strips bacon, coarsely chopped
1  medium onion, coarsely chopped
1  c. sour cream
2  egg yolks
1/2  c. grated Parmesan cheese
  salt and pepper to taste
2  Tbsp. fresh rosemary, chopped

      Preheat oven to 375 degrees;.  Do not separate rolls.  Unroll dough in sheet and press perforations together to form a rectangle.
      Saut bacon and onion over medium-high heat until bacon is brown and onions are limp.  Drain on paper towels.  Combine sour cream, yolks, Parmesan, salt and pepper in a small bowl.  It may be runny.
      Place rectangle of dough on a baking sheet.  Spread sour cream mixture over dough. Try to leave 1/2-inch border. Sprinkle bacon and onion mixture over top, then add the rosemary on top. Bake 15 to 20 minutes or until set and lightly browned.  Serve warm or at room temperature.
