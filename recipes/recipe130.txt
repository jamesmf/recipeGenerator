CHEESECAKE COOKIES

Crust:
1/4  c. (1/2 stick) butter
1/3  c. packed brown sugar
1  c. flour
1/2  c. chopped pecans

 
Filling:
1/4  c. currant jelly
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
1/4  c. sugar
1  egg
2  Tbsp. milk
2  Tbsp. lemon juice
1/2  tsp. vanilla

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees;.  For crust, cream butter in small mixing bowl.  Add sugar and flour.  Blend until mixture resembles cornmeal.  Stir in pecans.  Reserve 1 cup of mixture for topping. Press remainder onto bottom of 8-inch square baking pan.  Bake 15 minutes.  Cool.  Spread jelly over crust.
      For filling, beat cream cheese and sugar in a small mixing bowl.  Beat in egg and milk.  Add lemon juice and vanilla.  Turn into pan; sprinkle with reserved crumb mixture.  Bake 30 minutes. Cool on wire rack; cut into bars or squares.  Makes approximately 2 1/2 dozen cookies.
