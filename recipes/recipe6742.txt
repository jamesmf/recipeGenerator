BEAN BURGERS


1  lb. ground beef or ground chuck
1/2  c. chopped onion
1/4  tsp. chili powder
1  can bean and bacon soup
1/4  c. catsup
6  hamburger buns

      Fry grease out of beef.  Drain off.  Combine beef with soup, onion, diced, catsup and chili powder.  Spread evenly on bun halves.  Broil about four-inches from heat, about 12 or 15 minutes.
