MACARONI AND CHEESE - NEW JERSEY STYLE


1 1/2  c. elbow macaroni
3  Tbsp. butter or margarine
3  Tbsp. all-purpose flour
2  c. milk
1/2  tsp. salt
  dash of pepper
2  c. shredded sharp processed cheese
1  egg

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees;.  Cook macaroni in boiling salted water until tender; drain.  Melt butter; blend in flour.  Add milk. Cook and stir until thick.  Add salt, pepper and cheese; stir until cheese is melted.  Mix sauce with macaroni and egg.  Turn into 1 1/2-quart casserole dish.  Bake at 350 degrees; about 45 minutes or until bubbly and browned.  Makes about 6 to 8 servings.
