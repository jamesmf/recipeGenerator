LIME JELLO SALAD


1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
2 small  pkg. lime jello
1 large  can crushed pineapple
3  c. boiling water
1  c. chopped pecans

      Blend the cream cheese and jello together to form a smooth paste. Add the boiling water, stirring well. Add the pineapple, including the juice and nuts. Pour into mold and congeal.
