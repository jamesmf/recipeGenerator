SAUSAGE BALLS


2  c. Bisquick
2  lb. shredded Cheddar cheese
2  lb. sausage
1/2 to 1  tsp. crushed red pepper

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees;. Sift Bisquick. In a large mixing bowl, break up pork into chunks; gradually add in Bisquick, cheese and crushed red pepper. Mix well. With hands, roll into quarter sized balls and place on a cookie sheet. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes.
      To reheat, cover and heat in microwave and serve in a warm crock-pot.
