CORNBREAD SALAD


2  tomatoes, chopped
2  cucumbers, chopped
15  oz. pinto beans, drained
15  oz. corn
1  onion
1  c. green pepper, chopped
  cornbread

 
Dressing:
1  c. mayo
1  c. sour cream
1  packet Hidden Valley Ranch dressing

      Mix all vegetables together.  In large bowl, start with crumbled cornbread on the bottom, then vegetables and dressing. Continue to stack and end with dressing on top.
