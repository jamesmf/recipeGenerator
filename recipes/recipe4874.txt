LIME SALAD


2  pkg. lime Jell-O
3  c. hot water
1 (8 oz.)  can crushed pineapple
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
1  Tbsp. mayonnaise
1/2  c. nuts

      Mix Jell-O and hot water.  Add cream cheese and mayo.  Beat with whisk.  Add pineapple and nuts.  Pour in greased dish or mold.  Coconut may be sprinkled on top or added to mixture.
