BBQ


1/2  C. finely chopped celery (optional)
1/4  C. onion
2  Tbsp. green pepper (optional)
1  Tbsp. vinegar
3  Tbsp. brown sugar
1  Tbsp. mustard
1  C. tomato juice or ketchup and 1/4 C. water
1  lb. hamburger

      Brown hamburger, drain fat.  Mix all ingredients with the hamburger.  Simmer until vegetables are tender.
      Serve on buns.
