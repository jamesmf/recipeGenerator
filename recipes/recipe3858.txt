BISCUIT PIZZA SNACKS



1  can Hungry Jack layered biscuits
1  jar Pizza Quick
  shredded Mozzarella cheese

      Preheat oven to 400 degrees;. Separate biscuits. Put on greased pan. (Can of 5 will be 10 little pizzas.) Cook biscuits at 400 degrees; for 3 minutes. Take out and flip over. Add a teaspoon of Pizza Quick to each and sprinkle with cheese. Broil until cheese bubbles.
