STILL ANOTHER SALAD DRESSING


1 1/3  c. mayonnaise
2/3  c. catsup
3  Tbsp. dill pickle juice
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce or to taste
1  tsp. horseradish

      Mix all ingredients together. Refrigerate in container (preferably glass) with covering, overnight. Be careful when using for the first time; it's hot. Keep refrigerated when not in use.
      Serving Size: 2 tablespoons. Cal-60.5, CHO-6.3, Pro-.22, Fat-4.1
