MEXICAN CASSEROLE


1 large  pkg. tortilla chips
2  c. chicken broth
1  c. cooked, boned and chopped chicken
1  small onion, chopped
1  small bell pepper, chopped
1  can Ro-Tel tomatoes
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  can cream of chicken soup
1 1/2  c. cheese, grated

      Arrange tortilla chips in and up sides of large casserole dish. Mix all ingredients, except cheese. Pour into casserole dish and cover with grated cheese. Bake 1 hour in 300 degrees; to 350 degrees; oven.
