PAT'S CHICKEN


4  large, boneless chicken breasts
2  cans cream of chicken soup
1/4  c. mayonnaise
1/4  c. seasoned bread crumbs
  Parmesan cheese
2  pkg. frozen spinach

      Defrost spinach and squeeze dry. Spread it in a 9 x 12 oiled pan. Add chicken. Pour soup and mayonnaise over dish. Add seasoned crumbs, then sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.  Bake at 400 degrees; for 3/4 hour.
