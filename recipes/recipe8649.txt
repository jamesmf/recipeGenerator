CRESCENT CRUST TACO PIE


1 1/4  lb. ground beef
1/2  c. chopped onion
1  pkg. taco seasoning mix
1/2  c. water
1/3  c. sliced stuffed green olives
1  can refrigerated crescent dinner rolls
1  c. sour cream
1  c. shredded Cheddar cheese
2  c. crushed corn chips
  avocado slices and shredded lettuce (optional)

	Preheat oven to 375 degrees;.  Brown beef and onion. Drain well. Stir in seasoning mix, water and olives. Simmer 5 minutes.
	Separate crescent dough into 8 triangles.  Place into ungreased 9-inch pie pan, pressing to form crust.  Sprinkle 1 cup of corn chips over crust.  Spoon meat mixture over corn chips.  Mix sour cream with cheese and spread over meat mixture.  Sprinkle remaining corn chips over cheese mixture.  Bake for 20 to 25 minutes until crust is golden brown.  Serve in wedges.
	Optional: Top with avocado slices and shredded lettuce.
