SALISICCIG, ALLA RIGATONI


1  lb. Italian sausage
1  c. chopped onion
2  cloves garlic, minced
1 large  can tomatoes
1  can tomato sauce
1  tsp. salt
1  Tbsp. olive oil
2  Tbsp. chopped parsley
1  pkg. rigatoni

	Remove sausage from casing; brown with onion and garlic.  Add tomatoes, tomato sauce, salt, oil and parsley.  Bring to a boil and simmer, uncovered, 15 minutes.	Cook rigatoni as directed. Serve with sauce.
