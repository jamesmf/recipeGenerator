KRAUT SALAD


1 (16 oz.)  can kraut
1/2  c. pimentos
1/2  c. celery, chopped fine
1/2  c. onions
1/2  c. green bell pepper, chopped fine
1  c. sugar
1/2  c. vinegar

      Put kraut undrained in a bowl and layer other ingredients. Boil together sugar and vinegar. When mixture is cool, pour over ingredients. Let stand in icebox 12 hours before serving.
