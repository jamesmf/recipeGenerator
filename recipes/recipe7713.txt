CHICKEN SUPREME CASSEROLE


1  chicken, boiled and deboned or 6 chicken breasts
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  container sour cream (medium)
1 1/2  sticks butter
1  box Escort crackers (3 packets)
1/2 small  jar pimento
1/2  can sliced water chestnuts
1  pkg. frozen green peas (optional)

      Put half of crumbled crackers in bottom of casserole dish. Pour half of melted butter over crackers. Add cut up chicken (bite size). Mix soups, sour cream, pimentos, peas and chestnuts. Pour over chicken. Top with remaining crackers and butter. Cook at about 325 degrees; to 350 degrees; for 25 minutes until golden brown and bubbly. Serves 6 to 8 people.
