POPCORN BALLS


3  c. miniature marshmallows
1/4  c. margarine
2  Tbsp. orange gelatin
8  c. freshly popped popcorn
1/2  c. dry roasted peanuts
1/2  c. miniature chocolate chips (optional)

      In a 5-quart saucepan over medium to low heat, melt marshmallows and margarine, stirring frequently until smooth.  Stir in gelatin; remove from heat.  Add popcorn and peanuts to marshmallow mixture; stir gently until coated.  Stir in chocolate chips.  With greased hand, shape cooled mixture into balls.  Wrap in plastic wrap.
