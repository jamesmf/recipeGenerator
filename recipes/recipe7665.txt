HOG MAW


1  pig stomach
1 1/2  lb. sausage
10  large potatoes, diced
2  onions, chopped
1 1/2  Tbsp. salt
1  tsp. pepper

      Soak pig stomach in salt  water  2 hours.  Mix sausage, potatoes, onions, salt and pepper.  Stuff  in  pig stomach and sew shut the opening  with needle and thread.  Bake covered at 250 degrees; for 5 hours.  The first hour I bake at 350 degrees;, then turn to 250 degrees; the remaining 4 hours.  The  stomach must be poked with a fork every 30 minutes.
