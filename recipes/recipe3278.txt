SAUSAGE CHEESE BALLS


1  lb. hot bulk sausage
2  c. Bisquick
2  c. shredded cheese (Cheddar or Colby Jack)

      Mix raw sausage and Bisquick with hands until crumbly. Add cheese. Continue to mix thoroughly. Press into a large ball. Let rest for 10 minutes. Roll into 1-inch balls. Bake on cookie sheet for 20 minutes at 350 degrees;. Drain and cool on paper towels. Store in the refrigerator. Reheat in the microwave.
