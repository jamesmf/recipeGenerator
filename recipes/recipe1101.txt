ZERO DRESSING


1  c. tomato juice
1  Tbsp. onion, finely chopped
  pepper to taste
1  tsp. tarragon vinegar
1/8  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. celery seed

      Combine ingredients in jar with tightly fitted top.  Shake well before using.  Also can add chopped parsley, green pepper, mustard seed and horseradish.  One tablespoon yields 3 calories.
