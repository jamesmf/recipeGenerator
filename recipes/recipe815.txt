ROCK CORNISH HENS


2  Rock Cornish hens
  salt to taste
  paprika to taste
  pepper to taste
  oil
1  c. wild rice, cooked
1  apple, cored and chopped
2  Tbsp. celery, minced
2  Tbsp. onion, minced
1/4  c. walnut pieces
1  tsp. parsley

      Heat oven to 350 degrees;.  Wash hens and pat dry.  Season with salt, pepper and paprika.  Rub lightly with oil.  Combine remaining ingredients and toss.  Stuff both hens equally and truss.  Place hens in uncovered roasting pan and roast for 1 hour or until tender.  Serve with candied yams, buttered broccoli and cranberry salad.
