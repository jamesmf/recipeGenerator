SAUSAGE PINWHEELS


2  c. plain flour
3  tsp. baking powder
1/4  tsp. salt
1/3  c. shortening
3/4  c. buttermilk
1  lb. sausage

      Sift dry ingredients.  Work in shortening.  Add milk and mix well.  Divide dough in half.  Roll each half until 1/4-inch thick. Divide sausage in half and spread evenly over dough.  Roll each into a roll.  Slice and bake at 400 degrees; for 20 minutes.
