SAUSAGE NOODLE CASSEROLE


1  pkg. Bird Farm original sausage (any brand will do)
1  lb. ground beef
1  large onion, chopped
1  green bell pepper, chopped
1/2  c. water
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. egg noodles, cooked
2 (16 oz.)  cans cream-style corn
2  cans condensed chicken noodle soup
  bread crumbs

      Brown meat, onion and green peppers; drain. Add cooked noodles and corn. Mix and place in 4-quart casserole dish. Combine soup and water; pour over mixture. Top with bread crumbs. Bake 45 minutes at 375 degrees;.
