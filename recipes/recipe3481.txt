SALSA DIP SAUCE



2  medium tomatoes, ripe
1/4  medium size onion
4  sprigs parsley
2  cloves garlic
1  tsp. garlic powder
2  Tbsp. lemon juice
  Jalapeo pepper (1/2 for mild, 2/3 for medium)

      Put all ingredients in your gourmet chopper and mix to consistency desired.  The gourmet chopper makes a finer cut.
