CRANBERRY RELISH SALAD


1 (3 oz.)  box strawberry gelatin mix (dry)
1  c. boiling water
1/2  c. sugar
1/2  c. orange juice
1 (10 oz.)  can whole cranberry jellied sauce
1  medium tart apple, chopped fine
1/2  c. chopped pecans
1  c. miniature marshmallows

      In a large bowl, dissolve gelatin in boiling water. Stir in sugar, mixing well. Add all other ingredients, mixing well. Pour into serving dish and refrigerate 4 hours to overnight. Serve and enjoy.
