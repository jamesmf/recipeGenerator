BOB'S DELUXE CHICKEN


1  lb. boneless chicken
  salt and pepper
3  Tbsp. butter
1  small onion
1  c. sour cream
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  can cream of celery soup
1/2  c. sherry or white wine

      Brown chicken in butter.  Arrange chicken in casserole.  Mix remaining ingredients and pour over chicken.  Cover casserole. Bake at 400 degrees; for 1 hour.
