STEAK WITH HERB-SPICE RUB


1  Tbsp. chili powder
2  tsp. oregano
1 1/2  tsp. ground cumin
1 1/2  tsp. dry mustard
1 1/2  tsp. salt
1 1/2  tsp. freshly ground pepper
2  cloves pressed garlic
4  rib-eye steaks

	Marinate steaks overnight with whatever marinade that you like. Pour off marinade. Mix the 8 ingredients and rub on both sides of steak. Let sit for 20 to 30 minutes, then grill.
