BREAKFAST PIZZA


1  lb. sausage
1  tube crescent rolls
1  c. frozen loose packed hash browns, thawed
1  c. shredded cheese
5  eggs
1/4  c. milk
1/2  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper
2  Tbsp. Parmesan cheese

      Brown sausage and drain. Separate rolls into 8 pieces; place in ungreased 12-inch pizza pan with points in the center. Press over bottom and up sides to form a crust; seal perforations. Spoon sausage over dough. Sprinkle with potatoes. Top with cheese. In a bowl, beat together eggs, milk, salt and pepper; pour into crust. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese. Bake at 375 degrees; for 25 to 30 minutes. Serves 6 to 8.
