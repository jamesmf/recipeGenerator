HOMEMADE PEACH ICE CREAM


1 1/2  c. sugar
2  Tbsp. flour
1/2  tsp. salt
3  eggs, beaten
1  qt. extra rich milk
1/2  pt. whipping cream
1  Tbsp. vanilla extract
6  c. chopped peaches
1  c. sugar

      Mix peaches and 1 cup sugar.  Combine the 1 1/2 cups sugar, flour and salt; add eggs and blend well.  Add milk and cook slowly until slightly thickened.  Let cool.  Add whipping cream, vanilla and sweetened peaches.  Pour into freezer container and freeze. Yield:  1 gallon.
