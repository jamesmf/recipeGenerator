PINEAPPLE SHEET CAKE


2  c. flour
1 1/2  c. sugar
1  tsp. soda
1 (No. 2)  can pineapple, crushed
2  eggs
1/2  c. brown sugar
1/2  c. nuts

      Mix  flour, sugar and soda together;  add pineapple and eggs. Beat  well.  Pour  into  a greased sheet cake pan.  Add brown sugar  and  nuts separately on top.  Bake 30 minutes at 350 degrees;.
Icing:
1  c. evaporated milk
3/4  c. sugar
1  stick butter

      Combine ingredients.  Boil 3 minutes.  Pour over cake immediately when taken from oven.
