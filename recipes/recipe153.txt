DUTCH BUTTER COOKIES


3/4  lb. butter or 3 sticks oleo
1 1/3  c. sugar
1  egg
1 1/2  tsp. vanilla
3  c. sifted flour
1  tsp. soda
1/2  tsp. salt

      Mix butter and sugar until creamy.  Add egg and vanilla and beat well.  Add sifted dry ingredients and mix well.  Roll into small balls and place on ungreased cookie sheet.  Dampen cloth and put over small drinking glass.  Dip in white sugar and use to flatten cookie balls.  Bake at 325 degrees; for 15 minutes or until done.
