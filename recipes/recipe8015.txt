GOOD HOT DISH


7  oz. pkg. thin spaghetti (cook)
1  lb. hamburger (brown)
16  oz. stewed tomatoes with green peppers
3/4  c. tomato paste
2  Tbsp. sugar
2  tsp. salt
2  tsp. oregano leaves
2  tsp. basil leaves
1  tsp. garlic powder
1/4  tsp. pepper
1/3  c. butter
1/2  c. sliced olives
1/4  c. green onions, chopped
2  c. shredded cheese (American)

      Put in layers.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes uncovered.
