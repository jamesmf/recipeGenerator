PIZZA SNACKS


1  loaf cocktail rye
1/3  c. bacon bits (Bacos)
1  c. fat-free mayonnaise
1/2  c. sliced green olives with pimentos
1  c. shredded part-skim Mozzarella
3  Tbsp. chopped dried onion

      Mix all ingredients (except bread). Spread on bread. Place on cookie sheets and bake at 400 degrees; until cheese browns, about 5 to 8 minutes.
