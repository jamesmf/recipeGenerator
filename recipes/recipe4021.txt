FLOSSIE JOHNSONS BEETS


1  c. vinegar
1  c. sugar
2  Tbsp. pickling spices
2  c. water
1/2  tsp. salt
  beets, cooked, peeled and sliced

      Boil all ingredients except beets.  Pour mixture over beets, which have been packed in sterile, hot jars.  Seal and process for about 10 minutes in a boiling water bath.
