HAM AND SWISS BROCCOLI QUICHE


15  oz. Pillsbury All-Ready pie crust
1  Tbsp. flour
6  oz. cooked ham
  shredded cheese (Swiss)
1  c. fresh broccoli
1  tsp. chopped onion

 
Filling:
1  c. half and half or Cheddar
4  eggs, beaten slightly
1/4 to 1/2  tsp. dry mustard
1/8  tsp. pepper

      Heat oven to 350 degrees;. In medium bowl, combine half and half, eggs, dry mustard and pepper. Set aside.
      Layer ham, cheese, broccoli and onion in pie crust lined pan. Pour egg mixture over ham, cheese and broccoli. Bake at 350 degrees; for 40 to 50 minutes or until knife inserted in center comes out clean. Cool 5 minutes. Cut wedges. Garnish as desired. Makes 6 to 8 servings. Refrigerate leftovers.
