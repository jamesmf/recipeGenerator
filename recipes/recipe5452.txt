CHICKEN STEW


3  chicken breasts or whole chicken, cooked
1  c. cabbage
1  can peas or fresh peas
2  potatoes, peeled and diced
1  onion (small)
2 to 3  c. chicken broth
2  carrots, cut up
1  celery stalk, chopped
  salt and pepper to taste
1 large  can or jar stewed tomatoes
1  can corn
1  c. water

      Cook in crock-pot or on stove; simmer for 1 hour.  The last 5 minutes add macaroni or rice to thicken.  You can put anything you like or have leftover in it.  The more veggies, the better.
