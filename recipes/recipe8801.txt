SOFT-SHELL CHICKEN TACOS
(Mild)  

1  lb. ground chicken
1 small  can tomato sauce
  salt
  pepper
2  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  Tbsp. sugar
  sprinkling of chili powder
1  pkg. soft taco shells

 
Garnish:
  lettuce
  chopped tomato
  grated cheese (Cheddar or Mozzarella)

      Cook ground chicken in non-stick pan.  Add remaining ingredients.  Simmer 1/2 hour.  Place serving in center of soft shell.  Top with chopped tomatoes and lettuce.  Roll.  Sprinkle grated cheese on top.  Microwave short time, just to melt cheese.
