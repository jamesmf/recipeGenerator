GREEN BEAN AND ONION SALAD


2  c. fresh green beans
1/4  c. onions, sliced
  salt
  pepper
  dash of Italian seasoning
2  Tbsp. salad oil
3/4  Tbsp. wine vinegar

      Cook  green  beans  until done, but still firm.  Drain. Add onions, salt, pepper, Italian seasoning, oil and vinegar. Toss. Serve at room temperature or chilled.
