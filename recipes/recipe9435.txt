CAVATINI


1  box spiral noodles
1  pkg. pepperoni
2  pkg. sausage
1/2  c. chopped green pepper
1/2  c. chopped onion
1 to 2 large  jars your favorite spaghetti sauce
1 small  jar mushrooms (optional)
1 to 2  pkg. sliced Mozzarella cheese

	Cook pasta according to directions. Cook sausage. When	half done, add green pepper and onion. When done, drain. Drain pasta. Put all ingredients in large pan except for cheese. Mix well. Pour 1/2 of mixture in large baking dish. Put a layer of Mozzarella cheese. Pour remaining mixture on top of cheese. Put remaining cheese on top. Bake at 350 degrees; until warm and cheese has melted.
