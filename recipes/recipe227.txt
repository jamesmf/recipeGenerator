BLOSSOMS


1/2  c. sugar
1/2  c. butter
1  egg
1 1/3  c. flour
1  tsp. baking soda
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  c. brown sugar
2  Tbsp. milk
1/2  c. peanut butter
1  tsp. vanilla extract
48  kisses

      Cream sugar and butter.  Add egg.  Combine dry ingredients. Mix all ingredients together except kisses.  Shape dough in small balls.  Roll in sugar.  Place on ungreased cookie sheet.  Bake at 375 degrees; for 12 to 15 minutes.  Immediately top with a kiss.  Makes about 36 cookies.
