WHOLE WHEAT POPOVERS


1  c. sifted whole wheat flour
1  c. low-fat milk
2  eggs
2  tsp. vegetable oil
1/2  tsp. salt

      Preheat oven to 425 degrees;.  Generously coat 6 large or 8 small metal or glass muffin molds with vegetable cooking spray.  In a food processor, blender, electric mixer or by hand, combine the ingredients.  Mix or stir until the batter is smooth.  Place the sprayed muffin molds in the oven to preheat for 2 minutes.  Pour the batter into the preheated cups, filling 2/3 full, and return to the oven.  Bake in top third of the oven for 30 to 35 minutes or until puffy and golden brown.  Serve immediately.  Makes 6.
