LOLLIPOPS


1  c. sugar
1/2  c. corn syrup
1/4  c. water
1/4  tsp. orange, lemon or peppermint extract
  food coloring (orange, yellow or red)

      You will need 12 wooden or lollipop sticks.
      Combine sugar, corn syrup and water in 8-cup glass measure. Use wet pastry brush to wash sugar crystals from sides of measure. Microwave on High 9 to 12 minutes or until mixture reaches hard crack stage (300 degrees;), stirring every 2 minutes.  Stir in desired extract and food coloring.  Pour over sticks arranged on greased foil or pour into lollipop molds.  Let stand about 1 hour or until hard.  Wrap in plastic wrap.  Store in a cool, dry place.
      To Make Lollipop Molds:  Cut 1 inch off the top of twelve wax-coated 9 ounce paper drinking cups; discard bottoms of cups. Grease insides of top portions of cups.  Punch hole in side of each mold; insert stick.  Place molds on buttered foil.  Fill and proceed as directed.
