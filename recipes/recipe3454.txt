GRAHAM CRACKER NUTS

Makes 24:
24  cracker halves
  nuts
1/2  c. sugar
2  sticks margarine

 
Makes 48:
1  c. sugar
3  sticks margarine

      Place cracker halves on a cookie sheet. Sprinkle nuts on top. Bring the sugar and margarine to a boil for 3 minutes. Pour over crackers and nuts. Place in oven for 5 minutes at 350 degrees;.
