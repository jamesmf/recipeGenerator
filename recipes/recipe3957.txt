PINEAPPLE CHEESE BALL


2  (8 oz.) cream cheese, softened
1 small  can crushed pineapple, drained
2  tsp. seasoned salt
2  c. chopped nuts
1/4  c. chopped green pepper
2  Tbsp. onion, chopped fine

      Mix all ingredients together, except 1 cup nuts.  Shape into ball.  Roll cheese ball in 1 cup of the nuts.  Refrigerate.
