TACO SOUP


1  lb. ground meat, browned
1  large onion
1  can kidney beans
1  can ranch style beans
1  can pinto beans
1  can navy beans
1  can whole kernel corn
2  cans Rotel tomatoes
1  pkg. taco seasoning mix
1  package Original Hidden Valley Ranch dressing mix
1 1/2  c. of water

      Do not drain juice from beans, corn, or tomatoes.  Combine browned ground meat, onion, kidney beans, ranch style beans, pinto beans, navy beans, and corn. Simmer 30 minutes. Add taco seasoning, Ranch dressing mix and water. Stir well and simmer until heated through.
