CRANBERRY SALAD


1  lb. cranberries
1 tall  can crushed pineapple
1  orange, peeled
1  cup nuts
1  1/4 cups sugar
2 small  boxes raspberry jello
1  cup hot water

      Put the first 2 ingredients in the blender to be chopped. Then add the other ingredients in a bowl.  In another bowl dissolve jello in hot water, then mix with other ingredients.
