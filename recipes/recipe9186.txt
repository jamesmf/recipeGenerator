MEAT LOAF


1 1/2  lb. hamburger
1  c. oatmeal
1  small onion
1/2  green pepper
1 1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper
1 (6 oz.)  can tomato paste
1 (8 oz.)  can tomato sauce
1  egg
3  Tbsp. brown sugar
2  Tbsp. mustard
2  tsp. worcestershire sauce
1/2  c. water
  ketchup, optional

      Mix hamburger, oatmeal, onion, green pepper, salt, pepper, tomato paste, tomato sauce and egg.  Mix well and mound into a baking dish.  Mix brown sugar, mustard, worcestershire sauce and water in a shaker; pour over meat.  Ketchup may be poured over the top if desired.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour and 15 minutes.
