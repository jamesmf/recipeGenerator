ORANGE JULIUS


1/2  c. frozen orange juice concentrate
1/2  c. milk
1/2  c. water
1/4  c. sugar
1/2  tsp. vanilla
4  or 5 ice cubes
1  egg (if desired)

      Combine all ingredients in a blender container; cover and blend until smooth, about 30 seconds.  Serve immediately.  (A quick breakfast on the run.)
