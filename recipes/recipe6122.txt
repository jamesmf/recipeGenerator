CORN AND PEA SALAD


1  can LeSueur small peas, drained
1 11  oz. can shoepeg corn, drained
1  c. chopped onion
1  c. chopped celery
1/2  c. green pepper, chopped
1 4  oz. jar pimiento
1/2  c. vinegar
1/4  c. oil
1/2  c. sugar

      In a pan, heat vinegar, oil and sugar until sugar melts. Pour this over vegetables.  Serve cold.
