CHICKEN AND RICE CASSEROLE


2  hard-cooked eggs, chopped
1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can cream of mushroom soup
1  small onion, chopped
1 1/2  c. diced, cooked chicken (no skin)
1  c. cooked rice
1/2  c. chopped celery
1/4  c. reduced calorie mayonnaise
1  Tbsp. lemon juice
1/4  c. soft bread crumbs
1  Tbsp. dry butter substitute

      Combine eggs, soup, onion, chicken, rice, celery, mayonnaise and lemon juice, stirring well.  Spoon mixture into nonstick 2-quart casserole.  Combine bread crumbs and butter substitute; sprinkle over top of casserole.  Bake at 350 degrees;, uncovered, for 40 to 45 minutes or until bubbly.
