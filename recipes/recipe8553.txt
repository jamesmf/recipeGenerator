BEEFARONI SKILLET DINNER


1  lb. ground Beef, browned and drained
1  med. Onion, chopped
1 15  oz. can Pork & Beans
2  c. elbow Macaroni uncooked
1/2  c. Barbeque Sauce
1/4  c. brown Sugar
1  c. Water

      Brown ground beef, drain.  Add chopped onion. Stir well.  Add remaining ingredients.  Stir well.  Bring to a boil.  Reduce heat, cover and simmer until macaroni is tender. Add more water if needed.
