CHICKEN CACCIATORE


1  chicken, cut up and skinned
1  medium onion, chopped
1  medium green pepper, chopped
1 (6 oz.)  can tomato juice
1 (6 oz.)  can spicy cocktail tomato juice
1/2  tsp. oregano
  salt and pepper to taste
  Worcestershire sauce to taste
  soy sauce to taste
  mushrooms (optional)

      Place chicken pieces in dish. Add other ingredients on top of chicken. Cover tightly with aluminum foil. Bake at 350 degrees; for 2 hours.
