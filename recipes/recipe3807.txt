SHRIMP MOLD SPREAD


2  pkg. unflavored gelatin
1/2  c. cold water
1  can tomato soup (condensed)
3 (3 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
1  c. mayonnaise
3/4  c. chopped celery
3/4  c. chopped green onions
  dash of Worcestershire
  dash of Tabasco
  salt and pepper to taste
2 small  cans shrimp, chopped

      Dissolve gelatin in water; add soup and bring to a boil. Cool and add cream cheese and remaining ingredients except shrimp. Chill until slightly thickened; add chopped shrimp and pour into mold. Chill for several hours. Serve as a spread on crackers.
       Use a fish mold and decorate with sliced olives for eyes and pimento for the mouth.
