RICE MEAT BALLS


1  c. Minute rice (uncooked)
1  lb. ground beef
1  egg, slightly beaten
2  tsp. onion, grated
2  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. marjoram
  dash of pepper
2 1/2  c. tomato juice
1/2  tsp. sugar

	Combine rice with beef, egg, onion, salt, marjoram, pepper and 1/2 cup tomato juice. Mix lightly. Shape mixture into 18 balls and place in skillet. Add sugar to remaining 2 cups of tomato juice. Pour juice over meat balls in skillet. Bring mixture to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer, covered, 15 minutes, basting occasionally. Makes 6 servings of 3 meat balls each.
