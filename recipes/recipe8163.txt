EASY STUFFED CABBAGE


1  lb. ground beef
1  medium cabbage, cut up (not fine)
2  cans tomato soup
1  small onion
1  c. rice (dry)
  Parmesan cheese

      Brown ground beef with onions. Layer cabbage on bottom of 9 x 13 Pyrex baking dish. Add ground meat mixed with dry rice on top of cabbage. Add soup with 1 can of water. Top with Parmesan cheese (tall thin can). Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour and 20 minutes.
