REUBEN CASSEROLE II


1  pkg. hash browns
1 (14 oz.)  can sauerkraut
2 (12 oz.)  cans corned beef
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. Swiss cheese, shredded or 8 slices
  Thousand Island

	In a 9 x 13-inch baking dish, layer hash browns, then sauerkraut and then corned beef and top with Swiss cheese.	Bake for 1 hour at 350 degrees;.  Cut into squares and top with Thousand Island or Russian dressing.  May add dressing while eating.
