MANGO SALAD


2  c. water, divided
2 (3 oz.)  pkg. lemon jello
1 (3 oz.)  pkg. orange jello
1/4  c. sugar
1 large  can mangos with juice
1  (8 oz.) cream cheese (low-fat)
  Cool Whip
  pecans

	Dissolve jello in 1 cup boiling water; add sugar. In a separate bowl, blend mango, juice and cream cheese. (Can use blender.)  Add the jello mixture and 1 cup cold water. Pour into mold or dish. Chill. When firm, top each serving with Cool Whip and pecans.
