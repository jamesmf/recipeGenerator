CHICKEN TORTILLA CASSEROLE


4  whole chicken breasts (whole chicken may be used)
1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can cream of mushroom soup
1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can cream of chicken soup
1  can chili con carne (no beans)
1  can green chili salsa
1/2  lb. Mozzarella cheese, grated
1/2  can milk
1  medium onion, grated
1 small  can pitted olives
12  corn tortillas, cut in 1-inch squares
1/2  lb. Cheddar cheese, grated

	Wrap chicken in foil and bake in oven at 350 degrees; for 1 hour. Bone chicken and break into pieces.  Mix Soups, chili con carne, green chili salsa, milk, onion and olives.	Grease casserole.  Put layers of tortillas, chicken, sauce and cheese, about 2 or 3 layers.  Bake at 350 degrees;, uncovered, for 1 hour.  Serves 10 to 12. May be made the day before.
