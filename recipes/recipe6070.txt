STRAWBERRY SALAD

Crust:
1  c. flour
1/2  c. melted butter
1/4  c. brown sugar
2/3  c. chopped nuts

      Mix and press in baking pan or dish; bake at 350 degrees; for 8 to 10 minutes.
Middle Layer:
8  oz. pkg. cream cheese
1  c. powdered sugar
1  tsp. vanilla flavoring
1 small  carton Cool Whip

      Mix and spread over cooled crust.
Top Layer:
2  pkg. frozen strawberries
2 small  pkg. strawberry jello
2  c. boiling water

      Dissolve jello in boiling water.  Add strawberries and cool slightly.  Pour over cream cheese mixture and chill for several hours before serving.
