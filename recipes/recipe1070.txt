APPLE BUTTER


2  medium tart apples
  artificial sweetener (Equal)
1/4  tsp. cinnamon
1/8  tsp. cloves
1 1/2  tsp. unflavored gelatin
2  c. water
6  tsp. sugar
1/8  tsp. allspice
1/4  c. water

      Peel, core and quarter apples.  Combine in blender with 2 cups water, sweetener and spices.  Blend until smooth.  Cook over low heat until thickened, about 45 minutes.  In blender, sprinkle gelatin over 1/4 cup water and add to boiling apples and puree. Store in refrigerator.
