"PINK STUFF" FRUIT SALAD


1  large container cottage cheese
1 small  box cherry Jell-O
1  small container Cool Whip
2  (16 oz.) pineapple chunks, drained
2 small  cans mandarin oranges, drained
2  c. red seedless grapes, cut in two
3/4  c. pecans, chopped
3/4  c. shredded cheese (Colby and Monterey Jack or Cheddar)

      Mix cottage cheese with dry Jell-O. Add Cool Whip and fruit. Stir. Add nuts and cheese. Stir. Refrigerate overnight before serving.
