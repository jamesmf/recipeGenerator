HAMBURG SOUP


1 to 2  lb. hamburg
  potatoes
  onions (medium), chopped
  flour
  eggs

      Boil hamburg with just enough water to cover.  When no longer pink, add diced potatoes and onions.  Cook, covered, on low heat until potatoes are tender. Mix flour and eggs; drop by spoonful into mixture.  Cover; cook on medium for 10 to 15 minutes.
