BLUEGILL PARMESAN


1/4  c. butter, melted
1/2  c. dry bread crumbs
1/3  c. grated Parmesan cheese
2  Tbsp. minced fresh parsley
1  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. paprika
1/4  tsp. oregano
1/4  tsp. basil
1/4  tsp. pepper
1  lb. bluegill fillets

      Place butter in a shallow dish. In another dish, combine bread crumbs, Parmesan cheese and seasonings. Dip fillets in butter, then coat with crumb mixture. Bake at 350 degrees; for 20 minutes, uncovered, or until fish flakes easily with a fork.
