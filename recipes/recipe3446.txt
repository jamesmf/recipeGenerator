TUNA MARBELS


1 (8 oz.)  can tuna, drained
1  Tbsp. minced onion
1  tsp. lemon juice
1/2  Tbsp. mustard
1  egg
1/2  c. oatmeal
  cracker or bread crumbs

      Combine tuna, onion, mustard, oatmeal, egg and lemon juice. Mix well. Roll into 1-inch balls. Roll in bread crumbs. Fry in deep fat until golden brown.
