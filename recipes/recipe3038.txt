CREAM CHEESE BALL


2 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
4  Tbsp. margarine
1  pkg. Good Seasons Italian mix
  nuts, ground

      Mix cream cheese, margarine and Italian mix together.  Roll into ball.  Roll ball in ground nuts.  To make a dip instead of a ball, add enough milk to the cheese, margarine and Italian mix to dipping consistency and omit nuts.
