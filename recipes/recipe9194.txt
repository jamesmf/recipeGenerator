PORCUPINE MEATBALLS


1  lb. ground beef
1/4  c. long grain rice, uncooked
1  egg, beaten
2  Tbsp. minced onion
1/2  tsp. salt
1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can tomato soup, undiluted and divided
1  tsp. worcestershire sauce
2  Tbsp. oil
1  c. water

      Combine beef, rice, egg, onion, salt, 1/4 c. tomato soup and worcestershire sauce; mix thoroughly.  Form into meatballs, using about 2 Tbsp. mixture in each one.  Brown meatballs in hot oil. Add remaining tomato soup and water.  Cover and let simmer about 40 minutes or until rice is done.  Yield:  About 2 dozen meatballs.
