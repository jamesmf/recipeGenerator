SWEET AND SOUR SMOKIES


2 (16 oz.)  pkg. miniature smoked sausage links
2 (21 oz.)  cans cherry pie filling
1 (20 oz.)  can pineapple chunks, drained
3  Tbsp. brown sugar

      Place sausages in a slow cooker. In a bowl, combine the pie filling, pineapple and brown sugar. Pour over sausages. Cover and cook on low for 4 hours. Yields 16 to 20 servings.
