HAWAIIAN PORK CHOPS


4  pork chops
1/2  c. flour
1  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper
4  slices of pineapple

      Trim excess fat from pork chops.  In a bowl, combine flour, salt, and pepper; roll chops in flour mixture to coat.  Arrange chops in a baking dish; bake at 375 degrees; for 30 minutes.  Lay a slice of pineapple on each chop; return to 350 degrees; oven for 15 minutes.
