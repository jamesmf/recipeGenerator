MACARONI SALAD


1 small  box macaroni
1  cucumber, chopped
1  green pepper, chopped
1  stalk celery, chopped
1  onion, chopped
3  boiled eggs
1/2  pt. mayonnaise
3  Tbsp. milk
1  tsp. mustard
2  tsp. vinegar
1  Tbsp. sugar

      Cook macaroni according to directions on the box; drain.  Add the cucumber, green pepper, celery, onion and eggs.  Stir.  Make a dressing using the mayonnaise, milk, mustard, vinegar and sugar. Pour over macaroni.  Stir well.  Chill.
