ITALIAN SAUSAGE BITES


1  pkg. Italian sausage (5 links)
1 (14 1/2 oz.)  can diced tomatoes (undrained)
1 (1.5 oz.)  pkg. dry spaghetti sauce mix
1/2  c. chopped fresh parsley
2  Tbsp. grated Parmesan cheese

      Cut each sausage link into 10 slices.  Remove casing and place in a nonstick skillet over medium heat.  Cook 4 minutes or until well browned, turning once.  Remove from skillet; drain and set aside.  In a saucepan, combine diced tomatoes, sauce mix, parsley and Parmesan cheese.  Simmer, uncovered, 15 minutes.  Add sausage, stirring to combine.  Place in a chafing dish or slow cooker.  Serve with wooden party picks. Yield: 10 to 15 servings.
