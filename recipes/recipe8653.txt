CRUSTY BEEF, CHEESE AND NOODLE CASSEROLE


1  Tbsp. vegetable oil
1  onion, chopped
2  lb. ground beef
4 (10 1/4 oz.)  cans meatless mushroom sauce
  salt and pepper to taste
1  lb. fine noodles, cooked and drained
1  lb. shredded sharp Cheddar cheese

	Preheat oven to 325 degrees;.  Heat oil. Brown meat and onions. Add mushroom sauce, salt and pepper. Heat thoroughly.  Arrange in a casserole dish a layer of noodles, sauce and cheese. Repeat layers. Bake for 1 hour. Serves 8 to 10.
