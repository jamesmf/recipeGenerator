POTATO CASSEROLE


1/3  lb. Velveeta cheese, cubed
3  Tbsp. sour cream
1/4  c. chopped onion
2  tsp. garlic salt
1  jar diced pimentos, drained
1  jar sliced mushrooms, drained
1/2  stick margarine, melted
7  potatoes

      Peel and wash potatoes.  Pat dry with paper towel.  Cube potatoes.  Toss in a large mixing bowl with other ingredients until well coated.  Bake at 300 degrees; to 325 degrees;, uncovered, in a large casserole dish for 1 1/2 hours.  Stir once halfway through.
