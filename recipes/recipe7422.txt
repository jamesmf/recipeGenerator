CELERY SHRIMP CASSEROLE


1  lb. peeled shrimp
1/3  c. chopped celery
1/3  c. chopped green pepper
1/3  c. chopped onion
3  Tbsp. margarine
1  can condensed cream of celery soup
1/3  c. sliced water chestnuts
1  hard-boiled egg, chopped
1  Tbsp. lemon juice
1/4  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
2/3  c. cracker crumbs
1/2  c. shredded Cheddar cheese

      In a 2-quart saucepan, saut celery, green pepper and onions in margarine for 10 minutes.  Stir in all the remaining ingredients, except for cheese, and spoon the mixture in a 1 1/2-quart casserole dish.  Bake in 350 degrees; oven for 15 minutes. Sprinkle cheese on top and put back in oven until melted.  Serves 4.
