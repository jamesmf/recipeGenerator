EASY CUCUMBER PICKLES


6  sprigs fresh dill weed
12  cloves garlic
6  hot green peppers (small)
  cucumbers
1  qt. vinegar
1  c. noniodized salt

      Using 6 pre-sterilized jars, place 1 sprig dill, 2 cloves garlic and 1 pepper in each jar.  Fill with sliced cucumbers. Combine 2 quarts water, salt and vinegar in pan.  Bring to a boil. Pour over cucumbers and seal.  Let stand 3 weeks before using.
