QUICK CHICKEN PARMESAN


4  (4 oz.) skinned boned chicken breast halves
1  egg, lightly beaten
1/2  c. Italian seasoned bread crumbs
1 1/2  tsp. margarine, melted
1 3/4  c. tomato and garlic pasta sauce
1/2  c. shredded part-skim Mozzarella
1  Tbsp. grated Parmesan
1/4  c. chopped fresh parsley
3  c. hot cooked pasta
  cooking spray

      Place chicken between 2 sheets heavy-duty plastic wrap; flatten to 1/4-inch thick with a rolling pin. Dip chicken in egg and then bread crumbs. Melt butter in a large nonstick skillet with cooking spray over medium heat. Add chicken and cook 4 minutes on each side or until browned. Spoon pasta sauce over chicken; cover, decrease heat and simmer, covered, 10 minutes. Sprinkle with cheeses and parsley; cover and simmer 5 minutes or until cheese is melted. Spoon 3/4 cup pasta on 4 plates; top each with a chicken breast and divide sauce evenly. Makes 4 servings.
      Cal-470, CHO-49.7g, Pro-41g, Fat-10.4g.
