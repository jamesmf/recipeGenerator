CATFISH STEW WITH VEGETABLES


3  lb. catfish (boneless)
3  qt. water
1/4  c. diced bacon
1/4  tsp. pepper
2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. cayenne pepper
1  c. chopped onion
4  c. chopped tomatoes
2  c. diced potatoes
2  c. baby lima beans
2  c. corn

	Put catfish in 3 quarts of water. Cook until fish is tender. Add remaining ingredients except corn. Simmer for 1 hour. Add corn and cook for 10 more minutes.
