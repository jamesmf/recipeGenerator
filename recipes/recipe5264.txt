CUCUMBER SALAD


1  cucumber
1/4  sliced red onions
2  tbs. milk
1  tsp. lemon juice
  salt

	Peel the cucumber and cut it across in two. Then slice them in very thin pieces. Put the sliced cucumber pieces in salt water, let it remain for a few minutes, then squeeze out all the water and put the cucumber into a dish; add the rest of ingredients and mix well together.
Cucumber-Tomato Salad (Alternate Method):
2  medium-sized cucumbers
3  ripe tomatoes (3/4 lb.)
1/2  c. chopped red onion
2  Tbsp. chopped fresh dill
2  Tbsp. red-wine vinegar
4  Tbsp. olive oil
1/2  tsp. ground cumin
  salt and freshly ground pepper, to taste

	Peel the cucumbers and split them in half.  Scoop out the seeds with a spoon.  Slice the cucumbers crosswise.  There should be about 3 cups.  Core the tomatoes and cut them into 1/2-inch cubes.  There should be about 3 cups.  Put the onion, dill, vinegar, oil, cumin, salt and pepper in a bowl.  Beat briskly with a wire whisk.  Add cucumbers and tomatoes.	Toss well and serve. Serves 4 to 6.
