DIET DIP
(Low Calorie, Low-Fat)  

1/2  c. plain yogurt
1/4  c. low-fat mayonnaise
1 1/2  tsp. dry Hidden Valley Ranch salad dressing mix
1  tsp. dry Good Seasons Italian salad dressing mix
1  tsp. lemon juice

      Blend together.  Use as a dip for vegetables, or can use as a salad dressing.  Recipe can be doubled.  2 Tbsp. = 50 calories.
