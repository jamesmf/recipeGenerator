PIZZA SALAD


1  lb. spiral pasta
3/4  c. salad oil
1/2  c. wine vinegar
  2-3 bunches gr. onions
2  tsp. oregano
2/3  c. grated Parmesan
  2-3 lg. tomatoes, cut up
3/4  lb. cheddar cheese, cubed
1/3  lb. pepperoni cut in pcs.

      Cook pasta and run under cold water.  Drain & mix with remaining ingredients.
