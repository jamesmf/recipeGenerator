CHEESESTEAK POCKETS


1  tbsp vegetable oil
1  medium onion, sliced
14  oz frozen beef or chicken sandwich steaks, cut into 8 pieces
1  can Campbell's Condensed Cheddar Cheese soup
1  jar mushrooms, sliced and drained
4  pita breads, cut in half

      In medium skillet over medium high heat, heat oil.  Add onion and cook until tender.  Add sandwich steaks and cook 5 minutes or until browned, stirring often.  Drain off fat.  Add soup and mushrooms.  Heat to a boil.  Reduce heat to low and heat through. Spoon meat mixture into pita halves.
