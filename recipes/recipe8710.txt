FISH WITH PECAN BUTTER SAUCE


  fish (trout, redfish or other firm fleshed fish)
  Tony Chachere's Creole seasoning
  flour
  oil
  butter

	In a large frying pan, place equal amounts of oil and butter enough to cover the bottom of the pan.  Season the fish in the Creole seasoning.
	In a Baggie, add flour and additional seasoning and dredge each fillet with the mixture.  Remove off excess and place fillet in hot oil (350 degrees;).  Cook about 2 to 3 minutes per side.  Drain on paper towels.  Place hot fillets on serving plate.  Top each fillet with 1 to 2 tablespoons of Pecan Butter Sauce and 1 tablespoon of chopped pecans.
Pecan Butter Sauce:
1  stick butter
1/2  c. pecans (dry roasted)
2  Tbsp. onion, chopped
1  tsp. lemon or lime juice
1/2  tsp. Tabasco sauce
1  clove garlic, chopped

	Put all ingredients into a food processor and puree until well mixed, creamy and smooth. Serve with hot fish fillets.
	 Pecan Butter Sauce can be stored in airtight container in refrigerator for 3 to 4 weeks.
