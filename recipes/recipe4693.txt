CAULIFLOWER AND BROCCOLI SALAD


1  head cauliflower
1  head broccoli
1/2  lb. bacon
1  c. grated sharp cheese

 
Dressing:
1  c. mayonnaise
1/2  c. sugar
2 1/2  Tbsp. vinegar

      Wash and break cauliflower and broccoli into small pieces. Fry bacon.  Drain; crumble.  Add to cauliflower and broccoli.  Add cheese.  Mix ingredients for dressing.  Add, then toss lightly.
