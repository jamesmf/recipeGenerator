SPAGHETTI SAUCE


2  lbs. Ground Chuck
2  tbsp. Oil
14  oz. can Stewed Tomatoes, cut up
6  oz. Tomato Juice
1  lb. jar Prego spaghetti sauce
1  envelope Onion Soup mix
1/2  c. Grape Jelly

      Brown meat in oil until pink color disappears.  Crumble with fork.  Stir in tomatoes, tomato juice, prego, soup mix and grape jelly.  Cook, stirring often for 15-20 minutes until jelly melts and sauce is hot.
