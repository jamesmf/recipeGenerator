VEGGIE BARS


2  cans crescent rolls
8  oz. cream cheese, softened
1/4  c. sour cream
1/2  c. Miracle Whip
1  pkg. Ranch Style dressing dry mix
1/2  c. green bell pepper, finely chopped
1/2  c. broccoli, finely chopped
1/2  c. cauliflower, finely chopped
1/2  c. onion, finely chopped
1/2  c. carrots, grated
1/2  c. celery, finely chopped
2  Roma tomatoes, drained, seeded and chopped
3/4  c. Cheddar cheese, grated

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees;. Cover bottom of large cookie sheet with crescent rolls, patting to fit. Prick with fork. Bake 10 to 12 minutes. Cool.
      Cream dry dressing mix, cream cheese, Miracle Whip and sour cream. Spread on cooled crust. Combine vegetables and spread over cheese mixture. Top with grated cheese. Cover and refrigerate for several hours. Cut into small squares.
