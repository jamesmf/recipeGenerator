TACO SOUP


2  lb. ground beef
1  pkg. taco seasoning mix
1  pkg. Ranch dressing mix (dry)
1  jar picante sauce
1  can chilli beans
1 or 2  cans white Shoepeg corn (do not drain)
1  can Ro-Tel (do not drain)
1  qt. tomato juice or 2 cans diced tomatoes

      Serve with tortilla chips and grated cheese. Makes a large pot of soup.
