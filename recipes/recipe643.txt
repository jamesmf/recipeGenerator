DIABETIC RAISIN BARS


2  c. water
2  c. raisins
1  c. unsweetened applesauce
2  eggs
  Sweet 'N Low or sweetener equal to 1 c. sugar
1/2  c. oil
1  tsp. baking soda
1  tsp. cinnamon
1/2  tsp. nutmeg or allspice
2  c. flour
1  tsp. vanilla
  pinch of salt

      Boil water and raisins until water is gone; let cool.  Add applesauce, eggs, sweetener and oil; mix together.  Blend in the rest of the ingredients.  Spoon into greased 11 x 17-inch pan and bake at 350 degrees; for 35 minutes.  Bake longer if pan is smaller.  When cool, cut in bars.
