CORN CHOWDER


2  cans (14 1/2 oz.) creamed corn
3  cans (15 oz.) sliced potatoes (you can use red potatoes, chopped)
1 (12 oz.)  pkg. bacon
1  yellow onion
  salt and pepper to taste
1 small  pkg. frozen corn

      Chop bacon into small pieces and chop onion. Saut√© together until lightly brown. Drain potatoes. Add corn and creamed corn to bacon and onion. You can add milk and/or water to create density you want and simmer, stirring occasionally for 30 minutes.
