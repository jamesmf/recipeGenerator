BROCCOLI CASSEROLE


1 1/2  c. fresh broccoli, chopped fine
1  can cream of celery soup
1  stick butter, melted
1  c. Cheddar cheese, grated
2  eggs
2  Tbsp. onion, chopped
1  c. mayonnaise
  salt and pepper to taste
1  stack butter crackers, crushed

      Cook broccoli with small amount of water until done. Drain well. Mix with other ingredients, except crackers. Pour mixture into a greased baking dish. Sprinkle crackers on the top. Bake for 45 minutes at 350 degrees;.
