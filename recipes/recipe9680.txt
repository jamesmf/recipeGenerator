BREAKFAST BURRITO


1  Tbsp. light margarine
2  c. frozen O'Brien potatoes, thawed
3/4  tsp. chili powder
1  c. egg substitute
1/4  c. salsa
1/4  c. chopped green onions
4  (7-inch) fat-free flour tortillas
1/4  c. light or fat-free sour cream (optional)

      Melt margarine in large nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add potatoes and chili powder. Cook potatoes until tender, stirring occasionally.
      Mix together egg substitute, salsa and green onions. Add to skillet and cook until eggs are set, stirring occasionally. Heat tortillas according to package directions. Spoon mixture down center of tortilla. Top with sour cream if desired. Roll up in burrito fashion.
      These can be made ahead and put into freezer for up to 1 month. Omit sour cream. Do not heat tortillas. Spoon cooled mixture into tortillas, roll up and wrap in plastic wrap and place in a freezer bag.
      Serving Size:  1 burrito.  Calories:  232, Pro:  13gm, Fat: 4gm, CHO:  35gm.
