POLLY'S CHICKEN DELIGHT


4  chicken breasts, boneless
2  c. brown rice (cooked)
10  oz. (1 can) soup, cream of chicken (Healthy Choice)
10  oz. (1 can) soup, cream of broccoli
16  oz. (1 pkg.) sour cream, nonfat
1  c. Cheddar cheese, shredded
11  oz. (1 can) corn, fiesta or Mexican
1  bell pepper, chopped (optional)
1  onion, chopped (optional)
1  sleeve Ritz crackers, crushed

      Boil chicken until tender; then cut into small pieces.
      In a large bowl, combine all ingredients (except Ritz crackers).  Stir until well blended.  Pour into a 9x13-inch casserole dish. Top with crushed Ritz crackers.
      Bake at 350 degrees; for 35 minutes.
