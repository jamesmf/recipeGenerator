ANTIPASTO NAPOLI


2  c. tomato wedges
2  c. mushrooms, sliced
1/4  c. summer sausage, cut in slices
1  c. zucchini slices
1 (8 1/2 oz.)  can artichoke hearts, drained and quartered (optional)
1  c. Italian dressing
2  qt. torn assorted greens
  Parmesan cheese, grated

      Combine  tomatoes,  mushrooms,  sausage,  zucchini  and artichokes.  Add dressing;  cover and marinate in refrigerator 2 hours or more.  Drain, reserving marinade.  Toss vegetables with greens.  Serve  with  remaining marinade.  Sprinkle with grated Parmesan.
