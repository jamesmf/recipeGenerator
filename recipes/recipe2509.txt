CHEESE WAFERS


1/2  lb. butter or margarine
1/2  lb. grated sharp cheese
2 scant  c. sifted all-purpose flour
  dash of red pepper
  dash of garlic salt
  dash of onion salt
  dash of Worcestershire sauce

      Let  butter and grated cheese stand at room temperature for about an hour before mixing with remainder of ingredients into  a stiff dough.  You may use a cookie press or make small balls  and flatten to form wafers.  Bake for 10 to 12 minutes at 375 degrees;.
