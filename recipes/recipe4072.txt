"CHEX PARTY MIX"


1/4  c. margarine
5  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1 1/4  tsp. seasoned salt
1/4  tsp. garlic powder
2 2/3  c. Corn Chex cereal
2 2/3  c. Rice Chex cereal
2 2/3  c. Wheat Chex cereal
1  c. mixed nuts
1  c. pretzels

	Melt margarine in open roasting pan in preheated 250 degrees; oven. Stir in seasonings. Gradually add cereals, nuts and pretzels; stir to coat evenly. Bake 1 hour, stirring every 15 minutes. Spread on absorbent paper to cool. Store in large zipper bag.
