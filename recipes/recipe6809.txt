STEAK OVER RICE


1/4  c. butter
1  can mushrooms, drained
1  onion
1  round steak, cut into bite size pieces
2  Tbsp. flour
3  to 4 beef bouillon cubes
1  c. water
1  Tbsp. mustard
2  Tbsp. brown sugar
1 to 2  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
  salt and pepper to taste

      Brown meat, onions and mushrooms in butter.  Stir in flour. Add 1 cup water, bouillon cubes, mustard, brown sugar and Worcestershire sauce.  Cook slowly 1 to 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.  Serve over rice.
