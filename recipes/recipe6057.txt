BRIDESMAID SALAD


1 large  can crushed pineapple
1 large  can Queen Anne cherries, pitted
1  pt. mayonnaise
  dash of cayenne pepper
1  lb. marshmallows, quartered
1  lb. yellow cheese, grated
1  pt. whipping cream, whipped
  pinch of salt
4  c. hot water
3  pkg. lime jello

      Dissolve jello in hot water; add undrained fruit, marshmallows and cheese; let cool.  Add mayonnaise and whipping cream.  Let stand in refrigerator overnight.  Makes 50 two inch squares.
