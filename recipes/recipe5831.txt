CHRISTY'S TEXAS SALSA


1 large  can diced tomatoes
1  can Ro-Tel tomatoes (mild or hot, depending on your taste)
1/4  c. chopped onion
1  Tbsp. cumin
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. sugar
1 1/2  Tbsp. vinegar
1/4  c. or more fresh cilantro
2 garlic  cloves or 1 tsp. garlic powder
1  jalapeno (optional)

      Throw everything into a blender and pulse a few times. Put in jars and get your favorite chips! Enjoy! Will keep in your refrigerator for 2 to 3 weeks.
