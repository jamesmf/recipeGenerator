LEMON SQUARES


1  c. flour
1/2  c. margarine
1/4  c. powdered sugar
2  eggs
1  c. granulated sugar
1/2  tsp. baking powder
1/4  tsp. salt
2  Tbsp. lemon juice

      Heat oven to 350 degrees;.  Blend flour, butter and powdered sugar. Press evenly into an 8 x 8 x 2-inch pan.  Bake for 20 minutes. While crust is baking, beat rest of ingredients together.  Pour over crust and bake for 20 to 25 minutes more.  Do not overbake.
