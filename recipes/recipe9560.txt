HERBED CHICKEN


6  half chicken breasts
1/4  c. soft butter
1  Tbsp. paprika
1  tsp. thyme
1  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper
1  large carrot, sliced
2  slices bacon, diced
1/2  c. consomme

      Wash and dry chicken.  Mix butter and seasonings; spread on chicken pieces.  Put in casserole.  Add remaining ingredients. Cover.
       Bake in 400 degrees; oven until chicken and carrots are tender about 30 minutes if using boned chicken.
