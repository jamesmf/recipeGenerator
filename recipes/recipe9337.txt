JAMAICAN JERK CHICKEN


1  Tbsp. thyme
1 1/2  tsp. sage
3/4  tsp. nutmeg
2  Tbsp. garlic powder
1  Tbsp. allspice
3/4  tsp. cinnamon
1 1/2  tsp. black pepper
1 1/2  tsp. cayenne
2  Tbsp. salt
1  Tbsp. sugar
1  c. white onions, chopped
1  to 2 jalapeno peppers, chopped
3  green onions
1/4  c. olive oil
1/4  c. soy sauce
1/2  c. orange juice
1/4  c. white vinegar
  juice of one lime
  chicken, cut up

      Mix all together.  Marinade chicken in the above for at least one hour.  Cook in wok.
