HASH BROWN POTATO PIE


  five large eggs
1/2  c. milk
3 to 4  c. Ore-Ida Country Style hash browns, thawed
1/3  c. onion
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. hot pepper sauce
1 1/2  c. shredded sharp cheddar cheese
4  slices of bacon, cooked crisp and crumbled

      Heat oven to 350 degrees. Beat together eggs and milk in a medium bowl; stir in hash browns, onion, salt and hot pepper sauce. Stir in 1 c. of the cheese and 1/2 of the bacon. Pour into greased 9-inch pie plate. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until center is set. Sprinkle remaining bacon and cheese  over top of pie and continue baking for 3 to 4 minutes or until cheese is melted. This dish can be refrigerated over night and baked the following morning.
