CHICKEN VEGETABLE CASSEROLE


1  chicken, cooked and chunked
1  pkg. frozen mixed vegetables
2  cans mushroom soup
1  can milk
2  pkg. Stove Top stuffing
1  large onion, diced
1  c. celery, diced
1  stick margarine
1 1/2  c. water

      Simmer celery and onion in margarine until tender.  Add water and mix well.  Add Stove Top dressing and mix.  Pat in 9 x 13 x 2 pan.  Cook vegetables until tender.  Drain and layer onto dressing.  Add chicken.  Mix mushroom soup and milk (can use soup can to measure).  Pour over casserole.  Sprinkle with parsley. Bake at 350 degrees; for 35 minutes.  Cool 5 minutes.
