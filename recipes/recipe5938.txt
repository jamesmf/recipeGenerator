7 LAYER SALAD


1  head lettuce
1 large  can small garden peas
1  chopped onion
1  chopped green pepper
2  pkg. Hidden Valley dressing mix
2  c. grated cheese
1/2  c. bacon bits

      In large flat dish, cut up lettuce. Sprinkle garden peas over cut up lettuce, then sprinkle onions, then pepper over peas. Make up Hidden Valley dressing mix according to directions. Pour over salad in bowl. Now sprinkle cheese over dish, then sprinkle bacon bits over top of salad.
