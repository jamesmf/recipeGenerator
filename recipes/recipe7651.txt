EASY SPAGHETTI


2  lb. ground beef
1  medium onion, chopped
2  qt. tomato juice
1  qt. tomato paste or puree
1  tsp. ground oregano
1/2  tsp. garlic powder
16  oz. cooked spaghetti
  grated Parmesan cheese

      Cook ground beef and onion in  a  large  skillet  until meat is browned, stirring to crumble  meat;  drain well.  Stir in tomato  juice,  tomato paste  and  seasonings.  Cook  over medium heat stirring occasionally, about  10 minutes.  Stir in spaghetti and simmer 10 more  minutes.  Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
