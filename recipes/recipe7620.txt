ZESTY BEEF ENCHILADAS
(With Variations)  

1  lb. ground beef
1  jar (17 1/2 oz.) Pace Enchilada Sauce, divided
2  c. (8 oz.) shredded cheddar cheese or 4 cheese Mexican blend, divided
10  to 12 corn or flour tortillas (6 inches)

      In a skillet, cook beef until no longer pink; drain.  Stir in 1/2 c. sauce and 1 c. cheese.  Warm tortillas according to package directions.  Spread 1/2 c. sauce in a 13 x 9 x 2 inch baking dish. Spoon about 2 tablespoons meat mixture down the center of each tortilla.  Roll up and place seam-side down in baking dish.  Top with remaining sauce and cheese.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 20 minutes or until cheese melts.  Yield: 10-12 enchiladas.
Variations:

      Beefy Corn and Chile Enchiladas:  Use 1/2 lb. ground beef. After browning ground beef, add 1 cup whole kernel corn and 1/2 c. drained, canned green chilies.  Prepare as above.
      Cheesy Chili Enchiladas:  Omit ground beef and increase cheese to 3 cups.  Mix 2 c. of the cheese and 1 can (4 oz.) drained, chopped green chilies for filling.  Prepare as above.
      Seafood Enchiladas:  Omit ground beef.  Use 2 c. chopped cooked shrimp.  Prepare as above. Garnish with fresh cilantro.
      Beefy Skillet Enchiladas:  Use 8 corn tortillas (6 inches) and cut into 1 inch squares.  After browning ground beef, add enchilada sauce, tortillas and 1 c. cheese. Bring to a boil. Cover and cook over low heat 5 minutes or until hot.  Top with remaining cheese.
