CHICKEN CREPES


1/4  c. Celery, thinly sliced
1/4  c. Onion, chopped
1  tbsp. Butter or Margarine
1  can cream of chicken Soup
2  c. Chicken, cooked and chopped
1/4  c. Milk
8  Crepes

      In a saucepan, cook celery and onion with seasoning in butter until tender.  Add 1/2 c. soup and chicken.  Heat, stirring often. Spoon onto crepes. Roll up.  Keep warm.  Add remaining soup and milk to saucepan.  Heat.  Stir often.  Pepper to taste.  Place crepes on a serving dish and spoon sauce over them.
      This is one of my favorite!
