OVEN FRENCH TOAST


1  pat butter
4  eggs
1/2  c. milk
1/2  tsp. vanilla flavoring
8  slices bread

      Set oven at 300 degrees;.  Lightly grease a cookie sheet with sides with butter.  Mix eggs, milk and vanilla flavoring together with a fork.  Using a fork, dip both sides of the bread into the mixture and place on the cookie sheet.  Pour any remaining mixture over the bread.  Bake in the oven 10 minutes.  Remove from the oven.  Turn each piece over with a spatula.  Bake for 5 more minutes.  Serve with maple syrup, cinnamon, jam or brown sugar.
