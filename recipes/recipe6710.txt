CORNBREAD SKILLET CASSEROLE


2  eggs, slightly beaten
1  c. yellow corn meal
2  tsp. baking soda
1  tsp. salt
1 (17 oz.)  can cream-style corn
1  c. milk
1  lb. ground beef
1/4  c. vegetable oil
2  c. shredded Cheddar cheese
1  large onion, chopped
2  to 4 jalapeno peppers, finely chopped

      Combine first 6 ingredients in a bowl.  Set aside.  Cook ground beef until browned.  Drain well.  Set aside.  Pour half of corn meal mixture into a greased 10 1/2-inch cast-iron skillet. Sprinkle evenly with ground beef.  Top with cheese, onions and peppers.  Pour remaining batter over top.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 45 to 50 minutes.  Let stand 5 minutes before serving.  Serves 5 to 6.
      We serve with taco sauce and combo salad.
