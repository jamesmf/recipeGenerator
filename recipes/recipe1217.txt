SMACKEROONS


3  egg whites
1/4  tsp. cream of tartar
1/4  tsp. maple or almond flavoring
1/4  c. shredded coconut
2  tsp. sweetener
3  c. Rice Krispies

      Beat egg whites until foamy.  Add cream of tartar.  Continue beating until stiff but not dry.  Add sweetener and flavoring. Beat until well blended.  Fold in cereal and coconut.  Drop by teaspoon on a lightly greased cookie sheet.  Bake 12 to 15 minutes at 350 degrees;.
      Makes 2 dozen cookies, 15 calories each.
