MEAT LOAF WELLINGTON


1 (10 1/2 oz.)  can beef gravy, divided
1 1/2  c. cubed, day-old bread
1/4  c. chopped onion
1  egg
1  tsp. salt
2  lb. ground beef
1  (8 oz.) tube refrigerated crescent rolls

      In a bowl, combine 1/4 cup gravy, bread cubes, onion, egg and salt. Crumble beef over mixture and mix well. Press into a greased 9 x 5 x 3-inch loaf pan. Bake, uncovered, at 375 degrees; for 1 hour or until meat is no longer pink and a meat thermometer reads 160 degrees;. Remove loaf from pan; drain on paper towels. Place on a greased 13 x 9 x 2-inch baking pan.
      Unroll crescent roll dough; seal perforations. Cover the top and sides of meat loaf with dough; trim excess. Bake 10 to 15 minutes longer or until pastry is golden brown. Heat the remaining gravy; serve with meat loaf. Yield: 6 to 8 servings.
