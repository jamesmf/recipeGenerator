CHUTNEY-PEANUT SPREAD


8  oz. cream cheese, softened
1 to 2  tsp. curry powder
8  oz. chutney
1/3 to 1/2  c. green onion, chopped
1/2  c. shredded coconut
1  c. honey roasted peanuts

      Mix curry powder into softened cream cheese. Spread onto bottom of low-sided serving platter or dish. Cover with layer of minced chutney.  Next, put a layer of chopped green onion. Sprinkle coconut next, then finally the layer of honey roasted peanuts.  Let the guests spread their own on a sturdy cracker.
