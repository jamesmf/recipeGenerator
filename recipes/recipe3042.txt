FIFTH SUNDAY HAM ROLL-UPS


1 (8 oz.)  can crescent rolls
4 thin  slices ham (4 x 7-inch)
4  tsp. prepared mustard
4  oz. shredded Swiss cheese
2  Tbsp. sesame seed

      Heat oven to 375 degrees;.  Unroll dough into 4 long rectangles. Firmly press perforations to seal.  Place ham slices on rectangles.  Spread ham slices with mustard.  Sprinkle with cheese.  Starting at shortest side, roll up each rectangle.  Press edges to seal.  Coat rolls with sesame seed.  Cut each of the 4 rolls into 5 slices, forming 20 slices.  Place cut side down on ungreased cookie sheet.  Bake at 375 degrees; for 15 to 20 minutes or until golden brown.  Immediately remove from cookie sheet.  Serve warm.  Makes 20 appetizers.
