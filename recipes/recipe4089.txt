1/2 PENNY SNACKS


1  stick soft butter
1/2  lb. sharp Cheddar cheese, shredded
1/2  pkg. Lipton onion soup mix
1/2  tsp. salt
1  c. flour

	Combine butter and cheese. Add flour. Add salt. Add soup mix. Make into a roll and chill. Slice 1/4-inch thick and bake on a cookie sheet for 10 to 15 minutes at 375 degrees;.
