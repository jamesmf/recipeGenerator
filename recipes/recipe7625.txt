BARBECUED HAMBURGER


2  lb. hamburger
1  onion
1/2  c. catsup
2  Tbsp. brown sugar
2  Tbsp. vinegar
2  tsp. prepared mustard
1  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  tsp. salt

      Cook  hamburger  and onion in pan or skillet.  Add rest of ingredients  and  simmer  15 to 20 minutes.  Serve on warm buns.
