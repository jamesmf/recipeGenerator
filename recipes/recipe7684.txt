POUR PIZZA


1  lb. hamburger
1  onion, chopped
1  c. flour
1  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper
1/8  tsp. oregano
2  eggs
2/3  c. milk
1 (15 oz.)  can pizza sauce (pt.)
1 1/2 to 2  c. grated cheese

      Preheat  oven  to  400 degrees;.  Brown  hamburger  and  onion. Season with salt and pepper.  Set aside.  Combine flour, salt, pepper, oregano, eggs and milk, making  a soft batter.  Grease a 9 x 13-inch pan.  Sprinkle with  cornmeal.  Pour  batter  in pan. Sprinkle  meat over batter.  Bake  15  to  20  minutes. Spread pizza sauce and cheese over meat.  Bake  15  more  minutes. Yields 1 (9 x 13-inch) pan.
