BACON WRAPPED SHRIMP


  jumbo shrimp, peeled and deveined
1/2  slices bacon
  barbecue sauce

      Take jumbo shrimp; wrap 1/2 slice bacon around each one. Secure with toothpicks or put on skewers; cook on barbecue grill until bacon is crisp, approximately 1 1/2 hours.  Brush with barbecue sauce.
