ORIENTAL SALAD


1  lb. coleslaw mix
1/2  c. sunflower seeds
1  c. toasted almonds
1  pkg. Ramen noodles (beef)
1/2  c. vinegar
1/2  c. oil
1/2  c. sugar

      Toss slaw, seeds, almonds and crushed noodles.  Mix vinegar, oil, sugar and beef flavoring.  Pour dressing on at last minute. Not good soggy or leftover.
