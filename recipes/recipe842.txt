ORIENTAL CHICKEN CASSEROLE


2  Tbsp. margarine
2  Tbsp. flour
1 1/2  c. milk
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. Cheddar cheese
1  tsp. soy sauce
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
2  c. cooked rice
3  c. diced cooked chicken
1 small  can water chestnuts, sliced
1 (3 oz.)  can sliced mushrooms
1  c. sliced celery
1 (3 oz.)  can chow mein noodles

      Make cream sauce with margarine, flour and milk.  Add cheese, soy sauce, salt and pepper; stir until cheese melts.  Combine rice, chicken, water chestnuts, mushrooms and celery.  Place alternate layers of chicken-rice mixture and sauce mixture in 1 1/2-quart casserole.  Arrange noodles around edge of casserole and bake in 350 degrees; oven for 25 to 30 minutes.
