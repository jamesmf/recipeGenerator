ORIENTAL COLESLAW


  head of cabbage
8  scallions (use everything)
1/2  c. slivered almonds
1/2  c. sesame seeds
  Ramen noodles (do not use seasoning)

 
Dressing:
1/2  c. cider vinegar
1/2  c. sugar
1  tsp. pepper
1  tsp. salt
1/2  c. oil

      Chop cabbage and scallions; mix together.  Toast almonds and sesame seeds for 3 or 4 minutes.  Add to cabbage mixture.  Beat Ramen noodles with a hammer or rolling pin to crush.  Add to cabbage mixture.  Mix together all ingredients for the dressing and toss all together.
