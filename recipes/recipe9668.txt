STUFFED BASS


  4-5 lb. bass
4  Tbsp. melted butter
  Salt & pepper
1  can mushroom soup + 1/2 can water
1  medium onion, sliced
2  Tbsp. grated cheese

      Brush fish with melted butter; sprinkle with salt & pepper to taste. Spread stuffing over fish. Top with last 3 ingredients in order given.
      Bake 40 minutes at 250-350 degrees on lower rack of oven.
Stuffing:
1/4  c. chopped celery
1/4  c. chopped onion
2  Tbsp. butter
1 1/2  c. cooked rice
2  Tbsp. chopped parsley

      Brown celery & onion in butter. Combine rice, parsley and celery/onion mixture; blend well.
