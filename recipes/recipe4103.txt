TACO DIP


1  pkg. taco seasoning mix
2 (No. 303)  cans refried beans
1  small sour cream
1  c. grated sharp Cheddar cheese or Mexican 3 cheese

	Mix refried beans with taco seasoning. Put in baking dish; smooth sour cream on top and top with grated cheese. Heat in oven at 350 degrees; until cheese has melted. Serve hot or keep on warming tray and serve with taco chips.
