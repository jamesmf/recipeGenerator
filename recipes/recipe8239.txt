DEEP-DISH TACO SQUARES


1  lb. lean ground beef
1  c. Cheddar cheese, shredded
1/3  c. mayonnaise
1/3  c. sour cream
1  Tbsp. onion, finely chopped
1  c. biscuit baking mix
1/4  c. cold water
1  small tomato, sliced

	In a nonstick skillet, cook beef until no longer pink; drain. Remove from heat. Stir in cheese, mayonnaise, sour cream and onion. In a bowl, combine biscuit mix and water. Spread into an 8-inch square baking dish coated with nonstick cooking spray. Top with beef mixture and tomato. Bake, uncovered, at 375 degrees; for 20 minutes or until bottom crust is golden brown.
