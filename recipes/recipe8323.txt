CHICKEN PAPRIKA III


1  onion, chopped
4  Tbsp. shortening
1  Tbsp. paprika
1  tsp. black pepper
2  tsp. salt
4 to 5  lb. chicken pieces
1 1/2  c. water
1/2  pt. sour cream

 
Dumplings:
3  eggs, beaten
3  c. flour
1  tsp. salt
1/2  c. water

	Brown onion in shortening. Add seasonings and chicken; brown 10 minutes. Add water; cover and let simmer slowly until tender. Remove chicken. Add sour cream to drippings in pan and mix well. Add cooked dumplings. Arrange chicken on top. Heat through and serve. Serves 4 to 6.
	Dumplings: Mix all ingredients and beat with spoon. Drop batter by teaspoonful into boiling salted water. Drain well and add to Chicken Paprika.
