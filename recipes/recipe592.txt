LOW CHOLESTEROL ROCK CORNISH HENS


2  Rock Cornish hens
1/4  c. chopped green onions
1/4  c. dry vermouth
2  Tbsp. lemon juice
1  Tbsp. soy sauce
  paprika to taste
  fresh ground pepper to taste

      Wash hens and pat dry.  Split hens lengthwise.  Sprinkle green onions in a 12 x 8 x 2-inch baking dish, coated with vegetable cooking spray.  Place hens over onions.  Combine vermouth, lemon juice and soy sauce; pour over hens, then sprinkle lightly with paprika and pepper.  Bake at 450 degrees; for 15 minutes.  Reduce heat to 350 degrees; and bake an additional 25 minutes or until done.
