SANGRIA ROJA
(Sangria with Red Burgundy)  

1/2  gal. Burgundy wine
1  c. lemon juice
1  c. orange juice
1  orange, thinly sliced
1  lemon, thinly sliced
3  pineapple slices, cut into spears
1  qt. club soda
1/4  c. sugar or to taste

      Combine all the ingredients and stir to blend.  Add ice cubes and serve.  Yields about 1 gallon.
      Note:  If you prepare this earlier in the day and store it in the refrigerator, add the club soda at serving time.
