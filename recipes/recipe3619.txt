CHRISTMAS MIX


3  c. Corn Chex cereal
3  c. Rice Chex cereal
3  c. Cheerios (plain)
1  (12 oz.) bag red and green M&M's
1 (12 oz.)  jar or 2 1/2 c. salted peanuts
3  c. thin pretzel sticks, broken in half
1 to 1 1/2  lb. white chocolate

	Mix all ingredients except chocolate. Melt white chocolate and pour over mixed ingredients. Mix all together, then spread on wax paper or cookie sheet. Once completely cooled, break into little clusters.
