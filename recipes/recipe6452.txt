VEGETABLE PASTA SALAD


12  oz. spiral pasta, cooked and drained
6  green onions, thinly sliced
1  medium zucchini, thinly sliced
2  cups frozen broccoli and cauliflower, thawed and drained
1  1/2 cups thinly sliced carrots, parboiled
1  cup thinly sliced celery
1  can (2 1/4 oz.) sliced ripe olives, drained
1/2  cup frozen peas, thawed
1  jar (6 oz.) marinated artichoke hearts, drained and quartered (optional)

 
Dressing:
1/2  cup fat free mayonnaise
1/2  cup fat free Italian salad dressing
1/2  cup nonfat sour cream
1  Tbsp. prepared mustard
1/2  tsp. Italian seasoning

      In a large bowl, combine pasta, onions, zucchini, broccoli, and cauliflower, carrots, celery, olives, peas, and artichoke hearts. In a small bowl, combine dressing ingredients; mix well.  Pour over salad and toss.  Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.  Yield: 18 servings.  Serving size:  1/2 cup.
      Calories:  111.
