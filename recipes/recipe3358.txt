ARTICHOKE-CHEESE DIP


1  jar (6 1/2-oz.) marinated artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
1  c. mayonnaise
1  c. parmesan or romano cheese, grated
3 garlic cloves, minced or 1  tsp. garlic powder
1  loaf fresh French bread, cut into large cubes or thin slices

      Blend all ingredients except French bread together in small baking dish and bake at 350 degrees; for about 30 minutes or until hot and bubbly.  Serve with the French bread for dipping.
