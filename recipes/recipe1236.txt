DIVINITY


4  egg whites
8  Tbsp. nonfat dry milk
1  tsp. Sweet 'N Low
1  tsp. vanilla
1  tsp. almond extract

      Beat egg whites until stiff.  Add powdered milk; mix well.  Add extracts and Sweet 'N Low.  Spoon drop onto cookie sheet.  Bake at 275 degrees; for 45 minutes.  Remove from sheet and dust with cinnamon. Yield:  about 2 dozen (32 calories each).
