PARMESAN CHICKEN


4  to 6 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1  jar Ragu Traditional sauce
  parmesan cheese
  shredded mozzarella cheese

      Lightly spray casserole dish; place chicken breasts flat on bottom of dish (don't stack chicken).  Pour Ragu sauce over chicken; liberally sprinkle with parmesan cheese.  Cover with foil and bake at 350 degrees; for about 45 minutes.  Remove from oven and remove foil; very liberally sprinkle with mozzarella cheese. Return to oven for about 10 minutes to melt the cheese.
