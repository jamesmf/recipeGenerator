BEEF STROGANOFF


1  lb. beef stew or round steak
1  large onion, chopped
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1 (8 oz.)  carton sour cream
1 small  pkg. egg noodles

      Salt, pepper, & flour meat.  Brown in small amount of shortening.  Add onions & cook until soft; then add 3 or 4 cups water & cook until tender.  Add soup & cook 5 minutes longer.  Add sour cream.  Cook noodles & drain.  Then pour into meat mixture.
