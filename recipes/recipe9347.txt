SLUMGULLION


1  box or 1/4 bag large macaroni noodles, cooked
  onions, diced
1 1/2  lb. hamburger meat, sectioned
  chili powder
  salt, to taste
  water
2 8  oz. cans tomato sauce

      Cook noodles and set aside.  Put onions into skillet to cook. Add meat.  Cook on high.  Add lots of chili powder, salt slightly.  Stir in noodles with water.  Add tomato sauce and a little more water.  Let simmer 20 minutes.  Stir just enough to keep from sticking.
