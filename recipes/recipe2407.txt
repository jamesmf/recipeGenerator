FRUIT COCKTAIL CAKE


2  eggs
2  c. flour
1 1/2  c. sugar
1 1/2  tsp. soda
1 (No. 303)  can fruit cocktail

      Mix eggs, flour, sugar, soda and fruit cocktail in large bowl. Bake in 13 x 9 x 2-inch pan for 30 minutes at 350 degrees;.  Leave in pan.
Icing:
1 small  can evaporated milk
1  stick margarine or butter
1 1/4  c. sugar
1  tsp. vanilla

      Boil ingredients 3 minutes and pour over cake while hot.  Very good served warm.  Cut in squares.
