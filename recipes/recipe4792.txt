FROZEN BANANA SALAD


4  mashed bananas
1  c. sugar
1 1/2  c. buttermilk
1  medium Cool Whip
  chopped maraschino cherries (for color; optional)

      Mix bananas, sugar and buttermilk. Fold in Cool Whip and cherries. Pour into 8 x 8-inch or 9 x 9-inch dish and freeze. Remove from the freezer about 15 minutes before serving. Cut into squares and serve.
