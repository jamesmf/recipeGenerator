ONION NOODLE PIE


3  to 4 large onions
1/2  c. olive or peanut oil
1  egg
4  c. cooked noodles
  salt and pepper to taste
  paprika

      Cook onions in olive oil until dark brown and crispy.  Add and mix rest of ingredients except paprika.  Pour into pie plate. Sprinkle top with paprika.  Bake 30 to 40 minutes at 350 degrees;. Noodles should be crispy on top.
       This may be used as a side dish or main meal.
