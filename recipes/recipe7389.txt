CHICKEN BREASTS WITH MUSHROOMS AND SAUCE


4  skinless chicken breasts
6  oz. portabella mushrooms
1 large  can crushed tomatoes
1 small  can tomato paste
  Italian seasonings
1/4  c. Parmesan cheese
1/4  tsp. pepper
  garlic powder
  basil
1  Tbsp. sugar
1  tsp. oil

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees;.  Cook mushrooms in oil until soft.  Add onion and garlic powder.  Add tomatoes, paste and seasonings.  Let simmer for 1 hour.  Add sugar, pepper and Parmesan cheese.  Oil baking dish.  Lay chicken in dish.  Pour the sauce with mushrooms over top of chicken.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour or until tender.
