SMOKED SALMON PIZZA


1  (16-oz.) baked Italian bread shell
1  pkg. (4 oz.) cream cheese, softened
1/2  red onion, chopped
1/4  hot house cucumber, thinly sliced
1  pkg. (4-oz.) smoked salmon, cut crosswise into thin strips
1/4  c. fresh dill, cut into small sprigs
2  Tbsp. capers, rinsed and drained

      Bake bread shell as package directs.  Spread with cream cheese.  Top with onions, cucumbers, smoked salmon, dill and capers.  Slice or cut into small squares and serve.
