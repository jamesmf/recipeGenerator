HAM AND PEA SALAD


1 (16 oz.)  pkg. frozen peas, thawed
1/2  pound fully cooked ham, julienned sliced
1  cup shredded cheddar cheese
1  cup chopped red onion
1/3 - 1/2  cup ranch salad dressing

      In a serving bowl, combine the peas, ham, cheese and onion. Pour dressing over the top; toss to coat evenly.  Yields 6-8 servings.
