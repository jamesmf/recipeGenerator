CHICKEN ACAPULCO


1  chicken, whole
1  onion, chopped
4  oz. (1 can) green chilies
8  oz. (1 tub) sour cream
20  oz. (2 cans) soup, cream of chicken
8  oz. Cheddar cheese, shredded
10  (1 pkg.) tortillas (8 inch)
10  oz. (1 can) soup, Mexican cheese (optional)

      Cook chicken until tender.  Debone and chop chicken into bite-size pieces.
      Mix together:  chicken, onion, green chilies, sour cream, half (1 can) of the cream of chicken soup and 1/2 of the cheese.  Put a large spoonful of mixture in the middle of each tortillas.  Roll and place, seam side down, in a buttered 9x13-inch casserole dish. Spread rest (second can) of the cream of chicken soup on top.  If desired, Mexican cheese soup and be put on top of the chicken soup.  Sprinkle with rest of cheese.
      Bake at 350 degrees; for 20 minutes or until bubbly.
