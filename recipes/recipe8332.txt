CHICKEN WRAPS


1  box Spanish rice
1  pkg. (12) tortilla shells
1  c. red and green peppers, chopped
1/2  c. onions, diced
  lettuce, shredded
  Ranch dressing
  tomatoes, diced
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. Cheddar cheese
  Tyson breaded chicken strips

	Cook rice according to box directions; set aside. Deep-fry breaded chicken strips and set aside. Layer onions, peppers, tomatoes, lettuce, rice and cheese on shells. Drizzle with Ranch dressing and roll up.
