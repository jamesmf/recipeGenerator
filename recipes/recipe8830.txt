CABBAGE CASSEROLE


1  lb. ground beef (not browned before mixing)
1 (16 oz.)  can tomatoes
1  medium onion, chopped
1  c. uncooked rice
1  Tbsp. garlic
1  1/2 fresh green peppers, chopped
1  medium head cabbage, cut up or shredded
1  egg
1/2  c. cooking oil
1 1/2  tsp. salt

      Grate or cut up cabbage, peppers and onion. Mix all ingredients together in a large mixing bowl. Pour into a large pan; cover with foil paper and bake 1 1/2 hours at 350 degrees;.
