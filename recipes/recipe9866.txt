CHEESE POTATOES


3  to 4 large potatoes, peeled and diced
1  c. shredded Cheddar cheese
1  c. Mozzarella cheese
1  can celery soup
1  carton sour cream
1  chopped onion
  salt to taste

      Cook potatoes until done. Put in baking dish and mix in all other ingredients. Top with 1 cup cornflakes and 1/2 cup melted butter. Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour.
