BROCCOLI SOUP


1 large family size  can cream of mushroom soup
1 large  can milk (use the cream of mushroom soup can)
1 large  jar Cheez Whiz
2  pkg. chopped broccoli
1  onion, chopped
1/2 to 1  stick butter
1  can sliced mushrooms, drained

      Pour all ingredients in crock pot and mix well.  Let cook on high for about 2 hours, and then on low for 1 1/2 to 2 hours.
