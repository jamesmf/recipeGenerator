SAUSAGE CASSEROLE


1  lb. sausage
1  medium onion (diced)
1  cup water
1/2  cup rice
2  cans chicken noodle soup

      Brown sausage and onions together until done.  Drain.  Set aside.
      Cook rice in 1 cup of water until almost done.  Do not drain.
      Add rice to meat and onions. Add the 2 cans of chicken noodle soup.  Mix well.  Pour into casserole dish, bake covered at 350 degrees until rice is completely done.
