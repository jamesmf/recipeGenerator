CREAMY SWISS AND BROCCOLI SOUP


1  c. chopped ham
1  c. water
1 (10 oz.)  pkg. frozen chopped broccoli
2  c. milk
3  Tbsp. flour
2  c. (8 oz.) natural Swiss cheese, cubed
1/2  tsp. salt
  dash of pepper

      Boil ham in water for 10 minutes; add broccoli.  Cook until tender.  Gradually add milk to flour, stirring until well blended. Gradually add milk mixture to ham mixture, stirring constantly until mixture slightly thickens.  Simmer 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Add cheese and seasonings.  Cook until cheese begins to melt.  Serves 4 to 6.
