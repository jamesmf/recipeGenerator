LUCY'S RECIPE (HAMBURGER)


2  lb. hamburger
1  garlic
2  bunches scallion onions, chopped fine
2  large peppers, chopped fine
1  can whole tomatoes
1  c. tomato juice
1  pkg. noodles

      Brown hamburger. Add all ingredients, plus some butter, salt and black pepper to taste. Simmer as long as you like. Just before serving, add cooked noodles.
