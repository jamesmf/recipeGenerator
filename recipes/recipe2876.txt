PRALINE DELIGHT PIE


1/3  c. butter or margarine
1/3  c. firmly packed brown sugar
1/2  c. chopped pecans
3  c. milk
1 (5 oz.)  pkg. Jell-O vanilla pudding and pie filling
1  envelope Dream Whip whipped topping mix
1  baked 9-inch pie shell

      Heat butter and brown sugar with nuts in saucepan until melted. Spread in bottom of pie shell.  Bake at 450 degrees; for 5 minutes; cool. Prepare pie filling mix with milk as directed for pie.  Cool 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Measure 1 cup.  Cover with wax paper and chill thoroughly.  Prepare whipped topping mix as directed; fold in 1 1/3 cups pie filling.  Spread over filling in pie shell.  Chill; garnish with remaining whipped topping and additional pecans, if desired.
