HAM ROLLS


1  pkg. boiled ham (large size)
1  (8 oz.) cream cheese
2  Tbsp. mayonnaise
  few drops Worcestershire sauce
  sprinkle of garlic powder

      Mix  cream  cheese, mayonnaise, garlic powder and Worcestershire sauce and spread on slices of ham. Roll ham into roll. Chill and cut into 1/2 inch rolls. Serve on platter.
