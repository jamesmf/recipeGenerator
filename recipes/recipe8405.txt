BAKED CHICKEN SALAD


1/4  tsp. pepper
2  Tbsp. chopped onion
1  Tbsp. diced pimento
2  c. chopped celery
2  c. cooked, chopped chicken
1/2  c. slivered almonds
1/2  can cream of chicken soup
3/4  c. grated Swiss cheese
1/4  tsp. salt
1  c. salad dressing
1/3  c. chopped green pepper

      Combine all ingredients and put in a 2-quart casserole dish. Top with cheese.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 25 to 30 minutes.
