LAYERED SALAD


1  head lettuce, chopped
1  c. celery, chopped
10  oz. frozen peas
1/2  c. bell pepper, chopped
1  medium onion, chopped
1  c. shredded carrot
2  c. mayonnaise
2  Tbsp. sugar
8  oz. Mozzarella cheese, shredded
  bacon bits (if desired)

      Place a layer of lettuce. Add each vegetable in layers. Top off vegetables with more lettuce. Spread over lettuce 2 cups mayonnaise. Sprinkle 2 tablespoons sugar over mayonnaise. Add the Mozzarella cheese. Let set for 24 hours. Sprinkle bacon bits before serving, if desired.
