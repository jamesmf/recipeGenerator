CHAR SIU
(Chinese Roasted Pork)  

4  lb. pork butt, cut into 5 x 3 x 2-inch pieces
  garlic salt

 
Marinade:
1  c. sugar
1/2  c. soy sauce
2  Tbsp. honey
1  Tbsp. whiskey
1  tsp. red food coloring
1/2  tsp. Chinese 5-spice powder

      Rub pork with garlic salt and refrigerate for at least 1 hour. Combine marinade ingredients. Soak pork in marinade in refrigerator for 24 hours. Place meat on a rack over 1/4-inch of water in a shallow roasting pan. Roast 1 1/2 hours at 300 degrees;. Slice and serve with steamed Calrose or Jasmine rice.
