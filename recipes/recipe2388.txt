CLASSIC PECAN PIE


3  eggs
1  c. sugar
1  c. corn syrup (light or dark)
2  Tbsp. margarine or butter, melted
1  tsp. vanilla
1 1/2  c. pecan halves
1  unbaked 9-inch deep-dish pie shell

      Heat oven to 350 degrees;.  Beat eggs slightly in bowl.  Add sugar, margarine and vanilla.  Mix in pecans.  Set pie shell on heavy-duty baking sheet.  Pour filling into pie shell.  Bake in 350 degrees; oven 45 to 50 minutes, until knife inserted midway between center and rim tests clean.  Cool on rack to room temperature.
