POPPY SEED CHICKEN


8  oz. Ritz crackers, crushed
1  stick oleo, melted
2  cans Cream of Chicken soup
8  oz. sour cream
4  chicken breasts, cooked and cubed
1  Tbsp. poppy seed
1 1/2  c. cooked rice

      Mix Ritz crackers and oleo.  Spread half in bottom of 9x13 casserole.  Mix soup, sour cream, & poppy seed.  Add chicken & rice.  Pour into casserole over crackers.  Top with remaining Ritz. Bake at 350 degrees; for 50 minutes.
