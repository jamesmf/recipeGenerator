RANCH DRESSING


2  c. mayonnaise
2  c. buttermilk
1 1/2  tsp. chopped fresh parsley
1  tsp. onion powder
1  tsp. garlic powder
1  tsp. garlic salt
1/8  tsp. pepper

      Combine all ingredients in a small bowl, stirring well. Cover tightly and chill at least 8 hours. Serve dressing over salad greens. Yield: 4 cups.
