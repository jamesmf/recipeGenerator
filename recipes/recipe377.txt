BUTTERMILK BISCUITS



1  c. all-purpose flour*
1  tsp. baking powder
1/8  tsp. baking soda
1/8  tsp. salt
1/3  c. buttermilk or sour milk
2  Tbsp. cooking oil

      Stir together flour,  baking  powder,  baking  soda and salt in a medium bowl.  Stir  together buttermilk and oil then pour over flour mixture and mix well.  Knead dough gently on a lightly floured surface 10 to  12 times.  Roll or pat dough to 1/2-inch thickness.  Cut with a 2-inch biscuit cutter, dipping cutter in flour between cuts,  and  put on an ungreased baking sheet.  Bake at 450 degrees; for  10  to  12  minutes or until golden. Serve warm. Makes 8 biscuits.
      Note:  If desired, substitute 1/4 cup whole wheat flour or oat bran for 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour.
      Per Serving (1 Biscuit):  91 Calories, 4 g. Fat, 0 mg. Cholesterol and 99 mg.  Sodium.
