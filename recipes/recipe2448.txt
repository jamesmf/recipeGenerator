CAROLINA DELIGHTS


1  stick margarine
1 1/2  c. quick oats
1/2  c. milk
1/2  c. peanut butter
4  Tbsp. cocoa
1/4  tsp. vanilla
2  c. sugar

      Combine margarine, milk, cocoa and sugar in a saucepan.  Bring to boil over medium heat.  Boil for 4 minutes.  Add oats, peanut butter and vanilla; mix.  Drop by spoonful on wax paper.  Place in refrigerator to cool.
