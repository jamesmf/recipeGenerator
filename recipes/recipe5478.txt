BROCCOLI SALAD


2  bunches broccoli
1  pkg. sunflower seeds
10 to 12  slices bacon (crispy)
1  medium red onion
1 1/2  c. grapes
1  c. mayonnaise
1/3  c. sugar
2  Tbsp. red wine vinegar

      Cut broccoli into bite-size pieces.  Chop onion and cut grapes in half.  Add sunflower seeds.  Crumble cooked bacon and mix together.  Then mix mayonnaise, sugar and red wine vinegar, pour over salad mixture and chill in refrigerator.
