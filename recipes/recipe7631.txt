BELL PEPPER STUFFING


2  lb. hamburger
2/3  c. onion
1  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
1  qt. tomato juice
1  c. water
1 1/3  c. raw rice
2  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
2  c. shredded cheese
1  pt. tomato sauce
4  Tbsp. sugar

      Brown  hamburger  and  onion  until  brown; drain.  Add salt, pepper,  tomato  juice,  water, rice and Worcestershire sauce. Cover  and  simmer  until rice is tender.  Add cheese. Pour into 9 x 13-inch pan.  Cover with tomato sauce that sugar has been stirred in.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes.
      For  Bell  Peppers:  Precook  peppers  for 5 minutes in boiling salted water, which the tops have been cut off and the seeds removed.  Cool in cold water.  After adding cheese, fill peppers.  Bake  at  350 degrees; for 25 to 30 minutes.  Fills approximately  12  peppers.  They may be individually wrapped in foil and frozen.
