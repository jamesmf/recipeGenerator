ITALIAN TACO SALAD


1  lb. ground beef
3  c. crushed tortilla chips
2  c. shredded mozzarella cheese
2  c. shredded cheddar cheese
1 (10 oz.)  pkg. mixed salad greens
1 (8 oz.)  bottle zesty Italian dressing

      In a medium skillet, cook ground meat until brown. Remove and drain. In a large bowl, combine ground beef, tortilla chips, Mozzarella cheese, Cheddar cheese, and salad greens. Toss with salad dressing and serve.
