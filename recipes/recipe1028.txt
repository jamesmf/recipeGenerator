CROCK-POT PUMPKIN SPICED STEW


2  Tbsp. oil
1/3  c. chopped onion
1  clove garlic
1/2  green pepper, sliced
1  medium zucchini, diced
1  can whole tomatoes and juice
1  can pumpkin
1/8  tsp. red pepper
1  tsp. chili powder
1/2  tsp. oregano
  dash of black pepper
1  Tbsp. lemon juice
2  Tbsp. parsley, chopped
1/2  tsp. garlic powder
1/4  c. grated Cheddar cheese
  whole wheat crackers (optional)

      In skillet, heat oil; saut√© onion, garlic and green pepper. Cook until pepper is softened.  Place in crock-pot; add zucchini, tomatoes and juice and pumpkin.  Sprinkle spices on top.  Cook on high in crock-pot 3 hours.  Serve hot with grated Cheddar cheese on top of each serving.  Serves 6. Serve with crackers.
