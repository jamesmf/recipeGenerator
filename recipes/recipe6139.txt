BAKED POTATO SOUP


2/3  c. butter
2/3  c. all-purpose flour
7  c. milk
4  large baking potatoes, baked, cooled, peeled and cubed
4  green onions, sliced
12  strips bacon, cooked and crumbled
1 1/4  c. shredded cheddar cheese
1 8  oz. sour cream
3/4  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper

      In large soup kettle, melt butter.  Stir in flour; heat and stir until smooth.  Gradually add milk, stirring until thick.  Add potatoes and onions.  Bring to a boil, stirring constantly. Reduce heat; simmer for 10 minutes.  Add remaining ingredients; stir until cheese is melted.  Serve immediately.  Makes 8 to 10 servings.
