TACO CASSEROLE


1  pkg. crescent rolls
1 1/2  lb. hamburger
1  pkg. taco seasoning mix
1  container sour cream
1  bag taco Doritos chips
1  pkg. shredded taco cheese

      Unroll crescent rolls and place in bottom of a 9 x 13 cake pan. Mix taco seasoning in with browned hamburger and spread over crescent rolls. Spread sour cream over browned hamburger. Smash taco chips up and place over sour cream. Top with shredded taco cheese. Bake at 350 degrees; for 25 minutes or until cheese is melted and slightly brown.
      Cut into squares and each person can top it with diced tomatoes, shredded lettuce, sliced olives, diced onions, salsa or cheese dip before eating.
