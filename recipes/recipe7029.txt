DELMA'S MEAT LOAF


2  lb. ground chuck
2  eggs
1/2  c. Carnation milk
1/2  c. instant oatmeal
1  pkg. onion soup mix
1/2  c. meat loaf fixings (or 1/2 c. catsup)
  garlic, salt, pepper to taste

      Mix all together; shape into a loaf and bake at 350 degrees; until done. For 50 servings, make 8 of the above recipe. You may wish to add a little catsup or meat loaf fixings to the top of meat loaf the last 10 minutes of baking time.
