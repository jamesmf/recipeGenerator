WATER CHESTNUT APPETIZERS


2  c. whole water chestnuts, drained
  bacon strips (uncooked)
1/3  c. ketchup
1/2  c. sugar
2  Tbsp. soy sauce

      Wrap water chestnuts with bacon strips.  Fasten with toothpicks.  Bake 60 minutes at 300 degrees;.  Combine remaining ingredients to make sauce.  Drain off bacon grease and cover appetizers with sauce.  Bake another 30 minutes.
