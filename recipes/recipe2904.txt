HOLIDAY FRUIT BUTTER


1/2  c. water
8  large cooking apples, peeled and quartered
5  large ripe pears, cored and quartered
2  ripe bananas, peeled and sliced
1  tsp. grated orange rind
1/3  c. fresh orange juice

      Combine water, apples and pears in 5-quart kettle.  Cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until fruit is soft.  Stir in bananas.  Put fruit through food mill or sieve; return to kettle and stir in remaining ingredients.  Cook over medium heat until thick, about 1 hour, stirring frequently.  Cool, then pour into jars and refrigerate.  Makes 6 pints.
