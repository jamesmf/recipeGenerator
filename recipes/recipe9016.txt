PORCUPINE MEATBALLS


1  lb. ground beef
1/4  c. uncooked long-cooking rice
1/4  c. finely chopped veggies (onion, celery, green pepper, carrot)
1  egg
1  tsp. dried leaf herb (basil, oregano, thyme)
1/4  c. beef broth
1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can condensed tomato soup
1/2  c. beef broth
2  tsp. Worcestershire sauce

      Cook in 10-inch skillet 35 to 40 minutes.
