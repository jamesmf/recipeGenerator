IMPOSSIBLE BACON PIE


12  slices crispy fried bacon, crumbled
1  c. shredded Swiss cheese
1/3  c. chopped onion
2  c. milk
1  c. Bisquick
4  eggs
1/4  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper

      Heat oven to 400 degrees;.  Grease 10-inch pie pan.  Sprinkle bacon, cheese and onion in plate.  Beat remaining ingredients until smooth.  Pour into plate.  Bake until knife inserted between center comes out clean, 35 to 40 minutes.  Cool 5 minutes.
