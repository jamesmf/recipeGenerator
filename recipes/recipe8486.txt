ITALIAN BEEF


3 to 4  lb. beef roast
1  onion, chopped
1/2  tsp. onion salt
1/2  tsp. garlic salt
1  Tbsp. Italian seasoning
1/2  tsp. oregano
1/4  tsp. basil
1/2  tsp. seasoned salt
1  tsp. accent

      Put roast in covered roaster and fill halfway with water. Add remaining ingredients. Bake covered at 325 degrees; for 4 to 5 hours or in crockpot  until well done and tender.
      If using crockpot method, begin on high temperature until it starts to simmer then turn to low and proceed.
