HERMIT COOKIES


1/2  c. shortening
1  c. brown sugar
1  egg
2  Tbsp. water
1 1/2  c. plain flour
1/4  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. soda
1/4  tsp. cloves
1/2  tsp. cinnamon
1/4  tsp. nutmeg
3/4  c. candied fruit
1/2  c. raisins
1/2  c. pecans or walnuts

      Cream shortening, brown sugar and egg.  Mix together water, flour, salt, soda, cloves, cinnamon and nutmeg; add to first mixture.  Add remaining ingredients.  Spoon out on cookie sheet and bake 15 minutes at 325 degrees;.
