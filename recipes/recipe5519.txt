SPAGHETTI SAUCE


1/2  bushel tomatoes
2  garlic bulbs
3  green peppers
5 or 6  drops tabasco sauce
1  tsp. basil
1  Tbsp. oregano
3  pints of chopped onions
1  pint cooking oil
1/2  c. sugar
1/2  c. salt
8 small  cans tomato paste

      Cook tomatoes and run through a sieve.  Put tomatoes back into a large pot and cook all ingredients but the tomato paste together for about 2 hours.  Stir occasionally.  Then add tomato paste and simmer for 30 more minutes.  Put into hot jars, leaving 1/2 inch or more head room, and seal.
