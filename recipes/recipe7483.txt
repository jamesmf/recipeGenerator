SUMMER SPARERIBS


5  lb. boneless lean country-style pork ribs
1/4  c. Worcestershire sauce
1 1/2  c. onions, chopped
1  c. firmly packed light brown sugar
1  c. ketchup
1/4  c. vinegar
2  tsp. dry mustard
1 1/2  c. water
1/4  tsp. salt
1  tsp. pepper

      Place ribs in a shallow roasting pan.  In a bowl mix remaining ingredients; pour sauce over ribs.  Cover and bake at 350 degrees; for 3 hours or until tender.
