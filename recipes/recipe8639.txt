BEEF STROGANOFF


2  lbs. tenderloin or sirloin tips
1/4  C. oil
1  lrg. onion, diced coarse
1/2  lb. mushrooms, sliced
2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp pepper
1/2  tsp. thyme leaves
1/2  tsp. galic powder or 1 clove fresh garlic
1  C. water or beef broth
1/2  C. Heinz 57 Sauce
1/2  C. Burgundy (optional) or water
16  oz. sour cream

      Sauté beef tips in a skillet until browned.  Add onions and mushrooms and sauté another 5 minutes.  Add water, salt, pepper, thyme, and garlic.
      Place in 350 degree oven for 1 hour or until tender.  Stir every 20 minutes.  Add Heinz 57 Sauce and wine.  Blend and cook another 15 minutes.
      Thicken with flour and water mixture.  Add sour cream and blend in.  Cook another 5 minutes.
      Serve at once.
      Serve stroganoff over noodles.
