SPINACH STRAWBERRY SALAD

Dressing:
1/3  c. raspberry vinegar
1  tsp. salt
1/2  c. sugar
1  tsp. dry mustard
1  c. oil
1 1/2  Tbsp. poppy seeds

      In a mixer or blender, mix first 4 ingredients, add oil slowly.  Add poppy seeds.
Salad:
1  lb. spinach, torn
1  pint fresh strawberries
1/2  c. pecan halves
1  tsp. butter

      Brown pecans in butter, in oven or on stove.  Tear spinach, cut strawberries in half.  Toss all together with dressing just before serving.
