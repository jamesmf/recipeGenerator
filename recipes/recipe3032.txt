BACON AND TOMATO CUP


8  slices bacon, fried and crumbled
1  medium tomato, chopped
1/4  c. sliced green onions
3  oz. Swiss cheese, shredded
1  tsp. dried basil
1  (10 pack) canned buttermilk biscuits
1/2  c. mayonnaise

      Preheat oven to 375 degrees;.  Lightly grease 2 mini-muffin tins. Combine all ingredients, except biscuits, in medium bowl. Separate each biscuit into 2 biscuits.  Flatten and place in muffin tin.  Fill each with bacon mixture.  Bake for 10 to 12 minutes, until golden.
