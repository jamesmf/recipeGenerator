NO PEEP BEEF CASSEROLE


2  lb. lean stew meat, cut in 1-inch pieces
1  envelope onion soup mix
1/2  c. red wine
1  can mushroom soup
1 (4 oz.)  can mushrooms

      Combine all ingredients and put in covered casserole. Bake at 300 degrees; for 3 hours. Do not peep until 3 hours have passed. Serve over rice or noodles. Meat will be very tender.
