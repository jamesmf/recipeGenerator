CHIPPER CHICKEN


1  medium bag potato chips
1/4  c. butter
1  tsp. garlic salt
1  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper
2 to 3  lb. chicken breasts or favorite chicken pieces

	Preheat oven to 350 degrees;. Crush potato chips.  Melt butter and add seasonings. Roll chicken in seasoned butter and then roll in the potato chips. Place prepared chicken on cookie sheet (sprayed with Pam). Bake 1 1/2 hours.
