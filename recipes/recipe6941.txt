POTATO PUFF CASSEROLE


1  lb. ground beef
1/3  c. chopped onion
1/3  c. chopped green pepper
1  Tbsp. chili sauce or catsup
1 (10 oz.)  can cream of mushroom soup
1/4  c. water
1 (10 oz.)  pkg. frozen potato puffs

      In skillet, combine ground beef, onion and green pepper; cook over medium heat, breaking up meat with fork until meat is browned and onion and pepper are tender. Add chili sauce, undiluted soup and water. Turn into 1 1/2-quart casserole pan and cover top with frozen potato puffs. Bake, uncovered, at 375 degrees; for 35 minutes or until potato puffs are golden brown. Makes 4 servings.
