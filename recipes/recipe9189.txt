MEAT LOAF

Meat Loaf:
1 1/2  lb. hamburger
3/4  c. oatmeal
2  eggs, beaten
1/4  c. chopped onion
2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
1  c. tomato juice

      Mix and put in ovenproof dish; top with sauce.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour.
Sauce:
3  Tbsp. brown sugar
1/4  c. tomato catsup
1/4  tsp. nutmeg
1  tsp. dry mustard

      Mix and put on top of meat loaf.
