BROCCOLI SALAD I


1  lb. pkg. broccoli slaw
2  pkg. Ramen noodles, crushed (do not use seasoning)
5  green onions, chopped
4  oz. toasted almonds

 
Dressing:
1/2  c. vegetable oil
1/2  c. sugar
2  Tbsp. vinegar
2  Tbsp. hot mustard
2  Tbsp. soy sauce

	Pour dressing over first 4 ingredients and refrigerate.
