CHERRY CAKE


2  c. flour
3/4  c. sugar
3/4  c. vegetable oil
2  eggs
1 (21 oz.)  can cherry pie filling
2  tsp. vanilla
1  tsp. baking soda
1/2  tsp. cinnamon
1 (6 oz.)  pkg. chocolate chips

      Combine all ingredients and pour into 2 greased and floured cake pans.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 40 to 45 minutes.
No Cook Frosting:
1  c. sugar
1/2  c. milk
4  Tbsp. flour
2 to 3  tsp. vanilla
1  stick butter
1/2  c. shortening

      Heat sugar and milk on low temperature until sugar is dissolved, stirring occasionally.  Do not boil, then cool.  Add remaining ingredients.  Mix until thick.
