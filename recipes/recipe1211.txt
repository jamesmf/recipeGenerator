COCONUT SURPRISES


3  oz. cream cheese
1/4  tsp. orange rind
1/4  tsp. lemon rind
1  tsp. walnuts
3/4  tsp. artificial liquid sweetener
1/4  c. unsweetened moist coconut

      Whip cream cheese with spoon until light and fluffy.  Add orange rind, lemon rind, walnuts and sweetener; mix well.  Roll into 1-inch balls and roll in moist unsweetened coconut.  Chill.
      One cookie equals 34 calories, 1 fat exchange, 1 gram protein, and 2 grams fat.
