


1  large head cabbage, shredded
1  medium green pepper, chopped
1  large onion, chopped
1/2  tsp. mustard seed
1/2  tsp. celery seed
1  c. sugar
1  c. white vinegar
2/3  c. salad oil

      Combine first 5 ingredients; set aside.
      Bring sugar, vinegar and oil to a boil and, while hot, pour over cabbage mixture.  Do not stir until cold.  Cover and put in refrigerator.  Will keep for days.  Serves 10 to 12.
