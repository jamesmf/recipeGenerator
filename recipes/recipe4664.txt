ONION SOUP


1  very large onion
3  tbsp butter
6  beef bouillon cubes dissolved in 6 cups of hot water
  fresh ground pepper
  dash of sherry (optional)
  French or Italian Bread
  Melting Cheese of your choice

      Brown sliced onion in the butter until dark brown (caramel colored).  Add fresh pepper to taste.  Add beef bouillon.  Simmer 1/2 hour, add dash of sherry if you would like.  Toast bread slices dark - put one on each serving, add cheese and broil until cheese browns.
