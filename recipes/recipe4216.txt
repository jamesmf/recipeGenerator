CAJUN TORTILLA CHIPS


1 1/2  tsp. paprika
1  tsp. dried thyme
1/2  tsp. garlic powder
1/2  tsp. onion powder
1/2  tsp. black pepper
1/4  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. sugar
1/4  tsp. ground red pepper
10  (7-inch) tortillas, cut into 8 wedges
  cooking spray

      Preheat oven to 375 degrees;. To prepare Cajun seasoning, combine first 8 ingredients in a small bowl. To prepare chips, arrange the tortilla wedges on 2 baking sheets coated with cooking spray. Coat wedges in cooking spray. Sprinkle 2 teaspoons Cajun seasoning over wedges. Bake chips at 375 degrees; for 6 minutes or until crisp. Yield: 80 chips (serving size: 4 chips).
      You can also make pita chips with this recipe. Start with 5 pita, split in half and cut each half into 8 wedges. Then proceed with the recipe.
      Calories 63, Fat 1.4g.
