CHICKEN AND DUMPLINGS


4  cans chicken broth (8 cups homemade stock)
2  sticks butter
2  cans cream of chicken soup
2  cans chicken chunks
4  cans biscuits

      Mix top four ingredients. Bring to a boil. Pinch biscuits in pieces and drop in broth. Reduce heat slightly and cook until dumplings have doubled in size.
