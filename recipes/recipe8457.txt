QUICK AND EASY LASAGNA


1  lb. hamburger
1/2  lb. hot sausage, optional
1 large  jar Prego Spaghetti Sauce
1/2  c. chopped onion
1/2  c. chopped green pepper
1  lb. lasagna noodles
4  oz. mozzarella, shredded cheese
2  c. mild cheddar, shredded cheese

      Brown hamburger and hot sausage; add chopped onions and peppers.  Drain off grease, then mix with Prego spaghetti sauce. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 10 to 15 minutes.  Layer your casserole dish with meat sauce, noodles, and cheeses.  Preheat oven to 375 degrees; and bake for 30 to 35 minutes.
