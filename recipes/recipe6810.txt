CHILI-BEER BRISKET


1  (3 to 5 lb.) beef brisket
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper
3  onions, sliced
4  stalks celery, chopped
1  clove garlic, minced
1 (12 oz.)  bottle Heinz chili sauce
1/2  c. water (use to rinse bottle of chili sauce)
1  can beer

      Place meat in a roasting pan, fat side up. Put the chopped vegetables over the meat; pour the chili sauce and water over all. Cover and bake at 325 degrees; for 3 or 3 1/2 hours. Let cool 20 minutes. Slice thin and pour the beer over the brisket. Place back in the oven for 1 hour.
