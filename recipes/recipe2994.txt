HORMEL CHILI DIP
(Appetizer)  

2 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
2  cans Hormel Chili with beans
1  pkg. (2 cups) shredded cheddar cheese

      Spread cream cheese in bottom of 9 x 13 inch baking dish. Top with chili and cover with shredded cheese.  Put in a 350 degrees; oven and bake until cheese is melted.  Serve with Tostado chips or large corn chips.
