SQUASH CASSEROLE


1 1/2  c. cooked squash
1  c. medium sharp cheese, grated
3  Tbsp. pimento
2  eggs, beaten
1  c. cracker crumbs
3  Tbsp. onion
3  Tbsp. butter
1/4  c. celery
1  c. scalded milk
  salt and pepper to taste

      Combine all ingredients. Add milk last. Pour into a 6 x 12-inch greased dish. Bake 1 hour at 350 degrees;.
