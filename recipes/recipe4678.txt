ENCHILADA CHICKEN SOUP


1 (11 oz.)  can condensed fiesta nacho cheese soup (undiluted)
1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can condensed cream of chicken soup (undiluted)
2 2/3  c. milk
1 (10 oz.)  can chunk white chicken, drained
1 (10 oz.)  can enchilada sauce
1 (4 oz.)  can chopped green chilies
  sour cream
  nacho chips

      In a large saucepan, combine the soups, milk, chicken, enchilada sauce and chilies; mix well.  Cook until heated through. Garnish with sour cream and nacho chips.
