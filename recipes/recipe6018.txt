BROCCOLI CABBAGE SALAD


1  pkg. broccoli slaw
1  green pepper, chopped
4  green onions, chopped
1/2  c. slivered almonds
1  pkg. Ramen noodles, chicken flavor
3/4  c. oil
1/2  c. vinegar
1/2  c. sugar
1  c. sunflower seeds, optional

      In a large bowl mix broccoli slaw, green pepper, green onion, almonds and sunflower seeds.  Break or crush noodles and put on top of vegetables.  In a bowl mix oil, vinegar, flavoring pack from noodles and sugar; pour over vegetables.  Do not mix until 2 hours before serving.
