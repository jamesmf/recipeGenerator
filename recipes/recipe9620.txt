PIZZA CASSEROLE


2 1/2  lb. ground beef, browned & drained
7  oz. bag egg noodles, cooked & drained
2  cans cheddar cheese soup
2 (14 oz)  jars Ragu pizza sauce
  Mozzarella cheese
  Pepperoni slices

      Mix soup and ground beef, stir well. Add Pizza sauce and noodles. Pour into a large casserole dish, top with mozzarella cheese and pepperoni slices. Cover loosely with foil and bake 20 minutes at 350 degrees. Remove foil and bake 10 minutes longer. Makes a very large casserole. Will freeze well.
