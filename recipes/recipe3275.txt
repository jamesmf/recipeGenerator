SURPRISE CHEESE PUFFS APPETIZER


1  c. flour
1  stick butter
1  jar stuffed olives
1 (6 oz.)  jar sharp Old English cheese

      Blend together well. Form into small balls around a stuffed olive and place on a cookie sheet. Place in freezer until frozen. When ready to serve, bake for 10 minutes in a moderately hot oven (425 degrees;). When puffs are frozen, they may be placed in a plastic bag and used as needed. Do not thaw before baking. Makes 30 medium puffs.
