COLE SLAW


1  large head green cabbage
1  breen pepper diced
1  onion chopped
1  Tbsp. salt
1  cup boiling water
1/2  cup vinegar
1  cup salad oil
1  cup sugar
1  tsp. celery seed
1  tsp. mustard seed

      Mix cabbage, pepper, onion, and salt. Pour hot watter over cabbage and let stand 1 hour. Add remaining ingredients and chill over night. Will keep for days.
