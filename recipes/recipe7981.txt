SLOPPY JOE'S


1  lb. hamburger
1  Tbsp. minced onion
1/2  tsp. celery salt
1  can tomato soup
1/4  c. brown sugar
1/2  c. ketchup
  couple drops of liquid smoke
1  tsp. salt

      Brown hamburger, with salt.  Add onions and other ingredients.  Let simmer until warmed.  Add a little water if it is too thick.  Extra brown sugar can be added also if you like.
