HAM AND BEAN STEW


2 (16 oz)  cans baked beans
2  medium potatoes, peeled and cubed
2  cups cubed fully cooked ham
1  celery rib, chopped
1/2  cup water

      In a slow cooker, combine all ingredients; mix well.  Cover and cook on low for 7 hours or until the potatoes are tender. Yields 6 servings.
