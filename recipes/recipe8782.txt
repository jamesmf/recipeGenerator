E-Z QUICHE


2  pie shells
1  pt. half and half cream
5  eggs
3  Tbsp. flour
1  lb. chopped ham
1  lb. Swiss cheese
1  lb. shredded Cheddar cheese

      Mix half and half, eggs and flour well.  Line pie shell with Swiss cheese; fill bottom with ham.  Add egg mixture and cover with Cheddar cheese.  Bake at 350 degrees; until cheese is golden brown, about 30 to 40 minutes.  Let cool 10 to 15 minutes before serving.
      Variations:  Substitute ham with broccoli, bacon and onions, crab or your own favorites.
