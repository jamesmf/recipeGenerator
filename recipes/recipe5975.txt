TUNA SALAD


2 (6 1/2 oz.)  can tuna fish, drained and flaked
4  hard boiled eggs, chopped
1/4  c. chopped celery
1/2  c. sweet pickle relish, drained
1/2  tsp. salt
  pinch of pepper
1/2  c. Miracle Whip

      In mixing bowl combine all ingredients and mix well, but do not over mix.  Cover and chill 30 minutes.  Serve on sandwich with lettuce or on crackers.
