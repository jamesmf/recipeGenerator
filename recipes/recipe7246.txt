BEEF BURGUNDY


4 to 4 1/2  lb. beef (rump roast)
1  can beef broth
1  c. red wine
1  can cream of mushroom or celery soup

      Brown beef and onion. Add beef broth. Bake, covered, 1 1/2 hours at 350 degrees;. Add wine and continue baking for 2 hours. Add soup the last  hour of baking. Very good served over noodles or rice.
