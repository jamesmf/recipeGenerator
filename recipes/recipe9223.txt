BARBECUED SPARERIBS


1  Tbsp. ground mustard
1  Tbsp. chili powder
1/2  tsp. cayenne pepper
1/4  tsp. garlic powder
3  lb. pork spareribs
2/3  c. ketchup
1/2  c. water
1/2  c. chopped onion
1/4  c. lemon juice
2  Tbsp. vegetable oil
1  tsp. dried oregano
1  tsp. liquid smoke, optional
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper

      Combine mustard, chili powder, cayenne pepper and garlic powder; rub over ribs.  For sauce, combine the remaining ingredients; mix well and set aside.  Grill ribs, covered, over indirect heat and medium low coals for 1 hour or until tender, turning occasionally.  Baste with sauce during cooking, if desired.  Heat the remainder of the sauce, and serve with ribs.
