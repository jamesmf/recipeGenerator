ITALIAN BAKED CHICKEN


4  to 8 skinless chicken breasts
1/2  c. margarine
1  tsp. garlic
1/2  c. Italian salad dressing
1/4  c. Worcestershire sauce
1/4  c. lemon juice

	Place chicken breasts in casserole dish. Heat remaining ingredients until margarine melts. Pour heated mixture over chicken breasts. Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour or until done.
