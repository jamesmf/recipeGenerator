MUSHROOM BACON BITES


24  medium fresh mushrooms
12  bacon strips, halved
1  c. barbecue sauce

      Wrap each mushroom with a piece of bacon; secure with a toothpick. Thread onto metal or soaked bamboo skewers. Brush with barbecue sauce. Grill, uncovered, over indirect medium heat for 10 to 15 minutes or until the bacon is crisp and the mushrooms are tender, turning and basting occasionally with remaining barbecue sauce. YIELD: 2 dozen.
