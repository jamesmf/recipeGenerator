PUPPIES AND POTATOES


3  large potatoes
  butter
  salt
  pepper
  little milk or cream
1  pkg. hot dogs
  grated cheese

      Cook potatoes and mash them with butter, salt, pepper and a little milk or cream.  Boil one package of hot dogs.  Put hot dogs on a large cookie sheet.  Cover hot dogs with mashed potatoes. Indent mashed potatoes and fill with grated cheese.  Put in oven until cheese is melted.  Serve.
