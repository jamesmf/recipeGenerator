SHRIMP AND GROUND MEAT SPAGHETTI


1  lb. ground meat
1  lb. shrimp, peeled
1  pkg. angel hair noodles
3  cans tomato sauce (6 oz.)
1  can tomato paste (6 oz.)
12  oz. water
1  tsp. sugar
  season to taste
  white onion

	Put white onion and ground meat in a frypan; cook and drain. In a separate pan, saut shrimp. Once shrimp is cooked, add ground meat, tomato sauce, tomato paste, water and seasoning. Add sugar and cook on a medium fire. Boil noodles in a separate pot and drain. Add noodles and meat together; stir and let cool.
