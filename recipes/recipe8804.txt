SPINACH PIE


1/2  lb. bacon, cut in 1-inch pieces
3/4  c. chopped onion
1 (10 oz.)  pkg. frozen spinach
4  eggs, slightly beaten
1  c. milk
1/2  tsp. seasoned salt
  pie shell

      Cook bacon and add onions for the last 2 minutes; drain. Blend eggs, milk and salt.  Add spinach, onion and cooked bacon. Turn into 9-inch pie shell and bake at 375 degrees; until set, about 40 to 45 minutes.
