ZUCCHINI RICE CASSEROLE


1  lb. hamburger meat
1  small onion
3  c. tomatoes
1  tsp. chili powder
1/2  c. uncooked rice
  salt and pepper to taste
1  medium zucchini
1/4  lb. Velveeta cheese

      Brown 1 pound hamburger and 1 small chopped onion.  Drain. Add 3 cups tomatoes, 1 teaspoon chili powder, 1/2 cup uncooked rice, salt and pepper to taste.  Cook until rice is tender.  Add 1 medium zucchini, chopped and 1/4 pound Velveeta cheese cut up. Pour into greased Pyrex dish.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour.
