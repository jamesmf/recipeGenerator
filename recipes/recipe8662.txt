ENCHILADA CASSEROLE


1 1/2  c. chopped onion
1 1/2  lb. ground beef
1 small  can chopped green chilies
1 medium  can enchilada sauce
1 small  can evaporated milk
12  flour tortillas
1  c. grated Longhorn cheese

	Preheat oven to 350 degrees;.  Saut onions.  Add meat and brown.  Add green chilies, enchilada sauce and evaporated milk.  Spray 13 x 9 pan with Pam.  Layer tortillas in pan; add meat mixture and sprinkle with cheese.  Bake until cheese melts and bubbling.
