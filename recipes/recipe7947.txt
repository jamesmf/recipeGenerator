CREAMY PORK CHOPS AND POTATOES


6  pork chops
2  large baking potatoes (diced)
1  small onion (chopped)
1  can cream of chicken soup
1/2  cup milk

      Place potatoes and onions in casserole dish.  Mix soup and milk.  Pour over potatoes and onions.  Top with pork chops.  Cover and bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour and 15 minutes.
