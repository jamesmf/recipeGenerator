SPRING PEA SALAD


1  pkg. (10 -oz.) frozen peas
1  c. diced celery
1  c. cauliflower
2  Tbsp. pimiento
1  c. nuts (your choice: cashews, almonds, sunflower seeds)
1/4  c. crisply cooked crumbled bacon
1/2  c. sour cream
1  c. ranch dressing
1 small  clove garlic, minced
1/2  tsp. dijon mustard

      Rinse peas in hot water; drain.  Combine vegetables and bacon with sour cream.  Mix ranch dressing, mustard, and garlic; pour over salad mixture and toss gently.  Chill.  Just before serving, add nuts.
