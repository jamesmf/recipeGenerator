CHOCOLATE FUDGE PUDDING CAKE

Cake:
1  c. flour
2/3  c. sugar
2  tsp. baking powder
2  Tbsp. cocoa
  pinch of salt
1/2  c. milk
2  Tbsp. melted butter
1 scant  tsp. vanilla

 
Sauce:
1  c. brown sugar
2  Tbsp. cocoa

      Mix cake ingredients and pour into 10-inch baking dish.  Mix sauce ingredients and spread over the top.  Pour 1 1/2 cups boiling water over all.  Bake at 350 degrees; for approximately 25 minutes.  The cake will rise and the sauce will be on the bottom of the pan. When serving, turn each piece of cake over in the dishes so the sauce is on top.  Serves 8.
