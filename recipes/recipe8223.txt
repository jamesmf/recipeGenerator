BAKED STEW


1  can tomato soup
1  can golden mushroom soup
1  lb. hamburger
1/3  c. onion, diced
1/3  celery, diced
4  carrots, diced
5  medium potatoes, diced
  salt and pepper to taste

	Preheat oven to 350 degrees;. Mix the 2 soups together. Set aside. Brown onion, celery and hamburger in skillet. In a 9 x 13-inch pan, alternate vegetables, the meat, then soups. Bake 1 hour and 30 minutes.
