MARINATED SLAW


1  large head cabbage, shredded
2  medium onions, chopped
1  medium carrot, shredded
1  medium bell pepper, chopped
3/4  c. sugar
1  c. vinegar
1  Tbsp. salt
1  Tbsp. sugar
1/2  c. salad oil
1 1/2  Tbsp. mustard seed

      Combine cabbage, onions, carrot and bell pepper in a large bowl.  Sprinkle with the 3/4 cup sugar.  Mix well.  Let stand while the dressing is prepared.
      In boiler, combine vinegar, salt, sugar, oil and mustard seed. Bring to a boil and boil for 5 to 7 minutes.  Pour over the cabbage mixture while hot. Mix well.  Best if refrigerated for 24 hours.  Keeps well in refrigerator for a week.
