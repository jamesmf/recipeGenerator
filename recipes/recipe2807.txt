DINNER ROLLS


1  c. scalded milk
1  stick margarine
1  c. warm water
3  Tbsp. sugar
2  pkg. yeast
5  c. flour
1/2  c. sugar
3  beaten eggs
3  tsp. salt

      Melt margarine in scalded milk.  Refrigerate and let cool. Combine water, 3 tablespoons sugar and yeast.  Combine with cooled milk.  Add 5 to 6 cups flour, sugar, eggs and salt.  Keep sticky and let rise for about 1 1/2 hours.  Punch down and let rise again until double.
      Put flour on dough board and cut into rolls with glass.  Have a stick of margarine melted, dip rolls into margarine and fold over. Put into large pans and bake at 350 degrees; until browned.
