SOUTHWEST CHICKEN WRAPS


3  c. cooked chicken, chopped
8  oz. salsa
1 1/2  tsp. chili powder
1/4  tsp. garlic powder
1  c. shredded cheddar cheese
1  can black beans, drained
8  flour tortillas
1  medium tomato, diced
2  Tbsp. chopped green onions
1/2  c. guacamole, optional
1  tsp. chopped fresh cilantro, optional
1/2  c. sour cream, optional

      Combine chicken, salsa, chili powder, and garlic powder in a large skillet.  Mix well.  Cook over medium heat for 5 minutes. Remove from heat and add 3/4 c. cheese and can of black beans. Mix well.  Wrap tortillas in a kitchen towel and microwave on high for one minute.
      Spoon equal portions of chicken mixture and diced tomatoes onto each tortilla, including any of the optional ingredients. Roll up and serve immediately or save and warm up later.
