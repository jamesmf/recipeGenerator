EASY TOMATO ASPIC


1  c. boiling water
1 (3 oz.)  pkg. lemon gelatin
1  can tomato soup

      Dissolve gelatin in the boiling water.  Cool slightly.  Add soup, stirring to mix.  Pour into individual molds or a square glass dish.  Refrigerate.  Garnish with cottage cheese, stuffed olives or celery.
