APPLE CRANBERRY SALAD TOSS


1 (10 oz.)  pkg. salad greens
2  medium apples, sliced
1  c. walnut halves, toasted
1  c. dried cranberries
1/2  c. sliced green onions
3/4  c. Kraft Lite Done Right Raspberry Vinaigrette Dressing

      Toss salad greens, apples, walnuts, cranberries and onions in large salad bowl.  Add dressing, toss to coat.  Serve immediately.
