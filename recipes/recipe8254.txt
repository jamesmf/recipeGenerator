NO PEEK BEEF CASSEROLE


2  lb. beef stew meat (1-inch pieces)
1 (1 3/8 oz.)  envelope dry onion soup mix
1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can cream of mushroom soup
1 (4 oz.)  can whole mushrooms
1/2  c. quick cooking tapioca (red and white box)

	Combine all ingredients in crock-pot. Stir together well. Cover and cook 5 hours on high or 8 to 12 hours on low.
