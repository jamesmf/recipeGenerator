RUSSIAN CHICKEN


  Chicken breasts
  Canola oil for frying
  Russian Salad Dressing
1  can apricots, drained

      Brown chicken breasts in pan with canola oil.  Place in baking dish skin side up.  Brush Russian salad dressing over the chicken and add one can of drained apricots.  Bake at 350 degrees; until golden brown and tender (approximately 1/2 to 3/4 hour).  Serve over a bed of wild rice and garnish with steamed broccoli.
