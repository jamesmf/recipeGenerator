VANILLA PUDDING


2 3/4  c. milk
2  Tbsp. Sprinkle Sweet
2 1/2  Tbsp. cornstarch
1  tsp. vanilla

      Mix dry ingredients.  Add milk and stir until well mixed.  Cook over low-medium heat.  Bring to boil and boil gently 2 to 3 minutes, stirring constantly.  Remove from heat; stir in vanilla. Let stand 15 minutes.  Stir and chill until set.
      For Chocolate Pudding:  Add 2 tablespoons cocoa to dry mix.
      For Tapioca Pudding:  Add 1 tablespoon Minute tapioca to dry mix.  Let set 10 minutes before cooking.
