CRANBERRY MEATBALLS


1 (2 lb.)  pkg. frozen meatballs, thawed
1 (10 oz.)  jar apple jelly
1/2  c. cranberry juice cocktail
1/4  c. ketchup
2  Tbsp. brown sugar, packed
1 (12 oz.)  bottle chili sauce
2  Tbsp. lemon juice

      Place meatballs in a crock-pot. Mix remaining ingredients in bowl and pour over meatballs. Cook on low for 3 to 4 hours.
