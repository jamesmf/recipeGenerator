VIDALIA CHICKEN SALAD


4  c. chicken breast meat, chopped
1/4  c. chopped onion
1  lemon
3/4  c. mayonnaise
1  tsp. dry mustard
1/2  tsp. curry powder
1/2  c. slivered almonds, toasted
1  c. seedless grapes, halved

      Combine chicken meat, onion, grapes, and almonds in a large mixing bowl.  In a small bowl mix together mayonnaise, dry mustard, and curry powder.  Toss with chicken mixture to coat thoroughly.  Chill at least 1 hour to blend flavors.
