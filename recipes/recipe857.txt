CHICKEN CACCIATORE


1  (2 1/2 to 3 1/2 lb.) fryer, cut up
1/2  c. vegetable oil
1/2  c. chopped green onion
1/2  c. chopped celery
2  cloves garlic, chopped
1 (4 oz.)  can button mushrooms
1 1/2  c. canned tomatoes, strained
1/2 (6 oz.)  can tomato paste
1/2  c. dry sherry
2  tsp. sugar
  salt and pepper
2 1/2  c. water
3/4  lb. spaghetti

      Brown chicken in oil over medium heat; remove chicken.  Add onion and celery; fry slowly 15 minutes.  Stir in mushrooms, tomatoes, tomato paste, sherry, sugar and seasonings.  Add water and chicken; cook over low heat until chicken is tender.  Cook spaghetti; then remove chicken from sauce and place in serving dish.  Pour remaining sauce over drained spaghetti.
