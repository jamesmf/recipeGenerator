CHICKEN POT PIE


  3-4 chicken breasts or 1 whole chicken
2  cans mixed vegetables, drained
1  can whole corn, drained
2  cans cream of chicken soup
2 -3  cans of chicken broth

      Boil chicken until tender.  Save broth.  Mix all ingredients together.  Pour in 9 x 13 inch baking dish.  Add salt and pepper to taste.  Pour crust on top and bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour or until brown.
Crust:
1  stick butter (melted)
1  cup flour
1  cup milk

      Mix together until smooth. Pour on top of chicken mixture.
