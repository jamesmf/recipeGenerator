CREAM OF BROCCOLI SOUP


3/4  c. onion, diced
3  Tbsp. butter
6  c. water
6  chicken bouillon cubes
8  oz. fine noodles, cooked and drained
2  (10 oz.) bags broccoli, chopped
1/4  tsp. garlic
6  c. milk
8  oz. Velveeta cheese, cubed
