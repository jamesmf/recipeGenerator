CORN SALAD


2  cans shoepeg corn
2 small  cans tomatoes, drained
3/4  cup chopped cucumbers
3/4  cup chopped bell pepper
1/2  cup chopped onion

      Mix together.
Dressing:
2  T. vinegar
1/2  carton sour cream
4  T. mayonnaise
2  t. salt
1/2  t. dry mustard
1/2  t. black pepper
1/2  t. celery seed

      Mix together and pour over vegetables.
