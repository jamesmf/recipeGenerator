CUCUMBER SAUCE


1/2  c. mayonnaise
1/2  c. cucumber, peeled, seeded and shredded
1  tsp. grated lemon peel
2  tsp. fresh lemon juice
1  tsp. fresh dill or 1/4 tsp. dried dill weed
  salt to taste
  pepper to taste

      In a small mixing bowl, combine all ingredients, except salt and pepper.  Season to taste with salt and pepper.  Serve with fish!  Makes 1 cup.
