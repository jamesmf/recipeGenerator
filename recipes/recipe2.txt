CHOCOLATE KAHLUA CHEESECAKE

Crust:
1  c. graham cracker crumbs
1/4  c. butter, softened
2  Tbsp. sugar
1/2  tsp. cinnamon

 
Filling:
3 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
3/4  c. sugar
3  eggs
8  oz. semi-sweet chocolate
2  Tbsp. half and half
1  c. sour cream
1/4  c. very strong coffee
1/4  c. Kahlua
2  tsp. vanilla

      Crust:  Butter sides of 8 or 9-inch spring-form pan.  Mix first 4 ingredients with spoon and press crust mixture onto bottom and sides of pan.
      Filling:  Blend the cream cheese and sugar; add eggs, one at a time, blending until smooth after each addition.  Melt chocolate with half and half and add to cheese mixture.  Mix in sour cream, cooled coffee, Kahlua and vanilla.  Pour into crust in pan.  Bake 45 to 50 minutes at 350 degrees;.
