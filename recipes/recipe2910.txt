CHRISTMAS FUN FOR THE KIDS


2  c. flour
1/2  tsp. baking soda
1/2  tsp. salt
2  c. warm water
  cookie cutters
  small paint brush

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees;.  Blend all ingredients until well mixed. You may have to add more flour if too wet or more water if too dry. Flour working area and roll dough out until 1/4 inch thick.  Dip cookie cutter in the flour and cut out ornaments.  Place on lightly greased cookie sheet.  Use the end of the paint brush to punch a hole in the top to hang by.  Decorate with paints, glitter and ribbon.
