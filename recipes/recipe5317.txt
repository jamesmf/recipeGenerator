COOL PINK SALAD


1  can cherry or strawberry pie filling
1 large  can crushed pineapple, drained
1  can sweetened condensed milk
1  (12 oz.) container Cool Whip
3/4  c. walnut or pecan pieces

      Combine all ingredients and mix well. Chill in refrigerator until very firm. May be served as a congealed salad or put in 2 graham cracker crusts and served as a pie.
