TENNESSEE BEEF SKILLET


1  lb. ground beef
3/4  c. chopped onion
1 (16 oz.)  can tomatoes, cut up and drained
1 (15 oz.)  can kidney beans (undrained)
1/2  c. uncooked rice
1/2  c. water
3  Tbsp. chopped green pepper
1 1/2  tsp. chili powder
1/2  tsp. garlic salt
3/4  c. shredded cheese
  corn chips

      In a skillet, fry ground beef and onions.  Drain.  Stir in remaining ingredients except cheese and corn chips.  Bring to a boil; reduce heat, cover and simmer for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Serve over corn chips, then top with cheese.
