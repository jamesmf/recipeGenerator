CHOCOLATE GRAVY


3  Tbsp. flour
1/4  c. cocoa
1 1/4  c. sugar
  pinch of salt
1 large  can Milnot
1  c. water

      Sift dry ingredients; add Milnot and water. Cook over low heat, stirring frequently, until desired thickness. Add 1 tablespoon butter or oleo and 1/2 teaspoon vanilla. Delicious served hot over hot biscuits.
