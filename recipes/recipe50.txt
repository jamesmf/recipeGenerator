CHOCOLATE AMARETTO TRIFLE


1  pkg. devil's food cake mix (without pudding)
1 (3 oz.)  pkg. instant chocolate pudding
1 1/2  c. milk
1/2  c. Amaretto
12  oz. Cool Whip
5  or 6 crushed Heath bars (freeze before crushing)

      Prepare cake mix according to directions on package.  Bake in 9 x 13-inch pan and divide cake into halves.  Prepare pudding using 1 1/2 cups milk plus 1/2 cup Amaretto.  Cut one-half of cake into 1 or 2-inch squares; layer in bowl.  Then add one-half of the pudding, one-half of the candy bars and one-half of the Cool Whip. Repeat with second half of cake, pudding, candy bars and Cool Whip. Refrigerate at least 5 or 6 hours or overnight.  Serves 10 to 12.
