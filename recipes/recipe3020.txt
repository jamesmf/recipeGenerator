HOT BROCCOLI CHEESE DIP


2  c. shredded mild Cheddar cheese
1 (10 oz.)  pkg. frozen chopped broccoli, thawed and drained
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese, softened
1  c. sour cream
1  envelope Italian salad dressing mix

      Mix all ingredients until well blended.  Spoon into 9-inch pie plate.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 15 minutes or until cheese is melted. Garnish with green onions.  Serve with assorted vegetables or crackers.
