OVEN STEW


6  - 8 medium white potatoes, peeled & quartered
6  carrots, sliced
3  stalks celery, cut up
2  large onions, cut in quarters
3 - 4  lb. beef blade pot roast, cut into cubes
2  Tbsp. parsley flakes
2  Tbsp. tapioca
1 (48 oz.)  can V-8 juice

      Place vegetables, meat and seasoning in large Dutch oven or roasting pan.  Sprinkle tapioca on top and pour V-8 juice over the top.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 3 1/2 hours.  Stir a couple of times.  If juice is drying up, add more V-8 juice.  Serve over rice or noodles.
