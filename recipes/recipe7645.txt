CRISPY FRIED CHICKEN


  chicken pieces
  melted margarine
  finely crushed equal parts crackers and cornflakes
  salt and pepper

      Dip raw pieces of chicken  into  melted margarine, then roll in crumbs.  Place in well oiled, 9 x 13-inch baking dish, laying pieces side by side, not  crowding.  Sprinkle with salt and pepper.  Bake at 350 degrees; for an hour  or  until meat is brown and tender.
