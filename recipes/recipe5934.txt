SWEDISH CUCUMBER SALAD


2  cucumbers
1  small onion
1  Tbsp. vinegar
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. sugar
3/4  c. mayonnaise
1/2  c. sour cream
1  Tbsp. chopped fresh dill
1  Tbsp. chopped fresh parsley

      Slice cucumbers into mixing bowl.  Slice onion; add to cucumbers.  Toss with vinegar, salt and sugar.  Let stand 30 minutes.  Drain.  Mix mayonnaise, sour cream, dill and parsley. Add to cucumbers and mix well.
