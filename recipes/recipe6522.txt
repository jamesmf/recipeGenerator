LASAGNA


2  lb. ground beef
3 small  cans tomato sauce
12  oz. cottage cheese
2  eggs
6 large  slices Mozzarella cheese
  lasagna noodles
  garlic salt
  oregano
  Parmesan cheese

      Brown ground beef; drain. Add tomato sauce, garlic salt and oregano to taste. Mix cottage cheese and eggs. Layer beef mixture, noodles, beef mixture, egg, Mozzarella cheese, noodles, beef mixture, egg, Mozzarella cheese and beef mixture. Top with Parmesan cheese. Bake at 375 degrees; for 30 to 45 minutes.
