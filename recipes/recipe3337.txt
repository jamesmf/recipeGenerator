SWEET AND SOUR MEATBALLS


2  pkg. Armour frozen meatballs (20 oz.)
1 (16 oz.)  can sauerkraut
1  (14 oz.) chili sauce
2  c. brown sugar
1  can whole cranberry sauce

      Place meatballs in a 9 x 13 dish.  Blend last 4 items together.  Place on top of meatballs.  Heat at 350 degrees; for 1 hour.
