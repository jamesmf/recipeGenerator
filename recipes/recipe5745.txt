FRUIT SALAD


2  cans chunk pineapple
2  cans Mandarin oranges
1 large  jar maraschino cherries
3  to 4 bananas, sliced
2  pkg. vanilla instant pudding

      Drain fruit well; reserve pineapple juice.  Mix all fruit. Mix pudding mix and pineapple juice until smooth.  Add to fruit, tossing to coat all fruit.  Chill.
