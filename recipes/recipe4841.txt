FROZEN STRAWBERRY SALAD


1  (8 oz.) cream cheese, softened
2/3  c. sugar
1 (20 oz.)  can crushed pineapple, drained good
10  oz. strawberries, thawed
1  (12 oz.) Cool Whip
1/2  c. chopped walnuts

      Beat cream cheese and sugar until creamy.  Add strawberries and pineapple; mix well.  Fold in Cool Whip and nuts.  Put in loaf pan and freeze.  Take out of freezer 1 hour before serving.
