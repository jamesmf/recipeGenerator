HOT POTATO SALAD


8  large (not huge) potatoes peeled, cooked, and cubed
1  lb. Velveeta cheese, cubed
1  c. Hellmann mayonnaise
1/2  c. chopped onion
7  stripes of bacon, fried crisp, and crumbled
1 small  jar of stuff olives, sliced
2  c. Fritos, crumbled

      Mix potatoes, cheese, mayonnaise, and onions.  Put into greased 9 x 13 casserole dish.  Sprinkle on crumbled bacon, olives, and Fritos in that order.  Bake 350 degrees; oven for 30 minutes.
