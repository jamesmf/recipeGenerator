HASH BROWN CASSEROLE


1  lb. hash browns
1  can cream of chicken soup
8  oz. sour cream
2  c. Cheddar cheese, shredded
1/2  large onion, chopped (optional)
  salt and pepper (optional)
1  stick butter, melted
3  c. corn flakes

      Rinse frozen hash browns. Mix hash browns, soup, sour cream, cheese, onion, 6 tablespoons melted butter, salt and pepper. Spread in 4-quart baking dish. Mix 2 tablespoons melted butter and corn flakes. Spread on top of other mixture. Bake at 350 degrees; for 50 minutes.
