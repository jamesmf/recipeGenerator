KIELBASA BITES


3/4  cup - chili sauce
3/4  cup - grape jelly
1  1/2 tsp - soy sauce
1/2  tsp - cinnamon
1/2  tsp - nutmeg
1/2  tsp - ginger
2  tsp - dry mustard
4  tsp - red wine
1  1/2 lbs - cooked kielbasa, cut into 1 inch pieces

      Mix together chili sauce, jelly, spices, wine, mustard and soy sauce in a saucepan until mixture can be smoothly blended.  Add kielbasa and heat until kielbasa is hot.  Serve with toothpicks. Re-heated leftovers even taste better.
