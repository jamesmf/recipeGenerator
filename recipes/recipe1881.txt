PEPPER STEAK


2  lb. top round steak
2  large green peppers
2  onions, sliced
2  cloves garlic, minced
3  fresh tomatoes
2  Tbsp. olive oil
1/2  tsp. cumin, ground
  salt and pepper

      Cut round steak into long strips.  Cut green peppers into strips.  Cut tomatoes into quarters.  Lightly brown the meat in oil; add peppers, onion, garlic, tomatoes and cumin, then salt and pepper to taste.  Cover and cook for 20 minutes.  Serve with rice.
