PINEAPPLE SYRUP


2  c. unsweetened pineapple juice
1/2  c. apple juice
2  Tbsp. cornstarch
1/2  c. water
1 small  can crushed pineapple

      Heat together pineapple juice and apple juice.  Mix together cornstarch and water.  Add to hot juice and heat until thickened. Add crushed pineapple.  Stir.  Serve over pancakes, waffles or French toast.
