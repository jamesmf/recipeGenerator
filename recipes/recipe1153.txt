BAKED TOMATOES


6  firm tomatoes
2  Tbsp. margarine
1/4  c. bread crumbs
1/4  c. wheat germ
2  Tbsp. seasoned salt

      Wash and dry tomatoes, remove top portion from each.  Melt margarine and add remaining ingredients.  Place tomatoes in baking dish and cover with topping.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 10 to 15 minutes or until barely tender.  These can be prepared well in advance and stored in refrigerator before baking.  Serves 6.
      Exchanges per serving:  1 bread, 1 fat.
