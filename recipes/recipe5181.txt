TEMPURA


2  cups cold water
2  cups sifted, all-purpose flour
1  cup cornstarch
1/4  cup vegetable oil
2  eggs
1  tsp. white pepper
2  tsp. salt
2  tsp. baking powder

      In a large bowl, combine ingredients and mix until smooth. Let stand for 15 minutes.
      Use to fry shrimp-peeled, deveined and butterflied, or thinly slice chicken.  Also great with vegetables including: cauliflowerets, eggplant, onions, green peppers and sweet potato. Heat oil in deep fryer to 350 degrees;F.  If items are watery, sprinkle them with a little flour before coating.  Dip items in batter and coat thoroughly, allowing excess batter to drop into bowl.  Fry a few pieces at a time until golden brown, 2-3 minutes.  Check to make sure meat items are thoroughly cooked.
       Great as finger foods or for use in oriental dishes.  Serve with you favorite cocktail or hot sauce.
