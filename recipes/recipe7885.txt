SHAMI


2  lbs. beef stew
2  handfuls channa dal
2  med. onions, coarsely chopped
2  Tbsp. ginger/garlic paste
2  tsp. cayenne pepper
2  tsp. salt
1 small  bunch cilantro
3  cloves
5  cardamom pods
1/2  tsp. shah zeera (caraway)
  1-2 eggs

      Mix all of the ingredients and cook with water, 1 hour in pressure cooker or 2 hours on stovetop, over low heat.  Grind in food processor and add 1-2 eggs.  Make 2-inch balls; flatten into patties and pan-fry.
