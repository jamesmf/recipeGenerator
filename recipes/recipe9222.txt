SLOW COOKED LAMB CHOPS


4  bacon strips
4  lamb shoulder blade chops, trimmed
2 1/4  c. thinly sliced peeled potatoes
1  c. thinly sliced carrots
1/2  tsp. dried rosemary, crushed
1/4  tsp. garlic powder
1/4  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
1/4  c. chopped onion
2  garlic cloves, minced
1  can (10 3/4 oz.) cream of mushroom soup, undiluted
1/3  c. milk
1  jar (4 1/2 oz.) sliced mushrooms, drained

      Wrap bacon around lamb chops; secure with toothpicks.  Place in a slow cooker.  Cover and cook on high for 1 1/2 hours.  Remove chops; discard toothpicks and bacon.  Drain liquid from slow cooker.  Add potatoes and carrots; top with lamb chops.  Sprinkle with rosemary, garlic powder, salt, pepper, onion and garlic.  In a bowl, combine soup and milk; mix well.  Add mushrooms and stir. Pour over the chops.  Cover and cook on low for 4 to 6 hours or until meat and vegetables are tender.  Yield: 4 servings.
