APPLESAUCE DOUGHNUTS


1  egg, beaten
4  pkg. Sweet 'N Low
1/2  tsp. vanilla
1  tsp. lemon juice
2  tsp. melted butter
1/3  c. unsweetened applesauce
1/4  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. nutmeg
1/2  tsp. baking powder
1 1/3  c. flour
1/2  tsp. baking soda
1/4  tsp. cinnamon

      Mix egg and Sweet 'N Low together.  Add vanilla, butter and applesauce.  Mix well.  Sift dry ingredients and add to applesauce mixture.  Stir by hand.  Place dough in a bowl or on wax paper. Chill and roll out 1/2-inch on a lightly floured board.  Cut out with a floured cutter.  Heat oil; fry several at a time.  Drain on paper towels.  Can be frozen.
