ENSALADA VALENCIANA
(Valenciana Salad)  

1  large head endive or lettuce, chopped
  olives, pitted (as much as desired)
1  clove garlic, minced
1  finely minced onion
1  tomato, sliced
  salt
1  hard-boiled egg
3  Tbsp. oil
2  Tbsp. vinegar
1/4  lb. tuna, flaked

      Soak endive or lettuce in water; drain.  Place in salad bowl with olives, garlic, onion, tomato and salt.  Separate egg yolk from white.  Mash yolk.  Add oil and vinegar gradually.  Chop egg white; add tuna to salad.  Salt to taste; pour egg yolk mixture over salad.  Yield:  4 servings.
