WHITE CHICKEN CHILI


6  c. chicken stock
1  lb. Great Northern Beans (soaked overnight)
2  medium onions, chopped
2  garlic cloves, minced
6  c. cooked chicken, diced
1  c. salsa
2  jalapenos, seeded and diced
2  chili peppers, diced
1 1/2  tsp. oregano
2  tsp. cumin
1/4  tsp. cayenne pepper
1  Tbsp. vegetable oil
  Salt to taste
  Sour cream (optional)
  Monterey Jack Cheese (optional)

      Simmer beans, half the onions, and half the garlic 2 hrs in the chicken stock or until the beans soften, stirring frequently. Add chicken and salsa. Saut√© pepper, spices, and the remaining onions and garlic in the oil and add to the chili. Simmer for one more hour. Garnish with Sour cream or Monterey Jack Cheese.
      Can substitute 1/2 to 1 can of Rotel depending on your taste for the jalapenos and chilies.
