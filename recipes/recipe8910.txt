STUFFED CHICKEN BREASTS


2  c. Ricotta cheese
1  egg
1/2  lb. fresh spinach, washed, dried and chopped
2  cloves garlic, finely chopped
1/2  c. grated Parmesan cheese
1/2  c. grated Swiss cheese
  salt and pepper
4  whole chicken breasts with skin

      Mix all ingredients except chicken. Rinse and pat chicken breasts dry. Separate skin from flesh, leaving skin attached at center of each breast. Place a fourth of stuffing under the skin of each breast. Tuck skin edges under; secure with toothpicks if necessary. Place breasts in a baking pan skin side up and dot with butter. Bake at 350 degrees; about 35 to 45 minutes, basting every 10 minutes with juices.
