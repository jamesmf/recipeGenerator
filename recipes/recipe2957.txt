MINI HAM AND CHEESE BISCUITS


3  pkg. Pepperidge Farm dinner rolls (24 each)
1 1/2  pkg. Sunnyland sliced ham
2 (8 oz.)  pkg. shredded Mozzarella cheese
3  sticks margarine
4  Tbsp. poppy seeds
3  Tbsp. mustard
3  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  small onion, diced

      Chop sliced ham into very small pieces. Mix with shredded cheese. Take each package of rolls and slice in half; remove top and place ham and cheese mixture on bottom half. Put top back on.
       In a saucepan, combine remaining ingredients and melt butter. With rolls in original pan, pour sauce on top of rolls. Cover with aluminum foil. Heat in oven at 325 degrees; for 20 to 30 minutes or until thoroughly heated. Slice and serve warm.
