DIVINITY


2  c. sugar
1/2  c. light corn syrup
1/2  c. hot water
1/4  tsp. salt
2  egg whites
1  tsp. vanilla
1/2  c. chopped nuts

      Cook and stir sugar, corn syrup, water and salt until mixture comes to boil.  Cook to 250 degrees; without stirring.  Remove from heat. Set aside.  Immediately beat egg whites until stiff.  Pour hot syrup slowly over egg whites, beating constantly at high speed, about 5 minutes.  Add vanilla and beat until mixture forms soft peaks and begins to lose gloss.  Add chopped nuts.  Drop by spoonfuls on waxed paper.
