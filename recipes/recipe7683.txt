POPOVER PIZZA


1  lb. ground beef
1  large onion, chopped
1 (1.5 oz.)  pkg. spaghetti sauce mix
1 (15 oz.)  can tomato sauce
1/2  c. water
2  eggs
2  c. (8 oz.) shredded Mozzarella cheese
1  c. milk
1  Tbsp. oil
1  c. all-purpose flour
1/4  tsp. salt
1/2  c. grated Parmesan cheese

      Cook ground beef and onion in  a  large  skillet  until meat is browned, stirring to crumble  meat;  drain.  Add sauce mix, tomato sauce and water, stirring  well.  Simmer  10  minutes. Spoon meat mixture into a lightly  greased  9 x 13-inch pan; sprinkle with Mozzarella cheese.
      Beat  eggs, milk and oil until  foamy.  Add  flour  and salt. Beat until smooth.  Pour  batter  over  meat  mixture. Spread evenly.  Sprinkle with Parmesan  cheese.  Bake  at 400 degrees; for 30 minutes or until the top is  puffed  and  golden brown. Serve hot.
