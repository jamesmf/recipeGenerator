FRUIT SALAD DRESSING


2  c. small curd cottage cheese
1/2  c. diet strawberry jam
1/2  tsp. ground ginger
1/4  c. plain yogurt

      Stir cottage cheese together until well mixed; add and mix well the jam and ground ginger.  Add yogurt and blend well in blender. Serve over bite size chunks of banana, apple, pear and other fruit. Makes about 1 1/2 cups.
