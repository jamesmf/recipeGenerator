ZUCCHINI BREAD


2  c. sugar
3  eggs
1  c. oil
3  c. flour
1  tsp. vanilla
1  tsp. baking soda
1/2  tsp. baking powder
1  tsp. salt
1 scant  tsp. cinnamon
2  c. grated zucchini
1  c. raisins (steam and let sit for 1 hour; drain)
  nuts, cherries or pineapple (optional)

      Beat sugar, eggs and oil until fluffy.  Add flour, vanilla, baking soda, baking powder, salt and cinnamon.  Stir in zucchini and raisins.  Fill 2 or 3 loaf pans 1/2 full.  Bake at 325 degrees; for 3/4 to 1 hour.
