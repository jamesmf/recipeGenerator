HEARTY BEEF AND VEGETABLE CASSEROLE


4  c. thinly sliced potatoes
2  or 3 carrots, diced
1/4  c. chopped onion
1  lb. hamburger
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1/2  c. milk
2  Tbsp. catsup

      Boil  potatoes and carrots in salted  water  15  to  20 minutes.   Combine  soup,  milk and  catsup.  Brown  meat  and onion.   (With  canned  hamburger, heat  hamburger,  cook  the onions with potatoes.) In 2 1/2 quart dish alternate layers of vegetables, soup mixture and meat,  ending  with soup mixture. Cover and bake in a preheated 325 degrees; oven  for  30 to 35 minutes or until thoroughly heated.
