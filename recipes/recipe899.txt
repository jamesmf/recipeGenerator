SHRIMP DIP



2 (4 1/2 oz.)  cans shrimp, cooked and deveined
1  c. mayonnaise
1  c. sour cream
1/4  c. green onions
2  Tbsp. lemon juice
1/4  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper, ground
2  dashes of Tabasco sauce

      Stir all ingredients until mixed well.  Cover and chill. Serve with assorted crackers.  Makes 3 cups.
