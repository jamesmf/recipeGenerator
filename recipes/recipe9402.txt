BUTTERMILK PECAN CHICKEN


1  lb. Chicken Breasts for Milanesa, cut 1/4 inch thick
3/4  c. low-fat buttermilk
1  egg
2  Tbsp. oil
1  Tbsp. butter
1 3/4  c. dry breadcrumbs
2  tsp. Morton & Bassett Paprika
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. black pepper
1/2  c. pecans, finely chopped

      Whisk buttermilk with egg in a shallow bowl.  Combine breadcrumbs, paprika, salt, pepper and pecans on a separate plate or sheet of wax paper.  Dip chicken in buttermilk mixture, then place in breading; turn to coat both sides.  Heat oil and butter in a large non-stick skillet over medium-high heat; tilt skillet to coat bottom.  Add chicken and cook 3 to 5 minutes per side or until browned and crusty.  Remove from skillet when internal temperature of chicken reaches 165 degrees.  Serve immediately.
