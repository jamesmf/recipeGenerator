GOLDEN BAKED CHICKEN


2  c. mashed potato flakes
3/4  c. grated parmesan cheese
2  Tbsp. dried parsley flakes
1  Tbsp. paprika
3/4  tsp. garlic salt
3/4  tsp. onion powder
1/2  tsp. pepper
3  broiler/fryer chickens (3 to 4 lb. each), cut up and skin removed
1  c. butter or margarine, melted

      In a bowl, combine the potato flakes, parmesan cheese, parsley, paprika, garlic salt, onion powder and pepper.  Dip chicken into butter, then into potato flake mixture.  Place on 2 greased 15 x 10 x 1 inch baking pans.  Bake at 375 degrees; for 50 to 60 minutes or until juices run clear.  Yield: 12 servings.
