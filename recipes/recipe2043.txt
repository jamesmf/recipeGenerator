APRICOT DIVINITY


3  c. sugar
3/4  c. water
3/4  c. light corn syrup
1 (3 oz.)  pkg. apricot flavored gelatin
2  egg whites (room temperature)

      Combine sugar, water and corn syrup in a 3-quart saucepan. Cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until sugar dissolves. Cook over high heat without stirring until mixture reaches hard ball stage.
      Combine gelatin and egg whites in a large mixing bowl.  Beat until stiff peaks form.  Pour the sugar mixture in a very thin stream over egg whites while beating constantly at high speed of electric mixer.  Continue beating 5 to 10 minutes, until mixture holds its shape.
