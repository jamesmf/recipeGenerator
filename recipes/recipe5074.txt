GREEN PEA SALAD


1  can English peas, drained
1/4  c. almonds, slivered
1/2  c. sharp Cheddar cheese, cut into small cubes
1/4  c. mayonnaise
  pepper
1/2  c. diced celery
1/2  c. sweet pickles
  salt
1  Tbsp. finely chopped onion

      Mix English peas, celery, almonds, pickles, cheese, mayonnaise, salt, pepper and onion.
