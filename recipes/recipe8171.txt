GRANNY'S CHICKEN POT PIE


1  fryer
1  can Veg-All, drained
1  can English peas, drained
1  can cream of mushroom soup
  pie crust

      Cook chicken until tender; reserve the broth. After cooled, remove meat from bones. In large pot, put chicken broth, Veg-All, peas, soup, salt and pepper to taste. Add water if needed. Boil 5 to 10 minutes. Pour into 13 x 9 baking dish. Keep baking dish over the heat and let mixture continue to boil.
      Roll out pie crust dough 1/8-inch thick; cut into strips and drop small pieces into boiling mixture. Boil for 5 minutes. Cover the top of pie with strips of pie crust dough. Cook in oven at 400 degrees; until crust is golden brown.
