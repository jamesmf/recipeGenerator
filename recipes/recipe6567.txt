LATTICE TOP CHICKEN


1  can cream of potato soup
1  c. milk
2  c. cheese
1  can Durkee onions
1 (4 oz.)  pkg. crescent rolls
1  bag frozen veggies (broccoli, carrots, cauliflower)
2  c. cut up cooked diced chicken

      Combine soup, milk, chicken, veggies, 1/2 of cheese and 1/2 of French onions. Place in 8 x 12 baking pan. Bake, covered, at 375 degrees; for 20 minutes. Uncover. Separate crescent rolls into 2 rectangles. Cut into strips. Place on top crisscross of mixture. Bake 15 minutes. Top lattice with remaining cheese and onions. Bake, uncovered, 3 to 5 minutes until brown.
