CHICKEN SALAD (HOT)


2  c. chicken or turkey (I bake chicken breasts in pan covered with foil at 400~ for 1 hour)
2  c. chopped celery (small pieces)
1  tsp. grated onion
2  Tbsp. lemon juice
1  c. mayo (Hellmann's)
1/2  c. nuts (almonds or pecans)

      Mix together and put in casserole. Top with 1/2 cup grated cheese and 1 cup crushed potato chips. Bake at 350 degrees; for 25 minutes until chips are lightly browned.
