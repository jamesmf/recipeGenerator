BANANA BREAD



2  Tbsp. oleo
2  c. flour
1/8  tsp. salt
1 1/2  tsp. soda
2/3  c. fructose
2  eggs, lightly beaten
1  tsp. vanilla
1/2  c. buttermilk
3  ripe bananas, mashed

      Cream oleo and 1 cup flour mixed with salt, soda and fructose. Add beaten eggs and mix well.  Add rest of flour and buttermilk alternately, then vanilla and mashed bananas.  Put in greased pan (bottom only) and bake at 350 degrees;.
