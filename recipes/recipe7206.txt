VENISON JERKY


3  lb. venison
1  Tbsp. salt
1  Tbsp. onion powder
1  tsp. black pepper
1  c. Worcestershire sauce
3  Tbsp. liquid smoke

      Cut meat into small strips.  Marinate overnight in the seasoning mixture, stirring several times.  Drain; pat with paper towel.  Place meat on shallow pan or on rack or hang by toothpicks.  Place in oven at 140 degrees; for several hours until meat is dry.
