OKLAHOMA BABY BACK RIBS


4  lb. baby back ribs
1/2  c. honey
1/2  c. mustard
1/2  c. red wine vinegar
2  Tbsp. vegetable oil
4  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
2  tsp. butter
2  tsp. pepper
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. hot pepper sauce

      Place ribs, meat side up, in roasting pan.  Bake, uncovered, at 325 degrees; for 2 hours; drain.  Combine remaining ingredients in saucepan.  Bring to a boil over medium heat; simmer, uncovered, 30 minutes.  Cool at room temperature for 1 hour.  Brush sauce over ribs.  Reduce oven to 300 degrees; and bake, uncovered, basting occasionally, for 1 to 1 1/2 hours longer.
