BILL'S MEAT LOAF


3 to 5  lb. hamburger meat
  Salt
  Pepper
  Garlic salt
1  onion, chopped
1  bell pepper, chopped finely
1  egg
1  can tomatoes, crushed by hand (reserve liquid)
  Several crackers

      Mix all ingredients and put in loaf pan.  Punch holes in top. Put ketchup on top with liquid from tomatoes.  Cover with foil. Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour.  Remove foil and pour off liquid.  Bake uncovered for about 30 minutes until done.  Let cool for a few minutes and serve.
