MARGARITA COCKTAILS


1  can frozen lemonade concentrate (6 oz.)
6  oz. sweet and sour mix
1  egg white
5 to 6  oz. tequila
2 to 3  oz. Triple Sec

      Combine all ingredients in blender.  Fill with ice to suit and blend well.  Dip the rim of glasses first in lemon juice, then in salt.  Fill glasses with cocktail and serve.
