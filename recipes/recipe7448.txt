SCALLOP FRITTERS


1  lb. fresh or frozen scallops
1 1/3  c. sifted flour
2  tsp. baking powder
3/4  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper
1/2  c. scallop broth
2  eggs, slightly beaten

      Defrost scallops if frozen and dice.  Simmer 5 minutes in water to cover.  Drain and save 1/2 cup broth.  Mix and sift flour, baking powder, salt and pepper.  Add to diced scallops with broth and eggs; mix well.  Drop by tablespoons into a little hot vegetable oil.  Brown on both sides.  Drain on absorbent paper. Makes about 18.  Serve with canned tomato mushroom sauce heated to serving temperature.  Makes 6 servings.
