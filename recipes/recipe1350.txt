TEA STRAWBERRY SLUSH


4  tea bags (caffeine free)
1 to 1 1/2  c. frozen strawberries
1/2 (6 oz.)  can frozen lemonade (Adams)
1  c. ice cubes

      Steep tea using 2 cups of water and chill.  In a blender, combine tea, strawberries and lemonade concentrate.  Process until smooth.  With blender running, gradually add about 1 cup ice cubes, processing until ice is melted.  Garnish with fresh fruit.  Serve immediately.  Serves 4.
