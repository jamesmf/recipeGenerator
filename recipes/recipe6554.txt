CHICKEN MACARONI CASSEROLE


1/2  c. chopped onion
3  Tbsp. butter or oleo, melted
2 (10 3/4 oz.)  cans cream of chicken or celery
2  c. shredded Cheddar cheese, divided
1  c. milk
3 1/2  c. chopped cooked chicken
2 1/2  c. cooked macaroni
1/4  c. Ritz crackers, crushed

      Saut√© onion in butter in large skillet until tender. Add soups and 1 1/2 cups cheese. Gradually add milk. Cook over medium heat until cheese melts. Stir in chicken and macaroni. Pour mixture into greased 2 1/2-quart casserole. Sprinkle with cracker crumbs. Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes or until heated. Top with remaining 1/2 cup cheese and bake an additional 5 minutes.
