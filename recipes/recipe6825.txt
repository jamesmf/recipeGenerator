HALUPSIE (GERMAN DISH)


2  c. rice
2  lb. ground beef
2  eggs
1/2  c. milk
1  c. mashed tomatoes
1  medium onion
  salt and pepper to taste
  cabbage leaves
1  pt. mashed tomatoes

      Boil the rice and drain. Add the ground beef, eggs, milk, 1 cup mashed tomatoes, 1 medium onion, salt and pepper to taste. Place cabbage leaves in boiling water a few minutes. Put filling in cabbage leaves and roll together. Put in roaster. Pour 1 pint mashed tomatoes over the top and bake at 300 degrees; until cabbage is done.
