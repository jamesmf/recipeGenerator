FREEZER ZUCCHINI


7  c. sliced zucchini, or mixture of zucchini & yellow summer squash
1  c. green mango, chopped or sliced
1  c. onion, chopped or sliced
2  c. sugar
1  c. white vinegar
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. celery salt

      In a large bowl mix zucchini, mango and onion.  In another bowl, mix sugar, vinegar, salt and celery salt.  Pour over zucchini mixture and store in refrigerator 4 to 5 days.  Stir once a day.  Then put in containers and freeze.
