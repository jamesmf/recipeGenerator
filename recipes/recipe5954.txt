JAMBALAYA


1/2  c. diced ham
2  c. shrimp, cleaned and cooked
1/2  c. chopped onion
1/2  c. chopped green pepper
1  crushed garlic clove
2  Tbsp. oil
3 1/2  c. crushed tomatoes
1 1/2  c. water
1/2  tsp. thyme leaves
1/4  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper
1  bay leaf
3/4  c. long grain white rice

      Saut√© onion, green pepper and garlic in oil in large saucepan. Stir in ham, crushed tomatoes, water, seasonings and rice.  Heat to boiling.  Reduce heat; boil gently covered for 20 to 25 minutes or until rice is tender.  Stir occasionally.  Add shrimp.  Cook 5 more minutes.  Remove bay leaf and serve.
