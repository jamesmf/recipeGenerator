MACARONI PIZZA STYLE


1  lb. ground beef
1/2  c. chopped onion
8  oz. small elbow macaroni, cooked and drained
1  (15 oz.) Hunt's tomato sauce with tomato bits
1/4  c. water
1/2  tsp. garlic salt
1/2  tsp. basil
1/4  tsp. oregano
2  c. mozzarella cheese

      Brown beef and onion.  Mix seasonings in water.  Mix browned beef, cooked macaroni, seasonings and tomato sauce.  Pour 1/2 mixture in Pyrex dish; add 1/2 of cheese.  Pour remaining meat mixture and top with cheese.  Makes two 8 x 8 or one 8 x 12 inch pyrex dishes.  Freezes well for another meal.
