FRUIT SALAD


1  can pineapple tidbits
1  can Mandarin oranges
1 small  jar cherries
1 small  pkg. instant vanilla pudding
4  bananas
1/2  c. orange juice

	Empty pudding into saucepan. Add orange juice and juices from pineapple and oranges. Bring to a boil and cool. Cut up bananas. Cover with lemon juice. Drain off lemon juice and discard. Combine bananas, oranges, pineapple and cherries. Pour cooled pudding mixture over fruit; mix lightly and chill.
