CAMPBELL'S 1-DISH CHICKEN AND RICE BAKE


1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can Campbell's cream of mushroom soup
1  c. water
3/4  c. uncooked, white, long grain rice
1/4  tsp. each paprika and pepper
4  boneless chicken breasts

      Mix soup, water, rice, paprika and pepper in 2-quart shallow baking dish. Top with chicken. Season with additional paprika and pepper. Cover. Bake at 375 degrees; for 45 minutes or until done. Serves 4.
      For creamier rice, increase water to 1 1/3 cups.
