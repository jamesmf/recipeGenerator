DADDY'S FAVORITE


1  lb. hamburger
1  can tomato soup
1  can green beans
3  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/2  c. warm milk
1  egg, beaten
5  medium peeled potatoes, cut into small pieces

      Brown hamburger. May add a little onion and/or salt and pepper if you like. Add soup, green beans and Worcestershire sauce to hamburger once it is browned. Can either use remaining ingredients to make mashed potatoes or use instant, depending on your time frame. Bake at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes.
