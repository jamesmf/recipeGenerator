SEVEN LAYER SALAD

Salad:
1/2  c. shredded onion
1/2  c. chopped celery
1/2  c. shredded green pepper
2  shredded carrots
1 small  can early English peas
1/2  lb. shredded cheese
  bacon bits

      Layer in large bowl the ingredients.
Dressing:
1 1/2  c. mayonnaise
1  pkg. Hidden Valley dressing mix
1 1/2  c. buttermilk

      Combine ingredients in a small bowl. After you have layered salad ingredients in bowl, pour dressing over and let stand overnight.
