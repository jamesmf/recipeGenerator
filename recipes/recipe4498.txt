DUCK POND SALAD


3  large chicken breasts
1  pkg. chicken flavored Rice-A-Roni
2 small  jars Cara Mia artichoke hearts (6 oz. jars), drained (save juice)
1 or 2  bunches green onions, sliced
3/4  c. diced celery
1/2  c. diced green bell pepper
1  Tbsp. curry powder
1/2  c. mayonnaise or Miracle Whip

      Mix thoroughly the curry, mayonnaise and juice from artichokes. Bake chicken until tender and then cut up into bite size pieces. Cook rice according to the package directions; let cool. Cut artichoke hearts into pieces. Mix ingredients together and refrigerate overnight for maximum flavor.
