CORN BREAD SALAD


1  can diced tomatoes
1  1/2 medium peppers, diced
1  large diced onion
1  can whole kernel corn, drained
2  Tbsp. mayonnaise
8  oz. sour cream
2  Tbsp. sour cream
  cheddar cheese, shredded
  Real bacon bits

      Make corn bread; crumble in separate bowl.  Combine vegetable ingredients with mayonnaise and 2 Tbsp. sour cream.  Layer corn bread and vegetable mixture; then sprinkle on a little cheddar cheese and bacon bits.  Continue layering until ingredients are gone.  Spread  8 oz. sour cream on top layer; cover with 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese and some bacon bits.  Refrigerate overnight, if possible.
