BAKED FISH WITH PAPAYA SALSA


1 1/2 to 2  lb. thick, firm, fleshy fish fillets (sea bass, snapper, halibut, orange roughy)
1  c. Papaya Salsa

      Preheat the oven to 350 degrees;.  Place the fish in a baking dish. Spread the salsa on top.  Cover with foil and bake for 10 minutes. Remove the foil and continue to bake for 5 to 10 minutes longer or until the fish is tender and flaky.  Serve hot, garnished with cilantro sprigs.  Serves 4.
