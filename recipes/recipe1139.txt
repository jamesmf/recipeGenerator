TURKEY CROQUETTES


1  lb. ground turkey
1  Tbsp. chopped onion
1/2  Tbsp. Mrs. Dash, salt free
1  c. crushed wheat or corn flakes

      Mix well the turkey, onion and Mrs. Dash.  Shape into 3 ounce patties.  Roll in cereal and place on baking sheet.  Bake in 400 degrees; oven for 45 minutes or until fork will remove clean.
      For each 3 ounce serving this contains:  150 calories, 15 grams protein, 12 grams fat and 5 grams carbohydrates.
