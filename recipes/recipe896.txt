SPICY SHRIMP


1/4  c. margarine
3  Tbsp. minced parsley
2  cloves garlic, minced
1/2  c. clam juice
1  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  tsp. lemon juice
1/4  tsp. crushed red pepper
1/2  tsp. dried oregano
1/2  tsp. basil or thyme
1/4  tsp. pepper
1/4  tsp. salt
1 1/3  lb. medium shrimp (unshelled)

      In large skillet, melt margarine.  Saut√© parsley and garlic for 1 minute.  Add clam juice, Worcestershire sauce, lemon juice and remaining seasonings; mix well.  Add shrimp and cook over medium heat, stirring often, until they turn pink, 4 to 5 minutes. Remove shells while eating.
