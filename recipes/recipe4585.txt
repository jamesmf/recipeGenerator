BETTER THAN POTATO SALAD


4  c. cooked long grain rice
8  radishes, sliced
4  hard boiled eggs, chopped
1  medium cucumber, seeded and chopped
2  c. thinly sliced celery
1/2  c. chopped onion
1 1/2  c. mayonnaise
3  Tbsp. prepared mustard
3/4  tsp. salt

      In large bowl, combine rice, radishes, eggs, cucumber, celery, and onion. Make a dressing of mayonnaise, mustard, and salt; mix well and pour over rice mixture; toss. Cover and refrigerate at least 1 hour. Yields 12 to 14 servings.
