SALISBURY STEAK
(8 Carbs/Serving)  

2  lb. ground beef
2/3  c. onion, finely chopped
2  egg whites, slightly beaten
1/4  c. milk
3  Tbsp. horseradish
1  pkg. McCormick brown gravy mix

      In medium bowl, combine ground beef, onion, egg whites, milk, horseradish, salt and pepper, mixing lightly, but thoroughly. Shape into 4 oval 1/2-inch thick patties. Heat large nonstick skillet over medium heat until hot. Place beef patties in skillet; cook 7 to 8 minutes or until centers are no longer pink, turning once. Remove from skillet; keep warm.
       Make gravy according to directions. Pour over patties. Serves 4.
