BROCCOLI SALAD


2  bunches broccoli
1/2  c. raisins
1/2  c. pecans
1/2  c. red onions, chopped
1  c. mayonnaise
1/2  c. sugar
2  Tbsp. apple cider vinegar
12  slices bacon, cooked and drained

      Mix all ingredients except bacon together.  Let stand overnight.  Add bacon just before serving.
