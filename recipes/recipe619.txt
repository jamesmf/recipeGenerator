GOLDEN CROWNED CAULIFLOWER


1  medium cauliflower
1  c. low calorie mayonnaise
1  tsp. prepared mustard
1  c. low calorie grated cheese

      Steam cauliflower in a small amount of water until tender-crisp.  Combine mayonnaise and mustard.  Spread over drained cauliflower which has been placed in glass baking dish.  Sprinkle grated cheese on top.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 10 minutes or until cheese melts and topping "puffs" slightly.  Yields 6 servings; contains 100 calories per serving.
