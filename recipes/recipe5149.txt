CRUNCH SALAD


16  oz. cole slaw mix
1  c. toasted sliced almonds
1  c. roasted sunflower seeds
1  pkg. Beef Ramen Noodle Soup mix
1  bunch green onions (optional)

 
Dressing:
1/2  c. sugar
1/2  c. vegetable oil
1/2  c. vinegar
  Seasoning pkg. from Beef Ramen Noodle Soup Mix

      Mix dressing ingredients and refrigerate overnight.  Mix chilled dressing with salad ingredients and serve immediately.
