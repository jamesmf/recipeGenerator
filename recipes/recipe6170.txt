FROZEN CRANBERRY SALAD


1  can cranberry sauce
8  oz. cream cheese
3  Tbsp. mayonnaise
1  tsp. lemon juice
1 small  can crushed pineapple
1  c. pecans, chopped
1  (8 oz.) Cool Whip

	Mix with mixer the cranberry sauce and cream cheese. Add all other ingredients, stirring until Cool Whip is smooth. Freeze overnight.
