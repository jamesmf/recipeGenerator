BOSTON COOKIES


1  c. shortening
1 1/2  c. brown sugar
3  eggs
1/2  tsp. salt
1  tsp. cinnamon
1  tsp. soda mixed with 2 Tbsp. hot water
3  c. flour
1  c. raisins
1  c. nuts

      Cream shortening and brown sugar.  Add eggs, salt, cinnamon and soda with water mixture.  Stir in flour.  Add raisins and nuts. Drop onto greased cookie sheet.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 15 minutes.
