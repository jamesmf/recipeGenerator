BLT SALAD


1/4  cup buttermilk
1/4  cup mayonnaise
1/4  cup sour cream
1  Tbsp. fresh parsley chopped
1/4  tsp dried basil or oregano
1/8  tsp. garlic powder
8  cups torn lettuce
1  large tomato (sliced)
8 bacon  slices crisply cooked and crumbled

      Dressing:  Blend together buttermilk, mayonnaise, sour cream, parsley, basil, oregano, and garlic powder.
       Refrigerate 1 - 2 hours or until chilled.  Combine lettuce, tomato, bacon, toss with dressing.
