STRAWBERRY PRETZEL SALAD


2  c. crushed pretzels
3  Tbsp. sugar
3/4  c. melted butter
8  oz. cream cheese, softened
9  oz. Cool Whip
1/2  c. sugar
2 (3 oz.)  boxes strawberry Jell-O
2  c. boiling water
2 (10 oz.)  pkg. strawberries

      Mix pretzels, sugar and melted butter.  Spread in a 9 x 13-inch pan.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 10 minutes.  Cool.
      Cream the cream cheese, Cool Whip and sugar together.  Spread over pretzel crust.
      Dissolve the Jell-O in boiling water.  Add strawberries.  Let cool a little.  Spread on cream cheese layer.  Chill several hours.
