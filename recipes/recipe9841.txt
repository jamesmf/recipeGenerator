FRIED SWEET POTATOES


7  to 8 sweet potatoes, boiled, peeled and cut crosswise into 1/4-inch rounds
  salt and pepper
1/4  c. bacon drippings
  sugar

      Season potatoes with salt and pepper and chill.  Fry in bacon drippings and brown on each side.  Drain on paper towels; coat each side with sugar and serve very hot.
