SPICY BLACK BEAN RICE


1 (10 oz.)  can Rotel (or other brand diced tomatoes and green chiles)
1 (15 oz.)  can black beans
  Minute Rice (or other instant rice)
  Tortilla, corn chips, or  tortilla chips
  Pepperjack cheese

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees;.  Pour Rotel and beans into casserole dish.  Fill empty Rotel can with uncooked rice and stir into dish. Bake at 350 degrees; for about a half-hour or until rice is cooked.  Top with slices or shreds of pepperjack cheese.  Serve on tortillas, as dip with low-fat corn ships or alone as an entree.
