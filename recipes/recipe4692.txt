CHILI


1  lb. ground beef
1  medium onion
1  lb. can kidney beans
1  lb. tomatoes
1  Tbsp. chili powder
1  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper
1 (6 oz.)  can tomato paste
1/2  pkg. chili seasoning

      Brown ground beef and onion; drain.  Add other ingredients. Cook over medium heat until bubbling.  Turn to low and simmer 1 1/2 to 2 hours.
