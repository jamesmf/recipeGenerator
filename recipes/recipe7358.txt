STUFFED BEEF SHELLS


12  pasta stuffing shells
1  lb. ground beef
1  jar picante sauce (your choice, medium or hot)
1/2  c. water
1 (8 oz.)  can tomato sauce
1 (4 oz.)  can green chilies, drained
1  c. Monterey Jack cheese
1  can Durkee French onions

      Brown ground beef.  Combine picante sauce, water and tomato sauce.  Stir 1/2 of chilies, 1/2 cup of cheese and 1/2 can of onions; mix well.  Pour 1/2 of remaining sauce on bottom of a 10-inch round baking dish.  Stuff shells with beef mixture.  Top with remaining cheese and chilies.  Bake, covered, at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes.  Bake, uncovered, for 5 minutes longer.
