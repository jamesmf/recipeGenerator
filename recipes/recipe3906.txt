SHRIMP SPREAD FOR SANDWICHES


1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
1  Tbsp. lemon juice
1  small onion, grated
1/2  bell pepper, grated
1/4  c. celery, grated
1  lb. shrimp, cooked
1/4  c. mayonnaise

      Let cream cheese get room temperature; add lemon juice, onion, pepper and celery. Chop shrimp in food processor and add to cream cheese mixture. Add mayonnaise. Chill. Serve with crackers or spread on little rounds of bread.
