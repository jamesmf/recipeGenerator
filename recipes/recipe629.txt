BANANA NUT BREAD


1 1/4  c. flour
7  pkg. Sweet 'N Low
1  tsp. baking powder
3/4  tsp. baking soda
1  c. or 3 bananas, mashed
1  c. nuts
1/2  tsp. salt
1/3  c. margarine
2  eggs

      Grease and flour loaf pan.  Mix and stir all the ingredients together.  Bake 55 minutes in 350 degrees; oven until toothpick comes out clean.  Can be also made with chopped dates.
