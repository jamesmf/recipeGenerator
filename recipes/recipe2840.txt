FARM HOUSE BREAKFAST


2  doz. sausage links (pork), browned and drained
1  doz. eggs, softly scrambled
1  pt. cottage cheese
1  can undiluted Cheddar cheese soup

      Cut links into fourths; spread on bottom of ungreased 9 x 13-inch pan (glass).  Cover with scrambled eggs.  Cover with cottage cheese, then cover with Cheddar cheese soup.  Cover pan and refrigerate overnight.  Bake at 325 degrees; for 50 to 60 minutes, uncovered.  Serve hot with toast, muffins or bread.  Simple and elegant.
