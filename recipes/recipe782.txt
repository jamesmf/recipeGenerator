BAKED CHICKEN SALAD


2  Tbsp. butter or margarine
1  c. thinly sliced celery
1/2  c. chopped onion
1/2  c. mayonnaise
1/2  c. sour cream
1  Tbsp. lemon juice
1/2  tsp. salt
1/8  tsp. pepper
2  c. cubed, cooked chicken
1/2  c. slivered, toasted almonds
1 (4 oz.)  can sliced mushrooms, drained
1/4  c. potato chips

      In large skillet, melt butter over medium heat.  Add celery and onion; cook about 4 minutes.  Remove from heat.  Stir in mayonnaise, sour cream, lemon juice, salt and pepper until well blended.  Add chicken, almonds and mushrooms; toss to coat well. Spoon into 1 1/2-quart casserole.  Sprinkle with potato chips. Bake in 325 degrees; oven for 25 to 30 minutes.  Serves 6.
