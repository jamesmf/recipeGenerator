SEVEN LAYER SALAD


1  head lettuce, shredded
2  cucumbers, chopped
1  c. chopped celery
1  c. chopped pepper
1 1/2  c. chopped onions
8  strips bacon, cooked, drained and crumbled
1  box frozen peas
1 1/2  c. mayonnaise
2  Tbsp. sugar
2 1/2  c. shredded Cheddar cheese

      Arrange lettuce in bottom of trifle bowl.  In order and in layers, add cucumbers, celery, peppers, onions and peas.  Do not toss; leave in layers.  Spread mayonnaise evenly over layers of peas.  Sprinkle with sugar and cheese.  Cover and refrigerate at least 4 hours.  Sprinkle bacon over salad dressing before serving. Makes 10 to 12 servings.
