CREAM OF POTATO SOUP


1/4  c. onions, chopped
2  Tbsp. margarine or butter
1  c. milk
1  (8 oz.) cream cheese, cubed
1  can cream of celery soup
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  can Cheddar cheese soup
  dash of lemon juice
  salt and pepper to taste
4  c. parboiled potatoes, cubed
3/4  c. boiling water
1  chicken bouillon cube

	Saute onions in butter. Add milk and cream cheese. Mix until smooth. Add remaining ingredients. Simmer about 1 hour. Top with bacon bits and Cheddar cheese.
