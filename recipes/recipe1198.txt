LEMON CHEESECAKE


1  graham cracker crust
1  (3 oz.) lemon Jell-O (no sugar)
2  Tbsp. lemon juice
1  c. boiling water
1  pt. carton cottage cheese
8  oz. Cool Whip

      Mix Jell-O, lemon juice and water in blender.  Add cottage cheese and pour into bowl.  Fold in Cool Whip.  Pour into crust shell and refrigerate.
      One third the calories of regular cheesecake.
