CUCUMBER-CABBAGE SALAD

Salad:
1  cucumber, chopped
1/2  green pepper
1  c. onion
2  c. cabbage, chopped
1/2  box macaroni rings

      Boil macaroni rings and drain, cool.  In mixing bowl, add cucumbers, green pepper, onion, cabbage and noodles.
Dressing:
1/2  c. mayonnaise
1/4  c. vinegar
  sugar to taste
  dash of salt and pepper

      Mix together and pour over the ingredients in mixing bowl. Refrigerate for 4 hours.
