CHICKEN SALAD SUPREME


2 1/2  c. diced cold cooked chicken
1  c. celery, chopped fine
1  c. sliced white grapes
1/2  c. shredded browned almonds
1  tsp. salt
1  Tbsp. minced parsley
1  c. mayonnaise
1/2  c. whipping cream, whipped

      Combine and serve in lettuce cups with thin slices of chicken on top. Garnish with stuffed olives, sliced thin, or chopped ripe olives.
