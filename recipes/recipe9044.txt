NELL'S TURKEY BASTE


1  c. oil
1 1/2  Tbsp. Kitchen Bouquet
2  Tbsp. paprika
2  Tbsp. garlic
1 1/2  Tbsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper

      Mix all ingredients together and rub under skin of turkey, all over. Grease inside of brown bag. Place on a rack. Place turkey inside of bag. Close with paper clips. Put pan under rack and bake. After cooking appropriate time, remove bag and return to oven to brown.
