PEAR RELISH


1  peck pears
5  c. vinegar
4  c. sugar
5  medium onions
1  Tbsp. pickling spice
3  red peppers
3  green peppers
  hot pepper
3  tsp. turmeric

      Grind everything in food chopper except turmeric and pickling spice. Put into pot; boil 30 minutes. Add turmeric just before taking up. Should make 6 pints.
      Tie pickling spice in cloth and add while boiling.
