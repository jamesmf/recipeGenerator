PUMPKIN SPICE BREAD


3 1/2  c. White Lily self-rising flour
1  tsp. cinnamon
1/2  tsp. each:  cloves, nutmeg and allspice
2 1/2  c. sugar
1/2  c. packed brown sugar
1  c. canola oil
4  eggs, beaten
1 (16 oz.)  can pumpkin

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees;.  Grease and flour two 9 x 5 x 3-inch loaf pans.  Mix all dry ingredients together and add sugar, oil, eggs and pumpkin.  Pour batter into prepared pans and bake for 55 minutes or until done.
