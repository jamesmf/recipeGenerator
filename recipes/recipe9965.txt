DIXIE'S SOUTHERN CORN


4  cans white Shoepeg corn (Green Giant), drained
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. cream cheese
1  stick (1/2 c.) butter
  jalapeno peppers, chopped fine (use peppers according to your desire of hotness)

      Microwave cream cheese and butter together. Add corn and chopped peppers. Heat in microwave until bubbly and creamy.
