HOT CHICKEN SALAD


2  c. chicken or turkey
2  c. diced celery
2  Tbsp. lemon juice
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  c. toasted almonds
2  tsp. grated onion
1  c. mayonnaise (not Miracle Whip)

      Combine all ingredients and pile lightly into a baking dish. Sprinkle with 1/2 cup grated cheese and 1 cup crushed potato chips. Bake at 450 degrees; for 10 to 15 minutes.
