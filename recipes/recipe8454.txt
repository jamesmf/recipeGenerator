STOVE TOP MEAT LOAF


1 16  oz. pkg.  Stove Top Stuffing (any variety)
2  lbs. lean ground beef
1/2  c. Kraft original BBQ sauce, divided in half
2  eggs, beaten
1  c. water

      Mix water and all ingredients except 1/4 cup of barbecue sauce. Shape meat mixture into oval loaf in a 9 x 13 inch baking dish, top with remaining 1/4 cup barbecue sauce.  Bake at 375 degrees; for 1 hour or until center is no longer pink.  Makes 6 to 8 servings.
