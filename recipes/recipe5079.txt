COLD SLAW


1  cabbage
2  c. sugar
1/2  c. oil
2  medium onions
1  tsp. celery seed
4  tsp. sugar
1  tsp. dry mustard
1  tsp. salt
1  c. vinegar

      Shred cabbage. Slice onions. Place on top of cabbage. Sprinkle with 2 cups sugar. Put oil, vinegar, dry mustard, celery seed, salt and 4 teaspoons sugar in pan; bring to a boil. Pour over cabbage. Cover with lid or foil to steam. Allow to cool and refrigerate 4 hours before serving.
