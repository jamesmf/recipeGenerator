BEEF STEW


6  to 8 medium potatoes
3  to 4 carrots
1  medium onion
1  Tbsp. margarine
  dash of red pepper
1  beef bouillon cube
1  c. hot water
  salt to taste
1  can Bunker Hill rib eye gravy

      Peel and dice potatoes, carrots and onion.  Cover with water and cook until done.  Add remaining ingredients and simmer a few minutes.  Serve over cooked rice or sliced bread.
