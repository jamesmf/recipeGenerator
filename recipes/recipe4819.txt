VEGETABLE BARLEY SOUP


  any cut beef or bone
2  beef bouillon cubes
1 large  can V-8 juice
1  can chopped tomatoes
  chopped cabbage
  chopped onions and celery
  assorted vegetables (prefer fresh)
  barley

      Cover beef with water in large pot and cook about 20 minutes after water starts to boil.  Skim top.  Remove beef, cut into pieces and put back in stock.  Add V-8 juice, tomatoes and all other veggies.  Cook about 1 hour or until veggies are soft.  Add barley to pot of soup and cook according to package or cook barley in separate pot.
