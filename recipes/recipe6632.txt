QUICK DINNER


1  lb. hamburger
1  medium onion
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. macaroni, cooked and drained
1 large  can Italian crushed tomatoes
  Salt & pepper to taste

      Brown hamburger and onions together, add to the cooked macaroni, add tomatoes and cook for 1/2 hour on low.  Serve with salad and breadsticks.  (You can add cheese if you like.)  Makes enough for 4 - 6 people.  DELICIOUS.
