CREAM OF BROCCOLI SOUP


2  stalks broccoli
2  c. cold water
4  chicken bouillon cubes
1  c. light cream
1  c. milk
1  Tbsp. flour
1  c. American cheese, shredded

      Cook broccoli in water with bouillon cubes until tender. Blend cream, milk, flour and cheese in blender.  Mix slowly with broccoli mixture in pan.  Return to blender and whip for 1 minute. Return to pan and cook for 20 minutes (simmer low).
