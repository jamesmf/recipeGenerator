NATCHEZ COOKIES


2  sticks margarine
1  c. sugar
1  egg, separated
2  c. flour
2  tsp. cinnamon
1  c. nuts

      Cream margarine, sugar and egg yolk; beat well.  Add flour and cinnamon; mix well.  Beat egg white until foamy; spread batter thin on cookie sheet.  Brush egg white on top; sprinkle with chopped nuts.  Bake at 300 degrees; for 25 to 30 minutes.  When done, remove from cookie sheet at once.
