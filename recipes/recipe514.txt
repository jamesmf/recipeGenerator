CARROT COOKIES


1/2  c. boiling water
1/2  c. raisins
3/4 to 1  c. brown sugar
1/2  c. shortening
1  egg, unbeaten
1  tsp. lemon extract
1/2  c. finely shredded raw carrots
1 1/2  c. sifted all-purpose flour
2  tsp. baking powder
1/2  tsp. salt

      Pour boiling water over raisins and soak 5 minutes; drain. Combine sugar, shortening, egg and lemon extract in mixing bowl; beat well.  Stir in carrots and drained raisins.  Sift together flour, baking powder and salt; add to first mixture and blend well. Drop by teaspoonfuls onto greased baking sheet.  Bake at 375 degrees; for 10 to 12 minutes.  Makes 3 dozen.
