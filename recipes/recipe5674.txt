SEVEN-UP SALAD


1 large  pkg. cream cheese, softened
1  c. boiling water
1 small  pkg. lemon jello
1  tsp. vanilla
1 small  can crushed pineapple, drained
1  tsp. sugar
1  c. 7-Up  (cold)
1  c. pecans, chopped

	In a large bowl, mix the cream cheese, water and jello until well blended.  Add remaining ingredients and mix well.  Refrigerate before serving.
