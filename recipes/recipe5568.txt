ITALIAN MEATBALL SOUP


8  oz. refrigerated or frozen cooked Italian-seasoned or other meatballs
1  can (15 1/4 oz.) Del Monte sweet peas, drained
1  can (14 1/2 oz.) Del Monte diced tomatoes with basil, garlic and oregano
1  can (14 1/2 oz.) beef broth
  fresh basil leaves (optional)

      Combine all ingredients in large saucepan. Bring to boil; reduce heat. Simmer, uncovered about 10 minutes or until meatballs are heated through. Garnish with basil, if desired.
