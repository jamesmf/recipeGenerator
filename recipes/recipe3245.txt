BEEF AND BACON ROLL-UPS


1  lb - bacon halved lengthwise
2  lb - smoked cocktail sausage
1  lb - light brown sugar

      Wrap a slice of bacon around each sausage and secure with a toothpick.  Layer the wrapped franks and brown sugar half at a time in a slow cooker.  Cook on low for 3 to 4 hours stirring occasionally.
