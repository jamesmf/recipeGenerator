CAESAR SALAD


1/4  c. olive oil
1  clove garlic mint
4  tsp. fresh lemon juice
1/2  tsp. worcestershire sauce
1/4  tsp. freshly ground pepper
1  Tbsp. chopped anchovy fillets

      Assemble and mix ingredients.
Salad:
8  c. torn romaine
1  c. croutons
1/2  c. parmesan cheese

      Toss salad with dressing, add croutons and cheese, toss lightly.
