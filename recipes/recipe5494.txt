ROMAINE SALAD


1  c. walnuts
4  Tbsp. butter
4  green onions, chopped
1  bunch broccoli, chopped
1  head romaine lettuce
  Ramen noodles (not seasoning pkg.)

 
Dressing:
1  c. sugar
1/2  c. red wine vinegar
1  Tbsp. soy sauce
1  c. vegetable oil
  salt and pepper to taste

      Break lettuce into pieces.  Brown walnuts and noodles in butter.  Cool on paper towels.  Mix all ingredients together.  Mix all ingredients for the dressing.  Pour over the salad mixture and toss well.
