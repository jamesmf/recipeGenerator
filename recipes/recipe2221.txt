OLD FASHION EGG SALAD


1/4  c. mayonnaise
2  tsp. lemon juice
1  tsp. chopped onions
1/4  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
6  hard-boiled eggs, chopped
1/2  c. celery, chopped fine

      In a bowl, combine mayonnaise, lemon juice, onions, salt and pepper.  Stir in eggs and celery.  Cover and chill.
