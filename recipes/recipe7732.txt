CHICKEN ENCHILADAS


4  chicken breasts, cooked and cut up
2  cans chopped chilies
1  pt. sour cream
1  lb. Monterey Jack cheese, grated
1  c. Cheddar cheese, grated
2  cans cream of chicken soup
1  large onion, chopped
10  large 10-inch flour tortillas
  black olives

      Mix chicken, sour cream, Monterey Jack cheese, onion, chilies and soup. Set aside 1/3 of this mixture. Spoon remaining mixture into tortillas and roll. Place in large pan. Cover with the 1/3 mixture. Sprinkle with Cheddar cheese and olives. Bake at 350 degrees; for 35 to 40 minutes.
