TURKEY ALEXANDRA


4  c. cooked turkey or chicken, cubed
3  oz. medium noodles, cooked and drained
1/3  c. onion, chopped
1  Tbsp parsley, chopped
1/3  c. slivered almonds, toasted
1/4  c. Sherry
2 (10 3/4 oz.)  cans cream of mushroom soup
2  c. sour cream

 
Topping:
1/3  c. dry bread crumbs
1/4  tsp. rosemary
1  Tbsp melted butter or margarine
1/4  tsp. tarragon
2  tsp. parmesan cheese

	Mix turkey, cooked noodles, chopped onion, parsley, slivered almonds, Sherry, mushroom soup and sour cream.  Pour into greased 13 x 9-inch pan.  For topping, mix together dry bread crumbs, rosemary, melted butter or margarine, tarragon, cheese, and sprinkle over turkey mixture.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 35 to 40 minutes.
