STUFFED CLAMS


3/4  c. onions
1 (4 oz.)  can chopped mushrooms, drained
1/4  c. melted butter
3  Tbsp. flour
  salt
  dash of Worcestershire sauce
2  cans minced clams (do not drain)
  breadcrumbs

      Cook mushrooms and onion until tender; blend in seasonings. Thicken with flour. Put filling in oiled clam shells. Sprinkle with breadcrumbs.  Bake at 400 degrees; for 15 minutes.
