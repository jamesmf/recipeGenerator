MAMA WOODARD'S CHILI SAUCE


1  gal. chopped ripe tomatoes
1/2  c. chopped white onions
1/2  c. sweet green pepper
1/2  c. sweet red pepper
5  tsp. salt
1/2  c. brown sugar
1/2  c. white sugar
1/2  tsp. cayenne pepper
1  tsp. nutmeg
2  tsp. ginger
1  tsp. cinnamon
1  tsp. mustard seed
1  qt. vinegar

      Chop and peel tomatoes and onions.  Chop and add peppers. Combine remaining ingredients (except vinegar).  Add and bring the mixture to boiling.  Cook to desired consistency, about 2 hours. Add vinegar and boil a few minutes longer.  Put in jars and seal.
