HIDDEN VALLEY RANCH QUESADILLAS


1  pkg. Hidden Valley Ranch
3/4  c. mayonnaise
1/3  c. milk
1/3  c. chunky salsa
3  c. Monterey Jack cheese
10  flour tortillas
  vegetable oil

      Blend first 5 ingredients.  Refrigerate 1 hour.  Spread over 5 tortillas.  Place top.  Brush tops with oil.  Broil until golden brown.  Turn.  Broil until cheese is melted.  Cut into 8 wedges.
