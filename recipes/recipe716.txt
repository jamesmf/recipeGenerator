MOSTACCIOLI AND SAUSAGE


1 1/2  lb. link Italian sausage, sliced
1  c. chopped onion
3/4  c. chopped green pepper
2 (26 oz.)  jars Classico pasta sauce, any flavor
1/2  c. grated Parmesan cheese
1 (1 lb.)  pkg. mostaccioli, cooked and drained
2  Tbsp. olive oil

      In large saucepan, brown sausage; pour off fat.  Add onion and green pepper; cook and stir until tender.  Add pasta sauce and Parmesan cheese.  Bring to a boil; reduce heat.  Cover and simmer 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Toss hot cooked mostaccioli with oil to coat.  Serve with sauce.  Garnish as desired.  Makes 6 to 8 servings.
