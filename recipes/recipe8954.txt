TATER TOT CASSEROLE


1  lb. ground beef, cooked and drained
1  medium bag frozen Tater Tots
2  cans cream of mushroom soup or chicken
  grated cheese

      Spray pan with Pam. Layer the bottom with a row of Tater Tots. Mix soup and ground beef together. Pour on top of Tots. Top with cheese. Bake at 350 degrees; until cheese is melted.
