POTATO-TOPPED CHEESEBURGER CASSEROLE


1/2  lb. lean ground beef
1  small onion
1  Tbsp. melted shortening
1/2  tsp. salt
  dash of black pepper
1/2  tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1 (16 oz.)  can whole kernel corn, drained
1/2  c. shredded cheese
1/4  c. plus 2 Tbsp. catsup
1  egg yolk, slightly beaten
3/4  c. mashed potatoes

      Brown beef and onion in shortening; drain. Add salt, pepper, Worcestershire sauce, corn, cheese and catsup; mix well. Spoon into greased 1-quart casserole dish. Blend egg yolk and potatoes; spread over meat mixture or spoon around edge. Bake at 350 degrees; for 25 to 30 minutes. For more crisp topping, place under broiler for several minutes.
