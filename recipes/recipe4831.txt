CHINESE CABBAGE SALAD


1 (1 lb.)  pkg. pre-cut cole slaw
5  green onions
1  c. sunflower seeds, roasted
1  c. almonds, roasted
1 (3 oz.)  pkg. Ramen noodles, crushed
1/3  c. oil
1/4  c. white vinegar
1/4  c. sugar

      Salad:  Slice green onions.  Mix cole slaw, onions, sunflower seeds, almonds and noodles.
      Dressing:  Mix oil, vinegar, sugar and dry seasoning packet from Ramen noodles.  Toss dressing with salad.
      Note:  For a low-sodium version, omit seasoning packet.
