PORK CHOPS AND GRAVY


  thin pork chops
  mustard
  salt
  pepper
  flour
  oil
1  can chicken with rice soup
  hot biscuits

      Rub mustard on both side of pork chops; roll chops in flour that has been seasoned with salt and pepper. Brown chops slowly in skillet with oil. Drain oil out of skillet and add chicken with rice soup. Simmer; serve with or over biscuits.
