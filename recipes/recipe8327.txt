CHICKEN POT PIE II


1  pie crust
1/3  c. onion, chopped
1/2  tsp. salt
1 1/2  c. chicken broth
2/3  c. milk
2  c. frozen mixed vegetables, thawed
1/3  c. margarine
1/3  c. flour
1/4  tsp. pepper
1  can cream of chicken soup
2 1/2 to 3  c. cooked chicken, shredded

	In a medium saucepan, melt margarine over medium heat. Add onion; cook 2 minutes. Stir in flour, salt and pepper. Stir in broth and milk. Cook, stirring constantly, until bubbly and thickened. Add chicken and vegetables. Remove from heat and spoon into crust. Bake at 425 degrees; for 30 to 40 minutes.
