FRUITY MILK SHAKE


1 1/4  c. skim milk
2  cut-up frozen bananas
1/2  c. low-fat cream-style cottage cheese or yogurt
1  c. chopped fresh or frozen strawberries, peaches or blueberries
1  Tbsp. honey

      Combine all ingredients in blender and blend until smooth, adding 5 ice cubes, one at a time.  Makes 4 servings.
