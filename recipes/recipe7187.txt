CHICKEN PUFFS


1/2  c. butter
1  c. flour
1/4  tsp. salt
4  eggs

 
Filling:
  diced chicken, cooked
  diced celery
  diced onion
  salt
  pepper
  mayonnaise

      Melt butter in cup of boiling water; add flour and salt. Stir vigorously.  Cook and stir until mixture forms ball that doesn't separate.   Remove from heat; cool slightly.  Add eggs, one at a time, beating after each one until smooth.  Drop onto greased baking sheet using 1 level teaspoon dough for each puff. Bake at 400 degrees; for 20 minutes.  Remove, split and fill.
