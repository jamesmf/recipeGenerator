THREE CORN CASSEROLE


1  can cream-style corn
1  can whole kernel corn, drained
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. corn meal/bread mix
1  (16 oz.) container sour cream
1  stick butter/margarine, melted

      Mix 2 cans of corn and sour cream and melted butter/margarine in large bowl; one slightly beaten egg is optional. May add salt and pepper to taste. Pour corn mixture into casserole dish. Sprinkle corn meal/bread mix on top and fold in just until moistened. Bake at 350 degrees; for 50 minutes or until golden brown. Casserole will be firm but not dry.
