SALLIE'S CUCUMBER SALAD


2  to 3 cucumbers
  salt to taste
1/2  c. white vinegar
1/2  c. sour cream with chives
1/3  c. sugar
1  red onion, chopped

      Slice cucumbers and sprinkle with salt.  Place in refrigerator.  Mix all other ingredients.  Remove cucumbers from refrigerator and drain.  Mix everything together.  Refrigerate, stirring several times throughout the day before serving.
