HERBED MUSHROOMS
(In Cold Tomato Dressing)  

1  lb. large button mushrooms

 
Marinade:
1  c. olive oil
4  garlic cloves, minced
1  Tbsp. crushed red pepper
1  Tbsp. chopped fresh parsley
1  tsp. chopped fresh basil
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper

 
Cold Tomato Dressing:
3  tomatoes, peeled, seeded and chopped
3  shallots, minced
1/4  c. sherry
1/4  c. tomato juice
1/4  c. olive oil
  juice of 1 lime
2  Tbsp. chopped fresh parsley
2  Tbsp. chopped fresh basil

      Combine all marinade ingredients in a glass bowl.  Add mushrooms; toss and let marinate for 1 hour or more at room temperature.
      For the dressing, combine all ingredients in a bowl and refrigerate for 1 to 4 hours.  When ready to grill, divide marinated mushrooms among 4 skewers.  Grill 4 to 5 minutes per side.  Divide Cold Tomato Dressing among 4 serving plates and top each with skewer of mushrooms.  Serves four as appetizer or vegetable side dish.
