CHOCOLATE SOMBRERO CAKE


1  pkg. chocolate cake mix
1  pkg. instant chocolate pudding
1/2  c. cooking oil
1/4  c. coffee brandy
1/4  c. vodka
3/4  c. milk
4  eggs

      Mix all ingredients and beat for 4 minutes.  Pour into well-greased and lightly floured Bundt pan.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 45 to 50 minutes or until done.  Dust with confectioners sugar or frost with glaze.
Glaze:
1 1/2  Tbsp. vodka
1 1/2  Tbsp. milk
1 1/2  tsp. coffee brandy
1  c. confectioners sugar

      Mix and drizzle over warm cake.
