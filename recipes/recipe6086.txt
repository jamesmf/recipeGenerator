FRUIT SALAD


11  oz. (1 can) Mandarin oranges
15  oz. (1 can) apricots
15  oz. (1 can) pears
16  oz. (1 pkg.) strawberries (frozen)
6  oz. (1 can) orange juice concentrate
2  to 3 bananas, sliced

      Drain Mandarin oranges.  Drain and chop apricots and pears. Mix all ingredients together.  Refrigerate several hours before serving.
      Serving suggestions:  As a topping on cake; breakfast waffles and pancakes; or as a salad with ham or turkey.
