CRAB SWISS BITES


1 (8 oz.)  can crab meat, drained and flaked
1  Tbsp. grated onion
4  oz. shredded Swiss cheese
1/2  c. mayonnaise
1  tsp. lemon juice
1/4  tsp. curry powder
1  pkg. flaky style refrigerator rolls
1  can sliced water chestnuts

      Mix first 6 ingredients.  Separate each roll into three parts and place on cookie sheet.  Spoon on crab meat mixture.  Top with slice of water chestnut.  Bake at 400 degrees; for 10 minutes.  Makes 36.
