TUNA SPREAD


2 (7 oz.)  cans white or albacore tuna in water
4  oz. low-fat cream cheese (Neufchatel), cut into 6 pieces
2  Tbsp. mayonnaise (can use low-fat)
2  Tbsp. fresh lemon juice
1/4  c. sliced scallions
1/2  tsp. cayenne pepper
1/2  c. chopped parsley
1  Tbsp. soy sauce (can use low sodium)
1  Tbsp. sherry

      Mix tuna in blender or food processor until broken.  Add remaining ingredients and mix until fairly smooth; don't puree. Refrigerate until slightly thickened, about 1 hour.  Serve with whole grain crackers and fresh vegetables.
