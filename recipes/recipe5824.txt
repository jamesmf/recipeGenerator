UNDER-THE-SEA SALAD


1  pkg. lime Jell-O
1  pkg. lemon Jell-O
1 small  pkg. cream cheese
1/2  pt. whipping cream
1 large  can crushed pineapple

      Mix both packages Jell-O with 2 cups very hot water. Add cream cheese and 1 cup cold water. Mix well by hand. Stir in can of pineapple. Whip the cream and fold into mixture. Refrigerate overnight.
