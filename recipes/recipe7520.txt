CHILE RELLENOS BAKE


1/2  lb. lean ground beef
1/2  lb. pork sausage
1  c. onion, chopped
2  cloves garlic, minced
2  cans (4-oz. each) whole green chiles, drained
2  c. grated cheddar cheese, divided
4  eggs
1/4  c. flour
1 1/2  c. milk
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. Tabasco sauce

      In large skillet, crumble together beef, sausage, onion and garlic.  Cook over medium heat, stirring, until meat is browned; drain.  Line 9-inch by 13-inch baking dish with half of the chiles; top with 1 1/2 cups cheese.  Add meat mixture, top with remaining chiles; set aside.
      Beat together eggs and flour until smooth; add milk, salt and Tabasco.  Blend well.  Pour egg mixture over casserole.  Bake, uncovered at 350 degrees; for 40 minutes or until knife inserted comes out clean.  Sprinkle remaining 1/2 cup cheese on top.  Let stand 5 minutes before serving.  Serves 4.
