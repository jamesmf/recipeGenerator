COCA-COLA SALAD


1  can dark sour cherries
1  can crushed pineapple
1  c. pecans, chopped
1 (10 oz.)  bottle Coca-Cola
1 small  pkg. strawberry Jell-O
1 small  pkg. cherry Jell-O
1 large  pkg. cream cheese

      Drain juice from pineapple and cherries. Heat (do not boil) juice. Remove from heat. Dissolve strawberry and cherry Jell-Os in juice, then melt cream cheese with fork. Add pineapple, cherries, cream cheese, pecans and Coke. Chill in refrigerator for about 1 hour.
