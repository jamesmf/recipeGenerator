LEMON-BASIL MAYONNAISE


2  c. mayonnaise
1  garlic clove, crushed
1/2  c. firmly packed fresh basil leaves
1  Tbsp. grated lemon rind

      Process mayonnaise and garlic clove in a blender or food processor until smooth.  Add basil and lemon rind; pulse 3 or 4 times or until basil is finely chopped.  Store covered in refrigerator up to 1 week.  Makes 2 cups.  Good with fish.
