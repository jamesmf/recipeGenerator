APPLE-GRAPE SALAD

Dressing:
1  1/2 cups sugar
1/2  cup flour
1  1/2 cups water
1  tsp. vanilla
1  tsp. butter

 
Salad:
6  cups chopped apples
2  cups grapes
1  cup walnuts

      Bring the sugar, flour, water, vanilla, and butter to a boil and cook until thick.  Pour the cooked mixture over the apples, grapes, and walnuts.  You may add other fruit to your taste.
