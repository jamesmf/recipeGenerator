GOAT CHEESE QUESADILLAS

Salsa:
1  small yellow onion, chopped
2  large garlic cloves, chopped
1  Tbsp. olive oil
2  large roasted red bell peppers
2  Tbsp. chopped fresh basil
1/4  tsp. balsamic vinegar

 
Quesadillas:
6  (8-inch) flour tortillas
5 1/2  oz. soft goat cheese, divided in 3 portions
2  Tbsp. basil pesto
1  scallion (green part only), minced

      To Make Salsa: In nonstick skillet over medium-high heat, saut√© onion and garlic in hot oil until softened. Transfer mixture to blender or food processor. Add peppers, basil and vinegar; puree until smooth. Season with salt and set aside.
      To Make Quesadillas:  Spread each of 3 tortillas with 1 portion cheese and 2 tablespoons pesto.  Sprinkle with scallions; season with ground pepper.  Top with other tortilla; press together.  Heat a large nonstick skillet coated with cooking spray over medium-high heat.  Toast in skillet until golden on both sides (about 3 minutes).  Cut each quesadilla into 8 pieces (wedges).  Serve with salsa.  Makes 8 servings (3 wedges per serving); 216 calories, 8gm protein, 24gm carbohydrate, 10gm fat.
