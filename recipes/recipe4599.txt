MARSHMALLOW CHEESE SALAD


1  lb. marshmallows
1  pt. cottage cheese
1  can crushed pineapple, drained
8  oz. pkg. cream cheese
1/2  c. milk
1  c. whipped cream
1  c. chopped nuts

      Melt marshmallows and milk in double boiler. Cream two cheeses. Add melted marshmallows, slightly cooled. Cool. Add pineapple and nuts. Fold in whipped cream. Chill.
