TURKEY KIDNEY BEAN SALAD


1  c. cooked turkey, chopped
1 1/4  c. kidney beans, canned and drained
1/3  c. chopped sweet pickle
2/3  c. coarsely chopped celery
1  Tbsp. finely chopped onion
2  hard-cooked eggs, diced
1  tsp. salt
3  Tbsp. mayonnaise
1  tsp. prepared mustard
1  tsp. pickle liquid

      Combine all ingredients; toss lightly.  Chill at least 1 hour to blend flavors.  Yields 6 (1/2-cup) servings and contains about 205 calories per serving.
