CROCK-POT PATTIES


2  lb. hamburger
1  c. onion, diced
1/2  c. cracker crumbs
1  egg
1/2  c. Cheddar cheese, shredded
2  Tbsp. catsup
2  Tbsp. cream
  salt and pepper to taste
1  c. cream of mushroom soup

	Mix all but last ingredient together and form into patties. Roll in flour and brown in oil. Place in crock-pot in layers. Cover each layer with soup. Cook 4 hours on high.
