BARBECUED VENISON


2  slices bacon
1  lb. boneless venison, cut in 1-inch cubes
1/2  c. chopped onion
1/2  c. catsup
1/4  c. red wine vinegar
2  Tbsp. packed brown sugar
2  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  clove garlic, minced
  hot cooked rice

	Cook bacon until crisp; drain, reserving drippings. Crumble bacon; set aside. Cook meat and onion in drippings until meat is browned. Drain; set aside. In saucepan, combine catsup, vinegar, sugar, Worcestershire, garlic, 1/2 cup water, 1 teaspoon salt and 1/8 teaspoon pepper. Stir in venison, onion and bacon. Cover; cook until venison is tender, 45 to 55 minutes. Serve on rice. Serves 4.
