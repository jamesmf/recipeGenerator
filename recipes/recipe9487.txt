SANDWICH MAIN DISH


4  hamburger buns
  lunch meat or Spam
1  c. grated cheese
3  eggs
2  Tbsp. onions
  salt to taste
  pepper to taste
3  c. sweet milk

	Cut buns in half. Put meat slice in center of bun. Place in Pyrex dish. Sprinkle cheese over and finely chopped onion. Mix eggs and milk together, then pour over buns and bake in 350 degrees; oven for 45 minutes. Serve with salad and French fries.
