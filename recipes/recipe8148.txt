CHICKEN TORTELLINI ALFREDO


1 (9 oz.)  pkg. Di Giorno tortellini (any flavor)
2  c. broccoli flowerets
1 (10 oz.)  pkg. Di Giorno Alfredo sauce
1 (6 oz.)  pkg. Louis Rich grilled chicken breast strips

      Prepare pasta as directed on package, adding broccoli to water during last 3 minutes of pasta cooking time; drain.
      Heat sauce and chicken breast strips in saucepan on medium heat 5 minutes or until thoroughly heated. Toss all ingredients. Makes 3 servings.
