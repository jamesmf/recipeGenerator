PINEAPPLE CARROT SLAW


4  slices Dole pineapple in juice (one 8 oz. can)
4  Tbsp. pineapple juice
4  c. shredded cabbage
3  oz. shredded carrots (approximately 2 carrots)
2  Tbsp. mayonnaise
1/2  tsp. dill weed

      Cut pineapple into chunks.  Combine cabbage, carrots and pineapple chunks.  Mix together mayonnaise and dill weed until well blended.  Toss with salad.  Makes 2 servings.
