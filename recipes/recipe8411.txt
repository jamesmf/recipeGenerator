CHICKEN HAWAII


4  boneless chicken breasts
1/2  c. flour
1/3  c. salad oil
1  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper

 
Sauce:
1 large  can sliced pineapple in heavy syrup
1  c. sugar
2  Tbsp. cornstarch
3/4  c. cider vinegar
1  Tbsp. ginger
1  cube chicken bouillon
1  large green pepper, cut into rings

      Wash chicken and pat dry.  Dredge in flour and brown in hot oil.  Remove when brown to shallow roasting pan.  Sprinkle with salt and pepper.  Heat oven to 350 degrees;.  Make sauce.  Drain pineapple.  Add enough water to make 1 1/4 cups.  In medium saucepan, combine ingredients, except pineapple and pepper rings. Bring to a boil.  Boil 2 minutes.  Pour over chicken.  Bake 30 minutes; add pineapple and green pepper and bake for 30 minutes. May be served with rice.
