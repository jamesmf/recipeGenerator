ZUCCHINI HOT DISH


1  lb. hamburger
  chopped onion
1/2  tsp. garlic powder
1  lb. zucchini, cut in 1/4-inch cubes
8  oz. shredded Cheddar cheese
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1/4  can of water

      Brown hamburger and onion. Add garlic powder; drain. Boil cubed zucchini no longer than 5 minutes. Spray casserole with Pam. Layer hamburger, zucchini and 4 ounces of cheese; repeat. Mix soup and water. Pour over top. Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees; for 40 minutes.
