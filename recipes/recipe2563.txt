SOUPERBURGER


1  lb. ground beef
1/2  c. chopped onion
1  Tbsp. shortening
1  can Campbell's chicken gumbo, golden mushroom, tomato, vegetable or cream of mushroom soup
1  Tbsp. prepared mustard
  dash of pepper
6  buns, split and toasted

      In  skillet,  brown  beef  and cook onion in shortening until tender;  stir to separate meat.  Pour off fat.  Add soup and seasonings.  Cook 5 minutes;  stir now and then.  Serve on buns. Makes 6 sandwiches.
