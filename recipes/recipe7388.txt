CHICKEN AND RICE CASSEROLE


2 to 3  lb. boneless chicken breasts
2  cans cream of chicken soup
2  to 4 stalks celery, sliced thin
1  c. milk
2  c. uncooked rice
  salt and pepper or Molly McButter

      Spray large casserole dish with no-stick spray.  Mix soup, rice, celery and milk in baking dish.  Layer chicken breasts (skin side up) on top of mixture.  Season to taste.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 1/2 hours.  Uncover the last 1/2 hour.
