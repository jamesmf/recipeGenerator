SPAGHETTI SALAD


1  box thin spaghetti
1  cucumber, chopped fine
1  small red onion, chopped fine
2  medium ripe tomatoes, chopped
1  jar McCormick Salad Supreme salad mix
1 (16 oz.)  bottle Wish-Bone dressing

      Cook and drain spaghetti.  Rinse with cold water.  Mix rest of ingredients together and mix with spaghetti.  Let stand overnight.
