CHOPPED ITALIAN SALAD

Salad:
1  head romaine lettuce, cut into 1-inch squares
1/4  lb. pepperoni, chopped
1/3  c. drained sliced pimentos (one 4 oz. jar)
1/3  c. chopped red onion
1 1/2  c. drained, rinsed and chopped canned artichoke hearts (one 15 oz. can)

      Toss to combine all of the above ingredients.
Dressing:
3  Tbsp. red or white wine vinegar
1/4  c. olive oil
1/2  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
1/2  c. grated Parmesan cheese

      Mix dressing ingredients and toss with salad.
