RAMEN BROCCOLI SLAW


1  lb. pkg. broccoli slaw
2  pkg. Ramen noodles (chicken flavored)
1 small  pkg. slivered almonds
1/2  c. sunflower seeds
3 small  bunches green onions (optional)
1/2  c. sugar
1/2  c. vinegar
1/2  c. cooking oil (of choice)

	Mix together broccoli slaw, noodles (broken into small pieces), almonds, sunflower seeds, green onions and seasoning from noodles; set aside until ready to serve.
	Mix sugar, vinegar and oil together. Mix everything together when ready to serve.
