TACO SOUP


1  lb. ground beef
1  pkg. taco seasoning mix
1  onion, chopped
2 15  oz. cans corn
2 15  oz. cans kidney beans
2 4  oz. cans jalapeno peppers
1 46  oz. can V-8 juice

      Brown ground beef in a soup pot. Drain. Add rest of ingredients and bring to a simmer, with lid on simmer for 4 hours.
