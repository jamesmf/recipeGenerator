HOT CRANBERRY PUNCH


2 1/4  c. pineapple juice
1 3/4  c. water
2  c. cranberry juice
1  tsp. whole cloves
1 1/2  tsp. whole allspice
1/4  tsp. salt
1 1/2  c. brown sugar

      Put all juices into a 9 cup percolator.  Place spices and brown sugar into basket.  Bring to a boil and percolate for 10 minutes at simmer position.  Remove spices before serving.
