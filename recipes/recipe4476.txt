CHICKEN TORTILLA SOUP


1  can chicken (can use chicken breast instead)
1  can fiesta nacho soup
1  can enchilada sauce
1  can cream of chicken soup
1  c. milk

      Place all ingredients into pot or crock-pot. Stir well and heat thoroughly. Serve with tortilla chips on top and shredded Cheddar cheese.
      Flour tortilla shells can also be cut into strips and put on top of soup.
