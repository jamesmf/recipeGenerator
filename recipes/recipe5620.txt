NANA'S TRIPLE FRUIT SALAD


2  sm. pkgs. strawberry Jell-O
1 lrg or 2 sm.  cans cranberry jelly
10  oz. pkg. frozen strawberries, thawed
8  oz. can crushed or chunk pineapple, reserve juice

      Add water to the reserved pineapple juice to make 1 cup. Bring to a boil.
       Dissolve Jell-o in the boiling juice.  Blend in the cranberry jelly.  Beat until smooth.  Sir in thawed strawberries and pineapple.
      Mold in 2 quart mold or 9 x 13-inch pan.  Chill.
      Top with salad dressing (Miracle Whip) mixed with lemon juice and Cool Whip.
      Great with turkey!
