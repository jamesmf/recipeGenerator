BROCCOLI SALAD


2  bunches broccoli
1  bunch scallions
7  pieces bacon
2  c. Cheddar cheese
1/4  c. mayo
1/4  c. sugar
1 1/2  tsp. vinegar

      Mix mayo, sugar and vinegar. Wash broccoli; cut into pieces. Cut scallions into pieces. Cook bacon; cut into pieces. Mix together. Put cheese and mixture of mayo over top. Mix well.
