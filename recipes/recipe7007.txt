BAKED FISH AND VEGETABLES


2 to 3  lb. fish fillets
1/4  stick margarine
  salt and pepper
  TexJoy steak seasoning (or Tony Chachere's Creole Seasoning)
  potatoes, as desired
  carrots, as desired
1  onion, sliced
1  bell pepper, sliced
  garlic powder, to taste

      Place fish in iron skillet with margarine. Season fish lightly with TexJoy steak seasoning); sprinkle parsley flakes over fish. Peel and slice potatoes and carrots (use amount desired) around and over fish. Place sliced onion and bell pepper over all and sprinkle with more parsley flakes. Cover and bake at 400 degrees; until potatoes and carrots are done. Mighty good!
