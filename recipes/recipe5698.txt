HEIDI'S TART GARLIC MARINADE


3/4  c. vinegar
3/4  c. vegetable oil
1/4  c. hot sauce
1/2 to 3/4  c. soy sauce
1/4 to 1/2  c. Worcestershire sauce
1/2  c. dry vermouth
1/4  c. honey
1/4  c. wine vinegar
5  cloves garlic, crushed
1 1/2  tsp. dry mustard
  salt to taste
3/4  tsp. cracked black pepper

	Combine all ingredients in a saucepan and simmer 10 minutes. Store in refrigerator. Use with steak or poultry. Makes about 4 cups.
