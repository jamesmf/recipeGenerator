CARE-A-LOT CANDY


2  Tbsp. soft butter
1  Tbsp. maple syrup
1  c. powdered sugar
1/2  c. granola

      Mash butter.  Add syrup.  Pat sugar in little at a time.  When you cannot stir, use your hands.  Squeeze and pound candy until soft and smooth.  Pour in granola.  Mix with hands.  Make into small balls.  Pour into refrigerator.  Leave 1 hour.
