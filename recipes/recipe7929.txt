SHRIMP FRIED RICE


1  carrot, shredded
3  green onions, chopped
1  cup cooked shrimp
6  Tbsp. oil
2  eggs
3  cups cooked rice
2  Tbsp. soy sauce
1  cup cooked peas

      Heat 2 Tbsp. of oil in a skillet.  Fry eggs and reserve.
      Heat remaining 4 Tbsp. oil in a large skillet.  Add green onions and stir-fry until tender.  Add rice, soy sauce, shrimp, and carrots, stirring after each addition.  Add peas and eggs; stir-fry for one minute.
      Makes 3-5 servings.
