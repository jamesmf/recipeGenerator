AREQUIPE DESSERT


3  qt. milk
2  lb. sugar
  pinch of salt
1/4  tsp. baking soda
2  egg yolks
  cinnamon stick

      Combine ingredients in a saucepan and simmer until thickened. Stir constantly, preferably with a wooden spoon, until you see the saucepan while stirring (4 to 5 hours).  Add the egg yolks and let cook 2 or 3 minutes longer.  Remove cinnamon stick.  Let cool. This recipe must be watched constantly.
