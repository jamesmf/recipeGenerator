BEAUTIFUL BUTTERFLIED SHRIMP


2 1/2  lb. shrimp (15 to 20 count)
2  eggs, beaten
1  c. flour
1  tsp. paprika
3/4  tsp. salt
1/2  pt. milk or more

      Shell and clean shrimp.  Split down the back to butterfly, leaving tails on.  Make batter by combining remaining ingredients. Dip shrimp in batter, a few at a time.  Cook in deep fryer of hot oil at 375 degrees; for 3 minutes.  Serves 6 to 8.
