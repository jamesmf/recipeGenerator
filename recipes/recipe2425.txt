BLACK WALNUT CAKE


1  stick margarine, softened
1/2  c. vegetable oil
2  c. sugar
5  eggs, beaten
2  c. flour
1  tsp. soda
1  c. buttermilk
1 small  can coconut
1  c. black walnuts
1  tsp. maple flavoring

      Cream margarine and vegetable oil.  Add sugar and eggs. Combine flour and soda.  Add alternately with buttermilk.  Stir in maple flavoring.  Add coconut and nuts.  Bake in 3 greased and floured 8-inch pans at 350 degrees; for 25 to 35 minutes.
Frosting for Black Walnut Cake:
12  oz. pkg. cream cheese
3/4  stick margarine
2  tsp. maple flavoring
1 1/2  boxes confectioners sugar
1  c. black walnuts

      Cream the cream cheese and margarine.  Stir in other ingredients and frost cake.
