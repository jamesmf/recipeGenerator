SALSA


4  tomatoes (fresh)
3  bunches of green onions, sliced
1/2  red onion, chopped
2  to 3 jalapenos peppers, diced fine
1  lemon, juice only
1  tsp. garlic, chopped
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. pepper
1 to 2  Tbsp. cilantro (to taste)

      Boil tomatoes and then peel and chop.  Add rest of ingredients and blend to the desired texture.
