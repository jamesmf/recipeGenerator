CHEESY MOSTACCIOLI


1  16 oz bag mostaccioli pasta, uncooked
1-1/2  lbs ground beef
28  oz spaghetti sauce
11 oz  can of condensed cheddar cheese soup
1  tsp ground black pepper
1  tsp dried Italian seasoning
3  c (12 oz) shredded mozzarella cheese

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees;.  Cook pasta according to directions, drain.  In medium skillet, cook and stir beef until browned. Remove from heat, drain. Stir in sauce, soup, pepper and seasoning.  In greased 4 qt baking dish, combine pasta, sauce mix and 2 cups of cheese.  Sprinkle remaining cup of cheese on top. Bake 40 minutes.
      Serves 10-12.
