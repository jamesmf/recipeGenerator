MARGE'S WALDORF SALAD


2  c. diced tart apples
1  Tbsp. sugar
1/2  tsp. lemon juice
  Dash of salt
1  c. celery, diced
1/2  c. broken walnuts
1  c. red seedless grapes, halved
1/4  c. mayonnaise
1/2  c. whipping cream, whipped

      Sprinkle apple cubes with sugar, lemon juice and salt.  Add celery, nuts and grapes.  Fold mayonnaise into whipped cream. Gently fold into apple mixture.  Makes 6 servings.
