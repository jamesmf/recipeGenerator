CREAMY FRUIT SALAD


1 (11 oz.)  can mandarin oranges, drained
1 (8 1/4 oz.)  can peaches, drained
1 (8 oz.)  can pineapple chunks, drained
1  c. miniature marshmallows
4  oz. cream cheese, softened
1/2  c. plain yogurt
1/4  c. sugar

      In a bowl, combine peaches, oranges, pineapple and marshmallows.  In a small bowl beat cream cheese, yogurt and sugar until smooth.  Pour over fruit and toss to coat.  Refrigerate for 15 minutes.  Yield 4 servings.
