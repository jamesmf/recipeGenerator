CHERRY COKE SALAD


3/4  c. water
3/4  c. sugar
1  can cherry pie filling
2  pkg. cherry jello
1 (16 oz.)  can crushed pineapple, drained
1  Tbsp. lemon juice
1  can Coke
1/2  c. chopped nuts

      Bring water and sugar to a boil; add cherry pie filling. Bring to boil again. Add jello and stir until dissolved. Remove from heat; add pineapple, lemon juice, Coke and nuts. Stir together and refrigerate.
