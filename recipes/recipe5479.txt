BROCCOLI SLAW


1  pkg. broccoli slaw
1/2  c. chopped green pepper
1/2  c. chopped red pepper
5  green onions, chopped
1  pkg. Ramen Oriental noodles, toasted
1  pkg. sliced almonds, toasted

      Toast noodles and almonds for 10 minutes at 350 degrees;.
Dressing:
1/2  c. oil
1/4  c. sugar
2  Tbsp. vinegar
1  tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
1  tsp. Oriental seasoning from noodle pack

      Combine dressing with broccoli mixture.  Serve.
