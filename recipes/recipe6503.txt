HERBED STEAK


1  round steak
3  Tbsp. soy sauce
3  Tbsp. oil
3  Tbsp. ketchup
1 1/2  tsp. basil leaves
1  tsp. oregano leaves
1  Tbsp. garlic chips

      Mix all ingredients and brush all over steak. Salt and pepper. Refrigerate at least 5 hours; no longer than 24 hours. Cook at 250 degrees; for 3 hours.
