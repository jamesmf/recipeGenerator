BAKED CORNISH HENS


2  Cornish hens
  Tony Chachere's Creole Seasoning
1/2  c. water
2  c. wild rice

      Preheat oven to 350 degrees;.
      Split Cornish hens and sprinkle generously with Tony's Seasoning.  Pour water into a 9x13-inch aluminum foil-lined pan and place split hens in pan, preferable not touching.
      Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 hour and 15 minutes.
      Serve over wild rice.
