MUSTARD GLAZED FRUIT


1/4  c. butter or oleo
1/2  c. packed brown sugar
3  Tbsp. prepared mustard
1  can (1 lb.) sliced peaches, drained
1  can (14 1/2 oz.) pineapple chunks, drained
1  can (11 oz.) mandarin orange segments, drained
2  medium bananas,  peeled and cut into 1 inch pieces

	Melt butter in saucepan. Stir in sugar and mustard.  Heat to boiling, stirring occasionally.  Remove from heat and stir in fruits. Pour into an 8 x 8 x 2-inch baking dish.  Sprinkle lightly with nutmeg.  Bake 20 minutes at 375 degrees; or until all fruits are heated through.  Serve warm with ham, turkey, pork or cold cuts.
