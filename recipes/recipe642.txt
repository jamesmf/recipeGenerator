VANILLA FROZEN YOGURT


1  c. sugar
2  envelopes unflavored gelatin
  dash of salt
2  c. skim milk
5  c. plain low-fat yogurt
1  Tbsp. vanilla extract

      Combine sugar, gelatin and salt in a medium saucepan; add milk and let stand one minute.  Cook over low heat, stirring constantly, five minutes or until gelatin and sugar dissolve; let cool.  Stir in yogurt and vanilla; chill.  Pour mixture into freezer can of a gallon freezer.  Serve immediately or let ripen 1 hour.  Yields 10 1/2 cups; contains 84 calories per 1/2 cup serving.
