LENTIL LOAF


1  medium onion
1/2  tsp. sweet marjoram
2  c. cooked lentils
1  c. soy flour
  vegie-sal to taste
1/2  medium onion
1/2  tsp. savory
2  c. mashed potatoes
1  c. wheat germ

      Steam onion with the savory and marjoram.  Add remaining ingredients and mix well.  Bake in a loaf pan at 350 degrees; for 1 hour. Cool a few minutes and turn out of pan onto serving platter. Garnish and serve.
