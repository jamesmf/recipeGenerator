PATIO BEANS


6  slices bacon, cooked and crumbled
1  medium onion, chopped and saut√©ed in bacon drippings
1 (16 oz.)  can baked beans
1 (15 oz.)  can kidney beans
1 (15 oz.)  can lima beans
4  oz. sharp Cheddar cheese, cubed
1/2  c. brown sugar
1/2  c. ketchup

      In a 9 x 13 baking dish, combine beans, bacon, onion, cheese, brown sugar and ketchup.  Bake at 375 degrees; for 20 to 25 minutes, until mixture bubbles.
