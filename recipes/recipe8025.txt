WILD RICE HOT DISH


1 1/2  lb. hamburger
  onion
  garlic
  celery
1  c. cooked wild rice
1  can cream of mushroom soup
1  can cream chicken rice soup
1  can water
1  Tbsp. soy sauce
  mushrooms
  water chestnuts

      Brown hamburger.  Add onion, garlic and celery.  Add remaining ingredients.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 1 1/2 hours.
