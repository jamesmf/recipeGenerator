BABA GHANOUJ


1  medium eggplant
2  cloves garlic
  salt to taste
3  Tbsp. tahina
  juice of 2 lemons
2  Tbsp. water

      Pierce the eggplant and either broil in a barbecue or bake in the oven until wrinkled and soft.  Place under cold running water and peel.  Mash in a bowl.  Pound the garlic with salt, then add tahina, lemon juice and water.  Add this mixture to the mashed eggplant and mix well.  Spread on a platter and decorate with parsley leaves.  Serves 10 to 12.
