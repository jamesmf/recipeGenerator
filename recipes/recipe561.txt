ALOHA PUNCH


2 (6 oz.)  cans frozen unsweetened orange juice
1 1/2  c. water
1 (8 1/2 oz.)  can dietetic pineapple tidbits and juice
1/3  c. lemon juice
2  tsp. liquid sweetener
2  qt. low calorie ginger ale

      Combine orange juice, water, pineapple and juice, lemon juice and liquid artificial sweetener in serving container.  Just before serving, add ice cubes and ginger ale.
