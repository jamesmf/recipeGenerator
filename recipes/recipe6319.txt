CORNBREAD SALAD


1  box Jiffy Corn Muffin Mix
1  lb. bacon
1/2  cup sweet pickles, chopped
1  bell pepper, chopped fine
4  tomatoes, chopped fine
1  medium onion, chopped fine
1  cup mayonnaise
1/2  cup sweet pickle juice

      Prepare muffin mix according to directions and bake. Fry bacon until crisp; cool and crumble. Lightly toss all the chopped vegetables and bacon together; set aside.
       Combine the mayonnaise and pickle juice. Sprinkle with salt and pickles. Spread half the vegetable mixture on top of cornbread.  Drizzle half of the mayonnaise/pickle mixture on top of vegetable mixture. Repeat layering. Chill overnight.
