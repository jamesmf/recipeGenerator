TEXAS AVOCADO TEA SANDWICHES


1  ripe avocado
1/2  tsp. lemon juice
2  Tbsp. chopped toasted almonds
2  Tbsp. crisp, crumbled bacon
  dash of black pepper
  dash of cayenne
  dash of chili powder

      Mash avocado; add lemon juice. Stir in almonds and bacon. Add seasonings. Mix to creamy smoothness and spread between slices of tea sandwich bread.
