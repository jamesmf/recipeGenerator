SKYLER'S BEST BARBECUE SAUCE


1 1/2  c. brown sugar
1 1/2  c. ketchup
1  c. Jim Beam whiskey
1/2  c. red wine vinegar
1/2  c. water
1  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  Tbsp. liquid smoke
2 1/2  Tbsp. dry mustard
2  tsp. paprika
2  tsp. salt
1 1/2  tsp. black pepper
2  dashes hot pepper sauce

      In a blender, combine brown sugar, ketchup, whiskey, vinegar, water and Worcestershire sauce. Season with liquid smoke, mustard, paprika, salt, pepper and hot pepper sauce. Blend until smooth. Transfer to a medium saucepan. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes.
