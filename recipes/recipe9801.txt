POTATO CRUST QUICHE

Crust:
4  cups shredded raw potatoes (about 4 large)
1/2  cup diced onion
1  egg, beaten
1  cup flour
1/2  tsp. salt

 
Filling:
1  1/2 cups (6 oz.) shredded colby cheese (divided)
1/2  cup chopped onion
1  1/2 cups diced ham
1  1/2 cups broccoli
3  beaten eggs
1  cup light cream
1/2  tsp. salt
  dash of nutmeg and paprika

      Combine the crust ingredients.  Press into a well greased 10 inch deep pie plate.  Bake for 20 minutes at 400 degrees.  Remove. Reduce the oven to 350 degrees.
      Add 1 cup of the colby cheese, the onion, ham, and broccoli to the crust.  Mix the eggs, cream, salt, and nutmeg.  Pour over the other ingredients.  Sprinkle with paprika and bake for 60 minutes or until set.  Remove from the oven and top with 1/2 cup of the remaining cheese.  Let stand 5 minutes before serving.
