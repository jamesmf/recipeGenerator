PIMENTO AND CHEESE


12  oz. sharp Cheddar Cheese
4  oz. jar diced Pimentos
  Mayonnaise

      Grate cheese until finely grated.  Chop or crush pimentos into very small pieces.  Mix together with mayonnaise until creamy enough to spread or dip.
      To make more use: 1 lb. cheese, 6 oz. pimento and mayonnaise.
