CREAM OF ANYTHING SOUP


1/2  c. butter or margarine
1/2  c. flour
2 (14 1/2 oz.)  cans evaporated milk
  cheese (optional)
1 to 2  c. chopped meat or vegetables
2 to 3  c. broth

      Make a roux of butter and flour; add milk and make a thick white sauce.  If cheese is used, add here and whisk to melt.  Mix in leftover cooked vegetables or meat; stir until smooth.  Add broth to desired consistency.  Season to taste.
