SPINACH DIP


10  oz. (1 box) spinach (frozen), chopped
1/2  c. mayonnaise
8  oz. (1 tub) sour cream
1  c. water chestnuts, chopped
3  green onions, chopped
1 1/4  oz. (1 pkg.) vegetable soup mix (Knorr)

      Mix all ingredients together.  Chill mixture.
      Serve with chips or homemade bread.
