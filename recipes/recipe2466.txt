HOT CHOCOLATE MIX


1 (16 oz.)  jar Coffee-mate
1  (1 lb. 9 oz.) dry milk powder
2  lb. Nestle Quik
1  lb. powdered sugar

      Mix all ingredients together.  Try the different Coffee-mate flavors for "gourmet" flavors.  Makes great gifts when put into pretty containers.  Takes approximately 1/4 cup mix to 1 cup hot water.  Store in airtight container.
