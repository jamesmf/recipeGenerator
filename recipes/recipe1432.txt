CORNISH GAME HENS


4  Cornish game hens

 
Seasoning:
1/2  tsp. salt
1/2  tsp. garlic powder
1/2  tsp. chili powder

 
Sauce:
1/2  c. apple jelly
1/2  c. catsup
1  Tbsp. vinegar
1/2  tsp. chili powder

      Rub hens inside and out with seasonings and place on grill. Cook 1 hour or until tender, basting frequently during last 1/2 hour with sauce.  Serves 4.
