CORNBREAD SALAD


2  pkg. cornbread mix or your own cornbread recipe
12  slices crisp bacon, crumbled
1  bell pepper, diced
1  onion, diced
1  fresh tomato, chopped
1  c. Miracle Whip
  salt to taste

      Crumble cornbread. Add bacon, pepper, onion and Miracle Whip. Then add salt and tomatoes. Mix gently.
