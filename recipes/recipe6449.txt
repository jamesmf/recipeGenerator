TACO SALAD


1  lb. hamburger
1  bar cream cheese
1  pkg. taco seasoning
1  tub sour cream
  shredded cheddar cheese
  Taco shells or chips
  lettuce
  diced tomatoes

      Brown hamburger.  In a large bowl mix cream cheese, taco seasoning and sour cream together.   Stir in the hamburger.  Layer everything in a dish.  Serve with Taco shells or chips.
