CONCH CHOWDER


4  medium conchs
1  stalk of celery
1  can of celery soup
2  tsp. thyme
2  medium potatoes
2  Tbsp. A.1. sauce
2  large carrots
2  Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1  large onion
  water

      Bruise and boil conchs.  Cut into small pieces.  Fry out onion and celery in 1 tablespoon oil.  Add sauces and water.  Add rest of ingredients.  Boil for 1 hour.
