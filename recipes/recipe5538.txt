MACARONI SALAD


1  lb. macaroni, cooked and cooled
1  large onion
1  can Eagle brand milk
1  c. sugar
3/4  c. vinegar
4  large carrots, grated
1  large green pepper, chopped
2  c. mayonnaise
1  tsp. salt
1-1/2  tsp. celery seed

      Mix milk, sugar, vinegar, mayonnaise, salt and celery seed. Pour over macaroni, onion, carrots and  pepper. Will keep in refrigerator for 1 week and can be frozen.
