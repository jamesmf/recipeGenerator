


5  eggs
6  slices fresh white bread, cubed
1  tsp. salt
1  tsp. dry mustard
1  lb. sausage
1 (8 oz.)  pkg. grated Cheddar cheese
2  c. milk

      Crumble, brown and drain sausage. In a mixer, beat eggs. Add milk, salt and mustard. Stir in cubed bread and add cheese and the browned sausage. Put into an 8 x 12 Pyrex dish. Place in the refrigerator overnight. Bake 45 minutes at 350 degrees;. Sit out a few minutes before cutting into squares. Serves 8.
      This is a very good breakfast casserole that can be made ahead.
