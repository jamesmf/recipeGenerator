CHICKEN ENCHILADAS


2  c. cooked chicken, chopped
1/4  c. onion, minced
1 (4 oz.)  can chopped green chilies
2  c. shredded Colby/Monterey Jack cheese, divided
1  c. (8 oz.) sour cream
1 (10 3/4 oz.)  can cream of chicken soup
10  flour tortillas

      Combine chicken, onion, green chilies and 1 1/2 cups cheese. In a separate bowl, mix soup and sour cream. Stir 1/2 cup of soup/cream sauce into chicken mixture. Fill tortillas with 1/4 cup mixture and roll. Place enchiladas in baking dish. Top with remaining sauce and cheese. Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees; for 30 minutes or until bubbly. Serves 5.
