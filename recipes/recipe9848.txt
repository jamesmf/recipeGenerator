


2  lb. zucchini or yellow summer squash
3  Tbsp. chopped onion
3  eggs, beaten
1/2  tsp. Tabasco sauce
2  tsp. parsley flakes
  salt and pepper to taste
1/2  c. butter or margarine, melted
2  c. cracker crumbs

      Slice squash in 1/2-inch pieces.  Boil 3 minutes or until tender.  Drain; add onion, eggs and seasonings.  Mix until well blended.  Pour into 1-quart buttered casserole.  Mix butter and crumbs; sprinkle over squash.  Bake at 350 degrees; for 35 to 40 minutes until browned.  Makes 6 servings.
