MEXICAN SPAGHETTI


1  chopped bell pepper
1  chopped onion
1  Tbsp. Crisco or oil
1  lb. ground meat
1 small  jar pimento
1 small  can chopped olives
1  (8 oz.) tomato sauce
1  can Cheddar cheese soup
1 small  pkg. spaghetti, broken in small pieces
1  tsp. chili powder
1/2  tsp. salt
1  lb. grated cheese (Cheddar)

      Saut√© pepper, onion, hamburger meat and seasonings. Cook until well done. Then add other ingredients. Cook pasta until tender, then add all other ingredients and mix together. Put in casserole. Cover with cheese. Bake at 350 degrees; for 15 to 20 minutes until cheese melts.
